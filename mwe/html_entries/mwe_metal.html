METAL [497-b], s. DAtuH m., lohaH, tEjasaM, suvarRarUpyAdi n.; ‘base metal,’ kupyaM; ‘factitious metal,’ kftrimaDAtuH m.; ‘fused,’ rasaDAtuH m., rasaH; ‘inferior,’ upaDAtuH m.; ‘melting metals together,’ Avarttana; ‘the metals collectively,’ DAtujAtaM, azwaDAtu n. The eight metals are, ‘gold,’ suvarRaM; ‘silver,’ rUpyaM; ‘copper,’ tAmraM; ‘brass,’ pittalaM; ‘bell-metal, or a kind of pewter,’ kAMsyaM; ‘tin,’ trapu; ‘lead,’ sIsakaM; ‘iron,’ lohaH. Some make ‘steel,’ tIkzRAyasaM, one of the eight metals, instead of kAMsyaM.
