THROUGHOUT [802-b], adv. (From the beginning to the end) sAdyantaM, AdyantaM, SezaparyyantaM. — (Of time) expressed by paryyantaM, yAvat or A in comp.; thus, ‘throughout the month,’ mAsaparyyantaM; ‘throughout the year,’ varzaparyyantaM; ‘throughout the whole year,’ AvarzAntAt or varzAntaM yAvat (i. e. to the end of the year); ‘throughout life,’ yAvajjIvaM, jIvanaparyyantaM, AjIvanAntAt.
