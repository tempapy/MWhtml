PASSIVE [575-b], a. (Suffering without resistance) sahanaḥ -nā -naṃ, saha -hā -haṃ, sahiṣṇuḥ -ṣṇuḥ -ṣṇu, sahanadharmmakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, sahanavṛttiḥ -ttiḥ tti, sahanaśīlaḥ -lā -laṃ, sahanadharmmā -rmmā -rmma (n), titikṣuḥ -kṣuḥ -kṣu, ani- vārakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, apratīkārakaḥ &c. — (Opposed to active, not an actor) niśceṣṭaḥ -ṣṭā -ṣṭaṃ, niṣkriyaḥ -yā -yaṃ, apravarttakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, kriyāśūnyaḥ -nyā -nyaṃ, karmmapātraṃ, karmmādhāraḥ, karmmāśrayaḥ. — (In grammar, the passive voice) karmmaṇivācyaṃ, karmmaṇi ātmarnepadaṃ; ‘reflective passive,’ karmmaṇikarttṛvācyaṃ; ‘impersonal passive,’ bhāvavācyaṃ. The grammatical symbol for the passive is ḍhabhāvaḥ.
