TWILIGHT [815-b], s. sanDyA, sanDyAkAlaH, sanDyAsamayaH, sanDiprakASaH, vikAlaH -lakaH, sanDyAMSaH, utsUraH, sabaliH m., aDvAntaM, niSAdiH m. Twilight is personified as the daughter of Brahmā, and hence called pitfprasUH f., pitfsUH f., brahmaBUtiH f.; ‘the morning twilight,’ pUrvvasanDyA, prAksanDyA, sanDyA, dinAdiH m.; ‘the two twilights,’ sanDye f. du.
