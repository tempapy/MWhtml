(-śleṣayati -yituṃ), sandhā, saṃlagnīkṛ. — (Tie) yoktra (nom. yoktayati -yituṃ), granth (c. 9. grathnāti, c. 1. granthati -nthituṃ). — (Tie on) pinah (c. 4. -nahyati -naddhuṃ), ānah, ābandh, anubandh. — (Impress) praṇidhā. — (Make firm) dṛḍhīkṛ, sthirīkṛ, saṃstambh (c. 5. -stabhnoti -stambhituṃ, c. 10. -stambhayati -yituṃ).
