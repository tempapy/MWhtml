PIQUE [594-a], s. īṣatkopaḥ, īṣadvairaktyaṃ, īṣadviraktiḥ f., īṣadroṣaḥ, īṣadvituṣṭiḥ. To PIQUE [594-a], v. a. (Offend slightly) īṣat kup (c. 10. kopayati -yituṃ) or prakup or ruṣ (c. 10. roṣayati -yituṃ), īṣatkopaṃ or īṣadvairaktyaṃ jan. — (Stimulate, excite) uttap, uttij, praruc, pravṛt, utsah, protsah. — (Pique one's self) ātmānaṃ ślāgh (c. 1. ślāghate -ghituṃ), abhiman (c. 4. -manyate -mantuṃ), vikatth (c. 1. -katthate -tthituṃ); ‘to pique one's self on one's learning,’ paṇḍitammanyaḥ -nyā -nyaṃ bhū or as; ‘on one's good looks,’ darśanīyamānī &c. bhū.
