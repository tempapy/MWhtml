GROSSLY [322-b], adv. (Coarsely, not finely) sthūlaṃ, ghanaṃ, sāndraṃ, niviḍaṃ. — (Not delicately) aśaddhaṃ, asabhyaṃ, aśiṣṭaṃ, avinītaṃ, vinayavirodhena, maryyādāviruddhaṃ. — (Greatly, enormously) atyantaṃ, atiśayena, ati- mātraṃ, ati or mahā prefixed; as, ‘grossly indecent,’ atigarhitaḥ -tā -taṃ; ‘grossly criminal,’ mahāpātakī m. (n).
