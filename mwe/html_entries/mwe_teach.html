To TEACH [796-b], v. a. upadiś (c. 6. -diśati -deṣṭuṃ), śikṣ (c. 10. śikṣayati -yituṃ), anuśikṣ, abhiśikṣ, adhī in caus. (adhyāpayati -yituṃ, rt. i), paṭh in caus., śās &c., see To INSTRUCT. Sometimes the causal expresses the sense of ‘teach;’ as, ‘he teaches to dance,’ narttayati; ‘he teaches to read,’ pāṭhayati.
