BECAUSE OF [043-a], prep. (By reason of) hetoH, hetO, kAraRAt. — (For the sake of) arTaM, arTe, kfte, nimitte affixed to a crude noun; ‘because of that,’ anena hetunA, iti hetoH. ‘Because of’ is often expressed by the abl. c. of the noun; as, ‘be- cause of their unbelief,’ tezAm aviSvAsAt.
