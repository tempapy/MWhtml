BHAVĀNĪ [049-b], s. (The wife of the god Śiva, and the goddess of destruction; called Bhavānī in her amiable form) BavAnI, umA, mahAdevI, devI. — (The pure one) gOrI. — (As terrible in form and irascible in temper) durgA, kAlI, caRqI, caRqikA. — (As daughter of Himālā, the sovereign of the snowy mountains) pArvvatI, hEmavatI. — (As wife of Śiva) SivA, ISvarI, rudrARI, sarvvARI, mfqAnI. — (The all-auspicious) sarvvamaNgalA. — (The mother) ambikA. — (As the performer of austerities) kAtyAyanI, aparRA.
