AGAINST [010-b], prep. prati, aBi. — (Opposed to) virudDaH -dDA -dDaM, pratikUlaH -lA -laM, vipakzaH -kzA -kzaM. — (Over against) aBimuKaM, pratimuKaM, sammuKaM; ‘against the wind,’ prativAtaM, vAtABimuKaM; ‘against the stream,’ pratisotas ind. ‘Against’ may be expressed by arTe or arTaM, in such phrases as ‘one should store up money against misfortune,’ ApadarTe DanaM rakzet.
