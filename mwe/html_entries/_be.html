To BE [041-a], v. n. (To be in some state) as (c. 2. asti), As (c. 2. Aste). — (To enter into some state) BU (c. 1. Bavati, BavituM). — (To exist) as, vft (c. 1. varttate, varttituM), vid in pass. (vidyate), jan in pass. (jAyate). — (To be in or at) vft, sTA (c. 1. tizWati, sTAtuM); ‘be it so,’ evam astu. The verb ‘to be’ is often expressed by the use of the passive form of the Sanskrit verb, and is not unfrequently omitted altogether.
