ET-CAETERA [238-a]. Expressed by AdiH -di in the m. or n. gender and in the plur. or sing. numb.; as, ‘parrots, starlings, &c.,’ SukasArikAdayaH; ‘Indra, Kuvera, &c.,’ indrakuverAdayaH; ‘Of Agni, Pavana, &c.,’ agnipavanAdInAM; ‘the nose, eyes, &c.,’ nAsacakzurAdIni; ‘peace, war, &c.’ sanDivigrahAdi; ‘by libe- -rality, &c.,’ dAtnAdinA; ‘from liberality, &c.,’ dAnAdeH (for dAnAdinaH). Sometimes iti is prefixed to Adi; as, ‘the word chitralig, &c.,’ citraligityAdi. Sometimes AdikaH -kA -kaM, AdyaH -dyA -dyaM and praBfti are used for Adi; as, ‘gifts, &c.,’ dAnAdikaM; ‘Indra, &c.,’ indrAdyAH, or indrapraBftayaH pl.
