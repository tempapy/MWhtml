MUST [522-b], v. n. (Be necessitated). Expressed by the fut. pass. part.; as, ‘anger must be restrained,’ kopo niyantavyaḥ; ‘I must go, or it is to be gone by me.’ mayā gantavyaṃ; ‘I must do that,’ tan mayā karttavyaṃ or karaṇīyaṃ or kāryyaṃ. The adverb avaśyaṃ may be added; as, tan mayā avaśyaṃ karttavyaṃ. Sometimes the verb arh (c. 1. arhati -rhituṃ) has the sense of ‘must;’ as, ‘he must not receive that,’ na tat prāptum arhati. MUST [522-b] s. (Wine just pressed from the grape) navamadyaṃ, nūtanamadyaṃ. To MUST [522-b], v. n. (Grow mouldy and sour) amlībhū, durgandhībhū, virasībhū.
