MUST [522-b], v. n. (Be necessitated). Expressed by the fut. pass. part.; as, ‘anger must be restrained,’ kopo niyantavyaH; ‘I must go, or it is to be gone by me.’ mayA gantavyaM; ‘I must do that,’ tan mayA karttavyaM or karaRIyaM or kAryyaM. The adverb avaSyaM may be added; as, tan mayA avaSyaM karttavyaM. Sometimes the verb arh (c. 1. arhati -rhituM) has the sense of ‘must;’ as, ‘he must not receive that,’ na tat prAptum arhati. MUST [522-b] s. (Wine just pressed from the grape) navamadyaM, nUtanamadyaM. To MUST [522-b], v. n. (Grow mouldy and sour) amlIBU, durganDIBU, virasIBU.
