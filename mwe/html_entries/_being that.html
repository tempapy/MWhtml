BEING THAT [045-a], conj. (Since) yatas, yasmAt, yena, iti hetoH, yatohetoH. ‘Being,’ is often expressed by the loc. c. of sat, as, ‘the man being gone,’ puruze gate sati; ‘it being so,’ evaM sati, taTAtve, evamBUtvA. ‘Being’ may also be expressed by the abl. c. of the abstract noun in such phrases as ‘he being a good man,’ sajjanatvAt.
