Seven oceans are fabled to exist by the Hindūs, viz. ‘the ocean of salt,’ lavaRodaH; ‘of syrup or sugar-cane juice,’ ikzurasodaH; ‘of spirituous liquor,’ surodaH; ‘of ghee or clarified butter,’ GftodaH; ‘of milk,’ kzIrodaH; ‘of curds,’ daDyudaH; ‘of pure water,’ SudDodaH; ‘churning of the ocean,’ samudramanTanaM. The mountain mandaraH is fabled to have been used by the Suras and Asuras in the churn- ing of the ocean after the deluge, for the recovery or production of fourteen sacred things.
