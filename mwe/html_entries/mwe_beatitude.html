(s) or niHSreyaH, amftatA, kEvalyaM. — (Feli- city) paramasuKaM, paramAnandaH, SreyastvaM, suKaM. — (Desirous of) mumukzuH. BEATITUDE [853-a], s. The Hindūs divide final beatitude into four kinds, viz. 1. ‘absorption into the essence of the deity,’ brahmasAyujyaM; 2. ‘residence with him,’ salokatA; 3. ‘nearness to him,’ samIpatA; 4. ‘conformedness to his image,’ sarUpatA.
