To CONDESCEND [119-b], v. n. (Lay aside dignity, yield, stoop) garvvaṃ or gauravaṃ or darpam apās (c. 4. -asyati -asituṃ), mānaṃ or abhimānaṃ yaj (c. 1. tyajati, nyaktuṃ), or utsṛj, (c. 1. -sṛjati -sraṣṭuṃ), namrībhū, ānatagarvvaḥ -rvvā -rvvaṃ bhū, astagauravaḥ -vā -vaṃ bhū. — (Submit) vaśībhū. — (Consent, concur with) anuman (c. 4. -manyate -mantuṃ), upagam (c. 1. -gacchati -gantuṃ), anujñā (c. 9. -jānāti -jñātuṃ), anuvṛt (c. 1. -varttate -rttituṃ). The 2d or 3d pers. pres. of the rt. arh is used in such phrases as. ‘condescend to speak to me,’ māṃvaktum arhasi or arhatibhavān.
