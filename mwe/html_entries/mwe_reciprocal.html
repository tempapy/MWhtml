RECIPROCAL [662-a], a. parasparaḥ -rā -raṃ, anyonyaḥ -nyā -nyaṃ, itaretaraḥ -rā -raṃ, vyatikaraḥ -rā -raṃ, itaretarāśrayaḥ -yā -yaṃ, mithas in comp. The sense of “reciprocal” is often expressed by the prepositions vi, ā, ava, ati, prati, pra in combination. See MUTUAL. — (Alternate, in reciprocal succession) parasparānugāmī -minī -mi (n), parasparānuvarttī &c., anyonyānugāmī &c., anyonyānuvarttī &c.
