To DESERVE [174-a], v. a. and n. arh (c. 1. arhati -te -rhituM), arhaH -rhA -rhaM or upayuktaH -ktA -ktaM or ucitaH -tA -taM or yogyaH -gyA -gyam as or BU; ‘he deserves honour,’ mAnam arhati; ‘he deserves punishment,’ daRqam arhati; ‘he deserves to drink soma juice,’ somaM pAtum arhati; ‘does he deserve so large a salary or not?’ kim upayukto'yam etAvad varttanaM gfhlAty anupayukto vA; ‘he deserves death,’ baDArho'sti, maraRocito'sti. The future pass. part. may sometimes be used in this sense; as, ‘he deserves death,’ baDyo'sti; ‘he deserves honour,’ mAnyo'sti, pUjanIyo'sti.
