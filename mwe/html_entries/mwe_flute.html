FLUTE [279-b], s. (Instrument) veṇuḥ m., vaṃśaḥ -śī, muralī, sāneyī -yikā, sānikā, vivaranālikā, darduraḥ, nandaḥ; ‘sound of the flute,’ veṇuśabdaḥ, veṇudhvaniḥ m., kṣojanaṃ. — (Channel of a column) stambharekhā, stambhasītā. To FLUTE [279-b], v. n. (Play on the flute) veṇuṃ or vaṃśīṃ dhmā (c. 1. dhamati, dhmātuṃ) or pradhmā or vad in caus. (vādayati -yituṃ) or saṃvad or pravad. To FLUTE [279-b], v. a. stambhaparito dīrgharekhāḥ kṛ or nikhan (c. 1. -khanati -nituṃ).
