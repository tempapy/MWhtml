LONGITUDE [465-a], s. drādhimā m. (n), dhruvakaḥ. — (Position to east or west) rekhāntaraṃ, rekhāṃśaḥ; ‘of a country,’ deśāntaraṃ. — (Of the earth) rekhāṃśaḥ, rekhāparidhiḥ m., rekhāgaṇaḥ, rekhāsamūhaḥ. — (Of any point or object in the heavens) bhogaḥ; ‘of the sun,’ ravibhogaḥ; ‘of a planet reckoned from the vernal equinoctial point,’ sāyanaṃ. — (Meridian or circle of longitude) yāmyottaravṛttaṃ, rekhāvṛttaṃ, rekhāṃśavṛttaṃ.
