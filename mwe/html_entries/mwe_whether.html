WHETHER [839-b], adv. kiM, kiMvA, vA, uta. The particle vA, either with or without kiM, usually expresses the sense of ‘whether,’ as in the following examples; ‘whether you kill me or whether you release me,’ mAM mArayata vA muYcata vA; ‘do you know whether he is virtuous or not?’ vetsi kiM DArmmiko na vA; ‘whether or not he does that,’ tat karotu na karotu vA. — (Whether of twain, which of two) kataraH -rA -rat.
