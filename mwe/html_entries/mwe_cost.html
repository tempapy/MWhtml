COST [139-a], s. (Price) mūlyaṃ, arghaḥ. — (Expense) vyayaḥ; ‘high cost,’ durmūlyaṃ, bahumūlyaṃ, māhārdhyaṃ; ‘little cost,’ alpamūlyaṃ, alpavyayaḥ; ‘at the cost of life,’ prāṇavyayena; ‘at the cost of a large sum,’ bahudhanavyayena. To COST [139-a], v. n. argh (c. 10. arghayati -yituṃ). Usually expressed by the inst. c. of mūlyaṃ or vyayaḥ in conjunction with some other verb; as, ‘what does it cost?’ kiyatā mūlyena vikrīyate; ‘the building of the house cost a large sum of money,’ bahudha- navyayena gṛhaṃ nirmmitaṃ.
