COST [139-a], s. (Price) mUlyaM, arGaH. — (Expense) vyayaH; ‘high cost,’ durmUlyaM, bahumUlyaM, mAhArDyaM; ‘little cost,’ alpamUlyaM, alpavyayaH; ‘at the cost of life,’ prARavyayena; ‘at the cost of a large sum,’ bahuDanavyayena. To COST [139-a], v. n. arG (c. 10. arGayati -yituM). Usually expressed by the inst. c. of mUlyaM or vyayaH in conjunction with some other verb; as, ‘what does it cost?’ kiyatA mUlyena vikrIyate; ‘the building of the house cost a large sum of money,’ bahuDa- navyayena gfhaM nirmmitaM.
