SOEVER [756-b]. Expressed by prefixing the relative to the interro- gative, with the affixes cit, api or cana; thus, ‘whosoever,’ yaH kaScit; ‘whatsoever,’ yat kiYcit; ‘wheresoever,’ yatra kutracit; ‘whensoever,’ yadA kadAcit, or by doubling the relative, thus yo yaH, yad yat, yatra yatra, yadA yadA; ‘any soever,’ kaScit, kAcit, kiYcit; ‘at any time whatsoever,’ kadAcit.
