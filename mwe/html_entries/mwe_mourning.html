MOURNING [518-a], s. (Sorrow, lamentation) SokaH, SAcanaM, anuSocanaM KedaH, vilapanaM, vilApaH, paridevanaM -nA, rodanaM, rudanaM, krandanaM, AkrandanaM, duHKaM. — (Dress worn by mourners) amaNgalavezaH, aSuBavezaH, amaNgalakAlInavezaH, aSuBakAlInavezaH, SokamUcakavezaH. According to the Hindu notion defilement is contracted by the death of a relation; hence the following terms, expressive of a season of mourning, imply a season of impurity as, aSOcaM, ASOcaM, kAlASOcaM, aGAhaH; ‘one in such a state,’ ASOcI -cinI -ci (n).
