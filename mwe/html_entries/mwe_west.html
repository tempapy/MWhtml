WEST [838-b], s. (The quarter of the sky) paścimā, paścimadiśā, paści- madik f. (ś), paścimāśā, pratīcī, aparā, caramā, vāruṇī, madhyaḥ -dhyaṃ. The god Varuna is fabled as the Regent of the West. WEST [838-b], WESTERLY, WESTERN, a. paścimaḥ -mā -maṃ, pratyaṅ -tīcī -tyak (ñc), pratīcyaḥ -cyā -cyaṃ, pratīcīnaḥ -nā -naṃ, caramaḥ -mā -maṃ, aparaḥ -rā -raṃ, pāścātyaḥ -tyā -tyaṃ. ‘Madyadesha is west of Prayāga,’ madhyadeśaḥ prayāgāt paścimaḥ; ‘as far as the western ocean,’ āpa- ścimasamudrāt. — (Facing the west) pratyaṅmukhaḥ -khī -khaṃ, paścimābhi- mukhaḥ &c.
