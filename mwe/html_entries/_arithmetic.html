ARITHMETIC [852-a], s. vyaktaM, vyaktagaRitaM, saMKyAvidyA; ‘the eight rules of arithmetic,’ parikarmmAzwakaM. These are, 1. Addition, saNkalanaM; 2. Subtraction, vyavakalanaM; 3. Multiplication, guRanaM; 4. Divi- sion, BajanaM; 5. Raising to the square, vargaH; 6. Extracting the square-root, vargapadaM; 7. Raising to the cube, GanaH; 8. Extracting the cube-root, GanapadaM.
