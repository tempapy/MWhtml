To ANCHOR [017-a], v. a. lauhayantreṇa nāvaṃ vandh (c. 9. badhnāti banddhuṃ) or sthirīkṛ. ANCHOR [851-b], s. aṅkuśākāradantadvayayuktaṃ naubandhanaṃ.
