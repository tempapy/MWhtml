He is the son of Kasyapa by Vinatā, and younger brother of Aruna) garuqaH, garutmAn m. (-tmat), tArkzyaH, KageSvaraH, suparRaH, vizRuraTaH, vEnateyaH, vinatAsUnuH m., nAgAntakaH, pannagASanaH, BujagadAraRaH, sitAnanaH, dakzAyyaH. The son of Garuda is called sampAtiH -tikaH.
