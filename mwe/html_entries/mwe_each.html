EACH [214-b], a. (All) sarvvaḥ -rvvā -rvvaṃ, viśvaḥ -śvā -śvaṃ, sakalaḥ -lā -laṃ; ‘Each’ is usually expressed by prati or anu prefixed; as, ‘each day,’ pratidinaṃ, pratidivasaṃ, anudinaṃ, anvahaṃ; ‘each night,’ pratirātraṃ; ‘each month,’ pratimāsaṃ; ‘each one,’ pratyekaṃ. ‘Or by doubling the word; as,’ ‘each day,’ dine dine, divase divase, aharahaḥ; ‘in each house,’ gṛhe gṛhe; ‘each one,’ ekaikaḥ -kā -kaṃ, ekaikaśas, pṛthak pṛthak; ‘each other,’ parasparaṃ -rāṃ, anyonyaṃ, itaretaraṃ; ‘each man,’ ekaikajanaḥ, pratijanaṃ; ‘in each direction,’ pratidiśaṃ, sarvvadikṣu; ‘each man related his own dream,’ ekaiko janaḥ svaṃ svaṃ svapnam akathayat.
