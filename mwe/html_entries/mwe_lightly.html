LIGHTLY [454-a], adv. (With little weight) laghu, lāghavena, salāghavaṃ, aguru, alpabhāreṇa, alpapramāṇena. — (Easily) sukhena, anāyāmena, akaṣṭena, akleśena, ayatnena. — (Without reason) hetunā vinā, kāraṇena vinā. — (Nimbly) laghugatyā, capalaṃ. — (With levity) laghucetasā, cañcalaṃ, cañcalavat, asthiraṃ; ‘to think lightly of,’ laghūkṛ, tucchīkṛ.
