It BEHOVES [045-a], v. imp. Expressed by the future passive parti- ciple; as, ‘it behoves you to marry the damsel,’ tvayā kanyā voḍhavyā; or by the potential mood, as, tvaṃ kanyāṃ vaheḥ; or by the use of the verb arh (c. 1. arhati), as tvaṃ kanyāṃ voḍhum arhasi; ‘to act as it behoves one,’ yathocitaṃ kṛ.
