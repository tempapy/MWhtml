To APPERTAIN [021-b], v. n. (To belong to one's self) AtmasAt or AtmADInaH -nA -naM, or svADInaH -nA -naM, or svakIyaH -yA -yaM as. — (To belong, in general) sambanG in pass. (-baDyate). The sense of ‘appertaining’ may often be expressed by the root as or vid with the gen. case: as, ‘money appertains to me,’ mama DanaM vidyate.
