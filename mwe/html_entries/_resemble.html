To RESEMBLE [681-a], v. a. (Be like) sadfSaH -SI -SaM BU or as, sadfSIBU, anukf (c. 8. -karoti -karttuM), tulyaH -lyA -lyaM BU, samaH -mA -maM BU, samAnaH -nA -naM BU, upamaH -mA -maM BU, sarUpaH -pA -paM BU, anurUpaH -pA -paM BU; ‘the son resembles his father,’ putraH pitur anukaroti or putraH pitfrUpo Bavati or putraH pitrA sadfSAkAro Bavati; ‘no one re- sembles him,’ anena sadfSaH kaScin nAsti or tasya tulyaH or tena tulyaH or tena samo na ko'pi Bavati. A nominal verb may some- times express the sense of ‘resemble,’ thus; ‘he resembles a lion,’ siMhAyate; ‘it resembles heaven,’ svargAyate. — (Liken) upamA (c. 2. -mAti, c. 3. -mimIte -mAtuM), sadfSIkf, samIkf, samAna (nom. samAnayati -yituM).
