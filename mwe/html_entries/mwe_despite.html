DESPITE [176-a], s. (Malice) dveṣaḥ, drohaḥ, mātsaryyaṃ, īrṣyā, abhyasūyā, durbuddhiḥ f., nṛśaṃsyaṃ, daurjanyaṃ, daṃśaḥ. — (Opposition) pratirodhaḥ, virodhaḥ, pratiyogaḥ, pratikāraḥ, pratikūlatā; ‘in despite,’ anicchayā, viruddhaṃ, prātikūlyena, or expressed by the gen. c. of the pres. part. of the root dṛś ‘to see, ’ or miṣ; ‘in despite of thee,’ paśyatas tava; ‘in despite of the princes,’ miṣatāṃ pārthivānāṃ. To DESPITE [176-a], v. a. kup in caus. (kopayati -yituṃ), ruṣ in caus. (roṣayati -yituṃ), tap in caus. (tāpayati -yituṃ), santap; kliś (c. 9. kliśnāti, kleṣṭuṃ), vyath in caus. (vyathayati -yituṃ), duḥkh (c. 10. duḥkhayati -yituṃ).
