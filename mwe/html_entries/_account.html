ACCOUNT [004-b], s. (A computation) gaRitaM, vigaRanaM, gaRanA -naM, saNKyAnaM, leKA. — (Value, estimation) mUlyaM, arGaH, sammAnaM. — (Great value) bahumatiH. — (Narrative, explanation) kaTA, kaTanaM, upAKyAnaM, yaTAtaTaM, yaTAsTitaM; ‘on this account,’ atas ind., anena hetunA, iti hetoH; ‘on what account?’ kena hetunA or kena kAryyeRa; ‘on account of,’ hetoH, hetO, arTaM or arTe, kfte, nimitte, kAraRAt with the crude form; or expressed by simply putting the noun in the abl. c.; as, ‘on account of anger,’ kopAt; or by the indec. part. of uddiS; as, ‘on which account,’ yaduddiSya.
