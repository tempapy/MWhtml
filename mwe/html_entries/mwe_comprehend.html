(bhāvayati -yituṃ). — (Include) parigraha, antargaṇ (c. 10. -gaṇayati -yituṃ), parisamāp (c. 5. -āpnoti -āptuṃ), vyāp, pratisaṃhṛ (c. 1. -harati -harttuṃ). ‘Comprehending’ is expressed by ātmakaḥ -kā -kaṃ or rūpaḥ -pā -paṃ affixed; as, ‘comprehending two,’ dvyātmakaḥ -kā -kaṃ.
