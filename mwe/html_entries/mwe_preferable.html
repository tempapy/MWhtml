PREFERABLE [617-a], a. (Worthy to be chosen before, more desirable or excellent) varaM indec., varIyAn -yasI -yaH (s), varizWaH -zWA -zWaM, varaRIyaH -yA -yaM, pUrvvavaraRIyaH &c., aDikavaraRIyaH &c., pUrvvagrAhyaH -hyA -hyaM, praTamagrAhyaH &c., agragrAhyaH &c., SreyAn &c., SrezWaH &c., garIyAn &c., aDikaM prArTanIyaH -yA -yaM. The adverb varaM is usually followed by na ca or na punar; as, ‘death is preferable to poverty,’ varaM mftyUr na ca dAridraM; ‘death is preferable to intercourse with the base,’ varaM prARatyAgo na punar aDamAnAm upagamaH.
