PSEUDO [640-b]. A prefix expressed in Sanskrit by miTyA, mfzA, kftrima, ku &c. prefixed, or kalpa, deSIya &c. affixed; as, ‘a pseudo- poet,’ miTyAkaviH m., kukaviH, kavikalpaH, kavideSIyaH; pseudology,’ miTyAvAdaH, mfzAvAdaH; ‘pseudo-morphous,’ kftrimarUpaH -pA -paM; ‘pseudonymous,’ kftrimanAmA &c.
