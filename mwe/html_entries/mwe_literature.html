LITERATURE [459-b], s. (Learning) vidyā, śāstraṃ, śāstravidyā, akṣaraṃ, sāhityaṃ, vāṅmayaṃ. — (Collective body of literary productions) śāstrasamu- dāyaḥ, śāstrasamavāyaḥ, śāstrasamūhaḥ, often expressible by śāstra in the sing. number; as, ‘poetical literature,’ kāvyaśāstraṃ ‘branch of literature,’ aṅgaṃ, upaśāstraṃ.
