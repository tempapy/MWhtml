Animals are divided by the Hindūs into four classes, viz. 1. ‘Egg-born,’ aṇḍajāḥ m. pl.; 2. ‘Womb- born,’ jarāyujāḥ m. pl.; 3. ‘Generated by heat and damp,’ svedajāḥ m. pl.; 4. ‘Vegetable-born,’ udbhijjāḥ m. pl.
