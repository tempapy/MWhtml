ASTRONOMY [030-a], s. jyotiHSAstraM, jyotirvidyA, rASigrahanakzatrAdividyA, jyotizaM, svagolavidyA. ASTRONOMY [852-b], s. jyotizaM, nakzatravidyA. The astronomical divisions of time called Karaṇa karaRa are eleven in number: some of them are, kOlavaH, tEtilaH, baRik, BadrI or vizwiH f., SakuniH f., caturaNGriH, nAgaH, kintuGnaH &c. The divisions called Yoga yoga are twenty-seven in number, viz. vizkamBaH, prItiH, AyuzmAn, sOBAgyaM, SoBanaH, atigaRqaH, sukarmmA, DftiH, SUlaH, gaRqaH, vfdDiH, GruvaH, vyAGAtaH, harzaRaH, vajraH, sidDiH f., vyatIpAtaH, varIyAn, pariGaH, SivaH, sidDaH, sADyaH, SuBaH, SuklaH, brahmA, EndraH, vEDftiH.
