ABOUT [002-a], prep. (Encircling) pari prefixed. — (Near to, in time) prati. — (In number) deSIya affixed; as, ‘about twelve years old,’ dvAdaSavarzadeSIyaH; nyUnADikaM. — (Concerning, relating to) prati, pratIkzya, uddiSya. — (On account of) hetoH, hetO, arTaM, arTe, kfte, nimitte, kAraRAt, with the crude form, or sometimes the gen. c. of the preceding noun. — (Engaged in any thing) vyApftaH, nizWitaH, nirataH, with loc. c. — (Close to one's person) pArSvatas ind., nikawe, samIpaM. ABOUT [002-a], adv. (Circularly) paritas ind., aBitas ind. — (Nearly) prAyaSas or prAyas ind. — (Here and there) itastatas ind., sarvvatas ind. — (To be about to do any thing) expressed by udyataH, ‘prepared,’ with the infin.; as, ‘about to kill,’ hantum udyataH; or by the act. part. of the 2d fut.; as, gamizyan, ‘about to go,’ eDizyamARaH, ‘about to increase’; or, by the desid. adj.; as, jigamizuH, ‘about to go,’ mumUrzuH ‘about to die.’ ABOUT [850-a]. (On the point of) unmuKa &c., see under POINT.
