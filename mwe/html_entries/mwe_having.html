HAVING [335-a], part. (Possessing) daDat -DatI -Dat (t), daDAnaH -nA -naM, vidaDat -DatI -Dat (t), DArayan -yantI -yat (t), DArI -riRI -ri (n), daDaH -DA -DaM, SAlI -linI -li (n): often expressed by the affixes vat, mat, in, vin, min, vala; as, ‘having wealth,’ DanavAn -vatI -vat (t), DanI -ninI -ni (n); ‘having wisdom,’ DImAn -matI -mat (t); also expressible by upetaH -tA -taM, anvitaH -tA -taM, yuktaH -ktA -ktaM in comp.; as, ‘having wealth,’ DanopetaH &c.
