BOOK [058-b], s. pustakaM -kI, granTaH, patraM, patrikA, pustaM -stI; ‘a book of science,’ SAstraM. This last may be used in connexion with some other word to limit its application; as, ‘a book on law,’ DarmmaSAstraM; ‘a poetical book,’ kAvyaSAstraM. — (A manu- script, written book) leKyaM, liKitaM.
