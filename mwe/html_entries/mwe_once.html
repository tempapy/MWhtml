ONCE [551-a], adv. (One time) sakṛt, ekavāraṃ -re, ekakṛtvas, ekakāle, ekasamaye. — (On a certain time) ekadā, kadācit. — (Formerly) pūrvvaṃ, purā. See the word. (At once, at the same time) samakāle, ekakāle, ekavāre, ekadā. — (All at once) yugapat, yaugapadyena, ekapadaṃ -de. — (All at once in a body) saṅghaśas. — (All at once, suddenly) akasmāt. — (At once, immediately) sadyas, tatkāle, tatkṣaṇe. — (Once more) punarapi.
