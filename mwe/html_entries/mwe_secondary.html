SECONDARY [721-a], a. (Not primary) apraDAnaH -nA -naM, amuKyaH -KyA -KyaM, apraTamaH -mA -maM, aprAgryaH -gryA -gryaM, anuzaNgI -NgiRI &c., anuzaNgikaH -kI -kaM, gORaH -RI -RaM, gORikaH -kI -kaM, upasfzwaH -zwA -zwaM, guRaBUtaH -tA -taM; ‘secondary rank,’ apraDAnatA, apraDAnapadaM; ‘any thing secondary,’ apraDAnaM, upasarjanaM. — (Not first rate) maDyamaH -mA -maM, maDyamIyaH -yA -yaM, maDyamaguRakaH -kA -kaM, viguRaH -RA -RaM. The prepositions upa and anu often give the sense of ‘secondary;’ as, ‘a secondary planet,’ upagrahaH; ‘a secondary passion or flavour,’ uparasaH, anurasaH; ‘symptom,’ anubanDaH.
