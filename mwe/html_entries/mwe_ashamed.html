ASHAMED [027-a], a. hnīṇaḥ -ṇā -ṇaṃ, hnītaḥ -tā -taṃ, lajjitaḥ -tā -taṃ, vrīḍitaḥ -tā -taṃ, lajjamānaḥ -nā -naṃ, lajjāvān -vatī vat (t), trapitaḥ -tā -taṃ, trapamāṇaḥ -ṇā -ṇaṃ, trapānvitaḥ -tā -taṃ. — (To be ashamed) lajj (c. 6. lajjate, lajjituṃ), vilajj, saṃlajj; vrīḍ (c. 4. vrīḍyati, vrīḍituṃ); trap (c. 1. trapate, trapituṃ), apatrap, vyapatrap; hnī (c. 3. jihneti, hnetuṃ), with abl. or gen. of the thing of which one is ashamed. To be ashamed of any thing, may be expressed by kiñcid lajjāspadaṃ jñā. — (To make ashamed) hrī in caus. hrepa- yati -yitu).
