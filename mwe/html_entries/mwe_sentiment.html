SENTIMENT [728-a], s. (Thought, opinion, notion) mata, matiH f., budDiH f., kalpanA, manaHkalpanA, boDaH, saNkalpaH. — (Sense contained in words) arTaH aBiprAyaH, see SENSE. — (Feeling, passion) BAvaH, rasaH, rAgaH, zAqavaH, ruciH f., see PASSION. Eight sentiments or rasas are usually enumerated: 1. Love, SfNgAraH. 2. Mirth, hAsyaH -syaM. 3. Tenderness, karuRA or kAruRyaM. 4. Anger or rage, rOdraH -draM or kroDaH. 5. Heroism, vIraH -raM. 6. Terror, BayAnakaH -kaM or BayaM. 7. Disgust, bIBatsaH -tsaM. 8. Surprise, adButaH -taM or vismayaH. Sometimes a ninth is added, ‘tranquillity,’ SAntaH, or even a tenth, ‘paternal tenderness.’ vAtsalyaM.
