COUNTER [139-b], s. (Table to count money on) gaṇanaphalakaṃ, baṇikphalakaṃ, baṇijāṃ mudrāsanaṃ. — (Round piece to count with) gaṇanārthaṃ mudrā or kākinī or kaparddakaḥ or vaṭaḥ or varāṭakaḥ or aṣṭhīlā or akṣaḥ. COUNTER [139-b], adv. prati, abhi, pratikūlaṃ, viruddhaṃ, viparītaṃ, prātikūlyena, virodhena, pratimukhaṃ, pratilomaṃ, vilomaṃ; ‘a counter-charge,’ pratya- bhiyogaḥ; ‘a counter-pledge,’ pratipāṇaṃ.
