(pratyarpayati -yituṃ, rt. ṛ). pratipad in caus. — (Give, afford) dā, pradā, prayam (c. 1. -yacchati -yantuṃ), pratiyam, pratipad (c. 10. -pādayati -yituṃ), ṛ in caus. (arpayati -yituṃ), upasthā in caus., upakḷp. — (Cause to be, make, effect) kṛ, jan (c. 10. janayati -yituṃ), sādh (c. 10. sādhayati -yituṃ), utpad (c. 10. -pādayati -yituṃ), yuktaṃ -ktāṃ kṛ, viśiṣṭaṃ -ṣṭāṃ kṛ, kal (c. 10. kalayati -yituṃ).
