AND [017-b], conj. ca placed after the word which it connects with another. In many cases ‘and’ may be omitted in San- skrit, and the connection expressed by the use of the indeclinable participle; as, ‘the beasts assembled and informed the lion,’ paSuBirmilitvA siMho vijYaptaH. — (Moreover) apica, kiYca, aparaM, anyacca. — (And then) aTa.
