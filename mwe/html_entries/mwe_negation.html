NEGATION [529-b], s. nihnavaḥ, apahnavaḥ, nihnutiḥ f., apahnutiḥ f., apalāpaḥ, pratyā- khyānaṃ, apavādaḥ, asvīkāraḥ, niṣedhaḥ, pratyādeśaḥ. — (In logic) abhāvaḥ according to one division it is of two kinds, ‘universal negation,’ saṃsargābhāvaḥ; ‘relative or mutual negation,’ anyonyābhāvaḥ; according to another division it is of four kinds, viz. ‘extinction,’ pradhvaṃsābhāvaḥ; ‘antecedent non- existence,’ prāgabhāvaḥ; ‘reciprocal negation,’ anyonyābhāvaḥ; ‘simple non-existence,’ atyantābhāvaḥ.
