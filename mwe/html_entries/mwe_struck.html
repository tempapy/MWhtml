STRUCK [780-a], p. p. hataH -tA -taM, AhataH &c., samAhataH &c., aBihataH &c., prahataH &c., upahataH &c., vyAhataH &c., pratihataH &c., tAqitaH -tA -taM, AsPAlitaH -tA -taM; ‘with astonishment,’ vismayopahataH -tA -taM. The use of hata in the first member of a compound is very common; as, ‘struck with poverty,’ hatasampadaH -dA -daM, i. e. ‘one whose fortunes are struck or blasted.’
