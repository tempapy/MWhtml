(-man), hArddaM, praSrayaH. — (Passion of any kind) rAgaH, BAvaH, rasaH. — (Of the mind) manaso BAvaH. — (Of the body) SarIrasya BAvaH. — (Parental) vAtsalyaM; ‘I have great affection for him’ tena saha mama mahAn snehaH or tasminn anurAgavAn asmi. AFFECTION [851-a], s. (Fondness) prItiH f.; ‘object of affection,’ prIti- pAtraM; ‘paternal affection,’ sutasnehaH, putrasnehaH. — (Of the mind) manoDarmmaH, manovikAraH, manovikftiH f., manovfttiH f., vfttiH f., vikAraH, vikftiH f.; ‘the affection of love,’ kAmavikAraH; ‘of anger,’ kroDavikAraH. — (Of the body) vikAraH, BAvaH, vfttiH f., AkAraH, anuBAvaH; ‘of the eyes,’ netravikAraH. — (Of the mind or body) BAvaH. Of these there are three classes: 1. Fixed or regular passions, sTAyiBAvAH m. pl., eight in number, viz. ratiH f., hAsaH, SokaH, kroDaH, utsAhaH, BayaM, jugupsA, vismayaH. 2. Irre- gular passions, vyaBicAriBAvAH m. pl. or saYcAriBAvAH m. pl., thirty-two in number, viz. nirvedaH, glAniH f., SaNkA, asUyA, madaH, SramaH, AlasyaM, dEnyaM, cintA, mohaH, smftiH f., DftiH f., vrIqA, capalatA, harzaH, AvegaH, jaqatA, garvaH, vizAdaH, OtsukyaM, nidrA, apasmAraH, SayaH, viboDaH, amarzaH, avahitTA, ugratA, matiH f., upAlamBaH, vyADiH m., unmAdaH, maraRaM. Sometimes trAsaH and vitarkaH are added. 3. Honest and spontaneous emotions, sAttvikaBAvAH m. pl., eight in number, viz. stamBaH, svedaH, romAYcaH, vEsvaryaM, vepaTuH m., vEvarRyaM, aSru, pralayaH.
