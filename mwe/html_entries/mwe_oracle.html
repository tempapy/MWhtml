ORACLE [555-a], s. (Answer of a god to an inquiry made) śakunaṃ -naḥ, praśnaḥ, prasādaḥ; ‘through a voice,’ devavāṇī, ākāśavāṇī. — (The deity who gives the answer) praśnadevatā, prasādadevatā, praśnadevaḥ, śakunadevaḥ. — (The place where the answer is given) praśnasthānaṃ, śakunasthānaṃ, prasādasthānaṃ. — (A person whose opinion is of great authority) pramāṇaṃ, āptaḥ. — (A wise or authoritative sentence) āptavacanaṃ, āptoktiḥ f.
