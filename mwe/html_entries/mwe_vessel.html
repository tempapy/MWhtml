VESSEL [829-a], s. (Utensil for holding, as a pot, cup, &c.) pātraṃ, bhājanaṃ, bhāṇḍaṃ, amatraṃ, ādhāraḥ, dhārakaḥ; ‘drinking vessel,’ pānapātraṃ; ‘for holding water,’ udakapātraṃ, jalapātraṃ, ghaṭaḥ, kalaśaḥ. — (Vessel of the body, as a vein, &c.) śirā, nāḍiḥ -ḍī f., nāliḥ -lī, dhamanī, vāhinī, see TUBE. — (Boat, ship) nauḥ f., naukā, potaḥ, vahinī, vahitraṃ, taraṇī, jalayānaṃ, vohitthaṃ.
