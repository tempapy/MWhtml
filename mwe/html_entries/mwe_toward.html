TOWARD [808-a], TOWARDS, adv. (In the direction of) prati, anu, abhimukhaṃ, uddiśya; ‘towards the city,’ nagaraṃ prati, nagarābhimukhaṃ; ‘he set out towards the north,’ uttarāṃ diśatuddiśya prasthitaḥ. — (With respect to) upari, uddiśya, uddeśena, prati affixed; ‘towards me,’ mamopari, māmuddiśya, māṃ prati; ‘want of courtesy towards you,’ tavopari adākṣiṇyaṃ; ‘not behaving properly towards his father,’ piturupari asadṛśavyavahārī. — (Nearly) prāyas; ‘towards evening,’ abhisāyaṃ. TOWARD [808-b], TOWARDLY, &c. See APT, FORWARD, SHARP, &c.
