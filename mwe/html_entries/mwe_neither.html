NEITHER [530-b], pron. (Not either, not the one or the other) expressed by anyataraH -rA -raM or ekataraH -rA -raM or uBO, or simply by the dual in conjunction with the negative na; as, ‘neither of them can break the bow,’ Danur BaMktum ekataro na Saknoti or uBO na SaknutaH or simply na SaknutaH. NEITHER [530-b], conj. expressed by the negative na or na ca, or by the prohibitive mA. Sometimes in conjunction with vA; as, ‘he took neither food nor clothes for his journey,’ yAtrArTaM BakzyaM vastraM vA na jagrAha; ‘take neither food nor clothes,’ BakzyaM vastraM vA mA gfhARa; ‘do not eat the fruit neither touch it,’ PalaM mA KAdata mA spfSata; ‘he did not touch, neither did he smell,’ nAspfSad na cAjiGrat.
