ARJUNA [852-a], s. (The third and most celebrated of the five sons of Pāndu. He was the son of Pāndu only by courtesy, being in fact the child of his wife Pṛthā or Kuntī by the god Indra) arjunaH. — (As son of Pṛthā) pArTaH, kOnteyaH, kuntisutaH — (As son of Indra) EndriH, pAkaSAsaniH. — (The crested one) kirIwI m. — (The Conqueror) jizRuH. — (Wealth- acquiring) DanaYjayaH. — (Drawing the bow with the left hand) savyasAcI m. — (Having hair like the Euphorbia) guqAkeSaH. — (Son of Pāndu) pARquputraH; his bow is called gARqIvaH -vaM or gARqivaH -vaM.
