PRIVATIVE [626-b], a. (Causing privation) hānikaraḥ -rā -raṃ, hānikāraka -kā -kaṃ, lopakaraḥ &c., kṣayakaraḥ &c., nāśakaraḥ &c. — (Consisting in the absence of something) abhāvasūcakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, abhāvarūpaḥ -pā -paṃ, abhāvātmakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, abhāvadarśakaḥ &c. PRIVATIVE [626-b], s. (Word) abhāvaśabdaḥ, abhāvasūcakaśabdaḥ. — (Prefix, in grammar) napadaṃ, naprpadaṃ. Privative prefixes are such as a, an, nir, vi.
