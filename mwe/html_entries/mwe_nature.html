NATURE [526-b], s. (The goddess who is the material cause of the world and all created things. According to the Hindū mythology this goddess is unreal, and identified with Māyā or Illusion, and sometimes with the Śakti or per- sonified energy, or wife of a deity. She is in an especial manner the prototype of the female sex.
