To ENFORCE [228-b], v. a. (Give strength to) dṛḍhīkṛ, sthirīkṛ, draḍha (nom. draḍhayati -yituṃ), saṃstambh (c. 5. -stabhnoti -stambhituṃ, c. 10. -stambhayati -yituṃ), sabalaṃ -lāṃ -laṃ kṛ, tejaḥ or śaktiṃ dā. — (Put in act by force) balena or balātkāreṇa kṛ in caus. (kārayati -yituṃ) or pravṛt in caus. (-varttayati -yituṃ) or praṇī (c. 1. -ṇayati -ṇetuṃ) or nirvah in caus. (-vāhayati -yituṃ) or nriṣpad in caus. (-pādayati -yituṃ). The causal form of a verb may express the sense of en- -force; as, ‘to enforce payment,’ dā in caus. (dāpayati -yituṃ).
