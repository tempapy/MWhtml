To REQUIRE [680-b], v. a. (Ask, demand) prArT (c. 10. -arTayati -te -yituM), yAc (c. 1. yAcati -cituM), aBiyAc. — (Need, call for) AkAMkz (c. 1. -kAMkzati -kzituM), aBikAMkz, kAMkz, arh (c. 1. arhati -rhituM), prArT; or expressed by BU or as in conjunction with the nouns prayojanaM, upayogaH or kAryyaM. See To NEED. The desid. form may sometimes be used to express the sense of ‘require,’ thus; ‘three quantities being given, it is re- quired to find a fourth,’ trIn rASIn nirdiSya caturTo jijYAsyate (desid. pass. of jYA).
