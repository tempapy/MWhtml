RESPECTIVE [684-a], a. (Relative, not absolute) sApekzaH -kzA -kzaM, apekzakaH -kA -kaM, sasambanDaH -nDA -nDaM. See RELATIVE. — (Several) itaretaraH -rA -raM, anyonyaH -nyA -nyaM, see SEVERAL; ‘respective incli- nation,’ itaretarakAmyA. — (Own) expressed by doubling the pronouns sva, svakIya, svIya, svaka, nija, Atma, AtmIya &c. thus, ‘in their respective stations.’ svezu svezu sTAnezu or simply by doubling the noun; thus, ‘they dwell each in their respective houses,’ gfhe gfhe pratyekaM vasanti.
