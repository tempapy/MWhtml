To ENDURE [228-a], v. a. (Bear) sah (c. 1. sahate, soḍhuṃ), vipah, kṣam (c. 1. kṣamate, kṣantuṃ), saṃkṣam, tij in des. (titikṣate -kṣituṃ), mṛṣ (c. 1. mṛṣyati -te, marṣituṃ, (c. 10. marṣayati -yituṃ), dhṛ in caus. (dhārayati -yituṃ), upāgam (c. 1. -gacchati -gantuṃ), bhuj (c. 7. bhuṃkte, bhoktuṃ), dhairyyam ālas (c. 1. -lambate -mbituṃ) or avalamb. Sometimes expressible by śak; as, ‘I cannot endure to see him,’ taṃ draṣṭuṃ na śaknomi. To ENDURE [228-a], v. n. (Last, continue) sthā (c. 1. tiṣṭhati -te, sthātuṃ), cirasthāyī -yinī -yi bhū.
