BOTH [059-b], a. ubhau m. du., ubhayaḥ -yā -yaṃ, dvayaṃ; ‘on both sides,’ ubhayatas, ubhayatra; ‘in both ways,’ ubhayathā; ‘for both objects,’ ubhayārthaṃ; ‘both hands,’ hastadvayaṃ. BOTH [059-b], conj. (And) ca -ca. — (As well as) tathā. ‘Both’ may be expressed by the use of the indeclinable participle; as, ‘he both labours and suffers reproach,’ pariamya nindāṃ bhuṃkte.
