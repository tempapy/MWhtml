• maināka m. (fr. menā) N. of a mountain (son of Hima-vathima-vathimavat by Menā or Menakā, and said to have alone retained his wings when Indra clipped those of the other mountains; accord. to some this mountain was situated between the southern point of the Indian peninsula and Laṅkā), TĀr. (v.l. maināga), AV.Pariś.; MBh. &c. [834,3]
  • N. of a Daitya, Hariv. [834,3]
