IW. 379 &c.), MBh.; Hariv.; Pur. [855,1]
    • of a son of Kṛṣṇa, Hariv. [855,1]
    • of two kings of Kaśmīra, Rājat. [855,1]
    • of a potter, Pañcat. [855,1]
    • (with mahȏpā̑dhyāyamahopādhyāyamahamahāupaupāūpāadhyāya) of a preceptor, Cat. [855,1]
    • pl. the descendants of Yudhi-ṣṭhira (son of Pāṇḍu), Pāṇ. ii, 4, 66, Sch. [855,1]
