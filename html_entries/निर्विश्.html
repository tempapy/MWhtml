• nir-√viśnirviśnirviś
  • P. -viśativiśatinir-viśati (inf. -veṣṭumveṣṭumnir-veṣṭum), to enter into (acc. or loc.);
    (esp.) to settle in a home, become a householder (also with gṛheṣugṛheṣu);
    to marry, Kāv.; Pur.;
    to pay, render, offer (bhartṛpiṇḍambhartṛpiṇḍam), MBh.;
    to enjoy, delight in (acc.), Hariv.; Kāv. &c.;
    to go out or forth, W.;
    to embellish, MW.;
    to reward, ib. [558,1]
