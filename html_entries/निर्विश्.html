• nir-√viśnirviśnirviś
    • P. -viśatinirviś (inf. -veṣṭumnirviś), to enter into (acc. or loc.);
    • (esp.) to settle in a home, become a householder (also with gṛheṣugṛha);
    • to marry, Kāv.; Pur.;
    • to pay, render, offer (bhartṛpiṇḍam), MBh.;
    • to enjoy, delight in (acc.), Hariv.; Kāv. &c.
    • to go out or forth, W.;
    • to embellish, MW.;
    • to reward, ib. [558,1]
