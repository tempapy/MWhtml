• śaradśarad
  • f. (prob. fr. √śrāśrā, śṝśṝ) autumn (as the ‘time of ripening’), the autumnal season (the sultry season of two months succeeding the rains; in some parts of India comprising the months Bhādrabhādra and Āśvinaāśvina, in other places Āśvinaāśvina and Kārttikakārttika, fluctuating thus from August to November), RV. &c. &c. [1057,2]
  • a year (or pl. poetically for ‘years’ cf. varṣavarṣa), ib. [1057,2]
