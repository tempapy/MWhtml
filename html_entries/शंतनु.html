  • m. (also written śāṃtanuśāṃtanu) N. of an ancient king with the patr. IW. 375), RV.; MBh.; Hariv. &c. [1054,3]
  • (with cakra-vartincakravartincakravartin) N. of an author (son of Uddharaṇauddharaṇa, of the Tomaratomara race), Cat. [1054,3]
