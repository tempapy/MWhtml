    • force considered as a sixth organ of action (cf. karmȇndriyakarmendriyakarmaindriya), MBh. [722,3]
    • (the Buddhists reckon 10 forces, the ascetic Śaivas four, which according to Sch. on R. [B.] are sāmansāman, dānadāna, bhedabheda, and nigrahanigraha) [722,3]
    • Force personified as one of the Viśve-Devāḥ, MBh. [722,3]

    • stoutness, bulkiness, L. [722,3]

    • (L. also shape; body; semen virile; gum; blood; a young shoot; bone) [722,3]
