[1288,3]
    • of a Rākṣasa, R. [1288,3]
    • of a Tantra deity, Buddh. [1288,3]
    • of a Rājarṣi, MBh. [1288,3]
    • of a wicked king of the Videhas, MBh. [1288,3]
