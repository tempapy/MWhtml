• saudāsa m. (fr. su-dāssu-dāssudās and su-dāsasu-dāsasudāsa) N. of various kings (esp. of a son of king Su-dāsasu-dāsasudāsa, of the solar race, descendant of Ikṣvāku and Sagara in the thirteenth generation, and also called Mitra-saha and Kalmāṣa-pādakalmāṣa-pādakalmāṣapāda), TS.; MBh. &c.
