• upā̑ṅga n. (for 2. See upā̑ñj) a minor limb or member of the body, MārkP.; Sarvad. [213,1]
  • a subdivision [213,1]
  • a supplementary or additional work, secondary portion of a science, MBh.; Hariv. &c. (such as the Purāṇas, the Nyāya, Mīmāṉsā, and the Dharma-śāstradharma-śāstradharmaśāstras) [213,1]
  • N. of a class of sacred writings of the Jainas (eight are enumerated, the last of which includes four subdivisions) [213,1]
  • a sectarial mark (made with sandal &c.) on the forehead, L. [213,1]
  • (in mus.) a particular drum-like instrument. [213,1]
  • upā̑ṅga (for 1. See above) m. the act of smearing, anointing, Car. [213,1]
