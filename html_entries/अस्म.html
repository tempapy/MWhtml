• asma
  • (fr. a-sma), a pronom. base from which some forms (dat. a/smE, or asmE/ abl. asimA/t loc. asmin) of ida/m (q.v.) are formed [123,2]
  • also the base of the first person pl. acc. asmA/n ([= ἡμᾶς]), instr. asmA/Bis dat. asma/Byam abl. asma/t, in later, language also asmat-tas ([MBh. &c.]) gen. asmA/kam ([exceptionally asmA/ka, RV. i, 173, 10; AV.]) loc. asmA/su [123,2]
  • dat. loc. asme/ (only RV.; AV.; VS.) [123,2]
