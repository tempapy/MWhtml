• asmaasma
    • (fr. a-smaasmaasma), a pronom. base from which some forms (dat. asmaia, or asmaia abl. asimāt loc. asmina) of idamidamid (q.v.) are formed [123,2]
    • also the base of the first person pl. acc. asmān ([= ἡμᾶς]), instr. asmābhis dat. asmabhyam abl. asmatasmatasmad, in later, language also asmat-tasasmattasta ([MBh. &c.]) gen. asmākamasmāka ([exceptionally asmākaasmāka, RV. i, 173, 10; AV.]) loc. asmāsu [123,2]
    • dat. loc. asme (only RV.; AV.; VS.) [123,2]
