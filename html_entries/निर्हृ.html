• nir-√hṛnirhṛnirhṛ
    • P. Ā. -haratinirhṛ, °tenirhṛtanirhṛti, to take out or off, draw or pull out, extract from (abl.), expel, remove, destroy, RV. &c. &c.
    • to export (goods), Mn.;
    • to carry out (a dead body), ib.; Yājñ.;
    • to let (blood), Suśr.;
    • to purge, ib.;
    • (Ā.) to exclude or deliver from (abl.), TS.; ŚBr.;
    • to shake off, get rid of (acc.), Āpast.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to change or interchange (clothes), Mn. viii, 396;
    • to fix, Śulbas.;
    • to get, receive, SaddhP. :
    • Caus. -hārayatinirhṛ, to cause (a dead body) to be carried out, Kull. on Mn. v, 104 :
    • Desid. -jihīrṣatinirhṛ, to wish to take off or subtract, Śulbas. (cf. nir-jihīrṣunirjihīrṣunirnisjihīrṣu). [558,2]
