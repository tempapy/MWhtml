• nir-√hṛnirhṛnirhṛ
  • P. Ā. -haratiharatinirharati, °tenirhṛte, to take out or off, draw or pull out, extract from (abl.), expel, remove, destroy, RV. &c. &c.;
    to export (goods), Mn.;
    to carry out (a dead body), ib.; Yājñ.;
    to let (blood), Suśr.;
    to purge, ib.;
    (Ā.) to exclude or deliver from (abl.), TS.; ŚBr.;
    to shake off, get rid of (acc.), Āpast.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to change or interchange (clothes), Mn. viii, 396;
    to fix, Śulbas.;
    to get, receive, SaddhP. :
    Caus. -hārayatihārayatinirhārayati, to cause (a dead body) to be carried out, Kull. on Mn. v, 104 :
    Desid. -jihīrṣatijihīrṣatinirjihīrṣati, to wish to take off or subtract, Śulbas. (cf. nir-jihīrṣunirjihīrṣunirjihīrṣu). [558,2]
