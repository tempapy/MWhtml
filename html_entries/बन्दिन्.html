• 1. bandinbandin m.
    • (also written vandinvandin q.v., and m.c. °didī) a praiser, bard, herald (who sings the praises of a prince in his presence or accompanies an army to chant martial songs; these bards are regarded as the descendants of a Kṣatriya by a Śūdra female), Mn.; MBh. &c. [720,1]
  • 2. bandinbandin m.
    • (also written vandinvandin) a prisoner, captive, slave, BhP. [720,1]
    • plunder, spoil (See -grāhabandingrāha). [720,1]
