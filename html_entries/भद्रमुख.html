• bhadrabhadra—mukhamukha mfn.
    • one whose face (or whose look) confers prosperity, Kālid.; Daś.; Kād. &c. (only used in the voc. or in the nom. with the meaning of a 2nd pers. = ‘good or gentle sir’ pl. ‘good people’; accord. to Sāh. a prince is so to be addressed by the inferior characters in plays; in the Divyāv. it is a term of address to inferior persons).
