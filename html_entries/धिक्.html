• dhik ind., used as a prefix or as an interj. of reproach, menace or displeasure = fie! shame! out upon! what a pity! &c. (with acc., rarely gen. voc. or nom.), Up.; Lāṭy.; MBh.; Kāv. &c. (also dhig dhik, aho dhik, hā dhik, hā dhik kaṣṭam, hā̆ hā dhik &c.; dhik tvām or tava [also with astu] shame upon you!) [516,2]
