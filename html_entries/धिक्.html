• dhikdhik
  • ind., used as a prefix or as an interj. of reproach, menace or displeasure = fie! shame! out upon! what a pity! &c. (with acc., rarely gen. voc. or nom.), Up.; Lāṭy.; MBh.; Kāv. &c. (also dhigdhig dhikdhik, ahoaho dhikdhik, hāhā dhikdhik, hāhā dhikdhik kaṣṭamkaṣṭam, hā̆hā hāhā dhikdhik &c.; dhikdhik tvāmtvām or tavatava [also with astuastu] shame upon you!) [516,2]
