• abhi-vādanaabhivādanaabhivādana n.
    • respectful salutation (including sometimes the name or title of the person so addressed and followed by the mention of the person's own name) [69,1]
    • salutation (of a superior or elder by a junior or inferior, and especially of a teacher by his disciple; in general it is merely lifting the joined hands to the forehead and saying ahamahamahaah abhivādaye, I salute). [69,1]
