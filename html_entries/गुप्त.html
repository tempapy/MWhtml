  • gupta
  • m. (also) the era named after the Guptagupta dynasty (beginning, A.D. 319; hence the Guptagupta year 165 corresponds to, A.D. 484-85; in later times the years are called valaBI-saMvat from the rulers of Valabhivalabhi, and the era is spoken of as the Gupta-Valabhiguptavalabhi era). [1326,2]
