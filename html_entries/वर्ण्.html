• varṇvarṇ
  • (rather Nom. fr. varṇavarṇa) cl. 10. P. (Dhātup. xxxv, 83) varṇayativarṇayati (mc. also °tevarṇte; aor. avavarṇatavavarṇat; inf. varṇayitumvarṇayitum or varṇitumvarṇitum),
    to paint, colour, dye, Yājñ.; Daś.;
    to depict, picture, write, describe, relate, tell, explain, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to regard, consider, Kathās.;
    to spread, extend, MBh.;
    to praise, extol, proclaim qualities, W. :
    Pass. varṇyatevarṇyate (aor. avarṇiavarṇi), to be coloured or described &c., MBh.; Kāv. &c. [924,2]
