• varṇ (rather Nom. fr. varṇa) cl. 10. P. (Dhātup. xxxv, 83) varṇayati (mc. also °te; aor. avavarṇat; inf. varṇayitum or varṇitum),
    to paint, colour, dye, Yājñ.; Daś.;
    to depict, picture, write, describe, relate, tell, explain, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to regard, consider, Kathās.;
    to spread, extend, MBh.;
    to praise, extol, proclaim qualities, W. :
    Pass. varṇyate (aor. avarṇi), to be coloured or described &c., MBh.; Kāv. &c. [924,2]
