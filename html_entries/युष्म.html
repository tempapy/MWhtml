• yuṣmayuṣma
    • (fr. yuyuyū + smasma), the actual base of the 2nd pers. pron. in the pl. number (from which all cases except the nom. yūyamyūyam are derived, viz. yuṣmānyuṣmayuṣmat, yuṣmābhisyuṣmāyuṣma, yuṣmabhyam, yuṣmatyuṣmatyuṣmad, yuṣmākamyuṣmāka, in Ved. also acc. f. yuṣmāsyuṣmayuṣmā; loc. yuṣmeyuṣmayuṣmā, and gen. yuṣmākayuṣmāka; cf. Gk. ὕμμες, fr. ὑσμες). [855,3]
