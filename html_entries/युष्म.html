• yuṣma (fr. yu + sma), the actual base of the 2nd pers. pron. in the pl. number (from which all cases except the nom. yūyam are derived, viz. yuṣmān, yuṣmābhis, yuṣmabhyam, yuṣmat, yuṣmākam, in Ved. also acc. f. yuṣmās; loc. yuṣme, and gen. yuṣmāka; cf. Gk. ὕμμες, fr. ὑσμες). [855,3]
