• yuṣmayuṣma
    • (fr. yuyuyū + smasma), the actual base of the 2nd pers. pron. in the pl. number (from which all cases except the nom. yūyamyūyam are derived, viz. yuṣmān, yuṣmābhis, yuṣmabhyam, yuṣmatyuṣmatyuṣmad, yuṣmākamyuṣmāka, in Ved. also acc. f. yuṣmās; loc. yuṣme, and gen. yuṣmākayuṣmāka; cf. Gk. ὕμμες, fr. ὑσμες). [855,3]
