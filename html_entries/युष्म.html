• yuṣmayuṣma
  • (fr. yuyu + smasma), the actual base of the 2nd pers. pron. in the pl. number (from which all cases except the nom. yūyamyūyam are derived, viz. yuṣmānyuṣmān, yuṣmābhisyuṣmābhis, yuṣmabhyamyuṣmabhyam, yuṣmatyuṣmat, yuṣmākamyuṣmākam, in Ved. also acc. f. yuṣmāsyuṣmās; loc. yuṣmeyuṣme, and gen. yuṣmākayuṣmāka; cf. Gk. ὕμμες, fr. ὑσμες). [855,3]
