vīthivīthi or vīthīvīthī. f. (perhaps fr. √vīvī; cf. 1. vītavīta) a row, line, Kāv.; Rājat. [1005,1]
  • a road, way, street, MBh.; Kāv. &c. [1005,1]
  • a race-course, Śiś. v, 60 [1005,1]
  • a market, stall, shop, ib. ix. 32 [1005,1]
  • a row of pictures, pictures-gallery, Uttarar. (v.l. vīthikāvīthikā) [1005,1]
  • a partic. division of the planetary sphere (comprising 3 asterisms), VarBṛS. [1005,1]
  • a terrace in front of a house, L. [1005,1]
  • a sort of drama (having an amatory intrigue for its plot and said to be in one act and performed by one or two players), Bhar.; Daśar. &c. [1005,1]
  • vīthīvīthī
