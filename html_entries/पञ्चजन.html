• pañcapañca—janajana ⮩ pañcajanendra
  • m. (pl.) the 5 classes of beings (viz. gods, men, Gandharvagandharvas and Apsarasapsaras, serpents, and Pitṛpitṛs), TS.; ŚBr. &c. man, mankind, Hcar. (°nȇndrapañcajanendra m. prince, king, Rājat.) [576,1]
  • (ibc.) the 5 elements, MBh. [576,1]
  • N. of a demon slain by Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa, MBh.; R. &c. (cf. pāñcajanyapāñcajanya) [576,1]
  • of a son of Saṃhrādasaṃhrāda by Kṛtikṛti, BhP. [576,1]
  • of a Prajāpatiprajāpati, ib. [576,1]
  • of a son of Sagarasagara by Keśinīkeśinī, Hariv. [576,1]
  • of a son of Sṛñjayasṛñjaya and father of Soma-dattasomadattasomadatta, ib. [576,1]
