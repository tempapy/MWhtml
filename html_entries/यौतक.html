• yautakayautaka mf(īīi)n.
    • (fr. 1. yutakayutaka) rightfully or exclusively belonging to any one, being the property of any one, MBh. [859,1]
  • yautakayautaka n.
    • a present, Bhojapr. [859,1]
    • private property, (esp.) property given at marriage, a woman's dowry, presents made to a bride at her marriage by her father and friends (accord. to some also ‘a gift to the bridegroom’), Yājñ.; MBh.; Rājat. [859,1]
