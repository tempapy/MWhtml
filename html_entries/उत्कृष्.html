• ut-√kṛṣutkṛṣutkṛṣ
  • (ud-√kṛṣudkṛṣudkṛṣ) P. sometimes Ā. -karṣatikarṣatiutkarṣati, -teteutte, to draw or drag or pull up;
    to raise;
    to draw or take out;
    to extract;
    to pull or put off, MāṇḍUp.; MBh.; Ragh.; Suśr.; R. &c.;
    to put off, delay, Nyāyam.;
    to bend (a bow);
    to tear asunder:
    Caus. -karṣayatikarṣayatiutkarṣayati, to elevate, raise, increase, Sāh. :
    Pass. -kṛṣyatekṛṣyateutkṛṣyate, to be lifted or drawn up;
    to be raised, rise, become powerful, become eminent, MBh. &c. (cf. ut-kṛṣṭautkṛṣṭautkṛṣṭa). [176,2]
