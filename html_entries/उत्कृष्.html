• ut-√kṛṣutkṛṣutkṛṣ
    • (ud-√kṛṣudkṛṣ) P. sometimes Ā. -karṣati, -tetatā, to draw or drag or pull up;
    • to raise;
    • to draw or take out;
    • to extract;
    • to pull or put off, MāṇḍUp.; MBh.; Ragh.; Suśr.; R. &c.
    • to put off, delay, Nyāyam.;
    • to bend (a bow);
    • to tear asunder:
    • Caus. -karṣayati, to elevate, raise, increase, Sāh. :
    • Pass. -kṛṣyate, to be lifted or drawn up;
    • to be raised, rise, become powerful, become eminent, MBh. &c. (cf. ut-kṛṣṭautkṛṣṭautkṛṣṭa). [176,2]
