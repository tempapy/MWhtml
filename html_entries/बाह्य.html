    • diverging from, conflicting with, opposed to, having nothing to do with (abl. or comp.), ib. [731,1]
    • (with arthaartha), meaning external to (i.e. not resulting from) the sounds or letters forming a word, Pāṇ. i, 1, 68, Sch. [731,1]
