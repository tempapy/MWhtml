• vālakhilya n. (also written bāl°, of doubtful derivation) N. of a collection of 11 (accord. to some only 6 or 8) hymns of the Ṛg-veda (commonly inserted after viii, 48, but numbered separately as a supplement by some editors; they are also called vālakhilyāḥ, with or scil. mantrāḥ, or ṛcaḥ, and daśatī vālakhilyakā), Br.; ŚrS. &c. [946,2]
  • (°lya) pl. N. of a class of Ṛṣis of the size of a thumb (sixty thousand were produced from Brahmā's body and surround the chariot of the sun), TĀr.; MBh.; Kāv. &c. [946,2]
