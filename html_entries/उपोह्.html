• upȏhupoh
  • (upa-upaupa-upoh 1 √ūhūh; cf. upa-√vahupavahupavah) P. upȏhatiupohati, to push or pull or draw near, R.;
    to drive near, impel towards, BhP.;
    to push under, insert, KātyŚr.;
    to add, accumulate;
    to bring near, cause to appear, produce;
    Pass. upȏhyateupohyate, to draw near, approach (as a point of time), MBh. [216,2]
