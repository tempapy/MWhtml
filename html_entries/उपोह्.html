• upȏhupohupaupāuhūh
    • (upa-upaupeupasupoh 1 √ūhūh; cf. upa-√vahupavahupavah) P. upȏhatiupaupā, to push or pull or draw near, R.;
    • to drive near, impel towards, BhP.;
    • to push under, insert, KātyŚr.;
    • to add, accumulate;
    • to bring near, cause to appear, produce;
    • Pass. upȏhyateupaupā, to draw near, approach (as a point of time), MBh. [216,2]
