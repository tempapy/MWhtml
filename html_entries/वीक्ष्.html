• vī̑kṣvīkṣvivīīkṣ
    • (vi-√īkṣviīkṣ) Ā. vī̑kṣatevivī (ep. also P.), to look at, see, behold, ŚBr. &c. &c.
    • to look upon, regard (pitṛ-vatpitṛvatpitṛvatvadvadh, as a father), R.;
    • (with hṛdihṛdihṛd) to see in the heart, ponder, R.;
    • to consider, observe, discern, ascertain, understand, Mn.; MBh. &c.
    • to think fit or proper, Suśr.;
    • to look over, peruse, study, VarBṛS. :
    • Pass. vī̑kṣyatevivī, to be looked at &c.
    • to look like, appear, Vikr. [1004,2]
