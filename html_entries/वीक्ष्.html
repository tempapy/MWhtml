• vī̑kṣvīkṣ
  • (vi-√īkṣviīkṣviīkṣ) Ā. vī̑kṣatevīkṣate (ep. also P.), to look at, see, behold, ŚBr. &c. &c.;
    to look upon, regard (pitṛ-vatpitṛvatpitṛvat, as a father), R.;
    (with hṛdihṛdi) to see in the heart, ponder, R.;
    to consider, observe, discern, ascertain, understand, Mn.; MBh. &c.;
    to think fit or proper, Suśr.;
    to look over, peruse, study, VarBṛS. :
    Pass. vī̑kṣyatevīkṣyate, to be looked at &c.;
    to look like, appear, Vikr. [1004,2]
