• nābhasanābhasa mf(īīiin)n.
    • (fr. nabhasnabhasnabhanabh) celestial, heavenly, appearing in the sky, Var. [536,1]
    • (with yogayoga) N. of certain constellations, ib. (according to Bhaṭṭopala 2, divided into 4 classes, viz. 3 Āśraya-, 2 Dala-, 20 Ākṛti- and 7 Saṃkhyā- yogas). [536,1]
