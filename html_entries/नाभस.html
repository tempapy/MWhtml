• nābhasanābhasa
  • mf(īī)n. (fr. nabhasnabhas) celestial, heavenly, appearing in the sky, Var. [536,1]
  • (with yogayoga) N. of certain constellations, ib. (according to Bhaṭṭôpalabhaṭṭopala 2, divided into 4 classes, viz. 3 Āśrayaāśraya-, 2 Daladala-, 20 Ākṛtiākṛti- and 7 Saṃkhyāsaṃkhyā- yogayogas). [536,1]
