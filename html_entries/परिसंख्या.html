• 1. pari-saṃ-√khyāparisaṃkhyāparisaṃsamkhyā
    • P. -khyātikhyāti (inf. khyātum), to count, enumerate, ŚāṅkhŚr.; Mn.; MBh.;
    • to limit to a certain number, KātyŚr. Sch.;
    • to reckon up, calculate, add together, MBh.; R.; Suśr.;
    • to make good, restore, Car.; Jaim. Sch. [603,3]
  • 2. paripari—saṃkhyāsaṃkhyāsaṃkhya f.
    • enumeration, computation, sum, total, number, ŚāṅkhŚr.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [603,3]
    • (in phil.) exhaustive enumeration (implying exclusion of any other), limitation to that which is enumerated, Jaim.; Kull. on Mn. iii, 45 [603,3]
    • (in rhet.) special mention or exclusive specification, Kpr.; Sāh. [603,3]
