• śalyaśalya—parvanparvan
  • n. N. of the ninth book of the Mahā-bhāratamahābhāratamahābhārata, (this book describes how, on the death of Karṇakarṇa, Śalyaśalya, king of Madramadra, was appointed to the command of the Kurukuru army, and how a combat with maces took place between Śalyaśalya and Bhīmabhīma, and another great battle between Śalyaśalya and Yudhi-ṣṭhirayudhiṣṭhirayudhiṣṭhira, in which the former was at last killed). [1059,1]
