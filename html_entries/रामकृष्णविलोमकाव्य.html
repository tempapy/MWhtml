• rāmarāma—kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa—viloma-kāvyavilomakāvyavilomakāvya n.
    • N. of an artificial poem (by the astronomer Sūrya Paṇḍita) celebrating the praises of Rāma and Kṛṣṇa (read either backwards or forwards; cf. vilomā̑kṣara-kāvyavilomākṣarakāvyavilomaakṣarakāvya) [877,1]
