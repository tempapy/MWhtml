• saṃ-saṃsamsaṃśru√1. śruśruśrū
    • P. Ā. -śṛṇotisaṃśru, -śṛṇutesaṃśru, to hear or hear from (e.g. mukhātmukha, ‘from any one's mouth’), attend or listen attentively to (acc.), MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to assent, promise (loc. or dat.), ib.;
    • (Ā.) to be distinctly heard or audible, ŚāṅkhBr. (cf. Pāṇ. i, 3, 29, Vārtt. 2, Pat.) :
    • Pass. -śrūyatesaṃśru, to be heard or talked about or read about (yathāyathāyatha saṃśrūyate, ‘as people say’ or ‘as we read in books’), MBh. :
    • Caus. -śrāvayatisaṃśruśrāvayat, to cause to hear or to be heard, proclaim, announce (nāmanāma, ‘one's name’), relate or report anything (acc.)
    • to any one (acc. or dat.), Yājñ.;
    • to read out (See saṃ-śrāviśasaṃsam);
    • to make resound, MBh. [1118,2]
