    • Caus. -śrāvayati, to cause to hear or to be heard, proclaim, announce (nāmanāma, ‘one's name’), relate or report anything (acc.)
    • to any one (acc. or dat.), Yājñ.;
    • to read out (See saṃ-śrāviśasaṃsam);
    • to make resound, MBh. [1118,2]
