• sanatsanat—kumārakumāra m.
    • ‘always a youth’ or ‘son of Brahmā’, N. of one of the four or seven sons of Brahmā (cf. sanakasanaka; he is said to be the oldest of the progenitors of mankind [= vaidhātravaidhātra q.v.], and sometimes identified with Skanda and Pradyumna, he is also the supposed author of an Upa-purāṇa and other works; with Jainas he is one of the 12 Sārvabhaumas or Cakravartins [emperors of India]; the N. of Sanat-kumāra is sometimes given to any great saint who retains youthful purity), ChUp.; MBh.; Hariv. &c. [1141,1]
