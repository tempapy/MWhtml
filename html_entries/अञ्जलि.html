• añjali a See s.v. below. [11,1]
  • añjali b m. (√añj), the open hands placed side by side and slightly hollowed (as if by a beggar to receive food; hence when raised to the forehead, a mark of supplication), reverence, salutation, benediction [11,1]
  • a libation to the Manes (two hands full of water, udakā̑ñjali), VP. &c. [11,1]
  • a measure of corn, sufficient to fill both hands when placed side by side, equal to a kuḍava. [11,1]
