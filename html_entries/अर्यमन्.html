• aryamanaryaman
  • m. a bosom friend, play-fellow, companion, (especially) a friend who asks a woman in marriage for another, RV.; AV.; ŚBr.; TBr. [93,1]
  • N. of an Ādityaāditya (who is commonly invoked together with Varuṇavaruṇa and Mitramitra, also with Bhagabhaga, Bṛhaspatibṛhaspati, and others; he is supposed to be the chief of the Manes, Bhag. &c., the milky way is called his path [aryamṇaḥaryamṇaḥ panthāḥpanthāḥ, TBr.]; he presides over the Nakṣatranakṣatra Uttaraphalgunīuttaraphalgunī, VarBṛS.; his name is used to form different male names, Pāṇ. v, 3, 84), RV. &c. [93,1]
  • the sun, Śiś. ii, 39 [93,1]
  • the Asclepias plant, L. [93,2]
