• śambhalaśambhala
  • m. (also written sambhalasambhala) N. of a town (situated between the Rathaprārathaprā and Ganges, and identified by some with Sambhal in Moradābād; the town or district of Śambhalaśambhala is fabled to be the place where Kalkikalki, the last incarnation of Viṣṇuviṣṇu, is to appear in the family of a Brāhman named Viṣṇu-yaśasviṣṇuyaśasviṣṇuyaśas), MBh.; Hariv.; Pur. [1055,3]
