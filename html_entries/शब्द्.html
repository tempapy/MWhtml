• śabdśabd
  • (rather a Nom. śabdayaśabdaya fr. śabdaśabda) cl. 10. P. (Dhātup. xxxiii, 40) śabdayatiśabdayati, to make any noise or sound, cry aloud, Śiś.; Pañcat.; BhP.;
    to call, invoke, Śaṃk.; Kathās.;
    (śabdāpayatiśabdāpayati, °teśabdte), to call, address, R. :
    Pass. śabdyateśabdyate, to be sounded &c.;
    to be called, MBh.;
    (impers.) it is chattered, Nir. i, 18. [1052,2]
