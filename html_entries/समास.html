• 1. sam-āsasamāsasamāsa m.
    • (for 2. See sam-√āssamāssamasamāsamāsaā) throwing or putting together, aggregation, conjunction, combination, connection, union, totality (enaenaa, ‘fully, wholly, summarily’), Br.; ŚrS.; Mn. &c. [1158,2]
    • succinctness, conciseness, condensation (ibc. and -tassamāsatassamāsatasta, ‘concisely, succinctly, briefly’), KauṣUp.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [1158,2]
    • (in gram.) composition of words, a compound word (of which there are accord. to native grammarians, 6 kinds, viz. Dvaṃdva, Bahu-vrīhi, Karma-dhāraya, Tat-puruṣa, Dvigu, and Avyaya or Avyayī-bhāva [qq. vv.]; an improper compound is called asthāna-samāsaasthānasamāsa), Prāt.; Pāṇ. &c. [1158,2]
    • euphonic combination (= saṃdhisaṃdhisaṃdhin), VPrāt. [1158,2]
    • (in astron.) a partic. circle, Sūryas. [1158,2]
    • composition of differences, reconciliation (-samarthanasamāsasamarthana), L. [1158,2]
    • the part of a Śloka given for completion (= samasyāsamasyāsamasya), L. [1158,2]
