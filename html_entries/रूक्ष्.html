• rūkṣrūkṣ
  • (rather Nom. fr. rūkṣarūkṣa) cl. 10. P. rūkṣayatirūkṣayati (Vop. also rūkṣāpayatirūkṣāpayati; aor. arurūkṣatarurūkṣat),
    to be rough or harsh, Dhātup. xxxv, 56;
    to make dry or emaciated, ŚBr.;
    to soil, smear, VarBṛS.;
    to injure, offend, exasperate, Jātakam. [885,3]
