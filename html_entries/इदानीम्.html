• i-dānīmidānīmidānīm
  • ind. now, at this moment, in this case, just, even (with gen. of ahanahan e.g. idānīmidānīm ahnaḥahnaḥ, this present day, ‘now-a-days’; idānīmidānīm evaeva, just now; immediately; idānīmidānīm apiapi, in this case too; tatatata idānīmidānīm, thereupon, then), RV.; ŚBr.; Ait.; Ragh. [165,3]
  • (in rare cases it is an expletive, affecting but slightly the sense). [165,3]
