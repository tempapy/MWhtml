• kad cl. 1. Ā. kadate, cakāda (R. ed. Gorresio vi, 65, 23; but, R. ed. Bomb. vi, 86, 24 reads cakāra),
    to be confused, suffer mentally;
    to grieve;
    to confound;
    to kill or hurt;
    to call;

  • kad ind. (originally the neuter form of the interrogative pronoun ka), a particle of interrogation (= Lat. nonne, num), RV. [247,3]
  • anything wrong or bad, BhP. vii, 5, 28 (cf. below) [247,3]
  • = sukha, Nigh. [247,3]
