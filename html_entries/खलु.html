• khalukhalu
  • ind. (as a particle of asseveration) indeed, verily, certainly, truly, R.; Śak. &c. [338,2]
  • (as a continuative particle) now, now then, now further, RV. x, 34, 14; TS. &c.; (as a particle in syllogistic speech) but now, = Lat. atqui TBr.; ŚBr. &c. [338,2]
  • ([khalukhalu is only exceptionally found at the beginning of a phrase; it is frequently combined with other particles, thus athaathakhalu, uukhalu, vaivaikhalu, kh°khaluvai, = now then, now further, TS.; TBr.; ŚBr. &c.; in later Sanskṛt khalukhalu frequently does little more than lay stress on the word by which it is preceded, and is sometimes merely expletive; it is also a particle of prohibition (in which case it may be joined with the ind.p. [khalukhalu kṛtvākṛtvā, ‘desist from doing that’] Nir. i, 5[also °tamkhalutam] Pāṇ. iii, 4, 18; Śiś. ii, 70); or of endearment, conciliation, and inquiry, L.; nana khalukhalu, by no means, not at all, indeed not, R. &c.]) [338,2]
