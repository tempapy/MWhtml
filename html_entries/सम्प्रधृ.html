• sam-pra-√dhṛsampradhṛsampradhṛ
    • Caus. -dhārayatisampradhṛdhārayat, to direct towards, deliver over to (dat.), MBh.;
    • (with buddhimbuddhi) to fix the mind or thoughts steadily upon (loc.), determine, resolve, settle, decide, ib.; Śiś.;
    • (with or without buddhyābuddhi, manasāmanasāmanasamanas, or hṛdayehṛdayahṛdayāhṛdi) to ponder, reflect, consider, deliberate or think about (acc.), Mn.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.:
    • Pass. -dhāryatesampradhṛ, to be fixed upon or determined, MBh. [1175,2]
