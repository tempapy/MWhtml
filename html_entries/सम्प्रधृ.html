• sam-pra-√dhṛsampradhṛsampradhṛ
  • Caus. -dhārayatidhārayatisam-pra-dhārayati, to direct towards, deliver over to (dat.), MBh.;
    (with buddhimbuddhim) to fix the mind or thoughts steadily upon (loc.), determine, resolve, settle, decide, ib.; Śiś.;
    (with or without buddhyābuddhyā, manasāmanasā, or hṛdayehṛdaye) to ponder, reflect, consider, deliberate or think about (acc.), Mn.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.:
    Pass. -dhāryatedhāryatesam-pra-dhāryate, to be fixed upon or determined, MBh. [1175,2]
