• yāvatyāvatyāvad mf(atīatīatiat)n.
    • (fr. 3. yaya; correlative of tāvattāvattāvad, q.v.) as great, as large, as much, as many, as often, as frequent, as far, as long, as old &c. (or how great &c. = quantus, quot or qualis), RV. &c. &c. (yāvantaḥyāvat kiyantaḥkiyat, ‘as many as’, TBr.; yāvadyāvadyāvat vāvāvauūvāsvas yāvadyāvadyāvat vāvāvauū, ‘as much as possible’, ŚBr.; yāvatyāvat tāvattāvattāvad, ‘so much as’, in alg. applied to the first unknown quantity [= x] or so much of the unknown as its co-efficient number; in this sense also expressed by the first syllable yāyāyaiī cf. IW. 182; itiitiit yāvatyāvat in Comms. ‘just so much’, ‘only so’, ‘that is to say’, ‘such is the explanation’) [852,1]
  • yāvatyāvatyāvad ind.
    • as greatly as, as far as, as much or as many as [852,1]
    • as often as, whenever [852,1]
    • as long as, whilst [852,1]
    • as soon as, the moment that, until that, till, until, RV. &c. &c. (in these senses used with either pres. Pot. fut. impf., or aor., or with the simple copula). yāvatyāvatyāvad with the 1st sg. of pres., rarely of Pot., may denote an intended action and may be translated by ‘meanwhile’, ‘just’ [852,1]
    • yāvadyāvadyāvat yāvad-tāvatyāvadtāvat tāvattāvattāvad, ‘as gradually as-so’, ŚBr. [852,1]
    • yāvanyāvanyāvat nana, ‘while not’, ‘before’, ‘till’ [852,1]
    • ‘if not’, ‘whether not’ [852,1]
    • nana yāvattāvat, ‘scarcely-when’, ‘no sooner-than’ [852,1]
    • nana paramparampara or nana kevalam-yāvatkevalamyāvat, ‘not only-but even’. Sometimes yāvatyāvatyāvad is also used as a preposition with a prec. or following acc., or with a following abl., rarely dat., e.g. māsammāsamās ekaṃeka yāvatyāvatyāvad, ‘during one month’ [852,1]
    • sūryȏdayaṃsūryodayasūryasūryāudaya yāvatyāvatyāvad, ‘until sunrise’ [852,1]
    • sarpa-vivaraṃsarpavivarasarpa yāvatyāvatyāvad, ‘up to the serpent's hole’ [852,1]
    • yāvadyāvad or yāvad-āyāvadyāvadyāvatāa samāpanātsamāpana, ‘until the completion’ [852,1]
    • yāvadyāvadyāvat garbhasyagarbha paripākāyaparipāka, ‘until the maturity of the fetus’. Sometimes also with a nom. followed by itiitiit, e.g. antaantaanteantiantantuantasatantar itiitiit yāvatyāvat, ‘as far as the end’ [852,1]
    • pañcapañca yāvadyāvad itiitiit, ‘up to five’ [852,1]
  • yāvatyāvatyāvad ind.
    • or with another ind. word, e.g. adyaadya yāvatyāvat, ‘up to this day’. yāvatāyāvatāyāvat ind. as far as, as long as, Āpast.; R.; BhP. [852,1]
  • yāvatyāvatyāvad ind.
    • till, until (with Pot.), Lāṭy. (with nana, as long as not, before, BhP.) [852,1]
    • as soon as, the moment that, Cat. [852,1]
    • in as much as, Pat. [852,1]
  • yāvatyāvatyāvad ind.
    • yāvati-tāvatiyāvatitāvatitāvat, Daś. [852,1]
