• yāvat mf(atī)n. (fr. 3. ya; correlative of tāvat q.v.) as great, as large, as much, as many, as often, as frequent, as far, as long, as old &c. (or how great &c. = quantus, quot or qualis), RV. &c. &c. (yāvantaḥ kiyantaḥ, ‘as many as’ TBr.; yāvad vā yāvad vā, ‘as much as possible’ ŚBr.; yāvat tāvat, ‘so much as’, in alg. applied to the first unknown quantity [= x] or so much of the unknown as its co-efficient number; in this sense also expressed by the first syllable yā cf. IW. 182; iti yāvat in Comms. ‘just so much’, ‘only so’, ‘that is to say’, ‘such is the explanation’) [852,1]
  • yāvat ind. as greatly as, as far as, as much or as many as [852,1]
  • as often as, whenever [852,1]
  • as long as, whilst [852,1]
  • as soon as, the moment that, until that, till, until, RV. &c. &c. (in these senses used with either pres. Pot. fut. impf., or aor., or with the simple copula). yāvat with the 1st sg. of pres., rarely of Pot., may denote an intended action and may be translated by ‘meanwhile’, ‘just’ [852,1]
  • yāvad yāvad-tāvatyāvad-tāvatyāvadtāvat tāvat, ‘as gradually as-so’ ŚBr. [852,1]
  • yāvan na, ‘while not’, ‘before’, ‘till’ [852,1]
  • ‘if not’, ‘whether not’ [852,1]
  • na yāvattāvat, ‘scarcely-when’, ‘no sooner-than’ [852,1]
  • na param or na kevalam-yāvatkevalam-yāvatkevalamyāvat, ‘not only-but even.’ Sometimes yāvat is also used as a preposition with a prec. or following acc., or with a following abl., rarely dat. e.g. māsam ekaṃ yāvat, ‘during one month’ [852,1]
  • sūryȏdayaṃ yāvat, ‘until sunrise’ [852,1]
  • sarpa-vivaraṃsarpa-vivaraṃsarpavivaraṃ yāvat, ‘up to the serpent's hole’ [852,1]
  • yāvad or yāvad-āyāvad-āyāvadā samāpanāt, ‘until the completion’ [852,1]
  • yāvad garbhasya paripākāya, ‘until the maturity of the fetus.’ Sometimes also with a nom. followed by iti e.g. anta iti yāvat, ‘as far as the end’ [852,1]
  • pañca yāvad iti, ‘up to five’ [852,1]
  • yāvat ind. or with another ind. word e.g. adya yāvat, ‘up to this day.’ yāvatā ind. as far as, as long as, Āpast.; R.; BhP. ⮩ yāvatā [852,1]
  • yāvat ind. till, until (with Pot.), Lāṭy. (with na, as long as not, before, BhP.) [852,1]
  • as soon as, the moment that, Cat. [852,1]
  • in as much as, Pat. [852,1]
