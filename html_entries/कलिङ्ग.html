    • N. of a people and their country (the N. is applied in the Purāṇas to several places, but especially signifies a district on the Coromandel coast, extending from below Cuttack [Kaṭaka] to the vicinity of Madras), MBh.; Hariv.; VP. &c. [262,1]
  • kaliṅgakaliṅga m.
    • an inhabitant of Kaliṅga, Sāh. [262,1]
    • N. of a king of Kaliṅga (from whom the Kaliṅga people are said to have originated; he is sometimes mentioned as a son of Dīrghatamas and Sudeṣṇā, sometimes identified with Bali), MBh.; Hariv. &c. [262,1]
    • N. of a being attending on Skanda, MBh. (ed. Bomb.) ix, 45, 64 (v.l. kalindakalinda ed. Calc.) [262,1]
    • N. of several authors [262,1]
    • the fork-tailed shrike, L. [262,1]
    • Caesalpina Bonducella, L. [262,1]
    • Wrightia antidysenterica, L. [262,1]
    • Acacia Sirissa, L. [262,1]
    • Ficus infectoria, L. [262,1]
