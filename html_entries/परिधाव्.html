• pari-√dhāvparidhāvparidhāv
  • P. -dhāvatidhāvatipari-dhāvati (ep. also °teparidhāvte), to flow or stream round or through, RV.; Suśr.;
    to run or drive about (with mṛgayāmmṛgayām, ‘to hunt’), MBh.;
    to run or move round anything (with acc.), AV.; MBh. &c.;
    to run through or towards or after (with acc.), MBh.; Kāv. &c.:
    Caus. -dhāvayatidhāvayatipari-dhāvayati, to surround, encircle, MBh. [596,1]
