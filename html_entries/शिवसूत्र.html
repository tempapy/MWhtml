• śivaśiva—sūtrasūtra n.
    • (cf. spanda-sūtraspandasūtraspandasūtra) N. of the aphorisms of the Śaiva philosophy (attributed to the god Śiva), Sarvad. [1075,3]
    • N. of the 14 Sūtras with which Pāṇini opens his grammar (containing a peculiar method of arranging the alphabet or alphabetical sounds, said to have been communicated to him by the god Śiva) [1075,3]
