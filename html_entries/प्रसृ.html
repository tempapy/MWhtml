• pra-√sṛpra-√sṛpra√sṛ P. -sisartiprasṛ-sisartisisarti, (only Ved.) and sarati (sometimes also Ā. °te), to move forwards, advance (‘for’ or ‘against’ acc.), proceed (lit. and fig.), spring up, come forth, issue from (abl.), appear, rise, spread, extend, RV. &c. &c.;
    to break out (as fire, a disease &c.), MBh.; Pañcat. (v.l.);

    to agree, promise, Inscr. :
    Caus. -sārayatiprasṛ-sārayatisārayati, to stretch out, extend, VS. &c. &c.;

    to diffuse, circulate, exhibit, Var.; Śaṃk.;
    to prosecute, transact, Kād.;
    (in gram.) to change a semivowel into the corresponding vowel, Pat. :
