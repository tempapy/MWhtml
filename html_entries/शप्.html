• śapśap
  • (in gram.) a technical term used for the Vikaraṇavikaraṇa a (inserted between the root and terminations of the conjugational tenses in verbs of the 1st class; See vi-karaṇavikaraṇavikaraṇa, p.954). [1052,1]
  • śapśap
  • ind. a prefix implying assent or acceptance (as in śap-karotiśapkarotiśapkaroti, he admits or accepts), W. [1052,1]
  • śapśap [Roots]
