• ahanahan
  • n. the base of the weak and some other cases of aharahar, q.v. e.g. instr. ahnāahnā ([once ahanāahanā, RV. i, 123, 4]) [124,2]
  • dat. ahneahne [124,2]
  • loc. ahanahan (Ved.) or ahaniahani, or ahniahni, &c. [124,2]
  • nom. du. ahanīahanī (See also s.v. aharahar) and pl. ahāniahāni [124,2]
  • only Ved. are the middle cases of the pl. ahabhyasahabhyas ([RV.]), ahabhisahabhis ([RV., nine times]), and ahasuahasu ([RV. i, 124, 9]), while the later language forms them fr. the base ahasahas q.v. [124,2]
