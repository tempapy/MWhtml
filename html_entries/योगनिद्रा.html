• yogayoga—nidrānidrā
  • f. ‘meditation-sleep’, a state of half meditation half sleep (which admits of the full exercise of the mental powers; it is peculiar to devotees), light sleep, (esp.) the sleep of Viṣṇuviṣṇu at the end of a Yugayuga, Viṣṇuviṣṇu's Sleep personified as a goddess and said to be a form of Durgādurgā, MBh.; Ragh. &c. [857,1]
  • (accord. to others) the great sleep of Brahmābrahmā during the period between the annihilation and reproduction of the universe, MW. [857,1]
