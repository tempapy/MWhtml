• prati-√hṛpratihṛpratihṛ
  • P. Ā. -haratiharatiprati-harati, °teprate (ind.p. pratī-hārampratīhārampratīhāram, Kauś.; inf. prati-hartavepratihartavepratihartave, BhP.),
    to throw back, AV.;
    to strike or pound, Kauś.;
    to keep shut, close by pressure (an udder), TāṇḍBr.;
    to bring back, Lāṭy.;
    to deliver, offer, present, BhP.;
    to procure, ib.;
    (Ā.) to take i.e. eat, ChUp.;
    to join in the Sāmansāman hymns as Pratihartṛpratihartṛ (See below), Lāṭy. :
    Caus. hārayatihārayati, to have one's self announced to (gen.), Jātakam. :
    Desid. -jihīrṣatijihīrṣatiprati-jihīrṣati, to wish to requite or revenge, MBh. (cf. prati-jihīrṣupratijihīrṣupratijihīrṣu). [673,1]
