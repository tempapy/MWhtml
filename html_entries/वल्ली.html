• vallīvallī
  • f. (or vallivalli q.v.) a creeper, creeping plant (often fig. applied to arms, eyebrows, lightning &c.), Mn.; MBh. &c. [929,1]
  • a class of medicinal plants (= vidārīvidārī, sārivāsārivā, rajanīrajanī, and guḍucīguḍucī), Suśr. [929,1]
  • N. of various other plants (esp. = aja-modāajamodāajamodā, kaivartikākaivartikā, and cavyacavya), L. [929,1]
  • (only īī) N. of the sections of partic. Upaniṣadupaniṣads (e.g. of the Kaṭhakaṭha) [929,1]
  • = phala-vallīphalavallīphalavallī (q.v.), Āryabh. Sch. [929,1]
