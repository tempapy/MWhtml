• vallī f. (or valli q.v.) a creeper, creeping plant (often fig. applied to arms, eyebrows, lightning &c.), Mn.; MBh. &c. [929,1]
  • a class of medicinal plants (= vidārī, sārivā, rajanī, and guḍucī), Suśr. [929,1]
  • N. of various other plants (esp. = aja-modāaja-modāajamodā, kaivartikā, and cavya), L. [929,1]
  • (only ī) N. of the sections of partic. Upaniṣads (e.g. of the Kaṭha) [929,1]
  • = phala-vallīphala-vallīphalavallī (q.v.), Āryabh. Sch. [929,1]
