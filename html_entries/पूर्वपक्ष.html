• pūrvapūrva—pakṣapakṣa
  • m. the fore part or side, TBr. [643,3]
  • the first half of a lunar month, the fortnight of the waxing moon, TS.; Br.; Lāṭy. &c. (°kṣāhapūrvapakṣāha, a day in the first half &c., ĀpGṛ.) [643,3]
  • the first half of a year, KātyŚr. [643,3]
  • an action at law, the first statement of the plaintiff, first step in a law-suit, Yājñ.; Viṣṇ.; Nār. [643,3]
  • the first objection to an assertion in any discussion, the prima facie view or argument in any question, Śaṃk.; Suśr.; MārkP. (cf. IW. 99) [643,3]
