• ny-√asnyasnyas
  • P. -asyatiasyatiasyati, or -asatiasatiasati, to throw or cast or lay or put down (with loc. e.g. bhuvibhuvi, on the earth), AV. &c. &c.;
    to take off, MBh.; R.;
    to give up, resign (as life), R.;
    to set in the ground, plant (prarohānprarohān, shoots), Bālar.;
    (with loc.) to throw or hurl upon, pour or shed on or in, put or place or fix or insert in, turn or direct to, deposit with, intrust or commit to;
    to settle arrange, MBh.; Kāv. &c. (with citrecitre, ‘to place in a picture’, paint, depict; with śirasiśirasi, ‘to place on the head’, receive with reverence; with manasimanasi, ‘to call to mind’, reflect, ponder; with pathipathi, ‘to lay on the road’, give up);
    to bring forward, mention, Mall. :
    Caus. (pf. nyāsayāmnyāsayām āsaāsa or °sayāṃnyasayāṃ cakrecakre) to cause to put or lay down, MBh. [572,1]
