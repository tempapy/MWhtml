• aikyaaikya n.
    • (fr. ekaeka), oneness, unity, harmony, sameness, identity, MBh.; Ragh.; Sarvad. &c. [233,2]
    • identity of the human soul or of the universe with the Deity, MW. [233,2]
    • an aggregate, sum, Sūryas. [233,2]
    • (in math.) the product of the length and depth of excavations differing in depth. [233,2]
