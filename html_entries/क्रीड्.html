• krīḍkrīḍ [Roots]
  • cl. 1. P. krīḍatikrīḍati (or krīłatikrīłati, RV.; ep. also Ā.; perf. cikrīḍacikrīḍa, MBh.; fut. p. krīḍiṣyatkrīḍiṣyat, BhP. iii, 17, 24 ; aor. akrīḍītakrīḍīt, Bhaṭṭ.),
    to play, sport, amuse one's self, frolic, gambol, dally (used of men, animals, the wind and waves, &c.), RV.; VS.; AV. &c.;
    to jest, joke with (instr. or instr. with sahasaha or sārdhamsārdham; once acc. Mṛcch.), Mn.; Yājñ.; MBh. &c.:
    Caus. krīḍayatikrīḍayati, to cause to play, allow to play, MBh. i, 6440; iv, 329; BhP. [321,2]
