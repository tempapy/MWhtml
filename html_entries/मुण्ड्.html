• muṇḍmuṇḍ
  • (prob. artificial, to serve as the supposed source of the words below) cl. 1. P. to cut (khaṇḍanekhaṇḍane = chidichidi), Dhātup. ix, 40;
    to crush, grind, ix, 38 (v.l. for muṭmuṭ);
    cl. 1. Ā. ‘to cleanse’ or ‘to sink’ or ‘to shave’ (mārjanemārjane v.l. magnemagne and muṇḍanemuṇḍane), viii, 22 :
    Caus. muṇḍayatimuṇḍayati (or muṇḍāpayatimuṇḍāpayati, Divyâv.) See muṇḍayamuṇḍaya below. [822,1]
