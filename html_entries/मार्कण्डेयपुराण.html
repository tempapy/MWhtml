• mārkaṇḍeya—purāṇa n. N. of one of the 18 Purāṇas (so called from its supposed author Mārkaṇḍeya; it expounds the nature of Kṛṣṇa and explains some of the incidents of the Mahā-bhāratamahā-bhāratamahābhārata; it differs from the other Purāṇas in the form of its narrative rather than its sectarial character), IW. 387 n. 1; 514.
