• mārkaṇḍeyamārkaṇḍeya—purāṇapurāṇa
  • n. N. of one of the 18 Purāṇapurāṇas (so called from its supposed author Mārkaṇḍeyamārkaṇḍeya; it expounds the nature of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa and explains some of the incidents of the Mahā-bhāratamahābhāratamahābhārata; it differs from the other Purāṇapurāṇas in the form of its narrative rather than its sectarial character), IW. 387 n. 1; 514. [812,2]
