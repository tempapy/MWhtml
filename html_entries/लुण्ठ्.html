• luṇṭhluṇṭh
  • (cf. √ruṇṭhruṇṭh), to stir, agitate, MBh.;
    to go, Dhātup. ix, 61;
    to be idle;
    to be lame;
    to resist, ix, 58;
    to rob, plunder, ix, 41 (cf. nir-nirnirluṇṭh and vi-√luṇṭhviluṇṭhviluṇṭh) :
    Caus. luṇṭhayatiluṇṭhayati (Pass. luṇṭhyateluṇṭhyate), to cause to rob or plunder, Siṉhâs.;
    to rob, steal, plunder, sack, Rājat.; Kathās. [904,2]
