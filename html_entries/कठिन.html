  • kaṭhinakaṭhina n.
    • an earthen vessel for cooking, MBh.; R. [244,2]
    • a strap or pole for carrying burdens, Pāṇ. iv, 4, 72 (cf. vaṃśa-kaṭhinavaṃśakaṭhinavaṃśakaṭhina) [244,2]
    • a shovel, scoop, L. [244,2]
  • kaṭhinakaṭhina n.
    • (also) a garment made in a day and offered to a monk as a present, Buddh. [1323,3]
