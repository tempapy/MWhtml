• vi-vartavivartavivarta—vādavāda m.
    • a method of asserting the Vedānta doctrine (maintaining the development of the Universe from Brahma as the sole real entity, the phenomenal world being held to be a mere illusion or Māyā; cf. pariṇāma-vādāpariṇāmavādapariṇāmavāda), Madhus. [988,3]
