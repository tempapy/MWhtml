• vairāgyavairāgya n.
    • change or loss of colour, growing pale, Suśr.; Kām. [1025,2]
    • disgust, aversion, distaste for or loathing of (loc. abl., or comp.), Bhag.; Ragh. &c. [1025,2]
    • freedom from all worldly desires, indifference to worldly objects and to life, asceticism, Up.; MBh. &c. [1025,2]
