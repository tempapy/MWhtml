• pūrṇapūrṇa—pātrapātra mf(īīi)n.
    • a full vessel or cup, as much as will fill a vessel, a cupful (as a measure of capacity properly 256 handfuls of rice), ŚBr.; TBr.; GṛŚrS. (also īīi f., ŚāṅkhGṛ.) [642,2]
  • pūrṇapūrṇa—pātrapātra n.
    • a vessel full of rice presented at a sacrifice to the superintending and officiating priests, W. [642,2]
    • a vessel filled with valuable things to be distributed as presents (esp. a present made to any one who brings good news), Mālatīm.; Kād.; Hcar. [642,2]
