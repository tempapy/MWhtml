• utsukautsuka mfn.
    • (fr. sususū, ‘well’, with 1. udud in the sense of ‘apart’, and affix kaka), restless, uneasy, unquiet, anxious, R.; MBh. &c. [182,2]
    • anxiously desirous, zealously active, striving or making exertions for any object (cf. jayȏtsukajayajayāutsuka), R.; Pañcat.; Śak.; Megh. &c. [182,2]
    • eager for, fond of, attached to [182,2]
    • regretting, repining, missing, sorrowing for, Ragh.; Vikr.; Śak. &c. [182,2]
  • utsukautsuka n.
    • sorrow [182,2]
    • longing for, desire (See nirutsukanirutsuka). [182,2]
