  • Uh [Roots 2]
  • cl. 1. Ā. P. Uhati, -te (Ved. ohate), UhAM-cakAra, &c. (by native authorities not distinguished from 1. Uh above), to observe, mark, note, attend to, heed, regard, RV.; AV. xx, 131, 10;
    to expect, hope for, wait for, listen for, RV.;
    to comprehend, conceive, conjecture, guess, suppose, infer, reason, deliberate upon, MBh.; BhP.; Nyāyam.; Bhaṭṭ. &c.:
    Caus. Uhayati (aor. Ojihat), to consider, heed, MBh.;
    to cause to suppose or infer, Bhaṭṭ. [223,2]
