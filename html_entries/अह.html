• aha ind. (as a particle implying ascertainment, affirmation, certainty, &c.) surely, certainly, RV.; AV.; ŚBr. [124,1]
  • (as explaining, defining) namely, ŚBr. [124,1]
  • (as admitting, limiting, &c.) it is true, I grant, granted, indeed, at least, ŚBr. ([For the rules of accentuation necessitated in a phrase by the particle aha cf. Pāṇ. viii, 1, 24 seqq.]) [124,1]
  • aha n. (only Ved.; nom. pl. ahā, RV.; AV.; gen. pl. ahānām, RV. viii, 22, 13) = ahar q.v., a day [124,1]
  • often ifc. m(aha). (e.g. dvādaśā̑ha, try-ahatry-ahatryaha, ṣaḍ-ahaṣaḍ-ahaṣaḍaha, &c.) or n. (e.g. puṇyā̑ha, bhadrā̑ha, and sudinā̑ha) [124,1]
  • See also ahna s.v. [124,1]
  • 1 (also) a particle answering to ha in a preceding sentence (ha-ahaha-ahahaaha = μὲν --δέ), Gaṇar. [1318,3]
