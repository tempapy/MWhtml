• ahaaha
  • ind. (as a particle implying ascertainment, affirmation, certainty, &c.) surely, certainly, RV.; AV.; ŚBr. [124,1]
  • (as explaining, defining) namely, ŚBr. [124,1]
  • (as admitting, limiting, &c.) it is true, I grant, granted, indeed, at least, ŚBr. ([For the rules of accentuation necessitated in a phrase by the particle ahaaha cf. Pāṇ. viii, 1, 24 seqq.]) [124,1]
  • ahaaha
  • n. (only Ved.; nom. pl. ahāahā, RV.; AV.; gen. pl. ahānāmahānām, RV. viii, 22, 13) = aharahar q.v., a day [124,1]
  • often ifc. m(ahaaha). (e.g. dvādaśā̑hadvādaśāha, try-ahatryahatryaha, ṣaḍ-ahaṣaḍahaṣaḍaha, &c.) or n. (e.g. puṇyā̑hapuṇyāha, bhadrā̑habhadrāha, and sudinā̑hasudināha) [124,1]
  • See also ahnaahna s.v. [124,1]
  • 1 (also) a particle answering to haha in a preceding sentence (ha-ahahaahahaaha = μὲν --δέ), Gaṇar. [1318,3]
