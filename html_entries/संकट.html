• saṃ-kaṭasaṃkaṭasaṃsamkaṭa mf(āāa)n.

    • dangerous, critical, MBh. [1125,2]
    • (ifc.) crowded with, full of Kād. [1125,2]
  • saṃ-kaṭasaṃkaṭasaṃsamkaṭa m.
    • N. of a partic. personification (a son of Kakubh), BhP. [1125,2]
    • of a man, Rājat. [1125,2]
    • of a gander or flamingo, Kathās.; Pañcat.; Hit. [1125,2]
  • saṃ-kaṭasaṃkaṭasaṃsamkaṭa n.
    • a narrow passage, strait, defile, pass, MBh.; Kāv. &c., a strait, difficulty, critical condition, danger to or from (comp.; cf. prā̑ṇa-s°prāṇasaṃkaṭaprāṇapraprāsaṃkaṭa), ib. [1125,2]
