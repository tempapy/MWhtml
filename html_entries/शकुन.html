  • (with vasiṣṭhasya) N. of a Sāman, ĀrṣBr. [1046,2]
  • N. of an Asura, BhP. [1046,2]
  • pl. N. of a people, MBh.; Buddh. [1046,2]
  • śakuna n. any auspicious object or lucky omen, an omen or prognostic (in general; rarely ‘an inauspicious omen’), Kāv.; Kathās.; Pañcat. [1046,2]
