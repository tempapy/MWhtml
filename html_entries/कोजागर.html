• ko-jāgarakojāgarakojāgara
  • m. a kind of festival (night of full moon in month Āśvinaāśvina [September-October], celebrated with various games; accord. to some fr. kaḥkaḥ and jāgarajāgara, ‘who is awake?’ the exclamation of Lakṣmīlakṣmī, who descending on this night, promised wealth to all that were awake; hence the night is spent in festivity in honour of the goddess), LiṅgaP.
