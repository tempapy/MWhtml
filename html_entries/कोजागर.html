• ko-jāgarako-jāgarakojāgara m. a kind of festival (night of full moon in month Āśvina [September-October], celebrated with various games; accord. to some fr. kaḥ and jāgara, ‘who is awake?’ the exclamation of Lakṣmī, who descending on this night, promised wealth to all that were awake; hence the night is spent in festivity in honour of the goddess), LiṅgaP.
