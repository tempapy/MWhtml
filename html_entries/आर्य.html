  • one who is faithful to the religion of his country [152,2]

  • a man highly esteemed, a respectable, honourable man, Pañcat.; Śak. &c. [152,2]
  • a master, an owner, L. [152,2]

  • a Vaiśya, L. [152,2]

  • (with Buddhists [Pāli ayyo, or ariyo]) a man who has thought on the four chief truths of Buddhism (See next col.) and lives accordingly, a Buddhist priest [152,2]
