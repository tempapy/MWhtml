• himā̑layahimālaya
  • m. ‘abode of snow’, the Himâlayahimālaya range of mountains (bounding India on the north and containing the highest elevations in the world; in mythology personified as husband of Menāmenā or Menakāmenakā [by whom he had a son Mainākamaināka] and father of Pārvatīpārvatī, ‘daughter of the Mountain’, and of Gaṅgāgaṅgā, who, as the personified Ganges, is generally regarded as his eldest daughter), Bhag.; Kāv. &c. [1299,2]
  • the white Khadirakhadira tree, L. [1299,2]
  • himā̑layahimālaya
  • Nom. P. °yatihimālayati, to resemble the Himâlayahimālaya, Dhūrtas. [1299,2]
