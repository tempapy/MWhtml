• sam-prȇṣsampreṣsampreṣ
  • (-pra-prapra-sampreṣ 1 √iṣiṣ) P. -preṣyatipreṣyatisam-preṣyati, to send forth, throw, fling, cast, MW.;
    (in Vedic ritual) to summon, invite (cf. 2. prȇṣpreṣ), Br.; ŚrS. :
    Caus. -prȇṣayatipreṣayatisam-preṣayati, to send forth together, send away, despatch, send, dismiss, MBh.; R. &c.;
    to send a message to (gen.), MBh.;
    to direct (the thoughts) towards (loc.), Lalit. [1177,2]
