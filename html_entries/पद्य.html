• padyapadya mf(āāa)n.
    • (fr. 3. padpad and padapada) relating or belonging to a foot, RV.; Kāṭh. [584,1]

    • marked with footsteps, ib. iv, 4, 87, Sch. [584,1]
    • measuring a Pada in length or breadth, KātyŚr. (also in comp. with numerals; cf. ardha-ardhapadya, daśa-daśapadyadaśapadya) [584,1]
    • consisting of Padas or parts of verses, Br.; ĀśvGṛ.; RPrāt. [584,1]
    • consisting of one Pada, KātyŚr., Sch. [584,1]
    • forming the end, final, APrāt. [584,1]
  • padyapadya m.
    • a Śūdra, L. (cf. paj-japajjapajpatja) [584,1]
    • a part of a word, verbal element, RPrāt. [584,1]
  • padyapadya n.
    • a verse, metre, poetry (opp. to gadyagadya, prose), Vām.; Kāvyād.; Sāh. &c. [584,1]
    • N. of sev. hymns. [584,1]
