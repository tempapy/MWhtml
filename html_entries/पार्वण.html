• pārvaṇapārvaṇa mf(īīiin)n.
    • (fr. parvanparvan) belonging or relating to a division of time or to the changes of the moon (such as at new or full moon) [622,1]
    • increasing, waxing, full (as the moon; parvaṇauparvaṇa śaśi-divākarauśaśidivākaraśaśidivākara, moon and sun at the time of full moon, Ragh. xi, 82), GṛŚrS.; Kāv.; Pur. &c. [622,1]
  • pārvaṇapārvaṇa m.
    • a half-month (= pakṣapakṣa), Jyot. [622,1]
    • oblations offered at new and full moon, GṛS. [622,1]
