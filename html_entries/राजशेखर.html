• rājarāja—śekharaśekhara m.
    • (also with kavikavi, sūrisūrisūrīsūrin &c.) N. of various authors and teachers; (esp.) of a poet (son of Durdaka and Śīla-vatī, tutor of Mahêndrapāla, king of Kānyakubja; he lived in the 10th century and wrote 4 plays, viz. Pracaṇḍa-pāṇḍava or Bāla-bhārata, Bāla-rāmāyaṇa, Viddhaśāla-bhañjikā, and Karpūra-mañjarī) [874,2]
