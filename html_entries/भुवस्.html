(it becomes bhuvar in bhuvar-lokabhuvar-lokabhuvarloka, ‘the world of the air’ VP.) [760,3]
  • one of the mind-born sons of Brahmā, Hariv. 11506 [760,3]
  • N. of the 2nd and 11th Kalpa (q.v.), VāyuP. [760,3]
