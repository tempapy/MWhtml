    • (etym. doubtful) a small part of anything, any single part or portion of a whole, esp. a sixteenth part, RV. viii, 47, 17; TS.; ŚBr.; Mn. &c. [261,1]
    • a digit or one-sixteenth of the moon's diameter, Hit.; Kathās. [261,1]
    • (personified as a daughter of Kardama and wife of Marīci, BhP.) [261,1]
    • a symbolical expression for the number sixteen, Hcat. [261,1]
    • interest on a capital (considered as a certain part of it), Śiś. ix, 32 [261,1]
    • a division of time (said to be 1/900 of a day or 1-6 minutes, Mn. i, 64; Hariv.; or 1/1800 of a day or 0-8 minutes Comm. on VP.; or 2 minutes and 26 54/201 seconds, Suśr.; or 1 minute and 35 205/301, seconds, or 8 seconds, BhavP.) [261,1]
    • the sixtieth part of one-thirtieth of a zodiacal sign, a minute of a degree, Sūryas. [261,1]
    • (in prosody) a syllabic instant [261,1]
    • a term for the seven substrata of the elements or Dhātus of the human body (viz. flesh, blood, fat, phlegm, urine, bile, and semen; but according to Hemacandra, rasarasa, ‘chyle’, asthiasthi, ‘bone’, and majjanmajjan, ‘marrow’, take the place of phlegm, urine, and bile), Suśr. [261,1]
    • an atom (there are 3015 Kalās or atoms in every one of the six Dhātus, not counting the rasarasa, therefore in all 18090) [261,1]
    • (with Pāśupatas) the elements of the gross or material world, Sarvad. [261,1]
    • an embryo shortly after conception (cf. kalanakalana) [261,1]
    • a designation of the three constituent parts of a sacrifice (viz. mantramantra, dravyadravya, and śraddhāśraddhāśraddha, Nīlak. on MBh. [ed. Bomb.] xiv, 89, 3) [261,1]
