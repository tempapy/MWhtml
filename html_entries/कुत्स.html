• kutsakutsa m.
    • N. of a Ṛṣi (called Ārjuneya, author of several hymns of the RV.; when attacked by the demon Śuṣṇa, Indra defended him and killed the demon; but in other hymns [RV. i, 53, 10; ii, 14, 7; iv, 26, 1; viii, 53, 2] Kutsa is represented as persecuted by Indra), RV.; AV. iv, 29, 5; TāṇḍyaBr. [290,3]
    • N. of a descendant of Aṅgiras (author of the hymns, RV. i, 94-98; 100-115; ix, 97, 45 seqq.), ĀśvŚr. [290,3]
    • lightning, thunderbolt, Naigh.; Nir. [290,3]
    • (also) a distance of about 30 inches, L. [1325,1]
  • kutsakutsa m. pl.
    • (Pāṇ. ii, 4, 65) the descendants or the family of Kutsa, RV. vii, 25, 5; Lāṭy. [290,3]
  • kutsakutsa n.
    • the plant Costus speciosus or arabicus, L. (cf. kautsakautsa, puru-kutsapurukutsapurukutsa, &c.) [290,3]
