• prathamaprathama mf(āāa)n.
    • (for pra-tamapratama, superl. of 1. prapra; rarely declined as a pron., e.g. °masyāḥprathama, AV. vi, 18, 1; °meprathamaprathamā, PañcavBr. xxv, 18, 5; R. iv, 37, 11; Kir. ii, 44; cf. Pāṇ. i, 1, 33) foremost, first (in time or in a series or in rank) [678,3]
    • earliest, primary, original, prior, former [678,3]
    • preceding, initial, chief, principal, most excellent, RV. &c. &c. [678,3]
    • often translatable adverbially = ibc. (cf. below) and (amama ind.) firstly, at first, for the first time [678,3]
    • just, newly, at once, forthwith (also ātāta, Hariv.) [678,3]
    • formerly, previously (amama also as prep. with gen. = before, e.g. Mn. ii, 194; prathamam-anantaramprathamamanantaramanantara or paścātpaścātpaścapaścād, first—afterwards; prathamam-tatasprathamamtatastatatat, first—next) [678,3]
  • prathamaprathama m.
    • (in gram., scil. varṇavarṇa), the first consonant of a Varga, a surd unaspirate letter [678,3]
    • (scil. puruṣapuruṣa), the first (= our 3rd) person or its terminations [678,3]
    • (scil. svarasvara), the first tone [678,3]
    • in math. the sum of the products divided by the difference between the squares of the cosine of the azimuth and the sine of the amplitude [678,3]
