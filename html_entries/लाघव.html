• lāghavalāghava n.
    • (fr. laghulaghulaghū) swiftness, rapidity, speed, MBh.; R. [899,3]
    • alacrity, versatility, dexterity, skill, MBh.; Kāv. &c. [899,3]
    • lightness (also of heart), ease, relief, Mn.; Yājñ.; Suśr. [899,3]
    • levity, thoughtlessness, inconsiderateness, rashness, R.; Kathās. [899,3]
    • insignificance, unimportance, smallness, R.; Mālav.; MārkP. [899,3]
    • (in prosody) shortness of a vowel or syllable (opp. to gauravagaurava), Piṅg. [899,3]
    • shortness of expression, brevity, conciseness, Sarvad.; Kāty. Sch. [899,3]
    • lack of weight or consequence, derogation of dignity, slight, disrespect, MBh.; Kāv. &c. [899,3]
