• bhaṭṭibhaṭṭi—kāvyakāvya
  • n. ‘the poem of Bhaṭṭibhaṭṭi’, N. of an artificial poem by Bhaṭṭa-svāminbhaṭṭasvāminbhaṭṭasvāmin (originally called Rāvaṇa-vadharāvaṇavadharāvaṇavadha; celebrating the exploits of Rāmarāma and illustrating Sanskṛt grammar by the systematic application of all possible forms and constructions). [745,2]
