• bhaṭṭi—kāvya n. ‘the poem of Bhaṭṭi’, N. of an artificial poem by Bhaṭṭa-svāminbhaṭṭa-svāminbhaṭṭasvāmin (originally called Rāvaṇa-vadharāvaṇa-vadharāvaṇavadha; celebrating the exploits of Rāma and illustrating Sanskṛt grammar by the systematic application of all possible forms and constructions).
