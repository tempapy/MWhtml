• bhaṭṭibhaṭṭi—kāvyakāvya n.
    • ‘the poem of Bhaṭṭi’, N. of an artificial poem by Bhaṭṭa-svāmin (originally called Rāvaṇa-vadha; celebrating the exploits of Rāma and illustrating Sanskṛt grammar by the systematic application of all possible forms and constructions). [745,2]
