• śaṇīraśaṇīra
  • n. a bank or alluvial island in the middle of the river Śoṇāśoṇā, L. [1048,3]
  • an island enclosed by the branches of the river Sarayūsarayū at the point where it falls into the Ganges above Chupra (this spot is also called Dardarī-taṭadardarītaṭadardarītaṭa, ‘Dardarīdardarī-bank’), L. [1048,3]
