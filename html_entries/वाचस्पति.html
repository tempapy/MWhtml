• vācasvācasvācavāc—patipatipat m.
    • (vācas-vācasvācaspati) ‘lord of voice or speech’, N. of a divine being (presiding over human life which lasts as long as there is voice in the body; applied to Soma, Viśva-karman, Prajā-pati, Brahmā &c., but esp. to Bṛhas-pati, who is lord of eloquence, preceptor of the gods, and regent of the planet Jupiter), RV.; AV. &c. [937,2]
    • a master of speech, orator, BhP. [937,2]
    • N. of a Ṛṣi, a lexicographer, a philosopher &c., Sarvad.; Uṇ., Sch. &c. [937,2]
