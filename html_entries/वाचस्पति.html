• vācas—pati m. (vācas-vācasvācas-vācaspati) ‘lord of voice or speech’, N. of a divine being (presiding over human life which lasts as long as there is voice in the body; applied to Soma, Viśva-karmanviśva-karmanviśvakarman, Prajā-patiprajā-patiprajāpati, Brahmā &c., but esp. to Bṛhas-patibṛhas-patibṛhaspati, who is lord of eloquence, preceptor of the gods, and regent of the planet Jupiter), RV.; AV. &c. [937,2]
  • a master of speech, orator, BhP. [937,2]
  • N. of a Ṛṣi, a lexicographer, a philosopher &c., Sarvad.; Uṇ. Sch. &c. [937,2]
