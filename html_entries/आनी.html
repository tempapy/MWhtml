• ā-√nīānīānī
  • P. -nayatinayatiā-nayati (1. pl. ā-nayāmasiānayāmasiānayāmasi, AV. v, 25, 8; Impv. 2. sg. ā-nayaānayaānaya, 3. sg. ā-nayatuānayatuānayatu; pf. ā-nināyaānināyaānināya, AV. v, 17, 2, and ā-nināyaānināyaānināya, RV. viii, 21, 9 ; inf. -netavainetavaiā-netavai, ŚBr. ii, 1, 14, 16) Ā. (1. sg. -nayenayeā-naye, R.)
    to lead towards or near;
    to bring, carry to a place (acc. or loc.);
    to fetch, RV.; AV.; SV.; ŚBr.; MBh.; R.; Śak. &c.;
    (perf. periphr. -nayāmāsanayāmāsaā-nayāmāsa, MBh. iii, 2282) to cause to bring or fetch;
    to bring back or take back, MBh.; R.;
    to pour in, mix in RV.; VS.; ŚBr. &c.;
    to bring any one to, reduce to any state, MBh.;
    to deduce, calculate;
    to use, employ, prove:
    Caus. P. -nāyayatināyayatiā-nāyayati, to cause to be brought or fetched or led near, MBh.; R.; Ragh.; Kathās. &c.:
    Desid. -ninīṣatininīṣatiā-ninīṣati, to intend or wish to bring near, BhP. x, 89, 42. [140,2]
