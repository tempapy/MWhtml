• kṛtvaskṛtvas a ind.
    • at the end of a numeral or numeral adjective, -fold, times (e.g. daśa-kṛtvasdaśakṛtvas, ten times; bahu-k°bahukṛtvas, many times; pañca-k°pañcakṛtvaspañcakṛtvas, fivefold, Pāṇ. ii, 3, 64). In the Veda kṛtvaskṛtvas is used as a separate word (e.g. bhūribhūribhūrī k°kṛtvas, many times, RV. iii, 18, 4; pañcapañca k°kṛtvas, TS. vi), but according to, Pāṇ. v, 4, 17 and 20 (Vop. vii, 70) it is only an affix, and it is so used in classical Sanskṛt [304,1]
    • it was originally an acc. pl. fr. kṛtu, formed by the affix tutu fr. √1. kṛkṛ (cf. also kṛtkṛt in sakṛtsakṛtsakṛd.) [304,1]
