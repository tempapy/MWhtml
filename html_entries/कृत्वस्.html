• kṛtvaskṛtvas
  • ind. at the end of a numeral or numeral adjective, -fold, times (e.g. daśa-kṛtvasdaśakṛtvasdaśakṛtvas, ten times; bahu-k°bahukṛtvasbahukṛtvas, many times; pañca-k°pañcakṛtvaspañcakṛtvas, fivefold, Pāṇ. ii, 3, 64). In the Vedaveda kṛtvaskṛtvas is used as a separate word (e.g. bhūribhūri k°kṛtvas, many times, RV. iii, 18, 4; pañcapañca k°kṛtvas, TS. vi), but according to, Pāṇ. v, 4, 17 and 20 (Vop. vii, 70) it is only an affix, and it is so used in classical Sanskṛt [304,1]
  • it was originally an acc. pl. fr. kṛtukṛtu, formed by the affix tutu fr. √1. kṛkṛ (cf. also kṛtkṛt in sakṛtsakṛt.) [304,1]
