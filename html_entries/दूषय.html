• dūṣayadūṣaya
    • Nom. P. °yatidūṣayat (ep. also °tedūṣayat) to corrupt, spoil, contaminate, vitiate (of moral corruption also doṣayati See 2. duṣduṣ), AV.; Mn.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to dishonour or violate (a woman), Mn. viii, 364; MBh. &c.
    • (astrol.) to cause evil or misfortune, Var.;
    • to adulterate, falsify, MBh. xiii, 1683;
    • to object, refute, disprove, blame, ib.; Kathās. &c.
    • to retract or break (vācamvācamvācavācvācaṃ, one's word), MBh. xii, 7256;
    • to find fault with, accuse, MBh.; R.;
    • (paras-paramparasparamparasparaparasparampara, each other), Pañc. 450/451;
    • to offend, hurt, injure (gen.), R. ii, 74, 3; MBh. iv, 2228 &c. [489,1]
