• kṣemȇndra m. N. of a celebrated poet of Kaśmīr (surnamed Vyāsa-dāsavyāsa-dāsavyāsadāsa and flourishing in the middle of the eleventh century, author of the Bṛhat-kathābṛhat-kathābṛhatkathā (-mañjarī), Bhārata-mañjarībhārata-mañjarībhāratamañjarī, Kalā-vilāsakalā-vilāsakalāvilāsa, Rāmāyaṇa-mañjarīrāmāyaṇa-mañjarīrāmāyaṇamañjarī or -kathā-sārakathā-sārakathāsāra, Daśâvatāra-caritadaśāvatāra-caritadaśāvatāracarita, Samaya-mātṛkāsamaya-mātṛkāsamayamātṛkā, Vyāsâṣṭaka, Suvṛtta-tilakasuvṛtta-tilakasuvṛttatilaka, Loka-prakāśaloka-prakāśalokaprakāśa, Nīti-kalpatarunīti-kalpatarunītikalpataru, Rājâvalī) [333,1]
  • N. of a Śaiva philosopher (who is probably identical with -rājakṣemendra-rājarāja; he is the author of the Spanda-nirṇayaspanda-nirṇayaspandanirṇaya and Spanda-saṃdohaspanda-saṃdohaspandasaṃdoha) [333,1]
  • N. of the author of the Aucityâlaṃkāra and of the Kavi-kaṇṭhâbharaṇa. [333,1]
