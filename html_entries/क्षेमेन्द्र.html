• kṣemȇndrakṣemendrakṣemakṣemāindra m.
    • N. of a celebrated poet of Kaśmīr (surnamed Vyāsa-dāsa and flourishing in the middle of the eleventh century, author of the Bṛhat-kathā (-mañjarī), Bhārata-mañjarī, Kalā-vilāsa, Rāmāyaṇa-mañjarī or -kathā-sāra, Daśâvatāra-carita, Samaya-mātṛkā, Vyāsâṣṭaka, Suvṛtta-tilaka, Loka-prakāśa, Nīti-kalpataru, Rājâvalī) [333,1]
    • N. of a Śaiva philosopher (who is probably identical with -rājakṣemendrarāja; he is the author of the Spanda-nirṇaya and Spanda-saṃdoha) [333,1]
    • N. of the author of the Aucityâlaṃkāra and of the Kavi-kaṇṭhâbharaṇa. [333,1]
