• yathāyathā
  • ind. (yathaitatyathaitat or yathaivaitatyathaivaitat, ‘as for that’ ; yathā-tathāyathātathāyathātathā or yathāyathā - tenatena satyenasatyena, ‘as surely as’ - ‘so truly’) [841,2]
  • as, for instance, namely (also tadtad yathāyathā, ‘as here follows’), Up.; GṛŚrS.; Nir. [841,3]
  • as it is or was (elliptically), BhP. [841,3]
  • that, so that, in order that (with Pot. or Subj., later also with fut. pres., impf. and aor.; in earlier language yathāyathā is often placed after the first word of a sentence; sometimes with ellipsis of syātsyāt and bhavetbhavet), RV. &c. &c. [841,3]
  • that (esp. after verbs of ‘knowing’, ‘believing’, ‘hearing’, ‘doubting’ &c.; either with or without itiiti at the end of the sentence), Up.; MBh.; Kāv. &c. [841,3]
  • as soon as, Megh. [841,3]
  • as, because, since (yathā-tathāyathātathāyathātathā, ‘as’ - ‘therefore’), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [841,3]
  • as if (with Pot.), Daś.; Śak.; how (= quam, expressing ‘admiration’), Pāṇ. viii, 1, 37 Sch. [841,3]
  • according to what is right, properly, correctly (= yathāvatyathāvat), BhP.
