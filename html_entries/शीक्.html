• śīkśīk
    • (also written sīksīk) cl. 1. Ā. (Dhātup. iv, 1) śīkate (pf. śiśīke aor. aśīkiṣṭa &c. Gr.), to rain in fine drops, drizzle, sprinkle, wet, moisten, Hcar.; Bhaṭṭ.;
    • to go, move, Dhātup. iv, 11 (v.l.) :
    • Caus. śīkayati, to besprinkle, Hcar.;
    • cl. 10. ‘to speak’ or ‘to shine’ (bhāṣā̑rthebhāṣabhāṣāarthearthaarthiarthārtha or bhāsā̑rthebhāsaarthearthaarthiarthārtha), Dhātup. xxxiii, 116 ;
    • āmarṣaṇeāmarṣaṇa or marṣaṇemarṣaṇa, xxxiv, 20. [cf. Gk. κηκίω.] [1077,1]
