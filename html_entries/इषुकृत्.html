  • iṣu-kṛtiṣukṛtiṣukṛt
  • mfn. (only RV. i, 184, 3) = iṣ-kṛtiṣkṛtiṣkṛt, preparing, arranging ([NBD.]) [169,3]
  • (fr. √kṛtkṛt, ‘to cut’), hurting like an arrow ([Gmn.]), Sāy. reads iṣu-kṛtaiṣukṛtaiṣukṛta, and explains it by ‘made quick as an arrow.’ [169,3]
