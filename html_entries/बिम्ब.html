• bimbabimba mn.
    • (also written vimbavimba, or vimva, of doubtful origin, but cf. Uṇ. iv, 95 Sch.; ifc. f(āāa). ) the disk of the sun or moon, Kāv.; Pur. &c. [731,3]
    • any disk, sphere, orb (often applied to the rounded parts of the body), Kālid.; Pañcat. [731,3]
    • a mirror, ŚvetUp.; Kum. [731,3]
    • an image, shadow, reflected or represented form, picture, type, R.; BhP.; Rājat. [731,3]
    • (in rhet.) the object compared (as opp. to prati-bimbapratibimbapratibimba, ‘the counterpart’ to which it is compared), Sāh.; Pratāp. [731,3]
  • bimbabimba m.
    • a lizard, chameleon, Gaut. [731,3]
    • N. of a man, Rājat. [731,3]
  • bimbabimba n.
    • the fruit of the Momordica Monadelpha (to which the lips of women are often compared), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [731,3]
