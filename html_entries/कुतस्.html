• ku/tas
  • ind. (fr. 1. ku), from whom? (for the abl. case of 2. ka/), RV. i, 164, 18; AV. viii, 9, 4 [290,2]
  • (kutaH kAlAt, since what time? VP.) [290,2]
  • from where? whence? RV. &c. [290,2]
  • whereto? in which direction? BhP. viii, 19, 34 [290,2]
  • (A kutas, up to where? Pat.) [290,2]
  • where? R. vii, 23, 3 [290,2]
  • wherefore? why? from what cause or motive? because, Lāṭy. &c. (often in dramas before verses giving the reason of what precedes) [290,2]
  • how? in what manner? ChUp. &c. [290,2]
  • how much less? much less, ChUp.; MuṇḍUp.; MBh. &c. In a-kutas, which occurs in comp., kutas has an indefinite sense (e.g. a-kuto-mftyu, not fearing death from any quarter, BhP. iii, 17, 19; cf. a-kutoBaya) [290,2]
  • kutas is also indefinite when connected with the particles api, cid, cana (e.g. kuto 'pi, from any quarter, from any cause, Pañcat.; kuto 'pi kAraRAt, from any cause, Prab.; kutaS-cid, from any one, from anywhere, RV.; KaṭhUp. &c. (cf. akutaScid-Baya); kutaS cana, (with a negation preceding) from no side, RV.; TUp. &c.; to no side, R. v, 74, 21; yataH kutaS-cid from any person soever Comm. on KātyŚr.) [290,2]
