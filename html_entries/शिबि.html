  • N. of a son of Indraindra, MBh. [1072,1]
  • of Indraindra in the fourth Manv-antaramanvantaramanvantara (v.l. śikhinśikhin), VP. [1072,1]
  • of a son of Manumanu Cākṣuṣacākṣuṣa, BhP. [1072,1]
  • of a Daityadaitya (son of Saṃhrādasaṃhrāda), MBh. [1072,1]
  • a king of the Śibiśibis, VarBṛS. [1072,1]
  • a beast of prey, L. [1072,1]
