• aṣṭakaaṣṭaka mf(āāa or ikā)n.
    • (Śulb.; cf. Pāṇ. vii, 3, 45 Comm.) consisting of eight parts, ŚBr.; RPrāt. &c. [117,1]
    • one who is acquainted with the eight books of Pāṇini's grammar, Pāṇ. iv, 2, 65 Sch. [117,1]
  • aṣṭakaaṣṭaka m.
    • N. of a son of Viśvāmitra (author of the hymn, RV. x, 104), AitBr.; ĀśvŚr.; MBh. &c. [117,1]
  • aṣṭakaaṣṭaka n.
    • a whole consisting of eight parts (as each of the eight Aṣṭakas of the RV., or as, TS. i, or as Pāṇini's grammar &c.) [117,1]
