• ut-kalautkalautkala m.
    • (perhaps fr. ud-√kaludkal?) N. of the country Orissa (See Oḍra; the word is interpreted to mean ‘the glorious country’ [Hunter]; or ‘lying beyond, the outlying strip of land’ [Beames]; according to others it merely means ‘the country of bird-catchers’) [176,1]
    • N. of a son of Dhruva, BhP. [176,1]
    • of Su-dyumna, Hariv.; VP. &c. [176,1]
    • a porter, one who carries a burden or load, L. [176,1]
    • a fowler, bird-catcher, L. [176,1]
    • the inhabitants of the above country. [176,1]
