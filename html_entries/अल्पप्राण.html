• alpa—prā̑ṇa m. (in Gr.) slight breathing or weak aspiration (the effort in uttering the vowels, the semivowels y, r, l, v, the consonants k, c, ṭ, t, p, g, j, ḍ, d, b, and the nasals, is said to be accompanied with slight aspiration, but practically alpaprā̑ṇa is here equivalent to unaspirated, as opposed to mahā-prā̑ṇamahā-prāṇamahāprāṇa q.v.), Pāṇ. i, 1, 9 Sch. [95,3]
  • alpa—prā̑ṇa mfn. having short breath, not persevering, soon tired, Suśr. pronounced with slight breathing, Kāvyâd. [95,3]
