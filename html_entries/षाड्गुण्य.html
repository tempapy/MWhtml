• ṣāḍguṇyaṣāḍguṇya n.
    • (fr. ṣaḍ-guṇaṣaḍguṇaṣaḍṣaṭguṇa q.v.) the aggregate of the six qualities, Kathās. [1110,3]
    • the six good qualities or excellencies, Car.; Śiś. [1110,3]
    • the six measures or acts of royal policy, Mn.; MBh. &c. [1110,3]
    • six articles of any kind, multiplication of anything by six, W. [1110,3]
