• 1. nininī ind.
    • down, back, in, into, within (except, AV. x, 8, 7 always prefixed either to verbs or to nouns; in the latter case it has also the meaning of negation or privation [cf. ‘down-hearted’ = heartless]; sometimes w.r. for nisnisni) [538,3]
    • it may also express kṣepakṣepa, dānadāna, upa-ramauparamaupaupārama, ā-śrayaāśrayaāśraya, mokṣamokṣa &c., L. [538,3]
  • nininī
    • [cf. Zd. ni; Gk. ἐ-νί; Slav. ni-zu;Germ., ni-dar, ni-der, nieder; Angl.Sax. ni-ther, Eng. ne-ther, be-neath.] [538,3]
  • 2. nininī
    • (for nīnīni) mfn. See ṛta-niṛtaniṛtaninī. [538,3]
  • 3. nininī
    • (in music) the 7th note (for niṣadhaniṣadha). [538,3]
