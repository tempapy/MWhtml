• nini
  • ind. down, back, in, into, within (except, AV. x, 8, 7 always prefixed either to verbs or to nouns; in the latter case it has also the meaning of negation or privation [cf. ‘down-hearted’ = heartless]; sometimes w.r. for nisnis) [538,3]
  • it may also express kṣepakṣepa, dānadāna, upa-ramauparamauparama, ā-śrayaāśrayaāśraya, mokṣamokṣa &c., L. [538,3]
