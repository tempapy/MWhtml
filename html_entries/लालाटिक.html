• lālāṭikalālāṭika mf(īīi)n.
    • = -lālāṭalālāṭikalālāṭa q.v., KātyŚr. Sch. [900,3]
    • relating to fate or destiny (supposed to be written on the forehead), W. [900,3]
    • useless, low, vile, ib. [900,3]
  • lālāṭikalālāṭika m.
    • an attentive servant (as ‘one who watches his master's face or countenance’), L. [900,3]
    • an idler (prob. as ‘one who is always gazing at the countenance of others’), L. [900,3]
    • a mode of embracing, L. [900,3]
