    to deviate or diverge from the right line, be crooked or curved, bend, go crookedly or wrongly or deviously, stumble, fall, down, VS. &c. (See above) :

    to cause to go crookedly, lead wrong or astray, RV.;
    (Ā.) to go wrong or astray, ib. :
