• madhumadhu—parkaparka
  • m. (n., L.) a mixture of honey, an offering of honey and milk, a respectful offering to a guest or to the bridegroom on his arrival at the door of the father of the bride (sometimes consisting of equal parts of curds, honey and clarified butter) [780,1]
  • the ceremony of receiving a guest with it, AV.; GṛŚrS. &c. [780,1]
  • N. of a son of Garuḍagaruḍa, MBh. [780,1]
  • N. of works. [780,1]
