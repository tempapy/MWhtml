• ud-√vṛtudvṛtudvṛt
  • P. (pf. -vavartavavartaudvavarta) to go asunder, burst open, ŚBr. iv, 4, 3, 4 :
    Ā. -vartatevartateudvartate, to go upwards, rise, ascend, swell;
    to bubble up, overflow, Hariv.; BhP.; Suśr.;
    to be puffed up with pride, become arrogant or extravagant;
    to proceed from, originate, ŚBr. xiv, 5, 1, 5;
    to fall down, BhP. :
    Caus. P. -vartayativartayatiudvartayati, to beat to pieces, split, burst, RV. viii, 14, 13; TBr.; MBh.; Hariv.;
    to swing or throw out, Kauś.;
    to cause to swell up. [192,2]
