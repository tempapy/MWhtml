  • P. -ghoṣatighoṣatiud-ghoṣati, to sound;
    to cry out, MBh.;
    to fill with cries;
    to proclaim aloud, Sarvad. :
    Caus. -ghoṣayatighoṣayatiud-ghoṣayati, to cause to sound aloud, Rājat.;
    to declare aloud, proclaim, noise abroad, Mṛcch.; Kathās.; Rājat. [187,3]
