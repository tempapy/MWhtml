• pra-√duṣpraduṣpraduṣ
  • P. -duṣyatiduṣyatipra-duṣyati, to become worse, deteriorate, Suśr.;
    to be defiled or polluted, fall (morally), Mn.; Yājñ.;
    to commit an offence against (acc.), MBh.;
    to become faithless, fall off, ib. :
    Caus. -dūṣayatidūṣayatipra-dūṣayati, to spoil, deprave, corrupt, pollute, defile, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to abuse, blame, censure, R.;
    (with cittamcittam) to be angry, Divyâv. [680,1]
