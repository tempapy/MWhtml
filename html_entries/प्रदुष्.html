• pra-√duṣpraduṣpraduṣ
    • P. -duṣyatipraduṣ, to become worse, deteriorate, Suśr.;
    • to be defiled or polluted, fall (morally), Mn.; Yājñ.;
    • to commit an offence against (acc.), MBh.;
    • to become faithless, fall off, ib. :
    • Caus. -dūṣayatipraduṣdūṣayat, to spoil, deprave, corrupt, pollute, defile, MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to abuse, blame, censure, R.;
    • (with cittamcitta) to be angry, Divyāv. [680,1]
