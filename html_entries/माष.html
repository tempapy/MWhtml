  • a partic. weight of gold (= 5 Kṛṣṇalas = 1/10 Suvarṇa; the weight in common use is said to be about 17 grains troy), Mn.; Yājñ. [814,2]
  • a cutaneous eruption resembling beans, L. [814,2]
  • a fool, blockhead, L. [814,2]
  • N. of a man g. bāhv-ādibāhv-ādibāhvādi [814,2]
  • pl. (with or scil. akṛṣṭāḥ) ‘wild beans’, N. of a Ṛṣi-gaṇaṛṣi-gaṇaṛṣigaṇa (the children of Su-rabhisu-rabhisurabhi, to whom, RV. ix, 86, 1-10 is ascribed), RAnukr.; R.; Hariv. [814,2]
