• ati-√kram
  • to step or go beyond or over or across, (Ved. Inf. ati-kra/me, to be walked on RV. i, 105, 16);
    to pass, cross;
    to pass time;
    to surpass, excel, overcome;
    to pass by, neglect;
    to overstep, transgress, violate;
    to pass on or away;
    to step out;
    to part from, lose:
    Caus. -krAmayati, or -kramayati, to allow to pass (as time);
    to leave unnoticed. [13,2]
