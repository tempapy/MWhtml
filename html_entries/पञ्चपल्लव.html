• pañcapañca—pallavapallava n.
    • the aggregate of 5 sprigs or shoots of the Āmra, Jambū, Kapittha, Bīja-pūraka, and Bilva (according to others, of the Āmra, Aśvattha, Vaṭa, Parkaṭī, and Yajñôdumbara; or of the Panasa, Āmra, Aśvattha, Vaṭa, and Bakula, L.; or of the spondias, rose-apple, Bel or marmelos, citron, and wood-apple, W.)
