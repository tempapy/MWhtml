  • P. -tyajatityajatiparityajati (°teparite, R.; MārkP.; ind.p. -tyajyatyajyaparityajya),
    to leave, quit, abandon, give up, reject, disregard, not heed, Mn.; MBh. &c.;
    (with dehamdeham) to forsake the body i.e. die, BhP.;
    (with prāṇānprāṇān, or jīvitamjīvitam) to resign the breath, give up the ghost, Mn.; MBh.; Daś.; Vet.;
    (with nāvamnāvam) to disembark, MW. :
    Pass. -tyajyatetyajyateparityajyate, to be deprived or bereft of (instr.), Mn.; Pañc.; Hit. &c.:
    Caus. -tyājayatityājayatiparityājayati, to deprive or rob a person of (2 acc.), R. [595,2]
