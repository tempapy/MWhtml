• alaṃalaṃalamala—kārakāra m.
    • the act of decorating, R. ii, 40, 13, (ifc. f., R. 18, 6) ornament, decoration, ŚBr.; TBr. &c. (in rhetoric) an ornament of the sense or the sound (cf. arthā̑l° and śabdā̑l°) [94,2]
    • N. of a minister of Jaya-siṃha of Kaśmir (1129-1150 A.D.; he is also called Laṅkaka) [1316,3]
