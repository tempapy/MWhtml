• ut-thāutthāutthauttha
    • (ud-√sthāudsthāstha Pāṇ. viii, 4, 61; cf. ut-tambhuttambhut, col.1) P. Ā. (but not Ā. in the sense of, ‘rising, standing up’ Pāṇ. i, 3, 24) -tiṣṭhati, -tetatā (pf. -tasthautasthu aor. -asthāt &c.) to stand up, spring up, rise, raise one's self, set out, RV.; AV.; ŚBr.; Ragh.; Śak.; Bhag. &c.
    • to rise (from the dead), BhP.;
    • to rise (from any occupation), leave off;
    • to finish, AitBr.; ŚBr.; TāṇḍyaBr. &c.
    • to come forth, arise, appear, become visible, result;
    • to spring, originate from, RV.; AV.; ŚBr.; TS.; MBh.; Kathās. &c.
    • to come in (as revenues), Śak.;
    • to rise (for the performance of any action);
    • to be active or brave;
    • to make efforts, take pains with, strive for;
    • to excel, MBh.; R. &c.:
    • Caus. -thāpayati (aor. 1. sg. ud-atiṣṭhipamudut, AV. vii, 95, 2) to cause to stand up, raise, rouse, start, AV.; AitBr.; TBr.; ŚBr.; MBh.; Daś.; BhP. &c.
    • to set up, lift up, erect, Gobh.; MBh.; R.; Hit. &c.
    • to get out, Hit.;
    • to drive out, send out, push out, AitBr. : Kathās.; BhP.;
    • to excite;
    • to produce, Ragh.; Sāh.;
    • to arouse, awaken, raise to life, make alive, animate;
    • to stir up, agitate, ŚBr.; KauṣUp.; Hariv.; R.; Kathās. &c.:
    • Desid. -tiṣṭhāsati, to wish or intend to stand up, ŚBr. xi, 1, 6, 5;
    • to intend to leave off (a sacrifice), Nyāyam. [179,3]
