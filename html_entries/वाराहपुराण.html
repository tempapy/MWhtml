• vārāhavārāha—purāṇapurāṇa
  • n. N. of one of the 18 Purāṇapurāṇas (said to have been revealed to the Earth by Viṣṇuviṣṇu in his form of varāhavarāha q.v.; it contains an account of the creation, the various forms or incarnations of Viṣṇuviṣṇu, and a number of legends and directions relating to the Vaiṣṇavavaiṣṇava sect), IW. 514 &c. [944,2]
