• gaṇgaṇ
    • cl. 10. P. gaṇayati (ep. also Ā. °tetatā: aor. ajīgaṇat [Kathās. lxxviii] or ajag°, Pāṇ. vii, 4, 97 ; ind.p. gaṇayya, BhP. [with a-a neg., iv, 7, 15]),
    • to count, number, enumerate, sum up, add up, reckon, take into account, MBh.; R. &c.
    • to think worth, value (with instr. e.g. nana gaṇayāmi taṃtamta tṛṇenatṛṇa, ‘I do not value him at a straw’ MBh. ii, 1552);
    • to consider, regard as (with double acc.), Ragh. viii; xi; Daś.; Pañcat.; Gīt.; Kathās.;
    • to enumerate among (loc.), MBh. i, 2603; Daś.;
    • to ascribe, attribute to (loc.), Bhartṛ. ii, 44;
    • to attend to, take notice of (acc.; often with nana, not to care about, leave unnoticed), MBh. &c.
    • to imagine, excogitate, Megh. 107;
    • to count one's number (said of a flock or troop), Pāṇ. i, 3, 67; Kāś. [342,3]
