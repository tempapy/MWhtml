• vārṣikavārṣika mf(īīi)n.
    • belonging to the rainy-season, rainy, AV. &c. &c. (with āpasāpasāpa f. pl. rain-water, with dhanusdhanusdhanu n. a rainbow) [945,3]
    • growing in the rainy season or fit for or suited to it, W. [945,3]
    • others ‘a river, the water of which lasts the whole year,i.e. does not dry up in the hot season’ [945,3]
    • having water only during the rains (as a river), MBh. (cf. vārṣikȏdakavārṣikodakavārṣikaudakaūdaka) [945,3]
    • versed in calculating the rainy season g. vasantā̑divasantavasantāadādiādin [945,3]
    • sufficient or lasting for a year, Yājñ.; MBh. &c. [945,3]
    • yearly, annual, Gaut.; Pur. [945,3]
    • (ifc. after a numeral) lasting a certain number of years, being so many years old (cf. tri-trivārṣikatrivārṣika, pañca-v°pañca &c.) [946,1]
  • vārṣikavārṣika mn.
