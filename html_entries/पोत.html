• potapota m.

    • a fetus which has no enveloping membrane, L. [650,1]
    • cloth, a garment, L. [650,1]
    • the foundation of a house, L. (cf. poṭapoṭa) [650,1]
  • potapota mn.
    • a vessel, ship, boat, MBh.; Hariv.; Var.; Kāv. [650,1]
