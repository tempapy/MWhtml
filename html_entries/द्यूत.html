• dyūtadyūta n.
    • (m. only, MBh. ii, 2119; cf. Pāṇ. ii, 4, 31) play, gaming, gambling (esp. with dice, but also with any inanimate object), AV.; ŚrS.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [500,2]
    • (fig.) battle or fight, contest for (comp.), MBh. iii, 3037 &c. [500,2]
    • the prize or booty won in battle, ib. vii, 3966; ix, 760. [500,2]
