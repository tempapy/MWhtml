• 1. samsamsasaṃ

  • 2. samsamsasaṃ ind.
    • (connected with 7. sasa and 2. samasama, and opp. to 3. vivivī q.v.) with, together with, along with, together, altogether (used as a preposition or prefix to verbs and verbal derivatives, like Gk. σύν, Lat. con, and expressing ‘conjunction’, ‘union’, ‘thoroughness’, ‘intensity’, ‘completeness’ e.g. saṃ√yujsaṃyuj, ‘to join together’; saṃ-√dhāsaṃdhāsaṃdhasaṃdhādha, ‘to place together’; saṃ-dhisaṃdhisaṃdhinsaṃsamdhidhī, ‘placing together’; saṃ-√tapsaṃtapsaṃtap, ‘to consume utterly by burning’; sam-ucchedasamucchedasamuccheda, ‘destroying altogether, complete destruction’; in Ved. the verb connected with it has sometimes to be supplied, e.g. āpoāpo agnimagni yaśasaḥ saṃsaṃsamsa hihihī pūrvīḥ, ‘for many glorious waters surrounded Agni’; it is sometimes prefixed to nouns in the sense of 2. samasama, ‘same’; cf. samarthasamartha), RV. &c.
