sahasaha—rakṣarakṣa and °kṣassaharakṣas, see under 2. sahasaha, p. 1194, col. 2. [1193,1]sahasaha—rakṣarakṣa (VP.) or sahasaha—rakṣasrakṣas (saha-sahasaha-saharakṣa, TS.), m. one of the three kinds of sacrificial fire (that which receives the offering to the Rākṣasarākṣasas; the latter also, ‘a forest fire’, L.).
