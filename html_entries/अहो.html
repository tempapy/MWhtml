  • 2. ahoahoahu ind.
    • a particle (implying joyful or painful surprise) Ah! (of enjoyment or satisfaction) Oh! (of fatigue, discontent, compassion, sorrow, regret) Alas! Ah! (of praise cf. Pāṇ. viii, 1, 40 seq.) Bravo! (of reproach) Fie! (of calling, Kum. iii, 20) Ho! Halo! (of contempt) Pshaw! Often combined with other particles of similar signification, as ahoahoahuahasahaahar dhikdhikdhig or dhigdhigdhik ahoahoahu, ahoahoahuahasahaahar batabata, &c. [126,1]
