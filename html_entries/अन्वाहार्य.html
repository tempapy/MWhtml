• anv-āhāryaanvāhāryaanvāhārya
  • m. a gift, consisting of food prepared with rice, presented to the Ṛtvijṛtvij priest at the Darśapūrṇamāsadarśapūrṇamāsa ceremonies, TS. &c. [47,2]
  • anv-āhāryaanvāhāryaanvāhārya
  • (amam or akamakam), n. the monthly Śrāddhaśrāddha (q.v.) held on the day of new moon (according to, Mn. iii, 123 it should be of meat eaten after the presentation of a Piṇḍapiṇḍa or ball of rice). [47,2]
