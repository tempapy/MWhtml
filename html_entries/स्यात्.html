• syātsyāt
  • ind. (3. sg. Pot. of √1. asas) it may be, perhaps, perchance (esp. used in Jainajaina works and occurring in 7 formulas, viz. 1. syādsyād astiasti, ‘perhaps it is [under certain circumstances]’; 2. syānsyān nā̑stināsti, ‘perhaps it is not, &c.’; 3. syādsyād astiasti caca nā̑stināsti caca, ‘perhaps it is and is not [under certain circumstances]’; 4. syādsyād avaktavyaḥavaktavyaḥ, ‘perhaps it is not expressible in words’; 5. syādsyād astiasti cā̑vaktavyaḥcāvaktavyaḥ, ‘perhaps it is and is not expressible in words’; 6. syānsyān nā̑stināsti cā̑vaktavyaḥcāvaktavyaḥ, ‘perhaps it is not and is not expressible in words’; 7. syādsyād astiasti caca nā̑stināsti cā̑vaktavyaḥcāvaktavyaḥ ‘perhaps it is and is not and is not expressible in words'), Sarvad. [1273,2]
