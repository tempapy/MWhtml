• syātsyātsyasyād ind.
    • (3. sg. Pot. of √1. asasa) it may be, perhaps, perchance (esp. used in Jaina works and occurring in 7 formulas, viz. 1. syādsyādsyātsya astiasti, ‘perhaps it is [under certain circumstances]’; 2. syānsyasyāt nā̑stināstinanāasti, ‘perhaps it is not, &c.’; 3. syādsyādsyātsya astiasti caca nā̑stināstinanāasti caca, ‘perhaps it is and is not [under certain circumstances]’; 4. syādsyādsyātsya avaktavyaḥavaktavya, ‘perhaps it is not expressible in words’; 5. syādsyādsyātsya astiasti cā̑vaktavyaḥcaavaktavya, ‘perhaps it is and is not expressible in words’; 6. syānsyasyāt nā̑stināstinanāasti cā̑vaktavyaḥcaavaktavya, ‘perhaps it is not and is and is not expressible in words’; 7. syādsyādsyātsya astiasti caca nā̑stināstinanāasti cā̑vaktavyaḥcaavaktavya, Sarvad. [1273,2]
