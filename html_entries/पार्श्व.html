• pārśvapārśva a n.
    • (rarely m. g. ardharcā̑diardharcādiardharcaadādiādin; ifc. f(āāa). fr. 1. parśuparśu) the region of the ribs (pl. the ribs), side, flank (either of animate or inanimate objects), RV. &c. &c. [622,1]
    • the side = nearness, proximity (with gen. or ifc.; ayaḥayaḥayasayai, on both sides; amama, aside, towards; eeaāi, at the side, near [opp. to dura-tasduratasta]; ātāta, away, from; by means of, through), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [622,1]
    • a curved knife, ŚBr. [622,1]
    • a side of any square figure, W. [622,1]
    • the curve or circumference of a wheel, ib. [622,1]
    • (only) a multitude of ribs, the thorax, W. [622,2]
    • the extremity of the fore-axle nearest the wheel to which the outside horses of a four-horse chariot are attached, L. [622,2]
    • a fraudulent or crooked expedient, L. [622,2]

    • (du.) heaven and earth, L. [622,2]
