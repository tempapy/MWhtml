• 1. luklukluc
    • (only lukiṣyasi and lukita), prob. invented to explain the following word. [903,3]
  • 2. luklukluc
    • (prob. fr. √luñcluñc), and gram. term to express ‘the dropping out’ or, ‘disappearance’ of Pratyayas or affixes (the symbols luklukluc, lupluplubh, and śluślu are distinguished from lopalopa, q.v., and are called lumatlumat, as containing the syllable lulū). [904,1]
