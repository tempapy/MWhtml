• lukluk
  • (only lukiṣyasilukiṣyasi and lukitalukita), prob. invented to explain the following word. [903,3]
  • lukluk
  • (prob. fr. √luñcluñc), and gram. term to express ‘the dropping out’ or, ‘disappearance’ of Pratyayapratyayas or affixes (the symbols lukluk, luplup, and śluślu are distinguished from lopalopa, q.v., and are called lumatlumat, as containing the syllable lulu). [904,1]
