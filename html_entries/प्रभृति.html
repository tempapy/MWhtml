• pra-°bhṛtipraprabhṛti f.
    • (pra-praprabhṛti) bringing forward, offering (of sacrifice or praise), RV.; AV. [685,1]
    • a throw or stroke, RV. [685,1]
    • beginning, commencement, ŚBr. &c. &c. (ifc. = ‘commencing with’ or ‘et caetera’ e.g. munayaḥmuni somaśravaḥ-prabhṛtayaḥsomaśravasprabhṛti, ‘the Munis beginning with Somaśravaḥ’ i.e. ‘the Munis, Somaśravaḥ &c.’; in this sense also °tika) [685,1]
  • pra-°bhṛtipraprabhṛti ind.
    • (after an abl. adv. or ifc.) beginning with, from—forward or upward, since, GṛŚrS.; Mn.; MBh. &c. (e.g. bālyātbālya prabhṛtiprabhṛti, ‘from boyhood upwards’; janma-pr°janmaprabhṛtijanmaprabhṛti, ‘from birth’; adyaadya pr°prabhṛti, ‘beginning from to-day, henceforth’; tataḥtatastatatat or tadātadātad pr°prabhṛti, ‘thenceforth’ &c.) [685,1]
