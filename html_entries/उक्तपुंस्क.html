• ukta/—puMska
  • a (feminine or neuter) word of which also a masculine is mentioned or exists (and whose meaning only differs from that of the masculine by the notion of gender; e.g. the word gaNgA is not ukta-puMska, whereas such words as SuBra and grAma-RI are so; cf. BAzita-puMska), Vop. iv, 8. [171,3]
