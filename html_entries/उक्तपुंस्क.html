• uktaukta—puṃskapuṃska
  • a (feminine or neuter) word of which also a masculine is mentioned or exists (and whose meaning only differs from that of the masculine by the notion of gender; e.g. the word gaṅgāgaṅgā is not ukta-puṃskauktapuṃskauktapuṃska, whereas such words as śubhraśubhra and grāma-ṇīgrāmaṇīgrāmaṇī are so; cf. bhāṣita-puṃskabhāṣitapuṃskabhāṣitapuṃska), Vop. iv, 8.
