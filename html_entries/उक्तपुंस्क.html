• ukta—puṃska a (feminine or neuter) word of which also a masculine is mentioned or exists (and whose meaning only differs from that of the masculine by the notion of gender; e.g. the word gaṅgā is not ukta-puṃskaukta-puṃskauktapuṃska, whereas such words as śubhra and grāma-ṇīgrāma-ṇīgrāmaṇī are so; cf. bhāṣita-puṃskabhāṣita-puṃskabhāṣitapuṃska), Vop. iv, 8.
