• pavitrāropaṇapavitrāropaṇa
  • n. ‘putting on the Pavitrapavitra’, investiture with the Brāhmanical cord, (esp.) investing the image of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa or another deity with the sacred thread, N. of a festival on the 12th day of the light half of Śrāvaṇaśrāvaṇa or Aṣāḍhaaṣāḍha, Pañc. [611,1]
