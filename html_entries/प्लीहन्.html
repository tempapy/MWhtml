• plīhanplīhan
  • m. the spleen (from which and from the liver the Hindūs suppose the blood to flow), AV.; VS.; ŚBr.; Suśr. [715,1]
  • disease of the spleen (said to be equally applied to enlargement of the mesenteric glands &c.), Suśr. [715,1]
