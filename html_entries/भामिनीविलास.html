• bhāminī-vilāsabhāminī-vilāsabhāminīvilāsa m. N. of a poem by Jagannātha Paṇḍita-rājapaṇḍita-rājapaṇḍitarāja (the 1st ch. contains allegorical precepts, the 2nd amatory subjects, the 3rd an elegy on a wife's death, the 4th teaches that consolation is only attainable through worshipping Kṛṣṇa). [751,1]
