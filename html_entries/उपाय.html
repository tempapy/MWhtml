• upā̑yaupāya
  • &c. See p. 215, col. 2. [214,1]
  • upā̑yaupāya
  • m. coming near, approach, arrival, Bhartṛ. [215,2]
  • that by which one reaches one's aim, a means or expedient (of any kind), way, stratagem, craft, artifice, MBh.; Mn.; Yājñ.; Pañcat. &c. [215,2]
  • (esp.) a means of success against an enemy (four are usually enumerated, sowing dissension, negotiation, bribery, and open assault) [215,2]
  • joining in or accompanying (in singing), ŚāṅkhŚr. [215,2]
