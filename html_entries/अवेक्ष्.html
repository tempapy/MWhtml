• avȇkṣavekṣavaīkṣ
    • (√īkṣīkṣ), avȇkṣateavekṣatāava to look towards, look at, behold, TS.; ŚBr. &c.
    • (1. sg. īkṣeīkṣaīkṣā) to perceive, observe, experience, RV. viii, 79, 9; R.; Bhag. &c.:
    • Ā. (rarely P. e.g. 2. sg. avekṣasi, MBh. ii, 2158) to have in view, have regard to, take into consideration, Mn.; MBh. &c.:
    • Caus. avȇkṣayatiava, to cause to look at, KātyŚr. [111,1]
