• avȇkṣavekṣ
  • (√īkṣīkṣ), avȇkṣateavekṣate to look towards, look at, behold, TS.; ŚBr. &c.;
    (1. sg. īkṣeīkṣe) to perceive, observe, experience, RV. viii, 79, 9; R.; Bhag. &c.:
    Ā. (rarely P. e.g. 2. sg. avekṣasiavekṣasi, MBh. ii, 2158) to have in view, have regard to, take into consideration, Mn.; MBh. &c.:
    Caus. avȇkṣayatiavekṣayati, to cause to look at, KātyŚr. [111,1]
