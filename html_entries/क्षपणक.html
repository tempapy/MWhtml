• kṣapaṇakakṣapaṇaka
  • m. a religious mendicant, (especially a) Jainajaina mendicant who wears no garments, MBh. i, 789; Cāṇ.; Pañcat.; Kād. &c. [326,1]
  • N. of an author supposed to have lived at the court of king Vikramâdityavikramāditya (perhaps the Jainajaina astronomer Siddha-senasiddhasenasiddhasena). [326,1]
