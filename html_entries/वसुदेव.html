    • N. of the father of Kṛṣṇa (he was the son of Śūra, a descendant of Yadu of the lunar line, and was also called Ānaka-dundubhi q.v., because at his birth the gods, foreseeing that Viṣṇu would take a human form in his family, sounded the drums of heaven for joy; he was a brother of Kuntī or Pṛthā, the mother of the Paṇḍu princes, who were thus cousins of Kṛṣṇa; See 1. kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa), MBh.; Hariv.; BhP. &c.     • of a king of the Kaṇva dynasty, Pur.; Vās., Introd. [931,1]
    • of Kṛṣṇa, Pañcar. [931,1]
    • of the grandfather of the poet Māgha, Cat. [931,1]
    • (also with brahma-prasādabrahmaprasāda) of two authors, Cat. [931,1]
