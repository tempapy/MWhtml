• apa ind. (as a prefix to nouns and verbs, expresses) away, off, back (opposed to upa, anu, sam, pra) [47,3]
  • down (opposed to ud). [47,3]
  • When prefixed to nouns, it may sometimes = the neg. particle a e.g. apa-bhīapa-bhīapabhī, fearless; or may express deterioration, inferiority, &c. (cf. apa-pāṭhaapa-pāṭhaapapāṭha). [47,3]
  • (As a separable particle or adverb in Ved., with abl.) away from, on the outside of, without, with the exception of [47,3]
  • apa [cf. Gk. ἀπό; Lat. ab; Goth. af; Eng. of]. [47,3]
