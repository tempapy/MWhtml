• apaapa ind.
    • (as a prefix to nouns and verbs, expresses) away, off, back (opposed to upaupa, anuanu, samsamsa, prapra) [47,3]
    • down (opposed to udud). [47,3]
    • When prefixed to nouns, it may sometimes = the neg. particle aa e.g. apa-bhīapabhīapabhī, fearless; or may express deterioration, inferiority, &c. (cf. apa-pāṭhaapapāṭhaapapāṭha). [47,3]
    • (As a separable particle or adverb in Ved., with abl.) away from, on the outside of, without, with the exception of [47,3]
