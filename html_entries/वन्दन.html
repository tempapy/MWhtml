• vandanavandana m.
    • N. of a Ṛṣi (who was cast into a well, along with Rebha, by the Asuras, and rescued by the Aśvins), RV. [919,1]
  • vandanavandana n.
    • the act of praising, praise, RV. [919,2]
    • reverence (esp. obeisance to a Brahman or superior by touching the feet &c.), worship, adoration, Mn.; MBh. &c. [919,2]
    • a parasitical plant, AV.; Bhpr. [919,2]
    • a disease attacking the limbs or joints, cutaneous eruption, scrofula (also personified as a demon), RV. [919,2]
    • = vadanavadana, L. [919,2]
