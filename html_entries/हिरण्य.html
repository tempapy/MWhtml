• hiraṇyahiraṇya n.
    • (ifc. f(āāa). ; prob. connected with harihariharīharin, haritharitharid, hirihiri) gold (orig. ‘uncoined gold or other precious metal’; in later language ‘coined gold’ -or ‘money’), RV. &c. &c. [1299,3]
    • any vessel or ornament made of gold (as ‘a golden spoon’ Mn. ii, 29), RV.; AV.; VS.; Kauś. [1299,3]
    • a gold piece or coin (generally with suvarṇasuvarṇa as opp. to base metal), Br. [1299,3]
    • a cowry, L. [1299,3]
    • semen virile, L. [1299,3]
    • substance, imperishable matter, L. [1299,3]
    • a partic. measure, W. [1299,3]
    • the Datura or thorn apple, MW. [1299,3]
    • N. of a Varṣa (= hiraṇ-mayahiraṇmayamaya), MārkP. [1299,3]
  • hiraṇyahiraṇya m.
    • a kind of bdellium, L. [1299,3]
    • N. of a, Daitya, MBh.; Pañcar. [1299,3]
    • of a son of Agnîdhra (= hiraṇ-mayahiraṇmayamaya q.v.), MārkP. [1299,3]
    • of a king of Kaśmīra, Rājat. [1299,3]
  • hiraṇyahiraṇya mfn.
    • golden, made of gold, Mn.; MBh. [1299,3]
