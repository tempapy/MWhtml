• ā-kṛtiākṛtiākṛti—gaṇagaṇa
  • m. a list of specimens, collection of words belonging to a particular grammatical rule (not exhibiting every word belonging to that rule but only specimens, whereas a simple Gaṇagaṇa exhibits every word), Pāṇ.; Kāś. ([Examples of Ākṛtiākṛti-gaṇagaṇas are arśa-ādiarśaādiarśaādi, ādy-ādiādyādiādyādi, kaṇḍv-ādikaṇḍvādikaṇḍvādi, &c.]) [127,3]
