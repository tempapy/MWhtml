• ā-kṛtiākṛtiākṛtīākṛtikṛtīkṛtinkṛt—gaṇagaṇa m.
    • a list of specimens, collection of words belonging to a particular grammatical rule (not exhibiting every word belonging to that rule but only specimens, whereas a simple Gaṇa exhibits every word), Pāṇ., Kāś. [Examples of Ākṛti-gaṇas are arśa-ādiarśaādiarśaarśoarśasādiādīādin, ādy-ādiādyādiādiādīādiādīādin, kaṇḍv-ādikaṇḍvādikaṇḍukaṇḍūādiādīādin, &c.] [127,3]
