• ā-kṛtiā-kṛtiākṛti—gaṇa m. a list of specimens, collection of words belonging to a particular grammatical rule (not exhibiting every word belonging to that rule but only specimens, whereas a simple Gaṇa exhibits every word), Pāṇ.; Kāś. ([Examples of Ākṛti-gaṇas are arśa-ādiarśa-ādiarśaādi, ādy-ādiādy-ādiādyādi, kaṇḍv-ādikaṇḍv-ādikaṇḍvādi, &c.]) [127,3]
