• surasura
  • m. (prob. fr. asuraasura as if fr. a-suraasuraasura and as sitasita fr. a-sitaasitaasita; thought by some to be connected with 2. svarsvar) a god, divinity, deity (surāṇāṃsurāṇāṃ hantṛhantṛ m. ‘slayer of the gods’, N. of a partic. form of fire, son of Tapastapas), MaitrUp.; MBh. &c. [1234,2]
  • the image of a god, an idol, Viṣṇ. [1234,2]
  • a symbolical N. for the number ‘thirty-three’ (from the 33 gods; See devadeva), Gaṇit. [1234,2]
  • a sage, learned man, L. [1234,2]
  • the sun, L. [1234,2]
  • (said to be) = kṣurakṣura, MBh. [1234,2]
  • w.r. for svarasvara, ib. [1234,2]
