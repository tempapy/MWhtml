• 1. śīlśīl
    • (rather Nom. fr. śīlaśīla below) cl. 1. P. śīlati (pf. śiśīla &c.), to meditate, contemplate, Dhātup. xv, 16;
    • to serve, worship, ib.;
    • to act, do, practise, make, ib. :
    • Caus. (or cl. 10. P. Dhātup. xxxv, 26) śīlayati (aor. aśiśīlat), to do, make, practise repeatedly or exceedingly, be intent upon or engaged in (acc.), exercise, cultivate, Āpast.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to wear, put on Gīt.;
    • to visit, frequent, Bhām. (cf. śīlitaśīlita);
    • to exceed, excel, Vop. [1079,1]
  • 2. śīlśīl m.
    • N. of a man, Inscr. [1079,2]
