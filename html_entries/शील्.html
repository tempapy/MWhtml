• śīlśīl
  • (rather Nom. fr. śīlaśīla below) cl. 1. P. śīlatiśīlati (pf. śiśīlaśiśīla &c.), to meditate, contemplate, Dhātup. xv, 16;
    to serve, worship, ib.;
    to act, do, practise, make, ib. :
    Caus. (or cl. 10. P. Dhātup. xxxv, 26) śīlayatiśīlayati (aor. aśiśīlataśiśīlat), to do, make, practise repeatedly or exceedingly, be intent upon or engaged in (acc.), exercise, cultivate, Āpast.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to wear, put on Gīt.;
    to visit, frequent, Bhām. (cf. śīlitaśīlita);
    to exceed, excel, Vop. [1079,1]
