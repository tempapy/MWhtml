• sabhā—parvan n. N. of the second book of the Mahā-bhāratamahā-bhāratamahābhārata (describing the great assembly at Hastināpura and the gambling between Yudhi-ṣṭhirayudhi-ṣṭhirayudhiṣṭhira and Śakuni, in which the former staked all his possessions, including his wife Draupadī). [1151,3]
