• uttarauttara 1a mfn.
    • (compar. fr. 1. udud; opposed to adharaadhara; declined, Gram. 238 aa), upper, higher, superior (e.g. uttareuttarauttarā dantāsdanta, the upper teeth), RV.; AV.; TS.; ChUp.; Ragh. &c. [178,1]
    • northern (because the northern part of India is high), AV.; Mn.; Suśr.; Pañcat. &c. [178,1]
    • left (opposed to dakṣiṇadakṣiṇa or right, because in praying the face being turned to the east the north would be on the left hand), AV.; KātyŚr.; MBh. &c. [178,1]
    • later, following, subsequent, latter, concluding, posterior, future, RV.; AV.; KātyŚr.; MBh.; Ragh.; Hit. &c. (opposed to pūrvapūrva, &c. e.g. uttaraḥuttara kālaḥkāla, future time; uttaraṃuttaramuttara vākyamvākya, a following speech, answer, reply; phalamphala uttaramuttaramuttara, subsequent result, future consequence; varṣȏttareṣuvarṣavarṣāuttara, in future years) [178,1]
