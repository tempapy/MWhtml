• bhosbhosbhu
    • (fr. bhavasbhavabhu voc. of bhavatbhavatbhavad q.v.; before vowels and soft consonants bhobhobhu; before hard consonants bhosbhosbhu and bhoḥbhoḥbhosbhu; the latter form also in pause cf. Pāṇ. viii, 3, 17 &c. but there is occasional confusion of these forms, esp. in later literature; often also bhobhoḥ.), an interjection or voc. particle commonly used in addressing another person or several persons = O! Ho! Hallo!, in soliloquies = alas! ŚBr. &c. &c. (according to, L. a particle of sorrow and of interrogation). [768,2]
