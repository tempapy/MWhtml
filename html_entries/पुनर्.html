• punarpunar ind.
    • back, home, in an opposite direction, RV. &c. &c. (with √1. gamgamga, yāyāyaiī, to go back or away; with √dādāda, to give back, restore; with √bhūbhūbhu, to turn round; with √asasa and dat., to fall back upon) [633,2]
    • again, once more (also with bhūyasbhūyasbhūya), ib. (with √bhūbhūbhu, to exist again, be renewed, become a wife again, re-marry) [633,2]
    • again and again, repeatedly, ib. (mostly punaḥpunaḥpunaspunapupunar p°punar which with nana = nevermore) [633,2]
    • further, moreover, besides, ib. (also punarpunar aparamaparamapara; ādau-punar-paścātādaupunarpaścātpaścapaścād, at first-then-later) [633,2]
    • however, still, nevertheless, MBh.; Kāv. &c. (at the end of a verse it lays stress on a preceding athaatha vāvāvauū, apiapiap vāvāvauū, or vāvāvauū alone; punarpunar apiapiap, even again, on the other hand, also; kadākadākadakad p°punar, at any time, ever; kimkim p°punar, how much more or less? however; punar-punarpunarpunar, now-now; at one time - at another time).
