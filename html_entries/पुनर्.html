• punarpunar
  • ind. back, home, in an opposite direction, RV. &c. &c. (with √1. gamgam, yāyā, to go back or away; with √dādā, to give back, restore; with √bhūbhū, to turn round; with √asas and dat., to fall back upon) [633,2]
  • again, once more (also with bhūyasbhūyas), ib. (with √bhūbhū, to exist again, be renewed, become a wife again, re-marry) [633,2]
  • again and again, repeatedly, ib. (mostly punaḥpunaḥ p°punar which with nana = nevermore) [633,2]
  • further, moreover, besides, ib. (also punarpunar aparamaparam; ādau-punar-paścātādaupunarpaścātādaupunarpaścāt, at first-then-later) [633,2]
  • however, still, nevertheless, MBh.; Kāv. &c. (at the end of a verse it lays stress on a preceding athaatha vāvā, apiapi vāvā, or vāvā alone; punarpunar apiapi, even again, on the other hand, also; kadākadā p°punar, at any time, ever; kimkim p°punar, how much more or less? however; punar-punarpunarpunarpunarpunar, now-now; at one time - at another time).
