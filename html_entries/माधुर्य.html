• mādhuryamādhurya n.
    • sweetness, Kāv.; Suśr. [809,1]
    • loveliness, exquisite beauty, charm, MBh.; Kāv. &c. [809,1]
    • (with Vaiṣṇavas) a feeling of tender affection (for Kṛṣṇa like that of a girl for her lover), RTL. 141 [809,1]
    • (in rhet.) grace of style (esp. consisting in the employment of separated words in a sentence, as opp. to śleṣaśleṣa q.v.), Vām.; Kpr. &c. [809,1]
