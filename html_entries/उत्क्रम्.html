  • (ud-√kram) P. (and rarely Ā.) -krAmati, -kramati (Ved. impf. 3. pl. -akraman, AV. iv, 3, 1), -te (pf. 3. pl. -cakramus, ŚBr.) to step up, go up, ascend, AV.; VS.; TS.; ŚBr.; KātyŚr.; R.; Kathās. &c.;
    to step out, go out or away;

    to go over, pass over, omit;
    not to notice;
    to neglect, transgress, MBh.; R. &c.:
    Caus. P. -kramayati and -krAmayati, to cause to go up or ascend, TS.; ŚBr.; Kauś. &c.:
    Desid. -cikramizati, or -cikramizyati, to wish to go up or out, ŚBr.; ChUp. [176,3]
