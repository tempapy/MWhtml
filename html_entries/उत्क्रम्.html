• ut-√kramutkramutkram
    • (ud-√kramudkram) P. (and rarely Ā.) -krāmatiutkramkrāmat, -kramatiutkram (Ved. impf. 3. pl. -akramanutkram, AV. iv, 3, 1), -teutkramtatāti (pf. 3. pl. -cakramusutkram, ŚBr.) to step up, go up, ascend, AV.; VS.; TS.; ŚBr.; KātyŚr.; R.; Kathās. &c.
    • to step out, go out or away;
    • to pass away, die, ŚBr.; Mn.; Nir.; Kād. &c.
    • to go over, pass over, omit;
    • not to notice;
    • to neglect, transgress, MBh.; R. &c.:
    • Caus. P. -kramayatiutkram and -krāmayatiutkram, to cause to go up or ascend, TS.; ŚBr.; Kauś. &c.:
    • Desid. -cikramiṣatiutkram, or -cikramiṣyatiutkram, to wish to go up or out, ŚBr.; ChUp. [176,3]
