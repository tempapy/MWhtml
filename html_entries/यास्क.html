• yāskayāska
  • m. (fr. yaskuyasku) patr. of the author of the Niruktanirukta (or commentary on the difficult Vedic words contained in the lists called Nighaṇṭunighaṇṭus; he is supposed to have lived before Pāṇinipāṇini; cf. IW. 156 &c.), ŚBr.; RPrāt.; MBh. [852,3]
  • pl. the pupils of Yāskayāska, Pāṇ. ii, 4, 63 Sch. [852,3]
