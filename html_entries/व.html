    • the 3rd semivowel (corresponding to the vowels uuū and ūūu, and having the sound of the English v, except when forming the last member of a conjunct consonant, in which case it is pronounced like w; it is often confounded and interchanged with the labial consonant b). [910,1]

    • auspiciousness [910,1]
    • a dwelling [910,1]

    • the esculent root of the water-lily [910,1]
