• saraṇyū f. N. of a daughter of Tvaṣṭṛ (in, RV. represented as the wife of Vivasvat and mother of the two Aśvins, and accord. to some, of the twins Yama and Yamī, whom she brought forth in the form of a mare; accord. to some also N. of the Dawn), RV. x, 17, 2; cf. Naigh. v, 6; Nir. xii, 9. [1182,1]
