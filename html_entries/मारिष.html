• māriṣamāriṣa m.
    • (perhaps fr. Pāli mārisa = mādṛśamādṛśa, ‘colleague’; cf. mārṣamārṣa) a worthy or respectable man (esp. in the voc. as a term of address = ‘worthy friend’ or ‘dear sir’ ; in dram. applied to the manager or one of the principal actors), MBh.; Kāv.; BhP. [811,3]
    • Amaranthus Oleraceus, Bhpr. pl. N. of a people, MBh. [812,1]
