• ni-√kṣipnikṣipnikṣip
    • P. -kṣipatikṣipati, to throw or cast or put or lay down, throw &c. in or upon (loc. or upariupari), Yājñ.; MBh.; R. &c.
    • to pour in (kṣīraṃkṣīra śarāveśarāva), Pañc. iii, 135/136;
    • to deliver anything (acc.) to (loc., esp. hastehastehastahastā),
    • to give or hand over, deposit, intrust, Mn.; Yājñ.; MBh. &c.
    • to instal, appoint to (loc.), R.;
    • to lay aside, give up, leave, abandon, cast off, repel, MBh.; R. &c.
    • to put down figures, count, cipher, Lalit. [545,2]
