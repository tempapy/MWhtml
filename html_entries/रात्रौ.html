• rātri ⮩ rātraurātrau
  • ind., at night, by night; rātraurātrau śayanamśayanam, a festival on the 11th day of the first half of the month Āṣāḍhaāṣāḍha, regarded as the night of the gods, beginning with the summer solstice, when Viṣṇuviṣṇu reposes for four months on the serpent Śeṣaśeṣa [876,1]
