• rātri ⮩ rātraurātraurātrarātri ind.
    • , at night, by night; rātraurātraurātrarātri śayanamśayana, a festival on the 11th day of the first half of the month Āṣāḍha, regarded as the night of the gods, beginning with the summer solstice, when Viṣṇu reposes for four months on the serpent Śeṣa [876,1]
