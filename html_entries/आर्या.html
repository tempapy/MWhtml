• āryāāryāārya f.
    • a name of Pārvatī, Hariv. [152,2]
    • a kind of metre of two lines (each line consisting of seven and a half feet; each foot containing four instants, except the sixth of the second line, which contains only one, and is therefore a single short syllable; hence there are thirty instants in the first line and twenty-seven in the second); [152,2]
    • (also) N. of a Stotra &c. [1320,1]
