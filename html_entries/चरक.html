    • a spy, Naiṣ. iv, 116 [389,2]
    • a kind of ascetic, VarBṛ. xv, 1 [389,2]
    • a kind of medicinal plant, L. [389,2]
    • N. of a Muni and physician (the Serpent-king Śeṣa, who was the recipient of the Āyur-veda; once on visiting the earth and finding it full of sickness he became moved with pity and determined to become incarnate as the son of a Muni for alleviating disease; he was called Caraka because he had visited the earth as a kind of spy or caracara; he then composed a new book on medicine, based on older works of Agni-veśa and other pupils of Ātreya, Bhpr.) [389,2]
    • N. of a lexicographer [389,2]
