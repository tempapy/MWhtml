• draviḍadraviḍa m.
    • N. of a people (regarded as degraded Kṣatriyas and said to be descendants of Draviḍa, sons of Vṛṣabha-svāmin, Śatr.) and of a district on the east coast of the Deccan, Mn.; Var.; MBh. &c. [501,2]
    • collect. N. for 5 peoples, viz. the Āndhras, Karṇāṭakas, Gurjaras, Tailaṅgas, and Mahārāṣṭras (cf. dāviḍa below) [501,2]
    • N. of a son of Kṛṣṇa, BhP. [501,2]
    • of an author, Cat. [501,2]
    • pl. of a school of grammarians, ib. [501,2]
