sukhasukha—rātrirātri or sukhasukha—rātrikārātrikā, f. a partic. night of new moon (when there is a festival in honour of Lakṣmilakṣmi, celebrated with lighted lamps), Cat. [1221,2]
  • a night when a wife may be legally approached (cf. Mn. iii, 47), MW. [1221,2]
  • a quiet or comfortable night (cf. saurātrikasaurātrika). [1221,2]
