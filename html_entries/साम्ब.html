• sāmbasāmba
  • m. (also written śāmbaśāmba) N. of a son of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa and Jāmbavatījāmbavatī (in consequence of the curse of some holy sages who had been deceived by a female disguise which he had assumed, he was condemned to produce offspring in the shape of a terrific iron club for the destruction of the race of Vṛṣṇivṛṣṇi and Andhakaandhaka; he is said to have been instructed by Nāradanārada in the worship of the sun, and by Vyāsavyāsa in the ritual of the Magi), MBh.; Hariv.; Pur. [1207,1]
  • (also with śāstrinśāstrin) N. of various authors and teachers, Cat. [1207,1]
