    • silk [945,2]

    • a conch shell (= dakṣiṇā̑vartadakṣiṇāvartadakṣiṇadakṣiṇāāvarta) [945,2]
    • a sort of curl on the right side of a horse's neck (regarded as an auspicious mark) [945,2]
    • = -vīravārdaravīra (prob. w.r. for vārivārivārīvārin; cf. above). [945,2]
