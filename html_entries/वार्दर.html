  • the seed of the Mangifera Indica [945,2]
  • silk [945,2]
  • water [945,2]
  • a conch shell (= dakṣiṇāvartadakṣiṇāvarta) [945,2]
  • a sort of curl on the right side of a horse's neck (regarded as an auspicious mark) [945,2]
  • = -vīravīravārdaravīra (prob. w.r. for vārivāri; cf. above). [945,2]
