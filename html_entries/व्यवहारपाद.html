• vy-avahāravy-avahāravyavahāra—pāda m. the fourth part of a legal process, one of the four stages of a regular lawsuit (these four are, pūrva-pakṣapūrva-pakṣapūrvapakṣa, uttara-pakṣauttara-pakṣauttarapakṣa, kriyā-pādakriyā-pādakriyāpāda, nirṇaya-pādanirṇaya-pādanirṇayapāda, qq.vv.; cf. vyavahārasya prathamaḥ pādaḥ, Mṛcch. ix, 8/7), L.
