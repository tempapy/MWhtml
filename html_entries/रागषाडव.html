• rāgarāga—ṣāḍavaṣāḍava
    • (or -ṣāḍhavarāgaṣāḍava) m. a kind of sweetmeat or syrup compounded of grapes and pomegranates and the juice of Phaseolus Mungo (or of half ripe mango fruit with ginger, cardamoms, oil, butter &c.), R.; Suśr. (also written rāga-khāḍavarāgakhāḍavarāgakhāḍava cf. rāga-khāṇḍavarāgakhāṇḍavarāgakhāṇḍava). [872,2]
