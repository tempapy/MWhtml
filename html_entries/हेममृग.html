• hema—mṛga m. a golden deer (according to a story told in R. iii, the Rākṣasa Mārīca transformed himself into a golden deer and so captivated Rāma and his wife Sītā, that the former left his forest-home to pursue it; during his absence Rāvaṇa q.v., disguised as a mendicant, presented himself before Sītā and carried her off). [1304,3]
