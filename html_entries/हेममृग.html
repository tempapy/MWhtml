• hemahema—mṛgamṛga
  • m. a golden deer (according to a story told in R. iii, the Rākṣasarākṣasa Mārīcamārīca transformed himself into a golden deer and so captivated Rāmarāma and his wife Sītāsītā, that the former left his forest-home to pursue it; during his absence Rāvaṇarāvaṇa q.v., disguised as a mendicant, presented himself before Sītāsītā and carried her off).
