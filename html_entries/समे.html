• sam-ȇsamesame
  • (-āāsam-ā 5 √ii) P. -a͡itiaitisam-aiti (pr. p. -ȇyānaeyānasam-eyāna, MBh.), to come together, approach together, meet at or in (acc. or loc.) or with (instr. with or without samamsamam), go to or across, arrive at (acc.), RV. &c. &c.;
    to unite with (instr.) in marriage, R.;
    to enter, Hit.;
    to emulate, cope with (acc.), R.;
    (with yogamyogam) to lead or join together, form an alliance between (gen.) and (instr.), ŚvetUp. (= saṃ-gamayyasaṃgamayyasaṃgamayya, Śaṃk.) [1171,2]
