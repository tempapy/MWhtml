• āryaārya—satyasatya
  • n. (Pālipāli ariyasaccamariyasaccam) sublime truth [152,3]
  • (with Buddhists the cattaricattari ariyasaccāniariyasaccāni or ‘four great truths’ are, 1. life is suffering, 2. desire of life is the cause of suffering, 3. extinction of that desire is the cessation of suffering, 4. the eightfold path (See below) leads to that extinction.) [152,3]
