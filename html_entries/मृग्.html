• mṛgmṛg
  • (rather Nom. fr. mṛgamṛga; cf. √mārgmārg, mṛjmṛj) cl. 4. P., Ā. 10 (Dhātup. xxvi, 137; xxxv, 46) mṛgyatimṛgyati, mṛgayatemṛgayate (mc. also P. °timṛgti; pr. p. mṛgayāṇamṛgayāṇa, MBh.),
    to chase, hunt, pursue, RV. &c. &c.;
    to seek, search for or through, investigate, examine, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to visit, frequent, MBh.;
    to seek or strive after, aim at, endeavour to obtain (acc.), MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to desire or request or ask anything (acc.) from (abl., -tastasmṛgtas, gen. with or without sakāśātsakāśāt), Kathās. [828,2]
