• saṃ-√hansaṃhansaṃhan
  • P. -hantihantisaṃ-hanti, (ind.p. -hatyahatyasaṃ-hatya q.v.), to strike or put together, join, shut, close (eyes, wings, hands), RV. &c. &c.;
    to beat together, make solid, Suśr.;
    to put together i.e. frame, fabricate, ŚāṅkhŚr.;
    (Ā.) to rush together (in battle), meet, encounter (instr.), RV. vii, 56, 22;
    (jighnatejighnate), to meet as a friend (instr.), ib. ix, 14, 4;
    (P.) to break, crush, kill, destroy, ib. :
    Pass. -hanyatehanyatesaṃ-hanyate, to be put together or joined, join, unite (intr.), Śaṃk.;
    to become compact or solid, ŚBr. :
    Caus. -ghātayatighātayatisaṃ-ghātayati, to strike together, kill, destroy utterly, MW. [1122,2]
