• sānusānu
  • m. n. (accord. to Uṇ. i, 3 fr. √sansan; collateral form 3. snusnu) a summit, ridge, surface, top of a mountain, (in later language generally) mountain-ridge, table-land, RV. &c. &c. (L. also, ‘a sprout; a forest; road; gale of wind; sage, learned man; the sun’). [1202,3]
