• mahāmahāmahamah—pātakapātaka n.
    • a great crime or sin (5 such are enumerated, viz. killing a Brāhman, drinking intoxicating liquors, theft, committing adultery with the wife of a religious teacher, and associating with any one guilty of these crimes), Mn. (esp. xi, 54); Yājñ. &c. [797,2]
    • any great crime or heinous sin, W. [797,2]
