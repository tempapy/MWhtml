• upa-sarjana
  • n. (ifc. mf(A)n.) the act of pouring upon, KātyŚr. [210,2]
  • infusion, Car. [210,2]
  • an inauspicious phenomenon, eclipse, Mn. iv, 105 [210,2]
  • anything or any person subordinate to another, Mn. [210,2]
  • a substitute, representation, Nyāyam. &c. [210,2]
  • (in Gr.) ‘subordinate, secondary’ (opposed to praDAna), any word which by composition or derivation loses its original independence while it also determines the sense of another word (e.g. the word rAjan in rAjapuruza, ‘a king's servant or minister’, and the word apiSali in ApiSala, Pāṇ. i, 2, 43, &c.; vi, 2, 36; in a Bahu-vrīhibahuvrīhibahuvrīhi compound both members are upasarjana; in other compounds generally the first member, Pāṇ. ii, 2, 30; for exceptions See Pāṇ. ii, 2, 31) [210,2]
