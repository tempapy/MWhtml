• upa-sarjanaupasarjanaupasarjana n.
    • (ifc. mf(āāa)n.) the act of pouring upon, KātyŚr. [210,2]
    • infusion, Car. [210,2]
    • an inauspicious phenomenon, eclipse, Mn. iv, 105 [210,2]
    • anything or any person subordinate to another, Mn. [210,2]
    • a substitute, representation, Nyāyam. &c. [210,2]
    • (in Gr.) ‘subordinate, secondary’ (opposed to pradhānapradhāna), any word which by composition or derivation loses its original independence while it also determines the sense of another word (e.g. the word rājanrājan in rājapuruṣarājapuruṣa, ‘a king's servant or minister’, and the word apiśali in āpiśalaāpiśala, Pāṇ. i, 2, 43, &c. vi, 2, 36; in a Bahu-vrīhi compound both members are upasarjanaupasarjana; in other compounds generally the first member, Pāṇ. ii, 2, 30; for exceptions See Pāṇ. ii, 2, 31) [210,2]
