• paraśuparaśu—rāmarāma m.
    • ‘Rama with the axe’, N. of one of the three Rāmas (son of Jamad-agni and sixth Avatāra of Viṣṇu, he was a typical Brāhman and his history typifies the contests between the Brahmans and Kṣatriyas), Kāv.; Pur.; MWB. xiii, 1; RTL. 110; 270 (also -kaparaśurāmaka) [589,2]
    • N. of a prince and of sev. authors (also with garjaragarjara, devadeva, miśramiśra, munimunimunīmu), Cat. [589,2]
