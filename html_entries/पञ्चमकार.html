• pañcapañca—ma-kāramakāramakāra n.
    • the 5 essentials of the left-hand Tantra ritual (the words for which begin with the letter m, viz. madyamadya, wine; māṃsamāṃsa, meat; matsyamatsya, fish; mudrāmudrāmudra, intertwining of the fingers; and maithunamaithuna, sexual union), W. (cf. -tattvapañcamakāratattva and, RTL. 192). [576,3]
