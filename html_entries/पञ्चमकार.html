• pañcapañca—ma-kāramakāramakāra
  • n. the 5 essentials of the left-hand Tantratantra ritual (the words for which begin with the letter mm, viz. madyamadya, wine; māṃsamāṃsa, meat; matsyamatsya, fish; mudrāmudrā, intertwining of the fingers; and maithunamaithuna, sexual union), W. (cf. -tattvatattvapañca—matattva and, RTL. 192). [576,3]
