• pañca—ma-kārama-kāramakāra n. the 5 essentials of the left-hand Tantra ritual (the words for which begin with the letter m, viz. madya, wine; māṃsa, meat; matsya, fish; mudrā, intertwining of the fingers; and maithuna, sexual union), W. (cf. -tattvapañcamakāra-tattvatattva and, RTL. 192). [576,3]
