• a-yugmaayugmaayugma—pādayamakapādayamaka n.
    • (a species of alliteration) the repetition of the odd (i.e. the first and third) Pādas of a stanza (in such a manner that the sense of the sounds repeated is different in the first and third Pāda) Comm. on Bhaṭṭ. x, 10. [85,3]
