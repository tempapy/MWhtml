• vratayavrataya
  • Nom. P. vratayativratayati, to drink the (hot) Vratavrata-milk (also with payaspayas &c.), TS.; Kāṭh.; ŚBr.;
    to eat or drink after a fast, TāṇḍyaBr.; AitĀr., Sch., (w.r. avrajayatavrajayat) ;
    to observe a vow, MW.;
    to fast or practise any abstinence in consequence of a vow, ib.;
    to avoid certain kinds of food (as Śūdraśūdra food), Pāṇ. iii, 5, 21 Sch.;
    to eat together, MW. [1042,3]
