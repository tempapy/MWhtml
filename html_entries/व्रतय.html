• vratayavrataya
    • Nom. P. vratayati, to drink the (hot) Vrata-milk (also with payaspayaspayapi &c.), TS.; Kāṭh.; ŚBr.;
    • to eat or drink after a fast, TāṇḍyaBr.; AitĀr., Sch., (w.r. avrajayat) ;
    • to observe a vow, MW.;
    • to fast or practise any abstinence in consequence of a vow, ib.;
    • to avoid certain kinds of food (as Śūdra food), Pāṇ. iii, 5, 21 Sch.;
    • to eat together, MW. [1042,3]
