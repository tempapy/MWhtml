• pañcapañca—valkalavalkala
  • n. a collection of the bark of 5 kinds of trees (viz. the Indian, glomerous, holy, and waved-leaf fig-tree, and Calamus Rotang i.e. Nyag-rodha, Udumbaraudumbara, Aśvatthaaśvattha, Plakṣaplakṣa, and Vetasavetasa; but other trees are sometimes substituted), Rasar. [577,2]
