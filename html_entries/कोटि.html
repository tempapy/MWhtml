• koṭikoṭi
  • f. the curved end of a bow or of claws, &c., end or top of anything, edge or point (of a sword), horns or cusps (of the moon), MBh. &c. [312,3]
  • the highest point, eminence, excellence, Pañcat.; Ratnâv.; Sarvad. [312,3]
  • ‘a point or side in an argument or disputation’, (if there are two) ‘alternative’ See -dvayadvayakoṭidvaya below [312,3]
  • the highest number in the older system of numbers (viz. a Krore or ten millions), Mn.; Yājñ.; MBh. &c. [312,3]
  • the complement of an arc to 90 degrees [312,3]
  • the perpendicular side of a right-angled triangle, Sūryas. [312,3]
  • Medicago esculenta, L. [312,3]
