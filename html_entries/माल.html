• mālamāla m.
    • (derivation doubtful) N. of a district (lying west and south-west of Bengal), Megh. [813,2]
    • of one of the 7 islands, of Antara-dvīpa; L., of Viṣṇu, L. [813,2]
    • of the son of a Śūdra and a Sūta, L. [813,2]
    • pl. N. of a barbarous tribe or people, MBh. [813,2]
  • mālamāla n.
    • a field, Inscr.; MBh. [813,2]
    • a forest or wood near a village, L. [813,2]
    • fraud, artifice, L. [813,2]
    • (in some comp.) = mālā, a wreath, garland. [813,2]
