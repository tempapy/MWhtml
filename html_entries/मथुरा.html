  • mathurāmathurā
  • f. N. of various towns (esp. of an ancient town now called Muttra and held in great honour as the birthplace of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa; situated in the province of Agraagra on the right bank of the Yamunāyamunā or Jumna; described in VP. xii, 1 as having been founded by Śatru-ghnaśatrughnaśatrughna; accord. to Kull. on Mn. ii, 19 it forms part of a district called Brahmarṣibrahmarṣi, belonging to Śūra-senaśūrasenaśūrasena), AV.Pariś.; Hariv.; Pur. &c.
