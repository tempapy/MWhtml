• viralavirala mf(āāa)n.
    • (perhaps from vira = vilavila for bilabila + lala, ‘possessing holes’) having interstices, separated by intervals (whether of space or time), not thick or compact, loose, thin, sparse, wide apart, MBh.; Kāv. &c. [982,2]
    • rare, scarcely found, unfrequent, scanty, few, Kāv.; Kathās. &c. (ibc. and amama ind. sparsely, rarely, seldom; viralaḥvirala with or without ko'pi, one here and there) [982,2]
  • viralavirala n.
    • sour curds (= dadhidadhidadh), L. [982,2]
