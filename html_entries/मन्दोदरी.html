• mandȏdarī̑mandodarīmandamandāiīin f.
    • N. of Rāvaṇa's favourite wife (daughter of Maya and mother of Indra-jit; she advised her husband to deliver Sītā to Rāma, but he did not heed her; she is regarded as one of the five very chaste women, the other four being Ahalyā, Draupadī, Sītā, and Tārā), MBh.; R. &c. [788,2]
    • of one of the Mātṛs attending on Skanda, MBh. [788,2]
    • of the mother of the lexicographer Jaṭā-dhara, Cat. [788,2]
