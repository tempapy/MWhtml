• veṣṭveṣṭ [Roots]
  • (cf. viṣṭviṣṭ) cl. 1. Ā. (Dhātup. viii, 2) veṣṭateveṣṭate (pf. viveṣṭeviveṣṭe fut. veṣṭitāveṣṭitā &c. Gr.), to wind or twist round, Sāh.;
    to adhere or cling to (loc.), AV.;
    to cast the skin (said of a snake), R.;
    to dress, MW. :
    Caus. veṣṭayativeṣṭayati, °teveṣṭte (aor. aviveṣṭataviveṣṭat, or avaveṣṭatavaveṣṭat; Pass. veṣṭyateveṣṭyate),
    to wrap up, envelop, enclose, surround, cover, invest, beset, TBr.; Mn.; MBh. &c.;
    to tie on, wrap round (a turban &c.), MBh.; Rājat.;
    to cause to shrink up, ŚvetUp. :
    Desid. viveṣṭiṣateviveṣṭiṣate Gr.:
    Intens. veveṣṭyateveveṣṭyate, veveṣṭiveveṣṭi, ib. [1019,2]
