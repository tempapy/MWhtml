• prātiprāti—śākhyaśākhya n.
    • (fr. -śākhamprātiśākhyaśākha) a treatise on the peculiar euphonic combination and pronunciation of letters which prevails in different Śākhās of the Vedas (there are 4 Prātiśākhya one for the Śākala-śākhā of the RV.; two for particular Śākhās of the black and white Yajur-vedas, and one for a Śākhā of the AV.; cf. IW. 149, 150) [706,3]
