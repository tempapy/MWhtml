• yatasyatas
  • ind. (fr. 3. yaya, correlative of tatastatas, and often used as abl. or instr. of the relative pron.) from which or what, whence, whereof, wherefrom, RV. &c. &c. (yatoyato yataḥyataḥ, ‘from whichever’, ‘from whatever’, ‘whencesoever’; yatasyatas tataḥtataḥ, ‘from any one soever’, ‘from any quarter whatever’; yatayata evaeva kutaśkutaś caca, ‘from this or that place’, ‘whencesoever’) [841,2]
  • where, in what place, AV. &c. &c. [841,2]
  • whither, Kāv.; Var.; Kathās. (yatoyato yataḥyataḥ, ‘whithersoever’; yatasyatas tataḥtataḥ, ‘any whither’, ‘to any place whatever’) [841,2]
  • wherefore, for which reason, in consequence where of R.; BhP. [841,2]
  • as, because, for, since, AV. &c. &c. (often connecting with a previous statement) [841,2]
  • from which time forward, since when (also with prabhṛtiprabhṛti; yatoyato jātājātā, ‘ever since birth’), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [841,2]
  • as soon as, RV. iii, 10, 6 [841,2]
  • that (= ὅτι, also to introduce an oratio recta), Kāv.; Pur. [841,2]
  • in order that (with Pot.), BhP. [841,2]
