  • yoginī b f. a female demon or any being endowed with magical power, a fairy, witch, sorceress (represented as eight in number and as created by Durgā and attendant on her or on Śiva; sometimes 60, 64 or 65 are enumerated), Hariv.; Kathās. &c. (cf. RTL. 188, 189) [858,1]
  • N. of Durgā, L. [858,1]
  • (with Tāntrikas) a partic. Śakti [858,1]
  • (with Buddhists) a woman representing any goddess who is the object of adoration. [858,1]
