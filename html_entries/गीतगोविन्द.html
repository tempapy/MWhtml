• gītagīta—govindagovinda
  • n. ‘Govindagovinda (i.e. Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa) celebrated in song’, N. of a lyrical drama by Jayadevajayadeva (probably written in the beginning of the twelfth century; it is a mystical erotic poem describing the loves of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa and the Gopīgopīs, especially of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa and Rādhārādhā, who is supposed to typify the human soul).
