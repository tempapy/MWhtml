• yogā̑ṅga n. a constituent or part of the Yoga, means of attaining it (generally said to be 8 in number, viz. yama, niyama, āsana, prā̑ṇā̑yāma, pratyāhāra, dhāraṇā, dhyāna, and samādhi, or according to another authority 6, viz. āsana, prā̑ṇasaṃrodha, with the last four as enumerated above), Yogas.; Sarvad.
