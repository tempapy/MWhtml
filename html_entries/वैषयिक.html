    • relating to, concerning (comp.), Car. [1027,2]
    • belonging or relating to an object of sense, sensual, carnal, mundane, Pañcar.; HPariś. [1027,2]
  • vaiṣayikavaiṣayika m.
    • a sensualist, one addicted to the pleasures of sense or absorbed in worldly objects, L. (also -janavaiṣayikajana) [1027,2]
