• pra-°sūtapraprasūta mf(āāa)n.
    • (pra-praprasūta) procreated, begotten, born, produced, sprung (‘by’ or ‘from’ abl. or gen.; ‘in’ loc. or comp.; cf. Pāṇ. ii, 3, 39), Up.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [698,1]
  • pra-°sūtapraprasūta m. pl.
    • (or sg. with gaṇagaṇa) N. of a class of gods under Manu Cākṣuṣa, Hariv.; MārkP. [698,1]
  • pra-°sūtapraprasūta n.
    • a flower, L. [698,1]
    • any productive source, MW. [698,1]
    • (in Sāṃkhya) the primordial essence or matter, Tattvas. [698,1]
