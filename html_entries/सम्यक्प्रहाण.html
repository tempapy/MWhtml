• samyaksamyak—prahāṇaprahāṇa n.
    • (= Pali sammappadhāna) right abandonment, right effort, right exertion (with Buddhists of four kinds, 1 -to prevent demerit from arising, 2. get rid of it when arisen, 3. produce merit, 4. increase it), Divyâv. [1181,2]
    • Dharmas. 45; MWB. 50. [1181,2]
