• saṃ-√kuñcsaṃkuñcsaṃkuñc
  • (or -kuckucsaṃkuc) P. -kucatikucatisaṃkucati, to contract, shrink, close (as a flower), Kāv.; Kathās.; Suśr.;
    to contract, compress, absorb, destroy, Nir. Sch.:
    Pass. -kucyatekucyatesaṃkucyate, to shrink, be closed or contracted, Suśr. :
    Caus. -kocayatikocayatisaṃkocayati, to contract, draw in MBh.; Suśr.;
    to narrow, make smaller, lessen, Bhartṛ.;
    (Ā.) to withdraw, withhold, Subh. [1125,3]
