• 1. sātsātsasādh
    • a Taddhita affix which when put after a word denotes a total change of anything into the thing expressed by that word (See agni-agnisātagnisātsa, bhasma-sātbhasmasātbhasmasātsa &c.) [1200,1]
  • 2. sātsātsasādh
    • a Sautra root meaning ‘to give pleasure’ Pāṇ.; Vop. [1200,1]
  • 3. sātsātsasādh n.
    • N. of Brahman, L. [1200,1]
