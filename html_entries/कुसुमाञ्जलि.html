• kusumāñjalikusumāñjali
  • f. a handful of flowers (properly as much as will fill both hands), Ratnāv. [298,2]
  • N. of a philosophical work (written by Udayanaudayana Ācāryaācārya to prove the existence of a Supreme Being, and consisting of seventy-two Kārikākārikās divided into five chapters), Sarvad. [298,2]
