• mūla—tva n. the state of being a root or foundation or source, the having a foundation (tan-mūlatvāttan-mūlatvāttanmūlatvāt prajānāṃ rājā skandhaḥ, ‘the king is the stem of his subjects through their being his root’; veda-mūlatvaveda-mūlatvavedamūlatva, ‘the fact that the Veda is the original source of all knowledge’; śāstra-m°śāstra-mūlatvaśāstramūlatva, ‘the being founded upon the Śāstras’), Kām. (cf. tan-m°tan-mūlatvatanmūlatva). [826,3]
