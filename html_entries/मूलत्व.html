• mūlamūla—tvatva n.
    • the state of being a root or foundation or source, the having a foundation (tan-mūlatvāttanmūlatvatantatmūlatva prajānāṃprajānāprajaprajā rājārājarāj skandhaḥskandhasskandha, ‘the king is the stem of his subjects through their being his root’; veda-mūlatvavedamūlatva, ‘the fact that the Veda is the original source of all knowledge’; śāstra-m°śāstraśāstra, ‘the being founded upon the Śāstras’), Kām. (cf. tan-m°tantat). [826,3]
