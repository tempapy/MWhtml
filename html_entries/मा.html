  • 1. māmāma ind.
    • (causing a following ch to be changed to cch, Pāṇ. vi, 1, 74) not, that not, lest, would that not, RV. &c. &c. [804,1]
    • a particle of prohibition or negation = Gk. μή, most commonly joined with the Subjunctive i.e. the augmentless form of a past tense (esp. of the aor. e.g. māmāmamāsmas nononu vadhīr indraindra, do not slay us, O Indra, RV.; māmāmamāsmas bhaiṣīḥ or māmāmamāsmas bhaiḥbhaisbha, do not be afraid, MBh.; tapovana-vāsināmtapovanavāsin uparodhouparodha māmāmamāsmas bhūt, let there not be any disturbance of the inhabitants of the sacred grove, Śak.; often also with smasma e.g. māmāma smasma gamaḥ, do not go, Bhag. cf. Pāṇ. 3-3, 175; 176 in the sense of, ‘that not, lest’ also yathāyathāyatha māmāma e.g. yathāyathāyatha māmāmamāsmas vovasvaū mṛtyuḥmṛtyu pari-vyatkāpari itiitiit, that death may not disturb you, PraśnaUp.; or māyathā e.g. māmāmamāsmas bhūt kālā̑tyayokālātyayakālakālāatyaya yathāyathāyatha, lest there be any loss of time, R.; māmāmamāsmas nana with aor. Subj. = Ind. without a negative e.g. māmāmamāsmas dviṣodviṣadviṣ nana vadhīr mamamama, do slay my enemies, Bhaṭṭ. cf. Vām. v, 1, 9; rarely with the augmentless impf. with or without smasma e.g. ma͡inammamāenaaina abhibhāṣathāḥ, do not speak to him, R.; māmāma smasma karot, let him not do it, Pāṇ. vi, 4, 74 Sch.; exceptionally also with the Ind. of the aor. e.g., māmāma kālaskāla tvāmtvatu aty-agātatiatīaga, may not the season pass by thee, MBh.; cf. Pāṇ. vi, 4, 75 Sch.) [804,1]
    • or with the Impv. (in, RV. only viii, 103, 6, māmāmamāsmas nononunasna hṛṇītām [SV. hṛṇītās] agniḥagni, may Agni not be angry with us; but very often in later language e.g. māmāma krandakranda do not cry, MBh.; gacchagaccha vāvāvauū māmāmamāsmas vāvāvauū, you can go or not go, ib.; ripurripu ayamayamaya māmāmamāsmas jāyatām, may not this foe arise, Śāntiś.; also with smasma e.g. māmāma smasma kiṃkiṃ cidcidcit vacovacovacasvacavac vadavada do not speak a word, MBh.) [804,1]
    • or with the Pot. (e.g. māmāmamāsmas yamamyama paśyeyam, may l not See Yama; esp. mābhujema in RV.) [804,1]
    • or with the Prec. (only once in māmāmamāsmas bhūyātbhūya, may it not be, R. [B.] ii, 75, 45) [804,1]
    • or sometimes with the fut. (= that not, lest e.g. māmāma tvāṃ śapsye, lest I curse thee, MBh. cf. Vop. xxv, 27) [804,1]
    • or with a participle (e.g. māmāmamāsmas jīvanyojīvanī duḥkhadagdhoduḥkhadagdha jīvatijīvat, he ought not to live who lives consumed by pain, Pañcat.; gataḥgatagat sasa māmāma, he cannot have gone, Kathās.; maī̑vamprā̑rthyamarthya, it must not be so requested, BhP.) [804,1]
    • sometimes for the simple negative nana (e.g. kathamkathamkath māmāmamāsmas bhūt, how may it not be, Kathās.; māmāmamāsmas gantumgantu arhasi, thou oughtest not to go, R.; māmāmamāsmas bhūd āgataḥāgata, can he not i.e. surely he must have arrived, Amar.) [804,1]
    • occasionally without a verb (e. g. māmāma śabdaḥ or śabdamśabdaśabd, do not make a noise, Hariv.; māmāmamāsmas nāmanāma rakṣiṇaḥ, may it not be the watchmen, Mṛcch.; māmāmamāsmas bhavantambhavantabhavat analaḥanala pavanopavana vāvāvauū, may not fire or wind harm thee, Vām. v, 1, 14; esp. = not so e.g. māmāma prātṛdaprātṛda, not so, O Prātṛda, ŚBr.; in this meaning also māmāmamāsmas māmāma, māmāmamāsmas ma͡ivammamāevameva, māmāma tāvattāvat) [804,2]
    • in the Veda often with uuū (mȏmomumamāuū) = and not, nor (e.g. māmāmamāsmas maghonaḥ paripariparī khyatam mȏmomumamāuū asmākamasmāka ṛṣṇām, do not forget the rich lords nor us the poets, RV. v, 65, 6; and then usually followed by ṣuṣuṣū = sususū e.g. mȏmomumamāuū ṣuṣuṣū ṇaḥ nirṛtir vadhīt, let not Nírṛtir on any account destroy us, i, 38, 6) [804,2]
    • in ŚBr. smasma māmāma - momomu smasma = neither - nor (in a prohibitive sense). [804,2]
