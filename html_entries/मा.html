  • māmā
  • ind. (causing a following chch to be changed to cchcch, Pāṇ. vi, 1, 74) not, that not, lest, would that not, RV. &c. &c. [804,1]
  • a particle of prohibition or negation = Gk. μή, most commonly joined with the Subjunctive i.e. the augmentless form of a past tense (esp. of the aor. e.g. māmā nono vadhīrvadhīr indraindra, do not slay us, O Indraindra, RV.; māmā bhaiṣīḥbhaiṣīḥ or māmā bhaiḥbhaiḥ, do not be afraid, MBh.; tapovana-vāsināmtapovanavāsināmtapovanavāsinām uparodhouparodho māmā bhūtbhūt, let there not be any disturbance of the inhabitants of the sacred grove, Śak.; often also with smasma e.g. māmā smasma gamaḥgamaḥ, do not go, Bhag. cf. Pāṇ. 3-3, 175; 176 in the sense of, ‘that not, lest’ also yathāyathā māmā e.g. yathāyathā māmā vovo mṛtyuḥmṛtyuḥ pari-vyatkāparivyatkāparivyatkā itiiti, that death may not disturb you, PraśnaUp.; or māyathāmāyathā e.g. māmā bhūtbhūt kālā̑tyayokālātyayo yathāyathā, lest there be any loss of time, R.; māmā nana with aor. Subj. = Ind. without a negative e.g. māmā dviṣodviṣo nana vadhīrvadhīr mamamama, do slay my enemies, Bhaṭṭ. cf. Vām. v, 1, 9; rarely with the augmentless impf. with or without smasma e.g. ma͡inammainam abhibhāṣathāḥabhibhāṣathāḥ, do not speak to him, R.; māmā smasma karotkarot, let him not do it, Pāṇ. vi, 4, 74 Sch.; exceptionally also with the Ind. of the aor. e.g., māmā kālaskālas tvāmtvām aty-agātatyagātatyagāt, may not the season pass by thee, MBh.; cf. Pāṇ. vi, 4, 75 Sch.) [804,1]
  • or with the Impv. (in, RV. only viii, 103, 6, māmā nono hṛṇītāmhṛṇītām [SV. hṛṇītāshṛṇītās] agniḥagniḥ, may Agniagni not be angry with us; but very often in later language e.g. māmā krandakranda do not cry, MBh.; gacchagaccha vāvā māmā vāvā, you can go or not go, ib.; ripurripur ayamayam māmā jāyatāmjāyatām, may not this foe arise, Śāntiś.; also with smasma e.g. māmā smasma kiṃkiṃ cidcid vacovaco vadavada do not speak a word, MBh.) [804,1]
  • or with the Pot. (e.g. māmā yamamyamam paśyeyampaśyeyam, may l not See Yamayama; esp. mābhujemamābhujema in RV.) [804,1]
  • or with the Prec. (only once in māmā bhūyātbhūyāt, may it not be, R. [B.] ii, 75, 45) [804,1]
  • or sometimes with the fut. (= that not, lest e.g. māmā tvāṃtvāṃ śapsyeśapsye, lest I curse thee, MBh. cf. Vop. xxv, 27) [804,1]
  • or with a participle (e.g. māmā jīvanyojīvanyo duḥkhadagdhoduḥkhadagdho jīvatijīvati, he ought not to live who lives consumed by pain, Pañcat.; gataḥgataḥ sasa māmā, he cannot have gone, Kathās.; maī̑vamprā̑rthyammaīvamprārthyam, it must not be so requested, BhP.) [804,1]
  • sometimes for the simple negative nana (e.g. kathamkatham māmā bhūtbhūt, how may it not be, Kathās.; māmā gantumgantum arhasiarhasi, thou oughtest not to go, R.; māmā bhūdbhūd āgataḥāgataḥ, can he not i.e. surely he must have arrived, Amar.) [804,1]
  • occasionally without a verb (e. g. māmā śabdaḥśabdaḥ or śabdamśabdam, do not make a noise, Hariv.; māmā nāmanāma rakṣiṇaḥrakṣiṇaḥ, may it not be the watchmen, Mṛcch.; māmā bhavantambhavantam analaḥanalaḥ pavanopavano vāvā, may not fire or wind harm thee, Vām. v, 1, 14; esp. = not so e.g. māmā prātṛdaprātṛda, not so, O Prātṛdaprātṛda, ŚBr.; in this meaning also māmā māmā, māmā ma͡ivammaivam, māmā tāvattāvat) [804,2]
  • in the Vedaveda often with uu (mȏmo) = and not, nor (e.g. māmā maghonaḥmaghonaḥ paripari khyatamkhyatam mȏmo asmākamasmākam ṛṣṇāmṛṣṇām, do not forget the rich lords nor us the poets, RV. v, 65, 6; and then usually followed by ṣuṣu = susu e.g. mȏmo ṣuṣu ṇaḥṇaḥ nirṛtirnirṛtir vadhītvadhīt, let not Nírṛtirnirṛtir on any account destroy us, i, 38, 6) [804,2]
  • in ŚBr. smasma māmā - momo smasma = neither - nor (in a prohibitive sense). [804,2]
