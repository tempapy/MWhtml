• 1. āā
    • the second vowel of the alphabet corresponding to the a in far. [126,1]
  • 2. āā ind.
    • a particle of reminiscence, Pāṇ. i, 1, 14; Pat. [126,1]
    • also of compassion or pain [more correctly written 1. āsāsaā q.v.], and of assent, L. ([This particle remains unaltered in orthography even before vowels (which causes it to be sometimes confounded with 1. āsāsaā), Pāṇ. i, 1, 14.]) [126,1]
  • 3. āā m.
    • N. of Śiva, L. [126,1]
    • grandfather, L. [126,1]
  • āā f.
    • N. of Lakṣmī, L. [126,1]
