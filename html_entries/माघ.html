• māgha mf(ī)n. relating to the constellation Maghā, ŚārṅgS.; MBh. [805,3]
  • māgha m. (scil. māsa) the month Māgha (which has its full moon in the constellation Maghā, and corresponds to our January-February), ŚBr. &c. &c. [805,3]
  • N. of a poet (son of Dattaka and grandson of Suprabha-devasuprabha-devasuprabhadeva, author of the Śiśupāla-vadhaśiśupāla-vadhaśiśupālavadha, hence called Māgha-kāvyamāgha-kāvyamāghakāvya; cf. IW. 392 n. 2) [805,3]
  • of a merchant, Vīrac. [805,3]
