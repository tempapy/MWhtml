• māghamāgha mf(īīiin)n.
    • relating to the constellation Maghā, ŚārṅgS.; MBh. [805,3]
  • māghamāgha m.
    • (scil. māsamāsa) the month Māgha (which has its full moon in the constellation Maghā, and corresponds to our January-February), ŚBr. &c. &c. [805,3]
    • N. of a poet (son of Dattaka and grandson of Suprabha-deva, author of the Śiśupāla-vadha, hence called Māgha-kāvya; cf. IW. 392 n. 2) [805,3]
    • of a merchant, Vīrac. [805,3]
