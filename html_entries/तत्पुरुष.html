  • the servant of him, KātyŚr. vii, 1, 8 [433,2]
  • N. of a Kalpa period, MatsyaP. liii, 41 [433,2]
  • a class of compounds (formed like the word tat-puruṣatat-puruṣatatpuruṣa, ‘his servant’) in which the last member is qualified by the first without losing (as the last member of Bahu-vrīhibahu-vrīhibahuvrīhi compounds) its grammatical independence (whether as noun or adj. or p.) [433,2]
  • two subdivisions of these compounds are called Karma-dhārayakarma-dhārayakarmadhāraya and Dvi-gudvi-gudvigu (qq.vv.) [433,2]
