• kārṣā̑paṇakārṣāpaṇakārṣaāpaṇa mn.
    • (g. ardharcā̑diardharcādiardharcaadādiādin; cf. karṣ) ‘weighing a Karṣa’, a coin or weight of different values (if of gold, = 16 Māṣas See karṣakarṣa; if of silver, = 16 Paṇas or 1280 Kowries, commonly termed a Kahān; if of copper, = 80 Raktikās or about 176 grains; but accord. to some = only 1 Paṇa of Kowries or 80 Kowries), Mn. viii, 136; 336; ix, 282 [276,3]
    • (ifc.) worth so many Kārṣāpaṇas, Pāṇ. v, 1, 29 [276,3]
  • kārṣā̑paṇakārṣāpaṇakārṣaāpaṇa n.
    • money, gold and silver, L. [276,3]
  • kārṣā̑paṇakārṣāpaṇakārṣaāpaṇa m. pl.
    • N. of a warrior-tribe g. parśv-ādiparśuādiādin [276,3]
  • kārṣā̑paṇakārṣāpaṇakārṣaāpaṇa m.
    • the chief of this tribe, ib. and iv, 1, 177, Vārtt. 2. [276,3]
