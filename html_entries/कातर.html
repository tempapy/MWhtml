• kātarakātara mf(āāa)n.

[270,1]
  • kātarakātara m.
    • a kind of large fish (Cyprinus Catla, kātalakātala), L. [270,1]
    • N. of a man (See kātarāyaṇakātarāyaṇa) [270,1]
  • kātarakātara n.
    • (in sa-kātarasakātarasakātara) ‘timidity, despair, agitation’. [270,1]
