• pari-√vṛtpari-√vṛtpari√vṛt Ā. -vartateparivṛt-vartatevartate (ep. also °ti; ind.p. -vṛtyaparivṛt-vṛtyavṛtya, or parī-vartamparī-vartamparīvartam),
    to turn round, revolve, move in a circle or to and fro, roll or wheel or wander about, circumambulate (acc.), RV. &c. &c.;
    (with hṛdi or hṛdaye) to run in a person's mind, MBh.;
    to return, go or come back to (acc.), ib.;
    to be reborn in (loc.), VP.;
    (also with anyathā) to change, turn out different, MBh.; Kāv.;
    to abide, stay, remain, Mn.; MBh. &c.;
    to act, proceed, behave, R. :
    Caus. -vartayatiparivṛt-vartayativartayati, to cause to turn or move round or back or to and fro, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    (Ā.) to roll or bring near (Subj. -vartayāteparivṛt-vartayātevartayāte), RV. v, 37, 3;
    to overthrow, upset (a carriage), Hariv.;
    to invert, put in a reverse order, Mṛcch. v, 9/10 (read -vartyaparivṛt-vartyavartya for -vṛtyaparivṛt-vṛtyavṛtya);
    to change, barter, exchange, MBh.; Hariv. &c.;
    to renew (an agreement), Mn. viii, 154 &c.;
    to understand or explain wrongly (words &c.), MBh.; Kāv.;
    to turn topsy-turvy i.e. search thoroughly, R.;
    to destroy, annihilate, ib.;
    to straiten, contract, Car.;
    (Ā.) to cause one's self to be turned round (in having one's head shaved all round), TBr.; ŚBr. (cf. -vartanaparivṛt-vartanavartana) :
    Intens. -vavarttiparivṛt-vavarttivavartti, to turn (intrans.) continually, RV. i, 164, 11. [601,2]
