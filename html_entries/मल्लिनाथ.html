• mallimalli—nāthanātha m.
    • N. of a poet and celebrated commentator (also called Kolâcala or Peḍḍa Bhaṭṭa, father of Kumāra-svāmin and Viśvêśvara; he lived probably in the 14th or 15th century and wrote commentaries on the Raghuvaṉśa, Kumāra-sambhava, Megha-dūta, Śiśupālavadha, Kirātârjunīya, Bhaṭṭi-kāvya, Naiṣadīya &c.) [793,1]
