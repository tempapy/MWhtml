• malli—nātha m. N. of a poet and celebrated commentator (also called Kolâcala or Peḍḍa Bhaṭṭa, father of Kumāra-svāminkumāra-svāminkumārasvāmin and Viśvêśvara; he lived probably in the 14th or 15th century and wrote commentaries on the Raghuvaṉśa, Kumāra-sambhavakumāra-sambhavakumārasambhava, Megha-dūtamegha-dūtameghadūta, Śiśupālavadha, Kirātârjunīya, Bhaṭṭi-kāvyabhaṭṭi-kāvyabhaṭṭikāvya, Naiṣadīya &c.) [793,1]
  • of two authors on medicine and grammar, L. [793,1]
