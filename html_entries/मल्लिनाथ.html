• mallimalli—nāthanātha
  • m. N. of a poet and celebrated commentator (also called Kolâcalakolācala or Peḍḍapeḍḍa Bhaṭṭabhaṭṭa, father of Kumāra-svāminkumārasvāminkumārasvāmin and Viśvêśvaraviśveśvara; he lived probably in the 14th or 15th century and wrote commentaries on the Raghuvaṉśaraghuvaṃśa, Kumāra-sambhavakumārasambhavakumārasambhava, Megha-dūtameghadūtameghadūta, Śiśupālavadhaśiśupālavadha, Kirātârjunīyakirātārjunīya, Bhaṭṭi-kāvyabhaṭṭikāvyabhaṭṭikāvya, Naiṣadīyanaiṣadīya &c.) [793,1]
