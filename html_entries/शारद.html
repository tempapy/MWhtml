• śāradaśārada mf(īīi, or śāradīśāradīśāradin)n.
    • (fr. śaradśarad) produced or growing in autumn, autumnal, mature, AV. &c. &c. [1066,1]
    • (prob.) that which offers a shelter in autumn (against the overflowings of rivers; applied to puraspuraspura or ‘castles’; others ‘rich in years’, ‘old’), RV. i, 131, 4; 174, 2; vi, 20, 10 [1066,1]
    • new, recent, L. (perhaps, Bhartṛ. i, 47 in salilaṃsalila śāradamśārada; cf. also rajju-śāradarajjuśāradarajjuśārada and dṛṣac-chāradadṛṣacchāradadṛṣacdṛṣat) [1066,1]
    • modest, shy, diffident, L. [1066,1]
  • śāradaśārada m.
    • a year, L. [1066,1]
    • a cloud, L. [1066,1]
    • N. of various plants (a yellow kind of Phaseolus Mungo; Mimusops Elengi &c.), L. [1066,2]
    • autumnal sickness, W. [1066,2]
    • autumnal sunshine, ib. [1066,2]
    • N. of a teacher of Yoga (v.l. śābaraśābara), Cat. [1066,2]
  • śāradaśārada n.
    • corn, grain, fruit (as ripening in autumn), VarBṛS. [1066,2]
    • the white lotus, L. [1066,2]
