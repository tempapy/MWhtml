• saṃ-√diśsaṃdiśsaṃdiś
  • P. -diśatidiśatisaṃ-diśati (ind.p. -diśyadiśyasaṃ-diśya q.v.), to point out, appoint, assign, R.; Bhaṭṭ.;
    to state, tell, direct, command, give an order or message to (gen. or dat., sometimes two acc.; with abhi-dūtiabhidūtiabhidūti, ‘to a female messenger’), MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to despatch any one (acc.) on a message to (dat.), Kum. :
    Caus. -deśayatideśayatisaṃ-deśayati, to cause to point out or declare, invite or ask to speak about, MBh. [1143,2]
