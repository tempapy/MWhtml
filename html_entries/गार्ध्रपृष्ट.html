• gārdhragārdhra—pṛṣṭapṛṣṭa
  • for (gārdhra-spṛṣṭagārdhraspṛṣṭagārdhraspṛṣṭa; in Prākṛt geddha-paṭṭhaga), ‘touched (i.e. seized) by vultures’, a kind of death not sanctioned by the Jainajainas (probably with reference to the Pārsī custom of exposing corpses to vultures). [354,2]
