• sauptika—parvan n. N. of the 10th book of the Mahā-bhāratamahā-bhāratamahābhārata (describing how the three surviving Kuru warriors, Aśvatthāman, Kṛta-varmankṛta-varmankṛtavarman, and Kṛpa, after the destruction of their army, attacked the camp of the Pāṇḍavas by night and murdered them while asleep; the whole Pāṇḍava army was thus destroyed, except the five Pāṇḍu princes themselves, who, with Kṛṣṇa and Sātyaki, were stationed at some distance from the camp). [1253,2]
