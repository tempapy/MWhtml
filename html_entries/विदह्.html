• vi-√dahvidahvidah
  • P. -dahatidahatividahati, to burn up, scorch, consume or destroy by fire, RV.; MBh.;
    to cauterize (a wound), Suśr.;
    to decompose, corrupt, Car. :
    Pass. -dahyatedahyatevidahyate, to be burnt &c.;
    to burn, be inflamed (as a wound), Suśr.;
    to suffer from internal heat, ib.;
    to be consumed by grief, waste, pine, MBh.;
    to be puffed up, boast, R. (v.l. vi-katthasevikatthasevikatthase for vi-dahyasevidahyasevidahyase). [965,2]
