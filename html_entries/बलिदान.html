• balibali—dānadāna
  • n. the presentation of an offering to a deity (consisting of rice, milk, fruits &c. when presented to Viṣṇuviṣṇu, or of living victims when offered to Śivaśiva or Durgādurgā), Pur. [723,3]
  • presentation of grain &c. to all creatures, Cat. [724,1]
