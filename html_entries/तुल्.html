• tultul [Roots]
    • cl. 10. tolayati, or tul°tul (only tul°tul also fig.; Ā. MBh.)
    • to lift up, raise, Hariv.; R.; Bhaṭṭ. (fut. Pass. tolayiṣyate);
    • to determine the weight of anything by lifting it up, weigh, compare by weighing and examining, ponder, examine with distrust, MBh. &c.
    • to make equal in weight, equal, compare (with instr. e.g. nana brāhmaṇaisbrāhmaṇa tulaye bhūtambhūta anyat, ‘I do not compare any other being with Brāhmans’ BhP. v; or with an adv. terminating in -vatvatvadvadh), R.; VarBṛS. &c.
    • to counterbalance, outweigh, match, possess in the same degree, resemble, reach, Megh.; ŚārṅgP.;
    • (pf. p. tulitatulita), Bhartṛ. iii & Ragh. xiii, 75; [451,2]
