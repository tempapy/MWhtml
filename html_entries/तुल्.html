    to make equal in weight, equal, compare (with instr. e.g. na brAhmaREs tulaye BUtam anyat, ‘I do not compare any other being with Brāhmans’ BhP. v; or with an adv. terminating in -vat), R.; VarBṛS. &c.;
    to counterbalance, outweigh, match, possess in the same degree, resemble, reach, Megh.; ŚārṅgP.;
    (pf. p. tulita), Bhartṛ. iii & Ragh. xiii, 75; [451,2]
