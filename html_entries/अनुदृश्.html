• anu-√dṛśanudṛśanudṛś
  • (ind.p. -dṛśyadṛśyaanu-dṛśya, RV. x, 130, 7) to survey, behold;
    to keep in view or in mind, to foresee:
    Caus. P. -darśayatidarśayatianu-darśayati, to show, tell, teach:
    Pass. -dṛśyatedṛśyateanu-dṛśyate (also perf. Ā. -dadṛśedadṛśeanu-dadṛśe, RV. viii, 1, 34), to become or be visible. [33,2]
