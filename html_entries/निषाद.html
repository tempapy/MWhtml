• ni-°ṣādani-°ṣādani°ṣāda a m. N. of a wild non-Āryan tribe in India (described as hunters, fishermen, robbers &c.), VS.; Br.; MBh. &c. [561,3]
  • = bhilla, ‘the Bheels’ Kathās.; Mahīdh. [561,3]
  • a man of any degraded tribe, an out-caste (esp. the son of a Brāhman by a Śūdra woman), Mn. x, 8 [561,3]
  • the progenitor of the Ni-shad (said to have sprung from the thigh of Vena), MBh.; Hariv. [561,3]
  • (in music) N. of the first (more properly the last or 7th) note of the gamut, MBh. xiv, 1419 [561,3]
  • N. of a Kalpa, VāyuP. [561,3]
