• pañcapañca—cakṣuscakṣuscakṣu m.
    • ‘five-eyed’, N. of the Buddha (who was supposed to have the māṃsa-c°māṃsa, dharma-c°dharma, prajñā-c°prajñā, divya-c°divya and buddha-c°buddha i.e. the carnal eye, the eye of religion, the eye of intellect, the divine eye and the eye of Buddha), MW. (cf. Dharmas. lxvi). [576,1]
