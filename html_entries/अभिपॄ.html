• abhi-√pṝabhipṝabhipṝ
  • (Imper. 2. sg. -pṛṇīhipṛṇīhipṛṇīhi) to fill up, PārGṛ.;
    -pūryatepūryatepūryate, to become full or abundant, PārGṛ.; MBh. :
    Caus. -pūrayatipūrayatipūrayati, to make full, fill, ŚBr. &c.;
    to load with, Kathās.;
    to cover with (as with arrows), MBh. vi, 1721;
    to present with (instr.), Hariv.; Kathās.;
    (said of sorrows, &c.) to fill the heart of any one, overwhelm;
    to accomplish, R. vii, 35, 14. [65,2]
