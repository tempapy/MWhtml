• rājyā̑ṅgarājyāṅga
  • n. ‘limb of royalty’, a requisite of regal administration (variously enumerated as 7, 8, or 9, viz. the monarch, the prime minister, a friend or ally, treasure, territory, a stronghold, an army, the companies of citizens, and the Puro-hitapurohitapurohita or spiritual adviser), L. [875,2]
