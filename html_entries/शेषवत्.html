• śeṣaśeṣa—vatvatvadvadh mfn.
    • left alive, spared, MBh. [1089,1]
    • characterized by an effect or result (sometimes applied in logic to a posteriori reasoning), Nyāyas. [1089,1]
  • śeṣaśeṣa—vatvatvadvadh n.
    • an argument from effect to cause (one of three kinds of anumānaanumāna or inference, the other two being pūrva-vatpūrvavatpūrvavatvadvadh and sāmānyato-dṛṣṭasāmānyatodṛṣṭasāmānyatasdṛṣṭa). [1332,3]
