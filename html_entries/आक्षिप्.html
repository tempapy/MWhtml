• ā-√kṣipākṣipākṣip
  • -kṣipatikṣipatiākṣipati (ind.p. -kṣipyakṣipyaākṣipya) to throw down upon (loc.) or towards (dat.), MBh. &c.;
    to strike with a bolt, R. vi, 78, 5;
    to convulse, cause to tremble, Suśr.;
    to draw or take off or away, withdraw from (abl.), MBh. &c.;
    to chase or drive out of a place (abl.), disperse, MBh. iii, 539; BhP.;
    to put into (loc.), Suśr.;
    to point to, refer to, hint, indicate, Pāṇ. vi, 3, 34; Siddh.; Sāh. &c.;
    to refuse, object to (acc.), MBh. iii, 16117; Kāvyâd. &c.;
    to insult, deride, Mn. iv, 141; MBh. &c.;
    to excel so as to put to shame;
    (perf. -cikṣepacikṣepaācikṣepa) to challenge, call to a dispute &c. (dat.), Kathās. :
    Caus. (perf. -kṣepayāmkṣepayāmākṣepayām āsaāsa) to cause to throw down, MBh. iii, 15733. [128,3]
