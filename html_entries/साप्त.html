• sāptasāpta
  • n. or sāptasāpta (fr. saptansaptan, of which it is also the Vṛddhivṛddhi form in comp.) the number seven, a heptade, RV.; TS. [1204,2]
  • a team of seven horses (accord. to others m. and a proper N.), RV. viii, 55, 5. [1204,2]
  • sāptasāpta
  • n. (fr. saptisapti) a horse-race, running-match for horses or the prize given for one, RV. ii, 19, 7. [1204,2]
