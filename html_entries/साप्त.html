• 1. sāptasāpta n.
    • or sāptasāpta (fr. saptansaptan, of which it is also the Vṛddhi form in comp.) the number seven, a heptade, RV.; TS. [1204,2]
    • a team of seven horses (accord. to others m. and a proper N.), RV. viii, 55, 5. [1204,2]
  • 2. sāptasāpta n.
    • (fr. saptisaptisaptin) a horse-race, running-match for horses or the prize given for one, RV. ii, 19, 7. [1204,2]
