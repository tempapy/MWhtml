• prati-√sṛpratisṛpratisṛ
  • P. -saratisaratiprati-sarati (ind.p. -sāramsāramprati-sāram, ŚāṅkhBr.), to go against, rush upon, attack, assail (acc.), Hariv.;
    to return, go home, BhP.;
    to go round or from place to place (not only on the main road), TS. :
    Caus. -sārayatisārayatiprati-sārayati, to cause to go back, Car.;
    to put back again, restore to its place, Kālid.;
    to spread over, tip or touch with (instr.), Suśr.;
    to put asunder, sever, separate, SaddhP. :
    Pass. to void or emit per anum (?), Suśr. [672,3]
