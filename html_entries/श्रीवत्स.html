• śrīśrīśri—vatsavatsa m.
    • ‘favourite of Śrī’ N. of Viṣṇu, L. [1100,1]
    • a partic. mark or curl of hair on the breast of Viṣṇu or Kṛṣṇa (and of other divine beings; said to be white and represented in pictures by a symbol resembling a cruciform flower), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [1100,1]
    • the emblem of the tenth Jina (or Viṣṇu's mark so used), L. [1100,1]
    • a hole of a partic. form made through a wall by a housebreaker, L. [1100,1]
    • (in astron.) one of the lunar asterisms, Col. [1100,1]
    • N. of the eighth Yoga, MW. [1100,1]
    • N. of various authors (also with ācāryaācārya and śarmanśarman), Cat. [1100,1]
