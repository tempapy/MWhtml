• mādhavā̑cāryamādhavācāryamādhavaācārya m.
    • ‘the learned Mādhava’, N. of a celebrated scholar (author of the Sarva-darśana-saṃgraha, the Kālanirṇaya, the Nyāya-mālā-vistara &c. he was the brother of Sāyaṇa with whom he is by some identified), IW. 118 &c. [808,2]
