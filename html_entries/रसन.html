• 1. rasanarasana n.
    • (for 2. See p. 870, col. 3) the act of roaring or screaming or rumbling or thundering, any sound or noise, VarBṛS.; Bālar. [869,2]
    • croaking (of frogs), VarBṛS. [869,2]
  • 2. rasanarasana m.
    • (for 1. See p. 869, col. 2) phlegm or saliva (regarded as the cause to taste to the tongue), ŚārṅgS. [870,3]
  • rasanarasana n.
    • tasting, taste, flavour, savour, Yājñ.; MBh. &c. [870,3]
    • the tongue as organ of taste, Tarkas. [870,3]
    • the being sensible of (anything), perception, apprehension, sense, Sāh. [870,3]
