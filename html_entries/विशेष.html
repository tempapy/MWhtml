• vi-śeṣaviśeṣaviśeṣa a m.
    • (once in Pañcat. n.; ifc. f(āāa). ) distinction, difference between (two gen., two loc., or gen. and instr.), GṛŚrS.; MBh. &c. [990,2]
    • characteristic difference, peculiar mark, special property, speciality, peculiarity, Mn.; MBh. &c. [990,2]
    • a kind, species, individual (e.g. vṛkṣa-v°vṛkṣa, a species of tree, in comp. often also = special, peculiar, particular, different, e.g. chando-v°chandochandas, ‘a particular metre’, viśeṣa-maṇḍanaviśeṣamaṇḍanaviśeṣamaṇḍana, ‘a peculiar ornament’; argha-viśeṣāḥarghaviśeṣa, ‘different prices’), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [990,2]
    • (pl.) various objects, Megh. [990,2]
    • distinction, peculiar merit, excellence, superiority (in comp. often = excellent, superior, choice, distinguished e.g. ākṛti-v°ākṛti, ‘an excellent form’ ; cf. viśeṣa-pratipattiviśeṣapratipattiviśeṣapratipatti), Mn.; MBh. &c. [990,2]
    • (in gram.) a word which defines or limits the meaning of another word (cf. vi-śeṣakaviśeṣakaviśeṣaka and vi-śeṣaṇaviśeṣaṇaviśeṣaṇa) [990,3]
    • (in phil.) particularity, individuality, essential difference or individual essence (with the Vaiśeṣikas the 5th cate gory or Padârtha, belonging to the 9 eternal substances or Dravyas, viz. soul, time, place, ether, and the 5 atoms of earth, water, light, air, and mind, which are said to be so essentially different that one can never be the other), IW. 66 &c. [990,3]
    • (in medicine) a favourable turn or crisis of a sickness, Suśr. [990,3]
    • (in rhet.) statement of difference or distinction, individualization, variation, Kuval. (cf. viśeṣȏktiviśeṣoktiviśeṣaukti) [990,3]
    • a sectarian mark, any mark on the forehead (= tilakatilaka), L. [990,3]
    • (in geom.) the hypotenuse, Śulbas. [990,3]
    • N. of the primary elements or Mahā-bhūtas (q.v.), MaitrUp. [990,3]
    • the earth as an element, BhP. [990,3]
    • the mundane egg, ib. [990,3]
    • = virājvirāj, ib. [990,3]
