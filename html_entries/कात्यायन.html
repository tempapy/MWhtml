• kātyāyanakātyāyana m.
    • ‘descendant of Kati’ (See 2. katikati), N. of the author of several treatises on ritual, grammar, &c., Hariv. 1461 and 1768; R. ii, 67, 2; VPrāt.; Yājñ. i, 4 (he is also author of the Vārttikas or critical annotations on the aphorisms of Pāṇini, of the Yajur-veda Prātiśākhya, and of the Śrauta-sūtras, and is identified with Vara-ruci, the author of the Prākṛta-prakāśa) [270,1]
  • kātyāyanakātyāyana mf(īīi)n.
    • composed by Kātyāyana. [270,1]
