• 1. a-bhiabhiabhīabhī mfn.
    • fearless, MBh. [1315,1]
  • 2. abhiabhiabhī ind.
    • (a prefix to verbs and nouns, expressing) to, towards, into, over, upon. (As a prefix to verbs of motion) it expresses the notion or going towards, approaching, &c. (As a prefix to nouns not derived from verbs) it expresses superiority, intensity, &c. [61,1]
    • e.g. abhi-tāmraabhitāmraabhitāmra, abhi-navaabhinavaabhinava q.v. (As a separate adverb or preposition) it expresses (with acc.) to, towards, in the direction of, against [61,1]
    • into, ŚBr. and, KātyŚr. [61,1]
    • for, for the sake of [61,1]
    • on account of [61,1]
    • on, upon, with regard to, by, before, in front of [61,1]
    • over. It may even express one after the other, severally, Pāṇ. i, 4, 91 e.g. vṛkṣaṃvṛkṣa vṛkṣamvṛkṣa abhiabhiabhī, tree after tree [61,1]
