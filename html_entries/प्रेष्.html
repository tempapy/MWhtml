    Caus. prȇṣayati, to hurl, fling, cast, throw, MBh.; R.; Bhaṭṭ.;
    to turn or direct the eyes, Śak. ii, 2 (v.l. prȇrayantyā̑);
    to send forth, dismiss, dispatch, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to send into exile, banish, R.; Kathās.;
    to send word, send a message to a person (gen.), R. [712,3]
