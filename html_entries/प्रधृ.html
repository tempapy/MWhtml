• pra-√dhṛpradhṛpradhṛ
  • (only pf. Ā. -dadhredadhrepra-dadhre, with manasmanas), to set the mind upon anything (dat.), resolve, determine, MBh. :
    Caus. P. -dhārayatidhārayatipra-dhārayati, to chastise, inflict a punishment on any one (loc.; cf. daṇḍaṃ-√dhṛdaṇḍaṃdhṛdaṇḍaṃdhṛ), MBh.;
    to keep in remembrance, ib.;
    to reflect, consider, ib.; Pat.;
    (pradhārayantupradhārayantu w.r. for prapra dhārādhārā yantuyantu, ĀśvGṛ. iii, 12, 14). [681,1]
