• māsikamāsika mf(īīi)n.
    • relating to or connected with a month (See māgha-m°māghamāgha) [815,1]
    • monthly (i.e. ‘happening every month’, or ‘lasting for a month’ or ‘performed within a month’ &c.), Mn.; MBh. &c. [815,1]
    • payable in a month (as a debt), Pāṇ. iv, 3, 47 Sch. [815,1]
    • engaged for a month (as a teacher), ib. v, 1, 80 Sch. [815,1]
    • dedicated to a partic. month (as an oblation), ib. iv, 2, 34 Sch. [815,1]
  • māsikamāsika n.
    • (with or scil. śraddhaśraddha) a partic. Śrāddha or oblation to deceased ancestors performed every new moon, Mn. v, 140 &c. [815,1]
