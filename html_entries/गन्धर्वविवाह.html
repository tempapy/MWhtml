• gandharvagandharva—vivāhavivāha m.
    • ‘the form of marriage peculiar to the Gandharvas’, a marriage proceeding entirely from love without ceremonies and without consulting relatives (allowed between persons of the second or military class) [346,2]
    • cf. Mn. iii, 26. [346,2]
