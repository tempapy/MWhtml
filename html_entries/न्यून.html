• ny-ūnanyūnaninīūna mf(āāa)n.
    • (fr. nininī with ūnaūna) less, diminished, defective, deficient (opp. to ati-riktaatiriktaatirikta, adhikaadhika, pūrṇapūrṇa), destitute or deprived of (instr. or comp.), inferior to (abl.), Br.; GṛŚrS.; MBh.; Suśr. &c. [573,1]
    • (with pādaiḥpādaiḥpāda) having a defect in the feet, BhP. [573,1]
    • low, vile, base, mean, MBh.; Var.; Pur. [573,1]
  • ny-ūnanyūnaninīūna n.
    • euphem. = vulva, TS.; ŚBr. [573,1]
    • want or omission of one of the 5 members in a Nyāya argument, Nyāyas. [573,1]
