    • (accord. to some for sasvat and corresponding to Gk. ἥπας) perpetual, continual, endless, incessant, frequent, numerous, many (esp. applied to the ever-recurring dawns), RV. [1060,3]
    • all, every, RV.; AV.; TBr. [1060,3]
  • śaśvatśaśvat ind.
    • perpetually, continually, repeatedly, always, ever (śaśvatśaśvat purāpurāpura, from immemorial time; śaśvac-chaśvatśaśvat, again and again, constantly), RV. &c. &c. [1060,3]
    • at once, forthwith, directly (generally preceded or followed by haha; śaśvat-śaśvatśaśvatśaśvat, no sooner-than forthwith), ŚBr.; BhP. [1060,3]
    • it is true, certainly, indeed, Br. [1060,3]
