• virāṭavirāṭa
  • m. N. of one of the midland or northwest districts of India (perhaps Berar), VarBṛS. [983,1]
  • N. of an ancient king of a particular district in India, (the Pāṇḍavapāṇḍavas being obliged to live in concealment during the thirteenth year of their exile, journeyed to the court of this king and entered his service in various disguises), MBh.; Hariv. &c. [983,1]
  • N. of Buddhabuddha, L. [983,1]
