• yatrayatra ind.
    • (in Veda also yatrāyatra; fr. 3. yaya, correlative of tatratatra, and often used for the loc. of the relative pron.) in or to which place, where, wherein, wherever, whither, RV. &c. &c. (yatrayatra yatrayatra, ‘wherever’, ‘whithersoever’; yatrayatra tatratatra or yatrayatra tatrā̑pitatraapiapīapāpiāpīāp, ‘anywhere whatever’ or = yasmiṃsya tasminta, ‘in whatever’; yatrayatra tatratatra dinedina, ‘on any day whatever’; yatrayatra kutrakutra, with or without citcitcid or apiapiapīap, ‘everywhere’ or = yasminya kasminka, ‘in whatever’; yatrayatra kvakva caca or yatrayatra kvakva canacana, ‘wherever’, ‘in any place whatever’, ‘whithersoever’; yatrayatra kvakva caca, ‘anywhere whatever’; yatrayatra kvā̑pikvāpikvaapiapīapāpiāpīāp, ‘to any place’, ‘hither and thither’; yatrayatra vāvāvauū, ‘or elsewhere’) [841,2]
    • on which occasion, in which case, if, when, as, RV. &c. &c. (yatrayatra tatratatra, ‘on every occasion’, yatrayatra kvakva caca, ‘whenever’) [841,2]
    • in order that, RV. iii, 32, 14; ix, 29, 5 [841,2]
    • that (with Pot. after ‘to doubt, wonder &c.’), Pāṇ. iii, 3, 148 [841,2]
    • (with Pres.), Hit. i, 176 (v.l.) [841,2]
