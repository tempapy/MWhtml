• mārgmārg [Roots]
  • (properly Nom. fr. mārgamārga; cf. √mṛgmṛg) cl. 1. 10. P. (Dhātup. xxxiv, 39) mārgatimārgati, mārgayatimārgayati (ep. also Ā. mārgatemārgate; pf. mamārgamamārga Gr.; aor. amārgītamārgīt, ib.; fut., mā̑rgitāmārgitā, ib.; mārgeṣyatimārgeṣyati, R.; inf. mārgitummārgitum, R.; ind.p. mārgitvāmārgitvā, ib.),
    to seek, look for, MBh.; Hariv.; R.;
    to search through, ib.;
    to seek after, strive to attain, MBh.; Var.; BhP.;
    to endeavour to buy, Kathās.;
    to request, ask, beg, solicit anything from any one (with abl. of pers. and acc. of thing, or with two acc.), MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to ask (a girl) in marriage, Śatr.;
    (cl. 10. P. mārgayatimārgayati), to purify, adorn;
    to go (?), Dhātup. xxxii, 74. [812,2]
