• śunā-sīraśunāsīraśunāsīra
  • m. du. N. of two rural deities favourable to the growth of grain (prob. personifications of ‘share’ and ‘plough’; but identified by Yāskayāska with Vāyuvāyu and Ādityaāditya, by others with Indraindra and Vāyuvāyu or Indraindra and Sūryasūrya) [1082,3]
  • sg. (also written sun°sunāsīra) N. of Indraindra (cf. vasuṃdharā-śun°vasuṃdharāśunāsīravasuṃdharāśunāsīra), TS. &c. &c. [1082,3]
  • (pl.) a partic. class of gods (also written sun°sunāsīra), BhP. [1082,3]
