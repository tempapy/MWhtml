• 1. ud-√dhṛuddhṛuddhṛhṛ
    • P. Ā. -dharatiuddhṛ, -teuddhṛtauddhṛtiuddhṛtatāti (in many cases not to be distinguished from 2. ud-dhṛuddhṛudutdhṛhṛ below ; the impf. and pf. are the only forms clearly referable to this root), to bring out of, draw out, MBh.; R.;
    • to raise up, elevate, honour (See also 2. ud-dhṛuddhṛudutdhṛhṛ below) :
    • Desid. -didhīrṣatiuddhṛ, to wish to draw out, Caṇḍak.; Siddh. [189,1]
  • 2. ud-dhṛuddhṛudutdhṛhṛ
    • (ud-√hṛudhṛ, in some cases not to be distinguished from 1. ud-√dhṛuddhṛuddhṛhṛ) P. Ā. -dharatiuddhṛ, -teuddhṛtauddhṛtiuddhṛtatāti (p. -dharatuddhṛ, RV.; pf. 3. pl. uj-jaharusut, AV. iii, 9, 6; aor. -ahārṣamuddhṛ, AV.)
    • to take out, draw out, bring or tear out, pull out, eradicate;
    • to extricate, RV. x, 68, 4; AV. viii, 2, 15; xx, 136, 16; ĀśvGṛ.; KātyŚr.; MBh.; Śak. &c.
    • to draw, ladle up, skim, AV.; ŚBr.; Lāṭy.; R.;
    • to take away (fire, or anything from the fire), TS.; AitBr.; ŚBr.; KātyŚr. &c.
    • to raise, lift up, TS.; ĀśvGṛ.; MBh.; Mn. &c.
    • to rescue (from danger &c.), deliver, free, save, AV. viii, 2, 28; MaitrUp.; MBh.; Vikr. &c.
    • to put away or off, remove;
    • to separate, MBh.; BhP.; Suśr. &c.
    • to leave out, omit;
    • to except (See ud-dhṛtyauddhṛtyaudut);
    • to select, choose:
    • Ā. to take for one's self, AV. iii, 9, 6; TS.; AitBr.; ŚBr.; MBh.; Mn. &c.
    • to extend, elevate, raise;
    • to make strong or brisk or quick, MBh.; Suśr.; MārkP. &c.
    • to present, offer, Yājñ. i, 159; BhP. iv, 30, 47;
    • to root out, destroy, undo, MBh.; Ragh.; Prab. &c.
    • to divide (in math.) :
    • Caus. -dhārayatiuddhṛdhārayat, to raise, up-lift, MBh.;
    • to take for one's self, MBh. xiv, 1928 :
    • Desid. uj-jihīrṣatiut, to wish to draw out or to rescue, Mn. iv, 251; MBh. [189,2]
