  • svāminsvāmin
  • m. (fr. 1. svasva + minmin) an owner, proprietor, master, lord or owner of (gen. loc., or comp.), Mn.; MBh. &c. [1284,1]
  • a chief, commander (of an army), Vikr.; Subh. [1284,1]
  • a husband, lover (du. ‘husband and wife’), Āpast.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [1284,1]
  • a king, prince (in dram. used as a form of address = devadeva) [1284,1]
  • a spiritual preceptor, learned Brāhman or Pandit (used as a title at the end of names, esp. of natives of the Carnatic) [1284,1]
