• mantrmantr
    • (properly a Nom. fr. mantramantra, p. 785, col. 3), cl. 10. Ā. (Dhātup. xxxiii, 6) mantrayate (rarely P. °ti; Subj. mantrayaithe, °tetatā, Pāṇ. iii, 4, 95 Sch.; Pot. mantrayīta, MBh.; inf. mantrayitum, Pañcat.),
    • to speak, talk, say, RV. i, 164, 10;
    • to deliberate, take counsel, consult with (instr. with or without sahasaha) or about (dat.), ŚBr. &c. &c.
    • to resolve upon, determine to (inf.), MBh.;
    • to deliberate on, discuss (acc.), Mn.; MBh. &c.
    • to counsel, advise, propose any measure, give any one advice (with acc. of pers., or with gen. of pers. and acc. of thing), MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to consecrate with sacred or magical texts, enchant with spells or charms, MBh.; R. [787,1]
