• laṅghlaṅgh [Roots]
    • (cf. √raṃhraṃh and laghulaghulaghū) cl. 1. Ā. P. (Dhātup. iv, 34; v, 55) laṅghatilaṅghatī, °te (Gr. also lalaṅgha, °ghelaṅgh; aor. alaṅghīt, °ghiṣṭa; fut. laṅghitālaṅghita; °ghiṣyati, °te; inf. laṅghitum),

    • to overstep, transgress, violate, neglect, Yājñ.; Kāv.; Rājat.;

    • to frustrate, prevent, avert, R.; Mṛcch.;
    • to disregard, slight, offend, insult, injure, Mn.; MBh. &c.
    • to excel, surpass, outshine, obscure, eclipse, R.; Ragh.; Rājat.;
    • to remove, transport, Kāraṇḍ.;
