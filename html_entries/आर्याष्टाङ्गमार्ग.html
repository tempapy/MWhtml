• āryā̑ṣṭā̑ṅga-mārgaāryāṣṭāṅgamārgaāryaāryāaṣṭaaṣṭāaṅgaāṅgamārga m.
    • (Pāli ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo) ‘the holy eightfold path’ pointed out by Buddha for escape from the misery of existence: 1. right views, 2. right thoughts, 3. right words, 4. right actions, 5. right living, 6. right exertion, 7. right recollection, 8. right meditation. [152,3]
