• vi-√dhūvidhūvidhuvidhūdhu
    • P. Ā. -dhūnoti, -dhūnute (later also -dhunoti, -dhunute; inf. -dhavitum, or -dhotum),
    • to shake about, move to and fro, agitate, toss about (Ā. also ‘one's self’), RV. &c. &c.
    • to fan, kindle (fire), MBh.;
    • to shake off, drive away, scatter, disperse, remove, destroy, R.; Kathās.; BhP. &c.
    • (Ā.) to shake off from one's self, relinquish, abandon, give up, AV.; Mn.; MBh. &c.:
    • Pass. -dhūyate (ep. also °ti), to be shaken or agitated, MBh. :
    • Caus. -dhūnayati, to cause to shake about &c.
    • to shake violently, agitate, harass, annoy, MBh.; R. &c. [968,3]
