• kuku
  • a pronom. base appearing in kutaskutas, kutrakutra, kuvidkuvid, kuhakuha, kvakva, and as a prefix implying deterioration, depreciation, deficiency, want, littleness, hindrance, reproach, contempt, guilt [285,3]
  • originally perhaps kuku signified ‘how (strange!)’ [285,3]
  • as a separate word kuku occurs only in the lengthened form 3. kūkū q.v. [285,3]
  • kuku
  • f. the earth, Āryabh.; VarBṛS.; VarBṛ.; BhP. vi, 1, 42 [286,3]
  • the ground or base of a triangle or other plane figure, Comm. on Āryabh. [286,3]
  • the number ‘one.’ [286,3]
