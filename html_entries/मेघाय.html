• meghāyameghāya
  • Nom. Ā. °yatemeghāyate (= meghaṃmeghaṃ harotiharoti, Pāṇ. iii, 1, 17), to form clouds, become cloudy (only P. dat. °yatemeghāyate, °ylṣatemeghāylṣate, °ghitāyameghitāya), TS.;
    to resemble clouds, rise like clouds (°yitameghāyita n. impers.), Hcar.; Dhanaṃj. [832,1]
