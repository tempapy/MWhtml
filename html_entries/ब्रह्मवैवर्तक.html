brahmabrahma—vaivartavaivarta or brahmabrahmavaivartaka, n. ‘metamorphoses of Brahmābrahmā’ (who is identified with Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa), N. of a Purāṇapurāṇa (one of the most modern of the 18, containing prayers and invocations addressed to Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa with narratives about his loves for the Gopīgopīs and Rādhārādhā &c.), VP.; Pañcar. (IW. 514 &c.) [740,1]
