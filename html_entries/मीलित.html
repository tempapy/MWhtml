• mīlitamīlita mfn.
    • one who has closed his eyes, sleepy (only compar. °ta-taramīlitatara), ŚBr. [819,1]
    • closed, obstructed (opp. to muktamukta), PañcavBr. [819,1]
    • closed, unblown, partly opened (as eyes, blossoms &c.), Kāv.; Pur. [819,1]
    • disappeared, ceased to be, BhP. [819,1]
    • met, assembled, gathered together, Rājat. [819,1]
    • (in. rhet.) an implied simile (in which the similarity between two objects is only implied, as in the example: ‘women clothed in white are invisible in the moonlight, therefore they are as bright as moonlight’), Kpr.; Kuval. [819,1]
