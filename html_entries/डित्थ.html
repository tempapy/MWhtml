• ḍitthaḍittha ⮩ ḍitthatā ḍitthatva

(also -tātāḍitthatā f. -tvatvaḍitthatva n. ‘an act of a man of Ḍitthaḍittha's kind’), ; i, 2, 45; Kāś.; Bādar. ii, 4, 20 Sch.; Kpr.; Sāh. ii, 4/5; Tarkas. 59 [430,3]
