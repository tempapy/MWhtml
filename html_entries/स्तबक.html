    • a feather of a peacock's tail, BhP. [1257,3]
    • a tassel, Hariv. [1257,3]
    • a quantity, multitude, L. [1257,3]
    • a chapter or section (in such books as contain in their titles the words, latālatā, latikālatikā, mañjarīmañjarī &c.) [1257,3]
