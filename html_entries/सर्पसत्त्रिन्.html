• sarpasarpa—sattrinsattrin m.
    • ‘performer of a snake-sacrifice’, N. of king Janam-ejaya (a legend relates that to revenge the death of his father, who was killed by a snake-bite, he employed Mantras to compel the whole serpent-race to be present at a sacrifice, where all except a few chief snakes were destroyed), L.
