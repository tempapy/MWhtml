  • P. Ā. -taratitaratitarati, °teprate (Ved. also -tiratitiratitirati, °teprate; inf. -tiramtiramtiram),
    to go to sea, pass over, cross, ŚBr. &c. &c.;
    to set out, start, RV.; ŚBr.;
    (Ā.) to rise, thrive, prosper, RV.;
    to raise, elevate, augment, increase, further, promote, ib.; AV.; ŚBr.; MBh.;
    to extend, prolong (esp. with āyusāyus, ‘to promote long life’; Ā. ‘to live on, live longer’), RV. :
    Caus. -tārayatitārayatitārayati (aor. prā̑tītaratprātītarat), to extend, widen, MBh.;
    to prolong (life), AV.;
    to mislead, take in, deceive, Mṛcch.; Kathās.;
    to lead astray, seduce, persuade to (dat. or loc.), Ragh.; Kathās. [674,2]
