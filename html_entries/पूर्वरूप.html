• pūrvapūrvapūrvāpūru—rūparūpa n.
    • indication of something approaching, an omen, AV. [644,1]
    • something prior or antecedent to, (esp.) the symptom of occurring disease, Car.; Suśr. [644,1]
    • the first of two concurrent vowels or consonants (-tāpūrvarūpatāpūrvarūpatāta f.), TUp.; Prāt. &c. [644,1]
    • (in rhet.) a figure of speech which describes the unexpected return of anything to its former state, Kuval. [644,1]
  • pūrvapūrvapūrvāpūru—rūparūpa mf(āāa)n.
