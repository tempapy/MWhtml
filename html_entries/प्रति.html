  • against, in opposition to [661,2]
  • back, again, in return [661,2]
  • down upon, upon, on [661,2]
  • before nouns it expresses also likeness or comparison (cf. prati-candraprati-candrapraticandra) [661,2]
  • or it forms Avyayībhāvas of different kinds (cf. prati-kṣaṇamprati-kṣaṇampratikṣaṇam, prati-grahamprati-grahampratigraham, praty-agnipraty-agnipratyagni &c.; rarely ifc. e.g. sūpaprati, a little broth, Pāṇ. ii, 1, 9) [661,2]
  • or as a prep. with usually preceding acc., in the sense of towards, against, to, upon, in the direction of (e.g. śabdam p°, in the direction of the sound, R.; agnim pr°, against the fire, Mn.; also °ty-agnipraty-agnipratyagni ind., Pāṇ. vi, 2, 33 Sch.; ripum pr°, against the enemy, Mn.; ātmānam pr°, to one's self, Ratnâv.) ⮩ pratyagni [661,2]
  • opposite, before, in the presence of (e.g. rodasī pr°, before heaven and earth, RV.) [661,2]
  • in comparison, on a par with, in proportion to (e.g. indram pr°, in comparison with Indra, RV.; sahasrāṇi pr°, on a par with i.e. equivalent to thousands, ib.; also with abl. or -tasprati-tastas; cf. Pāṇ. i, 4, 92; ii, 3, 11) [661,2]
  • in the vicinity of, near, beside, at, on (e.g. yūpam pr°, near the sacrificial post, AitBr.; gaṅgām pr°, at or on the Ganges, R.; etat pr°, at this point, TS.; āyodhanam pr°, on the field of battle, MBh.) [661,2]
  • at the time of, about, through, for (e.g. phālgunam pr°, about the month Phālguna, Mn.; ciram pr°, for a long time, MBh.; bhṛśam pr°, often, repeatedly, Car.) [661,2]
  • or used distributively (cf. Pāṇ. i, 4, 90) to express at every, in or on every, severally (e.g. yajñam pr°, at every sacrifice, Yājñ.; yajñaṃ yajñam pr°, TS.; varṣam pr°, every year, annually, Pañcat.; in this sense often comp.; cf. above) [661,2]
  • in favour of, for (Pāṇ. i, 4, 90; e.g. pāṇḍavān pr°, in favour of the Pāṇḍavas, MBh.) [661,2]
  • on account of, with regard to, concerning (Pāṇ.; ib. e.g. sīmām pr°, concerning a boundary, Mn.; gautamam pr°, with regard to Gautama, R.) [661,3]
  • conformably or according to (e.g. mām pr°, according to me, i.e. in my opinion, Mālav.; cf. mām praty araṇyavat pratibhāti, ‘it seems to me like a forest’ Hit.; na bubhukṣitam prati bhāti kiṃ cit, ‘to a hungry man nothing is of any account’ Kāś. on Pāṇ. ii, 3, 2) [661,3]
