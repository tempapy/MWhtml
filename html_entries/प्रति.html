• 1. pratipratipratī ind.
    • (as a prefix to roots and their derivative nouns and other nouns, sometimes pratīpratīprati; for 2. See p. 664) towards, near to [661,2]
    • against, in opposition to [661,2]
    • back, again, in return [661,2]
    • down upon, upon, on [661,2]
    • before nouns it expresses also likeness or comparison (cf. prati-candrapraticandrapraticandra) [661,2]
    • or it forms Avyayībhāvas of different kinds (cf. prati-kṣaṇampratikṣaṇampratikṣaṇamkṣaṇa, prati-grahampratigrahapratigrahpratigrahagrah, praty-agnipratyagnipratypratipratīagni &c. rarely ifc., e.g. sūpapratisūpaprati, a little broth, Pāṇ. ii, 1, 9) [661,2]
    • or as a prep. with usually preceding acc., in the sense of towards, against, to, upon, in the direction of (e.g. śabdamśabdaśabd p°pratipratī, in the direction of the sound, R.; agnimagni pr°pratipratī, against the fire, Mn.; also °ty-agnipratyagnipratypratipratīagni ind., Pāṇ. vi, 2, 33, Sch.; ripumripu pr°pratipratī, against the enemy, Mn.; ātmānam pr°pratipratī, to one's self, Ratnāv.) [661,2]
    • opposite, before, in the presence of (e.g. rodasīrodasīrodas pr°pratipratī, before heaven and earth, RV.) [661,2]
    • in comparison, on a par with, in proportion to (e.g. indramindra pr°pratipratī, in comparison with Indra, RV.; sahasrāṇisahasra pr°pratipratī, on a par with i.e. equivalent to thousands, ib.; also with abl. or -taspratitasta; cf. Pāṇ. i, 4, 92; ii, 3, 11) [661,2]
    • in the vicinity of, near, beside, at, on (e.g. yūpamyūpa pr°pratipratī, near the sacrificial post, AitBr.; gaṅgāmgaṅgāgaṅga pr°pratipratī, at or on the Ganges, R.; etatetat pr°pratipratī, at this point, TS.; āyodhanamāyodhana pr°pratipratī, on the field of battle, MBh.) [661,2]
    • at the time of, about, through, for (e.g. phālgunamphālguna pr°pratipratī, about the month Phālguna, Mn.; ciramciramcira pr°pratipratī, for a long time, MBh.; bhṛśambhṛśambhṛśabhṛś pr°pratipratī, often, repeatedly, Car.) [661,2]
    • or used distributively (cf. Pāṇ. i, 4, 90) to express at every, in or on every, severally (e.g. yajñamyajña pr°pratipratī, at every sacrifice, Yājñ.; yajñaṃyajña yajñamyajña pr°pratipratī, TS.; varṣamvarṣa pr°pratipratī, every year, annually, Pañcat.; in this sense often comp.; cf. above) [661,2]
    • in favour of, for (Pāṇ. i, 4, 90; e.g. pāṇḍavānpāṇḍava pr°pratipratī, in favour of the Pāṇḍavas, MBh.) [661,2]
    • on account of, with regard to, concerning (Pāṇ.; ib., e.g. sīmāmsīmāsīma pr°pratipratī, concerning a boundary, Mn.; gautamamgautama pr°pratipratī, with regard to Gautama, R.) [661,3]
    • conformably or according to (e.g. māmmāmmāma pr°pratipratī, according to me, i.e. in my opinion, Mālav.; cf. māmmāmmāma pratypraty araṇyavataraṇyavat pratibhāti, ‘it seems to me like a forest’, Hit.; nana bubhukṣitambubhukṣita pratipratipratī bhātibhāti kiṃkiṃ citcitcid, ‘to a hungry man nothing is of any account’, Kāś. on Pāṇ. ii, 3, 2) [661,3]
    • as, for (after a verb meaning, ‘to regard or consider’; cf. Vikr. iv, 69) [661,3]
    • or as prep. with abl. in return or as compensation for, instead or in the place of (Pāṇ. i, 4, 92, Sch.) [661,3]
    • with abl. or -taspratitasta See above [661,3]
    • with abl. or gen. (?) to express, ‘about’, ‘at the time of’ (only pratipratipratī vastoḥ, ‘at daybreak’, RV.) [661,3]
    • as prep. with gen. = with reference to, Hariv. 10967. [661,3]
