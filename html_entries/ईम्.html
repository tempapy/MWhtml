• īmīmī ind.
    • (fr. pronominal base 3. iiīin), Ved. a particle of affirmation and restriction (generally after short words at the beginning of a period, or after the relative pronouns, the conjunction yadyad, prepositions and particles such as ātāta, utauta, athaatha, &c.) īmīmī has also the sense ‘now’ (= idānīmidānīm), and is by, Sāy. sometimes considered as an acc. case for enamena, RV.; VS.
