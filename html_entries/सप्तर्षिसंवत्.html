• saptarṣi-saṃvatsaptarṣisaṃvatsaptarṣisaṃvat
    • a year of the Saptarṣi era (the 1st year of which corresponds to the expired year 26, i.e. the 27th current year of the Kali-yuga; the hundreds of this era are often omitted, e.g. the Saptarṣi year 4869 will be called simply 69; the corresponding year of one of the centuries, A.D. is found by adding 24-25; thus 69 [for 4869] corresponds to, A.D. 1793-94). [1332,3]
