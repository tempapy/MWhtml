    • P. Ā. -jānātivijñā, -jānītevijñā, to distinguish, discern, observe, investigate, recognize, ascertain, know, understand, RV. &c. &c. (with nana and inf. ‘to know not how to’);

    • (esp. 3. sg., ‘it is known or understood’, ŚBr.; ‘it is recognized or prescribed’, scil. by authorities, GṛŚrS.; and in gram. māmāmamāsmas vi-jñāyivi with preceding nom., ‘let this not be considered as’) :

    • to ask or request anything, Hariv.;
    • to declare or tell that (two acc.), R.;
    • to apprise, teach, instruct, ask, beg (with acc. of pers.; and dat. of thing, or with arthamarthamarthaarth ifc., or pratipratipratī and acc.), ŚBr. &c. &c.
