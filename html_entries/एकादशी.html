• ekādaśīekādaśī
  • f. the eleventh day of a fortnight (on which fasting is considered an indispensable observance and very efficacious), MBh.; Kathās. &c. [230,1]
  • presentation of offerings to Pitṛpitṛs or deceased ancestors on the eleventh day after their death (on which occasion Brāhmans are fed, and the period of impurity for a Brāhman terminates) [230,1]
