• abhy-ud-dhṛabhyuddhṛabhyuddhṛ
  • (√hṛhṛ), Ved. to take out (especially one fire in order to add it to another), TS.; ŚBr. &c.;
    to take or draw out, draw (as water), MBh. &c.;
    to take up, lift up, ŚāṅkhŚr.; MBh. xii, 12322;
    to re-obtain, Yājñ. ii, 119;
    to elevate, render prosperous, MBh.; Sāh. &c.:
    Caus. (ind.p. -uddhāryauddhāryaabhyududdhārya) to raise, lift up, MBh. iii, 13326. [78,3]
