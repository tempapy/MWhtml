• bhekabheka m.
    • (probably onomat.; but cf. Uṇ. iii, 43) a frog, Up.; Kāv. &c. (accord. to Kathās. xx, 77 the croaking of frogs was caused by the curse of Agni who was betrayed by them to the gods when he took refuge in the water) [766,1]
    • a cloud, L. [766,1]
    • a timid man (= bhīrubhīru), L. (cf. bhelabhela) [766,1]
    • N. of a Niṣāda and a Brāhmaṇī, L. [766,1]
