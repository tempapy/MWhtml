• kuṣṭhakuṣṭha mn.
    • (fr. 1. kukukū + sthastha, Pāṇ. viii, 3, 97) the plant Costus speciosus or arabicus (used as a remedy for the disease called takmantakman), AV.; Kauś. 35; R. ii, 94, 23; Suśr. [297,3]
    • the plant Saussurea auriculata [297,3]
  • kuṣṭhakuṣṭha m.
    • (= kakundarakakundara) cavity of the loin [Comm.; but perhaps = kuṣṭhikākuṣṭhikā] VS. xxv, 6 [297,3]
  • kuṣṭhakuṣṭha n.
    • leprosy (of which eighteen varieties are enumerated, i.e. seven severe and eleven less so), Suśr.; Bhartṛ. i, 89; Kathās. [297,3]
    • a sort of poison, L. [297,3]
