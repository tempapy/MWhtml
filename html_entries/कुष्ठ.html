• kuṣṭha mn. (fr. 1. ku + stha, Pāṇ. viii, 3, 97) the plant Costus speciosus or arabicus (used as a remedy for the disease called takman), AV.; Kauś. 35; R. ii, 94, 23; Suśr. [297,3]
  • the plant Saussurea auriculata [297,3]
  • kuṣṭha m. (= kakundara) cavity of the loin [Comm.; but perhaps = kuṣṭhikā] VS. xxv, 6 [297,3]
  • kuṣṭha n. leprosy (of which eighteen varieties are enumerated, i.e. seven severe and eleven less so), Suśr.; Bhartṛ. i, 89; Kathās. [297,3]
  • a sort of poison, L. [297,3]
