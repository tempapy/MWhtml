• kaniṣkakaniṣka
  • m. N. of a celebrated king of Northern India (whose reign began in the first century of our era and who, next to Aśokaaśoka, was the greatest supporter of Buddhism; his empire seems to have comprised Afghānistān, the Panjāb, Yarkand, Kaṣmīr, Ladak, Agraagra, Rājputāna, Gujarāt, and Sindh), Rājat.
