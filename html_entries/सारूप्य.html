• sārūpyasārūpya n.
    • (fr. sa-rūpasarūpasasārūpa) sameness or similarity of form, identity of appearance, resemblance, likeness, conformity with (gen.), Mn.; MBh. &c. [1209,2]
    • assimilation to or conformity with the deity (one of the grades of Mukti or beatitude = sarūpa-tāsarūpatāsarūpatāta, sālokyasālokya), BhP. [1209,2]
    • (in dram.) a mistake caused by the mutual resemblance of two persons (as in Veṇīs. vs Yudhi-ṣṭhira takes Bhīma for Duryodhana and injures him), Bhar.; Sāh. [1209,2]
  • sārūpyasārūpya mf(āāa)n.
    • seasonable, fit, proper, suitable, Lalit. [1209,2]
