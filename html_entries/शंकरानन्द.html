• śaṃkarā̑nandaśaṃkarānanda
  • m. N. of a philosopher (son of Vāñchêśavāñcheśa and Veṅkaṭâmbāveṅkaṭāmbā, pupil of Ānandâtmanānandātman and guru of Sāyaṇasāyaṇa; author of the Ātma-purāṇaātmapurāṇaātmapurāṇa or Upaniṣad-ratnaupaniṣadratnaupaniṣadratna, containing the substance of a number of Upaniṣadupaniṣads in verse; and of many Commentaries on Upaniṣadupaniṣads and similar works.) [1055,1]
