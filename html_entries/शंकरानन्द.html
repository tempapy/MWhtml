• śaṃkarā̑nanda m. N. of a philosopher (son of Vāñchêśa and Veṅkaṭâmbā, pupil of Ānandâtman and guru of Sāyaṇa; author of the Ātma-purāṇaātma-purāṇaātmapurāṇa or Upaniṣad-ratnaupaniṣad-ratnaupaniṣadratna, containing the substance of a number of Upaniṣads in verse; and of many Commentaries on Upaniṣads and similar works.) [1055,1]
