• śaṃkarā̑nandaśaṃkarānandaśaṃkaraśaṃkarāanandaānanda m.
    • N. of a philosopher (son of Vāñcheśa and Veṅkaṭāmbā, pupil of Ānandātman and guru of Sāyaṇa; author of the Ātma-purāṇa or Upaniṣad-ratna, containing the substance of a number of Upaniṣads in verse; and of many Commentaries on Upaniṣads and similar works.) [1055,1]
