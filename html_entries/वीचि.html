• vīcivīci
  • f. (prob. fr. vivi + 2. añcañc) going or leading aside or astray, aberration, deceit, seduction, RV. x, 10, 6 [1004,3]
  • also m. (L.) and f(īī). (ifc. °civīci, or °cikavīcika) a wave, ripple, Kāv.; VarBṛS. &c. [1004,3]
  • ‘wave’ as N. of a subdivision of a chapter (called pravāhapravāha, ‘river’), Sadukt. [1004,3]
  • (prob. for a-vīciavīciavīci) a partic. hell, R. (L. also = sukhasukha, avakāśaavakāśa, svalpasvalpa, alpaalpa, āliāli, kiraṇakiraṇa). [1004,3]
