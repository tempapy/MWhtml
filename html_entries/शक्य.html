Vām. v, 2, 25), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [1045,1]
  • to be conquered or subdued, liable to be compelled to (inf.), MBh. [1045,1]
  • explicit, direct, literal (as the meaning of a word or sentence, opp. to lakṣya and vyaṅgya), Alaṃkāraś. [1045,1]
