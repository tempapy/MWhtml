• baṭubaṭu m.
    • (also written vaṭuvaṭu) a boy, lad, stripling, youth (esp. a young Brāhman, but also contemptuously applied to adult persons), MBh.; Kāv. &c. [719,2]
    • N. of a class of priests, Cat. [719,2]
    • a form of Śiva (so called from being represented by boys in the rites of the Śāktas), ib. [719,2]
    • Calosanthes Indica, L. [719,2]
