• sūcsūc
    • (rather Nom. fr. sūcasūca and sūcisūcisūcīsūcinsūc below) cl. 10. P. (Dhātup. xxxv, 21) sūcayati, to point out, indicate, show, manifest, reveal, betray (in dram. = ‘to indicate by gesture, communicate by signs, represent’), MaitrUp.; MBh.; Kāv. &c.
    • to trace out, ascertain, espy, MW. :
    • Pass. sūcyate (aor. asūci), to be pointed out or indicated, Kāv.; Kathās. &c.:
    • Intens. sosūcyate, Pat. on Pāṇ. iii, 1, 22. [1241,1]
