• vināvināvi
    • or vināvināvi ind. (prob. a kind of instr. of 3. vivivī) without, except, short or exclusive of (preceded or followed by an acc. instr., rarely abl.; cf. Pāṇ. ii, 3, 32; exceptionally ifc., e.g. śuci-vināśucivināvi, without honesty, satya-v°satyavināvi, without faith, Subh.), AV. xx, 136, 13 (not in manuscript), Mn.; MBh. &c. (sometimes vināvināvi is used pleonastically, e.g. nana tadtad astiasti vināvināvi devadeva yatyat tetatāti virahitaṃ hareharahari, ‘there is nothing, O god Hari, that is without thee’ Hariv. 14966).
