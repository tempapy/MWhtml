• balabala—rāmarāma
  • m. N. of the elder brother of Kṛṣṇakṛṣṇa and third of the Rāmarāmas (regarded as the 8th Avatāraavatāra of Viṣṇuviṣṇu, sometimes as an incarnation of the great serpent Śeṣaśeṣa or An-antaanantaananta; he is also called Balabala, Bala-devabaladevabaladeva, Bala-bhadrabalabhadrabalabhadra, and Halâyudhahalāyudha, cf. IW. 332 &c.), MBh.; Pur. [723,1]
