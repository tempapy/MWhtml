• bala—rāma m. N. of the elder brother of Kṛṣṇa and third of the Rāmas (regarded as the 8th Avatāra of Viṣṇu, sometimes as an incarnation of the great serpent Śeṣa or An-antaan-antaananta; he is also called Bala, Bala-devabala-devabaladeva, Bala-bhadrabala-bhadrabalabhadra, and Halâyudha, cf. IW. 332 &c.), MBh.; Pur. [723,1]
