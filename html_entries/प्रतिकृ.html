• prati-√kṛpratikṛpratikṛ
    • P. Ā. -karotipratikṛ, -kurutepratikṛkurutakurutā, (inf. pratī-kartumpratīkartu, BhP.), to do or make an opposition, AitBr.;
    • to return, repay, requite (good or evil [acc.], with gen. dat. or loc. of pers.), MBh.; R. &c.
    • to counteract, resist (acc. or gen.), ib.;
    • to treat, attend to, cure (a disease), Suśr.;
    • to repair, mend, restore, Mn.;
    • to pay back (a debt), Gaut. :
    • Caus. Ā. -kārayatepratikṛkārayat, to cause to be repeated, ŚBr. :
    • Desid. -cikīrṣatipratikṛ, to wish to take revenge on (acc. or loc.) for (acc.), MBh.; R. [664,3]
