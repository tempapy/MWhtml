• tāḍana mfn. beating, striking, hitting, hurting, R. G. i, 30, 17; BhP. viii, 11, 9 [441,3]
  • tāḍana n. striking, beating, thumping, whipping, chastising, hammering (of gold &c.), Yājñ. i, 155; MBh. &c. (often ifc. with the instrument, once [Pañcat.] with the object) [441,3]
  • (in astron.) touching, partial eclipse, VarBṛS. xxiv, 34 [441,3]
  • a kind of solemn act (performed with Kuṇḍas, Śārad. v, 3; or with Mantras, Sarvad.) [441,3]
