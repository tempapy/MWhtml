• satyam ind. (g. cā̑di and svar-ādisvar-ādisvarādi) truly, indeed, certainly, verily, necessarily, yes, very well (satyam-tusatyam-tusatyamtu, kiṃ tu, tathā̑pi, ‘it is true - but, yet, however’; yat satyam, ‘indeed, certainly’), RV. &c. &c. [cf. accord. to some, Gk. ἐτεός.] [1135,3]
