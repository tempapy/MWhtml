• satyamsatyam
  • ind. (g. cādicādi and svar-ādisvarādisvarādi) truly, indeed, certainly, verily, necessarily, yes, very well (satyam-tusatyamtusatyamtu, kiṃkiṃ tutu, tathāpitathāpi, ‘it is true - but, yet, however’; yatyat satyamsatyam, ‘indeed, certainly’), RV. &c. &c. [cf. accord. to some, Gk. ἐτεός.] [1135,3]
