• kṣaipra mfn. (fr. kṣipra), ‘produced by speaking quickly’, a term for a kind of Sandhi produced by changing the first of two concurrent vowels to its semivowel, RPrāt. [333,2]
  • the Svarita accent on a syllable formed with that Sandhi, RPrāt.; VPrāt.; APrāt. [333,2]
  • kṣaipra n. quickness, speediness g. pṛthv-ādipṛthv-ādipṛthvādi. [333,2]
