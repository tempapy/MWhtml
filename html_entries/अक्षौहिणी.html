• akṣauhiṇīakṣauhiṇī f.
    • an army consisting of ten anīkinīs, or 21870 elephants, 21870 chariots, 65610 horse, and 109350 foot. (Since an Anīkinī consists of 27 vāhinīs, and 27 is the cube of 3, akṣauhiṇīakṣauhiṇī may be a compound of 2. akṣaakṣa and vāhinīvāhinīvāhin; or it may possibly be connected with 1. akṣaakṣa, axle, car.)
