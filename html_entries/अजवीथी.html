• ajaaja—vīthīvīthīvīthi f.
    • ‘goat's road’, N. of one of the three divisions of the southern path, or one of the three paths in which the sun, moon, and planets move, comprehending the asterisms mūlamūla, pūrvā̑ṣāḍhapūrvapūrvāaṣāḍhaāṣāḍha, and uttarā̑ṣāḍhauttarauttarāaṣāḍhaāṣāḍha. [9,3]
