• karmakarma—kartṛkartṛ m.
    • (in Gr.), ‘an object-agent’ or ‘object-containing agent’ i.e. an agent which is at the same time the object of an action (this is the idea expressed by the reflexive passive, as in odanaḥodana pacyate, ‘the mashed grain cooks of itself’), Pāṇ. iii, 1, 62 (cf. Gr. 461, iii) [258,3]
    • m. du. (ārauāraāru) the work and the person accomplishing it. [258,3]
