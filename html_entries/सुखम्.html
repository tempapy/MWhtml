• sukhamsukham
  • ind. (also enaena, ātāt) easily, comfortably, pleasantly, joyfully, willingly (with inf. = ‘easy to’ e.g. sasa bhaviṣyatibhaviṣyati sukhaṃsukhaṃ hantumhantum, ‘he will be easy to kill’; sukham-nasukhamnasukhamna punarpunar ‘, rather - than’ e.g. sukhamsukham asūnasūn apiapi saṃtyajantisaṃtyajanti nana punaḥpunaḥ pratijñāmpratijñām, ‘they rather renounce life than a promise’; kadalī-sukhamkadalīsukhamkadalīsukham, ‘as easily as a Kadalīkadalī’), VS.; &c.
