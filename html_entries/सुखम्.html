• sukhamsukhamsukhasukh ind.
    • (also enaenaa, ātāta) easily, comfortably, pleasantly, joyfully, willingly (with inf. = ‘easy to’ e.g. sasa bhaviṣyatibhaviṣyat sukhaṃ hantumhantu, ‘he will be easy to kill’; sukham-nasukhamna punarpunar ‘, rather - than’ e.g. sukhamsukhamsukhasukh asūnasu apiapiapīap saṃtyajanti nana punaḥpunaḥ pratijñāmpratijñāpratijña, ‘they rather renounce life than a promise’; kadalī-sukhamkadalīsukhamkadalīsukhamsukhasukh, ‘as easily as a Kadalī’), VS.; &c. [1221,1]
