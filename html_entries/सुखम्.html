• sukham ind. (also ena, āt) easily, comfortably, pleasantly, joyfully, willingly (with inf. = ‘easy to’ e.g. sa bhaviṣyati sukhaṃ hantum, ‘he will be easy to kill’; sukham-nasukham-nasukhamna punar ‘, rather - than’ e.g. sukham asūn api saṃtyajanti na punaḥ pratijñām, ‘they rather renounce life than a promise’; kadalī-sukhamkadalī-sukhamkadalīsukham, ‘as easily as a Kadalī’), VS.; &c. [1221,1]
