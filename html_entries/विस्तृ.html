• vi-√stṛvistṛvistṛ
  • (or stṝstṝ) P. Ā. -stṛṇotistṛṇotivi-stṛṇoti, -stṛṇutestṛṇutevi-stṛṇute;
    -stṛṇātistṛṇātivi-stṛṇāti, -stṛṇītestṛṇītevi-stṛṇīte (ep. also -staratistarativi-starati; ind.p. -stīryastīryavi-stīrya, or -stṛtyastṛtyavi-stṛtya),
    to spread out, scatter, strew (sacrificial grass), R.;
    to expand, extend (wings), R.;
    to spread abroad, diffuse, divulge, BhP.;
    to enlarge or expatiate upon, speak diffusely about (acc.), MBh.; Kāv. (vi-stīryavistīryavistīrya ind. copiously, at large);
    (with vacanamvacanam) to exchange words, converse with (instr.), ib. :
    Pass. -stīryatestīryatevi-stīryate or -staryatestaryatevi-staryate (fut. -stariṣyatestariṣyatevi-stariṣyate), to be spread abroad or widely diffused, Mn.; Pañcat.; BhP.;
    to be explained, Divyâv. :
    Caus. -stārayatistārayativi-stārayati, to spread, extend, diffuse, display, Mn.; MBh. &c. (-stāryastāryavi-stārya, fully, copiously, at length);
    to discuss in detail, Kull.;
    to expose (wares for sale), Daś. [1001,3]
