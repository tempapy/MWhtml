• sāhasrasāhasra mf(īīi, or āāa)n.
    • (fr. sahasrasahasra) relating or belonging to a thousand, consisting of or bought with or paid for a thousand, thousand fold, exceedingly numerous, infinite, VS. &c. &c. [1212,2]
  • sāhasrasāhasra m.
    • an army or detachment consisting of a thousand men, W. [1212,2]
    • (pl.) N. of four Ekâhas at which a thousand (cows) are given as a fee, ŚrS. [1212,2]
  • sāhasrasāhasra n.
    • (ifc. f(āāa). ) an aggregate of a thousand or of many thousand, TBr.; MBh. &c. [1212,2]
