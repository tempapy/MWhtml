• hikkhikk [Roots]
    • cl. 1. Ā. P. (Dhātup. xxi, 1) hikkati, °tetatā (Gr. also pf. jihikka, °kke &c.), to hiccup (hiccough), sob, make a spasmodic sound in the throat, Pat.; VarBṛS.; Suśr. :
    • Caus. P. hikkayati (aor. ajihikkat), to cause to hiccup, Suśr.;
    • (Ā.) hikkayate, to injure, kill, Dhātup. xxxiii, 12 (v.l. hikkhikk for kiṣkkiṣk). [1298,1]
