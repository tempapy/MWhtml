• hikkhikk [Roots]

    Caus. P. hikkayatihikkayati (aor. ajihikkatajihikkat), to cause to hiccup, Suśr.;
    (Ā.) hikkayatehikkayate, to injure, kill, Dhātup. xxxiii, 12 (v.l. hikkhikk for kiṣkkiṣk). [1298,1]
