• kurukuru—kṣetrakṣetra a n.
    • ‘the field of the Kurus’, N. of an extensive plain near Delhi (the scene of the great battles between the Kurus and Pāṇḍus), AitBr.; ŚBr. &c. [294,1]
  • kurukuru—kṣetrakṣetra m. pl.
    • the inhabitants of that country (renowned for their bravery), Mn. vii, 193. (w.r. for kaurukṣ°) [294,1]
