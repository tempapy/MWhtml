• bhagīrathabhagīratha m.
    • (prob. fr. bhaginbhagin + ratharatha, ‘having a glorious chariot’), N. of an ancient king (son of Dilīpa and great-grandfather of Sagara, king of Ayodhyā ; he brought down the sacred Gaṅgā from heaven to earth and then conducted this river to the ocean in order to purify the ashes of his ancestors, the 60,000 sons of Sagara; cf. IW. 322), MBh.; R.; Pur. &c. [744,1]
    • N. of sev. authors (also with ṭhakkuraṭhakkura and meghamegha; cf. bhaginbhagin), Cat. [744,2]
    • of an architect of recent date, Inscr. [744,2]
    • of a mountain, Śatr. [744,2]
