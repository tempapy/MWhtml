• kuqava
  • mn. a measure of grain or of wood or of iron &c. (4th part of a Prasthaprastha, described by some as a vessel four fingers wide and as many deep and containing 12 Prakṛtiprakṛtis or handfuls; also said to contain 13 1/2 cubic Aṅgulaaṅgulas, or to contain 64 cubic Aṅgulaaṅgulas [ŚārṅgS.], or to be a finger and a half deep and three fingers each in length and breadth; in medicine it is equal to two Prakṛtiprakṛtis or thirty-two Tolakatolakas), MBh.; Jyot.; VarBṛS. &c.
