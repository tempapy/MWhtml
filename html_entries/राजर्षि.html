• rājarāja—rṣirṣi
  • m. (for -riṣiriṣirāja—riṣi) a royal Ṛṣiṛṣi or saint, Ṛṣiṛṣi of royal descent, that holy and superhuman personage which a king or man of the military class may become by the performance of great austerities (e.g. Purū-ravaspurūravaspurūravas, Viśvā-mitraviśvāmitraviśvāmitra &c. cf. devarṣidevarṣi and brahma-rṣibrahmarṣibrahmarṣi), ŚrS.; Mn.; Mbh. &c. [874,1]
  • N. of a son of Kalyāṇakalyāṇa and of various authors, Cat. [874,1]
