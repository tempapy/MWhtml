• rājarājarāj—rṣiṛṣiṛṣ m.
    • (for -riṣirājarṣiriṣiriṣ) a royal Ṛṣi or saint, Ṛṣi of royal descent, that holy and superhuman personage which a king or man of the military class may become by the performance of great austerities (e.g. Purū-ravas, Viśvā-mitra &c. cf. devarṣidevarṣi and brahma-rṣibrahmarṣibrahmaṛṣiṛṣ), ŚrS.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [874,1]
    • N. of a son of Kalyāṇa and of various authors, Cat. [874,1]
