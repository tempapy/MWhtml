• rāja—rṣi m. (for -riṣirājarṣi-riṣiriṣi) a royal Ṛṣi or saint, Ṛṣi of royal descent, that holy and superhuman personage which a king or man of the military class may become by the performance of great austerities (e.g. Purū-ravaspurū-ravaspurūravas, Viśvā-mitraviśvā-mitraviśvāmitra &c. cf. devarṣi and brahma-rṣibrahma-rṣibrahmarṣi), ŚrS.; Mn.; Mbh. &c. [874,1]
  • N. of a son of Kalyāṇa and of various authors, Cat. [874,1]
