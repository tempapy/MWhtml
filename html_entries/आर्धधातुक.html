• ārdhadhātuka mf(ā)n. (fr. ardha-dhātuardha-dhātuardhadhātu), ‘applicable to the shorter form of the verbal base’, a technical N. given to the terminations of the pf. and bened. and to any Pratyaya (q.v.) except the personal terminations of the conjugational tenses in P. and Ā., and except the Pratyayas which have the Anubandha ś, Pāṇ. iii, 4, 114-117; ii, 4, 36, &c.
