• vardhāpana n. (for 2. See col.2) the act of cutting the umbilical cord, (or) the ceremony in commemoration of that act, Vet.; Pur. [926,1]
  • (prob.) = vardhāpaka, presents distributed at the ceremony of cutting the umbilical cord, L. [926,1]
  • vardhāpana n. (for 1. See col.1; possibly 1. and 2. ought not to be separated) a birthday ceremony, festival on a birthday or any festive occasion, L. [926,2]
