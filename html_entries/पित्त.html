• pittapitta n.
    • (etym. unknown) bile, the bilious humour (one of the three humours [cf. kaphakapha and vāyuvāyu] or that secreted between the stomach and bowels and flowing through the liver and permeating spleen, heart, eyes, and skin; its chief quality is heat), AV.; &c. [627,1]
