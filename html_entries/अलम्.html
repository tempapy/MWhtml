• alam ind. (later form of aram q.v.), enough, sufficient, adequate, equal to, competent, able. (alam may govern a dat. [jīvitavai (Ved. [alaṃ haniṣyati, he will be able to kill, Pāṇ. iii, 3, 154 Sch.] or with an ind. [Pāṇ. iii, 4, 18; alaṃ bhuktvā, enough of eating i.e. do not eat more, alaṃ vicārya, enough of consideration].) [94,2]
