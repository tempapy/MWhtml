    • any pre-eminently strong or noble person, ‘a tiger among men’ (cf. ṛṣabhaṛṣabha, siṃhasiṃha) [1036,2]
    • Pongamia Glabra, L. [1036,2]
    • a red variety of the castor-oil plant, L. [1036,2]
    • N. of a Rākṣasa, VP. [1036,2]
    • of a king, Rājat. [1036,2]
