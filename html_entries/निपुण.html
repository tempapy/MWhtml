• nipuṇanipuṇa mf(āāa)n.
    • (said to be from a √puṇpuṇ), clever, adroit, skilful, sharp, acute, Mn.; MBh.; Suśr.; Kāv. &c. [550,1]
    • skilled in, conversant with, capable of (mostly comp. [cf. Pāṇ. ii, 1, 31 and g. śauṇḍā̑diśauṇḍaśauṇḍāadādiādin]; but also infin. [Kāv.] loc. [Var.] gen. [Vop. v, 29] or instr. [Pāṇ. ii, 1, 31]) [550,1]
    • kind or friendly towards (loc. or pratiprati, Pāṇ. ii, 3, 43) [550,1]
    • delicate, tender, Jātakam. [550,1]
    • perfect, complete, absolute (as purity, devotion &c.), Mn.; BhP. [550,1]
