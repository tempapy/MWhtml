• mārgamārga—śīrṣaśīrṣa mf(īīiin)n.
    • born under the constellation Mṛga-śiras, Pāṇ. iv, 3, 37 Sch. [812,3]
  • mārgamārga—śīrṣaśīrṣa m.
    • (also with māsamāsa) N. of the month in which the full moon enters the constellation Mṛga-śiras, the 10th or (in later times) the 1st month in the year = November-December, Kauś.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [812,3]
  • mārgamārga—śīrṣaśīrṣa f(īīiin or āāa).
    • (with or without paurṇamāsīpaurṇamāsī) the day on which the full moon enters the constellation Mṛga-śiras, the 15th day of the first half of the month Mārgaśīrṣa, GṛS.; MBh. [812,3]
