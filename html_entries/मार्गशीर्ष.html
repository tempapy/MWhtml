  • mārga—śīrṣa m. (also with māsa) N. of the month in which the full moon enters the constellation Mṛga-śirasmṛga-śirasmṛgaśiras, the 10th or (in later times) the 1st month in the year = November-December, Kauś.; Mn.; MBh. &c. [812,3]
  • mārga—śīrṣa f(ī or ā). (with or without paurṇamāsī) the day on which the full moon enters the constellation Mṛga-śirasmṛga-śirasmṛgaśiras, the 15th day of the first half of the month Mārgaśīrṣa, GṛS.; MBh. [812,3]
