• bhāgavatabhāgavata—purāṇapurāṇa n.
    • N. of the most celebrated and popular of the 18 Purāṇas (especially dedicated to the glorification of Viṣṇu-Kṛṣṇa, whose history is in the 10th book; and narrated by Śuka, son of Vyāsa, to king Parī-kṣit, grandson of Arjuna), IW. 331; 315 &c. [751,3]
