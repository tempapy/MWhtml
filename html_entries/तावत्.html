(yāvatāyāvatā kṣaṇenakṣaṇena tāvatātāvatā, ‘after so long time, in that time’, as soon as, Rājat. v, 110) [445,3]
  • just a little, Kir. ii, 48 [445,3]
  • (in alg.) an unknown quantity (also with yāvatyāvat) [445,3]
  • tāvattāvat
  • ind. (correlative of yāvatyāvat) so much, so greatly, to such an extent, in such a number, so far, RV.; AV. &c. (tāvat-tāvattāvattāvattāvattāvat, ŚBr. i, 8, 1, 6) [445,3]
  • so long, in that time, RV. x, 88, 19; ŚBr. i; Mn. &c. [445,3]
  • meanwhile, in the mean time (the correlative yāvatyāvat being often connected with a neg. e.g. tāvactāvac chobhatechobhate mūrkhomūrkho yāvatyāvat kiṃ-cinkiṃcinkiṃcin nana bhāṣatebhāṣate, ‘so long a fool shines as long as he says nothing’ Hit.; śocayiṣyāmyśocayiṣyāmy ātmānaṃātmānaṃ tāvadtāvad yāvanyāvan meme prāptamprāptam brāhmaṇyambrāhmaṇyam, ‘so long I will emaciate myself, as long as [i.e. until] I have obtained the state of a Brāhman’ R. i, 64, 19), ŚBr. xiv, 4, 2, 30; ChUp. vi, 14, 2; Mn.; MBh. &c. (also correlative of purāpurā [R. i, 28, 21], of yāvatāyāvatā nana, of yāvatyāvat preceded by purāpurā [MBh. xiii, 4556], or without any correlative [2727; Kathās.; Hit.]) [445,3]
  • at once, now, just, first (followed by anantaramanantaram [Hit.], aparamaparam [Pañcat.], apiapi [ib.], idānīmidānīm [Hit.], utauta [Śak.], caca [Daś.; Prab.], tatastatas [Mn.; iv, 174; Ragh. vii, 4f.], tad-anutadanutadanu [Megh.], tutu [Daś. vii; Vedântas.], paścātpaścāt [R. ii], punarpunar [Pañcat.], vāvā; very often connected with an Impv., rarely [MBh. iv, 888; R. ii, 56, 13] with a Pot., often with the 1st person of pr. or fut. [445,3]
