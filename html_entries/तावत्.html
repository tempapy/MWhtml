• tāvattāvattāvad mf(atīatīatiat)n.
    • (fr. 2. tata, Pāṇ. v, 2, 39; vi, 3, 91) so great, so large, so much, so far, so long, so many (correlative of yāvatyāvatyāvad; rarely of yaya or yathȏktayathoktayathayathāukta, Nal. &c.), RV. &c. (yāvatāyāvatāyāvat kṣaṇenakṣaṇenakṣaṇa tāvatātāvatātāvat, ‘after so long time, in that time’, as soon as, Rājat. v, 110) [445,3]
    • just a little, Kir. ii, 48 [445,3]
    • (in alg.) an unknown quantity (also with yāvatyāvatyāvad) [445,3]
  • tāvattāvattāvad ind.
    • (correlative of yāvatyāvatyāvad) so much, so greatly, to such an extent, in such a number, so far, RV.; AV. &c. (tāvat-tāvattāvattāvattāvad, ŚBr. i, 8, 1, 6) [445,3]
    • so long, in that time, RV. x, 88, 19; ŚBr. i; Mn. &c. [445,3]
    • meanwhile, in the mean time (the correlative yāvatyāvatyāvad being often connected with a neg. e.g. tāvactāvactāvat chobhate mūrkhomūrkha yāvatyāvat kiṃ-cinkiṃkimcincit nana bhāṣate, ‘so long a fool shines as long as he says nothing’ Hit.; śocayiṣyāmy ātmānaṃ tāvadtāvadtāvat yāvanyāvanyāvat mememamā prā̑ptamprāptapraprāāpta brāhmaṇyambrāhmaṇya, ‘so long I will emaciate myself, as long as [i.e. until] I have obtained the state of a Brāhman’ R. i, 64, 19), ŚBr. xiv, 4, 2, 30; ChUp. vi, 14, 2; Mn.; MBh. &c. (also correlative of purāpurāpura [R. i, 28, 21], of yāvatāyāvatāyāvat nana, of yāvatyāvatyāvad preceded by purāpurāpura [MBh. xiii, 4556], or without any correlative [2727; Kathās.; Hit.]) [445,3]
    • at once, now, just, first (followed by anantaramanantaramanantara [Hit.], aparamaparamapara [Pañcat.], apiapiap [ib.], idānīmidānīm [Hit.], utauta [Śak.], caca [Daś.; Prab.], tatastatastatatat [Mn.; iv, 174; Ragh. vii, 4f.], tad-anutadanutadtatanu [Megh.], tutu [Daś. vii; Vedântas.], paścātpaścātpaścapaścād [R. ii], punarpunar [Pañcat.], vāvāvauū; very often connected with an Impv., rarely [MBh. iv, 888; R. ii, 56, 13] with a Pot., often with the 1st person of pr. or fut. MBh. &c. the Impv. is sometimes to be supplied [itasitasita tāvattāvattāvad, ‘just come hither’; māmāma tāvattāvattāvad, ‘by no means, God forbid!’] Śak.; Mālav.; Vikr.; Prab.; sometimes arhasi with the inf. is used instead, R. i f.) [445,3]
    • (with nana or a-atāvattāvad) not yet, MBh. &c. (followed by yāvatyāvatyāvad, ‘while’ Kathās. xxvi, 23; tāvantāvantāvat na-apinaapiap nana, ‘not only not-but also not’ Kād.) [445,3]
    • very well, all right, Hcar. [445,3]
    • indeed, truly (e.g. dṛḍhasdṛḍha tāvadtāvadtāvat bandhaḥbandha, ‘the knot is tight I must admit’ Hit.; gatāgatagat tāvattāvattāvad, ‘she is indeed gone’ Kathās. xviii, 241), R. &c. [445,3]
    • already (opposed to ‘how much more’ or ‘how much less’), R. iv f.; Śak. [445,3]
    • really (= evaeva, sometimes connected with this particle e.g. vikrayasvikraya tāvadtāvadtāvat evaeva saḥsassa, ‘it is really a sale’), Mn. iii, 53; Hariv. 7110; R. &c. [445,3]
