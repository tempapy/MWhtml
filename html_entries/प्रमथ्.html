• pra-√mathpramathpramath
  • (or manthmanth) P. -mathatimathatipra-mathati, or -mathnātimathnātipra-mathnāti, to stir up violently, churn (the ocean), Ragh.;
    to tear or strike off, drag away, ŚBr.; MBh.; R.;
    to handle roughly, harass, distress, annoy, MBh.; Kāv. &c. (ind.p. -mathyamathyapra-mathya, violently, forcibly);
    to destroy, lay waste, MBh. :
    Caus. -māthayatimāthayatipra-māthayati, to assault violently, harass, annoy, MBh. [685,1]
