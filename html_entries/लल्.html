• lallal [Roots]
  • (cf. √laḍlaḍ) cl. 1. P. (Dhātup. ix, 77) lalatilalati (mc. also °telalte), to play, sport, dally, frolic, behave loosely or freely, MBh.; Kāv. &c.;
    to loll or wag the tongue (See below) :
    Caus. lālayatilālayati, °telalte (Pass. lālyatelālyate), to cause to sport or dally, caress, fondle, foster, cherish, ib.;
    to wave, flourish, Hariv.;
    to favour, Sāh.;
    (Ā.) to desire, Dhātup. (cf. under √laḍlaḍ). [897,3]
