• viśvā-mitraviśvāmitraviśvāmitra—sṛṣṭisṛṣṭi f.
    • Viśvāmitra's creation (in allusion to several things fabled to have been created by this saint in rivalry of Brahmā e.g. the fruit of the Palmyra in imitation of the human skull, the buffalo in imitation of the cow, the ass of the horse &c.), MW. [995,1]
