• dīkṣādīkṣā f.
    • preparation or consecration for a religious ceremony, undertaking religious observances for a partic. purpose and the observances themselves, AV.; VS.; Br.; ŚrS. &c. [480,3]
    • dedication, initiation (personified as the wife of Soma, RV. 25, 26, of Rudra Ugra or Rudra Vāmadeva, Pur.) [480,3]
    • any serious preparation (as for battle), MBh.; Hariv.; Kāv. [480,3]
    • self-devotion to a person or god, complete resignation or restriction to, exclusive occupation with (comp.; cf. viraha-virahadīkṣā, śāka-śākadīkṣāśākadīkṣā, śṛṅgāraśṛṅgāra.). [480,3]
