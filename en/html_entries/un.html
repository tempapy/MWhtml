UN. This prefix is expressed in Sanskrit by aa, anan, nirnir, vivi, apaapa, durdur &c., prefixed, or by hīnahīna, rahitarahita, śūnyaśūnya &c., affixed. In cases where the English word formed with ‘un’ is not given in this Dictionary, the student must take the simple noun and use one of the above prefixes or affixes.
