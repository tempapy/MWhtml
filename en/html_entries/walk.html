To WALK, v. n. (Go on the feet) pādābhyāṃpādābhyām gamgam (c. 1. gacchatigacchati, gantuṃgantum) or yāyā (c. 2. yātiyāti -tuṃtumtuṃ), pādābhyāṃpādābhyām kramkram (c. 1. krāmatikrāmati, c. 4. krāmyatikrāmyati, kramituṃkramitum), pādābhyāṃpādābhyām calcal or carcar; ‘backwards and forwards,’ gama-gamagama nāgamanaṃnāgamananāgamanam kṛkṛ. — (Walk about, as for pleasure, &c.) parikramparikram, vihṛvihṛ (c. 1. -haratiharatiharati -harttuṃharttumhartumharttuṃ), parikramaṃparikramaparikramam kṛkṛ. — (Walk off), see To DECAMP.
