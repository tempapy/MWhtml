SUN, s. sūryyaḥsūryyasūrya, raviḥravi m., bhānuḥbhānu m., arkaḥarka, ādityaḥāditya, bhāskaraḥbhāskara, savitā m. (tṛ), aryyamā m. (n), vivasvān m. (t), divākaraḥdivākara, sūraḥsūra. (n), dvādaśātmakaḥdvādaśātmaka. The 12 ādityas are the offspring of Aditi, and are-the emblems of the sun in each month of the year. Hence they are identified with the sun itself. Their names are sūryyaḥsūryyasūrya, varuṇaḥvaruṇa, vedāṅgaḥvedāṅga, bhānuḥbhānu m., indraḥindra, raviḥravi m., gabhastiḥgabhasti m., yamaḥyama, svarṇa-svarṇa retāḥretā m., divākaraḥdivākara, mitraḥmitra, viṣṇuḥviṣṇu m. (s), haṃsaḥhaṃsa, inaḥina, heliḥheli m., mihiraḥmihira, bhagaḥbhaga, taraṇiḥtaraṇi m., mitraḥmitra, bhaṭṭārakaḥbhaṭṭāraka, vradhnaḥvradhna, aruṇaḥaruṇa, viśvakarmmā m., varuṇaḥvaruṇa, viṣṇuḥviṣṇu, kṛṣṇaḥkṛṣṇa.
