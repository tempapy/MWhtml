(ś), mārgaḥmārga; ‘to point out the way,’ dikpradarśanaṃdikpradarśanadikpradarśanam kṛ, mārgaṃmārgamārgam dṛś in caus., mārgapradarśanaṃmārgapradarśanamārgapradarśanam kṛ; ‘this way, this way!’ ita itas; ‘which way?’ kutra, kāṃkām diśaṃdiśadiśam; ‘in both ways,’ ubhayatra. — (Manner, method, course) prakāraḥprakāra, rītiḥrīti f., vidhiḥvidhi m., vidhānaṃvidhānavidhānam, kramaḥkrama, mārgaḥmārga; ‘way to act,’ kriyāvidhiḥkriyāvidhi m., kṛtyavidhiḥkṛtyavidhi m., see MANNER. Often expressed by the affix dhā; as, ‘in one way,’ ekadhā; ‘in two ways,’ dvidhā; ‘in three,’ tridhā; ‘in a hundred,’ śatadhā; ‘in many,’ bahudhā, ane-ane kadhā; ‘in how many?’ katidhā; ‘that may be written in two ways,’ dvidhālekhyaḥdvidhālekhya -khyākhyā -khyaṃkhyakhyamkhyaṃ. Or by the affixes thā, tas, &c. as, ‘in both ways,’ ubhayathā, ubhayatas.
