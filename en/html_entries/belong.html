To BELONG, v. n. (To be one's own property) ātmīyaḥātmīya -yāyāyā -yaṃyayamyaṃ or svakīyaḥsvakīya -yāyāyā -yaṃyayamyaṃ or svādhīnaḥsvādhīna -nānānā -naṃnanamnaṃ asas. But the sense of ‘belonging’ is more usually expressed by the use of the roots asas or bhūbhū or vidvid with the gen. c.; as, ‘money be- longs to me,’ mamamama dhanaṃdhanadhanam vidyatevidyate or astiasti. — (To have relation to) sambandhsambandhsaṃbandh in pass. (-badhyatebadhyatebadhyate). — (To be fit, or becoming) yujyuj in pass. (yujyateyujyate); ‘this belongs to me,’ i. e. ‘this is my busi- ness,’ mamamama ayaṃayaayam vyāpāraḥvyāpāra.
