To BELONG¦, v. n. (To be one's own property) ātmīyaḥ -yā -yaṃ or svakīyaḥ -yā -yaṃ or svādhīnaḥ -nā -naṃ as. But the sense of ‘belonging’ is more usually expressed by the use of the roots as or bhū or vid with the gen. c.; as, ‘money belongs to me,’ mama dhanaṃ vidyate or asti. — (To have relation to) sambandh in pass. (-badhyate). — (To be fit, or becoming) yuj in pass. (yujyate); ‘this belongs to me,’ i. e. ‘this is my business,’ mama ayaṃ vyāpāraḥ.
