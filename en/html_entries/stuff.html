STUFF, s. (Matter, material) vastu n., dravyaṃdravyadravyam, prakṛtiḥprakṛti f., viṣayaḥviṣaya, padārthaḥpadārtha, pariṇāmidravyaṃpariṇāmidravyapariṇāmidravyam. — (Cloth) paṭaḥpaṭa, vastraṃvastravastram, vasanaṃvasanavasanam, see CLOTH — (House- hold furniture) gṛhopaskarādi n., gṛhabhāṇḍāni n. pl., gṛhadravyāṇi n. pl. — (Rubbish) tucchadravyaṃtucchadravyatucchadravyam, see RUBBISH, NONSENSE. To STUFF, v. a. (Fill to excess) atyantaṃatyantaatyantam pṝ (c. 10. pūrayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), ati-ati śayena tṛp in caus. — (Crowd together) sambādh, see To CROWD, v. a. To STUFF, v. n. atiśayena or atitṛpti pṝ in pass. (pūryyate) or ātmānaṃātmānaātmānam pṝ (c. 10. pūrayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), atitṛpti bhuj. See To CRAM, v. n.
