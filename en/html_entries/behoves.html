It BEHOVES, v. imp. Expressed by the future passive parti- ciple; as, ‘it behoves you to marry the damsel,’ tvayā kanyā voḍhavyā; or by the potential mood, as, tvaṃtvatvam kanyāṃkanyām vaheḥvahe; or by the use of the verb arh (c. 1. arhati), as tvaṃtvatvam kanyāṃkanyām voḍhum arhasi; ‘to act as it behoves one,’ yathocitaṃyathocitayathocitam kṛ.
