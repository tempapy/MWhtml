WEST¦, s. (The quarter of the sky) paścimā, paścimadiśā, paścimadik f. (ś), paścimāśā, pratīcī, aparā, caramā, vāruṇī, madhyaḥ -dhyaṃ. The god Varuna is fabled as the Regent of the West.
