BEING THAT, conj. (Since) yatasyatas, yasmātyasmāt, yenayena, itiiti hetoḥheto, yatohetoḥyatoheto. ‘Being,’ is often expressed by the loc. c. of satsat, as, ‘the man being gone,’ puruṣepuruṣe gategate satisati; ‘it being so,’ evaṃevaevam satisati, tathātvetathātve, evambhūtvāevambhūtvā. ‘Being’ may also be expressed by the abl. c. of the abstract noun in such phrases as ‘he being a good man,’ sajjanatvātsajjanatvāt.
