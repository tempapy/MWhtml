IDEA, s. buddhiḥbuddhi f., matiḥmati f., saṅkalpaḥsaṅkalpasaṃkalpa, manogataṃmanogatamanogatam, manasijaṃmanasijamanasijam, cintācintā, manaḥkalpitaṃmanaḥkalpitamanaḥkalpitam, manaḥkalpanāmanaḥkalpanā, kalpanākalpanā, vāsanāvāsanā, bhāvanābhāvanā, bodhaḥbodha; ‘with the idea that,’ ‘under the idea of,’ is expressed by the inst. c. of buddhiḥbuddhi; as, ‘with the idea that he was a tiger,’ vyāghrabuddhyāvyāghrabuddhyā; ‘with the idea of reaching,’ prāpaṇabuddhyāprāpaṇabuddhyā; ‘from an idea of (his) incapacity,’ apātrabuddhyāapātrabuddhyā. — (Abstract idea) bhāvaḥbhāva.
