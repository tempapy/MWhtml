BEHALF, s. (In behalf of) arthaṃarthaartham, arthearthe, hetoḥheto, hetauhetau, kāraṇātkāraṇāt, nimittenimitte, kṛtekṛte, generally affixed to the crude of a noun; ‘on my behalf,’ matkṛtematkṛte or madarthemadarthe; ‘on behalf of (his) son,’ putrahetoḥputraheto. — (Favour) anugrahaḥanugraha, prasādaḥprasāda.
