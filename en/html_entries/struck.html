STRUCK, p. p. hataḥhata -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, āhataḥāhata &c., samāhataḥsamāhata &c., abhihataḥabhihata &c., prahataḥprahata &c., upahataḥupahata &c., vyāhataḥvyāhata &c., pratihataḥpratihata &c., tāḍitaḥtāḍita -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, āsphālitaḥāsphālita -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ; ‘with astonishment,’ vismayopahataḥvismayopahata -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ. The use of hata in the first member of a compound is very common; as, ‘struck with poverty,’ hatasampadaḥhatasampadahatasaṃpada -dādā -daṃdadamdaṃ, i. e. ‘one whose fortunes are struck or blasted.’
