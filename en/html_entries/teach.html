(adhyāpayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ, rt. i), paṭh in caus., śās &c., see To INSTRUCT. Sometimes the causal expresses the sense of ‘teach;’ as, ‘he teaches to dance,’ narttayati; ‘he teaches to read,’ pāṭhayati.
