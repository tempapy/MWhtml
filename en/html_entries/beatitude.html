BEATITUDE, s. The Hindūs divide final beatitude into four kinds, viz. 1. ‘absorption into the essence of the deity,’ brahmasāyujyaṃbrahmasāyujyabrahmasāyujyam; 2. ‘residence with him,’ salokatāsalokatā; 3. ‘nearness to him,’ samīpatāsamīpatā; 4. ‘conformedness to his image,’ sarūpatāsarūpatā.
