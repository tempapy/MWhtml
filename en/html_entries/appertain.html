To APPERTAIN, v. n. (To belong to one's self) ātmasāt or ātmādhīnaḥātmādhīna -nānā -naṃnanamnaṃ, or svādhīnaḥsvādhīna -nānā -naṃnanamnaṃ, or svakīyaḥsvakīya -yāyā -yaṃyayamyaṃ as. — (To belong, in general) sambangh in pass. (-badhyatebadhyate). The sense of ‘appertaining’ may often be expressed by the root as or vid with the gen. case: as, ‘money appertains to me,’ mama dhanaṃdhanadhanam vidyate.
