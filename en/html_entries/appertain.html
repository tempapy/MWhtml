To APPERTAIN¦, v. n. (To belong to one's self) ātmasāt or ātmādhīnaḥ -nā -naṃ, or svādhīnaḥ -nā -naṃ, or svakīyaḥ -yā -yaṃ as. — (To belong, in general) sambangh in pass. (-badhyate). The sense of ‘appertaining’ may often be expressed by the root as or vid with the gen. case: as, ‘money appertains to me,’ mama dhanaṃ vidyate.
