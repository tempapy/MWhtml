PSEUDO. A prefix expressed in Sanskrit by mithyā, mṛṣā, kṛtrima, ku &c. prefixed, or kalpa, deśīya &c. affixed; as, ‘a pseudo- poet,’ mithyākaviḥmithyākavi m., kukaviḥkukavi, kavikalpaḥkavikalpa, kavideśīyaḥkavideśīya; pseudology,’ mithyāvādaḥmithyāvāda, mṛṣāvādaḥmṛṣāvāda; ‘pseudo-morphous,’ kṛtrimarūpaḥkṛtrimarūpa -pāpā -paṃpapampaṃ; ‘pseudonymous,’ kṛtrimanāmā &c.
