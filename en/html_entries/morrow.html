MORROW, s. (The day next after the present) paradivasaḥparadivasa, paradinaṃparadinaparadinam, śvastanadivasaḥśvastanadivasa, āgāmidivasaḥāgāmidivasa, kalyaṃkalyakalyam; ‘to-morrow, on the morrow,’ śvas, paredyus, paradine, paradivase, paredyavi, kalli, kalye, anyedyus. — (The next day subsequent to any day) uttaradivasaḥuttaradivasa, uttaradinaṃuttaradinauttaradinam, uttaredyus; ‘the day after to-morrow,’ paraśvasa; ‘to-morrow morning,’ śvaḥśva prabhāte.
