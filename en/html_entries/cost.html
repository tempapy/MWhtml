COST, s. (Price) mūlyaṃmūlyamūlyam, arghaḥargha. — (Expense) vyayaḥvyaya; ‘high cost,’ durmūlyaṃdurmūlyadurmūlyam, bahumūlyaṃbahumūlyabahumūlyam, māhārdhyaṃmāhārdhyamāhārdhyam; ‘little cost,’ alpamūlyaṃalpamūlyaalpamūlyam, alpavyayaḥalpavyaya; ‘at the cost of life,’ prāṇavyayenaprāṇavyayena; ‘at the cost of a large sum,’ bahudhanavyayenabahudhanavyayena. To COST, v. n. arghargh (c. 10. arghayatiarghayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ). Usually expressed by the inst. c. of mūlyaṃmūlyamūlyam or vyayaḥvyaya in conjunction with some other verb; as, ‘what does it cost?’ kiyatākiyatā mūlyenamūlyena vikrīyatevikrīyate; ‘the building of the house cost a large sum of money,’ bahudha-bahudhabahudha navyayenanavyayena gṛhaṃgṛhagṛham nirmmitaṃnirmmitanirmmitamnirmitanirmitam.
