To RESEMBLE¦, v. a. (Be like) sadṛśaḥ -śī -śaṃ bhū or as, sadṛśībhū, anukṛ (c. 8. -karoti -karttuṃ), tulyaḥ -lyā -lyaṃ bhū, samaḥ -mā -maṃ bhū, samānaḥ -nā -naṃ bhū, upamaḥ -mā -maṃ bhū, sarūpaḥ -pā -paṃ bhū, anurūpaḥ -pā -paṃ bhū; ‘the son resembles his father,’ putraḥ pitur anukaroti or putraḥ pitṛrūpo bhavati or putraḥ pitrā sadṛśākāro bhavati; ‘no one resembles him,’ anena sadṛśaḥ kaścin nāsti or tasya tulyaḥ or tena tulyaḥ or tena samo na ko'pi bhavati. A nominal verb may sometimes express the sense of ‘resemble,’ thus; ‘he resembles a lion,’ siṃhāyate; ‘it resembles heaven,’ svargāyate. — (Liken) upamā (c. 2. -māti, c. 3. -mimīte -mātuṃ), sadṛśīkṛ, samīkṛ, samāna (nom. samānayati -yituṃ).
