ABOUT, prep. (Encircling) pari prefixed. — (Near to, in time) prati. — (In number) deśīya affixed; as, ‘about twelve years old,’ dvādaśavarṣadeśīyaḥdvādaśavarṣadeśīya; nyūnādhikaṃnyūnādhikanyūnādhikam. — (Concerning, relating to) prati, pratīkṣya, uddiśya. — (On account of) hetoḥheto, hetau, arthaṃarthaartham, arthe, kṛte, nimitte, kāraṇāt, with the crude form, or sometimes the gen. c. of the preceding noun. — (Engaged in any thing) vyāpṛtaḥvyāpṛta, niṣṭhitaḥniṣṭhita, nirataḥnirata, with loc. c. — (Close to one's person) pārśvatas ind., nikaṭe, samīpaṃsamīpasamīpam. ABOUT, adv. (Circularly) paritas ind., abhitas ind. — (Nearly) prāyaśas or prāyas ind. — (Here and there) itastatas ind., sarvvatas ind. — (To be about to do any thing) expressed by udyataḥudyata, ‘prepared,’ with the infin.; as, ‘about to kill,’ hantum udyataḥudyata; or by the act. part. of the 2d fut.; as, gamiṣyan, ‘about to go,’ edhiṣyamāṇaḥedhiṣyamāṇa, ‘about to increase’; or, by the desid. adj.; as, jigamiṣuḥjigamiṣu, ‘about to go,’ mumūrṣuḥmumūrṣu ‘about to die.’ ABOUT. (On the point of) unmukha &c., see under POINT.
