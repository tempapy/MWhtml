SLING, s. (Instrument for throwing stones) gophaṇā, prastarakṣepaṇī, pāṣāṇakṣepaṇī, prastaraprakṣepaṇī, prakṣepaṇī, bhindapālaḥbhindapāla. — (Pole with ropes for carrying burdens) bhārayaṣṭiḥbhārayaṣṭi m. f., kācaḥkāca. To SLING, v. a. (Throw with a sling) gophaṇayā kṣip (c. 6. kṣipati, kṣeptuṃkṣeptum) or prakṣip. — (Hang so as to swing) udbandh, ūrddhvaṃūrddhvaūrddhvam bandh, āndola (nom. āndolayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), kāca (nom. kācayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ).
