TOWARD, TOWARDS, adv. (In the direction of) prati, anu, abhimukhaṃabhimukhaabhimukham, uddiśya; ‘towards the city,’ nagaraṃnagaranagaram prati, nagarābhimukhaṃnagarābhimukhanagarābhimukham; ‘he set out towards the north,’ uttarāṃuttarām diśatuddiśya prasthitaḥprasthita. — (With respect to) upari, uddiśya, uddeśena, prati affixed; ‘towards me,’ mamopari, māmuddiśya, māṃmām prati; ‘want of courtesy towards you,’ tavopari adākṣiṇyaṃadākṣiṇyaadākṣiṇyam; ‘not behaving properly towards his father,’ piturupari asadṛśavyavahārī. — (Nearly) prāyas; ‘towards evening,’ abhisāyaṃabhisāyaabhisāyam.
