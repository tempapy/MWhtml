PHALLUS, s. (Emblem of Śiva) liṅgaṃliṅgaliṅgam. According to the Mahratti Dictionary there are 12; viz. 1. mahākāleśvaraḥmahākāleśvara, 2. somanāthaḥsomanātha, 3. kedāreśvaraḥkedāreśvara, 4. viśveśvaraḥviśveśvara, 5. oṅkāreśvaraḥoṅkāreśvara, 6. ambakeśvaraḥambakeśvara, 7. ghṛṣaṇeśvaraḥghṛṣaṇeśvara, 8. nāganāthaḥnāganātha, 9. vaidyanāthaḥvaidyanātha, 10. bhīmāśaṅkaraḥbhīmāśaṅkara, 11. malli-mallimalli kārjunaḥkārjuna, 12. rāmeśvaraḥrāmeśvara.
