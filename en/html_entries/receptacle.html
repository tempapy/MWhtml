RECEPTACLE, s. ādhāraḥādhāra, pātraṃpātrapātram, bhājanaṃbhājanabhājanam, āśayaḥāśaya, nidhānaṃnidhānanidhānam, nidhiḥnidhi m., ādhānaṃādhānaādhānam, āśrayaḥāśraya, adhiśrayaḥadhiśraya, adhikaraṇaṃadhikaraṇaadhikaraṇam, garbhaḥgarbha, āspadaṃāspadaāspadam, vinyāsaḥvinyāsa, vījaṃvījavījam. The word āśayaḥāśaya is especially used in composition thus, ‘the receptacle of the blood,’ raktāśayaḥraktāśaya; ‘of the food,’ annā-annāannā śayaḥśaya, āmāśayaḥāmāśaya; ‘of bile,’ pittāśayaḥpittāśaya.
