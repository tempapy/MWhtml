To REDUCE, v. a. (Bring to a former state) pūrvvavat kṛ, pūrvvāvasthāṃpūrvvāvasthāmpūrvāvasthām or pūrvvasthitiṃpūrvvasthitimpūrvasthitim nī (c. 1. nayati, netuṃnetum) or ānī or gam in caus. — (Bring to any state) nī, ānī, gam in caus. (gamayati -yituṃ)yitumyituṃ, kṛ in comp.; ‘to reduce to poverty,’ dāridryaṃdāridryadāridryam gam, daridrīkṛ; ‘to subjection,’ vaśīkṛ; ‘to one's own power.’ svavaśīkṛ, ātmasātkṛ; ‘to ashes,’ bhasmasātkṛ, bhasmīkṛ; ‘to a heap of ashes,’ bhasmarāśīkṛ; ‘to nothing,’ niṣkiñcanīkṛ. Or expressed by āpanna or sampanna in comp.; as, ‘to reduce to poverty,’ dāri-dāri drāpannaṃdrāpannadrāpannam -nnāṃnnāmnnāṃ kṛ. — (Diminish, lower) nyūnīkṛ, alpīkṛ, laghūkṛ, ūnīkṛ, nyūn (c. 10. nyūnayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), ūn, hras (c. 10. hrāsayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), lagh (nom. laghayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), kan (c. 10. kanayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), śithilīkṛ; ‘to reduce one's strength,’ balaṃbalabalam or vīryyaṃvīryyavīryyamvīryavīryam śithilīkṛ. — (Make thin) kṛśīkṛ, kṣīṇīkṛ, māṃsakṣayaṃmāṃsakṣayamāṃsakṣayam kṛ, kṣi in caus. (kṣapayati -yituṃ)yitumyituṃ, śuṣkīkṛ. — (Subdue) vaśīkṛ, parāji, viji, ji, khaṇḍ. — (In arithmetic) rūpāntaraṃrūpāntararūpāntaram kṛ, rūpaṃrūparūpam, dā or kṛ. — (Reduce to order), see To METHODIZE, ARRANGE.
