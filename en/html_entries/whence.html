WHENCE, adv. (Interrog.) kutaskutas, kasmādkasmād deśātdeśāt &c., kasmātkasmāt sthā-sthāsthā nātnāt &c. ‘whence have you come?’ kutaḥkuta samāgato'sisamāgatosi. — (Indef.) yatasyatas, yasmādyasmād deśātdeśāt &c. ‘to return whence one came,’ pratigantuṃpratigantum yathāgataṃyathāgatayathāgatam.
