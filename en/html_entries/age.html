AGE¦, s. (Of the world) yugaṃ; ‘beginning of an age,’ yugādiḥ m.; ‘end of one,’ yugāntaḥ. The four ages of the Hindūs, corresponding in some degree with the golden age, silver age, brazen age, and iron age, are, 1. kṛtaṃ or satyaṃ, 2. tretā, 3. dvāparaḥ, 4. kaliḥ m.
