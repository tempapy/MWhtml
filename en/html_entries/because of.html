BECAUSE OF, prep. (By reason of) hetoḥheto, hetau, kāraṇāt. — (For the sake of) arthaṃarthaartham, arthe, kṛte, nimitte affixed to a crude noun; ‘because of that,’ anena hetunā, iti hetoḥheto. ‘Because of’ is often expressed by the abl. c. of the noun; as, ‘be- cause of their unbelief,’ teṣām aviśvāsāt.
