BECAUSE OF, prep. (By reason of) hetoḥheto, hetauhetau, kāraṇātkāraṇāt. — (For the sake of) arthaṃarthaartham, arthearthe, kṛtekṛte, nimittenimitte affixed to a crude noun; ‘because of that,’ anenaanena hetunāhetunā, itiiti hetoḥheto. ‘Because of’ is often expressed by the abl. c. of the noun; as, ‘be- cause of their unbelief,’ teṣāmteṣām aviśvāsātaviśvāsāt.
