BECAUSE OF¦, prep. (By reason of) hetoḥ, hetau, kāraṇāt. — (For the sake of) arthaṃ, arthe, kṛte, nimitte affixed to a crude noun; ‘because of that,’ anena hetunā, iti hetoḥ. ‘Because of’ is often expressed by the abl. c. of the noun; as, ‘because of their unbelief,’ teṣām aviśvāsāt.
