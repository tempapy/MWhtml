To WEIGH¦, v. a. (In a balance) tul (c. 10. tulayati -yituṃ), tūl (c. 10. tūlayati -yituṃ), tolanaṃ kṛ, tulanāṃ kṛ. — (Raise, as an anchor, &c.) uttul. — (Ponder) vigaṇ (c. 10. -gaṇayati -yituṃ), manasā vicar, see To PONDER; ‘to weigh alternatives,’ vikḷp (c. 10. -kalpayati -yituṃ). ‘Weighs upon,’ viḍambayati; ‘weighs down,’ see To OUTWEIGH. To WEIGH¦, v. n. (Have weight) bhārī -riṇī -ri bhū or as, bhāravān -vatī -vad as or bhū, bhārānvitaḥ -tā -tam as or bhū; ‘what does this weigh?’ etat kimbhāram asti or etasya kiṃ bhāraparimāṇaṃ. — (Be important) guruḥ -rvī -ru bhū.
