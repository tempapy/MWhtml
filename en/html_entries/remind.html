To REMIND, v. a. smṛ (c. 10. smārayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), saṃsmṛ, anusmṛ, budh (c. 10. bodhayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), udbudh, prabudh, pratibudh, sūc (c. 10. sūcayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), smāraṇaṃsmāraṇasmāraṇam kṛ, smṛtiṃsmṛtim or smaraṇaṃsmaraṇasmaraṇam jan (c. 10. janayati -yituṃyitumyituṃ), sūcanaṃsūcanasūcanam kṛ. The construction of the sentence will require 2 acc.; as, ‘the Kandalī with its flowers reminds me of her eyes,’ kusumaiḥkusumai kandalī smārayati māṃmām locane tasyāḥtasyā; ‘the Vimba reminds me of her lower lip,’ vimbaṃvimbavimbam smārayati mām adharaṃadharaadharam tasyāḥtasyā.
