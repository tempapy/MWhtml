THROUGHOUT¦, adv. (From the beginning to the end) sādyantaṃ, ādyantaṃ, śeṣaparyyantaṃ. — (Of time) expressed by paryyantaṃ, yāvat or ā in comp.; thus, ‘throughout the month,’ māsaparyyantaṃ; ‘throughout the year,’ varṣaparyyantaṃ; ‘throughout the whole year,’ āvarṣāntāt or varṣāntaṃ yāvat (i. e. to the end of the year); ‘throughout life,’ yāvajjīvaṃ, jīvanaparyyantaṃ, ājīvanāntāt.
