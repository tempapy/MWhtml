ATTENDED, p. p. (Accompanied) sahitaḥsahita -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, sametaḥsameta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, pari-paripari vāritaḥvārita -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, parivṛtaḥparivṛta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, vṛtaḥvṛta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, samanvitaḥsamanvita -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ. — (At- tended to) anuṣṭhitaḥanuṣṭhita -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ. When only two persons are re- ferred to, dvitīyadvitīya or sahāyasahāya may be used; as, ‘attended by Indrasena,’ indrasenadvitīyaḥindrasenadvitīya -yāyāyā -yaṃyayamyaṃ or indrasenasahāyaḥindrasenasahāya -yāyāyā -yaṃyayamyaṃ; ‘at- tended by servants,’ sapreṣyaḥsapreṣya -ṣyāṣyāṣyā -ṣyaṃṣyaṣyamṣyaṃ.
