BADLY, adv. duṣṭhuduṣṭhu, duṣṭaṃduṣṭaduṣṭam, abhadraṃabhadraabhadram. Often expressed by durdur, kuku, apaapa prefixed; as, ‘badly managed,’ durṇītaḥdurṇīta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ; ‘badly shaped,’ kurūpaḥkurūpa -pīpīpī -paṃpapampaṃ; ‘badly-behaved,’ duḥśīlaḥduḥśīla, kuśīlaḥkuśīla; ‘badly done,’ duṣkṛtaḥduṣkṛta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, apakṛtaḥapakṛta -tātātā -taṃtatamtaṃ.
