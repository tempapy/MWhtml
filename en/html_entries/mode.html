The musical modes are personified as either male or female. The male are called Rāgas; the female are Rāginīs, and are considered to be the wives of the Rāgas. The following are some of the names for the male: bhairavaḥbhairava, mālavaḥmālava, sāraṅgaḥsāraṅga, hindolaḥhindola, vasantaḥvasantavasaṃta, vasantadūtaḥvasantadūtavasaṃtadūta, pañcamaḥpañcama, dīpakaḥdīpaka, meghaḥmegha, naṭaḥnaṭa, naṭanārāyaṇaḥnaṭanārāyaṇa, kalpāṇaḥkalpāṇa, kāmabodhaḥkāmabodha, troṭakaḥtroṭaka, vibhāsaḥvibhāsa.
