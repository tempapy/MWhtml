DATE, s. (Time at which a letter, &c. is written) likhanakālaḥlikhanakāla, likhanadivasaḥlikhanadivasa, divasaḥdivasa, dinaṃdinadinam, tithiḥtithi m. f. — (Time at which a thing was done) kālaḥkāla, kālikatā, tatkālaḥtatkāla. — (Period, term) avadhiḥavadhi m., kālāvadhiḥkālāvadhi m., parimāṇaṃparimāṇaparimāṇam. — (Fruit) kharjjūraḥkharjjūrakharjūra, kāṭhinaḥkāṭhina; ‘out of date,’ avyavahāritaḥavyavahārita -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, niruktaḥnirukta -ktāktā -ktaṃktaktamktaṃ. To DATE, v. a. kālaṃkālakālam or divasaṃdivasadivasam or dinaṃdinadinam or tithiṃtithim likh (c. 6. likhati, lekhituṃlekhitum).
