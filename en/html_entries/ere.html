ERE, adv. or prep. prākprāk, pūrvvaṃpūrvvapūrvvampūrvapūrvam, purāpurā, agreagre; ‘ere he could come,’ tasyatasya āgamanātāgamanāt purvvaṃpurvvapurvvampurvapurvam or purāpurā; ‘ere the time appointed,’ prāgprāg vihitakālātvihitakālāt; ‘ere twelve years are over,’ prāgprāg dvādaśasamāḥdvādaśasamā; or, expressed by loc. absolute; as, ‘ere my child die,’ mamamama putreputre nana mṛtemṛte satisati.
