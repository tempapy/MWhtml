SIR, s. āryyaḥāryyaārya, mahāśayaḥmahāśaya, mahānubhāvaḥmahānubhāva, mahecchaḥmaheccha, mahājanaḥmahājana, sujanaḥsujana, śrīśrī prefixed; ‘O sir,’ āryyaāryyaārya. As an address of respect, ‘Sir’ may often be expressed in Sanskrit by bhavānbhavān with the 3d pers. of the verb; as, ‘enter, Sir,’ praviśatupraviśatu bhavānbhavān.
