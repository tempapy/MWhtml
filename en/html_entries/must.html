MUST, v. n. (Be necessitated). Expressed by the fut. pass. part.; as, ‘anger must be restrained,’ kopo niyantavyaḥniyantavya; ‘I must go, or it is to be gone by me.’ mayā gantavyaṃgantavyagantavyam; ‘I must do that,’ tan mayā karttavyaṃkarttavyakarttavyamkartavyakartavyam or karaṇīyaṃkaraṇīyakaraṇīyam or kāryyaṃkāryyakāryyamkāryakāryam. The adverb avaśyaṃavaśyaavaśyam may be added; as, tan mayā avaśyaṃavaśyaavaśyam karttavyaṃkarttavyakarttavyamkartavyakartavyam. Sometimes the verb arh (c. 1. arhati -rhituṃrhitumrhituṃ) has the sense of ‘must;’ as, ‘he must not receive that,’ na tat prāptum arhati. MUST s. (Wine just pressed from the grape) navamadyaṃnavamadyanavamadyam, nūtanamadyaṃnūtanamadyanūtanamadyam. To MUST, v. n. (Grow mouldy and sour) amlībhū, durgandhībhū, virasībhū.
