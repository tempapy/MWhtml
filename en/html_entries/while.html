WHILE, WHILST, adv. yāvatyāvat, yāvatkālaṃyāvatkālayāvatkālam, yatkālaṃyatkālayatkālam, yasminyasmin kālekāle, yatparyyantaṃyatparyyantayatparyyantamyatparyantayatparyantam, yadāyadā; ‘whilst he thought on this the messenger arrived,’ yāvatyāvat tactac cintayaticintayati dūtaḥdūta samāgacchatisamāgacchati. The sense of whilst may sometimes be expressed by madhyemadhye, see DURING, or by the locative absolute, or some similar form of expression; as, sasa tadevatadeva cintayaticintayati dūtaścadūtaśca samāgacchatisamāgacchati.
