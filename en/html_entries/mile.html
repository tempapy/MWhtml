MILE, s. The measure called krośaḥkrośa, or kos, is about two En- glish miles. One English mile will therefore be ex- pressed by arddhakrośaḥarddhakrośa; ‘three miles,’ sārddhakrośaḥsārddhakrośa; ‘four miles,’ krośadvayaṃkrośadvayakrośadvayam; ‘five miles,’ sārddhadvikrośaṃsārddhadvikrośasārddhadvikrośam; ‘six miles,’ krośatrayaṃkrośatrayakrośatrayam.
