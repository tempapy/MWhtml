ARJUNA, s. (The third and most celebrated of the five sons of Pāndu. He was the son of Pāndu only by courtesy, being in fact the child of his wife Pṛthā or Kuntī by the god Indra) arjunaḥarjuna. — (As son of Pṛthā) pārthaḥpārtha, kaunteyaḥkaunteya, kuntisutaḥkuntisuta — (As son of Indra) aindriḥaindri, pākaśāsaniḥpākaśāsani. — (The crested one) kirīṭī m. — (The Conqueror) jiṣṇuḥjiṣṇu. — (Wealth- acquiring) dhanañjayaḥdhanañjaya. — (Drawing the bow with the left hand) savyasācī m. — (Having hair like the Euphorbia) guḍākeśaḥguḍākeśa. — (Son of Pāndu) pāṇḍuputraḥpāṇḍuputra; his bow is called gāṇḍīvaḥgāṇḍīva -vaṃvavamvaṃ or gāṇḍivaḥgāṇḍiva -vaṃvavamvaṃ.
