LONGITUDE, s. drādhimā m. (n), dhruvakaḥdhruvaka. — (Position to east or west) rekhāntaraṃrekhāntararekhāntaram, rekhāṃśaḥrekhāṃśa; ‘of a country,’ deśāntaraṃdeśāntaradeśāntaram. — (Of the earth) rekhāṃśaḥrekhāṃśa, rekhāparidhiḥrekhāparidhi m., rekhāgaṇaḥrekhāgaṇa, rekhāsamūhaḥrekhāsamūha. — (Of any point or object in the heavens) bhogaḥbhoga; ‘of the sun,’ ravibhogaḥravibhoga; ‘of a planet reckoned from the vernal equinoctial point,’ sāyanaṃsāyanasāyanam. — (Meridian or circle of longitude) yāmyottaravṛttaṃyāmyottaravṛttayāmyottaravṛttam, rekhāvṛttaṃrekhāvṛttarekhāvṛttam, rekhāṃśavṛttaṃrekhāṃśavṛttarekhāṃśavṛttam.
