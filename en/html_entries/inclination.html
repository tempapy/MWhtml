INCLINATION¦, s. (Disposition, tendency) prāvaṇyaṃ, pravaṇatā, pravāhaḥ, pravṛttiḥ f., or more usually expressed by śīlaḥ or śīlatā -tvaṃ or by ālutā -tvaṃ affixed, or by the desid. form; as, ‘inclination to fall,’ patanaśīlatā -tvaṃ or patayālutā -tvaṃ or pipatiṣā. — (Desire) abhilāṣaḥ, icchā, kāṃkṣā, ākāṃkṣā, chandaṃ, chandas. — (Bending) namratā, natiḥ f., ānatiḥ f.; ‘inclination of a planet,’ paramāpamaḥ; ‘greatest inclination of a planet,’ vikṣepaghruvaḥ; ‘following one's inclination,’ kāmakārī -riṇī -ri (n), kāmacāraḥ -rā -raṃ.
