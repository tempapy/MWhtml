PER-CENT, PER-CENTAGE. The sense of these words may be expressed by uttaraḥuttara -rārārā -raṃraramraṃ; as, ‘one per-cent,’ ekottaraṃekottaraekottaram; ‘five per-cent,’ pañcottaraṃpañcottarapañcottaram. See also INTEREST. A ‘per- centage’ or commission is śulkaḥśulka -lkaṃlkalkamlkaṃ.
