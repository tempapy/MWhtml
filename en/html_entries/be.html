To BE, v. n. (To be in some state) asas (c. 2. astiasti), āsās (c. 2. āsteāste). — (To enter into some state) bhūbhū (c. 1. bhavatibhavati, bhavituṃbhavitum). — (To exist) asas, vṛtvṛt (c. 1. varttatevarttatevartate, varttituṃvarttitumvartitum), vidvid in pass. (vidyate)vidyate, janjan in pass. (jāyatejāyate). — (To be in or at) vṛtvṛt, sthāsthā (c. 1. tiṣṭhatitiṣṭhati, sthātuṃsthātum); ‘be it so,’ evamevam astuastu. The verb ‘to be’ is often expressed by the use of the passive form of the Sanskrit verb, and is not unfrequently omitted altogether.
