MONTH, s. māsaḥmāsa, mās m., pakṣadvayaṃpakṣadvayapakṣadvayam, pakṣayugaṃpakṣayugapakṣayugam, nākṣatraṃnākṣatranākṣatram, yavyaḥyavya, varṣāṃśaḥvarṣāṃśa, varṣāṅgaḥvarṣāṅga. The following are the twelve Hindu months, 1. caitraḥcaitra, corresponding to the last half of March and the first half of April, 2. vaiśākhaḥvaiśākha, April, May, 3. jyeṣṭaḥjyeṣṭa, May, June, 4. āṣāḍhaḥāṣāḍha, June, July, 5. śrāvaṇaḥśrāvaṇa, July, August, 6. bhādraḥbhādra or bhādrapadaḥbhādrapada, August, September, 7. āśvinaḥāśvina or āśvayujaḥāśvayuja, September, October, 8. kārttikaḥkārttikakārtika, October, November, 9. mārga-mārga śīrṣaḥśīrṣa, November, December, 10. pauṣaḥpauṣa, December, January, 11. māghaḥmāgha, January, February, 12. phālgunaḥphālguna, February, March. — (Lunar month) cāndramāsaḥcāndramāsa; ‘half of a lunar month,’ pakṣaḥpakṣa; ‘light or first half of the month,’ śuklapakṣaḥśuklapakṣa, śuddhapakṣaḥśuddhapakṣa, sitapakṣaḥsitapakṣa, pūrvapakṣaḥpūrvapakṣa, prathamapakṣaḥprathamapakṣa, devapakṣaḥdevapakṣa; ‘dark or second half of the month,’ kṛṣṇapakṣaḥkṛṣṇapakṣa, tamisraḥtamisra, tamisrapakṣaḥtamisrapakṣa, uttarapakṣaḥuttarapakṣa, aparapakṣaḥaparapakṣa, bahulaḥbahula; ‘last day of the half-month,’ pakṣāntaḥpakṣānta, pakṣāvasaraḥpakṣāvasara. — (Solar month) sauramāsaḥsauramāsa, sauraḥsaura; ‘a month old,’ māsajātaḥmāsajāta -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ, māsīnaḥmāsīna -nānā -naṃnanamnaṃ.
