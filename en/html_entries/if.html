IF, conj. yadiyadi, cetcet usually used with the indicative; as, ‘if he live he will behold prosperity,’ yadiyadi jīvatijīvati bhadrāṇibhadrāṇi paśyatipaśyati; ‘if there be need of me,’ yadiyadi mayāmayā prayojanamprayojanam astiasti; ‘if avarice were abandoned, who would be poor?’ tṛṣṇātṛṣṇā cetcet parityaktāparityaktā koko daridraḥdaridra. — (As if) ivaiva. — (If so) yadyevaṃyadyevayadyevam. — (If not) yadiyadi nana, nana cetcet, nono cetcet, nana vāvā.
