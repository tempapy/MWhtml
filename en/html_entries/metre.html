There is another class of metres regulated, not by the number of syllables, but by the number of syllabic instants or short syllables, each long syllable being supposed to contain two short. They are called, 1. āryyā, varieties pathyā, vipulā, capalā, mukhacapalā, jaghanacapalā, gītiḥgīti f., upagītiḥupagīti f., udgītiḥudgīti f., āryyāgītiḥāryyāgītiāryāgīti f., 2. vaitālīyaḥvaitālīya, varieties aupachandasikaḥaupachandasika, dohaḍikā.
