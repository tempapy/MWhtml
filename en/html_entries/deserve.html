To DESERVE, v. a. and n. arh (c. 1. arhati -tete -rhituṃrhitumrhituṃ), arhaḥarha -rhārhā -rhaṃrharhamrhaṃ or upayuktaḥupayukta -ktāktā -ktaṃktaktamktaṃ or ucitaḥucita -tātā -taṃtatamtaṃ or yogyaḥyogya -gyāgyā -gyamgyam as or bhū; ‘he deserves honour,’ mānam arhati; ‘he deserves punishment,’ daṇḍam arhati; ‘he deserves to drink soma juice,’ somaṃsomasomam pātum arhati; ‘does he deserve so large a salary or not?’ kim upayukto'yam etāvad varttanaṃvarttanavarttanamvartanavartanam gṛhlāty anupayukto vā; ‘he deserves death,’ badhārho'sti, maraṇocito'sti. The future pass. part. may sometimes be used in this sense; as, ‘he deserves death,’ badhyo'sti; ‘he deserves honour,’ mānyo'sti, pūjanīyo'sti.
