FROM, prep. In phrases where it denotes procession, deriva- tion, deliverance, extraction, separation, distance, emis- sion, removal, the reason or motive of any act, and the ground or cause of any thing, it is usually expressed by the abl. case. It is also expressed in some phrases by such words as sakāśātsakāśāt, madhyātmadhyāt, prabhṛtiprabhṛti, ārabhyaārabhya, or even by the prep. āā with the abl. c.
