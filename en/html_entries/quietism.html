QUIETISM, s. śamaḥśama, śāntiḥśānti f., nivṛttimārgaḥnivṛttimārga, vairāgyaṃvairāgyavairāgyam. According to the Vedānta system there are six stages of this state, or six duties to be performed by him who practices it, viz. śraddhāśraddhā, samādhānaṃsamādhānasamādhānam, titikṣātitikṣā, uparatiḥuparati f., damaḥdama, śamaḥśama.
