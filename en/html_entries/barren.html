BARREN, a. (As a woman) anapatyā, anapatyakā, ajātāpatyā, apra-apra jātā. The Hindūs divide barren women into three classes, viz. 1. ‘She who has no offspring,’ bandhyā; 2. ‘She who bears only one child,’ kākabandhyā; 3. ‘She whose off- spring die,’ mṛtabandhyā.
