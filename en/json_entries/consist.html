To CONSIST¦, v. n. saṃsthā (c. 1. -tiṣṭhati -sthātuṃ), parisaṃsthā, avasthā, viṣṭhā, sambhū, vṛt (c. 1. varttate -rttituṃ), saṃvṛt. — (Be comprised, be composed) antargataḥ -tā -taṃ bhū. But in this sense ātmakaḥ -kā -tmikā -kaṃ or rūpaḥ -pā -paṃ are used; as, ‘the army consists of elephants, horses, and infantry,’ hastyaśvapādātātmakaṃ balaṃ; ‘the country consists of valleys and mountains,’ darīparvvatarūpo viṣayaḥ.
