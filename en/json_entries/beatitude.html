BEATITUDE¦, s. muktiḥ f., mokṣaḥ, nirvāṇaṃ, siddhiḥ f., apavargaḥ, svargaprāptiḥ f., paramāgatiḥ f., śreyaḥ n. (s) or niḥśreyaḥ, amṛtatā, kaivalyaṃ. — (Felicity) paramasukhaṃ, paramānandaḥ, śreyastvaṃ, sukhaṃ. — (Desirous of) mumukṣuḥ. BEATITUDE¦, s. The Hindūs divide final beatitude into four kinds, viz. 1. ‘absorption into the essence of the deity,’ brahmasāyujyaṃ; 2. ‘residence with him,’ salokatā; 3. ‘nearness to him,’ samīpatā; 4. ‘conformedness to his image,’ sarūpatā.
