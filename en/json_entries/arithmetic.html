ARITHMETIC¦, s. vyaktaṃ, vyaktagaṇitaṃ, saṃkhyāvidyā; ‘the eight rules of arithmetic,’ parikarmmāṣṭakaṃ. These are, 1. Addition, saṅkalanaṃ; 2. Subtraction, vyavakalanaṃ; 3. Multiplication, guṇanaṃ; 4. Division, bhajanaṃ; 5. Raising to the square, vargaḥ; 6. Extracting the square-root, vargapadaṃ; 7. Raising to the cube, ghanaḥ; 8. Extracting the cube-root, ghanapadaṃ.
