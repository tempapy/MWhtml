SECONDARY¦, a. (Not primary) apradhānaḥ -nā -naṃ, amukhyaḥ -khyā -khyaṃ, aprathamaḥ -mā -maṃ, aprāgryaḥ -gryā -gryaṃ, anuṣaṅgī -ṅgiṇī &c., anuṣaṅgikaḥ -kī -kaṃ, gauṇaḥ -ṇī -ṇaṃ, gauṇikaḥ -kī -kaṃ, upasṛṣṭaḥ -ṣṭā -ṣṭaṃ, guṇabhūtaḥ -tā -taṃ; ‘secondary rank,’ apradhānatā, apradhānapadaṃ; ‘any thing secondary,’ apradhānaṃ, upasarjanaṃ. — (Not first rate) madhyamaḥ -mā -maṃ, madhyamīyaḥ -yā -yaṃ, madhyamaguṇakaḥ -kā -kaṃ, viguṇaḥ -ṇā -ṇaṃ. The prepositions upa and anu often give the sense of ‘secondary;’ as, ‘a secondary planet,’ upagrahaḥ; ‘a secondary passion or flavour,’ uparasaḥ, anurasaḥ; ‘symptom,’ anubandhaḥ.
