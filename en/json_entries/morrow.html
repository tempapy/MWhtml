MORROW¦, s. (The day next after the present) paradivasaḥ, paradinaṃ, śvastanadivasaḥ, āgāmidivasaḥ, kalyaṃ; ‘to-morrow, on the morrow,’ śvas, paredyus, paradine, paradivase, paredyavi, kalli, kalye, anyedyus. — (The next day subsequent to any day) uttaradivasaḥ, uttaradinaṃ, uttaredyus; ‘the day after to-morrow,’ paraśvasa; ‘to-morrow morning,’ śvaḥ prabhāte.
