• And, [014] conj. ca repeated after each word joined, or after all. In the case of nouns, ex. by Dvandva comps.; ‘the Sun and the Moon’ ādityacaṃdrau. 2 (With adjectives) gen. ex. by Karm. comps.; alasalulitamugdhāni aṃgakāni; (with numerals) by a comp. with adhika, uttara; ‘100 a. 8’ aṣṭādhikaṃ-aṣṭottaraṃ śataṃ. 3 (In the case of verbs) ex. by the use of gerunds; ‘go a. tell him’ gatvā tasmai kathaya; sometimes by vā in questions; jāta asti te mātā smarasi vā tātaṃ (U. 4). 4 api ca, anyacca, kiṃ ca. ‘A. then’ atha, tadanu, tataḥ; ‘a. yet,’ tathāpi, or ex. by ca-ca; asulabhā sakaleṃdu- -mukhī ca sā kimapi cedamanaṃgaviceṣṭitaṃ (V. 2).
