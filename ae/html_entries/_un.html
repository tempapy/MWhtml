• Un, [479] A prefix, ex. by a (an before vowels), sometimes by nir, vi, dus pr., hīna, rahita in comp.; ‘unaided’ asahāya, sāhāyyahīna; ‘undaunted’ nirbhīka, abhaya; ‘unmixed’ a- -miśra; ‘unwelcome’ apriya, anabhimata &c. ‘unattainable’ duṣprāpa (In cases where the word formed with ‘un’ is not given, the student should take the simple word or form it, and use the above prefixes. With nouns the sense of ‘un’ may be expressed by apa-nī 1 P, or hṛ 1 P; e. g. ‘unbar’ argalaṃ apanī, niragalīkṛ &c.).
