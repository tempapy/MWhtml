• ajita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Unconquered.
    2 Unexcelled, unsurpassed. m. (-taḥ)
    1 The second of the twenty-four principal Jaina Tīrthakaras or deified sages.
    2 A name of VIṢṆU.
    3 A name of BUDDHA.
    4 A name of ŚIVA.
    E. a neg. and jita, participle of ji to conquer. [013]
