• gāthā f. (-thā)
    1 A verse, a stanza.
    2 Metre, rhythm.
    3 A song, a chaunt or verse to be chaunted or sung.
    4 Prākṛta or any language not Sanscrit.
    6 The same as the Arya Metre, in which the verse contains sixty syllabic instants, variously arranged.
    E. gai to sing, than Uṇādi aff.
