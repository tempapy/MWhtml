• vyatīpāta [814] m. (-taḥ) 1 Great and portentous calamity, or a portent, indicating or occasioning it and therefore identified with it, as a comet, an earthquake, &c. 2 Disrespect, contempt. 3 The seventeenth of the astrological Yogas. 4 Day of new moon when it falls on a Sunday, and the moon is in certain mansions, Śravaṇā, &c. E. vi and ati before pata to fall, &c., aff. ghaña.
