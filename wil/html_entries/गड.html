• gaḍa [277] r. 1st cl. (gaḍati) To distil or drop, to run as a liquid; also, gala. (i) gaḍi r. 1st cl. (gaṇḍati) To affect the cheek, to be rough as the cheek, (obsolete.)
  • gaḍa [277] m. (-ḍa) 1 A kind of fish, the young of the Ophiocephalus lata, HAM. 2 Another species, (Cyprinus garra. HAM.) see gala. 3 An impediment, an obstacle. 4 A screen, a covering, a fence. 5 A moat, a ditch. 6 A country, part of Malva, Gerha or Gera Mendela. E. gaḍa to drop, affix ac.
