• mātula m. (-laḥ)
    1 A maternal uncle.
    2 Thorn apple, (Datura metel.)
    3 A sort of grain.
    4 A variegated snake. f. (-lā-lī or- lānī)
    1 The wife of a maternal uncle, &c.
    2 Hemp, (Cannabis sativa.)
    3 Common Bengal San, a sort of Crotolaria, (C. juncea.) (-lānī) Pulse of various kinds.
    E. mātṛ a mother and ḍulac aff., fem. aff. ṭāp or ṅīṣ with ānuk optionally inserted before the latter. [655]
