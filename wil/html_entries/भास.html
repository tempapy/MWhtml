• bhāsa(ṛ)bhāsṛ [619] r. 1st cl. (bhāsate) To shine.
    With pra and ni prefixed, To appear to, to seem likely or probable to.
  • bhāsa [619] mf. (-saḥ-sā) Light, lustre, shining. m. (-saḥ) 1 A vulture. 2 A sort of bird, described as a water-fowl. 3 A cock. 4 A station of cowherds. E. bhāsa to shine, ap aff.
