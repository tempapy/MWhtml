• bhāsa(ṛ)bhāsṛ r. 1st cl. (bhāsate) To shine.
    With pra and ni prefixed, To appear to, to seem likely or probable to. [619]
  • bhāsa mf. (-saḥ-sā) Light, lustre, shining. m. (-saḥ)
    1 A vulture.
    2 A sort of bird, described as a water-fowl.
    3 A cock.
    4 A station of cowherds.
    E. bhāsa to shine, ap aff. [619]
