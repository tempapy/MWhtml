• prakara [556] mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) Who or what does much or well. m. (-raḥ) 1 A heap, a quantity. 2 A bundle of flowers, a nosegay. 3 Aid, assistance, friendship. 4 Usage, custom, continuance of a similar practice. 5 Respect. 6 Seduction, abduction. n. (-raṃ) Aloe wood, (Agallochum.) f. (-rī) 1 Theatrical dress or disguise, as wearing a woman's garb, &c. 2 An episodical incident in a drama. 3 An open piece of ground, the meeting of four roads, or the proper site of any magical operations. E. pra before kṛ to make, aff. ṭa.
