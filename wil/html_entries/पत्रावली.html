• patrāvalī f. (-lī)
    1 Drawing lines along the form; staining some parts of the body with coloured and fragrant pigments, as with saffron, Sandal, &c.
    2 A mixture of young Aśvattha leaves with barley and honey.
    E. patra a leaf, and āvalī a line: see patralekhā, patraka, &c. [499]
