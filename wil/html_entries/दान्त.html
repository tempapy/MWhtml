• dānta mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntā-ntaṃ)
    1 Tamed, subdued, daunted.
    2 Bearing patiently privation, austerity, &c.
    3 Dental. m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 A donor, a giver.
    2 A well situated about the peak of a mountain.
    E. dama to tame, to pacify, affix kta, or danta a peak, &c. affix aṇ.
