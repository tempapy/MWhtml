• ahi m. (-hiḥ)
    1 A snake or serpent.
    2 The sun.
    3 A traveller.
    4 Lead.
    5 The name of a demon: see vṛtrāsura.
    6 A name of RĀHU, the ascending node.
    E. āṅ prefixed to hana to hurt, in Uṇādi affix; injuring all or every thing, the ā of āṅ is made short. [101]
