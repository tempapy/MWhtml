• varttana [738] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Fixed, stationary, stable. nf. (-naṃ-nī) 1 Staying, abiding, being, the property of a fixed presence. 2 A road, a way. 3 A ball at one end of a spindle to assist its rotatory motion. 4 Grinding. n. (-naṃ) 1 Livelihood, subsistence. 2 Occupation, profession. 3 Salary, hire, wages. 4 A ball of cotton from which the threads are spun. 5 A ball, a globe. 6 Appointing. 7 Turning. m. (-naḥ) A dwarf. E. vṛta to be, to be fixed, &c, aff. lyuṭ or yuc.
