• kāraṇottara n. (-raṃ) Special plea, denial of the cause of complaint, admission of the charge generally, but denial of the issue; as to a demand of debt, acknowledgment that it was incurred, but assertion that the loan has been repaid, &c.
    E. kāraṇ the cause, and uttara answer.
