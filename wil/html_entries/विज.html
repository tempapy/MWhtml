• vija(au ira)au vijira r. 3rd cl. (vivekti)
    1 To be distinct or separate, to differ.
    2 To separate or divide, especially by reason; to judge, to distinguish, to discriminate. (o, ī,) ovijī r. 6th cl. (vijate, or usually
    with ud prefixed udvijate) also r. 7th cl. (vinakti)
    1 To fear, to be afraid.
    2 To tremble with fear.
    3 To shake or tremble.
    4 To be agitated or distressed.
