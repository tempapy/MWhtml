• akṣayatṛtīyā f. (-yā) The third day of the lunar half of the month Vaiśākha. (April-May.)
    E. akṣaya durable, and tṛtīyā the third day: the consequences of meritorious actions performed on this day being permanent, as it is the first day of the Satya Yuga or the anniversary of creation. [004]
