• mūla r. 1st cl. (mūlati-te) To stand, to be rooted or firm. r. 10th cl. (mūlayati)
    1 To plant or transplant.
    2 To grow as a plant, to sprout, to shoot or germinate.
    With ud prefixed, to root up or out, to eradicate, to exterminate. [668]
  • mūla n. (-laṃ)
    1 A root, the root of a tree, &c.
    2 Origin, commencement.
    3 Capital, principal.
    4 Near, proximate.
    5 Own, peculiar, proper.
    6 The root of the Arum campanulatum.
    7 The original text of any work, as opposed to the Ṭīkā or comment. mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) The nineteenth lunar asterism, containing eleven stars, which appear to be the same as those in the SCORPION'S tail. f. (-lī) A small house lizard.
    E. mūla to stand, to be rooted or firm, aff. ka, or mū to bind, Uṇādi aff. kla. [668]
