• abhi [052] ind. A preposition and particle implying, 1 Superiority in place, rank, or power, (over, upon, against, above:) 2 Proximity, (near:) 3 Separation, (severally:) 4 Wish, desire: 5 Conjunction, particularizing, (to, with respect to.) Thus abhikramituṃ to overpower; abhigantuṃ to approach; abhikhyātuṃ to speak to; abhibhava disgrace; abhilāṣa desire; abhyagniṃ on the fire, &c. E. a neg. bhā to shine, and ki aff.
