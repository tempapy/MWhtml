• bhaṭa r. 1st cl. (bhaṭati)
    1 To hold or cherish.
    2 To hire or maintain.
    3 To speak. (i) bhaṭi r. 10th cl. (bhaṃṭayati) To deceive.
  • bhaṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 A warrior, a soldier, a combatant.
    2 A barbarian, or outcast of a particular tribe.
    3 A goblin.
    E. bhaṭa to maintain, aff. ac.
