• viṣṭi [792] mfn. (-ṣṭiḥ-ṣṭiḥ-ṣṭi) Working, labouring, a workman. f. (-ṣṭiḥ) 1 Unpaid labour, working without wages. 2 Occupation, act, action. 3 Hire, wages. 4 The seventh of the variable Karaṇas, or astrological periods so termed, each answering to half a lunar day. 5 Sending, dispatching. 6 Consigning to torture, casting into hell, &c. E. viśa to enter, (into pain by it,) and ktin or ktic aff.
