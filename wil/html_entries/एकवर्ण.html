• ekavarṇa [171] mfn. (-rṇaḥ-rṇā-rṇaṃ) 1 Of one caste or tribe. 2 Of one colour. 3 Of one quality. m. (-rṇaḥ) One unknown quantity, (in algebra.) f. (-rṇī) Beating time, marking musical measure by clapping the hands, or the instrument which does so, a castanet: see ekatālī. E. eka, varṇa a quality, a note, and ṅīṣ aff.
