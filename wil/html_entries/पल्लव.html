• pallava mn. (-vaḥ-vaṃ)
    1 A sprout, a shoot, the extremity of a branch bearing new leaves.
    2 A branch.
    3 Spreading, expansion.
    4 A wood.
    5 The red dye of lac.
    6 Love, the sentiment or passion.
    7 A catamite.
    8 A bracelet.
    9 Unsteadiness, moral or physical.
    E. pad the foot, lū to cut or break, ap aff. [519]
