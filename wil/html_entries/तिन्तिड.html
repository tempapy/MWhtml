• tintiḍa mf. (-ḍaḥ-ḍī) The tamarind tree, (Tamarindus Indica.) m. (-ḍaḥ)
    1 The name of a demon.
    2 Acid seasoning, sour sauce.
    3 An inauspicious period, the evil aspect of planets, &c. f. (-ḍī) Emblic myrobalan.
    E. tima to be damp or moist, ac affix, the root reduplicated, and ḍa substituted for the final ma. [376]
