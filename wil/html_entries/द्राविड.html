• drāviḍa m. (-ḍaḥ)
    1 A country, properly the coast of Coromandel from Madras to Cape Comorin, or the country in which Tamul is spoken.
    2 A native of Drāvira or Drāviḍa.
    3 A Brahman of Dravira, or rather of the south, five Drāviras being specified, or Drāvira, Karṇāṭa, Gujjerāta, Mahārāṣṭra, and Telanga.
    4 A particular number.
    5 Zedoary.
    E. draviḍa, and aṇ aff.
