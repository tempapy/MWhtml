• madhyadeśa m. (-śaḥ) The middle region; part of India, bounded by Kurukṣetra on the north, Allahabad on the south, the Himālaya mountains on the east, and the Vindhya mountains on the west; comprising therefore the modern provinces of Allahabad, Agra, Delhi, Oude, &c. the northern limit is elsewhere defined to be the disappearance of the Sarasvatī.
    E. bhadhya middle, and deśa country. [639]
