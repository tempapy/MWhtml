• badha r. 1st cl. (bībhatsate)
    1 To bind or tie.
    2 To hate, to despise, to loath.
    3 To suffer change or affection of the mind. r. 1st and 10th cls. (badhati bādhayati)
    1 To tie, to bind.
    2 To kill.
  • badha m. (-dhaḥ)
    1 Killing, slaughter, slaying, murder.
    2 A killer, a slaughter.
    E. badha substituted for hana to kill, aff. ghañ.
