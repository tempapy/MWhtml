• badha [598] r. 1st cl. (bībhatsate) 1 To bind or tie. 2 To hate, to despise, to loath. 3 To suffer change or affection of the mind. r. 1st and 10th cls. (badhati bādhayati) 1 To tie, to bind. 2 To kill.
