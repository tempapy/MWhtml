• āli mfn. sub. (-liḥ-liḥ) or lī-li
    1 Pure, honest, sincere.
    2 Useless, idle, unmeaning. f. (-liḥ-lī)
    1 A woman's female friend.
    2 A row or range, a continuous line.
    3 A ridge or mound of earth, crossing ditches, dividing fields, &c.
    4 A line, a race or family. m. (-liḥ)
    1 A scorpion.
    2 A bee: see ali.
    E. ala to adorn, to be able, &c. affix iṇa and the pen. made long; also ālī, affix ṅīp. [121]
