• kadru mfn. (-druḥ-druḥ-dru) Tawny. f. (-druḥ) The wife of KAŚYAPA the saint, and mother of the Nāgas or the serpent race, inhabiting the regions below the earth. m. (-druḥ) Tawny, (the colour.)
    E. kabha to desire, ḍu affix, and ra inserted; the final of the radical is irregularly changed to da; fem. affix ūñ. [186]
