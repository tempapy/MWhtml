• prākṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Low, common, vulgar, thence especially applicable to a provincial and peculiar dialect of the Sanskrit language.
    2 Natural.
    3 Belonging to or derived from the philosophical Prakṛti, illusory, material, &c. m. (-taḥ) A low man or one following a degraded profession. n. (-taṃ) Any dialect not Sanskrit, it is especially spoken by the female characters and the inferior personages of plays.
    E. prakati nature, and aṇ aff., or pra pre-eminently, and akṛta not made. [584]
