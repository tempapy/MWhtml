• samasya mfn. (-syaḥ-syā-syaṃ)
    1 To be collected together.
    2 To be made entire or complete. f. (-syā) Part of a stanza, proposed as a trial of skill to be completed.
    E. sama even or complete, kyac affix, and suk augment; or sam together, asa to direct, ṇyat aff.
