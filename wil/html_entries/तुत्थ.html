    1 To screen, to veil, to cover.
    2 To spread.
  • tuttha m. (-tthaḥ) Fire. f. (-tthā)
    1 Indigo.
    2 Small cardamoms. n. (-tthaṃ)
    1 A collyrium extracted from the Amomum zanthorrhiza.
    2 Blue vitriol, sulphate of copper, especially medicinally considered as an Añjana, or application to the eyes.
    E. tuda to pain, thak Uṇādi aff.
