• vāta [749] r. 1st cl. (vātayati) 1 To go. 2 To serve. 3 To be happy. 4 To give pleasure in travelling. 5 To blow gently, to fan or ventilate.
  • vāta [749] m. (-taḥ) 1 Air, wind. 2 Rheumatism, gout, inflammation of the joints. 2 Air, wind, as one of the humours of the body. E. vā to go, Uṇādi aff. tan.
