• panasa m. (-saḥ)
    1 The bread fruit or Jaka tree, (Artocarpus integrifolia.)
    2 A thorn.
    3 The name of a monkey. f. (-sā) A malady, pustular and phlegmonoid inflammation of the skin or external organs.
    2 A she monkey.
    3 A female demon.
    E. pana to praise or be praised, Uṇādi aff. asac. [501]
