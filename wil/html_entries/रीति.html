• rīti f. (-tiḥ-tī)
    1 Usage, custom, practice, local or traditionary observance.
    2 Oozing, leaking, distilling.
    3 Brass, pale brass, or prince's metal.
    4 Rust of iron.
    5 Calx of brass.
    6 Scoria of any metal, or the oxide formed on the surface of metals by their exposure to heat and air.
    7 Boundary, limit.
    8 Going, motion.
    9 Natural property or disposition.
    E. rī to go, to run out, &c., aff. ktin. [706]
