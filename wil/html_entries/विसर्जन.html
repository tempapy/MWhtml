• visarjana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Gift, donation.
    2 Quitting, relinquishing.
    3 Sending, dispatching.
    4 Dismissing, sending away.
    5 Throwing the image, &c. into holy water, as the concluding rite of the festival of a deity.
    E. vi before sṛja to let go, aff. lyuṭ. [794]
