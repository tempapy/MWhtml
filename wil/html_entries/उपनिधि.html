• upanidhi m. (-dhiḥ) A deposit, a pledge, property put into the care of a creditor, friend, &c.
    E. upa and ni before dhā to have, ki affix; in law this word ordinarily implies more especially a sealed or enclosed deposit, but according to some, it is any article entrusted to a friend which he may use whilst in his keeping.
