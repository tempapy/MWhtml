• toraṇa [386] mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ) 1 The ornamented arch of a door or gateway. 2 An outer door. 3 Decoration of the gate post. 4 A temporary and ornamental arch. 5 A mound or raised mark near a bathing place. 6 A triangle supporting a large balance. n. (-ṇaṃ) The neck, the throat. E. tura to hasten, affix lyuṭ; by which people pass or go.
