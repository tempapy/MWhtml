• veṇa (ṛ) veṇṛ r. 1st cl. (veṇati-te)
    1 To go, to move.
    2 To know.
    3 To reflect or remember.
    4 To discern.
    5 To hold or take a musical instrument.
    6 To play on an instrument.
    7 To take. The root is also read venṛ.
  • veṇa m. (-ṇaḥ) A musician, one by birth, the son of a woman of the Ambaṣṭha, and man of the Vaideha caste.
    E. veṇa to play on a musical instrument, aff. ac.
