• ṇa [361] The nasal Ṇ belonging to the third or cerebral class of consonants; in derivation and inflection this is usually changed to na or the dental N, and hence the derivatives from the following radicals occur under that letter.
  • ṇa [361] m. (ṇaḥ) 1 Knowledge. 2 Certainty, ascertainment. 3 A name of ŚIVA. 4 Vindudeva, said to be a Jaina deity. 5 An ornament. 6 A water or summer house. 7 One without good qualities. 8 A kind of sound, the sound of negation. 9 Gift, giving. E. ṇī to guide, &c. affix ḍa.
