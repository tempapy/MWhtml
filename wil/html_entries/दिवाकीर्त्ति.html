• divākīrtti m. (-rttiḥ)
    1 A man of a low or impure caste, a Caṇḍāla.
    2 A barber.
    3 An owl.
    E. divā by day, and kīrtti credit, (the barber is not allowed to operate at night,) or divā day, akīrtti disrepute; the Caṇḍāla and owl coming abroad only in the dark. [409]
