• māṇavaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A child, a boy not exceeding sixteen years of age, a manikin.
    2 A pupil, a scholar, a religious student.
    3 A man, an ignorant or ridiculous man, a childish man.
    4 A necklace of twenty strings.
    5 A species of the Anuṣṭubh metre.
    E. kan added to the last.
