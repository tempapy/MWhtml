• taraṇḍa [368] mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā or ṇḍī-ṇḍaṃ) A boat, a vessel. mn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍaṃ) 1 A raft or float, made of bambus, &c. tied together, and sometimes floated upon jars or hollow gourds inverted. 2 The float of a fishing line. 3 An oar. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A country. E. tṝ to cross, aṇḍan aff.
