• pañjikā f. (-kā)
    1 The ball or skein of cotton, from which the thread is spun: see the last.
    2 A perpetual commentary.
    3 A calendar, an almanack.
    4 A journal, a register.
    5 The register or journal of YAMA, the record of human actions.
    E. kan added to pañji; also piñjikā.
