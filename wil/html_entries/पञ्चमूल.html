• pañcamūla [494] n. (-laṃ) The assemblage of five roots, the Bel, Premna longifolia, Cassia, Gmelina arborea, and the Trumpet flower. f. (-lī) A similar aggregate of five roots, considered as the smaller one, viz. Hedysarum gangeticum, H. lagopodioides, Solanum melongena, S. jacquini, and Tribulus lanuginosus. E. pañca five, mūla a root; the fem. aff. has a diminutive import.
