• āśrita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Inhabiting, dwelling in, resorting to as a retreat or asylum.
    2 Following, practising, observing.
    3 Using, employing, having recourse to.
    4 Receiving any thing, as an inherent or integral part.
    5 Taking one's station at or on, as at a window or a seat, &c.
    6 A dependant.
    E. āṅ before śri to serve, kta aff.
