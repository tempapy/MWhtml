• dhavala [441] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 White. 2 Handsome, beautiful. m. (-laḥ) 1 White, (the colour.) 2 A capital ox. 3 The Grislea tomentosa. 4 Camphor. 5 An inferior Rāga or mode of music. n. (-laṃ) White pepper. f. (-lā or -lī) A white cow, &c. E. dhāva to be clean or pure, kalac affix, and the radical vowel made short.
