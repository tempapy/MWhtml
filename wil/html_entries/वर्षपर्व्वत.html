• varṣaparvvata m. (-taḥ) A mountainous range, supposed to separate the various Varṣas or divisions of the globe from each other: six ranges are enumerated from south to north; viz. Himavān, Hemakūṭa, Niṣadha, Nīla, Śveta, and Śṛṅgī or Śṛṅgavān; Meru constitutes the seventh; other names occur.
    E. varṣa as above, and parvvata a mountain. [740]
