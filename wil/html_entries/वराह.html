• varāha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A hog.
    2 A name of VIṢṆU in the third Avatāra or descent, in which he assumed the shape of a boar.
    3 A mountain.
    4 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus.)
    5 A sort of measure.
    6 One of the eighteen smaller Dvīpas or divisions of the universe.
    E. vara best, hana to injure, with āṅ prefix, and ḍa aff. [736]
