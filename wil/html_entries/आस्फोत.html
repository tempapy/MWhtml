• āsphota m. (-taḥ)
    1 Swallow wort, (Asclepias gigantea.)
    2 Mountain ebony, (Bauhinia variegata, &c.)
    3 A kind of echites or savanna flower, (E. dichotoma, Rox.) f. (-tā)
    1 A wild variety of jasmin.
    2 Clitoria ternatea.
    E. āṅ, sphuṭa to blow or expend, ac affix and ṭa changed to ta; or sphai to encrease, with kta affix, and ā converted to o; also sometimes āsphoṭa and āsphoṭā. [127]
