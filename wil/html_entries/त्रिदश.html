• tridaśa m. (-śaḥ) A god, a deity, an immortal.
    E. tri the third, and daśā state, being, (youth;) enjoying perpetual youth; or tri three, and daśa state, subject as well as mortals to the three conditions, of birth, being, and destruction.
  • tridaśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ) Thirteenth. f. (-śī) The thirteenth day of the fortnight; also trayodaśa.
