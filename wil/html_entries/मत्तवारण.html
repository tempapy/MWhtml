• mattavāraṇa [635] m. (-ṇaḥ) A furious elephant, or one in rut. n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 A painted wooden turret or veranda, on the top of a large building or palace. 2 An enclosure of trees, &c. round the walls of a princely residence. 3 Pounded betle nut. E. matta wild, and vāraṇṇa an elephant, or fence.
