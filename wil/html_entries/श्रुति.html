• śruti f. (-tiḥ)
    1 The Vedas, severally or collectively, scripture, holy writ.
    2 An ear.
    3 Hearing.
    4 Intelligence, news, rumour, report.
    5 (In music,) A division of the octave, a quarter tone or interval, of which twenty-two are enumerated; four constituting a major tone, three a minor, and two a semitone: the Śrutis are personified as nymphs.
    6 The constellation Śravaṇā.
    E. śru to hear, ktin aff.
