• sarala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Honest, sincere, candid, upright.
    2 Straight. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A sort of pine, (Pinus longifolia.)
    2 A bird, (Pavo bicalcarata.) f. (-lā) A variety of the plant called Teori.
    E. sṛ to go, (to spread fragrance abroad, &.,) kala Uṇādi aff.
