• kakkola [178] m. (-laḥ) A perfume, a plant bearing a berry, the inner part of which is a waxy and aromatic substance. n. (-laṃ) The berry, -possibly the fruit of the Cocculus Indicus. E. kaka to be proud, with kvip affix, kula to accumulate, ac affix; also with kan added kakkolaka (-kaḥ-kaṃ.)
