• kaṃsa [177] m. (-saḥ) A proper name; KAṂSA king of Mathura the uncle and enemy of KṚṢṆA, by whom he was slain; as the foe of the deity, he is considered an Asura or demon. mn. (-saḥ-saṃ) 1 A goblet, a drinking vessel; also kaṃśa and kāṃsya. 2 A metal, tutanag or white copper; also bell metal. 3 A measure. E. kamu to desire, saḥ Uṇādi aff.
