• vyāghra m. (-ghraḥ)
    1 A tiger.
    2 (In composition,) Best, pre-eminent.
    3 A variety of the castor oil plant.
    4 A tree, (Galedupa arborea.) f. (-ghrī) A prickly sort of nightshade, (Solanum jacquini.)
    E. vi and āṅ before ghrā to smell, Uṇādi aff. ka. [818]
