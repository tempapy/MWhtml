• gocara [298] m. (-raḥ) 1 An object of sense, as sound, shape, colour, &c. 2 A country, a district. 3 The house or mansion of a planet, or its presence in any sign which is that of a person's nativity. 4 Pasturage. E. go an organ of sense, &c. and cara what goes, from cara to go, gha aff.
