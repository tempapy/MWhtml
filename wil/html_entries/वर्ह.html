• varha r. 1st cl. (varhate) r. 10th cl. (varhayati)
    1 To speak or tell.
    2 To kill or hurt. r. 1st cl. (varhate)
    1 To cover.
    2 To be excellent or pre-eminent: in this sense it is more properly varha. r. 10th cl. (varhayati) To shine. [740]
  • varha n. (-rhaṃ)
    1 A peacock's tail.
    2 A leaf.
    3 A sort of perfume, commonly Ganṭhiāla.
    4 Train, retinue.
    E. vṛha to increase, aff. ac; also varha or barha to cover, &c. aff. ac; in the latter case it is written barha. [740]
