• asura mf. (-raḥ-rā) An Asura or demon: the Asuras are children of DITI by KAŚYAPA; they are demons of the first order, and in perpetual hostility with the gods. m. (-raḥ) The sun. f. (-rā)
    1 Night.
    2 A zodiacal sign.
    3 A prostitute. f. (-rī)
    1 Black mustard, (Sinapis ramosa.)
    2 The wife of an Asura.
    E. a neg. and sura a deity; or as to send, to cast, &c. and uran Uṇādi affix, ṭāp and ṅīp for the fem.; or a neg. and surā spirituous liquor, personified as a damsel produced at the churning of the ocean, who was rejected by the demons and received by the gods.
