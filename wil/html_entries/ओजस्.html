• ojas [175] n. (-jaḥ) 1 Light, splendor. 2 Manifestation, appearance. 3 Strength. 4 Support, stay. 5 Metallic lustre. 6 Vitality, the principle of vital warmth and action diffused throughout the body. 7 Virility, the generative faculty. 8 A form of style, elaborate style, abounding with compounds. 9 (In astrology,) Each alternate sign of the zodiac, first, third, fifth, &c. E. oja to live, and asun affix; or with ac affix oja.
