• sandha mfn. (-ndhaḥ-ndhā-ndhaṃ)
    1 Possessing as an integrant part, intimately blended with.
    2 Holding, possessing, having placed in or on.
    3 Joined, united. f. (-ndhā)
    1 Promise, assent, agreement.
    2 State, condition, the steady continuance in any state, steadiness, fixation.
    3 Intimate union or association, identification.
    4 Twilight.
    E. sam together, dhā to have or hold, aff. ka, or aṅ and ṭāp fem. aff. [889]
