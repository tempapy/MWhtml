• kūpaka [239] m. (-kaḥ) 1 The mast of a vessel. 2 A stake, &c. to which a boat is moored. 3 A leather oil vessel. 4 The hollow below the loins. 5 A funeral pile. 6 A well. 7 A temporary well, a hole dug for water in the dry bed of a rivulet. 8 A tree or rock in the middle of a stream. E. kan added to the preceding.
