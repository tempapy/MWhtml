• vikṣīṇaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 The chief of a class of demigods, enumerated amongst those attached to ŚIVA, and the ministers of destruction amongst men by plague, pestilence, &c.
    2 A meeting or assembly of the gods.
    3 A place prohibited to eaters of meat.
    E. vi before kṣīṇa wasted, (by whom,) and kan added. [760]
