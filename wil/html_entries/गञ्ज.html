• gañja mfn. subst. (-ñjaḥ-ñjā-ñjaṃ) A mine, a jewel mine. mn. (-ñjaḥ-ñjaṃ)
    1 A cow-house or station of cowherds.
    2 A treasury, a jewel room, the place where plate, &c. is preserved.
    3 A mart, a place where grain, &c. is stored for sale. m. (-ñjaḥ) Disrespect, contempt. f. (-ñjā)
    1 A tavern.
    2 A drinking vessel.
    3 A hut, a hovel, the abode of low people.
    4 The Gunja or Ratti, (Abrus precatorius:) see guñjā.
    E. gaji to sound, ac affix; fem. affix ṭāp.
