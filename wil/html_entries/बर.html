• bara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Best, excellent. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A boon, a blessing, &c.
    2 A son-in-law.
    3 Surrounding, encompassing.
    4 A catamite. n. (-raṃ) Saffron. f. (-ra,) Asparagus racemosus. f. (-rā) The three myrobalans. n. or ind. (-raṃ) Slightly desired, preferable.
    E. bṝ to select, &c. ap aff; the root is more usually written vṝ, and the derivative vara, q. v.
