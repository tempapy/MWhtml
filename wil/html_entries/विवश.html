• vivaśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ)
    1 Subject, subjected.
    2 Independant.
    3 Apprehensive of death, unwilling though about to die.
    4 Desirous of death, sedate at that period, or having the soul free from worldly cares and fears.
    5 Uncontrouled, unrestrained, unsubdued.
    E. vi before vaśa to wish, aff. ac.
