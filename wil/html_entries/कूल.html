• kūla r. 1st cl. (kūlati) To cover, to hide, to screen, to enclose: with anu prefixed, to protect, to be kind to, as to a follower or dependant. [239]
  • kūla n. (-laṃ)
    1 A bank, a shore.
    2 A heap, a mound.
    3 A pond or pool.
    4 The rear of an army.
    E. kūla to cover, ac aff. [239]
