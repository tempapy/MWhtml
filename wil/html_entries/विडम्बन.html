• viḍambana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Afflicting, distressing.
    2 Vexation, mortification.
    3 Frustrating, rendering vain.
    4 Imitation, copying, especially assuming a similar appearance or dress, disguise, masquerade.
    5 The supernatural assumption of a borrowed form, transformation.
    E. vi before ḍavi to heap, aff. yuc.
