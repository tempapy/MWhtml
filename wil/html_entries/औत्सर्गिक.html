• autsargika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Omitting, leaving.
    2 Terminative, completing, relating or belonging to a final ceremony, by which the rite is dismissed.
    3 Natural, inherent.
    4 Derivative, produced from or of, directly and naturally.
    E. utsarga abandoned, and ṭhañ aff.
