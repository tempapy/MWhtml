• abhivādana n. (-naṃ) Respectful salutation, a bow or prostration; including sometimes the name or title of the person so addressed, and followed by the declaration of the person's own name. Salutation of a superior or elder, by a junior or inferior, and especially of the teacher by his disciple. In general it is merely lifting the joined hands to the forehead, and saying, ahamamivādaye I salute.
    E. abhi in presence of, and vādana speaking. [055]
