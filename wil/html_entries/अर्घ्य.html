• arghya mfn. (-rghyaḥ-rghyā-rghyaṃ) Venerable, deserving worship. n. (-rghyaṃ) A respectful oblation to gods or venerable men, of rice, dūrbbā grass, flowers, &c. with water, and of water only in a small boat-shaped vessel. n. (-rghyaṃ) Wild honey.
    E. argha, and ya or yat aff. [069]
