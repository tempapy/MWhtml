• ma [630] The twenty-fifth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, corresponding to the letter M.
  • ma [630] m. (-maḥ) 1 A name of BRAHMĀ. 2 ŚIVA. 3 VIṢṆU. 4 YAMA. 5 Time, season. 6 Poison, venom. 7 A magical or mystical verse or formula. 8 The moon. 9 The abbreviated name of the fourth note of the gamut. f. (mā) 1 A name of LAKṢMĪ. 2 A mother. 3 Measure. 4 Light, lustre. 5 Knowledge. 6 Binding, tying. 7 Death. 8 A woman's waist. n. (maṃ) 1 Good fortune, auspiciousness. 2 Water. E. mā to measure, or mana to worship, &c., aff. ḍa.
