• kiśora mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Young, infantine. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A colt.
    2 The sun.
    3 Benjamin, (Styrax benzoin.)
    4 A youth, a lad, one from his birth to the end of his fifteenth year: a minor in law ajātavyavahāraḥ becoming, after his fifteenth year, subject to suits at law or jātavyavahāra.
    E. kim what, used contemptuously, śṛ to go, and oran Uṇādi affix; also kan added kiśorakaḥ [223]
