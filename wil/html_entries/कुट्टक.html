• kuṭṭaka [226] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) 1 What cuts. 2 What pounds or grinds. m. (-kaḥ) 1 A power in arithmetic or algebra, a quantity such, that a given number being multiplied by it, and the product added to a given quantity, the sum may be divisible without remainder by a given divisor. 2 A kingfisher. E. kuṭṭa to pound, vun aff.
