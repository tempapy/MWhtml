• aniruddha mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ)
    1 A spy, a secret emissary.
    2 Self willed, ungovernable, headstrong.
    3 Unobstructed, unrestrained. m. (-ddhaḥ) The son of PRADYUMNA, a form of KĀMA, and husband of UṢĀ. n. (-ddhaṃ) The rope for fastening cattle.
    E. a neg. niruddha restrained.
