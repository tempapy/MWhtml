• gaṇḍaśaila m. (-laḥ)
    1 A rock or rocky fragment fallen from a height, thrown down by an earthquake, a storm, &c.
    2 The forehead.
    E. gaṇḍa a cheek, and śaila a rock, the cheek of the mountain, or in the second instance, vice-versa, the mountain as it were of the cheek or face. [279]
