• pakva mfn. (-kvaḥ-kvā-kvaṃ)
    1 Mature, dressed, cooked, matured by nature or by art.
    2 Fully matured, on the eve of rottenness or decay.
    3 Perfect, come to perfection.
    4 Able, shrewd, experienced, mature, as the understanding.
    5 Grey, (the hair.)
    6 Digested.
    E. paca to cook or ripen, va substituted for kta aff. [490]
