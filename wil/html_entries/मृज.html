• mṛja(ū)mṛjū r. 2nd cl. (mārṣṭi) 1st and 10th cls. (mārjati mārjayati)
    1 To clean, to cleanse.
    2 To be clean or pure.
    3 To ornament.
    4 To sound.
    With apa or pra prefixed,
    1 To sweep, to sweep away.
    2 To clean. [670]
  • mṛja m. (-jaḥ) A musical instrument, probably a sort of tabor or drum. f. (-jā)
    1 Cleaning the body, by washing, anointing, &c.
    2 Purification, ablution.
    E. mṛja to cleanse, to sound, aff. ka or aṅ, and ṭāp aff. [670]
