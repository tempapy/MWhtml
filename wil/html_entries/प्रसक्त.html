• prasakta mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ)
    1 Eternal, constant, continual.
    2 Opened, expanded.
    3 Obtained, gained, attained.
    4 Attached to, devoted to, (person or thing,) engaged in.
    5 United to, connected with, accompanying.
    6 Applied to, used, employed. n. adv. (-ktaṃ) Continually, incessantly, eternally.
    E. pra before ṣañja to unite or embrace, aff. kta.
