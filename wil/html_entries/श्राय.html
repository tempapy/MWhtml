• śrāya mfn. (-yaḥ-yī-yaṃ) Sacred or belonging to the goddess ŚRĪ. m. (-yaḥ) Refuge, reliance, shelter or protection.
    E. śri to serve, aff. aṇ, or in the attributive form śrī the goddess, ghañ aff., and i substituted for the primitive vowel changed to aya, and a made long. [862]
