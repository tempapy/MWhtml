• vedana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Sensation, perception, knowledge conveyed by the senses.
    2 Pain, smart, agony.
    3 Knowledge.
    4 The ceremony of holding the ends of a mantle, to be observed by a Śūdra female, on her marriage with a man of either of the first three classes.
    5 Presenting, delivering. f. (-nī) The true skin or cutis.
    E. vida to know, &c., yuc aff.
