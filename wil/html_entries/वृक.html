• vṛka r. 1st cl. (varkate) To take or accept. [799]
  • vṛka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A wolf.
    2 A crow.
    3 The Vakapushpa tree, (Sesbana grandiflora.)
    4 A Shakal.
    5 Turpentine.
    6 Compounded perfume. f. (-kā) A plant, (Cissampelos hexandra.)
    E. vṛka to take, aff. ka, or vṛ to hide or screen, Uṇādi aff. kak. [799]
