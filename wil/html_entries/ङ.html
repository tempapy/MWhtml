• ṅa [312] The fifth consonant of the Sanskrit alphabet, and the nasal of the first class; the sound of this letter corresponds with that of the nasal N in Song, &c. or in the French word bon.
  • ṅa [312] m. (ṅaḥ) 1 An object of sense. 2 Desire, wish for any sensual object. E. ṅu to sound, affix ṅa.
