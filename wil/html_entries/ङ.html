• ṅa The fifth consonant of the Sanskrit alphabet, and the nasal of the first class; the sound of this letter corresponds with that of the nasal N in Song, &c. or in the French word bon. [312]
  • ṅa m. (ṅaḥ)
    1 An object of sense.
    2 Desire, wish for any sensual object.
    E. ṅu to sound, affix ṅa. [312]
