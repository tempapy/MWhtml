• aśvatara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Swift, speedy. mf. (-raḥ-rī) A mule. m. (-raḥ)
    1 One of the chiefs of the Nāgas or serpent race, inhabiting the regions under the earth.
    2 A Gandharba or celestial quirister.
    3 A male calf.
    E. aśva and tara from tṛ to pass, to go, affix ap; or ṣṭarac diminutive aff. [090]
