• smārtta mfn. (-rttaḥ-rttī-rttaṃ)
    1 Legal, canonical, according to the doctrines of the Smṛtis or inspired codes of law.
    2 Following or professing the doctrine of the law books.
    3 Memorial, relating to memory.
    4 Within memory, what may be remembered. m. (-rttaḥ) A Brahman following the revealed law.
    E. smṛti law collectively, and aṇ aff. of reference, &c. [958]
