• aghora [009] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Formidable, terrible. m. (-raḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 A worshipper of ŚIVA and DEVĪ in their terrific forms. f. (-rā) The fourteenth day of the dark half of the month Bhadra, upon which ŚIVA is worshipped. E. a implying resemblance, and dhora formidable.
