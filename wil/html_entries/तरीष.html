• tarīṣa [368] m. (-ṣaḥ) 1 The ocean. 2 Heaven or paradise. 3 Decorating, ornamenting. 4 A raft, a float. 5 Practice, business, profession. f. (-ṣī) 1 The daughter of INDRA. E. tṝ to cross or cause to cross, Uṇādi affix īṣan; in some of its senses this word and tabiṣa seem to be confounded: see tabiṣa and tāviṣa.
