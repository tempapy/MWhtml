• upakaraṇa [152] n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 Apparatus, paraphernalia, as the vessels and offerings at a sacrifice. 2 Implements, machines. 3 The insignia of royalty. 4 Sauces, condiments. 5 Helping, assisting. 6 Object of art or science, fabricating, composing, &c. 7 Means of subsistence, any thing supporting life. E. upa implying command or help, kṛ to make, lyuṭ aff.
