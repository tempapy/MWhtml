• paribhāṣā f. (-ṣā)
    1 A technicality, a conventional term in any science.
    2 (In Grammar,) A maxim given by the ancient grammarians as a summary interpretation of the rules of PĀṆINI.
    3 (In Medicine,) Prognosis.
    4 Abuse, ridicule.
    E. pari severally, and bhāṣā speech.
