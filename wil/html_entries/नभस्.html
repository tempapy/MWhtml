• nabhas [454] m. (-bhāḥ) 1 The month Śrāvaṇa, (July-August.) 2 A cloud. 3 The rains, or rainy season. 4 Smell. 5 The fibres in the root of the lotus. 6 A bird. 7 A grey-headed man. n. (-bhaḥ) Sky, atmosphere, æther or heaven. E. nabha to injure, to be annihilated, (at the end of the world,) Uṇādi affix asun, or naha to bind, and bha substituted for ha.
