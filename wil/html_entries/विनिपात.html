• vinipāta m. (-taḥ)
    1 Falling, falling down, (literally or figuratively, as from a height, or from dignity, virtue, &c.)
    2 Calamity, unavoidable evil, the infliction of the gods or of fate.
    3 Disrespect.
    4 Pain, distress.
    5 Death, destruction.
    E. vi and ni, prefixed to pata to fall, aff. ghañ. [772]
