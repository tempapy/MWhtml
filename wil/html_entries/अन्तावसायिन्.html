• antāvasāyin [039] m. (-yī) 1 A barber. 2 A man of a low tribe or cast, the offspring of a Niṣāda woman by a Caṇḍāla, whose business is to attend in places where dead bodies are burned. 3 The name of a saint. E. anta the end, ava and ṣo to cut, who pares the nails, &c. or ṣai to decay, ṇini aff.
