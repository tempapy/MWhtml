• dīkṣita [410] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Initiated. 2 Performed, (as the Dīkṣa ceremony.) 3 One by whom the preparatory ceremonies have been observed. m. (-taḥ) 1 An assemblage of priests for peculiar ceremonies, or for any sacrifice. 2 The pupil of an ascetick. E. dīkṣa to perform a sacrifice or to be initiated, affix kta.
