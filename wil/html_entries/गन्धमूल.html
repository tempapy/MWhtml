• gandhamūla m. (-laḥ) A plant with a fragrant and tuberous root, one of the Scitaminea, (Alpinia galanga:) see kulañjana. f. (-lā or -lī)
    1 A species of Curcuma, (C. reclinata, Rox.)
    2 The olibanum tree.
    E. gandha smell, and mūla a root, fem. affix ṭāp or ṅīṣ; also with kan affix gandhamūlaka.
