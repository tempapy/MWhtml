• misi f. (-siḥ-sī)
    1 A sort of fennel, (Anethum panmorium, Rox.)
    2 Another kind, (A. sowa.)
    3 Spikenard, (Valeriana jatamansi.)
    4 A sort of lovage, (Ligusticum ajwaen.)
    5 Common anise, (Pimpinella anisum.)
    E. masa to mete, aff. in, and optionally ṅīṣ; the radical vowel changed to i, deriv. irr.; also miśi and miṣi.
