• gṝ r. 6th cl. (girati or gilati) To swallow, to eat. r. 9th cl. (gṛṇāti) To sound, to sound intelligibly or articulately, to speak. r. 10th cl. (gīrayate)
    1 To know.
    2 To make known, to teach:
    With nir or ut prefixed, To reject, to vomit.
    With sam and ut,
    1 To throw up.
    2 To cry aloud. [297]
