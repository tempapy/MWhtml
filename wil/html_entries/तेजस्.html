• tejas [384] m. (-jāḥ) A name of Agni or fire. n. (-jaḥ) 1 Splendour, light, lustre. 2 Dignity, consequence. 3 Strength, power. 4 Ardour, spirit, energy. 5 Semen virile. 6 Fresh butter. 7 Impatience, inability to bear or put up with. 8 Gold. 9 Bile, the bilious humour. 10 Marrow, the brain, &c. 11 Fame, glory. 12 Sharpness, (metallic.) 13 Pungency. 14 Heat. 15 Bodily vigour. 16 Vivacity. E. tija to sharpen or polish, affix asun.
