• vilāsin [783] mfn. (-sī-sinī-si) Wanton, sportive, dallying. m. (-sī) 1 VIṢṆU. 2 The moon. 3 Fire. 4 A sensualist, an enjoyer. 5 A snake. 6 ŚIVA. 7 KṚṢṆA. 8 KĀMA or CUPID. f. (-sinī) 1 A woman. 2 A whore, a harlot. E. vilāsa pastime, &c., aff. ini or vi, lasa to shine, ghinuṇ aff.; also read vilāṣin.
