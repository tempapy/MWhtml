• vipāka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Cooking, dressing.
    2 Ripening, maturing.
    3 Flavour, taste..
    4 Unexpected or improbable result.
    5 Poverty, distress.
    6 Change of form or state.
    7 The consequences of actions, either in this or in a former birth; the operation of time in maturing their results.
    8 Assimilation of food, its conversion into a state differing from its original one, digestion.
    E. vi before paca to ripen, aff. ghañ. [775]
