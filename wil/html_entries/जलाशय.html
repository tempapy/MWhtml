• jalāśaya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Stupid, dull, cold, apathetic. m. (-yaḥ)
    1 A pond, a tank, a lake, a reservoir or any piece of water.
    2 The ocean. n. (-yaṃ) A fragrant grass, (Andropogon muricatum.)
    E. jala water, and āśaya an abode or receptacle, or jala for jaḍa cold, and āśaya disposition. [345]
