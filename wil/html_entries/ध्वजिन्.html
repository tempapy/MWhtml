• dhvajin mfn. (-jī-jinī-ji) Having a flag or sign, &c. m. (-jī)
    1 A Brahman.
    2 A mountain.
    3 A car or carriage.
    4 A snake.
    5 A horse.
    6 A peacock.
    7 A distiller or vender of spirituous liquors.
    8 A standard-bearer.
    9 An armiger, one having an emblem or sign. f. (-nī) An army.
    E. dhvaja symbol, ini aff. [449]
