• saura [947] mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) 1 Solar. 2 Celestial. 3 Spirituous. m. (-raḥ) 1 The planet SATURN. 2 A month, consisting of thirty rising and settings of the sun, a solar month. 3 A solar day, whilst the sun is in one degree of the ecliptic. f. (-rī) The wife of the sun. E. sura a deity, &c., mūra the sun, aṇ aff., or sūryya the sun, and ṅīṣ aff. for the fem. form.
