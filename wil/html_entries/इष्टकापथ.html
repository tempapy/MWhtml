• iṣṭakāpatha n. (-thaṃ) The root of a fragrant grass, (Andropogon muricatum).
    E. iṣṭa wished, and kāpatha a bad road, growing in or preferring unfrequented places: this word is sometimes divided, kāpatha being one name, and iṣṭa added to avadāha, another name: see avadāheṣṭa and avadāha. [134]
