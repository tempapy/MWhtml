• śrīparṇa [863] n. (-rṇaṃ) 1 A lotus. 2 A tree, the wood of which is used to procure fire by attrition, (Premna spinosa). f. (-rṇī) 1 A shrub, (Gmelina arborea.) 2 A medicinal plant, commonly Kāyaphal. 3 The silk-cotton tree. 4 An aquatic plant, (Pistia stratiotes). E. śrī beauty or the goddess, and parṇa a leaf.
