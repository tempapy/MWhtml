• vasa(au)auvasa [743] r. 1st and 10th cls. (vasati vasayati) To dwell or inhabit. r. 2nd cl. (vaste) To cover with dress, to wear as clothes. r. 10th cl. (vāsayati) 1 To be affectionate, to love. 2 To cut or divide. 3 To kill. 4 To accept. (ira, u) iravasu r. 4th cl. (vasyati) To be straight, to be unbending; literally or figuratively.
    With adhi prefixed, To sit upon, to occupy.
    With upa, To fast.
    With ni, 1 To dwell in or inhabit. 2 To dress.
    With pra, To dwell abroad.
    With sam, To cohabit.
