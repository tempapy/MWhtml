• kṛcchra mfn. (-cchraḥ-cchrā-cchraṃ)
    1 Attended with pain, painful.
    2 Wicked, sinful. mn. (-cchraḥ-cchraṃ) Bodily pain.
    2 Penance, expiation.
    3 Sin.
    4 Difficulty, labour, trouble.
    5 A kind of religious penance, according to some, the same as the Prājāpatya: (see prājāpatya;) according to others, as the Sāntapana: (see sāntapanaṃ)
    E. kṛta to cut, rak Uṇādi affix, and cha substituted for the final.
