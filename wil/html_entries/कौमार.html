• kaumāra [254] mfn. adj. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) 1 Maiden, virgin. 2 Juvenile. 3 Soft, tender. f. (-rī) One of the seven Mātṛs, the divine mothers or personified energies of the gods; the energy or Śakti of KUMĀRA or KĀRTTIKEYA. n. (-raṃ) 1 Youth, childhood, from birth to the age of five. 2 Maidenhood to the age of sixteen. E. kumāra a youth, and añ aff.; or kumāra the deity KĀRTTIKEYA, aṇ and ṅīṣ affixes.
