• dvīpin [435] mfn. (-pī-pinī-pi) Island, islander. m. (-pī) 2 A tiger. 2 An ounce or panther. E. dvīpa an island, ini affix; haunting the small woody islands that abound in the rivers in India, or dvīpa a tiger's spotted skin, derived from dvi two, i to go or be, pa affix: and ini as before.
