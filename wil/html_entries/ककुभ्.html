• kakubh [177] f. (-p) 1 Space, region or quarter, as east, west, &c. 2 Beauty, splendor. 3 Unornamented hair, or the hair hanging down as a tail. 4 A wreath of Champaka flowers. 5 A Rāgiṇī or personified mode of music. 6 A Śāstra or sacred treatise. E. ka wind, skubha to spread, kvip affix and the sa is dropped; or kaka to be proud, ubh aff.
