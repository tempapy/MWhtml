• malaya [646] m. (-yaḥ) 1 A mountain or mountainous range, from which the best Sandal wood is brought, answering to the western Ghāts in the peninsula of India. 2 The country that lies along the Malaya range, or the west coast of the peninsula, Malayālam or Malabar. 3 A garden. 4 The garden of INDRA. 5 One of the minor Dvīpas or divisions of the world. f. (-yā) A plant, commonly Tëori, (Convolvulus turpethum.) E. mala to hold or contain (Sandal wood), Uṇādi aff. kayan.
