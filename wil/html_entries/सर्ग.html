• sarga [907] m. (-rgaḥ) 1 Nature, natural property or disposition. 2 Tendency of a thing. 3 Creation. 4 Effort, perseverance. 5 Assent, agreement. 6 Relinquishment, abandoning, letting go or getting rid of. 7 Voiding, as excrement. 8 A chapter, a book, a section. 9 Certainty, ascertainment. E. sṛja to quit, &c., aff. ghañ.
