• narmmada mfn. (-daḥ-dā-daṃ) Delighting, making happy, giving pleasure. m. (-daḥ) The associate or companion of a person's sports or amusements. f. (-dā)
    1 The Narmadā river, which rising in the Vindhyā mountains, runs westward to the gulf of Cambay.
    2 A plant: see pṛkkā.
    E. narmma pleasure, and da what gives. [456]
