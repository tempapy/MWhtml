• sambhāvana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Considering, reflecting.
    2 Fame, celebrity.
    3 Fitness, suitableness.
    4 Adequacy, competency, ability.
    5 Possibility.
    5 Worship, honour.
    7 (In grammar,) The sense of the potential mood.
    8 (In rhetoric,) The use of the verb in the imperative or potential mood.
    9 (In Logic,) Doubt.
    E. sam before bhū to be, causal form, aff. yuc.
