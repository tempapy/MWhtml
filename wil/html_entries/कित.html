• kita r. 1st cl. (cikitsati)
    1 To cure, to administer remedies, to practise physic.
    2 To remove, to destroy.
    3 To chastise.
    4 (Usually with vi prefixed,) To doubt; this root is regular. r. 3rd cl. (ciketi) To know. r. 10th cl. (ketayati)
    1 To dwell.
    2 To desire; some authorities make it a regular root of the 1st cl. in these senses, and others deny its admitting of any conjugation. [221]
