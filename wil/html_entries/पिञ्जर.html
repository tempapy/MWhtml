• piñjara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Yellow or tawny, reddish yellow. m. (-raḥ) A sort of colour, tawny brown, a reddish yellow, or a mixture of red and yellow.
    2 A horse, probably a bay or chesnut horse. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Gold.
    2 Yellow orpiment.
    3 A cage.
    4 The ribs or the cavity formed by them, the thorax: see pañjara.
    E. piji to dye or tinge, arac aff.
