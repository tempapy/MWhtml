• bhrāmara n. (-raṃ)
    1 Whirling, going round.
    2 A dance performed in a ring, dancing round-about.
    3 A village.
    4 Epilepsy.
    5 Honey. m. (-raḥ) A sort of loadstone. f. (-rī)
    1 A name of PĀRVATĪ, from her having assumed the form of a bee, in order to contend with MAHĀSURA.
    2 A Yoginī, or female attendant on DURGĀ.
    E. bhramara a bee, &c. aff., aṇ or añ.
