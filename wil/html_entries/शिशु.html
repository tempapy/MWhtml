• śiśu m. (-śuḥ)
    1 The young of man or any animal, a child, a calf, a pup, &c.
    2 A pupil, a scholar.
    3 A boy under eight years of age.
    4 A lad, or boy not more than sixteen.
    E. śo to destroy, Uṇādi aff. u, and the root repeated, with i substituted for the semivowel to which the radical vowel is changed in its inflections. [847]
