• paramāṇu m. (-ṇuḥ)
    1 An atom, the invisible base of all aggregate bodies; thirty of them are supposed to form a note in a sunbeam; the lowest measure of weight; in Logic, six of them form the first perceptible object.
    2 A measure of time, the sun's passage past an atom of matter.
    E. parama first, and aṇu an atom.
