• cāra [323] m. (-raḥ) 1 Going, motion. 2 A spy, a secret agent or emissary. 3 Binding, a binding, a fetter, &c. 4 A prison, a house of confinement. 5 A kind of tree, the Piyal, (Buchanania latifolia, Rox.) see priyāla. 6 Wandering about, travelling. n. (-raṃ) A factitious poison. E. cara going, &c. affix aṇ.
