• hiṃsra mfn. (-sraḥ-srā-sraṃ)
    1 Mischievous, hurtful, injurious.
    2 Murderous.
    3 Formidable, terrible.
    4 Fierce, savage. m. (-sraḥ)
    1 BHĪMA.
    2 ŚIVA.
    3 A beast of prey. f. (-srā)
    1 Spikenard (Valeriana Jaṭāmāṃsī.)
    2 A shrub, (Abrus precatorious.)
    3 A sort of grain, (Coix barbata.)
    4 A vein, a nerve.
    E. hisi to hurt, Uṇādi aff. rak. [974]
