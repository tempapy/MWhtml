• kokila [250] m. (-laḥ) 1 The Kokila or Koïl, the black or Indian cuckoo, (Cuculus Indicus.) 2 A firebrand, a lighted coal. E. kuka to seize, (the heart,) and ilac affix: the Kokila makes a prominent figure in Hindu poetry, and is supposed, by his musical cry, to inspire pleasing and tender emotions.
