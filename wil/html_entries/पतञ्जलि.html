• patañjali m. (-liḥ)
    1 The name of a saint or Muni and teacher of the Yoga philosophy, thence called after him PĀTAÑJALA; also the author of the Mahābhāṣya or commentary on PĀṆINI.
    2 A name of the poet VARARUCI.
    E. pata falling, añjali the hands joined as a mark of respect; having fallen from heaven, it is said, in the shape of a small snake, into the hands of the saint PĀṆINI as he was performing this act of reverence. [497]
