• khaja r. 1st cl. (khajati) To churn or agitate. (i,) khaji (khañjati) To limp, to halt, to walk lame or ill.
  • khaja m. (-jaḥ) A ladle, a spoon. f. (-jā)
    1 Churning, stirring.
    2 Killing, destroying.
    3 The palm with the fingers extended.
    E. khaja to agitate, &c. affix ac.
