• varāhakrāntā [736] f. (-ntā) A plant, a sort of sensitive plant according to some authorities, but to others, a kind of moss, (Lycopodium imbricatum, Rox.:) it is also considered sometimes, but erroneously, as synonymous with varāhī Dioscorea. E. varāha a hog, and krānta passing.
