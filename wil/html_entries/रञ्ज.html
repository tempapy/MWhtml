• rañja (au) r. 1st cl. aurañja (rajati-te)
    4th cl. (rajyati-te) To dye, to tinge, to give tint or colour to any thing. r. 1st cl. (rajati) To be attached to or intent on, to have one's affections or passions engaged by any object.
    With anu prefixed, To be fond of or attached to.
    With apa and vi, To be averse, to dislike. [694]
