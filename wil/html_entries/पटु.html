• paṭu mfn. (-ṭuḥ-ṭuḥ-ṭvī-ṭu)
    1 Clever, dexterous, skilful.
    2 Diligent.
    3 Smart, sharp.
    4 Hale, healthy.
    5 Warm, hot.
    6 Blown, expanded.
    7 Cruel, ferocious, unmerciful, unfeeling.
    8 Harsh, contumelious, (speech, &c.)
    9 Fraudulent, crafty, a rogue or cheat.
    10 Loquacious, talkative. m. (-ṭuḥ)
    1 A small kind of cucumber, (Trichosanthes dioeca.)
    2 The leaf of the Trichosanthes. n. (-ṭu)
    1 Salt.
    2 A mushroom.
    E. pāṭa r. 10th cl. to speak, &c. Uṇādi aff. ḍu, and the radical vowel short.
