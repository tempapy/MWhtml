• dikpāla m. (-laḥ) A regent of a quarter of the universe, as INDRA of the east; AGNI of the south-east; YAMA of the south; NAIRṚTA of the south-west; VARUṆA of the west; MARUT of the north-west; KUVERA of the north; ĪŚĀNA of the south-east.
    E. diś, and pāla who protects: this word is applicable to the Dikpatis, (see the last,) as that term is to these also.
