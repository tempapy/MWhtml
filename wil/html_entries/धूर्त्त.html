• dhūrtta mfn. (-rttaḥ-rttā-rttaṃ)
    1 Fraudulent, crafty, dishonest, knavish, &c.
    2 Mischievous, injurious. m. (-rttaḥ)
    1 A gamester.
    2 A rogue, a cheat.
    3 Thorn apple, (Datura.) n. (-rttaṃ)
    1 Rust or iron filings.
    2 Black salt.
    E. dhurvva to injure, Uṇādi affix tan, deriv. irr.
