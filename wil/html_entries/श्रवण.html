• śravaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Produced in or under the sign ŚRAVAṆĀ. mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ)
    1 The ear.
    2 Hearing. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Service.
    2 Dripping, oozing. mf. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā) The twenty-third of the lunar asterisms, represented by three footsteps, and containing three stars or α, β and γ Aquilæ.
    E. śru to hear, &c., aff. lyuṭ or yuc; or śravaṇā the asterism, aṇ aff. of derivation, and the effect rejected. [862]
