• śikhara [842] mn. (-raḥ-raṃ) 1 The peak or summit of a mountain. 2 The top of a tree. 3 Point, end, top in general. 4 Horripilation. 5 The armpit. 6 A piece of a ruby or a gem, described as of the colour of the ripe pomegranate seed or a bright red. 7 The edge or point of a sword. f. (-rā) A plant, from the fibres of which bow-strings were made, (Sanseviera zeylanica.) E. śikhā a crest, and rac poss. aff., and the vowel made short.
