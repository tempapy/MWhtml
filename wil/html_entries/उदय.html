• udaya [146] m. (-yaḥ) 1 The rising of the sun and planets in general. 2 The eastern mountain, behind which the sun is supposed to rise. 3 Rising, ascending. 4 Light, splendor. 5 Prosperity, good fortune. 6 Creation, the rising of the world. 7 (In law) Income. E. ud up, iṇa to go, and ac aff.
