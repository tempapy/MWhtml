• sruva [960] mf. (-vaḥ-vā) A ladle with a double extremity, or two oval collateral excavations, made of wood, and used to pour Ghee upon the sacrificial fire. f. (-vā) 1 A shrub, (Sanseviera zeylanica.) 2 A tree, (Boswellia thurifera.) E. sru to drop or ooze, (from or by it,) aff. ka.
