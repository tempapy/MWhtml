• au The diphthong Au or Ou, having the same sound as in Our, and considered as the fourteenth vowel of the Nāgarī alphabet. [175]
  • au ind. An interjection of,
    1 Calling, (ho! hola.)
    2 Of addressing, (the vocative particle oh.)
    3 A particle of prohibition, and
    4 Of asseveration. [175]
  • au m. (-auḥ) The serpent ANANTA. f. (-auḥ) The earth. [175]
  • au ind. The mystical prefix proper for Śūdras: see om. [175]
