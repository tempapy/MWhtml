• svana r. 1st. cl. (svanati)
    1 To sound.
    2 (svanayati) To dress, to adorn.
    With vi or ava prefixed, in a limited sense, the initial is changed, (avaṣvaṇati or viṣvaṇati) To make a noise whilst eating. [962]
  • svana m. (-naḥ) Sound in general.
    E. svana to sound, aff. ap. [962]
