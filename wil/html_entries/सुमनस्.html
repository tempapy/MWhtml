• sumanas [933] m. (-nāḥ) 1 A god, an immortal. 2 A Paṇḍita, a learned man or teacher. 3 A student of the Vedas, and supplementary sciences. 4 A plant, (Cæsalpinia Bonducella.) 5 Wheat. 6 The Nimb tree. mn. (-nāḥ-naḥ) or fem. but then always plur. even in the singular acceptation. (-saḥ) A flower in general. f. sing. (-nāḥ) Great-flowered jasmine. E. su well, excellent, mana to think, asi aff.
