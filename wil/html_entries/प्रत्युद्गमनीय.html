• pratyudgamanīya [571] mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) 1 To be placed near, upon or before. 2 To be worshipped or reverenced. n. (-yaṃ) A pair of bleached cloths, or the upper and lower garments, as worn at meals and sacrifices, &c. E. prati and ut before gama to go, anīyar aff. of the future participle.
