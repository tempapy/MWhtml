• dravya [429] mfn. (-vyaḥ-vyā-vyaṃ) 1 Fit, proper, right, what is or ought to be. 2 Derived from or relating to a tree. n. (-vyaṃ) 1 Wealth, property. 2 Substance, thing, the receptacle or substratum of properties, &c. 3 Elementary substance, nine kinds of which are reckoned: viz. earth, water, fire, air, æther, time, space, soul, and intellect. 4 Brass. 5 A stake, a wager. 6 A drug, a medicament, any thing used in medicine. 7 Anointing, plastering. 8 Lac, the animal dye. 9 Extract, gum, resin, &c. derived from vegetables. 10 Modesty, propriety. 11 Spirituous liquor. E. dru a tree, affix yat.
