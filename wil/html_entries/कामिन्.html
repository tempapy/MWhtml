• kāmin mfn. (-mī-minī-mi)
    1 Impassioned, fond, loving, wanton.
    2 Cupidinous, desirous. m. (-mī)
    1 The moon.
    2 A lover, an uxorious husband.
    3 The ruddy goose.
    4 A pigeon.
    5 A sparrow. f. (-nī)
    1 A loving or affectionate woman.
    2 A timid woman.
    3 A woman in general.
    4 Spirituous liquor.
    5 A climbing plant.
    E. kama to desire, and ṇini aff. [212]
