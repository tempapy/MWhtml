• avarodha m. (-dhaḥ)
    1 The seraglio of a palace, the queen's apartment.
    2 A queen.
    3 A palace.
    4 A covering.
    5 Hindrance, obstruction.
    6 A fence, a pen, an inclosure, confinement.
    7 Restraint.
    E. ava, rudha to inclose or shut up, ghañ aff. [080]
