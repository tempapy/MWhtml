• phāṇṭa [596] mfn. (-ṇṭaḥ-ṇṭā-ṇṭaṃ) Readily prepared, made by an extemporaneous easy process, (decoction, &c.). n. (-ṇṭaṃ) 1 Diluted decoction, decoction easily prepared. 2 The first particles of butter that are produced by churning. E. phaṇa to prepare easily, aff. kta, deriv. irr.
