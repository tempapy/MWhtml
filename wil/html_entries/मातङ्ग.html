• mātaṅga [655] m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 An elephant. 2 A man of a degraded caste, a Cāṇḍāla, an outcaste. 3 A sort of divine being attendant on a Jina. 4 The sacred fig tree, (Ficus religiosa.) 5 A mountaineer, a barbarian. f. (-ṅgī) 1 The goddess PĀRVATĪ. 2 The wife of VAŚIṢṬHA. E. mataṅga a saint, an elephant, &c., and aṇ aff. of descent or pleonastic aff.
