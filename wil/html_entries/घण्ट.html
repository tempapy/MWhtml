• ghaṇṭa m. (-ṇṭaḥ) A sort of sauce, vegetables made into a pulp with water, turmeric, mustard seeds and capsicums. f. (-ṇṭā)
    1 A bell; also a plate of iron or mixed metal struck as a bell.
    2 A plant: see ghaṇṭāpāṭalī.
    E. hana to strike, ṭa affix, fem. ṭāp, and deriv. irr. [307]
