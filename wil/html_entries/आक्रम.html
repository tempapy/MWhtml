• ākrama m. (-maḥ)
    1 Going over or beyond.
    2 Surpassing.
    3 Spreading over or upon.
    4 Overloading.
    5 Attaining, overcoming, obtaining.
    6 Invading, attacking, falling upon.
    7 Might, valour.
    E. āṅ before krama to go, to mount, ascend, surpass, &c. affix ac.
