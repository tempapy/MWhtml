• saṅgraha [881] m. (-haḥ) 1 A compilation and abridgment. 2 A catalogue, a list or summary. 3 Quantity, collection. 4 Restraining, confining. 5 Taking, seizing. 6 Propitiating, pleasing, attaching. 7 Protecting, guarding. 8 A place where any thing is kept. 9 Assent, promise. 10 Elevation, loftiness. 11 Velocity. 12 Clenching the fist. 13 Effort, exertion. E. sam before graha to take, aff. ap.
