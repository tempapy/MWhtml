• bhārayaṣṭi f. (-ṣṭiḥ) A pole or yoke for carrying burthens; a stick carried over the shoulder, with strings attached to either end of it, forming at their lower extremity a noose, in which the articles to be conveyed are suspended.
    E. bhāra a burthen, and yaṣṭi a stick. [617]
