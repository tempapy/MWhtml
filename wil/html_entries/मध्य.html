• madhya [639] mfn. (-dhyaḥ-dhyā-dhyaṃ) 1 Middle, intermediate. 2 Right, proper, reasonable. 3 Low, vile. 4 Amongst, amidst. 5 Mean, (in astronomy,) as madhyachāyā middle or mean shadow. mn. (-dhyaḥ-dhyaṃ) 1 The west. 2 Mean or common time in music. 3 The middle, the centre. 4 The waist. f. (-dhyā) 1 A young woman, a girl arrived at puberty. 2 The middle finger. 3 A form of metre, a stanza of four lines containing three syllables in each. m. (-dhyaḥ) Cessation, rest, interval. n. (-dhyaṃ) 1 A very large number, ten Āntyas. 2 A horse's flank. E. māṃ beauty, dhā to have, aṇ aff., deriv. irr.
