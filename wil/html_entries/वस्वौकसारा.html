• vasvaukasārā f. (-rā)
    1 The capital of INDRA.
    2 Alakā, the residence of KUVERA.
    3 A lake attached to Alakā or Amaravati, and belonging to either deity.
    E. vasu the Vasus, okas a residence, rā to contain, with āṅ prefixed, and ka and ṭāp affs., the favorite haunts of these demigods. [745]
