• īśāna m. (-naḥ)
    1 A name of ŚIVA.
    2 Also of the same as regent of the north-east quarter.
    3 One of the Rudras.
    4 The sun as a form. of ŚIVA. mf. (-naḥ-nī) The silk cotton tree, (Bombax heptaphyllum). n. (-naṃ) Light, splendor. f. (-nī) A name of DURGĀ.
    E. īśa a master, &c. and ānac affix, and fem. ṅīṣ
