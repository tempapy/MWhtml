• haṭha r. 1st cl. (haṭhati)
    1 To leap or jump.
    2 To be wicked.
    3 To treat with violence, to oppress.
    4 To bind to a post.
  • haṭha m. (-ṭhaḥ)
    1 Violence.
    2 Oppression.
    3 Rapine. mf. (-ṭhaḥ-ṭhī) An aquatic plant, (Pistia stratiotes.)
    E. haṭha to treat with violence, aff. ac.
