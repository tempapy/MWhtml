• pratigraha m. (-haḥ)
    1 Acceptance, assent.
    2 Proper donation to Brahmans, whatever is a fit present to a Brahman at suitable periods.
    3 The acceptance of such a gift.
    4 A spitting pot.
    5 The reserve of an army, a detachment posted with the general four hundred yards in the rear of the line.
    6 The sun near the moon's node.
    E. prati again, &c. and graha taking.
