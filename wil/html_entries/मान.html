• māna r. 1st cl. (mīmāṃsate) To investigate, to seek or desire knowledge. r. 1st and 10th cls. (mānati mānayati) To respect, to revere, to worship.
    With apa or ava prefixed, to treat with disrespect. [656]
  • māna n. (-naṃ)
    1 Measure in general, whether of weight, length, or capacity.
    2 A measure, the fourth or eighth part of a khāri.
    3 The computation of the duration of a year, solar, lunar, sydereal, &c. m. (-naḥ)
    1 Arrogance, haughtiness, pride.
    2 Female arrogance or indignation.
    3 Taking, seizing.
    4 A blockhead.
    5 An agent.
    6 A barbarian.
    E. mā to measure, lyuṭ aff., or mana to revere, (one's self,) aff. ghañ. [656]
