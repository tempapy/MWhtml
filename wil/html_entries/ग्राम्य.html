• grāmya mfn. (-myaḥ-myā-myaṃ)
    1 Village-born, produced in or relating to a village.
    2 Vulgar, rude, rustic.
    3 Relating to a musical scale. m. (-myaḥ) A hog, a tame or a village hog. n. (-myaṃ)
    1 Rustic or homely speech.
    2 The Prākṛta and the other dialects of India, except the Sanscrit.
    E. grāma, and yat aff.
