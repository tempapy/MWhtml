• kuṭumbin mfn. (-mbī-mbinī-mbi)
    1 A peasant, a cultivator of the soil.
    2 One who supports or has a family. m. (-mbī) A householder, a pater-familias. f. (-mbinī)
    1 The wife of a householder and mother of a family, a matron.
    2 A small shrub used in medicine, a kind of moon plant: see kṣīriṇī.
    E. kuṭumba kindred, &c. and ini poss. aff. [226]
