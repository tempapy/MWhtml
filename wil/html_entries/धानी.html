• dhānī [442] f. (-nī) 1 Coriander. 2 A granary, a receptacle. 3 The site of a habitation. 4 The name of a river. 5 What has or contains, used in composition, as rājadhānī a royal capital, having the king's presence; matsyadhānī a net, &c. E. dhā to have, affix yuc, fem. affix ṅīp.
