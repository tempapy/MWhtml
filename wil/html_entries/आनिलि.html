• ānili m. (-liḥ)
    1 A name of the monkey HANUMĀN.
    2 BHĪMA.
    E. anila the wind, patronymic affix iña; HANUMĀN and BHĪMA being the sons of ANILA or the wind. f. (-lī) A name of SVĀTI, the fifteenth Nakṣatra or lunar mansion.
    E. anila and aṇ affix fem. ṅīp; ANILA being the ruling deity of the asterism. [113]
