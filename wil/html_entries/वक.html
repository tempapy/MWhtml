• vaka(i)vaki r. 1st cl. (vaṃkate)
    1 To be crooked, (literally or figuratively;) to be curved or bent, to be depraved or wicked.
    2 To bend, to make crooked.
    3 To go.
  • vaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A crane, (Ardea nivea.)
    2 A tree, (Sesbana grandiflora.)
    3 KUVERA.
    4 The name of a demon.
    5 A small apparatus for calcining or subliming metals or minerals, consisting of two crucibles, one inverted over the other, luted together and placed over a fire.
    E. vaca to speak, or vaki to be crooked, aff. ghañ or ac; in the latter case the nasal augment is not inserted.
