• bhalla [614] r. 1st cl. (bhallate) 1 To tell, to describe. 2 To hurt, to injure, to wound or kill. 3 To give.
  • bhalla [614] m. (-llaḥ) A bear. mn. (-llaḥ-llaṃ) A kind of arrow. f. (-llī) 1 An arrow, with a crescent-shaped head. 2 The marking nut plant; also bhallātakī. E. bhalla to hurt or kill, aff. ac.
