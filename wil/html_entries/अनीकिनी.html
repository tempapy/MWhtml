• anīkinī f. (-nī)
    1 An army, a host, forces.
    2 A certain force; one-tenth of an Akṣauhiṇī, containing two thousand, one hundred and eighty-seven elephants, and as many cars, six thousand, five hundred and sixty-seven horse, and ten thousand, nine hundred and thirty-five foot.
    3 A lotus.
    E. anīka an army, ini aff.
