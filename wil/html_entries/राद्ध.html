• rāddha mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ)
    1 Cooked, dressed.
    2 Perfected, as a religious vow; observed, as a fast, &c.
    3 Perfect, in mysterious or magical power, adept, initiated.
    4 Perfect in general, accomplished; finished, complete, mature, &c.
    E. rādha to accomplish or be accomplished, aff. kta. [703]
