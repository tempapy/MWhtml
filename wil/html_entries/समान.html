• samāna [896] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) 1 Like, similar. 2 Good, virtuous. 3 One, uniform, same. m. (-naḥ) 1 One of the vital airs, that which is considered essential to digestion. 2 A corresponding letter of the alphabet, as a long vowel to its short one, &c. E. sama all, ana to breathe, aff. ghañ, or sam and āṅ before ṇī to get, aff. ac.
