• yava [682] m. (-vaḥ) 1 Barley, (Hordeum hexastichon.) 2 The measure of a barley corn, considered as equal to six mustard seeds. 3 A natural line across the thumb at the second joint, when it may be compared to a grain of barley, and supposed to indicate good fortune. 4 Speed, velocity. 5 A double convex lens. E. yu to join or mix, aff. ap.
