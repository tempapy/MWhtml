• carmmaṇvatī f. (-tī)
    1 A river that runs across Bundelkhand into the Ganges, the modern Chambal.
    2 The plaintain tree, (Musa sapientum.)
    E. carmman skin, and matup poss. affix; na is changed to ṇa; it may also be dropped, and the word be written carmmavatī. [320]
