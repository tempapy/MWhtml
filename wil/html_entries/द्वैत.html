• dvaita n. (-taṃ)
    1 Duplication, doubling or being doubled.
    2 Duality, in philosophy, the assertion of two principles, as the distinctness of life and soul, spirit and matter, god and the universe.
    E. dvi two, tal affix, dvita double, two-fold, abstract affix aṇ. [435]
