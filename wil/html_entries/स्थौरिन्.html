• sthaurin m. (-rī)
    1 A pack-horse, one that carries burthens on his back like an ox or ass.
    2 A strong horse.
    E. sthaura strength, ini aff., or according to some authorities sthurā a pack-saddle, ini aff., deriv. irr.; it is also read sthūrin, sthurin, and sthorin. [954]
