• badarīśaila n. (-laṃ) A part of the Himālaya range, and a celebrated place of pilgrimage; the Bhadrināṭh of modern travellers, or a town and temple on the west bank of the Alakanandā river, in the province of Srinagar.
    E. badarī the jujube, and śaila mountain; also badarīvana n. (-naṃ) The wood of jujubes, &c.
