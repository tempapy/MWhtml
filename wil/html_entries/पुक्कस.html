• pukkasa mfn. (-saḥ-sī-saṃ) Low, vile. m. (-saḥ) A Caṇḍāla, or man of an impure and degraded tribe or occupation, born of the Niṣāda by a Śūdra female. f. (-sī)
    1 Indigo.
    2 A bud, a blossom.
    3 A woman of the Pukkasa tribe.
    E. pu for puṇya purity, ku bad (understood,) kasa to go, aff. ac, deriv. irr.
