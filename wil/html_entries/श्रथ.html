• śratha [861] r. 1st and 10th cls. (śrathati, śrathayati) 1 To hurt, to injure, to kill. 2 To let loose or discharge, to set free, to liberate. 3 To bind or tie. r. 10th cl. (śrāthayati) 1 To apply diligently, to endeavour sedulously. 2 To delight frequently. 3 To march. r. 10th cl. (śrathayati) To be weak or infirm. (i) śrathi r. 1st cl. (śraṃthate) 1 To be loose or lax, to be slack or flaccid. 2 To let loose, to loosen.
