• dundubhi m. (-bhiḥ)
    1 A sort of large kettle drum.
    2 A name of VARUṆA, regent of the ocean. The name of a Daitya or demon.
    3 A pair or couple. f. (-bhiḥ-bhī)
    1 A die or dice.
    2 Twice three spots on a dice, or a pair of dice, with three spots on each.
    E. dundu imitative sound, and bhā to utter, affix ki, or dyau heaven, ubha to fill, and deriv. irr. [414]
