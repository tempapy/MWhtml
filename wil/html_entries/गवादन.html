• gavādana n. (-naṃ) Pasture or meadow grass. f. (-nī)
    1 A hay rack, a trough for holding grass out of which cattle feed.
    2 A species of cucumber, (Cucumis madraspatanus, or, according to some, C. coloquintida.)
    3 A plant, (Clitoria ternatea:) see the preceding.
    E. go a cow, adana food, fem. affix ṅīp. [286]
