• yadu m. (-ṭuḥ)
    1 The name of a king, the ancestor of KṚṢṆA, and the eldest son of YAYĀTI, the fifth monarch of the lunar dynasty.
    2 A country on the west of the Jumna river about Mathurā and Vṛndāvana, over which YADU ruled, and named after him: according to some authorities, however, the kingdom of YADU, is the Dekshin or Peninsula of India. m. plu. (-vāḥ)
    1 The Yādavas, the people of YADU.
    2 Descendants of king YADU: more usually however yādavā.
