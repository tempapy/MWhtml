• vikālaka [759] m. (-kaḥ) Evening, twilight. f. (-likā) A perforated copper vessel, which is placed in a pan of water, and being filled at the expiration of a certain period, defines that period: a sort of Clepsydra, or water clock. E. vi before kāla time, and kan added, or vi before kala to go, aff. kvun.
