• sāntva r. 10th cl. (sāntvayati)
    1 To appease, to conciliate.
    2 To comfort; also ṣāntva. [918]
  • sāntva mn. (-ntvaḥ-ntvaṃ)
    1 Conciliation, reconciling, appeasing.
    2 Pleasing and conciliatory language.
    3 Cleverness, dexterity.
    E. sāntva to console or appease, ac aff. [918]
