• vikatthana [758] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) 1 Speaking idly. 2 Praising ironically. 3 Vaunting, boastful. n. (-naṃ) 1 Irony, ironical or unmerited praise. 2 Praise in general. 3 Assigning any thing to an unreal origin. 4 Boasting. 5 Repeating, proclaiming. E. vi implying difference or extension, and katthana praising.
