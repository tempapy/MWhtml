• maṇḍūkaparṇa mf. (-rṇaḥ-rṇī) A plant, (Bignonia Indica.) f. (-rṇī)
    1 Bengal madder, (Rubia Manjith.)
    2 A plant, (Siphonanthus Indica.)
    3 A sort of creeper, (Hydrocotyle Asiatica.)
    E. maṇḍūka a frog, and parṇa a leaf, compared to the animal in shape or colour. [634]
