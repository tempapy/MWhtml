• sanātana mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ)
    1 Eternal, continual, perpetual.
    2 Firm, fixed, permanent. m. (-naḥ)
    1 VIṢṆU.
    2 ŚIVA.
    3 BRAHMĀ.
    4 A guest of the Manes, or one who is to be fed whenever he chooses to attend the obsequial ceremonies or Śrāddhas. f. (-nī)
    1 The goddess LAKṢMĪ.
    2 A name of DURGĀ.
    3 SARASVATĪ.
    E. sanā always, and dyu or dyul aff.
