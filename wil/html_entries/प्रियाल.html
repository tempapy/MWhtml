• priyāla [590] m. (-laḥ) A tree, commonly Piyal, (Buchanania latifolia, ROXBURGH'S catalogue, but the name is applied to different trees.) E. pṛ to satisfy, aff. kālan and the radical vowel changed to ri; also with kan added priyālaka m. (-kaḥ) and with the vowel protracted, priyāla. m. (-laḥ) also as derived from pīya a Sautra root, pīyāla or piyāla, &c.
