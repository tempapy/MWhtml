• makaranda m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 The nectar or honey of a flower.
    2 The Kokila or Indian cuckoo.
    3 A bee.
    4 A fragrant kind of mango.
    5 A kind of jasmine, (Jasminum pubescens.)
    6 The filament of a lotus.
    E. makara the emblem of KĀMA, and do to destroy, i. e. to surpass, as an incentive to love, aff. ka, form irr. [630]
