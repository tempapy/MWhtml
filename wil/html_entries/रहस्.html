• rahas [699] n. subst. (-haḥ) 1 Solitariness, privacy. 2 Copulation. 3 A privity. 4 A religious or mystic truth. ind. or n. adv. (-haḥ) Solitary, private, in private, secretly, privily. E. raha to abandon, (society, &c.,) and asun aff., or rama to sport or play, with the same aff., and aha substituted for the radical finals.
