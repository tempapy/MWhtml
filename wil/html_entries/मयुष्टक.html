• mayuṣṭaka m. (-kaḥ) A sort of bean, (Phaseolus mungo.)
    E. maya to go, aff. u, mayu one who goes, ṣṭaka to impede, aff. ḍa, mayuṣṭa, and with the pleonastic addition, kun mayuṣṭaka; also with ac aff. after maya to go, mayaṣṭa, and mayaṣṭaka; and again with pa substituted for ma, mapaṣṭa, mapaṣṭaka, mapuṣṭa or mapuṣṭaka.
