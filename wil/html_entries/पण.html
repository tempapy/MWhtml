• paṇa r. 1st cl. (paṇate) also 10th cl. (paṇayati) To negociate, to treat or transact business. (paṇayati) To praise.
  • paṇa m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 Measure of account, in cowries or shells, twenty gandas or eighty cowries; also a weight of copper of similar value.
    2 Wages or hire.
    2 A stake at play, a bet, a wager.
    4 Gaming, playing.
    5 Playing with dice.
    6 Price.
    7 Wealth, property.
    8 A commodity for sale.
    9 Business.
    10 A distiller.
    11 A house, a dwelling.
    E. paṇa to do business, aff. ac.
