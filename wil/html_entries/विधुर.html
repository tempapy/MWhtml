• vidhura mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Agitated, distressed, overcome with anxiety, distress, &c.
    2 Bewildered, confused, (with fear, liquor, &c.)
    3 Separate, absent from a mistress or lover. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Separation.
    2 Agitation of mind from terror or distress, &c. f. (-rā) Curds mixed up with sugar and spices.
    E. vi before dhur a load, a burthen, aff. ap. [771]
