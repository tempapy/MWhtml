• udita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Said, spoken.
    2 Bound, tied.
    3 Risen, ascended.
    4 Grown, augmented.
    5 Born, produced.
    6 Incurred, experienced.
    7 High, tall, lofty.
    E. vada to speak, and kta affix; or ud up, and ita gone, past part. of iṇa to go. [147]
