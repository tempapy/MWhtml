• kinnara m. (-raḥ)
    1 A demigod attached to the service of KUVERA, a celestial quirister or musician.
    2 A kind of follower or attendant on a Jina or Jaina saint. f. (-rī) A female Kinnara, or chorister.
    E. kiṃ what, used contemptuously, (what kind of) nara a man: the Kinnara having, with the human figure, the head of a horse. [221]
