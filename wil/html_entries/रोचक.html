• rocaka [711] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) What pleases, brightens, strengthens, &c. m. (-kaḥ) 1 Hunger, appetite. 2 The plantain. 3 A sort of onion. 4 A stomachic, a carminative, any medicine, fruit or fossil, supposed to strengthen the tone of the stomach and restore lost appetite. E. ruca to shine, &c., and ṇvul aff.
