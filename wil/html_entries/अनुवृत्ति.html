• anuvṛtti f. (-ttiḥ)
    1 Complaisance, obliging or serving another. See anurodha.
    2 A subsequent or supplementary rule, completing what a preceding one has left to be explained.
    3 Imitating, doing or acting in like manner.
    E. anu before, vṛta to serve, and ktin aff. [035]
