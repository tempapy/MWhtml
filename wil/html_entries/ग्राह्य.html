• grāhya mfn. (-hyaḥ-hyā-hyaṃ)
    1 To be seized, to be taken, to be accepted.
    2 To be accepted as a rule or law, to be acknowledged or assented to.
    3 To be attached to or obeyed.
    4 To be admitted in evidence, &c.
    5 To be apprehended or arrested.
    E. graha to take, ṇyat aff. [306]
