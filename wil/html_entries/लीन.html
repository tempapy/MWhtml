• līna mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ)
    1 Embraced, clung or adhered to.
    2 Melted, dissolved.
    3 Dissolved with softness, overcome with passion.
    4 Diffused.
    5 Effaced, wiped away.
    6 Staying, being situated.
    7 Left, departed.
    E. lī to be in contact, &c., kta aff. [721]
