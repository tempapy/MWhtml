• dhanacchū f. (-cchūḥ) The Numidian crane.
    E. dhana the wealth, (of fields,) and ccho to destroy, affix ḍū. Note; as a synonym of kareṭavyā this word would seem to mean the bird stated: the name itself is more analogous to the Dhanesa of the dialects or the Indian Buceros.
