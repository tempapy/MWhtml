• kha [268] The second consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, being the aspirate of the preceding; it is written in the Roman character Kh.
  • kha [268] n. (-khaṃ) 1 Heaven. 2 Sky or ether. 3 An organ of sense. 4 Knowledge. 5 Happiness, pleasure. 6 Vacuity. 7 A dot, a cypher. 8 A city, a field. 9 Action. 10 Auspiciousness. 11 Talc. 12 The tenth constellation from any given one, or the sun's entrance into it. 13 Brahma the supreme spirit. m. (-khaḥ) A name of the sun. E. khana to dig, to penetrate, affix ḍa.
