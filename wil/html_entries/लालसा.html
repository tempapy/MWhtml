• lālasā mf. (-saḥ-sā)
    1 Ardent desire.
    2 Regret, sorrow, missing, wishing for any person or object absent.
    3 Soliciting, asking.
    4 The longing of pregnant women.
    5 Wanton sport, dalliance.
    E. lala to desire, to wish for, asaṇ Uṇādi aff. or laṣa to wish, or las to sport, aff. ghañ and ṭāp, and the root reiterated, deriv. irr.
