(-raṃ) 1 Dry ginger. 2 A sort of grass, (Cyperus pertenuis.) 3 A form of writing, the Devanāgarī alphabet. m. (-raḥ) 1 A husband's brother. 9 An orange. 2 A lecturer. 4 Denial of knowledge. 5 Fatigue. 6 Desire of final beatitude. f. (-rī) 1 A sort of Euphorbin. 2 A clever or intriguing woman.
