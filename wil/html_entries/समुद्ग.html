• samudga mfn. (-dgaḥ-dgā-dgaṃ)
    1 Who or what rises or goes up.
    2 Who or what pervades entirely. m. (-dgaḥ) A casket, a covered box.
    2 A stanza in which two or more of the Pādas correspond in sound, though in a different sense.
    E. sam and uṭ before gama to go, aff. ḍa. [900]
