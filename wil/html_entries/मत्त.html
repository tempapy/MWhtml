• matta [634] mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ) 1 Pleased, glad, delighted. 2 Intoxicated, (drunk with liquor.) 3 Intoxicated, (with pride, passion, &c.) 4 Furious, mad, insane. m. (-ttaḥ) 1 A furious elephant, or one in rut. 2 A buffalo. 3 The Kokila or Indian cuckoo. 4 The thorn apple, (Datura.) f. (-ttā) 1 Vinous liquor. 2 A species of the Paṅkti metre. E. mada to rejoice, &c. aff. kta.
