• kaṇṭakaphala m. (-laḥ)
    1 The Jaka or bread-fruit tree, (Artocarpus integrifolia.)
    2 Another plant: see gokṣura.
    3 The castor oil tree.
    4 The Datura.
    E. kaṇṭaka a spike, and phala fruit; the term is applicable to any plant, the fruit of which is invested with a hairy or thorny coat; also kaṇṭakiphala. [183]
