• kulācala [234] m. (-laḥ) A mountain, one of a class of seven principal mountains, which are supposed to exist in each Varṣa or division of a continent: those of Bhārata Varṣa or India are Mahendra, Malaya, Sahya, Śuktimān, Ṛkṣa, Vindhya, and Pāripātra. E. kula a race, and acala a mountain; also kulādri, &c.
