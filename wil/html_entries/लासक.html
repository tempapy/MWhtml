• lāsaka [720] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A dancer, an actor, a mime. 2 A peacock. 3 Embracing, enfolding, surrounding. 4 One who sports or gambols. n. (-kaṃ) A turret, a room on the top of a building. f. (-lāsikā or lāsakī) 1 A female dancer, a Nāc girl. 2 A wanton, a harlot. E. lasa to embrace, to be skilful, (in the art of dancing,) and aff. ṇvul.
