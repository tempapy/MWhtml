• śvasa r. 2nd cl. (śvasiti)
    1 To breathe.
    2 To live.
    With āṅ prefixed, To recover or revive, to console.
    With ut,
    1 To open, to expand.
    2 To console.
    With nir,
    1 To breathe out, to expire.
    2 To sigh.
    With vi, To have trust or faith in. [867]
