• naḍa r. 1st cl. (naḍati) To be thick or impervious. r. 10th cl. (naḍayati) To fall off or from.
  • naḍa m. (-ḍaḥ)
    1 A sort of reed, (Arundo tibialis, or karka;) also nala.
    2 A particular tribe whose employment is making a sort of glass bracelets.
    E. naḍa to be thick, affix ac.
