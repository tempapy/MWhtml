• mṛḍa r. 6th cl. (mṛḍati) r. 9th cl. (-mṛḍṇāti)
    1 To delight, to make happy.
    2 To rejoice, to be happy or delighted. r. 9th cl. (according to some authorities,) To grind or pound. [670]
  • mṛḍa m. (-ḍaḥ)
    1 A name of ŚIVA.
    2 The fire that completes an oblation. f. (-ḍānī) DURGĀ, the wife of ŚIVA.
    E. mṛḍa to be pleased or delighted, aff. ka, fem. aff. ṅīṣ, with ānuk augment. [670]
