• mā [654] r. 2d cl. (māti) 3d cl. (mimīte) 4th cl. (māyate) To mete, to measure: with anu prefixed, to infer, to conclude: with upa, to resemble; with pari, to measure; with pra, to be evidence or authority.
  • mā [654] ind. 1 A prohibitive and negative particle. 2 A particle implying doubt. E. mā to measure, aff. kvip; this particle is used chiefly prefixed to verbs, in the imperative mood, as mākuru do not do (any act), &c., it is also read sometimes, māṅ the final being redundant.
