• āmiṣa mn. (-ṣaḥ-ṣaṃ)
    1 Flesh.
    2 Enjoyment.
    3 An object of enjoyment, a pleasing or beautiful object, &c.
    4 A bribe.
    5 Coveting, longing for.
    6 Form.
    7 Lust, desire.
    8 Food.
    E. ama to be sick, to go, ṣṭisac Uṇādi affix and the antepen. lengthened.
