• komala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Soft, bland.
    2 Soft, low, sweet.
    3 Beautiful, pleasing, agreeable. f. (-lā) A plant: see kṣīrikā. n. (-laṃ) Water.
    E. kuṭa to be curved, kala Uṇādi affix, and muṭ inserted, formative irregular; or kama to desire, kalac affix, and u inserted with conversion. [251]
