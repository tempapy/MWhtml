• buddha [605] mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ) Known, understood. m. (-ddhaḥ) 1 A generic name for a deified teacher of the Bauddha sect, amongst whom numerous Buddhas are reckoned; the name is especially applied to the ninth incarnation of VIṢṆU and the apparent founder of the religion of the Buddhas; the term is often confounded, but improperly, with a Jina: see jina. 2 A sage, a wise or learned man. E. budha to know, aff. ktaḥ see budha.
