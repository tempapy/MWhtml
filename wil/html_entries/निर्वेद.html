• nirveda [477] mfn. (-daḥ-dā-daṃ) Not having the Vedas, infidel, unscriptural. m. (-daḥ) 1 Humility, self-disparagement. 2 Being neglected or disregarded by others. 3 Indifference, disregard of worldly objects. E. nir depreciative particle, vida to know, affix ghañ, or nir neg. veda the Vedas.
