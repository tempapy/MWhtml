• karāla mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Great, large.
    2 High, lofty.
    3 Formidable, terrible.
    4 Having projecting teeth. m. (-laḥ) Rosin, pitch, or a mixture of oil and the resin of the Śāl tree. n. (laṃ) A dark kind of Tulasī or Basil. f. (-lā) A kind of swallow wort, commonly Ananta-mul, (Hemisdemus Indicus). see śārivā. f. (-lī) One of the seven tongues of AGNI or fire.
    E. kara the hand, &c. lā with āṅ prefixed to take, and ka affix; or kara, and ala to be able, &c. affix aṇ, fem. do ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
