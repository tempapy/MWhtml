• uttarapakṣa m. (-kṣaḥ)
    1 A demonstrated truth, a direct conclusion.
    2 Course of argument, according to established truths.
    3 Second part of an argument, reply, refutation.
    4 The minor proposition in a syllogism.
    E. uttara subsequent, and pakṣa subject of an inference; also adding. ka, uttarapakṣaka. [141]
