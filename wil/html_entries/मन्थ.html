• mantha r. 1st cl. (manthati)
    1 To hurt or kill.
    2 To afflict.
    3 To suffer pain. 1st and 9th cls. (manthati mathnāti) To agitate, to stir, to churn. [642]
  • mantha m. (-nthaḥ)
    1 A churning. stick.
    2 The sun.
    3 A dish made of barley meal with ghee and water, a sort of gruel or porridge.
    4 A disease of the eyes, cataract or opacity.
    5 Rheum, excretion of the eyes.
    6 Killing, destroying.
    7 Agitating, stirring, churning.
    E. mantha to churn, &c., aff. ac. [642]
