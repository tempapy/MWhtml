• ṇuda [361] r. 6th cl. (au) auṇuda (nudati nudate) 1 To cast, to command, to direct, to throw or send; the act of ordering being considered analogous to that of throwing. 2 To go or proceed.
    With apa, To remove.
    With nir, 1 To throw out. 2 To confess, to acknowledge.
    With vi, To be happy.
    With saṃ in the causal To drive, (as horses.)
