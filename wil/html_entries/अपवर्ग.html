• apavarga [045] m. (-rgaḥ) 1 Final beatitude, the delivery of the soul from the body, and exemption from farther transmigration. 2 Abandoning, quitting. 3 The fruit or consequences of any completed act. 4 The completion of any act. 5 Any act brought to a conclusion. 6 End, completion. E. apa from, vṛja to forsake, ghañ affix, and ga substituted for ja.
