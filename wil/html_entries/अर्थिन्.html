• arthin mfn. (-rthī-rthinī-rthi)
    1 One who asks or begs for any thing.
    2 One who seeks to effect or gain a purpose or object. m. (-rthī)
    1 A servant.
    2 A follower, a companion or partisan.
    3 A plaintiff, a prosecutor.
    4 A beggar, a petitioner.
    E. artha asking, &c. ini aff. [070]
