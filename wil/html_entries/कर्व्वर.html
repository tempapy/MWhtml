• karvvara [199] m. (-raḥ) 1 A demon or imp. 2 A tiger. f. (-rī) 1 A name of DURGĀ. 2 The leaf of the asafoetida plant: see hiṅgupatrī. E. kṛ to injure, kkarac Uṇādi affix; if derived from karvva to go, &c. this word is read karvvara, but such a reading is probably inaccurate: see karvvara, also karvvura; it is sometimes considered as a various reading of this last, meaning, variegated.
