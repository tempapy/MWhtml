• rikta mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ)
    1 Empty, void.
    2 Poor, indigent.
    3 Unloaded, unburthened.
    4 Purged. n. (-ktaṃ)
    1 A wood, a forest.
    2 A vacuity, a vacuum. f. (-ktā) The fourth, ninth, or fourteenth days of the lunar fortnight.
    E. rica to void by stool, aff. kta.
