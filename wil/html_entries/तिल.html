• tila [377] r. 1st cl. (telati) To go. r. 6th cl. (tilati) and 10th cl. (telayati) 1 To be unctuous or greasy. 2 To oil or anoint.
  • tila [377] m. (-laḥ) 1 A plant bearing an oily seed; the oil and seed being both much used in Oriental cooking, (Sesamum orientale.) 2 A mole or spot, compared to a seed of sesamum. 3 A small particle or portion. E. tila to be unctuous, affix ka.
