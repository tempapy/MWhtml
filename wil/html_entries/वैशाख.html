• vaiśākha m. (-khaḥ)
    1 The month in which the moon is full near the southern scale, (April-May,) the first month in the Hindu calendar.
    2 A churning stick. n. (-khaṃ) An attitude in shooting, standing with the feet a span apart. f. (-khī) The day of full moon in the month Vaiśākha.
    E. viśākhā the constellation in which the moon is full this month, aṇ aff. or viśākhā said to mean here, pervading, revolving, aṇ aff.
