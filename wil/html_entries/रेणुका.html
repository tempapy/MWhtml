• reṇukā [709] f. (-kā) A sort of perfume and medicine, of a bitter and slightly pungent taste, and greyish colour; it is procured in grains about the size of those of pepper. 2 The wife of the saint JAMADAGNI and mother of PARAŚURĀMA. 3 A sort of pulse, (Ervum or Cicer lens.) E. reṇu sand, dust, and kan added.
