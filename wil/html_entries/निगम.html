• nigama [465] m. (-maḥ) 1 A town, a city. 2 A market, a fair. 3 Holy-writ, the Vedas collectively. 4 A road, a market road. 5 Trade, traffick. 6 A camp or caravan of itinerant dealers, as Banjaras, &c. 7 Certainty, assurance. E. ni affirmative prefix, gama to go, affix gha; by which people go, &c.
