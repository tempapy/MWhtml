• sthūla r. 10th cl. (sthūlayate) To increase, to fatten, to become big or bulky. [953]

    1 Fat, corpulent, bulky.

    3 Large, great.
    4 Coarse.
    5 Clumsy. n. (-laṃ)
    1 A heap, a quantity.
    2 A tent. f. (-lā)
    1 A sort of pepper, (Pothos officinalis)
    2 A pumpkin gourd. m. (-laḥ) The jack fruit tree.
