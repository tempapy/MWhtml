• grantha r. 1st, 9th and 10th cls. (granthati grathnāti granthayati) To string, to tie together, to connect or arrange in a regular series.
    With ud prefixed, To untie, to loosen. [303]
  • grantha m. (-nthaḥ)
    1 Stringing or tying together, (literally or metaphorically.)
    2 Wealth, property.
    3 A book or composition in prose or verse.
    4 A metre or measure of thirty-two syllables.
    E. grantha to dispose or arrange, affix ac. [303]
