• uḍumbara m. (-raḥ)
    1 Glomerous fig tree, (Ficus glomerata, Rox.)
    2 The threshold of a house.
    3 A eunuch.
    4 A species of leprosy with coppery spots.
    5 A kind of worm, supposed to be generated in the blood, and to produce leprosy. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Copper.
    2 A Karṣa, a measure of two Tolas.
    E. uḍu a constellation in the second case, vṛ to screen, and ap affix; it is also written uḍumbara and uṭumbara.
