• āvṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Enclosed, surrounded, (by a ditch, wall, &c.)
    2 Covered, screened.
    3 Invested, inclosed, involved.
    4 Spread, overspread, overcast.
    5 Filled with, abounding with. m. (-taḥ) A man of mixed origin, the son of a Brahman, by a woman of the Ugra caste.
    E. āṅ, vṛ to nourish, affix kta.
