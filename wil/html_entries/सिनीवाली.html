• sinīvālī f. (-lī)
    1 The day preceding that of new moon, or that on which the moon rises scarcely visible.
    2 The goddess UMĀ.
    E. sā good fortune, ini aff., fem. form, sinī a digit of the moon, vala to contain, aff. aṇ, fem. aff. ṅīṣ; several other etymologies occur. [925]
