• viparyyaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 Contrariety, opposition.
    2 Reverse in general, and thence applied to that of which reverse is predicated, as misery, calamity, (the reverse of fortune;) error, misapprehension, (the reverse of reason or truth;) inverted order or succession, (the reverse of that which is usual or prescribed,) &c.
    3 Destruction, annihilation.
    4 Perverseness of disposition.
    5 Change of purpose or conduct.
    6 Enmity, hostility.
    E. vi and pari implying reverse, and i to go, aff. ac; also with ghañ aff. viparyyāya m. (-yaḥ)
