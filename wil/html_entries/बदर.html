• badara [598] mf. (-raḥ-rī) 1 The jujube, (Zizyphus jujuba or scandens.) m. (-raḥ) The seed of the cotton pod. f. (-rā-rī) Cotton. f. (-rā) 1 A plant, (Mimosa octandra.) 2 A medicinal drug, commonly Varāhakrāntī. n. (-raṃ) 1 The fruit of the jujube. 2 The berry or pod of the cotton. E. bada to be firm, Uṇādi aff. ara; or if derived from vada to give information, it is read by some authorities badara.
