• suvarṇa mfn. (-rṇaḥ-rṇā-rṇaṃ)
    1 Of a good tribe or caste.
    2 Brilliant, bright.
    3 Of a good colour. n. (-rṇaṃ)
    1 Gold.
    2 A sort of Sandal wood.
    3 A kind of red chalk or ochre.
    4 Wealth, property.
    5 A flower, (Mesua ferrea.) mn. (-rṇaḥ-rṇaṃ) A weight of gold, equal to sixteen Māṣas, which at five Rattis to each Māṣa makes the Suvarṇa equal to about 175 grains Troy. m. (-rṇaḥ)
    1 A tree, (Cassia fistula.)
    2 A sort of sacrifice. f. (-rṇā)
    1 Aloe wood, the black kind.
    2 Turmeric.
    3 A bitter gourd, (Colocynth.)
    4 Sida, (Cordifolia, &c.)
    E. su excellent, varṇa tribe or colour, &c.
