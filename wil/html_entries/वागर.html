• vāgara [746] m. (-raḥ) 1 A sage, a holy man, one desirous of final emancipation. 2 A scholar, a Paṇḍita, a learned Brahman. 3 A hero, a brave man, one who is intrepid or fearless. 4 Certainty, ascertainment. 5 A whetstone. 6 An obstacle, an impediment, or cause of obstruction. 7 Submarine fire. 8 A wolf. E. vāk speech, ṛ to go, aff. ap.
