• jarāsandha m. (-ndhaḥ) A proper name, a celebrated king and warrior, sovereign of Magadha, father-in-law to KAṂSA, and foe to KṚṢṆA; he was slain in single combat by BHĪMA.
    E. jarā a female demon, and sandha connection, union; he was born in two halves, which were put together by the Rākṣasī JARĀ.
