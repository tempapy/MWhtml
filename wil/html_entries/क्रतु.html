• kratu [255] m. (-tuḥ) 1 Sacrifice, offering, worship. 2 An Aśvamedha, the sacrifice. 3 The name of a Muni, one of the seven principal Ṛṣis or saints, the offspring of BRAHMĀ, married to Kriyā, and the father of the 60,000 Bālikhilyas. 4 One of the ten VIŚVADEVAS. E. kṛñ to do, ktu Uṇādi aff.
