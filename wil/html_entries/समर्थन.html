• samarthana [895] n. (-naṃ) 1 Deliberation, deciding on the propriety or impropriety of any thing; also samarthanā. 2 Perseverance in an arduous undertaking, attempting difficulties or impossibilities. 3 Reconciling differences, allaying a dispute. 4 Objecting, objection. E. sam before artha to ask, or request, aff. lyuṭ.
