• gahvara nf. (-raṃ-rī) A cave, a cavern, a grotto, a recess in a rock or mountain, &c. n. (-raṃ)
    1 A thicket, a wood.
    2 Hypocrisy.
    3 Weeping, crying, but not violently. m. (-raḥ) An arbour, a bower.
    E. gāha to be agitated, and ṣvarac Uṇādi affix, the radical vowel made short. [286]
