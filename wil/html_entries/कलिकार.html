• kalikāra [202] m. (-raḥ) 1 A plant bearing a nut, which is used as a febrifuge, (Grey bonduc:) see pūrtikarañja. 2 The fork-tailed shrike. 3 Another bird, a small one with a yellow head, (Loxia philippensis.) 4 A name of NĀRADA. f. (-rī) A poisonous plant: see lāṅgalī. E. kali strife, &c. kṛñ to make, affix aṇ; also with kan added kalikāraka.
