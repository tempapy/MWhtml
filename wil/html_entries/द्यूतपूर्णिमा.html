• dyūtapūrṇimā [428] f. (-mā) The day of full moon in the month Kārttika, (October-November,) the night of which is spent in games of chance, in honour of the goddess of fortune, LAKṢMĪ. E. dyūta gaming, and pūrṇimā day of full moon; also with aṇ pleonastic affix added, and ṅīṣ fem. affix dyūtapaurṇimī, f. (-mī)
