• kañjara m. (-raḥ)
    1 A name of BRAHMĀ.
    2 The sun.
    3 The belly.
    4 An elephant.
    E. kañja a lotus, &c. ṛ to hate or injure, aca or ghañ affix kañjāra, the pen. made short; also derived by some from kaji, a Sautra root, to produce or grow, and ara Uṇādi aff. [180]
