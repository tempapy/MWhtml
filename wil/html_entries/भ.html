• bha [609] The twenty-fourth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, and aspirate of the last letter, or Bh.
  • bha [610] n. (bhaṃ) 1 A star. 2 An asterism. 3 A planet. m. (bhaḥ) 1 A name of ŚUKRA, regent of the planet VENUS. 2 A bee. 3 Error, delusion. f. (bhā) 1 Light, lustre, splendor. 2 A ray of light. f. (bhī) Fear. E. bhā to shine, or bhī to fear, aff. ḍa.
