• bha The twenty-fourth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, and aspirate of the last letter, or Bh. [609]
  • bha n. (bhaṃ)
    1 A star.
    2 An asterism.
    3 A planet. m. (bhaḥ)
    1 A name of ŚUKRA, regent of the planet VENUS.
    2 A bee.
    3 Error, delusion. f. (bhā)
    1 Light, lustre, splendor.
    2 A ray of light. f. (bhī) Fear.
    E. bhā to shine, or bhī to fear, aff. ḍa. [610]
