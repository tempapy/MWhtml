• u [136] The third short vowel of the Sanskrit alphabet, corresponding to the letter U, and pronounced as that is in the word full.
  • u [136] r. 1st cl. (u) uṅa (uvate) To sound, to make a particular sound or noise.
  • u [136] ind. An interjection, 1 of assent; 2 calling; 3 of compassion; 4 anger; and 5 of command. 6 An expletive. m. (-uḥ) 7 A name of ŚIVA. 2 Of BRAHMA.
