• jīmūta [351] m. (-taḥ) 1 A cloud. 2 A mountain. 3 A grass, (Andropogon serratus.) 4 A name of INDRA. 5 A nourisher, a sustainer. 6 A plant: see ghoṣaka. 7 A species of the Daṇḍaka metre. E. ji to excel or surpass, Uṇādi affix tan, and mūṭ augment, or jī for jīva life, and mūta who urines.
