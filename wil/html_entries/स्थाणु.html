• sthāṇu [951] mfn. (-ṇuḥ-ṇuḥ-ṇu) Firm, fixed, steady, stable. m. (-ṇuḥ) 1 ŚIVA. 2 A stake, a pin. 3 A post. 4 A spear, a dart. 5 A nest of white ants. mn. (-ṇuḥ-ṇu) 1 The trunk of a tree of which the branches have been lopped off. 2 The trunk of any tree. E. ṣṭhā to stand, Uṇādi aff. nu.
