• nyubja [490] mfn. (-bjaḥ-bjā-bjaṃ) 1 Crooked-backed, hump-backed. 2 Looking downwards. 3 Bent, crooked. 4 Convex. m. (-bjaḥ) 1 Disease, sickness, pain. 2 A sort of ladle made of Kusa grass. n. (-bjaṃ) 1 The fruit of the Kāmaraṅga, (Carambola averrhoa.) 2 A vessel used at Śrāddhas. E. nyubja to be crooked, aff. ghañ.
