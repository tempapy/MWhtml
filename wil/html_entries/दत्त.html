• datta mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ)
    1 Given, presented, made over, assigned.
    2 Preserved, guarded, protected. m. (-ttaḥ)
    1 The name of a king.
    2 A saint; also dattātreya.
    3 A common surname or title of a man of the Vaiśya or third tribe, at present common to the Kayeth class. n. (-ttaṃ) A gift, a donation.
    E. dā to give, affix kta.
