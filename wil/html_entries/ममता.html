• mamatā [643] f. (-tā) 1 Pride, arrogance, self-sufficiency. 2 The interest or affection entertained for other objects from considering them as belonging to or connected with one's self. 3 Individuality. E. mama mine, the possessive case of asmaṭ I, and tal aff. of the abstract; also with tva aff. mamatva.
