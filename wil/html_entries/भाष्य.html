• bhāṣya [619] mfn. (-ṣyaḥ-ṣyā-ṣyaṃ) To be said or spoken. n. (-ṣyaṃ) 1 A commentary, but particularly the explanation and application of a technical Sūtra or aphorism; hence applied to many of the annotations on the grammatical aphorisms of PĀṆINI, to comments on the Vedas, &c. 2 A sort of building. E. bhāṣa to speak, yat aff.
