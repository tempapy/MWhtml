• manda mfn. (-ndaḥ-ndā-ndaṃ)
    1 Slow, tardy.
    2 Dull, stupid, heavy.
    3 Foolish, a fool.
    4 Unlucky.
    5 Sick, diseased.
    6 Little.
    7 Idle, lazy.
    8 Drunken, addicted to drunkenness.
    9 Vile, wicked.
    10 Self-willed.
    11 Cold, phlegmatic, apathetic.
    12 Low, (as a tone.) m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 A name of SATURN.
    2 A sort of elephant.
    3 A name of YAMA.
    4 (In astronomy,) Anomaly.
    5 A destruction of the universe.
    E. madi to be lazy, &c. aff. ghañ. [642]
