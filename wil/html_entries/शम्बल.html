• śambala mn. (-laḥ-laṃ)
    1 Provender for a journey, stock for travelling expences.
    2 A bank, a shore.
    3 Envy, impatience or dislike of another's success. f. (-lī) A procuress, a bawd.
    E. śamba to go, to collect, alac aff.; also śaṃvala, and sambala or saṃvala.
