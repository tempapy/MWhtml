• narttaka [456] m. (-kaḥ) 1 An actor, a mime. 2 A juggler, a dancer, a player of any description. 3 A bard, a herald. 4 An elephant. 5 A reed, (Arundo karka, Rox.) 5 A peacock. f. (-kī) 1 An actress, female dancer or singing girl. 2 A female elephant. 3 A peahen. E. nṛta to dance or act, ṣvun aff.
