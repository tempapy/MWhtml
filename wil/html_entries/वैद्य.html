• vaidya [810] mfn. (-dyaḥ-dī-dyaṃ) 1 Medical, practising or relating to medicine. 2 Relating or conformable to the Vedas. m. (-dyaḥ) 1 A physician. 2 A tree, (Justicia ganderussa, &c.) 3 A learned man. 4 A follower of the Vedas, or one well read in them. f. (-dyā) A drug, commonly Kākoli. E. veda the medical or Āyur Vedas, or the Vedas in general, and yañ or ṣyañ aff.
