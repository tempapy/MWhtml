• agra [008] mfn. (-graḥ-grā-graṃ) 1 Chief, principal. 2 Prior, first. 3 Excessive, more, much. 4 Excellent, best. n. (-graṃ) 1 Front, fore part. 2 Top, summit, upper part. 3 End, point. 4 Goal, resting place. 5 Assemblage, multitude. 6 A weight equal to one Pala. 7 Limited alms, (four mouthfuls.) 8 (In astronomy) the sun's amplitude. E. agi to go, and the Uṇādi aff. ran the nasal of the derivative is dropped.
