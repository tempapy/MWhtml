• janya [340] mfn. (-nyaḥ-nyā-nyaṃ) 1 To be born or produced. 2 Fit for or favourable to men. m. (-nyaḥ) 1 A father. The friend, attendant, or companion of a bridegroom. f. (-nyā) The friend of a mother. 2 The relation or companion of a bride, a bridesmaid, &c. 3 Pleasure, happiness. 4 Affection. 5 A mother. n. (-nyaṃ) 1 War, combat. 2 Rumour, report. 3 A market, a fair. 4 A portent, one occurring at birth. E. jana to be born, &c. affix yat, or Uṇādi affix yuk.
