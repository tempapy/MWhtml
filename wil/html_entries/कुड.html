• kuḍa (śi) r. 6th cl. (kuḍati)
    1 To play or act as a child, to trifle.
    2 To eat.
    3 To heap. (i,) kuḍi r. 1st cl. (kuṃḍati)
    1 To confound or disorder with grief or pain.
    2 To form, to make.
    3 (kuṇḍate) To burn, to heat. r. 1st and 10th cls. (kuṃḍati kuṇḍayati) To preserve. [227]
