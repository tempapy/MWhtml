• vidala [767] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Opened, expanded, blown, (as a flower, &c.) 2 Rent, split. n. (-laṃ) 1 A shallow basket made of split bambus, a vessel of wicker work. 2 Split peas. 3 Pomegranate bark. 4 The cuttings or chips of any substance, that which has been pared or split. m. (-laḥ) 1 Dividing, separating. 2 A cake. 3 Mountain ebony, (Bauhinia variegata, &c.) E. vi before dala tearing, splitting.
