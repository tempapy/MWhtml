• aryya [072] mfn. (-ryyaḥ-ryyā-ryyaṃ) Excellent. m. (-ryyaḥ) 1 A master. 2 A man of the third tribe, the Vaiśya. f. (-ryyaḥ) 1 A woman of the third tribe. 2 The wife of a Vaiśya. E. ṛ to go, yat affix, and ṭāp or ṅīp for the fem.; ṅīṣ is affixed, when the word is fem. with reference to the male.
