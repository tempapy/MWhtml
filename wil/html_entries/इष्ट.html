• iṣṭa mfn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ)
    1 Wished, desired.
    2 Cherished, beloved.
    3 Worshipped, reverenced, respected. m. (-ṣṭaḥ)
    1 A lover, a husband.
    2 The Castor-oil tree. n. (-ṣṭaṃ)
    1 An act of sacrifice, an oblation, &c.
    2 An essential ceremony, as ablution, investiture, &c. Ind. (-ṣṭama) Voluntarily.
    E. iṣa to desire, or iṣa substituted for yaja to sacrifice, and kta aff. [134]
