• ña The nasal attached to the class of palatal letters, having something like the sound of N in the French word Singe, and expressed by that letter accented N. [358]
  • ña m. (ñaḥ)
    1 A name of ŚUKRA, regent of Venus.
    2 An ox.
    3 A heretic, an apostate.
    4 Singing.
    5 Jingling or inarticulate sound. [358]
