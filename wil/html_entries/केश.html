• keśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 Hair.
    2 A name of VARUṆA.
    3 A kind of perfume: see vālā.
    4 The name of a demon.
    5 A name of VIṢṆU. f. (-śī)
    1 A lock of hair on the crown of the head.
    2 The indigo plant.
    3 Indian spikenard.
    E. kliśa to bind, aṇ affix, and la rejected; or ka the head, and īśa to rule, affix ac; again, ka water or happiness, and īśa the lord or master.
