• mahāmātra [650] m. (-traḥ) 1 A king's minister or associate, any high officer in a kingdom: as a counsellor, a general, &c. 2 An elephant driver, a Mahout. 3 Superintendant of the elephants. 4 A man of wealth and consequence. f. (-trī) 1 The wife of a spiritual preceptor. 2 The wife of an officer of state, &c. E. mahā great, and mātra wealth or retinue, &c.
