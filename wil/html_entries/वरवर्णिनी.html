• varavarṇinī f. (-nī)
    1 An excellent woman.
    2 A virtuous woman.
    3 A woman in general.
    4 Turmeric.
    5 Lāc.
    6 A brownish yellow pigment; also rocanā and gorohanā.
    7 A plant, commonly Priyangu.
    8 A name of GAURĪ.
    9 SARASVATĪ.
    10 LAKṢMĪ.
    E. vara best, barṇa class or colour, aff. ini and ṅīṣ. [736]
