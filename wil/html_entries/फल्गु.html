• phalgu [596] mfn. (-lguḥ-lguḥ-lgu) 1 Pithless, sapless. 2 Vain, unprofitable, unmeaning, useless. 3 Weak, feeble. f. (-lguḥ) 1 The oppositeleaved fig tree, (F. oppositi-folia, Rox.) 2 The name of a river which is said to run underneath Gaya. 3 Falsehood, false or lying speech. 4 A red powder, usually of the root of wild ginger, coloured with sappan wood, and thrown over one another by the Hindus, at the Holi festival. 5 The spring season. E. phala to bear fruit, Uṇādi aff. guk
