• daśā [403] f. (-śā) or m. plu. (-śāḥ) 1 The ends of a piece of cloth, the end of a garment. 2 A state, a condition, a period or time of life, as youth, age, manhood, &c. 3 The wick of a lamp. 4 The mind or faculty, of understanding. 5 Aspect or position of the planets at birth. E. daśa to bite, to cut or divide, or be cut or divided, aṅ aff.
