• śrutādhyayanasampanna mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ) Well read in sacred science, conversant with the Vedas, and the branches of knowledge or philosophy, dependant on them.
    E. śruta heard, (knowledge of grammar, &c. that has been taught viva voce,) adhyayana perusal, (of the Vedas,) saspanna accomplished.
