• tuṅga [379] mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgā-ṅgaṃ) 1 High, elevated, lofty. 2 Chief, principal. 3 Passionate, hot. m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 A tree, (Rottleria tinctoria.) 2 A mountain. 3 The planet Mercury. 4 The superior apsis or aphelion of a planet. 5 Top, vertex, altitude. 6 The cocoanut tree. f. (-ṅgī) 1 A kind of basil, (Ocymum gratissimum.) 2 Turmerick. 3 Night. f. (-ṅgā) Bambu manna. E. tuji to guard, to dwell, to hurt, &c. affix ghañ.
