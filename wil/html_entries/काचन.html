• kācana n. (-naṃ) An enclosure, a string or tape which ties a parcel or bundle or papers, &c.
    E. kaca to tie, lyuṭ affix; also with kan added kācanaka m. (-kaḥ.) [207]
  • kācana ind. Some one, any one, (applied to feminine objects,) the first member is declinable.
    E. kā fem. of kima, cana added. [207]
