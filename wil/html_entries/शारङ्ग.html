• śāraṅga mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgī-ṅgaṃ) Of a variegated colour. m. (-ṅgaḥ)
    1 The Cātaka (Cuculus melanoleucos.)
    2 A deer.
    3 An elephant.
    4 A bee.
    5 A peacock. f. (-ṅgī) A musical instrument, a stringed instrument played with a bow, a sort of fiddle.
    E. śāra injuring or variegated, gama to go, or get, aff. khac; more usually, sāraṅga. [838]
