• pradhāna [572] n. (-naṃ) 1 Nature, the natural state of any thing, or the cause of the material world. 2 The SUPREME GOD. 3 Intellect, understanding. 4 Chief, principal, (in this sense, it is always neuter and confined to the singular number.) mn. (-naḥ-naṃ) 1 The first companion of a king, his minister, his eunuch or confident, &c. a courtier, a noble. 2 An elephant-driver. E. pra before dhā to have, aff. lyuṭ.
