• adṛṣṭa [020] n. (-ṣṭaṃ) 1 Casual and unseen danger, (as from conflagration, inundation, &c.) 2 Fortune, destiny, fate. 3 (In logic) Virtue or vice, as the eventual cause of pleasure or pain. mfn. (ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ) 1 Unseen, unforeseen. 2 Unknown, unfelt, not experienced. E. a neg. and dṛṣṭa seen.
