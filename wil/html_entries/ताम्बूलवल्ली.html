• tāmbūlavallī f. (-llī) A small plant bearing a pungent leaf, which with the areca nut, a small quantity of caustic lime (or Chunam) and catechu, and occasionally cloves, cardamoms, &c. form the Pan or betel of the Asiatic, (Piper betel.)
    E. tāmbūla the same, and vallī foot-stalk; also with kan added tāmbūlavallikā. [372]
