• anuttara [032] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Chief, principal. 2 Best, excellent. 3 Unable to answer, silent. 4 Fixed, firm. 5 Low, inferior. 6 South, southern. n. (-raṃ) A reply which is incoherent or evasive, and is therefore held to be no answer. E. an neg. and uttara subsequent, &c.
