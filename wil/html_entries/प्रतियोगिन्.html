• pratiyogin mfn. (-gī-ginī-gi) Who or what impedes or opposes.
    2 What co-operates with. m. (gī)
    1 An opponent.
    2 A partner, an associate.
    3 A counterpart.
    4 Any thing or object dependant upon another and not existing without it.
    E. pratiyoga, and ini aff. [566]
