• patra n. (-traṃ)
    1 A leaf.
    2 A vehicle in general; as a car, a horse, a camel, &c.
    3 The wing of a bird.
    4 The feather of an arrow.
    5 The leaf of the Laurus cassia.
    6 The leaf of a book.
    7 Goldleaf, &c. any thin sheet or plate of metal. nf. (-traṃ-trī) A letter, any written document or address.
    E. pata to go, ṣṭran aff. and one ta rejected; also sometimes pattra. [498]
