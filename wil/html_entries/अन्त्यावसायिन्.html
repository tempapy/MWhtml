• antyāvasāyin [039] mf. (-yī-yinī) A man or woman of low caste, especially one of seven classes: a Caṇḍāla, a Śvapaca or executioner, a Kṣattā or insect hunter, a charioteer, a bard, and a Vaideha or attendant on women. 2 A man or a woman of a particular caste living by the chase. See antāvasāyin.
