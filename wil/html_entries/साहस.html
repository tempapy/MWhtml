• sāhasa n. (-saṃ)
    1 Punishment, chastisement, fine.
    2 Violence, the perpetration of any act of rapine or aggression.
    3 Oppression, cruelty.
    4 Hatred.
    5 Rape, ravishment.
    6 Boldness, daring.
    7 Rashness.
    8 Oblation with fire.
    E. sahas strength, aṇ aff.
