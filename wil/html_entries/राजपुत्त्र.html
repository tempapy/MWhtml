• rājaputtra [701] m. (-ttraḥ) 1 The planet MERCURY. 2 A Kṣatriya or man of the military tribe. 3 A soldier by profession, but one of a mixed birth or fabulous origin. 4 The son of a king, a prince. f. (-ttrī) 1 Arabian jasmine. 2 A bitter gourd. 3 The musk shrew. 4 A kind of brass or bell-metal. 5 A princess. 6 A woman of the Rajput tribe. E. rāja a king or the moon, and puttra a son, a child.
