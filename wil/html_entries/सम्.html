• sam ind. A particle and prefix implying;
    1 Union, junction, (with, together.)
    2 Assemblage, collection.
    3 Beauty or perfection.
    4 Intensity. As a prefix it corresponds to con, co, com, &c. Before a consonant the final of this word is changed to Anusvāra, and is written saṃ.
