• arddhacandra m. (-ndraḥ)
    1 A crescent or half moon.
    2 The hand bent into a semicircle or the shape of a claw, as for the purpose of seizing or clutching any thing.
    3 The semicircular marks on a peacock's tail.
    4 An arrow, with a head like a crescent.
    5 The semicircular scratch of the finger nail. f. (-ndrā) A plant, vulgarly Teori, the black kind.
    E. arddha half, and candra moon, or with kan added arddhacandrakaḥ.
