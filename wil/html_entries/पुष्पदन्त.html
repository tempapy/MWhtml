• puṣpadanta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 The elephant of the north-west quarter.
    2 A chief of the Gandharbas or Vidyādharas, attendant upon ŚIVA.
    3 One of the Jinas or Jaina teachers.
    4 A Nāga or serpent of the infernal regions. m. dual. (-ntau) The sun and moon.
    E. puṣpa flower, and danta a tooth.
