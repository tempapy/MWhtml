• virakta mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ)
    1 Averse, indifferent, free from inclination or affection.
    2 Stoical, void of attachment to worldly objects.
    3 Empassioned, interested, feeling passion or regard for any person or any thing.
    E. vi privative or pleonastic, before rañja to feel passion, aff. kta.
