• nāyaka [462] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A guide, a leader, a conductor. 2 A chief, a head, pre-eminent, principal. 3 A general, a commander. 4 The central gem of a necklace. 5 (In Erotick poetry,) The man, the husband or lover. f. (-yikā) 1 A mistress, a wife, the female in the amatory poetry of the Hindūs. 2 A sort of goddess, an inferior form of DURGĀ, and attendant upon her: there are eight Nāyikās. E. ṇī to guide, ṇvul aff.
