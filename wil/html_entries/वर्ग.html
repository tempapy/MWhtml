• varga [737] n. (-rgaḥ) A class, a tribe, a multitude of similar things, whether animate or inanimate; as Ka-verga, the class of guttural letters; Tri-verga, a class of three (objects, as love, duty, and wealth); the Śūdra-varga, the Śūdra tribe. 2 A chapter, a book, a section. 3 A square number, (in arithmetic.) E. vṛja to quit, to except (any thing else), aff. ghañ.
