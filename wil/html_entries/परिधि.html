• paridhi [509] m. (-dhiḥ) 1 The circumference of a circle. 2 The disk of the sun or moon. 3 The periphery of a wheel. 4 A circle surrounding the globe, a great circle. 5 An epicycle. 6 The branch of the tree to which the victim at a sacrifice is tied. 7 A wooden frame round the hole in which a sacrificial fire is lighted. E. pari around, dhā to have, ki aff.
