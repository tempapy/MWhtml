• para [502] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Distant, removed, remote. 2 Best, preeminent. 3 Subsequent, after, following. 4 Other, different. 5 Inimical, hostile, estranged, adverse. 6 Addicted to, attached to, engaged in. 7 More, exceeding, as paraṃśataṃ more than a hundred, paro lakṣaḥ more than a Lac: these are also considered as distinct compounds, see the next. m. (-raḥ) 1 An enemy. 2 The life of BRAHMĀ. n. ind. (-raṃ) 1 Only, alone. 2 After, afterwards. subst. 1 Final beatitude. 2 (In Logic,) One species of common property, that which comprehends many objects, genus; or it is the same with existence, as the common property of all things. E. pṝ to fill, &c. aff. ap.
