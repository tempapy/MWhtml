• parañja m. (-ñjaḥ)
    1 An oil press.
    2 Froth, foam.
    3 The blade of a sword or a knife.
    4 A sword or scymitar. f. (-ñjā) The sound of instruments at a festival. n. (-ñjaṃ) The sword of INDRA.
    E. para in the second case, another, and ji to conquer, aff. ḍa; some works have paraja, and others parāñja in place of this word. [502]
