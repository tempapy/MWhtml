• jīviteśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ) Ruling life, master of being, (applicable to objects either of reverence or affection.) m. (-śaḥ)
    1 A name of YAMA, regent of death.
    2 The sun.
    3 The moon.
    4 A lover, a husband.
    5 Lord or master of existence.
    6 A drug to revive the dead.
    E. jīvita life, and īśa lord, ruler.
