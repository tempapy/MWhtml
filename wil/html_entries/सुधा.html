• sudhā f. (-dhā)
    1 Nectar, the beverage of immortality and sustenance of the gods.
    2 The nectar or honey of flowers.
    3 Juice.
    4 Plaster, mortar.
    5 A brick.
    6 The Ganges.
    7 Water.
    8 Lightning.
    9 The milk-hedge plant, (Euphorbia antiquorum, &c.)
    10 A plant, (Aletris Hyacinthoides.)
    11 Emblic myrobalan.
    12 Yellow myrobalan.
    E. su pleasure, dhe to drink, or dhā to have, to support, (life,) aṅ and ṭāp affs.
