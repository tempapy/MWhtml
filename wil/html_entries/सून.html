• sūna mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ)
    1 Blown, budded, (as a flower.)
    2 Empty, vacant.
    3 Born, produced. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Bringing forth, (as young,) parturition.
    2 A flower. f. (-nā)
    1 A daughter.
    2 The uvula.
    3 A shambles, a slaughter-house.
    4 A place or utensil in a house, where, or by which, insects may be killed, as the hearth, the slab, the pestle and mortar, the broom, and the water-pot.
    5 Hurting, injuring.
    6 Inflammation of the glands of the neck.
    7 A zone, a girdle.
    8 A ray.
    9 A river.
    E. ṣū to bear, &c., aff. kta, or ṣu the same, na Uṇādi aff.
