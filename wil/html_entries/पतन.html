• patana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Who or what goes, falls, descends, &c. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Falling.
    2 Alighting, descending.
    3 Falling from dignity, virtue, &c.
    4 Sin.
    5 Going.
    6 Substraction.
    7 The latitude of a planet.
    E. pata to fall, yuc or lyaṭ aff. [497]
