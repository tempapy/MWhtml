• pāruṣya n. (-ṣyaṃ)
    1 Abuse, reproach, scurrilous, opprobrious or unfriendly speech.
    2 The property of harshness, &c. in speaking.
    3 The forest or grove of INDRA.
    4 Aloe wood or Agallochum.
    5 Severity, violence, either in word or deed, as vākpāruṣyaṃ defamation, abuse, or violence of words, and daṇḍapāruṣyaṃ personal injury, assault or violence of blows. m. (-ṣyaḥ) A name of VṚHASPATI.
    E. paruṣa harsh, contumelious, &c. aff. ṣyañ.
