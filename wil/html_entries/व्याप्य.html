• vyāpya [819] mfn. (-pyaḥ-pyā-pyaṃ) 1 Permeable, penetrable. 2 Capable of containing any inherent property. n. (-pyaṃ) 1 An instrument or agent. 2 The thing or substance which may be the site or subject of attributes or inherent properties. 3 The subject of an inference, as fire inferred from the presence of smoke, &c. 4 A drug, (Costus speciosus.) E. vi before āpa to pervade, aff. yat.
