• parisara m. (-raḥ)
    1 Ground on the skirts of a river or mountain, or contiguous to a town, &c.
    2 Death, dying.
    3 Rule, precept, prescribed mode.
    4 Part, place, site.
    5 Width, breadth.
    E. pari about, or end, term, &c. sara going, from sṛ to go, aff. ap. [513]
