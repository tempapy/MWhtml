• aṣṭaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Eight.
    2 Eighth. n. (-kaṃ) The eight sections of PĀṆINI'S Grammar. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 The formulae of the Rig Veda as collected into eight portions, thence called Aṣṭakas.
    2 One acquainted with PĀṆINI'S rules or the formulae of the Veda.
    E. kana added to aṣṭa. [091]
