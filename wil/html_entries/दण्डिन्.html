• daṇḍin mfn. (-ṇḍī-ṇḍinī-ṇḍi) Having a staff or stick. m. (-ṇḍī)
    2 A doorkeeper, a warder, a porter, a staff or mace-bearer.
    2 A name of YAMA.
    3 A man of the fourth order or beggar.
    4 A mendicant carrying a staff.
    5 A mendicant of a particular order, derived from ŚAṂKARĀCĀRYA.
    6 A Jina or Jaina saint.
    E. daṇḍa a staff, &c. and ini aff. [397]
