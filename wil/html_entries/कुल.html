• kula r. 1st cl. (kolati)
    1 To accumulate.
    2 To be of kin, to act as of kin.
    3 To flow homogeneously.
    4 To count.
    With āṅ prefixed, to be attentive;
    with vi and āṅ to be bewildered, flurried or distressed. [233]
  • kula n. (-laṃ)
    1 Family, race, tribe or caste.
    2 A herd, a flock, &c. of animals of the same species.
    3 An inhabited country.
    4 A house, an abode.
    5 The body.
    6 The front or fore part.
    7 A blue stone: see kulattha. m. (-laḥ) An artist of celebrated descent. f. (-liḥ or -lī) A prickly nightshade: see kaṇṭakārī, &c. (-lī) A wife's elder sister.
    E. kula to accumulate, ka affix, or kuṅ to sound, and lak affix, or ku the earth, and la who takes or possesses, from lā with ḍa aff. [233]
