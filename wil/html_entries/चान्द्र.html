• cāndra mfn. (-ndraḥ-ndrī-ndraṃ) Lunar, relating to the moon, regulated by it, &c. m. (-ndraḥ)
    1 The Candrakānta or moon-gem.
    2 A month, a lunar month.
    3 The light fortnight or half month, during which the moon is on the increase. n. (-ndraṃ) The Cāndrāyaṇa observance: see cāndrāyaṇa. f. (-ndrī) Moonlight.
    E. candra the moon, and aṇ affix of relation.
