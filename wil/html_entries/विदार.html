• vidāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Tearing, rending.
    2 War, battle.
    3 An inundation, an overflow, the rising of a river or pond above its banks. f. (-rī)
    1 A plant, (Hedysarum gangeticum.)
    2 A climbing plant, (Ipomoea paniculata.)
    3 Bubo, swelling in the groin, &c.
    E. vi before dṛ to tear, to divide, aff. ghañ.
