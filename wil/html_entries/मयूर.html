• mayūra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A peacock.
    2 A flower, the coxcomb, (Celosia cristata.)
    3 A plant, (Achyranthes aspera.) f. (-rī)
    1 A peahen.
    2 A potherb, (Basella rubra, &c.)
    E. mi to scatter, Uṇādi aff. ūran or mahī the earth in the seventh case, mahyāṃ, ru to cry, ḍa aff., and the formation irr. [644]
