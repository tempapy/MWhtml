• piṭhara mf. (-raḥ-rī) A pot, a pan. n. (-raṃ)
    1 A churning stick.
    2 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus rotundus.)
    3 A sort of building, a hut or watch box made of bambus and mats, or as it is sometimes explained, a kind of store room or scullery.
    E. piṭha pain, rā to take, aff. ka.
