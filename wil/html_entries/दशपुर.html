• daśapura n. (-raṃ) A fragrant grass, (Cyperus rotundus;) see dāśapura.
    2 A district, part of Malva or Bundelkhand.
    3 A town or city.
    E. daśa for dāśa a fisherman, and pura or pūra to nourish, affix daśa; also daśa ten, and pura a city: a district of ten cities. [402]
