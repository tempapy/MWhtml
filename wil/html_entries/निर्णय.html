• nirṇaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 Certainty, positive conclusion.
    2 Doubt, discussion, investigation.
    3 (In Law,) Sentence, decision.
    4 (In the Mīmāṃsā,) The application of a conclusive argument.
    5 (In Logic,) Complete ascertainment.
    E. nir affirmative prefix, ṇī to guide, affix ac.
