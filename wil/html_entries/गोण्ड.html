• goṇḍa mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā-ṇḍaṃ) A person with a prominent navel. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A man of a low tribe, a mountaineer, especially inhabiting the eastern portion of the Vindhya range, between the Nerbudda and Kṛṣṇa, giving name to the province of Gondwana.
    2 A prominent navel, or a lump of flesh on the navel, &c.
    E. guḍi to enclose, &c. ac aff. [298]
