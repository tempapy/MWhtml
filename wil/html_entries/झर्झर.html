• jharjhara mf. (-raḥ-rī) A sort of drum. m. (-raḥ)
    1 The Kali yug, the present Yug or age of the world.
    2 The name of a river. f. (-rā) A whore. n. (-raṃ) A sound as of splashing or dropping.
    E. jharjha an imitative sound like that of water splashing, &c. and ra what makes, from rā with ḍa affix, or jharjha to censure, and aran Uṇādi aff. [357]
