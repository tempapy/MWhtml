• abja m. (-bjaḥ)
    1 The moon.
    2 A kind of tree, (Barringtonia acutangula.)
    3 The physician of the gods. See dhanvantari. mn. (-bjaḥ-bjaṃ) A conch. n. (-bjaṃ)
    1 A lotus.
    2 A large number, a million of millions.
    E. av for ap water, and ja who is born; from the situation of the things named, or their production at the churning of the ocean. [051]
