• pācana mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ)
    1 What cooks or ripens, &c.
    2 Digestive.
    3 Suppurative. n. (-naṃ)
    1 A medicinal preparation, an infusion, a decoction, &c. of various drugs, chiefly carminatives or gentle stimuli given to bring the vitiated humours in fever, &c. to maturity, a sort of diet drink.
    2 Penance, expiation. m. (-naḥ)
    1 Fire.
    2 Acidity, sourness. f. (-nī) A sort of myrobalan, (Terminalia chebula.)
    E. paca to dress, in the causal form, aff. lyuṭ.
