• vyaḍambaka m. (-kaḥ) The castor oil plant, (Ricinus communis.)
    E. vi before aḍa to strive, aff. ac, vyaḍa here said to mean excretion, avi to go, to cause, ṇvul aff.; form irr.; also with yuc aff. vyaḍambana m. (-naḥ) and by another reading, vyaḍambara; other etymologies are given.
