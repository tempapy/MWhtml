• dhur f. (-dhūḥ)
    1 The fore part of a carriage, the pole or the part where the yoke is fixed.
    2 Agitation, trembling.
    3 Reflection, recollection.
    4 A spark of fire.
    5 A burthen, a load.
    6 A part, a portion.
    7 Wealth.
    8 A name of the Ganges.
    E. dhurbba to hurt, affix kvip, and va rejected; also with ṭāp added, dhurā. [445]
