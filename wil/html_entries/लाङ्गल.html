• lāṅgala [719] n. (-laṃ) 1 A plough. 2 The penis. 3 The palm tree. 4 A sort of flower. 5 The main beam of a house. f. (-lī) 1 An aquatic shrub, (Jussieua repens.) 2 A creeping shrub, (Commelina salicifolia.) 3 Another creeper, (Nama repens.) 4 A plant, (Gloriosa superba.) E. lagi to go, to limp, &c., Uṇādi aff. kala, and the vowel made long.
