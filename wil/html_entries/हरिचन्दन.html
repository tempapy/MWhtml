• haricandana mn. (-naḥ-naṃ)
    1 A yellow and fragrant sort of Sandal wood.
    2 One of the trees of paradise. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Saffron.
    2 Moon-light.
    3 The farina of the lotus.
    4 The person of a lover or mistress.
    E. hari VIṢṆU, or yellow, and candana Sandal. [969]
