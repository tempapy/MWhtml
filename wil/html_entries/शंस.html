• śaṃsa (u,) śaṃsu r. 1st cl. (śaṃsati)
    1 To praise.
    2 To hurt.
    3 To wish.
    4 To calumniate.
    With abhi prefixed, To accuse wrongfully.
    With āṅ
    1 To hope.
    2 To speak.
    With pra, To praise, to flatter; this root and its derivatives are sometimes read with the palatal sibilant final or śaṃśa, &c.
