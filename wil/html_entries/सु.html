• su [927] r. 1st cl. (savati) 1 To bring forth, as young. 2 To possess power. 3 To go: see ṣu and ṣū.
  • su [927] ind. A particle and prefix analogous to good, well, or the Greek Eu, and implying, reverence, worship, honour; 2 Assent; 3 Encrease, prosperity; 4 Excess, exceeding; 5 Pain, distress; 6 Pleasure, delight. E. ṣu to go, aff. ḍu.
