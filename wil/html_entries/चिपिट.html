• cipiṭa mfn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭā-ṭaṃ) Flat-nosed.
    E. ni preposition, piṭañ affix in this sense, and ni changed to ci. m. (-ṭaḥ) Rice or grain flattened.
    E. piṭa to flatten, ka affix, the derivative reduplicated, and ca substituted for the radical; also with kan added cipiṭaka; also the pen. becoming u, cipuṭa. [327]
