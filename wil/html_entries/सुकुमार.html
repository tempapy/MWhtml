• sukumāra mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ)
    1 Soft, smooth, tender.
    2 Youthful, young. m. (-raḥ) A variety of the sugar-cane.
    3 A sort of grain, (Panicum colonum, &c.)
    4 A wild kind of Champak.
    5 The name of a Daitya. f. (-rā)
    1 Double jasmine.
    2 Great-flowered jasmine.
    3 The plantain.
    4 The name of a river.
    E. su very, exceedingly, kumāra soft, young.
