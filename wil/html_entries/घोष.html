• ghoṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 A station of herdsman.
    2 A herdsman.
    3 A creeping plant, commonly called by the same name Ghoṣa, and bearing white or yellow flowers, (Luffa pentandra and acutangula.)
    4 Bellmetal, tutenague, &c.
    5 Low thunder or the muttering of clouds.
    6 Sound.
    7 A proclamation.
    8 A gnat, a musquito.
    9 A term proper for the name of a Kāyastha. f. (-ṣā) A kind of fennel, (Anethum sowa:) see maghurikā.
    E. ghusi to shine, to sound, &c. affix ghañ.
