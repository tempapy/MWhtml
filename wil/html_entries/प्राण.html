• prāṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Full, replete, filled. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 Air inhaled, inspiration, breath.
    2 Air, wind.
    3 Life, vitality.
    4 A vital organ or part.
    5 Strength, power.
    6 Myrrh.
    7 Poetical talent or inspiration.
    8 A name of BRAHMĀ.
    9 A title of BRAHMĀ, the supreme spirit.
    10 An aspiration in the articulation of letters. m. plu. (-ṇāḥ) The five vital airs or modes of inspiration and expiration collectively.
    E. pra before ana to breathe, aff. ac.
