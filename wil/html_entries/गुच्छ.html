• guccha m. (-cchaḥ)
    1 A cluster of blossoms.
    2 A clump of grass.
    3 A necklace of thirty-two strings.
    4 A pearl necklace.
    5 A peacock's plumage or bundle of peacock's feathers.
    6 A bundle. f. (-cchī) A species of Bonduc: see karañja.
    E. gu to sound, cha affix, or gudha to play, and cchak affix, where bees, &c. sport.
