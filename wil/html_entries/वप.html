• vapa(ḍu)ḍuvapa [734] r. 1st cl. (vapati) 1 To sow seed. 2 To procreate. 3 To shave. 4 To weave.
  • vapa [734] m. (-paḥ) 1 Shaving. 2 Sowing seed. 3 Weaving. f. (-pā) 1 The mucous or glutinous secretion of the flesh or bones; according to some, also the marrow of the bones. 2 Any hole or cavity. E. vapa to sow seed, &c., aff. ac.
