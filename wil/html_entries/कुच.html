• kuca r. 1st cl. (kocati)
    1 To sound high, to utter a loud, shrill cry, as a bird.
    2 To go.
    3 To polish.
    4 To touch.
    5 To furrow or mark with lines.
    6 To be crooked.
    7 To write.
    8 To oppose.
    9 To contend.
    10 To be restricted or confined, to contract. r. 6th cl. (śi) śikuca. (kucati) or with saṃ prefixed, (saṃkocati or saṃkucati)
    1 To straiten, to narrow or contract.
    2 To be straitened, to shrink, to contract.
    With ut to bribe;
    with vi and ā, to expand. [225]
  • kuca m. (-caḥ) A breast, a pap.
    E. kuca to bind or confine, ka aff. [225]
