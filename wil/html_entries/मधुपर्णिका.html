• madhuparṇikā f. (-kā)
    1 The indigo plant.
    2 A tree, (Gmelina arborea.)
    3 Moon plant, (Asclepias acida.)
    4 A creeper of the cryptogamia class, (Lycopodium imbricatum, Rox.)
    5 A shrub, (Menispermum glabrum.)
    E. madhu honey, parṇa a leaf, aff. kan, fem. form; also madhuparṇī.
