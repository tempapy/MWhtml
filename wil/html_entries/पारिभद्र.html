• pāribhadra m. (-draḥ)
    1 The coral tree, (Erythrina fulgens.)
    2 The Nimb tree.
    3 A sort of pine, (Pinus Devadāru.)
    4 The Saral, also a sort of pine, (P. longifolia.)
    E. pari completely, bhadra auspicious, aff. aṇ; also with kan added, pāribhadraka. [529]
