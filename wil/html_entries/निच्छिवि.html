• nicchivi m. (-viḥ) A man of one of the degraded classes, born from the outcast Kṣatriya; the occupation of this caste is apparently public exhibition of tricks, prize-fighting, &c. being classed with the Jhallas, Mallas, and others.
    E. ni privative, chavi light or credit, i substituted for the penultimate. [466]
