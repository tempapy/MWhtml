• aya [064] r. 1st cl. (ayate) To go, to go to or towards.
    With prati to believe, to rely on. A preposition containing ra substitutes la before this root, as pra and para make plāyate, palāyate to fly, to run away.
  • aya [064] m. (-yaḥ) Good luck, favourable fortune. E. iṇa to go, and ac affix; happiness proceeding by it.
