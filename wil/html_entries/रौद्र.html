• raudra mfn. (-draḥ-drī-draṃ)
    1 Formidable, fearful, terrific.
    2 Sharp, acute.
    3 Relating or belonging to RUDRA or ŚIVA.
    4 Violent, wrathful, irascible. mn. (-draḥ-draṃ) Wrath, rage, (the sentiment as an object of poetical description) m. (-draḥ)
    1 Heat, warmth.
    2 The cold season.
    3 YAMA. f. (-drī) GAURI, the wife of ŚIVA.
    E. rudra ŚIVA, &c., and aṇ aff. [712]
