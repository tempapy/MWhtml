• barha [599] r. 1st cl. (barhate) 1 To be pre-eminent or excellent; (also varha, but less usually.) 2 To speak or tell. 3 To kill or hurt. 4 To cover. 5 To remember. 6 To spread. 7 To give. (In these senses it is more usually varha.) r. 10th cl. (barhayati) To hurt or kill; also varha.
  • barha [599] mn. (-rhaḥ-rhaṃ) 1 A peacock's tail. 2 A leaf. 3 Dependants, retinue. E. barha to spread, &c. aff. ac; this word and the corresponding forms are more usually derived from vṛha to increase, &c. and are written with the semivowel: see varha, varhis, &c.
