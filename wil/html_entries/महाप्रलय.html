• mahāpralaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 A destruction of the world, occurring after every period of 4,320,000,000 years.
    2 A total destruction of the universe, happening after a period commensurate with the life of BRAHMĀ, or 100 years, each day of which is equal to the period first stated, and each night of which is of similar duration; at the expiration of this term, the seven Lokas, with the saints, gods, and BRAHMĀ himself, are annihilated.
    E. mahā great, and pralaya destruction. [650]
