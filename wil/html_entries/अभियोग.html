• abhiyoga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 Attack, assault.
    2 Challenging to fight.
    3 War, battle.
    4 Energetic effort, exertion, perseverance.
    5 Reprimand, reproof.
    5 Prohibition, prevention.
    7 Contact, conjunction.
    8 (In law) A plaint, a charge, an accusation.
    E. abhi, yuja to joint, and ghañ affix, ja being changed to ga. [055]
