• prakaraṇa [556] n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 An introduction, a prologue or prelude. 2 A poetical fiction or poem, in which the story and principal persons are wholly imaginary; the term is especially applied to a dramatic poem. 3 A chapter, a section, a book, a place of pausing or stopping. 4 Treating with respect. 5 Doing much or well. f. (-ṇī) A minor drama of the same character as the Prakaraṇa, but of less extent; also prakaraṇikā. E. pra before kṛ to make, aff. lyuṭ.
