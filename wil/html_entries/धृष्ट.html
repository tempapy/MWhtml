• dhṛṣṭa mfn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ)
    1 Impudent, confident, bold, shameless.
    2 Forward, intrusive, presumptuous.
    3 Secured, obtained.
    4 Profligate, abandoned. m. (-ṣṭaḥ) A faithless husband. f. (-ṣṭā) A disloyal or unchaste woman.
    E. dhṛṣa to be confident, affix kta. [447]
