• śikṣā [842] f. (-kṣā) 1 One of the six Vedāṅgas or sciences attached to the Vedas: the proper pronunciation of the vocal sounds which occur in them, as explained by PĀṆINI. 2 A plant, (Bignonia Indica.) 3 Learning, study, the acquisition of knowledge. 4 Modesty, humility. E. śikṣa to learn, affs. aṅ and ṭāp.
