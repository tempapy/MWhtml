• dhātṛ m. (-tā)
    1 A name of BRAHMĀ.
    2 A title of VIṢṆU.
    3 A father, a parent. f. (-trī)
    1 A mother.
    2 A foster-mother, a nurse.
    3 The earth.
    4 Emblick myrobalan. mfn. (-tā-tī-tṛ)
    1 A cherisher, a protector, fostering, nourishing.
    2 Having, containing, possessing, a possessor, &c.
    E. dhā to have, to nourish, &c. affix tṛc.
