• viveka [785] m. (-kaḥ) 1 Discrimination, judgment, the faculty of distinguishing things by their properties, and classing them according to their real not apparent nature; in the Vedānta system, it is applied to the power of separating Brahma or invisible spirit, from the ostensible world, truth from untruth, or reality from illusion. 2 Discussion, investigation. 3 A reservoir, a bath. E. vi severally, vica to judge, aff. ghañ.
