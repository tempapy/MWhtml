• kajjala m. (-laḥ)
    1 A cloud. n. (-laṃ) Lampblack, considered as a collyrium, being applied upon the eyelashes or eyelids, medicinally or ornamentatively; also sulphuret of lead or antimony, when so used. f. (-lī) Sulphuret of mercury, Æthiop's mineral. f. (-lā or -lī) A kind of fish, (Cyprinus atratus,) or any kind peculiar to stagnant waters, whence the name.
    E. kat for kut bad, and jala water or juice, as the moisture of the collyrium, &c. [180]
