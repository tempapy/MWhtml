• haṃsa [967] m. (-saḥ) 1 A goose, a gander, a swan. 2 BRAHMA, the supreme soul. 3 VIṢṆU. 4 The sun. 5 A liberal or moderate prince, one not covetous nor ambitious. 6 A sort of ascetic or devotee. 7 A particular Mantra, or mystical or magical prayer. 8 A horse. 9 One of the vital airs. 10 Envy, malice. 11 ŚIVA. 12 (In composition,) Best, excellent. 13 Preceding, before, in front. 14 A spiritual preceptor. 15 A mountain. 16 A buffalo. 17 KĀMA. f. (-sī) A goose. E. han to hurt or kill, Uṇādi aff. sa.
