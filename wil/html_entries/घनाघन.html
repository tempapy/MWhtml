• ghanāghana [308] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) 1 Mischievous, cruel. 2 Even, uniform, without spaces or interstices. m. (-naḥ) 1 A name of INDRA. 2 A vicious elephant, or one in rut. 3 A rainy cloud. 4 Mutual collision. f. (-nā) A vegetable, (Solanum Indicum;) also kākamācī. E. ghana as above, reiterated and the final of the first made long.
