• vichanda m. (-ndaḥ) A building of several stories, and surrounded by a portico, or having a portico to the east and west only; a palace, a temple.
    E. vi before chanda wish, will; also with kan added, vichandaka, and derived from chṛdi to be splendid, with vun aff. vicharddaka. [763]
