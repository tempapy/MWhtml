(-lyaṃ) 1 A bambu rod or stake. 2 Any stake or thorn. 3 An arrow. 4 An iron crow. 5 Sin, crime. 6 Difficulty, embarrassment, distress. 7 An arrow, a thorn, or other extraneous substance which has lodged in the body. 8 Abuse, defamation. 9 Poison. m. (-lyaḥ) 1 A thorny shrub, (Vangueria spinosa.) 2 A porcupine. 3 A hedgehog. 4 A peg, a pin, &c. 5 A boundary. 6 A king, the maternal uncle of YUDHIṢṬHIRA. E. śala to go, aff. yat or ya Uṇādi aff.
