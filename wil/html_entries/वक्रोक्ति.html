• vakrokti [728] f. (-ktiḥ) 1 Equivoque, evasion, pun, the covert expression of something else than the words used naturally imply, either from the manner in which they are uttered, or some other sense of which they are susceptible. 2 Hint, insinuation. 3 Sarcasm. E. vakra crooked, dishonest, and ukti speech.
