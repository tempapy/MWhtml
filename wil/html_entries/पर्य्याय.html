• paryyāya [517] m. (-yaḥ) 1 Order, arrangement, regular and methodical disposal or succession. 2 Manner, kind. 3 Opportunity, occasion. 4 Manufacture, preparation, artificial production. 5 Property of substances, as bitter, cooling, &c. 6 Generic or specific character. 7 Comprehensiveness, aggregation. 8 The text of a vocabulary, or the order of the synonyms for any term. E. pari successively, aya to go or proceed, aff. ghañ.
