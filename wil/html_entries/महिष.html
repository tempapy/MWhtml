• mahiṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 A buffalo.
    2 The emblem of one of the Jaina saints.
    3 The emblem and vehicle of YAMA.
    4 The name of an Asura or demon slain by DURGĀ. f. (-ṣī)
    1 A female buffalo.
    2 The wife of a king, but especially the one who has been consecrated or crowned, a queen.
    3 A drug.
    E. maha to worship or be worshipped, Uṇādi aff. ṭiṣac, fem. aff. ṅīṣ.
