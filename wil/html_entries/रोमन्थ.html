• romantha n. (-nthaṃ) Ruminating, chewing the cud, or also eating, without chewing what has been once swallowed and again brought into the mouth.
    E. roga disease, and mantha to destroy or remove, aff. ac, deriv. irr.; this habit in animals being indispensable to digestion and health. [711]
