• puttra [541] m. (-ttraḥ) 1 A son. 2 A child, in the language of the Vedas. 3 The fifth mansion from the point of conjunction of the sun, and a zodiacal sign. f. (-ttrī) A daughter. du. always, (ttrau) Son and daughter. E. put, and trā to preserve, the hell, (from,) aff. ka; or pūñ to purify, aff. ttra; one ta may be rejected in writing this word, and its derivatives, leaving putra.
