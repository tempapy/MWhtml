• javana [346] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-nī-naṃ) Quick, fleet. m. (-naḥ) 1 A courser, a fleet horse. 2 A country, Ionia or Greece: see yavana. 3 A native of that country. 4 A sort of deer. n. (-naṃ) Speed, velocity. f. (-nī) 1 A screen surrounding a tent, a Kenāt. 2 A drug. E. ju to be quick, affix lyuṭ or yuc, and fem. ṅīṣ or ṭāp.
