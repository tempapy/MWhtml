• moha m. (-haḥ)
    1 Fainting, loss of consciousness or sense.
    2 Ignorance, folly, foolishness; it is applied especially to that spiritual ignorance which leads men to believe in the reality of worldly objects, and to addict themselves to mundane or sensual enjoyment.
    3 Pain, affliction.
    4 Error, mistake.
    5 Bewilderment, distraction.
    E. muha to be ignorant or foolish, aff. ghañ. [676]
