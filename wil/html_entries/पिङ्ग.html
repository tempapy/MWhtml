• piṅga [532] mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgā-ṅgaṃ) Of a tawny colour. m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 Tawny, (the colour.) 2 A rat. n. (-ṅgaṃ) A young animal. f. (-ṅgā) 1 A yellow dye: see gorocanā. 2 Asafoetida. 3 A tubular vessel of the body, which according to the Yoga system, is the channel of respiration and circulation for one side. 4 A name of DURGĀ. 5 Turmerick. 6 Bambu manna. (-ṅgī) A sort of Mimosa, (M. suma, Rox.) E. piji to colour, aff. ac; or piṅga tawny, aff. ṭāp, &c.
