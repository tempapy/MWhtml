• saṃvara [874] n. (-raṃ) 1 Water. 2 Self-control. 3 Covering, concealing. m. (-raḥ) 1 The name of a demon. 2 Collection, comprehension. 3 Contraction, compression. 4 A mound, a bridge, &c. E. sam before vṛ to choose, aff. ap, more usually derived from śamba or ṣamba to collect, and then written śambara or sambara q. v.
