• a The first letter of the alphabet, and inherent short vowel. [001]
  • a ind.
    1 No, not.
    2 A privative, prohibitive, and diminutive particle.
    3 An interjection of pity. It also implies degrees of difference, and similarity. As a negative prefix to words beginning with a vowel, a is changed to an, as a and anta form ananta. [001]
  • a m. (aḥ) A name of VIṢṆU. [001]
