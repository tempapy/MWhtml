• a [001] The first letter of the alphabet, and inherent short vowel.
  • a [001] ind. 1 No, not. 2 A privative, prohibitive, and diminutive particle. 3 An interjection of pity. It also implies degrees of difference, and similarity. As a negative prefix to words beginning with a vowel, a is changed to an, as a and anta form ananta.
