• śuṣira mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Perforated, bored, pierced, full of holes or spaces. n. (-raṃ)
    1 A hole, a vacuity, a chasm.
    2 A wind-instrument. m. (-raḥ)
    1 Fire.
    2 A rat. f. (-rā)
    3 A sort of perfume, commonly Nalī.
    2 A river in general.
    E. śuṣa to dry, Uṇādi aff. kirac. [853]
