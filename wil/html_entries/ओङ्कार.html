• oṅkāra m. (-raḥ) The mysterious name of the deity: see om. f. (-rā) A Bauddha Śakti, or female personification of divine energy.
    E. oma, and kāra affix implying a letter, the compound letter o; otherwise om, signifying assent, and kāra who makes; what complies with our wishes. [175]
