• niyāmaka [470] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Guiding, governing, what regulates or restrains. m. (-kaḥ) 1 A boatman, a sailor; but variously applied to one who rows, who steers, or who keeps a look-out from the mast head. 2 A pilot, a helmsman. 3 A charioteer. E. ni before yama to go or stop, affix ṇvul.
