• sandhāna [889] n. (-naṃ) 1 Holding together, uniting, joining. 2 Tying, binding. 3 Peace, alliance. 4 Association, company. 5 Supporting, reception, receiving, sustaining. 6 Mixing, joining, intimate union or combination. 7 A relish, something eaten to excite thirst. 8 Sour rice-gruel. 9 Pickles, &c. acid preparation of the Bel and other fruits. 10 Spirituous liquor. 11 Contracting the skin, &c. by astringent applications. nf. (-naṃ-nī) Distillation, distilling, the manufacture of spirituous liquors. f. (-nī) A braziery, a foundery, a place where the base metals are stored or wrought. E. sam together, ghā to hold, aff. lyuṭ.
