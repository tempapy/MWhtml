• raktapuṣpaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 The Palāśa tree, (Butea frondosa.)
    2 A small tree, (Andersonia rohitaca, Rox.)
    3 A red flower, (Ixora bandhuca, or Pentapetes phoenicea.)
    4 The silk-cotton tree.
    5 The coral tree.
    6 A tree from which a red dye is prepared, (Rottleria tinctorea.) f. (-ṣpikā) A sort of sensitive plant or species of Lycopodium.
    E. kan added to the preceding. [691]
