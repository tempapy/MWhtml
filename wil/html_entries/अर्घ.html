• argha r. 1st cl. (arghati) To hurt or injure. r. 10th cl. (arghayati) To cost, to be worth. [068]
  • argha m. (rghaḥ)
    1 Price, cost.
    2 Mode of worship or reverence.
    3 An oblation of various ingredients to a god or Brahman.
    E. argha to cost, or arhaṃ to worship, ha being changed to gha, affix ac. [068]
