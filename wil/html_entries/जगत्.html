• jagat [336] mfn. (-gan-gatī-gat) Moveable, loco-motive, transitory. nf, (-t-tī) The world, the universe. m. (-t) Air, wind. f. (-tī) 1 The earth. 2 People, mankind. 3 A sort of metre, a stanza of forty-eight syllables variously disposed so as to form thirty varieties. 4 A field of Jambu flowers. E. gama to go, Uṇādi affix ati, jaga substituted for the root, and ṅīp fem. affix, or gama as before, and śatṛ aff.
