• atridṛgja m. (-jaḥ) A name of CANDRA or the moon.
    E. atri a saint, dṛś the eye, and ja born, produced. Born from the eye of ATRI, “a flash of light from the eye of ATRI, was received by space, a goddess, the via lactea personified, who bore SOMA.” The following and other similar names refer to this legend. [019]
