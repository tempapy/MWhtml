• yāvaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Half-ripe barley.
    2 Awnless barley.
    3 Forced rice.
    4 A sort of kidney-bean, (Phaseolus.)
    5 A kind of pulse, (Dolichos biflorus.)
    6 A kind of pulse said to be peculiar to Kashmir.
    7 Lac.
    E. yu to mix, aff. vun, and aṇ added, or yāva as above, pleonastic aff. kan. [685]
