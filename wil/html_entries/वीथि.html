• vīthi f. (-thiḥ or -thī)
    1 A road.
    2 A row, a line.
    3 A terrace in front of a house.
    4 A stall, a shop.
    5 A sort of drama, in one act, and by one or by two performers, the dramatic narration of an amatory story or intrigue.
    E. vitha to ask or beg, aff. in, and the vowel made long; also ṅīṣ optionally added, and with kan aff., fem. form, vīthikā.
