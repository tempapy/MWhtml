• arha r. 1st cl. (arhati)
    1 To worship or honour.
    2 To be fit or worthy: in the first sense it is also r. 10th cl. (arhayati.)
  • arha mfn. (-rhaḥ-rhā-rhaṃ)
    1 Proper, fit, becoming.
    2 Deserving, entitled to, worthy. m. (-rhaḥ) INDRA. f. (-rhā) Worship.
    E. arha to worship, to be fit, ac aff.
