• khaṭa [269] r. 1st cl. (khaṭati) To desire, to wish, to seek or inquire.
  • khaṭa [269] m. (-ṭaḥ) 1 Grass. 2 Phlegm, the phlegmatic or watery humor. 3 A blind well. 4 An axe, a hatchet. 5 A closed or doubled fist, as for striking. 6 A plough. 7 A fragrant kind of grass: see kattṛṇa. E. khaṭa to seek, to wish, affix ac.
