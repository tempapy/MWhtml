• gara mn. (-raḥ-raṃ)
    1 Poison.
    2 An antidote. m. (-raḥ) Sickness, disease. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Sprinkling, wetting.
    2 The fifth of the eleven Karaṇas. f. (-rā) Swallowing. (-rī) A species of grass, (Andropogon serratus); also garāgarī.
    E. gara to hurt, and ac affix, or gṛ to wet or sprinkle, or again gṝ to swallow, affix ap. [283]
