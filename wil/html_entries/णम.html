• ṇama r. 1st cl. (namati)
    1 To salute with reverence or respect.
    2 To bow or bend, to bow down in homage or worship.
    3 To sound.
    With ava or āṅ or sam prefixed, To bend down, to decline.
    With pra, To bow, to salute.
    With ut, To lift up, to raise, to make straight or erect. [361]
