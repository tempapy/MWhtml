• padmalāñchana m. (-naḥ)
    1 A king, a sovereign.
    2 A name of BRAHMĀ.
    3 The sun.
    4 KUVERA, the god of wealth. f. (-nā)
    1 A name of LAKṢMĪ.
    2 A name of SARASVATĪ.
    3 A title of TĀRĀ, one of the forms of DURGĀ.
    E. padma a lotus, a Catta or parasol shaped like one, a fabulous gem, &c. and lāñchana a mark or designation. [501]
