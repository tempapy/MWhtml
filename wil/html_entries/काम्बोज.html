• kāmboja m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A native of Kāmboja, a foreign race going like the Yavanas, with the whole of the head shaved, one of the tribes originally Kṣatriya, but degraded by omission of the essential rites.
    2 A horse of the breed of Kamboya or Kamboja.
    3 A white mimosa.
    4 A tree, commonly Punnag, (Rottleria tinctoria.) f. (-jī)
    1 A plant, commonly Maṣāni: see māṣaparṇī.
    2 A white mimosa, as above.
    3 The Gunja plant.
    E. kamboja Kamboja or Kamboya, a country in the north of India, aṇa and ṅīṣ affix: the plants, &c. being supposed indigenous to that district.
