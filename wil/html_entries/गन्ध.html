• gandha [280] r. 10th cl. (gandhayate) 1 To injure, to hurt or kill. 2 To move, to go. 3 To ask or beg.
  • gandha [280] m. (-ndhaḥ) 1 Smell, odour. 2 A perfume, a fragrance or fragrant substance. 3 A diffusive fragrance. 4 Sulphur. 5 Connexion, relationship. 6 A neighbour. 7 A plant, (Morunga hyperanthera, &c.) 8 Pride, arrogance. 9 Small, diminutive, a little, a small quantity, &c. 10 Pounded Sandal. f. (-ndhā) The bud of the Champaca flower, (Michelia Champaca.) n. (-ndhaṃ) Aloe-wood. E. gandha to hurt, to move, &c. affix ac.
