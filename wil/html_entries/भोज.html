• bhoja m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A country, Patna and Bhāgalpur.
    2 The name of a sovereign of Oujein, who is supposed to have flourished about the end of the tenth century; he was a celebrated patron of learned men, and the nine gems, or poets, and philosophers, are often ascribed to his æra.
    3 A cowherd.
    E. bhuja to enjoy, aff. ac, and the final consonant unchanged. [628]
