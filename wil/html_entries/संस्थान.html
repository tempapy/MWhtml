• saṃsthāna [877] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Like, resembling. n. (-naṃ) 1 Form, figure, shape. 2 Death, dying. 3 Fabrication, construction. 4 A place where four roads meet. 5 Any place. 6 A mark, a spot, a sign. 7 A heap, a quantity. 8 Primary formation, the aggregation of the primitive atoms. 9 A vicinity, a neighbourhood, a common place of abode. E. sam before ṣṭhā to stay or stand, aff. lyuṭ.
