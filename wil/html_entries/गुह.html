• guha r. 1st cl. (ū) guhū (guhati) To cover, to cover as with clothes, to wear.
  • guha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A name of KĀRTTIKEYA.
    2 A name of VIṢṆU.
    3 A swift horse.
    4 The name of a Niṣāda, ruler of Sringavera, the friend of RĀMA.
    5 A name or title proper to persons of the writer caste. f. (-hā)
    1 A cave, a cavern.
    2 A pit, a hole in the ground.
    3 A plant, commonly Cākuliya: see siṃhapucchī.
    E. guha to conceal, affixes aṅ and ṭāp.
