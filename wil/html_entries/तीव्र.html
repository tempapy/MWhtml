• tīvra [379] mfn. (-vraḥ-vrā-vraṃ) 1 Much, excessive, endless, unbounded, unlimited. 2 Pungent. 3 Hot, warm. m. (-vraḥ) A name of ŚIVA. f. (-vrā) 1 A medicinal plant, Katuki. 2 Black mustard. 3 A sort of Dūrvā or bent grass, (gaṇḍadūrvvā) 4 The name of a river in the east of Bengal; also the Caṇḍī or Padmāvatī. adv. n. (-vraṃ) 1 Much, excessively, endless. 2 Violently, fiercely. subst. 1 A shore, a bank. 2 Tin. 3 Heat. 4 Pungency. E. tīva to be large, &c. rak aff.
