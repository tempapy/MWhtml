• yakṣa r. 10th cl. (yakṣayate) To worship, to honour or adore.
  • yakṣa m. (-kṣaḥ)
    1 A demigod, attendant especially on KUVERA, and employed in the care of his gardens and treasures.
    2 A name of KUVERA.
    3 The residence of INDRA. f. (-kṣī) The wife of KUVERA.
    E. yakṣa to worship, aff. ghañ.
