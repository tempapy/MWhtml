• yakṣa [677] r. 10th cl. (yakṣayate) To worship, to honour or adore.
  • yakṣa [677] m. (-kṣaḥ) 1 A demigod, attendant especially on KUVERA, and employed in the care of his gardens and treasures. 2 A name of KUVERA. 3 The residence of INDRA. f. (-kṣī) The wife of KUVERA. E. yakṣa to worship, aff. ghañ.
