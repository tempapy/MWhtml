• rūḍhi [708] f. (-ḍhiḥ) 1 Birth, production. 2 Growing, growth. 3 Fame, notoriety. 4 Traditional or customary meaning of words, as opposed to their etymological signification; it is especially applied to words of foreign or uncertain origin, and incapable of etymological analysis. E. ruha to grow, &c., aff. ktin.
