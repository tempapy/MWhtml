• dīrghamūla mfn. (-laḥ-lā-lī-laṃ) Having a long root. m. (-laḥ) A kind of Bilva tree. n. (-laṃ) A sort of sensitive plant. f. (-lā)
    1 A creeper, (Echites frutescens:) see śyāmalatā.
    2 A plant, (Hedysarum gangeticum;) also Śālaparṇī. f. (-lī) A plant, (Hedysarum alhagi.)
    E. dīrgha long, mūla a root.
