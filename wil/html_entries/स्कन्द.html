• skanda [947] (ira) skandira r. 1st cl. (skandati) 1 To go, to move. 2 To be dry or arid. 3 To effuse or emit.
    With ava prefixed, To assail, to assault.
  • skanda [947] m. (-ndaḥ) 1 SKANDA, or KĀRTTIKEYA, the son of ŚIVA, and military deity of the Hindus. 2 A king, a prince. 3 The body. 4 The bank of a river. 5 A clever or learned man. E. skanda to go, (to fly before whom; the enemies of the gods,) and ac aff.
