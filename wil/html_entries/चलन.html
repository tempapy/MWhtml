• calana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-nī-naṃ)
    1 Who or what goes or moves.
    2 Trembling, tremulous, shaking. m. (-naḥ)
    1 A foot.
    2 A deer, an antelope. f. (-nī)
    1 A short petticoat worn by common women.
    2 The tye of an elephant. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Wandering, roaming.
    2 Trembling, shaking.
    3 Going.
    E. cala to go, affix lyuṭ or yuc.
