• śakunta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 A bird.
    2 A kind of bird, the Indian vulture.
    3 Another sort of bird, described as of aquatic habits, perhaps a kind of maritime or fishing falcon.
    4 A sort of insect.
    5 The blue jay.
    E. śaka to be able, unta Uṇādi aff.; also śakuna, &c. [824]
