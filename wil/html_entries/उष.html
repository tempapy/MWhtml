• uṣa r. 1st cl. (oṣati) To burn. (u,) uṣu (oṣati). To kill or injure: with pra prefixed, this root makes proṣati, &c.
  • uṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 Early morning, dawn, day-break.
    2 A libidinous man.
    3 Bdellium.
    4 Saline earth. f. (-ṣā)
    1 A cow.
    2 Burning, scorching.
    3 A proper name, the wife of ANIRUDDHA.
    4 Night.
    5 A pot; also ukhā. ind. The end of night or day-break.
    E. uṣa to burn, ka affix and fem. do. ṭāp; the affix of the particle is kā.
