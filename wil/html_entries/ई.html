• ī The fourth letter of the alphabet, corresponding to I long, having the sound of ee in feel, and distinguished by an accent from the former, as ī.
  • ī m. (-īḥ) A name of KANDARPA. f. (īḥ) A name of LAKṢMĪ. Ind. An interjection of.
    1 Pain.
    2 Anger.
    3 Consciousness or perception.
    4 Consideration.
    5 Compassion.
  • ī r. 2nd cl. (eti)
    1 To go.
    2 To pervade.
    3 To conceive or become pregnant.
    4 To desire.
    5 To throw or direct.
    6 To eat. This root is not generally admitted, though it occurs in composition, as with ut (udīte) To appear. r. 4th cl. (ṅa) īṅa (īyate) To go.
