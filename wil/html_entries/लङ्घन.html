• laṅghana [715] n. (-naṃ) 1 Fasting, abstinence. 2 Going, proceeding. 3 Leaping, springing, going by leaps or jumps. 4 Jumping over or beyond. 5 Exceeding, transgressing, disregarding propriety, going beyond proper bounds. 6 Storming a fort, entering by escalade, &c. 7 Capturing a fort in any way. 8 One of a horse's paces, curvetting, bounding. E. laghi to go, to fast, &c., aff. lyuṭ.
