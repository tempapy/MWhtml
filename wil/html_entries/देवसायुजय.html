• devasāyujaya [424] n. (-jyaṃ) 1 Inferior deification, the state or being of the inferior gods. 2 Absorption into or identification with a deity. E. deva a god, sa with yuja to join or be joined, affix kvip, devasayuj junction with a deity, ṣyañ added, implying the state or condition.
