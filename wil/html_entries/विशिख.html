• viśikha m. (-khaḥ)
    1 An arrow.
    2 An iron crow. f. (-khā)
    1 A spade, a hoe.
    2 A highway, a broad or carriage road.
    3 A very minute arrow, a sort of needle or spindle.
    4 A barber's wife.
    5 A sick room, or the dwelling of the sick.
    E. vi implying possessed of, &c., and śikhā a crest.
