• saumyakṛcchra n. (-cchraṃ) A religious penance, subsisting for five days, severally, on sesamum after expression, the water of boiled rice, butter-milk mixed with water, water, and parched grain, and fasting on the sixth day.
    E. saumya relating to the moon, and kṛcchra penance.
