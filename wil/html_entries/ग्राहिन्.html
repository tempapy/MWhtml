• grāhin [306] mfn. (-hī-hiṇī-hi) 1 Taking, seizing, accepting, who or what takes. 2 Astringent, binding, constipating. m. (-hī) 1 A sedative, a narcotic. 2 The elephant or wood apple: see kapittha. f. (-ṇī) A small kind of Javāsa, perhaps a species of Hedysarum. E. graha to take, ṇini aff.
