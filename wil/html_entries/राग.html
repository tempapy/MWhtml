• rāga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 Colour, hue, tint, dye.
    2 Affection, prepossession, love, desire.
    3 Mental affection in general, as sorrow, joy, &c.
    4 Envy, impatience of another's success.
    5 A mode of music; of which six are enumerated, viz. Bhairava, Mālava, Sāraṅga, Hindola, Vasanta, Dīpaka, and Megha; they are personified in poetry and mythology.
    6 A king, a sovereign.
    7 The Raja Guṇa, the property or quality of passion.
    8 Greediness, cupidity.
    9 Colouring, painting, dying. f. (-gā) A sort of grain, commonly Raggy, much cultivated in the south of India, (Eleusine corocana, or Cynosurus corocanus.)
    E. rañja to colour, &c., aff. ghañ.
