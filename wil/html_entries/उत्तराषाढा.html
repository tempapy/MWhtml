• uttarāṣāḍhā [142] f. (-ḍhā) The twenty-first of the lunar mansions, figured by an elephant's tooth or a bed, and containing two stars, one of which is β in Sagittarius. E. uttara subsequent, and āṣāḍhā the constellation; to distinguish it from asterism twentieth: see pūrvvāṣāḍhā and āṣāḍhā.
