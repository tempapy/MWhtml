• kukkuṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 A cock.
    2 A wild cock.
    3 The offspring of a Śūdra or man of the fourth caste, by a Caṇḍāla woman.
    4 A whisp of lighted straw, a firebrand.
    5 A spark of fire. f. (-ṭiḥ or -ṭī) Hypocrisy, interested observance of religious duties. f. (-ṭī)
    1 A hen.
    2 A small house lizard.
    3 The silk cotton tree, (Bombax heptaphyllum.)
    E. kuk taking, from kuka with kvip affix, and kuṭa to cut or scratch, with ka affix; scratching up the earth, &c. or ku bad, kuṭa to cut, &c. and ka inserted. [224]
