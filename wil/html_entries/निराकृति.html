• nirākṛti mfn. (-tiḥ-tiḥ-ti)
    1 A person who has not duly gone through a course of study, especially applied however to the religious student, who has not duly read the Vedas.
    2 One who neglects the five great religious obligations.
    3 Shapeless, formless, viewless. m. (-tiḥ)
    1 Rejection, contradiction, opposition, disallowance.
    2 Obstacle, impediment.
    E. nirā implying privation or obstruction, kṛti making, causing; or nir privative, and ākṛti form.
