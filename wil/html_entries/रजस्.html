• rajas [693] n. (-jaḥ) 1 The second condition of humanity, the quality or property of passion; that which produces sensual desire, worldly coveting, pride, and falsehood, and is the cause of pain. 2 Dust. 3 The dust or pollen of a flower. 4 The menstrual evacuation. E. rañja to colour, to be attached to, Uṇādi aff. asun.
