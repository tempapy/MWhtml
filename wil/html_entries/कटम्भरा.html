• kaṭambharā f. (-rā)
    1 A plant, (Poederia fetida.)
    2 A medicinal plant, kaṭukīḥ see the preceding.
    3 A sort of cucumber, (Cucumis Madraspatanus.)
    4 A female elephant.
    5 Red arsenic.
    6 A plant, (Aletris hyacinthoides:) see murvvā.
    7 Hog-weed.
    E. as before, bhṛ to nourish, &c. [181]
