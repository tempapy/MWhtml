• tārkṣya m. (-rkṣyaḥ)
    1 A horse.
    2 A name of GARUḌA, the bird and vehicle of VIṢṆU.
    3 A name of ARUṆA, or the dawn personified, considered as the elder brother of GARUḌA.
    4 A car, a chariot.
    5 A snake.
    6 Gold.
    7 A tree, the Sal, (Shorea robusta.) n. (-rkṣyaṃ) A sort of collyrium. f. (-rkṣyī) A wild creeper.
    E. tṛkṣa to go, ghañ affix tārkṣa and yat added; again, tṛkṣa or tārkṣaṃ considered as a name of KAŚYAPA the father of ARUṆA, &c. ṣyañ or aṇ aff.
