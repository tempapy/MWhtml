• taṭastha mfn. (-sthaḥ-sthā-sthaṃ)
    1 Indifferent, alien, neuter.
    2 Situated on the bank or shore. m. (-sthaḥ) An indifferent person, one neither a friend nor a foe. n. (-sthaṃ) That property which is distinct from the nature of any thing, yet is the faculty by which it is known: in other words, spiritual essence unconnected with bodily wants or passions.
    E. taṭa a bank, a height, and stha who stands. [362]
