• sāmānya mfn. (-nyaḥ-nyā-nyaṃ) Common, general, generic, universal. n. (-nyaṃ)
    1 Kind, sort, specific or generic property or character.
    2 Common property, generality, community.
    3 Public affairs or business.
    4 Totality, entireness.
    5 A figure of rhetoric, the connexion of different objects by common properties. f. (-nyā) A female who is common to all men, a harlot.
    E. samāna common, (to many individuals,) yañ aff.
