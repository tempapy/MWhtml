• kuñcikā f. (-kā)
    1 A plant bearing a red and black seed, used as a weight, (Abrus precatorius:) see guñjā.
    2 The branch or shoot of a bamboo.
    3 A plant bearing a pungent seed, (Nigella Indica:) see kṛṣṇajīraka.
    4 A fish, commonly Cunchiya, in shape like an eel and eaten as one, (Unibranchapertura cunchiya.)
    5 A key.
    E. kuñca to go crookedly, &c. kvun affix, with the fem. termination.
