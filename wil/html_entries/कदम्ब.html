• kadamba m. (-mbaḥ)
    1 A plant, commonly Kadamba (Nauclea kadamba.)
    2 The mustard seed plant, (Sinapis dichotoma.)
    3 A kind of grass, (Andropogon serratum:) see devatāḍaka.
    4 Turmeric. n. (-mbaṃ) A multitude, an assemblage or collection.
    E. kada to confound, &c. ambac Uṇādi affix; also with kan added kadambaka. [185]
