• sūnṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 True.
    2 Fortunate, auspicious.
    3 Agreeable, pleasant, (as speech.)
    4 Dear to, beloved by. n. (-taṃ) Agreeable discourse or speech, but also true.
    E. su well, nṛta to dance, (or excel,) ghañ aff., and the vowel of the prefix made long. [941]
