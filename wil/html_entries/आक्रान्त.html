• ākrānta mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntā-ntaṃ)
    1 Surpassed, surmounted.
    2 Overcome, overrun.
    3 Pained, distressed.
    4 Accompanied, attended.
    5 Overspread.
    6 Obtained, possessed.
    7 Agitated or overcome by, as a feeling.
    E. ākrama to mount, surpass, &c. and part. affix kta.
