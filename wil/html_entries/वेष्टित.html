• veṣṭita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Surrounded, encompassed, enclosed.
    2 Stopped, secured from access.
    3 Bound, round, enveloped, wrapped up. n. (-taṃ)
    1 Encompassing, encircling.
    2 One of the gestures or attitudes of dancing, particular motion of the fingers or a crossing of the feet.
    E. veṣṭa surrounding, itac aff. or veṣṭa the verb, and kta aff.
