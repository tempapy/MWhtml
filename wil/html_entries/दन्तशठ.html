• dantaśaṭha [399] m. (-ṭhaḥ) 1 Elephant or wood-apple: see kapittha. 2 Common lime, (Citrus acida.) 2 A plant bearing an acid fruit, (Averhoa carambola.) 4 An orange. 5 Sourness, acidity. f. (-ṭhā) Woodsorrel, (Oxalis monadelpha.) E. danta a tooth, and śaṭha noxious, bad.
