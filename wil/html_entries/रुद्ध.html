• ruddha mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ)
    1 Surrounded, begirt, as with a fence or river, &c.
    2 Secured, protected from access.
    3 Obstructed, stopped, shut up.
    4 Opposed, stopped, impeded.
    5 Besieged, invested, blockaded. f. (-ddhā) A siege.
    E. rudha to close, &c., aff. kta.
