• janana [339] n. (-naṃ) 1 Birth, production. 2 Family, race, lineage. f. (-nī) 1 A mother. 2 Tenderness, compassion. 3 A plant, commonly Chakawat; also janani and janī. 4 A bat. 5 Arabian jasmin. 6 Valerian. mf. (-naḥ-nī) A parent, a progenitor. E. jana to bear or be born, affix lyuṭ, and ṅīṣ.
