• saṃvadana [874] n. (-naṃ) 1 Communication of tidings or intelligence. 2 Sight, seeing. nf. (-naṃ-nā) 1 Subduing by charms, overpowering any thing or person by magical compounds, or drugs, or mystical gems, &c. 2 A charm, an amulet, the article by which the above is effected. E. sam with vada to speak, yuc aff.
