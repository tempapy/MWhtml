• dikpati m. (-tiḥ) A regent of a quarter of the universe, as the Sun of the east; Saturn of the west; Mars of the south; Mercury of the north; Venus of the south-east; Rāhu of the south-west; Candra of the north-west; and Jupiter of the north-east.
