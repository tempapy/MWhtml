• antara [037] (noun and adverb) n. (-raṃ) 1 Interval, intermediate space. 2 Period, term. 3 Clothing. 4 Covering. 5 Difference. 6 Sake, behalf. 7 A hole or rent. 8 Own, the self. 9 Without, except. 10 Without, outside. 11 Opportune time. 12 Midst, the midst. 13 The supreme soul. 14 Like, similar. 15 Other. 16 (In arithmetic) Remainder, difference. E. anta end, and ra from rā to obtain, and ka aff. In consulting the meanings of this word, it is to be recollected, that vocables which are adverbs in English, are often nouns of the neuter gender in Sanscrit; this word is also a pronoun, in the 3d and 10th acceptations. when the latter means, without, or out of, any thing but a house or town: the whole of the interpretations may be referred to the ideas of, within and without.
