• vaidala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Wicker, of cane, wicker or basket work. n. (-laṃ)
    1 A shallow cup or saucer made of clay usually, though sometimes of metal, and sometimes of wood or wicker.
    2 Any seat or vessel of basket or wicker work. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A flat unleavened cake, made of flower, &c. with cumin or coriander seeds, and fried in Ghee.
    2 Any leguminous vegetable or grain, as peas, beans, &c.
    E. vi before dala to divide, aff. ac, and aṇ added.
