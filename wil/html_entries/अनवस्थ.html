• anavastha mfn. (-sthaḥ-sthā-sthaṃ) Unstable, unsteady.
    2 Incontinent. f. (sthā)
    1 Instability, absence of fixed state or condition. Uncertainty, doubt.
    3 Incontinence, dissoluteness.
    4 (In logic,) the abstract nature of a thing, independent of its existence.
    E. am neg. avasthā condition. [028]
