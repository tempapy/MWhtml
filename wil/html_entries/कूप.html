• kūpa [239] r. 10th cl. (kūpayati) 1 To be weak. 2 To weaken: see kupa.
  • kūpa [239] m. (-paḥ) 1 A well. 2 A hole, a hollow. 3 A mast. 4 A tree or rock in the midst of a river. f. (-pī) 1 A small well. 2 The navel. 3 A flask, a bottle. E. ku to sound, (frogs croaking in a well, &c.) and pa Uṇādi affix: the vowel is made long; also kūpaka.
