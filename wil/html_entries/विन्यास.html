• vinyāsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 Assemblage, collecting, collecting or depositing any thing.
    2 Site, place, receptacle, that in or on which any thing is placed or deposited.
    3 Orderly arrangement or disposition.
    4 Entrusting, depositing.
    5 A deposit.
    E. vi, and ni prefixed to asa to take, to throw or place, &c., aff. ghañ.
