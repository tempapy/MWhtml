• kalka mfn. (-lkaḥ-lkā-lkaṃ) Sinful, wicked. mn. (-lkaḥ-lkaṃ)
    1 Sediment, the deposit of oil, ghee, &c.
    2 Dirt, filth.
    3 Ordure, faeces.
    4 Sin.
    5 Hypocrisy.
    6 Pride.
    7 The beleric myrobalan.
    8 The wax of the ear.
    9 Levigated powder.
    E. kala to count, &c. kaḥ Uṇādi aff. [202]
