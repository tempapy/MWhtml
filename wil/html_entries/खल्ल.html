• khalla m. (-llaḥ)
    1 A kind of cloth or clothes, leather garments.
    2 A canal, a cut, a creek, a trench, a deep hole.
    3 The Cātaka, a kind of cuckoo.
    4 Leather.
    5 A leather water bag.
    6 A mill, a stone or vessel for grinding drugs, &c. f. (-llī) Shooting pain in the extremities.
    E. khala collecting, &c. and la what gets; also khallaka m. (-kaḥ)
