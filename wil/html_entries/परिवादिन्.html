• parivādin mfn. (-dī-dinī-di)
    1 Who or what reproaches, accuses, &c.
    2 Censured, abused.
    3 Calling out, crying, screaming. m. (-dī) An accuser, a reprover; (in Law,) a plaintiff. f. (-nī) A lute or a Vīṇā, with seven strings.
    E. parivāda as above, ini aff. or pari before vada to speak, dhiṇun aff. [512]
