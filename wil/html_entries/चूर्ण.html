• cūrṇa [330] r. 1st cl. (cūrṇayati) 1 To contract, to shrink, to close as an eye, to wink. 2 To throw, to send or direct. 3 To grind or pound.
  • cūrṇa [330] mn. (-rṇaḥ-rṇaṃ) 1 Powder, any pulverulent or minute division of substance. 2 Chalk, lime. 3 Aromatic powder, pounded Sandal, &c. 4 Pounded camphor. f. (-rṇī) 1 A Cowri, the shell used as a coin. 2 Selection of an unanswerable argument. 3 A river in Bengal. 4 The red powder scattered at the Holi festival. 5 Dust: see cūrṇi. E. cūrṇa to pound, &c. affix ghañ.
