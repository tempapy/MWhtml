• vaideha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A trader by caste, or profession.
    2 An attendant on the women's apartments: see the next. f. (-hī)
    1 SĪTĀ, the daughter of JANAKA.
    2 Long-pepper.
    3 The wife of a trader or woman of the Vaideha caste.
    4 A sort of pigment, commonly Rocanā.
    E. videha the country, aṇ aff. of origin or production, &c.: see the next. [810]
