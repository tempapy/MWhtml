• vāsanta mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntī-ntaṃ)
    1 Placed, situated.
    2 Vernal, relating to the season of spring, suitable to it, produced in it, &c.
    3 Young, in the spring of life.
    4 Diligent in the performance of religious ceremonies. m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 A camel.
    2 The Indian cuckoo.
    3 Any young animal, or a young elephant.
    4 A catamite.
    5 The southern wind, zephyr.
    6 A sort of kidney-bean, (Phaseolus mungo.)
    7 The black kind of Moong or bean.
    8 A plant, (Vangueria spinosa.)
    9 The Koïl or Indian cuckoo. f. (-ntī)
    1 A large creeper, (Gærtnera racemosa.)
    2 A species of jasmine, (Jasminum auriculatum.)
    3 Long pepper.
    4 Trumpet flower.
    5 A festival in honour of KĀMADEVA, held in the month Chaitra.
    6 A species of the Śarkarī metre.
    E. vasa to dwell, aff. jhac, and the vowel made long; or vasanta spring, and aṇ aff.
