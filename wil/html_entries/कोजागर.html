• kojāgara [250] m. (-raḥ) The day of full-moon in the month Aśvin, (September-October.) E. kaḥ who, jāgara wakeful; who is awake, the call of LAKṢMĪ, who, descending on this night, promised wealth to all that were awake: hence the night is spent in festivity, and games of chance in honour of the goddess.
