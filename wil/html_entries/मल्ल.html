• malla r. 1st cl. (mallate) To hold, to have. [646]
  • malla mfn. (-llaḥ-llā-llaṃ)
    1 Strong, stout, athletic, robust.
    2 Best, excellent. m. (-llaḥ)
    1 A wrestler, a boxer, by birth; the offspring of an outcaste Kṣatriya by a Kṣatriya female.
    2 A cup or vessel.
    3 The cheek and temples, the hemicranium,
    4 A kind of fish.
    5 The residue of an oblation. f. (-llā)
    1 A woman.
    2 Arabian jasmine.
    3 Ornamenting the person with coloured unguents.
    E. malla to hold, and aff. ac. [646]
