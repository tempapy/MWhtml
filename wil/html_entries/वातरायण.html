• vātarāyaṇa [749] m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 An arrow. 2 An arrow's flight, the discharge of an arrow, a bow shot. 3 A saw. 4 The peak of a mountain. 5 A mad man, one wild or intoxicated, either literally or figuratively. 6 An idler, one who lives to no purpose. 7 The Saral, a sort of pine tree. E. vātara, and ayana going.
