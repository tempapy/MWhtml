• paddhati [500] f. (-tiḥ-tī) 1 A road. 2 A line, a row or range. 3 A ritual, a manual, a work upon any act or ceremony, detailing the mode of its performance, and collecting the texts connected with it. 4 A family name, a surname or title. E. pada a foot, hana to strike, aff. ktin.
