• vivakṣita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Wished, desired.
    2 Purposed, intended.
    3 Literal, not figurative, (a word or phrase.)
    4 Intended to be spoken or said. n. (-taṃ) An object desired. f. (-tā) Purpose, wish, what one wishes to declare or speak.
    E. vaca to speak, desiderative form, kta aff.
