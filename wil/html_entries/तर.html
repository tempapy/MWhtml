• tara [368] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ) Who or what passes over or beyond. m. (-raḥ) 1 Passing over, crossing. 2 A float, a raft. 3 A road. 4 A tree. f. (rī) 1 A boat. 2 A club. 3 A clothes basket, a Pitāra. 4 Smoke. 5 A small wooden baling vessel. 6 The end of a garment. E. tṝ to cross, affix ap, fem. affix ṅīṣ; also with in affix tari, q. v.
