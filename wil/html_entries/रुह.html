• ruha(ñi au) ñiauruha r. 1st cl. (rohati)
    1 To grow from seed, to grow as a tree, &c.
    2 To be produced or become manifest.
    3 To be born.
    With adhi prefixed, To go up or over, to ascend.
    With adhi and āṅ, To ascend.
    With ava, To descend.
    With āṅ.
    1 To mount, to ride on.
    2 To rise, to ascend.
    With pra, To grow, to shoot forth as a branch or shoot taking fresh root. [708]
  • ruha mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ)
    1 Growing, springing up.
    2 Mounted, ascended.
    E. ruha to grow, aff. ka. [708]
