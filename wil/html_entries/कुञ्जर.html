• kuñjara m. (-raḥ)
    1 An elephant.
    2 (In composition,) Preeminent, as puruṣakuñjara an excellent man.
    3 Hair.
    4 A country. f. (-rā or -rī)
    1 A female elephant. (-rā)
    1 A plant, (Bignonia suave-olens.)
    2 A shrub, (Grislea tomentosa.)
    E. kuñja a tusk, an arbour, &c. and ra what forms or possesses. [225]
