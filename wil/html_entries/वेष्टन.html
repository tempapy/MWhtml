• veṣṭana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Surrounding, encompassing.
    2 A wall, an enclosure, a fence.
    3 A turban.
    4 A diadem, a tiara.
    5 The outer ear, or the meatus auditorius and the concha.
    9 An attitude in dancing, a particular disposition of the hands; also a crossing of the feet.
    7 Bdellium.
    E. veṣṭa to surround aff. lyuṭ.
