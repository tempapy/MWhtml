• vidāraka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A divider, a render or tearer.
    2 A tree or rock in the middle of a stream, dividing its course.
    3 A hole or pit for water sunk in the bed of a dry river, &c. f. (-rikā)
    1 A plant, (Hedysarum alhagi.)
    2 A bubo.
    E. vi before dṛ to divide, aff. ṇvul.
