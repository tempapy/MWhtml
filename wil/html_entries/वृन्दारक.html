• vṛndāraka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Handsome, agreeable, pleasing.
    2 Best, excellent, chief.
    3 Much, great.
    4 Reputable, respectable, eminent. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A deity, an immortal.
    2 A chief, the head or leader of a crowd or herd, &c.
    E. vṛnda a heap, and ārakan aff., implying pre-eminence. [802]
