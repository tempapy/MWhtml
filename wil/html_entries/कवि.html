• kavi mfn. (-viḥ-viḥ or -vī-vi) Learned, wise. m. (-viḥ)
    1 A poet.
    2 The sun.
    3 ŚUKRA, the regent of the planet Venus, and preceptor of the demons.
    4 A name of VĀLMĪKI, the author of the Rāmāyaṇa, the inventor of poesy.
    5 A name of BRAHMĀ. f. (-viḥ-vī) The bit of a bridle, or the reins altogether; also kavikā.
    E. ku to sound, to celebrate, and in Uṇādi aff.
