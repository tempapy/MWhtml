• pāda m. (-daḥ)
    1 A foot.
    2 A quarter.
    3 A hill at the foot of a mountain.
    4 A ray of light.
    5 The root of a tree.
    6 The foot or line of a stanza.
    7 The line of a hymn or stanza of the Ṛg Veda.
    8 The quadrant of a circle.
    E. pada to go, aff. ghañ. [525]
