• kṣara r. 1st cl. (kṣarati)
    1 To drop or let fall.
    2 To distil, to ooze, to trickle.
    With saṃ, To flow.
    With ā, r. of the 10th cl. (ākṣārayati) To accuse, to abuse.
  • kṣara m. (-raḥ)
    1 A cloud.
    2 Trickling, oozing. n. (-raṃ) Water.
    E. kṣara to drop, and ac aff.
