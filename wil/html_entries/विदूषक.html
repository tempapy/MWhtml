• vidūṣaka [768] mfn. (-kaḥ-ṣikā-kaṃ) 1 Facetious, witty, a wag, a jester. 2 Censorious, detracting, a detractor or abuser of other people. m. (-kaḥ) 1 An actor, especially an interlocutor with the audience between the acts or scenes, a jester, a buffoon. 2 (In dramatic language,) The humble and jocose companion of the principal character. 3 A catamite. E. vi before dūṣa to be or make bad, aff. kkun.
