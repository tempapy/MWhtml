• sahasrākṣa mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣī-kṣaṃ) Thousand-eyed; used figuratively, vigilant, all-perceiving, all-inspecting, all-powerful. m. (-kṣaḥ) INDRA.
    E. sahasra a thousand, and akṣa the eye: INDRA having been covered over with marks resembling the female organ, in consequence of a curse of GAUTAMA, those marks were changed subsequently to eyes by the relenting saint. [914]
