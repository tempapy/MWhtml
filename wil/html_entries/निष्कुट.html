• niṣkuṭa [481] m. (-ṭaḥ) 1 A grove near a house. 2 A field. 3 A door. 4 The private apartments, the Zenana. (E. nir without, kuṭa the house.) f. (-ṭā, also ṭiḥ or ṭī) Large cardamoms. E. nira out, kuṭa the shell or husk, aff. ka, with ṭāp fem. aff. or ki with ṅīṣ optionally added.
