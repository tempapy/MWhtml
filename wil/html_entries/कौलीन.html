• kaulīna n. (-naṃ)
    1 High birth, family descent.
    2 Rumor, report.
    3 Evil report, detraction.
    4 Combat of animals, of birds, snakes, &c.
    5 Cock-fighting, &c. contention of animals as a species of gambling.
    6 A privity, a privy part.
    7 An improper act. m. (-naḥ) The son of a female beggar.
    2 A follower of the left hand Śākta ritual.
    E. kulīna of a good family, affix śvaṇ. [254]
