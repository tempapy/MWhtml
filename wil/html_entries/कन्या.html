• kanyā [187] f. (-mpā) 1 A girl nine years old, a virgin. 2 A name of DURGĀ. 3 The sign of the zodiac, Virgo. 4 The socotrine aloe (Aloe perfoliata.) 6 Large cardamoms. 6 A species of metre of four lines, with four syllables in each. 7 A parasite plant. E. kana to shine, Uṇādi affix yak, fem. ṭāp.
