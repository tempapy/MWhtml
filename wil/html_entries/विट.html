• viṭa r. 1st cl. (veṭati)
    1 To sound.
    2 To curse. (i) viṭi r. 10th cl. (viṇṭayati) To decay.
  • viṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 A catamite, a pathic.
    2 A rogue, a cheat.
    3 An attendant on a dissolute character or courtezan.
    4 A paramour, a gallant.
    5 A mountain.
    6 The Khayar tree, (Mimosa catechu.)
    7 A medicinal salt: see viḍalvaṇa.
    8 A rat.
    9 The orange tree.
    10 A branch and its shoot.
    E. viṭa to sound, aff. ka.
