• sthāna [952] n. (-naṃ) 1 Place, spot, site, situation. 2 Stay, staying, continuance, being fixed or stationary and exempt from increase or diminution. 3 Likeness, resemblance. 4 Leisure, interval. 5 An open place in a town, a green, a plain, a square. 6 A house, a dwelling. 7 A section, a chapter, a book. 8 One of the three objects of government, the middle state, as neither loss nor gain, nor discomfiture nor success, nor expenditure nor accumulation, &c. 9 A town, a city. 10 Office, appointment. 11 Degree, station. 12 Halt. 13 Firmness of troops, keeping in array. E. ṣṭhā to stay, to be fixed, aff. lyuṭ.
