• prabhu mfn. (-bhu-bhvī-bhu)
    1 Strong, able.
    2 Always, eternal.
    3 A superior, an owner, a proprietor, a master or mistress, &c. m. (-bhuḥ)
    1 A master, a lord.
    2 A name of VIṢṆU.
    3 Quicksilver.
    4 Sound.
    E. pra pre-eminent, bhū to be, aff. kvip, and the final vowel made short. [574]
