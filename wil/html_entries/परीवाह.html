• parīvāha [515] m. (-haḥ) 1 A drain or channel made to carry off water from a pond that overflows. 2 A natural inundation or overflow. 3 Royal insignia. 4 A sword sheath, a scabbard. E. pari before vaha to bear, aff. ghañ, and the i of pari is in consequence optionally long; also parivāha.
