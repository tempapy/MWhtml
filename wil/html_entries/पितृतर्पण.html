• pitṛtarpaṇa [535] n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 The part of the hand between the middle finger and thumb, sacred to the manes. 2 Gifts in honour of deceased relations, distributed at the Śrāddhas or funeral ceremonies. 3 The act of throwing water out of the right hand at seasons of ablution, by way of offering to the manes, or deceased ancestors in general. E. pitṛ a father, a progenitor, and tarpaṇa satisfying.
