• kambu mfn. (-mbuḥ-mbū-mbu) Speckled, variegated. mn. (-mbuḥ-mbu)
    1 A conch, a shell. m. (-mbuḥ)
    1 A bracelet, a ring.
    2 A bivalve shell.
    3 An elephant.
    4 The neck.
    5 A vein or tubular vessel of the body, &c.
    E. kamba to go, u affix, or kama to desire; the form is irr.
