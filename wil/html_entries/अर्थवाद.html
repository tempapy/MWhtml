• arthavāda m. (-daḥ)
    1 Praise, eulogium.
    2 Declaration of purpose or object, speech or expression having a certain object, as stutyarthavādaḥ speech implying praise, nindārthavādaḥ speech implying censure.
    3 Amplification.
    E. artha substance, thing, and vāda speaking. [070]
