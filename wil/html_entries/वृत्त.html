• vṛtta [800] mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ) 1 Chosen, selected, appointed. 2 Done, performed, engaged in or undertaken. 3 Past, gone, been. 4 Over, finished. 5 Dead, deceased. 6 Read, studied, read through. 7 Covered. 8 Round. 9 Firm, hard. 10 Unobstructed, unimpaired. 11 Famous, celebrated. n. (-ttaṃ) 1 Verse, metre. 2 Conduct, observance of enjoined practice in private or social life. 3 Practice, profession, means of gaining a subsistence. 4 Procedure, event, occurrence. 5 A circle. m. (-ttaḥ) A tortoise. E. vṛta to be, &c., aff. kta.
