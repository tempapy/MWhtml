• nirṛti mfn. (-tiḥ-tiḥ-ti) Free from violence, tyranny, &c. m. (-tiḥ) A demigod and ruler of the south-west quarter: see nairṛti. f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Misfortune, calamity.
    2 Security, prosperity, immunity from tyranny or violence.
    E. nir implying reverse, ṛti good fortune, &c. [473]
