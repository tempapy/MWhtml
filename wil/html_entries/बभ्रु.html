• babhru [599] mfn. (-bhruḥ-bhruḥ or bhrūḥ-bhru) 1 Large, great. 2 Tawny, &c. 3 Baldheaded through disease. m. (-bhruḥ) 1 A name of VIṢṆU. 2 ŚIVA. 3 AGNI or fire. 4 A large ichneumon. 5 The name of a Muni or holy sage. 6 A rat. n. (-bhru) 1 A tawny or brown colour. 2 Any object of that colour. E. bhṛ to fill, aff. ku, and the initial repeated.
