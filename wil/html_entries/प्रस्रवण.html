• prasravaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 A pool of water formed by dripping of springs in the mountains.
    2 Dripping or fall of water, cascade, cataract.
    3 Washing away of rocks, &c. by the dripping of water.
    4 Oozing, leaking, dripping. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 Sweat, perspiration.
    2 Pissing, urining.
    3 A range of mountains in the peninsula; also Malayavān.
    E. pra before sru to drop, aff. lyuṭ; also praśrabaṇa.
