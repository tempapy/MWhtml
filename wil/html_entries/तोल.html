• tola mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) A Tola, a weight of gold or silver: it is stated in books at 16 Māṣas of 5 Rattis or 6½ grains each, and weighs therefore 105 grains troy: in practice it is calculated at 12 Māṣas jeweller's weight, and weighs nearly double, or 210 grains; but actually it is of the same weight as the Sicca or grs. 179(2/3).
    E. tula to weigh, affix ghañ; also with ṇvul affix tolaka, mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ.)
