• lomaśa [725] mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ) 1 Hairy, covered with or made of hair. 2 Woollen. m. (-śaḥ) 1 A ram. 2 The name of a Ṛṣi celebrated in the Mahābhārata. f. (-śā) 1 A fox. 2 A sort of Bonduc. 3 A plant, (Leea hirta.) 4 Indian spikenard, (Valeriana jatamansi.) 5 Cowach, (Carpopogon pruriens) 6 A medicinal plant, commonly Mahāmeda. 7 Sida cordifolia. 8 A female demi-divine being, an attendant on DURGĀ, one of the Śākinīs. 9 Green vitriol. 10 Orris root. E. loma hair or down, śa aff.
