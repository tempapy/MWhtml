• sauvīra n. (-raṃ)
    1 The fruit of the jujube.
    2 Antimony.
    3 Sour gruel. m. (-raḥ) A district, in the west of India, connected with the country along the Indus. m. pl. (-rāḥ) The people of Suvīra.
    E. suvīra the country so called, and aṇ pleonasm or aff. of derivation; also with yañ, aff. sauvīryya.
