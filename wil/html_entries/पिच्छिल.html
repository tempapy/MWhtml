• picchila mfn. subst. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Sauce mixed with rice gruel.
    2 Sauce, gravy, or condiments with water or Ghee.
    3 Broth, soup.
    4 Moist, and split pulse. f. (-lā)
    1 The silk cotton tree, (Bombax heptaphyllum.)
    2 A potherb, (Basella rubra and lucida.)
    3 A timber tree, (Dalbergia Sisu.)
    4 The name of a river.
    5 An esculent root, (Arum Indicum.) m. (-laḥ) The tamarisk, (Tamarix Indica). mfn. adj. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Having a tail.
    E. picchā rice water, scum, &c. and ilac aff. [533]
