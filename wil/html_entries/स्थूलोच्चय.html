• sthūloccaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 Incompleteness, deficiency, defect.
    2 The middle pace of an elephant, neither quick nor slow.
    3 Pimples on the face.
    4 A hill at the foot of a mountain, or a rocky fragment detached from its primitive site and forming a separate projection or promontory.
    5 A hollow at the root of the elephant's tusks.
    E. sthūla large, and uccaya lofty.
