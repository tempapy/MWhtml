• śauṭīra mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Proud, haughty. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A hero.
    2 An ascetic, one who abandons the world.
    3 A proud or haughty man, but of low tribe or occupation, an upstart.
    E. śauṭa to be proud, and īran Uṇādi aff.; also as derived from śauḍa the same, śauḍīra; also the short vowel being substituted for the diphthong, śuṭīra. [859]
