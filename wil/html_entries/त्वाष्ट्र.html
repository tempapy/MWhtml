• tvāṣṭra m. (-ṣṭraḥ) The name of a demon, also called VṚTRĀSURA. nf. (-ṣṭraṃ-ṣṭrī) One of the lunar mansions, the fourteenth or Citra. f. (-ṣṭrī)
    1 A wife of SŪRYA, or the sun; also called SAṂJÑĀ, the daughter of VIŚVAKARMA.
    2 A small car.
    E. tvaṣṭṛ the sun, or a carpenter, aṇ affix of relation or descent, and ṅīp fem. aff. [394]
