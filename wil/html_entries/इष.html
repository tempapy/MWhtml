• iṣa r. 4th cl. (iṣyati) To go. r. 6th cl. (icchati)
    1 To wish, to desire.
    2 To accept, to receive.
    With abhi, to desire:
    with prati, to engage, to promise. r. 9th cl. (iṣṇāti) To repeat an act, to do any thing again and again.
    With anu prefixed, to search after, to investigate or enquire.
  • iṣa m. (-ṣaḥ) The month Āśvina, (September-October.)
    E. iṣa to go, ka affix; also īṣa.
