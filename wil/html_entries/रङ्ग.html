• raṅga m. (-ṅgaḥ)
    1 Paint, colour, tint, dye.
    2 Dancing, acting, singing, &c.
    3 A field of battle.
    4 The place where acting, &c. is exhibited, a stage.
    5 A place answering to an amphitheatre. n. (-ṅgaṃ) Tin.
    E. ragi to go, aff. ac, or rañja to colour, aff. ghañ, the final changed and the nasal rejected.
