• daiva mfn. (-vaḥ-vī-vaṃ) Of or relating to divinity or a deity, divine, celestial, &c. mn. (-vaḥ-vaṃ) Destiny, fate, fortune. n. (-vaṃ)
    1 The part of the hand sacred to the gods; the tips of the fingers, (some exclude the thumbs.)
    2 One of the forms of marriage; the gift of a daughter at a sacrifice to the officiating priest. f. (-vī) A division of medicine, the medical use of charms, prayers, &c.
    E. deva a deity, and añ affix of reference or relation.
