• kalāvat m. (-vān) A name of Candra or the moon. f. (-tī)
    1 A celestial lute or Vīṇā.
    2 The mother of RĀDHĀ.
    3 An Apsaras.
    4 A mystical ceremony, initiation of the Tāntrika student, in which the goddess DURGĀ is supposed to be transferred from the water jar to the body of the novice.
    E. kalā a digit, or kala a sweet tone, and matup aff.
