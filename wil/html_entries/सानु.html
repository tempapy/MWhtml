• sānu mn. (-nuḥ-nu)
    1 Table land, level ground on the top or edge of a mountain.
    2 A wood.
    3 A road.
    4 A gale of wind.
    5 Point, end, top.
    6 A sage, a learned man or Paṇḍita.
    7 A shoot, a sprout.
    8 The sun.
    E. ṣaṇa to give (pleasure,) Uṇādi aff. ñuṇ. [918]
