• varyya [739] mfn. (-ryyaḥ-ryyā-ryyaṃ) 1 Chief, principal. 2 To be applied to for aid or a boon, &c. 3 To be chosen or selected. 4 To be yielded to or indulged. m. (-ryyaḥ) KĀMADEVA. f. (-ryyā) A girl choosing her own husband. E. vara to ask as a boon, or vṛ to choose, yat aff., form irr.
