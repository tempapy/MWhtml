• bhitti f. (-ttiḥ)
    1 A wall of earth or masonry.
    2 A thing broken or divided.
    3 A place, a part.
    4 Opportunity, occasion.
    5 An asylum.
    6 Breaking, tearing, dividing.
    7 A flaw, a defect.
    8 A rent, a fissure.
    9 A fragment, a bit, a broken piece or part.
    E. bhida to divide, to break, to tear, &c. aff. ktin. [620]
