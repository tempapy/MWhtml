• mūṣika mf. (-kaḥ-kā) A rat, a mouse. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A tree, commonly Siris, (Mimosa sirisha.)
    2 A thief, a plunderer.
    3 A country, the part of the Malabar coast between Quilon and Cape Comorin. f. (-kā)
    1 A plant, (Salvinia cucullata.)
    2 A crucible.
    E. mūṣa to steal, Uṇādi aff. kinan, and the vowel made long; also mūṣikā, and mūṣā, mūṣī.
