• cyuti f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Dropping, dripping, oozing.
    2 Falling, falling from.
    3 Deviating from.
    4 The vulva.
    5 The anus.
    E. cyu to fall, &c. affix ktin; also with ṅīṣ, cyutī; again with the radical vowel protracted cyūti &c. and without the semivowel cyuti, &c.
