• īkṣa r. 1st cl. (īkṣate) To see. It most usually occurs compounded with one or other of the prepositions, as
    with apa, to expect;
    with abhi, to gaze at;
    with ava, to inspect;
    with ut or upa, to expect or await;
    with upa, to abandon;
    with nir, to see;
    with pari, to try, to test, to assay;
    with pra, to see, to resemble, or look like;
    with vi, to inspect;
    with prati,
    1 to look for, to expect,
    2 to venerate;
    with sama, to compare, to contrast, to select;
    with prati and ut, to look up at. [134]
