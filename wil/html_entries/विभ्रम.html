• vibhrama [778] m. (-maḥ) 1 One of the classes of feminine actions proceeding from the passion of love, flurry, confusion. 2 Error, mistake, blunder. 3 Hurry, flurry. 4 Doubt, apprehension. 5 Beauty. 6 Whirling, going round. f. (-mā) Old age. E. vi before bhrama to err, &c., aff. ghañ.
