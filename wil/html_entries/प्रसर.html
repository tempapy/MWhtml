• prasara [581] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Who or what proceeds, projects, &c. m. (-raḥ) 1 Spreading, extending. 2 Space, room. 3 Occasion, opportunity. 4 Affectionate solicitation. 5 Speed, velocity. 6 Battle, war. 7 Multitude, assemblage. 8 An iron arrow. 9 A projection, a process. E. pra before sṛ to go, aff. ap or gha.
