• nikṣepa m. (-paḥ)
    1 A pledge, a deposit in general, or one which is counted or particularised in presence of the receiver, and left without cover or seal.
    2 Abandoning, parting with, throwing away.
    3 Sending or putting away.
    4 Wiping, drying.
    E. ni prefixed to kṣipa to throw or reject, affix ghañ.
