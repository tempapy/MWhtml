• gopa [299] mfn. (-paḥ-pā-paṃ) 1 A helper, a friend or patron. 2 A preserver, a cherisher. 3 Who or what hides or conceals. m. (-paḥ) 1 Superintendent of a district. 2 The head of a cowpen. 3 A herdsman, a cowherd, a milkman by caste and occupation. 4 A king. 5 Myrrh: see goparasa. f. (-pā or -pī) A plant, (Echites frutescens). f. (-pā) A milkwoman, a female cowherd. f. (-pī) 1 A cowherd's wife, but especially applied to those of Vṛndāvana, the companions and objects of KṚṢṆA'S juvenile sports, and who are considered sometimes as holy or celestial personages. 2 A protectress. 3 Nature, elementary nature. E. go the earth, &c. and pa what preserves; also in some senses gupa to hide, to protect, &c. affix ghañ, fem. affix ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
