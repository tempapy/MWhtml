• śatapatra n. (-traṃ) A lotus in general, (Nelumbium speciosum or Nymphæa nelumbo). m. (-traḥ)
    1 A peacock.
    2 The Sāras or Indian crane.
    3 A wood-pecker.
    4 A parrot, the king parrot or Loory. f. (-trā) A woman.
    E. śata a hundred, patra a leaf or feather. [827]
