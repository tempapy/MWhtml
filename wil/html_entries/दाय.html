• dāya [405] (ṛ) dāyṛ r. 1st cl. (dāyate) To give.
  • dāya [405] m. (-yaḥ) 1 Gift, donation. 2 A special gift, as a nuptial present, alms to a student at his initiation, &c. 3 Portion, inheritance. 4 Loss, destruction. 5 Breaking, dividing. 6 A place, a site. 7 Irony. E. dā to give, in the passive form, affix ṇa; that which is given; or do to cut, &c. that which is divided; or dāya to give, affix ac.
