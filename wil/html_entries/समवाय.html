• samavāya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 Multitude, assemblage, collection, aggregate, quantity.
    2 One of the categories or Padārthas of logic, intimate and constant relation, as of yarn and the cloth made of it.
    3 Connection of species in a genus, or parts of a whole.
    E. sam and ava before aya to go, aff. ghañ.
