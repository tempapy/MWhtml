• sāraṅga [920] mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgī-ṅgaṃ) Variegated, of a variegated colour. m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 Variegated colour. 2 A lion. 3 An elephant. 4 A deer. 5 The Cātaka, (Cuculus melanoleucos.) 6 The Koïl or Indian cuckoo. 7 A large kind of crane, (Ardea Sarunga, CAREY.) 8 A peacock. 9 A cloud. 10 A tree. 11 A parasol. 12 A garment, cloth, cloths. 13 Hair. 14 A lotus. 15 A flower. 16 A conch shell. 17 A jewel. 18 Gold. 19 A bow. 20 KĀMADEVA. 21 Sandal. 22 Camphor. 23 The earth. 24 Night. 25 Light. f. (-ṅgī) A sort of fiddle. E. sṛ to go, aṅgac Uṇādi aff., or sāra strength, &c., gama to go, khac aff.; also śāraṅga.
