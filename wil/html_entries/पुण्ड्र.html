• puṇḍra n. (-ṇḍraṃ)
    1 A red variety of the common sugarcane, (Saccharum officinarum.)
    2 The name of a Daitya or infernal being, considered as incarnate in ŚIŚUPĀLA.
    3 A mark or line made on the forehead with Sandal, &c.
    4 A sort of creeper, (Gærtnera racemosa.)
    5 A worm.
    6 A white lotus.
    7 The name of a country, the greater part of Bengal and part of Behar. m. plu. (-ṇḍrāḥ) The inhabitants of that country.
    E. puḍi to rub, aff. raka.
