• vilakṣa mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā-kṣaṃ)
    1 Astonished, surprised.
    2 Abashed, ashamed.
    3 Markless, having no mark or aim.
    4 Contra-indicated, having a character or mark contrary to what is usual or natural.
    5 Having no characteristic mark or property.
    E. vi before lakṣa to see, aff. ac. [782]
