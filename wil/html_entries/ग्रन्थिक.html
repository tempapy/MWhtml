• granthika m. (-kaḥ)
    1 An astrologer, a fortune teller.
    2 The youngest of the Pāṇḍus.
    3 A thorny plant: see karīra. n. (-kaṃ)

    2 A kind of resin, Bdellium.
    3 A perfume: see granthiparṇa.
