• viśeṣaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Discriminative, distinguishing, attributive, characteristic. mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ)
    1 A mark on the forehead made with Sandal, &c., and worn either as an ornament or sectarial distinction.
    2 An attribute, a predicate.
    3 An arrangement of poetical composition.
    E. vi before śiṣa to attribute, or distinguish, aff. ṇvul. [787]
