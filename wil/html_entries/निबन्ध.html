• nibandha [469] m. (-ndhaḥ) 1 Epistasis, suppression of urine or constipation. 2 Commentary, explanation of technical rules. 3 The Nimb tree: see nimba. 4 Binding, confinement. 5 Intentness on or attachment to. 6 A grant of property, an assignment of cattle or money for support, a corrody. 7 Fixed property, not moveable or fluctuating. n. (-ndhaṃ) Song, singing. E. ni before badhi to bind, affix dhañ.
