• āḍambara [108] m. (-raḥ) 1 Charge sounded by musical instruments. 2 The roaring of elephants. 3 A drum used in battle. 4 Commencement. 5 The eyelid. 6 Pride, arrogance. 7 Anger, passion. 8 Happiness, pleasure. n. (-raṃ) Rubbing and moulding the body. E. āṅ, dama to tame or subdue, barac affix; da is changed to ḍa.
