• avagraha [076] m. (-haḥ) 1 Taking, acceptance. 2 Taking off or away, seizure. 3 Disrespect. 4 Drought. 5 Obstacle, impediment. 6 An elephant's forehead. 7 A herd of elephants. 8 Nature, original temperament. 9 A sort of knowledge, a false idea. 10 An imprecation or term of abuse. E. ava before, graha to take or seize, ap affix: also avagrāha.
