• jayapatra n. (-traṃ) Record of victory or triumph; in law, the sealed and written award of the judge in favour of either party; in historical tradition, the label on the brow of a horse turned loose for the Aśvamedha sacrifice.
    E. jaya conquest, and patra a leaf; also with vana added jayapatraka. [341]
