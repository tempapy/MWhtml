• rudha r. 4th cl. (with anu prefixed) (anurudhyate)
    1 To desire, to wish for, to love.
    2 To be kind to.
    3 To consent, to concur.
    4 To lament or be sorry for. (ñi au ira) ñi aurudhir r. 7th cl. (ruṇaddhi ruṃddhe)
    1 To impede, to oppose, to obstruct, to block or confine.
    2 To invest, to blockade, to besiege.
    With abhi and sam prefixed, To prevent.
    With ava, To guard.
    With upa, To blockade.
    With prati or vi, To oppose, to resist.
    With sam and ni, To shut up, to surround or blockade. [708]
