• mahī f. (-hī)
    1 The earth.
    2 A river, the Mahī which rises in the province of Malva, and after pursuing a westerly course of about 280 miles, falls into the upper part of the gulf of Cambay.
    3 A cow.
    4 A potherb, (Hingtsha repens.)
    E. maha to worship or be worshipped, aff. i, and ṅīṣ added; also without the addition, mahi. [653]
