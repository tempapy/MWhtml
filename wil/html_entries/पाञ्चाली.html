• pāñcālī f. (-lī)
    1 A name of DRAUPADĪ, the wife of the five Pāṇḍu princes.
    2 A puppet, a doll.
    3 Melodious combination of five or six words.
    E. pañcāla the country so called, aff. of descent or reference aṇ, and fem. aff. ṅīp; coming from that region; also with kan added, pāñcālikā, and by making the second vowel short pāñcalikā. [522]
