• pṛśni mfn. (-śniḥ-śniḥ-śni)
    1 Small, short, thin.
    2 Delicate, feeble. f. (-śniḥ)
    1 A ray of light.
    2 A queen, the wife of SUTAPĀ, who being regenerated after death, became DEVAKĪ the mother of KṚṢṆA.
    3 The earth.
    E. spṛśa to touch, ni Uṇādi aff., and the initial rejected; also pṛṣṇi.
