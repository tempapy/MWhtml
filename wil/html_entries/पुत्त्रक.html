• puttraka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A son.
    2 A son, as it were, used as a term of endearment.
    3 A fabulous animal: see śarabha.
    4 A rogue, a cheat.
    5 A particular mountain.
    6 A sort of tree.
    7 A grasshopper. f. (-trakā or trikā) A daughter.
    E. putra a son, kan pleonasm; in the feminine form the substitution of i for the penultimate vowel is optional. [541]
