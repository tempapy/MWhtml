• liṅga [720] n. (-ṅgaṃ) 1 A mark, a spot, a stain, a sign, a token, &c. 2 The penis. 3 The phallus, or ŚIVA under that emblem. 4 Inference, probable conclusion. 5 The premises leading to a conclusion. 6 Nature or Prakṛti, according to the Sāṃkhya philosophy, which considers this as the active power in creation. 7 Gender, (as puṃliṅgaḥ the masculine gender, &c.) 8 The order of the religious student. 9 Symptom or mark of disease. E. ligi to go, &c., aff. ac.
