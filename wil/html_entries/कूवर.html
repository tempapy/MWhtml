• kūvara mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) Beautiful, agreeable, pleasing. mn. (-raḥ-raṃ) The pole of a carriage or wood to which the yoke is fixed. m. (-raḥ) A humpbacked man. f. (-rī) A carriage covered with a cloth or blanket.
    E. kū to sound, and vara from vṛ to cherish, with ap aff. [240]
