• śakuna [824] m. (-naḥ) 1 A bird in general. 2 A kind of bird, either the Indian vulture or the common kite, (Falco cheela); it is also applied to the Pondicherry eagle, (Falco ponticeriana.) 3 A sort of hymn or song, sung at festivals to solicit or secure lucky events. n. (-naṃ) Any lucky or auspicious object or omen. E. śaka to be able, una Uṇādi aff.
