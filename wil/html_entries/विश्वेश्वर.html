• viśveśvara m. (-raḥ) ŚIVA, under a form or appellation in which especially he is worshipped at Benares, where a celebrated temple is appropriated to him in the character of lord or god of the universe.
    E. viśva the universe, and īśvara god; also similar compounds, as viśveśa, viśvanātha, &c.
