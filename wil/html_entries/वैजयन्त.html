• vaijayanta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 The palace of INDRA.
    2 The banner or emblem of INDRA. f. (-ntī)
    1 A flag, a banner, an ensign.
    2 A small tree, (Sesbana Ægyptiaca.)
    3 Another tree, (Premna spinosa, &c.)
    4 A kind of garland.
    E. vi before ji to conquer, bhṛñ aff.
