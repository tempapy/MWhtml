• parivartta [511] m. (-rttaḥ) 1 Going or turning back. 2 Turning round, revolving. 3 Flight, retreat, desertion. 4 Exchange, barter. 5 Requital, return. 6 The end of a period of four ages, or destruction of the world. 7 A chapter, a book, a canto, &c. 8 The king of the tortoises, or the tortoise incarnation. E. pari severally, &c. vṛta to be, aff. ap.
