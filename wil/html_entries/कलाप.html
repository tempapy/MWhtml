• kalāpa [201] m. (-paḥ) 1 An ornament in general. 2 A zone, a string of bells worn by women round the wrist. 3 A peacock's tail. 4 Assemblage, multitude. 5 A quiver. 6 The moon. 7 A clever and intelligent man. 8 The name of a grammar of the Sanscrit language, ascribed to the god KĀRTTIKEYA. 9 A village where the destroyer KALKI is to be born. 10 A poem written in one metre. E. kalā an art, a division of time, &c. āpa to obtain, and aṇ aff.
