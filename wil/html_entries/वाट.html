• vāṭa mfn. adj. (-ṭaḥ-ṭī-ṭaṃ) Made of the Indian fig tree, of its wood, &c. subst. (-ṭaḥ-ṭī-ṭaṃ)
    1 An enclosure, a piece of enclosed ground, whether a simple enclosure, as a courtyard, or one for trees and plants, as an orchard, a garden, a plantation, &c.
    2 A road.
    3 A mud wall, a bound hedge or any enclosure of a town or village, &c.
    4 A sort of grain, (Panicum spicatum.)
    5 The groin. f. (-ṭī)
    1 The site of a house or building.
    2 A house, a dwelling.
    3 A plant: see vāṭyālaka.
    E. vaṭa to surround, aff. ghañ.
