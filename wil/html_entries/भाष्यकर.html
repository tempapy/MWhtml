• bhāṣyakara m. (-raḥ) A commentator or expounder of technical texts or aphorisms; commonly applied as an epithet of PĀTAÑJALI, the author of the Mahābhāṣya or commentary on the annotations of KĀTYĀYANA, on PĀṆINI'S grammatical aphorisms.
    E. bhāṣya as above, and kāra who makes; also with kṛt, bhāṣyakṛt, the same. [619]
