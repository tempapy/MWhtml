• sthala r. 1st cl. (sthalati) To stand, to be firm; also ṣṭhala. [951]
  • sthala nf. (-laṃ-lī) Place, site, soil, dry or firm ground. nf. (-laṃ-lā) A spot of dry ground prepared by art or drained and raised, &c. n. (-laṃ)
    1 A tent, a house of cloth.
    2 A mound, a hillock.
    3 Point, case, subject (of a description or discussion.)
    4 Part (of a book.)
    E. ṣṭhala to be firm, aff. ac. [951]
