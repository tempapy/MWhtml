• ṣū (ṅa) ṣūṅ r. 2nd cl. (sūte) r. 4th cl. (sūyate)
    1 To bear a child, to bring forth.
    2 To produce in general. r. 6th cl. (suvati) To direct, to send, to throw or cast. [871]
  • ṣū f. (-ṣūḥ) Birth, delivery, child-bearing.
    E. ṣū to bring forth, aff. kvip, and the consonant unchanged. [871]
