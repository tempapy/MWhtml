• kṛttikā f. (-kā)
    1 The third of the lunar mansions, or constellations in the moon's path, consisting of six stars, and corresponding to the Pleiades.
    2 (In mythology,) a nymph; one of six, the nurses of KĀRTTIKEYA.
    E. kṛta to cut, ktin and kan affixes; the figure of the asterism is a razor or knife. [243]
