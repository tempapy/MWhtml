• tamopaha [367] mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ) Lightening, enlightening, removing darkness physical or moral. m. (-haḥ) 1 The sun. 2 The moon. 3 Fire. 4 A name of Buddha, the founder of the Bauddha sect.
