• tamopaha mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ) Lightening, enlightening, removing darkness physical or moral. m. (-haḥ)
    1 The sun.
    2 The moon.
    3 Fire.
    4 A name of Buddha, the founder of the Bauddha sect.
    E. tamam darkness, apaha removing, from apa prefixed to hana to kill or destroy, affix ḍa; also tamohantṛ mfn. (-ntā-ntrī-ntṛ)
