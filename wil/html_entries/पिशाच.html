• piśāca m. (-caḥ) A sprite, a fiend, a malevolent being something between an infernal imp and a ghost, but always described as fierce and malignant. f. (-cī) A female imp, a she demon.
    E. piśa for piśita flesh, and aśa to eat, aff. aṇ, deriv. irr.; also with kan added, piśācaka, piśācikā. [537]
