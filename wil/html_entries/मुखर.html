• mukhara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Foul-mouthed, speaking harshly or scurrilously.
    2 Sounding, noisy.
    3 Speaking in ridicule of, rallying, mocking, &c. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A leader.
    2 A conch shell.
    3 Leading, preceding.
    4 A crow.
    E. mukha the mouth, and ra aff. implying depreciation, or rā to go, ḍa aff. [664]
