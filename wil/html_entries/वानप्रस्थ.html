• vānaprastha [751] m. (-sthaḥ) 1 The Brahman of the third order, who has passed through the conditions of student and householder, and has left his house and family for lonely meditation in woods and wilds; the hermit, the anchoret. 2 A tree, (Bassia latifolia.) 3 The Palāśa tree, (Butea frondosa.) E. vana a wood, aṇ aff., vāna a solitude in the woods, &c., prastha who goes forth to, from sthā to stay, with pra prefix, and ka aff.
