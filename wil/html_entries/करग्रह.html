• karagraha m. (-haḥ)
    1 Marriage.
    2 Levying or gathering a tax.
    3 A tax gatherer.
    4 Taking the hand.
    E. kara, and graha taking: one part of the ceremony of marriage is the placing of the right hand of the bride with the palm uppermost, in the right hand of the bridegroom; also karagrāha. [191]
