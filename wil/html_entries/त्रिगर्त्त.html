• trigartta m. (-rttaḥ)
    1 A country in the north-west division of India, or m. plu. (-rttaḥ) Its inhabitants: apparently part of Lahore, said, by Mr. WILFORD, to be the modern Tahora, but differently described in the Koṣas: see jālandhara and vāhlīka.
    2 A form of calculation, (gaṇitāntare.) f. (-rttā)
    1 A small worm.
    2 A lascivious woman, a wanton.
    3 A woman in general.
    4 A pearl.
    E. tri three, and gartta a hole, &c. [389]
