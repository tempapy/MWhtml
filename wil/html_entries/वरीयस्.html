• varīyas mfn. (-yān-yasī-yaḥ)
    1 Very large, largest, most large.
    2 Best, most preferable or excellent.
    3 Extremely young, tender, infantine. m. (-yān) The eighteenth of the astronomical periods called Yogas.
    E. vara best, or substituted for uru, and iyasun aff. of the irr. superlative.
