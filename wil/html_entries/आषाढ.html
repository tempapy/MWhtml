• āṣāḍha [125] m. (-ḍhaḥ) 1 The name of a month, (June-July.) 2 A staff of the wood of the Palāśa, carried by an ascetic in the month Āṣāḍha. 3 The Malaya mountain. E. āṣāḍhā the constellation, and aṇ affix. f. (-ḍhī) The day of full moon in the month Āṣāḍha. f. (-ḍhā) The twenty-first and twenty-second lunar mansion; it is most commonly compounded with pūrvva and uttaraḥ see pūrvvāṣāḍhā and uttarāṣāḍhā. E. a neg. saha to bear, aṇ and ṭāp aff.
