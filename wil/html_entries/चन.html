• cana r. 1st and 10th cls. (canati cānayati) To hurt, to injure in any manner, to wound or kill, &c.
    2 To believe, to confide.
    3 To make a sound. [316]
  • cana ind. A particle affixed to certain words, giving them an indefinite signification, as kadā when, kadācana some when, at sometime, kaḥ who kañcana some one: see cit.
    E. cana to sound, affix ac. [316]
