• lagha [714] r. 10th cl. (-lāghayati) 1 To taste. 2 To obtain. (i) laghi r. 1st cl. (laṃghate) 1 To go, to move. 2 To fast, to abstain from food. (laṅghati) 1 To dry, to dry up. 2 To diminish, to lessen. r. 10th cl. (laṅghayati) To speak.
    With ut or vi prefixed, To go beyond or over, to cross, to pass, to leap or spring over; also morally, to exceed, to transgress.
