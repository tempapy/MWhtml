• uccha r. 1st and 6th cls. (i.) ucchi (uñchati)
    1 To glean.
    2 To gather little by little. (ī.) ucchī (ucchati) or usually
    with vi prefixed, (vyucchati)
    1 To end or terminate, to leave off.
    2 To finish or conclude.
    3 To pass or quit.
    4 To bind.
    With pra, to wipe off. [138]
