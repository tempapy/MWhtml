• aśvattha m. (-tthaḥ)
    1 The holy fig tree, (Ficus religiousa.)
    2 The fruit of the tree.
    3 The time at which it bears. f. (-tthā) Day of fullmoon.
    E. a neg. śva a future period, and tha from sthā to remain; of less duration than the Vaṭa or Indian fig; there are several other etymologies.
