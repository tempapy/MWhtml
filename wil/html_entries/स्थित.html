• sthita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Steady, firm, immovable.
    2 Stayed, stopped.
    3 Determined, resolved, decreed, established.
    4 Upright, virtuous, steady in conduct.
    5 Agreed, engaged, contracted, promised.
    6 Risen, got up.
    E. ṣṭhā to stay or be, aff. kta. [953]
