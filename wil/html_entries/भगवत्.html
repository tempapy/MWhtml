• bhagavat [610] mfn. (-vān-vatī-vat) Respectable, worshipful, adorable, and hence the common appellative of a prince or deity. m. (-vān) One of the generic titles of a Jina or Jaina deified sage. f. (-tī) 1 A name of GAURĪ. 2 Any goddess. E. bhaga fortune, supremacy, &c. and matup poss. aff.
