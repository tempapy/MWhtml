• parikara mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) Who or what helps or assists. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A bed.
    2 Dependants, retinue, train.
    3 Multitude, numbers.
    4 A firm girth, a waist, a zone or sash.
    5 Commencement.
    6 Discrimination, judgment.
    7 Covert or indirect intimation of wish or purpose.
    8 Unusual meaning or expression.
    E. pari round about, kara making. [507]
