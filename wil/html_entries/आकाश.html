• ākāśa [103] mn. (-śaḥ-śaṃ) 1 The fifth element, aether, the sky or atmosphere. 2 Brahma as identical with aether. 3 Space, vacuity. E. āṅ and kāśṛ to shine, ghañ affix; every where shining; Ākāśa is the subtle and ethereal fluid, supposed to fill and pervade the universe, and to be the peculiar vehicle of life and sound.
