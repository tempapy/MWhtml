• subhaga [933] mfn. (-gaḥ-gā-gaṃ) 1 Pleasing to the eye, grateful to the sight. 2 Beloved, liked. 3 Fortunate, auspicious. m. (-gaḥ) 1 Borax. 2 The Asoca tree. 3 The Champac, (Michelia champaca.) f. (-gā) 1 A woman beloved by her husband, a favourite wife. 2 A respectable and auspicious mother. 3 A wild variety of Arabian jasmine. 4 Turmeric. 5 Holy basil. 6 A shrub, (Hedysarum Gangeticum.) E. su good, excellent, and bhaga fortune, beauty, &c.
