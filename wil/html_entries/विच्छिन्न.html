• vicchinna mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnī-nnaṃ)
    1 Cut, separated, severed.
    2 Absent or separated from.
    2 Divided, parted, portioned.
    4 Shared, divided equally.
    5 Crooked, curved.
    6 Scattered, dispersed.
    7 Hidden or cut off from view, intercepted.
    E. vi apart, chida to cut, aff. kta. [763]
