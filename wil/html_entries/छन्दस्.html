• chandas n. (-ndaḥ)
    1 Poetical metre of every kind, but it is also applicable particularly to the metre of the Vedas.
    2 Meaning, purport, object, intention.
    3 Wish, desire.
    4 The Vedas or scripture.
    5 Wilfulness, independence, uncontrolled or unrestrained conduct.
    E. cadi to gladden, &c. and asun Uṇādi affix, ca is changed to cha; also in some senses with a final vowel chanda, q. v. [334]
