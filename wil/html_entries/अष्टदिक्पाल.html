• aṣṭadikpāla m. plu. (-lāḥ) The regents of the cardinal points, as INDRA of the East; VAHNI of the S. E.; YAMA of the South; NAIRṚTA of the S. W.; VARUṆA of the West; MARUT of the N. W.; KUVERA of the North; and ĪŚA of the N.
    E. aṣṭadiś the eight points, and pāla who protects. [091]
