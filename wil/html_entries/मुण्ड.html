• muṇḍa mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā-ṇḍaṃ)
    1 Shaved, bald, having no hair on the head.
    2 Low, mean. mn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍaṃ)
    1 The head.
    2 The forehead. m. (-ṇḍaḥ)
    1 A bald pate.
    2 A barber.
    3 The name of a Daitya or demon.
    4 RĀHU, the personified ascending node.
    5 The trunk of a lopped tree. f. (-ṇḍā) Bengal madder, (Rubia manjith.)
    E. muḍi to shave or cut, aff. ghañ.
