• lepa(ṛ)lepṛ [723] r. 1st cl. (lepate) To go, to approach.
  • lepa [723] m. (-paḥ) 1 Plastering, smearing. 2 Food. 3 Mortar, plaster, Chunam. 4 Stain, spot, smearing. 5 The wipings of the hand which has offered funeral oblations to three ancestors, and which are considered as an oblation to ancestors in the 4th, 5th, and 6th degrees. E. lipa to smear, &c., aff. ghañ.
