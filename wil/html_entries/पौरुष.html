• pauruṣa mfn. subst. (-ṣaḥ-ṣī-ṣaṃ) The measure of a man, equal to the height to which he reaches with both arms elevated, and the fingers extended. adj. Manly, of or belonging to man. n. (-ṣaṃ)
    1 The property of manhood, virility, manliness.
    2 Action, or action incidental to the state of humanity.
    3 Semen virile.
    4 Strength, power, vigour.
    E. puruṣa a man, aff. aṇ.
