• āmnāya [117] m. (-yaḥ) 1 A Veda, or the Vedas in the aggregate. 2 Received doctrine, traditional and right. 3 Traditional usuage, family or national customs. 4 Advice, instruction in past and present usage. 5 A Tantra. 6 An element of being. a property of substance. E. āṅ, mnā to learn, to remember, ghañ and yuk affs.
