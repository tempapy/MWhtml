• lohita [726] mfn. (-taḥ-tā or tī-taṃ) Red, of a red colour. m. (-taḥ) 1 Red, the colour. 2 The planet MARS. 3 One of the male rivers. 4 The Rohi, a kind of fish, (Cyprinus Rohita, HAM.) 5 A sort of deer. 6 A snake. 7 A deity, a demi-god. 8 A sort of bean, (Ervum hirsutum.) 9 A form of array. 10 A tree, (Andersonia Rohitaka.) n. (-taṃ) 1 Blood. 2 A red kind of Agallochum. 3 Saffron. 4 Red sanders. 5 War, battle. f. (-tā) 1 A woman red with anger or with colour, &c. 2 A sort of creeper, (Lycopodium imbricatum.) E. ruha to grow, itan Uṇādi aff., and ra changed to la.
