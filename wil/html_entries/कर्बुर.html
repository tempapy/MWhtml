• karbura [197] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Variegated, of a spotted or variegated color. f. (-rā) 1 Trumpet flower, (Bignonia suave-olens.) 2 A sort of basil, (Ocymum gratissimum:) see varvarā. f. (-rī) A name of the goddess DURGĀ. n. (-raṃ) 1 Gold. 2 Water. 3 Datura, (the fruit.) (-raḥ) 1 A demon, an imp or goblin. 2 A variegated colour. 3 A plant, (Curcuma reclinata, Rox.) see śaṭhī. 4 Sin. 5 Rice growing amidst inundation. E. kaba to tinge or dye, urac Uṇādi affix and ra inserted, or karba to go, &c. and uran affix, also long, karbura; it is also often read with the dental ba, but perhaps erroneously.
