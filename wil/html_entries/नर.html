• nara [455] m. (-raḥ) 1 Man, individually or generally. 2 The Eternal, the divine imperishable spirit pervading the universe. 3 A name of ARJUNA. 4 VIṢṆU. 5 A gnomon. 6 A man or piece at chess, draughts, &c. 7 A Muni, an incarnation of VIṢṆU. n. (-raṃ) A fragrant grass, commonly Rāmkapūr. f. (-rī) Woman in general: see nārī. E. nṛ to lead or guide, affix ac.
