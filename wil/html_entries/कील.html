• kīla r. 10th cl. (kīlayati) To bind, to fasten, to stake, to pin.
  • kīla mf. (-laḥ-lā)
    1 Flame, lambent flame.
    2 Small, minute. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A stake, a pin, a bolt, a wedge, &c.
    9 A lance, a pike.
    2 A gnomon.
    4 A weapon.
    5 A post, a pillar.
    6 The elbow.
    7 A blow or punch with the elbow.
    8 A blow, a thump; (in the three last senses some make this also fem.)
    9 A post in a cow-house: see the next.
    E. kīla to bind, and ka aff.
