• hāhā [974] m. (-hāḥ) A Gandharba, a demi-god of an inferior order, attendant on KUVERA and the other gods, and especially the chanter of their praises. ind. An interjection of surprise, or grief, or pain. E. hā alas, interjection of sorrow, hā to abandon, kvip aff., or with asi, aff. hāhas, or hā interjection repeated.
