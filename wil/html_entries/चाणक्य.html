• cāṇakya m. (-kyaḥ)
    1 A name of the sage VĀTSYĀYANA.
    2 Name of a Brahman, the reputed author of a work on polity, and minister of CANDRAGUPTA. n. (-kyaṃ) The work of Cāṇakya, detached stanzas original or compiled, on morals and polity.
    E. caṇaka a saint, and yañ affix of descent.
