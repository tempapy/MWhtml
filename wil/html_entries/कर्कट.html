• karkaṭa [194] m. (-ṭaḥ) 1 A crab. 2 The sign of the zodiac, (Cancer.) 3 A kind of bird, the numidian crane. 4 A long gourd: see tumbī. 5 The fibrous root of the lotus. 6 The curved end of the beam of a balance, to which the strings supporting the scale are attached. 7 A compass. 8 The radius of a circle. f. (-ṭā) A plant, commonly Kurkavali, (Momordica mixta.) (-ṭī) 1 A kind of cucumber, (Cucumis utilatissimus, Rox.) 2 The fruit of the silk cotton tree. 3 A snake. 4 A water jar. E. karka a Sautra root, to laugh or smile, and aṭan affix, fem. affix ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
