• smṛti [959] f. (-tiḥ) 1 Recollection, remembrance, memory. 2 Law, the body of law, as delivered originally by MANU, YĀJÑAVALKYA, and other inspired legislators, to their respective pupils, and committed by them from recollection to writing. 3 A law book, a code of laws. 4 Wish, desire. 5 Understanding. E. smṛ to remember, aff. ktin.
