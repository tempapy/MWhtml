• śarad f. (-rat or raṭ)
    1 The season of autumn or the sultry season; the two months succeeding the rains; according to the Vaidikas, comprising the months Bhādra and Āśvina, and according to the Paurāṇikas, Āśvina and Kārttika, fluctuating thus from August to November.
    2 A year.
    E. śṝ to injure, adi Uṇādi aff.; also with ṭāp added śaradā.
