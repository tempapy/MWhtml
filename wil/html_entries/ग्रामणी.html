• grāmaṇī [305] mfn. (-ṇīḥ-ṇīḥ-ṇi) 1 Best, excellent. 2 Chief, pre-eminent, superintendent. 3 One who only thinks of enjoyment. m. (-ṇīḥ) A barber. f. (-ṇīḥ) 1 A whore, a harlot. 2 A female peasant or villager. 3 The indigo plant. E. grāma a village, nī to get or obtain, affix kvip.
