• vibhāgabhāj m. (-bhāk) One who shares in a portion, of property already distributed, applied especially to a son by a father and mother of the same tribe, born subsequent to a distribution of property amongst his parents and brethren, in which case he inherits the portion allotted or reserved to the parents.
    E. vibhāga a portion, and bhāk who shares. [777]
