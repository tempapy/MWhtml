• likṣā [720] m. (-kṣā) A nit, a young louse. E. a various reading of likṣā.
  • likṣā [720] f. (-kṣā) 1 A nit, a young louse, or the egg of a louse. 2 A poppy seed, considered as a measure of weight, or 1/6th of a mustard seed. E. lakṣa to mark, aff. ghañ, i substituted for the radical vowel and the deriv. irr.; also kka being substituted for the final likkā, and with kan added, fem. form, likṣikā again with ī substituted for the radical vowel līkṣā, &c.
