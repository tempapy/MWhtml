• bhinna [620] mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ) 1 Split, broken, torn, rent. 2 Divided, distinguished, other, different. 3 Separated, detached. 4 Blown, budded, opened. 5 Neglected, deviated from. 6 Connected, joined. m. (-nnaḥ) A flaw in a jewel. n. (-nnaṃ) 1 A bit, a portion. 2 (In arithmetic,) A fraction. E. bhida to break, to divide, aff. kta.
