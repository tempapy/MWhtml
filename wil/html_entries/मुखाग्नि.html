• mukhāgni [664] m. (-gniḥ) 1 The conflagration of a forest. 2 A sort of goblin with a face of fire. 3 Fire put into the mouth of the corpse at the time of lighting the funeral pile. 4 A sacrificial or consecrated fire. E. muca mouth, face, or chief, principal, and agni fire.
