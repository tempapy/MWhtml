• ḍimba m. (-mbaḥ)
    1 Affray, assault, conflict without weapons, mutual defiance, petty and predatory war, &c. see ḍamara.
    2 Sound or noise occasioned by terror.
    3 An egg.
    4 A chrysalis.
    5 The bladder.
    6 The spleen.
    7 The uterus.
    8 Fear, terror.
    9 Any young animal.
    10 A globe or ball.
    E. ḍī to fly, derive irr.
