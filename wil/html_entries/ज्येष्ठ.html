• jyeṣṭha [355] mfn. (-ṣṭhaḥ-ṣṭhā-ṣṭhaṃ) 1 Best, most excellent, pre-eminent. 2 Very old, oldest. 3 Elder, elder born. f. (-ṣṭhā) 1 One of the asterisms considered as lunar mansions; the eighteenth, comprising three stars, of which one is a Scorpionis, figured by a ring or earring. 2 The middle finger. 3 Misfortune, personified as a goddess. 4 The Ganges, (-ṣṭhā or ṣṭhī) A small house lizard. m. (-ṣṭhaḥ) The month Jyaiṣṭha or Jeyte, (May-June.) n. (-ṣṭhaṃ) Age, oldness. E. jyā to decay, to grow infirm or old, affix iṣṭhan, fem. affixes. ṭāp and ṅīṣ.
