• caryya [320] mfn. (-ryyaḥ-ryyā-ryyaṃ) To be gone, (in or by.) nf. (-ryyaṃ-ryyā) 1 Perseverance in religious austerities. 2 Due and regular observance of all rites or customs. 3 Following the rules of studentship. 4 Going. 5 Eating. 6 Practice, usage. 7 Behaviour. E. cara to go, and yat or ṇyat aff.
