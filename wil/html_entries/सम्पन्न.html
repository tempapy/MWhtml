• sampanna mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ)
    1 Accomplished, completed, effected, obtained.
    2 Prosperous, fortunate, thriving, happy.
    3 Possessed of, endowed with.
    4 Mature, full grown.
    5 Perfectly acquainted with or performing, (duty, &c.)
    E. sam with, pada to go, aff. kta; also with kan aff. sampannaka.
