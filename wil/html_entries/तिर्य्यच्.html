• tiryyac [377] or tiryyañc mfn. (tiryyaṅ tirañcī tiryyak) Moving tortuously, going crookedly or awry, (the neuter is also used adverbially: see tiryyak.) m. (-ryyaṅ) 1 An animal, a quadruped. 2 A bird, or any animal moving tortuously. f. (-ryyañcī) The female of an animal, a beast or bird. E. tiras crookedly, añca to go, affix kvip.
