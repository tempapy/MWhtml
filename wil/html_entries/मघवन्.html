• maghavan m. (-bā)
    1 INDRA.
    2 One of the twelve Cakravartīs, or universal monarchs of the Jainas.
    E. maha to sacrifice, kanin Uṇādi aff., and vuk augment, form irr.; before the vowel affs. of the second case plural, and the last five cases, the va of this word is changed to u, and thence becomes o as usual, as maghonaḥ, maghonā, maghone, &c. also with ṅīṣ aff. the fem. form is maghonī f. (-nī) The wife of INDRA. [631]
