• gṛhapati [296] m. (-tiḥ) 1 A householder, a man of the second class, or who after having finished his studies is married and settled. 2 A householder of particular merit, giving alms, and performing all the prescribed ceremonies, &c. 3 Maintenance of a sacred and perpetual fire. 4 Virtue, especially of a householder, as hospitality, &c. E. gṛha a house, and pati lord or master.
