• ṣaḍaṅga [869] mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgī-ṅgaṃ) 1 Having six limbs or members. 2 Having six parts, six ingredients, &c. n. (-ṅgaṃ) 1 Six parts of the body collectively; as the two arms, two legs, and the head, and waist. 2 The six supplementary parts of the Vedas, or grammar, prosody, astronomy, pronunciation, the meaning of unusual terms, and the ritual of the Hindu religion. E. ṣaṣ six, and aṅga a limb or part.
