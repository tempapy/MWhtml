• pākayajña m. (-jñaḥ) An oblation offered on building a house, liberating a bull, &c. or a religious rite of four kinds, oblation to the VIŚVADEVAS, offerings to the spirits, the permanent obsequial rites, and hospitality.
    E. pāka, and yajña sacrifice; the distribution of food being part of each of these ceremonies.
