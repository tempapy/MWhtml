• kṛtrimamitra m. (-traḥ) An acquired friend; one on whom benefits have been conferred, or from whom they have been received.
    E. kṛtrima made, and mitra a friend; also similar compounds in this or other senses, kṛtrimaśatru an acquired enemy, kṛtrimaudasīna one who is indifferent to benefits or injuries, neither an acquired friend nor foe.
