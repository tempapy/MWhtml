• dhṛtarāṣṭra m. (-ṣṭraḥ) A proper name; DHṚTARĀṢṬRA, the father of DURYODHANA, and uncle of the Pāṇḍu princes.
    2 A good king.
    3 A Nāga or serpent of the lower regions.
    4 A kind of bird, perhaps a sort of goose: see dhārttarāṣṭra. f. (-rī) A goose, the female bird only.
    E. dhṛta possessed, cherished, (by whom,) rāṣṭra a region.
