• kapardda m. (-rddaḥ)
    1 The braided hair of ŚIVA.
    2 A small shell used as a coin, a Cowri.
    3 A name of ŚIVA.
    E. ka water, (that of the Ganges,) pṛ in the deriv. form, nourishing, and da from dā to give; or ka happiness, &c. which will apply to both senses; also with kan added kaparddaka.
