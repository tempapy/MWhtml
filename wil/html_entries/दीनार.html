• dīnāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A gold ornament.
    2 A seal.
    3 A weight of gold, variously stated as two Kāṣṭhas, one Pala of thirtytwo Reitis, and also as the large Pala or hundred and eight Suvarṇas.
    4 A coin, a Dinar.
    E. dīna a pauper, and ṛ to go; what is given to the poor; or dī to waste, Uṇādi affix āran, and nuṭ augment. [410]
