• kṣura r. 6th cl. (kṣurati)
    1 To cut.
    2 To scratch, to make lines or furrows.
  • kṣura m. (-raḥ)
    1 A razor.
    2 A plant, (Barleria longifolia.)
    3 Also Tribulus lanuginosus: see gokṣura.
    4 A horse's hoof; also khura.
    5 The hoof of a cow, &c.
    6 The foot of a bedstead. f. (-rī) A knife.
    E. kṣura to scratch or cut, Uṇādi affix ran, and the final consonant rejected; also khura.
