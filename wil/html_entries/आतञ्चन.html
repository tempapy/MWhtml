• ātañcana [108] n. (-naṃ) 1 Calcining, adding a powder or flux to metals in fusion. 2 The flux so used. 3 Casting any thing into another to effect some change, as butter-milk into fresh milk to turn it. 4 Casting away, destroying. 5 Danger, calamity, as a pestilence or epidemic. 6 Speed, velocity. 7 Gratifying, satisfying. E. āṅ, tañcu to go, and lyuṭ aff.
