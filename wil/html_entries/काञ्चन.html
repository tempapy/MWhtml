• kāñcana [208] mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ) Golden, of gold. n. (-naṃ) 1 Gold. 2 Wealth. 3 A filament of the lotus. m. (-naḥ) 1 Mountain ebony, (Bauhinia variegata, &c.) 2 A tree bearing a yellow fragrant flower, (Michelia champaca.) 3 Another plant, (Mesua ferrea:) see nāgakesara. 4 Glomerous fig tree: see udumbara. 5 Common thorn apple, (Datura metel.) f. (-nī) 1 Turmeric. 2 A yellow pigment. E. kaci to shine, &c. lyuṭ affix, the radical vowel lengthened; this etymology gives the meaning gold, the others refer to their colour, &c. in which they resemble the metal.
