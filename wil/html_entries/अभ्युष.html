• abhyuṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 Grain, &c. half dressed, slightly scorched, or parched so as to be eaten from the hand.
    2 Bread.
    E. abhi before, uṣa to burn, ka affix; the vowel may be made also long, which gives abhyūṣa, or may be changed to o, and form abhyoṣa; some consider the root to be ūṣa, to be sick. [059]
