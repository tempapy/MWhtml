• pāṇḍu [523] mfn. (-ṇḍuḥ-ṇḍuḥ-ṇḍu) Pale or yellowish white. m. (-ṇḍuḥ) 1 Pale or yellowish white, (the colour.) 2 The name of a sovereign of ancient Delhi, and nominal father of YUDHIṢṬHIRA and the other four Pāṇḍava princes. 3 The jaundice. 4 A white elephant (?). f. (-ṇḍuḥ) A plant, commonly Maṣāni, (Glycine debilis.) E. paḍi to go, aff. ku, and the penultimate vowel made long.
