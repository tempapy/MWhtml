• tāntrika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Relating to the Tāntras, taught by them, following them, &c.
    2 Relating to a thread, &c. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A scholar, a man completely versed in any science.
    2 A follower of the doctrine taught by the Tantras.
    E. tantra truth, &c. and ṭhak aff.
