• didhiṣū [408] f. (-ṣūḥ) 1 A virgin widow remarried: see the preceding; also didhīṣū. 2 An elder sister unmarried, whose junior is a bride. m. (-ṣūḥ) 1 The husband of a woman married a second time: see didhīṣu. 2 Instability, want of firmness. E. didhi firmness, ṣo to abandon, kū Uṇādi aff.
