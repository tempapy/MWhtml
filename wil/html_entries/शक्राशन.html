• śakrāśana m. (-naḥ) A medicinal plant, (Wrightea antidysenterica.)
    E. śakra INDRA, aśana food: created by him; springing from the drops of Amṛta, which fell on the ground from the bodies of the monkies slain in the war with RĀVAṆA, and who were restored to life by INDRA by a shower of Amṛta, or the liquor of immortality.
