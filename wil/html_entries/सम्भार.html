• sambhāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Multitude, number, quantity, heap, assemblage.
    2 Maintaining, supporting.
    3 Provision, preparation, getting every thing ready or complete.
    4 Fulness, completion.
    5 Apparatus, necessaries, things required for any act or affair.
    E. sam before bhṛ to maintain, aff. ghañ. [904]
