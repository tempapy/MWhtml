• pratiloma mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ)
    1 Left, not right.
    2 Reverse, inverted, contrary to the natural course or order, against the hair or grain.
    3 Low, vile, base, depraved.
    4 In law, contrary to caste.
    E. prati reverting, and loman hair of the body, aff. ac. [566]
