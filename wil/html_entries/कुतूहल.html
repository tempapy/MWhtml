• kutūhala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Excellent, best, praised, celebrated.
    3 Surprising, wonderful. n. (-laṃ) Eagerness, vehemence, impetuosity.
    2 Desire, inclination.
    3 Curiosity.
    E. ku bad, tūla to send forth, ka affix, and ha inserted; or kutū an oil bottle, and hala to make lines or furrows; or ku the earth, and tuda to vex or tease; the etymologies however are all fanciful; the word is also written kautūhala. [228]
