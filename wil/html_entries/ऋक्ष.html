• ṛkṣa r. 5th cl. (ṛkṣṇoti) To kill, to hurt or seek to hurt. [167]
  • ṛkṣa mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā-kṣaṃ) Pierced, cut, divided. mn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣaṃ) A star, a constellation. m. (-kṣaḥ)
    1 A mountain in the peninsula, the temporary residence of RĀMA.
    2 A bear.
    3 A plant, (Bignonia Indica.)
    E. ṛṣa to go, Uṇādi affix sa, the radical final then becomes ka, and sa becomes ṣa; and ka and ṣa form the compound letter kṣa. [167]
