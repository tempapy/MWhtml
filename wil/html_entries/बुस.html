• busa r. 4th cl.) (busyati) To dismiss, to loose, to quit or leave.
  • busa n. (-saṃ)
    1 Chaff.
    2 Wealth.
    3 Dry cow-dung.
    4 The thick part of sour curds. f. (-sā) A younger sister, (in theatrical language.)
    E. busa to abandon, aff. ka; also buṣa, sa being changed to ṣa.
