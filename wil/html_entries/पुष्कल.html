• puṣkala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Excellent, eminent, chief, best.
    2 Much, many.
    3 Full, filled, complete.
    4 Near, approached.
    5 Good, salutary.
    6 Possessed of all good things. n. (-laṃ) The mountain Meru. m. (-laḥ)
    1 The holy place Puṣkara.
    2 The son of VARUṆA.
    3 A measure of eight Kuñcis or sixty-four handfuls; in some places it means four times a double handful.
    4 Alms to the extent of four mouthfuls of food.
    E. puṣa to nourish, and kalan Uṇādi aff.; otherwise puṣkara as above, and la interchanged with ra. [546]
