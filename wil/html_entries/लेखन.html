• lekhana [722] n. (-naṃ) 1 Writing, scripture. 2 The bark of the Bhoj tree, which is used for writing on in Nepal and Upper Hindustan. 3 Vomiting. 4 The leaf of the palm tree. 5 Scraping or cleaning the tongue. 6 Scarifying, scratching. 7 Reducing corpulency, thinning. m. (-naḥ) A sort of grass or reed, of which pens are made, (Saccharum spontaneum.) f. (-nī) A pen, a style. E. likha to write, aff. of the act or instrument lyuṭ.
