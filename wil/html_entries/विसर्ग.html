• visarga m. (-rgaḥ)
    1 Abandoning, relinquishment, getting rid of or free from any thing.
    2 Departure, going away.
    3 Separation.
    4 Gift, donation.
    5 Final emancipation or beatitude, exemption from worldly existence.
    6 Evacuation of excrement, voiding by stool, &c.
    7 The soft aspirate or Visarga, marked by two perpendicular dots (:), and as the substitute of the letters sa or ra, the termination of various inflections both of nouns and verbs.
    8 A division of the sun's course, the southern course.
    9 Light, splendour.
    E. vi before sṛja to let go, aff. ghañ.
