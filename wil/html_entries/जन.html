• jana r. 3rd cl. but peculiar to the Vedas (jajanti;) also (ī) janī r. 4th cl. (jayate)
    1 To be born or produced. r. 10th cl. (janayati) To generate or beget. [338]
  • jana m. (-naḥ)
    1 Man, individually or collectively, a man, mankind.
    2 The universe.
    3 A division of the universe or Loka, the residence of deified mortals: see janaloka.
    4 A low man, a wretch. f. (-nī) A mother, &c. see jani. f. (-nā) Birth, production.
    E. jana to be born, affix ac. [339]
