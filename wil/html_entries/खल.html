• khala r. 1st cl. (kharlati)
    1 To gather.
    2 To move.
  • khala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Low, vile, base.
    2 Low, inferior.
    3 Cruel, mischievous, mischief-making.
    4 Bad, wicked. mn. (-laḥ-laṃ)
    1 Earth, mould or soil.
    2 Place, site.
    3 Sediment, deposit of oil, &c.
    4 A granary, a threshing floor.
    5 A mill. m. (-laḥ)
    1 The sun.
    2 A tree with black blossoms: see tamāla.
    3 The Datura plant.
    E. khala to gather, (misfortune, &c.) affix ac.
