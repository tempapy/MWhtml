• karttṛ mfn. (-rttā-rttī-rttṛ)
    1 An agent, a doer, a maker, one who makes or does.
    2 One who practices what he knows. m. (-rttā)
    1 A name of BRAHMĀ.
    2 (In grammar,) The active noun, the nominative case.
    E. kṛ to do, and tṛc affix: it may also be read karttṛ. [196]
