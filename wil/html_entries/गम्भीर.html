• gambhīra [283] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Deep, (as water, but applied metaphorically to sound, intellect, &c. as in English.) 2 Dull, slow. m. (-raḥ) A Mantra or magical incantation written in prose in the Rig Veda. 2 A lotus. 3 The citron. f. (-rā) Hiccup, violent singultus. E. gama to go, īran Uṇādi affix, ma changed to bha, and num inserted: see gabhīra.
