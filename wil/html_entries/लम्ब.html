• lamba [716] mfn. (-mbaḥ-mbā-mbaṃ) 1 Spacious, capacious. 2 Great, large, broad, long, expanded either in breadth or length, or both. 3 Pendulous, depending. m. (-mbaṃ) 1 A bribe, a present. 2 Moving a man at a sort of backgammon or draughts. 3 A perpendicular, (in geometry.) 4 (In astronomy,) The arc between the pole and zenith of any place. f. (-mbā) 1 A name of LAKṢMĪ. 2 A name of GAURĪ or DURGĀ. 3 A bitter gourd. E. labi to fall, to sound, aff. ac.
