• vandana n. (-naṃ)
    1 The face or mouth.
    2 Obeisance to a Brahman or superior by touching the feet, &c. f. (-nī)
    1 Reverence, worship, adoring.
    2 Begging, soliciting, asking.
    3 The hip (?)
    4 A drug for reviving the dead. f. (-nā) Praise, praising, especially the gods or great men.
    E. vadi to praise, aff. lyuṭ or yuc, fem. aff. ṅīṣ or ṭāp. [734]
