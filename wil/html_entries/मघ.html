• magha(i)maghi r. 1st cl. (maṃghate)
    1 To go, to move.
    2 To begin moving.
    3 To begin.
    4 To move swiftly.
    5 To blame.
    6 To game.
    7 To cheat, to be addicted to dishonest practices. (maṃghati) To decorate, to adorn.
  • magha m. (-ghaḥ)
    1 One of the Dvīpas or divisions of the universe.
    2 A country, that of the modern Mugs, or Arrakan.
    3 A drug.
    4 Pleasure, happiness. n. (-ghaṃ) A kind of flower. f. (-ghā) The tenth lunar asterism, containing five stars figured by a house, apparently α. γ. ζ. η. and ν. Leonis: it is sometimes considered to be confined to the plural number (maghāḥ). f. (-ghā-ghī) A sort of grain.
    E. maha to worship, aff. kan, and ha changed to gha.
