• sāndra [918] mfn. (-ndraḥ-ndrā-ndraṃ) 1 Thick, coarse, gross. 2 Soft, bland. 3 Pleasing, agreeable. 4 Much, abundant. 5 Unctuous, oily. 6 Close, compact, but having interstices. 7 Stout, robust. 8 Clustering, collected. n. (-ndra) 1 A wood, a thicket. 2 A heap, a cluster. E. sa for saha with, together, adi to bind, rak aff.
