• pināka mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ)
    1 The bow of ŚIVA.
    2 A trident or threepronged spear.
    3 The trident of ŚIVA.
    4 A shower of dust. f. (-kī) A musical instrument, a sort of viol.
    E. pā to preserve, (the world,) Uṇādi aff. āka, the radical vowel changed to i, and num augment. [536]
