• parvvan [518] n. (-rvva) 1 A knot, a joint in a cane or body, &c. 2 A name given to certain days in the lunar month, as the full and change of the moon, and the 8th and 14th of each half month. 3 Particular periods of the year, as the equinox, solstice, &c. 4 The moment of the sun's entering a new sign. 5 A festival, a holy day. 6 Opportunity, occasion. 7 A chapter, a book, the division of a work. 8 A moment, an instant. E. pṝ to fill or please, Uṇādi aff. vanin.
