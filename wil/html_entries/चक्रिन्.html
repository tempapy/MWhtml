• cakrin mfn. (-krī-kriṇī-kri)
    1 Having or holding a discus, &c.
    2 Wheeled, having a wheel.
    3 Circular. m. (-krī)
    1 A name of VIṢṆU.
    2 A potter.
    3 The ruddy goose.
    4 A snake.
    5 An informer.
    6 A tumbler, one who exhibits tricks with a discus or a wheel.
    7 An oil grinder.
    8 An emperor, a Cakravartī: see cakravarttin.
    9 One who rides in a carriage.
    10 A crow.
    11 An ass.
    12 Cassia, (the tree.)
    E. cakra a wheel, &c. and ini poss. aff.
