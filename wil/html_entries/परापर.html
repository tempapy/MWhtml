• parāpara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Best and worst, prior and posterior, in front and behind, &c. n. (-raṃ) (In Logic,) Community of property intermediate between the greatest and smallest numbers: species, as being between genus and individual. m. (-raḥ) A Guru of an intermediate class, a term applied in the Tantras to the goddess DURGĀ.
    E. para, and apara another. [506]
