• bhāvita [619] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Occupied with, pervaded by, having as an inherent or pervading property. 2 Animated, inspired. 3 Imagined, conceived. 4 Perfumed, scented. 5 Mixed, (with any thing as an ingredient, as asafoetida or other fragrant substances in curry, &c.) 6 Obtained, got. 7 Promised, agreed, acknowledged. 8 Steeped, infused, digested, &c. 9 (In arithmetic,) Supposed, as the product of unknown quantities. E. bhū to be, passive or causal v., aff. kta.
