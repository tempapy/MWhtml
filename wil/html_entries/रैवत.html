• raivata m. (-taḥ)
    1 A mountain, the eastern part of the Vindhya range, or that part in which the Revā rises.
    2 A tree, (Cassia fistula.)
    3 A name of ŚIVA.
    4 A Daitya or demon.
    5 The fifth MANU of the present Kalpa.
    E. revā the Revā river, aff. añ, and ta added.
