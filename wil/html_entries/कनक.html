• kanaka [186] n. (-kaṃ) Gold. m. (-kaḥ) 1 The name of a tree which bears red flowers, (Butea frondosa:) see palāśa. 2 Thorn apple, (Datura metel, &c) 3 Another plant, (Mesua ferrea:) see nāgakeśara. 4 Mountain ebony, (Bauhinia variegata, &c.) see kāñcanāla. 5 A black sort of agallochum. 6 A shrub yielding a yellow fragrant flower, (Michelia champaca.) E. kana to shine, aka Uṇādi aff.
