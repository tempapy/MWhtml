• durllabha mfn. (-bhaḥ-bhā-bhaṃ)
    1 Difficult of attainment.
    2 Excellent, eminent.
    3 Dear, beloved. m. (-bhaḥ) A plant, (a sort of Hedysarum:) see durālabhā. f. (-bhā) A prickly nightshade.
    E. dur difficult, labha to gain, affix khal, but without any nasal. [417]
