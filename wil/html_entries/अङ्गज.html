• aṅgaja (-jaḥ) mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ).
    1 Produced or born of the body. n. (-jaṃ)
    1 Blood.
    2 Love, desire.
    3 The hair of the head.
    4 Sickness, disease.
    5 A son. f. (-jā) A daughter.
    E. aṅga the body, and ja what is born; also similar compounds, as aṅgajāta, aṅgajanma, &c.
