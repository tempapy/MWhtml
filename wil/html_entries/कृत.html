• kṛta (ī,) kṛtī r. 6th cl. (kṛntati) To cut, to divide. r. 7th cl. (kṛṇati) To surround, to encompass. [240]
  • kṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Done, made, performed.
    2 Injured, hurt.
    3 Fit, proper. n. (-taṃ)
    1 The first of the four ages of the world, the Satya yug.
    2 Fruit, consequence. adv.
    1 Enough, sufficient.
    2 Enough, completely finished.
    3 Enough, have done, no more.
    E. kṛ to do, to injure, &c. affix kta. [240]
