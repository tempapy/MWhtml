• vikala [758] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Confused, confounded, agitated. 2 Defective, imperfect. 3 Decayed, impaired, withered, waned. f. (-lā-lī) A woman in whom menstruation has ceased. n. (-laṃ) The 1/60th of a Kalā, the second of a degree. E. vi privative, and kalā a digit of the moon, &c.
