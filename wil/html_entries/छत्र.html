• chatra [333] n. (-traṃ) A parasol, an umbrella, the Indian catta. f. (-trā) 1 A kind of fennel, (Anethum sowa.) 2 A pungent seed, coriander. 3 A mushroom. 4 Anise. E. chada to cover, and ṣṭran Uṇādi affix; hence the word and its derivatives are also written with a double ta, chattra, &c.
