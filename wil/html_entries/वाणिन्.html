• vāṇin mfn. (-ṇī-ṇinī-ṇi)
    1 Having an arrow.
    2 Speaking. f. (-nī)
    1 A sharp or clever woman, an intriguing woman.
    2 An actress, a dancer, a Nāc girl.
    3 A furious woman, one literally or figuratively intoxicated.
    E. vāṇa an arrow, or vāṇi speech, ini and ṅīp affs.
