• mahāmuni [650] m. (-niḥ) 1 A name of AGASTYA. 2 A name of Buddha, or more properly, of any one of the sacred persons called by that name. 3 The epithet of a warlike saint, celebrated in the Mahābhārata. 4 Time. 5 A name of VYĀSA. n. (-ni) Coriander. E. mahā great, and muni saint.
