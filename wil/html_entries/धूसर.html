• dhūsara mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) Grey, of that colour. m. (-raḥ)
    1 Grey, (the colour.)
    2 A jackass.
    3 An oilman.
    4 A camel.
    5 A pigeon.
    6 Any thing of a grey tint. f. (-rī) A female Kinnara or chorister of heaven.
    E. dhū to agitate, (the mind,) sara Uṇādi aff. [447]
