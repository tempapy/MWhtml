• śrotriya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Modest, docile, well-behaved. m. (-yaḥ)
    1 A Brahman versed in the study of the Vedas.
    2 A Brahman following a particular branch or school of the Vedas.
    E. śrotra considered as substituted for chandas the metre of the Vedas, and khan aff.; or śrotra the ear, said also to mean the Vedas, and gha aff. [865]
