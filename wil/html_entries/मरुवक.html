• maruvaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A large thorny shrub, (Vangueria spinosa.)
    2 A species of basil (Ocymum), described as having small leaves and red flowers.
    3 A variety of the citron or lime, said to be a thorny plant, and to bear a small fruit.
    4 A flower, (Artemisia vulgaris.)
    5 A tiger.
    6 The ascending node.
    7 A crane.
    E. maru a sandy or dry soil, vā to grow or go, aff. ka, and kan added; it sometimes occurs, marūvaka.
