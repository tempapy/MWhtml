• liṅgin mfn. (-ṅgī-ṅginī-ṅgi)
    1 Having marks, &c.
    2 Indicated, characterized. m. (-ṅgī)
    1 An elephant.
    2 A hypocrite, a pretended devotee.
    3 An ascetic.
    4 A religious student.
    5 A worshipper of ŚIVA in the phallic type.
    E. liṅga a mark, and ini aff. [721]
