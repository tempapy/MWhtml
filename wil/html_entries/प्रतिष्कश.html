• pratiṣkaśa [567] m. (-śaḥ) 1 A spy, a secret emissary, a Hurkaru. 2 A companion. 3 A leader, a preceder. 4 A whip, a leather thong. E. prati before kaśa to hurt or kill, ac and suṭ augment; if it is used attributively to imply the application of a whip, the augment is not inserted, as pratikaśāśvaḥ a horse that is whipped on.
