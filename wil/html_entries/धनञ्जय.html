• dhanañjaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 The deity of fire.
    2 A name of ARJUNA.
    3 One of the Nāgas or infernal serpents.
    4 One of the five vital airs, that which is supposed to fatten.
    5 A tree, (Pentaptera arjuna.)
    E. dhana wealth, ji to conquer or possess, affix khañ. [436]
