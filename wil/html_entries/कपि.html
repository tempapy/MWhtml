• kapi m. (-piḥ)
    1 An ape or monkey.
    2 The monkey HANUMĀN.
    3 A title of VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA.
    4 Incense, storax or impure benzoin.
    5 Emblic myrobalan, (Phyllanthus emblica.)
    6 A kind of Bonduc or Bonducella.
    E. kapi to tremble, i Uṇādi affix, the nasal rejected. [188]
