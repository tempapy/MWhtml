• anu [031] ind. A preposition and prefix, implying 1 After. 2 Like, in same manner. 3 Under, inferior to. 4 Along, lengthwise. 5 With, together with. 6 In part of. 7 Severally, each, by each. 8 Near to. 9 In regard to. 10 To, out to, (calling.) 11 Orderly, methodical. As anukarttuṃ to act like; anugantuṃ to follow; anukamituṃ to proceed in order; anuśayituṃ to repent, &c. also, anurūpaṃ according to the form; anuviṣṇu according to VIṢṆU; anugaṃ gaṃ upon or along the Ganges. See also the following words. E. an to live, and ja aff.
