• tyāgin mfn. (-gī-ginī-gi) Who or what leaves, gives, excepts, &c. m. (-gī) A giver, a donor.
    2 A hero.
    3 An abandoner, a deserter, but chiefly applied to the religious ascetic, or him who abandons terrestrial objects, thoughts, passions, &c.
    E. tyāga as above, ini aff. [387]
