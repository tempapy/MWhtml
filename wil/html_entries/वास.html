• vāsa r. 10th cl. (vāsayati) To introduce another quality, but especially fragrant odors, to perfume, to incense, to fumigate, &c. (ṛ) vāsṛ r. 4th cl. (vāsyate) To sound, especially as a bird.
  • vāsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 A house, a habitation.
    2 Site, situation, abode, or place of staying or abiding.
    3 Cloth, clothes.
    4 Perfuming. mf. (-saḥ-sā) A plant, (Justicia ganderussa.)
    E. vasa to dwell, aff. ghañ, or vāsa to fumigate, aff. ac.
