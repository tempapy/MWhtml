• saraka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Going, moving, proceeding. mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ)
    1 Spirituous liquor, especially distilled from sugar, rum.
    2 Drinking spirits.
    3 A drinking vessel.
    4 Distribution of liquor.
    5 A continuous line of road. n. (-kaṃ)
    1 A lake, a pool.
    2 Heaven, sky.
    E. sṛ to go, Uṇādi. aff. vun.
