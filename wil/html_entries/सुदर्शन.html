• sudarśana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-nī-naṃ) Handsome, good looking. m. (-naḥ)
    1 The discus of KṚṢṆA.
    2 Mount Meru.
    3 The rose-apple, (Eugenia jambu.)
    4 The father of the eighteenth Jaina pontiff of the present era.
    5 One of the nine Śuklabalas or BALARĀMAS of the Jainas.
    6 A vulture. nf. (-naṃ-nī) The city of INDRA. f. (-nā)
    1 A drug.
    2 Order, command.
    3 A plant, (Menispermum glabrum.)
    4 A woman, a handsome woman.
    E. su good, darśana sight or appearance. [930]
