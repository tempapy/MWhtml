• yuga(i)yugi r. 1st cl (yuṃgati) To except, to abandon. [686]
  • yuga n. (-gaṃ)
    1 A pair, a couple, a brace.
    2 An age, as the Kṛta, Tretā, Dvāpara, and Kali.
    3 A lustre, or period of five years. m. (-gaḥ)
    1 A yoke.
    2 A measure of four cubits.
    3 A drug, commonly Ṛddhi.
    E. yuja to join, aff. ac, and the final changed. [686]
