• yuga(i)yugi [686] r. 1st cl (yuṃgati) To except, to abandon.
  • yuga [686] n. (-gaṃ) 1 A pair, a couple, a brace. 2 An age, as the Kṛta, Tretā, Dvāpara, and Kali. 3 A lustre, or period of five years. m. (-gaḥ) 1 A yoke. 2 A measure of four cubits. 3 A drug, commonly Ṛddhi. E. yuja to join, aff. ac, and the final changed.
