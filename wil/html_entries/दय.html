• daya (ñi) tridaya r. 1st cl. (dayate)
    1 To give.
    2 To move.
    3 To take.
    4 To protect.
    5 To hurt or kill.
  • daya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Compassionate, tender-hearted. mf. (-yaḥ-yā) Tenderness, compassion, clemency.
    E. daya to preserve, affix aṅ, fem. affix ṭāp.
