• guñja m. (-ñjaḥ) A cluster of blossoms, a nosegay. f. (-ñjā)
    1 A small shrub, (Abrus precatorius,) bearing a red and black berry, which forms the smallest of the jeweller's weights.
    2 The berry which averages about 1(5/16) grains troy, or the artificial weight called by this name, weighing about 2(3/16) grains: see raktikā, &c.
    3 A kettle drum.
    4 A low murmuring sound.
    5 A tavern.
    6 Reflection, meditation.
    E. guji to sound, affix ka; where the bees buzz. [290]
