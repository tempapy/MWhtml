• ama r. 1st cl. (amati)
    1 To go, to go to or towards.
    2 To serve or honour.
    3 To sound. 10th cl. (amayati) To afflict with sickness or pain from disease.
  • ama ind. (-am)
    1 Quickly.
    2 Little. mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Unripe, (as fruit, &c.) m. (-maḥ) Sickness.
    E. ama to go or to be sick. ac aff.
