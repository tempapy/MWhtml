• udvarttana [150] n. (-naṃ) 1 Going up, rising, ascending. 2 Grinding, pounding. 3 Rubbing or kneading the body. 4 Rubbing and cleaning it with fragrant unguents. 5 Unguents used for that purpose, or to relieve pains in the limbs, &c. 6 Rising, (figuratively) prosperity, elevation. 7 Misbehaving. 8 Springing up of plants or grain, &c. 9 Drawing out metal, laminating, wire drawing, &c. E. ud up, and varttana abiding, &c.
