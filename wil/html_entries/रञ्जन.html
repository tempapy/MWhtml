• rañjana mn. (-naḥ-naṃ)
    1 Colouring, dyeing, painting, &c.
    2 Affecting the heart, exciting passion, &c. n. (-naṃ) Red Sandal or Sappan wood. f. (-nī)
    1 The indigo plant, (Indigofera tinctoria.)
    2 Bengal madder.
    3 Another plant, commonly Śuṇḍārocanī, (a species of Crinum?)
    4 A flower, (Nyctanthes tristis.)
    E. rañja to colour, aff. yuc. [694]
