• śabda [829] r. 10th cl. (śabdayati) To sound, to make or utter sound; with the prefixes pra, prati, or vi, as well as uncompounded, this root means also:. 1 To promise. 2 To make clear or manifest, to explain. 3 To speak.
  • śabda [829] m. (-bdaḥ) 1 Sound in general. 2 A sound, a word. 4 (In grammar,) A declinable word, as noun, pronoun, &c. E. śabda to sound, aff. ac.
