• śabda r. 10th cl. (śabdayati) To sound, to make or utter sound; with the prefixes pra, prati, or vi, as well as uncompounded, this root means also:.
    1 To promise.
    2 To make clear or manifest, to explain.
    3 To speak.
  • śabda m. (-bdaḥ)
    1 Sound in general.
    2 A sound, a word.
    4 (In grammar,) A declinable word, as noun, pronoun, &c.
    E. śabda to sound, aff. ac.
