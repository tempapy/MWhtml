• śṛṅga n. (-ṅgaṃ)
    1 A horn.
    2 A mark, a sign.
    3 The top of a mountain.
    4 Sovereignty, mastership.
    6 Dignity, elevation.
    6 A fountain, an artificial one or jet d'eau.
    7 A lotus.
    8 Very sharp.
    9 Minutely fine. m. (-ṅgaḥ)
    1 A medicinal root, commonly Jīvaka.
    2 A Muni so named. f. (-ṅgī)
    1 Gold.
    2 A fish, (Silurus Singio, HAM.)
    3 A plant, (Betula.)
    4 Ṛṣabha, a medicinal root, shaped like a bull's horn.
    5 Another plant, a kind of Rhus, with an excrescence or gall in the bark, compared to a crab's horn.
    E. śṝ to injure, Uṇādi aff. gan, and the vowel made short. [855]
