• capala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Trembling, tremulous, shaking.
    2 Wavering, unsteady.
    3 Inconsiderately criminal.
    4 Swift, expeditious; (also in this sense, or swiftly, adv. n. (-laṃ)
    5 Momentary, instantaneous.
    6 Agitated violently, overcome with alarm, &c. m. (-laḥ)
    1 Quicksilver.
    2 A kind of stone.
    3 A fish.
    4 A thief. f. (-lā)
    1 The goddess LAKṢMĪ or fortune.
    2 Lightning.
    3 A whore.
    4 Long pepper.
    5 The tongue.
    6 Spirituous liquor.
    7 A species of the Aryā metre.
    E. capa to go, affix alac, or cupa to move, Uṇādi affix kala and the radical vowel changed.
