• gandhaphala m. (-laḥ)
    1 Elephant or wood apple: see kapittha.
    2 The Bel, (Ægle crataeva.) f. (-lā or -lī)
    1 A plant bearing a fragrant seed: see priyaṅgu.
    2 The gum olibanum tree. f. (-lī) The blossom of the Champaca: see campaka.
    E. gandha smell, and phala a fruit.
