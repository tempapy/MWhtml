• saraṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Going, moving, who or what goes or moves. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Oxidized iron, either in the state of rust or filings.
    2 Going, proceeding. f. (-ṇā or ṇī)
    1 A plant, (Pæderia fetida.)
    2 A sort of Teori.
    E. sṛ to go, aff. yuc, or lyuṭ fem. aff. ṭāp or ṅīṣ; also śaraṇa, &c. [906]
