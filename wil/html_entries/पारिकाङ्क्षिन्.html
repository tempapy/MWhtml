• pārikāṅkṣin m. (-ṅkṣī)
    1 An ascetick, one who devotes his days to devout contemplation.
    2 The religious mendicant.
    E. pari implying secession, (abandoning worldly wishes,) kāṅkṣi to desire, and ṇini aff.; or pāri derived from para, supreme felicity, and kāṃkṣin who desires. [529]
