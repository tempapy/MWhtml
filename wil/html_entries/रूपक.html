• rūpaka [709] n. (-kaṃ) 1 A drama, a dramatic poem. 2 Shape, form, figure. 3 A figure of rhetoric in general. 4 A particular class of rhetorical or poetical figures, including various modes of poetical description, or the illustration and exhibition of visible things and persons. 5 Identification of the objects of a comparison; their being not only like, but the very same. 6 Symptom of disease. f. (-pikā) Swallow wort. E. rūpa form, figure, aff. kan.
