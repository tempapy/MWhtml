• marāla mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Soft, mild, bland. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A sort of goose with red legs and bill, or perhaps the Flamingo.
    2 Lampblack, used as a collyrium.
    3 A duck.
    4 A horse.
    5 A cloud.
    6 A grove or garden of pomegranate trees.
    7 A rogue, a scoundrel.
    E. mṛ to die, aff. ālac.
