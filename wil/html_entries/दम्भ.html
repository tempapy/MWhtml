• dambha r. 5th cl. (u) dambhu (dabhnoti)
    1 To act deceitfully, to cheat, to impose upon, to trick or deceive.
    2 To split or divide. r. 10th cl. (dambhayati) To collect, to gather, to arrange or string. [400]
  • dambha m. (-mbhaḥ)
    1 Deceit, fraud, cheating.
    2 Hypocrisy.
    3 Sin, wickedness.
    4 Arrogance, pride.
    E. dambha to deceive, &c. affix ghañ. [400]
