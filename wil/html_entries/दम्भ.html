• dambha [400] r. 5th cl. (u) dambhu (dabhnoti) 1 To act deceitfully, to cheat, to impose upon, to trick or deceive. 2 To split or divide. r. 10th cl. (dambhayati) To collect, to gather, to arrange or string.
  • dambha [400] m. (-mbhaḥ) 1 Deceit, fraud, cheating. 2 Hypocrisy. 3 Sin, wickedness. 4 Arrogance, pride. E. dambha to deceive, &c. affix ghañ.
