• candrabhāgā f. (-gā-gī) The name of a river, the Chinab, one of the five streams of the Punjab.
    E. candrabhāga the name of a mountain, part of the Himāla range, where the river is said to have its source, affix of derivation aṇ, hence also it may be read cāndrabhāgā-gī.
