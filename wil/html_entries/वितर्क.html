• vitarka m. (-rkaḥ)
    1 Reasoning, discussion.
    2 Doubt, deliberation.
    3 A teacher, an instructor in divine knowledge.
    4 Consideration of probabilities, mental anticipation of alternatives, conjecture.
    E. vi implying discrimination, &c., tarka to reason or doubt, aff. ac. [766]
