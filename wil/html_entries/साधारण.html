• sādhāraṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇī-ṇaṃ)
    1 Like, similar.
    2 Common, belonging to all or many.
    3 Generic. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 A common rule or precept, one applicable to many persons or things.
    2 A specific or generic character, one common to all the individuals of a species, or all the species of a genus, &c. f. (-ṇī) A small bolt or bar.
    E. sa for saha with, dhāraṇa a having, aff. ac.
