• rāgacūrṇa m. (-rṇaḥ)
    1 A name of KĀMA.
    2 A tree yielding an astringent resin or extract, the wood of which is used in dying, (Mimosa catechu.)
    3 Red lead.
    4 The red powder thrown over one another by the Hindus, at the vernal festival named Holī.
    E. rāga passion or colour, and carṇa powder. [699]
