• ghanatola m. (-laḥ) The Cātaka, (Cuculus melanoleucos.)
    E. ghana a cloud, and tula to be equal, affix ghañ; who soars to the clouds in the rainy season, which, according to the fable, is the only time when he gets water to drink; some books have ghanatāla from tāla tune, or sound; whose music is (from) the clouds. [308]
