• kramuka [256] m. (-kaḥ) 1 The areca or betel nut, (Areca faufel or catechu.) 2 A kind of tree, a red sort of Lodh: see lāghra. 3 A kind of fragrant grass: see bhadramustaka. 4 The mulberry tree, (Morus Indica.) 5 The fruit of the cotton tree. f. (-kī) The areca tree. E. krama to go, uka aff.
