• carpaṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 The open palm of the hand with fingers extended.
    2 A plant, (Mollugo pentaphylla.)
    3 A quantity of bubbles or specks, (sphāravipula.) f. (-ṭī) A thin cake or biscuit of flour.
    E. carpa a Sautra root, to be low or flat, affix aṭac; also capaṭa and capeṭa.
