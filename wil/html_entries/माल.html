• māla [659] n. (-laṃ) A field. m. (-laḥ) 1 A man. 2 A mountain, a barbarian, one of a particular tribe. 3 A country, lying west and south-west of Bengal; Ramghur, &c., or according to WILFORD, Mālbhūm in Midnapur. 4 A name of VIṢṆU. f. (-lā) 1 A line, a row. 2 A garland, a string or wreath of flowers. 3 A chaplet of flowers. 4 A string of beads, a rosary. In rhetorical or poetical description, a series or string of epithets, similes, &c. E. mā fortune, lā to get or be, aff. aṅ and ṭāp; or mā to measure, Uṇādi aff. ra, and ra changed to la.
