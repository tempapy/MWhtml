• carācara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ)
    1 Moveable, locomotive.
    2 Shaking, trembling, unsteady.
    3 Wished, desired.
    4 Moveable and immoveable. n. (-raṃ)
    1 The world.
    2 Sky, atmosphere.
    3 Heaven, paradise.
    4 The aggregate of all things, whether inanimate or animate.
    E. cara to go, affix aca, and the derivative reiterated, āṅ inserted; or cara what goes, and acara what does not go. [319]
