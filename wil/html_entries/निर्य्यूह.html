• niryyūha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A chaplet, a crest, an ornament for the head.
    2 Extracted juice, or a decoction, &c.
    3 A door, a gate.
    4 A pin or bracket projecting from a wall to hang or place any thing upon.
    5 Wood placed in a wall for doves to build upon.
    E. nir out, out from, yuha to go, affix ka.
