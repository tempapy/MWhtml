• vṛṣākapi [803] m. (-piḥ) 1 KṚṢṆA or VIṢṆU. 2 ŚIVA. 3 AGNI, or fire. E. vṛṣa virtue, a not, kapi to shake or agitate, i aff., and the radical augment rejected; or vṛṣā INDRA, akapi untrembling, as before; or vṛṣa virtue, or vṛṣā INDRA, and kapi an ape, being the ape as it were of the greater gods: other etymologies occur.
