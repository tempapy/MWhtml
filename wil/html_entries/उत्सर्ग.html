• utsarga [144] m. (-rgaḥ) 1 Abandoning, quitting. 2 Resigning, retiring from. 3 Giving, donation. 4 Any precept or rule. 5 Presentation of any thing promised to a god or Brahman, with suitable ceremonies. 6 A particular ceremony preparatory to a course of the Vedas. 7 Dejection, excretion, voiding by stool, &c. E. ut before sṛja to quit, to leave, ghañ aff.
