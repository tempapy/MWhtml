• viriñci m. (-ñciḥ)
    1 BRAHMĀ.
    2 VIṢṆU.
    3 ŚIVA.
    E. vi implying various kinds, (of beings,) raca to make or create, in aff., the radical vowel changed to i, and num augment; also with ac aff. viriñca, and with yuc, viriñcana; some authorities omit the change of the radical vowel, and write virañca and virañci, but these seem to be less accurate.
