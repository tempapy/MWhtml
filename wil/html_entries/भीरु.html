• bhīru mfn. (-ruḥ-ruḥ or -rūḥ-ru) Timid, fearful. f. (-ruḥ-rūḥ)
    1 A timid woman.
    2 A plant, (Asparagus racemosus.)
    3 A sort of prickly night-shade.
    4 A shakal.
    5 A centipede.
    6 A tiger.
    7 A goat.
    E. bhī to fear, Uṇādi aff. kru; also ra being changed to u, bhīlu.
