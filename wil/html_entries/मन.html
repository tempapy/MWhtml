• mana r. 4th cl. (manyate) (u,) manu r. 8th cl. (manute)
    1 To know, to understand.
    2 To regard, to mind.
    3 To think, to conceive. r. 10th cl. (mānayate)
    1 To be dull or insensible.
    2 To be proud.
    3 To oppose, to stop. r. 1st and 10th cls. (manati mānayati) To honour, to respect, to worship.
    With anu prefixed, To assent to, to agree.
    With abhi, To desire.
    With ava, To disrespect.
    With sama, To concur, to agree.
  • mana n. (-naṃ) Indian spikenard.
    E. mana to respect, aff. ac.
