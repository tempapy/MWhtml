  • cha mfn. (chaḥ-chā-chaṃ)
    1 Pure, clean.
    2 Trembling, tremulous, unsteady. m. (chaḥ)
    1 Cutting, dividing.
    2 A part, a fragment. f. (-chā)
    1 Covering, concealing.
    2 An infant, a child, any young animal.
    E. cho to cut, or chada to cover, &c. affix ḍa.
