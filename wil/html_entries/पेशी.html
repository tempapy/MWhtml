• peśī f. (-śī)
    1 The egg of a bird.
    2 A blown bud.
    3 Spikenard, (Valeriana jatamansi.)
    4 A ball of flesh or meat.
    5 A sheath, a scabbard.
    6 A muscle.
    7 The name of a river.
    8 The name of a female imp or demon.
    E. piśa to be a component part, aff. in and ṅīp added; also peśi.
