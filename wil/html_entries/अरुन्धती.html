• arundhatī [067] f. (-tī) The daughter of KĀRDDAMA and wife of VASIṢṬHA, one of the seven Ṛṣis; she is also one of the Pleiades. E. a neg. rudha to confine, tan and ṅīp affixes; who does not confine or hinder good works. ARUNDHATĪ is considered as a pattern of conjugal excellence, and is invoked at the marriage ceremony by the bridegroom.
