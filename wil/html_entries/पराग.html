• parāga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 The pollen or farina of a flower.
    2 Dust.
    3 Fragrant powder used after bathing.
    4 Sandal.
    5 The name of a mountain.
    6 Fame, celebrity.
    7 An eclipse.
    8 Following one's own inclinations.
    E. parā implying supremacy, &c. and ga who goes.
