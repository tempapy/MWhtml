• sambandhin mfn. (-ndhī-ndhinī-ndhi)
    1 Possessing good qualities, learned, amiable, liberal, &c.
    2 Having adjoined or connected with, inherent.
    3 Related to, a relation, a connexion.
    4 Connected with, belonging or relating to.
    E. sambandha connexion, and ini aff. [904]
