• giri mfn. (-riḥ-riḥ-ri) Venerable, respectable, worshipful. m. (-riḥ)
    1 A mountain, a hill.
    2 A wooden ball with which children play.
    3 A disease of the eyes. f. (-riḥ)
    1 Swallowing.
    2 A small rat, a mouse.
    3 A title given to one order of the Dāsnāmī Gosains.
    E. gṝ to swallow, &c. i Uṇādi aff.
