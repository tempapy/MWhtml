• kauśika [255] (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) 1 Silk, silken. 2 Of the family of Kuśika, &c. m. (-kaḥ) 1 A name of INDRA. 2 A fragrant substance, (Bdellium.) 3 An owl. 4 A snake-catcher. 5 An ichneumon, (Viverra ichneumon.) 6 A title of VIŚVAMITRA. 7 One skilled in dictionaries. 8 A dictionary compiler. 9 Love, the passion. 10 The marrow. f. (-kī) 1 A river in Behar, the Kosi or Koosa. 2 A name of the goddess DURGA. n. (-kaṃ) Silk. E. kośa a sheathe, &c. ṭhak affix, and ṅīp for the feminine; also kauṣika, and kauṣikī.
