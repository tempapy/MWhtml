• udara n. (-raṃ)
    1 The belly.
    2 War.
    3 Any morbid abdominal affection, as liver, spleen, &c. f. (-rī) Enlargement of the abdomen from dropsy or flatulency.
    E. ud up, ṛ to go, ap affix; what goes up or swells with food, slaughter, &c. or ud prefixed to dṛ to burst, and ac or al Uṇādi aff. [146]
