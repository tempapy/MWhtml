• romaharṣaṇa [712] n. (-ṇaṃ) Horripilation, erection or rigidity of the hair of the body, conceived to be occasioned by and to express exquisite delight. m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 SŪTA, the pupil of VYĀSA, and supposed narrator of the events recorded in the Purāṇas. 2 Beleric myrobalan. E. roma the hair, and harṣṇa delighting.
