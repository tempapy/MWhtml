• trapa (ū, ṣa) trapūṣ r. 1st cl. (trapati) To be modest, to be ashamed.
  • trapa mf. (-paḥ-pā) Shame, modesty, bashfulness. f. (-pā)
    1 An unchaste woman, (a shame to her family.)
    2 Family, race.
    3 Fame, celebrity.
    E. trapa to be modest, affix aṅ, fem. affix ṭāp.
