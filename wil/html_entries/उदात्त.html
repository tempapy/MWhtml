• udātta [147] mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ) 1 Generous, gentle and bountiful. 2 Giving, a donor. 3 Great, illustrious. 4 Dear, beloved. 5 Accented. m. (-ttaḥ) 1 The acute accent, a high or sharp tone. 2 Gift, donation. 3 A musical instrument, a large drum. 4 An ornament, (in rhetoric.) E. uda high, up, and ātta pronounced, sounded; or from dā, to give or have, with āṅ prefixed, affix ktaḥ see anudātta.
