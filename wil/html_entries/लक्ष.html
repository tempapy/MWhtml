• lakṣa r. 10th cl. (lakṣayati-te)
    1 To see, to look at, to notice or inspect.
    2 To mark, to make any mark on.
    3 To define, to discriminate.
    With upa prefixed, To remark.
    With sam, To know, to understand. [713]
  • lakṣa nf. (-kṣaṃ-kṣā) A Lac, one hundred thousand. n. (-kṣaṃ)
    1 Fraud, disguise.
    2 A mark or butt.
    E. lakṣa to mark, or see, aff. ghañ. [713]
