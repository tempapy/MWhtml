• viṣayāyin [791] m. (-yī) 1 A king. 2 An organ of sense. 3 KĀMADEVA. 4 An epicurean, a sensualist, one heedful of objects of sense or attentive to worldly objects. 5 A materialist, one who disbelieves the existence of that which is not cognizable by the senses. E. viṣaya an object of sense, aya to go, to follow, and ini aff.
