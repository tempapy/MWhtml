• carmmakāra m. (-raḥ) The shoe-maker, a currier or worker in leather, the offspring of a Caṇḍāla woman by a man of the fisherman caste, or of a Vaideha female by a Niṣada. f. (-rī)
    1 The plant Carmaghās: see the preceding.
    2 The wife of a Carmakāra.
    E. carmma skin or leather, and kāra who does or makes.
