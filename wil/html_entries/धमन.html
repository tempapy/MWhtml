• dhamana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ)
    1 One who blows a bellows, a trumpet, &c.
    2 Cruel. m. (-naḥ)
    1 A reed, (Arundo tibialis or karka.)
    2 Blowing, (a wind instrument.) f. (-niḥ or -nī)
    1 Any tubular vessel of the body, as a vein, a nerve, &c.
    2 The neck.
    3 Turmerick.
    4 A sort of perfume; also haṭṭavilāsinī.
    E. dhama from dhmā, to blow, &c. affix yuc, fem. affix in or ṅīṣ or dhmā as before, Uṇādi affix ani, and ṅīp optionally added.
