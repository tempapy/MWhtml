• viplava [776] m. (-vaḥ) 1 Tumult, confusion, trouble. 2 Evil, calamity. 3 Rapine, extortion. 4 Sin, wickedness. 5 Predatory or irregular warfare. 6 Affray, scuffle. 7 Terrifying an enemy by shouts and gestures. 8 Confusion, perplexity, (of thought or speech.) E. vi before plu to go, aff. ap.
