• rica [706] r. 1st and 10 cls. (recati-recayati) To join, to unite. 2 To divide, to scatter or separate. 3 To purge. (auir) au ricira r. 7th cl. (riṇakti-riṃkte) To purge, to evacuate fæces frequently.
    With ati prefixed, To exceed, to be more than enough.
    With vi, To purge.
