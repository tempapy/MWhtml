• raktapitta [691] m. (-ttaḥ) Plethora, spontaneous hæmorrhages from the mouth, nose, rectum and cuticle, accompanied with fever and headache, vomiting, purging, &c. E. rakta red, and pitta bile, the bile being of that colour; or rakta blood, and pitta bile, it being supposed to be an affection of both fluids or humors.
