• mārga [658] r. 1st and 10th cls. (mārgati mārgayati) 1 To make or prepare. 2 To go. 3 To prepare the way, to make a road. 4 To feather an arrow. (mārgayati-te) To seek.
  • mārga [658] m. (-rgaḥ) 1 A road, a path or way. 2 Search, seeking, inquiry. 3 The anus. 4 Musk. 5 The month in which the moon is full in the asterism Mṛgaśīrṣa, (November-December.) 6 The constellation Mṛgaśīrṣa. 7 (In geometry,) A section. E. mṛga to inquire, aff. ghañ, or mṛga a deer, aff. aṇ.
