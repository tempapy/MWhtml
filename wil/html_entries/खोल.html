• khola (ṛ) khoḷ r. 1st cl. (kholati) To be lame, to limp, to be prevented from moving. khoḍa, khora, &c. are conceived by some authorities to be mere variations in the form of this root. [275]
  • khola mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Lame.
    E. khola to be lame, affix ac; also khora, &c. [275]
