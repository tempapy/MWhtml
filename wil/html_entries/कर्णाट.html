• karṇāṭa [196] m. (-ṭaḥ) A country, Karṇāṭa, whence the modern Karnatic: the name however was anciently applied to the central districts of the peninsula, including Mysūr. f. (-ṭī) 1 One of the Rāgiṇīs or female personifications of the musical modes, the bride of the Rāga MĀLAVA. 2 A plant. E. karṇa the ear, a prince, &c. and aṭa to go, affix ac.
