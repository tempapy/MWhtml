• rasāyana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Alchymy, chemistry.
    2 The employment of mercury in medicine.
    3 A medicine preventing old age, and prolonging life, the Elixir Vitœ of the alchymists.
    4 Poison.
    5 Butter-milk. m. (-naḥ)
    1 GARUḌA.
    2 An anthelmintic drug commonly Vireng, (Embelia ribes?)
    3 An alchymist. f. (-nī) A vessel conveying nutrition, a lacteal, an absorbent.
    E. rasa juice, quicksilver, &c., and ayana a road, a going, &c. [698]
