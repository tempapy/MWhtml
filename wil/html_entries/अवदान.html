• avadāna n. (-naṃ)
    1 Approved occupation.
    2 An act accomplished.
    3 Achievement, a great or glorious act.
    4 Breaking, dividing.
    5 The root of a fragrant grass; see avadāha.
    6 An oblation.
    E. ava, dai to cleanse, to purify, or dā to give, and lyaṭ aff. [078]
