• rahasya mfn. (-syaḥ-syā-syaṃ) Private, secret, either fit to be concealed or occurring in secret. n. (-syaṃ) A secret, a mystery, any thing hidden, recondite or mysterious. f. (-syā) The name of a river, perhaps the Sarasvatī, from its being supposed to flow in an unknown channel.
    E. rahas secret, and yat aff. [699]
