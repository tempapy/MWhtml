• veṣṭaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Surrounding, a surrounder, that which encompasses or encloses. n. (-kaṃ)
    1 A turban.
    2 Exudation, resin, gum, &c.
    3 Turpentine. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A wall, a fence.
    2 A pumpkin gourd.
    E. veṣṭa as above, with kan added, or veṣṭa to surround, aff. vun.
