• tarala [368] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Trembling, tremulous. 2 Libidinous, lecherous, wanton. 3 Luminous, splendid. 4 Hollow. 5 Liquid, liquefied. m. (-laḥ) 1 The central gem of a necklace. 2 A necklace. 3 Bottom, depth, lower or under part. f. (-lā) 1 Rice gruel. 2 Wine, vinous or spirituous liquor. 3 A bee. E. tṝ to pass, to go or move, affix kala.
