• bhaṇḍila mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 A messenger, one sent or dispatched.
    2 Fortunate, happy, prosperous, auspicious. m. (-laḥ) A tree, (Mimosa sirisha).
    E. bhaḍi to be happy, &c. Uṇādi aff. ilac; also ra being substituted for la, bhaṇḍira, or the short vowel being lengthened, bhaṇḍīla. [611]
