• ara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Swift, speedy. n. (-raṃ)
    1 The spoke or radius of a wheel.
    2 adv. Swiftly. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A Jaina division of time, the sixth of an Avasarpiṇī or Utsarpiṇī. See avasarpiṇī, &c.
    2 The eighteenth- Jaina Tīrthakara, or deified saint.
    E. ṛ to go, ap aff. [065]
