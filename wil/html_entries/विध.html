• vidha [770] r. 6th cl. (vidhate) 1 To administer, to govern, to rule or command. 2 To pierce, to perforate.
  • vidha [770] mf. (-dhaḥ-dhā) 1 Form, formula, rule. 2 Manner, kind, sort. 3 Fodder, the food of horses, elephants, &c. 4 Prosperity, thriving. 5 Hire, wages. 6 Act, action. 7 Piercing. E. vi before dhā to have, aff. ka, or aṅ, and fem. aff. ṭāp; or vidha to rule, &c., aff. ka; the masculine form, though it does occur, is not common, except in composition, when the word is used attributively, as dvividha of two sorts. mfn. (-dhaḥ-dhā-dhaṃ), &c.
