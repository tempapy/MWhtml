• kuṇapa mn. (-paḥ-pī-paṃ) Foul smelling, stinking. mn. (-paḥ-paṃ)
    1 A dead body, a corpse. m. (-paḥ)
    1 A stink, a foul smell.
    2 A spear. f. (-pī) A small bird, a kind of Maina or Sārika: see viṭsārikā.
    E. kvaṇa to sound, kapan affix, va is changed to its congener u; sound proceeding from the corpse, &c. by the escape of the vital air, &c. [227]
