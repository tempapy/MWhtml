• loṣṭa r. 1st cl. (loṣṭate) To heap, to assemble, to collect or gather into a heap. [725]
  • loṣṭa mn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭaṃ) A clod, a lump of earth. n. (-ṣṭaṃ) Rust of iron or iron filings.
    E. loṣṭa to heap, aff. ac; or lū to cut, Uṇādi aff. kta, with change of the radical vowel, and suṭ augment; also with kan added loṣṭaka. [725]
