• mūrcchana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Fainting, syncope, swooning.
    2 Mixing, meeting.
    3 A tone or semitone as placed in its scale, the seventh part of a Grāma or scale.
    4 Calcining quicksilver with sulphur, &c.
    E. murccha to faint, to become insensible, aff. lyuṭ. [667]
