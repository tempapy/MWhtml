• akarmman mfn. (-rmmā-rmmā-rmma)
    1 Idle, unoccupied, without work.
    2 Degraded, no longer performing essential rites. n. (-rmma)
    1 Absence of occupation.
    2 Loss or neglect of essential observances.
    3 Improper act, crime, offence.
    E. a neg. karmman act.
