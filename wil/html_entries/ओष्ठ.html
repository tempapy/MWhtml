• oṣṭha m. (-ṣṭhaḥ) The lip, especially the upper, &c. du. m. (oṣṭhau or oṣṭhādharau) The lips: see adhara. f. (-ṣṭhī) A creeper, bearing a red flower, to which the lip is commonly compared, (Bryonia grandis.)
    E. uṣa to burn, than Uṇādi affix, fem. affix ṅīṣ: see vimba.
