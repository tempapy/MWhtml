    2 A bull.
    3 A ray of light.
    4 The thunderbolt.
    5 The moon.
    6 The sun.

    1 The hair of the body.

    1 A cow.
    2 The eye.
    3 An arrow.
    4 A quarter, as the east, west, &c.

    6 The earth.
    7 A mother.
