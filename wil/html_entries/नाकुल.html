• nākula [458] mfn. (-laḥ-lī-laṃ) Relating or belonging to an ichneumon. f. (-lī) 1 A plant: the ichneumon plant, a vegetable supposed to furnish the mungoose with an antidote, when bitten in a conflict with a snake, (Serpent ophioxylon?) 2 The root of the Simul tree, (kukkuṭīkanda.) A sort of pepper, (Piper chavya.) E. nakula the ichneumon, affixes aṇ and ṅīṣ.
