• avanti [079] m. (-ntiḥ) 1 The name of a city, the modern Oujein. 2 The name of a river. E. ava to preserve, Uṇādi affix jhic; the derivative is irregular. Avanti, is one of the seven sacred cities of the Hindus, to die at which secures eternal happiness: the word is also written avantī and avantikā.
