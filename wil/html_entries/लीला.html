• līlā [721] f. (-lā) 1 A branch of feminine action, proceeding from love; or the imitation of a lover's manner, speech, gait, &c. by his mistress, to pass the time in his absence. 2 Play, sport, pastime in general. 3 Amorous or wanton sport. 4 A species of the Daṇḍaka metre. E. lī embrace, lā to get or give, aff. ka and ṭāp.
