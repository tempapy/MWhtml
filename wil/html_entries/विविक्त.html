• vivikta mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ)
    1 Lonely, solitary, desart.
    2 Pure, faultless.
    3 Separated, distinguished, disjoined, detached.
    4 Discriminative, judicious, a judge.
    5 Discriminated, judged.
    6 Profound, (as judgment or thought.)
    7 Intent on.
    E. vi before vica to be or make separate, aff. kta. [785]
