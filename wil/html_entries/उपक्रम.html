• upakrama [153] m. (-maḥ) 1 Deliberate commencement or undertaking; providing means, and anticipating consequences. 2 A beginning in general. 3 A particular ceremony preparatory to reading the Vedas. 4 A stratagem. 5 A means, an expedient. 6 Trying the fidelity, &c. of a counsellor or friend. 7 Valor. 8 Practice of medicine, physicking. 9 Flight, retreat. 10 Approach. E. upa over, &c. krama to go, affix ghañ.
