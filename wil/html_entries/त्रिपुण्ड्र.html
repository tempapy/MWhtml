• tripuṇḍra [390] n. (-ṇḍraṃ) Three curved horizontal marks, made across the forehead with cowdung, ashes, &c. they are worn especially by the followers of ŚIVA, or ŚAKTI, and are indispensable in proceeding to worship the former. E. tri three, triple, and puṇḍra a line; also with un added tripuṇḍraka.
