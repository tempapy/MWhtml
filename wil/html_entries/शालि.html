• śāli [840] n. (-li) 1 Rice in general, but especially in two classes; one like white rice growing in deep water, and the other a red sort, requiring only a moist soil: there are a great many varieties of this grain. 2 The civet or pole cat. E. śāḍa to float or swim, aff. ina, and ḍa changed to la.
