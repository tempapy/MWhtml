• pāñcāla mfn. (-laḥ-lī-laṃ) Belonging to, dwelling in, ruling over &c. the country of Pañcāla. m. (-laḥ)
    1 The sovereign of Pañcāla.
    2 The company or association of five trades, the carpenter, weaver, barber, washerman, and shoemaker.
    E. pañcāla the country so called, and aṇ aff. &c. [522]
