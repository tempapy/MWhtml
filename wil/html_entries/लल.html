• lala r. 1st cl. (lalati)
    1 To sport, to dally.
    2 To loll, as the tongue, &c.: see laḍa, of which it is considered as a various reading only. r. 10th cl. (lālayate)
    1 To desire, to wish for, to seek to obtain.
    2 To sport, to wanton.
    3 To lay. [717]
  • lala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Sporting, frolicking.
    2 Lolling, wagging.
    3 Wishing, desirous.
    E. laḍa or lala to sport, ac aff. [717]
