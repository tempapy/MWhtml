• hūm ind.
    1 A particle or doubt or consideration (humph, ha.)
    2 An interrogatory particle, (hey, indeed.)
    3 A particle of assent, (yes, well, so be it, amen.)
    4 An interjection, of anger.
    5 Of fear.
    6 Of reproach, contempt, (equivalent to be silent, tush, pish.)
    7 Of aversion, repulse, or dislike, &c.
    8 A magical or mystical monosyllable.
    9 An imitative sound.
    E. hveñ to call, aff. ḍūm.
