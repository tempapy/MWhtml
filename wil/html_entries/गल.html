• gala r. 1st cl. (galati)
    1 To eat.
    2 To ooze; also (ṅ) galaṅ r. 10th cl. (galayate) To drop, to ooze or distil.
    With ava prefixed, To fall, to fall down or off;
    With vi,
    1 To go, to approach.
    2 To bear or support. [285]
  • gala m. (-laḥ)
    1 The throat.
    2 The resin of the Sal tree.
    3 A reed, a large kind of the Saccharum cylindricum.
    4 A rope.
    5 A musical instrument.
    6 A kind of fish, a small kind of cyprinus, a subgenus of that class, (Cyprinus garra, HAM.)
    E. gala to eat, to drop, &c. affix ac, or gṝ to swallow, with ac, gara, and ra changed to la. [285]
