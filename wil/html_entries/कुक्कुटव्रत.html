• kukkuṭavrata n. (-taṃ) A religious observation, (worship of ŚIVA,) on the seventh of the light fortnight of Bhadra, by women, especially for the sake of obtaining offspring.
    E. kukkuṭa a fowl, and vrata religious rite; alluding to the fruitfulness of the hen. [224]
