• vibhāva m. (-vaḥ)
    1 A friend or acquaintance.
    2 One of the Bhāvas or classes of properties into which the objects of poetical composition are divided; the causative or exciting property, as dress, perfumes, &c. of amorous desire; extravagant gesture of mirth; distress or pain of tenderness; arms and tumult of wrath or heroism, &c.
    E. vi various, and bhāva property, disposition.
