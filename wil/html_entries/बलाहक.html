• balāhaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A mountain.
    2 A cloud.
    3 One of the Nāgas or infernal serpents.
    4 The name of a demon.
    E. bala strength, a the negative prefix to hā to abandon, aff.
    E. kvun strength, this word is differently derived also, and preferably written valāhaka, q. v. [600]
