• pūrtta mfn. (-rttaḥ-rttā-rttaṃ)
    1 Filled, full, complete.
    2 Covered, concealed. n. (-rttaṃ)
    1 An act of pious liberality, as digging a well, planting a grove, building a temple, &c.
    2 Nourishing, cherishing.
    E. pṝ to cherish or fill, aff. kta, and the vowel changed.
