• grāmayājaka m. (-kaḥ) The village priest, one who conducts the ceremonies for any or all classes, and is consequently considered as a degraded Brahman; it also applies to the attendant of an idol.
    E. grāma a village, yaja to sacrifice, in the causal form, affix vun; also with ṇini affix grāmayājin. [305]
