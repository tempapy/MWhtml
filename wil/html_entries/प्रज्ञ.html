• prajña mfn. (-jñaḥ-jñā-jñaṃ)
    1 Wise, learned; also prājña.
    2 Bandy-legged, having the knees far apart: see prajñu. f. (-jñā)
    1 A clever or sensible woman.
    2 Understanding, wisdom, knowledge.
    3 The goddess of arts and eloquence, SARASVATĪ.
    E. pra before jñā, to know, aff. ka. [560]
