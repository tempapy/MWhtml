• virāj m. (-rāṭ)
    1 A man of the regal or military class.
    2 Splendour, beauty.
    3 The first progeny of BRAHMĀ, who having divided himself into male and female, produced from the female, VIRĀJ; VIRĀJ then by himself produced the first Menu or SVAYAMBHUVA, who was the progenitor of the Prajāpatis, and the secondary framer of the visible world: in other places VIRĀJ is the same as BRAHMĀ, and assumer of the androgynous form in his own person.
    E. vi severally or separately, &c., rāja to shine, aff. kvip.
