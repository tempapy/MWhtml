• vāgduṣṭa mfn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ) Abusive, ill-spoken, speaking ill, either morally or grammatically. m. (-ṣṭaḥ) A Brahman who has past the proper time of life, without receiving the initiatory Mantra, the investiture of the thread, &c.
    E. vāk speech, duṣṭa bad, faulty. [747]
