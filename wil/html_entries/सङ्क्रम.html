• saṅkrama mn. (-maḥ-maṃ)
    1 Difficult progress, clambering up rocks, fording torrents, making way through almost impervious or inaccessible passes, &c.
    2 The means of effecting such a passage, a causeway, a bridge, &c. m. (-maḥ)
    1 Going, moving, travelling.
    2 The passage of a planetary body through the zodiac.
    3 Passage from one point to another, traversing, transit, transition.
    4 Concurrence.
    E. sam before krama to go, aff. ac. [880]
