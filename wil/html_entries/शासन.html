• śāsana [840] n. (-naṃ) 1 An order, an edict, a command. 2 A royal grant of land or of privileges, a charter, &c. usually inscribed on stone or copper. 3 A writing, a deed, a written contract or agreement. 4 A Śāstra or scripture. 5 Devotion, or devotional tranquillity, the government of the passions. 6 Governing, ruling, government. E. śāsa to order or direct, aff. lyuṭa.
