• gomedaka mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ)
    1 A gem or precious stone brought from the Himālaya and Indus, described as of four sorts, white, pale yellow, red, and dark blue, perhaps varieties of agate.
    2 A kind of poison: see kākola.
    3 Smearing the body with unguents.
    E. go the eye, mida to anoint, &c. affix ghañ and kan. [301]
