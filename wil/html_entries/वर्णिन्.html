• varṇin [738] mfn. (-rṇī-rṇinī-rṇi) Belonging to a caste or tribe, of that caste, &c. m. (-rṇī) 1 A painter. 2 A scribe, a writer. 3 A religious student. 4 A man of either of the four regular castes. f. (-rṇinī) 1 A woman, a wife. 2 Turmeric. E. varṇa colour, &c., ini aff.
