• tūla r. 1st cl. (tūlati)
    1 To dismiss, to send forth or turn out.
    2 To ascertain quantity or weight; some make this a root of the tenth class (tūlayati-te.) [382]
  • tūla n. (-laṃ)
    1 The mulberry, (Morus Indica.)
    2 Æther, the heaven or atmosphere. mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) Cotton. f. (-lā-lī) The wick of a lamp, or cotton twisted into a similar form for applying unguents, &c. (-lī)
    1 Indigo.
    2 A weaver's fibrous stick or brush.
    3 A painter's brush, &c. see tulī and turī; also tūli.
    E. tūla to dismiss, to send forth, affix ghañ, fem. affixes ṭāp and ṅīṣ. [382]
