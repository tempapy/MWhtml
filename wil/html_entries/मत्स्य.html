• matsya mf. (-tsyaḥ-tsyī) A fish in general. m. (-tsyaḥ)
    1 A particular fish, probably the Śapharī, or the fish in which VIṢṆU was incarnate in his fish Avatāra.
    2 A name of VIṢṆU, from his incarnation as a fish.
    3 A country, enumerated amongst the midland divisions of India, (Dinajpur and Rangpur.)
    4 One of the Purāṇas.
    E. madi to be pleased, Uṇādi aff. syat. [635]
