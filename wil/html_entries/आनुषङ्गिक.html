• ānuṣaṅgika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Proportionate, like, analogous, relative.
    2 Necessary, (as result, consequence.)
    3 Connected, concomitant with, adherent, inherent.
    4 (In grammar) Elliptical, including or agreeing with words not comprised in the sentence.
    E. anuṣaṅga relation or connexion, ṭhañ aff.
