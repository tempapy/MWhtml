• vitānaka n. (-kaṃ)
    1 An awning, a canopy, especially a cloth spread over a large open hall or court, or any open space in which dancing, singing, &c. are exhibited, at solemn or religious festivals.
    2 A heap, a quantity.
    3 An expanse.
    E. kana added to the preceding.
