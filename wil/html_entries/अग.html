• aga [005] r. 1st cl. (agati) To wind or move tortuously. See aka. With an indicatory i. agi (aṃgati) To go, move or approach.
  • aga [005] m. (-gaḥ) 1 A tree. 2 The sun. 3 A mountain. 4 A snake. 5 Moving crookedly, twining, twisting. E. a neg. and ga who goes, from gama to go, or aga to go tortuously, ac aff.
