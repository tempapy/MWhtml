• kalaha [201] mn. (-haḥ-haṃ) 1 War, battle. 2 Strife, contention. m. (-haḥ) 1 The sheath of a sword, a scabbard. 2 A road, a way. 3 Deceit, falsehood. 4 Violence without murderous weapons, abuse, beating, kicking, &c. E. kala a pleasing sound, and ha what destroys, from hana with ḍa aff.
