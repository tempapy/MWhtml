• nyāsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 A deposit, a pledge.
    2 Deserting, abandoning.
    3 Delivering, presenting.
    4 Mental delivering, consigning or entrusting any thing to the mind.
    5 Mental appropriation or assignment of various parts of the body to tutelary divinities, accompanied with certain prayers and gesticulations.
    E. ni before asa to throw or cast away, aff. ghañ. [489]
