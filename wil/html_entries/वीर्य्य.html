• vīryya n. (-ryyaṃ)
    1 Strength, vigour, power.
    2 Dignity, consequence.
    3 Fortitude, firmness.
    4 Semen virile.
    5 Splendour, lustre.
    6 Heroism, valour.
    7 Seed, (of plants, &c.)
    E. vīra a hero, and yat aff., or vīra to be strong, and ac and yat aff. [799]
