• svastika mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ) A temple of a particular form, with a portico in front. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Any lucky or auspicious object.
    2 The meeting of four roads.
    3 A building or palace of a peculiar shape, described as surrounded by a terrace or portion, on the north, west, and south sides, and having the door or entrance on the east.
    4 A kind of mystical figure, the inscription of which on any person or thing is generally considered to be lucky; it consists of 卐 and amongst the Jainas is the emblem of the seventh deified teacher of the present era.
    5 The crossing of the arms, as resembling the preceding.
    6 A kind of cake.
    7 A libertine.
    8 Garlic.
    E. kan added to the last. [964]
