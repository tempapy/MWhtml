• paryyāpti [517] f. (-ptiḥ) 1 Willingness, readiness. 2 Obtaining, acquiring. 3 Satisfaction, repletion, satiety. 4 Entireness, fulness, sufficiency. 5 Adequacy, competency, fitness. 6 Discrimination or distinction of objects according to their natural properties. 7 Preserving, guarding. 8 Warding off a blow. E. pari exceeding, āpti obtaining.
