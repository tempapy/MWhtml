• mṛṣārthaka [672] n. (-kaṃ) An impossibility, an absurdity, (in speech or composition;) the sense of which is evidently false, as kūrmmalomatanutrāṇaḥ, śaśaśṛṅgadhanurdharaḥ, his armour is of tortoise wool, and his bow the horn of a hare. E. mṛṣā falsely, artha meaning, kan added.
