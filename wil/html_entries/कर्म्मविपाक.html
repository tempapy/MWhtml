• karmmavipāka [198] m. (-kaḥ) 1 Future reward or punishment, the consequence of human acts in a subsequent birth. 2 A work on this subject, explaining expiatory rites to be performed in cases of disease, &c. supposed to be the punishment of offences in a previous existence. E. karmma, and vipāka, maturity.
