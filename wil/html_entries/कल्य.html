    2 Healthy, free from sickness.
    3 Clever, dexterous.
    4 Deaf and dumb.
    5 Auspicious, (speech or discourse.)
    6 Counselling, monitory.

    1 The dawn or break of day.
    2 Yesterday.
    3 Tomorrow. nf. (-lyaṃ-lyā)
    1 Spirituous liquor.
    2 Congratulation, good wishes, annunciation of good tidings. f. (-lyā) Emblic myrobalan: see harītakī.
