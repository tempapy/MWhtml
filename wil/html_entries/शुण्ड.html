• śuṇḍa m. (-ṇḍaḥ) The exudation from an elephant's temples. f. (-ṇḍā)
    1 A tavern.
    2 Spirituous liquor.
    3 An animal called the waterelephant, the hippopotamus perhaps.
    4 An elephant's proboscis or trunk.
    5 The stalk of the lotus.
    6 A whore.
    7 A bawd.
    E. śuṇa to go, aff. ḍa, fem. aff. ṭāp. [851]
