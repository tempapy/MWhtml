• sthiti f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Stay, staying, being fixed or stationary.
    2 Correctness of conduct, continuance in the path of duty.
    3 Limit, boundary, term.
    4 Stop, cessation, pause.
    5 Determination, order, decree.
    6 Honor, dignity.
    7 (In astronomy,) Duration of an eclipse.
    E. ṣṭhā to stay or stand, aff. ktin. [953]
