• takṣaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A carpenter.
    2 The Sūtradhāra, the manager and chief actor in the prelude of a drama.
    3 One of the principal Nāgas or serpents of Pātāla.
    4 The divine artist, VIŚVAKARMA.
    5 The name of a tree.
    E. takṣa to chip, to peel or plane, &c. affix vun.
