• ja [336] The third letter of the second class of consonants, corresponding to the letter J in jet.
  • ja [336] mfn. (jaḥ jā jaṃ) 1 Speedy, swift. 2 Eaten. 3 Victorious, triumphant, conquering or a conqueror. m. (-jaḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 A name of VIṢṆU. 3 Birth, production. 4 A parent, a progenitor. 5 Poison. 6 Enjoyment. 7 Light, lustre. 8 Speed. 9 An imp, a goblin. E. jana to bear or be born, ji to conquer, or other roots, and ḍa aff.
