• śrīvatsa m. (-tsaḥ)
    1 VIṢṆU.
    2 A particular mark, usually said to be a curl of hair, on the breast of VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA, and represented in delineations of him by ❈.
    3 A hole in a wall made for felonious purposes.
    4 The emblem of the tenth Jina, or the mark above ascribed to VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA.
    E. śrī LAKṢMĪ, and vatsa dear to.
