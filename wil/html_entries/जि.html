• ji r. 1st cl. (jayati)
    1 To excel, to be pre-eminent, to surpass.
    2 To conquer, to reduce or overcome.
    With vi or parā prefixed, (vijayate parājayate) To overcome, to conquer. [349]
  • ji mfn. (-jiḥ-jiḥ-ji) Victorious, conquering, a victor. m. (-jiḥ) A demon.
    E. ji to conquer, ki aff. [349]
