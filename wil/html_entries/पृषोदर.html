• pṛṣodara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Having a spot or circle on the belly.
    E. pṛṣat a drop of any thing liquid, and udara the belly, form irregular and special; the word is most commonly in use as a grammatical term, being the leading word of a class in which the formation of each is peculiar to itself, and being thence applied to designate the rule which comprehends the whole of the series.
