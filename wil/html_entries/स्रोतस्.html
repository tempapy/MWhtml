• srotas n. (-taḥ)
    1 A current, a natural or rapid stream.
    2 A river in general.
    3 The flow or course of water.
    4 Water.
    5 A spring.
    6 An organ of sense.
    E. sru to flow, Uṇādi aff. asun, and tuṭ augment; also with a final vowel srota, and palatial sibilant śrotas. [960]
