• laba(i)labi r. 1st cl. (laṃbate)
    1 To sound.
    2 To fall or tumble, to be prostrate.
    With ava prefixed,
    1 To hold, to support.
    2 To hang from or on, to depend from.
    3 To rest upon.
    4 To hang with the head downwards.
    With āṅ, To trust in, to depend on.
    With vi, To delay, to detain. [716]
