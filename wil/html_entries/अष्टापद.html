• aṣṭāpada [092] mn. (-daḥ-daṃ) 1 A kind of chequered cloth or board for drafts, dice, &c. 2 Gold. m. (-daḥ) 1 A spider. 2 A fabulous animal with eight legs, the Śarabha. 3 The mountain Kailāsa or abode of KUVERA. 4 A worm. 5 A pin or bolt. f. (-dī) A wild sort of jasmin. E. aṣṭa eight, and pada a quarter, place, foot, &c. a being made long.
