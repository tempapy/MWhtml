• dagdha mfn. (-gdhaḥ-gdhā-gdhaṃ) Burnt, scorched, consumed by fire. f. (-gdhā)
    1 The quarter where the sun is observable.
    2 An epithet of certain lunations, on which it is unlucky to do any thing, and religious rites are prohibited. n. (-gdhaṃ) A fragrant grass.
    E. daha to burn, affix kta.
