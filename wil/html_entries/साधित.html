• sādhita [917] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Amerced, fined, punished by fine, (as the culprit.) 2 Awarded, (as the thing or fine.) 3 Awarded to or in favour of, (as the person to whom the fine is to be paid.) 4 Recovered, (as a debt.) 5 Accomplished, finished. 6 Discharged, fulfilled. 7 Conferred, bestowed, enabled to procure or effect. E. sādha to accomplish, kta aff., with iṭ augment.
