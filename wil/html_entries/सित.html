• sita [923] mfn. (-taḥ-tā- or tī-taṃ) 1 White. 2 Bound, tied. 3 Finished, destroyed. 4 Completed, concluded. 5 Known. m. (-taḥ) 1 White, (the colour.) 2 The planet VENUS. 3 An arrow. n. (-taṃ) 1 Silver. 2 Sandal. f. (-tā) 1 Clayed or candied sugar. 2 Arabian jasmine. 3 A sort of Clitoria. 4 Bent grass with white blossoms. 5 Moonlight. 6 Spirituous liquor. 7 A handsome or well made woman. E. ṣi to tie or bind, aff. kta; or ṣo to destroy, kta aff., and i substituted for the radical vowel.
