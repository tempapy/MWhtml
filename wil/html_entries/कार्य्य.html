• kāryya mfn. (-ryyaḥ-ryyā-ryyaṃ)
    1 To be done.
    2 What ought to be done, fit, right. n. (-ryyaṃ)
    1 Cause, origin.
    2 Motive, object.
    3 Effect, result of a cause.
    4 Affair, business.
    5 Lawsuit, dispute.
    6 The denouement of a drama.
    7 In grammar, an adjunct, either as an affix, augment or substitute.
    E. kṛñ to do, and ṇyat aff.
