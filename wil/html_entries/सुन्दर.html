• sundara mfn. (-raḥ-rā or -rī-raṃ) Handsome, beautiful. m. (-raḥ) KĀMADEVA. f. (-rī)
    1 A handsome woman.
    2 A small timber tree, (Heritiera minor.)
    3 Turmeric.
    4 A species of metre.
    E. su good, excellent, dṛ to respect, aff. ap; or su, with undi to moisten, aff. aran.
