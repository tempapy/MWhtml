• sāhasra mfn. (-sraḥ-srī-sraṃ)
    1 Bought with a thousand.
    2 Paid per thousand, as interest, duty, &c.
    3 Relating or belonging to a thousand. m. (-sraḥ) An army or detachment, a thousand strong. n. (-sraṃ) An aggregate of many thousands.
    E. sahasra a thousand, and aṇ aff. [922]
