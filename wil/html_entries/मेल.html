• mela [674] mf. (-laḥ-lā) Meeting, union, assemblage; in the modern dialects, applied to a large concourse of people collected at stated periods for religious or commercial purposes, as at Haridvār, &c. f. (-lā) 1 Ink. 2 Antimony or any collyrium. E. mila to mix, to meet, aff. ghañ; also with lyuṭ, aff. melana.
