• ātmahan m. (-hā)
    1 A suicide, a self-murderer.
    2 A heretic, an unbeliever.
    3 A priest in a temple, a servant or attendant upon an idol.
    E. ātman and han who kills or destroys, self-destroying; the priest subsisting by appropriating to himself offerings to deities, for which future punishment is assigned. [110]
