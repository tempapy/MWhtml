• sādhu [917] mfn. (-dhuḥ-dhuḥ-dhvī-dhu) 1 Pleasing, beautiful. 2 Well-born, of honorable descent. 3 Right, fit, proper. 4 Good, virtuous, pious, pure. 5 Pure, classical, (as applied to language.) 6 Excellent, good. 7 Eminent. m. (-dhuḥ) 1 A Muni, a saint, a sage in general, one of subdued passions and contemplative habits. 2 A Jina, a deified saint venerated by the Jainas. 3 A usurer. 4 A derivative or inflected noun. f. (-dhuḥ or dhvī) A virtuous wife. E. sādha to accomplish or perfect, Uṇādi aff. uṇ.
