• siddhārtha [925] mfn. (-rthaḥ-rthā-rthaṃ) Successful, prosperous. m. (-rthaḥ) 1 The father of the last and most celebrated Jina or Jaina teacher of the present era. 2 White mustard. 3 A name of Sāhyasiṃha or Buddha. f. (-rthā) The mother of the fourth Jina. E. siddha complete, &c., and artha meaning or wealth.
