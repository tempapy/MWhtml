• mālika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) Relating to a garland, &c. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A florist, a flower-gatherer or seller.
    2 A sort of bird.
    3 A colorist, a painter. f. (-kā)
    1 Double jasmine, (Jasminum zambac, floribus multiplicatis.)
    2 A daughter.
    3 A necklace, an ornament of the neck.
    4 A garland of flowers, a wreath.
    5 The name of a river.
    6 A spirituous or vinous liquor.
    7 Linseed, (Linum utilatissimum.)
    8 A palace.
    E. mālā a garland, ṭhak aff., or kan added, fem. form.
