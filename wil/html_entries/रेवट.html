• revaṭa n. (-ṭaṃ) A conch shell with sinistral convolutions, or the spiral lines turning from the right to the left. m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 Oil of the Morunga tree.
    2 A plantain, the fruit.
    3 A hog.
    4 Dust.
    5 A whirlwind.
    6 A conjuror, a juggler, a snake dancer.
    E. reva to go, aṭan aff. [710]
