• samāsa [898] m. (-saḥ) 1 Contraction, abridgment, conciseness. 2 Composition of words, formation of compound terms, (in grammar.) 3 Composition of differences, reconciling quarrels. 4 Aggregation, assemblage, collection. 5 Whole. E. sam together, asa to throw or direct, aff. ghañ.
