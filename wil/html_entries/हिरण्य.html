• hiraṇya n. (-ṇyaṃ)
    1 Gold.
    2 Either of the precious metals, crude bullion, unwrought gold or silver.
    3 Silver, crude or wrought.
    4 A measure.
    5 A cowry.
    6 Semen virile.
    7 Imperishable matter, that which is eternal.
    8 Thing, substance.
    9 Wealth, property.
    E. hṛ to take, Uṇādi aff. kanyan, and kirac substituted for the radical letters. [976]
