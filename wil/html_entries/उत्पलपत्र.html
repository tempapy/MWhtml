• utpalapatra n. (-traṃ)
    1 The leaf of the lotus.
    2 The impression of a female nail.
    3 The Tilaka or mark on the forehead, made with sandal, &c. by the Hindus.
    4 A broad bladed knife or lancet.
    E. utpala a lotus, and patra a leaf; similarly shaped, &c. also kan added utpalapatraka.
