• matkuṇa m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 A bug.
    2 A flea.
    3 An elephant without tusks.
    4 A beardless man.
    5 The cocoanut.
    6 Armour for the thighs or legs.
    7 A buffalo. f. (-ṇā) The pudendum muliebre, without hair on the pubis.
    E. maṭ or mat pleasure, kvana to sound, aff. ac, the deriv. irr.
