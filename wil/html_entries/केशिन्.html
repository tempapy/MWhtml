• keśin mfn. (-śī-śinī-śi) Having fine hair. m. (-śī)
    1 A name of VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA.
    2 The name of a Daitya or demon killed by KṚṢṆA.
    3 A lion. f. (-śinī)
    1 A kind of grass, (Andropogon aculeatum.)
    2 Spikenard, (Valeriana jatamansi.)
    E. keśa hair, ini aff. [249]
