• vṛhadgṛha m. (-haḥ) A country, described as lying behind the Vindhya mountains, near the province of Malva, and perhaps comprising Bundelkhand, or the adjoining district.
    E. vṛhat large, and gṛha a house; also read vṛhadguha from guha a cave, the country being mountainous, and the people probably having been Troglodytes or dwellers in caves. [804]
