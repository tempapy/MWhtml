• daśakāmajavyasana n. (-naṃ) The aggregate of ten vices, proceeding from the quality of desire, viz. hunting, gambling, sleeping during the day, abusiveness, lechery, drunkenness, dancing, singing, playing, and hypocrisy.
    E. daśa ten, kāma desire, ja born, vyasana calamity. [402]
