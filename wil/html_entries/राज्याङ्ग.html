• rājyāṅga [703] n. (-ṅgaṃ) A requisite of regal administration; variously enumerated, as seven, eight, or nine, viz. first, the monarch; second, the prime minister; third, a friend or ally; fourth, treasure; fifth, territory; sixth, a strong hold; and seventh, an army; the eighth is the population or companies of citizens, and the ninth the Purohita or spiritual adviser. E. rājya royalty, and aṅga limb or part.
