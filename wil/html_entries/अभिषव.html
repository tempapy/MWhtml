• abhiṣava [056] m. (-vaḥ) 1 Religious bathing, ablution preparatory to religious rites. 2 Sacrifice. 3 Bathing in general. 4 Drinking the juice of the acid Asclepias. 5 Ferment, yeast, any substance producing vinous fermentation. n. (-vaṃ) Sour gruel. See kāñjikā. E. abhi, and su to mix, to stir, ap affix; and sa is changed to la.
