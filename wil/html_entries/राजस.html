• rājasa mfn. (-saḥ-sī-saṃ) Relating to, or derived, &c. from the quality of passion. f. (-sī)
    1 The goddess DURGĀ.
    2 The state of being in this world or the next, in which the Raja Guṇa or quality of passion predominates: it is divided into three classes; the first class comprises the Gandharbas, Yakṣas, Guhyakas, &c. the second, kings and heroes; and the third, boxers, wrestlers, gamblers, tipplers, actors, &c.
    E. rajas the quality of passion, aṇ and ṅīp affs. [702]
