• matsyaṇḍī f. (-ṇḍī) Coarse or unrefined sugar, the juice of the sugar-cane, either after its first boiling, or after it is partially freed from impurities by straining.
    E. manda slowly, syanda to trickle or ooze, aṇ aff., fem. aff. ṅīp, deriv. irr.; also with kan added, matsyaṇḍīkā. [635]
