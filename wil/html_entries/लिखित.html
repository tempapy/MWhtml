• likhita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Written.
    2 Drawn, delineated, painted: (painting being considered a sort of writing)
    3 Drawn as lines, scratched.
    4 Scarified. n. (-taṃ)
    1 Scripture, writing.
    2 A writing, a manuscript, a written book or paper.
    E. likha to write, aff. kta. [720]
