• vyāpaka [819] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) 1 Diffusive, comprehensive, spreading or extending widely. 2 (In law,) Comprehending all the points of an argument, pervading the whole plea. 3 (In logic,) Embracing the whole of an argument or objection. n. (-kaṃ) Essential and inherent property. E. vi before apa to pervade, ṇvul aff. implying the agent.
