• ākranda mfn. (-ndaḥ-ndā-ndaṃ) Who checks or restrains. m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 Crying, crying out.
    2 Calling.
    3 Weeping, sobbing.
    4 Sound.
    5 Violence.
    6 Furious or violent combat.
    7 A friend.
    8 A brother.
    9 A king, a lord.
    10 A usurper.
    11 A king who prevents an ally from aiding another.
    E. āṅ, kranda to cry, to sound, &c. ac aff.
