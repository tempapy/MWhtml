• hāra [973] mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) Who or what takes, &c. m. (-raḥ) 1 A porter, a carrier. 2 Taking, conveying. 3 A string or garland of pearls, &c. 4 War, battle. 5 (In prosody,) A long syllable. 6 (In arithmetic,) A divisor, also the denominator of a fraction. E. hṛ to seize, (the mind, &c.) and ghañ aff., or causal form, with aṇ aff.
