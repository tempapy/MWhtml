• stoma r. 10th cl. (stomayati)
    1 To praise, to eulogise.
    2 To flatter, to wheedle, to coax.
    3 To boast.
  • stoma mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Crooked, bent. m. (-maḥ)
    1 A heap, a number, a multitude, a quantity.
    2 Sacrifice, oblation.
    3 Praise, eulogium. n. (-maṃ)
    1 The head.
    2 Wealth.
    3 Grain, corn.
    4 A stick or staff bound with iron.
    E. ṣṭu to praise, man Uṇādi aff.
