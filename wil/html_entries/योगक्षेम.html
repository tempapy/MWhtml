• yogakṣema n. (-maḥ)
    1 Charge of property, keeping of cattle, &c. for another.
    2 Insurance or charge for secure conveyance of goods, and the profit on their sale.
    3 Profit, gain, keeping the old and making fresh gains.
    4 Such articles as are not divisible amongst coheirs.
    5 Welfare, well-being.
    6 National prosperity the good government of a state, or the means of maintaining it; as the Raja, minister, priest, &c. forming the administration.
    E. yoga profit, and kṣema safety or preservation; or yoga association, and kṣema patience.
