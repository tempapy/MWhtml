• vāśiṣṭha mfn. (-ṣṭhaḥ-ṣṭhī-ṣṭhaṃ) Relating to the sage VASIṢṬHA. f. (-ṣṭhī) The Goomti river, which rising in the Kemaün hills, pursues a winding and south-easterly course, and passing Lucknow and Jonpur falls into the Ganges, below Benares.
    E. vaśiṣṭha the saint, aṇ aff., derived from him. [755]
