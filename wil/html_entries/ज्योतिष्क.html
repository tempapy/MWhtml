• jyotiṣka m. (-ṣkaḥ)
    1 A planetary or heavenly body; the generic term for the sun, the moon, a planet, an asterism, a star: the word in the m. plu. (-ṣkāḥ) implies all these five.
    2 A tree, the wood of which is used to produce fire by attrition: see gaṇikārikā. f. (-kā) Heart-pea: see jyotiṣmatī.
    E. jyotis light, and ka what makes, from kṛ, with ḍa aff. [356]
