• tūvara [383] m. (-raḥ) 1 A bull without horns, though of an age to have them. 2 A beardless man. 3 A eunuch. 4 An astringent taste. f. (-rī) A fragrant earth. E. tu to injure, ṣvarac affix, and the vowel optionally long, hence also tuvara and tuvarī; with kan added tūvarikā and tūvarīkā.
