• hṛṣita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Astonished, surprised.
    2 Pleased, delighted.
    3 Exquisitely delighted, having the hair of the body erect with pleasure.
    4 Disappointed, baulked, deceived.
    5 Bent, bowed.
    6 Armed, accountred.
    E. hṛṣa to be pleased, aff. kta, with the augment iṭ. [978]
