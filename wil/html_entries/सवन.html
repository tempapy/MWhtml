• savana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Bathing, as a religious exercise, or preparatory to a sacrifice, purificatory ablution in general.
    2 A sacrifice in general.
    3 Extracting and drinking the juice of the acid Asclepias.
    4 Bearing as children, bringing forth young.
    E. ṣū to bear young, &c., Uṇādi aff. yuc.
