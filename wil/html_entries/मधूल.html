• madhūla m. (-laḥ) A sort of Bassia described as growing in watery or mountainous situations. f. (-lī)
    1 Liquorice.
    2 The sweet lime.
    3 The mango tree.
    4 A plant, (Sanseviera zeylanica.)
    E. madhu honey or spirit, lā to be or get, aff. ḍa, and the u of madhu irregularly long. [639]
