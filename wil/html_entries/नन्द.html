• nanda [453] mf. (-ndaḥ-ndī) Happiness, pleasure, felicity. m. (-ndaḥ) 1 One of KUVERA'S nine inestimable gems or treasure. 2 The cowherd NANDA, the foster father of KṚṢṆA. 3 A name of VIṢṆU. 4 A prince, the son of MAHĀNANDI. 5 A flute, one eleven inches long. f. (-ndā) 1 An earthen water jar. 2 The first, sixth, or eleventh day of the fortnight. 3 Prosperity, increase. 4 A husband's sister. f. (-ndī) INDRA'S garden. f. (-ndā or -ndī) A name of DURGĀ. E. nadi to be prosperous or happy, affix ac and ṭāp.
