• pariṇata mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Ripe, mature.
    2 Bowed, bent.
    3 Aimed at, directed to, inclined to. m. (-taḥ) An elephant stooping to strike with his tusks. n. (-taṃ) Capital, stock, wealth accumulated for the sake of profit.
    E. pari before nama to bend or bow, aff. kta.
