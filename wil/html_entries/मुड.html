• muḍa r. 6th cl. (muḍati)
    1 To quit, to leave.
    2 To clothe or cover. (i) muḍi r. 1st cl. (muṇḍate)
    1 To be pure or clean.
    2 To cleanse.
    3 To sink, (as in water.)
    4 To sink, (in estimation,) to be despised or disregarded. (muṇḍati)
    5 To shave or cut.
    6 To grind or pound. [665]
