• deśya [425] mfn. (-śyaḥ-śyā-śyaṃ) 1 What is proper to be proved or ordered. 2 Local. 3 Present at any transaction, on the spot. n. (-śyaṃ) 1 The postulate, the statement or exhibition of a question or argument. 2 The fact or charge to be proved or substantiated. m. (-śyaḥ) An eyewitness of any thing, one who was on the spot. E. diśa to show or command, affix ṇyat; or deśa, and yat aff.
