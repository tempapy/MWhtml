• dantadhāvana n. (-naṃ)
    1 A tooth brush, or a fibrous stick used for cleaning the teeth.
    2 Cleaning the teeth. m. (-naḥ)
    1 A tree yielding an astringent resin, (Mimosa catechu.)
    2 A plant, (Mimusops elengi:) see vakula.
    E. danta a tooth, and dhāva to clean, affix lyuṭ. [398]
