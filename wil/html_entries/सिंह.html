• siṃha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A lion.
    2 LEO, the sign of the zodiac.
    3 (In composition) Pre-eminent. f. (-hī)
    1 The egg-plant, (Solanum Melongena)
    2 A shrub, (Justicia.)
    3 A prickly nightshade.
    4 The mother of RĀHU.
    E. ṣica to sprinkle, ka Uṇādi aff. ha substituted for the final, and num inserted; or hisi to injure or kill, ac aff., and the radical consonants transposed. [923]
