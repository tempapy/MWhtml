• mecaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Black, dark, of a dark or black colour. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Black or dark-blue, (the colour.)
    2 The eye of a peacock's tail.
    3 A cloud.
    4 Smoke.
    5 A plant, (Hyperanthera morunga.) mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ) Darkness. n. (-kaṃ) Sulphuret of antimony.
    E. maca to mix, Uṇādi aff. vun, and the vowel changed to e. [673]
