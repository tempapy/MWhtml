• bandhura [599] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Uneven, undulating, wavy, partially even, and depressed. 2 Bowed, bent. 3 Pleasing, delightful, handsome, beautiful. 4 Deaf. 5 Injurious, mischievous. m. (-rā) A whore, a prostitute. f. plu. (-rāḥ) The meal of parched corn. E. bandha to bind, urac Uṇādi aff.; also with ūrac, aff. bandhūra.
