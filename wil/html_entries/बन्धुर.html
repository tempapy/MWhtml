• bandhura mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Uneven, undulating, wavy, partially even, and depressed.
    2 Bowed, bent.
    3 Pleasing, delightful, handsome, beautiful.
    4 Deaf.
    5 Injurious, mischievous. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A goose.
    2 A flower, (Pentapetes Phœnicea.)
    3 A drug, commonly Bireng.
    4 A bird.
    5 A crane.
    6 A drug, commonly Ṛṣabha. n. (-raṃ)
    1 A diadem, a crest.
    2 The dregs of oil, or oil cake. f. (-rā) A whore, a prostitute. f. plu. (-rāḥ) The meal of parched corn.
    E. bandha to bind, urac Uṇādi aff.; also with ūrac, aff. bandhūra.
