• mocaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A tree, (Hyperanthera morunga.)
    2 The silk cotton tree.
    3 The plantain tree.
    4 A plant, commonly Ghaṇṭāpārali.
    5 A devotee, one who has divested himself of worldly passion and desire.
    6 A liberator.
    E. muca to be let loose, aff. vun, or causal form, aff. ac, and kan added. [675]
