• śrantha r. 9th cl. (śrathnāti)
    1 To set free, to let loose, to liberate.
    2 To gladden, to delight frequently.
    3 To string, to tie together. r. 1st and 10th cls. (śranthati śranthayati) To arrange, to place in order. [861]
  • śrantha m. (-nthaḥ)
    1 Loosening, liberating.
    2 Looesness, flaccidity.
    3 Tying, binding, stringing.
    4 A name of VIṢṆU.
    E. śrantha to tie, &c., aff. ac. [861]
