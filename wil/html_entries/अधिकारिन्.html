• adhikārin [022] m. (-rī) 1 A superintendent, a ruler, a director. 2 A master, an owner. 3 A sage, one who is perfect in the principles and practice of the Vedānta. mf. (-rī-riṇī) (In law) The possessor of a right or title, as, dhanādhikārī heir to property; saṃskārādhikārī one who has a right to the essential ceremonies of the Hindu religion. E. adhikāra, and ini aff.
