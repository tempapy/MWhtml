• prabhṛti [574] f. (-tiḥ) 1 Manner, kind. 2 Etcetera, others, rest, remainder, (in composition;) as garmmutprabhṛtayaḥtṛṇaṃ the grass called Garmut and other sorts; munayaḥ somaśravaḥ prabhṛtayaḥ the Munis, Somaśravas, and the rest; tataḥ prabhṛti thence, after that. E. pra before bhṛ to nourish, aff. ktin.
