• mallika m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A bird, said to be a sort of goose with brown legs and bill.
    2 A shuttle.
    3 The month Māgha. f. (-kā)
    1 Arabian jasmine, (Jasminum zambac.)
    2 A kind of fish.
    3 An earthen vessel for holding oil, &c.
    4 A goblet, a cup, a drinking vessel.
    6 A river.
    E. kan added to malli. [647]
