• cañcala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Trembling, shaking, moving, unsteady.
    2 Fickle, inconsiderate, inconstant, (unsteady, metaphorically.) m. (-laḥ)
    1 The wind.
    2 A lecher, a libertine, a lover. f. (-lā)
    1 Lightning.
    2 LAKṢMĪ or the goddess of fortune.
    3 Longpepper.
    E. cala to go, in the reiterative form, num inserted.
