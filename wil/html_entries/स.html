• sa [872] The thirty-second consonant of the Nāgarī Alphabet, corresponding to S.
  • sa [872] m. (saḥ) 1 A snake. 2 ŚIVA. 3 VIṢṆU. 4 Air, wind. 5 The abbreviated name of the musical note ṣa j. 6 He, the nominative case masc. of tada the third personal pronoun: before many letters the Visarga is rejected. f. (sā) 1 The goddess LAKṢMĪ. 2 She, the nominative case fem. of taṭ. n. (saṃ) 1 Knowledge. 2 Meditation. 3 A carriage road. 4 A fence. Ind. (In composition,) With, together with; being substituted for sam or saha; combined with nouns it forms adjectives and adverbs; as sakopaḥ angry, sakopam angrily; sagarbbaḥ proud, sagarvvam haughtily; savinayaḥ modest, well behaved, savinayam respectfully; sasambhramaḥ flurried, sasambhramam flurriedly, &c. sānandaḥ joyful, sānandaṃ joyfully. E. ṣo to go, &c., aff. ḍa.
