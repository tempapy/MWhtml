• śāstrakṛt [841] m. (-kṛt) 1 A Ṛṣi, a holy personage of divine character. 2 An author in general. E. śāstra a Śāstra or scripture, &c., and kṛt the author; the principal compositions in Hindu religion, law, and literature, being attributed to sanctified and superhuman personages.
