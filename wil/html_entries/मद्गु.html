• madgu [637] m. (-dguḥ) 1 An aquatic bird, the shag. 2 An outcaste, the son of a Brahman by a woman of the bard, or panegyrist class, whose employment is hunting wild beasts. 3 A ship, a galley. E. masja to emerge, to dive, u aff., deriv. irr., or mada to be delighted, (in the water,) and guk Uṇādi aff.
