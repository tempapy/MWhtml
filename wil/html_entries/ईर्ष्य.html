• īrṣya r. 1st cl. (īrṣyati) To envy.
  • īrṣya mfn. (-rṣyaḥ-rṣyā-rṣyaṃ) Envious, envying. f. (-rṣyā)
    1 Envy or impatience of another's success.
    2 Spite, malice.
    E. īrṣya to envy, and ac and ṭāp affixes; also, but according to the best writers, inaccurately written īrṣā.
