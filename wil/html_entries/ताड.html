• tāḍa [371] m. (-ḍaḥ) 1 Beating, whipping, inflicting bodily chastisement. 2 Sound, noise. 3 A handful of grass, corn, &c. a sheaf. 4 A mountain. 5 A kind of grass, (Andropogon serratus.) f. (-ḍi or ḍī) A kind of palm, (Corypha talliera, Rox.) see tālī. E. taḍa to give pain, to beat, &c. affix ghañ.
