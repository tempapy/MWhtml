• yadṛcchā f. (-cchā)
    1 Wilfulness, independence, following one's own fancies.
    2 (In grammar,) A noun which is neither a generic nor specific term, nor noun of agency; one either not derived from authority, or not possessing meaning.
    E. yat what, which, ṛccha to go, aff. a and ṭāp. [680]
