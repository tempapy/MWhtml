• uddhṛta [149] mfn. (taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Raised, drawn up, as water from a well, &c. 2 Pulled up or out, eradicated or broken off. 3 Lifted up. 4 Raised, elevated. 5 Thrown up or upwards. 6 Vomited. 7 Separated, set apart. 8 Divided, partitioned. 9 Recovered. 10 Uncovered. 11 Dispersed, scattered. 12 Extracted, selected, taken from or out of. 13 Holding, containing. E. ud up, dhṛ to take, and kta. aff.
