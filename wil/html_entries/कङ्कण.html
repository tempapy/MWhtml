• kaṅkaṇa mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ)
    1 A bracelet or ornament of the wrist.
    2 A string or ribbon tied round the wrist.
    3 An ornament or trinket.
    4 A crest. f. (-ṇī) A small bell or tinkling ornament; also kiṅkiṇī and kaṅkaṇīkā.
    E. kaṃ happily, agreeably, kaṇa to sound, or the reiterative form of kaṇa and ac affix, fem. affix ṅīṣ. [178]
