• pala [518] r. 1st cl. (palati) To go, to move.
  • pala [518] n. (-laṃ) 1 A weight of gold or silver equal to four Karṣas or Suvarṇas; also of four or of eight Tolas, or in common use, of three Tolas, two Māṣas, and eight Rattis. 2 A moment, the sixtieth part of an Indian hour. 3 Flesh. m. (-laḥ) Straw after threshing, &c. E. pala to go, to move, aff. ap.
