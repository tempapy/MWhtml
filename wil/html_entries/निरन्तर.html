• nirantara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Coarse, gross, without interstices.
    2 Continuous.
    3 Uninterrupted, continual.
    4 Unbounded.
    5 Identical, not different.
    6 Unconcealed, not hidden or vanished.
    9 Not external, &c.
    E. nir not, antara interval, difference, &c. [471]
