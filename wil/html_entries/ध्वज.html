• dhvaja r. 1st cl. (dhvajati) also (i) dhvaji r. 1st cl. (dhvaṃjati) To go, to move. [449]
  • dhvaja mn. (-jaḥ-jaṃ)
    1 A flag or banner.
    2 A mark, a sign or symbol.
    3 The penis.
    4 The upper part of a skull carried on a stick, as a penance for the murder of a Brahman.
    5 A house situated to the east of any object.
    6 A flag-staff.
    7 (In Prosody,) An Iambic. m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A distiller.
    2 Pride.
    3 Hypocrisy, fraud.
    E. dhvaja to go, affix ac. [449]
