• viniyoga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 Giving or parting with any thing for a consideration, or in expectation of some advantage.
    2 Abandoning, leaving.
    3 Separation or detachment from.
    4 Impediment.
    5 Appointment to any office, &c.
    6 Application to or employment in.
    E. vi and ni, before yuja to join, aff. ghañ. [772]
