• pratyuktrama m. (-maḥ)
    1 Act or effort tending to a main object.
    2 First step or measure in any business.
    3 Declaration of war.
    4 Setting out to assail an enemy.
    E. prati and ut before krama to go, aff. ac; also pratyuktrāmaṇa and pratyuktrānti. [571]
