• jāla [348] n. (-laṃ) 1 A net. 2 A window, a lattice, an eyelet or loophole. 3 A multitude, an assemblage. 4 An unblown flower. 5 Pride, arrogance. 6 Magick, conjuring, illusion, super- natural deception. m. (-laḥ) 1 The Kadamba tree. 2 The young fruit of a gourd or cucumber. f. (-lī) 2 A small cucumber, (Trichosanthes diœca, Rox.) 2 Any medicament or drug. E. jala to encompass, to hide or screen, affix aṇ.
