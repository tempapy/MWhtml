• vela [807] (ṛ) veḷ r. 1st cl. (velati) 1 To go or move. 2 To shake, to tremble. r. 10th cl. (velayati) To count or declare the time.
  • vela [807] n. (-laṃ) A garden. f. (-lā) 1 Time. 2 Tide, flow, current. 3 Boundary, limit. 4 Sea-shore. 5 Easy or sudden death. 6 Sickness, disease. 7 The food of ŚIVA. 8 Speech. 9 Leisure, interval, opportunity. 10 The wife of Budha. 11 The gums. E. vela to go, aff. ac and ṭāp.
