• nābhi [461] f. (-bhiḥ-bhī) 1 The nave of a wheel. 2 Musk. mf. (-bhiḥ and bhiḥ or bhī) The navel. m. (-bhiḥ) 1 An emperor, a sovereign, a lord paramount. 2 A king, a chief. 3 A Kṣatriya or Hindū of the regal and military tribe. 4 The son of PRIYAVRATA. 5 A race, a family. E. naha to bind, Uṇādi affix iñi, and bha substituted for ha.
