• bandhana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Binding, tying, confining.
    2 A rope for tying cattle.
    3 Killing.
    4 Hurting, injuring. mfn. subst. (naḥ-nā or nī-naṃ) The implement of binding or tying, a rope, a chain, a bandage, a ligature, &c.
    E. bandha to tie, aff. lyuṭ or buc. [598]
