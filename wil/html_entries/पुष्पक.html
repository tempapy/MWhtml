• puṣpaka [546] n. (-kaṃ) 1 Calx of brass. 2 The chariot of KUVERA. 3 A disease of the eyes, albugo, specks on the eye. 4 A bracelet of diamonds or jewels. 5 A cup or vessel of iron. 6 A small earthen fire-place or furnace. 7 A sort of collyrium. 8 Green vitriol. E. kan added to the last.
