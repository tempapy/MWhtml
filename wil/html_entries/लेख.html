• lekha m. (-khaḥ)
    1 A letter, an epistle.
    2 A god, a deity. f. (-khā)
    1 A line, a mark or row, &c.
    2 Writing, hand-writing.
    3 Delineation, painting.
    E. likha to write, aff. ghañ; it is applied to the second sense, because the figures of the gods are written or delineated. [722]
