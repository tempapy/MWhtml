• bala r. 1st and 10th cls. (balati balayati)
    1 To live.
    2 To hoard grain.
    3 To prevent or obstruct wealth. r. 1st cl. (balate)
    1 To kill.
    2 To go. r. 10th cl. (bālayate) To explain. (bālayati) To support, nourish, rear, as a child. [599]
  • bala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Strong, stout, robust, powerful. m. (-laḥ)
    1 BALADEVA, the elder brother of KṚṢṆA, and the third of the three incarnations termed RĀMAS.
    2 A crow.
    3 A demon, slain by INDRA.
    4 The Varuṇa tree. n. (-laṃ)
    1 Strength, power.
    2 Bulkiness.
    3 Form, figure.
    4 An army, forces.
    5 Gum myrrh.
    6 Semen virile.
    7 The body.
    8 The leaf of a tree.
    9 Blood.
    10 Rigour, severity. f. (-lā) An aquatic plant, (Sida cordifolia.)
    E. bala to live, aff. ac; in some of the senses it may be derived from vala to go or surround, and may then be written with the semivowel: see vala. [599]
