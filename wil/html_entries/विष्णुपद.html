• viṣṇupada n. (-daṃ)
    1 The sky, heaven, atmosphere.
    2 The sea of milk.
    3 A lotus. f. (-dī)
    1 The Ganges.
    2 One of the twelve Saṅkrāntis or periods, at which the sun enters a sign of the zodiac, especially the first sign after the equinox.
    E. viṣṇu, and pad abode, or foot; where VIṢṆU resides or rests, or proceeding from his foot. [793]
