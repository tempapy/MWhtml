• gṛñjana [295] n. (-naṃ) Poisoned flesh, the meat of an animal destroyed by poison. m. (-naḥ) 1 Garlic, or a small variety of it of a red colour. 2 A turnip. 3 The tops of hemp chewed as an inebriating substance, the Gāñja. E. gṛji to sound, and lyuṭ affix; applied to the latter as causing eructation.
