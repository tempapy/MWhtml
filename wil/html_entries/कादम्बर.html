• kādambara n. (-raṃ) The surface or skim of coagulated milk. m. (-raḥ) A spirituous liquor distilled from the flowers of the Kadamba. f. (-rī)
    1 Wine, spirituous liquor in general.
    2 A name of SARASVATĪ.
    3 The female Koil or Indian cuckoo.
    4 The female of another bird, (Turdus salica, BUC.)
    E. kadamba the Kadamba flower, ra what gains or gets, from rā, affix ḍa.
