• chāyā [335] f. (-yā) 1 Shade. 2 Shadow, reflected image. 3 The wife of the sun. 4 Beauty, splendor, lustre. 5 Light. 6 Nourishing, cherishing. 7 A bribe. 8 A straight or continuous line. 9 A name of the goddess DURGĀ. 10 Darkness, obscurity. 11 The shadow of a Gnomon, especially as indicative of the position of the sun. E. cho to cut, (to cut off the light, &c.) and ya Uṇādi aff.
