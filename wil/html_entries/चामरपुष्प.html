• cāmarapuṣpa m. (-ṣpaḥ)
    1 The betel-nut tree, (Areca faufel or catechu.)
    2 A kind of grass, (Saccharum cylindricum.)
    3 The mango tree.
    4 A flower, (Pandanus odoratissimus.)
    E. cāmara a Chowri, and puṣpa a flower; whose flowers grow in clusters like the Indian Chowri.
