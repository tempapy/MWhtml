• prabodhana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Awakening, arousing, exciting, reviving.
    2 Instructing.
    3 Reviving the fragrance of a perfume, which had lost its scent. f. (-nī) The lunation on which VIṢṆU is supposed to wake from his sleep, the eleventh of the light half of Kartik; also prabodhinī.
    E. pra before budha to know or understand, aff. lyuṭ.
