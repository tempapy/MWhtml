• vivartta m. (-rttaḥ)
    1 Assemblage, collection, heap or multitude.
    2 Dancing.
    3 Going or turning round, revolving, returning.
    4 Human error, worldly ignorance, the confounding of truth and falsehood, or the mistaking of unreal objects and conceiving them to be what they are not, as mirage for water, a rope for a snake, &c.
    5 Modification, various or altered state or condition.
    E. vi before vṛta to be, aff. kta.
