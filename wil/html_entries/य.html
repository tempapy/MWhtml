• ya The twenty-sixth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, or semivowel Y; in Bengal, it is usually pronounced J. [677]
  • ya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Who goes, a goer, a mover. m. (yaḥ)
    1 Air, wind.
    2 Union, joining.
    3 Fame, celebrity.
    4 Barley.
    5 Light, lustre.
    6 Abandoning.
    7 YAMA. f. (yā)
    1 Pudendum muliebre.
    2 Going, proceeding.
    3 A car, a carriage.
    4 Prohibiting, restraining, checking.
    5 Religious meditation.
    6 Getting, obtaining.
    E. yaja to worship, yama to check, &c., aff. ḍa. [677]
