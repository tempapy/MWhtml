• sātvata [916] m. (-taḥ) 1 VIṢṆU. 2 BALADEVA. m. plu. (-tāḥ) The people of one of the countries of midland India, apparently inhabiting a district in the vicinity of the Pārijātra mountains, said to be descendants from outcaste Vaiśyas. f. (-tī) 1 One of the four great divisions of the drama, described as the representation of gentle and amiable sentiments or passions. 2 The mother of ŚIŚUPĀLA. E. satvata a proper name, &c., aṇ aff.
