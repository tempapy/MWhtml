• iḍā [130] f. (-ḍā) 1 The wife of BUDDHA, daughter of IKṢVĀKU. 2 The earth. 3 A cow. 4 Speed. 5 A tubular vessel, one of the principal channels of the vital spirit, that which is on the right side of the body. 6 Heaven. 7 The goddess of speech. E. ila to direct or send, ka affix, and ṭāp fem. la and ḍa are interchangeable: see ilā.
