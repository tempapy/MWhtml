• kṛmighna [244] mfn. (-ghnaḥ-ghnī-ghnaṃ) Vermifuge, anthelmintic. m. (-ghnaḥ) 1 A shrub used in medicine, as an anthelmintic, commonly Bireṅg, (Erycibe paniculata, Rox.) see biḍaṅga. 2 The onion. 3 The root of the jujube. 4 The marking nut plant. f. (-ghnī) Turmeric. E. kṛmi a worm, han to kill, and ṭak affix; also krimighna.
