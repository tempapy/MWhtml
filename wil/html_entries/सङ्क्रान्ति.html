• saṅkrānti f. (-ntiḥ)
    1 The actual passage of the sun or other planetary bodies from one sign of the zodiac into another.
    2 Passage in general, proceeding from one time or condition of life to another, from one place to another, &c.
    3 Going, proceeding in general.
    4 Imitation, reflexion.
    E. sam before krama to go, aff. ktin.
