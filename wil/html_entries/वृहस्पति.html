• vṛhaspati m. (-tiḥ) The regent of the planet JUPITER, identified astronomically with the planet; in Mythology, he is the son of AṄGIRAS, and preceptor of the gods.
    2 The name of a saint and law-giver.
    E. vṛhat great, illustrious, (as the deities,) and pati master or Guru, suṭ augment, and the final of the first member rejected; it also occasionally occurs vṛhatāṃpati.
