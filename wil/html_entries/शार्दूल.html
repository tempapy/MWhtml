• śārdūla [839] m. (-laḥ) 1 A tiger. 2 Another animal. 3 A Rākṣasa. 4 A sort of bird. 5 (In composition,) Pre-eminent, excellent. 6 A form of metre, a variety of the class Dhṛti, or stanza of four lines of 18 syllables each; also of the class Atidhṛti, in which there are 19 syllables in the line. E. śṝ to injure, ṭūlac aff.
