• vedhana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Piercing, perforation.
    2 Depth, (in measurement.) f. (-nī)
    1 An instrument for piercing an elephant's ear.
    2 A small gimblet or perforating instrument used to pierce gems, shells, &c.
    3 An augre, a gimblet, &c. or any similar instrument.
    E. vyadha to pierce, lyuṭ aff. [807]
