• horā f. (-rā)
    1 The rising of a sign of the zodiac.
    2 Part of the duration of a sign, the twenty-fourth part of a day, an hour.
    3 A mark, a line.
    4 A science or work in science, probably on astrology.
    E. hoḍa to go, to proceed, affs. aca and ṭāpa, and ḍa changed to ra. [980]
