• niveśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 Entrance, entering.
    2 Delivering, entrusting, depositing.
    3 A camp, a palace, the residence of a king or general.
    4 Any dwelling.
    5 Military array.
    6 Ornament, dress, decoration.
    7 Marriage.
    E. ni before viśa to enter, aff. ghañ.
