• vāri n. (-ri)
    1 Water.
    2 A vegetable perfume, commonly Bāla. f. (-riḥ)
    1 A name of SARASVATĪ, the goddess of speech.
    2 The place where an elephant is tied or fastened.
    3 A captive, a prisoner. f. (-riḥ or -rī)
    1 A water-pot, whether large or small, a pitcher, a jar.
    2 The rope that fastens an elephant.
    3 A hole or trap for catching elephants.
