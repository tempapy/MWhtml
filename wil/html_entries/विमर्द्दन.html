• vimarddana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Rubbing, pounding, grinding.
    2 Killing, destroying.
    3 The trituration of perfumes.
    4 Conjunction of the sun and moon, or eclipse.
    E. vi before mṛda to rub, &c., aff. lyuṭ; also vimarddaka m. (-kaḥ) and vimarddanā f. (-nā.) [778]
