• nirgranthaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Unattended, unaccompanied.
    2 Abandoned, deserted.
    3 Clever, expert, conversant. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A religious mendicant.
    2 A naked devotee.
    3 A gambler.
    E. nir not, grantha tie or connexion, affix kan; also read nirgranthika.
