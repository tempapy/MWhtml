• sugandhi [928] mfn. (-ndhiḥ-ndhiḥ-ndhi) 1 Fragrant, sweet-smelling. 2 Virtuous, pious. m. (-ndhiḥ) 1 A perfume, a fragrance. 2 A fragrant sort of mango. n. (-ndhi) 1 A drug and perfume, commonly Elavāluka. 2 The root of long-pepper. 3 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus rotundus). 4 Another sort, (Scirpus kyssoor.) 5 Coriander seed. E. su good, well, gandha smell, and i substituted for the final.
