• nidhana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Poor. mn. (-naḥ-naṃ)
    1 Race, family.
    2 Loss, disappearance, annihilation.
    3 Death, dying.
    4 The seventh asterism of the lunar mansions, reckoning from that under which a person is born. m. (-naḥ) The head of a family.
    E. ni before dhana to perish, affix ac, or ni neg. dhana wealth. [467]
