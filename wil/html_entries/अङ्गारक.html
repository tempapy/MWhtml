• aṅgāraka [010] m. (-kaḥ) 1 The planet Mars. 2 Charcoal, burning or extinguished. 3 Yellow or white amaranth. See kuruṇṭhaka. 4 Another plant, (Eclipta or Verbesina prostrata.) See bhṛṅgarāja. n. (-kaṃ) A medicated oil, prepared by boiling turmeric and various vegetable substances in common oil. E. aṅgāra and kan affix; resembling a burning brand.
