• śatadru f. (-druḥ) The Setlej river, which rises in the Himālaya mountains, in the vicinity it is supposed of the Rāvanhrad or lake, and running to the S. W. unites in the Pañjāb with the Beyah or Vipāśā, when it forms the Hyphasis of the Greeks, and falls into the Indus below Multan.
    E. śata a hundred (fold, or in a hundred branches), dru to flow, Uṇādi aff. ku. [827]
