• tu r. 2nd cl. (tauti or tavati)
    1 To go or move.
    2 To thrive or increase.
    3 To injure, to hurt or kill.
    4 To become full. According to some authorities this is merely a Sautra root. [379]
  • tu ind. A particle, implying.
    1 Difference, (but, or.)
    2 Disjunction, (but, again, further, other.)
    3 Connexion, (and, moreover.)
    4 Asseveration, (indeed.)
    5 An expletive. [379]
