• takṣa r. 1st cl. (takṣati)
    1 To cover.
    2 To skin or peel. (ū) takṣū r. 1st cl. (takṣṇoti) To pare, to plane, to make thin, to cut off, to chip or slice; used in any other sense, this root takes the regular form only, whether simple or compound, as with sam prefixed, To censure, (santakṣati,) &c.
