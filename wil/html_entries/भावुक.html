• bhāvuka mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Happy, well, right.
    2 Actually being or disposed to be.
    3 (In composition with other nouns,) Becoming what it was not before, as āḍhyaṃbhāvuka one who becomes rich. n. (-kaṃ) Happiness, auspiciousness. m. (-kaḥ) A sister's husband, (in theatrical language.)
    E. bhū to be, aff. ukañ; used in composition, the aff. is khukañ.
