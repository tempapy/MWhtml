• sārtha [921] mfn. (-rthaḥ-rthā-rthaṃ) 1 Opulent, wealthy. 2 Of like meaning or purport. 3 Significant, importing, having meaning. m. (-rthaḥ) 1 A multitude of similar animals. 2 A company of traders, a caravan, &c. 3 A number or multitude in general. 4 A wealthy man. E. sṛ to go, causal form, than Uṇādi aff., or sa for saha, &c., and artha wealth, or meaning.
