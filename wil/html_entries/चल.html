• cala r. 1st cl. (calati) To tremble, to shake or totter. r. 6th cl. (calati) To sport, to play, to frolic or wanton. r. 10th cl. (-cālayati) To cherish, to foster. (cālayati, calayati) To shake, to cause to shake. With pra, To fall as from a seat.
  • cala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Trembling, tremulous, unfixed or unsteady. m. (-laḥ) Trembling, shaking. f. (-lā)
    1 The goddess of fortune, LAKṢMĪ.
    2 Lightning.
    3 Incense.
    E. cala to go, affix ac, fem. affix ṭāp.
