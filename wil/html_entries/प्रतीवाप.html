• pratīvāpa m. (-paḥ)
    1 Calcining or fluxing metals.
    2 Throwing one substance into another to alter its form or state.
    3 Adding any thing to a medicine during or after decoction.
    4 A public danger or calamity, as a plague, &c.
    E. prati before vapa to spread, aff. ghañ, whence the vowel of the prefix is lengthened. [569]
