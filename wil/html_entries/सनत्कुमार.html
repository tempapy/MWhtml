• sanatkumāra [887] m. (-raḥ) 1 One of the four sons of BRAHMĀ, and eldest of the progenitors of mankind. 2 One of the twelve emperors of India according to the Jainas. E. sanat always, kumāra a youth: i. e. continent; retaining the purity of that age, or being devoid of human passion; otherwise, sanat BRAHMĀ, and kumāra son.
