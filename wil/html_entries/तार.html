• tāra mfn. adj. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 High, as a note in music.
    2 Radiant, shining, radiating.
    3 Clean, clear.
    4 Good, excellent, well flavoured, &c. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A high note or tone in music.
    2 Elegance of a pearl.
    3 A clear or beautiful pearl.
    4 Crossing, passing over, &c.
    5 A wire, a chord.
    6 The mystic monosyllable. Om.
    7 A mystical monosyllable in the Tantras.
    8 The name of a monkey chief, the son of BĀLĪ. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Silver.
    2 A pearl. mfn. subst. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 The pupil of the eye.
    2 A star in general, a planet, an asterism, &c. f. (-rā) A female deity peculiar to the Bauddha sect.
    2 The wife of VṚHASPATI.
    3 The wife of the monkey king SUGRĪVA.
    4 A name of the goddess DURGĀ.
    E. tṝ to pass or proceed; also in the causal, to cause to pass, and ac or ghañ aff. [373]
