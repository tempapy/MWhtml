• japa r. 1st cl. (japati)
    1 To speak, to say or tell.
    2 To meditate, to speak internally, to say to one's self.
    With upa prefixed, To divide.
  • japa m. (-paḥ) Muttering prayers, repeating inaudibly passages from the scriptures, charms, names of a deity, counting silently the beads of a rosary, &c. f. (-pā) The China rose, the flower or plant; also javā.
    E. japa to repeat inaudibly, affix ap; the flower is a sacred object.
