• prauḍha mfn. (-ḍhaḥ-ḍhā-ḍhaṃ)
    1 Full-grown.
    2 Confident, bold, arrogant.
    3 Impudent.
    4 Full, (as the moon.)
    5 Thick, heavy, (darkness, &c.).
    5 Forward, (as the hand to seize any thing.)
    7 Raised, lifted up.
    8 Controverted.
    9 Old.
    10 Married. f. (-ḍhā)
    1 A woman from thirty years of age to fifty-five.
    2 A description of the Nāyikā or mistress, a woman whose feelings are violent or impetuous, or one who is not in any awe of her lover or husband.
    E. pra before vaha to bear, aff. kta.
