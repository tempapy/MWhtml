• vītaṃsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 A cage, a chain or net, &c. for confining birds or beasts.
    2 An enclosure for tranquillising or taming them, an aviary, a menagerie.
    3 A place for keeping game, a preserve.
    E. vi before tasi to adorn, aff. ghañ, and the vowel of the prefix made long. [797]
