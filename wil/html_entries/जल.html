• jala r. 1st cl. (jalati)
    1 To be sharp.
    2 To be wealthy. r. 1st and 10th cls. (jalati jālayati) To cover, to hide or veil; to cover also as with a net, to encompass.
  • jala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Cold, stupid, apathetic, idiotic, &c. n. (-laṃ)
    1 Water.
    2 A kind of perfume: see hrīvera.
    3 The uterus of a cow.
    4 Frigidity, (moral, mental, or physical.)
    E. jala to hide, to encompass, &c. affix ac; also la being changed to its congener ḍa, jaḍa q. v.
