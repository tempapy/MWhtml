• svāhā ind. An exclamation used in making an oblation to the gods. f. (-hā)
    1 A personification of the preceding, as the wife of fire, and goddess presiding over burnt offerings.
    2 A female divinity, peculiar to the Bauddhas.
    E. su well, auspiciously, āṅ before hveñ to call, to invoke, (the gods,) and ḍā aff., or svāda to taste, aff. ā, and da changed to ha. [966]
