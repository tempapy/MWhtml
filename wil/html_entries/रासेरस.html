• rāserasa m. (-saḥ)
    1 An assembly, a company, a party.
    2 A game or festival peculiar to the cowherds: see rāsa.
    3 Love, desire, the passion or sentiment.
    4 Alchymy: see rasasiddhi.
    5 The sixth night after delivery, on which the family and friends sit up all night.
    6 Sport, mirth, pastime.
    7 Any festival or holiday.
    E. rāsa noise, &c., and rasa flavour, taste.
