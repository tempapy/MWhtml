• udghāṭana n. (-naṃ)
    1 The rope and bucket of a well, a leather bucket used for drawing water.
    2 Opening, unlocking.
    3 An opener, the instrument or means of opening, a key, &c.
    4 Hosting, raising, lifting up.
    E. uda up, ghaṭa to strive, affix lyuṭ; also with vuñ affix udghāṭaka.
