• kimpuruṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 A heavenly musician: see kinnara.
    2 A division of the universe, one of the nine Khaṇḍas or portions into which the world is divided, and described as the country between the Himācala and Hemakūṭa mountains.
    3 A despicable or low man.
    E. kim what, contemptuously, and puruṣa a man; also with the first vowel of that word lengthened, kimpūruṣa.
