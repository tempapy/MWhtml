• kathāprasaṅga mfn. (-ṅgaḥ-ṅgā-ṅgaṃ)
    1 Talkative, talking much and foolishly.
    2 Half-witted, foolish.
    3 A conjuror, a dealer in antidotes, &c. m. (-ṅgaḥ)
    1 Talking, conversation, speaking to or with.
    2 Rumour, report.
    E. kathā a fable, and prasaṅga who relates.
