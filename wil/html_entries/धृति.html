• dhṛti [447] m. (-tiḥ) Sacrifice, offering, religious rite or ceremony. f. (-tiḥ) 1 Holding, having. 2 Steadiness, firmness. 3 One of the astrological Yogas: see yoga. 4 Pleasure. 5 Satisfaction, content. 6 Happiness. 7 A metre; a stanza of four lines of eighteen syllables each. 8 The thirteenth Mātṛkā. E. dhṛ to have, &c. affix ktin.
