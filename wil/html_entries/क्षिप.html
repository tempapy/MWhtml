• kṣipa (au) aukṣipa r. 4th cl. (kṣipyati) (ña, ā) aukṣipañ. r. 6th cl. (kṣipati-te) To throw or cast, to direct or send. With adhi prefixed, To abuse, to censure or reproach.
    With ava, To throw down.
    With āṅ.
    1 To attract.
    2 To ridicule.
    With ut, To raise up, &c.
    With ni, To place, to deposit.
    With pra, To throw far or violently.
    With vi, To scatter, to disperse.
    With vi and ni, To deliver over, to resign, to leave.
    With sama and āṅ, To remove, to send away.
    With sam.
    1 To abridge, to compress or contract.
    2 To disappear.
  • kṣipa m. (-paḥ)
    1 Throwing, casting.
    2 Reviling.
    3 One who throws, &c. f. (-pā)
    1 Sending, casting, throwing, &c.
    2 Night. see kṣapā.
    E. kṣipa to throw, affix ka.
