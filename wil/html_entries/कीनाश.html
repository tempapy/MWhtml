• kīnāśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śī-śaṃ)
    1 A labourer or cultivator of the soil.
    2 Small, little.
    3 Covetous, niggardly.
    4 A slayer of cattle. m. (-śaḥ)
    1 A name of YAMA.
    2 A particular monkey.
    E. kī for kā or ku bad, vile, nāśa who destroys, the causal form of naśa to destroy, with ac aff. [223]
