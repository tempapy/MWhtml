• kalmāṣa [203] mfn. (-ṣaḥ-ṣā or ṣī-ṣaṃ) Of a mixed or variegated colour. m. (-ṣaḥ) 1 A variegated colour. 2 A mixture of black and white. 3 Black. 4 A demon or goblin. f. (-ṣī) The spotted cow of Jamadagni, the giver of all desires. E. kala to go, &c. kvip affix, kal, maṣa to injure, aṇ affix māṣa; what injures or triumphs over other colours.
