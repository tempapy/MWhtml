• atri [019] m. (-triḥ) The name of one of the seven Ṛṣis or saints, born from the eye of BRAHMĀ, married to ANASŪYĀ, daughter of KARDAMA MUNI, and the father of DATTA or DATTĀTREYA, DURVĀSAS and CANDRA. E. ada to eat and trip Uṇādi aff. The correct reading is attri, but the word is always written with one ta.
