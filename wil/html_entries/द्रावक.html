• drāvaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A kind of stone, (a loadstone.)
    2 A wit, a wag, a sharp or clever man.
    3 A thief.
    4 A libertine, a lecher.
    5 A pursuer, a chaser, one who causes to fly.
    6 A sort of Rasa or quality, n. (-kaṃ) Bee's wax.
    E. dru to fly, affix ṇvul. [429]
