• mahākāvya n. (-vyaṃ) A regular and classical poem.
    E. mahā great, and kāvya a poem: the title is applied especially to six works; the Kumāra Sambhava, Raghu Vaṃśa, and Megha Dūta of KĀLIDĀSA; the Kirātārjunīya of BHĀRAVĪ; the Naiṣadhīya of ŚRĪHARṢA, and the Śiśupāla Badha of MĀGHA.
