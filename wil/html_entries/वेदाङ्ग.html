• vedāṅga m. (-ṅgaḥ) A sacred science, considered as subordinate to, and in some sense, a part of the Vedas: six sciences come under this denomination; Śīkṣā, or the science of pronunciation and articulation; Kalpa the detail of religious ceremonies; Vyākaraṇa, or grammar; Candas, prosody; Jyotiṣ, or astronomy; and Nirukti, or the explanation of the difficult or obscure words and phrases that occur in the Vedas.
    E. veda the Vedas, and aṅga a member. [806]
