• agrahāyaṇa m. (-ṇaḥ) The name of a month; the eighth of the lunar year of the Hindus, when the moon is full near the head of Orion, or about November-December.
    E. agra and hāyana, year; according to the ancient system, the first month of the Hindu year: also āgrahāyaṇa. [008]
