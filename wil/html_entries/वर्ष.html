• varṣa r. 1st cl. (-varṣati) To be wet. [740]
  • varṣa mn. (-rṣaḥ-rṣaṃ)
    1 Rain, raining.
    2 Sprinkling, affusion.
    3 A year.
    4 A Varṣa, or division of the known continent; nine are reckoned; Kuru, Hiraṇmaya, Romānaka, Ilāvṛta, Hari, Ketumālā, Bhadrāśva, Kinnara, and Bharata.
    5 Jambu Dvīpa, or India.
    6 A cloud. m. plu. (-rṣāḥ) The rains or rainy season, containing two months, according to the Hindu classification of the seasons, which some systems consider to be Śrāvaṇa and Bhādra, and others, Bhādra and Āśvina; the duration of the monsoon is however longer, being reckoned from Āṣārha to Kārttika, or from the middle of June to the beginning of October. f. sing. (-rṣā) A sort of gramineous plant: see pṛkkā.
    E. vṛṣa to sprinkle, aff. ac, or vṝ to screen, &c., Uṇādi aff. sa. [740]
