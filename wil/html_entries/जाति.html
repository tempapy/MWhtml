• jāti [347] f. (-tiḥ) 1 Kind, sort, species, class, tribe. 2 Birth, production. 3 Lineage, race, family. 4 A fire place, a chimney. 5 Emblic myrobalan, (Phyllanthus emblica.) 6 A plant: see kāmpilla. 7 A kind of poetical metre. 8 A rhetorical ornament. 9 (In Arithmetic,) Assimilation, as viśeṣajāti assimilation of the difference, śeṣajāti of the remainder. 10 Proximate cause or occurrence of disease, &c. f. (-tiḥ or -tī) 1 Great flowered jasmin, (J. grandiflorum.) 2 Mace, nutmeg. f. (-tiḥ) The flower of the jasmin. E. jana to be born, affix ktic, and occasionally ṅīṣ added.
