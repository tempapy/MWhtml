• śatānanda [828] m. (-ndaḥ) 1 BRAHMĀ. 2 VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA. 3 The car of VIṢṆU. 4 The Muni GAUTAMA, the founder of the logical school of philosophy. 5 The eldest son of GAUTAMA, (according to the Rāmāyaṇa,) and Purohita or religious adviser of JANAKA of Mithila. E. śata a hundred or many, and ānanda who delights.
