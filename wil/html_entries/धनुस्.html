• dhanus mfn. (-nuḥ-nuḥ-nu) Armed with a bow. mn. (-nuḥ-nu)
    1 A bow.
    2 Sagittarius, the sign. m. (-nuḥ) The Piyal tree, (Buchanania latifolia, Rox.)
    E. dhana to produce, (as grain,) to throw forth, (darts, &c.) Uṇādi affix usi; also with u affix dhanu, and with ū affix, dhanū. [438]
