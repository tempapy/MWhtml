• śatika [828] mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) 1 Indicative of a hundred, (as a sign or omen.) 2 Doing or effecting any thing with a hundred. 3 Bearing tax or interest per hundred. 4 Bought with a hundred. 5 Changed with or for a hundred, &c. 6 Relating or belonging to a hundred. E. śata a hundred, and ṭhaka or ṭhañ or ṭhan aff.; also with yat aff. śatya in the same acceptations.
