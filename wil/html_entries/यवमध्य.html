• yavamadhya n. (-dhyaṃ) A form of penance, diminishing the food daily during the dark fortnight, fasting on the new moon, and gradually augmenting the food till the full moon. f. (-dhyā) A form of metre, a triplet, the divisions of which are of unequal length, the middle line having one syllable more than the first or last.
    E. yava barley, madhya centrical. [682]
