• nivṛtti [479] f. (-ttiḥ) 1 Cessation, completion, leaving off, rest, repose. 2 Discontinuance of worldly acts, cessation of emotions, separation from the world. 3 Denial, refusal. 4 Abolition, prevention. 5 Returning. E. ni negative prefix, vṛta to be, aff. ktin: see nirvṛtti.
