• karabha m. (-bhaḥ)
    1 The metacarpus, the hand from the wrist to the root of the fingers.
    2 A young camel or any young animal.
    3 A young elephant.
    4 A perfume, commonly Nakhi. f. (-bhī) A she camel.
    E. kṛ to reject, abhaca Uṇādi affix: see kalabha. [192]
