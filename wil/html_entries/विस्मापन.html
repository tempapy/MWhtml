• vismāpana [795] m. (-naḥ) 1 Deceit, illusion. 2 A magical or enchanted city, one that appears and disappears unexpectedly, or in unexpected situations, a city of the Gandharbas. 3 KĀMA or CUPID. 4 Astonishing, surprising, causing wonder or surprise. E. vi before smi to smile, causal form, aff. lyuṭ.
