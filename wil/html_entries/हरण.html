• haraṇa n. (-ṇaṃ) Taking, (either in a good or bad sense,) accepting, receiving, seizing, carrying off or away.
    2 A special gift, as a nuptial present, alms to a student at his initiation, &c.
    3 The arm.
    4 Semen virile.
    5 Gold.
    6 A Kowri.
    7 Boiling water.
    8 (In arithmetic,) Dividing, division. m. (-ṇaḥ) The hand.
    E. hṛ to take, aff. lyuṭ.
