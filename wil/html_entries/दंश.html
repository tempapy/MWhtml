• daṃśa r. 1st cl. (daṃśati) To bite, to sting.
  • daṃśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 A gadfly.
    2 A tooth.
    3 Biting, stinging.
    4 Armour, mail.
    5 Cutting, dividing, tearing.
    6 Fault, defect.
    7 A joint, a limb.
    8 The sting of a snake.
    9 Pungency. f. (-śī) A small gadfly.
    E. daṃśa to bite or sting, affix ac, diminutive affix ṅīṣ.
