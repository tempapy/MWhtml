• dhara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ) Who or what has or holds. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A mountain.
    2 A flock of cotton.
    3 The tortoise Avatāra.
    4 One of the demigods called VASUS. f. (-rā)
    1 The earth.
    2 The uterus or womb.
    3 Marrow.
    4 A vessel of the body.
    5 A golden globe or heap of valuables, representing the earth, and given to Brahmans.
    E. dhṛñ to have or hold, to contain or support, &c. affix ac, or ṭa fem. affix ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
