• kiyat mfn. (-yān-yatī-yat)
    1 How much, how many. ind. (yat)
    1 A little.
    2 How much, used chiefly in composition; as kiyatkāla how long, for a short time; kiyadavadhi or kiyatyaryyantaṃ how far, or for a little way.
    E. kim and vatup affix, and ya is substituted for the va.
