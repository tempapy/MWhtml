• vaikakṣaka [809] n. (-kaṃ) A garland or string of flowers worn as a scarf or like the sacrificial cord, over the left shoulder and under the right arm, hanging down below the hip. E. kan added to the last; also as derived from vi, and kakṣa with ṭhak aff. vaikakṣika n. (-kaṃ)
