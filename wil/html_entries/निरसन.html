• nirasana mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) What removes, expels, subdues, checks, &c. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Rejection, denial, contradiction, disallowance.
    2 Suppressing, checking.
    3 Appeasing.
    4 Expelling, ejecting.
    5 Spitting, spitting out any thing with sickness or disgust.
    6 Killing, destroying.
    E. nir out or forth, asa to throw, affix lyuṭ or yuc. [471]
