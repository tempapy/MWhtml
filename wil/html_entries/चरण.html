• caraṇa [319] mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ) 1 A foot. 2 The root of a tree. 3 A race, a family. 4 A portion of the Vedas. 5 Fixed or instituted observances. 6 The peculiarity of condition or conduct implied by the English affix hood, as manhood, priesthood, &c. 7 The fourth part of a stanza. 8 (In Prosody,) A dactyl. n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 Wandering, roaming, going round or about. 2 Eating. E. cara to go, affix lyuṭ.
