• vila [782] r. 6th cl. (vilati) 1 To wear, to put on, to clothe or cover. 2 To break or divide. r. 10th cl. (velayati) To throw, to cast, to direct or send.
  • vila [782] n. (-laṃ) 1 A hole, a chasm, a vacuity. 2 A cave, a cavern. m. (-laḥ) 1 One of INDRA'S horses. 2 A sort of reed or cane, (Calamus rotang.) E. vila to divide, &c., aff. ka.
