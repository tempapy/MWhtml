• nīvi f. (-viḥ-vī)
    1 Capital, principal, stock.
    2 Stake, wager.
    3 A cloth round a woman's waist, or the ends of the cloth passed round the loins so as to hold the whole together; on being loosed the lower members are exposed: the tie of drawers worn by women, &c.
    4 The outer tie of a packet, in which the offerings of a Śūdra at funeral obsequies are presented.
    E. ni before vyeñ to cover, Uṇādi aff. in and ṅīṣ optionally added; the i of ni is made long, and the radical finals rejected.
