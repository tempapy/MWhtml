• priyaṅgu [590] (-ṅguḥ) 1 A medicinal plant, and perfume, commonly known by the name Priyangu, and described in some places as a fragrant seed. 2 Panick seed, (Panicum italicum.) 3 Black mustard seed. 4 Long pepper. 5 A peculiar tree: see kaṭukī. E. priya desired, gama to go, aff. ḍun and mum aug.
