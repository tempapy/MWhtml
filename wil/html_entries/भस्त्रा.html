• bhastrā f. (-strā) A bellows, a large hide with valves, and a clay nozzle, which is used for this purpose.
    E. bhasa to shine, trana Uṇādi aff., fem. aff. ṭāp; also with ṅīṣ aff. bhastrī, and with kan in the fem. form added bhastrakā or bhastrikā optionally; and with the feminine termination retained also, bhastrākā.
