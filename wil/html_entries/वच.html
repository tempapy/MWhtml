• vaca(au)auvaca r. 1st cl. (vacati)
    2nd cl. (vakti) and 10th cl. (vācayati)
    1 To speak, to tell.
    2 To inform.
    3 To read, to expound: (the second cl. is the most usual form of the root, in which its inflections however are very irregular.)
    With pra, To begin to speak. [729]
  • vaca m. (-caḥ) A parrot. f. (-cā)
    1 Orris root, (Acorus calamus; also Zinziber Zedoaria.)
    2 The Sārikā, (Turdus Salica.)
    E. vaca to speak, ac aff. [729]
