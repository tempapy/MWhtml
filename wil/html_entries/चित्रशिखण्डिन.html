• citraśikhaṇḍina m. plu. (-ṇḍinaḥ) The seven sages or Ṛṣis, but especially as represented in the seven principal stars of the great bear; these sages are MARĪCI, ATRI, AṄGIRAS, PULAHA, PULASTYA, KRATU, and VASIṢṬHA.
    E. citra surprising, śikhaṇḍa a crest, and ini affix; sing. nom. case, śikhaṇḍin (-ṇḍī). [326]
