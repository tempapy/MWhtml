• khara [271] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Hot. 2 Sharp, pungent. 3 Sharp, sharp-edged, cutting. 4 Cruel, harsh. mn. (-raḥ-raṃ) Heat. m. (-raḥ) 1 An ass. 2 A Rākṣasa, the brother of RĀVAṆA. 3 A Daitya or demon in general. 4 A sort of prickly nightshade. 5 A crow. 6 A heron. 7 An osprey. f. (-rā) A kind of grass, (Andropogon serratus.) E. kha an organ of sense, and rā to get or give, affix ka.
