• āgneya [105] mfn. (-yaḥ-yī-yaṃ) Belonging or relating to fire or its deity. n. (-yaṃ) 1 The name of a city, (Devikote, on the Coromandel Coast.) 2 Blood. 3 Ghee or boiled butter. m. (-yaḥ) A name of the saint AGASTI. f. (-yī) 1 The wife of AGNI. 2 The southeast quarter, of which AGNI is the regent. E. agni fire or its deity, ḍhak affix which leaves ṇya.
