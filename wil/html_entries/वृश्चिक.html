• vṛścika [802] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A scorpion. 2 The sign Scorpio. 3 A hairy caterpillar. 4 A crab. 5 A sort of beetle found in cow-dung. 6 A centipede. 7 A thorny shrub, (Vangueria spinosa.) 8 The month when the sun is in Scorpio. f. (-kā) A potherb, (Basella.) E. vraśca to cut, Uṇādi aff. kikan, and the semivowel changed to the vowel.
