• vipralambha m. (-mbhaḥ)
    1 Tricking, deceiving, disappointing, deceiving by a false affirmation or by not keeping a promise.
    2 Separation, especially of lovers.
    3 Disunion, disjunction.
    4 Quarrel, disagreement.
    E. vi and pra implying reverse, labhi to get or gain, aff. ghañ.
