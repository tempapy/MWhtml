• mahauṣadha n. (-dhaṃ)
    1 Garlic.
    2 A plant, commonly Atis, (Betula.)
    3 Long pepper. nf. (-dhaṃ-dhī) Dry ginger. f. (-dhī) A potherb, (Hingtsha repens.)
    E. mahā great, and auṣadha a drug; this word is of course applicable to a great number of plants and substances. [654]
