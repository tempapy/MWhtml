• vāruṇī [754] f. (-ṇī) 1 Any spirituous liquor, or more properly, a particular kind prepared from hogweed, ground with the juice of the date or palm, and then distilled. 2 The west, the region of VARUṆA. 3 The 25th lunar asterism, of which VARUṆA is the ruling deity. 4 A species of Dūrbbā grass. E. varuṇa the deity VARUṆA, aṇ and ṅīṣ affs.
