• paśu [520] m. (-śuḥ) 1 An animal in general, a beast. 2 A goat. 3 A subordinate deity, and one of ŚIVA'S followers. 4 Any living being. 5 A sacrifice, an oblation. 6 The divine soul of the universe. 7 The glomerous fig tree. ind. See, behold. E. dṛśa to see, paśa substituted, and ku Uṇādi aff.
