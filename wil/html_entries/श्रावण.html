• śrāvaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇī-ṇaṃ)
    1 Produced in or under the asterism Śravaṇā.
    2 Relating to the ear or hearing. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 The month Śrāvaṇa or Śravaṇā, (July-August.)
    2 A heretic, an impostor. n. (ṇaṃ) Causing to be heard. f. (-ṇā) A plant, commonly Katuki. f. (-ṇī) Day of full moon in the month Śrāvaṇa.
    E. śravaṇā the asterism, &c., aff. aṇ. [862]
