• śaudra mfn. (-draḥ-drī-draṃ) Śaudra, servile, relating or belonging to the last class. m. (-draḥ) The son of a man of either of the first three classes by a Śūdra woman, the last of the twelve sons acknowledged in the ancient Hindu law.
    E. śūdra a Śūdra, and aṇ aff. [859]
