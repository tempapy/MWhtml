• agha [008] r. 1st cl. With an indicatory i. yaghi (aṃghati.) 1 To go. 2 To go swiftly. 3 To begin moving. 4 To begin. 5 To blame or censure. r. 10th cl. (aghayati) To sin, to commit sin.
  • agha [008] n. (-ghaṃ) 1 Sin. 2 Pain. 3 Passion. m. (-ghaḥ) Name of a demon; the general of KAṂSA. E. agha to go, and ac affix, removed by charity, &c.
