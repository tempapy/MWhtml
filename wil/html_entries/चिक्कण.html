• cikkaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ)
    1 Unctuous, greasy.
    2 Bland, emollient.
    3 Smooth, glossy.
    4 Slippery. m. (-ṇaḥ) The betel-nut tree. nf. (-ṇaṃ-ṇā or ṇī)
    1 The betel-nut.
    2 A good cow.
    E. cita to think of, Uṇādi affix kan, the final of the radical changed to ka. [324]
