• ijya mfn. (-jyaḥ-jyā-jyaṃ) A teacher. m. (-jyaḥ) VṚHASPATI, the teacher or Guru of the gods. f. (-jyā)
    1 A gift, a donation.
    2 Sacrificing, making offerings to the gods or manes.
    3 Worship, reverence.
    4 Meeting, union.
    5 A cow.
    6 A bawd or procuress.
    E. yaja to sacrifice, &c. kyap aff. [130]
