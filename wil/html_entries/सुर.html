• sura [934] r. 6th cl. (surati) 1 To possess supreme or superhuman power. 2 To shine; also ṣura.
  • sura [934] m. (-raḥ) 1 A god, a deity. 2 The sun. 3 A sage, a learned man. f. (-rā-rī) 1 Spirituous liquor in general personified: also as a nymph, produced at the churning of the ocean. 2 A drinking vessel. 3 A snake. E. ṣu to possess power, Uṇādi aff. rak, or ṣura to be radiant, ka affix, or surā wine (whose, that falling to the share of the gods,) or su excellent, rāja to shine, aff. ḍa, &c.
