• pratiyatna [566] mfn. (-tnaḥ-tnā-tnaṃ) Active, vigorous, diligent, making effort or exertion. m. (-tnaḥ) 1 Desire, wish. 2 Taking prisoner, making captive. 3 Comprehension. 4 Acting well or properly. 5 Acquiring a new virtue or accomplishment. 6 Making complete or perfect. 7 A making or preparing. 8 Retaliating, recrimination, revenge. 9 Opposition, counteraction. E. prati much or against, and yatna effort.
