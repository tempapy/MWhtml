• suparvvan m. (-rvvā)
    1 A god, a deity.
    2 An arrow.
    3 A bambu.
    4 A lunar day, in which religious ceremonies are particularly performed, as the new moon, and the eighth and the fourteenth days of each fortnight.
    5 Smoke.
    E. su good or much, and parvvan a knot or joint. [932]
