• vāha(ṛ)vāhṛ [757] r. 1st cl. (vāhate) To endeavour: the root is preferably read vāhṛ.
  • vāha [757] m. (-haḥ) 1 A horse. 2 A measure of capacity containing ten Kumbhas, or nearly equal to the weight of an English wey or a ton in freight. 3 A bull. 4 Air, wind. 5 The arm. 6 A buffalo. 7 A vehicle, a carriage or conveyance of any kind. 8 A bearer, a porter, a carrier of burthens, &c. E. vaha to bear, aff. ghañ.
