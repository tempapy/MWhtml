• kaumuda [254] m. (-daḥ) The month Kartik, (Oct.-Nov.) f. (-dī) 1 Moonlight. 2 A festival. 3 A festival in honour of KĀRTTIKEYA, held on the full moon of the month Kartik. 4 Day of full moon in the month Aśvin. 5 The same in the month Kartik. E. kumuda a white lotus, aṇ and ṅīṣ affixes, kaumudī moonlight, equally white or brilliant; the other senses come from this.
