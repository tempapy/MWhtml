• rāmagiri m. (-riḥ) The name of a mountain, variously applied, but especially assigned to a mountain called Compteh or Citrakūṭa in Bundelkund, and to another near Nagpore, called now Rāmtek.
    E. rāma the hero, and giri a mountain, the mountain of RĀMA; being one of his halting places in his progress from Oude to the Peninsula.
