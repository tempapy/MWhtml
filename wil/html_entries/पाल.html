• pāla r. 1st and 10th cls. (pālayati) To guard, to preserve or protect. [531]
  • pāla mfn. (-laḥ-lī-laṃ) Who or what guards or preserves. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A nourisher, a protector.
    2 A spitting pot. f. (-lī)
    1 The sharp edge of a sword.
    2 A woman with a beard.
    3 A louse.
    4 A line, a row or range.
    5 A pot, a boiler.
    E. pāla to nourish, aff. ac; ṅīṣ added; also with i aff. pāli. [531]
