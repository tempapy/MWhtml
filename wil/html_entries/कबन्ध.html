• kabandha mn. (-ndhaḥ-ndhaṃ) A headless trunk, especially retaining vitality. n. (-ndhaṃ) Water. m. (-ndhaḥ)
    1 A name of RĀHU.
    2 Also of a demon destroyed by RĀMA.
    3 The belly.
    E. kaṃ the head, and badha to injure, to lop, affix ac; also kaṃ water, and bandha binding, attaching. [190]
