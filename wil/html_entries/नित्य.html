• nitya mfn. adj. (-tyaḥ-tyā-tyaṃ)
    1 Eternal, everlasting, continual, perpetual; past, present, and future.
    2 Regular, fixed, invariable. n. adv. (-tyaṃ) Always, eternally, continually. sub. Indispensable rite or act. m. (-tyaḥ) The ocean. f. (-tyā)
    1 A name of PĀRVATĪ.
    2 The goddess MANASĀ.
    3 A Śakti, a form of DURGĀ, or personified energy of ŚIVA.
    E. ni particle implying perpetually, and tyap aff. [467]
