• kṛṣṇapāka m. (-kaḥ) A tree bearing a small fruit, which, when ripe, is of a black colour, commonly Karinda or Karonda, (Carissa carondas.)
    E. kṛṣṇa black, and pāka what is ripe, from paca to ripen, to cook, and ghañ affix; also kṛṣṇaphala, and kṛṣṇapākaphala. [246]
