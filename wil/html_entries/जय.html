• jaya [341] m. (-yaḥ) 1 Conquest, victory, triumph. 2 A name of YUDHIṢṬHIRA. 3 A proper name, the son of INDRA. 4 One of VIṢṆU'S doorkeepers. 5 The name of a king; also vijayanandana. 6 A kind of Mūṅg or bean, (Phaseolus mungo.) f. (-yā) 1 A name of the goddess PĀRVATĪ. 2 One of her female companions. 3 The 3rd, 8th, or 13th lunar day of either half month. 4 Yellow myrobalan, (Terminalia chebula.) 5 A plant: see jayantī. 6 A tree, commonly Ganiyari, (Premna spinosa.) 7 Hemp, (Cannabis sativa.) 8 A banner, a flag. E. ji to conquer or excel, affixes ac and ṭāp.
