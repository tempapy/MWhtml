• kola m. (-laḥ)
    1 A hog.
    2 A raft, a float.
    3 The haunch, the hip or flank.
    4 Embracing, an embrace.
    5 The planet Śani or SATURN.
    6 Plumbago, (Zeylanica, &c.)
    7 A country, Kaliṅga, the modern seacoast from Cuttak to Madras.
    8 A kind of weapon.
    9 An outcaste, one of a tribe degraded by SAGARA from the military order.
    10 A man of a mixed caste.
    11 A barbarian, a Kol, people inhabiting the hills and forests in central India. n. (-laṃ)
    1 The fruit of the jujube.
    2 The weight of one Tola.
    3 Black pepper. f. (-lā or -lī)
    1 The jujube tree.
    2 Long pepper.
    3 A kind of pepper, (Piper chavya, Rox.)
