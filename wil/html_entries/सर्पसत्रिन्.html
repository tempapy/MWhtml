• sarpasatrin m. (-trī) A name of the prince JANAMEJAYA, the son of PARIKṢIT.
    E. sarpa a snake, and saca a sacrifice, ini aff.; having compelled by Mantras all the snakes to be present at a sacrifice performed for their destruction, in retaliation of the death of his father who was killed by the bite of a snake; all were destroyed on this occasion except a few of the chiefs.
