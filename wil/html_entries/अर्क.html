• arka r. 10th cl. (arkayati)
    1 To praise.
    2 To heat or warm. [068]
  • arka m. (-rkaḥ)
    1 The sun.
    2 Crystal.
    3 Swallow wort, (Calotropis gigantea.)
    4 Copper.
    5 A name of INDRA.
    6 A Paṇḍita or learned man.
    7 An elder brother.
    8 Sunday.
    E. arca to worship or respect, ka Uṇādi affix, and the ca is dropped; what is worshipped or respected: or arka to heat, affix ac. [068]
