• trasa [388] (ī) trasī r. 1st and 4th cl. (trasati trasyati) 1 To fear, to be afraid. 2 To run, to go quickly. (i) trasi r. 1st and 10th cls. (traṃsati traṃsayati) 1 To speak. 2 To shine. Also trasa r. 10th cl. (trāsayati) 1 To hold. 2 To seize or take. 3 To oppose, to prevent or forbid. 4 To frighten.
  • trasa [388] mfn. (-saḥ-sā-saṃ) Moveable, loco-motive. n. (-saṃ) A wood, a forest. E. trasa to go, affix ac.
