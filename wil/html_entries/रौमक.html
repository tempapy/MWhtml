• raumaka [713] n. (-kaṃ) A kind of salt, according to some authorities, brought from a mountain in Ajmere, but in fact procured from a salt lake near the town of Sāmbher, about twenty miles west of Jayanagar. E. ruma the district of Ruma or Sāmbher, aff. aṇ, and kan added; also rauma, and raumalavaṇa.
