• prājña mfn. (-jñaḥ-jñā-jñaṃ)
    1 Patient in investigation.
    2 Wise, clever, sensible. m. (-jñaḥ)
    1 A Paṇḍita, a learned or wise man.
    2 A skilful or clever man. f. (-jñā) Knowledge, understanding. f. (-jñā-jñī) A clever or intelligent woman. f. (-jñī) The wife of a Paṇḍita.
    E. pra and āṅa before jñā to know, ka aff.
