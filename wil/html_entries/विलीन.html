• vilīna mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ)
    1 Liquid, liquified, semifluid, as melted oil or butter, &c.
    2 Disappeared, perished, removed from sight, either temporarily or permanently.
    3 Imagined, formed or infused, as in the mind.
    4 Contiguous to, in contact with, united or blended with.
    E. vi before lī to melt, &c., aff. kta.
