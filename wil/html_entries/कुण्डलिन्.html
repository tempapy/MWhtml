• kuṇḍalin mfn. (-lī-linī-li) Having an earring. m. (-lī)
    1 A name of VARUṆA regent of the water.
    2 A snake.
    3 A peacock.
    4 The spotted or painted deer. f. (-nī)
    1 A Śakti or form of DURGĀ, worshipped by the Tāntrikas.
    2 A plant, (Menispermum glabrum.)
    3 Curds boiled with Ghee and spices.
    E. kuṇḍala an earring, and ini affix; the rings on the snake, the eyes in the peacock's tail, &c., being compared to this ornament.
