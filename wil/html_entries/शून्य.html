• śūnya [854] mfn. (-nyaḥ-nyā-nyaṃ) 1 Empty, void. 2 Lonely, desart. n. (-nyaṃ) 1 Heaven, sky, æther. 2 A dot, a spot. 3 A cypher. 4 A vacuum. f. (-nyā) 1 A hollow reed. 2 The prickly pear. E. śuna a dog, yat aff. of fitness, and the vowel substituted for the semivowel, and made optionally long.
