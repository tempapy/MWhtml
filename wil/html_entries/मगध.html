• magadha [631] m. (-dhaḥ) 1 A country, South Behār. 2 An inhabitant of that country. 3 A bard, whose peculiar province is to sing the praises of a chief's ancestry in his presence, a family bard or minstrel. f. (-dhā) Long pepper. E. magadha a Kaṇḍvādi verb, to ask, aff. ac or magadha the country, aṇ aff., with alteration of the vowel optional; hence also māgadha.
