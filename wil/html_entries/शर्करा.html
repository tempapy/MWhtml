• śarkarā f. (-rā)
    1 A potsherd, the fragment of a broken pot.
    2 A stony nodule, or gravel.
    3 A soil or spot abounding in stony or gritty fragments.
    4 Clayed or candid sugar.
    5 A part, a piece.
    6 Gravel, (the disease.)
    E. śṝ to injure, Uṇādi aff. karan.
