• vidita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Known, understood.
    2 Promised, agreed, assented.
    3 Represented, submitted, solicited.
    4 Apprised, informed, who or what knows. m. (-taḥ) A sage, a learned man. n. (-taṃ) Information, representation.
    E. vida to know, &c., aff. kta. [768]
