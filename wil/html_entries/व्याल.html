• vyāla mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Wicked, villainous, bad.
    2 Cruel, fierce. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A snake.
    2 A beast of prey.
    3 A rogue, a cheat.
    4 A vicious elephant.
    5 A king.
    6 A species of the Daṇḍaka metre.
    E. vi and āṅ before ala to adorn, or aḍa to make effort, aff. ac; also vyāḍa. [820]
