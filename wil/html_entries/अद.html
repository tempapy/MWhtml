• ada [019] (au) r. 2d cl. (atti) To eat. The root is imperfect; ghasa being substituted for it in several inflections; as jaghāsa, &c. Implying injury, it is not conjugated in the middle voice, although the action be reciprocal: as vyatyadanti they bite, or devour each other. With an indicatory i, adi (andati) To bind.
