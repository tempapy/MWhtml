• kacchapa [179] m. (-paḥ) 1 A turtle, a tortoise. 2 One of KUVERA'S Nidhis or treasures. 3 An attitude in wrestling. 4 A flat kind of still. f. (-pī) 1 A female tortoise, also a small one. 2 A kind of lute, also the lute of SARASVATĪ. 3 A cutaneous disease, wart or blotch. E. kaccha a morass, and pa who cherishes, who inhabits watery places: the lute, &c. are so named from being similar in shape to the tortoise.
