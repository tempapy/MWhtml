• kuśika mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Squint-eyed. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A Muni or divine sage so named, the father of JAMADAGNI.
    2 A ploughshare.
    3 The Śāl tree, (Shorea robusta.)
    4 Beleric myrobalan: see vibhītaka.
    5 The sediment of oil.
    E. kuśa the grass, &c. ikan aff. [236]
