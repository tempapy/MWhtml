• niṣkāsita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Expelled, dismissed, turned out.
    2 Gone forth or out, issued.
    3 Placed, deposited.
    4 Placed over, appointed, stationed.
    5 Reviled, reproached.
    6 Opened out, blown, expanded.
    E. nir out, kasa to go, in the causal form, aff. kta, ra becomes ṣa or Visarga: see niḥkāsita; also niṣkāśita. [481]
