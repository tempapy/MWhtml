• vāsana [756] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Fit for clothes, or a dwelling, &c. n. (-naṃ) 1 Perfuming, fumigating or perfuming with fragrant vapour, or with incense, &c. 2 Cloth, clothes. 3 Abiding, abode. 4 A particular posture in which abstract meditation is performed; it is also a posture conceived to be peculiarly proper in princes and chiefs, and is then described as sitting with the knees bent and the feet turned backwards. 5 Knowledge. 6 A water-jar. 7 A box, a basket, a vessel, &c. 8 An envelope. 9 Any receptacle. 10 Steeping, infusing. f. (-nā) 1 The knowledge of any thing derived from memory, the present consciousness of past perceptions. 2 Fancy, imagination. 3 Trust, confidence. 4 Ignorance. E. vasa to dwell, &c., aff. yuc; or vasana clothes, and aṇ aff.
