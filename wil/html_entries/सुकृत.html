• sukṛta [927] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Virtuous, pious. 2 Befriended, assisted, treated with kindness. 3 Fortunate, auspicious. 4 Done well, properly, ably, &c. n. (-taṃ) 1 Virtue, moral merit. 2 Fortune, auspiciousness. 3 Kindness, bounty, friendly aid or assistance. 4 Any kind or friendly act. 5 Reward, recompense, especially heaven as the reward of virtue. E. su well, and kṛta made or done.
