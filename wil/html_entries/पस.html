• pasa r. 1st and 10th cls. (pasati-te pāsayati pasayati)
    1 To bind, to fetter, to tie, to string.
    2 To touch.
    3 To oppose, to obstruct, to hinder.
    4 To go, to move.
    5 To injure or kill. (i) pasi r. 1st and 10th cls. (paṃsati paṃsayati) To annual, to annihilate or destroy.
