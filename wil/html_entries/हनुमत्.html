• hanumat [968] m. (-mān) 1 The monkey chief HANUMĀN, the son of AÑJANĪ, by PAVANA, or the wind, the friend, ally and spy of RĀMACANDRA, in his invasion of Laṅkā. 2 A particular sort of monkey, (Simia sinica.) E. hanu the jaw, and matup aff.; also with hanū the same, hanūmat m. (-mān.)
