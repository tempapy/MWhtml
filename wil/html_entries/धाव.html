• dhāva [444] (u) dhāvu r. 1st cl. (dhāvati-te) 1 To go or move. 2 To run. 3 To cleanse. 4 To be clean or pure.
    With anu, 1 To know. 2 To run after.
    With abhi prefixed, To approach, to come before.
    With āṅ, To alight.
    With pari, To run fast.
    With vi, To shed or sprinkle.
    With sam and ugra, To run to meet.
