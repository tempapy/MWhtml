• paṅkti [491] f. (-ṅktiḥ-ṅktī) 1 A line, a row or range. 2 A sort of metre, a stanza of four lines, each line consisting of ten syllables. 3 The number ten, (in composition, ut infra.) 4 The earth, 5 Fame, celebrity. 6 Cooking, maturing. 7 Associating with, eating or sitting with, especially with persons of the same caste. 8 A company, a society, an assembly. E. paci to spread, to extend, to make evident, and ktin aff. or paca to cook, deriv. irr.
