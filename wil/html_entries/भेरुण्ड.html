• bheruṇḍa [627] mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā-ṇḍaṃ) Formidable, fearful. f. (-ṇḍā) One of the Yakṣiṇīs, or female attendants on DURGĀ. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A particular deity; a form of ŚIVA, according to the Tantras. n. (-ṇḍaṃ) Conception, impregnation. E. bhī to fear, deriv. irr.; it is sometimes read bheraṇḍa.
