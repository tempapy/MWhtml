• śamba r. 1st cl. (śambati) To go. r. 10th cl. (śambayati) To accumulate, to collect or heap together. [830]
  • śamba mfn. (-mbaḥ-mbā-mbaṃ) Happy, fortunate. m. (-mbaḥ)
    1 INDRA'S thunderbolt.
    2 The iron head of a pestle.
    3 An iron chain worn round the loins.
    4 The second ploughing of a field.
    E. śam happiness, and ba possessive aff., or śamba to accumulate, aff. ac: it is also read saṃva, śaṃva, and samba. [830]
