• plīhan m. (-hā) The spleen; the organ or the disease of it, as in English; in the latter sense, however, it is equally applied to the enlargement of the mesenteric glands, &c.
    E. pliha to increase, Uṇādi aff. kanin, and the vowel made long; otherwise being plihan; and with ka and ṭāp affs., and the vowel long or short plihā or plīhā.
