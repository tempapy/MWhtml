• prasṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Dispersed, extended, spread abroad.
    2 Stretched.
    3 Long, lengthened.
    4 Modest, humble.
    5 Swift, quick.
    6 Gone.
    7 Attached to, engaged in, occupied.
    8 Appointed. m. (-taḥ) The palm of the hand, hollowed as if to hold liquids. f. (-tā) The leg. n. (-taṃ) A measure of two Palas.
    E. pra before sṛ to go, aff. kta. [582]
