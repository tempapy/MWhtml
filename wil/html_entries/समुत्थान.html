• samutthāna [900] n. (-naṃ) Performance of work, occupation, effort, industry. 2 Positive indication or symptom of disease. 3 Rising, getting up. 4 Common growth or increase, (as of size or wealth, &c.) 5 Healing a wound or sore, cure or recovery from any injury. E. sam intensitive, uṭ up, sthā to stay or be, aff. lyuṭ.
