• mahādeva m. (-vaḥ) ŚIVA. f. (-vī) DURGĀ, the wife of ŚIVA.
    E. mahā great, and deva god; the epithet Mahā compounded with a substantive forms many appellatives of these deities especially; thus, ŚIVA is Mahākāla, Mahārudra, Maheśvara, &c. and DURGĀ is named Mahākāyā, Mahāsmṛti, Mahāmedhā, Mahāmohā, &c. &c. [649]
