• jūṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 The matted hair of ŚIVA.
    2 The clotted hair of an ascetic.
    E. jaṭa to collect, affix ac, ū inserted: see jaṭā; also with kan added jūṭaka. n. (-kaṃ) And with a short vowel juṭaka q. v; it is also used compounded with jaṭā in the same sense, as jaṭājūṭa. [353]
