• vinīta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Modest, humble, unassuming, unpretending.
    2 Compliant, governable, tractable.
    3 Placid, meek, virtuous, gentle, having the passions restrained, and the senses under subjection.
    4 Well-behaved, decent, decorous.
    5 Taken.
    6 Trained, (as a horse or ox, &c.)
    7 Thrown, sent, dismissed.
    8 Lovely, handsome.
    9 Led, conveyed.
    10 Chastised, punished.
    11 Plain, neat, (in dress, &c.) m. (-taḥ)
    1 A horse trained for the manege.
    2 A merchant, a trader.
    E. vi before ṇī to guide, aff. kta. [773]
