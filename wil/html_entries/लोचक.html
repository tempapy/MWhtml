• locaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A ball or lump of flesh or meat.
    2 The pupil of the eye.
    3 Stibium or lamp-black, &c. so used.
    4 An ornament, worn by women on the forehead.
    5 Blue or black vesture.
    6 An ear-ring.
    7 The plantain tree.
    8 A bow-string.
    9 A wrinkled or contracted eye-brow.
    10 Folly, stupidity.
    11 The rejected slough of the snake.
    E. loca to see, ṇvul aff.
