• kālika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Relating to time.
    2 Seasonable.
    3 Of long standing.
    4 Of a dark colour, black, blackish, &c. m. (-kaḥ) The curlew. n. (-kaṃ) A black kind of Sandal: see kālīyaka. f. (-kā)
    1 Cloudiness, a multitude or succession of clouds.
    2 A dark cloud threatening rain.
    3 A fog or mist, &c.
    4 The goddess GAURĪ.
    5 A terrific goddess, a form of CAṆḌĪ or DURGĀ.
    6 A female singer of Svarga.
    7 Blackness, a black colour.
    8 A fault or flaw in gold, &c.
    9 Spirituous liquor.
    10 A plant: see vṛścikapatra.
    11 A kind of potherb.
    12 The branch of a cucumber plant.
    13 Spikenard.
    14 A line of hair extending to the navel.
    15 A female crow.
    16 Price of a commodity on credit, or a price to be paid at a fixed period, or by instalments.
    17 Periodical interest paid by the month, &c.
    18 An animalcule formed by the fermentation of milk.
    19 Ink or blacking.
    20 A small bird, commonly Śyāma.
    21 A medicinal plant: see kākoli.
    E. kāla black, &c. ṭhak or kan affix.
