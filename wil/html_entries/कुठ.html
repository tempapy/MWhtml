• kuṭha (i,) kuṭhi 1st cl. (kuṇṭhati) To be idle. r. 10th cl. (kuṇṭhayati) To surround; ap is usually prefixed. [227]
  • kuṭha m. (-ṭhaḥ) A tree.
    E. ku the earth, and sthā to abide, ḍa affix; earth-abiding; the deriv. is irregular, and is sometimes written kuṭa, derived from kuṭa to be crooked, to bend or wave, affix ka. [227]
