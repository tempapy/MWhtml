• kuṭha [227] (i,) kuṭhi 1st cl. (kuṇṭhati) To be idle. r. 10th cl. (kuṇṭhayati) To surround; ap is usually prefixed.
  • kuṭha [227] m. (-ṭhaḥ) A tree. E. ku the earth, and sthā to abide, ḍa affix; earth-abiding; the deriv. is irregular, and is sometimes written kuṭa, derived from kuṭa to be crooked, to bend or wave, affix ka.
