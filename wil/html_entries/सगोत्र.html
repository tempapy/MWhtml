• sagotra [879] mfn. (-traḥ-trā-traṃ) Being of one family, of kin, related. m. (-traḥ) 1 A distant kinsman. 2 A kinsman of the same family name, or one sprung from a common ancestor; one connected by funeral oblations of food and water. n. (-traṃ) A family, a race or lineage. E. sa for samāna like, common, and gotra family.
