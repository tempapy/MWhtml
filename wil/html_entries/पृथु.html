• pṛthu [551] mfn. (-thuḥ-thu or thvī-thuḥ) 1 Large, great. 2 Smart, clever. m. (-thuḥ) 1 The fifth monarch of the solar dynasty in the second age. 2 A name of Agni or fire. f. (-thuḥ) 1 A pungent seed, (Nigella Indica.) 2 A medicinal substance, commonly Hiṅgupatrī. 3 Opium. E. pratha to be famous, aff. ku, and ra changed to ṛ.
