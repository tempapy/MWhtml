• kharu [272] mfn. (-ruḥ-ruḥ or -rūḥ-ru) 1 White. 2 Desirous of improper or prohibited things. 3 A fool, foolish, idiotic. 4 Harsh, cruel. m. (-ruḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 A horse. 3 A tooth. 4 Pride. 5 Love or the deity KĀMA. 6 White, the colour. E. khana to dig, &c. Uṇādi affix ku, and ra substituted for the final.
