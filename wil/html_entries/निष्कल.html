• niṣkala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Waned, diminished.
    2 Impotent, seedless, barren. m. (-laḥ)
    1 Pudendum muliebre.
    2 A name of BRAHMA.
    3 A fancied personification of BRAHMA, for religious ceremonies. f. (-lā or -lī) A woman past child bearing, in whom menstruation has ceased.
    E. nir gone, lost, kala a part, &c. [481]
