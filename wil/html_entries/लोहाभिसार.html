• lohābhisāra m. (-raḥ) Lustration of arms, ceremonies performed on the ninth of the light half of the month Aśvin; formerly a celebration observed by princes before opening a campaign, but now usually confined to the domestic decoration and worship of the soldier's weapons.
    E. loha a weapon, abhi about, sṛ to go, aff. ghañ; also lohābhihāra. [726]
