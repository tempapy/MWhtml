• utpala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Thin. n. (-laṃ)
    1 A blue lotus, (Nymphaea caerulea.)
    2 A plant, (a Costus:) see kuṣṭha.
    3 A water lily in general.
    4 A flower in general. f. (-lī) A kind of cake made with unwinnowed corn.
    E. ut up, pala to go or grow, ac affix, and fem. ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
