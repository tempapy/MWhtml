• sumukha mfn. (-khaḥ-khā or khī-khaṃ)
    1 Pleasing, agreeable.
    2 Lovely, handsome-faced. m. (-khaḥ)
    1 The son of GARUḌA.
    2 One of the serpents of Pātāla.
    3 A kind of potherb.
    4 A Paṇḍita, a learned man or teacher.
    5 GAṆEŚA.
    6 GARUḌA. n. (-khaṃ) The mark or scratch of a finger nail. f. (-khī) A species of the Triṣṭubh metre. f. (-khā or -khī)
    1 A handsome woman.
    2 A mirror.
    E. su excellent, mukha countenance. [934]
