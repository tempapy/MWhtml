• śaileya mfn. (-yaḥ-yī-yaṃ)
    1 Produced in the mountains, &c., mountainous, mountaineer.
    2 Mountain-like. n. (-yaṃ)
    1 A fragrant resin, (Storax or Benzoin.)
    2 A vegetable perfume, commonly Murā.
    3 Rock salt. m. (-yaḥ) A bee. f. (-yī) A name of PĀRVATĪ.
    E. śilā a mountain, ḍhak aff.
