• jāṅgala mfn. (-laḥ-lī-laṃ)
    1 Rural, picturesque, diversified with hill, vale, wood, and water, (country.)
    2 Wild, jaṅgala, barbarous, savage.
    3 Wild, (not tame, as an animal, &c.) m. (-laḥ) The francoline partridge. f. (-lī) Cowach, (Carpopogon pruriens.) n. (-laṃ)
    1 Flesh.
    2 Game, the flesh of deer, &c.
    E. jaṅgala a wilderness, and aṇ aff.
