• trasareṇu mf. (-ṇuḥ) An atom or mote visible in a sunbeam, especially considered as an ideal weight, either of the lowest denomination, or equal to three, or according to some, thirty invisible atoms. f. (-ṇuḥ) A wife of the sun.
    E. trasa moveable, and reṇu dust; also read tryamareṇu. [388]
