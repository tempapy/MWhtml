• kṛtti f. (-ttiḥ)
    1 The hide upon which the religious student sits, sleeps, &c. usually the skin of an antelope.
    2 The skin.
    3 The bark of the Bhojpatra, used for writing upon, for making hooka snakes, &c.
    4 One of the lunar mansions: see the next.
    E. kṛta to cut, ktin aff. [243]
