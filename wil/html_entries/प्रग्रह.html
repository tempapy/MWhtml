• pragraha [559] m. (-haḥ) 1 Taking, seizing. 2 Confinement, restriction, restraint, captivity. 3 A prisoner, a captive, either man or beast in confinement. 4 A rein, a rope or halter for horses, and cattle. 5 The string suspending a balance. 6 Favour, kindness. 7 An arm. 8 A ray of light. 9 A sort of Cassia, (C. fistula.) E. pra before graha to take or seize, aff. ap; otherwise with ghañ, aff. pragrāha.
