• kāmuka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kī-kaṃ) Cupidinous, desirous, lustful, libidinous. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A plant, Aśoka, (Jonesia asoca.)
    2 A creeping plant, (Gaertnera racemosa.)
    3 A sparrow.
    4 A bow. f. (-kā) A woman desirous of wealth, food, &c. (-kī) A woman libidinous or lustful.
    E. kama to desire, ukañ affix, fem. ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
