• lālāṭika [719] mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) 1 Relating to the forehead, worn on it, belonging to it, &c. 2 Contemplating the forehead, looking at it, &c. 3 Low, vile. 4 Relating to fate or destiny. mf. (-kaḥ-kī) 1 An attentive servant, one who watches a master's countenance, and learns by it what is necessary to be done. 2 An idler, one who is unable to work. m. (-kaḥ) A mode of embracing. E. lalāṭa the forehead, aff. ṭhak.
