• mahodaya [653] n. (-yaṃ) Kanoj, the ancient city and district. m. (-yaḥ) 1 Final beatitude, emancipation from vitality and absorption into the divine essence. 2 Prosperity, elevation, eminence. 3 Pride. 4 Abandoning, relinquishment. 5 A master, a lord. E. mahā great, and udaya rising, splendor, &c.
