• muca(ḷ au) au mucḷ [664] r. 6th cl. (muñcati-te) 1 To be loose or free. 2 To forsake or abandon. r. 10th cl. (mocayati) 1 To let loose, to let go, to discharge as arrows, to part with, as wealth, &c. 2 To gladden, to delight. (mocate) To deceive, to cheat. (i) muci r. 1st cl. (muṃcate) 1 To be vain or proud. 2 To be wicked. 3 To speak. 4 To grind or pound. 5 To cheat, to deceive.
    With pra, To give much.
    With vi, 1 To obtain liberation. 2 To give or bestow. 3 To lose.
