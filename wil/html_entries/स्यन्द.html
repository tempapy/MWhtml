• syanda [959] (ū) syandū r. 1st cl. (syandate) 1 To sprinkle, to drop, to ooze or distil. 2 To go.
    With anu prefixed, anuṣyandate or anusyandate to drop, &c. the sibilant being optionally changed after this and other prepositions.
  • syanda [959] m. (-ndaḥ) 1 Oozing, trickling. 2 Going, moving. 3 A car, a chariot. E. syanda to go, aff. ghañ.
