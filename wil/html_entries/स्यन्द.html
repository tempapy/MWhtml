• syanda (ū) syandū r. 1st cl. (syandate)
    1 To sprinkle, to drop, to ooze or distil.
    2 To go.
    With anu prefixed, anuṣyandate or anusyandate to drop, &c. the sibilant being optionally changed after this and other prepositions. [959]
  • syanda m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 Oozing, trickling.
    2 Going, moving.
    3 A car, a chariot.
    E. syanda to go, aff. ghañ. [959]
