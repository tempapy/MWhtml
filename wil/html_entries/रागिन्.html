• rāgin [700] m. (-gī-giṇī-gi) 1 Passionate, impassioned. 2 Colouring, painting. 3 Coloured, dyed. m. (-gī) 1 A dyer, a painter. 2 A libertine, a lover. f. (-ṇī) 1 A shrewd and intriguing woman. 2 A modification of a musical mode, of which five are assigned to each mode; in mythology, personified as a female, and the wife of the Rāga. E. rāga passion, and ini aff., or rañja to colour, &c., and dhinuṇ aff.
