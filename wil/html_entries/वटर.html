• vaṭara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Wicked, villainous.
    2 Slippery, unsteady. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A cock.
    2 A thief.
    3 A cloth worn round the head, a turban.
    4 A mat.
    5 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus.)
    6 A churning stick.
    E. vaṭa to surround, arac aff. [730]
