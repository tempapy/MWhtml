• śarabha m. (-bhaḥ)
    1 A fabulous animal, supposed to have eight legs, and to inhabit particularly the snowy mountains.
    2 A young elephant.
    3 A monkey in RAMA'S army.
    4 A camel.
    5 A grasshopper.
    6 A locust.
    E. śa to injure, Uṇādi aff. abhac or śara an arrow, bhā to shine, aff. ka. [832]
