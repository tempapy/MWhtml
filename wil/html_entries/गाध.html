• gādha (ṛ) gādhṛ r. 1st cl. (gadhate)
    1 To stand, to stay, to remain.
    2 To seek, to search or inquire for.
    3 To compile, string or heap together. [287]
  • gādha mfn. (-dhaḥ-dhā-dhaṃ) Not very deep. m. (-dhaḥ)
    1 Place, site.
    2 Desire of gain, cupidity, covetousness.
    3 Bottom, soundings.
    E. gādha to stand, &c. affix ac. [287]
