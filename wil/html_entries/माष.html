• māṣa [660] m. (-ṣaḥ) 1 A sort of kidney-bean, (Phaseolus radiatus.) 2 A jeweller's or goldsmith's weight, variously reckoned at five, eight, or ten Rattis, or seeds of the Abrus precatorius; the weight in common use, is about seventeen grains troy. 3 A blockhead, a fool. 4 A cutaneous disease. E. maṣa to hurt, aff. ghañ.
