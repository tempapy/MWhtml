• krūra [258] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Cruel, pitiless. 2 Hard, harsh. 3 Hard, solid. 4 Mischievous, destructive. 5 Formidable, terrible. 6 Hot, sharp. 7 Disagreeable. m. (-raḥ) 1 An odd sign of the zodiac, as the first, third, fifth, &c. which are of malignant influences. 2 A hawk. 3 A heron. 4 Red oleander. E. kṛta to cut, rak Uṇādi affix, and krū substituted for kṛta.
