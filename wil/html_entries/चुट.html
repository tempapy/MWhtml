• cuṭa r. 1st cl. (coṭati) and 10th cl. (coṭayati) To wane, to become small, low or shallow. r. 6th and 10th cls. (cuṭati coṭati) To cut, to pierce or divide; (i,) cuṭi r. 1st cl. (cuṃṭati) To become low or shallow. r. 1st and 10th cls. (cuṇṭati cuṇṭayati) To cut, &c. also cuṭṭa and cuḍa. [329]
