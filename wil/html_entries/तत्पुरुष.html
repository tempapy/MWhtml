• tatpuruṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 That or a certain person.
    2 A form of composition, (in Grammar,) usually consisting of two nouns, the first of which was in any case except the nominative or vocative; sometimes the inflection of the case is retained.
    E. tat that, and puruṣa man or person. [363]
