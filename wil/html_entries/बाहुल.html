• bāhula [605] m. (-laḥ) 1 Fire or its deity. 2 The month Kārttika. n. (-laṃ) 1 Mail worn on the arm. 2 A term in grammar, implying various or irregular formation, or construction, or such as is analogous to this word. E. bahula fire, and aṇ aff.; also with kan added, bāhulaka.
