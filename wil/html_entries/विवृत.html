• vivṛta m. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Expanded, extended, extensive, large.
    2 Evident, displayed, made manifest.
    3 Exposed, discovered.
    4 Opened.
    5 Explained, interpreted, expounded.
    6 Open (as the organs of speech for the articulation of vowels and sibilants.) f. (-tā) A disease, an ulcer attended with much pain and heat.
    E. vi before vṛ to be, aff. kta.
