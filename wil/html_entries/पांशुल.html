• pāṃśula mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Dusty, covered or spotted with dust. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A name of ŚIVA.
    2 One of ŚIVA'S weapons; a sort of pole armed at the upper end with transverse pieces, representing the breastbone and ribs adjoining, and surmounted by a skull; also khadvāṅga.
    3 A paramour, a gallant.
    4 A kind of tree, (Cæsalpinia bonducella.) f. (-lā)
    1 An unchaste woman.
    2 A woman during menstruation.
    3 The earth.
    E. pāṃśu dust, lac aff.
