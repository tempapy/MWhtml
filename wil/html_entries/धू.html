• dhū (ña) dhūñ r. 5th cl. (dhūnoti dhūnute) r. 6th cl. (dhūvati) r. 9th cl. (dhunāti dhunīte) r. 1st and 10th cls. (dhavati-te dhāvayati-te or dhūnayati) To shake or agitate, to make tremble.
    With ava prefixed,
    1 To abandon, to quit.
    2 To perceive.
    With nir,
    1 To destroy.
    2 To go or move.
    With vi, To make tremble or agitate.
