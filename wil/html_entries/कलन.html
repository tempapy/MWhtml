• kalana n. (-naṃ)
    1 A spot, a stain.
    2 An offence, fault, defect.
    3 Murmuring, sounding.
    4 An embryo or the first vestige of the foetus. m. (-naḥ) A sort of cane. f. (-nā)
    1 Subjection, submission.
    2 Chattering, talking.
    3 Shedding, emitting.
    E. kala to count, lyuṭ aff. [200]
