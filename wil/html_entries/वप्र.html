• vapra [734] mn. (-praḥ-praṃ) 1 A field. 2 A rampart, a mud wall, earth taken from the ditch of a town and raised as a wall or buttress, or as the foundation of a wall of masonry, &c. 3 The foundation of any building. 4 The gate of a fortified city. 5 A shore or bank. 6 Dust, earth. 7 A mound, a hillock. m. (-praḥ) A father. n. (-praṃ) Lead. f. (-prī) A hillock, an ant-hill. E. varu to sow, ran Uṇādi aff.
