• bāhudā f. (-dā) The name of a river, said to rise in the snowy chain of the Himālaya, and probably the modern Behut, the classical Hydaspes.
    E. bāhu an arm, and dā bestower; alluding to a legend of a saint's recovering his lost arms, by bathing in this stream. [605]
