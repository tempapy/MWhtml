  • kṣama mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ)
    1 Patient, resigned, enduring.
    2 Refraining from, forbearing.
    2 Able, adequate.
    4 Benevolent, friendly.
    5 Fit, appropriate, suitable, proper. f. (-mā)
    1 Patience.
    2 The earth.
    3 Night.
    4 A name of DURGĀ. n. (-maṃ) Propriety, fitness.
