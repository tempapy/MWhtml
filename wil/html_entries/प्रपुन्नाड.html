• prapunnāḍa m. (-ḍaḥ) A tree, (Cassia tora.)
    E. pra excellently, pupas manking, and ṇala to smell, aff. aṇ, the sa of pumas is rejected, and the la of the radical changed optionally to ḍa; the antepenultimate is occasionally short, and one of the conjunct na is sometimes rejected; whence the word is variously written, prapunnāla, or prapunnaḍa, or prapunāḍa, &c.
