• śukra [850] m. (-kraḥ) 1 The planet VENUS or its regent, the son of BHṚGU, and preceptor of the Daityas or Titans. 2 A name of fire. 3 The month Jyeṣṭha, (April-May.) n. (-kraṃ) 1 Semen virile. 2 A morbid affection of the iris, change of colour, ulceration, &c. accompanied by imperfect vision. E. śuc to grieve, &c., Uṇādi aff. ran, deriv. irr.
