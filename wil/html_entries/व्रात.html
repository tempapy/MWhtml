• vrāta [822] m. (-taḥ) 1 A multitude, an assemblage. 2 The descendant of an outcaste Brahman, &c. 3 The company and attendants at a marriage feast. n. (-taṃ) 1 Manual or bodily labour. 2 Day labour, employment of a precarious kind. E. vṛ to choose, aff. atac, and the vowel made long; or vrata a religious observance, or vrātya an outcaste, aff. aṇ.
