• vikaca [758] mfn. (-caḥ-cā-caṃ) 1 Blown, opened, expanded, spread, (as a flower, &c.) 2 Bald, having little or no hair on the head. m. (-caḥ) 1 A Bauddha mendicant. 2 The planet KETU, or the personified descending node. E. vi apart, separate, kaca to go, aff. ac, or vi privative, and kaca hair.
