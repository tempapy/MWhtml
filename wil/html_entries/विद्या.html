• vidyā f. (-dyā) Knowledge, learning, science, whether sacred or profane, though more especially the former: it is sometimes classed into fourteen divisions; or the four Vedas; the six Aṅgas, or grammar, astronomy, &c. the Purāṇas as the eleventh class; and the Mīmāṃsā or theology, Nyāya or logic, and Dharma or law, as the remaining three.
    2 The goddess DURGĀ.
    3 A tree, (Premna spinosa.)
    4 A magical pill or bolus, by putting which into the mouth a person has the power of ascending to heaven.
    E. vida to know, and aff. kyap. [768]
