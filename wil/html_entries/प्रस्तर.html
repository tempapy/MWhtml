• prastara [582] m. (-raḥ) 1 A stone or rock. 2 A jewel, a precious stone. 3 A couch, made of flowers or grass. 4 Any bed or couch. 5 A tabular form of representing the long and short vowels of poetical metre. 6 Notation of music. 7 A handful or bundle of Kuśa grass used at sacrifices. E. pra before stṛ to spread, aff. ap.
