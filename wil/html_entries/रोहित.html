• rohita mfn. (rohitaḥ rohitā or rohiṇī rohitaṃ) Red, of a red colour. m. (-taḥ)
    1 Red, (the colour.)
    2 The Rohi fish, (Cyprinus Rohita, HAM.)
    3 A kind of deer.
    4 A tree, (Andersonia Rohitaka, Rox.) n. (-taṃ)
    1 INDRA'S bow unbent and invisible to mortals.
    2 Safflower, (Carthamus tinctorius.)
    3 Blood.
    E. ruha to grow or go, Uṇādi aff. itan.
