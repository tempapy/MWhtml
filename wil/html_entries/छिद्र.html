• chidra [335] r. 10th cl. (chidrayati) 1 To pierce, to bore, to perforate, to make openings or holes. 2 To perforate the ear for rings, &c.
  • chidra [335] n. (-draṃ) 1 A hole, a vacuity, a perforation, &c. 2 Fault, defect, flaw, (moral or physical.) 3 A weak side. 4 Infirmity. E. chida to pierce, Uṇādi affix rak.
