• chidra r. 10th cl. (chidrayati)
    1 To pierce, to bore, to perforate, to make openings or holes.
    2 To perforate the ear for rings, &c. [335]
  • chidra n. (-draṃ)
    1 A hole, a vacuity, a perforation, &c.
    2 Fault, defect, flaw, (moral or physical.)
    3 A weak side.
    4 Infirmity.
    E. chida to pierce, Uṇādi affix rak. [335]
