• khura [274] r. 6th cl. (khurati) To cut, to scratch.
  • khura [274] m. (-raḥ) 1 A hoof, a horse's hoof, &c. 2 A razor. 3 The foot of a bedstead; also kṣura. 4 A sort of perfume, commonly Nakhi, apparently a dried shell fish, and of the shape of a hoof. E. khura to cut, ran Uṇādi affix, the radical ra is dropped.
