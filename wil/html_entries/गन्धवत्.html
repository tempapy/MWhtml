• gandhavat mfn. (-vān-vatī-vat) Scented, smelling, odoriferous. f. (-tī)
    1 The earth.
    2 The name of a city.
    3 Wine or vinous liquor.
    4 A kind of perfume: see murā.
    5 The mother of VYĀSA.
    6 A wild species of Ajväen: see yamānī.
    E. gandha smell, matup poss. affix fem. ṅīp.
