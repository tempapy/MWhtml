• madana [636] m. (-naḥ) 1 KĀMADEVA, the Hindu CUPID. 2 The season of spring. 3 A plant commonly Mayana, (Vangueria spinosa.) 4 Thorn apple, (Datura metel.) 5 A bee. 6 A sort of bean, (Phaseolus radiatus.) 7 Bee's wax. 8 A tree, (Mimosa catechu.) f. (-nā-nī) Spirituous or vinous liquor. E. mada to exhilarate or rejoice, aff. lyuṭ, fem. aff. ṭāp, or ṅīṣ.
