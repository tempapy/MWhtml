• aghamarṣaṇa [009] mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) An expiatory prayer: reciting mentally a particular passage from the Vedas, while a little water, in the palm of the right hand, is held to the nose; this forms a part of the daily ceremonies of the Brahmans. m. (-ṇaḥ) The author of the passage recited upon the above occasion. E. agha sin, and mṛṣa to bear with, or to sprinkle, yuca aff.
