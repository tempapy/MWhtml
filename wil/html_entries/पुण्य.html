• puṇya mfn. (-ṇyaḥ-ṇyā-ṇyaṃ)
    1 Virtuous, pure, righteous.
    2 Beautiful, pleasing.
    3 Fragrant. n. (-ṇyaṃ)
    1 Virtue, moral or religious merit.
    2 A good action.
    3 Purity, purification.
    4 A trough for watering cattle. f. (-ṇyā) Holy basil, (Ocymum sanctum.)
    E. pūñ to be or make pure, yat Uṇādi aff., and ṇvuk augment.
