• carmmin mfn. (-rmmī-rmmiṇī-rmmi)
    1 Having a hide or skin.
    2 Leather, made of leather, &c. m. (-rmmī)
    1 A soldier armed with a shield, a shield bearer.
    2 A tree of which the bark is used for writing upon, &c. see bhūrja, a sort of birch.
    3 One of ŚIVA'S doorkeepers: see bhṛṅgarīṭa.
    4 A plantain.
    E. carmma skin, a shield, and ini aff.
