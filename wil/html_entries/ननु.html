• nanu [453] ind. 1 A particle of interrogation, (how, what.) 2 Of affirmation, (certainly.) 3 Of assent. 4 A consolatory and kind expression. 5 A vocative particle, (ho, hola,) or one implying kindness, conciliation, (pray, good friend, &c.) 6 An inceptive particle, implying doubt or dissent, (may be, but, on the other hand.) 7 A responsive particle. 8 A particle of reproach. E. na negative, nuda to send or command, affix ḍu.
