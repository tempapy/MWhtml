• ropaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Placing in or upon.
    2 Planting.
    3 Raising.
    4 A disturbing or bewildering of the mind, a confusing, or confusion of ideas.
    4 Cicatrizing, healing.
    5 A healing application to sores or wounds.
    E. ruha to ascend, causal v., or rupa to bewilder, aff. lyuṭ.
