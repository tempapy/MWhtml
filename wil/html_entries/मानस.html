• mānasa [657] mfn. (-saḥ-sī-saṃ) Mental. n. (-saṃ) 1 The mind, the seat or faculty of reason, and feeling. 2 The lake Mānas, or Mānasarovara in the Himālaya mountains. 3 (In law,) Tacit or implied consent. f. (-sī) A goddess peculiar to the Jainas. E. manas the mind, aff. aṇ, or manas BRAHMĀ, the supreme mind, aṇ aff. of derivation, &c.
