• bhīṣaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ)
    1 Horrible, terrific, formidable.
    2 Hard, harsh.
    3 Exciting the sentiment of horror, applied to poetical compositions. m. (-ṇaḥ) The olibanum tree, (Boswellia thurifera.)
    2 A name of ŚIVA. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Horror, terror, the property that excites fear.
    2 The sentiment of horror as the object of poetical composition.
    E. bhī to fear, in the causal form, aff. lyuṭ or yuc. [621]
