• atikatha mfn. (-thaḥ-thā-thaṃ)
    1 Incredible, unworthy of belief or trust.
    2 Erring, deviating from the duties of a profession or cast. f. (-thā) Idle or unmeaning speech.
    E. ati excess or exceeding, and kathā speech: some works substitute for this word, itikatha q. v.
