• vallura n. (-raṃ)
    1 An arbour, a bower, a place overgrown with creeping plants.
    2 An uncultivated field or place overrun with wild grass.
    3 A sand, a desart, a place destitute of water.
    4 A field.
    5 A compound pedicle.
    6 A solitude, a wild.
    7 A thicket, a wood.
    E. valla to cover, ura aff.; also ballūra.
