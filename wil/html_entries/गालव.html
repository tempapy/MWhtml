• gālava [288] m. (-vaḥ) 1 A tree, the bark of which is used in dying, Lodh, (Symplocus racemosa, Rox.) 2 A pale species of the same: see lodhra. 3 A kind of ebony; also kenduka, (Diospyros glutinosa.) 4 A particular Muni or saint, a disciple of VIŚVĀMITRA. E. gāla flux, and ba what prevents; referring to the astringent qualities of the Lodh.
