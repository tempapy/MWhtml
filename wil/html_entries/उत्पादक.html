• utpādaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 A producer, a generator.
    2 What gives origin or production to, causative. m. (-kaḥ) A fabulous animal with eight legs: written also utyātakaḥ. n. (-kaṃ) Origin, cause. f. (-dikā)
    1 A white ant, (termes.)
    2 A potherb, (Hilancha repens.)
    3 Also, Basella, (rubra or lucida.)
    E. ut much, pada a foot, or pada to go, in the causal, and vun aff.
