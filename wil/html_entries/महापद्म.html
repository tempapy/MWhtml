• mahāpadma [650] m. (-dmaḥ) 1 One of the principal Nāgas or serpents of Pātāla. 2 One of KUVERA'S Nidhis or treasures. 3 A large number, one hundred thousand millions. 4 A Kinnara or attendant on KUVERA. n. (-dmaṃ) The large white lotus. E. mahā great, and padma a lotus.
