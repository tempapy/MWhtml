• kaiṭabha m. (-bhaḥ) A demon slain by VIṢṆU. f. (-bhā) A name of DURGĀ.
    E. kaiṭabha a demon in whose death the goddess was instrumental, by having at the prayers of BRAHMĀ roused VIṢṆU from his slumbers, during which two demons, Kaitabha and Madhu, were about to demolish BRAHMĀ; also kaiṭabhī. [249]
