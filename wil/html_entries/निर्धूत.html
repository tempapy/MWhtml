• nirdhūta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)

    2 Removed, departed.
    3 Broken, divided.
    4 Refuted.
    5 Thrown out.
    6 Suffered, undergone.
    7 Destroyed. m. (-taḥ) A man abandoned by his relatives or friends.
