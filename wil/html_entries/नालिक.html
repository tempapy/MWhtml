• nālika m. (-kaḥ) A buffalo. n. (-kaṃ) A lotus. f. (-kā)
    1 A tubular stalk.
    2 An instrument for piercing an elephant's ear.
    3 A plant, commonly Carmaghās.
    4 A sort of potherb, (Hibiscus cannabinus.)
    5 Any plant growing on a hollow stem, as Śaṇa, safflower, a mushroom, &c.
    E. nālī as above, and kan aff.
