• pratiśraya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 An assembly.
    2 A house, a dwelling.
    3 An asylum, a place of refuge.
    4 A place of sacrifice, the room or building where the sacrifice is performed.
    5 A place where food, &c. is given away.
    E. prati completely, śrī to serve, aff. ac. [567]
