• cakravat mfn. (-vān-vatī-vat)
    1 Circular, being in a ring or circle.
    2 Armed with a discus.
    3 Wheeled, &c. m. (-vān)
    1 The proprietor of an oil mill, or any one in which seeds are bruised.
    2 VIṢṆU.
    3 An emperor.
    E. cakra a wheel, &c. matup poss. aff. [313]
  • cakravat ind. In rotation, going round or revolving like a wheel.
    E. cakra, and vati aff. [313]
