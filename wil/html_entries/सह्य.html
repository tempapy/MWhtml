• sahya mfn. (-hyaḥ-hyā-hyaṃ)
    1 To be borne or suffered.
    2 Equal or adequate to.
    3 Sweet, agreeable.
    4 Powerful, strong. n. (-hyaṃ) Health, convalescence. m. (-hyaḥ) One of the principal ranges of the mountains of India; the mountainous country on the north-west side of the Peninsula or towards Poonah, &c., and in which the Godāvarī river takes its rise.
    E. ṣaha to bear, aff. yat.
