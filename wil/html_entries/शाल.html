• śāla (ṛ) śālṛ r. 1st cl. (śālate)
    1 To flatter, to praise.
    2 To boast.
  • śāla m. (-laḥ)
    1 A kind of fish, (Ophiocephalus wrahl, HAM.)
    2 The celebrated sovereign SĀLIVĀHANA.
    3 The name of a river. f. (-lā)
    1 A house.
    2 A hall.
    3 A large branch of a tree.
    E. śala to go, ghañ aff. or śāla to praise, aff. ac; in several of the meanings it is also written, sāla.
