• bhrama(u,)bhramu [628] r. 1st cl. (bhramati bhramyati) 1 To turn round, to move circularly. 2 To be unsteady or unfixed. 3 To roam, to wander; it is also figuratively applicable to mental unsteadiness, as to err, to wander, to be ignorant or mistaken, &c.
    With pari, To wander about.
    With vi, To play, to wanton.
    With sam, 1 To hurry. 2 To honour.
  • bhrama [629] m. (-maḥ) 1 Whirling, going round. 2 Straying, roaming. 3 Error, ignorance, mistake, misapprehension. 4 A whirlpool, an eddy. 5 A drain, a water-course. 6 A lathe, a potter's wheel, &c. E. bhrama to go round, aff. ap.
