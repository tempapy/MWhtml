• pāraśava [528] mfn. (-vaḥ-vī-vaṃ) Made or derived from an axe. m. (-vaḥ) 1 The son of a Śūdra woman by a Brahman. 2 A son by another's wife, an adulterine. 3 An iron weapon. E. para another, śatra to go, ac aff. and aṇ implying descent, added; or pāra crossing, śava a corpse, a living corpse; or paraśu an axe, aff. añ.
