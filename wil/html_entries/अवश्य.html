• avaśya mfn. (-śyaḥ-śyā-śyaṃ) Untameable, ungovernable. ind. (-śyaḥ) Certainly, necessarily.
    E. ava, śye to go, ḍam affix; irregularly formed: it occurs in composition with or without the nasal, as avaśyaṅkārī who does what is necessary, avaśyapācyaḥ to be necessarily cooked, &c.
    E. a neg. vaśa to subdue, affix yat. [081]
