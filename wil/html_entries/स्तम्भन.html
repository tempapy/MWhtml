• stambhana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Stopping, hindering, obstruction.
    2 Suppression of any of the bodily faculties by magical incantations.
    3 Stoppage of flow of blood, &c.
    4 Any article so employed, a styptic, an astringent. m. (-naḥ) One of the arrows of KĀMADEVA.
    E. ṣṭabhi to stop, aff. yuc or lyuṭ.
