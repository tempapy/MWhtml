• pārśva [530] mfn. (-rśvaḥ-rśvā-rśvaṃ) Near, proximate, by the side of. mn. (-rśvaḥ-rśvaṃ) 1 A side, the part of the body below the armpit. 2 The curve or circumference of a wheel. 3 Side of any square figure. n. (-rśvaṃ) 1 A multitude of ribs, the thorax. 2 A fraudulent or crooked expedient. m. (-rśvaḥ) The twenty-third Jina or Jaina pontiff. E. spṛśa to touch, śvaṇa Uṇādi aff., and pṛ substituted for the root, or parśu a rib, aff. aṇ.
