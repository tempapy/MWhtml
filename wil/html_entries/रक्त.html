• rakta [690] mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ) 1 Dyed, tinged, coloured, stained. 2 Red, of a red colour. 3 Fond of, attached to, affected with love or interest towards any object. 4 Pure, purified. 5 Sporting, engaging in play or pastime. m. (-ktaḥ) Red, the colour. n. (-ktaṃ) 1 Blood. 2 Saffron. 3 Copper. 4 Minium. 5 Vermilion. 6 The fruit of the Flacourtia cataphracta. f. (-ktā) 1 The Gunja plant, (Abrus precatorius.) 2 Lac. 3 Bengal madder. E. rañja to colour or dye, &c., aff. kta.
