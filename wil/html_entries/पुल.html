• pula r. 1st cl. (polati) 6th cl. (pulati) and 10th cl. (polayati) To be great or large, to be lofty or high, to be heaped up or piled.
  • pula mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Vast, great, extensive. m. (-laḥ) Erection of the hairs of the body, considered as a proof of exquisite delight. f. (-lā)
    1 One of the paces of a horse, the canter or gallop.
    2 The soft palate or uvula.
    E. pula to be great, ka aff.
