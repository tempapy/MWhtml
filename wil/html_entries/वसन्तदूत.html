• vasantadūta m. (-taḥ)
    1 The Kokila or Indian cuckoo.
    2 The month Chaitra, (March-April.)
    3 The fifth Rāga or musical mode, called also Hindola.
    4 The mango tree. f. (-tī)
    1 The trumpet flower.
    2 A large and beautiful creeper, (Gærtnera racemosa.)
    E. vasanta spring, and dūta the messenger.
