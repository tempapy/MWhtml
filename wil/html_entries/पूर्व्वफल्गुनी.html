• pūrvvaphalgunī f. (-nī) The eleventh lunar asterism; the first Phalgunī, the twelfth Nakṣatra, being termed Uttara or subsequent; this asterism figured by a couch contains two stars, one of which is δ Leonis.
    E. pūrvva first, and phalgunī the name of the two asterisms. [550]
