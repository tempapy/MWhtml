• samāhita [898] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Promised, agreed or assented to. 2 Absorbed in meditation, absent, abstracted or having the senses steadily held in subjection. 3 Concluded, demonstrated, following from what has been said. 4 Composed, reconciled, put an end to, as a contest or dispute. 5 Placed, deposited, delivered. 6 Pure, purified. 7 Finished, completed. 8 Cool, collected, firm. 9 Assembled. n. (-taṃ) (In rhetoric,) The expression or description of attentiveness. E. sam and āṅ implying collection or perfection, ghā to have or hold, aff. kta.
