• gūḍhaja m. (-jaḥ) The son of a concealed birth; born privately of a woman whose husband is absent, the real father being unknown; this is one of the twelve forms particularized in Hindu law.
    E. gūḍha hidden, and ja born; also compounded with puttra a son, gūḍhajaputtra.
