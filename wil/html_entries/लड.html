• laḍa [715] r. 1st and 10th cls. (laḍati laḍayati) 1 To frolic, to sport or dally. 2 To loll (the tongue.) 3 To use the tongue. 4 To stir with the tongue. 5 To stir, to agitate. 6 To pain. r. 10th cl. (-lāḍayati) 1 To nurse, to foster, to attend closely. 2 To shake. 3 To feel desire. (au i) aulaḍi r. 1st and 10th cls. (laṃḍati laṃḍayati) 1 To shine. 2 To toss or throw up. 3 To speak.
