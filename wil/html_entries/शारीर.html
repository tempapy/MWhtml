• śārīra mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ)
    1 Corporeal, bodily, belonging to or produced from the body.
    2 Spiritual in connexion with the body, incorporate. n. (-raṃ)
    1 The soul or spirit whilst incorporate.
    2 A drug(?)
    3 Excrement, excretion. m. (-raḥ) Personal chastisement, corporeal punishment.
    E. śarīra the body, and aṇ aff. [839]
