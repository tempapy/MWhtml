• diva [409] (u) divu r. 4th cl. (dīvyati) 1 To play, to sport, to play with, to romp or gambol. 2 To desire, to overcome, to be ambitious of surpassing, &c. 3 To deal, to traffic, to buy or sell. 4 To shine, to be splendid or beautiful. 5 To praise. 9 To be glad, to rejoice. 7 To be mad, to be wild or inflated with pride, passion, &c. 8 To be sleepy or sluggish. 9 To love, to desire. 10 To go. r. 10th cl. (devayati) 1 To suffer pain. 2 To ask, to beseech or beg. 3 To move, to go. 4 (-ti te) To lament, to wail, to mourn audibly. (i) divi r. 1st cl. (ditvati) 1 To please. 2 To be pleased.
  • diva [409] n. (-vaṃ) 1 Heaven, paradise. 2 Heaven, sky, atmosphere. 3 A day. 4 A wood, a thicket. E. diva to play, to shine, &c. affix kaḥ see the preceding.
