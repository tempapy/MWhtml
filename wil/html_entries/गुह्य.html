• guhya mfn. (-hyaḥ-hyā-hyaṃ)
    1 Secret, solitary, retired.
    2 Private, concealable, to be kept hidden or secret.
    3 Mysterious, mystical. m. (-hyaḥ)
    1 A tortoise.
    2 Hypocrisy.
    3 VIṢṆU. n. (-hyaṃ)
    1 A privity, an organ of generation, &c.
    2 The anus.
    E. guha to cover, kyap affix, deriv. irr. [294]
