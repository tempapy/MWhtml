• saṃyamana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Restraining, checking.
    2 Forbearance, self-denial or controul.
    3 Binding, confinement.
    4 Holding, detaining.
    5 A religious vow or obligation.
    6 A cluster of four houses. f. (-nī) The capital of YAMA.
    E. sam implying completeness, or entirely, and yama to restrain, aff. lyuṭ. [873]
