• truṭi f. (-ṭiḥ-ṭī)
    1 Cutting, breaking.
    2 Loss, destruction.
    3 Breaking a promise, &c.
    4 Small cardamoms, (Alpinia cardamomum.)
    5 A short time, a moment.
    6 Doubt, uncertainty.
    7 (Always feminine, though used attributively,) Little, small.
    8 An atom, or a visible atom composed of three subtler elementary particles.
    E. truṭa to cut or divide, affix ki and ṅīp optionally added. [392]
