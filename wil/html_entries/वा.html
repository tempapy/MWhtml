• vā r. 2nd cl. (vāti)
    1 To go or move.
    2 To blow as the wind.
    With nir prefixed, To blow out, to extinguish.
    3 To hurt, to injure. [746]
  • vā ind. A particle of,
    1 Comparison, (as, like, so;)
    2 Alternative or option, (or, either, else, sometimes;)
    3 Doubt and consideration, (or, whether;)
    4 Exception, (only, no other;)
    5 Asseveration or confirmation, (indeed, even, very;)
    6 A conjunction, (and.)
    7 An expletive.
    E. vā to go, aff. kvip, the va is sometimes changed to ba, and the word is read bā. [746]
