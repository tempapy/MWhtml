• tripuṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 Pulse, pease of three kinds.
    2 A bank or shore.
    3 A kind of vetch, (Cicer arietinum.)
    4 A cubit.
    5 A plant, (Ruellia longifolia.) f. (-ṭā-ṭī) A plant, (Convolvulus turpethum.) f. (-ṭā) Small cardamoms.
    2 Arabian jasmine.
    3 A goddess.
    E. tri three, and puṭa skin or coat of a seed, &c. [390]
