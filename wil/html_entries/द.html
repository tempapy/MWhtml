• da [394] The third letter of the dental class, and eighteenth consonant in the Nāgarī alphabet, corresponding to the letter D.
  • da [394] n. (daṃ) A wife. nf. (daṃ dā) 1 Gift, donation. 2 Dividing, cutting, breaking, &c. f. (dā) 1 Purifying, cleansing. 2 Preserving, cherishing. 3 Repentance. m. (daḥ) 1 A donor, a giver. 2 A mountain. 3 A gift, a thing given. E. dā to give, or do to cut, &c. affix ḍa.
