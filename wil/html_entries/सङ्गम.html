• saṅgama [881] m. (-maḥ) 1 Meeting, union, mixture, junction, the encounter of persons, the association of friends or lovers, the confluence of rivers, the fitness or adaptation of two things to each other, &c. 2 (In astronomy,) Planetary conjunction. E. sam together, like, suitably, gama going: see also saṅga, saṅgata, saṅgati, &c.
