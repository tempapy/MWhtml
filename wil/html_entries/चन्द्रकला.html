• candrakalā f. (-lā)
    1 A digit, or one-sixteenth of the moon's orb; each is personified as a female divinity, and worshipped in some Tāntrika ceremonies.
    2 A small drum.
    3 A fish commonly called Vacā or Bacā, (Pimelodus vacha, HAM.)
    E. candra the moon, and kalā a digit.
