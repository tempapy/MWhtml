• utkṣipta mfn. (-ptaḥ-ptā-ptaṃ)
    1 Thrown upwards, tossed.
    2 Thrown out, ejected.
    3 Vomited.
    4 Rejected, dismissed. m. (-ptaḥ) Thorn apple, (Datura metel and fastuosa.)
    E. ut up, and kṣipta thrown; in the last sense it alludes to the intoxicating qualities of the plant.
