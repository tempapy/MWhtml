• kāśyapa m. (-paḥ)
    1 The name of a saint; also called KAṆĀDA, the son of KAŚYAPA.
    2 A name of ARUṆA: see the next.
    3 A sort of deer.
    4 A tribe of Brahmans pretending to descend from KAŚYAPA.
    E. kaśyapa a saint, and aṇ affix of descent. n. (-paṃ) Flesh.
    E. kāśya spirituous liquor, and pa from pā to drink or cherish.
