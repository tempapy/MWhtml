• nṛsiṃha m. (-haḥ)
    1 VIṢṆU.
    2 The fourth Avatāra or descent of that deity, in the shape of a man, with the head and claws of a lion.
    3 A chief, a noble, a great or illustrious man.
    E. nṛ a man, and siṃha a lion; applied in composition, also to signify pre-eminence. [487]
