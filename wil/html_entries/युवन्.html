• yuvan mfn. (-yuvā-yuvati or -tī or yūnī-yuva)
    1 Young.
    2 Best, excellent.
    3 Endowed with native or natural strength. m. (-vā) A young man or one of the virile age, or from sixteen to seventy. f. (-tiḥ-tī or yūnī) A young woman, one from sixteen to thirty. (-tiḥ) Turmeric.
    E. yu to mix or associate, Uṇādi aff. kanin, the fem. form takes ti, and optionally adds ṅīṣ in one form, with which affix the semivowel va is changed to its congener u, and the two short vowels coalesce into one long one, making yūnī. [687]
