(-nā-nī) 1 A plant, commonly Śuṇḍārocanī, a sort of Crinum? f. (-nī) 1 A variety of Tëori, (Convolvulus turpethum.) 2 Red arsenic. E. ruca to shine, lyuṭ or yuc aff.
