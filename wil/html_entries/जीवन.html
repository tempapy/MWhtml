• jīvana m. (-naḥ)
    1 A plant, commonly Jiuti, (Odina wodier.)
    2 A son.
    3 A living being, an animal. f. (-nā or gī) A plant, commonly Jiyati: see jīvantī. f. (-nī) Jasmin. n. (-naṃ)
    1 Life, existence.
    2 Livelihood, profession.
    3 Water.
    4 Butter of new or one day-old milk.
    5 Marrow.
    E. jīva to live, affix lyu or lyuṭ. [352]
