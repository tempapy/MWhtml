• laṭa r. 1st cl. (laṭati)
    1 To be a child, to talk or be like one.
    2 To speak a little. [715]
  • laṭa m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 A block head, a fool, one speaking ignorantly or foolishly.
    2 Fault, defect.
    3 A thief.
    E. laṭa to be or talk as a child, &c., aff. ac. [715]
