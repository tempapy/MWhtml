• apasada m. (-daḥ) A low man, an outcaste; it especially applies to the children of six degrading connexions, or of a Brāhmaṇa with the women of the three lower classes; of a Kṣatriya with women of two, the Vaiśya and Śūdra; and of a Vaiśya with one of a lower class or a Śūdra.
    E. apa low, sada to abide, and ac aff.
