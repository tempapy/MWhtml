• vartti f. (-rttiḥ-rttī)
    1 Perfume for the person.
    2 The wick of a lamp.
    3 A lamp.
    4 A tent, a bougie.
    5 A collyrium prepared from various substances and dried in a lozenge form; it is exhibited ground with water or milk.
    6 A line, a ruled line.
    7 The ends of a cloth.
    E. vṛta to be, Uṇādi aff. in, ṅīp optionally added. [739]
