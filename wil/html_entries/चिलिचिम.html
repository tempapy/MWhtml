• cilicima m. (-maḥ)
    1 A kind of sprat, (Clupea cultrata, B. MSS?)
    2 A kind of prawn, (according to some.)
    E. cila to sport or play, affix ki, cili and reiterated, cilici sport, pastime, mā to mete, to find, ka affix, finding sport in marshy places amongst reeds, &c. this word has various forms, being read as above, and cilicīma, cilimīnaka, cilīcima, cilicimi, cilīma, &c. [328]
