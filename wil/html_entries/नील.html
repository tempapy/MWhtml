• nīla mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Blue, dark blue or black. m. (-laḥ)
    1 Black or dark blue, (the colour.)
    2 The blue mountain, one of the principal ranges of mountains, dividing the world into nine portions, and lying immediately north of Hāvrata or the central division.
    3 One of the monkey chiefs.
    4 One of the Nidhis or divine treasures of KUVERA.
    5 A gem, (the sapphire.)
    6 A name of the Buddha MAÑJUGHOṢA.
    7 The blue or hill Maina, a bird so called. n. (-laṃ)
    1 Indigo, the dye.
    2 A mark, a characteristick mark.
    3 A medical plant, apparently distinct form the Indigofera.
    4 Blue vitriol.
    5 Black salt.
    6 Poison.
    7 Antimony. f. (-lā)
    1 A blue fly.
    2 A Rāgiṇī or mode of music, personified as the wife of the Rāga Mallāra. f. (lī)
    1 The indigo plant, (Indigofera tinctoria.)
    2 A complaint of the eyes, a darkening of the pupil.
    3 A bruise, a black and blue mark on the skin.
    E. nīla to dye or tinge, ka aff. or nīlī indigo, an aff., implying being coloured or stained by it. [484]
