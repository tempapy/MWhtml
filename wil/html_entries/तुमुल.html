• tumula [380] mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) 1 Mingled or tumultuous combat. 2 Uproar, clangour, tumult, tumultuous sound or noise. m. (-laḥ) Beleric myrobalan. E. tu a Sautra root, to kill, to injure, &c. mulaka affix; or with the vowel long tumūla. mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) and la being changed to ra, tumura as above.
