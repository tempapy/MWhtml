• saṃjña [883] mfn. (-jñaḥ-jñā-jñaṃ) Knock-kneed. n. (-jñaṃ) A yellow fragrant wood. f. (-jñā) 1 Name, appellation. 2 Thought, mind, intellect. 3 Gesture, sign, gesticulation. 4 The sacred verse or Gāyatrī of the Vedas. 5 One of the wives of the sun. 6 (In grammar,) The technical name of any affix, &c. E. sam before jñā to know, aff. aṅ and ṭāp, that by which any thing is known.
