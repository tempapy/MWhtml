• kṛtānta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 A name of YAMA, son of SŪRYA, and regent of the dead, or death personified.
    2 Destiny, that is, the inevitable result of actions done in a past existence.
    3 A demonstrated conclusion, proved or established doctrine.
    4 Sinful or inauspicious action.
    5 Saturday, which is considered as an unlucky day. f. (-ntā) A perfume: see reṇukā.
    E. kṛta act, (actum) done, anta end, destruction. [242]
