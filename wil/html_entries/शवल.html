• śavala [834] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Variegated, of a variegated colour. 2 Imitative. 3 Articulate. m. (-laḥ) A variegated colour. f. (-lā-lī) 1 A brindled cow. 2 The cow of plenty, KĀMADHENU. n. (-laṃ) 1 Water. 2 A religious observance peculiar to the Bauddhas. E. śava to curse, kala aff., and pa changed to va.
