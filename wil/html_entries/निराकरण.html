• nirākaraṇa [472] m. (-ṇaṃ) 1 Obstruction, opposition, contradiction, rejection. 2 Refutation, reply. 3 Holding in no consideration, making light of, contempt, disesteem. 4 Expelling, turning out or away. 5 Throwing out or off. 6 Neglecting the chief sacrificial or religious duties. E. nir privative particle, and āṅ before kṛ to do or make, affix lyuṭ.
