• puraskṛta [543] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Made perfect, finished, polished. 2 Prepared. 3 Placed in front. 4 Revered, adored, worshipped. 5 Honoured, respected. 6 Sprinkled with holy water, initiated, anointed. 7 Promised, agreed. 8 Accused, calumniated. 9 Facing, and contending with an enemy. 10 Distrest or harassed by an enemy. 11 Anticipated. E. pura before, and kṛta made.
