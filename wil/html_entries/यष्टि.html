• yaṣṭi mf. (-ṣṭiḥ-ṣṭiḥ-ṣṭī)
    1 A staff, a stick.
    2 A staff armed with iron, &c. used as a weapon, a club, a mace.
    3 A necklace.
    4 Any creeping plant.
    5 Liquorice.
    6 A shrub, (Siphonanthus Indica.)
    7 A string, a thread, especially as strung with pearls, &c.
    8 A thread in general. m. (-ṣṭiḥ)
    1 A flag-staff.
    2 The arm and forearm.
    E. yakṣa to worship, aff. ktin, and the ka of the compound final rejected. [683]
