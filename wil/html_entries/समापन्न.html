• samāpanna [897] mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ) 1 Finished, done, completed, concluded. 2 Got, gained, obtained. 3 Distressed, afflicted. 4 Killed. 5 Accomplished, perfect, (in any branch of study.) 6 Come, occurred, happened. E. sam implying completion, &c., āṅ before pada to go, aff. kta.
