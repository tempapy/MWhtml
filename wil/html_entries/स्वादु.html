• svādu mfn. (-duḥ-du or -dvī-du)
    1 Sweet.
    2 Agreeable, desired.
    3 Handsome.
    4 Grateful to the palate, dainty, delicate. m. (-duḥ)
    1 The sweet taste or flavour, sweetness.
    2 Treacle, molasses.
    3 A medicinal root, commonly Jivaka. f. (-dvī or -duḥ) A grape.
    E. ṣvada to taste or be pleasing, uṇ Uṇādi aff. [965]
