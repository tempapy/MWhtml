• bhāga [616] m. (-gaḥ) 1 A portion, a share, a part. 2 Fate, fortune, luck. 3 A half rupee. 4 Part of any thing given as interest. 5 A division of time, the thirtieth part of a Rāśi or zodiacal sign. 6 A degree, the 360th part of the circumference of a great circle. 7 A fraction. E. bhaja to share, aff. ghañ.
