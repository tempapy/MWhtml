• kharbba [272] mfn. (-rbbaḥ-rbbā-rbbaṃ) A dwarf, short, low, of little size or stature. m. (-rbbaḥ) One of KUVERA'S nine Nidhis or treasures. n. (-rbbaṃ) A large number, ten million-millions. E. kharbba or kharvva to be small, to go, to move, affix ac; also kharvva.
