• lakṣmaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Prosperous, fortunate. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 The son of DAŚARATHA by SUMITRĀ, the half-brother and faithful companion of RĀMACANDRA.
    2 The Sāras or Indian crane. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 A mark, a spot or sign.
    2 A name, an appellation. f. (-ṇā)
    1 The female of the Sāras.
    2 A kind of drug.
    E. lakṣa to mark or see, Uṇādi aff. na, and muṭ augment; also with aṭ augment, lakṣaṇa as above. [713]
