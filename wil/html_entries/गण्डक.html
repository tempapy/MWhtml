• gaṇḍaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A rhinoceros.
    2 A mode of reckoning by fours.
    3 An obstacle or impediment.
    4 Separation, disjunction.
    5 Astrological science or a part of it. f. (-kī) The name of a river in the north of India, the Gandaki. f. (-kā) A lump, a ball.
    E. gaḍi to affect the cheek, &c. ṇvul affix, fem. affix ṅīp. [278]
