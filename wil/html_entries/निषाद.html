• niṣāda m. (-daḥ)
    1 The first of the seven musical notes, or more properly the last or highest of the scale.
    2 A man of a degraded tribe, or an outcaste, especially the son of a Brahman by a Śūdra woman.
    3 A fisherman.
    E. ni before ṣada to go, aff, ghañ; pervading the mind, &c.
