• pariṣkāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Decoration, embellishment.
    2 Cooking, dressing.
    3 Finishing, polishing.
    4 Initiation, purification by essential rites.
    5 Surrounding.
    E. pari implying ornament, and kāra making, sa inserted, and changed to ṣa; also in the same sense pariskāra. [513]
