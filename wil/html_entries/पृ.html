• pṛ r. 3rd cl. (pipartti)
    1 To protect, to nourish.
    2 To fill. 5th cl. (pṛṇoti) To satisfy, to please or content. r. 1st and 10th cls. (parati pārayatti) To fill, as the stomach or a vessel. r. 6th cl. (but with vi and āṅ prefixed, and ṅa aff.) vyāpṛṅ (vyāpriyate) To be busy or active, to labor, to make exertions: see pṝ. [551]
