• hasta [972] m. (-staḥ) 1 The hand. 2 An elephant's trunk. 3 The thirteenth lunar asterism, designated by a hand, and containing five stars, one of which is γ or δ Corvi. 4 A head of hair, or abundance of hair. 5 A cubit measured by the hand and arm, or from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger. 6 (In prosody,) An anapæst. n. (-staṃ) A bellows. E. hasa to laugh, Uṇādi aff. tan.
