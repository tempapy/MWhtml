• sudharmma [930] m. (-rmmaḥ) One of the principal disciples of Mahāvīra, the last Jina of the present age; he survived his master, and the different ascetics or candidates for holiness are traced from him in regular succession; none of the other ten disciples of Mahāvīra having left successors. f. (-rmmī) A council of the gods. E. su excellent, and dharmma virtue.
