• ci (ña) ciñ r. 1st, 5th and 10th cls. (cayati-te cinoti cayayati or capayati) To collect, to gather together, to heap, to assemble, to accumulate, &c.
    With apa, To lose, to squander.
    With upa or sam, To collect or acquire.
    With nir, To determine.
    With vi and nira, To determine exactly. (īra ṇa) īraciṇ r. 1st cl. (cayati) To injure, (peculiar to the Vedas.) [324]
