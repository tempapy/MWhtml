• kuṣmāṇḍa [237] mf. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā or -ṇḍī) A pumpkin gourd, (Cucurbita pepo). m. (-ṇḍaḥ) 1 One of a class of demigods attached to ŚIVA. 2 State of the womb in gestation. f. (-ṇḍī) 1 A name of DURGĀ. 2 A drug. 3 A religious ceremony, a certain rite performed as a penance or expiation; also kuṣmāṇḍaka.
