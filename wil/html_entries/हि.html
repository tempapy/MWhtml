• hi r. 5th cl. (hinoti)
    1 To go.
    2 To send.
    3 To grow or increase.
    4 To be in pain.
  • hi ind.
    1 Because, for, on account of.
    2 Assembly, certainly, (assertion.)
    3 Indeed, surely, (interrogation.)
    4 A particle, implying difference or distinction.
    5 An expletive.
    6 Ah, alas.
    7 An interjection of envy or contempt.
    8 An interjection of hurry.
    E. hi to go, vic aff.
