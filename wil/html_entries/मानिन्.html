• mānin mfn. (-nī-ninī-ni)
    1 Proud, arrogant, haughty.
    2 (In composition,) Who thinks or fancies. f. (-ninī)
    1 A woman, especially one displeased and indignant towards her lover.
    2 A plant, commonly Priyangu.
    3 A species of the Atiśarkarī metre.
    E. māna pride, ini aff.
