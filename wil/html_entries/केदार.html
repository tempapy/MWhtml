• kedāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A field, a meadow, a plain.
    2 A mountain.
    3 A name of ŚIVA.
    4 A particular place, the modern Kedār, part of the Himālaya mountains.
    5 A basin for water round the root of a tree.
    6 A bed in a garden or field.
    E. ka water, earth, &c. dṛ to tear or rend, ghañ aff.
