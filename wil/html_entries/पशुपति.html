• paśupati m. (-tiḥ) A name of ŚIVA.
    E. paśu living creature, and pati the master, the creator, or preserver; or paśu ŚIVA'S attendant, and pati master; or according to one legend, paśu an animal, which every deity acknowledged himself to be before his lord and master ŚIVA, when they solicited him to destroy the Asura TRIPURA.
