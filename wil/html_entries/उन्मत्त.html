• unmatta mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ)
    1 Insane, frantic, mad.
    2 Drunk, intoxicated. m. (-ttaḥ)
    1 The thorn apple, (Datura metel and fastuosa.)
    2 A plant, (Pterospermum acerifolium.)
    E. ut much, matta drunk or mad: applied to the plants, it alludes to their deleterious properties. [151]
