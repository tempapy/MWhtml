• pogaṇḍa mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā-ṇḍaṃ) Deformed, having a redundant or defective member. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A boy, one from his fifth to his sixteenth year.
    E. api certainly, gaḍi to be a part of the cheek, ac aff., the a of the prefix is rejected, and the i changed to o; otherwise, apa depreciative prefix, gama to go, and the deriv. irr.; also apogaṇḍa; in the second sense it also occurs paugaṇḍa. [554]
