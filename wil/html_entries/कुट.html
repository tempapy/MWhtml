    1 To make crooked.
    2 To be or become crooked.
    3 To be dishonest, to cheat or deceive. (i) kuṭi (kuṇṭati)
    1 To be stopped or obstructed, to be prevented from moving.
    2 To confound or disorder from pain or grief: see kuḍi r. 10th cl. (kuṭayate koṭayati)
    1 To cut: see truṭa.
    2 To heat: see kuṭṭa.

    1 A fort, a strong hold.

    6 A hammer, a mallet for breaking small stones.
