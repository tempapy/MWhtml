• kuṭa [226] (śi) r. 6th cl. (kuṭati) 1 To make crooked. 2 To be or become crooked. 3 To be dishonest, to cheat or deceive. (i) kuṭi (kuṇṭati) 1 To be stopped or obstructed, to be prevented from moving. 2 To confound or disorder from pain or grief: see kuḍi r. 10th cl. (kuṭayate koṭayati) 1 To cut: see truṭa. 2 To heat: see kuṭṭa.
  • kuṭa [226] mn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭaṃ) 1 A water pot. mf. (-ṭaḥ-ṭī) A house; also kuṭi. m. (-ṭaḥ) 1 A fort, a strong hold. 2 A tree: see kuṭha. 6 A hammer, a mallet for breaking small stones. 4 A mountain. f. (-ṭī) 1 A kind of perfume, commonly Mura. 2 A bawd, a procuress or go-between. 3 A nosegay, a bundle or tuft of flowers or vegetables. E. kuṭha to go crookedly, &c. kaḥ aff.
