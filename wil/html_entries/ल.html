• la [713] The twenty-eighth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, the letter L.
  • la [713] m. (laḥ) 1 INDRA. 2 Cutting. f. (lā) 1 Giving. 2 Receiving, taking. f. (lī) 1 Embracing, adhering or clinging to. 2 Liquifying, melting. E. lā to give or take, &c., aff. ka or ḍa, fem. aff. ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
