• navaratna n. (tnaṃ)
    1 Nine precious gems, or a pearl, ruby, topaz, diamond, emerald, lapis lazuli, coral, sapphire, and one called Gomeda.
    2 The nine men of letters at the court of VIKRAMĀDITYA, or DHANVANTARI, KṢAPAṆAKA, AMARASIṂHA, SAṄKU, VETĀLABHAṬṬA, GHAṬAKARPARA, KĀLIDĀSA, VARĀHAMIHIRA, and VARARUCI.
    E. nava, and ratna a jewel. [457]
