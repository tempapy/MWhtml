• pulāka mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ)
    1 Shrivelled or blighted grain.
    2 Rice water, (according to some medical authorities.)
    3 A lump of boiled rice.
    4 Brevity, compendium, abbreviation.
    5 Smallness, a little.
    6 Dispatch, celerity.
    E. pula greatness, aka to be or go, aff. ac.
