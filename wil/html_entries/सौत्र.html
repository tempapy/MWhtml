• sautra mfn. (-traḥ-trī-traṃ) Preceptive, formulary, according to rule or precept. m. (-traḥ)
    1 A Brahman.
    2 A radical, the only application of which is to form derivative nouns, and not like other roots, capable of conversion into a verb.
    E. sūtra a thread, a rule, &c., aṇ aff. [946]
