• dīpālī f. (-lī)
    1 The day of new moon in the month Aśvin or Kārttika, (September-October,) a festival with nocturnal illuminations in honor of KĀRTTIKEYA; the Divalī, as it is usually termed.
    2 A row or range of lamps.
    E. dīpa a lamp, and ālī a line; also dīpāvalī the same. [411]
