• kavaca [204] mn. (-caḥ-caṃ) 1 Armour, mail. 2 A drum used in battle, a kettle drum. 3 An amulet, a charm. 4 A tree, (Hibiscus populneoides:) see garddabhāṇḍa. 5 The mystical syllable Hum, forming part of a Mantra, and considered as a preservative like armour; it is also inscribed on a Birch leaf, &c. and worn as an amulet; being carried about the person as a charm: see the next. E. ku to sound, ac Uṇādi aff.
