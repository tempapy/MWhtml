• sūca r. 10th cl. (sūcayati) To give information, to espy.
  • sūca m. (-caḥ) The shoot of the Kuśa grass. f. (-cī) A needle. see sūci. (-cā)
    1 Gesticulation.
    2 Sight, seeing.
    3 Piercing.
    E. siva to sew, caṭ Uṇādi aff., and ū substituted for the final, or sūca to make known, aff. ac and ṭāp.
