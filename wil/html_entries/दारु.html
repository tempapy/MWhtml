• dāru mfn. (-ruḥ-rvvī-ru)
    1 Liberal, munificent, a giver, a donor.
    2 An artist.
    3 Tearing, rending, a tearer. mn. (-raḥ-ru) Wood, timber. n. (-ru)

    2 Brass.
    E. dṝ to tear or split, Uṇādi affix ñuṇ, or dā to give, and ru Uṇādi aff.
