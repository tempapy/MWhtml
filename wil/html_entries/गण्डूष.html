• gaṇḍūṣa mf. (-ṣaḥ-ṣā)
    1 A mouthful.
    2 A handful.
    3 A handful of water, water, &c. held in the hollowed palm of the hand for rincing the mouth, &c.
    4 Filling the mouth, rincing it, &c.
    5 The tip of an elephant's trunk.
    E. gaḍa to drop, ūṣaṇ Uṇādi affix, and num inserted.
