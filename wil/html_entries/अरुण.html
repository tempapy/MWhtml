• aruṇa [067] m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 A name of the sun. 2 Also of his charioteer, or the dawn personified as the son of KAŚYAPA by VINATĀ. 3 The colour of the dawn, dark red, or the mixture of red and black. 4 Tawny, (the colour.) 5 The redness of sun-set. 6 A kind of leprosy, with red spots and insensibility of the skin. 7 A dumb man. f. (-ṇā) 1 A plant, (Betula.) See ativiṣā. 2 Madder, (Rubia manjith.) 3 Another plant, commonly Tëori. See triṣṭat. 4 A black kind of the same. 5 Colocynth or bitter apple. 6 The plant that yields the red and black berry from which the jeweller's weight, the Ratti, is derived. See guñjā. mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Tawny, dark red, &c. E. ṛ to go, and unana Uṇādi aff.
