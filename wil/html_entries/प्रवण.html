• pravaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ)
    1 Declivity, declining, steep.
    2 Bent, bowed.
    3 Attached or adhering to, filled with, possessed of, endowed with.
    4 Curved, crooked.
    5 Swift.
    6 Generous.
    7 Humble, modest.
    8 Wasted, decayed. m. (-ṇaḥ) A place where four roads meet.
    E. pra before vana to sound, &c. aff. ac.
