• vyūha m. (-haḥ)
    1 Military array, the arrangement of troops in various positions; as daṇḍavyūhaḥ the array in line, bhogavyūhaḥ in column, maṇḍalavyūhaḥ in circle, asaṃhatavyūhaḥ in mixed order; also various fanciful forms, as the śakaṭaḥ or car-shape, makaraḥ or marine monster-like, patākā flag-shape, &c.
    2 A flock, a multitude.
    3 Logic, reasoning.
    4 Making, manufacture.
    5 The body.
    E. vi before ūha to reason, aff. ghañ. [821]
