• paitra mfn. (-traḥ-trī-traṃ) Paternal, ancestral, relating or belonging to a parent or progenitor. n. (-traṃ)
    1 The part of the hand between the thumb and forefinger, or according to some the root of the forefinger.
    2 A year, month, day, &c. of the Pitṛs.
    E. pitṛ a parent, aff. aṇ. [554]
