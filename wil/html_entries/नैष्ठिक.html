• naiṣṭhika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Relating to state or condition.
    2 Belonging to the character or office of a perpetual student.
    3 Firm, fixed. m. (-kaḥ) The Brahman who continues with his spiritual preceptor, and always remains in the condition of the religious student.
    E. niṣṭhā certainty, ṭhak aff. [489]
