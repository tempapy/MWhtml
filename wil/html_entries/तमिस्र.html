• tamisra [367] m. (-sraḥ) The dark half of the month, from the full to the change. f. (-srā) 1 A dark night, or one during the wane of the moon. 2 Great or extensive darkness. 3 The night of new moon. 4 Any night. n. (-sraṃ) 1 Darkness. 2 Anger, wrath. E. tamasa darkness, ra added, and i substituted for the penultimate.
