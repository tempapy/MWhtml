• lavaṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ)
    1 Salt, saline.
    2 Handsome, beautiful. n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Sea salt.
    2 Rock or fossile salt.
    3 Factitious salt, or salt obtained by boiling clay found near the sea-shore, or any earth impregnated with saline particles. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 The saline or salt taste, saltness.
    2 The sea of salt water.
    3 The name of a Rākṣasa mentioned in the Rāmāyaṇa. f. (-ṇā)
    1 Light, lustre, beauty.
    2 A small river in Tirhut.
    E. lū to cut, (rawness, or to assist digestion,) aff. lyu, and the final letter made nasal by special rule.
