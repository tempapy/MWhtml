• asa (ña) r. 1st cl. (asati-te)
    1 To go or move.
    2 To take or receive.
    3 To shine. (la) r. 2d cl. (asti) To be. (ya, u, ira) r. 4th cl. (asyati-te) To throw or direct.
    With anu prefixed, to sit down:
    with apa, to quit or abandon:
    with ni, to deposit:
    with nir, to expel:
    with pari and upa, to sit around:
    with pra, to throw away, to reject, to confute:
    with vi, to divide:
    with vi and āṅ, to class, to arrange:
    with sama and ni, to abandon the world:
    with sama, to collect, to combine or compound. [093]
