• māsika [660] mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) 1 Monthly, relating or belonging to a month. 2 Payable in a month, (a debt, &c.) 3 Hired by the month, (a servant.) 4 Lasting for a month. 5 Happening or occurring at the end of a month, what will occur a month hence, &c. n. (-kaṃ) A particular Śrāddha, or obsequial sacrifice performed every day of new moon. E. māsa a month, and ṭhak or ṭhañ aff.
