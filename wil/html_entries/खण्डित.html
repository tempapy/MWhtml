• khaṇḍita [270] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Cut, torn, broken in pieces. 2 Destroyed. 3 Broken as allegiance, disobeyed against, rebelled. 4 Refuted, controverted. 5 Scattered, dispersed. 6 Disappointed. 7 Betrayed, abandoned, (as a lover.) f. (-tā) A woman whose husband or lover has been guilty of infidelity. E. khaḍi to cut, affix kta.
