• bhela mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Ignorant, unwise, foolish.
    2 Fearful, timid.
    3 Unsteady, figuratively or literally. m. (-laḥ)
    1 A raft, a float.
    2 The name of a Muni, the supposed author of some medical works.
    E. bhī to fear, Uṇādi aff. ran, and the final changed to la. [627]
