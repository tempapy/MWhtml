• śālmali mf. (-liḥ-lī)
    1 The silk cotton tree, (Bombax heptaphyllum.)
    2 One of the seven Dvīpas, islands or great divisions of the known continent; denominated from the tree first mentioned, said to grow there: it is surrounded by the sea of Ghee or clarified butter.
    E. śala to go, causal form, kvip aff. śāl exuding, emitting, mala to hold, aff. in, and ṅīṣ optionally added; also with ac aff. śālmala, and with the first vowel short, śalmali, &c. [840]
