• karṇika mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Having large or long ears.
    2 Having ears.
    3 Having a helm. m. (kaḥ-) A steersman. f. (-kā)
    1 An ear-ring or ornament of the ear.
    2 The pericarp of a lotus.
    3 The middle finger.
    4 The tip of an elephant's trunk.
    5 A fruit stalk.
    6 A pen or small brush.
    7 A plant, (Premna spinosa, &c.) see agnimantha.
    E. karṇaṃ the ear, &c. ika affix; or kan and ṭāp aff. [196]
