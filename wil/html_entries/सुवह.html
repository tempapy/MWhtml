• suvaha [937] mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ) 1 Bearing or conveying well or pleasantly. 2 Pleasant of burthen, to be borne with pleasure. 3 Patient, enduring. f. (-hā) 1 A flower, (Nyctanthes tristis.) 2 A shrub, (Mimosa octandra.) 3 Rasan, conjectured to be the ichneumon plant, (Ophiorrhyza mangos.) 4 A scandent shrub, (Cissus pedata.) 5 The gum Olibanum tree, (Boswellia thurifera.) 6 A Vinā, or lute. 7 A medicinal plant, commonly Teöri, (Convolvulus turpethum.) E. su well, much, or pleasantly, and vaha bearing.
