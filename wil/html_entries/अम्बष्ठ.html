• ambaṣṭha [062] m. (-ṣṭhaḥ) 1 The name of a country, stated to be in the eastern division of India, and supposed by Mr. WILFORD, to be the abode of the AMBASTOE of Arrian. 2 The offspring of a man of the Brahman, and a woman of the Vaiśya tribe, a man of the medical cast. f. (-ṣṭhā) 1 A sort of jasmin, (Jasminum auriculatum.) 2 A plant, (Cissampelos hexandra.) See banatiktikā. 3 Woodsorrel, (Oxalis corniculata, Rox.) E. ambā a mother, sthā to stand, and ka affix, what cherishes-like a mother.
