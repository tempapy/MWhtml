• aho [101] ind. A particle and interjection of, 1 Reproach, (fie!) 2 Compassion. 3 Regret, (ah! oh!) 4 Calling, (ho! hola!) 5 Praise, flattery, approbation. 6 Fatigue, weariness. 7 Surprise, (ah!) 8 Doubt, deliberation, (either, or.) 9 Contempt, sneering. 10 An expletive. E. a neg. hā to go, and ḍo aff.
