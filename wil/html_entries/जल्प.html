• jalpa r. 1st cl. (jalpati)
    1 To speak articulately, to say or tell.
    2 To prattle, to talk much or idly.
  • jalpa m. (-lpaḥ)
    1 Dissention, wrangling.
    2 Prate, babble.
    3 (In Logic,) Refutation of an adversary's arguments, and assertion of one's own.
    E. jalpa to speak, affix ap.
