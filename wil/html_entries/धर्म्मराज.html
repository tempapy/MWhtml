• dharmmarāja [440] m. (-jaḥ) 1 A Jina or deified saint, according to the Jaina sect. 2 A name of YAMA, regent of the dead. 3 A name of YUDHIṢṬHIRA. 4 Also of ARJUNA. 5 A king in general. E. dharmma virtue, justice, and rāja who shines, affix ac; or rāja from rājana a king; lord of justice.
