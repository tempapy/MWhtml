• punarvasu [542] m. (-suḥ) The seventh of the lunar asterisms, containing according to some authorities two, and to others, four stars; (in this sense it is properly confined to the dual number punarvvasū, though in the Vedas it is used in the singular.) 2 A name of VIṢṆU. 3 The name of a saint, and grammarian; also KĀTYĀYANA. 4 A name of ŚIVA. 5 Commencement of wealth. 6 A Loka or division of the universe. E. punar again, vasa to dwell, aff. ku.
