• ketu m. (-tuḥ) KETU the dragon's tail or descending node; in astronomy, the ninth of the planets; in mythology, a demon: the body of SAIṂHIKEYA severed from the head (RĀHU), by VIṢṆU, at the churning of the ocean, but immortal, by having tasted the Amṛta.
    2 A banner, a flag.
    3 A mark, a sign, a symbol, &c.
    4 Light.
    5 A comet, a falling star, &c.
    6 Disease.
    7 A pigmy race inhabiting Kusa Dvīpa, the progeny of JAIMINI.
    E. cāya to worship, tu Uṇādi affix, ki substituted for the root.
