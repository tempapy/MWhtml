• viṣṭambha m. (-mbhaḥ)
    1 Impediment, hindrance, obstacle.
    2 Stopping, staying.
    3 Placing in or upon.
    4 A disease, obstruction of urine or fæces, ischury, constipation.
    5 Paralysis, loss of motion.
    E. vi before stabhi to stop or hinder, aff. ghañ. [792]
