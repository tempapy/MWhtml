• tripād [390] m. (-pād) 1 A name of VIṢṆU. 2 Fever, (personified.) E. tri three, and pāda foot or part; three-footed, alluding to VIṢṆU'S three steps which paced the universe; and the demon of fever is described in the Purāṇas, with three feet, and three heads, alluding probably to its three stages of cold, hot, and sweating.
