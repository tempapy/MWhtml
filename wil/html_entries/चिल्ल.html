• cilla [328] r. 1st cl. (-cillati) 1 To be loose, to be slack or flaccid. 2 To indicate one's meaning. 3 To act wantonly.
  • cilla [328] mfn. (-llaḥ-llī-llaṃ) Blear-eyed. m. (-llaḥ) 1 The Bengal kite, (Falco cheela, Lath.) 2 A bleared or sore eye. f. (-llī) 1 A potherb, said to be a small kind of Chenopodium: see vāstūka. 2 A cricket. E. cilla to be flaccid or loose, affix ac: see culla, or ni preposition, lac in the second sense, and cil substituted for ni.
