• cāmuṇḍā f. (-ṇḍā)
    1 A name of DURGĀ.
    2 One of the Mātṛkās.
    E. ca the moon, and muṇḍa the head; having a head like the moon; or cā for caṇḍa the name of a demon, and muṇḍa the skull; having seized the decapitated head of the demon: in either case the deriv. is irregular: see carmmamuṇḍā, &c.
