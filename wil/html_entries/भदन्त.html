• bhadanta [612] mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntī-ntaṃ) 1 Reverend, venerable, worshipped or adored. 2 Brilliant, splendid. m. (-ntaḥ) 1 A Saugata, a Bauddha, a heterodox or sceptical philosopher. 2 A devotee, an ascetic. E. bhadi to be happy or pleased, to shine, Uṇādi aff. jhac, and the nasal rejected.
