• kāsa r. 1st cl. (ṛ) kāsṛ (kāsate)
    1 To make a bad sound, or one indicating disease; to cough.
    2 To shine: see kāśa. [220]
  • kāsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 Cough, catarrh.
    2 A species of reed or long grass, (Saccharum spontaneum.)
    3 Morunga, (Hyperanthera, &c.) see śobhāñjana.
    E. kāsa to cough, to make an unpleasant sound, ac affix; also kāśa to shine, &c. see kāśa. [220]
