• piñjala [533] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Confounded, overcome with terror or grief. m. (-laḥ) An army panick-struck, or in great disorder. n. (-laṃ) 1 The leaf of the Kuśa grass. 2 Yellow orpiment. f. (-lī) Two blades of Kusa grass, used as a vessel, or to take up articles with, at sacrifices. E. piji to hurt, &c. aff. alac.
