• ū The sixth vowel of the Sanscrit alphabet, corresponding to U long, and having the sound of that letter in the word rule. [165]
  • ū ind.
    1 An incipient particle.
    2 An interjection of compassion.
    3 Of regard. m. (ūḥ) The moon.
    E. ava to go, kvip affix, va becomes ū. [165]
