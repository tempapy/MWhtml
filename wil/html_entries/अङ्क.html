• aṅka [009] (ka, ta) r. 10th cl. (aṅkayati) 1 To number or count. 2 To mark.
  • aṅka [009] n. (-ṅkaṃ) 1 A mark or spot. 2 The flank or part above the hips. 3 A species of dramatic entertainment. 4 The act of a play, &c. 5 Fault, offence. 6 A line, a stroke. 7 Mimic war or conflict. 8 Ornament, decoration. 9 Place, abode. 10 Proximity, proximate. 11 A chapter or section. 12 The body. 13 A cypher, an arithmetical sign. E. añca to go, and the Uṇādi affix ka; or aṅka to stain or spot, ac aff.
