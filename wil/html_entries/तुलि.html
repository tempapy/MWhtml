• tuli f. (-liḥ or -lī)
    1 A fibrous stick or brush used by weavers for cleaning the threads of the woof.
    2 A painter's brush, or fibrous stick used for that purpose: see tūlikā.
    E. tula to resemble, &c. affix ka, fem. affix in or ṅīṣ; also with la changed to ra, turi, turī. [381]
