• tāmbūla nf. (-laṃ-lī)
    1 Areca, (Areca faufel or catechu.)
    2 Betel, (Piper betel,) or its pungent leaf, which together with the areca nut and catechu, and sometimes caustic lime and spices, is eaten very generally by the natives of the east. n. (-laṃ) The areca nut. m. (-laḥ) Śaṇa, a plant, (Crotolaria juncea.)
    E. tama to desire, affix ulac, and vukr augment, the radical vowel made long. [372]
