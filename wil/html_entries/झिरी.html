• jhirī f. (-rī) A cricket.
    E. jhi imitative of the cricket's chirp, rā to make, affixes ḍa and ṅīṣ; also jhillī; also jhirikā, kan being added; also i pen. being made long jhirīkā, or changed to u, jhirukā; the first vowel also being lengthened jhīrikā, and jhīrukā, &c. [358]
