• bhaṅga [611] m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 Breaking, splitting. 2 A chasm, a fissure, a division. 3 Defeat, discomfiture. 4 Disappointment. 5 Fear. 6 Dishonesty, fraud, circumvention, cheating. 7 Going, motion. 8 A wave. 9 A water-course, a canal. 10 Disease in general. 11 A disease, (hemiplegia, palsy.) 12 Interruption of the voice. f. (-ṅgā) 1 Hemp, (Cannabis sativa.) 2 A plant, commonly Teori. 3 A fibrous plant from which a sort of flax is obtained, (Crotolaria juncea.) E. bhañja to break, aff. ac.
