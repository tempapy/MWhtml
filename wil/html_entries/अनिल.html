• anila [030] m. (-laḥ) 1 Air or wind, considered also as a deity. 2 A subordinate deity, forty-nine of whom form a class, ANILAS or winds. 2 One of another class of demigods or Vasus. 4 Wind, as one of the humors of the body. 5 Rheumatism, paralysis, or any affection referred to disorder of the wind. E. ana to breathe, ilaṭ aff. f. (-lī) The fifteenth Nakṣatra or lunar mansion. See svāti. E. anila and the fem. termination: the wind being the ruling deity of the constellation.
