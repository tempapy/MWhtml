• vaiṣṇava mfn. (-vaḥ-vī-vaṃ) Relating or belonging to VIṢṆU. m. (-vaḥ) A follower of VIṢṆU. f. (-vī)
    1 One of the Mātṛs, the personified energy or Śakti of VIṢṆU.
    2 A name of DURGĀ.
    3 A flower, (Clitoria ternatea.)
    4 Sacred basil, (Ocymum sanctum.)
    E. viṣṇu VIṢṆU, aṇ aff., fem. aff. ṅīṣ. [812]
