• gandhapuṣpa m. (-ṣpaḥ)
    1 A kind of cane, (Calamus roatang.)
    2 Another plant, (Alangium hexapetalum:) see aṅkoṭa.
    3 Sebesten, (Cordia myxa.) f. (-ṣpā)
    1 The indigo plant.
    2 Ganiyāri, (Premna spinosa, &c.) see gaṇikārī. n. (-ṣpaṃ) Flowers and Sandal presented together at seasons of worship.
    E. gandha smell, and puṣpa flower; having odoriferous flowers. [281]
