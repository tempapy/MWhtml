• śārikā f. (-kā)
    1 A bird, the proper or hill Maina, (Gracula religiosa;) it is also applied to the small bird usually called Maina in Bengal, (Turdus salica, BUC.) of which several varieties are distinguished by the Hindus.
    2 A bow or stick that may be used for playing the Vīṇā or any stringed instrument.
    E. śṝ to injure, aff. vun.
