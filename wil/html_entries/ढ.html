• ḍha The aspirate of the preceding letter corresponding to Ḍh: the use of this as an initial letter is very limited, and several of the words in which it occurs can scarely be regarded as pure Sanscrit. [360]
  • ḍha m. (ḍhaḥ)
    1 A large drum.
    2 A dog.
    3 A dog's tail.
    4 Sound.
    5 A serpent. [360]
