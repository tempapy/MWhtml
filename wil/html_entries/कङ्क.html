• kaṅka [178] m. (-ṅkaḥ) 1 A heron. 2 YAMA or death. 3 A false or pretended Brahman. 4 A title of YUDHIṢṬHIRA, from his assuming the disguise of a Brahman. 5 A man of the second or military tribe. 6 One of the eighteen division of the continent. 7 The brother of KAṂSA. 8 A kind of mango. f. (-ṅkā) 1 A daughter of UGRASENA. 2 A sort of sandal. E. kaki to go, ac aff.
