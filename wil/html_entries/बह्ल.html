• bahla [603] r. 1st cl. (bahlate) 1 To be pre-eminent or excellent, (also vahla, but less usually.) 2 To speak or tell. 3 To kill or hurt. 4 To cover, to spread. 5 To give, (also bahla in these five senses, and more accurately.) 6 To remember. r. 10th cl. (bahlayati) To shine.
