• pitrya mfn. (-tryāḥ-tryā-tryaṃ)
    1 Paternal, ancestral.
    2 Obsequial, relating to deceased progenitors. m. (-tryaḥ) A father's elder brother. n. (-tryaṃ) The part of the hand between the forefinger and thumb, sacred to the manes. f. (-tryā)
    1 One of the lunar asterisms, also Magha.
    2 Day of full moon.
    3 Ceremonies performed in honour of the deceased on the day of full moon.
    E. pitṛ a father, and yat aff., rīṅ substituted for ṛ. [536]
