• tama [367] (ira u) irtamu r. 4th cl. (tāmyati) 1 To desire. 2 To be distressed in body or mind.
  • tama [367] m. (-maḥ) The quality of darkness incident to humanity: see tamas. mf. (-maḥ-mā) A tree bearing black blossoms. n. (-maṃ) 1 Darkness, gloom. 2 The point of the foot. f. (-mā-mī) Night. E. tama to be disturbed, to be fatigued, &c. affixes ac, and ṭāp or ṅīṣ.
