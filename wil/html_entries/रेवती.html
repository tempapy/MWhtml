• revatī f. (-tī)
    1 The wife of BALARĀMA, the brother of KṚṢṆA.
    2 The last of the Nakṣatras or lunar asterisms, containing thirty two stars, figured by a tabor; one of the stars is ζ piscium.
    3 One of the Mātṛs or energies of the gods.
    4 A cow.
    E. revata the name of a king, the father of BALARĀMA'S wife, and ṅīṣ aff. [710]
