• vadhū f. (-dhūḥ)
    1 Woman in general, a female.
    2 A wife.
    3 A young wife, one recently married, a bride.
    4 A son's wife.
    5 A sort of bulbous root, (Curcuma reclinata.)
    6 A gramineous plant, (Trigonella corniculata:) see pṛkkā.
    7 A plant, (Echites frutescens.)
    E. vaha to bear, u aff., and ha changed to dha; also derived from ṣadha to bind, in which case it is written vadhū.
