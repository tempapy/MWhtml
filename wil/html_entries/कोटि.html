• koṭi [250] f. (-ṭiḥ) 1 The edge or point of a sword. 2 The end of a bow. 3 A number, a Krore or ten millions. 4 Eminence, excellence. 5 The upright side of a triangle. 6 The complement of an are to 90°. 7 A plant, Piring or Asparac, (Medicago esculenta.) E. kuṭa to be crooked, and iṇ Uṇādi affix; also koṭī.
