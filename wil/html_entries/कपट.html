• kapaṭa mn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭaṃ) Fraud, deceit, cheating, circumvention.
    E. ka. BRAHMĀ, paṭa to go, ac affix; what extends even to BRAHMĀ; or ka the head, and paṭa a covering, screening the head as it were. f. (-ṭī) A measure equal to the capacity of the hollows of the two hands joined.
