• pradīpana mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ)
    1 Inflaming, illuminating.
    2 Stimulating, exciting, who or what does so. n. (-naṃ) The act of inflaming, exciting, &c. m. (-naḥ) A sort of mineral poison, of a red colour, and caustic operation.
    E. pra before dīpa to inflame, aff. lyuṭ. [572]
