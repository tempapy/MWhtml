• triśaṅku m. (ṅkuḥ)
    1 The name of a king of the solar race, famous for attempting to go to heaven in his mortal frame, by the aid of VIŚVĀMITRA.
    2 A cat.
    3 A grasshopper.
    4 A bird, the Cātaka, (Cuculus melanoleucos.)
    5 A fire-fly.
    E. tri three, śaṅku a dart, ṭa aff. [391]
