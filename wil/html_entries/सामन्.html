• sāman n. (-ma) The Sāma Veda, the third of the four Vedas, the prayers of which composed in metre, are always sung or chanted: the Chāndogya Upaniṣad belongs to this Veda.
    2 Conciliation, reconciling, appeasing.
    3 Calming, tranquillizing.
    4 Speaking kindly or tenderly. f. (-nī) A rope, for tying cattle.
    E. ṣo to destroy, (sin,) Uṇādi aff. manin, and ṅīṣ added for the fem. form. [919]
