• mitra n. (-traṃ)
    1 A friend.
    2 An ally; in this form it is always neuter; but it is convertible into an attributive, and is then of three genders, (mitraḥ mitrā mitraṃ) Friendly, a friend. m. (-traḥ) The sun. f. (-trī) One of the wives of DAŚARATHA, the mother of ŚATRUGHNA.
    E. mida to be affectionate, aff. ktra; hence more properly written mittra.
