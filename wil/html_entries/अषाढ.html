• aṣāḍha mfn. (-ḍhaḥ-ḍhā-ḍhaṃ) Produced or occurring in the month Āṣārha. m. (-ḍhaḥ)
    1 The month Āṣāḍha, commencing with the sun's entrance into Gemini, (June-July.)
    2 A student's staff. f. (-ḍhā) A name common to the twentieth and twenty-first lunar mansion: see pūrṣvāṣāḍhā and uttarāṣāḍhā.
    E. see āṣāḍha, the initial being made short. [091]
