• alambuṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 The palm of the hand with the fingers extended.
    2 Vomiting, sickness.
    3 The name of a Rākṣasa or goblin. f. (-ṣā)
    1 One of the Apsarasas or nymphs of Svarga.
    2 Madder, (Rubia manjith.)
    3 A sort of sensitive plant.
    4 A barrier, a line or any thing not to be crossed.
    E. alam, and busa to injure, affix ka.
