• gavaya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 A species of ox, the Gayal, erroneously classed by Hindu writers amongst the kinds of deer, (Bos gavaeus.)
    2 A monkey chief: the son of VAIVASVATA. f. (-yī) The female Gayal.
    E. gu to sound, and aya affix, form irregular; or go a cow, and ya from yā to obtain, affix ḍa, resembling a cow. [286]
