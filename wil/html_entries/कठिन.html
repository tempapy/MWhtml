• kaṭhina mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ)
    1 Hard, solid.
    2 Unkind, severe.
    3 Paralysed, rendered stiff or rigid.
    4 Difficult, obscure. nf. (-naṃ-nā) An earthen vessel. f. (-nā) A sweetmeat made with refined sugar. (-nī or nikā) Chalk.
    E. kaṭha to be confounded, and inac Uṇādi affix, fem. affixes ṭāp and ṅīṣ. [182]
