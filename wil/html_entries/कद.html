• kada or (-i.) kadi r. 1st cl. (kadate and kandate)
    1 To be confused, to suffer mentally, to grieve.
    2 To confound.
    3 To kill or hurt; also kadi, (kaṃdati)
    1 To call.
    2 To cry or shed tears. [185]
  • kada m. (-daḥ) A cloud.
    E. ka water, and da what gives. [185]
