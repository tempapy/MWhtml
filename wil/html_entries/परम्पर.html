• parampara [504] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Successive, proceeding from one to another, from father to son, &c. m. (-raḥ) 1 A sort of deer. 2 A great-great-grandson. f. (-rā) 1 Race, progeny, lineage. 2 Order, method, continuous arrangement, regular series or succession. E. para subsequent, repeated, and the nasal augment.
