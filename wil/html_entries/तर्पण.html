• tarpaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Satisfaction given or received; either the act of pleasing, or the state of being pleased.
    2 Satiety, fulness.
    3 A religious rite, presenting water to the manes of the deceased or to the Pitṛs collectively; also to the gods either generally or individually.
    4 Fuel used on sacrificial occasions. f. (-ṇī) A plant; also guruskandha.
    E. tṛpa to satisfy or be satisfied, affix lyuṭ. [369]
