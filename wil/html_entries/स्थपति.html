• sthapati mfn. (-tiḥ-tiḥ-ti) Chief, best, principal. m. (-tiḥ)
    1 The performer of the VṚHASPATI sacrifice.
    2 A guard or attendant of the women's apartments.
    3 A sovereign, a chief.
    4 An architect, a master carpenter or builder.
    5 A carpenter, a wheel-wright.
    6 A charioteer.
    7 KUVERA.
    E. stha who is or who is placed, and pati master, lord.
