• skhalana [948] n. (-naṃ) 1 Stumbling, slipping, tripping. 2 Stumbling or falling from virtue. 3 Tripping in speech, making a mistake or blunder in speech or pronunciation. 4 Knocking or falling together, shock, collision. 5 Striking or rubbing one thing against another. E. skhala to go, to drop, aff. lyuṭ.
