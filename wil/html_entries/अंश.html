• aṃśa [001] r. 10th cl. (aṃśayati) To separate or divide. See aṃsa.
  • aṃśa [001] m. (-śaḥ) 1 A share or portion. 2 A part. 3 A shoulder, the shoulder blade. 4 (In arithmetic) a fraction. 5 The numerator of a fraction. 6 A degree of latitude or longitude, &c. See aṃsa. E. aṃśa to divide, ac affix.
