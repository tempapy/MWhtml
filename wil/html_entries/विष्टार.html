• viṣṭāra m. (-raḥ) A form of metre, a species of the order termed Paṅkti, consisting of a verse of four lines, the first and last of which consist of eight syllables each, and the second and third of twelve each.
    E. vi before stṛ to spread, aff. ghañ, and sa changed to ṣa. [792]
