• ṭuṇṭhuka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Low, little, vile.
    2 Cruel, harsh. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A plant, (Bignonia Indica.)
    2 A black species of catechu.
    3 A small bird, (Silvia sutoria.) f. (-kā) A plant, commonly Aknidhi: see ṭaṅginī.
    E. ṭuṇṭhu imitative sound, and ka what utters.
