• palāśa [518] mfn. (-śaḥ-śī-śaṃ) 1 Green. 2 Unfeeling, unmerciful, cruel. n. (-śaṃ) A leaf. m. (-śaḥ) 1 A tree bearing red blossoms, (Butea frondosa.) 2 A demon, a goblin. 3 A sort of Curcuma, (C. reclinata, Rox.) 4 Ancient Behār or Magadhā. f. (śī) 1 Lac. 2 A kind of creeper. E. pala going, or flesh, aśa to spread, to eat, &c. aff. aṇ or ac.
