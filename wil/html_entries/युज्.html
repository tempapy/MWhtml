• yuj n. (-yuk) A pair, a couple. m. (-yuk yug) A sage, one who devotes his time to abstract contemplation. ind. (-jau) The twin sons of AŚVINĪ, and physicians of Svarga. m. (-yuṅ) A joiner, one who joins or unites. f. (-yuk) A measure of three hundred poles.
    E. yuja to join, to meditate, &c., aff. kvin; also yujir the same, with the same aff., when na is inserted after the vowel, before the first five affixes in the masc. and fem. genders. [686]
