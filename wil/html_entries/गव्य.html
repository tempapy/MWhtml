• gavya mfn. (-vyaḥ-vyā-vyaṃ)
    1 (Of or belonging to a cow, as milk, curds, &c.)
    2 Proper or fit for cattle. nf. (-vyaṃ-vyā) A bowstring. f. (-vyā)
    1 A multitude of cows.
    2 A measure of two Kos: see gavyūti. n. (-vyaṃ) A colouring substance, a yellow pigment or dye.
    E. go a cow, &c. and yat aff. [286]
