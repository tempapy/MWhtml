• gaṇita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) Numbered, counted, reckoned, calculated. n. (-taṃ)
    1 The science of computation, comprising arithmetic, algebra, and geometry, distinguished as pāṭīgaṇitaṃ, vījagaṇitaṃ and rekhāgaṇitaṃ.
    2 Calculating, numbering.
    E. gaṇa to count, affix kta. [278]
