• culla [330] r. 1st cl. (cullati) 1 To manifest one's meaning. 2 To dally, to wanton or coquet.
  • culla [330] mfn. (-llaḥ-llā-llaṃ) Blear-eyed. m. (-llaḥ) A blear eye. f. (-llī) 1 A chimney, a fire place. 2 A funeral pile. E. culla to indicate meaning, affix ac; or ni preposition, lac affix implying moisture of the eyes, and cul substituted for ni.
