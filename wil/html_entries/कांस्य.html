• kāṃsya [205] mn. (-syaḥ-syaṃ) A goblet, a drinking vessel. n. (-syaṃ) 1 White copper or brass, queen's-metal, any amalgam of zinc and copper. 2 A musical instrument, a sort of gong or plate of bell-metal struck with a stick or rod. 3 A measure: see kaṃsa, &c. E. kasi to injure, ṇyat aff.
