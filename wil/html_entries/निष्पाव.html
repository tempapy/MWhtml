• niṣpāva mfn. (-vaḥ-vā-vaṃ) Certain, indubitable. m. (-vaḥ)
    1 Winnowing, cleaning corn, &c.
    2 Wind, air.
    3 The wind of the winnowing sieve or basket.
    4 Straw, chaff.
    5 A legume, a pod.
    6 A sort of pulse, (Phaseolus radiatus.)
    E. nir before pū to purify, aff. ghañ. [482]
