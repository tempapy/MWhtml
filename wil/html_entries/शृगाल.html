• śṛgāla m. (-laḥ)
    1 A shakal or jackal.
    2 A coward, a poltroon.
    3 A rogue, a cheat.
    4 An ill-natured or harsh-speaking man.
    5 A demon so named.
    6 KṚṢṆA. f. (-lī)
    1 Flight, retreat.
    2 A fox.
    3 A she jackal.
    E. śṛja to create or abandon, (deceit, &c.) kālan aff., or śṛṃga a horn, a negative prefix, lā to possess, aff. ka, deriv. irr.; also sṛgāla, &c.
