• ghaṭṭa [307] r. 1st and 10th cls. (ghaṭṭate ghaṭṭayati) To move.
    With pari prefixed, To spread or diffuse.
    With vi, To rub gently, to scratch.
  • ghaṭṭa [307] m. (-ṭṭaḥ) A Ghat, a quay or landing place, steps on the side of a river, &c. leading to the water's edge. f. (-ṭṭī) A small or inferior landing place, private stairs, &c. E. ghaṭṭa to go, affix ac, and with ṅīṣ affix, implying diminution.
