• karatoyā f. (-yā) The Karatoyā river, a river in the north-east of Bengal.
    E. kara the hand, and toya water: at the wedding of ŚIVA and PĀRVATĪ, the water which had been poured into the hand of the former, constituted, upon its being thrown on the ground, the source of this river. [192]
