• puṣpa [546] r. 4th. cl. (puṣpati) To flower, to blossom.
  • puṣpa [546] n. (-ṣpaṃ) 1 A flower in general. 2 The menses. 3 Expansion, expanding. 4 The vehicle or car of KUVERA. 5 A disease of the eyes, specks on the eye, albugo. f. (-ṣpā) The capital of KARṆA, or Bhāgalpur. E. puṣpa to flower, &c. ac aff.
