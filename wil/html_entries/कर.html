• kara [191] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ) Who or what does, makes or causes, as kiṅkaraḥ A servant, who does any thing; bhayaṅkaraḥ frightful, what causes fear; upakaraḥ who gives aid; apakaraḥ who does ill, &c. m. (raḥ) 1 A ray of light, a sun or moonbeam. 2 Royal revenue, toll, tax, impost, &c. 3 The hand. 4 Hail. 5 An elephant's trunk. E. kṛñ to do, &c. ṭa or ap aff.
