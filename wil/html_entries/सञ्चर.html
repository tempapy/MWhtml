• sañcara [883] m. (-raḥ) A defile, any narrow or difficult pass, a road along the edge of a mountain, or a bridge over a mountain stream, &c. 2 Difficult passage, travelling along almost impracticable routes. 3 A road, a way. 4 The body. 5 Killing. E. sam before cara to go, aff. gha; also sañcāra.
