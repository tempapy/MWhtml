• ghuṣa (i) ghuṣi r. 1st cl. (ghuṣate) To render beautiful or brilliant. (ira) ghuṣira r. 1st cl. (ghoṣati) To do any act except manifest sentiment by sound. r. 10th cl. (ghoṣayati)
    1 To manifest sentiment or thought by sound, to speak, to affirm, to declare or proclaim.
    2 To make various sounds.
    3 To praise.
    With āṅ prefixed, To weep incessantly. [310]
