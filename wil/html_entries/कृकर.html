• kṛkara [240] m. (-raḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 One of the five vital airs, that which assists in digestion. 3 A kind of partridge: see kṛkaṇa. 4 A sort of pepper, (P. chavya.) 5 The oleander tree. E. kṛka the throat, and ra what gets or receives; or kṛ imitative sound, and kara what makes, from kṛ to make or do, affix ap.
