• añjalikārikā [014] f. (-kā) 1 A kind of sensitive plant, (Mimosa natans,) or according to some, a different plant, (Lycopodium imbricatum.) E. añjali, and kārikā making; from the shape or closing of the petals, like the hollowed palms of the hand. 2 A doll, a puppet. E. as before.
