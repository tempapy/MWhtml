• āḍhaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kā-kaṃ) A measure of grain equal to four Prasthas, or containing nearly 7 lbs. 11 oz. avoirdupois: in Bengal an Araka is equal to two mans or 164 lbs. avds. f. (-kī)
    1 A kind of pulse, (Cytisus cajan.)
    2 A fragrant earth: see tubarī.
    E. āṅa, ḍhoka to go, irregularly aca, fem. ṅīp, or ṭāpa with ika inserted. [108]
