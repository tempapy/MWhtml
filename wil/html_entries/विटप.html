• viṭapa mn. (-paḥ-paṃ)
    1 The branch of a tree with its new sprout or shoot.
    2 A new shoot.
    3 A branch.
    4 Expansion, spreading.
    5 A clump, a cluster.
    6 The perinæum or septum of the scrotum. m. (-paḥ) The keeper of pathics.
    E. viṭa a branch, &c., and pā to cherish, aff. ka, or viṭa to sound, Uṇādi aff. apa, with the radical vowel unchanged.
