• kumuda n. (-daṃ)
    1 The white esculent water lily, (Nymphaea esculenta.)
    2 The red lotus, (N. rubra.)
    3 Silver. m. (-daḥ)
    1 The elephant of the south-west quarter.
    2 One of the monkey heroes of the Rāmāyaṇa.
    3 One of the Nāga or serpent race.
    4 A Dānava or infernal deity.
    5 Camphor. f. (-dā)
    1 An aquatic plant, (Pistia stratiotes.)
    2 A tree, (Gmelina arborea:) see gambhārī. (-dī) A medicinal and aromatic shrub, commonly Kāyaphal.
    E. ku the earth, &c. muda to be pleased, and ka aff.
