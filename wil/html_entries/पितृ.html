• pitṛ [535] m. (-tā) A father. dual (-rau) Mother and father, parents. plu. always. (-raḥ) 1 Paternal ancestors. 2 The manes, or the deceased and deified progenitors of mankind, inhabiting a peculiar region or heaven, or according to some, the orbit of the moon. E. pā to nourish, Uṇādi aff. tṛc.
