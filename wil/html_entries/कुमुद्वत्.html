• kumudvat [231] mfn. (-dvān-dvatī-dvat) A place abounding in the flowers of the Nymphaea esculenta. f. (-tī) 1 The same. 2 A multitude of lotus flowers. 3 The flexible stalk of a water lily. 4 A sort of water lily, (Menyanthes Indica or cristata.) E. kumuda the water lily, and matup poss. affix, or vati affix of comparison, fem. ṅīṣ.
