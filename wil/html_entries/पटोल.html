• paṭola [495] m. (-laḥ) A kind of cucumber, commonly Palwal, (Trichosanthes dioeca.) n. (-laṃ) A sort of cloth, a kind of chintz. f. (-lī) A small cucumber, either the same as above, (Trichosanthes dioeca,) or another kind, (Luffa acutangula.) E. paṭa to go, in the causal form, aff. olac; removing malady.
