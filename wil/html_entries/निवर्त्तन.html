• nivarttana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Returning, turning or coming back.
    2 Bringing back.
    3 Reforming, repenting.
    4 Desisting from.
    5 Giving back, returning.
    6 Stopping, ceasing.
    7 A measure of land 20 rods, or 200 cubits square.
    E. ni before vṛta to be, lyuṭ aff.
