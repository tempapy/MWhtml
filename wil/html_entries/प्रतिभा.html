• pratibhā f. (-bhā)
    1 Understanding, intellect, especially as opening or expanding.
    2 Sharpness, brilliancy of conception.
    3 Light, splendor.
    4 Reflected light.
    5 Audacity, boldness, confidence, consequence.
    E. prati before bhā to shine, affs. ḍa and ṭāp.
