• kṣāra [263] m. (-raḥ) 1 Glass. 2 Juice, essence. 3 Molasses, treacle. 4 Salt. 5 Ashes. 6 A rogue, a cheat. 7 Borax, borate of soda. 8 Alkali, either soda or potash. 9 Caustic alkali, one species of cautery. n. (-raṃ) A factitious or medicinal salt, commonly black salt: see kṛṣṇalavaṇa. E. kṣara to drop, or distil, affix aṇ.
