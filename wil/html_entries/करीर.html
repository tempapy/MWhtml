• karīra mn. (-raḥ-raṃ) The shoot of a bamboo; also karira. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A thorny plant, described as growing in desarts, and fed upon by camels, commonly Karit, (Capparis aphylla, Rox.)
    2 A water jar. f. (-rā or rī)
    1 The root of an elephant's tusk.
    2 A cricket, a small grasshopper, &c.
    E. kṛ to injure, to throw. or send, &c. and īraṇ aff.
