• evam ind. A particle implying.
    1 Likeness, (as, so, like.)
    2 Assent, (yes, verily.)
    3 Sameness of manner, (thus, in this way.)
    4 Certainly, (indeed, assuredly, even so.)
    5 Command, (so, thus, &c.)
    6 An expletive.
    E. iṇa to go, vam affix; also eva. [173]
