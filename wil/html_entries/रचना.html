• racanā [693] f. (-nā) 1 Orderly and becoming arrangement or disposition in general. 2 Decoration or dressing of the hair. 3 Stringing flowers, &c., weaving wreaths, chaplets, or similar decorations. 4 Military array, the arrangement of troops. 5 Literary composition, whether in verse or prose. 6 Suspending garlands, &c. at the doors of a house or building on festival occasions. 7 Making or preparing any thing. E. raca to make, to manufacture, aff. yuc.
