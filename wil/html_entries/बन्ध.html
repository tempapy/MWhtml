• bandha r. 9th cl. (badhnāti) r. 10th cl. (bandhayati) To bind or tie;
    with āṅ prefixed, To loosen, to let loose.
    With anu,
    1 To follow.
    2 To be affixed or attached to.
    With ni, to disunite.
    With sama, to connect.
  • bandha m. (-ndhaḥ)
    1 A pledge, a deposit.
    2 A binding, a tie or fetter, &c.
    3 Binding, tying.
    4 The body.
    E. bandha to tie, aff. ac.
