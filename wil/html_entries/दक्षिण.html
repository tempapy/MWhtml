• dakṣiṇa [395] mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) 1 Candid, sincere, honest, upright. 2 Right, (not left.) 3 South, southern. 4 Dependent, subject. 5 Clever, able, dexterous. f. (-ṇā) 1 A present to Brahmans, upon solemn or sacrificial occasions. 2 The south. 3 Completion of any rite, fixing or establishing any act or place. 4 A form or figure of DURGĀ, in which the right side is said to be advanced. 5 Donation to Brahmans, personified as a goddess, said to be born from KṚṢṆA'S right side. E. dakṣa to prosper, &c. Uṇādi affix inan.
