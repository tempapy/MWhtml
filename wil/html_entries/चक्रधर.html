• cakradhara [312] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Having a wheel, &c. m. (-raḥ) 1 A village or common tumbler or juggler. 2 One who holds a wheel, a discus, &c. 3 The manager or owner of many villages. 4 A name of VIṢṆU or KṚṢṆA, who is represented as holding a discus in one hand. 5 A snake. E. cakra a wheel, &c. and dhara who holds.
