• protha [592] (ṛ) prothṛ r. 1st cl. (prothati-te) 1 To be able, adequate or competent. 2 To be complete or ample. 3 To destroy, to subdue or overpower.
  • protha [592] mfn. (-thaḥ-thā-thaṃ) 1 Travelling, wayfaring, a traveller. 2 Notorious, famous. 3 Placed, fixed. mn. (-thaḥ-thaṃ) The nose of a horse or the tip of it. m. (-thaḥ) 1 The loins or hip. 2 Old clothes. 3 The embryo or fœtus. E. pru to go, Uṇādi aff. thak.
