• tana (u) tanu r. 8th cl. (tanoti-tanute) To expand, to spread, to extend, to diffuse, to dilate, &c. physically or morally. r. 1st and 10th cls. (tanati tānayati)
    1 To confide, to believe or have faith in.
    2 To aid, to assist.
    3 To be harmless.
    4 To sound.
    5 To pain or afflict with disease.
    6 (With a preposition,) To lengthen, to stretch, to expand, &c. as in the 8th cl.
