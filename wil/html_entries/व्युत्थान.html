• vyutthāna [821] n. (-naṃ) 1 Independence, following one's own inclination. 2 Opposition, contradiction, doing that which is prohibited. 3 Obstruction, prohibition, hindering or opposing any one. 4 Completion of religious contemplation, the end. of a period of abstraction. E. vi and ut before sthā to stay or be, aff. lyuṭ.
