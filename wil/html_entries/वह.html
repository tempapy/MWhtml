• vaha [745] r. 1st cl. (vahati) To bear, to carry, to convey or transport. (vahate) To flow as a stream. (i) vahi r. 10th cl. (vaṃhayati) To shine. r. 1st cl. (vaṃhate) To grow or increase; this last root is more properly bahi q. v.
  • vaha [745] m. (-haḥ) 1 Bearing, conveying. 2 Any vehicle or means of conveyance, as a horse, a car, &c. 3 The shoulder of an ox. 4 Air, wind. 5 A road, a way. 6 Any male river. 7 A measure of four Droṇas. f. (-hā) A river in general. E. vaha to bear, aff. gha.
