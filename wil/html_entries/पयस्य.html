• payasya [502] mfn. (-syaḥ-syā-syaṃ) 1 Made from milk, (curds, butter, cheese, &c.) 2 Relating to or connected with milk or water. f. (-syā) 1 A shrub, (Asclepias rosea.) 2 A medicinal kind of moon plant. 3 A drug: see kṣīrakākolī. 4 Curds of two milk whey. 5 Any plant yielding a milky sap, as the Euphorbia, the Asclepias, &c. E. payas milk, yat aff.
