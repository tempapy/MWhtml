• kunta [229] mf. (-ntaḥ-ntī) 1 A bearded dart, a lance. m. (-ntaḥ) 1 A species of grain, (Coix barbata, Rox.) 2 A small animal, an insect. 3 Passionateness. f. (-ntiḥ or -ntī) 1 The wife of PĀṆḌU, and mother of the three elder PĀṆḌAVA princes by as many gods. 2 The gum olibanum tree, (Boswellia thurifera.) 3 Bdellium, a fragrant resin. 4 The wife of a Brahman. E. ku bad, and anta end, destroying ill, enemies, &c. or ka the head, and unda to give pain, ta substituted for da, fem. affix in or ṅīṣ.
