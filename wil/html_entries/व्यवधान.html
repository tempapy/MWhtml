• vyavadhāna m. (-naṃ)
    1 Covering, disappearance, either the thing which conceals, or the state of being concealed from sight.
    2 Intervening, intervention, space.
    3 The intervention of a letter, (in grammar.)
    E. vi and ava before ghā to have, aff. lyuṭ. [816]
