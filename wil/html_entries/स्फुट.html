• sphuṭa [957] r. 1st cl. (spheṭate) r. 6th cl. (sphuṭati) To blow, to blossom, to open as a bud or flower. r. 10th cl. (sphoṭayati or with āṅ prefixed āsphoṭayati) also (ira) sphuṭira r. 1st cl. (sphoṭati) and (i) sphuṭi r. 1st cl. (sphuṇṭati) 1 To divide, to break, to tear, to pierce. 2 To hurt or kill. sphuṭi r. 10th cl. (sphuṇṭayati) To laugh at or with, to jest, to joke.
  • sphuṭa [957] mfn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭā-ṭaṃ) 1 Blown, opened, expanded, (as a flower.) 2 Apparent, manifest, evident. 3 Known, understood. 4 Plain, distinct. 5 Spread, diffused. 6 White. 7 Burst, broken. 8 Rent, torn. 9 (In astronomy,) True or apparent, as sphuṭasūryyagatiḥ true or apparent motion of the sun. f. (-ṭā) The expanded hood of a snake. E. sphuṭa to blow, as a flower, &c. aff. ka.
