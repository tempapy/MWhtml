• caitra m. (-traḥ)
    1 The month Chaitra or Cheyt, (March-April.)
    2 One of the ranges of mountains dividing the continent into portions or Varṣas: see varṣa.
    3 A Bauddha or Jaina religious mendicant.
    4 The son of BUDHA by CITRĀ. n. (-traḥ)
    1 A monument erected to the dead, a column or block of wood, a tree, &c. so considered.
    2 A small temple.
    E. citrā the star, or citra wonderful, &c. affix aṇ.
