• arhat mfn. (-n-ntī-t)
    1 Venerable, respectable.
    2 Praised, celebrated. m. (-n)
    1 A Jina or Jaina saint and teacher.
    2 A Jain or follower of the doctrines of Jina.
    3 A religious mendicant of that sect.
    E. arha to worship, &c. and śatṛ affix, of the present participle. [072]
