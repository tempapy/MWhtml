• dhanya [438] mfn. (-nyaḥ-nyā-nyaṃ) 1 Fortunate, well fated, lucky. 2 Good, virtuous. m. (-nyaḥ) 1 An infidel, an atheist. 2 A virtuous or fortunate man. f. (-nyā) 1 Emblic myrobalan. 2 A nurse. 3 Coriander. n. (-nyaṃ) Wealth. E. dhana to produce, (grain, &c.) or dhana wealth, and yat affix of fitness.
