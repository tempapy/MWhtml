• hastin m. (-stī) An elephant. f. (-nī)
    1 A female elephant.
    2 A female; one of the four kinds into which they are classed, and described as of low stature, corpulent habits, curly hair, dark complexion, libidinous appetite, and furious passions.
    3 A drug and perfume; also haṭṭavisnāsinī.
    E. hasta a trunk, and ini aff. [972]
