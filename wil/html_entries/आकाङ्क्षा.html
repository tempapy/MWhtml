• ākāṅkṣā f. (-ṅkṣā)
    1 Wish, desire.
    2 Purpose, intention.
    3 Looking at, to, or towards.
    4 Enquiry, asking.
    5 The presence of a word in a sentence indispensable to the sense.
    6 The significancy of a word.
    E. āṅ prefixed to kākṣi to desire, ac and ṭāp aff.
