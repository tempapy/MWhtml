• ra The twenty-seventh consonant of the Nāgarī Alphabet, corresponding to the letter R.
  • ra m. (raḥ)
    1 Fire or Agni.
    2 Heat, warmth.
    3 Burning, scorching.
    4 Love, desire.
    5 Speed. f. (-rā)
    1 Giving.
    2 Gold. f. (rī) Going, moving.
    E. rā to give, rama to play, &c., aff. ḍa.
