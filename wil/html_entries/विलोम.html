• viloma mfn. (-maḥ-mī-maṃ) Reverse, opposite, contrary, backward, against the hair or grain. m. (-maḥ)
    1 Reverse, opposite course or order, &c.
    2 A snake.
    3 VARUṆA, regent of the waters.
    4 A dog. n. (-maṃ) A water-wheel or machine for raising water. f. (-mī) Emblic myrobalan.
    E. vi implying difference, &c., and loma the hair of the body; also with kan added vilomaka.
