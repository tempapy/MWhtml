• bhāṇḍa m. (-ṇḍaṃ)
    1 Any vessel, a pot, a cup, a plate, &c.
    2 Any musical instrument.
    3 Any implement or utensil.
    4 Capital, principal.
    5 An ornament in general.
    6 An ornament round a horse's neck and breast.
    7 The bed of a river, the space between its banks.
    8 Mimickry, buffoonery, the practice of the Bhaṇḍa or professional jester. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A tree, (Hibiscus populneoides.)
    E. bhadi to be auspicious, aff. ac, or bhaṇa to sound, aff. ḍa, deriv. irr., or bhaṇḍa a jester, and aṇ aff. [616]
