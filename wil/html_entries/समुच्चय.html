• samuccaya [899] m. (-yaḥ) 1 Assemblage, collection, either in thought or fact. 2 Conjunction of words or sentences, the power of the particle and, or also; but it implies the conjunction of different objects of one act or different acts with the same object, as īśvaraṃ guruñca bhajasva worship GOD, and (or also) the Guru. E. sam and ut before ci to collect, aff. ṇac.
