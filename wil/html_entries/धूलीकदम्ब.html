• dhūlīkadamba m. (-mbaḥ)
    1 A species of the Kadamba tree, (Nauclea cordifolia.)
    2 Tila, a plant.
    3 A tree: see varuṇa (Tapia cratæva.)
    E. dhalī dust, and vadamba Kadamba, whose pollen perhaps resembles that of the Kadamba, or having a similar quantity of blossoms; also with kan added dhūlīkadambaka; these words are also written with a short i, as dhūlikadambaka. [446]
