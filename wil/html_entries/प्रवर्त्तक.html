• pravarttaka [578] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Inciting, stimulating, inducing, who or what sets in action. m. (-kaḥ) 1 The original instigator of any act, the author, the principal. 2 An arbiter, a judge. n. (-kaṃ) The entrance of the first person of the drama on the close of the introduction. E. pra before vṛta to be, aff. ṇvul.
