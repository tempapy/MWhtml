• cikura mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Rashly criminal, inconsiderately guilty, punishing or injuring others without consideration. m. (-raḥ)
    1 Hair.
    2 A mountain.
    2 A snake.
    4 A musk rat.
    5 A kind of bird.
    6 A kind of tree.
    E. ci imitative sound, and kura to utter, affix ka.
