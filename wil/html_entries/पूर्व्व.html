• pūrvva r. 1st cl. (pūrvvati) To fill. 10th cl. (pūrvvayati)
    1 To dwell.
    2 To invite: the root is written either pūrbba or pūrvva. [549]
  • pūrvva mfn. (-rvvaḥ-rvvī-rvvaṃ)
    1 First, former, prior, preceding, initial.
    2 East, eastern.
    3 All, entire.
    4 Before, in front of. m. plu. (-rvvāḥ) Ancestors, forefathers. f. (-rvvā) The east. n. (-rvvaṃ) An ancient tradition.
    E. pūrbba or pūrvva to fill, to dwell, &c. aff. ac; hence the derivative, and its compounds may be written either with ba or va; the second of the duplicate consonants may also be rejected, making it pūrba or pūrva. [549]
