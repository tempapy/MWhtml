• tha The seventeenth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, and second of the dental class; the aspirate of the preceding letter, and expressed by Ṭh. [394]
  • tha n. (thaṃ)
    1 Auspiciousness.
    2 Fear, terror.
    3 Preserving, preservation. m. (-thaḥ)
    1 A mountain.
    2 Eating.
    3 A disease.
    E. sthā to stand, affix ḍa. [394]
