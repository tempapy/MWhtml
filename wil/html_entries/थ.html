• tha [394] The seventeenth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, and second of the dental class; the aspirate of the preceding letter, and expressed by Ṭh.
  • tha [394] n. (thaṃ) 1 Auspiciousness. 2 Fear, terror. 3 Preserving, preservation. m. (-thaḥ) 1 A mountain. 2 Eating. 3 A disease. E. sthā to stand, affix ḍa.
