• kardama m. (-maḥ)
    1 Mud, mire, clay.
    2 Sin.
    3 A Prajāpati, the son of BRAHMĀ by CHĀYĀ, and he father of Kapila: (this personage appears to be an innovation in Hindu mythology.) f. (-mī) A plant. n. (-maṃ) Flesh.
    E. karda to sound badly, and ama Uṇādi aff. [197]
