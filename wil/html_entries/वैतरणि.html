• vaitaraṇi [810] f. (-ṇiḥ-ṇī) 1 The river of hell. 2 The mother of the Rākṣasas. E. vi various, taraṇa crossing, aff. iñ or aṇ, and ṅīṣ aff., or vi prohibitive, taraṇī crossing; or vitaraṇa giving, (liberality in life securing a passage;) or again, vi priv. taraṇī a boat, passed without a boat; the affix is the same.
