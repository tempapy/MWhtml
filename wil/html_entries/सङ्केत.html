• saṅketa [880] r. 10th cl. (saṅketayati) 1 To call or invite. 2 To counsel or advise. 3 To fix or appoint a time.
  • saṅketa [880] m. (-taḥ) 1 Engagement, agreement, appointment, convention. 2 Condition, provision, the circumstances under which any thing is necessarily or naturally effected. 3 Sign, gesture, gesticulation. E. saṅketa to appoint a time, &c., aff. ac.
