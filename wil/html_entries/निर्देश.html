• nirdeśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 Order, command, authoritative instruction or direction.
    2 Description, designation.
    3 Depicting, pointing out or exhibiting.
    4 Certainty, ascertainment.
    5 Saying, telling.
    6 Vicinity, proximity.
    E. nir before diśa to show, affix ghañ. [475]
