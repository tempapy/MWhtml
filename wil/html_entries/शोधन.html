• śodhana [858] mfn. (-naḥ-nā-naṃ) Cleaning, purifying, &c., that which cleanses, refines, &c. n. (-naṃ) 1 Cleaning, cleansing, purifying. 2 Correcting, freeing from faults or errors. 3 Correcting, as a writing. 4 Subtraction, (in arithmetic.) 5 The refining of metals. 6 A sort of refining, practised for chemical or medical purposes; exposing the metals to heat, and then sprinkling them with the urine of cows, &c. 7 Payment, acquittance. 8 Fæces, ordure. 9 Green vitriol. m. (-naḥ) The lime. f. (-nī) A broom. E. śugha to be or make pure, aff. lyuṭ or yuc.
