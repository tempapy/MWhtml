• vivasvat m. (-svān)
    1 A god.
    2 The sun.
    3 ARUṆA, the charioteer of the sun.
    4 The seventh or present MANU; also VAIVASVATA. f. (-tī) The city of the sun.
    E. vi implying variety, vasa to cover or hide, affix kvip, vivas here said therefore to imply various covering, as a garment of light, i. e. the rays of the sun, and matup poss. aff. [784]
