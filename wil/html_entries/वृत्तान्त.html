• vṛttānta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 Tidings, rumor, intelligence.
    2 Sort, kind, difference.
    3 Mode, manner.
    4 Whole.
    5 Interval, rest, leisure, opportunity.
    6 Topic, subject.
    7 A tale, a story.
    8 Property, nature.
    9 Alone, solitary.
    E. vṛtta observance, &c., and anta, end, term.
