  • sparśa [956] m. (-rśaḥ) 1 Touch, contact. 2 The thing touching or feeling. 3 Gift, donation. 4 Sickness, disease. 5 Morbid heat. 6 A consonant of either of the five first classes of the alphabet. 7 An enemy, a disease, &c., the agent or instrument of pain, trouble, or distress. 8 Air, wind. f. (-rśā) A wanton, an unchaste woman. E. spṛśa to touch, aff. ghañ.
