• phalaka [595] mn. (-kaḥ-kaṃ) 1 A shield. 2 A bench. 3 A plank. 4 A layer. m. (-kaḥ) 1 A bone, the os frontis, or bone of the forehead. 2 A leaf or page for writing on. 3 A plant, (Mesua ferrea.) n. (-kaṃ) The buttocks. E. kan added to the preceding, or phala to split or divide, aff. vun.
