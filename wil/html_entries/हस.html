• hasa (e) hase r. 1st cl. (hasati)
    1 To laugh.
    2 To laugh at, to ridicule: (this root does not take the middle voice when it implies reciprocal action, as vyatihasanti they laugh at each other.) [972]
  • hasa m. (-saḥ) Laughter, laughing, laugh.
    E. hasa to laugh, aff. ap. [972]
