• musala n. (-laṃ)
    1 A pestle, a wooden pestle used for cleaning rice.
    2 A club. f. (-lī)
    1 A plant, (Curculigo orchioides.)
    2 A house lizard.
    E. musa to break, aff. kalac; it is also read with its compounds and derivatives muṣala, muṣalī, and sometimes muśala, &c.
