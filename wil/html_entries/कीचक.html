• kīcaka [223] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A bamboo whistling or rattling in the wind. 2 Any hollow bamboo. 3 A reed. 4 A Daitya or infernal demon so named. 5 A particular Rākṣasa or goblin. 6 A kind of tree. 7 A prince, the son of KEKAYA. E. cīka to touch, to sound, vun Uṇādi affix, and the radical consonants transposed.
