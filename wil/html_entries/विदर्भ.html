• vidarbha [767] mf. (-rbhaḥ-rbhā) A district and city to the south-west of Bengal, the modern Bara-Nagpur or Berar proper. m. (-rbhaḥ) Any dry or desart soil. E. vi privative, and darbha the sacred grass; not growing in that country in consequence of the curse of a saint, whose son had died of a wound from a blade of the grass.
