• sāhasika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Castigatory, inflicted as punishment.
    2 Perpetrated by violence.
    3 Violent, felonious, rapacious, brutal, cruel.
    4 Impetuous, rash.
    5 Bold, daring. m. (-kaḥ) A robber, a free-booter.
    E. sāhasa violence, and ṭhak aff. [922]
