• palaṅkaṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 A Rākṣasa, an imp or goblin. mf. (-ṣaḥ-ṣā) The Bdellium tree. f. (-ṣā)
    1 A plant, (Ruellia longifolia.)
    2 The ichneumon plant: see rāsnā.
    3 The Palāśa tree, (Butea frondosa.)
    4 Lac, the animal dye.
    5 A creeper: see muṇḍīrī.
    E. pala flesh, and kaṣa what injures, aff. khac.
