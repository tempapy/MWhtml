• garbhāgāra [285] n. (-raṃ) 1 An inner and private room, the female apartments, the lying-in chamber. 2 The sanctuary of a temple, the chamber where the object of worship is placed. E. garbha the foetus, and āgāra an abode, hence it is also applied to the womb: see garbhāśaya.
