• mukti f. (-ktiḥ)
    1 Final beatitude, the delivery of the soul from the body and exemption from further transmigration; the re-absorption of the emancipated spirit into its great primary source, identification with GOD.
    2 Liberation, setting or becoming free or loose.
    E. mugha to let loose, aff. ktin. [663]
