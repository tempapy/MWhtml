• dvāḥsthitadarśaka m. (-kaḥ) A warder, a porter, a chamberlain.
    E. dvār a door, sthita staying, and darśaka who shows, who introduces to the prince or great man to whom the mansion belongs; also dvāḥsthita and darśaka severally; also dvāḥsthitadarśin, m. (-rśī) [431]
