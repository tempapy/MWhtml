• varhis [741] m. (-rhiḥ) 1 Fire. 2 Light, lustre, splendour. mn. (-hiḥ) 1 Sacrificial grass, (Poa cynosuroides.) 2 Sacrifice, oblation. 2 Ganṭhiāla, a vegetable perfume. E. vṛha to increase, aff. isi, or varha to excel, &c., with the same aff.; in the latter case this word and its corresponding ones are written with an initial consonant barhis, &c.
