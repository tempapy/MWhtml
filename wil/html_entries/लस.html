• lasa [718] r. 1st cl. (lasati) 1 To embrace. 2 To sport or play. r. 10th cl. (lāsayati) 1 To be skilful, to do any thing skilfully or scientifically. 2 To work.
    With ut prefixed, 1 To shine, to be brilliant or splendid. 2 To be happy or delighted.
    With vi, To gambol, to sport, especially amorously.
