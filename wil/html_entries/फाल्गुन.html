• phālguna m. (-naḥ)
    1 A name of ARJUNA.
    2 A sort of tree, (Pentaptera Arjuna.)
    3 A month, commonly called by the name Phālguna, (February-March.) f. (-nī)
    1 Day of full moon on the month Phālguna, on which the Holī or great vernal festival of the Hindus is celebrated.
    2 A name common to the eleventh and twelfth lunar asterisms, distinguished by the epithets first and last, or pūrvva and untaraḥ see pūrvvaphālgunī, &c.
