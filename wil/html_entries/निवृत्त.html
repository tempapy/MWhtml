• nivṛtta [479] mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ) 1 Ceased, stopped. 2 Abstaining from worldly acts, &c. 3 Abstracted from or independant of worldly motives. 3 Desisting from or repenting of any improper conduct. 4 Returned, turned back. 5 Gone, departed. 6 Finished, completed. E. ni prohibitory prefix, vṛta to be, aff. kta; see nirvṛtta.
