• kalatra n. (-traṃ)
    1 The hip and loins.
    2 A wife.
    3 A royal citadel, a strong hold or fastness.
    E. gaḍa to distil, &c. atrac affix, ka is substituted for ga, and ḍa and la are interchangeable: see kaḍatra; or kala weak, feeble, and tra, from trai to preserve, affix ḍa. [200]
