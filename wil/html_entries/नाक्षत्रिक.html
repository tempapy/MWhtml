• nākṣatrika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) Relating or belonging to the lunar asterisms. m. (-kaḥ) A month of twenty-seven days, each day being the period of the moon's passage through a lunar asterism. f. (-kī) The state or condition to which a person is subjected agreeably to the asterism presiding over his nativity.
    E. nakṣatra as above, and ṭhak aff. [458]
