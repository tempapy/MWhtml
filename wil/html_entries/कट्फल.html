• kaṭphala m. (-laḥ) A small tree found in the north-west of Hindustan, the bark and seeds of which are used in medicine, and as aromatics; the fruit also is eaten; the common name is Kayaphal. f. (-lā)
    1 A plant, (Gmelina arborea, Rox.)
    2 The egg plant, &c.
    E. kaṭ instead of kaṭu acrid, and phala fruit, fem. affix ṭāp.
