• rāsa (ṛ) rāsṛ r. 1st cl. (rāsate) To sound. [705]
  • rāsa m. (-saḥ)
    1 Confused noise, uproar, hubbub.
    2 Sound in general.
    3 Speech, discourse.
    4 A chain.
    5 A festival amongst the cowherds, including songs and dances, especially the circular dance as performed by KṚṢṆA and the Gopīs or cowherdesses.
    6 A game played by children.
    E. rasa to sound, ghañ aff. [705]
