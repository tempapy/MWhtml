• śṛṅgāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Love, the passion or sentiment as an object of poetical description or dramatic representation.
    2 Copulation, coition.
    3 Marks made with red-lead on an elephant's head and trunk by way of ornament. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Cloves.
    2 Redlead.
    3 Fragrant powder for the dress or person.
    4 Ginger.
    5 Agallochum.
    E. śṛṅga eminence, dignity, ṛ to go to or obtain, and aṇ aff.; being the chief of the poetical Rasas; or śṝ to hurt, āran Uṇādi aff. with num, and guk augments.
