• upasaṃhāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Witholding, refusing.
    2 Taking away.
    3 Excepting, excluding.
    4 Destruction, death, end.
    5 Collection, assemblage.
    6 A compendium.
    7 Brevity, conciseness.
    8 Attacking, invasion.
    9 (In logic,) Refutation.
    E. upa and sam before hṛ to take, ghañ aff. [158]
