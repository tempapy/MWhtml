• samaṣṭhilā [895] f. (-lā) A sort of potherb, said to grow in watery places; according to some authorities, a kind of cucumber, to others a shrub, called commonly gāṃḍaraddhavī. E. sama together, sthā to stay, ilac aff. deriv. irr. or sama with, asthi a bone, (a seed,) lā to get or have, aff. ka, also samaṣṭhīlā.
