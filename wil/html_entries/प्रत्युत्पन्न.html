• pratyutpanna mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ)
    1 Reproduced, regenerated.
    2 Prompt, ready.
    3 (In arithmetic,) Multiplied. n. (-nnaṃ)
    1 Multiplication.
    2 The product of a sum in multiplication.
    E. prati again, utpanna produced, or prati and ut before panna made, done.
