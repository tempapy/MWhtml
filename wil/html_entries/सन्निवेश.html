• sanniveśa [891] m. (-śaḥ) 1 An open space, either in a town or its vicinity, where the people take exercise or diversion. 2 Vicinity, proximity, neighbourhood. 3 Fabrication, manufacture, construction, preparation. 4 Putting in, causing to enter. 5 Union, junction. 6 Assemblage, collection. 7 The collective form or figure of an asterism. E. sam and ni before viśa to enter, aff. ghañ.
