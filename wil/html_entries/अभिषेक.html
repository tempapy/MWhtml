• abhiṣeka m. (-kaḥ) Bathing, sprinkling.
    E. abhi before sica to sprinkle, and ghañ affix; the word is often used for initiation, royal unction, &c. sprinkling with the water of the Ganges, or water in which various articles have been immersed, being an essential part of the rites: also for a religious ceremony, including the presentation of a variety of articles, fruits, gems, &c. along with water or fluid substances, for the bathing of the deity to whom worship is offered.
