• viṣayin [791] mfn. (-yī-yinī-yi) 1 Attached to objects of sense, carnal, sensual. 2 Conversant with or interested in worldly occupations or enjoyments. n. (-yi) An organ of sense. m. (-yī) 1 A king. 2 The deity KĀMA. 3 A sensualist. 4 A materialist, one who denies the existence of any thing which is not an object of sense. 5 A man of business or of the world. E. viṣaya an organ of sense, aff. in.
