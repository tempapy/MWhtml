• pāṇḍara [523] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Pale or yellowish white. m. (-raḥ) 1 Pale or yellowish white, (the colour.) 2 A plant, commonly Marüa or Marūvaka. n. (-raṃ) 1 Many-flowered jasmine. 2 Red chalk. E. paḍi to go, ara Uṇādi aff., and the radical vowel made long; also pāṇḍura.
