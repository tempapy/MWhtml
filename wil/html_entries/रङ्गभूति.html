• raṅgabhūti m. (-tiḥ) The day of full moon in the month Aśvin, (September-October,) on the night of which LAKṢMĪ descended, promising wealth to all who were awake.
    E. raṅga dancing and singing, and bhūti being, the night being spent in playing ancient chess, in Nāces, &c. [693]
