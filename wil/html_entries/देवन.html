• devana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Sport, play, pastime, &c.
    2 Emulation, desire to excel or overcome.
    3 Affair, business, profession.
    4 Gaming.
    5 Splendour, lustre, beauty.
    6 Light.
    7 Praise.
    8 Motion.
    9 Grief, sorrow.
    10 A garden, a play or pleasure ground.
    11 A lotus. m. (-naḥ) A die or dice. f. (-nā)
    1 Service.
    2 Sport, wanton sport or pastime.
    E. diva to play, &c. affix lyuṭ, or yuc, fem. ṭāp. [422]
