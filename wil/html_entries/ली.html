• lī r. 4th cl. (līyate) r. 9th cl. (lināti)
    1 To join, to adhere or cling to, to be in union or connexion with.
    2 To obtain. r. 10th cl. (layati lāyayati lāpayati lālayati or līnayati) To melt, to liquefy, to fuse or dissolve.
    With āṅ, To waste away.
    With pra and vi, To acquire or gain. [721]
  • lī f. (-līḥ) Embracing.
    E. lī to embrace, kvip aff. [721]
