• kārṣāpaṇa mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ) A weight or measure of different values, as synonymous with Karṣa; it is of gold, weighing sixteen Māṣas, or about 176 grains; of silver, it is in value sixteen Paṇas of kowries, or in tale 1280 commonly termed a Kāhan: of copper it weighs eighty Rattis, or the same as of gold, about 176 grains; according to some, it is the same as a Pan of kowries, or in tale eighty. n. (-ṇaṃ) Money, gold and silver. m. (-ṇaḥ) A husbandman.
    E. karṣa a Karṣa of silver, āpaṇa custom, habit, and the vowel of karṣa lengthened; also with kan added kārṣāpaṇakaḥ see karṣapaṇa and kārṣika. [216]
