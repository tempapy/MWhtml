• aka r. 1st cl. (akati) To wind or move tortuously. With an indicatory i. aki (aṃkati)
    1 To mark or bespot.
    2 To go. See aṅka.
  • aka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Moving tortuously.
    2 One who goes crookedly.
    3 Pain, affliction.
    4 Sin.
    E. a priv. and ka happiness, or aka to move crookedly, aff. ac.
