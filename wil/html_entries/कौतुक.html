• kautuka [253] n. (-kaṃ) 1 Eagerness, vehemence, impatience. 2 Joy, pleasure, happiness. 3 Sport, pastime. 4 A festival, festivity. 5 Wish, inclination. 6 Curiosity. 7 Kind or friendly greeting, civility, salutation. 8 The marriage thread or ring. 9 The enjoyment of public diversions. 10 Song, dance, shew or spectacle. 11 Season of enjoyment. E. kutuka, and aṇ aff.
