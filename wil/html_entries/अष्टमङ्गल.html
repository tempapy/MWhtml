• aṣṭamaṅgala m. (-laḥ) A horse with a white face, tail, mane, breast, and hoofs. n. (-laṃ) A collection of eight lucky things to be assembled on certain occasions, as at a coronation, &c. as a lion, a bull, an elephant, a water jar, a fan, a flag, a trumpet, and a lamp; or a Brahman, a cow, fire, gold, ghee, the sun, water, and a king.
    E. aṣṭa eight, and maṅgala auspicious. [092]
