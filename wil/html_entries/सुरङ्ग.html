• suraṅga m. (-ṅgaḥ)
    1 The orange.
    2 Bright or good colour or dye. n. (-ṅgaṃ)
    1 Red sanders.
    2 Vermilion. f. (-ṅgā) A hole cut in a wall to break into a house, &c. see suruṅgā.
    2 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus.)
    3 Crystal.
    E. su well, and raṅga colour.
