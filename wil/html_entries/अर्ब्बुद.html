• arbbuda [072] mn. (-daḥ-daṃ) 1 Ophthalmia. 2 A hundred millions. m. (-daḥ) 1 The name of a mountain in the west of India, commonly Abū, a place of pilgrimage, especially to the Jains. 2 An indolent swelling. E. arba to injure, udac affix; the root and derivative are also written with the dental letter.
