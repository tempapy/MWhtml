• prayatna n. (-tnaṃ)
    1 Continued or persevering effort, exertion.
    2 Act, action.
    2 (In logic,) Active effort of three kinds, engaging in any act, prosecuting it, and completing it.
    4 Caution, care.
    5 Articulation of sounds.
    E. pra intensitive, yatna effort.
