• upadrava [154] m. (-vaḥ) 1 Tyranny, oppression. 2 National distress, whether the act of the seasons or the king, famine, exaction, &c. 3 National commotion, rebellion. 4 Violence. 5 A supervenient disease, one brought one whilst a person labours under another. E. upa, dru to go, ac aff.
