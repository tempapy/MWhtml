• pārāyaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Totality, entireness, completeness.
    2 Going over, reading or doing any thing completely.
    3 Going across.
    4 Reading a Purāṇa, or causing it to be read. f. (-ṇī)
    1 The goddess SARASVATĪ.
    2 Action, act.
    3 Considering, meditating.
    4 Light.
    E. pāra the boundary or limit, and ayana going to, extending: when used attributively it remains always neuter.
