• sāvana mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ) Natural, (in astronomy) as Sāvana Dina, a natural day, from sunrise to sunrise. m. (-naḥ)
    1 The conclusion of a sacrifice, the ceremonies by which it is terminated.
    2 An employer of priests for a sacrifice.
    3 The deity VARUṆA.
    4 A month of thirty solar days.
    E. ṣū to bear or produce, aff. lyuṭ, and aṇ added. [922]
