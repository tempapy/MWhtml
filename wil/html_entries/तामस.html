• tāmasa mfn. (-saḥ-sī-saṃ)
    1 Affected by or appertaining to the third quality, that of darkness or vice: belonging to it, as a Purāṇa, a system of philosophy, a ceremonial rite, &c.
    2 Dark, of or belonging to darkness. m. (-saḥ)
    1 A snake.
    2 An incendiary, a malignant and mischievous man, a villain.
    3 An owl.
    4 The fourth Menu. f. (-sī)
    1 A dark night.
    2 A name of the goddess DURGĀ. n. (-saṃ) The quality of darkness.
    E. tamas the quality of darkness, &c. affix aṇ. [372]
