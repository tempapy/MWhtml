• revā f. (-vā)
    1 The Nerbadda, or Narmadā river, which rises in the mountain Āmrakūṭa, or Amerkantak, in the province of Gondvāna, and runs nearly due west about 750 miles, when it falls into the sea below Baroach.
    2 RATI, the wife of KĀMA.
    3 The indigo plant.
    E. reva to flow, aff. ac. [710]
