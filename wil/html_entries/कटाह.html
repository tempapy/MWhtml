• kaṭāha m. (-haḥ)
    1 A shallow boiler for oil or butter.
    2 A frying pan.
    3 A winnowing basket.
    4 A turtle's shell.
    5 A young buffalo whose horns are just appearing.
    6 A Dvīpa or division of the known continent, so called.
    7 Hell, the infernal regions.
    8 A well.
    E. kaṭa much, &c. hana to destroy, with āṅ prefixed, and ḍa aff.
