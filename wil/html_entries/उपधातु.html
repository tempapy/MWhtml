• upadhātu m. (-tuḥ)
    1 An inferior mineral, a semi-metal: seven are specified; pyrites, sulphate of copper, talc, antimony, red orpiment, yellow orpiment, and calx of brass.
    2 Secondary secretion, as the milk, menses, adeps, sweat, teeth, hair, and lymph.
    E. upa minor, and dhātu mineral, &c.
