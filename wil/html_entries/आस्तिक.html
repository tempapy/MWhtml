• āstika m. (-kaḥ) The name of a Muni or saint.
    E. asti who is, and vuñ affix; from an expression of his father, (he is in the womb,) pronouncing his conception to have taken place before there were any external indications of it. mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) A believer, pious, faithful.
    E. asti as before, saying that there is a GOD, &c. in opposition to the nāstika an atheist, and ṭhak aff. [127]
