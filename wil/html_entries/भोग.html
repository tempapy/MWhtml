• bhoga [628] m. (-gaḥ) 1 Pleasure, enjoyment. 2 Wealth. 3 Nourishing, cherishing. 4 Eating. 5 A snake's body. 6 A snake's expanded hood. 7 Hire. 8 The hire of dancing girls or courtezans. 9 A snake. 10 An army in column. 11 (In arithmetic,) The numerator of a fraction. E. bhuja to eat, &c., aff. ghañ.
