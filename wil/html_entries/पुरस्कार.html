• puraskāra [543] m. (-raḥ) 1 Making perfect or complete, finishing, polishing. 2 Preparing, getting ready. 3 Arranging, putting in array. 4 Placing before. 5 Honouring, respecting. 6 Worshipping. 7 Sprinkling with holy water. 8 Promise. 9 Accusation. 10 Attacking, assailing. 11 Anticipating, expecting. E. puras, and kāra making.
