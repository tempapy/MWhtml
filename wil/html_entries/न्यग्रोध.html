• nyagrodha m. (-dhaḥ)
    1 The Indian fig tree, (Ficus Indica.)
    2 A fathom measured by the arms extended.
    3 The Śami, (Mimosa albida.) f. (-dhī)
    1 A plant, (Salvinia cucullata, Rox.)
    2 A medical plant, commonly Mohānā.
    E. nyak short, rudha to impede, aff. aṇ. [489]
