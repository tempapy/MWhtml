• sambhoga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 Enjoyment, pleasure, delight.
    2 Coition, copulation.
    3 A Jaina or Bauddha Śasana or edict, &c.
    4 One branch of the Śṛṅgāra rasa, or sentiment of love, happy or successful love, meeting or union of lovers.
    5 A catamite.
    6 Use, employment.
    E. sam with, bhoga enjoyment.
