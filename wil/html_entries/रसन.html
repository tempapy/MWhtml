• rasana [698] n. (-naṃ) 1 Tasting, taste. 2 Sound, noise. f. (-nā) 1 The tongue. 2 A plant, commonly Rasan. 3 A woman's girdle, a sort of chain worn round the loins. 4 A plant, (Pæderia foetida.) E. rasa to sound or taste, aff. yuc; or aśa to eat, yuc Uṇādi aff., and raśa substituted; also raśanā.
