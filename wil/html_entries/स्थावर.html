• sthāvara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Fixed, stationary, stable, immoveable.
    2 Regular, established. m. (-raḥ) A mountain. n. (-raṃ)
    1 A bow-string.
    2 Immoveable property, land or houses.
    3 Family property, jewels, &c. which have been long in a family, and which ought not to be sold or given away.
    E. ṣṭhā to stand, varac aff.
