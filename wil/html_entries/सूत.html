• sūta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Born, engendered.
    2 Sent, dispatched.
    3 Drank.
    4 Gone, departed. m. (-taḥ)
    1 A charioteer.
    2 A carpenter.
    3 A man of a mixed race, descended from a Kṣatriya father, and mother of the sacerdotal tribe; his occupation is managing horses and driving cars.
    4 A bard, an encomiast.
    5 The sun. mn. (-taḥ-taṃ) Quicksilver. f. (-tā) A woman lying in.
    E. ṣū to bring forth, kta aff. [940]
