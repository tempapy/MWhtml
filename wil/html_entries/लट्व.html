• laṭva m. (-ṭvaḥ)
    1 A horse.
    2 A division of music.
    3 A low and wild tribe, a tribe of mountaineers. f. (-ṭvā)
    1 A bird said, to be a kind of sparrow.
    2 A fruit, a variety of the Karanja.
    3 A bad or unchaste woman.
    4 Curls on the forehead or hanging down on the face.
    5 Safflower.
    6 A Sweetmeat.
    E. laṭa to be like a child, Uṇādi aff. kvan.
