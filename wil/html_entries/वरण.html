• varaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Appointing, selecting, choosing.
    2 Surrounding, inclosing.
    3 Screening, covering.
    4 Nourishing, supporting. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 An outer building, an enclosure, a wall of masonry, &c. raised on a mound of earth.
    2 A causeway, a bridge.
    3 A camel.
    4 A tree, (Capparis trifoliata.)
    5 Any tree. f. (-ṇā) A rivulet, running past the north of Benares, into the Ganges, now called the Berṇā.
    E. vṛ to choose or appoint, aff. cayu.
