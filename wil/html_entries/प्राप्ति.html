• prāpti [588] f. (-ptiḥ) 1 Gain, profit. 2 Acquiring, getting, obtaining. 3 Improvement, success. 4 Rise, ascent. 5 Collection, assemblage, quantity. 6 Happy denouement, successful termination of a plot. 7 Guessing, discovering from a hint. 8 One of the eight superhuman faculties, the power of obtaining every thing. E. pra before āpa to get or obtain, aff. ktin.
