• apavarttana [045] n. (-naṃ) 1 Agitating, shaking. 2 Taking away, removal, resumption. 3 Transferring, removing from one place to another. 4 Abbreviation, abridging. 5 Division by a common measure, reduction of a fraction to its lowest terms. E. apa away, vṛta to be, lyuṭ aff.
