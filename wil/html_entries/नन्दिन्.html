• nandin [453] mfn. (-ndī-ndinī-ndi) Happy, rejoiced, delighted. m. (-ndī) 1 The name of one of ŚIVA'S chamberlains, and chief attendants. 2 The Indian fig tree. 3 Pārspīpal, (Hibiscus populneoides.) 4 The speaker of the prelude or prologue to a drama. f. (-ndinī) 1 A name of the goddess PĀRVATĪ. 2 GAṄGĀ, the river goddess. 3 A husband's sister. 4 A fabulous cow, related to the cow of plenty, and the property of the sage VAŚIṢṬHA. 5 The Alakanandā river. 6 The mother of the writer VYĀḌI. E. nanda happiness, ini aff.
