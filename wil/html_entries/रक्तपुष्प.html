• raktapuṣpa m. (-ṣpaḥ)
    1 Nerium odorum, the red variety.
    2 A small tree, (Andersonia rohitaca, Rox.) mf. (-ṣpaḥ-ṣpā) The silk-cotton tree, (Bombax heptaphyllum.) f. (-ṣpī)
    1 The trumpet flower, (Bignonia suave-olens.)
    2 Bryony, (Bryonia grandis.)
    E. rakta red, and puṣpa flower, whence it may be applied to any plant bearing red blossoms. [691]
