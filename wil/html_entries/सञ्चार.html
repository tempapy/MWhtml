• sañcāra [883] m. (-raḥ) 1 Difficult progress. 2 Difficulty, distress. 3 Leading, guiding. 4 Inciting. 5 Impelling, setting in motion. 6 Contagion, communication or transmission of disease. 7 Course, transition. 8 A gem supposed to be in the head of a serpent. E. sam before cara to go, aff. ghañ: see sañcara, or sam and cara to go, causal v., ac aff.
