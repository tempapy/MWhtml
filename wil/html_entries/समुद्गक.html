• samudgaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A casket, a covered box.
    2 A sort of stanza: one, each half of which consists of the very same words but in different acceptations, those of one half being respectively the the attributives or adjectives to those of the other.
    E. sam and uṭ before gama to go, aff. ḍa and kan added.
