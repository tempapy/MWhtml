• payovrata n. (-taṃ) Living upon milk for a month, which with prayer and residence in a cow house, is an expiation for receiving an unsuitable present; offering of milk to VIṢṆU, and subsisting upon it for twelve days; also for one or for three days as a religious act.
    E. payas milk, and vrata religious observance.
