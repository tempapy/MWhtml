• paiśāca mfn. (-caḥ-cī-caṃ) Infernal, demoniacal, relating or belonging to a Piśāca or goblin. m. (-caḥ) A mode of marriage, the ravishment of a girl by her lover. f. (-cī) A present to a friend, or to secure friendly regard made at a religious ceremony.
    E. piśāca an imp, aff. aṇ. [554]
