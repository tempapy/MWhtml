• viṣṭara m. (-raḥ)
    1 A tree.
    2 A handful of Kuśa or sacred grass.
    3 A seat, a stool, a chair, a couch, &c.
    4 The seat of the Brahman, either real or in effigy, as presiding at a sacrifice.
    5 A bed.
    6 A seat made of twenty-five straws of Kuśa grass tied up in a sheaf.
    E. vi before stṛ to spread, aff. apa, and sa changed to ṣa. [792]
