• sambādha adj. m. only, but having the three genders when the last term of a compound adjective. (-dhaḥ)
    1 Narrow, contracted, impassible, either from being naturally confined, or from being blocked up or crowded.
    2 Crowded. Subst.
    1 The vulva.
    2 The road to tartarus or hell.
    3 Fear, dread.
    E. sam before bādha to impede, aff. ac. [904]
