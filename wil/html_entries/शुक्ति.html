• śukti [850] f. (-ktiḥ) 1 A pearl oyster. 2 A conch. 3 A small shell. 4 A cockle. 5 A sort of perfume, in appearance a dried shell fish. 6 A portion of the skull used as a cup, &c. 7 A curl or feather on a horse's neck or breast. 8 A weight of two Karṣas or half a Pala. A disease of the cornea, formation of specks on it. E. śuc to sorrow, &c., aff. ktic.
