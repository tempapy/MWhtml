• vitaṇḍa [766] m. (-ṇḍaḥ) 1 A sort of lock or bolt with three divisions or wards. 2 An elephant. f. (-ṇḍā) 1 Controversy, argument, the subversion of another's opinion or interpretation, and establishment of one's own. 2 Criticism. 3 An esculent root, (Arum colocasia.) 4 Benzoin or styrax (?) 5 The Oleander plant, (Nerium odorum.) 6 A ladle, a spoon. E. vi before taḍi to beat, aff. ac and ṭāp.
