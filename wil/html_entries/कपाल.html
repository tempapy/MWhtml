• kapāla m. (-laḥ-laṃ)
    1 The skull, the cranium.
    2 Either half of a water jar.
    3 Any flat bone.
    4 A beggar's bowl.
    5 Multitude, assemblage, collection.
    6 A species of leprosy.
    7 A treaty of peace on equal terms.
    E. kapi to tremble, and kālan Uṇādi affix; or ka the head, and pāla what cherishes or protects.
