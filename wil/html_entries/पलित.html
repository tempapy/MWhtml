• palita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-knī-taṃ) Greyhaired, old. m. (-taḥ) An old man. f. (-tā) An old woman. n. (-taṃ)
    1 Greyness of the hair.
    2 Much or ornamented hair.
    3 Heat, burning.
    4 Mud, mire.
    5 Benzoin.
    E. pala to go, (towards death,) Uṇādi aff. kta, or pala flesh, &c. and itac aff. [519]
