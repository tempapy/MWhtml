• camara n. (-raṃ)
    1 A Chowri or long brush, most usually made of the tail-hairs of the Yak or Bos Grunniens, and employed to whisk off insects, flies, &c.
    2 The name of a demon. f. (-rī) A compound pedicle. mf. (-raḥ-rī) A kind of deer, or rather the Bos Grunniens, erroneously classed by the Hindu writers amongst the deer.
    E. cama to eat or be eaten, ara aff.
