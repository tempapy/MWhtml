• kandala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 The cheek, or the cheek and temple.
    2 A portent, a natural phoenomenon supposed to announce evil.
    3 A new shoot or spring.
    4 A low minute tone.
    5 Reproach, censure. m. (-laḥ)
    1 Gold.
    2 War, battle. f. (-lī)
    1 A species of deer of which the hide is used.
    2 A sort of tree, the banana: see kadalī.
    3 A flag.
    E. kanda a root, &c. and la from lā to take, fem. affix ṅīp.
