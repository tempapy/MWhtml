• cātura [322] mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) 1 Perceptible, visible. 2 Speaking well or amiably. 3 Driving, one who drives, a driver, a charioteer, &c. 4 Clever, able, ingenious. 5 Relating to four. f. (-rī) Dexterity, ability, cleverness. m. (-raḥ) A small round pillow for resting the cheek upon. n. (-raṃ) A carriage, a cart holding four people. E. catura dexterous, or catur four, aṇ affix; also with kan added cāturaka.
