• tarka r. 10th cl. (tarkayati)
    1 To shine.
    2 To speak.
    3 To reason, to discuss.
    4 To doubt or apprehend. [369]
  • tarka m. (-rkaḥ)
    1 Doubt or disputation, discussion, reasoning.
    2 Wish, desire.
    3 Supplying an ellipsis.
    4 Cause, motive.
    5 The science of reasoning, logic.
    6 (In Logic,) Reduction to absurdity, a conclusion opposed to or disproving the premises.
    E. tarka to infer, to reason, &c. affix ghañ. [369]
