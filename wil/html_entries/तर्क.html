• tarka [369] r. 10th cl. (tarkayati) 1 To shine. 2 To speak. 3 To reason, to discuss. 4 To doubt or apprehend.
  • tarka [369] m. (-rkaḥ) 1 Doubt or disputation, discussion, reasoning. 2 Wish, desire. 3 Supplying an ellipsis. 4 Cause, motive. 5 The science of reasoning, logic. 6 (In Logic,) Reduction to absurdity, a conclusion opposed to or disproving the premises. E. tarka to infer, to reason, &c. affix ghañ.
