• ṣaḍguṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) Six-fold, six times. n. (-ṇaṃ) An assemblage of six qualities or properties. mf. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā) The six acts of a king, in his military character, or peace, war, marching, halting, sowing dissension, seeking protection.
    E. ṣaṣ, and guṇa a time. [870]
