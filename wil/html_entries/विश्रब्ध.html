• viśrabdha [787] mfn. (-bdhaḥ-bdhā-bdhaṃ) 1 Trusted, confided in, confided to. 2 Patient, tranquil. 3 Lowly-minded, not lofty, not magnanimous. 4 Excessive, exceeding, much. 5 Firm, steady. 6 Rested, reposed. E. vi before śrama to be weary or srambha to trust, &c. aff. ktaḥ see visrabdha.
