• suṣama [938] mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) 1 Beautiful, handsome, pleasing. 2 Same, even, all. f. (-mā) 1 Exquisite beauty. 3 A period of time peculiar to the Jainas, the second division or Ara of a large period, containing six such portions, and that in which steady happiness is enjoyed by mankind: (the word however more usually occurs written sukhamā, as derived from sukha pleasure.) E. mu handsome, sama even, all, and the sa changed after this particle.
