• aṅgulitoraṇa n. (-ṇaṃ) Three semicircular lines drawn across the forehead, with sandal, &c. a sectarial mark, especially of the followers of Śakti.
    E. aṅguli a finger, and toraṇa the ornamented arch of a gateway, to which the mark made by the fingers upon the forehead may be compared. [011]
