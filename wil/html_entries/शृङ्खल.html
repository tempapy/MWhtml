• śṛṅkhala subst. mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) A belt or chain worn round a man's body.
    2 A chain for confining an elephant.
    3 A chain, a fetter in general.
    E. śṛṅga a horn, here said to mean a link, and khala to collect, aff. ac; or śiñjā the tinkling of a chain, and skhala to go, aff. ac, deriv. irr. [855]
