• kulika mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Of a good family, well-born. m. (-kaḥ) One of the eight chiefs of the Nāgas or serpent race, described as having a half moon on the top of his head, and being of a monkey hue.
    2 An artist or artificer of eminent birth.
    3 A thorny plant, (Ruellia longifolia.)
    4 The chief or headman of a tribe or caste.
    E. kula family, &c. kan affix, and i substituted for a. [234]
