• tulā [381] f. (-lā) 1 Measure by weight. 2 A measure or weight of gold and silver, 100 Palas, or about 145 ounces troy. 3 A balance, especially a fine balance, goldsmith's or assay scales. 4 The sign of the zodiac, Libra. 5 Resemblance, likeness, equality, similarity. 6 A vessel. 7 Sloping beams or timbers in the roof of a house. E. tula to weigh or to resemble, affixes ka and ṭāp.
