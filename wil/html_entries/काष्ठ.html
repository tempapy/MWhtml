• kāṣṭha [220] n. (-ṣṭhaṃ) Wood. f. (-ṣṭhā) 1 A quarter or region of the world, space, tract. 2 Place, site. 3 Limit, boundary. 4 A measure of time, the thirtieth part of a Kalā, or eighteen twinklings of the eye. 5 Excellence, superiority. 6 A plant, (Curcuma zanthorhiza, Rox.) E. kāśa to shine, Uṇādi affix kyan, śa changed to ṣa, and tha after ṣa becomes ṭha.
