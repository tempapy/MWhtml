• somasiddhānta m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 One of the Buddhas.
    2 A rationalist of a sect of Śaivas.
    3 A particular doctrine, a system of theological philosophy, followed by a branch of the worshippers of ŚIVA.
    E. soma the moon, (as the teacher of the doctrine,) and siddhānta truth, (whose.) [945]
