• mahāghoṣa [649] m. (-ṣaḥ) A great noise, a loud sound. n. (-ṣaṃ) A market, a fair. f. (-ṣā) 1 A plant, commonly called Karkaṭaśṛṅgī or Crab's horn, described as a climbing and milky plant, and supposed to be the Asclepias geminata. 2 Gum olibanum tree. E. mahā great, ghoṣa sound, &c.
