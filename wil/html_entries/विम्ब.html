• vimba mn. (-mbaḥ-mbaṃ)
    1 The disk of the sun or moon.
    2 An image, a picture, a shadow, a reflected or represented form.
    3 The gourd of the Momordica monadelpha. nf. (-mbaṃ-mbā or -mbī) A cucurbitaceous plant with red fruit, (Momordica monadelpha.)
    E. vi to go, to shine, vana Uṇādi aff., and mus augment, with the radical vowel made short. [779]
