• vivarttana n. (-naṃ)
    1 Going round, circumambulating an altar, &c.
    2 Revolving, turning round.
    3 Whirling, causing to go round.
    4 Returning.
    5 Passing through in succession, as various lives, various hells, &c.
    5 Being, abiding.
    E. vi before vṛta to be, aff. lyuṭ.
