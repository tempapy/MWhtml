• nalina n. (-naṃ)
    1 A lotus or water lily, (Nelumbium speciosum, or Nymphaea nelumbo.)
    2 Water.
    3 Indigo. m. (-naḥ)
    1 The Indian crane.
    2 A small fruit, Karonda. f. (-nī)
    1 An assemblage of lotus flowers.
    2 A place abounding in lotuses.
    3 The flexible stalk of a water lily.
    4 A pond in which the lotus grows or may grow.
    5 The Ganges of heaven.
    6 The fermented and intoxicating juice of the cocoanut.
    E. nala to be bright, Uṇādi affix inac. [456]
