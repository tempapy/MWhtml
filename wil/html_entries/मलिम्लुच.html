• malimluca m. (-caḥ)
    1 A thief.
    2 Burning, blazing.
    3 Fire, air, wind,
    4 The intercalary lunar month, introduced every third year, in order to approximate the lunar, and solar modes of computation.
    5 A gnat, a musquito.
    6 Frost.
    E. mali possession, and mluca to steal, aff. ka.
