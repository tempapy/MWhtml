• nikāya m. (-yaḥ)
    1 An assemblage of persons performing like duties, a congregation, an audience.
    2 A flock, a multitude.
    3 A butt, a mark.
    4 A house, a habitation.
    5 The Supreme Being.
    E. ni in or within, ci to collect, affix ghañ, and ka substituted for the radical initial. [465]
