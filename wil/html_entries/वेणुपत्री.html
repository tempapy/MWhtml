• veṇupatrī f. (-trī) A plant, one synonym of which, Hingupatri, appears to relate to the Asafœtida or some similar plant, whilst the corresponding compound Banspatra both in Hindi and Bengali, is applied to a species of Amaranthus, (A. lanceifolius, Rox., or A. atro-purpureus, Rox.)
    E. veṇu a bambu, and patra a leaf, fem. aff. ṅīṣ, having long slender leaves, like those of the bambu. [805]
