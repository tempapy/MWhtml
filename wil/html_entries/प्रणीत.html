• praṇīta [562] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Dressed (as food), cooked, &c. 2 Made, done, constructed. 3 Thrown, cast, sent. 4 Entered, approached. 5 Proper, fit. 6 Said, declared. 7 Delivered, given. 8 Decreed, sentenced, awarded. 9 Composed, written. m. (-taḥ) Fire, consecrated by prayers or mystical formulæ. f. (-tā) A vessel used at sacrifices, a sort of cup. E. pra before ṇī to get, aff. kta.
