• jha The ninth consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet; the aspirate of the preceding letter, and corresponding in power to Jh. [357]
  • jha mfn. (jhaḥ jhā jhaṃ)
    1 Asleep, sleeping.
    2 Lost, destroyed. m. (jhaḥ)
    1 A name of VṚHASPATI, preceptor of the gods.
    2 A name of the chief of the Daityas or demons.
    3 Sound, or a sort of sound like the splashing of water, or the clashing of cymbals, jangling, clanging, &c.
    4 High wind.
    5 A stray, a thing lost or mislaid.
    6 Playing a tune, beating time. f. (jhā) The descent of a cascade, a water fall.
    E. jhaṭa to collect, affix ḍa. [357]
