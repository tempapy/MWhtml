• ghṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Sprinkled.
    2 Illumined. n. (-taṃ)
    1 Ghee, clarified butter, or butter which has been boiled gently, and allowed to cool: it is then used for culinary and religious purposes, and is highly esteemed by the Hindus.
    2 Water. f. (-tā) The name of a tree: see ghṛtamaṇḍā.
    E. ghṛ to sprinkle, Uṇādi affix kta. [310]
