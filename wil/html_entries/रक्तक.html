• raktaka [690] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A plant bearing a red blossom, (Pentapetes phoenicea; it is also applied to the Ixora bandhuca.) 2 Globe amaranth, (Gomphræna globosa, the red variety.) 3 Red garments or cloth. 4 A man affected with passion or fondness. 5 A gamboller, a player, one who plays or sports. 6 Safflower. E. kan added to the preceding.
