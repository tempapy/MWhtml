• puṇḍarīka [540] m. (-kaḥ) 1 The elephant of the south-east quarter. 2 A leopard. 3 A sort of snake, (Amphisbæna.) 4 A fragrant kind of mango. 5 Fever affecting an elephant. 6 A variety of the sugarcane. 7 A silk worm (koṣakāra?) 8 A student's waterpot. 9 White, the colour. 10 A sort of leprosy. n. (-kaṃ) 1 A white lotus. 2 A lotus in general. 3 A white umbrella. 4 A kind of drug. E. puḍi to rub or grind, or according to some, to adorn, Uṇādi aff. īka, and the form irr.
