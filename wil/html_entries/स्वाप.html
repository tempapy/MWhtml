• svāpa m. (-paḥ)
    1 Sleep, sleeping.
    2 Sleepiness, sloth.
    3 Paralysis, palsy, loss of sensation.
    4 Ignorance.
    5 Dreaming, a dream.
    6 Temporary and partial palsy from pressure on a nerve, numbness, the sleep of a limb, &c.
    E. ṣvapa to sleep, aff. ghañ. [965]
