• dharmmasaṃhitā f. (-tā) A code of laws, especially the work of some saint or divine person, as MANU, YĀJÑAVALKYA, &c.
    E. dharmma virtue, and sam prefixed to dhā, to have or possess, affix kta, fem. affix ṭāp; a compilation made for the purpose of teaching and maintaining virtue. [440]
