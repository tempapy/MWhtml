• candana mn. (-naḥ-naṃ) Sandal, (Sirium myrtifolium;) it implies either the tree, the wood, or the unctuous preparations of the wood, held in high estimation as perfumes. n. (-naṃ) Red Sandal wood: see rakracandana. m. (-naḥ) A kind of ape. f. (-nī) The name of a river.
    E. cadi to gladden, to delight, affix lyuṭ.
