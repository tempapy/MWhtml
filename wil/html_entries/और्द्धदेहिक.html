• aurddhadehika [176] mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ) Obsequial, funeral. n. (-kaṃ) Obsequies of a deceased person; whatever is given or performed on a person's decease, as burning, offering cakes, giving alms, &c. E. ūrddha or ūrddhva above, deha the body, and ṭhak affix; hence also aurddhvadehika.
