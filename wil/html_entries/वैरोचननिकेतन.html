• vairocananiketana n. (-naṃ) Pātāla the intermediate region between the earth and Tartarus or Naraka, over part of which BALI presides.
    E. vairocana the prince BALI, and niketana abode; the sovereign of this name being an incarnate Daitya or hereditary foe of the gods, and being invested with a sovereignty in the lower regions after his humiliation by VIṢṆU. [812]
