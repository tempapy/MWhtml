• ālu f. (-luḥ) A pitcher, a small water jar. mn. (-luḥ-lu)
    1 An esculent root, (Arum campanulatum:) in the modern dialects this name is applied to the yam, potatoe, &c.
    2 A raft, a float. m. (-luḥ) An owl.
    E. ṛ to go, and uṇ affix, āru what goes, (in the earth, water, &c.) the initial is lengthened, and ra is changed to la.
