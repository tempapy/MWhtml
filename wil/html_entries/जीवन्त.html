• jīvanta mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntī-ntaṃ) Living, existent. m. (-ntaḥ)
    1 Life, existence.
    2 A medicament, a drug. f. (-ntī)
    1 The name of a tree, commonly Jiyati, (Celtis orientalis.)
    2 A plant, (Menispermum glabrum:) see guḍūcī.
    3 The Śami or Sāen tree, (Mimosa albida.)
    4 A parasite plant.
    5 Yellow myrobalan, (Terminalia chebula, Rox.)
    E. jīva to live, affix jhac; fem. affix ṅīp; also with kan added jīvantikā, &c.
