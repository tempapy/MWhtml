• tripada mfn. (-daḥ-dā-dī-daṃ)
    1 Three-footed.
    2 Having three lines or divisions, (a stanza.)
    3 (In Arithmetic,) Trinomial. n. (-daṃ)
    1 A tripod. f. (-dā) A verse of three lines, as the Gāyatrī. f. (-dī)
    1 A creeper, (Cissus pedata.)
    2 The girth of an elephant.
    E. tri three, and pada a foot.
