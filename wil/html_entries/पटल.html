• paṭala [495] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) A heap, a number, a quantity or multitude. mf. (-laṃ-lī) 1 A book. 2 A tree. 3 A stalk. nf. (-laḥ-lā) A roof, a thatch. n. (-laṃ) 1 A cover, a coat, an enclosing or surrounding skin or membrane. 2 A place, a spot, a circle or district. 3 A mark on the forehead with Sandal wood 4 A basket. 5 Train, retinue. 6 A coat of the eye. E. paṭa to go, kalac aff.
