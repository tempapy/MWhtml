• vyañjana n. (-naṃ)
    1 A mark, a spot, a sign, a token.
    2 Paraphernalia, insignia.
    3 The beard.
    4 A privy part, either male or female.
    5 Sauce, condiment.
    6 A consonant. nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Figurative expression.
    2 Irony, sarcasm.
    E. vi before añja to make clear or evident, lyuṭ aff. [813]
