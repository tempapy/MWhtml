• śilpin mfn. (-lpī-lpinī-lpi)
    1 An artist, an artificer or artisan.
    2 Belonging or relating to a mechanical profession or art. f. (-lpinī) A drug or herb, a kind of grass said to be known by the name of Lahānasipi, and described as sweet and cooling, and bearing seeds of tonic and restorative properties; also śilpikā, &c.
    E. śilpa art, and ini aff.
