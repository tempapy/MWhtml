• balātkāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 Violence, oppression, exaction.
    2 (In law) The detention of the person of a debtor by his creditor, and the violent measures taken by the latter, (flogging, &c.) to recover his debt.
    E. bala strength, ata to go, aff. kvip, balāt, and kāra making, doing. [600]
