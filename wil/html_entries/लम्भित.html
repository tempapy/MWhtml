• lambhita mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)

    2 Applied to, employed, adapted.
    3 Spoken to, addressed.
    4 Heightened, improved, rendered more perfect or intense.
    5 Cherished.
    6 Gained.
    7 Abused.
    E. labha to gain, causal form, or labhi to revile, kta aff.
