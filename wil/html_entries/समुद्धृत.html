• samuddhṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Ill-behaved.
    2 Thrown up (as food, &c.)
    3 Raised or drawn up.
    4 Extricated, lifted out or from.
    5 Taken as a share, set apart, divided.
    6 Seized, possessed of.
    E. sam and uṭ before dhṛ to have, or hṛ to take, aff. kta. [901]
