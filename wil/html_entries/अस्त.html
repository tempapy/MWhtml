• asta Sautra root. 10th cl. (astayati) To obscure or eclipse.
  • asta mfn. (-staḥ-stā-staṃ)
    1 Thrown, cast.
    2 Sent, dispatched.
    3 Set, obscured.
    4 Removed, set aside. m. (-staḥ)
    1 The western mountain, behind which the sun is supposed to set.
    2 Sun-set. n. (-staṃ) Death, end.
    E. asta to obscure or eclipse, ac affix, or asa to throw, &c. and kta aff.
