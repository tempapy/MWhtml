• asta [097] Sautra root. 10th cl. (astayati) To obscure or eclipse.
  • asta [097] mfn. (-staḥ-stā-staṃ) 1 Thrown, cast. 2 Sent, dispatched. 3 Set, obscured. 4 Removed, set aside. m. (-staḥ) 1 The western mountain, behind which the sun is supposed to set. 2 Sun-set. n. (-staṃ) Death, end. E. asta to obscure or eclipse, ac affix, or asa to throw, &c. and kta aff.
