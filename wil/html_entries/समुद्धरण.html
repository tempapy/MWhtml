• samuddharaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Food vomited or thrown up.
    2 Drawing up, raising, lifting (as water from a well, &c.)
    3 Eradicating a tree, &c. pulling up by the roots.
    4 Extricating, lifting out.
    5 Taking out from, as a part or share.
    E. sam and uṭ before hṛ to take, aff. lyuṭ. [900]
