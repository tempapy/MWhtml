• kakṣāvekṣaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A doorkeeper.
    2 A guard of the queen's apartments.
    3 Keeper of a royal garden.
    4 A painter.
    5 A poet.
    6 A debauchee.
    7 Eagerness of feeling, strength of sentiment.
    E. kakṣā an enclosure, and abekṣa to see, vun aff. [178]
