• nitamba [466] m. (-mbaḥ) 1 A woman's buttocks. 2 The buttocks or posteriors in general, or as it is sometimes applied, the circumference of the hip and loins. 3 The shoulder. 4 The side of a mountain. 5 A bank or shore. E. ni prefixed to tamba to go, affix ac or tama to go, with ba aff.
