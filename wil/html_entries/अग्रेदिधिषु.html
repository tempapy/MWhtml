• agredidhiṣu m. (-ṣuḥ) A man of either of the three first classes, whose wife has been before married, and has borne him children.
    E. agre principal, and dighiṣu the husband of a twice married woman. f. (-ṣuḥ) A younger sister married before her elder; also agredighiṣū. [008]
