• ga [275] The third consonant of the Nāgarī alphabet, the letter. G.
  • ga [275] mfn. (-gaḥ-gā-gaṃ) Who or what goes, used chiefly in composition: as adhagaḥ a traveller, who goes a road; apagā what goes down, (a river, &c.) m. (-gaḥ) 1 A name of the deity GAṆEŚA. 2 A Gandharba or celestial musician. f. (gā) A song. n. (-gaṃ) Song, singing. E. gama to go or gai to sing, affix ḍa, fem. affix ṭāp.
