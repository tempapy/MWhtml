• dharaṇa mn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇaṃ)
    1 Holding, possessing, having.
    2 A sort of measure, a weight of ten Palas.
    3 A weight of silver, sixteen Māṣas. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 The Himāla mountain considered as king of mountains.
    2 The sun.
    3 A world.
    4 A breast, a female breast.
    5 Rice.
    6 A ridge of land answering the purpose of a bridge, the edge of a river, the skirt of a mountain, a bridge. f. (-ṇī)
    1 The earth.
    2 The silk cotton tree.
    3 A vessel of the body.
    4 An esculent root.
    E. dhṛ to hold, to uphold or support, affix lyuṭ, fem. affix ṅīp. [438]
