• arjuna m. (-naḥ)
    1 The third of the Pāṇḍavas, the son of INDRA and friend of KṚṢṆA.
    2 The name of a king with a thousand arms. See kārttavīryya.
    3 A tree, (Pentaptera arjuna.)
    4 A peacock.
    5 The only son of his mother.
    6 White, (the colour.) nf. (-naṃ-nī)
    1 Grass.
    2 Slight inflammation of the conjunctiva or white of the eye. f. (-nī)
    1 A cow.
    2 UṢĀ, the wife of ANIRUDDHA.
    3 The Karatoya river in the north of Bengal.
    4 A procuress, a bawd. mfn. (-naḥ-nī-naṃ) White.
    E. arja to gain, unan Uṇādi affix, fem. ṅīp, also read arjjuna.
