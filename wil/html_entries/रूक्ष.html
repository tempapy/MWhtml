• rūkṣa [708] r. 10th cl. (rūkṣayati) To be harsh.
  • rūkṣa [708] mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā-kṣaṃ) 1 Harsh, rough, not smooth or soft. 2 Rough to the taste, astringent, austere, not bland or sapid. 3 Rugged, uneven. 4 Harsh, unkind, unfriendly. m. (-kṣaḥ) A tree in general. E. rūkṣa to be harsh or rough, aff. ka.
