• rūkṣa r. 10th cl. (rūkṣayati) To be harsh. [708]
  • rūkṣa mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā-kṣaṃ)
    1 Harsh, rough, not smooth or soft.
    2 Rough to the taste, astringent, austere, not bland or sapid.
    3 Rugged, uneven.
    4 Harsh, unkind, unfriendly. m. (-kṣaḥ) A tree in general.
    E. rūkṣa to be harsh or rough, aff. ka. [708]
