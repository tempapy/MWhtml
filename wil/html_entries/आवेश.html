• āveśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 Entrance, entering.
    2 Pride, arrogance.
    3 Indistinctness of idea, apoplectic or epileptic giddiness.
    4 Absorption of the faculties in one wish or idea, devotedness to an object.
    5 Demoniac frenzy, possession, &c.
    E. āṅ before viśa to enter, ghañ aff.
