• kṛpa (ū, ḷ) ūkṛpḷ r. 1st cl. (kalpate) To be able. r. 1st and 10th cls. (kalpati, kalpayati)
    1 To think or imagine.
    2 To mix.
    3 To paint or colour. r. 1st cl. (kṛpati) To have pity or mercy. r. 10th cl. (kṛpayati) To be weak. In the first roots la ḹ is substituted for ṛ. [244]
  • kṛpa m. (-paḥ)
    1 The name of a sage, the brother-in-law of DROṆA.
    2 A name of VYĀSA, the compiler of the Vedas and Purāṇas. f. (-pā) Tenderness, compassionateness. f. (-pī) The wife of the sage DROṆA.
    E. kṛpa to be able, &c. aṅ aff. [244]
