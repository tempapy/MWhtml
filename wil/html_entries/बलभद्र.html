• balabhadra m. (-draḥ)
    1 BALADEVA, as above.
    2 ANANTA, the great serpent, considered as identified with the preceding, BALADEVA being regarded by some authorities as an incarnation of ANANTA.
    3 A strong or robust man.
    4 A tree, commonly Lodh. f. (-drā)
    1 A young girl, a maiden.
    2 A drug, commonly Trāyamāṇa.
    E. bala strength, and bhadra auspicious, excellent.
