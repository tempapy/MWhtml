• khaṇḍa [270] mn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍaṃ) 1 A piece, a part, a fragment, a portion. 2 A chapter, a section. 3 A term in an equation. m. (-ṇḍaḥ) A flaw in a jewel. 2 Treacle or molasses partially dried or candied. n. (-ṇḍaṃ) 1 A sort of sugar cane. 2 Black salt. E. khaḍi to break, and ghañ affix, or khana to tear, affix ḍa, and na changed to ṇa.
