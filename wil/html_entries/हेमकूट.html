• hemakūṭa m. (-ṭaḥ) One of the ranges of mountains dividing the known continent into nine Varṣas; this range is the second south of Ilāvṛta, or the central division, and is immediately to the north of the Himālaya, forming with it the boundaries of the Kinnara Varṣam.
    E. hema gold or golden, and kūṭa a peak. [979]
