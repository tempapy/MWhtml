• karmmakara [197] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ) 1 A hired laborer. 2 A servant of any kind, (as a pupil, &c.) not a slave. 3 An agent, any one who does work or business. m. (-raḥ) A name of YAMA, regent of the dead. f. (-rī) 1 A plant, (Aletris hyacinthoides:) see mūrvvā. 2 Bryonia grandis: see nimbikā. 3 A female servant or slave. E. karmma work, act, and kara who does or performs; also karmmakāra.
