• pañcalakṣaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ) A Purāṇa or mythological poem.
    E. pañca five, and lakṣaṇa a mark; a Purāṇa should comprehend five topics; the creation of worlds, their destruction and renovation, the genealogy of gods and heroes, the reigns of the MANUS, and the actions of their descendants.
