• kṝ r. 6th cl. (kirati) To throw, to scatter. r. 9th cl. (kṛṇati) or (gi, ñ) gikṝñ (kṛṇāti-kṛṇīte) To hurt, to injure. r. 10th cl. (kārayate) To know.
    1 With apa prefixed, ^a To draw furrows, to make lines. ^b To disperse.
    2 With ava, To throw, to cast.
    3 With āṅ, To fill up, to cover over.
    4 With prati, To hurt, to injure.
    5 With vi, To throw, to caste, to send, to scatter widely.
    6 With sama, To gather, to collect.
    7 With abhi, in the passive voice, to be upset, overcome or overwhelmed. [247]
