• granthi [303] m. (-nthiḥ) 1 The joint or knot of a reed or cane, &c. 2 A tie, the knot of a cord, &c. 3 A joint or articulation of the body. 4 A plant, commonly Ganṭhiāla: see granthiparṇa. 5 Crookedness, distortion. 6 A complaint, knotting of the vessels as in varicocele, &c. E. grantha to connect, &c. Uṇādi affix in.
