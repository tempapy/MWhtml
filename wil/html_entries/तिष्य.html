• tiṣya mfn. (-ṣyaḥ-ṣyā-ṣyaṃ) Auspicious, fortunate, lucky. m. (-ṣyaḥ)
    1 The eighth Nakṣatra or lunar mansion, an asterism figured by an arrow, and containing three stars, of which one is δ Cancri.
    2 The month Pauṣa, (December-January.)
    3 The Kali Yug. the fourth and present age. f. (-ṣyā) Emblic myrobalan, (Phyllanthus emblica.)
    E. tuṣa to please or delight, affix kyap, and it substituted for the penultimate.
