• kanda mn. (-ndaḥ-ndaṃ)
    1 A bulbous or tuberous root.
    2 One of an esculent sort, (Arum campanulatum.)
    3 Garlic. m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 A cloud.
    2 An affection of the feminine organ, considered as a fleshy excrescence, but apparently prolapsus uteri.
    E. kadi to wet, &c. ac affix, or kaṃ water, and dā to give. [186]
