• vimāna [779] mn. (-naḥ-naṃ) 1 A car or chariot of the gods, sometimes serving as a seat or throne, and at others carrying them through the skies self-directed and self-moving. 2 Any car or vehicle. 3 A horse. 4 A palace, the palace of an emperor or supreme monarch. 5 Disrespect, dishonour. 6 Measure. E. vi before mana to understand, with ghañ aff., or mā to measure, aff. lyuṭ, or vi priv., māna honour.
