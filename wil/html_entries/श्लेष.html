• śleṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 Union, junction, the proximity of contact.
    2 Association, society, presence.
    3 Embracing, an embrace.
    4 Adhering or clinging to, &c.
    5 A figure of rhetoric, choice or connexion of words so as to admit of a double interpretation, a species of paronomasia or pun.
    E. śliṣa to embrace, aff. ṇa. [866]
