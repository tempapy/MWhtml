• niṣkṛti f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Pardon, absolution.
    2 Expiration.
    3 Escaping from, avoiding the consequence of any thing.
    4 Undoing, doing away.
    5 Disregarding, neglecting.
    6 Acquittance, compensation, discharge of a debt or obligation.
    E. nir neg. kṛti doing.
