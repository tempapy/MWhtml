• dṛḍha mfn. (-ḍhaḥ-ḍhā-ḍhaṃ)
    1 Much, exceeding; (in this sense it is also an adverb declinable in the neuter gender.)
    2 Hard, firm.
    3 Able, powerful.
    4 Bulky, massive, solid.
    5 Strong.
    6 Confirmed. n. (-ḍhaṃ) Iron.
    E. dṛha to increase, affix kta. [420]
