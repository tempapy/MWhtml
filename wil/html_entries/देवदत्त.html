• devadatta mfn. (-ttaḥ-ttā-ttaṃ) Given by the gods, god-given. m. (-ttaḥ)
    1 The younger brother of the legislator Buddha.
    2 The conch of ARJUNA.
    3 One of the vital airs, that which is exhaled in yawning.
    E. deva a deity, and datta given; the name is often used in grammatical and other examples, in the place of some one, a certain person, &c.
