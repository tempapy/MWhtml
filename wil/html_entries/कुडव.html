• kuḍava m. (-vaḥ) A measure of grain, wood, iron, &c. the fourth part of a Prastha, described by some as a vessel four fingers wide, and as many deep, and containing twelve Prakṛtis or handfuls: it is also said to contain thirteen and a half cubic Aṅgulas; or to be a finger and a half deep, and three fingers each length and breadth. In medicine it is equal to two Prakṛtis or thirty-two Tolakas.
    E. kuḍa to heap, kavan affix; also kuḍapa and kuṭapa. [227]
