• ba The twenty-third consonant of the Nāgarī Alphabet, corresponding to the letter B, and often confounded with the analogous semivowel ba or V, with which some grammarians consider it to be at all times optionally interchangeable.
  • ba m. (-baḥ)
    1 A name of VARUṆA.
    2 A water jar.
    E. baṇa to sound, &c. aff. ḍa.
