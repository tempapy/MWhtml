• pragalbha mfn. (-lbhaḥ-lbhā-lbhaṃ)
    1 Bold, confident.
    2 Prompt, ready.
    3 Resolute, energetic.
    4 Illustrious, eminent.
    5 Strong, able.
    6 Shameless, impudent, audacious. m. (-lbhaḥ) The fire used for oblations at the birth of a child. f. (-lbhā) A woman who courts her husband's caresses, a bold or confident woman.
    E. pra much, galbha to be proud, aff. ac. [558]
