• stambaghana m. (-naḥ)
    1 A small hoe for weeding or eradicating clusters of gramineous plants.
    2 A sickle or instrument for cutting corn.
    3 A basket for holding the heads of wild rice, &c. when cut.
    E. stamba a cluster, (of stalks,) a sheaf, hana to destroy, and ap aff.; also with kac, aff. stambaghna, in each case gha being substituted for ha. [949]
