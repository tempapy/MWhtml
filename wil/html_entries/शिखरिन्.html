• śikharin [842] mfn. (-rī-riṇī-ri) Crested, peaked, pointed. m. (-rī) 1 A mountain. 2 A tree. 3 A plant, (Achyranthes aspera.) 4 A strong-hold, a hill-fort. 5 The lapwing. f. (-riṇī) 1 A form of metre, a species of the class Atyaṣṭi or verse of four lines of 17 syllables each. 2 A dish of curds and sugar with spices. 3 An excellent woman. 4 Arabian jasmine. 5 A line of hair extending across the navel. E. śisvara a point or peak, and ini aff.
