• muñja m. (-ñjaḥ)
    1 A sort of grass, from the fibres of which a string is prepared, of which the triple thread worn by the Brahman as a girdle should be formed, (Saccharum munja, Rox.)
    2 The Brahmanical girdle, or in common use, the sacred string or cord.
    3 An arrow.
    E. muji to sound, aff. ac.
