• dā (ṇa) dāṇa r. 1st cl. (yacchati, or
    with sam prefixed saṃyacchate;) also (ḍu ñ) ḍudāñ r. 3rd cl. (dadāti) To give, to present.
    With āṅ prefixed (ādatte), To take, to accept or receive.
    With pra, To give, to give to.
    With vi and āṅ, To open.
    With sama and āṅ, To select. (pa) dāp r. 2nd cl. (dāti) To cut. [404]
