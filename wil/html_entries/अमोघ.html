• amogha mfn. (-ghaḥ-ghā-ghaṃ) Productive, fruitful, not vain or barren. m. (-ghaḥ) The name of a river. f. (-ghā)
    1 Trumpet flower, (Bignonia suave-olens.)
    2 A plant of which the seed is used as a vermifuge, (Erycibe paniculata, Rox.) See biḍaṅga.
    3 Yellow myrobalan. See harītakī.
    E. a not, mogha vain, barren. [062]
