• avakṛṣṭa mfn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ)
    1 Expelled, turned out.
    2 Removed, sent away, drawn aside.
    3 Extracted.
    4 Pulled.
    5 Inferior, low.
    6 Degraded, outcaste. m. (-ṣṭaḥ) A servant who performs the lowest office, a sweeper, a waterman, &c.
    E. ava out of, and kṛṣṭa drawn.
