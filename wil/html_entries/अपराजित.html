• aparājita [044] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) Unconquered, unsurpassed. m. (-taḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 Also of VIṢṆU. 3 A sage. f. (-tā) 1 A name applied to several plants. 2 (Clitoria ternatea,) see girikarṇī. 3 Aeschynomene sesban, see jayā. and, 4 Another plant, see aśanaparṇī. 5 A name of the goddess DURGĀ. 6 A species of the śarkarī metre of four lines, with fourteen syllables in each line. 7 The north-east quarter. E. apara another, a neg. and jita conquered, excelled, &c.
