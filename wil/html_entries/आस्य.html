• āsya n. (-syaṃ)
    1 The face.
    2 The mouth. mfn. (-syaḥ-syā-syaṃ) Belonging or relating to the mouth or face.
    E. asa to throw or direct, and ṇyat affix, or āṅ before syanda to go, and ḍa affix; to which food goes or is directed. f. (-syā) Stay, abiding, resting.
    E. āsa to sit, and ṇyat aff.
