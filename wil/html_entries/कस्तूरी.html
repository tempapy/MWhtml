• kastūrī [205] f. (-rī) 1 Musk, the animal perfume so called, as brought from Kashmir, Napal, and western Asam or Butan, the latter is said to be the best. 2 A plant, (Hibiscus abelmoschus.) 3 Another plant, (Amaryllis zeylanica.) E. kasa to go, and tūrac affix; its scent going or spreading; the deriv. is irr.; also kan being added, kastūrikā and kasturikā as above.
