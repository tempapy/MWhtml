• cikka r. 10th cl. (cikkayati) To give pain. [324]
  • cikka mfn. (-kkaḥ-kkī-kkaṃ) Flat-nosed. m. (-kkaḥ) A musk rat. f. (-kkā) A mouse.
    E. cik imitative sound, and ka who utters, from kai with ḍa affix; or cikka to give pain, and ka affix; or in the last sense, ni preposition changed to cik, and kac added. [324]
