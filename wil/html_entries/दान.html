• dāna (u) dāna r. 1st cl. irr. (dīdāṃsati-te)
    1 To cut.
    2 To straighten or make straight.
    3 To be straight.
  • dāna n. (-naṃ)
    1 Gift, giving, donation.
    2 The fluid that flows from the temples of an elephant in rut.
    3 Nourishing, cherishing.
    4 Purification.
    5 Cutting, dividing.
    6 A present, a special gift.
    7 Beating, striking.
    8 Wild honey.
    E. dā to give, or do to cut, affix lyuṭ.
