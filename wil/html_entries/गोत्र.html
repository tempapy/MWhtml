• gotra [298] n. (-traṃ) 1 Family, race, lineage, kin. 2 A name, an appellation. 3 A forest. 4 A field. 5 A Catta, an umbrella or parasol. 6 A road. 7 Knowledge of futurity, inspiration. 8 A genus, a class or species. 9 A caste, a tribe or subdivision of it into families, as in that of the Brahman; twenty-four Gotras are reckoned, supposed to be sprung from and named after celebrated teachers, as ŚĀṆḌILYA, KAŚYAPA, GAUTAMA, BHĀRADVĀJA, &c. 10 Increase. 11 Wealth. f. (-trā) 1 The earth. 2 A herd of kine. m. (-traḥ) 1 A mountain. E. gu to sound, and tra Uṇādi affix, or go the earth, &c. and tra what preserves, from trai with ka aff.
