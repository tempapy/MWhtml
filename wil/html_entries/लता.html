• latā [716] f. (-tā) 1 A creeper, a creeping or winding plant in general. 2 A branch. 3 A plant, commonly Priyangu. 4 A gramineous plant, (Trigonella corniculata.) 5 Heart-pea, (Cardiospermum haliacacabum.) 6 The large Bengal creeper, (Gærtnera racemosa.) 7 A sort of grass, (Panicum dactylon.) 8 A vegetable and medicinal substance, commonly Latākastūrī or the musk-creeper, said to grow in the Dekshin. 9 Thread. E. lata a Sautra root, to hurt, to enfold, &c., aff. ac and ṭāp.
