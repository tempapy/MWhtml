• vikṛti f. (-tiḥ-tī)
    1 Change of any kind, as of purpose, mind. form, nature, &c. either permanent or temporary.
    2 Sickness, disease, change from the natural or healthy state.
    3 Fear, apprehension.
    4 Spirituous liquor.
    5 A species of metre: the stanza consists of four lines of two syllables each, variously arranged.
    E. vi implying alteration or reverse, kṛ to make, aff. ktin. [759]
