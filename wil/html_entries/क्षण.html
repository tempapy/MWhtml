• kṣaṇa [261] (u) kṣaṇu r. 8th cl. (kṣaṇati kṣaṇute) To kill, to injure.
  • kṣaṇa [261] m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 A measure of time, equal to thirty Kalās or four minutes. 2 A moment. 3 A festival. 4 Vacation from work, state of being unemployed. 5 Leisure, opportunity. 6 A certain day of the fortnight, as the full, change, &c. see parvan. 7 Dependence, servitude. 8 The centre, the middle. n. adv. (-ṇaṃ) For a moment. E. kṣaṇa to injure, affix ac.
