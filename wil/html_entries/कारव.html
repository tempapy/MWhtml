• kārava [214] m. (-vaḥ) A crow. E. kvā an imitative sound, rava who sounds. f. (-vī) 1 A kind of Anise, (Anethum sowa.) 2 Another plant, (Celosia cristata.) 3 The asafoetida plant or its leaf, Hiṅguparṇī. 4 A kind of fennel, (Nigella Indica.) 5 A small gourd. E. ka water, āṅ before ru to go, aṇ and ṅīp affs.
