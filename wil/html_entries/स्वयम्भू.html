• svayambhū m. (-mbhūḥ)
    1 BRAHMĀ.
    2 A Jina or Jaina deified sage.
    3 An emperor of the world according to the Jainas.
    4 Time.
    5 Love.
    6 VIṢṆU.
    7 ŚIVA.
    E. svayam self, bhū to exist, kvip aff., the self-existent; also read with a short final, or svayambhu m. (-bhuḥ)
