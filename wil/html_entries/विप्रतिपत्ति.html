• vipratipatti f. (-ttiḥ)
    1 Perplexity, confusion.
    2 Various acquirement.
    3 Mutual connexion with or relation to.
    4 Conversancy.
    5 Contradiction, difference, discrepancy.
    6 Contest, dispute.
    7 Opposition of interests.
    E. vi privative, or implying variety, and pratipatti knowledge. [775]
