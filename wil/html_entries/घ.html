• gha The fourth consonant of the Sanskrit alphabet, being the aspirate of the preceding letter, and corresponding to Gh.
  • gha m. (-ghaḥ)
    1 A bell.
    2 A rattling or gurgling sound. f. (ghā)
    1 A tinkling ornament worn by women round the waist.
    2 Striking, killing.
    3 Wetting, sprinkling.
    E. hana to kill, affix ḍa, and gha substituted for the radical letter, or the same affix, with ghṛ to sprinkle, &c.
