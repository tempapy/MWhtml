• girija mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ) Mountain-born, mountaineer, &c. m. (-jaḥ) The Mahwa tree, (Bassia.) f. (-jā)
    1 A name of the goddess PĀRVATĪ, as the daughter of the personified HIMĀLA mountain.
    2 The Shaddock or pamplemouse, (Citrus decumana.)
    3 A plant considered as a white species of Rasnā: see rāsnā.
    4 The hill plantain.
    5 Jasmine.
    6 A pebble, a small stone. n. (-jaṃ)
    1 Talc.
    2 Benzoin or gum benjamin; it is also confounded with styrax, another gum resin.
    3 Bitumen.
    4 Iron.
    E. giri, and ja born.
