• śrama (ñi ira, u) śramu r. 4th cl. (śrāmyati)
    1 To perform acts of austere devotion, to undergo mortification or penance.
    2 To be wearied or fatigued.
    3 To be distressed.
  • śrama m. (-maḥ)
    1 Military exercise.
    2 Weariness, fatigue.
    3 Labour, exertion, toil.
    E. śrama to be wearied, aff. ac.
