• sammati f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Wish, desire.
    2 Order, command.
    3 Agreement, assent, similarity of opinion, or purpose.
    4 Approbation.
    5 Self or real knowledge.
    6 Regard, affection, love.
    7 Respect, homage.
    E. sam implying union or perfection, and mati mind, understanding. [905]
