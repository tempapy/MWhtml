• kumbhakāra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A potter.
    2 A kind of wild fowl. f. (-rī)
    1 A mineral substance, used as an application to strengthen the eyes and beautify the eye lashes.
    2 Red arsenic.
    3 The wife of a potter.
    E. kumbha a jar, &c. and kāra what makes, from kṛ to make, and aṇ aff. [231]
