• pusta r. 10th cl. (pustayati)
    1 To respect.
    2 To disregard, to treat with disrespect.
    3 To bind.
    4 To smear. [547]
  • pusta mfn. (-staḥ-stā-staṃ)
    1 Filled.
    2 Covered. n. (-staṃ)
    1 Smearing, anointing, painting, plastering, &c.
    2 Any thing made of metal, wood or clay, &c. nf. (-staṃ-stī) A book, a manuscript.
    E. pusta to bind, ac aff. [547]
