• api [047] ind. A preposition and particle corresponding to even, though, yet, assuredly, &c. and implying 1 Interrogation. 2 Earnest interrogation or enquiry. 3 Doubt. 4 Possibility. 5 Reproof; and, 6 Conjunction. 7 It is often an expletive. E. a neg. and pi to go, with kvip aff.
