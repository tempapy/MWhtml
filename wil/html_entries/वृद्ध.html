• vṛddha mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ)
    1 Old, aged, ancient.
    2 Full grown, large, expanded to the proper size.
    3 Wise, learned.
    4 Heaped, accumulated.
    5 Increased, augmented. m. (-ddhaḥ)
    1 An old man, or one past seventy.
    2 A sage, a saint. f. (-ddhā) An old woman, either one past child-bearing, or one with grey hair. n. (-ddhaṃ) Benzoin, (Styrax benzoin.)
    E. vṛdha to increase or grow, aff. kta.
