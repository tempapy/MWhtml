• govinda m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 One of the most usual appellations of KṚṢṆA or VIṢṆU in that form.
    2 A name of VṚHASPATI, regent of JUPITER.
    2 A cowkeeper, one who protects, cherishes or owns cattle.
    E. go language, here the language of the Vedas especially, and vinda who knows, from vida to know, with śa affix; also go heaven, a cow, vida to obtain, by whom heaven is obtained, or who obtains felicity by protecting kine. [301]
