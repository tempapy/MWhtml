• lapa(ṛ)lapṛ r. 1st cl. (lapati) To speak, to utter.
    With anu prefixed,
    1 To talk like, to mimic.
    2 To repeat.
    With apa, To deny.
    With āṅ, To address, to speak to,
    With pra, To talk incoherently.
    With prati, To gain, to get.
    With vi, To lament, to bewail.
    With vi and pra, To contradict, to dispute.
    With sama,
    1 To converse, to discourse.
    2 To be cunning or crafty. [716]
