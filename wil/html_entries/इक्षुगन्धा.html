• ikṣugandhā f. (-ndhā)
    1 A kind of reed, (Saccharum spontaneum.)
    2 Barleria longifolia, (this and its synonyms are also applied to Capparis spinosa and Tribulus lanuginosus: see kokilākṣa and gokṣuraka.)
    3 Convolvulus paniculatus: bhūmikuṣmāṇḍa.
    E. ikṣu and gandha smell; having the odor of the sugar cane.
