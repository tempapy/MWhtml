• ambarīṣa [062] mn. (-ṣaḥ-ṣaṃ) 1 A frying pan. 2 War, battle. m. (-ṣaḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 Also of VIṢṆU. 3 A young animal, a colt, a boy. 4 The name of a king of the solar race, celebrated as a worshipper of VIṢṆU. 5 A division of hell. 6 The sun. 7 Repentance. 8 A tree, (Spondias mangifera.) E. abi to sound, and īṣan Uṇādi affix, but irregularly formed.
