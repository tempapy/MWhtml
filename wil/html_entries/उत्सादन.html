• utsādana [144] n. (-naṃ) 1 Going up, ascending, rising. 2 Raising, elevating. 3 Destroying, overturning. 4 Cleaning the person with perfumes. 5 Rubbing or chafing the limbs. 6 Ploughing a field twice or thoroughly. 7 Healing a sore, causing it to fill up. E. ut much, ṣada to destroy, in the causal form, affix lyuṭ.
