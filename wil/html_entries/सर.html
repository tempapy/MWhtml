• sara [906] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Who or what goes, occurring chiefly in composition, as anusara, avasara, &c. m. (-raḥ) 1 The thick part or coagulum of curds or milk, cream, &c. 2 Going, motion. 3 An arrow. 4 Saltness, salt. n. (-raṃ) 1 A lake, a pool. 2 Water. mf. (-raḥ-rā) or rī) A cascade, a water-fall; also śara. E. sṛ to go, aff. ap.
