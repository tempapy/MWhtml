• gṛhabalibhuj [296] m. (-bhuk) 1 A sparrow. 2 A kind of crane, (Ardea nivea.) 3 A crow. E. gṛha a house or a wife, bali grain, &c. used in oblations, or any edible grain, and bhuj who eats; that is, who eats the food of his female, or the fragments of offerings fallen in or about the house; also gṛhavalipriya.
