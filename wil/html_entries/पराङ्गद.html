• parāṅgada [505] m. (-daḥ) A name of ŚIVA. E. para another, (DURGĀ,) aṅga form, and da who gives; forming with the goddess a deity, half male and half female; or better, para an enemy, KĀMADEVA, aṅga body, and da who gives; restoring the body of that deity to shape, after having reduced it to ashes in a rage.
