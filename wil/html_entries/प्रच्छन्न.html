• pracchanna [560] mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ) 1 Covered, clothed. 2 Unavowed, disguised. 3 Private, concealed, secret. n. (-nnaṃ) 1 A private door within a house. 2 A lattice, a loop hole. 3 Any private door. E. pra before cchada to hide, aff. kta, what is concealed or secret.
