• dhī (o ṅa) o dhīṅ r. 4th cl. (dhīyate)
    1 To contain, to hold.
    2 To slight, to disregard or despise.
    3 To accomplish.
    With antar, To disappear, to become invisible.
  • dhī f. (-dhīḥ) Understanding, intellect.
    E. dhyai to meditate, affix kvip, the final diphthong rejected, and the semi-vowel changed to its congener i, which becomes long.
