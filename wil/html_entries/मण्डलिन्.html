• maṇḍalin mfn. (-lī-linī-li)
    1 Being in a circle, made up into a coil or ball.
    2 Surrounded by. m. (-lī)
    1 A large species of snake.
    2 A cat.
    3 A polecat.
    4 A dog.
    5 The sun.
    6 The ruler or governor of a Maṇḍala or district.
    E. maṇḍala a circle, and ini aff. [634]
