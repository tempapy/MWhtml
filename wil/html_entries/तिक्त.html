• tikta mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ)
    1 Bitter.
    2 Fragrant. m. (-ktaḥ)
    1 A bitter taste, bitterness.
    2 Fragrance, perfume.
    3 A medicinal plant, (Echites antidysenterica.)
    4 A tree, (Capparis trifoliata:) see varuṇa. f. (-ktā) Katuki, a medicinal plant. n. (-ktaṃ) A medicinal plant, (Mollugo pentaphylla.)
    E. tija to sharpen, (the appetite,) affix kta. [375]
