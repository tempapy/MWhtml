• tikta [375] mfn. (-ktaḥ-ktā-ktaṃ) 1 Bitter. 2 Fragrant. m. (-ktaṃ) A medicinal plant, (Mollugo pentaphylla.) E. tija to sharpen, (the appetite,) affix kta.
