• miśra [662] r. 10th cl. (miśrayati) To mix, to join, to blend, to combine.
  • miśra [662] mfn. (-śraḥ-śrā-śraṃ) Mixed, mingled, blended. n. (-śraṃ) Mixing, mixture. m. (-śraḥ) 1 An elephant, the fourth of the four classes in which elephants are arranged. 2 A respectable person; in which sense it is a common affix to proper names; or compounded with āryya, as āryyamiśrāḥ. E. miśra to mix, aff. ac.
