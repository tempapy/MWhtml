• uparati f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Stopping, ceasing.
    2 Refraining from sensual enjoyment.
    3 Abstaining from prescribed acts.
    4 Great or exquisite pleasure.
    5 Death.
    E. upa reverse or excess, rama to please, ktin affix; also with ghañ affix uparama or uparāma. [157]
