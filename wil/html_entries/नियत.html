• niyata mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Self-governed, subdued, restrained.
    2 Attentive, fixed or intent upon.
    3 Constant, permanent.
    4 Ascertained, certain, fixed.
    5 Inevitable.
    6 Positive, definite.
    7 Permeable, what may be spread through or over. n. adv. (-taṃ) Always, constantly. subst. Elementary or crude matter, the recipient of attributes or properties.
    E. ni before yama to restrain, affix kta.
