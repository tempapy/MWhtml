• śūla r. 1st cl. (śūlati)
    1 To disorder, to distemper or disease.
    2 To make a loud noise.
    3 To impale, to transfix. [855]
  • śūla mn. (-laḥ-laṃ)
    1 Sharp pain in general, or especially in the belly, as cholic, &c. or in the joints, from rheumatism or gout.
    2 A weapon, a pike, a dart.
    3 An iron pin or spit.
    4 A banner, an ensign.
    5 An astrological Yoga, that of the 9th lunar mansion.
    6 Death, dying. f. (-lā)
    1 A whore.
    2 An instrument used for putting criminals to death, a stake for impaling them.
    E. śūla to disease, &c., aff. ac. [855]
