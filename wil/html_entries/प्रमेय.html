• prameya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ)
    1 Proveable, what may or ought to be proved.
    2 Measurable, finite. n. (-yaṃ) The thing to be proved. In logic, the second head, or topic, including twelve objects, as the soul, body, &c. or the form and end of existence.
    E. pra before mā to measure, ya aff. [576]
