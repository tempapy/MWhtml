• yāyāvara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Going repeatedly.
    2 Wandering, vagrant. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A horse fit for the Aśvamedha sacrifice.
    2 The saint JARATKĀRU.
    3 A Brahman who has preserved his household fire.
    4 A vagrant mendicant.
    E. yā to go, in the reiterative form, and varac aff.
