• cheda r. 10th cl. (chedayati) To cut, to cover, to divide, but especially into two parts.
  • cheda m. (-daḥ)
    1 Cutting, dividing.
    2 A part, a portion.
    3 Solving, dissipating, (as doubt.)
    4 The denominator of a fraction.
    5 The divisor.
    E. chida to cut, affix ghañ.
