• sthāyibhāva m. (-vaḥ)
    1 Fixed or permanent condition.
    2 A feeling or passion, which is the object of poetical or dramatic description, and is classed sometimes as one of three, but more usually as one of two orders of such objects; this order being opposed especially to the Vyabhicārī Bhāvas: the feelings or conditions termed Sthāyi are eight, viz. desire, mirth, sorrow, heroism, anger, fear, aversion, and astonishment.
    E. sthāyin fixed, and bhāva property. [952]
