• puru mfn. (-ruḥ-ruḥ-ru) Much, many, exceeding. m. (-ruḥ)
    1 The name of a king, the sixth monarch of the lunar line.
    2 Heaven, or the world and residence of immortals.
    3 The farina of a flower.
    4 The name of a Daityas. f. (-ruḥ) The name of a river, said to run a little to the north-west of the Sarsūti.
    E. pṝ to cherish or fill, Uṇādi aff. ku. [543]
