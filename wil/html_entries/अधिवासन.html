• adhivāsana [023] n. (-naṃ) 1 Perfuming or scenting the person, &c. 2 Abiding, staying. 3 A religious ceremony; touching a vessel containing perfumes, flowers, and other things previously presented to an idol, or offering perfumes, &c. to idols, as a preliminary ceremony. E. adhi, and vāsa to fumigate, lyuṭa aff.
