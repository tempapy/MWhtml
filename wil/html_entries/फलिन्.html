• phalin mfn. (-lī-linī-li)
    1 Bearing fruit, fruitful.
    2 Productive of consequences. m. (-lī)
    1 A sort of fish, commonly Phalāi (Mystus kapirat.)
    2 A tree, especially in fruit. f. (-nī)
    1 A medicinal plant, commonly Priyangu.
    2 A potherb, (Echites dichotoma.)
    3 A flower, (Celosia cristata.)
    E. phala fruit, and ini aff. [596]
