• koṣātaka [253] m. (-kaḥ) Hair. f. (-kī) 1 A small species of cucumber, (Trichosanthes diaeca:) 2 A cucurbitaceous plant, commonly called Turai, (Luffa acutangula.) 3 Another kind, (L. pentangula.) 4 A kind of creeper: see ghoṣa. 5 A plant, (Achyranthes aspera;) also apāmārga. 6 Night. E. koṣa a sheathe, &c. ata to go, affixes kvun and ṅīṣ; also kośātaka and koṣātakī.
