• yati [679] m. (-tiḥ) 1 A sage, whose passions are completely under subjection. 2 A religious mendicant, especially one of the Jaina sect. 3 Stopping, ceasing, rest, term. 4 Restraining, controlling, checking. f. (-tīḥ) 1 A pause in poetry or metre, a stop, a rest. 2 Union, joining. 3 Passion, sentiment. 4 A widow. E. yata to endeavour, &c., aff. in, or yam to restrain, aff. ktin.
