• duṣṭa [418] mfn. (-ṣṭaḥ-ṣṭā-ṣṭaṃ) 1 Low, vile. 2 Weak, impotent. 3 Wicked, depraved. 4 Spoiled, injured. 5 Violated. 6 Contaminated, degraded. 7 Worthless. f. (-ṣṭā) A harlot, a wanton. n. (-ṣṭaṃ) A kind of Costus, (C. speciosus:) see kuṣṭa. E. duṣa to be corrupt, &c. affix kta.
