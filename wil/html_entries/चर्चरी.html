• carcarī [319] f. (-rī) 1 A festival, festive sport. 2 A kind of song. 3 Striking the hands to beat time. 4 Curled or woolly hair. 5 Musical symphony or, the recitation of scholars. 6 The noise made at festivals, &c. 7 Flattery, dishonest praise. E. carca to speak or say, to sound, &c. rala and ṅīṣ affs.
