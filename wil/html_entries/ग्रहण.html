• grahaṇa [304] mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇaṃ) A prisoner, captive, confined. n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 Taking, seizure. 2 Receiving, acceptance. 3 Assent, agreement. 4 Respect. 5 The hand. 6 An eclipse. 7 Comprehension, the taking or receiving of instruction, or the acquirement of any science. 8 Sound. 9 An organ of sense. 10 In grammar, Exception. E. graha to take, affix lyuṭ.
