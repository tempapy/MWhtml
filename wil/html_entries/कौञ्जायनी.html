• kauñjāyanī f. (-nī)
    1 The wife of a Brahman or any venerable personage.
    2 A female descendant of Kuñja.
    E. kuñja, and phak affix, fem. affix ṅīṣ; this word may be masculine, kauñjāyana. m. (-naḥ) meaning a male descendant of Kuñja; also kaiñjāyanya formed from kuñja by cyañ affix m. sing. (-nyaḥ) sense; also plur. (-nāḥ) [253]
