• vallabha mfn. (-bhaḥ-bhā-bhaṃ)
    1 Beloved, desired, dear.
    2 Supreme, superintending. m. (-bhaḥ)
    1 A lover, a husband, a friend.
    2 A horse with good marks.
    3 A superintendant, an overseer.
    4 The chief herdsman. f. (-bhā) A mistress, a wife.
    E. valla to cover, abhac Uṇādi aff.
