• śada (au ḷ) au śadḷ r. 1st and 6th cls. (śīyate)
    1 To perish gradually, to wither or decay.
    2 To fall.
    3 To throw down.
    4 To go, to move. [829]
  • śada m. (-daḥ) A vegetable, any edible vegetable product, as fruits, roots, &c.
    E. śada to wither, aff. ac. [829]
