• kuṭumba [226] r. 10th cl. (kuṭumbayate) To support a family.
  • kuṭumba [226] m. (-mbaḥ) 1 A name. 2 A kinsman, a relation by descent. 3 A connexion, a relation by the mother's side by marriage, &c. 4 Offspring, progeny. 5 Family, race. E. kuṭumba to support a family, and ac aff.
