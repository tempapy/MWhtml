• kuṭumba r. 10th cl. (kuṭumbayate) To support a family. [226]
  • kuṭumba m. (-mbaḥ)
    1 A name.
    2 A kinsman, a relation by descent.
    3 A connexion, a relation by the mother's side by marriage, &c.
    4 Offspring, progeny.
    5 Family, race.
    E. kuṭumba to support a family, and ac aff. [226]
