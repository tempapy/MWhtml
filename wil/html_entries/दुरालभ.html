• durālabha [415] mfn. (-bhaḥ-bhā-bhaṃ) 1 Unfit to be touched or taken, difficult of handling. 2 Difficult of attainment. f. (-bhā) A prickly plant, commonly Javāsa, (Hedysarum alhagi.) E. dur, bad, hardly, labha to attain, with āṅ prefixed, affix khala, fem. affix ṭāp: see the next.
