• bhīmarathī f. (-thī)
    1 A particular night, supposed to be the ordinary period of human life; the seventh night, in the seventh month, of the seventy-seventh year; a person after this period is exempt from all religious or specific observances.
    2 The name of a river in the Himālaya mountains.
    E. bhīma formidable, ratha a car. [621]
