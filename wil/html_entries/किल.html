• kila [222] r. 6th cl. (kilati) 1 To be or become white. 2 To play. r. 10th cl. (kelayati) To send, to throw or cast.
  • kila [222] ind. An aptote signifying. 1 News, (so said, so reported.) 2 Likelihood, (probably, possibly.) 3 Conciliatory expression. 4 Assertion, certainty, (indeed, verily, assuredly.) 5 Falsehood, inaccuracy. E. kila to send or order, affix ka.
