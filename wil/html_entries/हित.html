• hita [975] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ). 1 Suitable, proper, fit, worthy, right. 2 Gone, proceeded. 3 Held, taken, seized, placed. 4 (In medicine,) Proper, as diet or menstrua, suitable to the case or the medicines taken. 5 Friendly, affectionate, kind. 6 Useful. m. (-taḥ) A good, a benefit. (-tā) A causeway, a dike. E. dhā to have or hold, kta aff.
