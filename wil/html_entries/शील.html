• śīla [849] r. 1st cl. (śīlati) 1 To meditate, to consider, to reflect profoundly and abstractedly. 2 To worship, to adore. r. 1st and 10th cls. (śīlati śīlayati) To practise, to exercise, (as the memory, &c.) to repeat. r. 10th cl. (śīlayati) 1 To wear. 2 To have or be endowed with.
  • śīla [849] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Possessed of, endowed with, practising, versed in, &c. 2 Behaving, conducted. 3 Well-behaved, well-disposed. mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) 1 Nature, quality. 2 Disposition, inclination. 3 Good conduct or disposition, steady and uniform observance of law and morals. 4 Beauty. m. (-laḥ) A large snake. E. śīla to meditate, to learn, &c., aff. ghañ; or śī to sleep, and lak Uṇādi aff.
