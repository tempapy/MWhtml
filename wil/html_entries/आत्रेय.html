• ātreya [110] m. (-yaḥ) 1 The name of a Muni or saint, the son of ATRI, applicable to DATTA, DURVĀSAS, and SOMA. 2 An essential humor or juice of the body. f. (-yikā or -yī) A woman during her courses. f. (-yī) 1 The name of a river in the north of Bengal. 2 The wife of ATRI. 3 Any woman of the Brahmanical. order. E. atri a saint so called, ḍhak patronymic aff.
