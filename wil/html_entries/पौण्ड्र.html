• pauṇḍra [555] m. (-ṇḍraḥ) 1 A country, extending from Rangpur across the Ganges to the Jangel Mehals and part of south Behar, including therefore the greater part of Bengal. 2 A sort of sugar-cane of a pale straw colour. E. puṇḍra the same, aff. aṇ; also with kan added pauṇḍraka.
