• apūrvva [048] mfn. (-vvaḥ-rvvā-rvvaṃ) 1 Wonderful, extraordinary. 2 Unprecedented, unpreceded. 3 Excellent, not exceeded. 4 Not easterly. n. (-rvvaṃ) The remote or unforeseen consequence of an act, as heaven of religious rites, a consequence not immediately preceded by its cause. E. a neg. pūrvva prior.
