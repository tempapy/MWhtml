• dhāman [443] n. (-ma) 1 The body. 2 A house, a dwelling. 3 Dignity, consequence, eminence. 4 Spirit, pride, especially martial pride, heroism. 5 Light, splendour, radiance. 6 A ray of light. 7 A place, a spot, a country. 8 Birth. E. dhā to contain, (life, a tenant, &c.) and manin aff.
