• haripriya mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Liked or loved by VIṢṆU, &c. m. (-yaḥ)
    1 A tree, (Nauclea Cadamba.)
    2 ŚIVA.
    3 A fool, a blockhead.
    4 Armour, mail. n. (-yaṃ)
    1 A dark sort of Sandal wood.
    2 The fragrant root of the Andropogon muricatum. f. (-yā)
    1 LAKṢMĪ, the wife of VIṢṆU.
    2 Tulasi or basil.
    3 The twelfth day of the lunar fortnight.
    4 The earth.
    E. hari VIṢṆU, and priya beloved. [970]
