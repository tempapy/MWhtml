• upapada n. (-daṃ)
    1 Small, diminutive.
    2 The subsidiary term in a compound word.
    3 The first member of a compound word.
    4 An aptote, a word sometimes used as an adverb and sometimes as an inflected noun.
    5 A secondary noun in a sentence in conjunction or apposition.
    E. upa before pada a foot, or an inflected word. [156]
