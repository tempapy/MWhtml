• pitṛgaṇa m. (-ṇaḥ) A class of progenitors, the sons of the Ṛṣis or Prajāpatis: the principal classes are the Sonasads descended from VIRĀṬA, the Agniṣvāttas from MARĪCI, Barhiṣads from ATRI, Somapās from BHṚGU, Haviṣmats from AṄGIRAS, Ājyapas from PULASTYA, Sukālins from VASIṢṬHA; there are also the Agnidagdhas and Anagnidagdhas, Kāvyas and Saumyas.
    E. pitṛ father (of mankind), gaṇa a troop. [535]
