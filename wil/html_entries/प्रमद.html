• pramada mfn. (-daḥ-dā-daṃ)
    1 Mad, intoxicated, figuratively with passion or literally with liquor.
    2 Violent.
    3 Careless.
    4 Impassioned. m. (-daḥ)
    1 Joy, pleasure, delight, rapture.
    2 Datura, (D. metel.) f. (-dā) A woman, a handsome woman.
    E. pra before mada mad, drunk. [575]
