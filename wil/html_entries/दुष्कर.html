• duṣkara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-rī-raṃ)
    1 Difficult to be done.
    2 One who behaves ill, does wrong, &c. wicked, bad. n. (-raṃ)
    1 Æther, atmosphere.
    2 The tree of plenty.
    3 Doing any thing with pain or difficulty.
    E. dur bad, and kara what does, from kṛ with khal aff. [417]
