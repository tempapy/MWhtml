• bāla [603] mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) 1 Ignorant, unwise, uninstructed. 2 Young, infantine, a child. mf. (-laḥ-lā) 1 An infant, a child; it usually means the young child, under five years old, but is equally applicable till sixteen years of age. 2 A colt. 3 A five year old elephant. 4 An elephant's tail. 5 A horse's tail. 6 Any tail. 7 Hair. 8 A sort of fish, (Cyprinus Rohita, HAM.) mn. (-laḥ-laṃ) A perfume, perhaps a sort of fragrant grass, (Andropogon schænanthus.) f. (-lā) 1 Small cardamoms. 2 A woman, a female. 3 Aloes. 4 Another plant, commonly Acnadhi. 5 The cocoa-nut. 6 Turmeric. 7 A sort of jasmine. 8 A flower, (Hibiscus tortuosus, Rox.) 9 Pure, (applied specially to a female animal, as a heifer, &c. fit for an oblation.) f. (-lī) A sort of ear-ring. E. bala to live, aff. ghañ, it sometimes occurs, but less accurately perhaps, with a palatial initial.
