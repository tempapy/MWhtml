• dhiṣṇya n. (-ṣṇyaṃ)
    1 A place, a spot, a country.
    2 A house.
    3 A star, an asterism.
    4 Fire.
    5 Power, strength. m. (-ṣṇyaḥ) A name of AGNI, the deity of fire.
    2 A name of ŚUKRA, regent of VENUS.
    E. dhṛṣa to be proud or overbearing, deriv. irr.; the word is also written dhiṣṭya or dhiṣṭhya. [444]
