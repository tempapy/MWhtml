• ūrmmi [167] mf. (-rmmiḥ) or f. only, (-rmmī) 1 A wave. 2 A current, the flowing of water. 3 A fold or plait in a garment. 4 Light, manifestation. 5 Speed, velocity. 6 Mental suffering, distress, uneasiness. 7 Pain. 8 Missing, regretting. 9 Human infirmity, as grief, illusion, hunger, thirst, decay, death. 10 Association. 11 Number, quantity. E. ṛ to go, mi Uṇādi affix, ū substituted for the initial letter.
