• mahat mfn. (-hān-hatī-hat)
    1 Great, large, bulky.
    2 Much, many.
    3 Best, excellent, illustrious. n. (-t)
    1 Kingdom, dominion.
    2 The intellectual principle, the second principle in creation. adv. Much, very, exceeding. f. (-tī)
    1 The Vīṇā or lute of NĀRADA, containing seven or one hundred strings.
    2 The egg-plant, (Solanum melongena.)
    E. maha to worship, ati Uṇādi aff. In composition, mahā is generally substituted, ā being inserted in place of the final.
