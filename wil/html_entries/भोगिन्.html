• bhogin [628] mfn. (-gī-ginī-gi) Enjoying, possessing, an enjoyer, &c. m. (-gī) 1 A snake. 2 A king, a prince. 3 A barber. 4 The head man of a village. 5 A person who accumulates money for a particular expenditure. f. (-nī) 1 The capital of the Nāgas. 2 A royal concubine. E. bhoga enjoyment, aff. ini.
