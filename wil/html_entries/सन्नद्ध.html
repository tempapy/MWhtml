• sannaddha [891] mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ) 1 Armed, mailed, accoutred. 2 Arranged, arrayed, prepared for battle. 3 Murderous, felonious, provided with arms for the destruction of others. 4 Wearing amulets, provided with charms, &c. 5 Near, at hand. E. sam before naha to bind, aff. kta.
