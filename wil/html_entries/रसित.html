• rasita [699] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Gilded, plated, washed or spread with gold, &c. 2 Sounded. 3 Having taste, or flavour, or sentiment. m. (-taḥ) 1 The rattling of thunder. 2 Voice, sound in general. 3 Wine, vinous liquor. E. rasa to sound, aff. kta, or rasa fluid, (metal, &c,) and itac aff.
