• vibhāvarī f. (-rī)
    1 Night.
    2 Turmeric.
    3 A bawd.
    4 A harlot.
    5 The shreds of a garment torn in a scuffle (?).
    E. vi before bhā to shine, (with stars,) kvanip aff., fem. aff. ṅīṣ, and rac substituted for the final; also vibhāva amorous excitement, rac aff. [777]
