• namas ind.
    1 Gift, present.
    2 Bowing, bending, salutation, obeisance.
    3 An inarticulate cry.
    4 The term used in connexion with the name of a deity in the fifth case to signify veneration, as rāmāya namaḥ salutation, glory or reverence to RĀMA.
    E. nama to bend, and affix asun.
