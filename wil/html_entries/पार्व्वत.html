• pārvvata mfn. (-taḥ-tī-taṃ) Mountain, produced in the mountains. m. (-taḥ) A tree called the large Nimb, (Melia sempervirens.) f. (-tī)
    1 A name of DURGĀ, in her capacity of daughter of HIMĀLA, the sovereign of the snowy mountains.
    2 DRAUPADĪ, the wife of the Pāṇḍu princes.
    3 A female cowherd or Gopī.
    4 The olibanum tree, (Boswellia thurifera.)
    5 Another tree, (Celtis orientalis.)
    6 A red clay, commonly called Saurāṣṭrī, or Surat earth.
    7 A name applied to several rivers.
    E. parvvata a mountain, aṇ aff. of descent or relation.
