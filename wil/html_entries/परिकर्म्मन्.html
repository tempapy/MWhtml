• parikarmman n. (-rmma)
    1 Personal decoration, dressing, painting or perfuming the body.
    2 Perfuming the person after bathing.
    3 Cleansing, purifying.
    4 Arithmetical computation. m. (-rmmā) A servant, a slave.
    E. pari implying ornament, and karmman business.
