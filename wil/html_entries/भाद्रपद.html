• bhādrapada m. (-daḥ) The month Bhādra.
    E. bhadrapadā the asterism, and aṇ aff. f. (-dā) A name common to the twenty-sixth and twenty-seventh lunar asterisms, distinguished by the epithets prior, and subsequent, or pūrvva and uttaraḥ see pūrvvabhādrapadā, &c.
    E. bhadrā said to mean a cow, and pada foot, aṇ added optionally, whence it is often read bhadrapadā. [617]
