• karkaśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ)
    1 Violent.
    2 Hard.
    3 Intangible.
    4 Cruel.
    5 Unfeeling, unmerciful.
    6 Harsh, unkind.
    7 Miserly. m. (-śaḥ)
    1 A plant, commonly Sunda rocani, (Crinum?)
    2 A kind of cassia, (Cassia esculenta.)
    3 Sugar cane.
    4 A sword, a scymitar. f. (-śī) Wild jujube; also karkkaśikā.
    E. kṛ to injure, vic affix, kaśa with the same meaning, and ac, highly injurious: or karkka a Sautra root, and śaḥ aff.
