• mṛga r. 4th cl. (mṛgyati) r. 10th cl. (mṛgayate) To seek, to pursue, to hunt, to chase.
  • mṛga m. (-gaḥ)
    1 A deer, an antelope.
    2 An animal in general.
    3 Research, inquiry, investigation.
    4 Asking, soliciting, begging.
    5 A kind of elephant, one with white marks on his forehead.
    6 The fifth lunar constellation, more usually Mṛgaśiras: see mṛgaśiras.
    7 The month in which the moon is full in the constellation Mṛgaśiras.
    8 A particular sacrifice.
    9 Musk.
    10 Hunting, chase.
    11 One of the four classes into which men are divided, in erotic works. f. (-gī)
    1 A description of woman, perhaps the same as the one termed tritriṇī, the corresponding female to the male called mṛga.
    2 A female deer, a doe.
    3 A species of the Madhya metre.
    E. mṛga to search, to chase, aff. ghañ.
