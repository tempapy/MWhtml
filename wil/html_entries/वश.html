• vaśa r. 2nd cl. (vaṣṭi) To desire, to will, to wish.
  • vaśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ)
    1 Humbled, subdued, tamed, overpowered.
    2 Enthralled, subdued by charms and incantations, fascinated, charmed, enchanted. mn. (-śaḥ-śaṃ) Wish, desire. n. (-śaṃ)
    1 Authority, supremacy, mastership.
    2 Subjection, submission, the state of being completely tamed and over-powered.
    3 Birth. m. (-śaḥ) The residence of harlots. f. (-śā)
    1 A woman.
    2 A wife.
    3 A daughter.
    4 A cow.
    5 A barren cow.
    6 A female elephant.
    7 A husband's sister.
    E. vaśa to desire, aff. ap, fem. aff. ṭāp.
