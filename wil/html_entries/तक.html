• taka r. 1st cl. (takati)
    1 To laugh at or deride.
    2 To bear.
    With vi and ati prefixed, and implying reciprocity (ṣyatitakate) To retort a laugh. (i) taki r. 1st cl. (taṅkati) To live in distress, to be in poverty or pain. (taṃkate) To go, to move. [362]
  • taka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) That.
    E. tat that, kan pleonasm added. [362]
