• sama mfn. pron. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ)
    1 All, whole, entire. (adj.)
    2 Like, similar.
    3 Good, virtuous.
    4 Full, complete.
    5 Same, even, equal.
    6 Indifferent, impartial.
    7 Mean. f. (-mā) A year. n. (-maṃ)
    1 A figure of rhetoric, identity or sameness of objects compared to one another.
    2 (In geometry,) A mean; a fourth proportional to the two perpendiculars and the link or segment.
    E. sama to be confused or unconfused, aff. ac.
