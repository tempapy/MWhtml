• mokṣa [675] r. 1st and 10th cls. (mokṣati mokṣayati) To let go, to release, to discharge, to send, to cast or throw.
  • mokṣa [675] m. (-kṣaḥ) 1 Final and eternal happiness, the liberation of the soul from the body, and its exemption from further transmigration. 2 Death. 3 Liberation, freedom. 4 A plant, commonly Ghaṇṭāpārali. 5 Acquittance of an obligation, as by a son to his father. E. mokṣa to let loose or free, aff. ac.
