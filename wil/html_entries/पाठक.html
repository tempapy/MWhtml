• pāṭhaka m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A lecturer, a public reader of the Purāṇas or other sacred works, or a Paṇḍita who declares what is the law or custom according to the scriptures.
    2 A spiritual preceptor.
    3 A reader, a student.
    E. paṭha to read, ṇvul aff., or paṭha, causal v. vun aff.
