• vikaṭa mfn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭā-ṭaṃ)
    1 Large, great.
    2 Formidable, frightful, hideous, horrible.
    3 Large-toothed.
    4 Beautiful, pleasing.
    5 Changed in form or appearance.
    6 Obscure, obsolete. n. (-ṭaṃ) A bile, a tumour. f. (-ṭā) A female divinity peculiar to the Bauddhas.
    E. vi implying separation or expansion, kaṭa to go or be, aff. ac, or with kaṭac aff. [758]
