• saudāminī [946] f. (-nī) 1 Lightning. 2 One of the Apsarasas or nymphs of Svarga. 3 A particular sort of lightning. 4 A city so named. E. sudāman a cloud, a mountain, or INDRA'S elephant, aṇ and ṅīṣ affs. with the augment ik; also omitting the augment, saudāmanī, and dropping the penultimate vowel, saudāmnī.
