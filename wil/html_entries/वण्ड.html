• vaṇḍa mfn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍā-ṇḍaṃ)
    1 Maimed, defective, crippled, but especially in the hands.
    2 Impotent, emasculated. m. (-ṇḍaḥ)
    1 A man who is circumcised or has no prepuce.
    2 An ox without his tail. f. (-ṇḍaḥ) An unchaste woman.
    E. vaṇa to sound, &c., aff. ḍa. [731]
