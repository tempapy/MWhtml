• śikhaṇḍin m. (-ṇḍī)
    1 A peacock.
    2 A peacock's tail.
    3 A cock.
    4 An arrow.
    5 A Ṛṣi, either of the seven represented by the stars of the greater bear: see citraśikhaṇḍin.
    6 The son of DRUPADA, by metamorphosis: see below. f. (-nī)
    1 A kind of jasmine, (J. auriculatum.)
    2 A shrub, (Abrus precatorius.)
    3 The daughter of DRUPADA, afterwards changed to a male.
    E. śikhaṇḍa a crest, and ini poss. aff.
