• maṭha [632] r. 1st cl. (maṭhati) 1 To be confused or disturbed in mind. 2 To dwell, to inhabit. (i) maṭhi r. 1st cl. (maṃṭhate) To bewail, to regret, to remember with sorrow or regret.
  • maṭha [632] m. (-ṭhaḥ) 1 A school, a college, the residence of young Brahmans, prosecuting sacred studies. 2 A building inhabited by ascetics of the same order. 3 A temple. 4 A cart, a carriage. E. maṭha to dwell, aff. ac.
