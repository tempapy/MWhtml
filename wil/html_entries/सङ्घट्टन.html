• saṅghaṭṭana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Meeting, encountering, occurrence.
    2 Union, junction, contact, cohesion.
    3 Close contact, cleaving or adhering to, the intertwining of wrestlers, the embraces of lovers, &c.
    4 Friction, rubbing together.
    E. sam before ghaṭṭa to move, aff. yuc.
