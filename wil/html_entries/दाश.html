• dāśa [406] (ṛ) dāśṛ r. 1st cl. (dāśati-te) 10th cl. (dāśayate) To give. 5th cl. (dāśnoti) To hurt, to injure or kill: see dāsa.
  • dāśa [407] m. (-śaḥ) 1 A fisherman, the son of a Niṣada by a woman of the Ayogava caste. 2 A servant. f. (-śī) 1 A female slave, &c. E. dāśṛ to give, affix ac: see dāsa.
