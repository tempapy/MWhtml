  • dāśa m. (-śaḥ)
    1 A fisherman, the son of a Niṣada by a woman of the Ayogava caste.
    2 A servant. f. (-śī)
    1 A female slave, &c.
    E. dāśṛ to give, affix ac: see dāsa. [407]
