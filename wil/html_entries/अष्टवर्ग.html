• aṣṭavarga m. (-rgaḥ) A class of eight principal medicaments, they are mostly the roots of plants from the hills, which have not been determined, and are named: Ṛṣabha, Jīvaka, Meda, Mahāmeda, Ṛddhi, Vṛddhi, Kākolī and Kṣīrakākolī.
    E. aṣṭa eight, and varga a class. [092]
