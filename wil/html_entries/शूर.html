• śūra r. 10th cl. (śūrayate)
    1 To be valiant or powerful.
    2 To make great effort or exertion. (ī) śūrī r. 4th cl. (śūryyate)
    1 To hurt or kill.
    2 To be stupid.
  • śūra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A hero.
    2 The sun.
    3 A lion.
    4 A boar.
    5 The grandfather of KṚṢṆA.
    6 The Śāl tree, (Shorea robusta.)
    E. śu to bear, Sautra root, Uṇādi aff. kran, and the vowel made long, or śūra to be brave, aff. ac.
