• kātyāyana m. (-naḥ)
    1 The name of a celebrated lawgiver and divine sage.
    2 An appellation of VARARUCI a poet.
    E. kātya, and phak pleonastic affix. f. (-nī)
    1 A name of DURGĀ.
    2 A middle- aged widow.
    E. ṅīṣ added to kātyāyana; the daughter of the saint KĀTYĀYANA, (DURGĀ,) or resembling the wife of a sage in dress and austerity, (the widow.)
