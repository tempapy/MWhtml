• kāruja m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A piece of mechanism, any product of manufacture.
    2 An ant hill, a hillock.
    3 A young elephant.
    4 Sesamum growing spontaneously.
    5 A plant, (Mesua ferrea.)
    6 Froth, foam.
    7 Red orpiment.
    E. kāru an art or artist, and ja what is born or produced.
