• rasajña mfn. (-jñaḥ-jñā-jñaṃ) Discriminating or acquainted with tastes, sentiments, &c. m. (-jñaḥ)
    1 A poet, a writer who understands the different Rasas or sentiments to be described.
    2 An alchymist, one who has obtained a command over the magical properties of mercury.
    3 A physician, a medical preparer and administerer of mercurial and chemical compounds. f. (-jñā) The tongue.
    E. rasa as above, and jña who or what knows. [698]
