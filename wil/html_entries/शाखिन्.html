• śākhin mfn. (-khī-khinī-khi) Branched, branching, having branches, (literally or figuratively.) m. (-khī)
    1 A tree.
    2 A Veda.
    3 An inhabitant of the northern districts bordering on India, a Turk or Tartar.
    4 The name of a king.
    E. śākhā a branch, aff. ini. [836]
