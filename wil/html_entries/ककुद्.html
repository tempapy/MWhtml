• kakud f. (-t) or kakuda mn. (-daḥ-daṃ)
    1 The hump on the shoulders of the Indian bull.
    2 An ensign or symbol of royalty, as the white parasol, &c.
    3 Chief, pre-eminent.
    4 The peak or summit of a mountain.
    E. ka happiness, ku to sound, affixes kvip and tuk; ta becomes da; or with a final vowel, taking an additional affix ac.
