• cūḍā f. (-ḍā)
    1 A single lock of hair left on the crown of the head at the ceremony of tonsure.
    2 A peacock's crest.
    3 Any crest, plume, diadem, &c.
    4 The head.
    5 Top, summit.
    6 An upper house, a room on the top of a house, &c.
    7 A kind of bracelet.
    8 A small well.
    9 Tonsure; more commonly cūḍākaraṇaṃ.
    E. cūla to elevate, aṅ and ṭāp affixes, la changed to ḍa, and the vowel lengthened.
