• brahmarātri m. (-triḥ)
    1 A name of YĀJÑAVALKYA, a saint and legislator.
    E. brahma BRAHMĀ, rā to give, ṣṭrin aff.; conferring the knowledge of BRAHMĀ upon JANAKA in return for his bounty.
    2 A night of BRAHMĀ, equal to a thousand divine ages.
    E. brahma BRAHMĀ, and rātri night. [607]
