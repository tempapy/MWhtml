• raktikā f. (-kā)
    1 A plant, (Abrus precatorius).
    2 The seed of the Abrus precatorius used as a weight, or a fictitious weight, denominated from the seeds originally so used; the seed weighs about 1 and (5/16) troy grain, the artificial ratti or Raktikā should be double that weight, but in common use it averages nearly 2 grains and a quarter.
    E. rakta red, kan aff., fem. form. [692]
