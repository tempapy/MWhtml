• kāṇḍīra mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) Armed with an arrow, an archer. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A creeping plant, commonly Kāṇḍa Bel, (Momordica charantia.)
    2 A plant, (Achyranthes aspera.) f. (-rā) Bengal madder, (Rubia manjith): see mañjiṣṭhā.
    E. kāṇḍa an arrow, a stem, &c. and īrac aff. [208]
