• chatrabhaṅga [333] m. (-ṅgaḥ) 1 Widowhood. 2 Wilfulness, independence. 3 Subversion of dominion, loss of empire, deposition, &c. E. chata a parasol, and bhaṅga a breaking; in the first two senses chatra is used metaphorically, for any cover or defence, as a husband, &c. in the last it is used by synecdoche, a parasol being one of the ensigns of royalty.
