• saindhava mfn. (-vaḥ-vī-vaṃ)
    1 Produced or born in Sindh.
    2 Marine, aquatic, ocean or river-born. m. (-vaḥ) A horse. mn. (-vaḥ-vaṃ) Rock salt. n. (-vaṃ) In dramatic literature, musical expression of disappointment with Prākṛta words. f. (-vī) One of the Rāgiṇīs.
    E. sindhu a river, &c., aṇ aff.
