• dhvāṅkṣa m. (-ṅkṣaḥ)
    1 Any aquatick bird, as a crane, a gull, &c. feeding upon fish.
    2 A crow.
    3 One of the Nāgas.
    4 A beggar. f. (-ṅkṣī)
    1 A drug: see kākolī.
    2 A female imp or fiend.
    E. dhvāṅkṣa to cry, affix ac; it and its derivatives may also be read dhmākṣa, &c. [449]
