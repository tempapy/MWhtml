• nigraha [466] m. (-haḥ) 1 Aversion, disfavour, discouragement, dislike. 2 Abusing, blaming. 3 Binding, confinement, capture, arrest. 4 A binding, a tie. 5 Restraint, subjugation. 6 Suppression, putting down. 7 A boundary, a limit. 8 Administering medicine. 9 Killing. 10 A name of KṚṢṆA. E. ni before graha to take or seize, affix ap.
