• gandhamādana mn. (-naḥ-naṃ) The name of a particular mountain, forming the division between Ilāvṛtta and Bhadrāśva or to the east of Meru. m. (-naḥ)
    1 A large black bee.
    2 Sulphur.
    3 The name of one of the monkey chiefs. f. (-nī) Spirituous liquor; also in some books read gandhamādinī.
    E. gandha smell, mada to inebriate in the causal form, and lyuṭ aff. [281]
