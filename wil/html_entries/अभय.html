• abhaya n. (-yaṃ)
    1 Absence or removal of fear.
    2 The root of a fragrant grass, (Andropogon muricatum.) f. (-yā) Yellow myrobalan, (Terminalia citrina.) mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) Fearless, undaunted.
    E. a neg. and bhaya fear; the plant removing the dread of disease.
