• viṣama mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ)
    1 Difficult, (of access,) rough, uneven.
    2 Difficult, &c. (of comprehending,) as a book.
    3 Painful, difficult, troublesome, (in general.)
    4 Odd, (in numbers.)
    5 Unparalleled, unequalled.
    6 Wicked, dishonest.
    7 Fearful, frightful, awful. n. (-maṃ)
    1 Difficulty, pain.
    2 Unevenness, inequality.
    3 Oddness, (in numeration.)
    4 An inaccessible place, a thicket, a precipice.
    5 (In rhetoric,) Description of unusual or incompatible cause and effect.
    E. vi privative or contra-indicative, before sama even, smooth, equal, &c., and sa after the i of the prefix, changed to ṣa. [790]
