• anupāta [033] m. (-taḥ) 1 Falling subsequently upon, alighting or descending upon in succession. 2 Going, proceeding. 3 Following. 4 A degree of latitude opposite to one given, the antoeci. 5 Proportion, (in arithmetic.) 6 Arithmetical progression. E. anu, and pāta falling, &c.
