• masūra [647] mf. (-raḥ-rā) A sort of pulse or lentil, (Ervum hirsutum, and Cicer lens) f. (-rā) A whore. f. (-rī) 1 Small-pox. 2 A small round pillow. E. masa to weigh, Uṇādi aff. ūran, masūra, the pulse; the other meanings refer to this, implying resemblance to the pea in figure, &c.
