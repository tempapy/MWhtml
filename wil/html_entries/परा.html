• parā [505] ind. A participle and prefix implying, 1 Supremacy. 2 Liberation. 3 Inverted order. 4 Pride, contumely. 5 Encounter, mutual presence. 6 Excess, exceeding. 7 Overcoming, surpassing. 8 Going. 9 Killing, destroying, injuring. As parākrama superior power or valour; parājaya defeat; parābhava disgrace; parāvarta turning back, &c. see the following. E. pṝ to fill, aff. ap and ā added.
