• ṭaṅka [359] mn. (-ṅkaḥ-ṅkaṃ) 1 A kind of elephant or wood apple. nīlakapittha. 2 Borax. 3 A spade or hoe. m. (-ṅkaḥ) 1 A hatchet or stone cutter's chisel. 2 A sword, a scimitar, a sacrificial hatchet or knife. 3 A scabbard, a sword-sheath. 4 Anger, wrath. 5 A weight (of silver) equal to four māṣas. 6 A coin. 7 Pride, arrogance. 8 A cleft, a chasm. 9 A peak. f. (-ṅgā) 1 The leg. 2 The edge or side of a mountain. E. ṭaki to bind, affix ac; or ṭa imitative, kai to emit sound, affix ḍa, and num augment.
