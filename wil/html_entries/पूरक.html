• pūraka [549] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) 1 Filling, completing, or that which fills or completes. 2 Filling up. m. (-kaḥ) 1 A citron, (Citrus medica.) 2 (In arithmetic,) The multiplier. 3 Closing the right nostril, and drawing up air through the left, a religious ceremonial. 4 The funeral cake or cakes that must be given to complete the obsequial rites. E. pūra to fill, aff. kvun.
