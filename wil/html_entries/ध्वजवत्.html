• dhvajavat [449] mfn. (-vān-vatī-vat) Having a mark or sign. m. (-vān) 1 An ensign, a standard-bearer. 2 A distiller, a vender of spirituous liquors. 3 A Brahman who having slain another, carries the skull of the murdered man with him to places of pilgrimage by way of penance. E. dhvaja as above, matup aff.
