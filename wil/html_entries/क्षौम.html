• kṣauma [268] mfn. (-maḥ-mī-maṃ) Linen, made of linen, covered with it, &c. f. (-mī) Linen or flax, (Linum usitatissimum.) mn. (-maḥ-maṃ) 1 An airy room on the top of a house. 2 An apartment on the top of a hall. 3 The back of an edifice. 4 A fortified place in front of a building. 5 A building of a particular form. 6 Wove silk: 7 Linen cloth. E. kṣoma q. v. aṇ added.
