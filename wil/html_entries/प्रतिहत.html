• pratihata [568] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Disappointed. 2 Opposed, obstructed. 3 Fallen, overthrown. 4 Sent, dispatched. 5 Hated, disliked. 6 Tied, bound. 7 Struck, hurt, killed, especially in return or requital. 8 Repulsed, repelled. 9 Reflected. E. prati before hata hurt or killed.
