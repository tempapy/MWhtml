• samāhāra [898] m. (-raḥ) 1 Aggregation, collection, assemblage, either in fact or thought. 2 Contraction, abridgement. 3 Composition of words (in grammar). 4 Conjunction of equal words or sentences, the power of the particle and. 5 A particular form of composition, a subdivision of the class Dvanda, in which several words are joined together, and the compound is a term in the neuter gender, as ahinakulaṃ the snake and mungoos. 6 The combination of two letters of the alphabet into a syllable, which designates all the letters intermediate between the two of which it consists. E. sam together, āṅ before hṛ to take or convey, and ghañ aff.
