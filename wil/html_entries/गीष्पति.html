• gīṣpati m. (-tiḥ)
    1 A name of VṚHASPATI, regent of JUPITER and preceptor of the gods.
    2 A Paṇḍita or learned teacher.
    E. gir speech, in the nominative case, gīḥ and pati master, Visarga becomes sa and sa is here changed irregularly to ṣa; the compound however is sometimes written differently, thus gīḥpati, gīrpati or gīspati.
