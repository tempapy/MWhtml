• sūci [939] f. (-ciḥ-cī) 1 A needle. 2 Piercing, perforating. 3 Indication of a passion or feeling by signs, gesture, gesticulation. 4 A mode of dancing. 5 A mode of array, a sharp file or column. 6 A triangle formed by the sides of a trapezium produced to the point of meeting. 7 A cone, a pyramid. 8 (In astronomy,) The earth's disc in computing eclipses. 9 An index, a catalogue. E. sūca to make known, aff. in, and ṅīṣ optionally added; or siva to sew, &c., Uṇādi aff. caṭa; see sūca.
