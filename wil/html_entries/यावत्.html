• yāvat [685] mfn. (-yāvān, yāvatī yāvat) As much, how much, such. ind. 1 As much as. 2 As many as. 3 As long as. 4 As far as, unto, until; the correlative of tāvat. 5 When, (i. e. as much, applied to time.) E. yat what or which, ḍavatup aff., and ā substituted for the pronominal final.
