• agnibhū m. (-bhūḥ) A name of SKANDA, the Hindu deity of war. See kārttikeya and skanda.
    E. agni and bhū being; being, or born, from fire. ŚIVA having cast his seed into flame, for the purpose of generating a warrior, able to subdue TĀRAKA, a demon, SKANDA was produced. n. (-bhu) Water, which is generated, in the Hindu cosmogony, from fire. [006]
