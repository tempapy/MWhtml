• śukla mfn. (-klaḥ-klā-klaṃ) White, of a white colour. m. (-klaḥ)
    1 White, (the colour.)
    2 One of the astronomical Yogas. n. (-klaṃ)
    1 Silver.
    2 Fresh butter.
    3 A disease of the cornea, opacity, albugo. m. (-klaḥ) One half of a month: see śuklapakṣa. f. (-klā)
    1 A name of SARASVATĪ.
    2 Clayed or candied sugar.
    E. śuca to be pure, &c., Uṇādi aff. ran, and ra changed to la. [850]
