• trikālajña mfn. (-jñaḥ-jñā-jñaṃ) Omniscient. m. (-jñaḥ)
    1 A divine sage.
    2 A deity.
    3 A name of Buddha, the founder of the Bauddha sect.
    E. tri three, kāla time, and jña who knows, acquainted with the past, present, and future; also similar compounds as the next, &c.
