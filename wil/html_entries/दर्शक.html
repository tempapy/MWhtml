• darśaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) Who or what shows, displays, explains, makes clear, &c. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A doorkeeper, a warder.
    2 An exhibiter, one who points out or shows any thing.
    3 A skilful man, one conversant with any science or art, &c.
    E. dṛśa to see, vun aff. [401]
