• sthira [953] mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ) 1 Firm, fixed, steady, immovable. 2 Hard, solid. 3 Permanent, durable, lasting. 4 Firm, steady, (morally,) uninfluenced by pleasure or pain, &c. 5 Cool, collected. 6 Constant, faithful. m. (-raḥ) 1 A deity, an immortal. 2 KĀRTTIKEYA. 3 A mountain. 4 A tree. 5 Final emancipation from existence. 6 The planet SATURN. 7 A bull. f. (-rā) 1 The earth. 2 A shrub, (Hedysarum gangeticum.) 3 A medicinal root, commonly Kākolī. 4 Silk-cotton tree. E. ṣṭhā to stay or be, Uṇādi aff. kirac.
