• vida r. 2nd cl. (vetti vedi) To know.
    With ni prefixed, To be sad or depressed.
    With sam, (saṃvitte) To meditate, to know, to gain holy knowledge by abstract meditation. r. 4th cl. (vidyate) To be, to exist, to be present, to exist actually. r. 7th cl. (vinte) To consider, to reason. r. 10th cl. (vedayate)
    1 To be sensible.
    2 To know.
    3 To declare, to describe or explain; (in this sense ni is usually prefixed.)
    4 To dwell, to inhabit.
    5 To be fixed or steady.
    6 To suffer pain.
    With ni, or vi and ni prefixed, To deliver, to impart or communicate.
    With prati, To deliver, to give. (au ḷ) au viṭḷ r. 6th cl. (vindati-te) To gain, to get, to acquire or possess.
    With pari, To marry first.
