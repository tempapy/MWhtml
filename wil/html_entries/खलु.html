• khalu ind.
    1 A particle of prohibition.
    2 An expletive.
    3 An expression of endearment or conciliation.
    4 An expression indicating inquiry.
    5 An expression of asseveration or ascertainment, (certainly, indeed.)
    6 Only, merely.
    E. khala to gather, affix u.
