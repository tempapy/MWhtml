• vipakṣa mfn. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā-kṣaṃ) Opposed or adverse to, contrary, inimical. m. (-kṣaḥ)
    1 An enemy, an adversary.
    2 A disputant, an opponent.
    3 Exception, (in grammar, &c.)
    4 (In logic,) An argument or syllogism in which the proposition to be maintained is wanting.
    E. vi implying opposite, contrary, and pakṣa part or side. [774]
