• vyāpta [819] mfn. (-ptaḥ-ptā-ptaṃ) 1 Pervaded, occupied or penetrated by) thoroughly and essentially, (as the universe by spirit, &c., 2 Celebrated, famous. 3 Filled, full. 4 Placed, fixed. 5 Obtained. 6 Encircled, encompassed, surrounded. 7 Open, apart, outspread, expanded. E. vi before āpa to pervade, aff. kta.
