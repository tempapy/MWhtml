• tuṇḍikerī f. (-rī)
    1 The cotton plant.
    2 A sort of gourd, (Momordica monadelpha.)
    2 A large boil or swelling on the palate.
    E. tuṇḍikā the mouth or beak, ṛ to go, affixes aṇ and ṅīṣ; also with kan added tuṇḍikerikā. f. (-kā) and with in fem. affix tuṇṭhikeri. f. (-riḥ) also tuṇḍakerī and tuṇḍikeśī. [379]
