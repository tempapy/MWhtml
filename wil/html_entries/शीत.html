• śīta [848] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Cold, chilly, frigid. 2 Idle, lazy. 3 Cold, dull, apathetic, stupid. 4 Decocted, boiled. n. (-taṃ) 1 Cold, coldness. 2 Water. 3 Cold weather or the six months of the rainy, dewy and cold seasons. m. (-taḥ) A small tree, (Cordia myxa.) 2 A kind of ratan, (Calamus fasciculatus.) 3 A plant, (Marsilea quadrifolia.) 4 The Nīmb tree. 5 Camphor. f. (-tā) 1 The wife of RĀMA. 2 A furrow. E. śyai to go, aff. kta, the fem. form more usually occurs sītā, q. v.
