• āsana [126] n. (-naṃ) 1 A stool, a seat. 2 Maintaining a post against an enemy. 3 The withers of an elephant, the part where the driver sits. 4 Halting, stopping. 5 Sitting in some peculiar posture, as in the custom of devotees, &c. eighty-four kinds are enumerated: see padmāsana, &c. m. (-naḥ) A tree, (Pentapheca tomentosa.) also asana f. (-nā-nī) Stay, abiding, sitting. (-nī) 1 A shop, a stall. 2 A small seat, a stool. E. āsa to abide, and yuc or lyuṭ aff.
