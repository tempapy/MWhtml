• kendra n. (-ndaṃ)
    1 The centre of a circle.
    2 The argument of an equation.
    3 The distance of a planet from the first point of its orbit in the fourth, seventh, or tenth degree. It is variously applied in combination, as patanakendraṃ argument of the latitude; dvitīyakendraṃ the distance of the higher apsis form a planet in any point of its orbit; śīghrakendraṃ commutation, the distance of the sun from a superior planet or of an inferior planet from the sun; mandakendraṃ argument of anomaly.
