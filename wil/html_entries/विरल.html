• virala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Fine, delicate, thin, (but with interstices.)
    2 Loose, relaxed.
    4 Apart, wide, separated by an interval.
    4 Remote, rare, occurring at distant or repeated intervals of time. n. (-laṃ) Sour curds.
    E. vi apart, rā to go or be, aff. kalan.
