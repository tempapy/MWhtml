• reka(ṛ,)rekṛ r. 1st cl. (rekate)
    1 To suspect or think probable.
    2 To excite or entertain doubt: in the last case with āṅ usually prefixed. [709]
  • reka mf. (-kaḥ-kā)
    1 Doubt, uncertainty.
    2 Fear, apprehension. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Looseness, purging.
    2 An outcaste, a low man.
    3 A frog.
    E. reka to suspect, to fear, aff. ac, or rica to purge, aff. ghañ. [709]
