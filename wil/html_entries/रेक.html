• reka(ṛ,)rekṛ [709] r. 1st cl. (rekate) 1 To suspect or think probable. 2 To excite or entertain doubt: in the last case with āṅ usually prefixed.
  • reka [709] mf. (-kaḥ-kā) 1 Doubt, uncertainty. 2 Fear, apprehension. m. (-kaḥ) 1 Looseness, purging. 2 An outcaste, a low man. 3 A frog. E. reka to suspect, to fear, aff. ac, or rica to purge, aff. ghañ.
