• pūrvvaja [550] mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ) 1 Born or produced before either in time or place. 2 Eastern, born in the east. 3 Elder. m. (-jaḥ) 1 An elder brother. 2 The son of the elder wife, even though last born. m. plu. (-jāḥ) 1 The deified progenitors of mankind. 2 Ancestors, forefathers in general. E. pūrvva formerly or first, ja born.
