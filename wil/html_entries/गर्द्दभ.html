• garddabha m. (-bhaḥ)
    1 Smell, odour.
    2 An ass. f. (-bhī)
    1 A she-ass.
    2 An insect, a kind of beetle springing from cow dung.
    3 A disease of the skin, eruption of round, red, and painful spots; also gardabhikā.
    4 A creeper, (Clitoria ternata.) n. (-bhaṃ) The white lotus.
    E. garda to sound, Uṇādi affix abhac. [284]
