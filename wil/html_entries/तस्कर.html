• taskara m. (-raḥ)
    1 A thief, a robber.
    2 The ear.
    3 A kind of potherb, (Medicago esculenta.)
    3 A tree, (Vangueria spinosa:) see madana. f. (-rī) A passionate woman.
    E. tat that, used depreciatingly, and kara who does, suṭ substituted for the final of tat. [370]
