• śraviṣṭha [862] mfn. (-ṣṭhaḥ-ṣṭhā-ṣṭhaṃ) Produced in or under the sign ŚRAVIṢṬHĀ. f. (-ṣṭhā) The twenty-fourth lunar asterism, also called DHANIṢṬHĀ, corresponding to the DOLPHIN. E. śrava an ear, matup poss. aff., iṣṭhan aff. of the superlative, and the possessive rejected; the attributive is formed by the aff. aṇa, and that again or its effect optionally rejected.
