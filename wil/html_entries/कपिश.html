• kapiśa mfn. (-śaḥ-śā-śaṃ) Brown, of a brown colour. m. (-śaḥ)
    1 Brown, (the colour,) a compound of black and yellow.
    2 Incense, styrax or coarse benzoin. f. (-śī) A spirit, a sort of rum. f. (-śā) The mother of the demons called Piśācas.
    E. kapi an ape, and śaḥ affix; of the colour of an ape. [189]
