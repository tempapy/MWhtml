• hariṇa mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇī-ṇaṃ) Yellowish white. m. (-ṇaḥ)
    1 A deer.
    2 Yellowish white, (the colour.)
    3 White.
    4 VIṢṆU.
    5 ŚIVA.
    6 The sun.
    7 A goose.
    8 A minor division of the world. f. (-ṇī)
    1 A doe.
    2 A golden image.
    3 A woman, one of the four kinds, the same as the Citriṇī, or female of the man termed Mṛga.
    4 A form of metre, a variety of the class termed Atyaṣṭi or verse of four lines of 17 syllables each.
    5 Green: see harita.
    6 Yellow jasmine.
    E. hṛ to take, Uṇādi aff. inac.
