• tūlikā f. (-kā)
    1 A pencil, a painter's brush, or a stick with a fibrous extremity used as one.
    2 A mattress or quilt, a bed, a down or cotton bed.
    3 An ingot mould.
    4 A rod, &c. dipped into crucibles to try if their contents are in fusion.
    5 A wick or twist of cotton either for a lamp or for applying unguents, &c.
    E. tūlī as above, and kan added. [383]
