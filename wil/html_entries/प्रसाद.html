• prasāda [582] m. (-daḥ) 1 Clearness, cleanness, brightness. 2 Favour, kindness, propitiousness, approbation. 3 Well being, welfare. 4 Composure, calmness of mind. 5 Poetry written in an elegant but an easy style. 6 Perspicuity. 7 Connexion, association. 8 Food that has been offered to an idol, or of which a spiritual teacher has partaken. E. pra before sada to go, aff. ghañ.
