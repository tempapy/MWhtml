• pīta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Of a yellow colour.
    2 Drank, quaffed. m. (-taḥ)
    1 Yellow, the colour.
    2 Safflower.
    3 A topaz, a yellow gem.
    4 A yellow pigment, prepared from the urine of kine. n. (-taṃ)
    1 Drinking.
    2 Yellow orpiment. f. (-tā)
    1 Turmerick.
    2 A medical plant, commonly Atais, (Betula.)
    E. pā to drink, aff. kta; imbibed, literally, or figuratively, by the eye sight, &c.
