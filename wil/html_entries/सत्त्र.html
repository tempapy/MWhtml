• sattra [884] n. (-ttraṃ) 1 Sacrifice, oblation. 2 Liberality, munificence. 3 Covering, clothing, concealing. 4 Fraud, roguery, cheating. 5 A wood, a forest. 6 Wealth. 7 A house. 8 A tank, a pond. E. sada to go, Uṇādi aff. tran; in writing, one ta may be omitted, and it occurs, satra.
