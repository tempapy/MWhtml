• yantra [680] r. 1st and 10th cls. (yantrati yantrayati) To restrain: see yatri.
  • yantra [680] n. (-ntraṃ) 1 A machine in general, any implement or apparatus. 2 A diagram of a mystical nature, or astrological character. 3 A sort of vessel. 4 Restraining, checking, controlling. E. yama to check, Uṇādi aff. tra.
