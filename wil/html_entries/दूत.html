• dūta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) Sent, despatched. m. (-taḥ)
    1 A messenger or envoy, a news-carrier.
    2 An ambassador. f. (-tiḥ-tī) A female messenger, a confidante, a procuress, a go-between, &c.
    E. du to go, Uṇādi affix kta, and the vowel made long; or dū to suffer, affix kta, fem. affix ktin or ktic, and optionally ṅīp.
