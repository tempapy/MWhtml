• sarvvauṣadhigaṇa m. (-ṇaḥ) A class of certain principal drugs, consisting of Murā, Valerian Jaṭāmāṃsī, Orris root, Benzoin, Zedoary, Kurh, or Costus speciosus, red saunders, and Cyperus.
    E. sarvva all, auṣadhi a drug, and gaṇa a class; also with varga the same, sarvvauṣadhivarga m. (-rgaḥ) [911]
