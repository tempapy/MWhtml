• paca (ḍu, ṣ) ḍupacaṣ r. 1st cl. (pacati) To mature by cooking or ripening, to boil, to dress, to ripen; (i) paci r. 1st and 10th cls. (paṃcati-te paṃcayati)
    1 To make evident or apparent.
    2 To represent, to state fully.
    3 To spread. [492]
  • paca mfn. (-caḥ-cā-caṃ) Who or what cooks, matures, &c. m. (-caḥ) Cooking, maturing, &c.
    E. paca to cook, aff. ac. [492]
