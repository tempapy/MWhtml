• śatabhiṣā f. (-ṣā) The twenty-fifth of the Nakṣatras or lunar mansions, containing 100 stars, one of which is λ Aquarii.
    E. śata a hundred, bhiṣaj to cure, aff. ḍa; requiring many physicians; as the Hindus say DHANVANTARI himself cannot cure any person affected with disease, whilst the moon is in this asterism. [828]
