• stamba [949] m. (-mbaḥ) 1 A shrub, a plant that has no particular or decided stem. 2 A clump of grass, a sheaf of corn, &c. 3 The post to which an elephant is tied. 4 A mountain. n. (-mbaṃ) 1 A post, a pillar in general. 2 Stupidity, insensibility. E. ṣṭhā to stand or abide, ambac Uṇādi aff.
