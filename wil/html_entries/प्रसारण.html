• prasāraṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 Going about, spreading.
    2 Expanding, extending.
    3 Unfolding.
    4 Surrounding an enemy.
    5 The dispersion of an army by detachments for that purpose, or for collecting forage. f. (-ṇī) A plant, (Pœderia foetida.)
    E. pra before sṛ to go, aff. lyuṭ; also prasaraṇa. [582]
