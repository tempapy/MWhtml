• gokṣura [298] m. (-raḥ) The name of a plant; also Gokhura, (Ruellia longifolia, Rox. it is also applied in Bengal to Tribulus lanuginosus.) n. (-raṃ) A cow's hoof. E. go a cow, and kṣura a hoof, the thorns getting between the hoofs of cattle; also with kan added gokṣuraka, and with khura a hoof, gokhura.
