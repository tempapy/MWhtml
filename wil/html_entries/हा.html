• hā r. 3rd cl. (o ka,) ohāk (jahāti)
    1 To quit, to leave, to abandon.
    2 To wander or deviate: (o ḍa,) ohāṅ (jihate) To go, to move. [973]
  • hā ind.
    1 An interjection of weariness.
    2 Of sorrow, pain.
    3 An exclamation of pleasure.
    4 A term of reproach.
    E. hā to abandon, aff. kvip; the word corresponds to ah, alas, oh, ha, &c. [973]
