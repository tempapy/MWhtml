• ṣu [871] r. 2nd cl. (sauti) 1 To bear, to bring forth. 2 To possess power or supremacy. (ña) ṣuñ r. 5th cl. (sunoti sunute) 1 To bathe, preparatory to a sacrifice. 2 To distil, to extract a spirit. 3 To churn, to stir or agitate. 4 To pain.
    With abhi, 1 To sprinkle. 2 To bathe.
    With pra, To bear or bring forth.
