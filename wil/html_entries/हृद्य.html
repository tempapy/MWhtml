• hṛdya [978] mfn. (-dyaḥ-dyā-dyaṃ) 1 Dear, beloved, cherished, desired. 2 Grateful, pleasant, agreeable. 3 Produced in or from the heart. 4 Affectionate, kind. m. (-dyaḥ) A Mantra of the Vedas for effecting any one's subjection or fascination. f. (-dyā) Vṛddhi, a medicinal root, so named. n. (-dyaṃ) Cassia bark. E. hṛd the heart, yat aff.
