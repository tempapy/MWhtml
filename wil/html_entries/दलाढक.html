• dalāḍhaka [402] m. (-kaḥ) 1 Sesamum growing wild or spontaneously. 2 An aquatic plant, (Pistia stratiotes.) 3 A plant commonly Nagesara, (Mesua ferrea.) 4 A tree, (Mimosa sirisha.) 5 Red chalk. 6 Cuttle fish bone. 7 A moat, a ditch. 8 A sort of jasmine, (J. pubescens.) 9 An elephant's ear. 10 The driver of an elephant. 11 A hurricane, a high wind. E. dala as above, ḍhaukṛ to go, with āṅ prefixed, affix ac, deriv. irr.
