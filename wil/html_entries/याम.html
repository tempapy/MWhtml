• yāma mfn. (-maḥ-mī-maṃ) Relating to YAMA, done by him, ordered by him, &c. (-maḥ)
    1 The eighth part of a day, a watch of three hours.
    2 Forbearance.
    3 Cessation, term.
    E. yā to go, (through the day,) man Uṇādi aff., or yama to restrain, aff. ghañ or yama, and aṇ aff. [684]
