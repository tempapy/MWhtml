• ūha r. 1st cl. (ūhate) To reason, to apprehend and infer: with pra and vi, to intermit; with vi, to array in order of battle; with sam prefixed, (samūhati or -te) to assemble or be assembled.
  • ūha mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ)
    1 Reasoning.
    2 Completing a defective sentence or verse, supplying an ellipsis.
    E. ūha to deliberate, ghañ affix, or with lyuṭ affix, ūhana.
