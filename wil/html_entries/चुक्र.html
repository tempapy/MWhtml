• cukra [329] m. (-kraḥ) 1 Sorrel. 2 Sourness. n. (-kraṃ) 1 Acid seasoning. 2 Vinegar made by the acetous fermentation of grain. f. (-krā or krī) 1 Indian sorrel, (Rumex vesicarius.) 2 Hogplum. 3 The tamarind. E. caka to satisfy, Uṇādi affix rak, and the radical vowel changed to u; also with kan, cukraka.
