• mada r. 1st cl. (mādayate) (i, ir) madi r. 1st cl. (mandati), and (ī, ir) madī r. 4th cl. (mādyati)
    1 To be glad, to be satisfied or content, to rejoice.
    2 To be proud. (i) madi r. 1st cl. (mandati)
    1 To praise.
    2 To sleep, to doze, to be lazy or sluggish.
    3 To be stupid, to be an idiot or fool.
    4 To be elegant.
    5 To go or approach. mada r. 10th cl. (mādayate) To cause or justify satisfaction or contentment. madī r. 1st cl. causal form (mādayati)
    1 To delight, to make glad, to exhilarate.
    2 To madden or intoxicate, literally or figuratively.
    2 To be poor or distressed.
    4 To be proud.
    With ni prefixed, (nimādayati) To articulate. [636]
  • mada m. (-daḥ)

    6 Insanity, madness.
    7 Semen virile.
    8 Musk.
    9 A male river.
    10 Passion, desire. f. (-dī)
