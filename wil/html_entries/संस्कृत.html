• saṃskṛta [877] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Wrought, made, artificially produced. 2 Excellent, best. 3 Decorated, ornamented. 4 Cooked, dressed. 5 Cleaned, cleansed. 6 Purified. 7 Finished, perfect, completed. 8 Consecrated. 9 Initiated. m. (-taḥ) 1 A word regularly formed, a regular derivative. 2 A learned man. 3 A man of the three first classes who has received all the purificatory rites. n. (-taṃ) Language formed by perfect grammatical rules, the classical and sacred language of the Hindus. E. sam implying completion or perfection, kṛta made, and suṭ augment.
