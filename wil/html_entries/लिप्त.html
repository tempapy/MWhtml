• lipta mfn. (-ptaḥ-ptā-ptaṃ)
    1 Smeared, anointed, plastered, spread.
    2 Eaten.
    3 Envenomed, spread or touched with any poisonous substance.
    4 Embraced, united, connected with, &c.
    5 Defiled or contaminated by.
    6 Stained, soiled.
    E. lipa to smear, aff. kta, or in the last sense lī to cling to, Uṇādi aff. ta, with puṭ augment, and the radical vowel made short. [721]
