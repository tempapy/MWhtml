• ta The first consonant of the fourth or dental class, corresponding to the letter T, in tongue.
  • ta m. (taḥ)
    1 A thief.
    2 Nectar, the food of the immortals.
    3 An outcaste, a barbarian or Mleccha.
    4 The flank, the hip or haunch.
    5 A tail.
    6 The womb.
    7 A wicked man.
    8 A tree.
    9 A jewel.
    10 A Buddha. n. (taṃ)
    1 Passing, crossing.
    2 Virtue, sanctity.
    E. tṛ to cross, &c. affix ḍa.
