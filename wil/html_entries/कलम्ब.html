• kalamba [200] m. (-mbaḥ) 1 An arrow. 2 The stalk of a potherb. 3 The Kadamba, (Nauclea cadamba:) see kadamba. n. (-mbaṃ) Calumba root, (Menispermum calumba.) f. (-mbī) or m. and f. (-mbaḥ-mbī) A kind of potherb, (Convolvulus repens, &c.) see kaḍambī. E. kaḍa to delight, ambac affix, la substituted for ḍa, fem. affix ṅīṣ; also kalambaka.
