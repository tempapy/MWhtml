• lāṭa mfn. (-ṭaḥ-ṭā-ṭaṃ)
    1 Old or worn, spoiled, shabby, (as clothes, ornaments, &c.)
    2 Childish. m. (-ṭaḥ)
    1 Cloth, clothes.
    2 Fault, defect.
    3 Idle or inebriate language.
    4 Repetition of words in the same sense, but a different application.
    5 The name of a country, the upper part of the Dekhin, Lār or Larice.
    E. laṭa to be childish, aff. ghañ, or lāṭa Kaṇḍvādi root, to live, aff. ac, or laṭa childish, aṇ aff. [719]
