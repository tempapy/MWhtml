• vyāghāta m. (-taḥ)
    1 Obstacle, impediment.
    2 Striking, beating.
    3 Destroying, destruction.
    4 The thirteenth of the astronomical Yogas.
    5 A tree, (Cassia fistula.)
    6 A rhetorical figure, the production of two different effects from a similar cause or by similar agency.
    E. vi and āṅ before hana to strike, aff. ghañ. [818]
