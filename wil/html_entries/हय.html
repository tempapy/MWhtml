• haya [968] r. 1st cl. (hayati) 1 To move. 2 To worship. 3 To sound. 4 To be weary.
  • haya [968] m. (-yaḥ) 1 A horse. 2 A man of a particular class. 3 The number seven. 4 INDRA. 5 The Yak. 6 (In prosody,) A foot of four short syllables, a proceleusmaticus. f. (-yī) A mare. E. haya to go, or hi to go, aff. ac.
