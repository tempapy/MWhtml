• cihna Sautra root. 10th cl. (cihnayati) To mark, to spot, to stamp.
  • cihna n. (-hnaṃ)
    1 A mark of any kind, a spot, stain, sign, symbol, &c.
    2 A banner, a standard.
    3 A symptom.
    E. caha to pound, affix nak, and the radical vowel changed to i, or cihna to mark, ka aff.
