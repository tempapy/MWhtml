• cola [332] mf. (-laḥ-lī) A short jacket, a bodice. m. (-laḥ) or m. plu. (-lāḥ) A country, the modern Tanjore, the name is also supposed to apply to a part of Birbhum, in Bengal. E. cula to be high or to elevate, affix aca, and la being changed to ḍa, coḍa ut sup. or cola a proper name, that of the king of the country applied to the country.
