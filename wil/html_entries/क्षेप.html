• kṣepa m. (-paḥ)
    1 Sending, dismissing.
    2 Throwing, casting.
    3 Pride, haughtiness.
    4 Delay, dilatoriness.
    5 Disrespect, contempt.
    6 Passing away time.
    7 Abuse, reviling.
    8 A clump of flowers, &c.
    9 (In arithmetic, &c.) Additive quantity, addendum.
    E. kṣipa to throw, affix ghañ. [267]
