• cūrṇi [331] m. (-rṇiḥ) A sum of 100 Cowries, the small shells used as coin. 2 A selection or picking out of an unanswerable argument. 2 The grammar of PATAÑJALA, a comment on PĀṆINI'S grammar. E. cara to go, to pass, (current,) ni Uṇādi affix, and ū substituted for the radical vowel, or cūrṇa to direct, &c. affix ki.
