• karṇin mfn. (-rṇī-rṇinī-rṇi)
    1 Having an ear, relating to the ear, &c.
    2 Long eared.
    3 Barbed, (as an arrow.)
    4 Having a helm. m. (-rṇī)
    1 The name of a mountain, one of the seven principal ranges of the mountains, dividing the universe.
    2 The side of the neck, the part near the ear.
    3 A steersman. f. (-ṇinī) A disease of the uterus, prolapsus or polypus uteri.
    E. karṇa, and ini aff.
