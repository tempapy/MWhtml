• vṛtti f. (-ttiḥ)
    1 Livelihood, profession, means of acquiring subsistence.
    2 Style or character of dramatic representation, or composition, considered to be of four sorts, or Kauśikī, which exhibits love or passion; Bhāratī, which appears to implies declamation especially; Sātvatī, of which the subject is virtue and heroism; and Ārabhaṭī, which treats of magic, delusion, wrath, and battle.
    3 Style in general.
    4 Gloss, comment, explanation, exposition.
    5 Being, abiding, staying.
    6 Seizing, stopping, withholding, restraining.
    7 Circumference of a circle.
    E. vṛta to be, aff. ktin. [801]
