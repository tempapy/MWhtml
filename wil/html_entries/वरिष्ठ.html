• variṣṭha mfn. (-ṣṭhaḥ-ṣṭhā-ṣṭhaṃ)
    1 Largest, greatest, most large.
    2 Heaviest, mightiest.
    3 Best, dearest, most preferable or beloved. n. (-ṣṭhaṃ)
    1 Copper.
    2 Pepper. m. (-ṣṭhaḥ) The Francoline partridge.
    E. var substituted for uru large, or varu best, and iṣṭhan. affix of the irr. superlative.
