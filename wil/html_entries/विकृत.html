• vikṛta mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) Entertaining the feeling of disgust, averse, estranged.
    2 Sick, diseased.
    3 Altered, changed, either in form or feeling.
    4 Imperfect, unfinished, incomplete.
    5 Overcome by feeling or passion. n. (-taṃ) Disgust, aversion.
    E. vi implying reverse, and kṛta made. [759]
