• kathaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 A narrator, a relator, one who recites a story, or who publicly reads and expounds the Purāṇas, &c.
    2 One who speaks or tells. mf. (-kaḥ-kā) The speaker of a prologue or a monologue.
    2 A professional story teller.
    E. katha to speak, to relate, vun aff.
