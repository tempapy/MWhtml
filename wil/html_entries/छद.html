• chada r. 1st and 10th cls. (chadati-te and chadayati, or more commonly chādayati.)
    1 To cover, to clothe, to veil, to hide, or remove from view: (āṅ is usually prefixed to the root in these senses.)
    2 To march. (i) chadi r. 1st cl. (chaṃdati) To live or breathe, to be or make strong; also r. 1st and 10th cls. (chandati chaṃdayati) To cover, &c. as above. [333]
  • chada m. (-daḥ)
    1 A leaf.
    2 A wing.
    3 A plant: see granthiparṇī.
    4 A tree bearing dark blossoms: see tamāla.
    E. chada to cover, &c. aff. gha. [333]
