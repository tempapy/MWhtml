• samuddhata mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ)
    1 Ill-mannered, behaving ill or improperly, conducting one's self discreditably.
    2 Raised or drawn up.
    3 Upheld, supported.
    4 Arrogant, proud, puffed up with pride, &c.
    E. sam and uṭ before hana to hurt or kill, aff. kta.
