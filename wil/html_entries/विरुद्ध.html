• viruddha [781] mfn. (-ddhaḥ-ddhā-ddhaṃ) 1 Opposed, hindered. 2 Reverse, contrary, opposite. 3 Inconsistent, (in argument,) incongruous, a nonsequitur. 4 Opposed in quality, as sweet with sour, &c. 5 Disagreeing with each other, (as articles of medicine or food.) 6 Hostile, adverse. 7 Excluded, disqualified. 8 Surrounded, blockaded. E. vi before rudha to stop, aff. kta.
