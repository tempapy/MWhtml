• pīṭha mfn. (-ṭhaḥ-ṭhī-ṭhaṃ)
    1 A stool, a seat, a chair.
    2 The seat of the religious student, made properly of Kuśa grass.
    3 A place where some limb of PĀRVATĪ is supposed to have fallen, as after Daksha's sacrifice, her dead body was carried about by ŚIVA, and cut in pieces by the discus of VIṢṆU: there are fifty-one such places.
    4 Complement of a segment, (in Geometry.)
    E. piṭha to feel pain, to be weary, aff. ghañ; deriv. irr.
