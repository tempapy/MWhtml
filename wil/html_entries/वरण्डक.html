• varaṇḍaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 Fearful, terrified.
    2 Large, great.
    3 Miserly, a miser. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A howdah, a chair or canopy on the back of an elephant.
    2 A wall.
    3 Eruption or pimples on the face.
    4 A ball, a globe.
    E. varaṇḍa as above, and kan added.
