• kuṭaja [226] m. (-jaḥ) A medicinal plant, commonly Coraya, (Echites antidysenterica, Rox.) the seeds are used as a vermifuge: see indrayava. 2 A name of the saint AGASTYA, 5 Also of DROṆA a sage and warrior: see droṇa. E. kūṭa a mountain peak, and ja what is produced; the vowel of kūṭa being made short.
