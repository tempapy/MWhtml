• kṣema mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Happy, well, prosperous, right, &c. mn. (-bhaḥ-maṃ)
    1 Happiness, well being.
    2 Preserving, protecting, keeping what is acquired.
    3 Final emancipation or eternal happiness.
    4 The proper term of civil address to a Vaiśya, as kṣemamasti, kṣemaṃ bhavatu, Is it well, or may it be well, &c. f. (-mā) A name of DURGĀ.
    E. kṣi to remove, and man Uṇādi affix; what removes or destroys sorrow, &c. [267]
