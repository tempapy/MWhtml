• utthāna n. (-naṃ)
    1 Effort, exertion.
    2 Manhood, manly exertion.
    3 An army.
    4 War, battle.
    5 A book.
    6 Joy, pleasure.
    7 Rising, getting up.
    8 A court yard.
    9 A shed where sacrifices are made.
    10 A term, a limit.
    11 Evacuating by stool, &c.
    12 Business of a family or realm, as the care of subjects or dependants.
    13 Reflexion.
    14 Proximate cause of disease.
    E. ut above, sthā to stand, and lyuṭ affix, sa is dropped.
