• prāmāṇika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Exercising or proceeding from some evidence or authority, &c.
    2 Being of authority. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 A president, the chief or head of a trade, &c.
    2 A learned man, one who supports his arguments by reference to books, &c.
    E. pramāṇa testimony, authority, aff. ṭhañ.
