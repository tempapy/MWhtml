• niṣadha m. (-dhaḥ)
    1 The name of a mountain or mountainous range, forming one of the principal ranges of the universe, and described as lying immediately south of Hāvrata, and north of the Himāla range.
    2 A country in the south-east division of India.
    3 The sovereign of Niṣadha.
    4 A musical note; also niṣādra.
    5 Hard, solid.
    E. ni before ṣada to go, aff. ac and da irregularly changed to dha.
