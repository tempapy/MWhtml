• kuṣṭha [236] n. (-ṣṭhaṃ) 1 Leprosy, of which eighteen varieties are enumerated, seven great or severe, and eleven of minor importance. 2 A plant, a kind of costus, (Costus speciosus:) 3 A sort of poison. E. kuṣa to extract, and kthan Uṇādi affix, or ku bad, ill, and stha being, staying.
