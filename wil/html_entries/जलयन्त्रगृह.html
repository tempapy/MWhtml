• jalayantragṛha n. (-haṃ) A house or fabric erected in the midst of water, or subterraneous apartments constructed in the bank of a river, for retiring to in the hot season; a summer house, &c.
    E. jalayantra a waterwork, and gṛha a house; also similar compounds, as jalayattraniketana, jalayantramandiraṃ, &c. [344]
