• parīkṣā f. (-kṣā)
    1 Discrimination, investigation, examination, test, trial, experiment.
    2 Trial by ordeal of various kinds: see divya.
    E. pari intensitive prefix, before īkṣa to see, aff. aṅ and ṭāp; looking into any thing or person closely and minutely. [514]
