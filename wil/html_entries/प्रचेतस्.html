• pracetas mfn. (-tāḥ-tāḥ-taḥ) Happy, delighted. m. (-tāḥ)
    1 VARUṆA, the regent of water.
    2 The name of a saint and lawgiver. m. plu. (-saḥ) The ten sons of PRĀCĪNAVARHIS by the daughter of VARUṆA, and the progenitors of DAKṢA.
    E. pra best, first, cetas the heart. [560]
