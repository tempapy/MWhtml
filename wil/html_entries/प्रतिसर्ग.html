• pratisarga [567] m. (-rgaḥ) 1 The portion of a Purāṇa, which treats of the destruction and renovation of the world. 2 Secondary creation, or the creation of the world by the agency of BRAHMĀ, and other divine beings regarded as the agents of one Supreme Being. E. prati severally, sarga going.
