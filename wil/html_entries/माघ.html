• māgha m. (-ghaḥ)
    1 One of the months of the Hindu year, (JanuaryFebruary.)
    2 A poet, the author of the ŚIŚUPĀLA Badha, or as it is named after him the Māgha Kāvya, one of the great profane poems of Hindu literature. f. (-ghī) A potherb, (Hingtsha repens.)
    E. maghā the asterism, in which the moon is full this month, and aṇ aff. [654]
