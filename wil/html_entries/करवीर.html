• karavīra m. (-raḥ)
    1 A fragrant plant, (Oleander or Nerium odorum.)
    2 The name of a demon.
    3 A sword or scymitar.
    4 A cemetery, a place for burning or interring the dead. f. (-rā) Red arsenic. (-rī)
    1 A name of ADITI, the mother of the gods.
    2 A good cow.
    3 A woman who has borne a son, a mother.
    E. kara here said to mean a root, vīra to become evident, and ac affix, or kara hand, vi prefixed to īra to go, &c. affix ka.
