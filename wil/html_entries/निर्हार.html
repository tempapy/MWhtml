• nirhāra [478] m. (-raḥ) 1 Extracting, drawing out, rooting up, &c. 2 Putting forth or out. 3 Evacuation of any of the natural excrements of the fæces, urine, sweat, &c. 4 Burning, combustion. 5 Giving away, selling, exchanging, &c. 6 Diffusive fragrance. 7 Accumulation of wealth, a hoard, a private purse or hoard. E. nir out, hṛ to take, affix ghañ.
