    1 Death, dying.
    2 Abode, abiding, being or being stationary.
    3 Abiding, being or residing with.
    4 Duration, continuance.
    5 State or condition, (of life.)
    6 Contiguity, connexion.
    7 Heap, accumulation.
    8 Restraint.
