• mañca(u,)mañcu r. 1st cl. (mañcati) To go. [632]
  • mañca m. (-ñcaḥ)
    1 A bed, a bedstead.
    2 A platform, a scaffold.
    3 An elevated shed raised on bambus in a cornfield, &c., where a watchman is stationed to protect the corn from cattle, birds, wild beasts, &c.
    4 A sort of throne or chair of state, or the platform on which it is raised, the dais.
    E. maci to be high or tall, aff. ac. [632]
