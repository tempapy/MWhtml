• netra [487] mfn. (-traḥ-trī-traṃ) A leader, a guide, one who guides or leads. n. (-traṃ) 1 The eye. 2 The root of a tree. 3 Bleached or wove silk. 4 A car, a carriage. 5 The string of a churning rope. 6 An enema pipe. mn. (-traḥ-traṃ) Any tubular vessel. f. (-trī) 1 A river. 2 The goddess LAKṢMĪ. E. ṇī to guide or gain, Uṇādi aff. ṣṭran, fem. aff. ṅīṣ.
