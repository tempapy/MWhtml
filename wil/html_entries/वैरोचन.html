• vairocana m. (-naḥ)
    1 The third of the Buddhas, called Dhyāni Buddhas.
    2 BALI, the sovereign so named.
    3 The son of AGNI.
    4 The son of SŪRYA, or the sun.
    5 A Siddha, a sort of demi-god or deified mortal.
    E. virocana the sun, &c., and aff. of descent aṇ.
