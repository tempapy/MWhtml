• atasa m. (-saḥ)
    1 Wind, air.
    2 The self, the soul.
    3 Cloth made from bark.
    4 A weapon. f. (-sī)
    1 Common flax, (Linum usitatissimum.)
    2 Śaṇa, Bengal sun, a kind of flax, (Crotolaria juncea.)
    E. ata to go, and the Uṇādi affix asaca, when fem. with ṅīṣ added.
