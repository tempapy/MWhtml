• vimala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ)
    1 Clean, pure, (either literally, as clothes, &c., or figuratively, as the heart or mind.)
    2 Beautiful.
    3 White.
    4 Transparent. f. (-lā)
    1 A plant, commonly Carmaghās.
    2 A sort of soil. m. (-laḥ) An Arhat or Jaina deified sage. n. (-laṃ) Silver gilt.
    E. vi privative, mala dirt. [778]
