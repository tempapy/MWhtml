• udvaha [150] mf. (-haḥ-hā) A son or a daughter. m. (-haḥ) 1 The vital air that conveys nourishment, &c. upwards. 2 A chief, a leader, the head of a family or force. mfn. (-haḥ-hā-haṃ) 1 Who or what takes up or away. 2 Best, most eminent. E. ud further, vaha what bears; carrying on one's name, &c.
