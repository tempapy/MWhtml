• bhāvana nf. (-naṃ-nā)
    1 Mental perception, recollection, the present consciousness of past ideas or perceptions.
    2 Imagination.
    3 Religious and abstract meditation.
    4 Looking about, (literally or figuratively,) observing, investigating.
    5 Causing to be.
    6 Decorating any person or object with flowers, perfume, &c., scenting, anointing.
    7 Steeping, infusion, especially repeatedly drying the article by day and keeping it moist at night.
    8 (In arithmetic,) Composition.
    9 (In law,) Ascertainment, proof.
    E. bhū to be, in the causal form, to bring present or into being, aff. yuc. [618]
