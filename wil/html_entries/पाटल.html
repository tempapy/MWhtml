• pāṭala mfn. (-laḥ-lā-laṃ) Of a pink or pale red colour. m. (-laḥ)
    1 Pale red, rose colour.
    2 Rice ripening in the rains. f. (-lā)
    1 The Trumpet flower, (Bignonia suave-olens.)
    2 Red Lodh.
    3 A name of DURGĀ. nf. (-laṃ-lā) The flower of the Bignonia suave-olens.
    E. pāṭa extension, and lā to get, aff. ḍa; also pāṭali. [522]
