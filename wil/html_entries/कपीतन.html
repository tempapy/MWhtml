• kapītana [189] m. (-naḥ) 1 A tree bearing an acid fruit, (Spondias mangifera:) see āmrātaka. 2 Another tree, (Hibiscus populneoides:) see garddabhāṇḍa. 3 A species of Mimosa, (Mimosa sirisha, Rox.) 4 The holy fig tree, (Ficus religiosa.) 5 The betel nut tree, (Areca faufel or Catechu.) 6 The name of another plant, (Crataeva marmelos.) E. kapi an ape, and tana what spreads; sheltering or feeding monkies; the vowel is made long irregularly.
