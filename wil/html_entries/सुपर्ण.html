• suparṇa m. (-rṇaḥ)
    1 GARUḌA, the bird and vehicle of VIṢṆU.
    2 A tree, (Cassia fistula.)
    3 A cock.
    4 A being of a superhuman character, or GARUḌA, and other birds of an equally fanciful description; one of those classes first created by the BRAHMĀDIKAS, and included in the daily presentation of water to deceased ancestors, &c. f. (-rṇā or rṇī)
    1 The mother of GARUḌA.
    2 A number of lotuses, or a pool abounding with them.
    E. su handsome, and parṇa a leaf or feather.
