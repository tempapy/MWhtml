• nāla mfn. (-laḥ-lā or lī-laṃ)
    1 A hollow or tubular stalk, the stalk of the water lily, &c.
    2 Any pipe or tube. n. (-laṃ) Yellow orpiment. f. (-lī)
    1 A stalk or culm.
    2 An instrument for perforating an elephant's ear.
    3 A period of time, a Ghari, twentyfour minutes.
    4 Any tubular vessel of the body.
    5 The pulse.
    E. ṇala to bind, affix ṇa, fem. affix ṭāp, and ṅīṣ, &c. [462]
