• mukunda m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 A name of VIṢṆU.
    2 Gum olibanum.
    3 A precious gem.
    4 Quicksilver.
    5 One of KUVERA'S Nidhis, or divine treasures.
    E. muku liberation, (from passion, &c.) in the second case mukuṃ, and ṭā to give, aff. ka, or muca to liberate, or muku as before, unda to wet, to sprinkle, aff. ac, deriv. irr.
