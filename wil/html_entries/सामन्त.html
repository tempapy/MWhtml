• sāmanta [919] mfn. (-ntaḥ-ntī-ntaṃ) 1 Limitative, boundary, bounding. 2 Bordering, neighbouring. m. (-ntaḥ) 1 The chief of a district. 2 A neighbour. 3 An attendant or companion of a chieftain, a leader, a captain, a champion. n. (-ntaṃ) Neighbourhood. E. samanta end, term, aṇ aff. of relation.
