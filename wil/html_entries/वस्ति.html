• vasti [744] mf. (-stiḥ) 1 The lower belly, hypogastric or pubic region. 2 The bladder. 3 A glyster, syringe, or bag made of bladder or gut, with a wooden or metallic nozzle. 4 Abiding, dwelling, staying. plur. only (-stayaḥ) The ends of a cloth. E. vasa to wear as clothes, Uṇādi aff. ti, or vasa to abide, ktin aff.
