• mauli mf. (-liḥ-lī or -lī)
    1 A lock of hair on the crown of the head.
    2 Hair ornamented and braided round the head.
    3 A diadem, a Tiara.
    4 The head. f. (-lī) The earth.
    E. mūla the root or base, aff. iñ or mūla a name, and iñ aff. of descent, or mū to bind, li aff., and the radical vowel changed. [676]
