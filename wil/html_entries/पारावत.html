• pārāvata m. (-taḥ)
    1 A dove, a pigeon.
    2 A monkey.
    3 A sort of ebony, (Diospyros glutinosa.)
    4 A mountain. f. (-tī)
    1 The name of a river in the peninsula.
    2 The fruit of the Annona reticulata.
    3 A form of song, peculiar to the cowherds.
    E. para another, (life, &c.) ava to go, to preserve, śatṛ participial aff., and aṇ added. [529]
