• rucaka [707] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) 1 Agreeable, pleasing. 2 Sharp, acrid. 3 Tonic, stomachic. m. (-kaḥ) 1 The citron, (Citrus medica.) 2 An ornament of the neck or breast. 3 A tooth. 4 A pigeon. n. (-kaṃ) 1 Salt. 2 Natron, alkali. 3 Borax. 4 A garland, a chaplet. 5 A curl on a horse's neck. 6 The woody Cassia. 7 Any auspicious or fortunate object. 8 A perfume, commonly Rocanā. 9 An anthelmintic medicine, commonly Virenga, (Embelia ribes?) 10 A sort of temple. 11 A stomachic. E. ruca to shine, &c. in the causal form, kvun aff.; the embellisher, the polisher, &c.
