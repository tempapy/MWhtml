• rāmāyaṇa n. (-ṇaṃ)
    1 The first epic poem of the Hindus, recording the adventures of RĀMA the son of DAŚARATHA, sovereign of Oude; written by the poet VĀLMIKI.
    2 A name of several poems on the life and adventures of RĀMA.
    E. rāma the prince so named, and ayana abode.
