• dārvvī f. (-rvvī)
    1 A sort of Curcuma, (C. zanthorrhizon.)
    2 A kind of pine: see devadāru.
    3 Turmerick.
    4 Gojihwa, a potherb.
    5 A collyrium extracted from an infusion of the Curcuma zanthorrhizon.
    E. dṝ to tear or dissipate (disease), ñuṇ affix, fem. affix ṅīṣ: see dāru. [406]
