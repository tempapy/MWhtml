• sthāpana [952] n. (-naṃ) 1 Placing, founding, fixing, erecting. 2 Ordering, directing. 3 Fixing or concentrating the thoughts upon the object of meditation, abstraction, mental controul. 4 A ceremony performed in the month of utero-gestation. 5 A dwelling, a habitation. f. (-nā) Ordering or arranging as a drama, stage management. f. (-nī) A plant, (Cissampelos hexandra.) E. ṣṭhā to stay or stand, causal form, aff. lyuṭ or yuc.
