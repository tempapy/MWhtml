• vana [732] r. 1st and 10th cls. (vanati vanayati) 1 To sound. 2 To serve, to honour. 3 To help. 4 To be distressed. (u) vanu r. 1st and 10th cls. (vanati vanayati) 1 To act, to transact business. 2 To injure; also r. 8th cl. (vanute) To ask or beg.
  • vana [732] nf. (-naṃ-nī) A forest, a wood, a grove. n. (-naṃ) 1 Water. 2 A residence, a dwelling or abode. 3 A house. 4 A cascade or fountain. E. vana to sound, to serve, to seek, &c., aff. ghañ.
