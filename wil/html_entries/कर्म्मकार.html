• karmmakāra mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 A worker, one who does any business, an artisan, a mechanic.
    2 One who performs any act. m. (-raḥ) A blacksmith, one of the progeny of the divine artist VIŚVAKARMA.
    2 A bull.
    E. karmma an act, kṛñ to perform, affix aṇ. [197]
