• guḍa [290] (śi) r. 6th cl. (guḍati) To defend, to guard or preserve. (i) guḍi r. 1st and 10th cls. (guṇḍati guṇḍayati) 1 To surround, to enclose. 2 To pound, to grind, to reduce to powder. 3 (According to some authorities,) To preserve.
  • guḍa [290] mfn. (-ḍaḥ-ḍā-ḍaṃ) Round, globular. m. (-ḍaḥ) 1 A globe or ball. 2 Treacle, molasses, the first thickening of the juice of the cane by boiling. 3 An elephant's trappings or armour. 4 A mouthful. f. (-ḍā) 1 A plant, (Euphorbia tirucalli:) see snuhī. 2 Powder, pounded substance. E. guṅ to sound, affix ḍa.
