• yā r. 2nd cl. (yāti)
    1 To go, to go to.
    2 To obtain, to get.
    With anu prefixed, To follow.
    With abhi, To approach, to reach.
    With āṅ, To come, to arrive.
    With upa, To give way, to yield.
    With nir,
    1 To go out or forth.
    2 To go readily or quickly.
    With pra, To go to.
    With prati, To go to or towards.
    With prati and ut, To meet, to advance to.
    With sama and abhi, To approach.
    With sama and āṅ, To arrive. [683]
