• dīpaka [411] mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ) 1 Making luminous or beautiful. 2 Kindling, inflaming. 3 Exciting, rendering intense, (a feeling, &c.) 4 Tonic, stimulant. m. (-kaḥ) 1 An aromatick seed, (Ligusticum ajväen, Rox.) 2 Small cumin. 3 Celosia cristata. 4 Saffron. 5 A lamp. 6 A falcon. 7 A name of KĀMA. 8 One of the Rāgas or modes of music. f. (-pikā) 1 One of the Rāgiṇīs, or female personifications of the musical modes. 2 A title of various books, (the illustrator, the illuminator.) 3 Moonlight. n. (-kaṃ) A figure of rhetoric, dilating upon an idea, or accumulation of expressions tending to one object. E. dīpa to shine, in the causal form, affix ṇvul.
