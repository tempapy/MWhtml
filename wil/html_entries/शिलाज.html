• śilāja mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ) Produced in a mountain or rock, fossile, mineral. n. (-jaṃ)
    1 Storax or Benzoin, the resins of the Styrax benzoinum or S. officinale, which are usually confounded by the Hindus.
    2 Iron.
    E. śilā a rock, and ja born; the balsams being supposed to be fossile productions, and to exude from the soil of mountainous places; being confounded with bitumen or petroleum. [845]
