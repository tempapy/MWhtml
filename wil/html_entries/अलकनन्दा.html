• alakanandā f. (-ndā)
    1 A young girl from eight to ten years old.
    2 A river, the Alakanandā, a stream that runs from the Himālaya mountains, and falls into the Ganges near Srīnagar. It is also a name of the terrestrial Ganges.
    E. alakā, and nandā who delights, the final of the first dropped.
