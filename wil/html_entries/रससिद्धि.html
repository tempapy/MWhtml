• rasasiddhi f. (-ddhiḥ) The knowledge of alchymy, the possession of peculiar familiarity with mercury, obtained by the performance of chemical operations conjoined with certain mystical and magical rites, and the securing thence to the adept, of happiness, health, and wealth, the power of transmuting metals, and the art of prolonging life.
    E. rasa mercury, and siddhi accomplishment, especially of some supernatural object.
