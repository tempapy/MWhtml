• daṇḍadhara mfn. (-raḥ-rā-raṃ)
    1 Having a stick or staff.
    2 Having authority to punish. m. (-raḥ)
    1 A name of YAMA.
    2 A king.
    3 A potter.
    4 A mendicant carrying a staff.
    E. daṇḍa punishment, a stick, &c. and dhara who holds or possesses; also daṇḍadhāra m. (-raḥ) daṇḍadhārin, &c.
