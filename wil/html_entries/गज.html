• gaja r. 1st cl. (gajati) To be drunk, to be confused or inebriated: r. 1st and 10th cls. (gajati gajayati) also (i) gaji r. 1st cl. (gañjati) To sound. [276]
  • gaja m. (-jaḥ)
    1 An elephant.
    2 A measure of length, the Gaz, a yard, a measure of two cubits.
    3 A mound of earth sloping on both sides, on which a house may be erected.
    4 A small hole in the ground for a fire, over which to prepare medicines.
    E. gaja to sound, to roar, affix ac [276]
