• kasa r. 1st cl. (kasati) To move, to go or approach: with vi prefixed, to open or expand as a flower, to blow. (i) kasi r. 2nd cl. (kaṃste)
    1 To go.
    2 To command.
    3 To destroy: this root is also read by some authorities, kasa (kaste) or kaśi (kaṃṣṭe.)
  • kasa m. (-saḥ) A touchstone: see kaṣa. f. (-sā) A whip: see kaśā and kaṣā.
