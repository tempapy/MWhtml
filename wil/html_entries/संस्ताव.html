• saṃstāva m. (-vaḥ)
    1 The place occupied at a sacrifice by the Brahmans reciting hymns and prayers.
    2 Celebrating or hymning in chorus, the repetition of the verses of the Vedas by a number of Brahmans.
    E. sam together, (in chorus,) ṣṭu to praise, (the gods,) ghañ aff.
