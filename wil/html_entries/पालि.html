• pāli [531] f. (-liḥ) 1 The sharp edge of a sword, or any cutting instrument. 2 The tip of the ear. 3 A line, a row or range. 4 A mark, a spot, a stain. 5 A louse. 6 A woman with a beard. 7 A boundary, a limit. 8 A raised bank, a causeway, a bridge. 9 A circumference. 10 Maintenance of a scholar during the period of his studies, by his teacher. 11 Praise, eulogium. 12 The hip or haunch. 13 A Prastha or measure so called. E. pāla to nourish, i aff., and in most cases, ṅīṣ being optionally added, also pālī: see pāla.
