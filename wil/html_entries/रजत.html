• rajata mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) White. n. (-taṃ)
    1 A necklace.
    2 Silver.
    3 Gold.
    4 Elephant's teeth, ivory.
    5 The name of a lake.
    6 The name of a mountain, perhaps Kailāsa: see rajatādri.
    7 White, (the colour.)
    8 Blood.
    9 An asterism, a constellation.
    E. rañja to colour, &c., atac Uṇādi aff.
