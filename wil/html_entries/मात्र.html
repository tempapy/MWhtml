• mātra [656] n. (-traṃ) 1 The whole, the entire thing or class of things. 2 (adv.) Only, solely (exclusive and identical, the very thing.) 3 The primitive subtle or invisible type of visible elementary matter. 4 A pleonastic addition to words. f. (-trā) 1 Requisite, material. 2 Quantity, measure. 3 A little. 4 An ear-ring. 5 Wealth, substance. 6 A short vowel. 7 A moment. 8 Quantity in metre or prosody, a syllabic foot. 9 The upper or horizontal limb of the Nāgarī characters. E. mā to measure, Uṇādi aff. can, fem. aff. ṭāp.
