• sṛ [942] r. 1st cl. (sarati) r. 3d cl. (sasartti) To go. 1st cl. (sarati or dhāvati) To go fast, to run, (dhai being substituted for the root.)
    With anu prefixed, 1 To follow. 2 To conform to.
    With apa, To go back, to withdraw or remove.
    With abhi, 1 To spread out or abroad. 2 To go to or with, to accompany or attend, or to meet upon appointment.
    With upa, To approach, to advance;
    With nir, To go forth or out.
    With pra, 1 To proceed. 2 To extend. 3 To project.
    With vi, 1 To go separately or apart. 2 To come or arrive. 3 To forego, to quit or leave.
