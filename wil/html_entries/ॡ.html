• ḹ [170] The long vowel corresponding to the preceding short one, Ḹ of Ḷ.
  • ḹ [170] aptote. 1 A divine female. 2 The female nature. 3 A mother. m. (-ḹḥ) A name of ŚIVA. f. (-ḹḥ) 1 The mother of the demons. 2 The mother of the all-bestowing cow. ind. A mystical letter used once or repeatedly in many Mantras or mystical formulae. The letter ṝ and ḷ are employed in the same manner: they have apparently no signification, but are personified and invested with marvellous properties in the Tantras.
