• yojana n. (-naṃ)
    1 GOD, the SUPREME BEIṄG, the soul of the world.
    2 A measure of distance equal to four Krośas, which at 8000 cubits or 4000 yards to the Krośa or Kos, will be exactly nine miles; other computations make the Yojana but about five miles, or even no more than four miles and a half.
    2 Joining, union, junction.
    E. yuja to join, aff. lyuṭ. [689]
