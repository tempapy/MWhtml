• vāja [748] mn. (-jaḥ-jaṃ) A wing. m. (-jaḥ) 1 The feather of an arrow. 2 Speed. 3 Sound. 4 A Muni. n. (-jaṃ) 1 Ghee or clarified butter. 2 Water. 3 A Mantra or prayer which concludes a sacrifice. 4 Rice offered at a Śrāddha or obsequial ceremony. 5 Rice in general. 5 The acidulous mixture of ground meal and water left to ferment. E. vaja to go, aff. ghañ.
