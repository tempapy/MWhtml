• antyaja mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ) Younger, latest born. m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A Śūdra or man of the fourth tribe.
    2 A man of one of seven inferior tribes; a washerman, currier, mimic, Varūra, fisherman, Meda or attendant on women, and mountaineer or forester.
    E. antya inferior, and ja who is born: born from the feet of BRAHMĀ; also antyajāta.
