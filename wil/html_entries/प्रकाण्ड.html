• prakāṇḍa mn. (-ṇḍaḥ-ṇḍaṃ)
    1 The stem of a tree, the part between the root and the branches.
    2 A branch, a shoot.
    3 Excellence, happiness, (or used as an attributive without admitting variation of gender,) excellent, best, happy.
    E. pra implying excess or superiority, kama to desire, aff. ḍa, and the penultimate vowel made long by special rule. [556]
