• vipratipanna mfn. (-nnaḥ-nnā-nnaṃ)
    1 Bewildered, perplexed, confused, uncertain.
    2 Conversant or acquainted with in various ways, severally or mutually.
    3 Connected with.
    4 Contested, disputed.
    5 Contradicted.
    6 Opposed, (as interests.)
    E. vi, and pratipanna ascertained, &c.
