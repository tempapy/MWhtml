• padmāsana [501] n. (-naṃ) A posture in religious meditation, sitting with the thighs crossed, one hand resting on the left thigh, the other held up with the thumb upon the heart; the eyes directed to the tip of the nose. 2 A seat or throne made in the shape of a lotus, one especially on which idols are placed. m. (-naḥ) 1 A name of BRAHMĀ. 2 The sun. E. padma a lotus, and āsana sitting.
