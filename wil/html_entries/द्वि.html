• dvi dual only. mf. (-dvau) n. (-dve) Two. m. (-dviḥ) Two, (of times, &c.) it is only used in composition, as atidvi a thing more than two, and inflected like other nouns in iḥ in composition with other numerals, it is changed to dvā, as dvādaśa twelve, dvāviṃśati twenty-two, dvātriṃśat thirty-two, &c.
