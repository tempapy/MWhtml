• parigha m. (-ghaḥ)
    1 A bludgeon, a stick mounted with iron, or an iron club.
    2 Killing, striking, destroying.
    3 The nineteenth of the astronomical Yogas.
    4 The pin or bolt of a door.
    5 A glass vessel.
    6 A pike, a spear.
    7 A water jar.
    8 The outer door or gate of a palace: see paligha.
    E. pari round about, hana to kill, ap aff., gha substituted for the root.
