• upaniṣad [155] f. (-ṣat or -ṣat) 1 A portion of the religious writings of the Hindus; the theological part, and the Vedānta or argumentative part of the Vedas, either detached from or comprised in the principal work. 2 Virtue, moral merit. 3 Truth as the principle of divine being. 4 A neighbouring mansion. 5 A lonely place. E. upa and ni prefixed to ṣada to go, affix kvip; in which abide the essential parts of religion, &c.
