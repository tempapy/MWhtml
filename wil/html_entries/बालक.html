• bālaka [603] m. (-kaḥ) 1 A boy, an infant: see the last. 2 A fool, a blockhead. 3 The tail of a horse or elephant. 4 A finger ring. 5 A bracelet. 6 A perfume; also drīvera. f. (-likā) 1 A female infant. 2 Sand. 3 The knot or flower of an ear-ring. 4 The rustling of leaves. 5 Small cardamoms. n. (-kaṃ) A sort of Hibiscus, (H. mutabilis.) E. kan added to the preceding.
