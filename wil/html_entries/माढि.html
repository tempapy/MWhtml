• māḍhi [655] f. (-ḍhiḥ) 1 The germ of leaves, the young leaf before it opens. 2 Distress, affliction. 3 Evidence or declaration of distress. 4 A back or double tooth. 5 The hem or border of a garment. 6 Poverty, indigence. 7 Anger, passion. E. maha to worship, aff. ktin; the final becomes ḍha, and the preceding vowel is made long.
