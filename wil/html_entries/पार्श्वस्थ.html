• pārśvastha mfn. (-sthaḥ-sthā-sthaṃ) Staying near or close to, or at the side. m. (-sthaḥ)
    1 A sort of chorus to the Indian drama, an actor in the prelude, and interpreter of the plot.
    2 An associate, a companion.
    E. pārśva the side, (of the manager, &c.) stha who stays.
