• nāgadanta [459] m. (-ntaḥ) 1 Elephant's tooth or ivory; also the tooth of the elephant. 2 A shelf, a pin or bracket projecting from a wall, and used to hang things upon. f. (-ntī) 1 A sort of sun-flower, (Heliotropium Indicum.) 2 A whore. E. nāga an elephant, and danta a tooth.
