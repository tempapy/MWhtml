• klīva (ṛ) klībṛ r. 1st cl. (klīvate)
    1 To be impotent.
    2 To be timid or modest. [260]
  • klīva mfn. (-vaḥ-vā-vaṃ)
    1 Weak, impotent.
    2 Idle, slothful. mn. (-vaḥ-vaṃ)
    1 The neuter gender.
    2 A eunuch.
    E. klīva to be weak or timid, affix ka; also read klīva. [260]
