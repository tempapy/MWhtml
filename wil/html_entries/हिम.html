• hima [975] mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Cold, frigid. n. (-maṃ) 1 Frost, snow. 2 Cold. 3 Sandal. 4 Fresh butter. 5 Tin. 6 A pearl. 7 A lotus. m. (-maḥ) 1 The Sandal tree. 2 The moon. 3 Camphor. 4 The Himālaya mountain. f. (-mā) 1 Small cardamoms. 2 Reṇukā, a perfume. 3 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus.) E. hana to hurt, mak Uṇādi aff., and hi substituted for the root.
