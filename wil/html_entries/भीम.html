• bhīma mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Horrible, fearful, terrific. n. (-maṃ) Horror, terror. m. (-maḥ)
    1 A name of ŚIVA.
    2 One of the five Pāṇḍu princes.
    3 A kind of sorrel, (Rumex vesicarius.) f. (-mā)
    1 A name of DURGĀ.
    2 A whip.
    3 A sort of perfume: see rocanā.
    E. bhī to fear, Uṇādi aff. mak.
