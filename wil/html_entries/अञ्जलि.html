• añjali [014] m. (-liḥ) 1 The cavity formed by putting the hands together, and hollowing the palms: being in this form carried to the forehead, it forms the appropriate salutation from inferiors of respectability to their superiors. 2 A measure, a Kuḍava. See kuḍava. E. añja to go, and lic Uṇādi aff.
