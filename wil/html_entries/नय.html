• naya [455] r. 1st cl. (-nayate) To go, to move: see ṇaya and ṇī.
  • naya [455] mfn. (-yaḥ-yā-yaṃ) 1 Fit, right, proper. 2 Leading, conducting, (who or what does so.) m. (-yaḥ) 1 Guiding, directing, (literally or figuratively, as in morals, &c.) 2 Polity, civil and military government. 3 The theological part of the Vedas. 4 A sort of dice or men for playing with. 5 A guide, a conductor. E. naya to go, affix ghañ, or ṇī to guide, &c. affix ac.
