• kambala m. (-laḥ)
    1 A blanket.
    2 A chief of the Nāgas or serpents.
    3 A small worm.
    4 A dewlap.
    5 An upper cloth or garment.
    6 A sort of deer. n. (-laṃ) Water.
    E. kama to desire, kalaḥ Uṇādi affix, and ba inserted; or kam the head, water, &c. and bala to be strong, affix ac.
