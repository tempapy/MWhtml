• ṇida (i) ṇidi r. 1st cl. (nindati-te praṇindati) also (ṛ) ṇidṛ r. 1st cl. (nedati-te praṇedati)
    1 To abuse, to censure, to reproach, to reprove or revile, to despise or condemn, &c. (The second root means also)
    2 To approach, to come near. The change of ṇa after a preposition is optional in the derivatives of the first of these roots.
