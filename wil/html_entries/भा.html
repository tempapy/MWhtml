• bhā r. 2nd cl. (-bhāti)
    1 To shine, to be luminous, splendid or beautiful.
    2 To be pleased.
    3 To be angry.
    4 To blow.
    5 To be, to exist.
    With āṅ, to flash.
    With pra or vi, to shine brightly. [616]
  • bhā f. (-bhā)
    1 Light.
    2 Beauty.
    3 The shadow of a gnomon. m. (bhāḥ) The sun.
    E. bhā to shine, aff. aṅ and ṭāp. [616]
