• rañjaka mfn. (-kaḥ-kā-kaṃ)
    1 What incites or affects.
    2 What colours, &c. m. (-kaḥ)
    1 Colouring, dyeing.
    2 A colorist, a painter.
    3 A dyer.
    4 A stimulus, an inciter of affection, &c.
    5 Biliary humour on which vision depends.
    E. rañja to colour, vuna aff. [694]
