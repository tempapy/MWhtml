• tīrabhukti [378] m. (-ktiḥ) Tirhut, a province in the east of central Hindustan, lying on the north of Behar. E. tīra a bank, and bhukti here signifying a limit: being bounded on the west and east by the Gandaki and Kauśikī rivers, on the south by the Ganges, and on the north, by the skirts of the Himāla mountains.
