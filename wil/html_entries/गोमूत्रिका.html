• gomūtrikā [300] f. (-kā) 1 A kind of grass, described as growing in corn fields, of a reddish colour, and eaten by cattle, commonly Tāmbāda (tāṃvaḍa.) 2 A verse the second half of which repeats nearly all the syllables of the first. 3 A form of calculation. E. go, mūtra urine, kan affix; operating on cows as a diuretic.
