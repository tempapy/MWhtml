• pakṣapāta [490] m. (-taḥ) 1 Adopting a side or an argument whether right or wrong, partisanship. 2 A partisan, an adherent, who or what sides with. 3 The moulting of birds considered to proceed from fever. E. pakṣa, and pāta who causes to go, or pakṣa a wing, and pāta falling.
