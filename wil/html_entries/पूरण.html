• pūraṇa [549] mfn. (-ṇaḥ-ṇā-ṇī-ṇaṃ) Filling, completing. n. (-ṇaṃ) 1 Act of filling, completing or making up. 2 The final obsequial ball or cake. 3 Multiplication, (in arithmetic.) 4 A fragrant grass, (Cyperus rotundus.) 5 Rain, raining. nf. (-ṇaṃ-ṇī) The cross threads in weaving a piece of cloth. m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 A bridge, a causeway. 2 The ocean. 3 A medicinal oil or embrocation. f. (-ṇī) The silk cotton tree. E. pūra to fill, aff. lyuṭ or yuc.
