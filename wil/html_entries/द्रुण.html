• druṇa [430] r. 6th cl. (druṇati) 1 To hurt, to injure. 2 To go, to move, to approach. 3 To bind, to make crooked or curved.
  • druṇa [430] m. (-ṇaḥ) 1 A scorpion. 2 A bee. 3 A rogue, a cheat. f. (-ṇā) A bow string. n. (-ṇaṃ) A bow. 2 A sword, a scimitar. E. druṇa to injure, affix ka.
