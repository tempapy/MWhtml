• śulva r. 10th cl. (śulvayati)
    1 To measure.
    2 To create.
  • śulva subst. mfn. (-lvaḥ-lvā-lvī-lvaṃ)
    1 A rope, a cord, a string. n. (-lvaṃ)
    1 Copper.
    2 A sacrificial observance.
    3 Institute, law.
    4 A quantity of water.
    E. śulva to measure, aff. ac; or śugha to be pure, &c., Uṇādi aff. van, and dha changed to la.
