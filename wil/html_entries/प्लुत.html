• pluta [594] mfn. (-taḥ-tā-taṃ) 1 Jumped, leaped, gone by leap or jump, &c. 2 Thrown or tossed up. m. (-taḥ) 1 Bounding, capering, one of a horse's paces. 3 Leaping, jumping, tumbling. mn. (-taḥ-taṃ) The third sound given to vowels; the protracted or continuous sound, being three times the length of the short vowel, and occupying three moments in its utterance. E. plu to go by leaps, aff. kta.
