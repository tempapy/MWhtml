• ratni mf. (-tniḥ-tnī)
    1 A sort of cubit, measured from the elbow to the end of the closed fist; it is also computed at twenty-one breadths of the middle of the thumb.
    2 The closed hand, the fist.
    E. ṛ to go, katnic aff., the vowel changed to the semivowel, otherwise aratni, and as derived from sṛ to go, saratni.
