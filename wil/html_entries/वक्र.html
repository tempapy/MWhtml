• vakra mfn. (-kraḥ-krā-kraṃ)
    1 Crooked, curved, bowed or bent.
    2 Cruel, malignant.
    3 Dishonest, fraudulent.
    4 Indirect, evasive. n. (-kraṃ) The winding course of a river or water-fall, the arm or bend of a stream.
    2 A species of the Anuṣṭubh metre. m. (-kraḥ)
    1 A name of SATURN.
    2 The planet MARS.
    3 RUDRA or ŚIVA.
    4 The demon TRIPURA.
    E. vaki to go crookedly, rak Uṇādi aff.
