• sambhrama m. (-maḥ)
    1 Haste, hurry.
    2 Fear, terror.
    3 Flurry, confusion, haste or hurry arising from joy, fear, &c.
    4 Error, ignorance.
    5 Turning round, whirling, revolving.
    6 Respect, reverence.
    E. sam before bhrama to revolve, &c., aff. ac. [905]
