• sindhu [925] m. (-ndhuḥ) 1 The ocean, the sea. 2 The Indus. 3 The country along the Indus or Sindh. 4 The juice that exudes from an elephant's temples. 5 An elephant. 6 A division of a Rāga or musical mode. 7 A small tree, (Vitex negundo.) f. (-ndhuḥ) A river in general. E. syanda to trickle or flow, u Uṇādi aff., the form. irr.
