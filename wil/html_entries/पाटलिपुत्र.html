• pāṭaliputra n. (-traṃ) The name of a city, supposed to be the ancient Palibothra, and the modern Patna.
    E. pāṭali the trumpet flower, pū to be pure, tran aff.; also with kan added, pāṭaliputraka; it is also said to be named after its founder and sovereign, pāṭaliputra being a proper name.
