• pratipādana m. (-naṃ)
    1 Gift, donation.
    2 Ascertaining, determining, rendering clear or intelligible.
    3 Action, worldly conduct or interest.
    4 Accomplishing, effecting.
    5 Producing, causing.
    E. prati severally, and pada to go, causal v., aff, lyuṭ.
