• puruṣa m. (-ṣaḥ)
    1 A man, generally or individually, a male, mankind.
    2 The soul.
    3 God, the Supreme Being.
    4 VIṢṆU.
    5 BRAHMĀ.
    6 Life, the living principle.
    7 An atom.
    8 A follower of the Sāṃkhya philosophy.
    9 A friend.
    10 A tree: see punnāga.
    11 A sort of tree, commonly Tila or Tilaka. n. (-ṣaṃ) The mountain Meru.
    E. pura the body, ṣama to abide, aff. ka, and the vowel changed; or pura to precede, Uṇādi aff. kuṣan; also purūṣa. [543]
