• tāmrapuṣpa m. (-ṣpaḥ)
    1 A kind of ebony, (Bauhinia variegata.)
    2 A plant, (Kæmpferia rotunda.) f. (-ṣpā) Trumpet flower, (Bignonia suave-olens). f. (-ṣpī)
    1 Lythrum fruticosum: see dhātṛpuṣpikā.
    2 Trumpet flower.
    3 Red Tëori: see trikṛt.
    E. tāmra coppery red, and puṣpa a flower, bearing red blossoms.
