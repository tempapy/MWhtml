• prabhāvat [574] mfn. (-vān-vatī-vat) Luminous, radiant, splendid. f. (-tī) 1 The mother of MALLI the 19th of the Jinas or Jaina saints. 2 The lute of one of the demigods attendant on ŚIVA, or other principal deities. 3 A form of poetical metre. E. prabhā light or splendour, and matup poss. aff.
