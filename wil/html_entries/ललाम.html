• lalāma mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) Beautiful, agreeable, charming, (in this sense some authorities confine it to the neuter gender, and to its use compounded with another word.) n. (-maṃ)
    1 A mark or sign.
    2 A banner, a flag, a symbol or ensign.
    3 A mark on the forehead.
    4 A horn.
    5 A tail.
    6 Majesty, dignity.
    7 Chief, principal.
    8 A horse.
    9 An ornament.
    10 A horse's ornament.
    11 A coloured mark on the forehead of a horse or bull, &c.
    12 A mane.
    13 A line, a row. f. (-nī) An ornament worn in the ear.
    E. lala to play, &c., aff. kvip, ama to go, to contribute to or occasion, aff. aṇ; also with kanin, aff. lalāman. [717]
