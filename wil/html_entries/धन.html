• dhana [436] r. 1st. cl. (dhanati) To sound. r. 3rd cl. (dadhanti) To bear or produce, (grain, &c.): restricted to the Vedas.
  • dhana [436] n. (-naṃ) 1 Property of any description, thing, substance, wealth. 2 Wealth in cattle, property in herds. 3 A term of endearment. 4 (In Algebra,) The affirmative quantity or plus. E. dhana to produce, (a crop,) affix ac.
