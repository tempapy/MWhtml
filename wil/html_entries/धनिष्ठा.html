• dhaniṣṭhā [437] f. (-ṣṭhā) The twenty-fourth Nakṣatra or lunar mansion; it comprises four stars, apparently α β γ and δ Delphini, and is figured by a drum or tabor. E. dhana to produce, (as corn,) affix ac and matup added, dhanavat again, iṣṭhan added, and matup rejected, fem. affix ṭāp.
