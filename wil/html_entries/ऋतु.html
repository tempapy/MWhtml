• ṛtu m. (-tuḥ)
    1 A season, (the Hindu year is divided into ar seasons, each consisting of two months.)
    2 The menstrual evacuation.
    3 The time favourable for procreation, or sixteen days in each month.
    4 A month.
    5 A kind of collyrium
    6 Light.
    E. ṛ to go, tu Uṇādi aff.
