• tuvara [382] mfn. (-raḥ-rī-raṃ) 1 Astringent. 2 Beardless. mn. (-raḥ-raṃ) An astringent taste. f. (-rī) 1 A fragrant earth. 2 A kind of lentil, (Cytisus cajan:) see āḍhakī. E. tu a Sautra root, to remove, (disease, &c.) thvarac and affix ṅīṣ; also kan being added, in the fem. form tuvarikā, f. (-kā.)
