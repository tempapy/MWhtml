• vṛti f. (-tiḥ)
    1 Selecting, choosing, preferring, appointing.
    2 Soliciting, asking, requesting.
    3 Surrounding, encompassing.
    4 An enclosure, a place enclosed for particular cultivation, and especially that of the Piper betel, which in many parts of India is surrounded and screened my mats.
    5 Hiding, secreting.
    E. vṛ to choose, aff. ktin. [800]
