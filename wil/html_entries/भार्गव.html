• bhārgava m. (-vaḥ)
    1 A name of PARAŚURĀMA.
    2 A name of ŚUKRA regent of VENUS, and preceptor of the Titans or demons.
    3 An archer.
    4 An elephant. f. (-vī)
    1 The goddess PĀRVATĪ.
    2 The goddess LAKṢMĪ.
    3 Bent grass, (Panicum dactylon, the Agrostis linearis of LINNÆUS; according to the Hindus, a black variety.)
    E. bhṛgu a Muni, the ancestor of PARAŚURĀMA, &c., and aṇ aff. of descent. [618]
