• karaja mfn. (-jaḥ-jā-jaṃ) Produced in or from a hand, a ray, &c. m. (-jaḥ)
    1 A finger nail.
    2 The name of a timber tree, commonly Karanja, (Galendupa arborea, Rox.) n. (-jaṃ) A perfume, commonly Nakhi, resembling a nail in appearance.
    E. kara the hand, and ja produced; or kaṃ pleasure, rañja to colour, &c. aca affix: see the next.
