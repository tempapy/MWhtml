• auparodhika mfn. (-kaḥ-kī-kaṃ)
    1 Proceeding from or relating to kindness or favour.
    2 Opposing, impeding, connected with or relating to a check or hindrance.
    E. uparodha, and ṭhak affix. m. (-kaḥ) A staff of the wood of a particular tree, (the Pīlu.)
    E. uparodha willingness to oblige, &c. ṭhak aff.
