• uttama [141] mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) 1 Chief, principal, first, best. 2 Greatest, highest. m. (-maḥ) 1 The third Menu, son of PRIYAVRATA. 2 The best kind of lover or hero. f. (-mā) 1 An excellent woman: one who is handsome, healthy, and affectionate. 2 A plant. (Asclepias rosea.) E. ut much, tama to desire, affix ac.
