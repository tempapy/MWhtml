• kunda mn. (-ndaḥ-ndaṃ) A kind of jasmin, (J. multiflorum or pubescens.) m. (-ndaḥ)
    1 A turner's lathe.
    2 Olibanum, the resin of the Boswellia thurifera: see kundaru.
    3 One of KUVERA'S nine treasures.
    4 The Nerium plant.
    E. ku to sound, dana Uṇādi affix, and num inserted, or ku the earth, unda to hurt, and aṇ affix; also with kan added kundaka.
