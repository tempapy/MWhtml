• vāma [751] mfn. (-maḥ-mā-maṃ) 1 Left, not right. 2 Reverse, contrary, inverted. 3 Beautiful, pleasing. 4 Vile, base, wicked. 5 Short. 6 Crooked. m. (-maḥ) 1 A name of ŚIVA. 2 KĀMA or CUPID. 3 An udder, a breast. 4 A potherb, (Chenopodium album.) 5 An animal, a sentient being. 6 A snake. n. (-maṃ) Wealth, possessions. f. (-mā) 1 A woman. 2 GAURĪ. 3 LAKṢMĪ. 4 SARASVATĪ. f. (-mī) 1 A mare. 2 A she-ass. 3 A young female elephant. 4 The female of the jackal. E. vā to go, Uṇādi aff. man.
