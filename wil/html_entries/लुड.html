• luḍa r. 1st cl. (loḍati) To agitate, to stir, to churn. r. 1st and 6th cls. (loḍati luḍati)
    1 To be in connexion or contact with any thing, to join, to adhere to, to rest in or on, &c.
    2 To embrace. r. 6th cl. (-luḍati) To cover, to conceal. (i) luḍi r. 10th cl. (luṇṭhayati)
    1 To be agitated.
    2 To plunder.
