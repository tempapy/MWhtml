• maitrāvaruṇi m. (-ṇiḥ) A name of AGASTYA.
    E. mitravaruṇa a sage, the father of this saint, and iñ aff.; or mitra the sun, and varuṇa VARUṆA, alluding to a legend of the birth of the saint from a contest between these deities, for the possession of URVAŚĪ one of the courtezans of INDRA'S heaven.
