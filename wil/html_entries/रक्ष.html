• rakṣa r. 1st cl. (rakṣati) To guard, to protect, to cherish, to preserve.
    With pari, To save from ruin.
  • rakṣa mf. (-kṣaḥ-kṣā)
    1 Preserving, protecting.
    2 Lac. f. (-kṣā)
    1 Ashes.
    2 A sort of bracelet, a twist of thread or tinsel, with a small packet containing a few carminative seeds, bound round the wrist at particular periods, especially the day of full moon in Śrāvaṇa.
    E. rakṣa to preserve, aff. ac.
