• rāśi [705] m. (-śiḥ) 1 A heap, a quantity. 2 A sign of the zodiac. 3 Arithmetical or algebraic quantity. 4 A part and its divisor, or numerator and denominator. 5 A congeries of number or quantity. E. aśa to pervade, iṇ Uṇādi aff. and ruṭ initial augment; or raśa to sound, with the same aff.
