• odhṛta- (looks as if = avadhṛta; but ava-dhṛ is not so used, and it is no doubt really a false Sktization of a MIndic equivalent of Skt. uddhṛta), removed, laid down (of a burden): Mv ii.95.4 (rājā Subāhuḥ) rājakṛtyato odhṛtabhāro, having laid down the burden of (lit. from) royal duties. Perh. cf. AMg. ohariya, ppp., taken down, placed down (Ratnach.), supposedly for avadhṛta. [159,2]
