• skandhākṣa, adj. or subst. m. (in Skt. once as n. of an attendant of Skanda; BR Augen auf den Schultern habend), (1) in Mvy 8837, in a list of deformities, acc. to Tib. shoulder-eye, phrag mig, which acc. to Das is applied to a kind of spirits having eyes on their shoulders (also crab); (2) n. of a yakṣa: Māy 81.
