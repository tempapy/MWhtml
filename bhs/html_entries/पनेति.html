• paneti, read paṇeti (= Skt. paṇayati), evaluates, rates, in LV 330.13 (vs) kāyā sarva paneti, he rates all bodies (at their true worthlessness); Tib. rtogs, perceive, know. The alternative, which seems unattractive, would be to assume a strangely distorted MIndic form of Skt. pra-jñā- (cf. AMg. paṇṇāyati etc.). [318,2]
