• kapāṭa, in LV 376.13 (vs), to be read approximately: nīvaraṇa-kapāṭāś ca pañca mayehā pradāritā sarve, perh. all the five doors (gates, as obstacles) of the hindrances have been cleft by me here. Note however that AMg. kavāḍa = kapāṭa is a homonym of kavāḍa = kapāla; I suspect that the latter is really meant here (falsely Sktized), in some such sense as hard shells. [167,2]
