• daśavarga, adj. or subst. (= Pali dasavagga), consisting of a group of ten; a quorum of ten (monks); required acc. to Pali Vin. i.319.33 (cf. 31) normally, ‘in the middle regions,’ for ordination: MSV ii.205.11 ff., cf. 203.16 ff.; Mv i.2.15 daśavargena gaṇena upasaṃpadā (q.v.); Bhīk 18b.5 bhikṣūṇāṃ daśavarge maṇḍalake (see s.v. dvādaśavarga); certain sins to be confessed before such a group, Śikṣ 169.1 āpattir daśavarge ṛjukena deśayitavyā.
