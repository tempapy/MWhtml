• kula-dūṣaka, m. (= Pali °saka), in MSV iii.88.4 written °ṣika, injurer or spoiler of families (this is a saṃghāvaśeṣa sin): Mvy 8380; Prāt 485.1; MSV iii.15.22. Corresp. to Pali Vin. iii.184.9 ff.; acc. to 185.1 ff. the ‘injury’ or ‘spoiling’ consists in the erring monk's imposing on lay families improper services. [188,1]
