• Kacaṅgalā (= Pali Kajaṅgalā, °la), (1) n. of a town: Jm 113.22 °lāyāṃ, loc.; Av ii.41.5 f., id.; (2) n. of an old woman who lived in this town, who had been the mother of the Bodhisattva in former births and whom [Page164-a+ 71] the Buddha initiated as a nun: Av ii.41.6 ff. (this is an embroidered form of the Pali story of the nun Kajaṅgalā, AN v.54 ff.); MSV i.20.4 ff. [163,2]
