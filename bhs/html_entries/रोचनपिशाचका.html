• rocana-piśācakā, v.l. °piśācanakā (nom. pl.; Sen. em. °piśācikā), some sort of product of the art of the śaṅkha-valaya-kāra; the adjoining words in Mv ii.473.11 suggest that it is a small article made of ivory, perh. for holding unguents or the like (and made in the shape of a piśācī? so Senart suggests).
