• rajata, nt., some kind of disease, in lists of diseases: Mvy 9540; Bhīk 17a.1 (rajabhaṃ, doubtless corruption for °taṃ); MSV iv.68.17. Acc. to Tib. on Mvy = glog pa, which Jä. equates with lhog pa (which Tib. gives for lohaliṅga, q.v.), a large ulcer, sore, or carbuncle. Chin. a skin disease. [449,2]
