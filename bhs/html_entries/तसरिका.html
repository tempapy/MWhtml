• tasarikā (from Skt. tasara, shuttle), in Divy 83.24 (prose), acc. to Index weaving, as operation of the shuttle; this force of the suffix would seem peculiar, but the con- text gives little help: (Śacy api…) tasarikāṃ kartum ārabdhā. In the preceding sentence Śakra, in disguise, vastraṃ vāyitum ārabdhaḥ. Perhaps tasarikāṃ kartum, to make the (little) shuttle, means only to operate the shuttle. [250,2]
