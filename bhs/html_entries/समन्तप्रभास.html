• Samantaprabhāsa, reading of SP edd. in most occurrences for n. of a future Buddha, which it is predicted that Ājñāta Kauṇḍinya will become, SP 206.11, and also n. of 500 future Buddhas which 500 ‘great disciples’ will become, 207.2. But the true reading in both places is prob. Samantaprabha (1), with Kashgar rec. In the same account, °prabha is read without v.l. in 207.9 and 208.10, guaranteed by meter; in 208.5 text reads prabhā- sanāmasya (referring to the former case), but Kashgar rec. Samantaprabha-; in 209.4 text Samantaprabhāsasya, metr. very bad, Kashgar rec. and one Nep. ms. °prabhasya, better metrically (°prābhasya is required). [563,1]
