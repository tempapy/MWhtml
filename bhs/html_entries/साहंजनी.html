• Sāhaṃjanī or °ni (°nin?; no distinctively fem. forms, such as oblique -īye, noted), n. of a hermitage locality (āśramapada) in the Himālayas, home of the ṛṣi Gautama (4): Mv ii.210.1; of the ṛṣi Kāśyapa (4), iii.143.12 °nī, n. sg.; 146.15 °nīṃ, acc. sg.; in 150.17 and 151.13 Senart prints °niṃ, but in the former 1 ms., in the latter both, read °nīṃ; 151.4 °nisya (v.l. °nīsya); also home of ṛṣi Kāśyapa (4) iii.362.12 ff.
