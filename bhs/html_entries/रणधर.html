• raṇadhara, m., some member of a ship's crew, acc. to Tib. and Chin. oarsman: Mvy 3854 = Tib. ru skya ḥdzin pa. In a list of members of a ship's crew; see s.v. pauruṣeya 2. The word prob. occurred in the original form of Av i.200.5; ii.61.9, but has been lost by textual corruption. [450,1]
