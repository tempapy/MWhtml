• pada (= Pali id.), sentence, complete utterance, in contrast with nāman, word, and vyañjana, sound (same triad in Pali, PTSD s.v. pada, 4): Mvy 1998 (-kāyaḥ), see s.v. kāya (2); defined AbhidhK. LaV-P. ii.238 as = vākya, a complete statement which makes sense; this may perhaps be the mg. in agra-pada; where the context contains no contrasting word for word, that common Skt. meaning of pada may ordinarily be assumed; see s.v. vyañjana for one or two such passages where pada is thus ambiguous (word or sentence).
