• āli, f. (m. ? nom. āliḥ), (1) small ditch (for water): Mvy 4177 = Tib. yur phran; cf. the Pali (and Skt. Lex.) meaning dike; (2) a-series (i.e. a plus āli), name for a series of syllables (chiefly vowels and combinations of a or ā with semivowels), used as a magic formula in Sādh, and defined there 478.7 ff. Cf. kāli. [106,1]
