• saṃparivartayati (mg. 1 = Skt. parivartayati), (1) interchanges, mixes: so 'pi bhikṣur āsīl lābhī grāhikaḥ, tena mātsaryābhibhūtena lābhaḥ °titaḥ, yo vārṣikaḥ sa haimantikaḥ, pariṇāmitaḥ (and vice versa) Śikṣ 59.4; (2) wrings (the hands): hastau °yati mukhaṃ ca vibhaṇḍayati Divy 263.13, (°yasi) 15; mukhaṃ vibhaṇḍya hastān °vartya 575.24; (3) rolls (the eyes, in a fit): (akṣīṇi) °ti MSV i.285.17; (4) upsets: palyaṅkān (cots) api °yanti MSV iv.121.15.
