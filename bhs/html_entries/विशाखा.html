(= Pali Visākhā Migāramātā): Divy 77.28; 466.24; Av i.224.3; ii.9.7; Karmav 87.15; 97.3 (in 97.3 spelled Viśākhyā); MSV ii.53.16 ff. (her story in full, different in many ways from the Pali). [500,2]
