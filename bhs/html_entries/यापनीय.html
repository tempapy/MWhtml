• yāpanīya, adj. or subst. nt. (to Skt. yāpana plus īya; = Pali id., Vin. i.59.10, with khamanīya), livable, comfortable: kaccit te bhagavan kṣamaṇīyaṃ kaccid yāpanīyaṃ kaccid dhātavaḥ pratikurvanti SP 429.4, I hope, Lord, things are tolerable and comfortable for you, and that your bodily elements are working all right?; °ya- tara, compv., Divy 110.2, see s.v. kṣemaṇīya.
