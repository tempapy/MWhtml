• antarīkṣa, adj. (= antarikṣa; Skt. ānt°), of the atmosphere, a class of gods, see deva: LV 266.1, 4; 396.14; 401.1. As noun, antarīkṣa also occurs in Skt., but much more commonly in BHS (= antarikṣa): e.g. SP 23.14; 69.10; LV 75.7; 218.18; Mv i.31.4; 33.5; 179.10; Divy 324.28; 340.5; Suv 84.9; RP 45.20; Gv 117.15.
