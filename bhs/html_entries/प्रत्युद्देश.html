• pratyuddeśa, m. (substantially = Skt. uddeśa), direction, region, place, sometimes app. with added sense of specific, particular (prati): yehi °śehi teṣāṃ…gamano, tehi uddeśehi…Mv ii.251.19, similarly tehi tehi °śehi 252.2; anuhimavante (10 °ta-) °deśe iii.29.9, 10; dakṣi- ṇāpathe sāgaramukho nāma dikpratyuddeśas, tatra… bhikṣuḥ prativasati Gv 62.13, a place or region; so also 63.5; 529.4, dik-pra°; (samudrakaccho) nāma digmukha- praty° Gv 456.13 (the same place is called a janapada 466.16); (dakṣiṇāpathe samudravetāḍī) nāma pratyuddeśas tatra…udyānaṃ Gv 99.11 (the same place is called a pradeśa in 100.9-10).
