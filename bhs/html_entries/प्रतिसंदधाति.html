• pratisaṃdadhāti, °saṃdheti, also in Mv paṭi° (cf. next; = Pali paṭisaṃdahati; specialized use of Skt. prati-saṃ-dhā; Pali and BHS generally but wrongly understood as if medio-passive), connects, makes connexion, again (with a new body), substantially = takes on a new existence, so is reborn: °dadhāti Mvy 2171; paṭisaṃdheti Mv iii.65.9 and °dhenti 12; pratisaṃdheti 65.14, 19, and (mss. °veti) 66.1 (twice) and 3; ppp. °dhita, re-created, °tāni…Devadattena kuśalamūlāni MSV iv.236.4. See next. The Pali verb is recorded in PTSD only from Miln. 32(.12 ff.); it occurs elsewhere, at least in Miln., e.g. 46.5 ff. [372,1]
