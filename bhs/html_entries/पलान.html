• palāna, pple. (= JM. palāṇa, Pischel 567, cf. prec., and § 34.19), fled, or sometimes seemingly with pass. force, put to flight, with instr. of person causing the flight, e.g. bhagavatā te (mss. tena) amanuṣyakā (demons) palānā Mv i.270.11--12; so several times Mv i.283.7 ff., regularly with instr.; used absolutely, without instr., fled, Mv ii.172.16; 235.2; iii.350.1. Cf. also palāyaka, with v.l. palānaka, and prapalāna. [337,1]
