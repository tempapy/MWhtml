(of a pratyeka- buddha); yaḥ punar bhiksuḥ sāṃghike vihāre uparivihāyasi kuṭikāyām…niṣīded…Prāt 506.5--6; in Mvy 8374, MSV iii.87.19, abbreviated designation of one of the saṃghāvaśeṣa sins, consisting in a monk's building a hut for himself in an improper place or manner, in violation of Prāt 480.8 ff.
