• vyāskandaka-, a kind of samāpatti, q.v.: Mvy 1497 (Mironov vya°). Tib. renders by snrel zhi, which usually = vyatyasta (q.v. 2), and Mvy ed. suggests reading so; cf. vyutkrāntaka. It would seem to be, like the latter, a manner of skipping from one trance-state to a non-adjoining one. But Chin. has the equivalent of nāsaṃjñāyatana, which as Ting says seems ‘an abbre- viation’ (the last part) of naivasaṃjñānāsaṃjñāyatana. [519,1]
