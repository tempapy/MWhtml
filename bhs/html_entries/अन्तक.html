• antaka, adj. (or subst.; = Pali adj. anta [cf. ānta], Skt. antya; a MIndic form; not destructions with Senart), low, vile (person or thing): Mv iii.186.2--3 na ātapo tāpayati, antakā tāpayanti māṃ; antakāś ca…te tāpenti na ātapo. Cf. line 4 itvaraṃ khu ayaṃ tāpo; see itvara, which is the clue to the mg. of antaka. [037,2]
