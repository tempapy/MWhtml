• nigha, m. (also nīgha, q.v., and cf. anigha), evil, sin: Mvy 7308 = Tib. sdig pa, sin. In Pali only anigha, anīgha seem to be in real use; nigha and nīgha are given in comms. and said to mean dukkha; they have the look of ab- stractions from anigha (anīgha); alternatively the comms. analyze an-īgha. Real etym. of anigha uncertain. But BHS seems clearly to have used nigha, and probably nīgha, independently (tho perhaps by secondary back- formation from an°). [295,1]
