• itaretara, adj. (= Pali itarītara; the Skt. word not in this sense but only reciprocal, cf. Wackernagel II.1 § 60a, note), any sort of, this or that, any at all, miscellaneous, hit-or-miss: Mvy 2216 netaretareṇa saṃtuṣṭiḥ; Mv iii.348.4 °reṇa ca piṇḍapātreṇa saṃtuṣṭo bhaveyaṃ; RP 13.9 alpecchā itaretarair abhiratā(ḥ), pleased (satisfied) with anything at all; 16.5 bhavati ca itaretareṇa tuṣṭaḥ.
