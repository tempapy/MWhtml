• karma-vācanā (= Pali kamma-vācā; PTSD defini- tion does not fit very well Vin. i.317.30 ff., where this Pali word is used exactly like the BHS), the stating of the matter that has been moved in the assembly of monks or nuns, see s.v. jñapti; it takes the form of a demand that they consent by silence, or oppose by speaking; in the case of jñapti-caturtha the question is triple, prathamā, dvitīyā and tṛtīyā karma°: Mvy 8663--6; Bhīk 18b.4, MSV iii.13.6 f.
