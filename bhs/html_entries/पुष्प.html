• puṣpa, m., (1) as common noun, flower (regularly nt.): puṣpāṃ = °pān, acc. pl., LV 92.16; (2) n. of a plurality of former Buddhas, puṣpa-nāmaka or the like, numbering 300: Mv i.46.3, but 3 crores, 58.4; 61.13; n. of an individual former Buddha, iii.239.7--8, 432.12; oc- curs also as v.l. for Puṣya (1), q.v., in mss. and some edd.; (3) n. of a future Buddha (cf. Puṣya 2): Mv ii.355.12 = iii.279.17; (4) n. of a king: Puṣpa-nāmo Mmk 625.22 (possibly means with a name containing the element puṣpa, and may even refer to more than one king). [349,2]
