• Mahākāla (cf. Pali Mahākāḷa, n. of a nāga king and of a mountain; see s.vv. kāla, kālaka), (1) n. of a yakṣa: Māy 12; (2) n. of a gandharva: Suv 161.18; (3) n. of a deity, doubtless borrowed from Hinduism (Mah° = Śiva): Sādh 583.1 (here Vajra-Mah°), etc.; (4) n. of a mountain: Kv 91.13 (see s.v. Kāla). [422,1]
