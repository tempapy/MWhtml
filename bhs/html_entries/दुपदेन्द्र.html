• dupadendra (MIndic for Skt. dvi°; cf. du-; dupada not recorded in Pali; AMg. dupaya acc. to Sheth), (1) king: in Gv 259.20 (vs) read, yada sa niṣkramī dupadendrā (or °dro), when that king retired from the world; (2) n. of a former Buddha: in Gv 258.8 (vs) read with 2d ed., sap- tamu teṣa āsi Dupadendraḥ.
