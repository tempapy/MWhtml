• paridāha, m. (Skt. only lit. burning, and fig. mental anguish; = Pali pariḷāha; cf. paridahyati, paridāgha, and niṣparidāha), (1) fever (disease): Mvy 9485, in list of diseases, Tib. tsha ba, heat; (2) feverish longing, ardent desire, substantially = tṛṣṇā, as a vice: kāmeṣu °haḥ LV 246.13; in lists of vices, LV 52.14; Śikṣ 198.8; Dbh 25.4.
