• 1 dvīpika, m., a certain insect: °kaḥ Mvy 4918 = Tib. sril (silkworm) or mug pa (moth, acc. to Jä. also worm). Seems likely to be related to Deśīn. 5.53 dīviā, glossed upadehikā (Skt. Lex., said to be = Skt. upadīkā, a kind of ant). [275,1]
  • 2 -dvīpika, ifc. adj., in pratyantadvīpikānāṃ (brāh- maṇānāṃ) Suv 14.4 (prose), belonging to the (outlying) continents. [275,1]
