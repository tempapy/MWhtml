• vaṭṭanā (valī, i.e. °nā-āvalī; = Pali °ḷi), (string of) bead(s); so to be read for Senart's em. vaṭṭanaveṇī Mv ii.125.16; 127.4; 128.9; 129.12; and (em. veṣṭanaveṇī) 231.17. The mss. are all corrupt but clearly point to (ā)valī, in accordance with Pali, rather than veṇī in which Senart follows the LV parallels, see vartanā(-veṇī); the mg. is the same and is explained under the latter word.
