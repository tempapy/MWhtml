• maṇḍa-pūla, m. (so Mironov, no v.l.; Kyoto ed. v.l. muṇḍa°; cf. pūla), acc. to Chin. boots, short boots (definitely not slippers but shoes with uppers): °laḥ Mvy 8968 = Tib. lham yu can; Jä. and Das cite a word lham yu chad (acc. to Das = muṇḍa-pulā) as meaning a sort of slippers to which cotton leggings are attached. MSV iv.208.7 muṇḍapūlā by em., ms. puṇḍapolāḥ. [415,2]
