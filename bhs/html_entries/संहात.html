• saṃhāta (m.; = Skt. or BHS saṃghāta, as such in Skt. but not in mg. 2; § 2.35; not recorded in MIndic), (1) mass, collection: (Jīvakena vaidyarājena…) bhaiṣa- jyataru-saṃhāta-mayaṃ dārikārūpaṃ kṛtaṃ Śikṣ 159.9; sarvaratnayamaka-saṃhāto (Bhvr. adj., with kūṭāgāro 14) Gv 6.17; (2) in acchaṭā-°ta, = acchaṭā-(q.v.)-saṃghāta: ekācchaṭā-°ta-mātram api Śikṣ 214.11, for a single snap of the fingers (jiffy, trice).
