• ārya-satya, nt. (= Pali ariya-sacca), (the four) noble truth(s): listed, Mvy 1310 ff.; Dharmas 21 (duḥkḥam, samudayaḥ, nirodhaḥ, mārgaḥ); SP 179.2--3; Mv ii.138.4, Bbh 38.9 (as in Dharmas); full statements of all four, Mv iii.331.17 ff.; LV 417.2 ff. The standard names are duḥkham, duḥkhasamudayaḥ, duḥkhanirodhaḥ, duḥkhani- rodhagāminī pratipat. [105,1]
