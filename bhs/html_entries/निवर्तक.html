• nivartaka, f. °ikā, adj. (to caus. of next, q.v.), creative, productive: (tṛṣṇāyāḥ…) janikāyā nivartikāyā(ḥ) LV 417.11 (prose; no v.l.), which is creative and productive (Tib. sgrub pa for niv°); occurs in the 3d ārya-satya, in the Dharmacakrapravartanasūtra, but unparalleled in other versions; sarvakāma-nivartakam SP 211.2 (prose), effecting all desires.
