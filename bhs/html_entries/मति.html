• Mati, (1) n. of a prince, son of the Buddha Candra- sūryapradīpa: SP 19.2; (2) n. of a brahman youth, pre- vious incarnation of Dharmaruci; corresponds to Megha- datta of Mv, Megha of Pali, as associate of the previous incarnation of Śākyamuni (here called Sumati) under Dīpaṃkara: Divy 247.2 ff.; (3) n. of a prince, previous incarnation of Śākyamuni (is this the same as Sumati of Divy?): Samādh p. 52 lines 21 (here text satī, read mati) and 30; (4) n. of a teacher (a Buddha?) in the kṛta yuga: Laṅk 365.3; also called Mahāmati (5); in 365.7 apparently a different Mati (a Buddha, nāyakaḥ) is named, a later one, tho still in the kṛta yuga (Suzuki's transl. is wrong on this).
