• a-praṇidhi, adj. or subst. (= a-praṇihita, q.v.), (the state) that is free from desire, longing, or purpose: LV 296.8 (vs), read: śūnyānimittapraṇidhīrava muñcamānāḥ (understand -animitta-apraṇidhi-; m.c. a for ā), emitting a sound (concerning) the void, causeless, purposeless (state, i.e. mokṣa, or nirvāṇa); cf. Śikṣ 6.15, s.v. apraṇihita. [047,1]
