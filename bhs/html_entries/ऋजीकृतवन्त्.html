• ṛjī-kṛta-vant, adj. (perf. act. pple.), having made straight: LV 10.11 (prose) adhimuktim ṛjīkṛtavataḥ (gen. sg.). Regular Skt. would be ṛjū°; the only v.l. cited is ms. A ṛjvī°; even Calc. ṛjī°; Tib. also draṅ por byas pa, having made straight. Presumably ī is due to analog. influence of stem-form in ī from stems in a, ā, i, ī before kṛ-. [151,1]
