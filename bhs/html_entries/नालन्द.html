• Nālanda, nt. (Pali Nālandā, also Nāla, Nālaka, Nālika), or °dā, n. of a village near (one-half yojana acc. to Mv) Rājagṛha, birthplace and family home of Śāriputra; also called Nālada acc. to Av ii.186.6: rājagṛhasya ardha- yojane nālanda-grāmakaṃ (v.l. nālandaṃ) nāma grāmaṃ Mv iii.56.6; śrī-nālandā Mvy 4120.
