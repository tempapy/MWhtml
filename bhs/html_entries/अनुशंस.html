• anuśaṃsa, m.; °sā, f.; also ānuśaṃsa °sā; and ānṛśaṃsa, Buddhacarita 6.12, mss. (Johnston em. anu- śaṃsa; Tib. supports anu° or ānu°, rjes-su); acc. to Kern SBE 21.336 note 1, ānṛśaṃsa in title of SP chap. 18, but KN ed. has °nu° (quantity of preceding a- obscured by saṃdhi); La Vallée-Poussin, Bodhicaryāvatārapañjikā 22 n. 3, assumes ānṛ° as orig. form; Pali only ānisaṃsa; BHS mss. often °saṃś° for °śaṃs°; benefit, blessing, advantage, profit, as derived from virtuous actions of various kinds; Pali lists five (PTSD; other lists occur), and five are often mentioned in BHS, but they are different for different works of merit, and other numbers (as 10, 18) also occur; I have not found the PTSD list in BHS.
