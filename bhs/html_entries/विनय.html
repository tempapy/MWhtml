• Vinaya, m. (= Pali id.), (book of) discipline, one of the sections of the canon: Mvy 1414 (after sūtram, abhi- dharmaḥ); sūtra-vinayābhidharmeṇa Laṅk 290.8; Vinaye, in the (text of the) V., Karmav 59.18; 60.9; 71.10; 158.17. In Śikṣ 190.4, kim-ākāraṃ śrutaṃ bodhisattva-vinaye praśastaṃ, Bendall in text and transl. understands bodhi- sattvavinaya as n. of a work, but I believe it means merely in the training of Bodhisattvas; there follows immediately a citation from the Akṣayamati-sūtra.
