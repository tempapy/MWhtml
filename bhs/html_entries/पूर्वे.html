• pūrve, and m.c. pūrvi, adv. (= Pali pubbe; in Skt. pūrvam), of old, in former time; first, beforehand: in prose of Mv, otherwise hardly found except in vss, but note pūrve SP 158.13 (prose, only Kashgar rec. and one Nep. ms.); pūrve adattvā Mv ii.56.4, not having first given; Mv i.26.7; ii.98.14 (pūrve vā saṃnivāsena, either by dwelling together in a former birth…); 121.1 (pūrve aśrutā caiva aśrutapūrvā ca, cf. parallels LV 246.9 aśrutapūrvā(ḥ), only, and Pali MN i.240.30 pubbe assutapubbā); 130.14; 192.19, 21; 337.13; 356.19; 469.10 (so with mss.); iii.332.13, 15, 18, and 333.2 (in some v.l. pūrvaṃ, once followed by Senart), etc.; the rest are only vss: RP 21.14; 23.8; Gv 230.13; LV 163.16; 164.1; 196.5; 215.15; 231.11; 331.6; 340.15; 376.6; 386.14; pūrvi, m.c., LV 170.11; 365.13; cf. also the foll. cpds., and see pūrvasyām, pūrvikāyām.
