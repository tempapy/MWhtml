• paripiṇḍayati (only noted in gdve. and ppp.; denom., cf. prec. and next; Skt. ppp. °ḍita, acc. to pw zusammen- geballt; AMg. ppp. °ḍiya, accumulated, Ratnach.), (1) makes into the shape of a ball or lump: suptoragarājabhoga- paripiṇḍitaṃ (like a sleeping snake-king's coils…) paryaṅ- kaṃ baddhvā Divy 567.6; (2) accumulates (so Skt. piṇḍita): buddhadharmāḥ °ḍayitavyāḥ Gv 69.5 (2d ed. absurdly paripīḍay°); °ḍita-kuśalamūlāḥ Mv i.142.2; suparipiṇḍita- śukladharmāṇāṃ Dbh 11.10, and so read in LV 404.20 for text °paripaṇḍita°, q.v.
