• Roruka (var. Rauruka), nt. (= Pali Roruka), (1) n. of a town, capital of the Sauvīras (Pali Sov°): Mv iii.208.18; home of Rudrāyaṇa, Divy 544.23 ff.; in 545.26 (mss.), and usually later in the story (550.1--11; 551.6; 552.17 ff.; 556.16 ff.; 559.4; 563.12 ff.), called Rauruka; (2) n. of a city in the south (and so apparently not the same as 1, which is in the northwest): Gv 453.18, 25.
