• sthera (= sthavira, cf. thera; Pali thera), old; an elder: sthero na tāvatā bhavati (pronounce bhoti) Ud xi.11 (required by meter; later v.l. sthaviro, unmetr); sthero ti ucyate xi.12 (later v.l. sthavira ucyate, where meter is preserved by omitting the MIndic ti = iti). The first of these corresp. to Pali Dhp. 260 na tena thero hoti, where meter requires either thaviro, or with vv.ll. bha- vati, or so hoti. [611,2]
