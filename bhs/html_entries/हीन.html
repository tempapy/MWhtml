• hīna, nt. (rare in Skt. and not in this precise mg.), the low; hīnāya, with āvartati (mss. vartati; = Pali hīnāya āvattati, once vattati acc. to text Jāt. i.276.16), returns to the low = gives up monkish life, returns to the world: hīnāyāvartanti (mss. °yaṃ vart°) kāmehi Mv iii.47.14 (prose). Cf. hīnāyāvarta. [620,2]
