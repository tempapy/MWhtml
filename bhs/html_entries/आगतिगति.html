• āgati-gati, nt. dvandva, coming and going (in succes- sive births): Mv i.4.7 (prose), read sattvānām āgatigati (so one ms., the rest °gami; Senart em. °gatiṃ) vividhaṃ (so, or °dha, all mss.; Senart em. °dhāṃ) bhagavāṃ abhijñāsi. Cf. Mv i.9.2 (vs) sattvānām āgatiṃ gatiṃ (here āgatiṃ, rather than °ti, is required by meter). The two words are often collocated in this sense in Pali; this use of each of them individually is, however, not foreign to Skt. [088,1]
