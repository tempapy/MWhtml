• anubuddhi (f.; to Skt. anu-budh-; cf. next, and anubodha, °dhana), an awakening, enlightenment, making up of the mind, with infin.: adyāvaboddhum amṛtaṃ anu- buddhi śāstā LV 299.12 (vs), last word by em., best ms. śāstar, others vary; read śāstur, gen. sg., today the teacher makes up his mind to win knowledge of what is deathless; anubuddhi, n. sg. With Lefm.'s em., anubuddhi could be 3 sg. aor. of anu-budh (Chap. 43, s.v. budh 2). [031,2]
