• chava, adj. (also śava, q.v.; Pali chava, homonymous with chava = Skt. śava, corpse, and perhaps ultimately the same word, cf. Senart, Mv i.583), base, vile, wretched: śūrāṃ sāhasikāṃ chavāṃ (acc. pl.) Mv i.278.10; pattrāhāro chavāvāsī Mv iii.435.22, having a wretched dwelling. [235,2]
