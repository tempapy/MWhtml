• Viṣṇu, (1) n. (i.e. prob. an element in the compound name) of a large group of kings: prabhanāmā sahasrāṇi viṣṇunāmā tathaiva ca Mmk 625.24 (vs), thousands with names containing prabha, and also containing Viṣṇu; in 26 a single one of them, perhaps referred to as named Viṣṇu, simply: teṣām apaścimo rājā viṣṇunāmā bhavi- ṣyati; (2) n. of a yakṣa leader: Māy 235.31.
