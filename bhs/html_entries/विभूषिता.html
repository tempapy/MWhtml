• vibhūṣitā, (1) (? to Skt. vibhūṣin plus -tā), magni- ficence, splendor, ornate condition: na śraddadhī mahyam imāṃ vibhūṣitāṃ SP 113.10 (vs), he has not believed, ‘this magnificence is mine’; no v.l. in KN; WT cite ms. Ḱ as vibhūṣāṃ (Skt.), which they em. to vibhūtāṃ, implausibly; Tib. ḥbyor ba (read pa), wealth, treasure; vibhūṣāṃ of Ḱ looks like a secondary change, to a familiar Skt. word; all the other three pādas of the stanza are jagatī (sup- porting °ṣitāṃ); the same form prob. occurs in prose in: tāṃ divyāṃ vibhūṣitāṃ (ms. °tān, mere orthographic var.) dṛṣṭvā Av i.68.4, having seen this magnificence (Speyer em. to °ṣikāṃ, which is unrecorded and implausible); (2) (ppp. of vi-bhūṣ-) n. of an apsaras: Kv 3.10.
