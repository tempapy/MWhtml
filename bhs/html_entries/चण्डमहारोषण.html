• Caṇḍamahāroṣaṇa, acc. to Bhattacharya, GOS 26 p. viii, n. of a Bodhisattva, ‘the principal figure in the Tantra of that name’: Sādh 171.15, 172.2 etc. Is he not another name for, or form of, Mañjuśrī? His sādhanas follow those of the latter in Sādh; both have the name or epithet Pañcavīra (read °cīra)-kumāra, q.v. He is in any case an angry personage. [223,1]
