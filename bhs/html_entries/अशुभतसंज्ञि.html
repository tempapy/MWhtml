• aśubhata-saṃjñi(n), engaging in contemplation of the offensiveness (of corpses; see aśubha-bhāvanā, saṃjñā), Śikṣ 328.10 (vs). I take aśubhata- as = aśu- bhatā (m.c.), offensive-ness, so substantially = aśubha (-saṃjñā, with suffix -in). Bendall assumes the adverbial suffix -ta(s). [080,1]
