• saṃjñotpāda, m.c. °pada (cf. -saṃjñām utpādayati, see s.v. saṃjñā 5), formation of ideas or conceptions, notions: read in Mv i.215.6 = ii.18.3 (vs) sādhū ti nirā- miṣehi saṃjñotpadehi (mss. saṃjñāpad° or saṃjñotpād°, the latter unmetr.; Senart adopts the former) kṣapenti taṃ kālaṃ, with cries of approval (the divine guardians of the infant Bodhisattva and his mother) pass the time in productions of ideas (instigation of notions) that are free from worldliness.
