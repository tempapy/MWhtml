• saṃbhinna-pralāpa, m. (cf. prec.; in Pali corresp. to sampha-ppalāpa, the prior member of which is obscure), confused, senseless talk; defined Śikṣ 74.1--2 (vss) pūr- vottarābaddhapadaṃ nirarthakam asaṃgataṃ, abaddhaṃ …proktaṃ; occurs exclusively, or nearly so, as one of the ten akuśala karmapatha, q.v.; abaddha-pralāpa, q.v., used once instead; Mvy 1694 = Tib. tshig bkyal ba, or ṅag ḥkhyal ba, talking nonsense; Mv ii.99.9; Divy 302.8; Gv 155.17; Śikṣ 73.15; 172.1; Dbh 24.18; Bbh 168.16; 304.17; Karmav 79.10.
