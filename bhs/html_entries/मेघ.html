• Megha, (1) n. of a previous incarnation of Śākya- muni under Dīpaṃkara, who predicted his enlighten- ment: Mv i.2.1; 232.1 ff. (corresponds to Divy Sumati (4), q.v., and to Pali Sumedha, while Pali Megha corresp. to our Meghadatta, q.v.); (2) n. of another Bodhisattva, a Dravidian (dramiḍa): Gv 2.7; 72.13 ff.; Śikṣ 36.14 (quotation from Gv 76.26); 95.6 (allusion to events of Gv 73.14 ff.).
