• sostika (nt., = Skt. svastika, cf. Pkt. satthia; in other mgs., Pali sotthika; § 3.117), the cross-legged posture in sitting: niṣaṇṇa °kena LV 241.12 (vs). Despite variants in mss., this is the only true reading, and is supported by Tib. (skyil mo kruṅ), contrary to Foucaux's statement (Notes p. 156; there is in Tib.'s rendering of this line no equivalent of narendraḥ, which is read in Calc. and some mss.). [606,2]
