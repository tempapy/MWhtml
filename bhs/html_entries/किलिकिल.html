• kilikila, nt., and °lā, f. (cf. Skt. kilakilā, and kilaki- lāyate, °layati, also Pali kilikilāyati), a loud noise (onoma- topoetic): nt. Mv ii.410.7 °la- (acc. to text in comp.; but read °lā with mss.; perh. fem.), of noises made by the army of Māra, in attacking the Bodhisattva; °lāni Mv iii.312.13, of applause; fem. Mvy 2800; Divy 459.16, of astonishment; Samādh 19.8 of joy, applause; AsP 203.12 (read kilikilā with most mss. for text kila°), of joy, applause. Usually associated or cpd. with hāhākāra and prakṣve- ḍita. See next. [184,1]
