• sūcaka, m. (cf. Skt. sūcī, sūcikā; Pali sūcikā, both needle and door-bolt), (1) Mvy 5587 °kaḥ = Tib. gzuṅ(s) gzer, which means bar, orig. peg (in a wall; Das), not railing, balustrade (BR) but part of one, = sūcikā; (2) pl. °kāḥ, lit. piercing, needling, ep. of certain ‘winds’ in the body: Śikṣ 248.13 (see s.v. kṣuraka).
