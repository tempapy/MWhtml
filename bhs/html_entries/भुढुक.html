• ? Bhuḍhuka, or better Bhudhuka, ed. at Laṅk 366.14 (vs), but apparently by em.; mss. Pudruka, Bud- dhaka; Tib. Bhu-dhu-ka (dental dh!); I see no reason to assume domal ḍh, but the true form is very incertain. Seems to be the name of an astronomical work: bhu- dhuka-jyotiṣādyāni bhaviṣyanti kalau (so read) yuge. [410,1]
