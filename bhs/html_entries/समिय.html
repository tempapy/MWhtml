• samiya-, acc. to Senart MIndic for samyak-; perfectly: sarvaśaḥ samiya-mārdavānvitā Mv i.64.5 (vs); mss. unmetr. samīyā-. But the word may represent AMg. samiyā- (Skt. *śami-tā), tranquillity, which accords well with mārdava-; or a Pkt. form (AMg. samiya) of Skt. śamita, Pali samita, calm, as separate word (pl.; in either case final a m.c. for ā, as in Senart's interpretation).
