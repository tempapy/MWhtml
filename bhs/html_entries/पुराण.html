• Purāṇa (= Pali id.), n. of a sthapati of Prasenajit, associate of Iṣiḍatta, Divy 77.27; 466.23, or Ṛṣidatta, spelled Riṣidatta in Av i.224.3, and Ṛddhidatta in mss. Av ii.9.7. He, and perhaps his associate, were doubtless referred to in MSV ii.70.4, which must contain a lacuna as well as more than one corruption. See also next. [348,1]
