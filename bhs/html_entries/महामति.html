• Mahāmati, (1) n. of a Bodhisattva who plays a leading rôle in Laṅk, 1.10 etc.; (the same, or another Bo- dhisattva?) Mmk 40.16; 63.4; 68.20; (2) n. of a yakṣa: Mvy 3370; (3) n. of a lay-disciple: Gv 51.11; (4) n. of a king: Gv 360.22; (5) in Laṅk 365.2 (vs) apparently a different person from (1), a pupil of the Buddha Viraja, called Mati (4), q.v., in 365.3 (vs).
