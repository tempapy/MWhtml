• ayuta, nt. (m. in Skt. only Mbh. Crit. ed. 3.21.24; in BHS noted as m. Mvy 7998), in Skt. only defined as 10,000; so also Mvy 8054 = Tib. khri; but oftener = 100 koṭis or 1,000,000,000 = Tib. ther ḥbum, so defined LV 147.20 (cited Mvy 7955), also Mvy 7998, and presumably also 7701, 7827 (in these at least higher than koṭi, and between this and niyuta); in Sukh 31.1 a very much higher number, listed between nayuta and akṣobhya.
