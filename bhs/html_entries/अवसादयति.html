• avasādayati (= Pali apasādayati, in both mgs.; cf. prec. two), (1) rejects, repulses: Mv iii.184.11 tāye māṇa- vikāye…avasādito, rejected by this girl (with whom he was in love); (2) censures, rebukes: Bbh 150.22 (mṛdv- aparādhaṃ mṛdu-vyatikramaṃ…) mṛdvyā (text mṛdhvyā) avasādanikayā avasādayati; so also 151.3; Bbh 107.16 toṣayatā utsāhayatā anavasādayatā, by him gratifying, enthusing, not reproaching; Bbh 179.18--20 bodhisattvaḥ avasādanārhān…pravāsanārhān kliṣṭacitto nāvasādayati, avasādayati vā na ca daṇḍakarmanā samanuśāsti; °yitavyaḥ MSV iii.53.11.
