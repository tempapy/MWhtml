• Reṇu (1) (= Pali id., DN ii.230.24 ff.), n. of the son and successor of King Diśāṃpati: Mv iii.204.11 ff. In LV 171.1 he seems to have the epithet Diśāṃpati himself, and further, by a confusion in the story, he is represented as a previous incarnation of Śākyamuni: Reṇu bhū (= abhūḥ) Diśāṃpati.In both the Mv and the Pali versions it is the purohita Jyotipāla (Mahāgovinda) who was the future Buddha. In Mvy 3580 called Sureṇu. (2) n. of a former Buddha: LV 171.13. [457,1]
