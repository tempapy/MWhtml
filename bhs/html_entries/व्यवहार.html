(āhvānāya saṃketaḥ, etc.); saṃvṛti-°ra Sukh 42.11, see saṃvṛti; (2) motion, gesture: (hasta-) °reṇa (contemptu- ously) uddeṣṭum ārabdhaḥ MSV ii.190.9; so also hasta- vyavahārakeṇa ib. 188.12. [516,2]
