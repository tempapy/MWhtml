• Piṇḍola Bharadvāja (Divy, Mmk, MSV) or Bhārad° (Karmav), = Pali Pi° Bhāradvāja, n. of one of Buddha's disciples, called ‘chief of lion's-roarers (siṃhanādin; so in Pali sīhanādin, °dika)’: Divy 399.30 ff.; 404.1 (represented as still living in the time of Aśoka, to whom he presents himself); Mmk 111.12; Karmav 62.5; MSV i.183.18 ff.
