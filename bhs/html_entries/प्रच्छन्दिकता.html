• -pracchandika-tā, desirous-ness, eager-ness, in tīvra- pracchandikatayā bodhisattvamārgaṃ paryeṣante Gv 492.26, with ardent eagerness…; cf. Pali tibba-chanda. No *pracchanda, eager desire, seems to be recorded anywhere, but it seems implied as an intensive to Skt. chanda, underlying this adjective. [357,2]
