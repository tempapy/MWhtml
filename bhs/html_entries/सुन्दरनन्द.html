• Sundarananda (= Pali id.) = Nanda 1, a half- brother of Śākyamuni (so definitely stated Mv iii.176.13) who became his disciple; asked Yaśodharā to marry him after Śākyamuni's retirement, Mv ii.69.3; 72.7; referred to as a Śākyan youth, Mv ii.25.11; 74.17 ff.; LV 144.15 ff.; 154.2 ff.; as a Buddhist disciple, Mv i.75.1; iii.41.4; SP 2.7; Mvy 1057 (text Sundarī° or °ra°, but Mironov °ra° with no v.l.); Karmav 38.12; 85.9.
