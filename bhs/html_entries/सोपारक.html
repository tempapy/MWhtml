• Sopāraka (once in late Skt., pw; Sopāra, °raya; for Skt. Śūrpāraka; Divy v.l. Sūr°, Pali Suppāraka; see also Supāraga, Saupāraka), n. of a city and country: yadā Thapakarṇi gṛhapati °rakāto (so with mss.)…mahāsa- mudram okasto Mv i.245.3. Sūrpāraka or Śūr° in Divy (24.10 ff.) is the home of this merchant and his brothers, including Pūrṇa; likewise Stavakarṇika belongs to Sau- pāraka in Av ii.166.6; Senart misunderstands and em. wrongly.
