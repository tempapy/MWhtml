• khaḍgin, (1) m., or khaḍgina, nt. (cf. khaḍga), a high number: Mmk 262.16 (vs) nikhaḍgaṃ (q.v.) tad vidur mantrī nikhaḍgaṃ cāpi khaḍginam (follows line 15 cited s.v. khaḍga, and seems to mean that khaḍga, nikhaḍga, and khaḍgin(a) are interchangeable terms); (2) m., a Pratyekabuddha (because he is often compared to a rhi- noceros, Skt. khaḍgin; see s.v. khaḍgaviṣāṇa): Mmk 67.9, 14; 122.7; 156.7; 238.5; also pratyeka-khaḍgin, 108.23; 112.20; 114.21; 169.26.
