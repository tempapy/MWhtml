• Nirvāṇāṅgulimālika (loc. °ke), n. of one or (more likely, with Suzuki and Bendall and Rouse) two works: Laṅk 258.4 (v.l. Nirmāṇ°, one ms. Nirvāṇe 'ṅgu°), cited Śikṣ 133.4 (mss. °aka). The first work, Nirvāṇa(-sūtra), presumably means the (Mahā-) parinirvāṇa-sūtra; for the second see Aṅgulimālika, °līya. [304,2]
