• udaka-rākṣasa, m. (Pali °rakkhasa), water-ogre, who lives in water and devours bathers: tatra ca udakahrade udakarākṣaso (mss. °sa) prativasati punar-punaḥ janaṃ snapayantaṃ māreti Mv ii.77.17 (and in foll. lines); satkāyadṛṣṭy-udakarākṣasa-gṛhītāḥ Dbh 28.25. Also daka- rākṣasa; see s.v. daka. [128,1]
