• galita, of Buddha's voice, perhaps fluent: Mv i.315.3 [Page211-a+ 71] °tam (so or °tam mss.; Senart em. gaditaṃ, but his note retracts the em.) āviṣṭaṃ bhāṣati, metr. dubious; i.171.11 galita-pada-saṃcayavatī, of Buddha's voice. It would perhaps be rash to read agalita, which is used in Pali in the sense of (not dropping,) fluent, distinct, agreeable (CPD). Relation of the two forms obscure. [210,2]
