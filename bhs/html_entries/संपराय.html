• saṃparāya, m. (= Pali id., from Skt. and BHS sāṃparāya; see this), future state, a life after death (cf. abhisaṃparāya): gamanīyo saṃparāyo, one has to go to a future life (i.e. to die) Mv iii.217.10; 219.1; Bbh 221.20 saṃparāya-sukhito; 7.1 °ya-gurukaḥ; as in Pali often contrasted with dṛṣṭa dharma, Mv iii.211.15 = 212.2 dṛṣṭe (dṛṣṭa-) dharme hitārthaṃ vā saṃparāyasukhāya vā; similarly Divy 207.26; Bbh 198.9; Mvy 2982.
