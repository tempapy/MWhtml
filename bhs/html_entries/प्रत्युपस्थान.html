Gv 85.4; (pratyutpannasarvabuddhadharmasamudāgama-) °nena Gv 467.4; (pratyayasya) a-praty° Bbh 34.6, praty° 7; (2) (cf. pratyupasthita; Pali paccupaṭṭhāna, at least as [Page378-b+ 71] v.l. gilāna-pacc° for text gilānupaṭṭhāna DN iii.191.3), waiting on, tending: mātāpitror a-pratyupasthānam Kar- mav 40.11; mātāpitroḥ °naṃ 40.17.
