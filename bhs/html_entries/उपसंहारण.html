• upasaṃhāraṇa (nt.; = prec.), provision, furnishing: Bbh 270.12--13 priyāpriya-saṃyogaviyogopasaṃhāraṇa- tayā, by the fact of provision (for creatures in need) of association or dissociation with pleasant or unpleasant things (respectively), i.e. by the fact that he (the Bodhisattva) sees to it that creatures are thus looked out for.
