• pratighāta, m. (= Pali paṭi°; substantially = prec., but not standardly associated with anunaya; see however Gv 351.24, under anunaya), malice, hatred; seems to be contrasted with maitrī: LV 430.11 aprati- ghāta-maitrī- (a dvandva), non-malice and kindliness; Śikṣ 101.4 pratighātaṃ na janayet, maitracittaḥ sadā bhavet; 128.9; KP 27.7.
