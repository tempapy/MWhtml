• avārṣika, adj. (or subst. ?), (1) (an ascetic practice) that consists of not going abroad in the rainy season: LV 248.18 avārṣikair (sc. ātāpanaparitāpanaiḥ, l. 15); Tib. dbyar mi byuṅ ba; (2) (a monk) who does not observe the (rule for not wandering during the) rains: MSV ii.154.12; read so with v.l. and Mironov in Mvy 9426 (Tib. dbyar gnas su ma zhugs pa).
