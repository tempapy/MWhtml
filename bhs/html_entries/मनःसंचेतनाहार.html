• manaḥsaṃcetanāhāra, m. (= Pali mano-s°; °nā plus āhāra), also saṃcetanikāhāra, one of the four kinds of ‘food’ (see s.v. kavalīkārāhāra), viz. living on hope (in the belief that food is, or will soon be, available; see LaVallée-Poussin, AbhidhK. iii.124 f.): Mvy 2286; the prior member is (manaḥ)saṃcetanā, AbhidhK. iii.121.
