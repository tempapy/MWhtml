• -pṛthakkārakam, adv., in siktha-pṛ°, separating the lumps of rice: na…(we will eat) Mvy 8582; corresp. to Pali sitthāvakārakaṃ, see CPD s.v. avakārakaṃ (‘scat- tering about’). This is supported in sense by Prāt 534.1 which is printed śista-vikiraṃ, with note: ‘Corr. śiṣṭa’; Chin. sans jeter des morceaux; it seems clear that either the MIndic (Pali) sittha, or a Sktized (perh. hyper-Skt.) form of it, was intended; Chin. suggests this rather than śiṣṭa, what is left.
