• apa-savyakam, adv. (= Skt. and Pali °vyam, which in Skt. means to or on the right, also to or on the left, see BR 1.296, 5.1009; in Pali only the latter, except for a lexical citation borrowed from a Skt. lexicon, CPD), in Mmk 125.13 kṛtvā vā apasavyakam, (if I understand the passage rightly, can only mean) keeping on the right, i.e. showing respect, not disrespect.
