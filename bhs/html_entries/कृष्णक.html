• kṛṣṇaka, (1) adj. (= Skt. kṛṣṇa plus ka), fem. °ikā, black, blackish: Divy 352.20 kṛṣṇikāṃ paṭṭikāṃ; 23, 24, 26 kṛṣṇikānāṃ (sc. paṭṭikānāṃ; all these prose); on the basis of these we must surely read in Divy 352.19 (prose) kṛṣṇikā paṭṭikā (ed. kṛṣṇika-p°) dattā pāṇḍurikā ca; in these there is certainly no dim. force; there is prob. dim. (pejorative) force in SP 113.12 (vs) kucelakā (so read) kṛṣṇaka hīnasattvāḥ (repulsively black in complexion); na ca (cā?) chavī kṛṣṇika (m.c. for °kā) tasya bhoti SP 293.2 (vs); (2) n. of two nāga kings: Māy 248.1 (see s.v. kṛṣṇa, 4).
