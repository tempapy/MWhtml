• pailottika, adj. (see also prec.), thin (of a blanket): MSV ii.90.7 °kena vā dvipuṭena, in contrast with ghanena vā ekapuṭena; Tib. seṅ bzhig na, thin; made of thin cloth (of a robe), or pieced out by adding such a cloth, ib. 160.15; 161.2; here Tib. gso maḥi seṅ ras (same for plotikā, q.v.).
