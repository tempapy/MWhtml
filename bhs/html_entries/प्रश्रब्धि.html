• praśrabdhi, prasr°, f. (= Pali passaddhi; see prec. and next, also pratipraś°), alleviation, calming, of pain etc., sarvopadrava-prasrabdhi- Śikṣ 255.14; (after bodily pain has been alleviated, in the prec.,) sa kāyikaṃ prasrab- dhi-sukhaṃ labdhvā…Av i.32.4; hence, calm, serenity, lack of any disturbance, bodily or mental, kāya-prasrabdhiḥ citta-°dhiḥ Bbh 110.6; (kāyika-)caitasika-prasrabdhi-jana- kaṃ Bbh 207.14; praśrabdhisukha- Gv 165.22; (mis- printed °mukha-) 189.12; prasrabdhiḥ (alone) Mvy 1940; as one (5th in the list) of the seven (saṃ)bodhyaṅga, q.v., Mvy 993; Dharmas 49; LV 34.7; Dbh 39.8 (so also Pali passaddhi).
