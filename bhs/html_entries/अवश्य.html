• avaśya, osa (m. or nt.; = AMg. osa; Skt. avaśyāya, once Lex. avaśyā, f.), hoarfrost, as symbol of transitoriness, used of life or worldly things: LV 214.8 (vs) osavindūpamā …śūnyasvabhāvā(ḥ), like drops of hoarfrost (fleeting and vain); Samādh 22.6 jīviti svapnanibhe cali 'vaśye (Réga- mey's note states that Tib. renders hoarfrost, which he unwisely abandons); Śikṣ 18.12 (vs, cited from Candra- pradīpa Sūtra = Samādh, but not the same vs) jīvite cañcale 'vaśye māyāsvapnanibhopame. In the LV osa could be m.c. for osā = Skt. (Lex.) avaśyā; but the loc. (a)vaśye in the others points to a m. or nt. stem; AMg. osa is given by Ratnach. as m. [076,1]
