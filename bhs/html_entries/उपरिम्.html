• uparim, adv., postp. (= Skt. upari; 3 times in prose of Mv, otherwise in vss), (1) adv. on high, above: SP 190.5 upariṃ ca khe dundubhayo vineduḥ; 325.3 u° ca devā 'bhihananti tūryān; 331.5; 364.7; Mv ii.62.8 (vs) heṣṭā…upariṃ ca; (2) adv., further: Mv iii.447.8 (prose) noparim itthatvam (q.v.); (3) postp. with gen., above, on top of: Mv ii.137.11 (prose) mīḍhaparvatasya uparim anupalipyamāno caṃkramaṃ cakrame (mss., Senart em. caṃkrame); Mv ii.15.4--5 (prose) bodhisattvamātur na kvacid upariṃ (so mss.!) uparimena gacchati, does not pass aloft anywhere above the bodhisattva's mother. (Senart's em. is bad.) [139,2]
