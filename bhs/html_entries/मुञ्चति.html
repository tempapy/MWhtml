• muñcati, emits words, speech; in Skt. and Pali used with object vācaṃ or the like, but here absolutely, no object noun expressed (also pramuñcati, vimuñcati, in the same context): kalyāṇikāṃ vimuñceta naiva muñ- ceta pāpikām Ud vii.8 (vācaṃ is clearly understood with the f. adj., but does not occur in the passage); similarly viii.9 muñcamāno.
