• vyābādhati, °te, °dhayate, also written vyāvādhati, and at least in mss. vyāvadhati, °te, vyāvahati (cf. vyābā- dha, °dhika, a-vyā°; = Pali vyābādheti, byā°), oppresses, injures, violates, harms: na kaṃcit sattvaṃ vyābādhati Mv i.207.2; ii.10.12 (here vyāpādayati; as Childers and PTSD point out, there is confusion between these two roots; cf. s.v. avyābādha); iii.341.10 (here mss. vyāvahati, Senart em. vyābādhati); with gen. of personal object, na ca tasya rāgo vyāvadhiṣyate SP 419.5 (v.l. vyāvādh°; WT vyābādh°); na teṣāṃ rāgo vyāvādhiṣyati SP 481.4 (v.l. vyāvah°; WT vyābādh°); with acc. object, na cāsya kāyaṃ vyābādhate sma LV 152.20; netrāṇi vyābādhayate mūrchāṃ ca saṃjanayati Divy 105.12 (prose).
