• vijñāpaka, adj. or subst. (= Pali viññāpaka; Skt. vijñāpayati plus -aka), instructing; instructor: in su-vi° SP 301.1, 10; LV 403.5, 9; 404.8, Bhvr., lit. having a good instructor, but in the sense of making things easy for an instructor, i.e. easily instructed (proved by SP passage, s.v. viśodhaka, q.v.).
