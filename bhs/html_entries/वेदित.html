• vedita (nt.; orig. ppp. of Skt. vedayate, and so = vedayita, both forms occurring in Pali, but vedita in PTSD recorded only as ppp.), sensation, feeling: in saṃjñā- vedita-nirodha, q.v.; also in Śikṣ 24.11 aparyātta-vedita- cittaḥ, of a Bodhisattva, not having his sensations and mind overpowered (by disturbing elements; wrongly Ben- dall and Rouse; see aparyātta).
