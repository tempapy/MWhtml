• pṛthivī-parpaṭaka, m. (so read with Index; text °parvaṭaka; Mironov °paryaṭaka, vv.ll. °parv°, °parp°), prob. = bhūmi-, bhū-p°, a kind of edible mushroom: Mvy 5287 = Tib. sa zhag, lit. earth-fat (-grease), acc. to Das bitumen (or rock-oil, petroleum); follows pṛthivī-rasa and precedes vanalatā and pṛthivī-maṇḍa.
