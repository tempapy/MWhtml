• lasa, adj. (otherwise recorded only in neg. a-lasa), active, quick-moving: suku (= Skt. śuko) lasu (= laso; only v.l. in mss. rasu) guṇadhara…LV 167.1 (vs), (when you were once incarnate as) a virtuous parrot, quick-darting. No other interpretation seems possible, unless we em. to a deriv. of lap-, cf. AMg. lava, speaking, and BHS -lāpika. Tib. seems to omit the word. [461,2]
