• Etadagra, n. of a work, or section of one, which = or contains the Dakṣiṇāvibhaṅga sūtra: Karmav 156.12 Bhagavataitadagre Dakṣiṇāvibhaṅge sūtra uktam. It is not the Etadagga-vagga AN i.23 ff. (cf. Agratā-sūtra); nor does Lévi's identification with AN Book 4, No. 15 (ii.17) seem certain, since the precise quotation does not occur there. [155,2]
