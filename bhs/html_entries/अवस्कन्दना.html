• avaskandanā (cf. Skt. avaskandana, nt., accusation, legal attack; to ava-skand-, attack, usually in military sense), (verbal) attack; injurious speech; °skandanā is read for °sphaṇḍanam in a text parallel to Śikṣ 172.1 idam agraṃ pāruṣyāṇāṃ yad utāryāṇāṃ avasphaṇḍanam, see Bendall's note p. 408; Tib. gnod pa, injure.
