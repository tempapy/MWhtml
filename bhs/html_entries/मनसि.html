• manasi- (also manasī-, q.v.)-karoti (= Pali ma- nasi-k°; very rare in Skt., essentially Buddhist word; cf. manasi-, manasī-, manas-kāra, which seem not to occur in Skt. at all), concentrates the mind on, reflects (intently) on, with acc. (a rare case of gen. s.v. manasī-k°): sādhu ca suṣṭhu ca °kuru Mvy 6315; SP 38.10; 346.6; Sukh 5.1; °karoti LV 378.16; °kurvanti SP 72.15 (KN printed, as sometimes elsewhere, as two words); °karotha Mv i.334.1, 2, etc.; °kurvāṇasya Gv 504.19; °kariṣyanti SP 478.7; °kartavya Av ii.140.8; °kṛta (ppp.) SP 60.14; very common, often (as in some of the above) with parallel synonyms like cintayati; esp. with yoniśaḥ (or ayoniśaḥ), q.v. for examples.
