• Bharadvāja (cf. Bhāra°, the only form recorded for Pali in DPPN), (1) n. of a disciple of Śākyamuni (in lists of names; not clear which of the several Pali disciples named Bhāra° is meant): SP 2.6; Sukh 92.8, see also Piṇḍola Bhara°; (2) gotra-name of the Buddha Candra- sūryapradīpa: SP 18.5; (3) n. of a yakṣa: Māy 236.26; (4) n. of a monk, former incarnation of Śākyamuni: MSV i.211.3 ff.
