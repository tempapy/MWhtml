• paśca, adj., subst. (= AMg. paccha- in comp., M. paccha, separate word; cf. pacchā, pacche, and paścā- kāla), later, last (of time): yaśodharā sarvapaścā (last of all) āgatā Mv ii.72.16; paści (m.c. for paśce) kāle Samādh p. 10, line 1 (so read); p. 12, line 28; p. 19, line 20 (kālasmi); as subst., later time, id. p. 19 line 22 sa paści nirvṛtaḥ. In SP 45.13 (vs) na tu (read tatu with WT) paśca bhāsate, paśca = paścāt m.c.; in SP 92.13 (vs) paścakāle doubtless also m.c. for paścāt-(or paścā-, q.v.)-kāle. [338,1]
