Maitreya) Sukh 64.7 ff.; 92.10; (2) n. of a future Buddha, but seemingly distinguished from Maitreya: Mv iii.330.8; (3) n. of an attendant on the Bodhisattva (Śākyamuni) in a previous birth: Jm 3.4 (query: to be identified with Maitreya?).
