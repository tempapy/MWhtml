• bāhulika, adj. (= Pali id. or bāhullika), luxurious in manner of life, acc. to Tib. esp. in eating, gluttonous, gourmandizing; always with śaithilika: (eṣa sa… śramaṇo) Gautama āgacchati sma, śaithiliko bāhulikaḥ LV 407.19; (same situation) Mv ii.241.3 (Senart wrongly em. °kaṃ, mss. °ko); iii.329.4 (here Senart keeps °ko of nss.); śaithilikā bhavanti, bāhulikā bhavanti Śikṣ 64.4.
