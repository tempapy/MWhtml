• baddhamālā, having a garland bound on, n. of the 2d bodhisattva-bhūmi; so read for ed. °mānā Mv i.76.14. It is inconceivable that such a name as the mss. present should be applied to a bhūmi; and my em. is supported in sense by puṣpamaṇḍitā, n. of the 3d bhūmi immediately following. This word cannot, therefore, be cited (with Renou, Etudes de gram. scte., 1936, p.18) as an example of -māna added to a ppp. (§ 34.1). [397,1]
