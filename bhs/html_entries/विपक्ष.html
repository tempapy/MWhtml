• -vipakṣa, adj., in karma deśāntara-vipakṣaṃ, an act which has its fruition (result) in a foreign country: Karmav 30.20; 50.12, 14; 57.4; 64.13. So the mss. always, kept by Lévi in 30.20, otherwise em. to vipākaṃ; but the form is clearly a hyper-Sktism for MIndic (Pali) vipakka = Skt. vipakva, confused with MIndic vipakkha = vipakṣa. [490,2]
