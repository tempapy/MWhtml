However, Ratnach. records (without citation from literature) an AMg. vatta = Skt. vyakta, defined singing while making the syllables and sounds distinct, an excellent mode of singing. May not the Pali vattāni, and our word, be equivalents of this? Our word might then be a false Sktization, or error, instead of vyaktāni.] [466,2]
