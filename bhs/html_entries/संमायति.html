• saṃmāyati (as in Skt. Dhātup., 4th class pres. to sam-mā-; not recorded otherwise), is equal to (instr.): gaṇikāvīthijanena °ti (so one ms. correctly; Senart em. wrongly) Mv ii.173.12, it (the crowd) was equal (in number) to the people of the harlot's street. Perhaps merely pass. of sam-mā-, with act. ending, lit. is equated; but māyet AsP 157.19 supports a māyati = Skt. māti, act. [580,2]
