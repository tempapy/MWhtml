• Varakalyāṇa (= Pali id.), n. of a king, son of Ka- lyāṇa, q.v., and father of Upoṣadha, q.v. In Mv i.348.8 text is corrupt; the form Rava (v.l. Rāva) prob. represents Vara(-kalyāṇa), but in one ms. seems also confused with Roca, q.v., who should have been named earlier in the list. [470,1]
