• kāmakārika-tā, condition (or power) of making at will: Mv iii.335.14 (repeated 16, 18, etc.) (idaṃ rūpaṃ ce, v.l. ced, bhikṣavaḥ ātmā abhaviṣyat,) na ca (so read with [Page177-a+ 71] mss., Senart va) rūpaṃ ābādhāya duḥkhāya saṃvarteta, ṛdhyāc ca rūpe kāmakārikatā, evaṃ me rūpaṃ bhavatu evaṃ mā bhavatu (etc.),…one could make what he pleased in regard to his form… See in Pali Vin. i.13.19 ff., essentially the same passage (but without this word).
