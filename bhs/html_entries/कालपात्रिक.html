• kāla-pātrika, adj. or subst. m., (a monk) with black (alms-) bowl: Mvy 8749; rendered lit. in Tib. and Chin.; the Kyōtō ed. suggests that it means a bad, unworthy monk, nothing that it is followed by saṃjñā-bhikṣu and pratijñā-(bh°), qq.v.; this is confirmed MSV iv.67.2, 3 (term of abuse). See kālapattrika. [180,1]
