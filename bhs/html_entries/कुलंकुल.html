• kulaṃkula, m. (= Pali kolaṃkola), one destined to be reborn in several families before liberation: Mvy 1011 = Tib. rigs nas rigs su skye ba; Dharmas 103 deva-k° manuṣya-k°. One of the stages of a śrāvaka; cf. ekavīcika. In corresp. Pali passage AN i.233.15 kolaṃkolo hoti dve vā tīṇi vā kulāni saṃdhāvitvā saṃsāritvā dukkhassa antaṃ karoti. See also kulākula. [188,1]
