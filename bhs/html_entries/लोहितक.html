• lohitaka, m. (1) some sort of insect: °kā prāṇakā kālaśīrṣakā (bodhisattvasya) pādatalehi yāvaj jānumāṇ- ḍalāni chādayitvā asthānsuḥ Mv ii.137.4; repeated 138.19 with °ka-prāṇakā; (2) n. of a town: Mv iii.328.2; also Lohitavastuka, 327.20, and Rohitavastu, q.v.; see also Kamaṇḍaluka; (3) n. of two nāga kings: Māy 247.14; cf. Skt. Lohita, BR s.v., 2 k. See also lohitakopadhāna. [465,2]
