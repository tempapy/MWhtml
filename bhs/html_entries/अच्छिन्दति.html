• acchindati (= Pali id., for Skt. āchinatti; Senart always emends to ācch°), (1) takes away, removes, with gen. of person, acc. of thing: acchinditvā Mv ii.434.3, 9 (teṣāṃ bhrātṛṇāṃ); iii.7.17 (gulikām); in Mv iii.403.12, 14, read ahaṃ na (14…naiva) kasyaci demi nāpi acchindāmi (with mss., misinterpreted by Senart), I do not give to anyone nor take away from anyone; (2) abolishes, puts away: acchet(t)va (tṛṣṇāṃ) Mv iii.285.6, 9 (= Pali acchejja, acchijja, same vs, SN i.127.3, 6).
