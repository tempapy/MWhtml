• Asaṃpramoṣa, m., n. of a samādhi: Mvy 526; ŚsP 1416.22 (misprinted °modho); 1417.2. Cf. Bodhicittāsaṃ- pramoṣa. As common noun (neg. of Skt. saṃpramoṣa, disappearance, loss, see s.v. saṃpramuṣyate) the word hardly needs recording; it means non-disappearance, non- deprivation, non-loss, e.g. Mvy 785; 961; etc. [083,2]
