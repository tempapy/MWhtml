• apagata-kālaka, adj. (= Pali °kāḷaka; cf. kālaka 2), having no black spots, of a garment: Divy 617.8 vastram apa° (in Pali also vatthaṃ apa°); MSV iii.142.19 id.; also (as in Pali) free from moral guilt (a delinquent monk attains this state when all his penitential rites have been correctly performed), MSV iii.76.18; 79.1.
