• abhyantaroṣṭha, adj. (or ābhy°; saṃdhi makes ambiguity), lit. having a lip in between (the two halves of a cleft lip), referring to an extreme form of hare-lip in which a flap hangs down between the two halves of the cleft lip: SP 350.8 nābhyantaroṣṭho…bhavati. [060,2]
