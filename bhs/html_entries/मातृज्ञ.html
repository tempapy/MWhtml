• mātṛjña, adj. (ending app. analog. to Skt. kṛtajña), mother-honoring: Mv iii.131.16. Almost always followed by pitṛjña, śrāmaṇya, brāhmaṇya, honoring fathers, monks, brahmans; corresp. to Pali matteyya (or me°), petteyya, sāmañña, brahmañña; also (in both languages) neg. forms in a-: LV 24.15 = Mv i.198.6 = ii.2.2; Mv i.46.9; ii.423.17; Bbh 267.5; mātāpitṛjña-tā, śrāmaṇya-tā, brāhmaṇya-tā Karmav 40.16.
