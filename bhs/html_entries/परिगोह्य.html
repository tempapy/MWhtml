• ? parigohya, in Mv i.196.2 tripauruṣa-parigohyāni, is or represents a word meaning circumference (epithet of iṣikāni, so ed., see iṣika); mss. corrupt, see Senart's note. The same word, whatever it was, should surely be read as final member of the word read by Senart tripauruṣoc- cāni iii.228.13 (see s.v. udvedha). [321,2]
