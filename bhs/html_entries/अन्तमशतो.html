• antamaśato (°sato), adv. (blend of antamaśas and antatas, qq.v.), so much as, Ger. sogar: (Mv i.7.10, Senart °masato, but mss. indicate rather antaśas;) Mv i.211.13 = ii.15.12 °masato (vv.ll. °śato, antamato); iii.92.14 °sato; in Mv i.211.5 Senart antamaśato, mss. corruptly anasato; parallel ii.15.5 antaso.
