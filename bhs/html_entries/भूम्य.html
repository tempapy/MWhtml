• bhūmya, adj. (= Pali bhumma, of gods, also crea- tures, bhūtāni), of the earth; common in Mv, not noted elsewhere (= bhauma); almost always used of a class of gods, with deva, q.v.: Mv i.40.11, 14; 229.11, 14: 239.20; 240.3; 332.15; ii.138.6 ff.; 314.4; 349.20; iii.319.8 (same passage LV 401.1, bhauma); 334.13; on ii.348.16 see s.v. deva (read with mss. bhūmyāṃ va carā); also, rarely, (bhūtāni) bhūmyāni Mv i.290.16.
