• anyena, adv. (1) elsewhere, to another place: Mv iii.425.13 aho punar me āśramāto…anyena prakrameyā, but I wish he would depart elsewhere, away from my hermit- age; (2) anyena…anyena (= Pali aññena…aññena), by one way…by another: Mv iii.59.18 anyena śāriputro …anyena maudgalyāyano. [042,1]
