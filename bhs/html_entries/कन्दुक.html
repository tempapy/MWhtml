• kanduka, and kandu-kāra(ka), or (vv.11.) kaṇḍu°, m. (presumably from Skt. kandu, AMg. id. or kaṇḍu, iron pan), an artisan of some sort, presumably maker (seller) of iron pans; only in nom. pl. in long lists of persons of various occupations: Mv iii.113.9 kaṇḍukā(ḥ), v.l. kandrukā; 442.14 mss. kandukā(ḥ), Senart em. kaṇḍ°; 443.2 kandu-kārakā(ḥ), v.l. kaṇḍukāro (intending °rā).
