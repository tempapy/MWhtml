• -kṛta, (1) in dṛṣṭi-kṛta, q.v., lit. perh. (what has) become…, or (subst.) matter of…; seems = (dṛṣṭi-)gata; (2) acc. to Senart = kṛtya (q.v. 2) as equivalent of adj. formation or gen. case-form, in Mv ii.274.4 udyānakṛtā āsanā, allegedly les sièges du jardin. But does it not mean quite literally and simply seats made in the park? In Mv ii.245.5 read with mss. karaṇḍe mālakṛto, in the garland- maker's basket (stem māla-kṛt). [190,1]
