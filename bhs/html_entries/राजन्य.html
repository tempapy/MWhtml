• rājanya, adj. (seems not recorded elsewhere in this sense), belonging to a king, royal: of a park, udyāna, Mv ii.112.9, 11; Senart em. rājakya, q.v.; but he keeps rājanyaṃ ii.452.1 (of an āmravanaṃ) and °nyāni kaṇṭhe- guṇāni (so mss.) 463.3. In the vicinity of all these passages rājakya occurs; possibly it should always be read (with Senart on 112.9, 11). [454,1]
