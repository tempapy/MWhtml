• utsarpita, adj. (ppp. of ud plus sṛp, caus.), app. laid by as balance, left over (from a sum of money): Divy 23.11 aparam utsarpitam; 16 (nāsmābhiḥ kiṃcid uddha- rīkṛtaṃ) kiṃ tv aparam utsarpitaṃ tiṣṭhati, (we have not incurred any debt,) but the rest remains put aside as left over. [127,1]
