• utphikā, Mvy 9542 (so Mironov), or utpika (m. or nt. ?), 9541 (and v.l. Mironov), n. of a disease; Tib. either glo baḥi (of the lungs), or mgoḥi (of the head), glog pa (on which see rajata; ulcer? cancer?). Chin. apparently a disease characterized by insanity. See also uvyadha. [126,1]
