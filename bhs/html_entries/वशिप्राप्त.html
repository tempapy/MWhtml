• vaśiprāpta, adj. (= Pali vasippatta, acc. to PTSD only in comp. with ceto-, e.g. AN ii.6.17, glossed in comm. iii.5.5 cittavasībhāvaṃ patto, evarūpo khīṇāsavo hoti; idha pana anāgāmī kathito), arrived at (state of being) master, in control; elsewhere vaśitā-pr° (see vaśitā); see also s.v. vaśin: note Mv i.198.4 = ii.2.1 kulavaśiprāpta, attained to mastery over (other) families, said of the family in which the Bodhisattva is born in his last existence; the LV 24.14 parallel to this has vaśitā for vaśi; cetovaśi- prāpta, of a Buddha, Mv i.34.12, cf. Pali, above, and LV 425.22 s.v. vaśin; sa (Maitreyaḥ) vaśiprāptaḥ sarvabodhi- sattvavaśitāsu Gv 489.24; anuttarajñānajñeya-vaśiprāp- tena (of Buddha) Divy 210.4; anuttarajñānajño vaśiprāpto (of Buddha) Divy 546.29; see also vaśībhāvaprāpta.
