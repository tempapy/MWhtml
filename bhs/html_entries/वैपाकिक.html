• vaipākika, adj., subst. (Skt. vipāka plus -ika), produced by maturation (of actions): in Laṅk 283.4, 11 said of a class of Buddhas, contrasted with nairmāṇika, q.v.; in Laṅk 34.2 vipāka-stha is used as equivalent; in Laṅk 292.13 °kikād, from (as a result of) maturation (of actions), seemingly subst., one of four sources of abhijñā.
