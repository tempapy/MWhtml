• mithyājīva, m. (= Pali micchājīva), wrong way of getting a living; for a monk, there are five such: Bbh 168.23, listed 21--22 as kuhanā etc., also listed Mvy 2493--2497, see s.v. kuhana (or °nā); the others are lapana (°nā), naiṣpeṣikatā (°tva), naimittikatā (°tva), and lābhena lābha-niścikīrṣā (°ṣutā), or °niṣpādanā.
