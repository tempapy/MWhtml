• aruṇodghāṭa- (Mv), or aruṇodghāṭana- (LV), break of dawn (= Skt. aruṇodaya), cpd. with -kāla-samaye (kāla once omitted): Mv i.229.7 (here Senart em. °ṇopagh°, wrongly); ii.133.7; 284.16; 415.18; 422.6; 431.11; LV 345.20; 350.8. The stems udghāṭa and °ṭana both occur in Skt. in mg. opening, but do not seem to be used of the dawn. [065,2]
