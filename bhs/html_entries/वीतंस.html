• vītaṃsa (m.; Pali id.; Skt. see below), falcon: sa hatas tv itaraṃ hanti °seneva pakṣiṇaḥ Ud xiv.2, he however, himself destroyed, destroys others, as birds (are killed) by a falcon; so, acc. to Chakravarti, Tib. (khra) and Chin.; same word in same vs in Pali Therag. 139, rendered PTSD and Mrs. Rhys Davids decoy-bird, which is less plausible; in Skt. only Lex. and once in Harṣac. (pw), said to mean bird-net, snare, or any implement used in catching birds, which here is impossible. [505,2]
