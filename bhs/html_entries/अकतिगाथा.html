• akati-gāthā (cf. Pkt. akai, unnumbered, infinite), having numberless (a-kati) verses, or an infinite(ly numerous) verse; of the sāvitrī stanza, Divy 638.2. In an examination on Vedic lore: paṭhatu bhavān sāvitrīṃ. (Answer:) [Page001-b+ 61] akatigāthā.--katyakṣarā sāvitrī, katigaṇḍā, katipadā. (Answer:) caturviṃśatyakṣarā, trigaṇḍā, aṣṭākṣarapadā.
