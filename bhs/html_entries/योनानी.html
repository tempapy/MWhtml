• yonānī (= Skt. yavanānī, Pat. on Pāṇ.4.1.49, Vārt. 3), Greek writing, in a list of scripts; § 22.10; so read for mss. yonārī (Senart yāvanī, more remote and less plausible): Mv i.135.5. Followed by brahma-vāṇī, for which LV 125.21 has brahmavali-lipi; and before this, Tib. on LV ya ba na-ḥi yi ge, writing of the Yavanas (not in Skt. text of LV). Cf. śakānī. [448,2]
