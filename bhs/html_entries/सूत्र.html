• sūtra, nt. (also sūtrānta, q.v.), (1) (= Pali sutta) discourse, as a type of Buddhist sacred text (pravacana), one of twelve, Mvy 1267, or nine, Dharmas 62; (2) also = sūtra-piṭaka, q.v. (= Pali sutta-piṭaka), the (collection of) discourses, one of the three grand divisions of the Buddhist canon: Mvy 1412; Divy 333.7; sūtravinayābhi- dharmeṇa Laṅk 290.8. [604,2]
