• tala, m. or nt. (= AMg. id. = Skt. bhūtala), surface of the ground: yehi iha kīṭakamardanāni vā kārāpitāni bhavanti talamardanāni vā…Mv i.21.12; tala-m° acc. to Senart stamping on the ground; but perhaps breaking up the surface of the ground, as in digging; reprobated as injurious to living creatures.
