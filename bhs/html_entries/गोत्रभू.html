• gotrabhū, f. (cf. Pali id., a member of the religious communion, see s.v. gotra, 1), in Laṅk 266.2 āśritā sarva- bhūteṣu gotrabhūs tarkavarjitā, nivartate kriyāmuktā jñānajñeyavivarjitā, apparently a fem. collective or abstract, the ‘communion of saints’, corresponding to the [Page216-b+ 71] Pali masc. which refers to an individual person. Suzuki, the original source, which is certainly impossible; the word must have some relation to the Pali word, on which see especially Childers, and Lévi, Sūtrāl. iii.1, note. [216,1]
