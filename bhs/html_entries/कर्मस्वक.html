• karmasvaka, adj. (= Pali kammassaka), having (one's own) action as one's property, i.e. subject to the inevitable results of one's own action (and not involved in any other person's): Śikṣ 46.18 (vs) (bhikṣūṇāṃ bhin- navṛttānāṃ parivādaṃ niśamya ca,) karmasvakā bhavi- ṣyāmo…(substantially = we will mind our own business). [171,1]
