• udguraṇa, or udgūr°, or udgīr°, nt. (ud plus gṛ [girati? or gurate?]; Skt. records udgoraṇa once), brandishing (of weapons): Mvy 8472 udguraṇam (Mironov udgūr°, v.l. udgur°) = Tib. gzas pa, brandishing (Das); SP 271.9 (vs) daṇḍa-udgūraṇāni, by em., kept without note in WT; mss. udgīr° (except Kashgar rec. which is altered).
