• Utkaṭa, m., nt., or °ṭā, f., n. of a town (droṇamukha, °khya, q.v.): Mvy 5285 °ṭo nāma droṇamukham; Divy 620.12 °ṭaṃ nāma droṇa° (acc.), 28 utkaṭadroṇamukhyaṃ; 621.10 yenotkaṭaṃ droṇamukhaṃ (nom., nt.), 19 °ṭān [Page120-b+ 71] (abl.); fem. 620.21 °tāṃ nāma droṇamukhaṃ (acc.), °ṭā 26. From a verbally close Pali parallel DN i.87.6 it appears that the town called in Pali Ukkaṭṭhā (see DPPN) is the same; see Puṣkarasārin. [120,1]
