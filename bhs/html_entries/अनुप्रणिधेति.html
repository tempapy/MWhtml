• anu-praṇidheti (not in Pali), = praṇidheti, makes a vow (to persist towards enlightenment); °dheti Mv i.119.15; °dhemi (mss. anuparidehi) 323.1; °dhesi, aor., 335.11; °hitaṃ, ppp., i.63.3. See anupraṇidhi, which is commoner than the verb. If there is any difference be- tween this and praṇi° without anu, it does not appear clearly; anu does not necessarily imply after (a gift or meritorious act), tho this often precedes; in Mv i.323.1 however none is mentioned.
