• lehari, m. (so Kyoto ed., text, v.l. lohāri), or (Index) leharika, Mironov lohariḥ, vv.ll. lehari, lehāri, lehārika, courier, letter-carrier: Mvy 3816 (precedes lekhahārika, q.v.) = Tib. baṅ chen pa. Presumably related to JM. lehāriya, on which see Meyer, Kauṭ. Arth. 679 (derives it from lekhacārika; perh. rather from lekhahārika, MIndic leha°, with haplology? the two forms are synonyms and bracketed in Mvy). Prob. read in Mvy lehāri or °rika. [463,2]
