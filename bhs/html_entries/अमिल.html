• amila (v.l. āmila, q.v.), m. (AMg. amila, ‘a cloth made in the Amila country; a woollen cloth’, Ratnach.; there is no country called Amila in Kirfel; cf. also Pali āmilāka, a certain kind of woolen cloth), Mvy 9172 = Tib. beḥu ras yug chen, which seems to mean about what the Chin. means, viz., large cloth of calf's hair.
