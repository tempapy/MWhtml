• anavalīna, anolīna, adj. (= Pali anolīna), not shrinking, not downcast (so CPD), not despondent; ano° SP 279.10; Śikṣ 100.17 (ms., wrongly em. to anālīnaḥ; Tib. cited as zhum pa med, not faint-hearted); parallel with viśārada confident SP 278.9; 354.13; aprakampyo'navalīno 'navadīrṇo LV 323.17; anavalīna-citta with not downcast mind SP 324.11; Gv 202.5.
