• avyavacāra, m., (neg. of vyavacāra, q.v.), non- consideration, contempt, despising (Tib. khyad du gsod pa, despise): Mvy 8510, in śikṣāpadadravyatāvyavacāraḥ, which I analyze °dravyatā-avyavacāraḥ, as the Tib. and the sense seem to require (the situation is that of Vin. iv. 143.16--19; the Pali equivalent is vivaṇṇaka, line 19). It seems hardly possible that vyavacāra, without a neg., should have this mg. [079,2]
