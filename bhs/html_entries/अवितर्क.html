• avitarka(-dhyāyin), (= Pali avitakka-jhāyī, SN 1.126.27), (devoted to the [2d] stage of dhyāna) in which there is no ratiocination: Mv iii.284.4, Senart avitarka- (mss. avitakāyaṃ, avitarkaṃ kāyaṃ)-dhyāyī (mss. -dhyā- yino, which is possible in sense and meter and should perhaps be kept, despite the Pali parallel -jhāyī).
