• Bhaiṣajyaguruvaiḍūrya- (also °dūrya-)-prabha, n. of a Tathāgata: ŚsP 1.10 (°dū°), and (m.!) of a work in which he is prominent, Mvy 1404; the latter called °pra- bha-sūtra, Śikṣ 13.12; the name adds -rāja after °prabha-, Śikṣ 174.5 (in citation from the work), etc.; Mmk 7.9 (°dū°); the work is called °prabharāja-sūtra Śikṣ 174.1.
