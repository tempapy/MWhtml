• tṛṃhaṇa(-tā), (Skt. Gr., n. act. from the Vedic and Gr. root tṛh), crushing: vātamaṇḍalībhūtaṃ sarvāvaraṇa- nivaraṇa-tṛṃhaṇa-tayā Gv 495.22--23, it is like a whirl- wind, because it crushes all obstructions and hindrances. But Śikṣ 6.6, citing this passage, reads °tṛṇa-vikiraṇatayā, scattering like grass, for °tṛṃh°. [256,1]
