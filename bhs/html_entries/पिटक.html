• piṭaka, nt., m. (= Pali id.), = prec.; of the Buddhist canon, in tripiṭaka, q.v.; also in Bodhisattva-piṭaka, collection of writings on bodhisattvas: Mmk 22.12 (°kaṃ… bhāṣiṣye, referring to Mmk itself or its doctrine), et passim in Mmk (not noted elsewhere, but cf. -piṭakīya); (also, m., as in Skt., blister, pustule, swelling on the skin: [krodhāvi- ṣṭasya mahānagnasya] yāval latāṭe piṭakās tiṣṭhanti…Gv 504.6.) On piṭakā see piṭṭakā. [344,2]
