• gṛhāvāsa, m. (= Pali gharāvāsa; cf. Skt. gṛha-vāsa), living at home, in the householder's state, contrasted with ascetic life: RP 12.16 (prose), where Finot em. gṛha°; Jm 181.21 (mss.; Kern em. gṛha°); Mv iii.50.12, text gṛhā°, to be sure with v.l. gṛha°. Pali seems to support gṛhā° adequately, tho elsewhere (e.g. Mv ii.69.1; 117.19) the regular Skt. gṛha° occurs. [214,2]
