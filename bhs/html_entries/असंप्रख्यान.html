• a-saṃprakhyāna, nt. (neg. of saṃ°, q.v.), lack of clarity, obscurity, confusion: Mvy 2672 (follows saṃ°); 2478 = Tib. mi gsal ba (in a list of vices and faults). Note that saṃprakhyāna, q.v., is equated with a- saṃmoṣa, non-confusion, implying synonymity of saṃ- moṣa and asaṃprakhyāna. [083,2]
