• abhiprasāda (m., to next, or its caus.; cf. abhi- prasanna; Pali abhipassāda, said to mean only faith, belief), either the making well-disposed, or the making to believe: Mvy 8352 an-abhiprasannānām abhiprasādāya; Divy 423.8 buddhaśāsanābhiprasādārthaṃ, in order to make well-disposed, or believing (in).
