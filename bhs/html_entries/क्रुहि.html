• [kruhi, Mv i.280.14 and ii.405.15, so Senart with mss.; but read brūmi in both. Note that mss. at ii.405.15 point to (a)ham as subject. In a number of other places the Mv mss. read kruhi or krumi (ii.50.2, 12; 51.2, 12; 52.2; 181.16; 193.10; 256.15; 294.13; 482.12; iii.17.18; 214.4), but Senart always em. brūhi or brūmi; also in i.307.1 mss. krutha, Senart em. brutha, intending brūtha. Sometimes a corresponding Pali passage proves the emendation right, e.g. Mv iii.214.4 = DN ii.242.16 brūhi; Mv ii.50.2 = Jāt. v.387.21 vadāmi (Mv brūmi). In my opinion Senart should have made a clean sweep of these erroneous forms and adopted brūmi in i.280.14 and ii.405.15.] [197,2]
