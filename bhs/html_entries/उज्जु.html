• ujju, adj. (= Pali id., beside uju; Skt. ṛju; cf. next, ṛjju, and anujjuka), straight, right; usually in vss where jj could be m.c., but once in prose in Mv iii.225.1 ujjucittā; the rest in vss: Mv iii.436.3 °gateṣu; LV 133.20 ujju karitva kāyaṃ; 138.20 ujju-bhraṣṭā; 295.8 sadojjupraṣṭho (so read; = sadā ujju°).
