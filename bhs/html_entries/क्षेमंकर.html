• Kṣemaṃkara, (1) n. of a former Buddha: Divy 242.1 ff.; he lived in Kṣemāvatī; in the same story in Pali Khemaṃkara is the n. of the king of the city, who in Divy (242.5) is Kṣema (in Pali also alternatively Khema); in Pali the Buddha Kakusaṃdha is born under this king (in Divy Krakucchanda appears in a sequel to the story, 254.3); (2) n. of an apparently different former Buddha: Av i.110.10 ff.; (3) n. of a son of King Brahmadatta of Benares: Av ii.46.6; 49.8; (4) n. of a Bodhisattva: Gv 442.16; (5) n. of a nāga: Mvy 3336.
