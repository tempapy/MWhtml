• taṭṭa, taṭṭu, taṭṭuka (m. or nt.; = Pali taṭṭaka, a flattish bowl; otherwise unrecorded), some kind of bowl [Page247-2b+ 29] or dish: taṭṭukaṃ (acc.) Śikṣ 58.1; taṭṭa-kāra, bowl-maker, Mv ii.468.14, 18; iii.442.17 (here mss. tadva°, Senart wrongly em. taddhu°); taṭṭu-kāraka, id., Mv iii.113.13 (so read for taddhu°); taṭṭv-ākāra, bowl-shaped, to be read in Divy 342.26; 343.5, for text tapv-āk°; this passage cited Śikṣ 58.1 taṭṭākāra (taṭṭa-āk°), but 58.5 taṭṭukākāra (taṭṭuka-āk°); miswritten khaṭvākāra, see khaṭu (2).
