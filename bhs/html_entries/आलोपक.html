• -ālopaka, m. or nt. (from prec. plus suffix ka), in ekālop° and saptālop°, the practice of eating only (one, or seven) mouthful(s) of almsfood: LV 248.21, 22, ekālopa- kair, and saptālopakaiḥ; so read for °lāp° of both edd., no v.l.; cf. Pali ekālopika, sattālopika, DN i.166.11 and 12, one who adheres to these respective practices. Confirmed by Tib. kham. [107,1]
