• Dharmaruci, (1) (= Pali Dhammaruci) n. of a disciple of Śākyamuni: Mv i.246.3, 6, 12 (later incarnation of Meghadatta, q.v.); Divy 236.2 ff. (here a number of the same stories are told of him as in Pali); Divy chap. 18 is called Dharmarucy-avadāna, Divy 262.6; (2) n. of one of the four deities of the bodhi-tree: LV 401.21. [281,1]
