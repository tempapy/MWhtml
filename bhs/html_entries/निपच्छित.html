• nipacchita (related to nepatthita, q.v.), seems to be denom. ppp., clothed: Kalpanāmaṇḍitikā, Lüders, Kl. Skt. Texte 2, p. 44, q.v. Since cch and tth are often confused, possibly nipatthita (or even ne°) should be read. However, acc. to Pischel 280 AMg. has nevaccha and nevacchiya, by the side of ṇevattha and ṇevatthiya, Ratnach.; and Pischel believes that cch is the only ‘correct’ spelling. [297,1]
