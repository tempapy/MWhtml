• aśāśvatam (after anucchedam) in LV 423.10 (prose) applied to the (dharma-)cakra, is misunderstood by Fou- caux (non immobile). Like everything in the universe acc. to the śūnyavāda, it is neither annihilable nor eternal; neither of these attributes, nor any attribute contrastable with an opposite, can be predicated of anything. Tib. rtag pa med pa, not eternal. See śāśvatoccheda and references there to Laṅk. [080,1]
