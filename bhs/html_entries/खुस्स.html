• khu-ssa, cpd. particle, acc. to Senart = Skt. khalu svid: Mv ii.184.8 (vs) mā khu-ssa me nāga kṛtaṃ na jāne, do not, O nāga, fail to recognize (gratefully) what I have done (for you). In Pali -ssu, -su, -si occur for svid (Geiger 22; 111.1); PTSD s.v. su^3 states that sa and assa occur, allegedly for Skt. sma, but no such forms are cited in their alphabetic positions in PTSD, nor have I found any other record of them. Cf. sva (?). [206,1]
