• lumba, a kind of tree, acc. to Senart (who em. thus for mss. lamba) the plakṣa, in lumbodyānaṃ Mv i.99.6 (vs), applied to what is otherwise known as the Lumbinī grove; since in line 8 lumbinī (q.v., 2) seems to be a tree- name, the em. seems plausible, tho no other record of lumba (or lamba) or lumbinī as a tree-name has been noted.
