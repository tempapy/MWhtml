• bhaṇe (1 sg. mid. of Skt. bhaṇati; = Pali id.), I say! look here!: in Mv i.320.3, 6, 14; 321.1, 11; 322.11, used by Jyotipāla in addressing his friend Ghaṭikāra; otherwise only bho (twice ho) bhaṇe, and always used by a person of superior social station, usually a king, in speaking to subjects or inferiors: Mv i.272.15; ii.74.17 (here Sunda- rananda, to the populace); 151.6, 10; 152.14--15, 19; 154.10, 14; 155.11; 165.14; 426.10; 427.14; 436.7; 478.17; iii.102.10; 111.16; 441.19; ho (for bho) bhaṇe iii.160.19; 166.17.
