• eka-ghana, adj. (= Pali id.), in one mass, entire, said of a Tathāgata's body that is not divided into separate relics: SP 240.11 (here Kern condensed, inaccurately); 259.15 (na ca śarīraṃ dhātubhedena bhetsyate,) ekaghanaṃ cāsya śarīraṃ bhaviṣyati saptaratnastūpaṃ praviṣṭam. [152,1]
