• tiṣṭhatu (3 sg. impv. of sthā; = Pali tiṭṭhatu; so far as I know, not so used in Skt.), be it so! all right! as formula of assent: tiṣṭhatu tāva LV 287.19 (vs), in Svastika's response to the Bodhisattva's request for grass; rendered by Tib. freely, khyod bzhes śig, do you take it! (qy: did Tib. possibly take tāva as = tava, and understand literally let it remain yours?). [254,1]
