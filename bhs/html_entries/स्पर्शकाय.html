• sparśa-kāya (m.; = Pali phassakāya, six in number, DN iii.243.23, cakkhu-saṃphasso, sota-, ghāna-, jivhā-, kāya-, and mano-), the group of (six) contacts (of the sense- organs with their objects): ābādhavipramukto 'si sparśa- (so all Nep. mss., Kashgar rec. °śaṃ, ed. wrongly em. °śaḥ)-kāye tavānagha SP 301.5 (vs), are you free from trouble in your group-of-sensory-contacts?
