• sparśāhāra, m. (= Pali phassāhāra), touch-food, ingested by contact (acc. to AbhidhK. iii.121, cf. also 95 f., ‘contact’ comes from association of sense-organ, object, and cognition), one of the four, or, in Dharmas, five, kinds of food (āhāra): Mvy 2285; Dharmas 70. See s.v. kavalīkārāhāra. [612,2]
