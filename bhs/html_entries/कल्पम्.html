• kalpam, adv. (from kalpa, q.v.; = Pali kappaṃ), lit. and orig. for a kalpa, = for a long time: SP 227.5 (prose) (yaḥ…tathāgatasya) saṃmukhaṃ kalpam avarṇaṃ bhāṣet, who should for a long time speak ill before (= to- wards) a T. (The translators misunderstand.) The verse account has (229.7) yaś caiva…jinasya saṃmukhaṃ śrāved avarṇaṃ paripūrṇakalpam,…for a full kalpa, which may therefore be the mg. of 227.5; but the Pali adv. is well established. [173,1]
