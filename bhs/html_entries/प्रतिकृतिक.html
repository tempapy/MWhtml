• -pratikṛtika, in kṛta-prati°, adj. (to Skt. pratikṛti, app. not recorded in this sense of requital, repayment, but Skt. pratikaroti and Pali paṭikara are so used), one of the six upāya of a Bodhisattva, containing, consisting of or concerning requital (repayment) for (favors previously) done (by the Bodhisattva; he declines any recompense, other than virtuous life by his debtor): Bbh 264.9; de- scribed 270.9 ff.
