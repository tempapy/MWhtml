• ? sam-anvāsayati (cf. Pali opt. anu-v-āseyyā, CPD s.v. anu-v-āsati), attends (?): eṣa te 'dya samanvāsayāmy aham Gv 487.10 (vs); but meter is wrong (syllable anv- should be short); prob. some corruption. I have also thought of reading samanvāśayāmy, here today I wish (prīti…vipulām, in prec. line) for thee; cf. āśayan, desiring, Bhāg.P. 9.1.37 (BR 7.219), and Pali āsayānā, desiring, Jāt. iv.291.22; but this does not correct the meter.
