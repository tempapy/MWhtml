• muñja-balbaja-jāta, adj. (= Pali °babbaja-bhūta), become (confused) like muñja and balbaja (reeds or grasses after they have been woven into ropes): Mvy 5392. This is the mg. given the Pali word by DN comm. ii.496.1 ff., and is clearly right. All the parallel terms, on which see guṇāvaguṇṭhita-bhūta, refer to confusion. [434,1]
