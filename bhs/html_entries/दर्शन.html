(= Pali dassana, nt., in sippa-d°), exhibition (of skill in arts or exercises): kumāro…darśanaṃ dāsyati Mv ii.73.18; 74.4, 7; kumā- reṇa…darśano dinnaḥ 75.18; darśana-śatāni vartanti iii.57.9, hundreds of exhibitions (as entertainments at a festival).
