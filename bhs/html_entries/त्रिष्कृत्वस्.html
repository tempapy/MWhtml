• triṣ-kṛtvas (Skt. tri-k°, once, pw; triṣk°, also Vedic, blend of this with tris, thrice; in Class. Skt. -kṛtvas is ordinarily not used with dvi-, tri-), thrice: Laṅk 3.4; Śikṣ 98.11 (prose); tṛṣkṛtva, m.c., Śikṣ 99.4 (vs). For triṣ- before other representatives of Skt. -krtvas, see s.v. -kṛtvā.
