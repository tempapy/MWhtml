• kṣudrikā, SP 129.10, and kṣudrīka, 129.7, small; both vss, quantities metrically required; both f., with oṣadhi; in both Kashgar rec. cited as khuḍḍīka, which would be unmetrical in 10. Related to Skt. kṣudra(ka); see s.v. khudrāka. For kṣudrīka Burnouf cites his mss. as reading khuḍḍāka or khuḍḍīka. [200,2]
