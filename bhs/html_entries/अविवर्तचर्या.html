• avivarta-caryā (= avaivarta-, anivartana-caryā), course that is not liable to turning back (backsliding): Mv i.63.13. The text explains by vivartanti saṃsaranti vivar- tacaryā (cf. vivarta, world-evolution); but I agree with Senart, note on i.1.3, that this is mere fantasy and wholly misleading. [078,2]
