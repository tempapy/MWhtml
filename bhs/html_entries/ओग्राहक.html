• ogrāhaka, m., one who grasps (intellectually), acquires (knowledge): Mv iii.373.3 (vs) ogrāhako ca paripṛcchamāno (…evaṃkaro prajñāvāṃ bhoti). Prob. false Sktization of Pali uggāhako (vā paripucchako siyā, same line as above, Jāt. v.148.19). Alternatively, might be false Sktization of a Pali *ogāhaka to ogāhati, °heti (= Skt. ava-gāh-), penetrates (used once with object lokanāthassa sāsanaṃ, Pv. comm. 287.12). [158,2]
