• khalu-paścād-bhaktika, adj. or subst. m. (= Pali khalupacchābhattika; see Childers s.v. pacchābhattiko, where the word is fully explained with Buddhaghosa's gloss), not (khalu, as in Pali) eating after (the time when one should cease): Mvy 1133 (Tib. zas phyis mi len pa, not taking food after) and Dharmas 63 (as one of the 12 dhūtaguṇa); Śikṣ 135.15 yadi punaḥ khalupaścādbhaktiko (one word) bodhisattvo vā glāno bhavati; AsP 387.5 sacet khalu° bhaviṣyati; MSV iii.122.6.
