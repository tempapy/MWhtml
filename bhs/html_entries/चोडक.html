• coḍaka, m. or nt. (= prec.; Skt. colaka Lex. and rarely lit., Schmidt, Nachträge), garment(s), clothing: with bhakta (like coḍa) bhakta-coḍaka-paramo vatāyam Śikṣ 20.17 (prose); otherwise, coḍaka-dhovakasya, washer- man (of clothes), and coḍakāni dhovīyanti Mv ii.466.4, others in sequel; ujjhita-coḍakā (discarded garments) sāhartavyā Mv iii.264.11; coḍaka- Divy 415.6.
