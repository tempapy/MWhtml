• deśa, nt. (otherwise m.), part (here, of the body; not common in Skt., but see śarīradeśebhyaḥ BṛhU 4.4.3; common in AMg., see Ratnach. s.v. desa 5): na ca te 'ntarā kāyu kadāci cālito, na hasta-pādaṃ no pi cānya deśam SP 161.9 (vs; mss., except Kashgar rec. grīva for deśa; KN em. cānyad-aṅgam, kept by WT without note), and your body never moved, not your hands or feet, nor any other part.
