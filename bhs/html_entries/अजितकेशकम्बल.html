Pūraṇa Kāśyapa), named with the others: °laḥ Mvy 3548 (Ajita-keś°, as cpd.; so also Mironov); Divy 143.11; Av i.231.4 (ms. Kesa°); °lasya Mv i.253.14; °lī, nom. sg. Mv i.256.20; iii.383.16. [007,2]
