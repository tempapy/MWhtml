• indriya, (1) nt. (Pali also uses the word of this group, see PTSD s.v., B, Nos. 15--19), one of the five moral faculties (śraddhā, vīrya, smṛti, samādhi, prajñā), to which correspond five powers (bala) with the same names: listed Mvy 976--981; Sūtrāl. xviii.55 (and cf. xi.12, Transl. 106, n. 10); Dharmas 47; each treated as a dharmāloka- mukha, LV 33.17--20; mentioned, with balas, but not listed, SP 47.2; 80.1; (2) a high number, Gv 106.3 sattven- driyasya.
