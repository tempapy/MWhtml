• pṛṣṭhima, once (Mv ii.126.6, prose) °maka, adj. (to pṛṣṭhi or Skt. pṛṣṭha, § 22.13), of the back, back-, rear: °makaṃ (sc. kāyaṃ) Mv ii.126.6, back (part of the body), contrasted with purimaṃ kāyaṃ; the LV (similarly Pali MN) parallels (see s.v. purima 2) have pṛṣṭhi-kaṇṭakaṃ (or Pali equivalent), and so also (or spelled °ṭhakam) in repetitions Mv ii.127.10; 128.15; 129.17; but pṛṣṭhimaṃ, sc. kāyaṃ, Mv ii.232.15 (vs, same incident); adverbs, °mena (āhatā) Mv ii.455.18, in the back, behind; °mena °maṃ samanubaddho Mv ii.255.2, 4; iii.28.2; 53.11, pursuing ever behind; °mena °maṃ anugacchati iii.291.7; pṛṣṭhena pṛṣṭhimaṃ samanubaddhā iii.296.9; pṛṣṭhimaṃ (but mss. pṛṣṭiṃ me, or se) āruhitvā Mv ii.479.19, mounting on (a person's) back.
