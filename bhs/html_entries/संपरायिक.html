• saṃparāyika, adj. (= Pali id., Skt. sāṃpa°; cf. saṃparāya), of a life after death: Ud iv.26 (Lévi JA 1912, Pt. 2, 269), contrasted with dṛṣṭadhārmika. But Chakra- varti, Ud p. 48, assumes that sāṃpa° was read, as in Skt.; is Lévi's form a misprint? (There are rather numerous misprints in this text.) The Skt. (or Sktized) adj. sāṃ°, q.v., is otherwise well known in BHS: Ud v.22; Mvy 2981; 8355; Mv iii.212.6, 8, 9; Bbh 176.24, etc. [575,2]
