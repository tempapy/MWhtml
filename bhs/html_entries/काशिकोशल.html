• Kāśi-kośala, and °laka, n. of a (single) people, or part of a people (the Kośalas): Mv i.350.5 and 12 °lena rājñā; 7 °lakā manuṣyā(ḥ); 10 °la-rājño. Contrasted with the Kośalas of Śāketa, Śāketā api Kośalā(ḥ) i.350.19. On the relation between the Kośalas and Kāśi see DPPN s.v. Kāsi. [181,2]
