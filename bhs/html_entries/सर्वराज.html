• sarvarāja (cf. Skt. sarvarāj, rare), emperor, universal king: (tasya, sc. to Prince Puṇyaraśmi, son of King Arciṣ- mant, mātāpitṛbhyāṃ) koṭiḥ kanyānāṃ dattā, jñātisaṃ- ghena koṭiḥ, naigamajānapadaiḥ koṭiḥ, sarvarājena koṭiḥ kanyānāṃ dattā abhūvan RP 41.15; it seems that sarvarāja must refer to a feudal superior of the prince's father, who was a rājan, 36.18.
