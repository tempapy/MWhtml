• sv-ādharṣa-ka, adj. Bhvr., subject to easy attack: kulaṃ bahustrīkaṃ alpapuruṣaṃ °kaṃ bhavati, supra- dharṣakaṃ (q.v.)…Bhīk 4a.3. Antonyms of Skt. dur- ādharṣa, duṣpradharṣa; the nouns ādharṣa, pradharṣa are otherwise unrecorded. The corresponding Pali, Vin. ii. 256.17, has suppadhaṃsiya, but no equivalent of svā- dharṣaka.
