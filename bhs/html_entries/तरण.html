• taraṇa, nṭ. (Skt. id., fording (a river), so also Tib. rgal ba, below; AMg. id., defined Ratnach. swimming, crossing), perhaps boating, rowing, or swimming, in lists of arts and sports: javite plavite taraṇe LV 156.10; °ṇam, after javitam, plavitaṃ, Mvy 5001 = Tib. rgal ba. Foucaux's Tib. rgyal in LV, prob. error for rgal; he renders la natation. [250,1]
