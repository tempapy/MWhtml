• phaleṣin, adj. (Skt. phala plus -eṣin; = Pali °sin, in same vs as Mv, Therag. 527 = Jāt. i.87.2), seeking (to bring forth) fruit, ep. of trees, acc. to Pali: (read) °ṣiṇo Mv iii.93.11. The Mv is very corrupt and has omitted the word for tree (drumāḥ, Pali dumā), but there is no doubt that the text was substantially the same; Senart with mss. s for ṣ, but one ms. has ṇ for (Senart's) n!
