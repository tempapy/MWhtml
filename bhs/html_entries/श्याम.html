LV 166.3 (vs, a m.c.); Śyāmaśiri Mv ii.214.8, 16; 217.5, 6, 7; Śyāmaka Mv ii.210.12, 13; RP 22.1; Śyāmaka-śiri, often with v.l. °ka-ṛṣi, Mv ii.215.6, 7, 13, 15, 18; 216.2, 17, 20; 217.10; 218.1 etc.; (2) Syamu (acc. sg., a m.c.) ṛṣi LV 166.19 (vs), n. of an ascetic who demanded of the Bodhisattva in a former birth that he count the leaves on a tree, which he did.
