• avarabhāgīya, adj. (Pali oraṃbhāgiya), (binding) to lower states (viz. to the kāmadhātu), only said of the first five saṃyojana (q.v.): Mvy 2156 (without mention of saṃyojana); Divy 533.24; MSV ii.87.7. They cause rebirth in a state of desire (kāmabhave) also in Pali, MN comm. iii.143.21; in Pugg. 22.11--12 called personal, internal (ajjhatta). See ūrdhvabhāgīya. [073,2]
