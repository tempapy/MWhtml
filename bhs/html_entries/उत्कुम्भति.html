• utkumbhati, prob. rears (of a balky horse; so guessed from context): KP 108.2 (yatra…) aśva(ḥ) skhalati utkumbhati vā khaḍunka- (q.v.)-kriyā vā karoti, when a horse stumbles or rears or acts viciously. Tib. seems to have no correspondent for this word; the several Chin. versions, likewise, fail to clarify it; they seem to have only two verbal expressions for three of the BHS. [121,1]
