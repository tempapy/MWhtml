• ? pelavaka, m. (mss. pelacaka, palavaka), in Mv ii.161.2, see passage quoted s.v. anuśrotaṃ. Senart as- sumes P. as proper name of a horse; it might as well be a common noun, horse (cf. pelin, horse acc. to Wilson); but the whole passage, esp. this word, is doubtful and likely to be corrupt.
