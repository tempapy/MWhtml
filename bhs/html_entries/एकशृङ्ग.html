• Ekaśṛṅga, °gaka, n. of the hero of what the colophon Mv iii.152.19 calls Nalinīye rājakumārīye jātakam; later iii.272.17 it is referred to as Ekaśṛṅgajātakaṃ (punaḥ kartavyaṃ); °śṛṅga iii.144.17 ff.; °śṛṅgaka (prose) 144.18; 145.7 ff. He corresponds to Skt. Ṛśyaśṛṅga, Pali Isisiṅga (in the Naḷinikā-jātaka, 526), and doubtless is meant by the maharṣi Ṛṣiśṛṅga, q.v.; both occur Māy 256.31. [153,2]
