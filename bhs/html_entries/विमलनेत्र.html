• Vimalanetra, (1) n. of a past Buddha: Sukh 6.6; (2) n. of a future Buddha (predicted rebirth of the Bo- dhisattva Śrīgarbha): SP 21.13 (prose); called Vimalā- granetra in vs 26.6; (3) n. of an ancient prince, previous incarnation of Bhaiṣajyasamudgata: SP 457.9 ff.; cf. 470.11; (4) n. of a Bodhisattva: Mvy 677; Gv 3.1; (5) n. of the ‘leader-jewel’ (see pariṇāyaka 2) of a certain cakravartin: Gv 418.11.
