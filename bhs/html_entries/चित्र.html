• Citra, (1) (= Pali Citta gahapati) n. of a lay disciple of the Buddha, with the epithet gṛhapati: MSV iii.21.9 ff.; Jm 115.25; (2) n. of a nāga king: Mvy 3268, v.l. Vicitra, which Mironov reads with v.l. Citta; Tib. ris bkra (each word alone elsewhere renders Skt. citra); Māy 246.30. [230,1]
