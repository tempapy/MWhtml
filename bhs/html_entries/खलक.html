• khalaka, (1) m. or nt. (= Skt. khala), threshing-floor or granary: Kv 28.18 (prose) mahā-khalake mahāntaṃ rāśiṃ kuryāt; (2) m. (= Skt. khala; AMg. khalaya, app. in this sense, Ratnach.), rogue, or adj. roguish, evil, bad: perhaps read khalakā(ḥ) for text khaṇḍakā(ḥ) Mmk 73.13; see s.v. khaṇḍaka. [204,1]
