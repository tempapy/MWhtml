• anuttarya, nt. (= Pali °riya; also ān°; CPD regards an° as w.r. for ān°), excellence, supremacy; they number six, Mvy 1573--79: darśana, śravaṇa, lābha, śikṣā, paricaryā, anusmṛti (each cpd. with anut°). A corresponding list in Pali, see PTSD s.v. anuttariya. A different one is nivāsānuttarya, Mv iii.320.5, see s.v. nivāsa; here ānutt° may have been intended. [027,1]
