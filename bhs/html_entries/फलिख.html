• phalikha, (m.? = Skt. parigha, AMg. phaliha, see § 2.29 and Pischel 208), bar (as to a door or gate): in Mv ii.379.5 (vs) read phalikha-(mss. °khaṃ)-bhujo achambhī, as proved by same line Śikṣ 303.3 parigha-bhujo…; accordingly, read doubtless phalikha-(so mss. in iii.228.9; in i.195.12 mss. phaṭikāya-, sphaṭikāya-)-phalakāni (so, or with v.l. °khāni, mss. in i.195.12; mss. very corrupt in iii.228.9) Mv i.195.12; iii.228.9 (same passage, prose); Senart reads phaṭika- the first time, phalika- the second; in a description of parts of gates or doors. In both of these last passages the next sentence contains another part of the gate, printed by Senart phalaka-stārā (pl.); see s.v. phalaha [396,1]
