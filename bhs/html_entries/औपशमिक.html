• aupaśamika, adj. (= Pali opasamika; in Skt. only as technical term of Jainas; from Skt. upaśama plus -ika; cf. an-aupa°, and upa°), tending to tranquillity; usually, as in Pali, ep. of dharma: Mv ii.33.3 dharmaṃ…aupa- samikaṃ (so mss. and ed.); 41.9 dharmaṃ tu opasamikaṃ (v.l. aupas°); Av ii.107.7 dharmaś ca…aupaśamikaḥ; Bbh 24.13 (hitānvayaḥ svaparārtho bodhisattvasya)… aupaśamikaś ca.
