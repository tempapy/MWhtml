• dviruttarapadasaṃdhi-lipi, a kind of writing: LV 126.8; Tib. tshig lan lan gñis su gdab paḥi tshig gi mtshams kyi (yi ge), which Foucaux renders, (écriture) du saṃdhi (liaison) d'un mot répété deux fois, which does not make much sense to me, nor can I see how it renders the Tib. (which however I find obscure). The text continues: yāvad- daśottarapadasaṃdhi-lipiṃ; this time Tib. is rather dif- ferent, tshig gi mtshams bcur brgyud paḥi bar gyi (yi ge), which Foucaux renders as before (with ten for two); it seems to mean writing up to a series of ten (forming) a combination of words, or writing of combinations of words in which up to ten words follow one another. Possibly dvirut- tara° means no more than writing of two words in com- bination. [274,2]
