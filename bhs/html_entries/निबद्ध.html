• nibaddha, ppp. of ni-bandh, in aṣṭāpada-ni°, adorned, laid out, marked out with (or, in) a checkerboard: Mvy 6062 = Tib. mig maṅs (checkerboard) ris su bris pa (drawn or painted in the form of); SP 65.10 and 145.1 suvarṇa- sūtrāṣṭāpadanibaddhaṃ (with gold threads…). This seems to have been a fairly standard expression; vinibaddha [Page297-b+ 71] (q.v., 2) was also used; the use of vinaddha (q.v.) in the same cpd. seems to be a mere corruption. [297,1]
