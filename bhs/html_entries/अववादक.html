• avavādaka, adj. (to avavadati; = Pali ovādaka), instructing, admonishing; m., instructor (in religion); nt. also used of a text: Divy 48.26 yas teṣām ṛṣir avavādakaḥ; 385.8 °kānām agro; 493.5 (ko 'smākam) avavādaka ājñaptaḥ; 254.10 kulāvavādako, a family (religious) instructor, of a rich man; Gv 171.14 mahāyānasyāvavāda- kāni (are instructors in the Mahāyāna) mama kalyāṇami- trāṇi; nt., of a work, Rājāvavādakam, see this.
