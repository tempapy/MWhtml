• Brahmāyu(s), (1) °yu, n. of the purohita of Brah- madatta king of Benares, in the Dharmapālasya Jātaka (previous incarnation of the Buddha, and father of Dhar- mapāla 1): Mv ii.77.9 ff.; (2) n. of the purohita of King Śaṅkha: Divy 60.23 ff. (showing stem in °yus, as °yuṣe 61.6); (3) °yu (= Pali id.), n. of a brahman who became a disciple of the Buddha: Karmav 157.5 (°yu- in cpd.). [404,2]
