• ciratarakena, adv. (to compv. cira-tara plus -ka), in or after a longer time: yuṣmākaṃ evaṃ cira° vāro bhaviṣyati imehi duvehi potakehi jātehi Mv i.362.3, the lot (of death) will fall on you after a longer time, if these two fawns are born. But mss. ciratanakena (em. Senart); possibly a deriv. of Skt. cirantana is intended. [231,1]
