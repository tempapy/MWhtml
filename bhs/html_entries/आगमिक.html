• āgamika, m., (a monk) that arrives at (or, returns to) a monastery; visitor, guest, arrival. Not in Pali, which uses āgantuka (= Skt.) instead as pendant to gamika (q.v.): Mvy 8748, Tib. ḥoṅs pa, arrival. Note that 8746 also has āgantuka, defined Tib. blo bur du ḥoṅs pa, sudden arrival. [088,1]
