• Vaji (the usual form in mss. of Mv, Senart always Vajji), or Vajji (= Pali Vajji) = Vṛji, q.v., n. of a people and country; associated with (Skt.) Malla, and with Licchavi, who in Pali are one unit in the Vajji con- federacy, but the two are also treated as interchangeable: Mv i.34.9 (prose), text vajji-, v.l. vaji-; 264.13 (vs), mss. vajim abhimukho, read vajiṃ a°, m.c.; Senart vajji; 326.2 (prose), mss. vajiṣu or varjiṣu; ii.419.9 (prose), mss. -vaji-or -vajri-; iii.421.5 (vs), vaji-required by meter, mss. vajī-, vajrī-, Senart vajji-.
