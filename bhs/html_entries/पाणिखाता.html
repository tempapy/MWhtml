• Pāṇikhātā (°ta, °da or °dā; cf. (so Senart; mss. °dāto) nadīto 313.8. Cf. Pāṇikhāte, loc. sg., a sacred bathing place, Mbh. Crit. ed. 3.81.75. [339,2]
