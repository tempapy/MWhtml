• antarīkṣecara, adj. m. (with deva; cf. Pali antalik- khacara, which seems not to be recorded of any class or category of gods), moving or living in the atmosphere, n. of a class of gods, see deva; = antarikṣa, °īkṣa, āntar- ikṣavāsin: Mv i.40.14; 229.15; 240.4; ii.138.12; 348.16; in all after bhūmya deva, and before other classes of kāmāvacara gods.
