• utsaktikā, perhaps attitude of head thrown back or held up stiffly: Mvy 8542 notsaktikayā, instr. (of monk's behavior); °kā-kṛta, one who is in that attitude, Mvy 8608. In the former, Tib. mgo mi (g)zar, with the head not stiff or abruptly straight, so also in the latter except that mgo, head, is lacking; Chin. acc. to Ting's Dict. with robe inside-out. [126,1]
