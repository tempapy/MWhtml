Ḱ) or °ru (with 3 mss., KN) in SP 85.4 (vs), for durbalam of both edd., which the preceding and parallel sudurbalaṃ makes improbable; Tib. ḥjig (ñe) supports a form of bhid-; read for the next word bhairavaṃ, with all Nep. mss. of KN, also Ḱ, and Tib. ḥjigs, for KN's em. itvara; this word (q.v.) is inappropriate here, yet similar enough in mg. to have helped in the creation of bhit(t)vara, as if bhid- plus tvara, modelled on i-tvara and Skt. ga-tvara; the latter has a mg. virtually identical with that required here.
