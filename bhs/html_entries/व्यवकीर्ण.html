• vyavakīrṇa, ppp. of vy-ava-kṛ scatter (= Pali vokiṇṇa, but see below), interrupted, broken up, halting (of speech): na vyava° = a-vyava°, q.v., Śikṣ 126.1 na vyavakīrṇa-vacanaḥ, his speech is not halting, broken. In Skt., and acc. to PTSD in Pali (vokiṇṇa), the only meaning seems to be filled, pervaded, thoroughly mixed (with, instr.); in this sense also BHS, e.g. Dbh 53.21. But cf. Pali abbokiṇṇa, CPD.
