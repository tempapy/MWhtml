• antike, adv., postpos., in reference to, with gen.: SP 107.4--5 daridrapuruṣas tasya gṛhapater antike pitṛ- saṃjñām utpādayet, would conceive the notion of father in reference to that householder, i.e. would think of him as his father; similarly SP 286.1; also LV 244.1 naiṣa mamāntike viśiṣṭasaṃjño bhaven (see saṃjñā 5); Mv i.30.9 devānām…antike cittāni pradūṣayitvā, because [Page040-b+ 71] they conceived evil thoughts in reference to the gods; Divy 212.2 sattvānām antike…anukampā; Av i.264.10 bha- gavato 'ntike cittaṃ prasādayām āsuḥ, in reference to the Blessed One; Av i.287.1 arhato 'ntike.
