• prati-vaśa, adj., obedient (to), subject to the will (of), with gen.: mama °śāś ca syuḥ Ud xiii.4, in a vs = Pali Dhp. 74 mam’ evātivasā assu. The Pali ativasa is isolated, strange, and difficult (see CPD), and BHS prativaśa seems a more natural and comprehensible form, tho also un- paralleled as far as I know. [367,2]
