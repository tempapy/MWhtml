• avadāna, nt. (= Pali apadāna), n. of a part, or parts, of the Buddhist canon (and of other Buddhist works): Mvy 1273; colophons of Divy, Av, etc. See also sāvadānam. The word avadāna occurs in Skt.; its exact mg. is much disputed; see e.g. Speyer, Av Preface p. I ff. [072,1]
