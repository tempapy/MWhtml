• vṛndi, or vṛndī (related to Skt. vṛnda, orig. swelling, mass, as m. in Suśr. tumor; = Pali, AMg. bondi; see Edgerton, JAOS 69.229, anticipated, as I learned too late, by Lüders, KZ 52.106--9, with relevant materials not included by me; cf. voṇṭa), body (orig. solid mass or the like): vaḍḍā (see vaḍḍa) ca vṛndi (in this pāda mss. lack 3 syllables; perh. read ca vidyate vṛndi, or, ca vṛndī, which in this position makes better meter than vṛndi, bhavati) prajñā ca te na vidyate Mv ii.249.19 (vs), so mss., you have a big body, but you have no wit; = Pali Jāt. ii.160.11 mahatī vata te bondi na ca paññā tadūpikā. Senart failed to note the parallel and emended the text badly. [507,1]
