• antikāt, adv., postpos. (1) than, with gen.: mam- āntikād abhirūpatara Divy 75.1, 6; pūrvikānām antikād Divy 115.14; 117.14, 28; tavāntikāt Divy 85.29; (asya…) puṇyaskandhasyāntikād asau paurvakaḥ puṇyaskandhaḥ śatatamīm api kalāṃ nopaiti Vaj 35.7, in comparison with this…; (2) on the part of, with gen., substantially = by: SP 109.5 tathāgatasyāntikād eṣu dharmeṣv abhiyuk- tā(ḥ), charged, employed in these (inferior) conditions of life on the part of (i.e. by) the T.; wrongly Burnouf and Kern.
