(so also Mironov); Divy 3.8, 9, 12 Śroṇaḥ Koṭīkarṇaḥ (°ṇo); 3.26, also 17.9, and colophon 24.8, Koṭīkarṇas, alone; both names separately 4.12 ff., repeat- edly; Śroṇa alone 7.29 ff., repeatedly.
