• māḍa (cf. māla 2; Pali māḷa, māla), m. pl. māḍāḥ Mvy 5548 (v.l. and Mironov māṭāḥ), some sort of building: Tib. rṅa (drum) khaṅ (house, building), or sṅa (= pūrva) khaṅ; Chin. has two renderings, perh. intended like the two Tib. glosses; could the first Tib. mean drum-shaped hall? cf. maṇḍala-māḍa. PTSD derives the Pali word from Tamil māḍam, house. [428,1]
