• ? bhumī, LV 155.11 (vs), so Calc. and Lefm., who cites all mss. as bhūmī (unmetr.): na ca utthitu āsani no ca bhumī; Tib. gdan las ma bzheṅs (from seat not arise) brtsol ba ma mchis su (not making any effort; brtsol = vyāyāma, vyavasāya). Possibly read bhramī, aor. of root bhram? and did not stir. [410,1]
