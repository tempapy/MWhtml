• muktikā, (1) adj., f. of muktaka (which is used at least once in Skt. in this sense, BR s.v.), isolated, unaccompanied: with jñapti, Bhīk 15b.3, or jñāpti, Mvy 8659, qq.v., isolated motion, unaccompanied by (one or three) supplementary questions (contrasts in Mvy with jñāpti-dvitīyam and °caturtham, explained s.v. jñapti): = Tib. gsol ba gcig pu, isolated question (demand, proposal). Seems not recorded in Pali; (2) pearl (so Skt. Lex., cf. Skt. muktā, mauktika): Mvy 5952; -maṇi- °kasya (in nt. sg. dvandva) SP 88.11 (vs). Cf. lohita-m°. [433,2]
