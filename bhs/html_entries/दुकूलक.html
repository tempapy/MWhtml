• dukūlaka (nt.?; Skt. °la plus -ka svārthe), a certain fine textile fabric: °kānāṃ tatha varakauśakānāṃ Śikṣ 302.6 (vs, may be m.c.); paṭe caiva °ke Mmk 131.21 (vs, may be m.c.); °laka-sūkṣma, subst. nt., a fine garment made of dukūla(ka): °maṃ vā Bhīk 22b.4 (prose), in a list of various textile fabrics. See also daukūlaka. [266,1]
