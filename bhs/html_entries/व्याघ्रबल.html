• Vyāghrabala, n. of a yakṣa: Māy 61(?). The mss. read the line: yakṣau siṃhabalau yau tu Siṃhavyāghra- balābalau; see Lévi's note for the evidently puzzled trans- lations; but at least one Chin. supports the theory that Siṃhabala and Vyāghrabala are the two names, ā in balābalau being lengthened m.c.
