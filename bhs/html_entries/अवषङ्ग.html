• avaṣaṅga, m., some part of a building: Mvy 5570 = Tib. ya phubs (? top roof?). Prof. Lo Ch'ang-pei informs me that the Chin. translation now means grand, huge or empty, but that the ‘radical’ of the sign suggests a pos- sible original meaning roof. In MPS 34.61 acc. to Tib. large beam, gduṅ bo che. [076,1]
