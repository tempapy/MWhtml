• -kariṇa, adj. or subst. ifc., doing or one who does: Mmk 57.2 pañcānantarya-kariṇasyāpi, even of a doer of the five deadly crimes (see ānantarya). (JM. kariṇa = Skt. karin, elephant; MIndic for Skt. karin, which is recorded in this sense only once from Kāś. in pw, and nowhere else in any dialect). [169,2]
