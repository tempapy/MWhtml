• kulāyaka, adj., m., confined to worldly existence: Divy 562.9 (vs) etac ca dṛṣṭveha parivrajanti, kulāyakās te na bhavanti santaḥ; te sarvasaṅgān abhisaṃprahāya, -na garbhaśayyāṃ punar āvasanti.Prob. from kulāya, cage, and lit. caged, confined; less likely cpd. of kula plus āyaka (Skt. gr.) or *ayaka (cf. Skt. aya). [188,1]
