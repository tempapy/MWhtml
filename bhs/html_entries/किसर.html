• kisara, (1) adj. and subst. (= Pali kasira, Skt. kṛcchra), difficulty, only in akisara-lābhin and alpa- kisareṇa, qq.v. The use of the former, beside akṛcchra-l°, proves that the two forms were not felt as identical. In Pali also (Geiger 59.2) kicchena kasirena are used together, and evidently taken as ‘different words’. Pkt. only kiccha; our kisara (perhaps influenced by Pali-Pkt. kisa = kṛśa) has no recorded parallel in MIndic; (2) m. or nt., ‘eine Art wohlriechender Stoff’: Kalpanāmaṇḍitikā, Lüders, Kl. Skt.
