• ? garbholika, in upari-garbholikaṃ (to be read as one word; sc. āsanam) AsP 488.20, having (some sort of) cover on top; parallel to goṇikāstīrṇaṃ, kāsikavastrapratyā- staraṇaṃ (read kāśi°), etc. The word is otherwise unknown, obscure in etymology, and perhaps corrupt. Possibly read for the cpd. a word containing ubhayato-lohita-(upadhā- naṃ, or the like), cf. Sukh 67.8; Mv ii.115.16--17; iii.70.2 (all similar passages). [210,2]
