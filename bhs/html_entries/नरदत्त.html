• Naradatta, (1) n. of a nephew and pupil of the ṛṣi Asita: LV 101.2 ff.; in Mv as in Pali named Nālaka; (2) n. of a Bodhisattva: ŚsP 6.8; (3) n. of a virtuous man (satpuruṣa q.v.): SP 3.11 (Kashgar rec. Nāla°; Burnouf Ratnadatta, noting that all mss. but one read Nara°; Tib. mes byin, which should render Pitāmaha- datta, doubtless understanding Nara as the Primeval Spirit, also sometimes called Pitāmaha).
