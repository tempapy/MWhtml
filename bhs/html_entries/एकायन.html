• (ekāyana, adj. with mārga (also in Skt. in literal sense; = Pali ekāyano maggo), narrow (path), traversible only by one at a time: Divy 158.22 (teṣām, sc. ṛṣīṇām, āgacchatāṃ Bhagavatā) ekāyano mārgo 'dhiṣṭhitaḥ; here app. in the literal, physical sense. In Pali it is also applied to the religious ‘Path’, prob. in the sense of traversible only by One (the Buddha); but several other interpretations are offered in the comms., e.g. MN comm. i.229.)
