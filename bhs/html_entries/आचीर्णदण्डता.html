• ācīrṇa-daṇḍa-tā (v.l. acīrṇa°, so Mironov without v.l., but a- seems impossible), practise (or occupation) of wielding a club: Mvy 3812 = Tib. dbyug pa gcod pa, app. wielding a club; Chin. seems to mean use by a horseback- rider or messenger of a stick in driving his horse. The next word in Mvy is dūtaḥ, messenger. [089,2]
