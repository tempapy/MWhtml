• hastokta, adj. (hasta-ukta; cf. Skt. hasta-stha), declared to be in the hand (= power); only °ktaṃ cakravar- tirājyam, referring to the imperium which the Bodhi- sattva renounced by retiring from the world: °ktaṃ (ca) °jyam apahāya Mv ii.68.20; 117.19; (Śuddhodana speaks of the glory which he would have shared if his son had not retired from the world,) asmākaṃ °ktaṃ °jyam abhaviṣyat iii.107.9. Cf. hastatva. [619,1]
