• alpotsuka-tā (= Pali appossukka-tā), abstr. to prec.: LV 393.21--394.1 alpotsukatāyai bhagavataś cittam abhinataṃ na dharmadeśanāyām, the mind of the Lord inclined to being unconcerned (with the fate of the world), not to teaching the Law; similarly 394.5; 396.7; 398.1; freedom from desires, Bbh 219.24.
