• kālāpadeśa, m. (see apadeśa), acc. to Tib. cited by Wogihara ‘black doctrine’, nag-po bstan pa; but this can hardly be right; perhaps ‘timely expression’; something which a Bodhisattva must know, along with mahāpadeśa (of which the precise mg. is also unknown; Tib. renders literally): Bbh 108.10--11 °śa-mahāpadeśāṃś ca yathābhū- taṃ prajānāti; 257.2 °śaṃ ca mahāpadeśaṃ ca yathā° praj°; 108.25 °śa-mahāpadeśa-kuśalo bodhisattvaḥ.
