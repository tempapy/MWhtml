• a-śrāmaṇya, adj. (= Pali asāmañña), not devoted to monks; regularly with amātṛjña, apitṛjña, and abrāhmāṇya, see s.v. amātṛjña for references; in Mvy 2460 °yam, nt., following abrāhmaṇyam, also nt., but just before apitṛjñāḥ, amātṛjñāḥ, m. pl.; perhaps collective, the group of those who are not devoted to monks?
