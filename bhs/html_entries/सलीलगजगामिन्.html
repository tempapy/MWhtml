• Salīlagajagāmin (cf. līḍa), n. of a former Buddha: Mvy 100 = Tib. glaṅ po che ltar ḥgyiṅ (misprinted ḥgyin) zhiṅ bzhud pa, departing while looking down (haughtily) like a great elephant: LV 5.14 = Tib. ṅom bag (surely intending the word given by Jä. and Das as ṅo ḥbab, with look cast down, bashful) glaṅ poḥi ḥgros (with walk of elephant). Senart reads °gāmī by em. Mv i.139.2; but see Savilambagāmin. [586,2]
