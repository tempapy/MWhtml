• avīci, m. or f. (in Skt. only m.; rarely also f. in Pali, see CPD s.v.), n. of a hell (= Skt. and Pali id.), a hot hell acc. to Mvy 4927 and Dharmas 121; passim. Fem. e.g. RP 30.16 (vs) dāruṇam (short -a- m.c.) avīcīm (end of line). Most forms, of course, are ambiguous as to gender. Cf. āvīci. [078,2]
