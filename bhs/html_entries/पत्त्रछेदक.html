• pattrachedaka (so Index and Mironov, text °ika), m., Mvy 3792, acc. to Tib. glegs (board, esp. panel of a door, Mvy 5567) ḥbra (?this word I have not found) mkhan (one who is concerned with); so, perhaps, board or door-panel cutter; but AMg. patta-chejja is said to mean art of shooting leaves (with an arrow), and cf. patta-chejjaka, activity of one who shoots down leaves from a tree(!). Cf. Jacobi, Ausgew. Erz. 19.37 (here piercing leaves with pebbles). In any case, designation of some trade or occu- pation. Chin. is obscure; Jap. paper-maker (note pattra also of leaves used for writing!). [317,1]
