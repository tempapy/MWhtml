• paudgalika, adj. (Pali puggalika; to pudgala plus ika; in different sense recorded pw 4.302), individual, personal, always contrasted with sāṃghika, sometimes also staupika: sāṃghikāḥ puṣpavṛkṣāḥ…°ka-paribho- gena bhuktāḥ Divy 342.19,…were put to individual, personal (not selfish with Index) use; staupikaṃ sāṃ- ghikaṃ…vittaṃ °kaṃ ca RP 29.8; similarly Śikṣ 63.14; Bbh 166.26; MSV ii.123.19.
