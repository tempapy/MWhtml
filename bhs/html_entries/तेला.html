• telā, a high number: Gv 106.9. Cf. prec., and bhelā, which occurs in the same context with this. There are three sets of numbers in -el- preceded by various con- sonants; in one set the stem-vowel a is added, in another ā, in another u. The t- and bh-forms of Mvy seem dup- lications of each other, but both appear in one Gv version (106.9 and 11), viz. the one ending in ā. [257,1]
