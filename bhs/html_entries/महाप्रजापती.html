• Mahāprajāpatī (= Pali Mahāpaj°), n. of the sister of Mayā, after whose death she took care of the infant Bodhisattva; later made the head of the order of nuns; regularly with addition of her family name Gautamī (once, at least, written Go°), q.v.: SP 268.6 ff.; LV 100.14, 16; 118.14, 16; 121.17; 201.9; 228.4; 229.15, 18; Mv ii.165.2; 233.2; iii.116.7 ff.; 142.6; 256.13 ff.; Mvy 1068; Bhīk 3a.2 et alibi; without the word Gautamī, SP 2.9; Mv i.355.17; Divy 391.7; Suv 239.9; Av ii.4.9; 21.6, 13; 33.5; 50.3.
