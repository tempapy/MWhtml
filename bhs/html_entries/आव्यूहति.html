• āvyūhati = āyūhati, exerts oneself towards, carries out, performs, with acc.: MadhK 298.13; 517.20 sa na kaṃcid dharmam āvyūhati nirvyūhati tasyaivam anā- vyūhato 'nirvyūhatas traidhātuke cittaṃ na sajjati. Is nirvyūhati a near-synonym of āvyūhati, as niryūhati certainly is of āyūhati in Gv? Or is it (as assumed by Tib. and La Vallée-Poussin) an antonym of āvyūhati, as niryūhati is of āyūhati in Laṅk, and as (a)niryūha is of (an)āyūha? See s.vv anāyūha, anāvyūha, anirvyūha. [109,1]
