But best mss. have saṃjñā for saṃjñi; meter will be satisfied by saṃjña, and we could interpret svaka-m-eva, with hiatus-bridging m; then, my very own notion is… In SP 112.8 (vs) read prob. with Nep. mss. pitā svakasya, his own father; svakasya = Skt. svasya = ātmanaḥ.
