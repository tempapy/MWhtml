• śuddhāntika, adj. with parivāsa (= Pali suddhanta- parivāsa), a kind of probation imposed for concealed saṃghāvaśeṣa offenses when the period of concealment was unknown: MSV iii.72.11; 73.7. Acc. to the Pali Vin. comm., it lasted for a time equal to that elapsed since the offender's ordination (see SBE 17.417, note 1). [530,2]
