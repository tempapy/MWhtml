• agasti-harītakī, a kind of plant or tree: Mvy 5781; Tib. a-gas (partial transliteration). In Mironov agasti and harītakī are presented as separate items, perhaps cor- rectly; agasti is recorded as n. of a plant or tree in Skt. But the Kyōtō ed. interprets Agasti- in the cpd. as the ṛṣi-name. [004,1]
