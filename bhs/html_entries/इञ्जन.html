• iñjana, nt., or °nā, f. (both = Pali id.; cf. an-i°, prec., and next), motion, wavering, vacillation (of mind; body; hair, as a very small and delicate part of the body): LV 259.9 (vs) na ceñjanaṃ nāpi manyana-pracāraṃ (so read, see s.v. manyanā); Av i.88.7 (prose) na ca śakitaṃ bhagavato romeñjanam api kartuṃ; Gv 128.6 (prose) sarveñjana-manyana-(read so with 2d ed.) -spandana- prapañcanāpagatacittam; Gv 253.14 (vs) na ca tubhya iñjana (could be nt. or f.)…manyana-spandanā na ca prapañcā; (the rest are fem.) LV 366.3 (vs) no ca kāyeñ- janā (n. sg.); Mv ii.414.19 (vs) na sattvasārasya karonti iñjanā (acc. sg.; Senart em. °nāṃ); RP 12.15 (vs) citta- iñjanā (read as cpd.); 13.14 (vs) citteñjanā; KP 136.8 (vs) sarveñjanā-manyana-vipramuktaḥ; Dbh.g. 26(52).4 bhāvi tatha abhāve iñjanā nāsti kācit.
