• ṛtuka, adj. (ifc.) and subst. nt. (Pali -utuka, in sabbotuka), (1) of, belonging to, a season: Divy 167.8 kālartukaiś (of the time and season) copakaranair anuvi- dhīyate; Mmk 27.21 yathartukena (text yathā°) vā sugandhapuṣpeṇa; (2) fruit of the season: Divy 531.5 (navaśasyāni navaphalāni) navartukāni; so MSV i.7.6; Divy 531.8 navaiḥ phalaiḥ navaiḥ śasyakair navartukaiḥ.
