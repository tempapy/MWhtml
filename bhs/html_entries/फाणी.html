• phāṇī(-kṛta), (cf. Skt. phāṇita and Lex. phāṇi, f.), (mixed with) syrup or treacle: °kṛtaṃ mudgayūṣaṃ hareṇu- kayūṣaṃ…LV 264.16 (prose). Senart by em. puts phāṇi- kṛtam into Mv ii.204.19, relying on the LV passage; but a comparison of the corrupt Mv mss. with Pali MN i.245.19-- 20 seems to make it clear that Mv should be read: mudga- kulattha-hareṇuka-kalāya-kṛta-yūṣam upabhuñje.
