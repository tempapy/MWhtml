• kulala, m. (n. pl. °lā; = Pali id.), some bird of prey, associated with gṛdhra and kāka (as in Pali with gijjha and kāka): Mv i.7.4 °lā ca gṛdhrā ca kākolūkā ca; 11.11. One might identify it with Skt. and Pali kurara, osprey; but the latter is not used in Pali in close association with gijjha and kāka. In AMg. defined as a vulture, or (another) kind of bird. [188,1]
