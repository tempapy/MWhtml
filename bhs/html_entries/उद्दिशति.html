• uddiśati, poses, proposes (a mathematical problem; Tib. rtsis mgo phogs, set the beginning of a calculation): LV 146.12 tatra bodhisattvaś coddiśati, ekaś ca śākyaku- māro nikṣipati (figures, works out the problem) sma; na ca pariprāpayati sma bodhisattvasyaikadvau…yāvat pañcāpi śākyakumāraśatāni yugapatkāle nikṣipanti sma, na ca pariprāpayanti sma; tato bodhisattva āha: uddiśata yūyam, ahaṃ nikṣepsyāmīti…(19) pañcāpi śākyaku- māraśatāni yugapad uddiśanti sma, na ca pariprāpayanti sma bodhisattvasya nikṣipataḥ; 146.22; in 147.1 samuddi- śanti, same mg. The Tib. renders nikṣipati by brtsis (brtsi, fut.), rtsis, calculate, figure. In same sense, uddiśati sma Mvy 6657 = Tib. sbaṅs (? sbyaṅs?) pa, or luṅ phog pa (?); Chin. uses the word used for BHS vyākaraṇa (3). [130,2]
