• prasavāyitā (so mss.; could be denom. ppp. to Skt. prasava), delivered (of a child): tayā (by her, the mid- wife) sā °tā Divy 484.8 (ed. em. prasavāpitā, ppp. of MIndic caus. to Skt. prasavati, bears); the reading of the mss., and my interpretation, seem supported by the close parallel tayā prasavitā 485.12, by her she was delivered, where the form in °tā can at any rate not be caus., and seems likely to be denom. from prasava (rather than a MIndic ppp. to prasavati, = Skt. prasūtā).
