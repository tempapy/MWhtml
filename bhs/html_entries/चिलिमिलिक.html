• cilimilika, nt., or ciliminikā (var.; so Mironov and BR), Mvy 8984, some kind of cloth, or article made of cloth (BR Halschmuck, clearly wrong); cf. Pali cilimikā, cimilikā; AMg. cilimiṇī, curtain, cloth used as curtain, also cilimiligā, °miliyā, °milī. Tib. bar thaṅ (? not in Dictt.); Chin. seems to mean mattress of coarse hair (or wool).
