• Guhyakādhipati (and see Āguhyakā°), Guhya- kendra, also twice Guhyādhipati (otherwise guhya for Skt. guhyaka seems unrecorded; the cpds. in Skt. refer to Kubera), epithets of Vajrapāṇi, q.v.: LV 66.5…ma- hāyakṣasenāpatayo Guhyakādhipatiś ca nāma yakṣaku- laṃ yato Vajrapāṇer utpattis, te…; plainly stated as ep. of Vajrapāṇi, Guhyakādhi° Mmk 36.2 etc.; Guhyādhi° Mmk 36.6, 21 (both prose; in 21 misprinted Guhyădhi°); Guhyakendra Mmk 548.7. Note that Vajrapāṇi is also called Yakṣendra (Mmk 25.12) and the like. [214,1]
