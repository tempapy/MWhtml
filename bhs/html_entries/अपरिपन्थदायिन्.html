• a-paripantha-dāyin, adj., not causing fear or danger, not a source of alarm (to anyone? to himself?), said of a successful performer of certain magic rites (cf. Skt. bhaya- dāyin, and BHS paripantha which as in Pali is a near- synonym of bhaya): so read in Mmk 291.13 (text apantha- dāyī, n. sg.); 297.28 (text apatthadāyī); 299.3 (text aparipatthadāyī).
