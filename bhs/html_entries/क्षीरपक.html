• kṣīrapaka, adj. (= Pali khī°, only with vaccha; Skt. kṣīrapa is recorded only of humans; °paka not in Skt.), suckling, only with vatsa, calf: Mv iii.259.9 vatso iva kṣīrapako; Ud iii.3 and xviii.4 vatsaḥ kṣīrapaka iva (these vss correspond to Pali Ud vii.4 and Dhp. 284 respectively, which have khī°).
