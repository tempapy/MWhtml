• niṣpadi (m.c. for °dī ? = niṣpad, and Skt. niṣpatti; Sheth cites a Pkt. ṇippaddi [!possibly read ṇippadi?] by the side of ṇippatti and ṇipphatti), production, attain- ment, accomplishment: tasya mi niṣpadi bheṣyati adya LV 287.8, 10 = Tib. de ni de riṅ ṅa la ḥgrub par ḥgyur, that today for me at accomplishment arrives. It seems that niṣpadi must be a nom. sg.; this seems confirmed by 287.6 where Lefm. prints the same reading, but Tib. has thob par, obtaining, getting, = prāpti, instead of ḥgrub [Page309-a+ 71] par = niṣpadi, thus confirming the v.l. of the best mss. prāpti bhaviṣyati instead of niṣpadi bheṣyati; it would be very forced, in view of n. sg. prāpti (and for other reasons), to see anything but a n. sg. in niṣpadi.
