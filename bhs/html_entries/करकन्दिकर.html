• karakandikara, m., ? ep. of Buddha: Mv iii.269.4 (vs) śrāvakayugaṃ daśabalo āmantraye karakandikaro. Senart can do nothing with it; neither can I; I have thought of divaṃkara-karo, corrupted and metathetized, sun- rayed, cf. Pali divaṃkara = divākara, but it does not yield the meter required.
