• Padmottara, in Mv spelled also Padumot°, Padu- muttara, (1) (= Pali Padumuttara), n. of a former Buddha, one of the standard list of 24 in Pali, in most BHS cases doubtless the same individual: Mv iii.240.2; 241.14; 243.3, 4, 22; 244.18; 247.5; 248.8; LV 5.4; 172.13; Gv 206.13; Mmk 499.22; a group of 500 Buddhas of this name (Pad- mot°) are recorded Mv i.58.1, 7; 61.16; (2) n. of a future Pratyekabuddha: Av i.128.6; (3) n. of one or two Bodhi- sattvas: Gv 442.1; ŚsP 50.10.
