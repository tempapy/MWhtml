• ? Kutarārkaka, n. of a yakṣa in Kurukṣetra: Māy 57; dual dvandva, Tarārkakutarārkakau; division be- tween the two names uncertain, but Tarārkaku-tarārkakau seems implausible. Lévi, p. 97, refers to Tarantukārantu- kayor (loc. du.) Mbh. 3.81.178 (Calc. 3.7078), which he calls the name of two yakṣas; but it is obviously the name of two localities (acc. to BR, tīrthas) in Kurukṣetra. [186,1]
