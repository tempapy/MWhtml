• kṛtaka, adj. (perhaps essentially identical with Skt. kṛtaka, but with peculiar tinge of mg. as opp. of akṛtaka, q.v.): created (person or thing), fashioned, material or tangible (person or thing): Laṅk 60.14; 61.1, 9, etc.; 176.11; 187.9. Mistranslated ‘creator’ (!) by Suzuki e.g. at 61.9. [190,1]
