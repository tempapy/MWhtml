• mīḍha, m. (= Pali mīḷha; cf. also prec.), dung: Mvy 6966; mahato (mss. mahānto) mīḍha-parvatasya upariṃ Mv ii.137.11; mīḍhaṃgirī LV 197.3, mountains of dung; varjeti kāmāṃ yatha mīḍha-kumbhaṃ Mv ii.377.21 (vs), like a pot of dung; this is a favorite comparison, esp. with mīḍha-ghaṭa, LV 173.20; 174.3; 213.5; RP 19.9 (vs; text °ghatāṃ, read °ghaṭāṃ, acc. pl.); mīḍha-viṣayā(ḥ) LV 127.18; mīḍhāvaliptaḥ Śikṣ 173.8.
