• Kuśa, (1) n. of a king, previous incarnation of the Buddha (in the 7th bhūmi): Mv i.128.13 ff.; (2) = Pali Kusa (hero of Kusa Jātaka); his story is told in Mv twice at great length, Mv ii.433.19 ff. and iii.8.3 ff.; (kuśajātakaṃ samāptam iii.27.21;) also MSV i.100.11 ff.; probably referred to (rather than 1 above) in Mvy 3566, in list of cakravartin kings.
