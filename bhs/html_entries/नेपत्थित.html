• nepatthita, or (?) nepathyita (denom. ppp. to Skt. nepathya), clothed, garbed: some such form (possibly with Prakritic v for p) must be read in Divy 48.24; 49.16; 159.12; 342.2; 463.26; 558.22; Av i.284.11; 347.9; MSV i.71.19; in all these the mss. (followed by edd.) are corrupt, showing such readings as neva (naiva) sthita, nopasthita, (once, Av i.347.9) nepathyasthita, in Divy 436.26 te panthitā; cf. also nipacchita, representing the same form.
