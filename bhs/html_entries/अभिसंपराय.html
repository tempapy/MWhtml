• abhisaṃparāya, m. (= Pali id.; based on the adv. Pali °rāyaṃ, prepos. cpd., see CPD; cf. saṃparāya), future state: LV 88.20 (kā…gatir…) ko 'bhisaṃparāyaḥ (in Pali also correlated with gati); Divy 187.16 and 200.5 kā gātiḥ kā upapattiḥ ko °yaḥ; in Bbh 25.16 and 28.1 dṛṣṭe dharme abhisaṃparāye vā (ca) in the present state or (and) in a future one (in this last phrase Pali uses instead the adv. °rāyaṃ, see CPD: diṭṭhe c’ eva dhamme abhisam- parāyaṃ ca DN iii.83.19 etc.).
