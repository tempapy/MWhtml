• naiḥsargika, adj. (= niḥsargika, q.v.; also written naisar°), involving forfeiture: °kāpattiḥ Mvy 9309 (text nai-sa°, Index naiḥs°, so Mironov with v.l. nai-sa°); °kāḥ pāyattikāḥ (see this; in Pali the corresponding pācittiya offenses include the nissaggiya) Mvy 8383; in both Tib. spaṅ ba, giving up, renouncing, or the like; applied to robes (cīvarāṇi), subject to forfeiture: Divy 19.26 (here mss. nai-sa°); 21.21.
