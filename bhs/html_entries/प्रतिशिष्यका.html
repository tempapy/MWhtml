• ? -pratiśiṣyakā, in śiṣya-pra°, perhaps (pupil's) pupilhood: (yasya tāvad) vayaṃ śiṣya-pratiśiṣyakayāpi na tulyāḥ Divy 153.14, of whom (viz. the Buddha), you see, we are not equal to the pupil's pupilhood (having been shown to be inferior to Cunda, the novice-attendant of Śāriputra, the disciple of Buddha).
