• Saṃtuṣita (= Pali °sita), n. of the chief of the Tuṣita gods (his official position esp. suggested by LV 363.21 °to devaputraḥ sārdhaṃ tuṣitakāyikair devair; also 302.6; 364.13): LV 44.10; 59.2; 241.2; 441.18; Mv i.208.13; 230.13; 263.20; ii.11.1; Mvy 3137; Mmk 45.8; 69.6; Bbh 343.5. Cases where a plurality (as if = tuṣitāḥ as a class) seems suggested are only apparent; see s.v. Suyāma for instances in Gv, Dbh.g., and RP. [556,1]
