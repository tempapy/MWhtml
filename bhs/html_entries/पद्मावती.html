• Pad(u)māvatī, (1) n. of a girl of miraculous birth who became the wife of King Brahmadatta of Kāmpilya; heroine of the ‘Pad(u)māvatī parikalpa’ (colophon Mv iii.170.10): Mv iii.155.7 ff. (mss. vary between Padumā° and Padmā°, Senart prints the former); (2) n. of a devaku- mārikā in the northern quarter: Mv iii.309.8 (Padumā°) = LV 391.3 (Padmā°, meter rectified by a ‘patch-word’), vs; (3) n. of a wife of King Aśoka, mother of Kunāla: Divy 405.17.
