• pratihārayati, °reti (prob. denom. from Skt. prati- hāra, usher, rather than caus. to prati-har-), announces: (speaking to the king's pratihāra) Śuddhodanasya prati- hāretha, Asito ṛṣi darśanakāmo; pratihāreṇa rājño nive- ditaṃ Mv ii.31.12; has announced, causes to be announced: rājñaḥ pratihārayām āsa Jm 118.13 (that he wished an audience).
