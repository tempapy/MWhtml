• niyati, absorption, addiction (sc. to worldly things): Śikṣ 19.18 (na…parigraho…) nādhyavasānaṃ, na niyatiḥ, na tṛṣṇānuśayaḥ kartavyaḥ. Not so recorded in Skt. or Pali; but Skt. niyata is used similarly (BR s.v. yam plus ni, 3: sich beschränkend…ganz bei einer Sache seiend). Elsewhere niyanti, q.v., an irregular formation, is used in the same sense. [298,2]
