• ? karha-cid, adv. (cf. Skt. karhi-cid; = Pali karaha- ci, only after kadā-ci, and in same mg. acc. to DN comm. i.110.3), ever, at any time: in one old ms. of SP inserted after kadācid SP 257.1 (prose), Baruch, Beiträge zum Saddharmapuṇḍarīkasūtra, 23 and 28; omitted in all other mss. The coincidence with Pali usage is so extra- ordinary that I am inclined with Baruch to regard it as old. [171,1]
