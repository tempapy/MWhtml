• sūtrānta, m. (= Pali suttanta), a Buddhist sūtra (1) text: °ta-vaiyākaraṇābhigīto Mv iii.122.21 (vs), so virtually as mss., and as meter requires; °to LV 4.18, of the LV itself; Mvy 805; 1435 (°taḥ); Divy 274.14; Bbh 46.8 (°tān); etc., common; forms in -āṃ, before vowels even -ām, are (with Weller 23) to be understood as acc. pl. m., not acc. sg. f.; so, ya imām (!) evaṃ bhadrikāṃ sūtrāntāṃ pratikṣepsyanti LV 88.14 (prose); similarly 88.16.
