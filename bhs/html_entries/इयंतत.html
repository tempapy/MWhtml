• iyaṃtata, adj., of such an extent, so great, or (here) so small: Sukh 32.1 (prose) tad yathā sa ekavindur iyaṃtataḥ sa prathamasaṃnipāto (see saṃnipāta) 'bhūt. Could this be an error for iyantaḥ = Skt. iyān, n. sg. m. of iyant? But I have not noted such MIndic morphology in the prose of Sukh. Emendation to iyattakaḥ (Vedic only and rare) is not attractive.
