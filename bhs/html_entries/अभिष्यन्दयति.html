• abhiṣyandayati, °deti (= Pali abhisandeti; caus. to °ṣyandati), drenches, saturates, irrigates, (a) lit. of plants etc., Mv iii.301.17 vāriṇā °deti pariṣyandeti (q.v.); ppp. °dita, SP 122.8 (plants and seeds); Mvy 6584 (= Tib. brlan pa, sbaṅs pa, drenched, wet); fig. of kuśalamūlāni, roots of merit, °dayiṣyati, fut., Gv 457.18; (b) of mental organs or states, °dita-niścita-citto Gv 151.13; -karuṇā- snehābhiṣyandita-saṃtāno 189.9; in yoga practice, °dayati softens, makes supple (one's own person), as in Pali (CPD), Mvy 1646 = Tib. mṅon du mñen par byed pa, makes very supple; here too followed by pariṣyandayati.
