• iñjita (= Pali id.), (1) primarily ppp. of iñjate, q.v.; see iñjita-tva; (2) subst. nt., motion, movement (literal and physical): Mv i.305.21 (vs) prāsādiken' iṃjitena (read ijitena; § 2.73; cf. ijya- forms s.v. iñjate) pravi- śantāṃ (buddhasya śrāvakān), entering with serene walk; (3) subst. nt., mobility, vacillation, unsteadiness; = iñjana or °nā, and like these often associated with manyanā (or manyita), spandita (miswritten sya°), and other qualities deprecated in religious life: Mvy 7218 °tam = Tib. gyo ba, foll. by syanditam; SP 336.3 (vs) varjitvā iñjitā (v.l. °tān) manyitāni ca; 372.7 (prose) iñjita-manyita- prapañcitāni jñāsyati; Dbh 64.13 sarveñjita-manyanā- syandita-vikalpāpagato bhavati; Bbh 339.18 (prose) (-abhiniveśa-, q.v.) -sarveñjitāni…prahīyante; 340.21 sarvābhiniveśeñjitaprahāṇataś ca. See also an-iñjita. [113,2]
