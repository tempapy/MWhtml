• yadbhūyasikīya, m., sc. vinaya (= Pali yebhuy- yasikā, sc. kiriyā, described MN ii.247.19 ff., see Childers and PTSD s.v.), (procedure) decided by vote of a majority (of monks), one of the 7 adhikaraṇa-śamatha: Mvy 8634. In MSV ii.207.12 yadbhūyaiṣīyaṃ (nt., with karma) = this; it follows tatsvabhāvaiṣīyaṃ and imitates it in ending (by secondary corruption?); it is however repeated below, 207.19 etc. [444,1]
