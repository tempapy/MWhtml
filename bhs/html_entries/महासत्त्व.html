• mahāsattva, m. (1) ‘great Being’, standard ep. of Bodhisattvas: Mvy 626 et passim (very often follows bodhisattva); (2) n. of a prince, hero of Vyāghrīparivarta (ch. 18) of Suv: Suv 206.13 ff. Several times in the verse version, 225.11, 234.13, 238.14, Mahāsattva-varo (°varaḥ) is printed as if a cpd., tho the name otherwise appears as Mahāsattva also in vss, e.g. 226.6 etc.; perhaps better division would be Mahāsattva (m.c. for °vo, °vaḥ) plus (adj.) varaḥ.
