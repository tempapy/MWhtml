• [? kāla-veṣin, n. sg. °veṣī, said of the Bodhisattva: LV 160.22 and 161.11 (prose), parallel and evidently equivalent to kālākālajña or kālajña, respectively. One ms. in 160.22 (H, generally a poor one) and three (H and B) in 161.11 read kāla-gaveṣī, and this seems the only possible reading: seeking the right occasions.
