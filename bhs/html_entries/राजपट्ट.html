• rājapaṭṭa, nt. (in Skt. said to mean an inferior sort of diamond), a kind of (blue) dye-stuff, in a list of dyes: Mvy 5921 = Tib. (m)thiṅ śiṅ, variously defined as the indigo plant, indigo (dye or color), mountain blue (the mineral azurite), and (Das) ‘monolith of turquoise’; in MSV ii.142.9 ms. cited rājavadyādayas (Tib. thiṅ śiṅ); ed. em. rājapaṭyād°, but prob. read rājapaṭṭād° (or rājavaṭṭād°?).
