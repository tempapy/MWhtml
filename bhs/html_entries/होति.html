• hoti (= Pali id.), for Skt. bhavati, see Chap. 43, s.v. bhū (2). The pres. hoti is rare compared to bhoti; on the other hand, in futures of the hohiti (hohati) type, ho- is much commoner than bho-; and it is doubtful whether bhoṣyati (instead of bheṣyati) should be recognized at all as fut.
