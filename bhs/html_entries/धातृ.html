• dhātṛ = dhātrī, nurse: mātṛbhir dhātṛbhiś (both edd., no v.l.) ca rudantībhiḥ parivṛtāḥ SP 160.14 (prose), surrounded by their weeping mothers and nurses. The form has phonetic aspects (§ 3.95) but may and doubtless should be regarded as in part, and perhaps primarily, a riming adaptation to the preceding mātṛbhiḥ. [284,1]
