• uṣṭradhūmaka (m.?), lit. camel-smoke, a kind of worm, cause of calamities: KP 93.2 yasmiṃ…deśe °ka kṛṣṇaśira uttānaśāyī bhavati, sa deśa sopadravaḥ…Tib. srin bu rṅa mo dud ka zhes bya ba, the worm called camel- smoke. See Das s.v. rṅa mo ṅud (mistake for dud) ka, where full confirmation of the KP passage is found. [149,2]
