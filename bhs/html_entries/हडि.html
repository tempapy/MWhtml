• haḍi (Skt. Lex. once, said to be m., BR; AMg. id., said to be f., Ratnach.), fetter made of wood; so Tib., confirmed by SP, below; always associated with, usually immediately followed by, nigaḍa, iron shackles: haḍi- nigaḍa LV 337.9 (vs) = Tib. śiṅ sgrog daṅ ni lcags sgrog, wooden fetters and iron fetters; dārvāyasmayair (so text, see this) haḍi-nigaḍa-bandhanair SP 440.4 (prose); dārumayair ayomayair haḍi-nigaḍair iha baddha ban- dhanaiḥ SP 450.1 (vs); haḍi-nigaḍa- Mv i.18.1 (so mss., Senart wrongly em. hasti-ni°); Divy 250.29; 365.4; 435.17; Gv 353.11 (see s.v. kaṭaka); haḍi-daṇḍa-bandha- nigaḍāś Gv 213.24 (vs). Cf. prec. [617,1]
