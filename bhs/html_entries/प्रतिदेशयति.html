• pratideśayati, (1) (= Pali paṭideseti; cf. pratidi- śati), confesses (a sin): taṃ (sc. pāpaṃ) pratideśayamī ahu sarvaṃ Bhad 8; also with obj. pāpam Suv 34.4; Kv 31.5; Mmk 146.4; (pāpakaṃ karma…) °yāmi Sādh 55.14 (prose), etc.; with āpattiṃ Śikṣ 65.6; misc. LV 379.13 (see s.v. atyaya); Bbh 263.21; gdve. impersonal, °śayitavyaṃ Prāt 525.5, he must confess; (2) renounces, rejects; only noted in AsP 328.6 bodhicittaṃ (so read) °śaya pratiniḥsṛja (q.v.; the two are synonyms here), and 14 yad etat tvayā pūrvaṃ śrutaṃ tat pratideśaya, yat tvayā pūrvaṃ parigṛhītaṃ tat pratiniḥsṛja (thus Māra tempts Bodhisattvas).
