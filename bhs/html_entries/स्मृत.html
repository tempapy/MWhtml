• smṛta, ppp. as adj. (= Pali sata, regularly followed by saṃpajāna), mindful, in a state of full awareness or consciousness, very common with following saṃprajā- na(nt): smṛtaḥ saṃprajānan Mvy 1480 (Tib. dran pa, = smṛti,…can, possessing); Av i.228.1; ii.197.13, etc.; smṛto saṃprajāno Mv i.206.4 = ii.9.20; i.218.10 = ii.20.11 etc.; smṛtāyāṃ saṃprajānāyāṃ Mv i.205.8 = ii.9.4 (of the Bodhisattva's mother). Sometimes replaced by smṛtimant, q.v. [614,1]
