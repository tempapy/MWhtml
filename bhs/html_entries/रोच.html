• roca (Vedic as adj. once; not in Pali; M. roa once in different mg.), (1) nt., light (or, possibly, adj., shining): sarvabuddhānusāsanīcakrarocaṃ nāma prajñāpāramitā- mukhaṃ Gv 449.20; (2) m., a kind of flower: Mvy 6183 = Tib. me tog mdog mdzes, fair-colored flower or rose; (3) (= Pali Roja 2 in DPPN) n. of an ancient king, in Pali and Mvy son of Mahāsaṃmata; in Mv, acc. to the (corrupt) ms. which contains the word, son of Kalyāṇa and grandson of Mahāsaṃmata: Mvy 3553; in Mv i.348.8 the v.l. of one ms. shows that this name was in the list, tho it is confused with Rava q.v.; (4) (= Pali Roja 1 in DPPN) n. of a minister of the Mallas in Pāpā: MSV i.282.2 ff. (uncle of Ānanda, but unbelieving; converted by Buddha). [457,1]
