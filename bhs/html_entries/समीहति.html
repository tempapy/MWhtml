• ? samīhati (in Pali may, it seems, mean moves, intrans.: ākāsamhi °ti Vv.5.1, comm. 35.15 ff…carati gacchati), ppp. (vāyu-)samīhita, stirred (by the wind), based on act. (caus.?) mg. (°hayati?): °tā kisalayās LV 326.4 (vs). The mg. is certain, the reading less so; some mss. °samīritā; but the occurrence of māra-samīritāḥ in the prec. line suggests that this v.l. may have been only a lect. fac.; most and the best mss. are reported with °samīhitā.
