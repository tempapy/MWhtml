• vrata-pada, (m. or) nt. (cf. Pali vata-pada, not recorded of this group), point of religious practice; five, viz. the five śikṣāpada, q.v.: MSV iii.128.10 ff.; in MSV [Page521-a+ 7] iv.249.2 ff. the five vra°, as taught by Devadatta, are listed as āraṇyakatva, vṛkṣamūlikatva, traicīvarikatva, pāṃsu- kūlikatva (no fifth named; these are dhūtaguṇa).
