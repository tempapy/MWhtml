• abhiprāya, m., difference: Divy 222.20 (na…) kaścid viśeṣo vā 'bhiprāyo vā nānākaraṇaṃ vā. Based on use of Pali adhippāya (= Skt. abhiprāya) intention, but also difference; on the latter mg. see CPD s.v. The old Buddhist word adhippāya was Sktized, keeping this new mg. in addition to its normal Skt. mg. Compare with Divy passage the Pali, ko viseso ko adhippāyo kiṃ nānākaraṇaṃ MN i.64.23--24. [054,2]
