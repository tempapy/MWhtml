• dhanuṣkārika or °kā, a kind of flowering tree: (puṣpavṛkṣāṇāṃ…) sumanā-vārṣikā-dhanuṣkārikādīnāṃ Divy 628.15 (prose). Obviously = the commoner dhā- nuṣkārin or °rika, °rikā (°rīka), q.v. But in Pali recorded only with dhan°: dhanukārika (in a cpd.) Jāt. v.420.7, which (tho prose) is glossed in the comm. 422.28, which replaces this word with dhanukārī(ti), glossing it with dhanupātaḷi (otherwise unknown). [275,2]
