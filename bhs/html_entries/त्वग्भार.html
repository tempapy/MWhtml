• tvagbhāra (m.), part of a tree, some part of the bark or a kind of bark: °ra-taś ca Divy 628.1, see s.v. phalgu 1. One is tempted to em. to tvaksāra- (Pali tacasāra); the fact that sārataḥ follows is no objection, since it means as to the pith; but unless the mss. actually read tvak-, the misreading implied is not easy to assume.
