• niṣṭhātva, nt. (cf. Skt. niṣṭhā, used in same mg.), assurance, certitude: °tvaṃ tatra gacchethā(ḥ) LV 90.6 (prose), be assured on this point. In akopyaṃ tac cakram atyantaniṣṭhatvāt LV 422.17 (prose), the last word must be interpreted as atyanta-niṣṭha, Bhvr., having infinite certitude (or the like; Skt. niṣṭhā) plus -tva. [308,2]
