• rutā = ruta, voice, speech: in LV 286.20--21 (prose) read, ekarutā-(Lefm. °tāṃ, misprint?)-sarva-ruta-ravaṇī (Lefm. °racanī, v.l. °ramaṇī; our reading proved by Tib. skad cig gis skad thams cad sgrog pa daṅ, by one speech proclaiming all speech), ep. of the Buddha's voice or speech; this form, in prose, confirms rutā in Mvy 482--3, cited s.v. ravita, despite the fact that the same passage in Sūtral. xii.9, comm. (p.80, lines 19--20) has ruta. [456,1]
