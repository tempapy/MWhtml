• niryāṇika, adj. (to prec., 3; = Pali niyyā°, nīyā°; also nairyā°, q.v.), conducive to deliverance, emancipation: nāyaṃ mārgo niryāṇiko Mv ii.200.8; in LV 239.14 Lefm. ayaṃ khalv Ārāḍasya dharmo na nairyāṇiko, with ms. A only, other mss. and Calc. niry°; Tib. ṅes bar ḥbyuṅ ba ma yin te (see niryāti and niryāta 2). Cf. also nir- vāṇika. [303,2]
