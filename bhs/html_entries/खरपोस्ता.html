• kharapostā, n. sg., n. of a yakṣa: Māy 33. See Lévi 74, who thinks-postā preserves a relative of Skt. pustaka, which acc. to Gauthiot, MSL 19 (1915).130, was borrowed from Iranian pōst, skin; ass-skin is what at least one Chin. transl. renders; but there is some suggestion of a reading lomā (Skt. loman) instead of -postā.
