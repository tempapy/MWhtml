• vikhāditaka, nt. (to ppp. of prec., q.v.; = Pali vikkhāyitaka), a corpse devoured (by animals); -saṃjñā, contemplation of the notion of such a corpse, one of the aśubha-bhāvanā (q.v.) or -saṃjñā: Mvy 1161; Bhīk 27a.2 (printed vikhyād°); ŚsP 59.11; 1258.7 (printed corruptly vivādika-saṃjñā); 1431.20.
