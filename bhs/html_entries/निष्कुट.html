• niṣkuṭa, nt. (AMg. nikkhuḍa, acc. to Ratnach. balcony, entrance), small room (so Tib., khaṅ phran), cell (Kern), chambre (Burnouf): SP 83.4 (vs) bahūni cā (so read) niṣkuṭa (separate word, n. pl.) saṃkaṭāni uccāra- pūrṇāni jugupsitāni, and there were many small rooms (closets), narrow, full of excrement, revolting.
