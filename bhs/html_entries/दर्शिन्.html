• -darśin (from Skt. darśa, aspect, plus -in), having the aspect of, resembling, like: tathāgatadarśī ca veditavyo SP 226.8 (prose), and he is to be regarded as like a T. (because he has similar qualities and behavior; cf. lines 1--2 above, sa hi…tathāgato veditavyaḥ; so Kern, clearly rightly; Burnouf, wrongly, as having seen the T.). [262,2]
