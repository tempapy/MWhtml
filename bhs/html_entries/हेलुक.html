• heluka (m. or nt.), a high number: Gv 106.3; prob. a doublet for the next, for which the same line also has loka (read aloka or āl°), before this, and separated from it only by indriya which no other list contains; this, like heluga in Mvy 7741, is followed by durbuda (Mvy dra- buddha), and then by haruṇa (in both alike).
