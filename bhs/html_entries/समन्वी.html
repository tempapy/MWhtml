• samanvī, in Gv 254.11 (vs), app. a semi-MIndic contracted form for samanvitaḥ, provided: dvātriṃśalak- ṣaṇa-°vī so 'nuvyañjanai (read anu°?) racitagātraḥ; the meter is perfect in the first pāda, and may be partly responsible for this form, which I derive from a con- traction of a MIndic form resembling AMg. samaṇṇia (with Skt. nv restored); see § 3.118.
