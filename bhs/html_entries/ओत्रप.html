• otrapa, otrapya (nt.?; cf. an-otrapa, apatrāpya, Pali ottappa; the short vowel in the penult is doubtless of MIndic character, and the single p, when it occurs, doubtless m.c., as in an-otrapa), modesty, shame, decency: Mv ii.357.14, mss. hirī-otrasya (v.l. -otasya)-saṃpanno; Senart °ottappa°, but read rather °otrapya°; LV 329.13 lajji (read as separate word) hirotrapāt tu, Lefm., but most and best mss. end in -patta for -pāt tu, and this may be the orig. form (abl., see § 8.56).
