• sāmudraka-lekhaka, m. (or adj.), Mvy 8898; v.l. samudraka-lekha, so Mironov (and °lekhaka, from Minayeff, pw 7.381, suggesting em. to sāmu°), one who is marked with (unfavorable) bodily marks and lines (in a list of bodily deformed persons); cf. Skt. samudra, and prec.; Tib. lag pa (hand) ḥjas te stebs (or ltebs) pa (= ?); Chin. one who has a weak and crooked hand; Jap. one with unlucky lines on the hand. [592,2]
