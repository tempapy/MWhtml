• tantrākulajāta, adj. (= Pali tantākulakajāta; see under prec.), become confused like an (entangled) web (or warp): LV 205.11 (so mss., wrongly em. in ed.). The Tib. transl. under prec. fits this, which is proved by Pali to be the orig. form, rather than tantra-jāla-.
