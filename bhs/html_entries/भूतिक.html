Mus, cited s.v. saṃjīva;] abhūtikāś ca bhūtāś ca Laṅk 239.9, or °kā ca bhūtāni 368.14, having no (real) origin or basis; (2) adj. (to bhūta, in different senses, plus -ika, cf. Skt. and BHS bhautika), what is derived from the elements (in this sense AMg. bhūtiya, and cf. Pali cātummahābhūtika): Laṅk 355.2 janma bhūtānāṃ bhūtikasya ca (cf. bhautikam in prec. line); from bhūta in another mg., sarvabhūtikā balir deyā Mmk 39.18 (prose), an offering to all goblins (or creatures?) is to be made.
