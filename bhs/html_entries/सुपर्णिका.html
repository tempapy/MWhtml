• suparṇikā, in Divy 190.12 suparṇikā kuṭi (mss.; ed. kuṭir; perhaps kuṭī?), either (hut) made of fair leaves, leafy, or perhaps (hut) made of the suparṇikā plant; several plants are reported as called by that name in Skt. Lexx.; see pw. No adjective *suparṇaka is recorded. [599,2]
