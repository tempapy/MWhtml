• antodaka, adj. (for antar(r) or Pali anto, within, plus (u)daka water, prepos. cpd.), within the water: Mv iii.318.7 (prose) antodakāni (utpalāni etc.); in Mv ii.121.9 antodake, perhaps in (a place that is within the) water; this could be two words, = Skt. antar udake, but the parallel suggests that it too is a cpd.
