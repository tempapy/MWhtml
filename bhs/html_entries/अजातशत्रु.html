• Ajātaśatru (= Pali Ajātasattu), n. of a king of Magadha, son of Bimbisāra: SP 5.5 (attends the Buddha); Divy 55.17; 279.20 ff.; 369.9; 380.18; 545.8; Av i.54.6 ff.; 83.6; 88.7; 308.5 ff.; ii.182.1 ff.; Śikṣ 274.3--4 (from Tathā- gataguhya-sūtra); Mmk 590.2 (in the sequel repeatedly referred to as Ajātākhya, e.g. ajātākhyo nṛpottamaḥ 592.7); Karmav 45.3; 49.20 (summary of his story), et alibi.
