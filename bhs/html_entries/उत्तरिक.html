• uttarika (Pali, see below), in Śikṣ 332.1 (vs) ăjīvika- dharma-carāṇāṃ uttarikāṇa anuttarikāṇāṃ, of Ājīvika- sectarians, superior and not superior(?); acc. to Bendall and Rouse, Transl., those who have or those who have not the higher aim, which is hardly illuminating. See Bendall's note in Text p. 414 for report of a Chin. interpretation, for which I find no basis in Indic. In Pali uttarika is reported only Nett. 50.10 °kānaṃ phalānaṃ pattiyā; obviously superior or the like. Pali has no anuttarika. [124,1]
