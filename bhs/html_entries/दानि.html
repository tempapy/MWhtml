• dāni, dāniṃ, dānim (before vowel), (= Pali dāni, before vowel dānim, Childers; for Skt. idānīm) now; dāni SP 113.3; 170.3 (vss); in prose in Mv, i.17.10; 18.10; 21.4, 5; i.226.14 = ii.29.17; i.227.4; 232.2, 7; ii.26.5, etc., very common; also App. to Jm (= Mv) 240.5; in vss of Mv, dāni i.142.15; 143.1; 155.1, 5; i.204.19 = ii.8.14; in i.154.12 (vs) Senart kiṃ dāni ā°, but read dānim with 2 mss., the others dānīm, unmetr.; i.221.21 = ii.24.8 (vs) dānim, before vowel; in ii.11.12 (vs) dāniṃ, before cons., m.c.; in ii.6.18 (vs, = i.203.1, where Senart prints dāni) and i.209.3 (vs) Senart em. dāniṃ, m.c., for mss. dāni.
