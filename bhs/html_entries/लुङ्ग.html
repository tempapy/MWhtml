• ? luṅga: in Divy 650.22 iti luṅgādhyāyaḥ, colophon to a chapter which is not given in the text; the word is not mentioned in Index or notes; the following colophons suggest that the chapter may have dealt with signs or omens of some sort. Skt. Lex. luṅga = mātuluṅga, citron, (fruit or tree); otherwise not recorded. [462,2]
