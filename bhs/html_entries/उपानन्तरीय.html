• upānantarīya, nt., secondary deadly sin (upa plus ān°, or, without upa, ānantarya, q.v.): pañcopānantarī- yāṇi Mvy 2329, title of Chap. 123; Mironov, instead, pañcānantarya-sahagatāni, but Tib. contains ñe ba, usually = upa. Five such are listed 2330--4: mātur arhatyā dūṣa- ṇam, niyatabhūmisthitasya bodhisattvasya māraṇam, śaiksasya māraṇam, saṃghāyadvāraharanam (see āya- dvāra, 2), stūpabhedanam. [146,1]
