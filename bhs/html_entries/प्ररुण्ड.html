• praruṇḍa, adj., quasi-ppp. (on etym. see ruṇḍa), (1) weeping: te…praruṇḍā (mss. °ḍo) aśrukaṇṭhā rudan- mukhā paridevetsuḥ Mv ii.217.11, they lamented, weeping, with tears in their voices (lit. throats), with tearful faces; (2) as periphrasis for past tense, wept: rājā °ḍo Mv ii. 216.19, the king wept; devī °ḍā 426.16; (parivāro) °ḍo 427.12; Alindā °ḍā 427.17; others iii.263.5 (thrice).
