• marṣayati (caus. to Skt. mṛṣ-), asks to be excused from, declines (politely): marṣehi grāmavaram Mv iii.192.1, [Page420-b+ 71] ask (the king, who had offered a present of a village) to excuse you from the boon of a village, i.e. decline it. (This seems a natural development of Skt. mṛṣ-, caus.; Senart finds it troublesome.) [420,1]
