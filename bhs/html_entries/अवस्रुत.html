• avasruta, adj. (= Pali avassuta), wet, dripping, and so foul, rotten, orig. of a tree the inside of which is rotten, Pali AN iv.171.9 (rukkhāni) antopūtīni avassutāni kasam- bujātāni; fig., usually in Pali, and in BHS, of evil monks: Mvy 9138 antarpūty (read as separate word, see antaḥpūti) avasrutaḥ; foll. by kaṣambaka-jātaḥ (see kaśambaka); MSV i.50.7 antaḥpūtir °taḥ kaṣambaka-jātaḥ.
