• goṣṭhika (once written gau°; not in this sense in Skt., but only as adj., relating to a guild; not in Pali; but = AMg. goṭṭhiya, °iga), m., usually pl., (boon-)companion, member of a friendly group: Mv iii.375.13 triṃśa(d) goṣṭhikā udyāna- bhūmiṃ nirgatā; id. 15 and 376.1--2 tehi goṣṭhikehi; 376.5, 7, 8; Av i.93.6; saṃbahulāś ca goṣṭhikā…163.7; °kānāṃ śataṃ nirgataṃ 377.14; ii.53.9 ff. (in 55.9 ms. gauṣṭhika, see Speyer's note); 100.13 ff.; MSV ii.78.2. Obscure is Mv iii.121.9 (vs), in which Śuddhodana addresses the Buddha: purā tuvaṃ goṣṭhika-tūlikāsu…; Senart suggests doubt- fully coussins, divans où on est assis de compagnie (tūlikā, mattress, Skt.).
