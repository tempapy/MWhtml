• saṃdhi-cchettar, m. (primarily, doubtless, = next, but here paradoxically used in a good sense, like aśraddha and akṛtajña, qq.v.; see saṃdhi 6), one who cuts the bonds (of existence): aśraddhaś cākṛtajñaś ca °ttā ca yo naraḥ…(sa) vai tūttamapūruṣaḥ Ud xxix.33 (= Pali Dhp. 97, where saṃdhichedo; comm. vaṭṭasaṃdhiṃ saṃ- sārasaṃdhiṃ chetvā ṭhito).
