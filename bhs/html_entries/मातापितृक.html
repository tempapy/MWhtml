• mātāpitṛka, adj. (cf. prec. and next but one), inherited, innate, natural, contrasting with what is due to training: (the Bodhisattva had not received martial or athletic training, so that Yaśodharā's father refused him as a son-in-law; but he,) mātāpitṛkena balena Mv ii.75.13, by natural, innate strength, threw a dead elephant over seven city walls.
