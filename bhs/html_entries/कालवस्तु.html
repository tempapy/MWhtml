• kālavastu, nt., abode of death (i.e. place of desolation; so Senart): Mv iii.360.1 (vs) uddahyatu imaṃ nagaraṃ kālavastuṃ (n. sg.) karīyatu. But possibly, as suggested by PTSD s.v. tāla-vatthu, a corruption for tāla-vastu (or tālāvastu); the Pali word means destroyed utterly. [180,2]
