Devāvataraṇa) Divy 150.22; °śye (here mss. Saṃkāśye) nagare 'vatīrṇo 'haṃ Divy 401.22; Saṃkaśye Mmk 88.14, see s.v. Devā- vatāra; Śaṃkaśye (so text, read Saṃ°) tathā kṛtvā ṛddhir janapade tadā (so text; read ṛddhīr, ṛddhiṃ?) Mmk 582.24 (vs); this is followed by the two lines cited s.v.
