• śūnyaka, adj. (Skt. Gr. id., AMg. sunnaya; = Skt. śūnya; pejorative force may be suspected, at least in some cases), empty, vain: AsP 346.9, see s.v. asāraka; °ka dharma nirātmaka sarve Samādh 19.30 (vs); saṃskāra nirīha °kāḥ LV 176.3 (vs); (kṣetrakoṭinayutāni bahūni) °kāni puruṣapravarehi Mv i.122.3 (vs), empty (in lit. sense) of Buddhas, containing none.
