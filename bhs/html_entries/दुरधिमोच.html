• duradhimoca, °cya, hard to strive zealously towards (see adhimukti and its congeners): SP 185.9 duradhimo- [Page266-b+ 71] cyaṃ (tathāgatajñānaṃ; Kashgar rec. °mucyanīyaṃ, cf. adhimucyanā, °natā); said apropos of the fact that disciples of the Buddha in many past existences are still in the same stage of disciplehood; AsP 185.2, 5, et passim, duradhimocā (prajñāpāramitā); read with 2d ed. dura- dhimocaṃ Gv 321.19 for text durabhimocaṃ (etat sthā- naṃ, sc. anuttarā samyaksaṃbodhi).
