• prākṛtaka, adj. (= Skt. °ta; cf. prec.), common, vulgar: °kena veṣeṇa Mv ii.449.10, in mean garb (con- trasted with royal garb); ordinary, natural, as distinguished from supernatural, divine: śrotrendriyaṃ…anāvilaṃ prākṛtakaṃ ca tāvat SP 357.10 (vs), and yet his power of hearing is still natural (not ‘divya’); similarly 359.12 (vs); in both -ka might be m.c.
