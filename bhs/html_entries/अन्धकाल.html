• andhakāla (m. or nt.), (1) time of being blind: Kv 59.4, said of a son by his parents, (jarākāle yaṣṭibhūto) andhakāle mārgasyopadarśakaḥ (so read for text °opa- mardakaḥ; foll. by maraṇakāle piṇḍadātā); (2) n. of a hell: Kv 18.14 (read Andhakāra? but this is not recorded as n. of a hell).
