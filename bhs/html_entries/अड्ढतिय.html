• aḍḍhatiya (= Pali id., acc. to CPD by haplol. for aḍḍhatatiya = Skt. ardhatṛtīya), or aḍḍhā°, two and a half. Senart's Index cites only aḍḍhātiya, which is read in text Mv iii.72.12 (mss. āvṛttiya) and 13 (mss. aṭṭātiya or āṭṭā°), while aḍḍhatiya is read in text Mv ii.312.10 (mss. aṭṭāti, omitting ya) and iii.75.7 (mss. aṭṭatiya).
