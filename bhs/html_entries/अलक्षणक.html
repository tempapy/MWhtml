• alakṣaṇaka, adj., with Buddha, (a Buddha) with- out the (32) marks (so Burnouf Intr. 378, note 1), i.e. a substitute for a Buddha, a ‘near-Buddha’ (not actually a Buddha but a saint living in the absence of a B. and ‘Buddha-work’, buddha-kārya), said of Upagupta: Divy 348.24; 350.28; 356.20; 357.24; 385.8; MSV i.4.3.
