• [upasthāpana-, see upasthāna-kāri; read upas- [Page144-a+ 71] thāna-. To be sure PTSD defines Pali upaṭṭhāpana by attendance, service; but in the only passage cited, Vin. iv.291.13, cf. the comm. 27, it certainly is causative: causing (someone else) to attend, wait upon. That meaning is impossible in Mv iii.37.5, 8, 12.] [143,2]
