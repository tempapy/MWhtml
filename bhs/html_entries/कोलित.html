• Kolita (or Kolika; = Pali, both; in Mv i.27.4 and 28.4 most mss. Kolika, Senart always °ta), the given name of Maudgalyāyana: Mv i.5.2; 27.4; 28.4; 30.3; 62.10, 12; story of his conversion, iii.56.16 ff.; in Mvy 1048 Kolita is named in a list of śrāvakas, not juxtaposed with Maudgalyāyana, who is named earlier in the same list.
