• citrika, nt., apparently wonder, marvel, thrilling and [Page230-b+ 71] pleasing thing or experience: nāsti loke citrikaṃ yaṃ me na parityaktaṃ tasya arthasya āharaṇatāye Mv ii.279.16, said by the Bodhisattva as a basis for his noble pride (ārya-māna); in a series of like formulas, the preceding containing ramaṇīyatā in place of citrikaṃ, and the next aiśvaryaṃ.
