• pratisaṃvedaka, adj. (to °vedayati), (1) experiencing (pleasure or pain): sukhaduḥkhāprati°kāny (sapta vij- [Page371-a+ 71] ñānāni, other than ālayavijñāna) Laṅk 221.5, they do not experience… (so Tib. mi myoṅ ba, as cited in Suzuki's Index; he translates they do not give exact information regarding…); (2) in Mvy 9413 kula-prati°kaḥ = Tib. khyim so sor bsgo ba, speaking (or, giving orders) to families individually, and so Chin.; in default of context or known Pali parallel the exact mg. is not clear; app. °vedaka in caus. sense to °vedayati (2).
