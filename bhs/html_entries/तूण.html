• tūṇa, and tūṇaka (m. or nt.; cf. tuṇa, and AMg. tūṇaya), a musical instrument, perhaps a kind of drum (= tuṇa?): tūṇa- (Senart em. tūṇava-)-paṇava-vīṇā- (so read; Senart veṇu-) Mv iii.113.5; kācit (of the harem- women) tūṇakaṃ Mv ii.159.5; in same scene iii.407.18 kāci tūṇaṃ; in these passages little evidence as to the nature of the instrument; cf. kumbhatūṇa, apparently also a kind of drum.
