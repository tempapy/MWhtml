• advaya, nt., non-duality, as designation of the Buddhist doctrine. In Mvy 1717 advayam (Tib. gñis su med pa, non-duality) is listed among paramārtha-paryāyāḥ, synonyms for the true doctrine; advayasaṃjñā udapāsi Mv i.237.14, consciousness of non-duality arose in him (so that he resolved to become a Buddha).
