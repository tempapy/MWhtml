• Puṣkarasārin, (1) n. of a brahman, ruler of the droṇamukha Utkaṭa, q.v.: Divy 620.11 ff. As suggested in DPPN, prob. identical with Pali Pokkhara-sāti or °sādi, dwelling in Ukkaṭṭhā; but the Divy story about him seems not paralleled in Pali. See also s.v. Pūraśāyin; (2) n. of a king of Taxila, app. = Pali Pukkusāti: MSV ii.26.12 ff.; 31.15 ff. [349,1]
