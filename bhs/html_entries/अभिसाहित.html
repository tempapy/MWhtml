• abhisāhita, ppp., prob. to be read in Mv iii.217.16 = 218.2 = 222.5 for Senart's em. abhisāhṛta; doubtless = AMg. sāhiya (which Ratnach. derives from Skt. sv-āhita, rendering well got), acquired, collected; for abhisaṃhita, which is used in Skt. in substantially this sense; see § 3.3. The mss. read:…prabhūtā mānuṣyakā bhogā dhārmikā [Page060-a+ 71] dharmalabdhā dhārmikena balena abhisāhitā (218.2 abhyāhṛtā) abhisaṃmūḍhā (Senart em. abhisamūḍhā, q.v.). [059,2]
