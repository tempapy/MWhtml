• āryapuṅgala, m. (= Pali ariya-puggala), a model human personality: LV 423.13 parijñātam (so read with v.l. for text °nam) āryapuṅgalair (said of the dharma- cakra). Prob. refers to persons in the eight stages of (Hīnayāna) religious development, Dharmas 102; see aṣṭamaka, śaikṣa. [105,1]
