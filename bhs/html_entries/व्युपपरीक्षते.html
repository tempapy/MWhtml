• vyupaparīkṣate, and by haplology vyuparīkṣate, once °ti, fut. °kṣiṣyati (= upaparīkṣ°), investigates or reflects thoroughly, on religious truths: sa evaṃ (i.e. in words just quoted) °kṣamāṇas Śikṣ 122.1, he thus reflecting; °kṣamāṇo Dbh 31.17; vyuparīkṣate Dbh.g. 28(54).16 (last syllable lacking in mss.; vyupaparī° would be hypermetrical here and in the two other Dbh.g. occurrences); vyuparīk- ṣamāṇo 29(55).11; also introducing a question, evaṃ vyupaparīkṣate, katamena…Dbh 32.7; vyuparīkṣate, katama hetu…Dbh.g. 11(347).20; or a problem, vyupa- rīkṣiṣyati (so mss., Nobel em. vyuparīkṣeta), yenāyaṃ… sūtrendrarājas…pracaret Suv 113.4 (prose), he will ponder on how this… (the proved occurrence thrice in Dbh.g. of the haplological form justifies Nobel in keeping it here in prose, with his mss., but I see no advantage in his em. of fut. to opt.).
