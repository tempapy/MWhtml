• Śivi, n. of the well-known king (also spelled Śibi); besides versions in Av No. 34 and Jm No. 2, which resemble those of Skt. and Pali, a new version occurs in MSV ii.132.12 ff.; the king bleeds himself daily for six months to supply a prescription for a sick man. [528,2]
