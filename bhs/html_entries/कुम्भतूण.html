• kumbhatūṇa, m. or nt. (Pali °thūṇa; see the derīvs. following; sometimes spelled with n for ṇ, but never with th for t, which should always be kept, as Senart belatedly recognized, iii.472; cf. also tūṇa(ka), and tuṇa), some musical instrument, in Pali acc. to Dictt. a kind of drum (Skt. tūṇava said to be a flute): Mv ii.52.15 (the corrupt mss. clearly indicate °tūṇaṃ, acc. sg., as the true reading); followed by mṛdaṅga-.
