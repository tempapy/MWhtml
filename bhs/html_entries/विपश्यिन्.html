• vipaśyin (cf. prec. two; miswritten Vipaścin Divy 141.16; Mmk 397.11; = Pali vipassi(n), both mgs.), (1) adj., having insight (into, in comp.): sarvāvaraṇabuddha- samudra-°nā bodhisattvasamādhinā Gv 37.6; sarvāvara- ṇavigatena °nā kauśalyena 60.7; (2) n. of a past Buddha, as in Pali the sixth before Śākyamuni, and so often as ‘first of seven Buddhas’, see s.v. tathāgata; otherwise named as a former Buddha: Mv i.2.5; 294.19; iii.240.6; 241.16; 243.14; 244.4; 245.18 ff.; 247.10; 249.1; Gv 206.12; stories of incidents which occurred during his Buddhahood, Mv ii.271.7 ff.; Divy 141.16; 227.21 ff.; 282.19 ff.; Av i.137.7 ff.; 349.3 ff.; ii.70.11; 96.3; 109.3; Kv 14.12.
