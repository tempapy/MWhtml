• vattu-śiras (Mironov vandu°, v.l. in both calu°, perh. for Skt. caru, kettle, pot; preceded by ghaṭā-śiras, q.v., for which Mironov has vattu-ś° without v.l.), big- head(ed), acc to Tib. klad (glad) po che, also Chin. and Jap.: Mvy 8808. If we could accept the v.l. calu, and assume it = Skt. caru, the word would be comprehensible. [469,1]
