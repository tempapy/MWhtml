• ehipaśyika, adj. (= Pali ehipassika; based on ehi paśya, come see!; also aihi°, q.v.), that invites (every man) to come and see, ep. of the Buddha's dharma (and, in Mv, vinaya; in Pali, of dhamma): Mv iii.200.10 (svā- khyāto…tathāgatasya…) dharmavinayaḥ sāṃdṛṣṭika akāliko ehipaśyikaḥ aupanayikaḥ etc. (essentially same formula in Pali and in passages cited s.v. aihipa- śyika).
