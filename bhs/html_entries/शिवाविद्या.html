Pali DN i.9.7 aṅga-vijjā vatthu-v° khatta-(v.l. khetta-) v° siva-v°…sakuṇa-v°; comm. i.93.19 sivavijjā ti susāne parivasitvā santikaraṇavijjā; sigālarūtavijjā ti pi vadanti (Divy proves the latter right, with its śivā; śiva, m., also jackal in Skt., tho rarer).
