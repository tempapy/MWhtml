• mahāsaṃvidhāna, nt., the Great Arrangement or Preparation, made by a Bodhisattva in the Tuṣita heaven when he is about to descend to earth for his last existence: Mv i.198.12; ii.2.7. It consists in the departure of many devas from heaven to be reborn on earth so as to support the Bodhisattva in his mission. [426,2]
