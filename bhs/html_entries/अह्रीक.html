• (ahirī,) ahrīka, ahirīka, adj. (= Pali ahirīka; neg. to hrī plus -ka; in Skt. Lex. ahrīka only = a Buddhist!), shameless: ahirīkāś (mss. ahīrikāś) ca bhavanti Mv i.110.8 (prose); ahrīkeṇa Jm 98.19 (prose); ahirīko anotrapo Mv iii.11.4 (vs; here used of a very ugly man, without other evident cause for the epithet); in Ud xx.5 oldest ms. ahirī (Bhvr.), later ms. ahrīko (Sktizing).
