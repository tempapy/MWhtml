• mayūrāṅkī, a jewel, prob. turquoise: Mvy 5969 = Tib. gyu (turquoise) phal pa (common), or sbur len (which, or sbur loṅ, is defined by Jä. as amber, by Das as an unnamed gem; but in Mvy 5970 sbur len, or loṅ, occurs with other expressions for Skt. puṣparāga, topaz). [419,2]
