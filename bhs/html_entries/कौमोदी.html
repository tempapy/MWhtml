• kaumodī (= Skt. kaumudī), festival of the full-moon day: LV 72.19 (prose), °dyām iva cāturmāsyām; all mss. °mod°, perhaps by analogy of relatives of root mud (moda, etc.); or cf. Skt. kaumodakī (?). The phrase kaumudī cāturmāsī occurs twice in Mv, but no form of this word with medial o for u is otherwise recorded. [196,1]
