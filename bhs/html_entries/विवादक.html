• vivādaka, m. (to Skt. vivāda, °dayati; Pali id., one who quarrels), either quarrelsome person (as Pali), or (= Skt. °da plus -ka svārthe) quarrel: no bhuyo (m.c. for bhūyo) puravarasmi dehināṃ lobhadoṣakalahā vivādakā (several mss. °da-tā) LV 54.9 (vs),…no disputes due to greed or hatred, no quarrelers (or quarrels).
