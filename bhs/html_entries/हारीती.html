• Hārītī, n. of a deity (referred to as a yakṣiṇī, rākṣasī, or bhūta-mātar): SP 400.7 (Burnouf °ti; a rākṣasī); Māy 241.12 (rākṣasī); Suv 1.8; 85.4 (associated with yakṣas); 3.12 (bhūtamātā); 162.16 (id.); as yakṣiṇī Mmk 608.16; mahāyakṣiṇī Mmk 44.1; Sādh 103.9 etc.; in LV 202.10 yakṣas are called Hārītī-putra (so read, text Hāritī°, best ms. cited as Harītī°).
