• ekatya-śāśvatika, m. (= Pali ekacca-sassatika, PTSD s.v. sassatika), one who holds that some things are eternal: Bbh 67.(22-)24 śāśvatadṛṣṭikānām śāśvatadṛṣṭiṃ (qq.v.) nāśayati, tad yathā, pūrvāntakalpakānāṃ (see pūrvānta) śāśvatavādinām (q.v.) ekatya-śāśvatikānāṃ. Cf. LaV-P. AbhidhK. v.14 (partiellement éternalistes). [153,1]
