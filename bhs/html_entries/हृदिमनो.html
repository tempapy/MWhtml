• ? hṛdimano, obscure and prob. corrupt, in otherwise corrupt and metr. impossible line (first of an āryā): evam (mss.) ekaikasya vaśibhūtasya dhārenti hṛdimano (so 2 mss., 3 hṛmano, one hṛtmano) Mv i.116.9 (vs). The word could be n. pl. or gen. sg.; it is possible, but by no means evident, that it contains the stem hṛdi (prec.). Senart's note is not helpful. [621,1]
