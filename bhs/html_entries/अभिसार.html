• abhisāra, m. (nowhere recorded; corresponds to Pali abhihāra, also BHS, see s.v., which in Pali is cpd. with bhatta-, cf. bhaktābhisāra below; abhi-harati has a mg. appropriate to this in Skt., and abhi-sarati does not; only in Divy, MPS, and MSV; var. twice atisāra), gift, present, honorarium: Divy 6.18, 20 (here given to a bringer of good tidings); usually of food offered to the Buddha and or his monks, Divy 187.23, and almost always in the cpd. bhaktābhi° offering of food, MPS 6.8; Divy 43.22 (mss. °āti°); 65.2; 81.16; 85.16; 97.3 (ed. °saras); 177.26 (mss. °āti°); 183.21; 285.2--3; 286.26; of a physician's fee, MSV i.218.10 f.; ii.25.20.
