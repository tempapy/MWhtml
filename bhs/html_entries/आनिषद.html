• ? āniṣada (= Pali ānisada, nt.), sit-down, rump, buttocks: Mv ii.125.13 (prose), mss. evam evā (me) anuśī- dana (Senart em., ‘desperately’ as he says, parśukā) abhūnsuḥ. True approximate reading proved by Pali parallel, MN i.80.14 = 245.29, ānisadaṃ hoti.In Mv pl. verb seems to indicate that the noun was pl. (for dual); the stem may have been āniṣīdana, or the like.
