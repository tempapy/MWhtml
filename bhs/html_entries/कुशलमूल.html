• kuśala-mūla, nt., usually pl. (= Pali kus°), root(s) of merit; Pali has three, alobha, adosa, amoha; the same, with adveṣa = Pali adosa, in Mvy 1936--8; Dharmas 138; two other kinds named separately Mvy 1208--9, abhisa- mayāntikaṃ ku°, and kṣayajñānalābhikaṃ ku°; a different list of three in Dharmas 15, bodhicittotpāda, āśayaviśuddhi, ahaṃkāra-mamakāra-parityāga; Mvy 7417 avaropita- kuśalamūla, one who has planted (see avaropayati) roots of merit; very many other occurrences, e.g. LV 429.14; Mv (see kuśala-puṇya) i.134.3; 142.11; Divy 23.18; 65.10; 95.25; Av i.4.2, et passim; often referred to in praṇidhāna as basis for making the ‘earnest wish’.
