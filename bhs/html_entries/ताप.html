• tāpa- = tapas, ascetic practice (a mg. unrecorded for tāpa or its MIndic equivalents); in Mvy 1608, chapter- title, tāpa-saṃvāra-paryāyāḥ, words for ascetic practices and restraints; saṃvāra is also unparallelled in the sense of saṃvara; Tib. dkaḥ thub, which regularly = tapas, and sdom pa, which regularly = saṃvara. Perhaps read tapaḥ-saṃvara-; but Mironov (p. vi) cites both words as in Kyoto ed. [251,2]
