• Śatavarga Āgama, holy text of 100 chapters: Karmav 46.11; 70.7; 157.13. See Lévi's notes ad loc. and p. 11 f. It seems to be, or to include, the Saṃyukta Āgama, but the precise mg. is doubtful; Lévi thinks possibly it may mean the total collection of Āgamas, or at least Saṃy. with Madhy. [522,2]
