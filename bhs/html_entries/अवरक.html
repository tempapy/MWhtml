• avaraka, adj. (= Pali oraka; -ka prob. pejorative), wretchedly poor, inferior, mean: °keṇa kuśalamūlena SP 456.3; LV 89.22; AsP 79.12 (all prose); °kam, quasi- substantive (?) nt., Mvy 2701 = Tib. ṅan ṅon sordid, etc. (Das). In some cases the mss. of Divy and Av read avaraka for avavaraka, q.v., which Mironov reads for Mvy 2701. [073,2]
