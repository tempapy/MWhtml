• Ghoṣila (corresp. to Pali Ghosaka or Ghosita), n. of a gṛhapati (so Divy 541.19 ff., 575.30 ff.), one of the ministers of King Udayana: Divy 529.6; 531.19; 541.19 ff.; 575.30 ff. The same person is doubtless meant by the gṛhapati Ghoṣila mentioned Mv ii.2.13; Karmav 157.7. [220,2]
