• Bhadrajit, n. of a disciple of Buddha: Sukh 2.3; in form the name = Pali Bhaddaji, but actually it is evident that it is a substitute for Bhadrika (2), since it follows the names of the four other bhadravargīya; perh. it was altered under the influence of Aśvajit, the second of this list. [406,1]
