• āgacchati (cf. āgama, āgamayati), (1) is recorded, is handed down by tradition: LV 39.15 yathā brāhmaṇānāṃ mantravedaśāstrapāṭheṣv āgacchati, tādṛśenaiva rūpeṇa etc.; 105.8 yathā hy asmākaṃ…mantravedaśāstreṣv āgacchati, nārhati…kumāro 'gāram adhyāvasitum; (2) seeks, hunts for: Av ii.150.5 paścācchramaṇam (q.v.) āgacchati na ca pratilabhate, seeks…and does not find.
