• prāṇa, m. (= Pali pāṇa; cf. next; Skt. only prāṇin), (1) usually, living being, creature, in very inclusive sense: SP 27.15; LV 394.19 (bahuprāṇakoṭyaḥ, all mss.); Mv i.3.3 (prāṇa-koṭīṣu); 13.14 (pīḍayanti bahu prāṇāṃ); 128.8 (prāṇāṃ jīvitād vyaparopayanti); 145.8; 247.1; ii.6.2 (vs, prāṇeṣu, for prāṇiṣu, same vs, i.202.5); (2) rarely, animal (excluding human and superhuman beings), see prāṇaka 2: ye te sthalacarāḥ prāṇā…sarvāṇi prāṇaka-(NB!)- jātīni samānetha…ye kecij jalacarā prāṇāḥ…sarva- bhūtehi yajñaṃ yajiṣyāmi Mv ii.95.9-11; note at end of this story, te sarve prāṇaka-jātīyo…osṛṣṭā 99.17; (3) once even more specifically of insects, = prāṇaka 3: niḥprāṇenodakena Mmk 37.19 (prose).
