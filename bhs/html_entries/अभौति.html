• ? abhauti-, in Laṅk 17.5, text abhauti-bhautikānām; Suzuki things created and uncreated; the mss. are very confused; some simply abhautikānāṃ; perhaps read [Page060-b+ 71] abhautika-bhaut° (and perhaps render rather of things non-elemental and elemental). Surely no stem abhauti should be recognized. See bhautika (2); one possible reading for the above passage might be bhūta-bhauti- kānām, of gross elements and (subsidiary elements or) sense-objects.
