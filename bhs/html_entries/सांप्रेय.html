• sāṃpreya, adj. (= Pali sappāya; etym. of both obscure; see a-sāṃ°, and CPD s.v. asappāya), (suitable, fit, proper, see a-sāṃ°;) esp. of food (so also in Pali), wholesome: Av i.255.1 °yaṃ bhojanam; ii.110.2 °ya-bhoja- nena (Tib. phan pa, beneficial, wholesome); MSV ii.43.9 (Tib. ḥphrod pa); 45.17 (kriyā). Cf. kula-sāṃ°. [592,2]
