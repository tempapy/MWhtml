• sparśita, ppp. (of caus. of Skt. spṛś-; in this mg. = Pali phassita, phussita), lit. made to touch, i.e. closed, of door-bolts: kūṭāgāre śayitvā tvaṃ nivāte (text nirvāte) sparśitārgaḍe (so read for text °tāgate), āsīno vṛkṣamūleṣu kaccin na paritapyase Divy 559.12(--13), having slept in a secure tower with locked door-bolts, are you not tormented sitting at the roots of trees?; the em. is proved by Pali MN i.76.5--6 kūṭāgāraṃ…nivātaṃ phassitaggaḷaṃ (other parallel texts phussi°); and in Mv ii.115.12 read (kūṭāgārāṇi…) nivātāni sparśitārgaḍāni (see Crit. App.; mss. sparśārga°). [612,2]
