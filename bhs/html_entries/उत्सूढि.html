• utsūḍhi (f. ?), n. sg. °ḍhiḥ, (1) Mvy 1789 = Tib. spro ba, joy; so Mironov (v.l. both edd. utsṛcitaḥ); (2) Mvy 7683 = Tib. mthan pa (?not in Tib. Dictt.; perhaps = ḥthan pa, firmness, but Chin. obscurity, confusion, hence Mvy Kyoto ed. suggests em. unmūḍhiḥ). Probably = utsoḍhi, q.v.; both firmness and joy are meanings of Skt. utsāha, from the same root. [127,2]
