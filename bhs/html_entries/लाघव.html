• lāghava (nt.; cf. Skt. id.; Pali lahu, lahuka, seems to approach a mg. similar to this more closely than Skt. laghu etc., but I have found no precise parallel), buoyancy, not sinking, of a fishline, taut because a fish has caught the bait: tena sūtra-lāghavena jānāti, gṛhīto matsya iti Śikṣ 94.18.
