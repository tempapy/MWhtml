• Raurava, m. (= Pali Roruva), n. of a hot hell, very common: often followed by Mahāraurava, from which in Mv i.5.13. it seems to be specially distinguished as ekānta-raurava (mahā-r° occurs in 14); the two referred to as dvau ca rauravau Mv i.9.8 = iii.454.7 (vs); foll. by mahā-r°, Mvy 4923; Dharmas 121; Divy 67.22; 138.6; 366.28; Av i.4.8, etc.; without mahā-r°, Mv iii.274.11; Kv 18.12, et alibi.
