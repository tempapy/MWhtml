• suvyapadeśa-kṣema, adj., acc. to Senart something like auspicious thru good (physical) signs or characteristics, of the infant Bodhisattva: so vadati etha (in 2d and 3d occurrences, so avaca hanta) paśyatha °kṣemaṃ mama putraṃ Mv i.152.14 = 226.8 = ii.29.11 (vs, but meter imperfect, very possibly corrupt); spoken by Śuddhodana to gods pretending to be astrologers.
