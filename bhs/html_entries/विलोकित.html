• vilokita, nt., (1, as in Skt., look, gaze: bhagavāṃ… nāga-°tena vilokayanto Mv iii.281.4; nāga-°tenāvalokya Mvy 6371; see ālokita-vi°, avalokita, vyavalokita, and āryamahāsiṃhavilokita;) (2) fig. (in this mg. = Skt. and Pali vilokana), consideration, reflection: (bodhisattvas tuṣitavarabhavanasthitaś catvāri) mahā-°tāni vilokayati sma…kāla-°taṃ dvīpa-°taṃ deśa-°taṃ kula-°taṃ LV 19.7--9, and similarly Mv i.197.10--12; ii.1.1--3 (as to the time, continent, country, and family in which he is to be reborn for the last time).
