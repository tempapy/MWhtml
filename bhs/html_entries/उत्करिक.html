• Utkarika, n. of a merchant: Divy 227.26 tatrānya- taraś cotkariko nāma baṇig…Cf. Otkarika (the same person as Utk°). There seems to be no reason to connect this n. pr. with aukarika (okkarika), qq.v., as has been assumed by Feer, Speyer (on Av, Index, s.v. okkarika) and Cowell and Neil (Index to Divy). But perhaps we should read cautk° in 227.26 and assume Otk° as the name. [120,2]
