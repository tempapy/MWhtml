• kalandaka (or °ntaka? karanta? variants in mss. also karañjaka, kalaṇḍaka; = Pali kaḷandaka, Jāt. vi.224.6 mudukābhisiyā mudu-citta-kaḷandake, comm. sukhasaṃ- phassacittattharake), spread, cover for a seat: Mv i.306.9 (vs) kāśikapratyāstaraṇaṃ (? em.; mss. °tyottaraṇaṃ) suvicitra-kalandakaṃ (so 1 ms., the other °ṇḍakaṃ; Senart em. wrongly) maṇivicitraṃ; ii.38.3 (vs) read essentially with mss.: atha kanaka-m-aṣṭapādā suvicitra- kalandakā (or, with mss., °ntakā-ḥ?) maṇivicitrā (? mss. °tro or °tra); ii.470.8, for Senart's em. kilaṃjakā (pi kriyanti) mss. seem to intend karaṃjakā; context fits our word as to mg.; note, however, that in the next line (9) mss. have karantā (Senart em. karaṇḍā) pi kriyanti, which might be meant for kalanta(ka), or be another form of the word here concerned.
