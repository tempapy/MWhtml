• medhī, acc. to PTSD (s.v. medhi, citing no passage from Pali!) = Skt. methi, pillar, as part of a stūpa. But the context suggests rather one of several (here three) concentric galleries running around a stūpa (as at Boro- budur), or the story-structures supporting them: Divy 244.9 f. (stūpasya…catvāri sopānāny) ārabdhāni kā- rayitum, yāvad anupūrveṇa prathamā medhī tato 'nupūr- veṇa dvitīyā tatas tṛtīyā medhī yāvad anupūrveṇāṇḍam (see aṇḍa). This is confirmed by Tib. ḥkhor sa = medhī, Bailey, JRAS 1950.180; read medhyāṃ for yaṣṭyāṃ Divy 47.23. [439,1]
