• pratibhāgeti (denom. to Skt. pratibhāga; cf. prati- bhajati; pw regards pravibhāga as the ‘correct’ form of [Page366-b+ 71] the noun), shares, gives a share in: striyāye pratibhāgehi Mv ii.426.8, give me a share in (i.e. the use of) a woman. So by Senart's em., which seems probable; mss. °bhānehi or °bhāṇehi. [366,1]
