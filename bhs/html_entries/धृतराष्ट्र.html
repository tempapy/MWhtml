• Dhṛtarāṣṭra (in mg. 1 and 2 = Pali Dhataraṭṭha), (1) n. of one of the four ‘world-guardians’, see mahārā- ja(n); guardian of the east and lord of gandharvas; (2) (see s.v. dhṛtarājya) n. of a haṃsa-king (previous birth of the Bodhisattva): Gv 399.26; Jm 127.24; also n. of the haṃsa-king in the story which = the Pali Nacca Jātaka (32), MSV ii.92.17 ff.; (3) n. of a former Buddha, or (probably) of two such: Mv i.138.1; iii.235.1; (4) n. of one of Śuddhodana's palaces: LV 49.1; Mv ii.5.5 ff.
