• nirmāṇa-kāya, m., body of magic transformation: Mvy 118; Dbh.g. 6(342).22. In Mvy contrasts with dhar- ma-k° (3) and saṃbhoga-k°; see s.v. kāya, end. In Sūtrāl. ix.60, 63 nairmāṇikaḥ k°; Lévi métamorphique. See also Mus, Barabudur (II) 643 ff. (corps d'artifice). In Laṅk 241.7 nirmāṇa-kāyair may be an early occurrence of this; see Suzuki's transl., and Studies p. 145; cf. also Laṅk 314.2. Cf. nairmāṇikaṃ (q.v.) kāyaṃ Laṅk 73.9 (vs), but the same vs 276.10 reads nairvāṇikaṃ. [302,1]
