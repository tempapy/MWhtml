• Sunetra, (1) n. of a former Buddha (or several?): Mv i.137.12; iii.235.10; Mmk 64.1; 68.27; 130.3; 365.17; 499.23; (2) n. of a future Buddha: Mv ii.355.6 = iii.279.11; [Page599-b+ 71] Gv 441.25; (3) n. of a previous incarnation of Śākyamuni: RP 23.16; Laṅk 141.5; (4) n. of a Bodhisattva: Gv 2.26; (5) (= Pali Sunetta, 4 in DPPN) n. of an ancient teacher: Karmav 37.1, 3; perhaps the same is meant by the maharṣi S. of Māy 257.3; (6) n. of a son of Māra, favorable to the Bodhisattva: LV 310.12; (7) n. of a śreṣṭhin's son: Gv 51.23; (8) n. of a rākṣasa prince, guardian of the Bodhi- sattvasaṃgītiprāsāda (see saṃgīti 1) in Kapilavastu: Gv 432.25; (9) n. of a yakṣa leader: Māy 235.11.
