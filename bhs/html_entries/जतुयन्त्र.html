• jatu-yantra, loc. °tre, lit. lac-machine, n. of some art, in a list of those in which the Bodhisattva excelled; so read in LV 156.22, for Lefm. jala-yantre; mss. vary, A (the best) cited as janu- (read jatu-)y°; Calc. jatu°, con- firmed by Tib. rgya skyegs kyi ḥkhrul ḥkhor, machine of lac. The precise mg. is obscure to me; in corresponding lists of arts I have found nothing similar, unless jantunā Mv i.129.9 (in a corrupt and obscure line, included in a passage of similar tenor) conceals jatunā, instr. of jatu.
