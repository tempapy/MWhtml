• prapāṭikā (cf. Pali papaṭikā, splinter, shoot, sprout), (1) shoot, creeper: so acc. to Mironov, and pw 7 App. (Minayev), for Kyoto ed. Mvy 433 latikā (so Index, text misprinted laṭikā), Tib. khri śiṅ, creeper; (2) in ayas-pra° Mvy 7014 (Mironov °ṭīkā, v.l. prapaṭika) = Tib. lcags kyi (of iron) tsha tsha, prob. lit. shoot of iron, acc. to Jap. sparks cast off from red-hot iron; Chin. seems to support this.
