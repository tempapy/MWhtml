• Viraja (cf. also Virajas), (1) n. of two former Bud- dhas in the same list: Mv iii.231.11; 234.6 (prose); (2) n. of a former Buddha, of the kṛta age (see 364.6): Laṅk 364.14 (vs; he was of the Kātyāyana gotra); 365.6 (vs); (3) nt., °jaṃ, n. of the Buddhakṣetra of the future Buddha Padmaprabha: SP 65.8 (prose); in 68.2 (vs) it is called the lokadhātu Virajā (f.), or (v.l.) °jo (m.).
