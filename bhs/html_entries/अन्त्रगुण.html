• antra-guṇa, m., nt. (= Pali anta-guṇa, nt.; cf. guṇa 2), intestinal tract, mesentery: Mvy 4025 °guṇa, = Tib. gñe ma the twisted part of the colon or great gut (Jäschke); the coiled intestine (Zam. ap. Das); Mv i.8.8; Divy 375.14 °guṇān; Śikṣ 81.13 (read with mss. antrāṇy antraguṇaṃ; n. sg.); 209.9 antrāṇy antraguṇa(ḥ); Gv 328.19 °guṇa-, stem. See also āntraguṇa. In BHS seems to be masc. except in Śikṣ 81.13, and sometimes pl. [040,2]
