• [vinaddha is read for (aṣṭāpada-)nibaddha (or viniba- ddha) in SP 244.10 and 337.12 suvarṇasūtrāṣṭāpada- vinaddhā, °dhāṃ; in the latter no v.l., and WT the same (but no reliance can be placed on either ed.); in the former, Nep. mss. reported °pada-bhinaddhā, WT °pada-nibaddhā. The word vinaddha is nowhere recorded except in AV, where it means unbound. It should surely be emended to either nibaddha, q.v., or vinibaddha, q.v.] [489,1]
