Text: nityāntareṇa manasā kṛtamokṣa- buddhiḥ, mārgaṃ (? mss. āryaṃ) va (mss. ca) tāva mama putra cara prasīda, jīvāmi yāvac ca ahaṃ (so mss.)…, (Śuddhodana pleads with the Bodhisattva to wait for his own death before renouncing the world; text in part uncertain;) with constantly internal mind fixing your will on salvation (i.e. keeping it to yourself, not doing any- thing about it yet)…as long as I live… The word nitya, at any rate, seems to have its Skt. meaning.] [295,2]
