• khu, indecl. (also kho, hu, which are much less common; Pali only kho, except khv before vowels; AMg. khu and hu; all by the side of khalu = Skt. khalu), of course, obviously, as everyone knows; clearly, certainly, you may be sure; rarely in prose, Mv i.348.16; ii.165.15; some- times khu is written in vss when a long syllable is required, e.g. Mv ii.199.19; 200.4 (Senart em. kho); but in e.g. Mv i.69.15 (vs) khalu is written, Senart em. khu, m.c.; other cases of khu in vss, consistent with meter, are SP 113.8; 229.13; 295.2; LV 91.17; 342.4; 366.2; Sukh 22.4, 15; Mv i.11.3 (em.); 126.13; 142.15; 150.3 = 218.4; 204.18; ii.6.10; 141.7--10 (em.); 143.2; 194.14; 201.4; 316.6; iii.77.14 (v.l. kho); 82.12; 134.20; 186.4; 259.17; 386.12; 452.14, et alibi. Cf. khu-ssa. [205,2]
