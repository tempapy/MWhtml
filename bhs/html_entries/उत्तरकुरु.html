• Uttarakuru, if Senart's text is right, in Mv i.103.10 designates an inferior region or people in which successful Bodhisattvas are not reborn: °ruṣu nopapadyanti (along with pretas, asuras, and animals). But mss. all vary: utte ca kuruṣu, uttame ca kuleṣu, (one only) uttara ca kuruṣu. Doubtful. As n. of one of the dvīpas, see dvīpa. [123,1]
