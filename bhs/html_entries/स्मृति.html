• smṛti, f. (= Pali sati; hardly distinguishable from some aspects of Skt. id.), mindfulness, (full) consciousness or awareness, esp. in samyak-s° = Pali sammā-sati, the 7th stage of the 8-fold Noble Path (mārga), in lists of its stages, such as Mvy 1003; Tib. dran pa, which seems to parallel the various aspects of Skt. and BHS smṛti. Cf. prec. and smṛtimant. [614,1]
