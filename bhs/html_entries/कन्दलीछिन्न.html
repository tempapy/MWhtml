• kandalī-chinna, adj. (m.), ? Mvy 8797; °nna-ka, id., MSV iv.68.14; Chin. one who is in debt; Tib. gtaḥ (= pledge, pawn) gam(s) pa (? perhaps to ḥgam pa, one mg. of which acc. to Jä. is to threaten, menace). A kind of person who should not be initiated into the order; seems possibly to correspond to Pali kaṇḍara-chinna, Vin. i.91.10, interpreted as one the tendons of whose feet are cut. [167,2]
