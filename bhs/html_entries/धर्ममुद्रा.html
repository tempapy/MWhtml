• dharma-mudrā, ‘seal’ of the doctrine: iya…°drā SP 92.13 (vs); also dharma-svabhāva-mudrā, ‘seal’ of the true nature of the doctrine: bhāṣiṣyate °drām SP 28.8 (vs); deśem’ imāṃ °drām 47.8 (vs). Tib. renders literally, phyag rgya, and so Burnouf, le sceau; Kern, closing word of my law, fixed nature of the law, (unmistakable) stamp of the nature of the law; probably the last rendering comes close to what is meant.
