• turiya (nt. or m.; = Pali id., MIndic for Skt. tūrya), musical instrument; common in vss of LV, e.g. 54.6 (v.l. tūry°); 161.17 (v.l. tūry°); 164.20; 169.22; sometimes turiya and tūrya in the same sentence, LV 175.15 and 16 (no v.l.). But in Mv iii.122.16 (vs) turiya is Senart's em., mss. tūrya (which is metrically inferior). [255,2]
