• udāra, adj. (= audāra, °rika; neither Skt. udāra nor Pali udāra, uḷāra, seems so used), coarse: Samādh 22.26 (vs) yasya codāra saṃjñādi nāmarūpasmi vartate, visabhāgāya saṃjñāya udāraṃ citta jāyate, if in the nāma-rūpa of someone there works a coarse saṃjñā etc., then, since this saṃjñā is not homogeneous [with the pure mind], the mind becomes coarse too (Régamey).
