• upaccheda, m. (= Pali id.; see upacchinatti, an- upaccheda, etc.), cutting off, severance, destroying: dhar- mopa° the cutting off of the states of existence, of conditioned existence, in a formulaic list, followed usually by tṛṣṇākṣayo virāgo nirodho nirvāṇam: Mv ii.285.20; iii.200.11; 314.5; LV 392.16 (separate from prec., for which mss. samartho); in LV 395.22 text sarvatamopacchedaḥ, no v.l., read perh. (sarva-?)dharmopa°; āhāropa° Mv iii.65.18; -dāri- dryopa° Śikṣ 190.19; yamalokopa° Śikṣ 215.2; vṛttyupa° Karmav 41.8; vaiśāradyopa° Karmav 41.26. [135,1]
