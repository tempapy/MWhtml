• saṃkhyāta, in steye-saṃkhyātam, prob. read so, as adv. (= Pali theyya-saṃkhātaṃ = theyyacitto avahara- ṇacitto Vin. iii.46.34), with intent to steal: (yā punar bhikṣuṇī…) pareṣām adattaṃ steye saṃkhyātaṃ (printed as two words) ādadīta Bhīk 25a.2--3; steye °taḥ (as two words, so also Mironov) Mvy 5352 = Tib. rku bar bgraṅ ba, rendered by Das steyasaṃkhyātam, to count as stealing.
