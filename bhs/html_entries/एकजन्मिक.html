• eka-janmika (cf. -janmika), adj., in, subject to, a single birth: Divy 422.7 mā tāvat tavaikajanmikasya maraṇabhayāt…harṣo notpannaḥ, can it be that you, from fear of death, in a single birth…have not conceived joy…; similarly 422.18 (vs) mā tāvad ekajanmikasya… [152,2]
