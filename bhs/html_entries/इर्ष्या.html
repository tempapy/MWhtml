• irṣyā = Skt. īrṣyā, jealousy: all mss. at LV 52.13; 372.17. Weller 20 would em. to īrṣyā; but this may be only Sktization of semi-MIndic ir°, cf. iryā- etc. As Weller notes, irṣyā is found as v.l. in some mss. of Mv (i.37.6; 44.13, four of six mss.; iii.27.17; 164.19); tho in all these cases at least one ms. has īr°, the form ir° may have been original.
