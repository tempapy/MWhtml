• anuvarga (m. ? = Pali °vagga, see below), some part or accessory of a city gate: dvinnāṃ varṇānāṃ anuvargā abhunsuḥ (sc. teṣāṃ dvārāṇāṃ) Mv i.195.(11--)12. Cf. Pali Bv i.14 tulā saṃghāṭānuvaggā sovaṇṇaphalakatthatā. CPD interprets anuvaggā as adj., citing comm., = anurūpā. But this is proved wrong by Mv; like tulā, which occurs in the prec. line of Mv, it must be a part of a structure. See Senart's note for a conjectural attempt at interpreta- tion. [033,1]
