• kārāhva (Skt. kārā-āhvā), what is called a prison: Mv iii.105.9 (vs), read with mss. bandhasya kathaṃ mukto vacanena kārāhvam abhikrameya, how, having been freed from a bond by a word, would one enter into what is called a prison (viz. sensual life)? Meter is to be sure bad, but the sense is perfect; Senart em. wrongly. [179,1]
