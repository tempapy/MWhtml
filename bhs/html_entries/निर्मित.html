• nirmita (= Pali nimmita), (1) ppp. of nirminoti, q.v.; (2) nt., a magic creation: bhagavān °taṃ visarjayati Divy 138.13; Av i.4.12; nirmitopamaṃ māyopamaṃ SP 137.10, like a magic creation, an illusory thing (mirage); (3) as n. of a class of gods, = nirmāṇarati; so very clearly in Mv ii.349.13 (vs) °tā (devāḥ), the verse equivalent of nirmāṇaratī 348.17 (prose); elsewhere, SP 235.1--2 (prose, see s.v. samāvartayati); 237.2, 6; LV 45.11; 50.5 (read nirmitāś for nim°); 213.15; 215.13; 219.8; sg., one of the class, 241.2; (4) n. of a former Buddha: Mv iii.237.11; (5) n. of a Bodhisattva: Gv 442.3.
