• ? avadarpita, in LV 275.8 sv-avadarpita-vimala- buddhir; textually uncertain; mss. vary greatly on ava°; many omit vimala; Tib. śin tu (= su) dkar baḥi (white, often = avadāta) blo (= buddhi) daṅ ldan pa (having), apparently omitting vimala, and suggesting that it is based on avadāta, white, i.e., bright, instead of avadarpita, which is nowhere recorded and seems senseless, as do the alternative readings of the other BHS mss.
