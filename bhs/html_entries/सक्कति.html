• -sakkati (= Pali id., Pkt. -sakkai, both apparently only in cpds.), in cpds. ava-(o-), pari-, perhaps anu- (see s.v. anuśakya), moves, goes. Derivation obscure; Pischel 302 from ṣvaṣk-; Andersen, Pali Reader s.v. osakkati, from [Page544-b+ 71] sṛp (curiously, the mss. of Mv read avasappanti in i.23.10, see avasakkati). [544,1]
