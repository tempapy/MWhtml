• ṛjuka, adj. (also ujjuka; = Pali ujuka, ujjuka; Skt. ṛju plus ka), straight, right: SP 324.2 (but see s.v. ujjuka); KP 8.1 (prose) ṛjukasya bodhisattvasya ṛjukalakṣaṇāni; Śikṣ 285.9 (prose) (āśayo…) ṛjukaḥ akutilatvāt; MSV i.47.4; adv. °kena, straight out, straightforwardly, honestly (before a daśavarga of monks), Śikṣ 169.1 (prose) āpattir daśavarge ṛjukena deśayitavyā; adv. ṛjukam, id., Bbh 6.17 (prose) arthikeṣu ca sattveṣu ṛjukaṃ pratipadyate, na māyāśāṭhyenainān vilobhayati; abstr. °ka-tā, Gv 186.8 (prose) mārgakuṭilatāṃ mārgarjukatām anuvilokya (here literally, straightness).
