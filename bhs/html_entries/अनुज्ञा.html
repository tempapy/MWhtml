• -anujñā, in lokānujñā Mv i.78.17, one of the 8 samudācāra (q.v., 1) of Bodhisattvas in the 1st bhūmi. Senart p. xxvii l'adieu au monde. But if I am right in relating this list to the 10 bhūmipariśodhakā dharmāḥ of Dbh p. 19 (sec. UU), see esp. lines 20-23, it must correspond to lokajñatā, see Dbh 19.12--15; knowledge of (the varying traits of) people. The Mv may even contain a corruption of lokajñatā; equally bad corruptions occur often in its mss. [026,2]
