• pratimānayati (in this sense not in Skt.; whether so used in Pali I am not sure; I have been unable to find the majority of references for paṭimāneti in PTSD), provides, presents with (instr., usually food): āhāreṇa pratimānetvā Mv i.302.5; °netha iii.304.2; °nito, ppp., iii.414.11; (scilicet) a woman, Mv iii.2.15 (vs), mss. corrupt, perh. read: stryāgārāthiko iha gacche me prati- mānaya, I came here wanting a woman; provide me (with one); daivatāni bhūtāni vṛṣṭyā °yanti Jm 69.3 (vs), the deities present creatures with rain.
