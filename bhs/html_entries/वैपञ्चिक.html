• vaipañcika, m. (see under prec.), soothsayer (prec. by naimittika): Mvy 3794 = Tib. mtshan mkhan; LV 186.15 (prose; best ms. vaipañcanika, v.l. with Calc. °camika); 228.9 (prose); Divy 474.26 °kā(ḥ) by em., but corrupt mss. look more like °canikā(ḥ). Kern, IF. 31.196, derives this group from a ‘misunderstood Pkt. *vepañ- janika’ to Pali veyyañjaniya, Jāt. iv.233.24, from vyañ- jana; improbably, in my opinion. [510,2]
