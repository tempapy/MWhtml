• (avamardita-cittam, SP 108.5, rendered by Kern mentally developed; similarly Burnouf. But Skt. avamardati and Pali omaddati mean only distress or the like. The form means having his spirit depressed, crushed; note that it follows viditvā; it is not parallel with the words preceding viditvā, but is modified by the following udārasaṃjñayā; the phrase is explained by what follows.)
