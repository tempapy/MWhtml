• Śāriputra (= Pali Sāriputta; also Śāli°, Śāradva- tī-p°, Śārisuta), n. of one of Buddha's two chief disciples, the other being (Mahā-)Maudgalyāyana; orig. named Upatiṣya, q.v. for story of his conversion; once also called Tiṣya, q.v. Śāli°;) SP 2.2; 29.2 ff.; 60.1 ff.; 264.17 ff.; LV 1.12; 443.13; Divy 153.5; 182.21; 268.6; 314.15 ff.; 330.6 ff.; 394.21 ff.; 486.25 ff.; 542.24 ff.; Av ii.69.7 etc.; Sukh 2.6; 92.5; Karmav 161.17.
