• vilekhya, m., Mvy 5235 °yaḥ, defined in Tib. and Chin. as regret or impressing on the mind, paying careful heed; same definition for ālekhya (or °kha), vipratisāra, and kaukṛtya, 5234, 5236, 5237. Obviously related to vilekha, which is recorded as v.l. in both edd. of Mvy and should perhaps be read for this. [498,2]
