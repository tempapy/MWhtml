• parigraha, nt. (Skt. only masc.), property: SP 85.4 (vs), here predicate noun to subj. gṛham, neut.; perhaps by assimilation of gender; (may be m. or nt.,) (saddharma- puṇḍarīkaṃ nāma) dharmaparyāyaṃ…sarvabuddha- parigrahaṃ…saṃprakāśayām āsa SP 181.6, perhaps which is the property of all the Buddhas (one Chin. transl. protected, guarded, or preserved by the Buddhas); vaipulya- sūtrāṇa parigrahe SP 98.3 (vs), in the acquisition of the… (substantially so used in Skt.).
