• abhisaṃbhāvayati (= Skt. and Pali °bhavati; caus. recorded only once in Bhāg. P. in mg. salute, and not in Pali; cf. abhisaṃbhuṇati), attains, brings to realization: only in formula, bhagavān samyaksaṃbuddho yadarthaṃ samudāgato tadarthaṃ (or tam ar°) abhisaṃbhāvayitvā, common in Mv, e.g. i.4.12; 34.1; ii.115.6; iii.90.18; 382.8.
