• Bhadrapāla, (1) n. of the first of the 16 ‘virtuous men’ (satpuruṣa): SP 3.10 (with list of 16 names); RP 2.2 (characterized as such, but without names of the others); (2) n. of a Bodhisattva: SP 383.1; Mvy 692; (and prob.) Mmk 311.16 (more likely than the ‘satpuruṣa’). A Bhadrapāla Sūtra exists in Chin., and a BHS fragment of it is recorded by Thomas ap. Hoernle MR 88 ff. [406,1]
