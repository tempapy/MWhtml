• Duṣprasaha, n. of a Buddha in a remote Buddha- kṣetra and different universe, but contemporary with Śākyamuni; he stopped preaching when the latter ‘turned the wheel of the law’: Mv iii.342.3 ff.; the same personage may be meant (tho this incident is lacking) by Buddhas of seemingly exceptional renown so named at Sukh 70.1, and (spelled Duḥprasaha) Mmk 64.1; 130.3.
