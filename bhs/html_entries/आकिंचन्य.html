• ākiṃcanya, m. sg., one who is in the stage called ākiṃcanyāyatana: Buddhacarita xii.63 (wrongly queried by Johnston); pl., the gods who are in that stage, Mmk 19.10. In Bbh 49.17--18 āyatana, at the end of the cpd. ākāśavijñānākiṃcanya-naivasaṃjñānāsaṃjñāyatanam, is to be taken with each of the four members, including this. [087,1]
