• ātmapūrvāpara, adj., perhaps having continuity with itself: Mv ii.1.13--14 °raṃ ca taṃ kulaṃ bhavati, of the family in which the Bodhisattva is born. Corresponds to labdha-pūrvāpara Mv i.198.3; not in corresponding passage LV p. 24. Senart keeps the form with mss., but in his note suggests that it is false Sktization of MIndic atta- = ātta- (synonym of labdha-). [092,2]
