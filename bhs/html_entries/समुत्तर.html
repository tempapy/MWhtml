• *samuttara, a crossing over (cf. next), in an-a-samut- taro Mv i.14.10 (vs), having no crossing over, that cannot be crossed over, see under an-a-. Senart assumes that °tara is for °tāra (next), presumably m.c.; meter does indeed require a short penult. But neither °tara nor °tāra seems to be recorded except here and in the Divy passages, which are substantially repetitions of a single passage. [571,2]
