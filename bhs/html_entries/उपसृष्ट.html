• ? upasṛṣṭa, in sopasṛṣṭāmbaravasanā MSV ii.23.7, would seem to mean she (a wife whose husband had gone away), abandoning the wearing of (fine) garments (kleśair bādhitum ārabdhā). But Tib. seems to have been different; it is cited as de yaṅ (should = sāpi) kha zas zhim po daṅ (= with well-tasting food). [143,2]
