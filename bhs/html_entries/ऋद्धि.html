• ṛddhi, f. (= Skt. id., Pali iddhi), supernatural or magic power, hardly significantly different from its Skt. use; ṛddhi-vaśitā Mvy 779, one of the 10 vaśitā of a Bodhisattva; knowledge of ṛ° is the fifth abhijñā, q.v.; ṛddhi-prātihārya, see prāti°; ṛddhi-balatā and -vaśitā, Mv iii.67.2; aiśvarya-ṛ° Mv ii.166.8 simply the magic power of aiśvarya, as in Skt. (otherwise Senart); see ṛddhi-pāda separately. [151,2]
