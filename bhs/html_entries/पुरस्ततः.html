• purastataḥ, adv. (from Skt. purastāt, cf. prec., or Pali puratthaṃ, plus.-tah: = Pali puratthato, misdefined in PTSD), to the east, on the eastern side: Pāṇḍavasmiṃ purastataḥ Mv ii.198.17 (vs) = Pali Sn 416 Paṇḍavassa puratthato. Render Mv on (Mt.) Pāṇḍava, on its eastern part. Confirmed by Jāt. i.66.14 puratthābhimukho. [347,2]
