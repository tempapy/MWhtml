• anupalabdhi, f. or adj. (Bhvr.), = an-upalambha, q.v.: (the being) not to be grasped or conceived (mentally): °dhi-hetuḥ Mvy 4461 (as term in logic); atyantānupalabdhi- cakraṃ (sc. dharma-c°) LV 423.4, completely ungraspable; gambhīraṃ…dharmacakraṃ grāhānupalabdhi-tvāt LV 422.11 profound…because it cannot be grasped by (mental) grasping; °dhi-tvāt (also) KP 148.3; Laṅk 162.13.
