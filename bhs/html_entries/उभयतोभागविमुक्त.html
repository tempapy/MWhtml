• ubhayato-bhāga-vimukta, adj. (= Pali ubhato- bhāga-vimutta), emancipated from both parts: Mvy 1028 (misprinted °bhaga°; corr. Index); Divy 404.24 (read: ubhau hi tau ubhayatobhāgavimuktau); Bhīk 27b.1; cf. AbhidhK. LaV-P. ii.205; vi.275--7. Often edd. (Pali and BHS) print ubha(ya)to as separate word, wrongly; it modifies bhāga- directly, as part of the cpd. Tib. on Mvy gñis kaḥi cha las…from both parts; Pali MN comm. iii.188.7 = DN comm. ii.514.3 dvīhi bhāgehi vimutto, arūpasamāpattiyā rūpakāyato vimutto, maggena nāma- kāyato (i.e. from the skhandha other than rūpa) vimutto. The AbhidhK. vi.276 has a different interpretation acc. to LaV-P., delivered from the obstacle of the passions (impu- rities, kleśāvaraṇa), and from the obstacle to (the 8) vimokṣa (which is stated to be akarmaṇyatā, l'impuissance corporelle [Page148-a+ 71] et mentale). This seems to be a later reinterpretation; the Pali comms. make it simpler, referring to the physical and mental constituents. Which was meant in our texts is not clear from the contexts. [147,2]
