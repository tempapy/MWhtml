• Veruḍiṅga, m. or nt., v.l. °ḍidga; in a majority of occurrences (Mv i.326.10 and 327.6--8) both mss. Vebha°, which has support in Pali Vebhaliṅga (v.l.) or Vehaliṅga (see this in DPPN; Veka° is also recorded); Senart, with Mv i.319.8, 9 only, Veru°; n. sg. °go i.319.8, °gaṃ 326.10: n. of the village, on the site of the later Mārakaraṇḍa, which was the home of Ghaṭikāra. The orig. form seems to have been Vebhaḍiṅga. For other forms, see Vai- bhiḍiṅgī. [509,1]
