• rajasvara, adj., prob. intended as equivalent of Skt. rajasvala, connected with rajas, and interpreted as pas- sionate, subject to passion: kathaṃ bhoti rajasvaro Mv iii.384.3 (vs), and rājye bhoti rajasvaro id. 6. The vss = Pali Dhp. comm. iii.231.21 and 233.3, which read rajissaro (Bhvr., as if raja-īśvaraḥ, having passion as his master; our word may be a mangled form of this). [449,2]
