• vallarī, once °ri, (1) (= AMg. id.) a musical instru- ment, acc. to Tib. three-stringed lute (vīṇā): Mvy 5019 = Tib. pi-waṅ (vīṇā) rgyud gsum pa (three-stringed); veṇu- vallari-sughoṣakā Divy 221.24 (only case written °ri); always in cpd. lists of instruments, Divy 315.12; 317.23; 320.6; 459.4; (2) a stalk or panicle of rice-kernels: śāli-°ryo MSV ii.61.12, Tib. ḥbras kyi (of rice) sñe ma (regularly ear of corn); = śīrṣan, q.v. (Cf. Childers and PTSD s.v. vallarī, citing a Pali Lex. with mg. a compound pedicle.) [473,1]
