• saṃrañjanīya, adj., = prec.: °yaḥ Mvy 2941 (so Index, and Mironov, text misprinted saṃraj°); (tathā- gato…) °yāṃ kathāṃ pravartayati LV 416.14; with dharma, as Pali sārāṇīya (see sārāyaṇīya) with dhamma, °yaṃ dharmaṃ samādāya vartataḥ (3 dual) Divy 404.15 (the sequel shows that it consists of mutual attentions); °ya-dharmeṣv anuvartanatā Śikṣ 183.17, conforming to sociable (companionable, pleasing, friendly) principles (of conduct).
