• āghāta, m. (= Pali id.), malicious feeling, anger, hatred: Mvy 2104 (Tib. kun nas mnar sems pa, inaccurately, very tormented spirit); Mv i.79.15 āghāta-bahulāś ca bhavanti (a cause of backsliding of Bodhisattvas); Av ii.129.3 yo 'bhūt sattveṣv āghātaḥ sa prativigataḥ (anger had characterized the person referred to); Bbh 161.12 āghāta-cittaḥ pratigha-citto vā; Karmav 27.17 mā te bhaviṣyati āghātaś cākṣāntiś ca.
