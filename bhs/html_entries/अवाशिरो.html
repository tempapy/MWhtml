• avāśiro, adj. n. sg. m. (or could be adv., acc. sg. nt.; = Pali avaṃsira(s), avasira(s), Skt. avākśiras), head downwards, headlong: Mv iii.454.6 = 457.4 atha (457.4 mā; so Senart; mss. apparently lack any such word, except that one reads nā in 457.4) ghorarūpaṃ narakaṃ prapatiṣye avāśiro (so clearly the mss. read or intend; vv.ll. avāraśiro, avaśiro, unmetr.; Senart em. avākśiro).
