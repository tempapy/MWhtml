• ratnapadmavikrāmin, (1) adj., walking on jewel- lotuses, i.e., with such lotuses appearing under their every step (so Chin. versions of SP; see Burnouf's note, Lotus, p. 364): SP 66.6 (prose), of Bodhisattvas in a future buddhakṣetra; (2) n. of a Buddha: Śikṣ 169.15. [451,2]
