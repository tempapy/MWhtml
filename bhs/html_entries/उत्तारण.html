• uttāraṇa (nt.; in this sense not Skt. or Pali; see uttarayati), carrying out (of a promise): Sikṣ 126.7 prati- jñottāraṇavacanā vā; a neg. is needed; either delete preceding punctuation and understand a-prati°, in samdhi with preceding vā; or read with ms. of Bodhicaryav. pañjikā on 5.54 (ed. 124.3, with note) pratijñānuttāraṇa- vacanā; in either case, not carrying out one's promise. La Vallée Poussin erroneously corrects to the reading of Bendall's text of Śikṣ. [124,2]
