• Dhānyadrāhyāyaṇa (so read for ed. °drāyāyaṇa; see the readings of the corrupt mss., some of which sup- port this, the obviously correct reading), pl., n. of a brah- manical gotra: Divy 635.19. This passage is omitted in two Chin. translations, my colleague Prof. Rahder informs me. Cf. Dhyāna-drāhyāyaṇī, where Chin. seems more favorable to initial Dhā- rather than Dhyā-. It seems that one or the other of these two must be an error, and per- haps Dhānya- should be read in both. [284,2]
