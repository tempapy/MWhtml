• chavi (= Skt., Pali id., skin), bark (of a tree): kovi- dārasya chavigandhaḥ Gv 501.11 (prose). Acc. to the English of Ratnach., chavi may have this mg. in AMg.; this is a translation of Hindi chāl, which seems to mean both skin and bark; whether the AMg. word also means bark I do not know.
