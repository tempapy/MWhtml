• cetika, nt. (prob. hyper-Skt. for MIndic cetiya, but cf. caityaka) = ceti, cetiya, caitya; only in vss: LV 389.10 cetikaṃ (v.l. ceṭikāṃ), parallel to 388.12 cetiyaṃ (v.l. ceṭikā, °ko); dattvā patākāṃ bhagavata cetikeṣū Śikṣ 302.3, parallel to Mv ii.375.9 cetiyeṣu (mss. corruptly jātīyeṣu); so in Śikṣ 303.4, 7; 304.4, 8, etc., cetika regularly where Mv parallels (ii.379.7 etc.) have cetiya.
