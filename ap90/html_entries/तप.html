• tapa a. [tap-ac]
      1 Burning, warming, consuming by heat.
      2 Causing pain or trouble, distressing.
    • --paḥ
      1 Heat, fire, warmth.
      2 The sun.
      3 The hot season; Śi. 1. 66.
      4 Penance, religious austerities.
    Comp.
    • --atyayaḥ, --aṃtaḥ the end of the hot season and the beginning of the rainy season; ravipītajalā tapātyaye punaroghena hi yujyate nadī Ku. 4. 44; 5. 23; Ś. 3. 12. --ātmaka a. practising austerities. [0530-a]
