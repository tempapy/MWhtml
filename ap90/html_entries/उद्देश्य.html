uddeśya pot. p.
      1 To be illustrated or explained.
      2 To be intended or aimed at.
      3 That to which one refers or which one has in view.
      --śyaṃ
      1 The object in view, an incentive.
      2 The subject of an assertion (opp. vidheya); see the word anuvādya also. [0307-c]
