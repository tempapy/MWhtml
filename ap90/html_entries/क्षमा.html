• kṣamā [kṣam-aṅ]
      1 Patience, forbearance, forgiveness; kṣamā śatrau ca mitre ca yatīnāmeva bhūṣaṇaṃ H. 2; R. 1. 22; 18. 9; tejaḥ kṣamā vā naikāṃtaṃ kālajñasya mahīpateḥ Śi. 2. 83.
      2 The earth.
      3 An epithet of Durgā.
    Comp.
    • --jaḥ the planet Mars.
    • --bhuj, bhujaḥ a king. [0435-b]
