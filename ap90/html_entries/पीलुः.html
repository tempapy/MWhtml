• pīluḥ [pīl-u] 1 An arrow. --2 An atom. --3 An insect. --4 An elephant. --5 The stem of the palm. --6 A flower. --7 A group of palm trees. --8 A kind of tree. --9 A heap of bones. --10 The central part of the hand. --lu n. The fruit of the Pīlu tree --Comp. --patraḥ the Mūrvā plant. --parṇī {1} a kind of drug. {2} N. of two plants. --pākavādin m. a Vaiśeshika (one who maintains the doctrine that heat acts only on the atoms of matter, as of a jar, and not on the whole body).
