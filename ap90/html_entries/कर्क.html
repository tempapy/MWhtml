• karka a. [kṛ-ka Uṇ. 3. 40]
      1 White.
      2 Good, excellent.
    • --rkaḥ
      1 A crab.
      2 Cancer, the fourth sign of the zodiac.
      3 Fire.
      4 A water-jar.
      5 A mirror.
      6 A white horse. [cf. Pers. kark; L. cancer; Gr. korkinos].
    Comp.
    • --cirbhaṭī small cucumber. [0381-b]
