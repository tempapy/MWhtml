• jatu n. [jaṇ-ḍa toṃ'tādeśaḥ Uṇ. 1. 18]
      1 Lac; Pt. 1. 107.
      2 A kind of red dye.
    • --tuḥ, --tūḥ f. A bat.
    Comp.
    • --aśmakaṃ red arsenic.
    • --gṛhaṃ a house made of lac (such as was built by Duryodhana in order to burn up the Pāṇḍavas).
    • --putrakaḥ a man at chess.
    • --maṇiḥ a mole, a natural mark on the body.
    • --rasaḥ lac. [0505-a]
