pāraṃparyaṃ [paraṃparaiva svārthe ṣyañ]
      1 Hereditary succession, continuous order.
      2 Traditional instruction, tradition.
      3 Intermediation.
    Comp.
      --upadeśaḥ traditional instruction, tradition, regarded by the Paurāṇikas as a pramāṇa or proof. [0696-a]
