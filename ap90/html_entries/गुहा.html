• guhā
      1 A cave, cavern, hidingplace; guhānibaddhapratiśabdadīrghaṃ R. 2. 28, 51; dharmasya tattvaṃ nihitaṃ guhāyāṃ Mb.
      2 Hiding, concealing.
      3 A pit, hole in the ground.
      4 The heart.
      5 Intellect.
    Comp.
    • --āhita a. placed in the heart.
    • --caraṃ Brahman.
    • --mukha a. ‘cave-mouthed,’ widemouthed, open-mouthed.
    • --śayaḥ {1} a mouse. {2} a tiger or lion. {3} the Supreme soul. [0464-b]
