• hṛdya a. [hṛdi spṛśyate manojñatvāt hṛd-yat]
      1 Hearty, cordial, sincere.
      2 Dear to the heart, cherished, dear, desired, beloved; Bv. 1. 69.
      3 Agreeable, pleasant; charming; Māl. 1. 4, 8. 4, R. 11. 68.
      4 Affectionate, kind.
    Comp.
    • --gaṃdhaḥ the Bilva tree.
    • --gaṃdhā the great-flowered jasmine.
    • --gaṃdhaṃ {1} small cumin. {2} sochal salt. [1174-c]
