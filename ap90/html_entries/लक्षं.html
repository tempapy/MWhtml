• lakṣaṃ [laks-ac] 1 One hundred thousand (m. also in this sense); icchati śatī sahasraṃ sahasrī lakṣamīhate Subhāṣ.; trayo lakṣāstu vijñeyāḥ Y. 3. 102. --2 A mark, butt, aim, target; pratyakṣavadākāśe lakṣaṃ baddhvā Mu. 1. --3 A sign, token, mark. --4 Show, pretence, fraud, disguise; lakṣasuptaḥ sthitosmi Dk. ‘feigning sleep’. --Comp. --adhīśaḥ a person possessing a lac or lacs.
