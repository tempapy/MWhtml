• gauṇa a. (ṇī f.) [guṇamadhikṛtya pravṛttā gauṇī, tata āgataḥ aṇ] 1 Subordinate, secondary, unessential. --2 (In gram.) Indirect or less immediate (opp. mukhya or pradhāna); gauṇe karmaṇi duhyādeḥ pradhāne nīhṛkṛṣvahāṃ Sk. --3 Figurative, metaphorical, used in a secondary sense (as a word or sense). --4 Founded on some resemblance between the primary and secondary sense of a word; as in gauṇīlakṣaṇā. --5 Relating to enumeration or multiplication. --6 Attributive. --Comp. --pakṣaḥ the minor or weaker side of an argument &c.
