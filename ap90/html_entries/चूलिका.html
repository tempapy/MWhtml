• cūlikā [cul samuccaṃye ṇvul pṛṣo° dīrghaḥ]
      1 The crest or comb of a cock.
      2 The root of an elephant's ear.
      3 (In dramas) The hinting or indication of the occurrence of any event by characters behind the stage; aṃtarjavanikāsaṃsthaiḥ sūcanārthasya cūlikā S. D. 310; e. g. in the beginning of the 4th act of Mv. [0498-a]
