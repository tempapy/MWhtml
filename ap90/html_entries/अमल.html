• amala a. [na. ba.]
      1 Free from dirt or impurities, pure, undefiled, stainless, spotless; Ku. 7. 32, 33; amalāḥ suhṛdaḥ Pt. 2. 171 pure, sincere.
      2 White, bright, shining; karṇāvasaktāmaladaṃtapatraṃ Ku. 7. 23; R. 6. 80. --lā
      1 N. of the goddess Lakṣmī.
      2 The navel cord.
      3 N. of a tree (Mar. āṃvałā) Emblica Officinalis Gærtn; also of a plant (sātalāvṛkṣa) also n. in this sense.
    • --laṃ
      1 Purity.
      2 Talc.
      3 The Supreme Spirit.
    Comp.
    • --ātman a. of pure or undefiled mind.
    • --patatrin m. (trī) the wild goose.
    • --ratnaṃ, --maṇiḥ a crystal. [0150-b]
