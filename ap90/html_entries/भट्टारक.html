• bhaṭṭāraka a. (rikā f.) Venerable, worshipful &c. see bhaṭṭāra above.
    • --kaḥ
      1 A sage, saint.
      2 The sun.
      3 A god, deity.
      4 (In dramas) A king.
      5 An epithet applied to great and learned men.
    Comp.
    • --vāraḥ, --vāsaraḥ Sunday. [0807-c]
