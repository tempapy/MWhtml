dūṣita a. [duṣ-ṇic-kta]
      1 Corrupted, defiled, spoiled.
      2 (a) Hurt, injured. (b) Marred, spoiled, frustrated; Ś. 5. 9. (c) Blinded, obscured, injured; Ku. 4. 8.
      3 Damaged, demoralized.
      4 Blamed, censured.
      5 Falsely accused, traduced, vilified.
      --tā A girl who has been violated.
      --taṃ A fault, offence; U. 4. 14. [0573-a]
