vahaḥ [vah-kartari ac]
      1 Bearing, carrying, supporting &c.
      2 The shoulder of an ox.
      3 A vehicle or conveyance in general.
      4 Particularly, a horse.
      5 Air, wind.
      6 A way, road.
      7 A male river (nada).
      8 A measure of four Droṇas.
      9 A current, stream. [0954-c]
