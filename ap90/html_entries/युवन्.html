• yuvan a. [yautīti yuvā, yu-kanin Uṇ. 1. 154] (yuvatiḥ --tī or yūti f.; compar. yavīyas or kanīyas; superl. yaviṣṭha or kaniṣṭha) 1 Young, youthful, adult, arrived at puberty. --2 Strong, healthy. --3 Excellent, good. --m. (nom. yuvā, yuvānau, yuvānaḥ, acc. pl. yūnaḥ, instr. pl. yuvabhiḥ &c.) 1 A young man, a youth; sā yūni tasminnabhilāṣabaṃrdhaṃ śaśāka śālīnatayā na vaktuṃ R. 6. 81. --2 A younger descendant (the elder being still alive); jīvati tu vaṃśye yuvā P. IV. 1. 163; I. 2. 65, II. 4. 58, IV. 1. 90. --Comp. --khalati a. (tiḥ --tī f.) bald in youth. --gaṃḍaḥ an eruption on the cheeks or face of young men. --jarat a. (tī f.) appearing old in youth, prematurely old. --rāj m., --rājaḥ an heirapparent, a prince-royal, crownprince; (asau) nṛpeṇa cakre yuvarājaśabdabhāk R. 3. 35. [0895-c]
