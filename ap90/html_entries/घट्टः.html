• ghaṭṭaḥ [ghaṭṭ-ghañ]
      1 A landing place, steps on the side of a river leading to the waters (Mar. ghāṃṭa).
      2 Stirring, agitating.
      3 A tollstation.
    Comp.
    • --kuṭī a toll station. °prabhātanyāya see under nyāya.
    • --jīvin m. {1} a ferryman. {2} a man of a mixed tribe; (vaiśyāyāṃ rajakājjātaḥ). {3} attendant at a landing place. [0476-a]
