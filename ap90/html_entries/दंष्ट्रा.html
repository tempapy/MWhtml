• daṃṣṭrā [daṃś ṣṭran ṭāp] A large tooth, tusk, fang; prasahya maṇimuddharenmakaravaktradaṃṣṭrāṃkurāt Bh. 2. 4; R. 2. 46; daṃṣṭrābhaṃgaṃ mṛgāṇāmadhipataya iva vyaktamānāvalepā nājñābhaṃgaṃ sahaṃte nṛvara nṛpatayastvādṛśāḥ sārvabhaumāḥ .. Mu. 3. 22.
    Comp.
    • --astraḥ, --āyudhaḥ a wild boar. --karāla a. having terrible tusks.
    • --viṣaḥ a kind of snake. [0553-1]
