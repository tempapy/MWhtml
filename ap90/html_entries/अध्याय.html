• adhyāya a. [i-ghañ P. III. 3. 21] (At the end of comp.) A reader, student, one who studies; vedādhyāyaḥ a student of the Vedas; so maṃtra°--yaḥ 1 Reading, learning, study, especially of the Vedas; praśāṃtādhyāyasatkathā ( nagarī) Rām. --2 Proper time for reading or for a lesson; °jñāḥ pracakṣate Ms. 4. 102, see anadhyāya also. --3 A lesson, lecture; adhīyate'smin adhyāyaḥ P. III. 3. 122; so svādhyāyo'dhyetavyaḥ --4 A chapter, a large division of a work, such as of the Rāmāyaṇa, Mahābhārata, Manu Smṛti, Pāṇini's Sūtras &c.
