upariṣṭāt ind. [cf. P. V. 3. 31]
    • 1 As an adverb it means (a) Over, above, from above, on high; parahitakaraṇe nopariṣṭānna cādhaḥ Bh. 3. 131; Y. 1. 106. (b) Further or later on, afterwards; kalyāṇāvataṃsā hi kalyāṇa saṃpadupariṣṭādbhavati Māl. 6; idamupariṣṭāt vyākhyātaṃ in the sequel. (c) Behind (opp. purastāt).
    • 2 (As a preposition) it means (a) Over, upon (with gen., rarely acc.); Śi. 11. 3. (b) Down upon. (c) Behind (with gen.).
      Comp. N. of a Vedic metre consisting of 43 or 44 syllables.
      f. a verse of the Triṣṭubh metre, the last line of which consists of eight syllables.
      N. of a Vedic metre having four Pādas, the first of which contains 12 syllables, each of the three others having only eight. [0329-a]
