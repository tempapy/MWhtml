• cūrṇakaḥ [cūrṇa svārthe ka] Grain fried and pounded. --kaṃ 1 A fragrant powder. --2 A style of prose-composition which is easy, does not contain hard letters, and has very few compounds; akaṭhorākṣaraṃ svalpasamāsaṃ cūrṇakaṃ viduḥ Cand. M. 6. --3 Explaining in prose the purport of a foregoing verse. [0497-c]
