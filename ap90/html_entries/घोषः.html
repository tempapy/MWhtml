• ghoṣaḥ [ghuṣ-bhāve ghañ] 1 Noise, tumult, cry or sound in general; sa ghoṣo dhārtarāṣṭrāṇāṃ hṛdayāni vyadārayat Bg. 1. 19; so ratha°, tūrya,° śaṃkha° &c. --2 The thundering of clouds; snigdhagaṃbhīraghoṣaṃ Me. 64. --3 Proclamation. --4 Rumour, report. --5 A herdsman; haiyaṃgavīnamādāya ghoṣavṛddhānupāsthitān R. 1. 45. --6 A hamlet, station of cowherds; gaṃgāyāṃ ghoṣaḥ K. P. 2; ghoṣādānīya Mk. 7. --7 (In gram.) The soft sound heard in the articulation of the soft consonants. --8 A Kāyastha. --9 A vowel. --10 A gnat, mosquito. --11 An epithet of Śiva. --ṣaṃ Bell-metal.
