• baṃdhula a. [baṃdh-ulac] 1 Bent, curved, inclined. --2 Pleasing, delightful, attractive, beautiful. --laḥ 1 A bastard; paragṛhalalitāḥ parānnapuṣṭāḥ parapuruṣairjanitāḥ parāṃganāsu . paradhananiratā guṇeṣvavācyā gajakalabhā iva baṃdhulā lalāmaḥ Mk. 4. 28 (which is an answer given by the bandhulas themselves to the Vidūṣaka's question bhoḥ ke yūyaṃ baṃdhulā nāma). --2 An attendant in a harlot's chamber. --3 The tree called baṃdhūka q. v.
