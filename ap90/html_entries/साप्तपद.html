• sāptapada a. (dī f.), sāptapadīna a. [cf. P. V. 2. 22] Formed by walking together seven steps, or by talking together seven words; yataḥ satāṃ saṃnatagātri saṃgataṃ manīṣibhiḥ sāptapadīnamucyate Ku. 5. 39 (where the latter sense appears better); Pt. 2. 43, 4. 103. --daṃ, --naṃ 1 Circumambulation of the nuptial fire by the bride and bridegroom in seven steps (which makes the marriage tie irrevocable). --2 Friendship, intimacy.
