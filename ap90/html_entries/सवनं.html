• savanaṃ [su sū vā --lyuṭ]
      1 Extracting the Soma juice or drinking it.
      2 A sacrifice; atha taṃ savanāya dīkṣitaḥ R. 8. 75; Ś. 3. 27.
      3 A libation, sacrificial libation.
      4 Bathing, purificatory ablution.
      5 Generation, bearing or bringing forth children. [1110-a]
