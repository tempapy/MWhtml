• pari(rī)hāsaḥ
      1 Joking, jesting, jest, mirth: merriment; tvarāprastāvoyaṃ na khalu parihāsasya viṣayaḥ Māl. 9. 44; parihāsapūrvaṃ ‘jokingly or in jest’ R. 6. 82; parihāsavijalpitaṃ Ś. 2. 18 ‘uttered in jest’; parīhāsāścitrāḥ satatamabhavan yena bhavataḥ Ve. 3. 14; Ku. 7. 19; R. 9. 8; Śi. 10. 12.
      2 Ridiculing, deriding.
    Comp.
    • --vedin m. a buffoon, jester, a witty person. [0683-a]
