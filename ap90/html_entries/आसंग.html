• āsaṃga a. Uninterrupted, perpetual. --gaḥ 1 Attachment, devotion (to any object) (to enjoy or protect it); sukha° lubdhaḥ K. 173; U. 3; Bh. 3. 60. --2 Intentness, close application. --3 Contact, adnerence, clinging; (paṃkajaṃ) saśaivalāsaṃgamapi prakāśate Ku. 5. 9; 3. 46; vratativalayāsaṃgasaṃjātapāśaḥ Ś. 1. 33; Mu. 1. 14; anāsaṃgaḥ absence of consolation; Māl. 2. --4 Association, connection, union; tyaktvā karmaphalāsaṃgaṃ Bg. 4. 20; so kāṃtāsaṃga &c. --5 Fixing, fastening to. --6 Pride about the authorship of a thing (kartṛtvābhimāna). --7 That which is fastened; cf. uttarāsaṃga. --8 Waylaying (?). --gaṃ A kind of fragrant earth; (saurāṣṭramṛttikā). --gaṃ ind. Without interruption, eternally. [0268-a]
