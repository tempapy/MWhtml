• ānupūrvaṃ, --rvyaṃ --rvī [anupūrvasya bhāvaḥ ṣyañ tato vā ṅīṣi yalopaḥ]
      1 Order, succession, series; Ms. 2. 41.
      2 (In law) The regular order of the castes; ṣaḍānupūrvyā viprasya kṣatrasya caturo'varān Ms. 3. 23.
      3 (In logic) Conclusion regularly or syllogistically drawn. [0242-b]
