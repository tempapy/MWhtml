• matkuṇaḥ
      1 A bug; matkuṇāviva purā pariplavau Śi. 14. 68.
      2 An elephant without tusks
      3 A small elephant.
      4 A beardless man.
      5 A buffalo.
      6 The cocoa-nut tree.
      7 A flea.
    • --ṇaṃ An armour for the legs or the thighs.
    • --ṇī Pudendum (of a young girl).
    Comp.
    • --ariḥ hemp. [0835-a]
