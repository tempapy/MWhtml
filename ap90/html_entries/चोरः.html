co(cau)raḥ
      1 A thief, robber; sakalaṃ cora gataṃ tvayā gṛhītaṃ V. 4. 16; iṃdīvaradalaprabhācoraṃ cakṣuḥ Bh. 3. 67.
      2 Any dishonest dealer.
      3 One that steals or captivates the heart.
    Comp.
      --karman n. theft.
      --gata a. robbed.
      --rūpaḥ a clever thief. [0497-a]
