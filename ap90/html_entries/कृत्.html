• kṛt a. [kṛ-kvip] (Generally at the end of comp.) Accomplisher, doer, maker, performer, manufacturer, composer &c. pāpa°, puṇya°, pratimā° &c. --m. 1 A class of affixes used to form derivatives (nouns, adjectives &c.) from roots. --2 A word so formed. --Comp. --aṃtaḥ a word ending with a kṛt affix. --lopaḥ the rejection of kṛt affixes. [0418-a]
  • kṛt {vI.v} {c6c} P. (kṛṃtati-kṛtta) To cut, cut off, divide, tear asunder, cut in pieces, destroy; praharati vidhirmarmacchedī na kṛṃtati jīvitaṃ U. 3. 31, 35; Bk. 9. 42; 15. 97; 16. 15; Ms. 8. 12. {vII.v} {c7c} P. (kṛṇatti, kṛtta) 1 To spin. --2 To surround, encompass. --3 To attire. [0420-a]
