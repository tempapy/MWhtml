• īṣā [īṣ-ka]
      1 The pole or shafts of a carriage or a plough.
      2 A part of a chariot.
    Comp.
    • --daṃhaḥ the handle of a plough; Śi. 18. 43. --daṃta a. with projecting teeth. (--taḥ) {1} an. elephant with a large tusk or tooth. {2} the handle of a plough; Śi. 18. 38. {3} the tusk of an elephant. [0284-b]
