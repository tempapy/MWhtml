• kauṭa a. (ṭī f.) [kūṭa-añ] 1 Living in one's own house; hence, independent, free. --2 Domestic, homely, homebred. --3 Fraudulent, dishonest. --4 Snared. --ṭaḥ 1 Fraud, falsehood. --2 Giving false evidence. --Comp. --jaḥ the Kuṭaja tree. --takṣaḥ (opp. grāmatakṣaḥ) an independent carpenter, one who works at home on his own account and not for the village. --sākṣin m. a false witness. --sākṣyaṃ false evidence, perjury. [0427-b]
