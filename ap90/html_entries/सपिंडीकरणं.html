• sapiṃḍīkaraṇaṃ 1 The performance of a particular Śrāddha in honour of deceased relatives called sapiṃḍa q. v., to be performed at the end of one full year after the death of a relative, but now usually performed on the 12th day after death as part of the funeral obsequies. --2 Giving a person the rights of a Sapiṇḍa or kinsman. [1091-c]
