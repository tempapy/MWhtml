• atipat {c1c} P. 1 To pass over; neglect, omit, transgress. --2 To fly by, beyond, or over; to cross --Caus. 1 To delay, neglect; see atipātya below. --2 To disrespect, offend; ye satyameva hi gurūnatipātayaṃti Mu. 3. 34 transgress, violate; atipātitakālasādhanā Ki. 2. 42. --3 To cause to fly past or by. --4 To make ineffectual; saṃśamanamevaṃ saṃśodhanamatipātayati Suśr. --5 To snatch away, drag away.
