• atyāhitaṃ [ādhā-ādhāre kta, atyaṃtamādhīyate tannivāraṇārthaṃ mano dīyate yasmin Tv.]
      1 A great calamity, danger or evil, misfortune, mishap, accident; na kimapyatyāhitaṃ Ś. 1, kiṃ atyāhitaṃ sītādevyāḥ; -na kevalaṃ °taṃ sāpavādamapi U. 2; mayā °tamupalabdhaṃ V. 4; kimidamuccairatyāhitaṃ Mv. 6; śrutamatyāhitaṃ M. 4 evil or evil news; oft. as an exclamation, ‘Ah, me!’ ‘alas! alas!’, ‘how bad it is!’ Māl. 3, 7; V. 5.
      2 A rash or daring deed (jīvānapekṣikarma); atyāhitaṃ kimapi rākṣasakarma kuryāt Mv. 4. 50 rash and demoniacal deed; pāṃḍuputrairna kimapyatyāhitamāceṣṭitaṃ bhavet Ve. 2. [0038-a]
