• aparigraha a. Without possessions or belongings, attendants &c quite destitute, as in nirāśīraparigrahaḥ --haḥ
      1 Non-acceptance, rejection, renunciation, one of the several kinds of yamas (mental restraints) stated in Yoga Śāstra by Patañjali.
      2 Destitution, poverty. [0110-a]
