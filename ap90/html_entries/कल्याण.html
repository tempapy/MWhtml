• kalyāṇa a. (ṇā or ṇī f.) [kalye prātaḥ aṇyate śabdyate, aṇ-ghañ] 1 Blessed, happy, lucky, fortunate; tvameva kalyāṇi tayostṛtīyā R. 6. 29; Me. 109. --2 Beautiful, agreeable, lovely. --3 Excellent, illustrious. --4 Auspicious, salutary, propitious, good; U. 2. 2; kalyāṇānāṃ tvamasi mahasāṃ bhājanaṃ viśvamūrte Māl. 1. 3. --ṇaṃ 1 Good fortune, happiness, good, prosperity; kalyāṇaṃ kurutāṃ janasya bhagacāṃścaṃdrārdhacūḍāmaṇiḥ H. 1. 212; tadrakṣa kalyāṇaparaṃparāṇāṃ bhoktāramūrjasvalamātmadahaṃ R. 2. 50; 17. 11; Ms. 3. 60; so °abhiniveśī K. 104. --2 Virtue. --3 Festival. --4 Gold. --5 Heaven. --ṇī 1 A cow. --2 Holy or sacred cow; R. 1. 87. --3 A young cow, heifer; U. 4. --Comp. --kṛt a. {1} doing good, beneficial, good; Bg. 6. 40. {2} propitious, lucky. {3} virtuous. --dharman a. virtuous. --bījaṃ a kind of pulse; (Mar. masurā). --vacanaṃ a friendly speech, good wishes.
