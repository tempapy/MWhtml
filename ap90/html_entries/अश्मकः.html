aśmakaḥ [aśmeva sthiraḥ, ivārthe kan]
    • 1 N. of a sage.
    • 2 N. of a country in the south; tathāśmakāḥ puliṃdāśca kaliṃgāśca viśeṣataḥ Rām.; (probably an old name of Travancore); according to Bṛ. S. 14. 22 it is in the north-west.
    • 3 The inhabitants of the country (pl.).
    • 4 A part of the sālva country or its inhabitants; or its king. [0202-a]
