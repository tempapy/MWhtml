• vighaṭṭ 10 U.
      1 To strike down, disperse, scatter, scare away; Śi. 1. 64; Bh. 3. 54.
      2 To rub, strike, rub against; kāraṃḍavānanavighaṭṭitavīcimālāḥ Rs. 3. 8; 4. 9; vighaṭṛitānāṃ saraladrumāṇāṃ Ku. 1. 9 Ki. 8. 45; Śi. 8. 24, 13. 41.
      3 To break, violate (as an agreement).
      4 To break asunder, burst open (as a door). [0969-a]
