      1 A bamboo.
      2 Sharpening, whetting.
      3 Kindling.
      4 Rendering bright.
      5 Polishing.
      6 A reed.
      7 The point of an arrow, the edge of a weapon.
    • --nī
      1 A mat.
      2 A tuft.
      3 A tuft of hair on the head of a horse. [0537-c]
