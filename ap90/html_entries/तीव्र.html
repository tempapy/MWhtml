• tīvra a.
      1 Severe, intense, sharp, acute, violent, poignant, pungent, impetuous; vilaṃghitādhoraṇatīvrayatnāḥ R. 5. 48 ‘strong or violent efforts’; &c. U. 3. 35; Ś. 1. 33, 5. 7.
      2 Hot, warm.
      3 Flashing.
      4 Pervading.
      5 Endless, unlimited.
      6 Horrible, dreadful.
    • --vraḥ
      1 Sharpness.
      2 Śiva.
    • --vraṃ
      1 Heat, pungency.
      2 A shore.
      3 Iron, steel.
      4 Tin.
    • --vraṃ ind. Violently, sharply, excessively.
    Comp.
    • --ānaṃdaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
    • --gati a. quick, swift.
    • --gaṃdhā cumin seed.
    • --pauruṣaṃ {1} daring heroism. {2} heroism (in general).
    • --vedanā acute or sharp pain.
    • --saṃvega a. {1} of strong impulse, resolute. {2} very poignant or sharp. [0540-b]
