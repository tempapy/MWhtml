• gucchaḥ 1 A bundle, bunch (in general). --2 A bunch of flowers, a cluster of blossoms, a clump (of trees &c.); akṣṇornikṣipadaṃjanaṃ śravaṇayostāpicchagucchāvaliṃ Gīt. 11; Ms. 1. 48; Śi. 6. 50; Y. 2. 229. --3 The plumage of a peacock. --4 A necklace of pearls (in general). --5 A pearl necklace of 32 (or, according to some, of 70) strings. --Comp. --ardhaḥ a pearl necklace of 24 strings. (--rdhaḥ, --rdhaṃ) half of a cluster. --kaṇiśaḥ a kind of corn. --patraḥ the palm tree. --phalaḥ {1} the vine. {2} plantain tree. [0459-c]
