aghoṣa a. [nāsti ghoṣo yasya yatra vā]
    • 1 Hard-sounding, See below.
    • 2 Devoid of cow-herds.
      The hard sound of a consonant, hollowness of sound with which all hard consonants and the Visarga are pronounced (one of the 11 kinds of bāhyaprayatna, See P. VIII. 2. 1), or the consonants so pronounced (khayāṃ yamāḥ khayaḥ ẖkaḫpau visargaḥ śaraeva ca . ete śvāsānupradānā aghoṣāśca vivṛṇvate). [0016-c]
