• ārātrikaṃ [arātrāvapi nirvṛttaṃ ṭhañ] 1 Waving a light (or the vessel containing it) at night before an idol (Mar. āratī oṃvāłaṇeṃ); sarveṣu cāṃgeṣu ca saptavārān ārātrikaṃ bhaktajanastu kuryāt. --2 The light so waved; śirasi nihitabhāraṃ pātramārātrikasya bhramayati mayi bhūyaste kṛpārdraḥ kaṭākṣaḥ Śaṅkara.
