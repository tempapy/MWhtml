• apatha a. [nāsti paṃthā yatra] Pathless, roadless; °tho deśaḥ, °thā nagarī &c. --thaṃ, --thaḥ (also apaṃthāḥ P. V. 4. 72, II. {4} II. 4. 30) 1 Not a way, absence of a way or road, pathless state; a bad or wrong road (lit.); (fig.) irregularity, deviation, a moral irregularity or deviation, a wrong road, bad or evil course; apathe padamarpayaṃti hi śrutavaṃtopi rajonimīlitāḥ R. 9. 74; 17. 54; na kaścidvarṇānāmapathamapakṛṣṭopi bhajate Ś. 5. 10 follows evil ways; kāritastvamapathe pada mayā Ki. 13. 45, 64. --2 Heresy, heterodoxy (in opinions). --3 The vulva. --thā N. of several plants. --thaṃ ind. By or in the wrong way, astray; apathaṃ vartate Sk. --Comp. --gāmin a. pursuing evil courses; heretical. --prapanna a. taking to evil ways (as man); spent or used out of place, misapplied, misspent (as money &c.); H. 3. 123.
