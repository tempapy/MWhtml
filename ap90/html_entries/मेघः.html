• meghaḥ [mehati varṣati jalaṃ, mih-ghañ kutvam]
      1 A cloud; kurvannaṃjanamecakā iva diśo meghaḥ samuttiṣṭhate Mk. 5. 23, 2, 3 &c.
      2 A mass, multitude.
      3 N. of one of the six Rāgas (in music).
      4 A fragrant grass.
