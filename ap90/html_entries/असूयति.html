• asūyati Den. P. (P. III. 1. 27)
      1 To envy, to be jealous of; kathaṃ citragato bhartā mayā'sūyitaḥ M. 4.
      2 To detract from; murmur, grumble at, be displeased with, scorn, be discontented with or angry with (with dat. of person or thing); asūyaṃti sacivopadeśāya K. 108; asūyaṃti mahyaṃ prakṛtayaḥ V. 4; nyasanāya sasaurabhasya kastarusūnasya śirasyasūyati Śi. 16. 20; Bg. 3. 31; sometimes with acc.; asūyaṃti hi rājāno janānanṛtavādinaḥ Mb. --Caus. To cause to murmur at. [0214-b]
