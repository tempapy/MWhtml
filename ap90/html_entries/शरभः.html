• śarabhaḥ [śṝ-abhac Uṇ. 3. 122]
      1 A young elephant.
      2 A fabulous animal said to have {8} legs and to be stronger than a lion; śarabhakulamajihmaṃ proddharatyaṃbu kūpāt Rs. 1. 23; aṣṭapādaḥ śarabhaḥ siṃhaghātī Mb.
      3 A camel.
      4 A grass-hopper.
      5 A locust. [1035-c]
