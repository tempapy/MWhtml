kavacaḥ, --caṃ [cf. Uṇ. 4. 2]
      1 An armour, coat of mail, a mail.
      2 An amulet, a charm, a mystical syllable (huṃ-hūṃ) considered as a preservative like armour.
      3 A kettledrum.
    Comp.
      --patraḥ the birch tree.
      --hara a. {1} wearing armour. {2} old enough to wear an armour; kavacaharaḥ kumāraḥ Sk.; cf. varmahara in R. 8. 94. (--raḥ) a boy, child. [0389-a]
