• apakarṣaṇa a. Taking or drawing away, removing, diminishing (opp. vardhaka); na cāsti sadṛśaṃ tena kiṃcitsthaulyāpakarṣaṇaṃ Suśr. --ṇaṃ
      1 Removing, taking away; drawing away or down, depriving (one) of, drawing out, extracting.
      2 Lessening, making inferior, detraction.
      3 Superseding.
      4 Denying; abolishing. [0104-a]
