• śābda a. (bdī f.) [śabda-aṇ] 1 Relating to or derived from a word. --2 Relating to or depending on sound (opp. ārtha.). --3 Verbal, oral. --4 Sounding, sonorous. --5 Nominal (as inflection). --bdaḥ A grammarian. --bdī N. of Sarasvatī. --Comp. --bodhaḥ perception or apprehension of the sense of words. --vyaṃjanā insinuation founded on words.
