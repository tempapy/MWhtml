• varaṃḍaḥ [vṛ-aṃḍac]
      1 A multitude, group.
      2 A pimple or eruption on the face.
      3 A veranda.
      4 A heap of grass.
      5 The string of a fish-hook.
      6 pocket. (The word varaṃḍalaṃbuka in yadidānīmahaṃ varaṃḍalaṃbuka iva dūramutkṣipya pātitaḥ Mk. 1. is of doubtful meaning; it seems to mean ‘an over-hanging or projecting wall’, which, if raised high, is sure to topple down; so in the case of the Sūtradhāra whose expectations were raised very high only to be cruelly disappointed).
