amā a. [na mā-kā] Measureless. --ind. Ved.
    • 1 At home, in the house; kāmaścaratāmamābhūt Rv. 2. 38. 6.
    • 2 In this world, here below (ihaloke).
    • 3 With, near, close to; amaivāsāṃ tadbhavati Śat. Br.
    • 4 Together with, in conjunction or company with, as in amātya, amāvāsyā q. v.; amākṛ to draw near, have near oneself. --f.
    • 1 The day of the new moon, the day of the conjunction of the sun and moon; amāyāṃ tu sadā soma aṣedhīḥ pratipadyate Vyāsa.
    • 2 The sixteenth digit of the moon.
    • 3 The fifteenth digit also. --m. The soul.
      Comp. a. Ved. met, come together.
      the end of the day of new moon.
      f. living at home during life, growing old at home; Rv. 10. 39. 3; being without husband in the same dwelling with her parents, as a maiden ( pitṛṣad); Rv. 2. 17. 7. --parvan n. the sacred time of amā, day of new moon. [0150-b]
