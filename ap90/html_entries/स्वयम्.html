• svayam ind.
      1 Oneself, in one's own person (used reflexively and applicable to all persons, such as myself, ourselves, thyself, himself &c. &c., and sometimes used with other pronouns for the sake of emphasis); viṣavṛkṣopi saṃvardhya svayaṃ chettumasāṃprataṃ Ku. 2. 55; yasya nāsti svayaṃ prajñā śāstraṃ tasya karoti kiṃ Subhāṣ.; R. 1. 70; 3. 20, 2. 56; Ms. 5. 39.
      2 Spontaneously, of one's own accord, without trouble or exertion; svayamevotpadyaṃta evaṃvidhāḥ kulapāṃśavo niḥsnehāḥ paśavaḥ K.
