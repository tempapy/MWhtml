• gir a. [gṝ-kvip vā ṭāp] Ved. Addressing, invoking. --f. (nom. sing. gīḥ; instr. dual gīrbhyāṃ &c.)
      1 Speech, words, language; vacasyavasite tasmin sasarja giramātmabhūḥ Ku. 2. 53; 3. 72; bhavatīnāṃ sūnṛtayaiva girā kṛtamātithyaṃ Ś. 1; pravṛttisārāḥ khalu mādṛśāṃ giraḥ Ki. 1. 25; Śi. 2. 15; Y. 1. 71.
      2 Invocation, praise, song.
      3 N. of Sarasvatī, the goddess of speech and learning.
    Comp.
