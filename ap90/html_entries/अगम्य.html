• agamya a. [na gaṃtumarhati, gam-yat na. ta.] 1 Not fit to be walked in or approached, unapproachable, inaccessible (lit. & fig.); yogināmapyagamyaḥ &c., --2 Inconceivable, incomprehensible; manaso'gamya īśvaraḥ God transcends mind (conception or thought); yāḥ saṃpadastā manasopyagamyāḥ Śi. 3. 59, See under gamya also. --Comp. --rūpa a. of unsurpassed or inconceivable nature, form, &c. °rūpāṃ padavīṃ prapitsunā Ki. 1. 9.
