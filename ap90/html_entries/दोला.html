dolā, dolikā
      1 A litter, palanquin.
      2 A swing, hammock (fig. also); āsītsa dolācalacittavṛttiḥ R. 14. 34; 9. 46; 19. 44; saṃdehadolāmāropyate K. 207.
      3 Swinging, fluctuation.
      4 Doubt, uncertainty.
      5 The Indigo plant.
    Comp.
      --adhirūḍha, --ārūḍha a. (lit.) mounted on a swing; (fig.) uncertain, irresolute, disquieted.
      --yuddhaṃ uncertainty of success, a fight with varying success; Śi. 18. 80. [0582-b]
