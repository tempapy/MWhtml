• lalāṭaṃ [laḍ-ac ḍasya laḥ, lalamaṭati aṭ-aṇ vā Tv.] The forehead; likhitamapi lalāṭe projjhituṃ kaḥ samarthaḥ H. 1. 21; N. 1. 15. --Comp. --akṣaḥ an epithet of Śiva. --taṭaṃ the slope of the forehead, the forehead itself. --paṭṭaḥ, --paṭṭikā {1} the flat surface of the forehead. {2} a tiara, fillet. --rekhā {1} a line on the forehead. {2} a wrinkled brow. {3} a coloured sectarial mark on the forehead. --lekhā the line on the forehead.
