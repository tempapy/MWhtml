nir ind. A substitute for nis before vowels and soft consonants conveying the senses of ‘out of’, ‘away from’, ‘without’, ‘free from’, and may be frequently expressed by ‘less’, ‘un’, used with the noun; see the compounds given below; see nis and cf. a also.
