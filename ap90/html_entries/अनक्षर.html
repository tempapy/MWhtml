• anakṣara a. [na. ba.]
      1 Unable to speak, mute, dumb, unlettered; mukhamanakṣaraṃ svākṛteḥ Bh. 2. 56.
      2 Unfit to be uttered.
    • --raṃ [ apraśastānyakṣarāṇi yatra] Abusive language, foul or abusive words, censure. --adv. Without the use of words, not expressed by words, mutely, dumbly; °raṃ papraccha K. 219, 143; °vyaṃjitadaurhṛdena R. 14. 26. [0055-a]
