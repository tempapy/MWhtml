• āścarya a. [ā-car-ṇyat suṭ P. VI. 1. 147] Marvellous, wonderful, extraordinary, astonishing, strange, curious; āścaryo gavāṃ doho'gopena Sk.; tadanu vavṛṣuḥ puṣpamāścaryameghāḥ R. 16. 87; °darśano manuṣyalokaḥ Ś. 7. --ryaṃ 1 A wonder, miracle, marvel; kimāścaryaṃ kṣāradeśe prāṇadā yamadūtikā Udb.; karmāścaryāṇi U. 1 wonderful deeds; K. 65; Mv. 1; Bg. 11. 6, 2. 29. --2 Surprise, wonder, astonishment; °maya Bg. 11. 11. --3 A strange appearance, prodigy. --4 (Used as an exclamation) A wonder, how strange or curious; āścaryaṃ paripīḍito 'bhiramate yaccātakastṛṣṇayā Chāt. 2. 4; usually with yacca, yatra or yadi with a following potential or future. --Comp. --bhūta a. wonderful, being an object of wonder; K. 8.
