• deśya a. [diś-karbhaṇiṇyat deśa-yat vā] 1 To be pointed out or proved. --2 Local, provincial. --3 Born in a country, native. --4 Genuine, of genuine descent. --5 Being on the spot or place (where anything is due). --6 Not far from, almost; see deśīya above. --śyaḥ 1 An eye-witness of anything abhiyoktā diśeddeśyaṃ Ms. 8. 52.-53. --2 The inhabitant of a country. --śyaṃ The statement of a question or argument, the thing to be proved or substantiated (pūrvapakṣa). [0580-c]
