• vyavasthā {c1c} A. 1 To be placed asunder. --2 To be arranged in due order. --3 To be settled or fixed, become permanent, vacatīyamidaṃ vyavasthitaṃ Ku. 4. 21. --4 To rest or depend upon. --Caus. 1 To place or rest upon, direct towards; Ku. 7. 75. --2 To arrange, manage, adjust. --3 To settle, resolve, decree. --4 To separate, place apart. --5 To do, perform. --6 To lay down a rule or law, enact a law.
