• vyāvṛtta p. p.
      1 Turned a way from, withdrawn from; vyāvṛttā yatparasvebhyaḥ śrutau taskaratā sthitā R. 1. 27; V. 1. 8.
      2 Separated from, singled out.
      3 (a) Excluded, set aside; different from; ayamartho'smād vyāvṛttaḥ Tarka K. (b) Not being found or existing in, absent from (frequently used in phil. in this sense); dhūmo jalahadādervyāvṛttaḥ Tarka K.; Mu. 5. 10.
      4 Revolved, turned round.
      5 Encompassed, surrounded.
      6 Desisting, ceased from; Ku. 2. 35.
      7 Split asunder.
    Comp.
    • --gati a. desisting from any course of action. [1024-b]
