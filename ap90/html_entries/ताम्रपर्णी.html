• tāmraparṇī N. of a river rising in the Malaya mountain. It appears to be the same as the Tāmbaravāri of the present day which rises in the eastern declivity of the western Ghats, runs through the district of Tinnevelly, and falls into the gulf of Manar; cf. R. 4. 49, 50 and B. R. 10. 56. [1194-a]
