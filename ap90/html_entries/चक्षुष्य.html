• cakṣuṣya a. [cakṣaṣe hita yat]
      1 Goodlooking, agreeable to the sight, pleasing, beautiful: Śi. 8. 57.
      2 Good for the eyes.
      3 Produced from the eye.
    • --ṣyaḥ, --ṣyā A collyrium or application to the eyes.
    • --ṣyā A pleasing or agreeable woman. [0481-c]
