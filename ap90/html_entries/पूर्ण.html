• pūrṇa p. p. [pur-kta ni°]
      1 Filled, filled with, full of; opt. in comp; taṃ tathā kṛpayāviṣṭaṃ aśrupūrṇākulekṣaṇaṃ Bg. 2. 1; so śoka°, jala° &c.
      2 Whole, full, entire, complete; R. 3. 38.
      3 Fulfilled, accomplished.
      4 Ended, completed.
      5 Past, elapsed.
      6 Satisfied, contented.
      7 Full-sounding, sonorous.
      8 Strong, powerful.
      9 Selfish, or self-indulgent.
      10 Drawn, bent (as a bow).
    • --rṇā
      1 An epithet of the fifteenth digit of the moon.
      2 N. of the fifth, tenth, and fifteenth lunar days or tithis.
    • --rṇaṃ Ved.
      1 Abundance, plenty.
      2 Water.
