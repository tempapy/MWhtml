• utsah 1 A.
      1 To be able, have power or energy; (expressed by ‘can’); dare, venture (with inf.); tavānuvṛttiṃ na ca kartumutsahe Ku. 5. 65; Ś. 5; Mu. 4. 14; Śi. 14. 83; Bk. 3. 54, 5. 59, 14. 89; sometimes with acc. and dat. also; Pt. 1.
      2 To attempt, be prompted or incited; Ki. 1. 36; to cheer up, not to sink or give way; ajñavannotsahethāstvaṃ Bk. 19. 16.
      3 To feel at ease, enjoy pleasure; kṣaṇamapyutsahate na māṃ vinā Ku. 4. 36.
      4 To go forward, march on; K. 249. --Caus. To encourage, excite, instigate, incite; varuṇotsāhitena vedhasā K. 22; Bk. 9. 69. [0299-a]
