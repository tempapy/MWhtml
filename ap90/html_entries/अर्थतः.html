arthataḥ ind. [artha-tasil]
    • 1 With reference to the meaning or a particular object; yaccārthato gauravaṃ Māl. 1. 7 depth of meaning; Śi. 7. 28.
    • 2 In fact, really, truly; na nāmataḥ kevalamarthato'pi Śi. 3. 56; ityādiṣṭamarthato bhavati Mv. 3.
    • 3 For the sake of money, gain or profit; aiśvaryādanapetamīśvaramayaṃ lokorthataḥ sevate Mu. 1. 14.
    • 4 On account of, by reason of.
    • 5 By reason of wealth or a particular purpose; arthataḥ puruṣo nārī yā nārī sārthataḥ pumān Mk. 3. 27. [0167-c]
