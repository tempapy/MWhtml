kauve(be)ra a. (rī f.) [kuverasyedaṃ aṇ]
      1 Belonging to or coming from Kubera; yānaṃ sasmāra kauberaṃ R. 15. 45. --rī The north (the direction presided over by Kubera); tataḥ pratasthe kauverīṃ bhāsvāniva raghurdiśaṃ R. 4. 66.
      2 The Śakti or female energy of Kubera. [0428-c]
