• itihāsaḥ [fr. iti-ha-āsa(3rd. pers. sing. Perf. of as to be); so it has been]
      1 History (legendary or traditional); dharmārthakāmamokṣāṇāmupadeśasamanvitaṃ . pūrvavṛttaṃ kapāyuktamitihāsaṃ pracakṣate ..
      2 Heroic history (such as the Mahābhārata).
      3 Historical evidence, tradition (which is recognized as a proof by the Paurāṇikas).
    Comp.
