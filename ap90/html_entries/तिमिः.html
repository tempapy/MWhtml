• timiḥ 1 The ocean. --2 A kind of whale or fish of an enormous size; R. 13. 10. --3 A fish in general. --4 The figure of a fish produced by drawing two lines, one intersecting the other at right angles. --Comp. --koṣaḥ the ocean. --jaṃ a kind of pearl. --dhvajaḥ N. of a demon killed by Indra with the assistance of Daśaratha. (It was in the fight with this demon that Kaikeyī saved the life of Daśaratha while in a swooning fit and got from him two boons which she afterwards used to send Rāma into exile). [0538-a]
