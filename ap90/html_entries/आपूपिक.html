• āpūpika a. (kī f.) [ apūpaṭhak] 1 A good maker of apūpa (cakes). --2 Accustomed to eat cakes. --3 Fond of cakes, eating cakes (with benefit). --4 Selling cakes. --5 Baking cakes. --6 Forming an ingredient of cakes, good for cakes (as guḍa). --kaḥ A baker; confectioner. --kaṃ A multitude of cakes (apūpānāṃ samūhaḥ).
