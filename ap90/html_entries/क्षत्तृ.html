• kṣattṛ m. [kṣad saṃjñāyām tṛc Uṇ. 2. 91] 1 One who cuts or carves anything. --2 An attendant, a doorkeeper. --3 A coachman, charioteer. --4 A man born of a Śūdra man and Kṣatriya woman; cf. Ms. 10. 9. --5 The son of a female slave; ( e. g. vidura). --6 Brahmā. --7 A fish. --8 One who fights from a chariot. --9 The manager of a treasure (koṣādhyakṣa). [0434-c]
