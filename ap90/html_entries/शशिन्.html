śaśin m. [śaśo'styasya ini]
      1 The moon; śaśinaṃ punareti śarvarī R. 8. 56, 6. 85; Me. 41.
      2 Camphor,
    Comp.
      --īśaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
      --kalā a digit of the moon; Mu. 1. 1. --kāṃtaḥ the moon-gem. (--taṃ) a lotus.
      --koṭiḥ a horn of the moon.
      --grahaḥ an eclipse of the moon.
      --jaḥ an epithet of Budha or Mercury (son of the moon).
      --prabha a. having the lustre of the moon, as bright and white as the moon; R. 3. 16. --bhaṃ {1} a water-lily. {2} a pearl.
      --prabhā moonlight.
      --bhūṣaṇaḥ, --bhṛt m., --mauliḥ, --śekharaḥ epithets of Śiva.
      --lekhā a digit of the moon. [1037-c]
