• dhmā {c1c} P. (dhamati, dhmāta; caus. dhmāpayati) 1 To blow, breathe out, exhale. --2 To blow (as a wind-instrument), produce sound by blowing; śaṃkhaṃ dadhamau pratāpavān Bg. 1. 12, 18; R. 7. 63; Bk. 3. 34; 17. 7. --3 To blow a fire, excite fire by blowing, excite sparks; ko dhamecchāṃtaṃ ca pāvakaṃ Mb. --4 To manufacture by blowing. --5 To cast, blow, or throw away.
