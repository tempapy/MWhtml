• vyatīpātaḥ 1 Total departure, complete deviation. --2 Any great portentous calamity, or a portent foreboding a great calamity. --3 Disrespect, contempt. --4 The seventeenth of the astronomical Yogas. --5 The day of full-moon (when it falls on a Monday). --6 A malignant or evil aspect of the sun and moon (considered to be inauspicious for the performance of any action). [1017-c]
