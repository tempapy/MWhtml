• śākaḥ --kaṃ [śak-ghañ]
      1 A vegetable, pot-herb, herb, any edible leaf, fruit or root used as a vegetable; dillīśvaro vā jagadīśvaro vā manorathān pūrayituṃ samarthaḥ . anyairnṛpālaiḥ paridīyamānaṃ śākāya vā syāllavaṇāya vā syāt Jag. --kaḥ {1} Power, strength, energy.
      2 The teak tree.
      3 The Śirīṣa tree.
      4 N. of a people; see śaka.
      5 An era; especially the era of Sālivāhana.
      6 N. of the sixth Dvīpa.
