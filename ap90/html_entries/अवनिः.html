avaniḥ --nī f. [av-ani Uṇ. 2. 101]
    • 1 The earth; oft used at the end of comp. meaning ‘ground’, ‘place’; kānanāvanau Dk. 7, 13; līlāvanau place of recreation 25.
    • 2 A finger (Nir.).
    • 3 A river; Rv. 1. 190. 7.
    • 4 N. of a plant.
    • 5 Course, bed of a river. avaniṃgata a. fallen prostrate on the ground.
      Comp. --nāthaḥ, --patiḥ, --pālaḥ lord of the earth, king; patiravanipatīnāṃ taiścakāśe caturbhiḥ R. 10. 86, 11. 93. --caraḥ a. roving over the earth, vagabond.
      a mountain Śi. 16. 78. --tala the surface of the earth.
      the globe.
      a tree. [0183-b]
