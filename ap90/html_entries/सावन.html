• sāvana a. (nī f.) [savanaṃ yāgāṃgaṃ snānaṃ somani ṣpīḍanaṃ vā tasyedamaṇ] Relating to, or comprising, the three savanas. --naḥ 1 An institutor of a sacrifice, or one who employs priests at a sacrifice. --2 The conclusion of a sacrifice, or the ceremony by which it is concluded. --3 N. of Varuṇa. --4 A month of thirty solar days. --5 A natural day from sunrise to sunset. --6 A particular kind of year. [1120-c]
