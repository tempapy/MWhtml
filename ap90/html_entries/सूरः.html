• sūraḥ [suvati prerayati karmaṇi lokānudayena, sū-kran; Uṇ. 2. 24] 1 The sun. --2 The Arka plant. --3 The Soma. --4 A wise or learned man. --5 A hero, king. --Comp. --cakṣus a. radiant as the sun. --sutaḥ an epithet of Saturn. --sūtaḥ the charioteer of the sun, i. e. Aruṇa. [1135-c]
