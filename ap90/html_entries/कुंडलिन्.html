• kuṃḍalin (nī f.)
      1 Decorated with ear-rings.
      2 Circular, spiral.
      3 Winding, coiling (as a serpent). --m.
      1 A snake.
      2 A peacock.
      3 An epithet of Varuṇa, and of Śiva.
      4 The spotted or painted deer.
    • --nī A form of Durgā or Śakti. [0408-c]
