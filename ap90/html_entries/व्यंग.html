vyaṃga a. [vigatamaṃgaṃ yasya]
      1 Bodiless.
      2 Wanting a limb, deformed, mutilated, maimed, crippled.
      3 Illarranged.
      4 Lame.
      --gaḥ
      1 A cripple.
      2 A frog.
      3 Dark spots on the cheek.
    Comp.
