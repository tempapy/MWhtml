pāśupata a. (tī f.) [paśupateridaṃ aṇ] Coming from or relating or sacred to Paśupati.
      --taḥ
      1 A follower and worshipper of Śiva.
      2 A follower of the doctrines of Paśupati.
      --taṃ The Pāśupata doctrines; (for the Pāśupata doctrines, see Sarva. S.).
    Comp.
      --astraṃ N. of a missile presided over by paśupati or Śiva (which Arjuna acquired from Śiva). [0699-c]
