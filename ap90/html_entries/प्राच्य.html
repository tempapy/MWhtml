• prācya a. [prāci bhavaḥ yat] 1 Being or situated in front. --2 Being or living in the east, eastern, easterly. --3 Prior, preceding, previous. --4 Ancient, old. --cyāḥ (pl.) 1 ‘The eastern country’, the country south or east of the river Sarasvatī. --2 The people of this country. --Comp. --bhāṃṣā the eastern dialect, language spoken in the east of India.
