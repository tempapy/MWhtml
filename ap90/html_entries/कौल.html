• kaula a. (lī f.) [kule bhavaḥ aṇ cf. P. IV. 2. 96]
      1 Relating to a family, ancestral, hereditary.
      2 Of a noble family, well-born.
    • --laḥ A worshipper of śakti according to the left hand ritual.
    • --laṃ The doctrine and practices of the left hand Śāktas (for a short description of kauladharma see Karpūr. 1, speech of bhairavānaṃda). [0428-b]
