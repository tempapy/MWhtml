• jaṭhara a. [jāyate jaṃturgarbho vāsmin jan-ara ṭhāṃta deśaḥ Tv.] 1 Hard, stiff, firm. --2 Old. --3 Bound, tied. --raḥ, --raṃ 1 The stomach, belly, abdomen; jaṭharaṃ ko na bibharti kevalaṃ Pt. 1. 22. --2 The womb. --3 A hole, cavity. --4 The interior of anything. --5 The bosom. --6 Morbid affection of the bowels. --Comp. --agniḥ the digestive fire of the stomach, the gastric fluid. --āmayaḥ dropsy. --jvālā, --vyathā bellyache, colic. --yaṃtraṇā, --yātanā pain endured by the child in the womb.
