• ghātaḥ [han-ṇic ghañ] 1 A blow, stroke, bruise, hit; jyāghāta Ś. 3. 13; nayanaśaraghāta Gīt. 10; so pārṣṇighātaḥ, śiroghāta &c. --2 Killing, hurting, destruction, slaughter; viyogo mugdhākṣyāḥ sa khalu ripugha tāvadhirabhūt U. 3. 44; paśughātaḥ Gīt. 1; Y. 2. 159; 3. 252. --3 An arrow. --4 Power. --5 The product of a sum in multiplication. (In comp. translated by ‘inauspicious’; °divasaḥ). --Comp. --caṃdraḥ the moon when in an inauspicious mansion determined by one's natal zodiacal sign. --tithiḥ an inauspicious lunar day. --nakṣatraṃ an inauspicious constellation. --vāraḥ an inauspicious day of the week. --sthānaṃ a slaughter-house, place for execution.
