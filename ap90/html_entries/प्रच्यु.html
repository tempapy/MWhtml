• pracyu {c1c} A. 1 To move away, go away, withdraw, retreat. --2 To fall off from, drop down, fall down. --3 To forsake, abandon (as religion). --4 To be ejected or driven, be dislodged, be deprived of, lose (with abl.). --5 To flow out, stream forth. --6 To drive, urge on. --Caus. 1 To move, shake. --2 To displace, dislodge, eject, expel. --3 To cause to fall, ruin.
