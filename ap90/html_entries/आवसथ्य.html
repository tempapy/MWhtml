āvasathya a. [āvasatha-ñya] Being in a house.
      --thyaḥ The sacred fire kept in the house, one of the five fires used in sacrifices; see paṃcāgni.
      --thyaḥ --thyaṃ A dwelling for pupils and ascetics.
      --thyaṃ
      1 Placing a sacred fire within a house.
      2 A house. [0260-b]
