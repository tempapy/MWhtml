irā [i-ran Uṇ. 2. 28; iṃ kāmaṃ rāti rā-ka vā Tv.]
    • 1 The earth.
    • 2 Speech.
    • 3 The goddess of speech, Sarasvatī.
    • 4 Water.
    • 5 Food.
    • 6 Spirituous liquor.
    • 7 Any drinkable fluid; a draught (especially of milk).
    • 8 Refreshment, comfort (Ved. in the last three senses).
      Comp. {1} N. of Varuṇa, of Viṣṇu and of Gaṇeśa. {2} a king, sovereign.
      a. Ved. whose milk is a refreshment or enjoyment. (--raḥ) the milk ocean.
      a. earth-born, terrestrial. {2} aquatic. (--raṃ) hail; so irāvaraṃ.
      N. of Kāma or Cupid.
      pimples on the head. [0280-a]
