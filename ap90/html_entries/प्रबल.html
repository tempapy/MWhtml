• prabala a.
      1 Very strong or powerful, mighty, valorous (as a man); R. 3. 60; Rs. 3. 23.
      2 Violent, strong, intense, excessive, very great; prabalatamasāmevaṃ prāyāḥ śubheṣu hi vṛttayaḥ Ś. 7. 24, prabalapurovātayā vṛṣṭyā M. 4. 2; prabalāṃ vedanāṃ R. 8. 50.
      3 Important.
      4 Abounding with.
      5 Dangerous, destructive.
    • --laḥ
      1 N. of a Daitya.
      2 A sprout (pallava).
    • --laṃ ind. Exceedingly, much. [0752-a]
