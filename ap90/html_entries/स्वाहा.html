• svāhā 1 An oblation or offering made to all gods indiscriminately. --2 N. of the wife of Agni. --ind. An exclamation used in offering oblations to the gods (with dat.); iṃdrāya svāhā; agnaye svāhā &c. --Comp. --kāraḥ utterance of the exclamation Svāhā; svāhāsvadhākāravivarjitāni śmaśānatulyāni gṛhāṇi tāni. --patiḥ, --priyaḥ Agni or fire. --bhuj m. a god, deity. [1162-c]
