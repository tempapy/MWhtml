• taraṇi a. [tṝ-ani] Ved.
      1 Passing through, pervading (as the sun).
      2 Quick, energetic, unremitting.
      3 Saving, carrying over, benevolent --ṇiḥ
      1 The sun.
      2 A ray of light
      3 The Arka plant.
      4 Copper.
    • --ṇiḥ, --ṇī f. A raft, boat.
    Comp.
    • --dhanyaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
    • --peṭakaḥ an oval bowl of wood for baling a boat.
    • --ratnaṃ a ruby. [0545-b]
