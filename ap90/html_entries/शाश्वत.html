śāśvata a. (tī f.) [śaśvad bhavaḥ aṇ]
      1 Eternal, perpetual, everlasting; śāśvatīḥ samāḥ Rām. (= U. 2. 5) ‘for eternal years’, ‘ever more’ 'for all time to come'; U. 5. 27; R. 14. 14.
      2 All.
      --taḥ
      1 N. of Śiva.
      2 Of Vyāsa.
      3 The sun.
      --taṃ Heaven.
      --taṃ ind. Eternally, perpetually, for ever. [1042-b]
