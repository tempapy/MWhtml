jña a. [jñā-ka] (At the end of comp.)
      1 Knowing, familiar with; kāryajña, nimittajña, śāstrajña, sarvajña &c.
      2 Wise; as in jñaṃmanya thinking oneself to be wise.
      --jñaḥ
      1 A wise and learned man.
      2 The sentient soul.
      3 The planet Mercury.
      4 The planet Mars.
      5 An epithet of Brahmā. [0518-b]
