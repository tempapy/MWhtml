• amutra ind. (opp. iha) [adas-tral] 1 There, in that place, therein; amutrāsan yavanāḥ Dk. 127. --2 There (in what precedes or has been said), in that case. --3 There above, in the next world, in the life to come; yāvajjīvaṃ ca tatkuryādyenāmutra sukhaṃ vaset; yattu vāṇijake dattaṃ neha nāmutra tadbhavet Ms. 3. 181; Bg. 6. 40. --4 There; anenaivārbhakāḥ sarve nagare'mutra bhakṣitāḥ Ks. --5 Thither, that way. --Comp. --bhūyaṃ Ved. being in the other world; dying.
