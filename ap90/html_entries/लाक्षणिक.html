• lākṣaṇika a. (kī f.) [lakṣaṇayā bodhayati ṭhak]
      1 One who is acquainted with marks or signs.
      2 Characteristic, indicatory.
      3 Having a secondary sense, used in a secondary sense (as a word, as distinguished from vācya and vyaṃjaka q. v.); syādvācaṃko lākṣaṇikaḥ śabdo'tra vyaṃjakastridhā K. P. 2.
      4 Expressing indirectly or figuratively.
      5 Secondary, inferior.
      6 Technical.
    • --kaḥ A technical term. [0926-c]
