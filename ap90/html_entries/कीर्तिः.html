• kīrtiḥ f. [kṝt-ktin]
      1 Fame, renown, glory; iha kīrtimavāpneti Ms. 2. 9; vaṃśasya kartāramanaṃtakīrti R. 2. 64; Me. 45.
      2 Favour, approbation.
      3 Dirt, mud.
      4 Extension, expansion.
      5 Light, lustre, splendour.
      6 Sound.
      7 Mention, speech, report.
    Comp.
    • --bhāj a. famous, celebrated, renowned. (--m.) an epithet of Droṇa, the military preceptor of the Kauravas and Pāṇḍavas.
    • --śeṣaḥ survival or remaining behind only in fame, leaving nothing behind but fame: i. e. death; cf. nāmaśeṣa, ālekhyaśeṣa; sarasīva kīrtiśeṣaṃ gatavati bhuvi vikramāditye Vās. [0405-b]
