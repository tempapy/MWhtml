apratiṣṭha a.
    • 1 Not stable or firmly fixed, fluctuating, not made permanent; U. 5. 25.
    • 2 Thrown away, unprofitable, useless; Ms. 3. 180.
    • 3 Disreputable, infamous.
      N. of a hell.
      Instability, ill-fame, ill-repute, dishonour.
      Brahma (svadhāmapratiṣṭhitaṃ brahma). [0121-c]
