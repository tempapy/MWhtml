• keśin m. [keśa-ini]
      1 A lion.
      2 N. of a Rākṣasa slain by Kṛshṇa.
      3 N. of another Rākṣasa who carried Devasenā and who was slain by Indra.
      4 An epithet of Kṛṣṇa.
      5 One having fine hair.
    Comp.
    • --nipūdanaḥ, --mathanaḥ epithets of Kṛṣṇa; Bg. 18. 1. [0424-a]
