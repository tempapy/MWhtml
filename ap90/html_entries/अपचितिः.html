• apacitiḥ f. (P. VII. 2. 30 Vārt.)
      1 Loss, decay, decline, destruction.
      2 Expense.
      3 Atonement, amends, compensation, expiation of sin.
      4 Exclusion.
      5 Punishing.
      6 N. of a daughter of Marīci.
      7 Honouring, worshipping, showing reverence, worship; vihitāpacitirmahībhṛtā Śi. 16. 9 (where it also means loss, destruction hāni), 1. 17; 15. 34; K. 324; Ki. 6. 46; 11. 9. [0105-b]
