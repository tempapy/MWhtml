• tairthika a.
      1 Sacred, holy.
      2 Coming from a sacred place.
      3 Frequenting sacred places or shrines.
    • --kaḥ
      1 An ascetic.
      2 One who propounds a new religious or philosophical doctrine.
    • --kaṃ Holy water (such as that brought from a sacred bathing-place). [0546-c]
