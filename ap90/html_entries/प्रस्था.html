• prasthā {c1c} A. 1 To set out, depart; pārasīkāṃstato jetuṃ pratasthe sthalavartmanā R. 4. 60; Ku. 3. 22. --2 To advance, march towards. --3 To walk, move; R. 1. 89. --4 To stand firmly. --5 To be established. --6 To approach, come near. --Caus. 1 To cause to retire. --2 To send away, dismiss, despatch; tau daṃpatī svāṃ prati rājadhānīṃ prasthāpayāmāsa vaśī vaśiṣṭhaḥ R. 2. 70. --3 To drive away, banish, expel; Ku. 6. 7. --4 To urge forwards, push on.
