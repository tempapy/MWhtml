cākrika a. (kī f.) [cakreṇa carati ṭhak]
      1 See cākra above.
      2 Relating to a company or circle.
      --kaḥ
      1 A potter.
      2 An oil-maker; Y. 1. 165 (= tailika according to Mit.; śākaṭika or cartman according to others).
      3 A proclaimer.
      4 A bard, chorister.
      5 A coachman, driver. [0488-c]
