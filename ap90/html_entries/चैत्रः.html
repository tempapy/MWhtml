• caitraḥ [ci ṣṭraṇ; citrameva svārthe aṇ; citrāyāṃ bhavaḥ aṇ vā]
      1 N. of a lunar month in which the full moon stands in the constellation Citrā (corresponding to March-April).
      2 A Buddhist mendicant.
      3 One of the seven ranges of mountains dividing the continent into Varṣas.
    • --traṃ A temple, monument for the dead.
    Comp.
    • --āvaliḥ f. the full-moon-day of Chaitra.
    • --sakhaḥ an epithet of the god of love. [0498-c]
