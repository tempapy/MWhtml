• sārvabhauma a. (mī f.) Relating to, consisting of, the whole earth, universal. --maḥ 1 An emperor, a universal monarch; nājñābhaṃgaṃ sahaṃte nṛvara nṛpatayastvādṛśāḥ sārvabhaumāḥ Mu. 3. 22. --2 N. of the elephant presiding over the north, the quarter of Kubera. [1120-a]
