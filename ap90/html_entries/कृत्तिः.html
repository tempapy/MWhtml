• kṛttiḥ f. [kṛta-ktin] 1 Skin, hide (in general); Mu. 3. 20. --2 Especially, the hide of an antelope on which a religious student sits. --3 The bark of the birch-tree used for writing upon &c. --4 The birch-tree. --5 One of the lunar mansions, Pleiades. --6 A house. --Comp. --vāsaḥ, --vāsasa m. an epithet of Śiva; sa kṛttivāsāstapase yatātmā Ku. 1. 54; M. 1. 1.
