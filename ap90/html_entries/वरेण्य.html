• vareṇya a. [vṛ-enya] 1 To be wished for, desirable, eligible; anena cedicchasi gṛhyamāṇaṃ pāṇiṃ vareṇyena R. 6. 24. --2 (Hence) Best, most excellent, pre-eminent, most worthy or distinguished, chief; vedhā vidhāya punaruktamiveṃdubiṃbaṃ durīkaroti na kathaṃ vidūṣāṃ vareṇyaḥ Bv. 2. 158; tatsaviturvareṇyaṃ bhargo devasya dhīmahī Rv. 3. 62. 10; R. 6. 84, Bk. 1. 4; Ku. 7. 90. --ṇyaṃ Saffron.
