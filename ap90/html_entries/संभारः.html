saṃbhāraḥ

      3 An ingredient, a constituent part.
      4 Multitude, heap, quantity, assemblage; as in śastrāstrasaṃbhāra.
      5 Fulness.
      6 Wealth, affluence.
      7 Maintenance, support. [1106-a]
