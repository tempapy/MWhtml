apārtha [apa-artha], apārthaka a.
      1 Useless, unprofitable, worthless; sarvametadapārthaṃ te kṣipraṃ tau saṃprasādaya Mb.
      2 Meaningless, unmeaning, senseless; apārthaṃ bahu bhāṣate Suśr. --rthaṃ Senseless or incoherent talk or argument (regarded as one of the faults of composition in rhetoric); yogyatāsattyākāṃkṣāśūnyaṃ vākyaṃ Gautama; paurvāparyāyogādapratibaṃdhārthamapārthakaṃ; cf. also Kāv. 3. 128; samudāyārthaśūnyaṃ yattadapārthamitīṣyate .
    Comp.
      --karaṇaṃ advancing a false plea in a law-suit. [0116-b]
