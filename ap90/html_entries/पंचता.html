paṃcatā, --tvaṃ
      1 Five-fold state.
      2 A collection of five.
      3 The five elements taken collectively.
      4 Death, dissolution; paṃcatāṃ --tvaṃ gam, --yā &c. means ‘to be resolved into the five elements of which the body consists’, ‘to die or perish’; paṃcatāṃ, tvaṃ nī ‘to kill or destroy’; paṃcabhirnirmite dehe paṃcatvaṃ ca punargate . svāṃ svāṃ yonimanuprāpte tatra kā paridevanā ..; Ratn. 3. 3.
