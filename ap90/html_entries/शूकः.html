• śūkaḥ, --kaṃ
      1 The awn of barley &c., beard.
      2 A bristle; vṛtaṃ ca khalu śūkaiḥ Bv. 1. 24.
      3 Point, tip, sharp end.
      4 Tenderness, compassion.
      5 A kind of poisonous insect.
      6 The bristle or sharp hair of insects.
    • --kā A cowach.
    Comp.
    • --kīṭaḥ, --kīṭakaḥ a kind of insect or worm covered with bristles.
    • --dhānyaṃ any awned grain, (as barely).
    • --piṃḍiḥ, --ḍī, --śiṃbā, --śiṃbikā, --śiṃbī cowach (kapikacchu). [1052-a]
