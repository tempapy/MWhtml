• mūlaka a.
      1 (At the end of comp.) Rooted in, springing from, founded or based on; bhrāṃtimūlaka ‘based on error.
      2 Born under the constellation Mūla.
    • --kaḥ, --kaṃ
      1 A radish.
      2 An esculent root.
      3 A sort of Yam.
    • --kaḥ A kind of poison. Comp. --paṇaḥ a handful of radishes &c. (for sale).
    • --potikā a radish. [0875-a]
