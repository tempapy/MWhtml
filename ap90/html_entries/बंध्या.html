baṃdhyā
      1 A barren woman; na hi baṃdhyā vijānāti gurvī prasavavedanāṃ Subhāṣ.
      2 A barren cow.
      3 A kind of perfume (bāla).
    Comp.
      --tanayaḥ, --putraḥ sṛtaḥ, or duhitṛ, --sutā &c. the son or daughter of a barren woman; i. e. a wild impossibility, anything that does not and cannot exist; eṣa baṃdhyāsuto yāti khapuṣpakṛtaśekharaḥ; see khapuṣpa. [0788-c]
