• aṇiḥ m. --ṇī [aṇati śabdāyate aṇ-in] 1 The point of a needle. --2 A linchpin, the pin or bolt at the end of a pole of carriage. --3 A limit. --4 The corner of a house (used for killing animals &c.). --Comp. --māṃḍavyaḥ [ aṇyā cihnito māṃḍavyaḥ] N. of a sage said to have been impaled on an aṇī or linch-pin.
