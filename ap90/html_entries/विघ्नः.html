• vighnaḥ [vihan-ka] (rarely n.)
      1 An obstacle, interruption, impediment, a hindrance; kuto dharmakriyāvighnaḥ satāṃ rakṣitari tvayi Ś. 5. 14; mūrto vighnastapasa iva naḥ Ś. 1. 33; huṃkāreṇeva dhanuṣaḥ sa hi vighnānapohati Ś. 3. 1; Ku. 3. 40.
      2 Difficulty, trouble. Comp. --īśaḥ, --īśānaḥ, --īśvaraḥ epithets of Gaṇeśa. °vāhanaṃ a rat.
    • --kara, --kartṛ, --kārin a. opposing, obstructing.
    • --dhvaṃsaḥ, --vighātaḥ removal of obstacles.
    • --nāyakaḥ, --nāśakaḥ, --nāśanaḥ epithets of Gaṇeśa.
    • --pratikriyā removal of impediments; R. 15. 4. --rājaḥ --vināyakaḥ, --hārin m. epithets of Gaṇeśa.
    • --siddhiḥ f. removal of obstacles. [0969-b]
