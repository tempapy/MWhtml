• jāmitraṃ The seventh zodiacal sign from the natal sign (lagna); tithau ca jāmitraguṇānvitāyāṃ Ku. 7. 1. ( jāmitraṃ lagnātsaptamaṃ sthānaṃ Malli.) Note--Some derive the word from jāyā, because in astrology, the jāmitra sign indicates the future good-luck of one's wife (jāyāmitraṃ?); but the word is obviously connected with the Greek diametron.
