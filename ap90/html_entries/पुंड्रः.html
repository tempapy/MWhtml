• puṃḍraḥ [puṃḍ-bhedane raka] 1 A kind of sugar cane (red-variety). --2 A lotus in general. --3 A white lotus. --4 A mark or line (on the forehead) made with sandal &c., sectarial mark. --5 A worm. --6 The Atimukta creeper. --ḍrāḥ pl. N of a country and its inhabitants. --Comp. --keliḥ an elephant. [0707-a]
