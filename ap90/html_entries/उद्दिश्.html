• uddiś 6 U.
      1 To point out, signify, declare, denote, mention, tell; prathamoddiṣṭamāspadaṃ Ku. 6. 35; Ms. 8. 52, 3. 182; Me. 30; yathoddiṣṭavyāpārā Ś. 3; aneḍamūka uddiṣṭaḥ śaṭhe Med. denotes or signifies.
      2 To enunciate, prophesy; tvaṃ sādhubhiruddiṣṭaḥ Ś. 5.
      3 To refer or allude to, have reference to; smaramuddiśya Ku. 4. 38; Ś. 6, see uddiśya below.
      4 To mean, intend, aim at, direct towards, destine for, assign to, dedicate to; K. 40; uddiṣṭāmupanihitāṃ bhajasva pūjāṃ Māl. 5. 25; phalamuddiśya Bg. 17. 21.
      5 To explain, teach, advise; satāṃ kenoddiṣṭaṃ viṣamamasidhārāvratamidaṃ Bh. 2. 28. [0307-b]
