• pakṣman n. [pakṣa-mānin] 1 An eyelash; saṃlilagurubhiḥ pakṣmabhiḥ Me. 90, 47; R. 2. 19, 11. 36. --2 The filament of a flower. --3 The point of a thread, a thin thread. --4 A wing. --5 The leaf of a flower. --Comp. --kopaḥ, --prakopaḥ irritation produced in the eye by the lashes turning inwards.
