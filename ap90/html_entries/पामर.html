• pāmara a. (rā --rī f.) [pāman-ra]
      1 Diseased with scab, scabby.
      2 Vile, wicked.
      3 Low, vulgar, base.
      4 Foolish, stupid.
      5 Poor, helpless; Ud. D. 5. --raḥ
      1 A fool, an idiot; valgaṃti cetpāmarāḥ Bv. 1. 72.
      2 A wicked or low man.
      3 One engaged in the most degrading occupation. [0695-a]
