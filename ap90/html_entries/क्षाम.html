• kṣāma a. [kṣai-kartari kta]
      1 Scorched, singed.
      2 Diminished, thin, wasted; emaciated, lean; kṣāmakṣāmakapolamānanaṃ Ś. 3. 10; madhye kṣāmā Me. 82; kṣāmacchāyaṃ bhavanamadhunā madviyogena nūnaṃ 80, 89.
      3 Slight, little, small.
      4 Weak, infirm.
    • --maḥ An epithet of Viṣṇu.
    • --mā The earth.
    • --maṃ Destruction.
    Comp.
    • --āsyaṃ unwholesome diet. [0436-a]
