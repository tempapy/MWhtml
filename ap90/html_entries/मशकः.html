maśakaḥ [maś-vun]
      1 A mosquito, gnat; sarvaṃ khalasya caritaṃ maśakaḥ karoti H. 1. 81; Ms. 1. 85.
      2 A particular disease of the skin.
      3 A leather water-bag.
      4 N. of a district in Śākadvīpa inhabited by Kṣatriyas.
    Comp.
      --kuṭiḥ --ṭī f., --varaṇaṃ a whisk for scaring away mosquitos.
      --harī a mosquito-curtain. [0849-a]
