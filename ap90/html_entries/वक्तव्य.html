• vaktavya pot. p. [vac-tavya] 1 Fit to be said, told, spoken or declared; tattarhi vaktavyaṃ na vaktavyaṃ (frequently occurring in Mbh.). --2 To be spoken about. --3 Reprehensible, blameable, censurable. --4 Low, vile, base. --5 Accountable, responsible. --6 Dependent. --vyaṃ 1 Speaking, speech. --2 A precept, rule, dictum. --3 Blame, censure, reproach.
