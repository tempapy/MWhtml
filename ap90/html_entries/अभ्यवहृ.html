• abhyavahṛ {c1c} P. 1 To throw, fling, cast. --2 To collect, draw in, procure, obtain. --3 To use as food or drink, eat; saktūn piba dhānāḥ khādetyabhyavaharati P. III. 4. 5 Sk. --Caus. 1 To cause to throw down (in water). --2 To cause to take or eat (as food), feed (one with something); śaknoṣi kimanena śāliprasthena saṃpannamannamasmānabhyavahārayituṃ Dk. 131, 72, 132; to take or eat (oneself). --3 To lay or put on (snares &c.). --4 To attack; get one to oppose another.
