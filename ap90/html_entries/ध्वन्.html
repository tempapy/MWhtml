• dhvan {c1c} P. (dhvanati, dhvanita) To sound, produce or utter sounds, buzz, hum, echo, reverberate, thunder, roar; bibhidyamānā iva dadhvanurdiśaḥ Ki. 14. 46; ayaṃ dhīraṃ dhīraṃ dhvanati navanīlo jaladharaḥ Bv. 1. 60; kapirdadhvāna meghavat Bk. 9. 5; 14. 3; dhvanati madhupasamūhe śravaṇamapidadhāti Gīt. 5. --Caus. (dhvanayati) To cause to sound, ring (as a bell); but dhvānayati ‘to cause to articulate indistinctly’. [0603-b]
