• kuruḥ (pl.) 1 N. of a country situated in the north of India about the site of the modern Delhi; śriyaḥ kurūṇāmadhipasya pālanīṃ Ki. 1. 1; cirāya tasmin kuravaścakāsate 1. 17. --2 The kings of this country. --ruḥ 1 A priest. --2 Boiled rice. --Comp. --kṣetraṃ N. of an extensive plain near Delhi, the scene of the great war between the Kauravas and Pāṇḍavas; dharmakṣetre kurukṣetre samavetā yuyutsavaḥ Bg. 1. 1; Ms. 2. 19. --kṣetriyogaḥ a solar day in the course of which three lunar days, three asterisms and three yogas occur. --cillaḥ a crab. --jāṃgalaṃ = kurukṣetra q. v. --rāj m., --rājaḥ an epithet of Duryodhana. --vistaḥ a weight of gold equal to about 700 Troy grains. --vṛddhaḥ an epithet of Bhīṣma.
