• abhijit a. [abhi-ji-kvip] 1 Victorious, conquering completely. --2 Helping in conquering completely. --3 Born under the constellation abhijit P. IV. 3. 36, see abhijita --m. 1 N. of Viṣṇu. --2 N. of a sacrifice, part of the great sacrifice called gavāmayana; Ms. 11. 75, also used for atirātra q. v. --3 N. of a star; N. of one of the lunar mansions. --n. 1 The 8th Muhūrta of the day, midday (fit for a Śrāddha ceremony). --2 N. of a lagna favourable to setting out. --Comp. --muhūrtaḥ The 8th Muhūrta or period comprising 24 minutes before and 24 minutes after noon.
