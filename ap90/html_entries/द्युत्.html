      1 To illuminate, irradiate; Bk. 8. 46; Ku. 6. 4.
      2 To make clear, explain, elucidate.
      3 To express, mean. --WITH abhi (Caus.) to illuminate; R. 6. 36. --vi to shine, be bright; vyadyotiṣṭa sabhāvedyāmasau naraśikhitrayī Śi. 2. 3; 1. 20. [0583-b]
