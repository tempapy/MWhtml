• samudayaḥ
      1 Ascent, rising up(of the sun).
      2 Rise (in general).
      3 A collection, multitude, number, heap; sāmarthyānāmiva samudayaḥ saṃcayo vā guṇānāṃ U. 6. 9.
      4 Combination.
      5 The whole.
      6 Revenue.
      7 Effort, exertion.
      8 War, battle.
      9 Day.
      10 The rear of an army.
    • --yaṃ
      1 The rising of a planet &c.
      2 An auspicious moment (lagna). [1100-b]
