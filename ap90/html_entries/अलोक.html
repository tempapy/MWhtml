aloka a.
    • 1 Not having space (Ved.).
    • 2 That which cannot be seen, as in lokāloka ivācalaḥ R. 1. 68 (na lokyata ityalokaḥ Malli.); see lokāloka also.
    • 3 Having no people.
    • 4 One who does not go to any other world after death (not having performed meritorious deeds).
    • 1 Not the world.
    • 2 End or destruction of the world; absence of people; rakṣa sarvānimāllo̐kān nālokaṃ kartumarhasiṃ Rām.
    • 3 The immaterial or spiritual world.
    • 4 The nether world (pātāla).
    • 5 A Ṛtvij or any such priest.
    • 6 One who is not a seer or observer.
      A kind of bird.
      Comp. a. extraordinary, uncommon; °guṇastanūjaḥ Māl. 1. 10. [0174-a]
