• aloka a. 1 Not having space (Ved.). --2 That which cannot be seen, as in lokāloka ivācalaḥ R. 1. 68 (na lokyata ityalokaḥ Malli.); see lokāloka also. --3 Having no people. --4 One who does not go to any other world after death (not having performed meritorious deeds). --kaḥ, --kaṃ 1 Not the world. --2 End or destruction of the world; absence of people; rakṣa sarvānimāllo̐kān nālokaṃ kartumarhasiṃ Rām. --3 The immaterial or spiritual world. --4 The nether world (pātāla). --5 A Ṛtvij or any such priest. --6 One who is not a seer or observer. --kā A kind of bird. --Comp. --sāmānya a. extraordinary, uncommon; °guṇastanūjaḥ Māl. 1. 10.
