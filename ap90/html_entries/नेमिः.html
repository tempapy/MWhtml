• nemiḥ --mī f.
      1 The circumference, ring or felly of a wheel; upoḍhaśabdā na rathāṃganemayaḥ Ś. 7. 10; cakranemikrameṇa Me. 109; R. 1. 17, 39.
      2 Edge, rim.
      3 A windlass.
      4 A circumference (in general); udadhinemi R. 9. 10.
      5 A thunderbolt.
      6 The earth.
    • --miḥ The tree tiniśa.
    Comp.
    • --vṛtti a. following the course of, acting like, the outer rims of the wheel; R. 1. 17. [0648-c]
