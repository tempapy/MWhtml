• mālya a. [mālāyai hitaṃ yat] Proper for or relating to a garland. --lyaṃ 1 A garland, wreath; mālyena tāṃ nirvacanaṃ jaghāna Ku. 7. 19; Ki. 1. 21. --2 A flower; Bg. 11. 11; Ms. 4. 72. --3 A chaplet or garland worn on the head. --Comp. --āpaṇaḥ a flowermarket. --jīvakaḥ a florist, garlandmaker. --puṣpaḥ a kind of hemp. --vṛttiḥ a florist.
