akasmāt ind. [na kasmāt kiṃcitkāraṇādhīnatvaṃ yatra aluk sa.]
      1 Accidentally, suddenly, unexpectedly, all of a sudden; akasmādāgaṃtunā saha viśvāso na yuktaḥ H. 1 coming by chance, an accidental visitor.
      2 Without cause or ground, causelessly, in vain; nākasmāt śāṃḍilī mātā vikrīṇāti tilaistilān Pt. 2. 65; °deva bhartṛdveṣyatāṃ gatāsi Dk. 135; nākasmādapriyaṃ vadet Y. 1. 132; atha nu ripurakasmād dveṣṭi naḥ putrabhāṃḍe Mv. 2. 44; kathaṃ tvāṃ tyajedakasmātpatirāryavṛttaḥ R. 14. 55, 73.
