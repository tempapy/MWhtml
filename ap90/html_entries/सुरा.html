surā
      1 A spirituous liquor, wine; surā vai malamannānāṃ Ms. 11. 94; gauḍī paiṣṭhī ca mādhvī ca vijñeyā trividhā surā 95.
      2 Water.
      3 A drinking vessel.
      4 A snake.
    Comp.
      --ākaraḥ a distillery.
      --ājīvaḥ, --ājīvin m. a distiller.
      --ālayaḥ a tavern, dram-shop. --āsavaḥ spirituous liquor.
      --udaḥ the sea of spirituous liquor.
      --karaḥ the cocoa-nut tree.
      --grahaḥ a vessel for holding liquor.
      --dhvajaḥ a flag or sign hung outside a tavern.
      --pa a. {1} a drinker of spirituous liquor. {2} pleasant, agreeable. {3} wise, sage.
      --pāṇaṃ, --pānaṃ the drinking of wine or liquor.
      --pātraṃ, --bhāṃḍaṃ a wineglass or cup.
      --bhāgaḥ yeast.
      --maṃḍaḥ the froth or scum of spirituous liquor during fermentation. --saṃdhānaṃ distillation of spirituous liquor.
      --suḥ {1} a drunkard. {2} a heretic. [1132-b]
