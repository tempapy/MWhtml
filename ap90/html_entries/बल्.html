• bal
      I. 1 P. (balati)
      1 To breathe or live.
      2 To hoard grain.
      II. 1 U. (balati-te)
      1 To give.
      2 To hurt, injure, kill.
      3 To speak.
      4 To see, mark.
      III. 10 U. (balayati-te) To live.
      IV. 10 A. (bālayate) To describe. --Caus. (bālayati-te) To nourish, support. [0789-b]
