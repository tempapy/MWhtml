aṇiman m. [aṇu-imanic], aṇutā-tvaṃ
    • 1 Minuteness, smallness, thinness, leanness, fineness.
    • 2 Atomic nature.
    • 3 The superhuman power of becoming as small as an atom, one of the 8 powers or siddhis of Śiva; (aṇimā laghimā prāptiḥ prākāmyaṃ mahimā tathā . īśitvaṃ ca vaśitvaṃ ca tathā kāmāvasāyitā) [0028-b]
