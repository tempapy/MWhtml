anibhṛta a.
      1 Not private or reserved, public, open, not hidden.
      2 Immodest, bold.
      3 Unsteady, not firm, tremulous; °kareṣvākṣipatsu priyeṣu Me. 68; °velāvīcibāhuḥ Ki. 3. 60, 13. 66; asau saṃdhyāśaṃkhadhvaniranibhṛtaḥ khe vicarati Māl. 2. 12 not hidden, loud; Śi. 10. 66. See nibhṛta also. [0064-b]
