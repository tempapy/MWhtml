nāman n. [mnāyate abhyaṃsyate namyate arbhidhīyate artho'nena vā]
      1 A name, appellation, personal name (opp. gotra); kiṃ nu nāmaitadasyāḥ Mu. 1. 1; nāma grah ‘to address or call upon by name;’ nāmagrāhamarodītsā Bk. 5. 5; nāma kṛ or dā, nāmnā or nāmataḥ kṛ ‘to give a name, call, name’; cakāra nāmnā raghumātmasaṃbhavaṃ R. 3. 21, 5. 36; tau kuśalavau cakāra kila nāmataḥ 15. 32; caṃdrāpīḍa iti nāma cakre K. 74; mātaraṃ nāmataḥ pṛccheyaṃ Ś. 7.
      2 The mere name; saṃtaptāyasi samsthitasya payaso nāmāpi na jñāyate Bh. 2. 67 ‘not even the name, i. e. no trace or mark is seen’ &c. Pt. 1. 250.
      3 (In gram.) A noun, substantive (opp. ākhyāta); tannāma yenābhidadhāti sattvaṃ; sattvapradhānāni nāmāni Nir.
      4 A word, name, synonymous word; iti vṛkṣanāmāni.
      5 Substance (opp. guṇa).
      6 Water.
      7 Ved. Mark, sign, token.
      8 Form, mode, manner.
