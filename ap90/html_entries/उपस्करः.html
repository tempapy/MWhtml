• upaskaraḥ 1 Any article which serves to make anything complete, an ingredient. --2 (Hence) Condiment or seasoning for food (as mustard, pepper &c.). --3 Furniture, appurtenance, apparatus, instrument (upakaraṇa); upaskaro rathasyāsīt Mb.; Śi. 18. 72. --4 Any article or implement of household use (such as a broomstick); Y. 1. 83; 2. 193; Ms. 3. 68, 12. 66, 5. 150. --5 An ornament. --6 Censure, blame.
