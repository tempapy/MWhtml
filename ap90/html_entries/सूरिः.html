• sūriḥ [sū ktrin]
      1 The sun.
      2 A learned or wise man, a sage; athavā kṛtavāgdvāre vaṃśe'sminpūrvasūribhiḥ R. 1. 4; Śi. 14. 21.
      3 A priest.
      4 A worshipper.
      5 A title of respect given to Jaina teachers; e. g. mallināthasūri.
      6 N. of Kṛṣṇa. [1135-c]
