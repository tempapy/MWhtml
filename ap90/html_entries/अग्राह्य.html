• agrāhya a. Not acceptable, that which ought not to be taken or accepted as a gift, present &c. °hyaṃ śivanirmālyaṃ patraṃ puṣpaṃ phalaṃ jalaṃ; not to be perceived, admitted or trusted; not to be considered or taken into account. --hyā N. of the clay or mṛttikā which ought not to be taken for purposes of purification.
