pātana a. [pat-ṇic lyu lyuṭ vā] Felling. cutting down.
      --naṃ
      1 Causing to fall down, bringing or throwing down, knocking down.
      2 Throwing, casting.
      3 Humbling, lowering.
      4 Removing. N. B. pātanaṃ may have different meanings according to the noun with which it is used; e. g. daḍasya pātanaṃ ‘causing the rod to fall’, i. e. chastising; garbhasya pātanaṃ ‘causing the fœtus to fall’, causing an abortion. [0692-b]
