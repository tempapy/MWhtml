valkalaḥ --laṃ [val-kalan kasya netvaṃ]
      1 The bark of a tree.
      2 A garment made of bark, bark-garment; iyamadhikamanojñā valkalenāpi tanvī Ś. 1. 20, 19. R. 12. 8; Ku. 5. 8; haimavalkalāḥ 6. 6 ‘wearing golden bark-dresses’; (cf. cīraparigrahāḥ in Ku. 6. 93).
    Comp.
      --saṃvīta a. clad in bark. [0951-b]
