• mukulaḥ --laṃ
      1 A bud; āvirbhūtaprathamamukulāḥ kaṃdalīścānukacchaṃ Me. 21; R. 9. 31; 15. 99.
      2 Anything like a bud; ālakṣyadaṃtamukulān (tanayān) Ś. 7. 17.
      3 The body.
      4 The soul or spirit.
      5 A bud-like junction of the fingers. (mukulīkṛ means ‘to close in the form of a bud’; Ku. 5. 63). [0867-c]
