• pradoṣa a. Bad, corrupt.
    • --ṣaḥ
      1 (a) A fault, defect, sin, offence. (b) Transgression, violation.
      2 Disordered condition, such as mutiny, rebellion.
      3 Evening, nightfall, the first part of the night; tamaḥsvabhāvāste'pyanye pradoṣamanuyāyinaḥ Śi. 2. 98 (where pradoṣa primarily means ‘corrupt’ or ‘bad’); vrajasuṃdarījanamanastoṣapradoṣaḥ Gīt. 5; Ku. 5. 44; R. 1. 93; Rs. 1. 12.
    Comp.
    • --āgamaḥ nightfall.
    • --kālaḥ --samayaḥ --velā evening-time, nightfall.
    • --timiraṃ evening darkness, the dusk of early night; kāmaṃ pradoṣatimireṇa na dṛśyase tvaṃ Mk. 1. 35. --ramaṇīya a. delightful in the evening. [0749-a]
