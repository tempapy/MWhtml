• id ind. 1 A particle of affirmation, even, just, only; especially in strengthening a statement; arthajña itsakalaṃ bhadramaśnute Nir. --2 It is often added to words expressing excess or exclusion; viśva it, eka it &c. --3 At the beginning of sentences it often adds emphasis to pronouns, prepositions &c. (Its place is taken by eva in classical Sanskrit). [0276-a]
