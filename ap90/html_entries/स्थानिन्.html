• sthānin a. [sthānamasyāsti rakṣyatvena ini] 1 Having a place. --2 Having fixedness, permanent. --3 Having a substitute. --m. 1 The original form or primitive element, that for which anything else is substituted; sthānivadādeśo'nalvidhau P. I. 1. 59. --2 The form so substituted for anything. --3 That which has a place, or is actually expressed. [1148-c]
