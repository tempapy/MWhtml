• samāhāraḥ 1 A collection, an aggregate, assemblage; Māl. 9. --2 Composition of words. --3 Conjunction of words or sentences. --4 A subdivision of Dvandva and Dvigu compounds, expressing an aggregate. --5 Abridgment, contraction, conciseness. --6 Combination of two letters of the alphabet into a syllable (= pratyāhāra q. v.). [1098-c]
