āṣāḍhaḥ [āṣāḍhīpūrṇimā asminmāse aṇ]
    • 1 N. of a Hindu month (corresponding to June and July); āṣāḍhasya prathamadivase Me. 2; śete viṣṇuḥ sadāṣāḍhe kārtike pratibodhyate V. P.
    • 2 A staff of the Palāśa wood carried by an ascetic; athājināṣāḍhadharaḥ pragalbhavāk Ku. 5. 30.
    • 3 The Malaya mountain.
      The 20th and the 21st lunar mansions, usually called pūrvāṣāḍhā and uttarāṣāḍhā.
      The day of full moon in the month of Āṣāḍha.
      Comp. a. produced in the month of Āṣāḍha. (--vaḥ, --bhūḥ) the planet Mars. [0267-a]
