• ḍāmara a. 1 Terrific, dreadful, awful; paryāptaṃ mayi ramaṇīyaḍāmaratvaṃ saṃdhatte gaganatalaprayāṇavegaḥ Māl. 5. 3. --2 Riotous, tumultuous. --3 Resembling, having the appearance (i. e. lovely, beautiful); ratigalite lalite kusumāni śikhaṃḍakaḍāmare (cikure) Gīt. 12. --raḥ 1 An uproar, rout, affray, riot. --2 The bustle and confusion of festivity or strife. --3 Any surprising sight. --4 N. of a mixed caste.
