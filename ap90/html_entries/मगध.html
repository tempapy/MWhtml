magadha The country of the Magadhas or south Behar. Its old capital was girivraja (or rājagṛha) which consisted of five hills vipulagiri, ratnāgiri, udayagiri, śoṇagiri, and vaibhāra- (or vyāhāra-) giri. Its next capital was Pātaliputra q. v. Magadha was also called kīkaṭa in later literature. [1195-a]
