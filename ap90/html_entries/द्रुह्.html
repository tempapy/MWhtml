druh 4 P. (druhyati, drugdha)
      1 To bear malice or hatred.
      2 To seek to hurt or injure, plot maliciously or revengefully, meditate mischief; (generally with the dat. of the object of hatred); yānveti māṃ druhyati mahyameva sātretyupālaṃbhi tayālivargaḥ N. 3. 7; Bk. 4. 39. [0585-c]
      druh a. (At the end of comp.) (Nom. sing. dhruk-g, dhruṭ-ḍ) Injuring, hurting, plotting or acting as an enemy against; Śi. 2. 35; Ms. 5. 90. --f. Injury, damage. [0585-c]
