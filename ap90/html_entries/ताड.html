tāḍa a. [taḍ bhāve ac] Beating, striking.
      --ḍaḥ
      1 A blow, knock, thumb, whipping, chastisement.
      2 Noise, sound.
      3 A sheaf.
      4 A mountain.
    Comp.
      --gha a. beating with a whip or strokes of any kind.
      --ghaḥ, --ghātaḥ an artificer who beats or hammers, a smith. [0526-a]
      tāḍa, tāḍanaṃ &c. see under taḍ. [0534-b]
