• mṛ 6 A. (but P. in the Perfect, the two Futures and the Conditional) (mriyate, mamāra, amṛta, mariṣyati, martuṃ, mṛta) To die, perish, decease, depart from life. --Caus. (mārayati-te) To kill, slay. --Desid. (mumūrṣati)
      1 To wish to die.
      2 To be about to die, be on the point of death. [0875-a]
