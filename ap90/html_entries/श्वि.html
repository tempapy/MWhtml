śvi 1 P. (śvayati, śiśvāya-śuśāva, aśvat-aśvayīt-aśiśviyat, śvayiṣyati, śvayituṃ, śūna)
      1 To grow, increase (fig. also), to swell (as the eye); rudato'śiśviyaccakṣurāsyaṃ hetostavāśvayīt Bk. 6. 19, 31; 14. 79, 15. 30.
      2 To thrive, prosper.
      3 To go, approach, move towards. --WITH ud {1} to swell, increase, grow; prabalaruditocchūnanetraṃ (mukhaṃ) Me. 84. {2} to be proud, be puffed up with pride. [1063-c]
