śobhana a. (nī f.) [śobhate śubhū-lyu]
      1 Shining, splendid.
      2 Handsome, beautiful, lovely.
      3 Good, auspicious, fortunate.
      4 Richly decorated.
      5 Moral, virtuous.
      --naḥ
      1 N. of Śiva.
      2 A planet.
      3 A burnt offering for the production of happy results.
      --nā
      1 Turmeric.
      2 A beautiful or virtuous woman; tadidaṃ parirakṣa śobhane bhavitavyapriyasaṃgamaṃ vapuḥ Ku. 4. 44.
      3 A sort of yellow pigment (= gorocanā q. v.).
      --naṃ
      1 Beauty, lustre, brilliance.
      2 A lotus. [1056-b]
