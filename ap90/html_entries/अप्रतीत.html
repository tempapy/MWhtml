• apratīta a.
      1 Not pleased or delighted.
      2 Unapproached; unassailable.
      3 Unopposed.
      4 (In Rhet.) Not understood or clearly intelligible (as a word), one of the defects of a word (śabdadoṣa); apratītaṃ yatkevale śāstre prasiddhaṃ K. P. 7, i. e. a word is said to be apratīta if it be used in a sense which it has in particular classes of works only (and not in general or popular use); e. g. samyagjñānamahājyotirdalitāśayatājuṣaḥ (where āśaya = vāsanā occurs in Yoga Śāstra only).
