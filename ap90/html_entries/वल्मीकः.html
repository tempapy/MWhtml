• valmīkaḥ --kaṃ [val-īka muṭ ca Uṇ. 4. 25] An ant-hill, a hillock thrown up by white ants, moles &c. dharmaṃ śanaiḥ saṃcinuyādvalmīkamiva puttikāḥ Subhāṣ.; Me. 15; Ś. 7. 11. --kaḥ 1 Swelling of certain parts of the body, elephantiasis. --2 The poet Vālmīki. --Comp. --śīrṣaṃ a kind of antimony (used as collyrium).
