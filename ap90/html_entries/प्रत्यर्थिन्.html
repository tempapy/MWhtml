• pratyarthin a. (nī f.) 1 Hostile, opposing, inimical to; nāsmi bhavatyorīśvaraniyogapratyarthī V. 2. --2 Emulating. --3 Contradicting. --m. 1 An opponent, adversary, enemy. --2 A rival, equal, match; caṃdro mukhasya pratyarthī. --3 (In law) A defendant; sa dharmasthasakhaḥ śaśvadarthipratyārthināṃ svayaṃ R. 17. 39; Ms. 8. 79; Y. 2. 6. --4 An obstacle or impediment. --Comp. --bhūta a. coming in the way, become an obstacle; Ku. 1. 59.
