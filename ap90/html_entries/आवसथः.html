• āvasathaḥ [āvas-athac Uṇ. 3. 116] 1 A dwelling, dwelling-place, residence, house, habitation; nivasannāvasathe purādbahiḥ R. 8. 14. --2 A resting place; asylum. --3 A dwelling for pupils and ascetics. --4 A village. --5 A particular religious observance. --6 A fire-sanctuary, a place where sacrificial fire is preserved.
