kuṃjaraḥ [kuṃjo hastihanuḥ so'syāsti, kuṃja-ra, P. V. 2. 107 Vārt.]
    • 1 An elephant.
    • 2 Any thing pre-eminent or excellent of its class (at the end of comp. only). Amara gives the following words used similarly: --syuruttarapade vyāghrapuṃgavarṣabhakuṃjarāḥ . siṃhaśārdūlanāgādyāḥ puṃsi śreṣṭhārthavācakāḥ ...
    • 3 The Aśvattha tree.
    • 4 The lunar asterism called hasta.
    • 5 Hair.
      A female elephant.
      Comp. the division of an army consisting of elephant-corps.
      the Aśvattha tree. --arātiḥ {1} a lion. {2} Śarabha (a fabulous animal with 8 feet).
      an elephant-catcher. [0407-a]
