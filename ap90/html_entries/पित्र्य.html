pitrya a. [pituridaṃ priyaṃ vā pitṛta āgataṃ vā yat]
      1 Paternal, patrimonial, ancestral.
      2 (a) Relating or sacred to the deceased ancestors; Ms. 2 59. (b) Obsequial.
      --tryaḥ
      1 The eldest brother.
      2 The month of Māgha.
      --tryā
      1 The constellation called Maghā.
      2 The day of full as well as new moon.
      --tryaṃ
      1 The lunar mansion called Maghā.
      2 The part of the hand between the fore-finger and the thumb (sacred to the Manes). [0703-a]
