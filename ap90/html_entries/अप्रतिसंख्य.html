• apratisaṃkhya a. Unobserved, unnoticed; °khya (khyā) nirodhaḥ unobserved nullity, annihilation of an object; one of the three topics included by the Buddhas under the category of nirūpa, the other two being pratisaṃkhyānirodha wilful destruction, as of a jar by a mallet, and ākāśa the ethereal element.
