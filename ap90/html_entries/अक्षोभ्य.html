akṣobhya a. [kṣobhyate vicālyate; kṣubh-ṇic karmaṇi yat. na. ta.] Immoveable, imperturbable; akṣobhyaḥ sa navopyāsīt R. 17. 44 was unassailable --bhyaḥ
    • 1 A particular sage (taṃtrokto dvitīyavidyopāsakaḥ taddevatāyāḥ śirasi nāgarūpeṇa sthitaḥ ṛṣibhedaḥ; akṣobhyosyā ṛṣiḥ proktaḥ --Tv.).
    • 2 N. of a Buddha.
    • 3 An immense number, said to be 100 vivara.
      Comp. [akṣobhāya hitaṃ akṣobhyaṃ] a sort of kavaca or armour referred to in Tantras. [0008-c]
