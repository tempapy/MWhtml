catuṣṭaya a. (yī f.) [catvāro'vayavā vidhā asya tayap]
      1 Four fold, consisting of four; purāṇasya kavestasya catu rmukhasamīritā . pravṛttirāsīcchabdānāṃ caritārthā catuṣṭayī .. Ku. 2. 17. --yaṃ A group or collection of four; ekaikamapyanarthāya kimu yatra catuṭayaṃ H. Pr. 11; Ku. 7. 62; māsacatuṣṭayasya bhojanaṃ H. 1.
      2 A square.
      3 The first, fourth, seventh, and tenth signs of the zodiac.
      4 The centre of a circle. [0484-a]
