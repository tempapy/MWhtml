• anehas a. [na hanyate, han-asi, dhātoḥ ehādeśaḥ nañi hana eha ca Uṇ. 4. 223] (lit.) Not killed or destroyed or obstructed; Ved. without a rival, incomparable, unattainable, inaccessible; unobstructed, not liable to be hurt or injured; śaṃbhuvaṃ maṃtraṃ devā anehasaṃ Rv. 1. 40. 6, 4; 6. 50. 3. --m. (hā-hasau &c.) Time (not being liable to be destroyed). [0087-b]
