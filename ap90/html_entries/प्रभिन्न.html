prabhinna p. p.
      1 Severed, split, cleft, divided.
      2 Broken to pieces.
      3 Cut off, detached.
      4 Budding, expanded, opened.
      5 Changed, altered
      6 Deformed, disfigured.
      7 Relaxed, loosened.
      8 Intoxicated, in rut; Ku. 5. 80.
      9 Pierced, bored.
      10 Different, distinct.
      --nnaḥ An elephant in rut.
    Comp.
