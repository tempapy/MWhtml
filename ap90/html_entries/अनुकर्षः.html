anukarṣaḥ --rṣaṇaṃ
      1 Dragging after, drawing along; attraction in general.
      2 Summoning or invoking (by means of spells).
      3 Grammatical attraction, application or attraction of a word in a preceding rule to a subsequent rule, word &c. nāyamanukarṣaṇārthaścakāraḥ Mbh. on P. II. 2. 4; see also Sk. on P. VI. 1. 127.
      4 [anukṛṣyate svasaṃbaddhena cakreṇa] The axle-tree or bottom of a carriage.
      5 Delayed performance of a duty. [0068-a]
