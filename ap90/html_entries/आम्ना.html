• āmnā {c1c} P. 1 (a) To hand down traditionally or in sacred texts; bhavatpraṇītamācāramāmanaṃti hi sādhavaḥ Ku. 6. 31; evaṃ kila sūtrakārā āmanaṃti Māl. 7. (b) Hence, to regard, consider, mention, lay down; tvāmāmanaṃti prakṛtiṃ puruṣārthapravartinīṃ Ku. 2. 13, 5. 81; M. 1. 4; Bk. 18. 5; Kām. 8. 24; U. 5; Mv. 4. 30. --2 To keep in mind, commit to memory, learn, study, repeat; āmananmaṃtramuttamaṃ Bk. 17. 30; yad brahma samyagāmnātaṃ Ku. 6. 16 learnt. --3 To celebrate, hail.
