medas n. [med-asun]
      1 Fat, marrow one of the seven dhātus of the body and supposed to lie in the abdomen; Ms. 3. 182; Y. 1. 44.
      2 Corpulence, fat of the body: medaśchadekṛśodaraṃ laghu bhavatyutthānayogyaṃ vapuḥ Ś. 2. 5.
      3 Excessive fatness, morbid corpulence.
    Comp.
      --arbudaṃ a fatty tumour.
      --kṛta m. n. flesh.
      --grathiḥ a fatty tumour.
      --jaṃ, --tejas n. a bone.
      --dharā a membrane in the abdomen containing the fat.
      --piṃḍaḥ a lump of fat.
      --vṛddhiḥ f. {1} increase of fat, corpulence. {2} enlargement of the scrotum. [0878-c]
