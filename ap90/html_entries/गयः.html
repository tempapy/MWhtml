• gayaḥ
      1 N. of the people living round Gayā and the district inhabited by them.
      2 N. of an Asura.
      3 Wealth.
      4 House, household, family.
      5 Offspring, progeny.
      6 The sky.
      7 One's own place or abode.
    • --yā N. of a city in Behar which is a place of pilgrimage. [0452-c]
