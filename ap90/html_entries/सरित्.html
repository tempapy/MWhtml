sarit f. [sṛ-iti]
      1 A river; anyasaritāṃ śatāni hi samudraṇāḥ prāpayaṃtyabdhiṃ M. 5. 19.
      2 A thread, string.

      --varā (also saritāṃvarā) N. of the Ganges.
