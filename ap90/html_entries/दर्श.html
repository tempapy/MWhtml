• darśa, darśaka, darśana &c. See under dṛś. [0559-a]
  • darśa a. [dṛś bhāve ghañ] Seeing, looking. --rśaḥ 1 Sight, view, appearance, (usually in comp.); durdarśaḥ, priyadarśaḥ &c. --2 Ocular evidence or proof. --3 The day of the new moon ( amāvāsyā); ekatrasthitacaṃdrārkadarśanād darśa ucyate. --4 The new moon. --5 The half-monthly sacrifice, a sacrificial rite performed on the day of the new moon. --Comp. --paḥ a god. --yāminī the night of the new moon. --vipad m. the moon.
