• gopya a. [gup karmaṇi yat]
      1 To be protected.
      2 To be kept secret or hidden; Pt. 1. 100.
      3 To be kept, to be taken care of.
      4 To be cherished --pyaḥ
      1 A servant, slave.
      2 A son of a female slave.
    Comp.
    • --ādhiḥ a pledge to be carefully preserved. [0463-a]
