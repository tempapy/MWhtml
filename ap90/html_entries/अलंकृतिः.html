• alaṃkṛtiḥ f.
      1 Decoration.
      2 An ornament; karṇālaṃkṛti Amaru. 13.
      3 A rhetorical ornament, a figure of speech; tadadauṣau śabdārthau saguṇāvanalaṃkṛtī punaḥ kvāpi K. P. 1; yo vidvān manyate kāvyaṃ śabdārthāvanalaṃkṛtī . asau na manyate kasmādanuṣṇamanalaṃ kṛtī ..; Chandr. 1; sālaṃkṛtiḥ śraṃvaṇakomalavarṇarājiḥ Bv. 3. 6 (where a° has senses 2. and 3.). [0173-a]
