• kṣi
      I. 1 P. (kṣayati, kṣita or kṣīṇa)
      1 To decay or waste.
      2 To rule, govern, be master of.
      II. 1 5 9 P. (kṣayati, kṣiṇoti, kṣiṇāti)
      1 To destroy, affect, ruin, corrupt; na tadyaśaḥ śastrabhṛtāṃ kṣiṇoti R. 2. 40.
      2 To diminish, cause to waste away; R. 19. 48.
      3 To kill, injure.
      4 To spend, pass (as time); kati punarahaṃ vāsarāṇi kṣayiṣye Ud. S. 83.
      III. 6 P. (kṣiyati)
      1 To abide, stay, dwell.
      2 To inhabit.
      3 To remain.
      4 To go, move, approach. --Pass. (kṣīyate) To waste, wane; decay, be diminished (fig. also) pratikṣaṇamayaṃ kāyaḥ kṣīyamāṇo na lakṣyate H. 4. 66; pratyāsannavipattimūḍhamanasāṃ prāyo matiḥ kṣīyate Pt. 2. 4; Amaru. 93; Bh. 2. 19. --Caus. (kṣayayati or kṣapayati)
      1 To destroy, remove, put an end to; mamāpi ca kṣapayatu nīlalohitaḥ punarbhavaṃ parigataśaktirātmabhūḥ Ś. 7. 35, R. 8. 47; Me. 53.
      2 To spend or pass (as time). [0436-a]
