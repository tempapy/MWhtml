• karkaśa a. [karka-śa] 1 Hard, rough (opp. komala or mṛdu); suradvipāsphālanakarkaśāṃ gulau R. 3. 55, 12. 41, 13. 73; airāvatāsphālanakarkaśena hastena pasparśa tadaṃgabhiṃdraḥ Ku. 3. 22, 1. 36; Śi. 15. 10, 16. 18, 63. --2 Harsh, cruel, merciless (words, conduct &c.). --3 Violent, strong, excessive; tasya karkaśavihārasaṃbhavaṃ R. 9. 68. --4 Hardy, strong, muscular, robust. --5 Strict, imperative, peremptory; Mv. 2. 11. --6 Desperate. --7 Ill-conducted, unchaste, unfaithful (as a woman). --8 Incomprehensible, difficult to comprehend; tarke vā bhṛśakarkaśe mama samaṃ līlāyate bhāratī P. R. --śaḥ A sword. [0381-c]
