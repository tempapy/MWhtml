mūrtiḥ f. [murcch-ktin]
      1 Anything which has definite shape and limits, material element, matter, substance.
      2 A form, visible shape, body, figure; Mu. 2. 2; R. 3. 27; 14. 54.
      3 An embodiment, incarnation, personification, manifestation; karuṇasya mūrtiḥ U. 3. 4; Pt. 2. 159.
      4 An image, idol, a statue.
      5 Beauty.
      6 Solidity, hardness.
    Comp.
      --dhara, --saṃcara a. embodied, incarnate; dharmo bā mūrtisaṃcaraḥ Mv. 1. 10; U. 6. 10. --paḥ a worshipper of an image, one who is in charge of an idol. [0874-a]
