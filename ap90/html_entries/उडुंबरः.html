• uḍuṃbaraḥ 1 N. of a tree; Ficus Glomerata (Mar. auduṃbara). --2 The threshold of a house. --3 A eunuch. --4 A part of a sacrifice. --5 A kind of leprosy with copper spots (--raṃ also). --6 A kind of worm said to be produced in the blood and to produce leprosy. --raṃ 1 The fruit of the uduṃbara tree. --2 Copper. --3 A Karṣa, a measure of two tolas. --Comp. --dalā, --parṇī the plant Croton Polyandra.
