vimala a.
      1 Pure, stainless, spotless, clean (fig. also).
      2 Clear, limpid, pellucid, transparent (as water); vimalaṃ jalaṃ.
      3 White, bright.
      --laḥ An Arhat.
      --laṃ
      1 Silver-gilt.
      2 Talc.
    Comp.
      --adriḥ the mountain Girnār in Gujerāt (famous for its inscriptions).
      --dānaṃ an offering to a deity.
      --maṇiḥ a crystal. [0986-b]
