ātatāyin a. or s. [ātatena vistīrṇena śastrādinā ayituṃ śīlamasya Tv.]
    • 1 ‘One whose bow is stretched to take another's life’, endeavouring to kill some one; a desperado; guruṃ vā bālavadhvau vā brāhmaṇaṃ vā bahuśrutaṃ . ātatāyinamāyāṃtaṃ hanyādevāvicārayan .. Ms. 8. 350-1; Bg. 1. 36.
    • 2 Any one who commits a heinous crime, such as a thief, ravisher, murderer, incendiary, a felon &c agnido garadaścaiva śastronmatto dhanāpahaḥ . kṣetradāraharaścaitān ṣaḍ vidyādātatāyinaḥ .. Śukra. °tā, --tvaṃ murdering, stealing, destroying &c. [0232-c]
