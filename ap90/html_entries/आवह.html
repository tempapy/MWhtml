• āvaha a. (As last member of comp.) Producing, leading or tending to, bringing on, kleśāvahā bharturalakṣaṇā'haṃ R. 14. 5; so duḥkha°, bhaya°, kṣaya° &c. --haḥ 1 N. of one of the seven winds or bands of air, usually assigned to the bhuvarloka or atmospheric region between the bhūrloka and svarloka. --2 One of the seven tongues of fire. [0260-c]
