• śūra a. [śūr-ac] Brave, heroic, valiant, mighty; śūnyeṣu śūrā na ke K. P. 7. --raḥ
      1 A hero, warrior, valiant man.
      2 A lion.
      3 A boar.
      4 The sun.
      5 The Śāla tree.
      6 N. of a Yādava, the grandfather of Kṛṣṇa.
      7 The Arka plant.
      8 The Citraka tree.
    Comp.
    • --kīṭaḥ a contemptible warrior; Mv. 6. 32. --mānaṃ arrogance, vaunting.
    • --mānin m. a boaster, braggart.
    • --saṃna m. pl. N. of the country about Mathurā or the inhabitants of that country; R. 6. 45. [1052-c]
