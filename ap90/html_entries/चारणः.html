• cāraṇaḥ [cārayati kīrtiṃ car-ṇic lyu]
      1 A wanderer, a pilgrim.
      2 A wandering actor or singer, a dancer, mimic, bard; Ms. 12. 44.
      3 A celestial singer, heavenly chorister; Ś. 2. 14.
      4 A reader of scriptures.
      5 A spy.
    Comp.
    • --dārāḥ female dancers, actresses. [0490-c]
