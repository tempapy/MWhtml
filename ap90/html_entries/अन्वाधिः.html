anvādhiḥ [anu paścādādhīyate, dhā-ki]
    • 1 (In civil law) {1} A bail, deposit or security delivered to a third person to be handed over ultimately to the right owner; arthamārgaṇakāryeṣu anyāsminvacanānmama . dadyāstvamiti yo dattaḥ sa ihānvādhirucyate ...
    • 2 A second deposit.
    • 3 [anugataḥ saṃtataḥ ādhiḥ] Constant anxiety, remorse, repentance, regret (after the committal of a bad act). [0101-b]
