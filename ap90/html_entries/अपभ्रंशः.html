• apabhraṃśaḥ 1 Falling down or away, a fall; atyārūḍhirbhavati mahatāmapyapabhraṃśaniṣṭhā Ś. 4 v. l. ending in a (precipitate) fall. --2 A corrupted word, corruption; ghara is an apabhraṃśa or corruption of gṛha; (hence) an incorrect word whether formed against the rules of grammar or used in a sense not strictly Sanskrit; see apaśabda. --3 A corrupt language, one of the lowest forms of the Prākṛta dialect used by cow-herds &c. (in kāvyas); (in Śāstras) any language other than Sanskrit; ābhīrādigiraḥ kāvyeṣvapabhraṃśa iti smṛtāḥ . śāstreṣu saṃskṛtādanyadapabhraṃśatayoditam .. Kāv. 1. [0108-a]
