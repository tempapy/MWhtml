• laukika a. (kī f.) [loke viditaḥ prasiddho hito vā ṭhaṇ] 1 Worldly, mundane, terrestrial, earthly. --2 General, common, usual, ordinary, vulgar; U. 1. 10. --3 Of every-day life, generally accepted, popular, customary; Ku. 7. 88. --4 Temporal, secular (opp. ārṣa or śāstrīya); Ms. 3. 282. --5 Not sacred, profane (as a word or its sense); vākyaṃ dvividhaṃ vaidikaṃ laukikaṃ ca T. S.; (see Mbh. quoted under loka 8). --6 Belonging to the world of; as in brahmalaukika. --kāḥ (pl.) 1 Ordinary men, men of the world. --2 The human race, mankind. --kaṃ Any general or worldly custom. --Comp. --jña a. knowing the ways of the world, acquainted with worldly customs; vanaukasopi saṃto laukikajñā vayaṃ Ś. 4.
