• kopaḥ [kup-bhāve ghañ]
      1 Anger, wrath, passion; kopaṃ na gacchati nitāṃtabalopi nāgaḥ Pt. 1. 123; na tvayā kopaḥ kāryaḥ do not be angry.
      2 (In medicine) Morbid irritation or disorder of the humours of the body; i. e. pittakopa, vātakopa. &c.
    Comp.
    • --ākula, --āviṣṭa a. enraged, furious.
    • --kramaḥ {1} an angry or passionate man. {2} the course of anger.
    • --dīpta, --jvalita a. inflamed with anger.
    • --padaṃ {1} cause of anger. {2} pretended anger.
    • --vaśaḥ subjection to anger.
    • --vegaḥ violence, fury of anger. [0409-c]
