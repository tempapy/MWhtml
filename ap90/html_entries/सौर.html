• saura a. (rī f.) [sūrasya idaṃ mūro devatāsya vā aṇ] 1 Relating to the sun, solar. --2 Sacred or dedicated to the sun. --3 Worshipping the sun. --4 Celestial, divine. --5 Relating to spirituous liquor. --raḥ 1 A worshipper of the sun. --2 The planet Saturn. --3 A solar month. --4 A solar day. --5 The plant called Tumburu. --6 N. of Yama, the god of death. --raṃ N. of a collection of hymns (extracted from the Ṛgveda) addressed to Sūrya. --Comp. --naktaṃ a particular religious observance. --māsaḥ a solar month (comprising thirty risings and settings of the sun). --lokaḥ the sun's sphere. [1141-c]
