upādhyāyaḥ [upetyādhīyate asmāt, upa-adhi-i-ghañ]
    • 1 A teacher or preceptor in general.
    • 2 Particularly, a spiritual teacher, religious preceptor; (by Y. 1. 35, a sub-teacher who instructs for wages only in a part of the Veda and is inferior to an ācārya; ekadeśamupādhyāyaḥ); cf. Ms. 2. 141; --ekadeśaṃ tu vedaraya vedāṃgānyapi vā punaḥ . yo'dhyāpayati vṛttyarthamupādhyāyaḥ sa ucyate .. see adhyāpaka and under ācārya also.
      A female preceptor.
    • 1 A female preceptor.
    • 2 The wife of a preceptor. [0337-b]
