      2 Tortuous, winding; krośaṃ kuṭilā nadī Sk.
      3 (fig.) Insincere, fraudulent, dishonest; a° Pt. 1. 126. --lā
      1 N. of Sarasvatī.
      2 A kind of perfume.
      --laṃ
      1 N. of a plant (tagara).
      2 Tin.
