• abhiṣyaṃ(syaṃ)daḥ 1 Oozing, flowing, trickling. --2 Weakness of, or running at, the eyes. --3 Great increase or enlargement, surplus, excess, superfluous portion; svargābhiṣyaṃdavamanaṃ kṛtvevopaniveśitaṃ (oṣadhiprasthaṃ) Ku. 6. 37 by drawing off the surplus population i. e. by emigration (abhiṣyaṃdaḥ atirekaḥ atiriktajana iti yāvat tasya vamanaṃ niḥsāraṇaṃ kṛtvā sthitamiva); cf. also R. 15. 29 Hemacandra's remarks thereon.
