ādhidaivika a. (kī f.) [ adhidevaṭhañ]
      1 Relating to avideva or tutelary deity (as a Mantra); adhiyajñaṃ brahma japedādhidaivikameva ca Ms. 6. 83.
      2 Caused by fate (as pain &c.); according to Suśruta pain is of three kinds; ādhyātmika, ādhimautika or āvidaivika. [0239-c]
