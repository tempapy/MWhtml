bhavitavya pot. p. About to take place, about to happen, likely to be, often used like bhāvya impersonally, i. e. in the neuter gender and singular number, with the instrumental of the subject and the predicative word; tvayā mama sahāyena bhavitavyaṃ Ś. 2; guruṇā kāraṇena bhavitavyaṃ Ś. 6. --vyaṃ What is destined to happen; bhavitavyaṃ bhavatyeva yadvivarmanasi sthitaṃ Subhāsh [0810-c]
