apahāraḥ
      1 Taking or carrying away, stealing, plundering, removing, killing, destroying; karṇanāsāpahāreṇa bhaginī me virūpitā Rām. by the cutting of ears and nose; nidrāpahāra, viṣa°.
      2 Concealing, dissembling; kathamātmāpahāraṃ karomi Ś. 1 how shall I dissemble myself, conceal my real name and character; see apahāraka below.
      3 Spending or using another's property.
      4 Loss, damage. [0114-c]
