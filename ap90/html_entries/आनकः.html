ānakaḥ
    • 1 [ānayati utsāhavataḥ karoti an-ṇic-ṇvul Tv.] A large military drum (beaten at one end), a double drum, a drum or tabor in general; paṇavānakagomukhāḥ sahasaivābhyahanyaṃta Bg. 1. 13.
    • 2 The thunder-cloud. Comp. --duduṃbhiḥ epithet of Vasudeva, father of Kṛṣṇa; cf. Hariv. vasudevo mahābāhuḥ pūrvamānakaduṃdubhiḥ . jajñe yasya prasūtasya duṃdubhyaḥ prāṇadandivi .. ānakānāṃ ca saṃhrādaḥ sumahānabhavaddivi. (--bhiḥ, --bhī f.) a large drum or dhol, kettle-drum (beaten at one end).
      N. of a country. [0240-c]
