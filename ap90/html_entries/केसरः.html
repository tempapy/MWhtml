• kesa(śa)raḥ, --raṃ
      1 The mane (as of a lion); na haṃtyadūre'pi gajānmṛgeśvaro vilolajihvaścalitāgrakesaraḥ Rs. 1. 14; Ś. 7. 14.
      2 The filament of a flower; nīpaṃ dṛṣṭvā haritakapiśaṃ kesarairardharūḍhaiḥ Me. 21; Ś. 6. 17; M. 2. 11; R. 4. 67; Śi. 9. 47.
      3 The Bakula tree; raktāśokaścalakisalayaḥ kesaraścātra kāṃtaḥ Me. 78; Ku. 3. 55.
      4 The Punnāga tree.
      5 The fibre (as of a mango fruit).
      6 Saffron.
      7 The hair.
    • --raṃ
      1 A flower of the Bakula tree; R. 9. 36.
      2 Gold.
      3 Sulphate of iron.
    Comp.
    • --acalaḥ an epithet of the mountain Meru. --amlaḥ the citron.
    • --varaṃ saffron. [0424-b]
