āsattiḥ f. [ā-sad-ktin]
    • 1 Meeting, junction.
    • 2 Intimate union, nearness, close contact; kimapi kimapi maṃdaṃ maṃdamāsattiyogāt U. 1. 27.
    • 3 Gain, profit, acquirement.
    • 4 (In Logic) Proximity, the absence of interruption in the apprehension of what is said, relation between two or more proximate terms and the sense conveyed by them; kāraṇaṃ sannidhānaṃ tu padasyāsattirucyate Bhāṣā P. 83; vākyaṃ syād yogyatākāṃkṣāsattiyuktaḥ padoccayaḥ S. D. 2. [0268-b]
