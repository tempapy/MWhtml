• sairaṃdhrī, sairiṃdhrī 1 A maid-servant or female attendant in the women's apartments (a woman of the mixed tribe described in sairaṃdhra 2). --2 An independent female artisan working in another person's house. --3 An epithet of Draupadī (assumed by her when she acted as servant to Sudheṣṇā, queen of Virāṭa.). [1138-c]
