• śaṃbūkaḥ
      1 A bivalve shell (śaṃbūkā also in this sense).
      2 A small conch-shell.
      3 A snail.
      4 The edge of the frontal protuberance of an elephant.
      5 N. of a Śūdra (who practised penance though forbidden to his caste and was in consequence slain by Rāma; see inter alia U. 2. and R. 15). [1034-b]
