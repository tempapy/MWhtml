locana a. (nī f.)
      1 Illuminating, brightening.
      2 Visible.
      --naṃ [ locyate'nena loc-karaṇe lyuṭ]
      1 Seeing, sight, viewing.
      2 The eye; śeṣānmāsān gamaya caturo locane mīlayitvā Me. 110.
    Comp.
      --gocaraḥ, --pathaḥ, --mārgaḥ the range of sight, sphere of vision.
      --hitā blue vitriol. [0934-b]
