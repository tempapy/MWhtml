• vaṭaḥ [vaṭ-ac]
      1 The fig-tree; ayaṃ ca citrakūṭayāyini vartmani vaṭaḥ, śyāmo nāma U. 1; R. 13. 53.
      2 A small shell or cowrie.
      3 A small ball, globule, pill.
      4 A round figure, a cipher.
      5 A kind of cake.
      6 A string, rope (n. also in this sense).
      7 Equality in shape.
    Comp.
    • --patraṃ a variety of the white basil. (--trā) a jasmine.
    • --vāsin m. a Yakṣa. [0940-a]
