• dhvaṃs {c1c} A. (dhvaṃsate, dhvasta) 1 To fall down, fall to pieces, be reduced to dust or powder; Bk. 15. 93; 14. 55. --2 To drop, sink, despond; Māl. 9. 44. --3 To perish, be ruined or decayed. --4 To be eclipsed; Mu. 3. 28. --5 To be covered with dust. --6 To scatter or sprinkle over. --7 To go. --Caus. (dhvaṃsayati) To destroy, ruin, remove. [0603-a]
