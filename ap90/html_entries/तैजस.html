• taijasa a. (sī f.) [tejaso vikāraḥ aṇ] 1 Bright, splendid, luminous; U. 2. 12. --2 Made up or consisting of light; taijasasya dhanuṣaḥ pravṛttaye R. 11. 43. --3 Metallic. --4 Passionate. --5 Vigorous, energetic. --6 Powerful, intense. --saḥ The highly refined or subtle essence (Vedānta phil.). --saṃ 1 Any metal. --2 Ghee. --3 Intensity, severity. --4 Vigour, energy, might. --Comp. --āvartanī a crucible.
