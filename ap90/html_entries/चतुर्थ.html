• caturtha a. (rthī f.) [caturṇāṃ pūraṇaḥ ḍaṭ yuk ca] The fourth. --rthaḥ The fourth letter of any class. --rthaṃ A quarter, a fourth part. --Comp. --aṃśa a. receiving a fourth part. (--śaḥ) a quarter or fourth part. --āśramaḥ the fourth stage of a Brāhmaṇa's religious life, Saṃnyāsa. --phalaṃ the second inequality or equation of a planet. --bhakta a. eating the fourth meal. --bhāj a. receiving a fourth part of every source of income from the subjects, as a king; (this is allowed only in times of financial embarrassments, the usual share being a sixth.). [0483-c]
