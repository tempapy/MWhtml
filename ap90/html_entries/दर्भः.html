• darbhaḥ [dṛbh-ghañ ac vā] A kind of sacred grass (Kuśa grass) used at sacrificial ceremonies &c. Ś. 1. 7; R. 11. 31; Ms. 2. 43, 3. 208, 4. 36.
    Comp.
    • --aṃkuraḥ a pointed blade of darbha grass; Ś. 2. 12. --anūpaḥ a watery place full of darbha grass.
    • --āhvayaḥ the Munja grass.
    • --patraṃ a kind of grass (kāśa).
    • --saṃstaraḥ a bed of Kuśa grass. [0558-c]
