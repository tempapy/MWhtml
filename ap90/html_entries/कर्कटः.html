• karkaṭaḥ
      1 A crab.
      2 Cancer, the fourth sign of the zodiac.
      3 A kind of crane.
      4 The fibrous root of a lotus.
      5 A thorn.
      6 The curved end of the beam of a balance.
      7 A kind of coitus (ratibaṃdha).
      8 The radius of a circle.
      9 Compass, circuit.
      10 A kind of pin or wedge.
