bhīta p. p. [bhī-kta]
      1 Frightened, terrified, alarmed, afraid of (with abl.); na bhīto maraṇādasmi Mk. 10. 27.
      2 Fearful, timid.
      3 Placed in danger, imperiled.
      --taṃ Fear, dread.
      --taṃ ind. Timidly.
    Comp.
      --bhīta a. exceedingly afraid. [0818-a]
