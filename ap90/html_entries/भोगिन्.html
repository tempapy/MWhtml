• bhogin a. [bhoga-ini] 1 Eating. --2 Enjoying. --3 Suffering, experiencing, enduring. --4 Using, possessing (at the end of comp. in these four senses.) --5 Having curves. --6 Having hoods. --7 Devoted to enjoyment, indulging in sensual pleassures; Pt. 1. 65 (where it has sense 6 also). --8 Rich, opulent. --m. 1 A snake; gajājinālaṃbi pinaddhabhogi vā Ku. 5. 78; R. 2. 32, 4. 48, 10. 7, 11. 59. --2 A king. --3 A voluptuary. --4 A barber. --5 The headman of a village; --6 The lunar mansion āśleṣā. --nī A woman belonging to the king's harem, but not consecrated with him, the concubine of a king. --Comp. --iṃdraḥ, īśaḥ Śeṣa or Vāsuki. --kāṃtaḥ wind, air. --bhuj m. {1} an ichneumon. {2} a peacock. --vallabhaṃ sandal.
