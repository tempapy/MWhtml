• muktiḥ f. [muc-ktin]
      1 Release, liberation, deliverance.
      2 Freedom, emancipation.
      3 Final beatitude or emancipation, absolution of the soul from metempsychosis; adhigatya jagatyadhīśvarādaya muktiṃ puruṣottamāttataḥ N. 2. 1 (where pukti has sense {1} also).
      4 Leaving, giving up, abandoning, avoiding; saṃsargamuktiḥ khaleṣu Bh. 2. 62.
      5 Throwing, hurling, letting off, discharging.
      6 Unloosing, opening.
      7 Discharge. paying off (as a debt). Comp. --kṣetraṃ an epithet of Benaras.
    • --margaḥ the way to final beatitude.
    • --muktaḥ frankincense. [0868-b]
