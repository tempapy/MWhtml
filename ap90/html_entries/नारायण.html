• nārāyaṇa (bhaṭṭanārāyaṇa) Author of the Veṇī-Saṃhāra. He must have flourished before the 9th century as his work is frequently quoted by the poet Anandavardhana in his work called dhyanyāloka. This poet flourished under the reign of Avantivarman 855-884 A. D. (Rāj. T. 5. 34) [1191-b]
