• stimita a. [stim-kartari kta] 1 Wet, moist. --2 (a) Still, unruffled, calm; kṣubhitamutkalikātaralaṃ manaḥ paya iva stimitasya mahodadheḥ Māl. 3. 10. (b) Fixed, rigid, unmoved, motionless, steady; vācaspatiḥ sannapi so'ṣṭamūrtau tvāśāsyaciṃtāstimito babhūva Ku. 7. 87; 2. 59; stimitavikasitānāṃ Māl. 1. 27; R. 2. 22, 3. 17, 13. 48, 79; U. 6. 25. --3 Closed, shut; R. 1. 73. --4 Benumbed, paralysed. --5 Soft, tender. --6 Gratified, satisfied. --taṃ 1 Moisture --2 Fixity, steadiness. --Comp. --vāyuḥ still air. --samādhiḥ steady contemplation.
