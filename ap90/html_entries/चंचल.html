• caṃcala a. [caṃc-alac, caṃcaṃ garti lāti la-ka vā Tv.] 1 Moving, shaking, trembling, tremulous; śrutvaiva bhītahariṇīśiśucaṃcalākṣīṃ Ch. P. 27; caṃcalakuṃḍala Gīt. 7; Amaru. 79. --2 (Fig.) Inconstant, fickle, unsteady; bhogā meghavitānamadhyavilasatsaudāminīcaṃcalāḥ Bh. 3. 54; Ki. 2. 19; manaścaṃcalamasthiraṃ Bg. 6. 26. --laḥ 1 The wind. --2 A lover --3 A libertine. --lā 1 Lightning. --2 Lakṣmī, the goddess of wealth.
