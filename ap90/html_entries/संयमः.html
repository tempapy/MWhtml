• saṃyamaḥ
      1 Restraint, check, control; śrotrādīnīṃdriyāṇyanye saṃyamāgniṣu juhvati Bg. 4. 26, 27; so saṃyamadhanaḥ &c.
      2 Concentration of mind, a term applied to the last three stages of Yoga; dhāraṇādhyānasamādhitrayamaṃtaraṃgaṃ saṃyamapadavācyaṃ Sarva. S., Ku. 2. 59.
      3 A religious vow.
      4 Religious devotion, practice of penance; Ś. 4. 16.
      5 Humanity, feeling of compassion.
      6 Any religious act on the day preceding a vow or course of penance. [1067-a]
