• dāsī
      1 A female servant or slave.
      2 The wife of a fisherman.
      3 The wife of a Śūdra.
      4 An altar.
      5 A harlot.
    Comp.
    • --putraḥ, --sutaḥ the son of a female slave.
    • --śrotriyaḥ a Brāhmaṇa (knowing the Vedas) attached to a female slave.
    • --sabhaṃ a collection of female slaves. (The gen. sing. dāsyāḥ enters into some compounds, but loses its literal sense; e. g. dāsyāḥputraḥ, --sutaḥ ‘a whore-son’, used as a term of abuse; dāsyāḥputraiḥ śakunilubdhakaiḥ Ś. 2; but dāsyāḥ sadṛśī ‘like a female slave’). [0564-b]
