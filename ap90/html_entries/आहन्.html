āhan 2 P.
      1 To strike, hit, beat; kuṭṭimamājaghāna K. 10; parasya śira āhaṃti Sk.; Śi. 7. 17; durjayān kariṇaḥ … āhanyāt Kām. 19. 60; said to be Atm. when the object is some limb of one's own body; āhate śiraḥ; but cf. ājaghne viṣamavilocanasya vakṣaḥ Ki. 17. 63; so āhadhvaṃ mā raghūttamaṃ; Bk. 8. 15, 5. 102 (see Sk. on P. I. 3. 28 also).
      2 To strike, ring, beat (as a bell, drum &c.); Bk. 1. 27, 17. 7, Me. 66; R. 17. 11.
      3 To kill, slaughter. [0271-b]
