• giri a. [gṝ-i kicca Uṇ. 4. 142] Venerable, respectable, worshipful.
    • --riḥ
      1 A hill, mountain, an elevation; paśyādhaḥkhanane mūḍha gigyo na pataṃti kiṃ Subhāṣ.; nanu pravāte'pi niṣkaṃpā girayaḥ Ś. 6.
      2 A huge rock.
      3 A disease of the eyes.
      4 An honorific title given to Saṃnyāsins; e. g. ānaṃdagiriḥ
      5 (In math.) The number ‘eight’.
      6 A ball with which children play (geṃduka).
      7 A cloud.
      8 A peculiar defect in quicksilver.
    • --riḥ f.
      1 Swallowing.
      2 A rat; mouse (written also girī in this sense).
