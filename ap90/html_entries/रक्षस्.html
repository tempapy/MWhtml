rakṣas n. [rakṣyate havirasmāt, raks-asun]
      1 An evil spirit, a demon, an imp, a goblin; caturdaśasahasrāṇi rakṣasāṃ bhīmakarmaṇām . trayaśca dūṣaṇakharatrimūdhārno raṇe hatāḥ .. U. 2. 15.
      2 Ved. Hurt, injury.
    Comp.
      --īśaḥ, --nāthaḥ an epithet of Rāvaṇa.
      --ghnaḥ white mustard. (--ghnaṃ) sour rice-gruel.
      --jananī night.
      --pāśaḥ a contemptible demon.
      --sabhaṃ an assembly of demons. [0900-b]
