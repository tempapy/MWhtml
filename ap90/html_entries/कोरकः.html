• korakaḥ --kaṃ [Uṇ. 5. 35] 1 A bud, an unblown flower; saṃnaddhaṃ yadapi sthitaṃ kurabakaṃ tatkorakāvasthayā Ś. 6. 3. --2 (fig.) Any thing resembling a bud, i. e. partially opened but not fully developed; rādhāyāḥ statakorakopari calannetro hariḥ pātu vaḥ Gīt. 12. --3 The fibres of the stalk of a lotus. --4 A kind of perfume.
