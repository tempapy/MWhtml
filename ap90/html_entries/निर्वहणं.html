• nirvahaṇaṃ
      1 End, completion; Śi. 14. 63.
      2 Maintainig, carrying to the end, sustaining; mānasya nirvahaṇaṃ Amaru. 24.
      3 Destruction, annihilation.
      4 (In dramas). The catastrophe, the last stage in which the action of the play is brought to a head, the denouement; tatkiṃnimittaṃ kukavikṛtanāṭakasyeva anyanmukhe'nyannirvahaṇe Mu. 6. [0635-a]
