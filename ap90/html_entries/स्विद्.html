• svid ind. A particle of interrogation or inquiry, often implying ‘doubt’, or ‘surprise’, and translateable by ‘what’, ‘hey’. ‘hallo’, ‘can it be that’. It is added to interrogative pronouns in this sense or to give an indefinite sense; kāsvidavaguṃṭhanavatī nātiparisphuṭaśarīralāvaṇyā Ś. 5. 13; Me. 14. It is sometimes used disjunctively in the sense of ‘either,’ ‘or’, with nu, uta, vā &c. see Ki. 8. 35, 12. 15, 13. 8, 14. 60; āho also. [1163-a]
