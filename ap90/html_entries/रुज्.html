ruj
      I. 6 P. (rujati, rugṇa)
      1 To break to pieces, destroy; R. 9. 63, 12. 73; Bk. 4. 43.
      2 To pain, injure, disorder, afflict with disease, (sometimes with gen); rāvaṇasyeha rokṣyaṃti kapayo bhīmavikramāḥ Bk. 8. 120.
      3 To bend.
      II. 10 U. (rojayati-te) To hurt, kill. [0914-b]
      ruj, rujā f. [ruj-kvip vāṭāp]
      1 Breaking, fracture.
      2 Pain, torment, pang, anguish; aniśamapi makaraketurmanaso rujamāvahannabhimato me Ś. 3. 4; kva rujā hṛdayapramāthinī M. 3. 2; caraṇaṃ rujāparītaṃ 4. 3.
      3 Sickness, malady, disease; R. 19. 52.
      4 Fatigue, toil, effort, trouble.
      5 A ewe.
      6 Leprosy.
    Comp.
      --kara a. causing pain, sickening. (--raḥ) a disease, sickness, illness.
      --pratikriyā counteraction or treatment of disease, curing, practice of medicine.
      --bheṣajaṃ a medicine.
      --sadmat n. feces, excrement. [0914-b]
