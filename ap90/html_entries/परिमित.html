parimita p. p.
      1 Moderate, sparing.
      2 Limited.
      3 Measured, meted out.
      4 Regulated, adjusted.
    Comp.
      --ābharaṇa a. wearing a few ornaments, moderately adorned.
      --āyus a. short-lived.
      --āhāra, bhojana a. abstemious, eating little food.
      --katha a. saying or speaking little, using measured words; Me. 83. [0678-c]
