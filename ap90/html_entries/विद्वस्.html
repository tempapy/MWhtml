• vidvas a. [vid-kvasu] (Nom. sing. m. vidvān; f. viduṣī; n. vidvat)
      1 Knowing (with acc.); ānaṃdaṃ brahmaṇo vidvān na bibheti kadācana; tava vidvānapi tāpakāraṇaṃ R. 8. 76; Ki. 11. 30.
      2 Wise, learned. --m. A learned or wise man, scholar; kiṃ vastu vidvan gurave pradeyaṃ R. 5. 18.
    Comp.
    • --kalpa, --deśīya, --deśya a. (vidvatkalpa, vidvaddeśīya, vidvaddeśya) slightly learned, a little learned.
    • --janaḥ (vidvajjanaḥ) a learned or wise man, sage. [0976-a]
