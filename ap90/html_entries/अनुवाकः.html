• anuvākaḥ [anūcyate iti, vac ghañ kutvaṃ P. II. 4. 29 Vārt.] 1 Repeating, reciting, reading. --2 A subdivision of the Vedas, section, chapter. --3 Chapter or section referring to a compilation from the Ṛgveda or Yajurveda (ṛgyajuḥsamūha). --Comp. --anukramaṇī N. of a table of contents attributed to Śaunaka.
