baddha p. p. [baṃdh-karmaṇi kta]
      1 Bound, tied, fastened.
      2 Chained, fettered.
      3 Captured, caught.
      4 Confined, imprisoned.
      5 Put or girt on.
      6 Restrained, suppressed, withheld.
      7 Formed, built.
      8 Cherished, entertained.
      9 Combined, united.
      10 Firmly rooted, firm.
      11 Shut, stopped, closed.
      12 Inlaid, studded.
      13 Composed (as verses).
      14 Formed, contracted; Ku. 1. 20.
      15 Manifested, displayed.
      16 Entangled, involved.
      17 Congealed, clotted (as blood).
      18 Effected, caused, formed, produced; baddhaṃ jālaka Ś. 1. 30; 2. 6; U. 6. 17; Māl. 3. 7.
