yauvataṃ [yuvatīnāṃ samūhaḥ aṇ]
      1 An assemblage of young women; avadhṛtya divopi yauvatairna sahādhītavatīmimāmahaṃ N. 2. 41.
      2 The quality of a young woman (beauty &c.), the state of being a young woman; aho vibudhayauvataṃ vahasi tanvi pṛthvīgatā Gīt. 10. (surasuṃdarīrūpaṃ). [0898-c]
