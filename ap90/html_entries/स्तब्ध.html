• stabdha p. p. [staṃbh karmaṇi kartari vā kta] 1 Stopped, blocked up, obstructed. --2 Paralysed, senseless, stupefied, benumbed. --3 Motionless, immoveable. --4 Fixed, firm, hard, rigid, stiff. --5 Obstinate, stubborn, hard-hearted, stern. --6 Coarse. --Comp. --karṇa a. pricking up the ears. --roman m. a hog, boar. --locana a. having motionless or unwinking eyes (said of gods).
