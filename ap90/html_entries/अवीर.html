• avīra a.
      1 Unmanly, effeminate, cowardly.
      2 Having no son (as a woman).
      3 Helpless.
      4 Destitute of men.
    • --rā A woman who has neither sons nor husband; ajātaputrā vidhavā sā'vīrā parikīrtitā; (opp. vīrā which is thus defined; patiputravatī nārī vīrā proktā manīṣibhiḥ); anarcitaṃ vṛthā māṃsamavīrāṃyāśca yoṣitaḥ Ms. 4. 213; Y. 1. 163. [0197-a]
