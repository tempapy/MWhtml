• sphuraṇaṃ [sphur-lyuṭ] 1 Throbbing, quivering, trembling (in general). --2 Throbbing or quivering of certain parts of the body (indicating good or bad luck). --3 Breaking forth, arising, starting into view. --4 Gleaming, flashing, glittering, shining, twinkling. --5 Flashing on the mind, crossing the memory. [1155-a]
