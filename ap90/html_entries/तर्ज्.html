tarj 1 P., 10 A. (often P. also) (tarjati, tarjayati-te, tarjita)
      1 To threaten, menace, terrify; sakhīmaṃgulyā tarjayati Ś. 1; ahitānaniloddhūtaistarjayānniva ketubhiḥ R. 4. 28, 11. 78, 12. 41; Bk. 14. 80.
      2 To scold, revile, censure, blame; Bk. 6. 3, 8. 101, 17. 103.
      3 To mock, deride. [0533-a]
