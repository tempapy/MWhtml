prāṃtaḥ [prakṛṣṭoṃ'taḥ]
      1 Edge, margin, border, skirt, verge; prāṃtasaṃstīrṇadarbhāḥ Ś. 4. 7.
      2 Corner (as of the lips, eyes &c.); Māl. 4. 2; oṣṭha°, nayana°.
      3 Boundary, extremity.
      4 Extreme verge, end; yauvanaprāṃta Pt. 4.
      5 A point, tip.
      6 The back part. Comp. A --ga a. living close by.
      --durgaṃ a suburb outside the walls of a town, a town near a fort.
      --virasa a. tasteless in the end.
      --śūnya a. see prāṃtaraśūnya.
      --stha a. one who inhabits the borders. [0773-c]
