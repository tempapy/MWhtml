• gaṃḍakaḥ [gaṃḍa svārthe ka]
      1 A rhinoceros.
      2 An impediment, obstacle.
      3 A joint, knot.
      4 A mark, spot.
      5 A boil, tumour, pimple.
      6 Disjunction, separation.
      7 A coin of the value of four cowries.
      8 A mode of reckoning by fours.
      9 Astrological science.
    Comp.
