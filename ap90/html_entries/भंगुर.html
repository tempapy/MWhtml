• bhaṃgura a. [bhaṃj-ghurac] 1 Apt to break, fragile, brittle. --2 Frail, transitory, transient, perishable; āmaraṇāṃtāḥ praṇayāḥ kopāstatkṣaṇabhaṃgurāḥ H. 1. 188; Śi. 16. 72. --3 Changeful, variable. --4 Crooked, bent. --5 Curved, curled; śaśimukhi tava bhāti bhaṃgurabhrūḥ Gīt. 10. --6 Fraudulent, dishonest, crafty. --raḥ The bend of a river. --Comp. --niścaya a. vacillating, unsettled in mind.
