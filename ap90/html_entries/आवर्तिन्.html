āvartin a.
    • 1 Whirling or turning upon itself, returning; ābrahmabhuvanāllokāḥ punarāvartino'rjuna Bg. 8. 16; kālāṃtarāvarti H. 1. 207.
    • 2 Melting, mixing &c. --m. (--rtī) A horse having curls of hair on various parts of the body (considered as a sign of auspiciousness).
    • 1 A whirlpool.
    • 2 N. of a plant (ajaśṛṃgī). [0262-b]
