animakaḥ [an-jīvane śabde ca, bhāve bāhu° iman ityanimaḥ jīvanaṃ, tena kāyati prakāśate, kai-ka. Tv.]
      1 A frog (tasya maraṇepi punarujjīvanāt).
      2 A cuckoo.
      3 A bee (ubhayorapi tayoḥ madhuraśabdena prakāśamānatvāt).
      4 The filament of a lotus, padmakeśara.
      5 N. of the tree madhūka Med. [0064-b]
