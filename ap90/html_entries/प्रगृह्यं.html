pragṛhyaṃ
      1 A vowel which is not liable to the rules of Saṃdhi or euphony and which is allowed to be written and pronounced separately; īdūdeddbivacanaṃ pragṛhyaṃ P. I. 1. 11 (i. e. the final ī, ū and e of the dual terminations of a word or any grammatical form).
      2 Remembrance.
      3 A sentence. [0728-a]
