• vihṛ {c1c} P. 1 To take away, seize away. --2 To remove, destroy. --3 To let fall, shed (as tears); as in bāṣpaṃ viharati. --4 To pass (as time). --5 To amuse, or divert oneself. sport, play; viharati haririha sarasavasaṃte Gīt. 1; gaṃdhamādanavanaṃ vihartuṃ gatā V. 4; U. 3. 6. --6 To go in various directions. --7 To live. --8 To change, alternate.
