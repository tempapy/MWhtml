• aritra a. [gacchatyanena; ṛ-itra P. III. 2. 184] Ved.
      1 Propelling, urging onwards.
      2 Protecting on all sides.
    • --traṃ
      1 An oar; lolairaritraiścaraṇairivābhitaḥ Śi. 12. 71.
      2 A rudder, helm.
      3 A ship, boat.
      4 A part of a carriage.
      5 A Soma vessel.
    • --traḥ A Soma vessel. [cf. L. aratrum; Gr. eretmos].
    Comp.
    • --gādha a. ‘oardeep,’ shallow (water).
    • --paraṇa a. Ved. crossing by means of oars. [0161-b]
