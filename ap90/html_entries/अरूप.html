arūpa a.
      1 Formless, shapeless.
      2 Ugly, deformed.
      3 Dissimilar, unlike.
      --paṃ
      1 A bad or ugly figure.
      2 The Pradhāna of the Sāṃkhyas and Brahma of the Vedāntins.
    Comp.
      --hārya a. not to be attracted or won over by beauty; arūpahāryaṃ madanasya nigrahāt Ku. 5. 53. [0162-c]
