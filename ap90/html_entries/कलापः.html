• kalāpaḥ 1 A band, bundle; muktākalāpasya ca nistalasya Ku. 1. 42 a round necklace of pearls; raśanākalāpaḥ a zone of several strings. --2 A group or whole collection of things; akhilakalākalāpālocana K. 7. --3 A peacock's tail; taṃ me jātakalāpaṃ preṣaya maṇikaṃṭhakaṃ śikhinaṃ V. 5. 13; Pt. 2. 80; Rs. 1. 16, 2. 14. --4 A woman's zone or girdle; (oft. with kāṃcī or raśanā &c.); Bh. 1. 57, 67; Rs. 3. 20; Mk. 1. 27. --5 An ornament in general. --6 The rope round an elephant's neck. --7 A quiver. --8 An arrow. --9 The moon. --10 A shrewd and intelligent man. --11 A poem written in one metre. --12 A tuft (jaṭā°) or knot of braided hair. --pī A bundle of grass.
