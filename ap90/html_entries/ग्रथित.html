• grathita p. p. [graṃth saṃdarbhe kta nalopaḥ]

      2 Composed; kālidāsagrathitavastunā nāṭakena Ś. 1. varṇaiḥ katipayaireva grathitasya svarairiva Śi. 2. 72.
      3 Arranged, classed.
      4 Thickened, coagulated.
      5 Knotty.
      6 Hardened.
      7 Hurt, injured.
      8 Seized, taken possession of.
      9 Overcome.
