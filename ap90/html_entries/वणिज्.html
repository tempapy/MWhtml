• vaṇij m. [Uṇ. 2. 70]
      1 A merchant, trader; yasyāgamaḥ kevalajīvikāyai taṃ jñānapaṇyaṃ vaṇijaṃ vadaṃti M. 1. 17.
      2 The sign Libra of the zodiac. --f. Merchandise, trade.
    Comp.
    • --karman n., --kriyā traffic, trade.
    • --janaḥ {1} merchants (collectively). {2} a trader, merchant.
    • --pathaḥ {1} trade, traffic. {2} a merchant. {3} a merchants's shop, a stall. {4} the sign Libra of the zodiac.
    • --vṛttiḥ f. trade, traffic; Bh. 3. 81. --sārthaḥ a caravan. [0940-b]
