• budha a. [budh-ka]
      1 Wise, clever, learned.
      2 Intelligent.
      3 Waking, awaking.
    • --dhaḥ
      1 A wise or learned man; nipīya yasya kṣitirakṣiṇaḥ kathāṃ tathādriyaṃte na budhāḥ sudhāmapi N. 1. 1.
      2 A god; N. 1. 1.
      3 The planet Mercury; rakṣatyenaṃ tu budhayogaḥ Mu. 1. 6 (where budha has sense
      1 also); R. 1. 47; 13. 76. --dhā Spikenard.
    Comp.
    • --janaḥ a wise or learned man.
    • --tātaḥ the moon.
    • --dinaṃ, --vāraḥ vāsaraḥ Wednesday.
    • --ratnaṃ an emerald.
    • --sutaḥ an epithet of Purūravas. [0799-a]
