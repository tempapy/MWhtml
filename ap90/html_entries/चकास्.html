cakās 2 P. (rarely A.)( cakāstiste, cakāsāṃcakāra, acakāsīt cakāsita)
      1 To shine, be bright; gaṃḍaścaṃḍi cakāsti nīlanalinaśrīmocanaṃ locanaṃ Gīt. 10; cakāsataṃ cārucamūrucarmaṇā Śi. 1. 8; Bk. 3. 37.
      2 (Fig.) To be happy or prosperous; vitanvati kṣemamadevamātṛkāścirāya tasmin kuravaścakāsate Ki. 1. 17. --Caus. To cause to shine, illuminate; Śi. 3. 6. --WITH vi to shine, be bright. [0480-a]
