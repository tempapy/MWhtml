• baṃdhura a. [baṃdh-urac] 1 Undulating, wavy, uneven; Śi. 7. 34; Ku. 1. 42, U. 6. 25; Mv. 6. 30. --2 Bent, inclined, bowed; baṃdhuragātri R. 13. 47; (= saṃnatāṃgi). --3 Crooked, curved. --4 Pleasing, handsome, beautiful, lovely; kathaṃ nu taṃ baṃdhurakomalāṃguliṃ Ś. 6. 12 (where it may mean ‘undulating’ also); samastaśāstrasmṛtibaṃdhure mukhe K. 3. --5 Deaf. --6 Injurious, mischievous. --raḥ 1 A goose. --2 A crane. --3 A drug. --4 An oil-cake. --5 The vulva. --6 The baṃdhujīva tree. --rāḥ (m. pl.) Parched corn or meal thereof. --rā An unchaste woman. --raṃ A diadem. [0788-b]
