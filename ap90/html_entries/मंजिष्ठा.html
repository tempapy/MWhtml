maṃjiṣṭhā [atiśayena maṃjimatī iṣṭhan matupo lopaḥ Tv.] Bengal or Indian madder.
    Comp.
      --mehaḥ a kind of urinary disease.
      --rāgaḥ {1} the colour of the Indian madder. {2} (fig.) attachment as charming and durable as the colour of the madder, i. e. durable or permanent attachment. [0832-b]
