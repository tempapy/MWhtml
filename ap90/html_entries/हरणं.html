• haraṇaṃ [hṛ-bhāve lyuṭ]
      1 Seizing, taking.
      2 Carrying away, carrying off, removing, stealing; kanyāharaṇaṃ Ms. 3. 33, R. 11. 74.
      3 Depriving of, destroying; as in prāṇaharaṇaṃ.
      4 Dividing.
      5 A gift to a student.
      6 The arm.
      7 Semen virile.
      8 Gold.
      9 A nuptial present. (= yautaka q. v.)
      10 A shell, cowrie
      11 Boiling water. [1165-c]
