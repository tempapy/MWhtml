• sanna p. p. [sad-kta]
      1 Sitting down, settling down, lying.
      2 Dejected, sunk down, downcast.
      3 Drooping, relaxed; Ku. 3. 51.
      4 Weak, low, feeble.
      5 Wasted away, decayed.
      6 Perished, destroyed.
      7 Still, motionless.
      8 Shrunk.
      9 Adjacent, near.
      10 Gone, departed.
      11 Sunk, low (in tone &c).
    • --nnaḥ The tree called piyāla.
    • --nnaṃ A little, a small quantity.
    Comp.
    • --kaṃṭha a. choked.
    • --harṣa a. desponding, cheerless. [1089-c]
