āsravaḥ [āsru-ap]
    • 1 Pain, affliction, distress.
    • 2 Flowing, running.
    • 3 Discharge, emission.
    • 4 Fault, transgression.
    • 5 The foam on boiling rice.
    • 6 (With Jainas) The impulse called yoga or attention which the soul participates in the movement of its various bodies; it is defined as the ‘action of the senses which impels the soul towards external objects’; it is good or evil according as it is directed towards good or evil objects.
