aprakṛta a.
    • 1 Not principal or chief, incidental, occasional.
    • 2 Not relevant to the subject under discussion, not to the point, irrelevant; see prakṛta, prastuta; aprakṛtaṃ anusaṃdhā ‘to beat about the bush’, not to come to the point.
      (In Rhet.) upamāna i. e. the standard of comparison (opp. prakṛta or upameya). [0121-a]
