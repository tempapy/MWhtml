utpīḍa a. Pressing out.

    • 2 (a) Gush, gushing flow, rushing column or mass; bāṣpotpīḍaḥ K. 296; utpīḍa iva dhūmasya mohaḥ prāgāvṛṇoti māṃ U. 3. 9; nayanasalilotpīḍaruddhāvakāśāṃ Me. 91. (b) Overflow, excess; pūrotpīḍe taḍāgasya parīvāhaḥ pratikriyā U. 3. 29; Māl. 6, 7.
    • 3 Froth, foam. [0297-c]
