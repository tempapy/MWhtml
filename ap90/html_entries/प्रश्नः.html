• praśnaḥ [pracch-bhāve naṅ]
      1 A question, query; an inquiry, interrogation (avijñātapravacanaṃ praśna ityabhidhīyate); anāmayapraśnapūrvakaṃ Ś. 5 ‘with an inquiry about (your) well-being or health’.
      2 A judicial inquiry or investigation.
      3 A point at issue, a subject of controversy, controverted or disputed point; iti praśna upasthitaḥ.
      4 A problem for solution or calculation; ahaṃ te praśnaṃ dāsyāmi Mk. 5.
      5 Inquiry into the future.
      6 A short section of a work.
      7 Basket-work.
    Comp.
    • --upaniṣad n. N. of an Upaniṣad consisting of six questions and six answers. --dūtiḥ -tī f. a riddle, an enigma.
    • --vivākaḥ an arbitrator, umpire. [0763-a]
