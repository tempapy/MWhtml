• kṛśa a. [kṛś --kta ni°] (Compar. kaśīyas; superl. kraśiṣṭha) 1 Lean, weak, feeble, emaciated; kṛśatanuḥ, kṛśodari &c. --2 Small, little, minute (in size or quantity); suhṛdapi na yācyaḥ kṛśadhanaḥ Bh. 2. 28. --3 Poor, insignificant; Ms. 7. 208. --Comp. --akṣaḥ a spider. --aṃga a. lean, thin. (--gaḥ) an epithet of Śiva. (--gī) {1} a woman with a slender frame. {2} the Priyaṅgu creeper. --udara a. {1} thin-waisted; V. 5. 16; Ku. 5. 42. {2} having the belly reduced in bulk; Ś. 2. 5.
