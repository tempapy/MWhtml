• vyāvṛt {c1c} A. 1 To turn away from, turn back; sahabhuvā vyāvartamānā hniyā Ratn. 1. 2. --2 To be turned or withdrawn from, to be averse to; viṣayavyāvṛttakautūhalaḥ V. 1. 8. --3 To become separate or distinct from. --4 To turn round, revolve. --5 To go down, set (as the sun). --6 To cease to exist, perish. --7 To be opened or split asunder. --Caus. 1 To restrict, limit, exclude, arrest; overrule, annul, (as a rule &c.); tuśabdaḥ pūrvapakṣaṃ vyāvartayati Ś. B.; apavāda ivotsargaṃ vyāvartayitumaśviraḥ R. 15. 7. --2 To dissuade from, cause to turn away from; neyamasmādvyavasāyātkathaṃcidapi śakyate vyāvartayituṃ K. 172. --3 To destroy, remove; V. 5. 16. --4 To turn round, cause to revolve. --5 To separate from. --6 To scatter, strew.
