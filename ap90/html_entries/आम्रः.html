āmraḥ [am gatyādiṣu ran dīrghaśca Uṇ. 2. 16] The mango-tree.
      --mraṃ The fruit of the mango-tree.
    Comp.
      --āvartaḥ {1} N. of a tree (āmrātaka). {2} inspissated mango juice. (--rtaṃ) the fruit of āmrātaka.
      --kūṭaḥ the name of a mountain; sānumānāmrakūṭaḥ Me. 17. --gaṃdhakaḥ N. of a plant (samaṣṭhilavṛkṣa).
      --peśī [āmrasya peśīva] a portion of dried mango-fruit.
      --vaṇaṃ [āmrasya vanaṃ cf. P. VIII. 4. 5] a grove of mango trees; sohamāmravaṇaṃ chittvā Rām. [0250-b]
