• rāśiḥ [aśnute vyāpnoti, aś-iñ dhātoruḍāgamaśca; cf. Uṇ. 4. 132]
      1 A heap, mass, collection, quantity, multitude; dhanarāśiḥ, toyarāśiḥ, yaśorāśiḥ &c.
      2 The numbers or figures put down for any arithmetical operation (such as adding, multiplying &c.).
      3 A sign of the zodiac.
    Comp.
    • --adhipaḥ, the regent of an astrological house.
    • --gata a. {1} heaped, piled up. {2} summed up.
    • --cakraṃ the zodiac.
    • --trayaṃ the rule of three.
    • --nāman n. a name given to a child taken from the Rāśi under which he is born.
    • --bhāgaḥ a fraction. °anubaṃdhaḥ the addition of fractions. --bhogaḥ the passage of the sun, moon, or any planet through a sign of the zodiac. [0912-a]
