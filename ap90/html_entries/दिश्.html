• diś {c6c} U. (diśati-te, diṣṭa; desid. didikṣati-te) 1 To point out, show, exhibit, produce (as a witness); sākṣiṇaḥ saṃti metyuktvā diśetyukto diśenna yaḥ Ms. 8. 57, 52, 53. --2 To assign, allot; iṣṭāṃ gatiṃ tasya surā diśāṃti Mb. --3 To give, grant, bestow upon, deliver or make over to; bāṇamatrabhavate nijaṃ diśan Ki. 13. 68; R. 5. 30, 11, 2, 16. 72. --4 To pay (as tribute). --5 To consent to; R. 11. 49. --6 To direct, order, command. --7 To allow, permit; smartuṃ diśaṃti na divaḥ surasuṃdarībhyaḥ Ki. 5. 28. --Caus. (deśayati-te) 1 To show, point out, allot, assign. --2 To teach, communicate, tell, inform. --3 To direct, order. --4 To confer, bestow. [0566-a]
