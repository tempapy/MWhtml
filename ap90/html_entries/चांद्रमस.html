• cāṃdramasa a. (sī f.) [caṃdramasa idam aṇ] Relating to the moon, lunar; labdhodayā cāṃdramasīva lekhā Ku. 1. 25; caṃdraṃ gatā padmaguṇānna bhuṃkte padmāśritā cāṃdramasīmabhikhyāṃ 1. 43; R. 2. 39; Bg. 8. 25. --sī N. of the wife of Bṛhaspati.
    • --saṃ
      1 The constellation mṛgaśiras.
      2 The stars in Orion. [0490-a]
