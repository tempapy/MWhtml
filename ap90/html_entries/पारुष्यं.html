• pāruṣyaṃ [paruṣasya bhāvaḥ ṣyañ] 1 Roughness, ruggedness, hardness. --2 Harshness, cruelty, unkindness (as of disposition). --3 Abusive language, abuse, reproach, scurrilous language, insult; Bg. 16. 4; Y. 2. 12, 72. --4 Violence (in word or deed); Ms. 8. 6, 72; 7. 48, 51. --5 The garden of Indra. --6 Aloe wood. --ṣyaḥ An epithet of Bṛhaspati.
