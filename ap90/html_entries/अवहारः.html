• avahāraḥ [avahṛ-kartari ṇa P. III. 1. 141] 1 A thief. --2 A shark, a waterelephant. --3 Taking away the wealth of invited Brāhmaṇas. --4 Temporary cessation of hostilities, suspension of arms, truce. --5 Removing to the camp; tato'vahāraṃ sainyānāṃ tava teṣāṃ ca bhārata Mb. --6 Cessation, stop (in general). --7 Summoning, inviting. --8 Any object fit to be brought near (upanetavyaṃ). --9 Apostacy, abandoning a sect or caste. --10 Re-delivery, redeeming.
