• anvāhāryaḥ (also --ryaṃ) --ryakaṃ [anu māsi māsi āhriyate, karmaṇi ṇyat] 1 A sacrificial gift or offering presented to the priests (Sāy. anvāharati yajñasaṃbaṃdhidoṣajātaṃ pariharatyanena ityanvāhāryo nāma ṛtvigbhyo deya odanaḥ). --2 The monthly Śrāddha performed in honor of the manes on the day of new moon; pitṝṇāṃ māsikaṃ śrāddhamanvāhāryaṃ vidurbudhāḥ Ms. 3. 123. --Comp. --pacanaḥ the southern sacrificial fire used in the anvāhārya sacrifice.
