• dahana a. (nī f.) (citraka). --7 The constellation kṛttikā. --naṃ 1 Burning, consuming by fire (fig. also); R. 8. 20. --2 Cauterizing. --3 Sour gruel. --Comp. --arātiḥ water. --upalaḥ the sun-stone. --ulkā a fire-brand. --ketanaḥ smoke. --priyā Svāhā, wife of Agni. --sārathiḥ wind.
