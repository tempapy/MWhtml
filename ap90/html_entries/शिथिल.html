• śithila a. [ślath-kilac pṛṣo° Uṇ. 1. 53] 1 Loose, loosened, slackened, relaxed. --2 Untied, unfastened; Ś. 2. 6. --3 Severed, fallen from the stalk; arkasyopari śithilaṃ cyutamiva navamallikākusumaṃ Ś. 2. 8. --4 Languid, enfeebled, unnerved. --5 Weak, feeble; aśithilapariraṃbha U. 1. 24, 27 'fast or close embrace'. --6 Flaccid, flabby. --7 Dissolved. --8 Decayed. --9 Ineffective, futile, vain. --10 Inattentive, careless; Pt. 4. 116. --11 Loosely done, not strictly or rigidly performed. --12 Cast off, abandoned. --laṃ 1 Laxity, looseness. --2 Slowness. (śithilīkṛ means {1} to loosen, unfasten, untie. {2} to relax, slacken. {3} to weaken, impair, enfeeble. --4 to give up, abandon; R. 2. 41. śithilībhū {1} to be slackened or relaxed. {2} to fall off from; Mk. 1. 13).
