• odanaḥ, --naṃ [uṃd-yuc Uṇ. 2. 76] 1 Food, boiled rice; e. g. dadhyodanaḥ, ghṛta°, guḍa°, māṃsa° &c. --2 Grain mashed and cooked with milk. --3 A cloud. (Sometimes odana is prefixed to the names of pupils to denote that the pupil's object is more to be fed by his master than be taught; e. g. odanapāṇinīyāḥ P. VI. 2. 69 Sk.) --nī The plant (balā) Sida Cordifolia. --Comp. --āhvayā, --āhvā, --odanikā N. of a medicinal plant (mahāsamaṃgā). [0360-c]
