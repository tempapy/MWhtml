• śvan m. [śvi-kanin ni° Uṇ. 1. 158] (Nom. śvā, śvānau, śvānaḥ; acc. pl. śunaḥ; śunī f.) L. 29. --phalaṃ lime or citron. --phalkaḥ N. of the father of Akrūra. --bhīruḥ a jackal. --yūthyaṃ a pack of dogs. --vṛttiḥ f. {1} the life of a dog (to which servitude is often likened); sevāṃ lāghavakāriṇīṃ kṛtadhiyaḥ sthāne śvavṛttiṃ biduḥ Mu. 3. 14; Ms. 4. 6. {2} servitude, service; Ms. 4. 4. --vyāghraḥ {1} a beast of prey. {2} a tiger. {3} a leopard. --han m. a hunter.
