ākarṣaṇa a. Attracting, carrying to another place.

    • 2 Seduction.
    • 1 A curved stick for pulling down fruits, flowers &c. (standing on elevated places); any instrument for pulling.
    • 2 A variety of Mudrā (or mark on the body). [0223-a]
