• lubh {vI.v} {c6c} P. To bewilder, confound, perplex. {vII.v} {c4c} P. (lubhyati, lubdha) 1 To covet, long for, desire eagerly (with dat. or loc.); tathāpi rāmo lulubhe mṛgāya. --2 To allure, entice. --3 To be bewildered or perplexed, go astray. --Caus. (lobhayati-te) 1 To make greedy, cause to long for, produce or excite desire for; pupluve bahu lābhayan Bk. 5. 48. --2 To excite lust. --3 To entire, seduce, allure, attract: lobhyamānanayanaḥ ślathāṃśukairmekhalāguṇapadaurnitaṃbibhiḥ R. 19. 26. --4 To derange, disorder, disturb.
