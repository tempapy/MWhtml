• samāgam 1 To come or meet together, assemble. --2 To become joined or united, to associate, keep company with. --3 To have sexual intercourse with. --4 To come together, be in conjunction (as planets). --5 To come near, approach. --6 To return. --7 To find, meet with. [1095-c]
