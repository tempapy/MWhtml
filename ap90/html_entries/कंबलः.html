• kaṃbalaḥ [Uṇ. 1. 106]
      1 A blanket (of wool); kaṃbalavaṃtaṃ na bādhate śītaṃ Subhāṣ.; kaṃbalāvṛtena tena H. 3.
      2 A dewlap.
      3 A sort of deer.
      4 An upper garment of wool.
      5 A wall.
      6 A small worm.
    • --laṃ Water.
    Comp.
    • --vāhyakaṃ a kind of carriage covered with a coarse blanket, and drawn by oxen. [0379-a]
