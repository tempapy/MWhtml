• śārdūlaḥ [śṝ-ūlal duk ca] 1 A tiger. --2 A leopard or panther. --3 A demon, Rākṣasa. --4 A kind of bird. --5 A kind of animal called śa bha. (at the end of comp.) An eminent or distinguished person, foremost; as in naraśārdūlaḥ; cf. kuṃjara. --Comp. --carman n. a tiger's skin. --vikrīḍitaṃ {1} a tiger's play; kaṃdarpo'pi yamāyate viracayan śārdūlavikrīḍitaṃ Gīt. 4. {2} N. of a metre; see App. {1} [1041-b]
