• manyuḥ [man-yuc Uṇ. 3. 20] 1 Anger, wrath, resentment, indignation, rage: R. 2. 32, 49; 11. 46. --2 Grief, sorrow, affliction, distress; U. 4. 3; Ki. 1. 35; Bk. 3. 49. --3 Wretched or miserable state, meanness. --4 A sacrifice. --5 Spirit, mettle, courage (as of horses). --6 Ardour, zeal. --7 Pride. --8 An epithet of Śiva. --9 Of Agni.
