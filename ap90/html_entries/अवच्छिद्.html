• avacchid 7 U.
      1 To cut off, separate, tear in pieces, break asunder.
      2 To discriminate, distinguish, characterize.
      3 To define, limit, modify (as by time, space &c.,) used in Nyāya; see avacchinna below.
      4 To detach, excerpt.
      5 To interrupt. [0179-a]
