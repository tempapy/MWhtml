• kṣodaḥ [kṣuṭ-ghañ] 1 Pounding, grinding. --2 The stone on which anything is ground or powdered, a mortar. --3 Any ground substance, flour. --4 Dust, particle, any small or minute particle; U. 3. 2. --Comp. --kṣama a. capable of standing a test, scrutiny or investigation. [0438-c]
