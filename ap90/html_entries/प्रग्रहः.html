• pragrahaḥ 1 Holding or stretching forth, holding out. --2 Laying hold of, taking, grasping, seizing. --3 The commencement of an eclipse. --4 (a) A rein, bridle; dhṛtāḥ pragrahāḥ avataratvāyuṣmān Ś. 1; Śi. 12. 31. (b) A whip, lash, scourge. --5 A check, restraint. --6 Binding, confinement. --7 A prisoner, captive. --8 Taming, breaking (as an animal). --9 A ray of light. --10 The string of a balance. --11 A vowel not subject to the rules of Saṃdhi or euphony; see pragṛhya. --12 N. of Viṣṇu. --13 The arm. --14 A leader, guide. --15 Kindness, favour. --16 The Karṇikāra tree.
