• avarṇa a. 1 Colourless, having no marks. --2 Bad, low, destitute of good qualities. --rṇaḥ 1 Scandal, ill-repute, stigma, spot; soḍhuṃ na tatpūrvamavarṇamīśe R. 14. 38. --2 Blame, censure; na cāvadadbharturavarṇamāryā 57 spoke no ill words. --3 Epithet of 18 letters according to thasva, dīrgha, pluta, udātta, anudātta &c. --Comp. --vādaḥ reproach, censure.
