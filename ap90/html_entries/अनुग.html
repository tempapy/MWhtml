anuga a. [anugacchatīti, gam-ḍa] (In comp.) Following, going after; corresponding or tallying with, adequate to; sadā pṛṣṭhānugaḥ pure Pt. 1. 59; gītānugaṃ vāri mṛdaṃgavādyaṃ R. 16. 64; Me. 47; kāmakrodhavaśānugaṃ Ms. 2. 214; gotrarikthānugaḥ piṃḍaḥ 9. 142 governed or regulated by; 8. 239. --gaḥ A follower, (obedient) servant, companion; tadbhūtanāthānuga R. 2. 58, 9. 82; bala° followed by an army, being the head of an army.
