prīta p. p. [prī-kartari kta]
      1 Pleased. delighted, rejoiced, gladdened; prītāsmi te putra varaṃ vṛṇīṣva R. 2. 63; 1. 81, 12. 94.
      2 Glad, happy, joyful; Me. 4.
      3 Content.
      4 Dear, beloved.
      5 Kind, affectionate.
    Comp.
      --ātman, --citta, --manas a. delighted at heart. [0778-a]
