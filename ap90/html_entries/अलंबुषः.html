• alaṃbuṣaḥ
      1 Vomiting.
      2 The palm of the hand with the fingers extended.
      3 Prahasta, the minister of Rāvaṇa.
      4 A demon killed by Ghaṭotkacha.
    • --ṣā
      1 N. of a nymph or of a class of nymphs.
      2 A kind of plant (lajjālu).
      3 A barrier, a line of water drawn to prevent entrance ( anyāpraveśārthaṃ dattā jalarekhā). [0173-a]
