• adakṣiṇa a. [na. ta.] 1 Not right, left. --2 [na. ba.] Not bringing in Dakṣiṇā to the priests; without any gifts (as a sacrifice); mṛto yajñastvadakṣiṇaḥ Pt. 2. 94. --3 Simple, weakminded, silly; mene'tha satyameveti parihāsamadakṣiṇā Rām. --4 Not handy, skilful or clever; awkward. --5 Unfavourable.
