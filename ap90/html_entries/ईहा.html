īhā [īh-a]
      1 Wish, desire; jalanidhimakarottarītumīhāṃ Rām.; see anīha also.
      2 An undertaking, act.
      3 Effort, exertion, activity; īhātaśceddhanaṃ bhavet Ms. 9. 205.
    Comp.
      --arthin a. aiming at any object, seeking wealth; Ms. 2. 37. --mṛgaḥ [īhāpradhāno mṛgaḥ] {1} a wolf. {2} an artificial deer. {3} a division of the drama consisting of four acts; for definition &c. see S. D. 518 = vṛkaḥ a wolf. [0284-c]
