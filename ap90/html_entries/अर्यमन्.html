• aryaman m. [aryaṃ śreṣṭhaṃ mimīte, mā-kanin nipātoyaṃ Uṇ. 1. 156] 1 The sun; proṣitāryamaṇaṃ meroraṃdhakārastaṭīmiva Śi. 2. 39. --2 The head of the Pitṛs or Manes; pitṝṇāmaryamā cāsmi Bg. 10. 29. --3 The constellation uttarāphalgunī. --4 N. of the arka plant. --5 One of the Ādityas. --6 A bosom-friend, play-fellow. --Comp. --devā N. of the 12th lunar mansion.
