kuṃdaḥ, --daṃ [Uṇ. 4. 98] A kind of jasmine (white and delicate); kuṃdāvadātāḥ kalahaṃsamālāḥ Bk. 2. 18; prātaḥ kuṃdaprasavaśithilaṃ jīvitaṃ dhārayethāḥ Me. 113; Ś. 5. 19. --daṃ The flower of this plant; alake bālakuṃdānuviddhaṃ Me. 65, 47. --daḥ
    • 1 An epithet of Viṣṇu.
    • 2 A fragrant oleander.
    • 3 One of the nine treasures of Kubera.
    • 4 The number ‘nine’.
    • 5 A lotus.
    • 6 A turner's lathe.
      Comp. a turner. [0409-c]
