dhīrā The heroine of a poetic composition who, though jealous of her husband or lover, suppresses all outward manifestation or expression of her resentment in his presence, or as the Rasamañjarī puts it, vyaṃgyakopaprakāśikā dhīrā; see S. D. 102-105 also.
    Comp.
      --adhīrā the heroine of a poetic composition who, being jealous of her husband or lover, alternately expresses and conceals her jealousy; (vyaṃgyāvyaṃgyakopaprakāśikā dhīrādhīrā Rasamañjarī). [0598-a]
