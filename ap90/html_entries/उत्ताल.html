• uttāla a.
      1 Great, strong; anuttālatālavṛṃtavāta K. 251.
      2 (a) Violent, loud (as sound); Māl. 1; Śi. 12. 31. (b) Roaring, gusty (wind); Māl. 5. 4.
      3 Formidable, terrific, fierce; uttālāsta ime gabhīrapayasaḥ puṇyāḥ saritsaṃgamāḥ U. 2. 30; °tumula U. 6; Śi. 20. 68; Māl. 5. 11, 23.
      4 Huge, of monstrous shape; °tāḍakā Mv. 1. 37.
      5 Arduous, difficult.
      6 Manifest; distinctly visible; Śi. 18. 54.
      7 Speedy, swift.
      8 Best, excellent; Śi. 12. 31.
      9 Elevated, lofty, tall; Śi. 3. 80. --laḥ An ape.
    • --laṃ A particular number. [0295-a]
