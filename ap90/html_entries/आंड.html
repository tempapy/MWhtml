• āṃḍa a. [aṃḍe bhavaḥ aṇ] Born from an egg (as a bird).
    • --ḍaḥ An epithet of Hiraṇyagarbha or Brahmā.
    • --ḍaṃ
      1 A multitude of eggs, brood.
      2 Scrotum.
    • --ḍau (Ved.) The testicles.
    Comp.
    • --ja a. born from eggs. (--jaḥ) a bird or a serpent. (--jaṃ) the body of a bird. [0232-a]
