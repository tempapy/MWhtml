• śvāsaḥ [śvas-ghañ] 1 Breathing, breath, respiration, heaving; adyāpi stanavepathuṃ janayati śvāsaḥ pramāṇādhikaḥ Ś. 1. 30; Ku. 2. 42. --2 A sigh, panting. --3 Air, wind. --4 Asthma. --Comp. --ucchvāsaḥ exhalation and inhalation, respiration. --kāsaḥ asthma. --kuṭhāraḥ a drug used to cure asthma. --dhāraṇaṃ = prāṇāyāma. --rodhaḥ suspension or obstruction of breath --hikkā a kind of hiccough. --hetiḥ f. sleep.
