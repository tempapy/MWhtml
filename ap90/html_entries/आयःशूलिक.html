• āyaḥśūlika a. (kī f.) [ ayaḥśūlaṭhak P. V. 2. 76] Active, diligent, indefatigable.
    • --kaḥ A man who, in order to gain an object, uses forcible instead of gentle means ( tīkṣṇopāyena yo'nvicchetsa āyaḥśūliko janaḥ) cf. K. P. 10; ayaḥśūlena anvicchati (scil. arthān) ityāyaḥ śūlikaḥ. [0250-c]
