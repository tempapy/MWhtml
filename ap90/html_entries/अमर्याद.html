amaryāda a. [na. ba.]
      1 Exceeding due limits or bounds, transgressing every bound, disrespectful, improper; maryādāyāmamaryādāḥ striyastiṣṭhaṃti sarvadā Pt. 1. 142; tādṛśaṃ tvamamaryādaṃ karma kartuṃ cikīrṣasi Rām.
      2 Boundless, infinite.
      --dā Transgression of due limits or bounds, impropriety of conduct, forwardness, disrespect, violation of due respect. [0150-a]
