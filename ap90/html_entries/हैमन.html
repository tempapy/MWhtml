• haimana a. (nī f.) [hemaṃta eva hemaṃte bhavo vā aṇ talopaḥ] 1 Wintry, cold; Śi. 6. 55, Ki. 17. 12. --2 Pertaining to winter, i. e. long (as nights); Śi. 6. 77. --3 Growing in or suitable for winter; haimanairnivasanaiḥ sumadhyamāḥ R. 19. 41. --4 Golden, made of gold. --naḥ 1 The month Mārgaśīrsha. --2 The winter season (= hemaṃta q. v.).
