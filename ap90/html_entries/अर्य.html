arya a. [ṛ-yat]
    • 1 Excellent, best.
    • 2 Respectable.
    • 3 Attached, true, devoted.
    • 4 Dear, kind.
    • 1 A master, lord; aryaḥ premṇā no tathā vallabhasya Śi. 18. 52; Śānti. 1. 18.
    • 2 A man of the third tribe, Vaiśya.
    • 1 A mistress.
    • 2 A woman of the Vaiśya tribe.
      The wife of a Vaiśya.
      Comp. the mistress of an Ārya.
      the wife of a true or legitimate husband.
      a Vaiśya of rank. [0170-b]
