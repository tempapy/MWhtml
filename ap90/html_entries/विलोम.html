• viloma a. (mī f.) 1 Inverted reverse, inverse, contrary, opposite. --2 Produced in the reverse order. --3 Backward. --maḥ 1 Reverse order, inversion. --2 A dog. --3 A snake. --4 N. of Varuṇa. --maṃ A waterwheel, machine for raising water from a well. --Comp. --utpanna, --ja, --jāta, --varṇa a. ‘born in the reverse order’; i. e. born of a mother whose caste is superior to the father's; cf. pratilomaja also. --kriyā, --vidhiḥ {1} a reverse action. {2} a rule of inversion (in math.). --jihvaḥ an elephant.
