• vadanaṃ [udyate'nena vad karaṇe lyuṭ]
      1 The face; āsīdvivṛttavadanā ca vimocayaṃtī Ś. 2. 12; so suvadanā, kamalavadanā &c.
      2 The mouth; vadane viniveśitā bhujaṃgī piśunānāṃ rasanāmiśreṇa dhātrā Bv. 1. 111.
      3 Aspect, look, appearance.
      4 The front point.
      5 First term (in a series).
      6 The summit or apex of a triangle.
    Comp.
    • --āsavaḥ saliva.
    • --śyāmikā {1} blackness of the face. {2} a kind of disease. [0941-b]
