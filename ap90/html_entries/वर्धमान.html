vardhamāna a. [vṛdh --śānac] Growing, increasing.
      --naḥ
      1 The castor-oil plant.
      2 A kind of riddle.
      3 N. of Viṣṇu.
      4 N. of a district (said to be the same as the modern Bardvāna).
      5 Sweet citron.
      --naḥ, --naṃ
      1 A pot or dish of a particular shape, lid.
      2 A kind of mystical diagram.
      3 A palace or temple built in the form of the above diagram.
      4 A house having no door on the south side.
      --nā N. of a district (the modern Bardvāna).
    Comp.
      --puraṃ the city of Bardvāna. [0949-b]
