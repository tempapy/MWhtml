• ramā [ramayati ram-ac] 1 A wife, mistress. --2 N. of Lakṣmī, the wife of Viṣṇu, and Goddess of wealth. --3 Good luck, fortune. --4 Riches. --5 Splendour. --6 N. of the eleventh day in the dark half of Kārtika. --Comp. --kāṃtaḥ, --nāthaḥ, --patiḥ epithets of Viṣṇu. --priyaḥ Viṣṇu. (--yaṃ) a lotus. --veṣṭaḥ turpentine. [0905-a]
