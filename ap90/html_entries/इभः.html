ibhaḥ [i-bhan-kicca Uṇ. 3. 151]
      1 An elephant.
      2 Fearless power or servants, dependants (Ved.).
      --bhī A female elephant. [cf. L. ebur].
    Comp.
      --ariḥ a lion.
      --ākhyaḥ, --keśarā the plant nāgakeśara.
      --ānanaḥ N. of Gaṇeśa; cf. gajānana.
      --uṣaṇā, --kaṇā a kind of aromatic plant ( gajapippalī).
      --gaṃdhā N. of a plant (the fruit of which is poisonous). --nibhīlikā {1} shrewdness, sagacity, sharpness. {2} hemp (bhaṃgā).
      --pālakaḥ the driver or keeper of an elephant.
      --poṭā [poṭā puṃlakṣaṇā ibhī] a young female elephant.
      --potaḥ a young elephant, a cub.
      --bharaḥ a collection of elephants.
      --yuvatiḥ f. a female elephant. [0279-c]
