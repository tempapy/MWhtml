sthaṃḍilaṃ [sthal-ilac nuk lasya ḍaḥ Tv.]
      1 A piece of ground (levelled, squared and prepared for a sacrifice), an altar; niṣeduṣī sthaṃḍila eva kevale Ku. 5. 12.
      2 A barren field.
      3 A heap of clods.
      4 A limit, boundary.
      5 A land-mark.
    Comp.
      --śāyin m., also sthaṃḍileśayaḥ an ascetic who sleeps on the bare Sthandila or sacrificial ground.
      --sitakaṃ an altar. [1147-a]
