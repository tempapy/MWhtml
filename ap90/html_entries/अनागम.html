• anāgama a.
      1 Not come, not present.
      2 [na. ba.] (In law) Without the title-deed or document of possession (such as purchase deed &c.), anything possessed from time immemorial and without any documentary proof; °upabhogaḥ enjoyment of property without such a deed.
    • --maḥ
      1 Non-arrival.
      2 Non-attainment. [0060-c]
