arṇava a. Being agitated, foaming, restless (Ved.); full of water (Sāy.).
      [arṇāṃsi saṃti yasmin, arṇas-va salopaḥ P. V. 2. 109 Vārt.].
    • 1 A stream, flood, wave.
    • 2 The (foaming) sea, ocean (fig. also); śoka° ocean of grief; so ciṃtā°; jana° ocean of men; saṃsārārṇavalaṃghana Bh. 3. 10.
    • 3 The ocean of air.
    • 4 N. of a metre.
    • 5 N. of the sun or Indra (as givers of water).
      Comp. the extremity of the ocean.
      {1} N. of a plant agnijāra. {2} the moon. (--vā) Lakṣmī. (--vaṃ) nectar.
      a. sea-born, marine. (--jaṃ, jaḥ) cuttle-fish.
      a boat or ship.
      {1} ‘inhabiting the ocean’, N. of Varuṇa, regent of the waters. {2} N. of Viṣṇu. [0165-c]
