• paiśāca a. (cī f.) [piśācena nirvṛttaḥ aṇ] Demoniacal, infernal. --caḥ 1 The eighth or lowest of the eight forms of marriage in Hindu law, in which a lover ravishes a maiden without her consent when she is sleeping, or intoxicated, or deranged in intellect; suptāṃ mattāṃ pramattāṃ vā raho yatropagacchati . sa pāpiṣṭho vivāhānāṃ paiśācaścāṣṭamo'dhamaḥ Ms. 3. 34; Y. 1. 61. --2 A kind of demon or piśāca. --cī 1 A present made at a religious ceremony. --2 Night. --3 A sort of gibberish spoken on the stage by demons, one of the lowest forms of Prākṛta.
