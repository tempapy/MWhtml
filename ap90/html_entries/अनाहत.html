anāhata a.
    • 1 Unbeaten, unwounded, intact.
    • 2 [āhataṃ chedo bhogo vā tannāsti yasya] New and unbleached (as cloth) (Mar. koreṃ).
    • 3 Not produced by beating (as sound).
    • 4 Not multiplied.
      The 4th of the mystical Cakras in the body, (taṃtraśāstre prasiddhaṃ hṛdayasthitaṃ suṣumṇāmadhyasthaṃ dvādaśadalapadmaṃ); śabdo brahmamayaḥ śabdo'nāhato yatra dṛśyate . anāhatākhyaṃ tat padmaṃ munibhiḥ parikīrtitam .. [0063-c]
