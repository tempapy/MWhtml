mādhvīkaṃ [madhunā madhūkapu'peṇa nirvṛttaṃ īkak]
      1 A kind of spirituous liquor, distilled from the flowers of the tree called Madhūka; cacāma madhu mādhvīkaṃ Bk. 14. 94.
      2 Wine distilled from grapes; sādhvī mādhvīka ciṃtā na bhavati bhavataḥ Gīt. 12 (= madho Com.).
      3 A grape.
    Comp.
      --phalaṃ a kind of cocoa-nut. [0859-c]
