• vṛṣalī 1 An unmarried girl twelve years old; particularly, a girl remaining unmarried at her father's house in whom menstruation has commenced; piturgehe ca yā nārī rajaḥ paśyatyasaṃskṛtā . bhrūṇahatyā pitustasyāḥ sā kanyā vṛṣalī smṛtā ... --2 A woman during menstruation. --3 A barren woman. --4 The mother of a stillborn child. --5 A Śūdra female, or the wife of a Śūdra. --Comp. --patiḥ the husband of a Śūdra woman. --sevanaṃ intercourse with a Śūdra female.
