upakram 1 A., 4 P.
      1 To go near, approach, come to, advance towards; rājñastasyājñayā devī vasiṣṭhamupacakrame Mb.; Bk. 8. 25.
      2 To do, perform, set about; yathoktaṃ nipuṇamupakrāṃtavān Dk. 120; mādhavasamakṣamuttaramupakramiṣye Māl. 3, 2. 7; Y. 3. 200.
      3 To begin, commence. (with inf. or dat.); (Atm.); prasabhaṃ vaktumupakrameta kaḥ Ki. 2. 28; pratigrahītuṃ… trilocanastāmupacakrame ca Ku. 3. 66; R. 17. 13.
      4 To go against, attack, assail, upacakramāte vāgbhiḥ Rām.
      5 To make advances (of love) to; sarvopāyairupakramya sītāṃ Rām.; win over; Dk. 65.
      6 To step over, stride, traverse.
      7 To treat, act towards, attend on (as a physician); cure or heal (as wounds); yatnādupakramyatāṃ Ś. 3 v. l.; sarvathopakrāṃtaḥ Dk. 68; °vraṇā 75. [0316-c]
