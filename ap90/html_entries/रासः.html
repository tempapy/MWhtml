• rāsaḥ 1 An uproar, a din, confused noise. --2 A sound in general. --3 Speech. --4 A kind of dance practised by Kṛṣṇa and the cowherds but particularly the gopis or cowherdesses of Vṛndāvana; utsṛjya rāse rasaṃ gacchaṃtīṃ Ve. 1. 2; rāse harimiha vihitavilāsaṃ smarati mano mama kṛtaparihāsaṃ Gīt. 2; also Gīt. 1. --5 A chain. --Comp. --īśvarī N. of Rādhā. --krīḍā, --maṃḍalaṃ a sportive dance, the circular dance of Kṛṣṇa and the cowherdesses of Vṛndāvana.
