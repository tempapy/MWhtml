• avaṣṭaṃbhamaya a. (yī f.) Golden, made of gold, or as large as a post; raghoravaṣṭaṃbhamayena patriṇā R. 3. 53 (a° is usually rendered in the above manner, but from the immediate context, it should more properly mean ‘full of dignified boldness’, ‘breathing defiance’). [0189-c]
