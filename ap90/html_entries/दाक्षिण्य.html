dākṣiṇya a. [dakṣiṇasya bhāvaḥ ṣyañ] Relating to a sacrificial gift.
      --ṇyaṃ
      1 (a) Politeness, civility, courtesy; tasya dākṣiṇyarūḍhena nāmnā magadhavaṃśajā R. 1. 31. (b) Kindness; V. 1. 2; Bh. 2. 22; Māl. 1. 8.
      2 Insincere or overcourteous conduct of a lover (towards his beloved); Ś. 6. 4; it is thus defined: --dākṣiṇyaṃ ceṣṭayā vācā paracittānuvartanam.
      3 The state of relating to or coming from the south; snehadākṣiṇyayoryogāt kāmīva pratibhāti me V. 2. 4 (where the word has sense {1} or {2} also).
      4 Concord, harmony, agreement.
      5 Honesty, candour.
      6 Talent, cleverness. [0562-c]
