• vṛṣabhaḥ [vṛs-abhac kicca] 1 A bull. --2 Any male animal. --3 Anything best or eminent of its class (at the end of comp.); dvijavṛṣabhaḥ Ratn. 1. 5, 4. 21. --4 The sign Taurus of the zodiac. --5 A kind of drug; cf. ṛṣabha. --6 An elephant's ear. --7 The orifice or hollow of the ear. --Comp. --īkṣaṇaḥ N. of Viṣṇu. --gatiḥ, --dhvajaḥ epithets of Śiva; R. 2. 36; Ku. 3. 62.
