• vāhanaṃ [vāhayati vah-ṇic lyu lyuṭ vā] 1 Bearing, carrying, conveying. --2 Driving (as a horse). --3 A vehicle, conveyance of any kind; Ms. 7. 75; N. 22. 45. --4 An animal used in riding or draught, as a horse; sa duṣprāpayaśāḥ prāpadāśramaṃ śrāṃtavāhanaḥ R. 1. 48, 9. 25, 60. --5 An elephant.
