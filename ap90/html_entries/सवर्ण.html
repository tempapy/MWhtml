savarṇa a. [samāno varṇo yasya]
      1 Of the same colour.
      2 Of like appearance, like, resembling; durvarṇabhittiriha sāṃdrasudhāsavarṇā Śi. 4. 28; Me. 18; R. 9. 51.
      3 Of the same caste or tribe.
      4 Of the same kind, similar.
      5 Belonging to the same class of letters, requiring the same effort (of the organs of speech) in pronunciation; tulyāsyaprayatnaṃ savarṇaṃ P. I. 1. 9. [1110-a]
