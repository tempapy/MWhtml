stavaka a. (vikā f.) [stu-vun] Praising, eulogizing.
      --kaḥ
      1 A panegyrist, praiser.
      2 Praise, eulogium.
      3 A cluster of blossoms.
      4 Bunch of flowers, nosegay, tuft, bouquet.
      5 A chapter or section of a book.
      6 A multitude; cf. stavaka also. [1145-a]
