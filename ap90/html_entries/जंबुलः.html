• jaṃbulaḥ [jaṃbuṃ tannāmaphalaṃ lāti lā-ka]
      1 A kind of tree (= jaṃbū q. v.).
      2 The Ketaka plant.
    • --laṃ Jest or jesting compliments addressed to the bride and the bridegroom by the friends and female relatives of the bridegroom (or of the bride).
    Comp.
    • --mālikā the same as above. [0507-b]
