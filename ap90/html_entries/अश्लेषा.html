• aśleṣā [na śliṣyati yatrotpannena śiśunā, śliṣ-ghañ Tv.]
      1 The 9th Nakṣatra or lunar mansion containing five stars.
      2 Disunion, disjunction.
    Comp.
    • --jaḥ, --bhavaḥ, --bhūḥ N. of Ketu, i. e. the descending node.
    • --śāṃtiḥ f. an expiatory ceremony performed on account of the birth of a child under the Aśleṣā. [0203-b]
