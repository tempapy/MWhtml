pradyotaḥ
      1 Irradiating, lighting, illuminating.
      2 Splendour, light, lustre.
      3 A ray of light.
      4 N. of a king of Ujjayinī, whose daughter Vatsa married; pradyotasya priyaduhitaraṃ vatsarājo'tra jahne Me. (considered as an interpolation by Malli.); Ratn. 1. 10. [0749-b]
