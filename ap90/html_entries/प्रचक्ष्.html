• pracakṣ 2 A.
      1 To say, speak, lay down; svajanāśru kilātisaṃtataṃ dahati pretamiti pracakṣate R. 8. 86.
      2 To tell, relate.
      3 To consider, regard, deem.
      4 To name, call; yo'syātmamaḥ kārayitā taṃ kṣetrajñaṃ pracakṣate Ms. 12. 12; 2. 17; 3. 28; 10. 14. [0728-b]
