• kṣatriyaḥ [kṣatre rāṣṭre sādhu tasyāpatyaṃ jātau vā ghaḥ Tv.] A member of the military or second caste; brāhmaṇaḥ kṣatriyo vaiśyastrayo varṇā dvijātayaḥ Ms. 10. 4. --yaṃ The rank or power of the Kshatriya class.
