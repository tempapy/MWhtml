• viniyogaḥ 1 Separation, parting, detachment. --2 Leaving, giving up, abandoning. --3 Employment, use, application, disposal; babhūva viniyogajñaḥ sādhanīyeṣu vastuṣu R. 17. 67; prāṇāyāme viniyāgeḥ &c. --4 Appointment to a duty, commission, charge; viniyogaprasādā hi kiṃkarāḥ prabhaviṣṇuṣu Ku. 6. 62. --5 An obstacle, impediment.
