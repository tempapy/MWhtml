muṣkaḥ [muṣ-kak]
      1 A testicle.
      2 The scrotum
      3 A muscular or robust man.
      4 A mass, heap, quantity, multitude.
      5 A thief.
    Comp.
      --kacchūḥ f. an eruption on the scrotum.
      --deśaḥ the region of the scrotum.
      --śūnyaḥ a eunuch, a castrated person.
      --śophaḥ swelling of the testicles. [0872-b]
