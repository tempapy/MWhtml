• aniyata a.
      1 Uncontrolled, unrestricted.
      2 Indefinite, uncertain, not fixed; irregular (forms also); °velaṃ āhāro'śyate Ś. 2 at irregular hours.
      3 Causeless, casual, incidental, occasional; °ruditasmitaṃ (vadanakamalakaṃ) U. 4. 4; Māl. 10. 2.
    Comp.
    • --aṃkaḥ an indeterminate digit (in Math.).
    • --ātman a. not self-possessed, whose soul is not properly controlled.
    • --puṃskā a woman loose in conduct, unchaste.
    • --vṛtti a. {1} having no regular or fixed employment or application (as a word). {2} having no regular income. [0064-c]
