• abhimarśaḥ, --rśanaṃ, --marṣaḥ, --rṣaṇaṃ
      1 Touch, contact; uṣṇāṃśukarabhimarṣāt Śi. 4. 16.
      2 Assault, violence, attack, outraging, touching carnally, sexual intercourse; kṛtābhimarṣāmanumanyamānaḥ Ś. 5. 20 carnally touched or embraced, seduced, outraged; parābhimarśo na tavāsti Ku. 5. 43 (Malli. = paradharṣaṇaṃ); Ms. 8. 352, Y. 2. 284. [0135-c]
