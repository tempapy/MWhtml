abhisārin a. Going to meet, visiting; attacking, rushing out, going forth; yuddhabhisāriṇaḥ U. 5. --ṇī
    • 1 = abhisārikā see above.
    • 2 N. of a species of the triṣṭubh metre in which the Pādas contain 12 instead of 11 syllables, and which is therefore said to approach (abhisarati) another metre called jagatī. [0141-c]
