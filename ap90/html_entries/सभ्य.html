• sabhya a. [sabhāyāṃ sādhu yat] 1 Belonging to an assembly. --2 Fit for society. --3 Refined, polished, civilized. --4 Well-bred, polite, civil, courteous; R. 1. 55, Ku. 7. 29. --5 Confidential, trusty, faithful. --bhyaḥ 1 An assessor. --2 An assistant at an assembly. --3 A person of honourable parentage. --4 The keeper of a gaming-house. --5 The servant of the keeper of a gaming-house. --6 N. of one of the five sacred fires. [1092-c]
