vapus a. [vap-usi] Handsome, beautiful (Ved.) --n.
      1 (a) Body, person; (smaraṃ) vapuṣā svena niyojayiṣyati Ku. 4. 42; navaṃ vayaḥ kāṃtamidaṃ vapuśca R. 2. 47; Śi. 10. 50. (b) Form, figure, appearance; likhitavapuṣau śaṃkhapadmau ca dṛṣṭvā Me. 80; parighaḥ kṣatajatulyavapuḥ Bṛ. S. 30. 25.
      2 Essence, nature; Ms. 5. 96.
      3 Beauty, a beautiful form or appearance.
      4 Ved. A wonderful phenomenon, wonder.
      5 Ved. Water.
    Comp.
      --guṇaḥ, --prakarṣaḥ excellence of form, personal beauty; saṃdhukṣayaṃtīva vapurguṇena Ku. 3. 52; vapuḥprakarṣādajayadguruṃ raghuḥ R. 3. 35; Ki. 3. 2. --dhara a. {1} embodied. {2} beautiful.
      --sravaḥ a humour of the body. [0944-a]
