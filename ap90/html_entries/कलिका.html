kalikā A digit of the moon. [0386-c]
      kalikā, --kaliḥ f.
    • 1 An unblown flower, a bud; cūtānāṃ ciranirgatāpi kalikā badhnāti na svaṃ rajaḥ Ś. 6. 3; kimāmrakalikābhaṃgamārabhase Ś. 6; Rs. 6. 17; R. 9. 33.
    • 2 A digit, streak.
    • 3 The bottom or peg of the Indian lute. [0387-b]
