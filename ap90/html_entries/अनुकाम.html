anukāma a. [kāmasya sadṛśaḥ anurūpo vā]
      1 Agreeable to desire, according to one's desire.
      2 [anukāmayate kam-ac] Desirous, lustful.
      --maṃ ind. At will, according to desire or wish, as desired, at pleasure.
      --maḥ [ anurūpaḥ kāmaḥ] Proper or worthy desire; desire; °kṛt fulfilling one's desires. [0067-b]
