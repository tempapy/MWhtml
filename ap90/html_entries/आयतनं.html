• āyatanaṃ [āyataṃte'tra, yat ādhāre lyuṭ] 1 Place, abode, house, resting-place; (fig. also); śūlāyatanāḥ Mu. 7 hangmen; snehastadekāyatanaṃ jagāma Ku. 7. 5 was centred in her; R. 3. 36; sarvāvinayānāmekaikamapyeṣāmāyatanaṃ K. 103; °mṛgeṇa 130 domestic deer; Chāṇ. 32; (hence) a receptacle, home, support, seat. --2 The place of the sacred fire, altar, shed for sacrifices. --3 A sanctuary, sacred place; as in devāyatanaṃ, maṭhāyatanaṃ &c. --4 The site of a house, groundplot. --5 A barn. --6 An inner seat (with Buddhists who consider the five senses with manas as the six Āyatanas). --7 The cause of disease.
