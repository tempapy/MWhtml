svābhāvika a. (kī f.) [ svabhāvādāgataḥ ḍhañ] Belonging to one's own nature, innate, inherent, peculiar, natural; svābhāvikaṃ vinītatvaṃ teṣāṃ vinayakarmaṇā . mumūrccha sahajaṃ tejo haviṣeva havirbhujāṃ R. 10. 79, 5. 69, Ku. 6. 71. --kāḥ m. pl. A sect of Buddhists who accounted for all things by the laws of nature. [1162-b]
