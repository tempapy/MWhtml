• hiṃsā [hiṃs-a]
      1 Injury, mischief, wrong, harm, hurt (said to be of three kinds:--kāyika ‘personal’, vācika ‘verbal’, and mānasika ‘mental’); ahiṃsā paramo dharmaḥ.
      2 Killing, slaying, destruction; R. 5. 57; 3. 313; Ms. 10. 63.
      3 Robbery, plunder.
    Comp.
    • --ātmaka a. injurious, destructive.
    • --karman n. {1} any hurtful or injurious act. {2} magic used to effect the ruin or injury of an enemy (= abhicāra q. v.).
    • --prāṇin m. a noxious animal.
    • --rata a. delighting in mischief.
    • --ruci a, intent on or delighting in mischief; Māl. 5. 29, --samudbhava a, arising from injury. [1171-a]
