• abhibhūti a. That which defeats, conquers &c. --tiḥ 1 Predominance, prevalence, excessive or superior power. --2 Conquering, defeat, subjugation; abhibhūtibhayādasūnataḥ sukhamujjhaṃti na dhāma māninaḥ Ki. 2. 20. --3 Disrespect, disgrace, humiliation. --Comp. --ojas a. of superior or predominant power. (--n.). superior power. [0134-b]
