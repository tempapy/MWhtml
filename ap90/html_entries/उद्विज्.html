• udvij {c6c} A. (P. epic.) 1 To be grieved or afflicted, be agitated; nodvijetprāpyaṃ cāpriyaṃ Bg. 5. 20; tremble, shake (lit. and fig.). --2 To fear, be afraid of, shrink from, abhor (with abl.); tīkṣṇādudvijate Mu. 3. 5; Bg. 12. 15; lokāpavādānnodvignaṃ K. 197 nāyamudvijituṃ kālaḥ svāmikāryāt Bk. 7. 92. --3 To be tired or sick of, be disgusted with; jīvitādudvijamānena Māl. 3; sometimes with gen.; yā mamodvijate nityaṃ sādya māmavagūhate Pt. 4. 76. --4 To grieve, afflict, frighten. --Caus. 1 To trouble, harass, afflict, oppress; udvejayatyaṃgulipārṣṇibhāgān Ku. 1. 11; udvejitā vṛṣṭibhiḥ 5; mlecchairudvejyamānā Mu. 7. 19. --2 To terrify, frighten; udvejitāḥ kūjitaiḥ U. 2. 29. --3 To produce disgust or abhorrence; ramaṇīyāpyudvejayati K. 12; sīdhupānodvejitasya M. 3; Ś. 2.
