ṛdh 4 5 P. (ṛdhyati, ṛdhnoti, ānardha, ārdhīt, ardhituṃ, ṛddha); sometimes 7 P. (ṛṇaddhi)
    • 1 To prosper, flourish, thrive, succeed; nābrahma kṣatramṛdhnoti Ms. 9. 322.
    • 2 To grow, increase (fig. also).
    • 3 To cause to succeed, make prosperous.
    • 4 To satisfy, gratify, please, propitiate; Māl. 5. 29.
    • 5 To accomplish. --Desid. īrtsati or ardidhiṣati. [0350-a]
