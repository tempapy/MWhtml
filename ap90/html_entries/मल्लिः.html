• malliḥ --llī f. [mall-in vā ṅīp] A kind of jasmine. --Comp. --gaṃdhi n. a kind of agallochum. --nāthaḥ N. of a celebrated commentator who probably lived in the fourteenth or fifteenth century; (he has written commentaries on raghuvaṃṇa, kumārasaṃbhava, meghadūta, kirātārjunīya, naiṣadhacārati, and śiśupālavadha). --patraṃ a mushroom. [0848-c]
