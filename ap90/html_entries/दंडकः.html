daṃḍakaḥ
      1 A stick, staff &c.
      2 A line, row.
      3 N. of a metre; see App. I.
      --kaḥ, --kā, --kaṃ N. of a celebrated district in the Deccan situated between the rivers Narmadā and Godāvarī; (it was a vast region said to be tenantless in the time of Rāma); prāptāni duḥkhānyapi daṃḍakeṣu R. 14. 25; kiṃ nāma daṃḍakeyaṃ U. 2; kvāyodhyāyāḥ punarupagamo daṃḍakāyāṃ vane vaḥ U. 2. 13, 14, 15. [0555-c]
