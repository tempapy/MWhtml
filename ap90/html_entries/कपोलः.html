kapolaḥ [Uṇ. 1. 66] A cheek; kṣāmakṣāmakapolamānanaṃ Ś. 3. 10, 6. 14; R. 4. 68. --lī The knee-cap.
      Comp. any object against which anything (especially the cheeks) is rubbed; Ki. 5. 36. --phalakaḥ the (broad) cheeks.
      f. the temples and cheeks; or excellent (i. e. broad) cheeks; cf. gaṃḍamitti.
      the flush in the cheek. [0377-c]
