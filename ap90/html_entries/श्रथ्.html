• śrath
      I. 1 9 P. (śrathati, śrathnāti) To hurt, injure, kill.
      II. 1 P., 10 U. (śrayati, śrāthayati-te)
      1 To hurt, kill.
      2 To untie, loosen, liberate, release.
      III. 10 U. (śrayapati-te)
      1 To make efforts, be occupied or busy.
      2 To be weak or infirm.
      3 To be glad. [1058-b]
