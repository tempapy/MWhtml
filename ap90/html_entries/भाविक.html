bhāvika a. (kī f.) [bhāvena nirvṛttaṃ ṭhak]
      1 Natural, real, inherent, innate.
      2 Sentimental, pervaded by a feeling or sentiment.
      3 Future.
      --kaḥ An equation involving the products of unknown quantities.
      --kaṃ
      1 Language full of love or passion.
      2 (In Rhet.) A figure of speech which consists in describing the past or future so vividly that it appears to be actually present. It is thus defined by Mammaṭa; pratyaṃkṣā iva yadbhāvāḥ kriyaṃte bhūtabhāvinaḥ . tadbhāvikaṃ K. P. 10. [0815-a]
