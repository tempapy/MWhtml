• sāt ind. A Taddhita affix added to a word to show that something is completely changed into the thing expressed by that word, or that it is left at the complete disposal or control of that thing; bhasmasāt bhū ‘to be completely reduced to ashes’; agnisātkṛtvā M. 5; bhasmasātkṛtavataḥ pitṛdviṣaḥ pātrasācca vasudhāṃ sasāgarāṃ R. 11. 86; vibhajya merurna yadarthisātkṛtaḥ N. 1. 16; so brāhmaṇasāt, rājasāt &c. Śi. 14. 36. [1114-a]
