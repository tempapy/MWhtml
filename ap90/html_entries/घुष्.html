• ghuṣ {vI.v} {c1c} P., {c10c} U. (ghoṣati, ghoṣayatite, ghuṣita, ghuṣṭa or ghoṣita) 1 To sound, make any sound or noise. --2 To cry or proclaim aloud, announce or declare publicly; sa sa pāpādṛte tāsāṃ duṣyaṃta iti ghuṣyatāṃ Ś. 6. 22; ghoṣayatu manmathanideśaṃ Gīt. 10; iti ghoṣayatīva ḍiṃḍimaḥ kariṇo hastipakāhataḥ kvaṇan H. 2. 86; R. 9. 10. --3 To praise. --4 To fill with cries, make resonant. {vII.v} {c1c} A. (ghuṣate) To be beautiful or brilliant. [0478-a]
