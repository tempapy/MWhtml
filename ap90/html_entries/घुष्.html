ghuṣ
      I. 1 P., 10 U. (ghoṣati, ghoṣayatite, ghuṣita, ghuṣṭa or ghoṣita)
      1 To sound, make any sound or noise.
      2 To cry or proclaim aloud, announce or declare publicly; sa sa pāpādṛte tāsāṃ duṣyaṃta iti ghuṣyatāṃ Ś. 6. 22; ghoṣayatu manmathanideśaṃ Gīt. 10; iti ghoṣayatīva ḍiṃḍimaḥ kariṇo hastipakāhataḥ kvaṇan H. 2. 86; R. 9. 10.
      3 To praise.
      4 To fill with cries, make resonant.
      II. 1 A. (ghuṣate) To be beautiful or brilliant. [0478-a]
