ślāghā [ślāgh a]
      1 Praise, eulogy, commendation; karṇajayadrathayorvā kātra ślāghā Ve. 2.
      2 Self-praise, boast; hate jarati gāṃgeye puraskṛtya śikhaṃḍinaṃ . yā ślāghā pāṃḍuputrāṇāṃ saivāsmākaṃ bhaviṣyati Ve. 2. 4.
      3 Flattery.
      4 Service.
      5 Wish, desire.
    Comp.
