• ālayaḥ, --yaṃ [ālīyate'smin, ālī-ac]
      1 An abode, a house, a dwelling; ālayaṃ devaśatrūṇāṃ sughoraṃ khāṃḍavaṃ vanaṃ Mb.; na hi duṣṭātmanāmāryā nivasaṃtyālaye ciraṃ Rām.; sarvāñjanasthānakṛtālayān Rām. who lived or dwelt in Janasthāna.
      2 A receptacle, seat, place; himālayo nāma nagādhirājaḥ Ku. 1. 1; so devālayaṃ, vidyālayaṃ &c fig. also; duḥkha° Bg. 8. 15; guṇa°.
      3 Contact.
    • --yaṃ ind. Till destruction. [0259-a]
