abhinava a. [ādhikyena navaḥ, bhṛśārthe abhiratra]
      1 (a) Quite new or fresh (in all senses); padapaṃktirdṛśyate'bhinavā Ś. 3. 8; 5. 1; °kaṃṭhaśoṇita 6. 26; Me. 98; R. 9. 29; °vā vadhūḥ K. 2 newly married. (b) Quite young or fresh, blooming, youthful (as body, age &c.); Ś. 1. 19; U. 5. 12; the younger; °śākaṭāyanaḥ; °bhojaḥ &c. (c) Fresh, recent.
      2 Very young, not having experience.
      --vaḥ [abhinu ap] Praise to win over, flattery.
    Comp.
      --udbhid --daḥ a new shoot or bud. --caṃdrārdhavidhiḥ a ceremony performed at the time of the new moon. --tāmarasaṃ {1} a fresh-blown lotus. {2} a kind of metre.
      --yauvana, --vayaska a. youthful, very young.
      --vaiyākaraṇaḥ one who has newly begun his study of grammar. [0131-b]
