vārttā
      1 Staying, abiding.
      2 Tidings, news, intelligence; sāgarikāyāḥ kā vārtā Ratn. 4.
      3 Livelihood, profession.
      4 Agriculture, the occupation of a Vaiśya; R. 16. 2; Ms. 10. 80; Y. 1. 311.
      5 The eggplant.
      6 N. of Durgā.
      7 (In Rhet.) The mere mention of facts without any rhetorical embellishment.
    Comp.
      --āraṃbhaḥ a commercial undertaking or business.
      --mātraṃ {1} mere report. {2} superficial acquaintance with any subject, shallow knowledge.
      --vahaḥ, haraḥ {1} a messenger. {2} a chandler.
      --vṛttiḥ one who lives on agriculture. --vyatikaraḥ general or common report. [0961-c]
