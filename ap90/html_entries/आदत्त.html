ādatta, ātta p. p. [ā-dā-kta]

      2 Agreed to, undertaken, begun.
      3 Attracted.
      4 Drawn out, extracted; gāmāttasārāṃ raghurapyavekṣya R. 5. 26; °balaṃ 11. 76 taken away.
