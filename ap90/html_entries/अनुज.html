• anuja --jāta p. p. Born after, later, younger; rāmamanujātaḥ P. III. 4. 72; asau kumārastamajo'nujātaḥ R. 6. 78; pumāṃsamanurudhya jātā pumanujā Sk.; so stryanujā.
    • --jaḥ, --jātaḥ
      1 A younger brother; Ms. 5. 58.
      2 A cadet; born again, after born, younger, later.
      3 Taking after.
      4 Born again, invested with the sacred thread.
    • --jā, --jātā
      1 A younger sister.
      2 N. of a plant (trāyamāṇālatā).
    • --jaṃ N. of a plant (prapauṃḍarīka).
    Comp.
    • --avara a. lower than the younger, youngest. [0070-a]
