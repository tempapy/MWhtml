• netṛ m. [nī-tṛc]
      1 One who leads or guides, a leader, conductor, manager, guide (of elephants, animals &c.); R. 4. 75, 14. 22, 16. 30; Me. 69; netāśvasya srudhnaṃ srughnasya vā Sk.; Mu. 7. 14.
      2 A director, preceptor; Bh. 2. 88.
      3 A chief, master, head.
      4 An inflictor (as of punishment); Ms. 7. 25.
      5 An owner.
      6 The hero of a drama.
      7 The numeral ‘two’.
      8 N. of Viṣṇu. [0648-a]
