• śaniḥ [śo-ani kicca Tv.]
      1 The planet Saturn (the son of the sun and represented as of a black colour or dressed in dark-coloured clothes).
      2 Saturday.
      3 N. of Śiva.
    Comp.
    • --jaṃ black pepper.
    • --pradoṣaḥ a term for the (evening) worship of Śiva performed on the thirteenth day of a lunar fortnight when it falls on a Saturday.
    • --prasūḥ f. an epithet of Chāyā, wife of the sun; cf. saṃjñā.
    • --priyaṃ a sapphire.
    • --vāraḥ, --vāsaraḥ Saturday. [1032-a]
