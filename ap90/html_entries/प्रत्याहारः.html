pratyāhāraḥ
      1 Drawing back, marching back, retreat.
      2 Keeping back, withholding.
      3 Restraining the organs.
      4 Dissolution of the world,
      5 (In gram.) The comprehension of several letters or affixes into one syllable, effected by combining the first letter of a Sūtra with its final indicatory letter, or in the case of several Sūtras, with the final letter of the last member; thus aṇ is the pratyāhāra of the Sūtra aïuṇ; ac (vowels) of the four Sūtras aïuṇ, ṛḷk, eoṅ, aiauc; hal of the consonants; al of all letters.
      6 Abridgment. [0746-c]
