drohaḥ [druh bhāve-ghañ]
      1 Plotting against, seeking to hurt or assail, injury, mischief, malice; adrohaśapathaṃ kṛtvā Pt. 2. 35; Bg. 1. 38; Ms. 2. 161, 7. 48, 9. 17.
      2 Treachery, perfidy.
      3 Wrong, offence.
      4 Rebellion.
    Comp.
      --aṭaḥ {1} a religious impostor, hypocrite, impostor. {2} a hunter. {3} a false man.
      --ciṃtanaṃ a malicious thought, malice prepense, a thought or attempt to injure.
      --buddhi a. bent on mischief or evil design. (--ddhiḥ f.) a wicked or evil purpose. [0585-c]
