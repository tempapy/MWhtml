kapāṭaḥ --ṭaṃ [kaṃ vātaṃ pāṭayati tadgatiṃ ruṇaddhi Tv.]
    • 1 Leaf or panel of a door; kapāṭhavakṣāḥ pariṇaddhakaṃdharaḥ R. 3. 34; svargadvārakapāṭapāṭanapaṭurdharmopi nopārjitaḥ Bh. 3. 11.
    • 2 A door; Śi. 11. 60.
      Comp. {1} the opening of a door. {2} a door-key.
      a house-breaker, thief.
      a. broad-chested; R. 3. 34. --saṃdhiḥ {1} the junction of the leaves of a door. {2} a manner of multiplying. --saṃdhikaḥ a disease of the ear. [0377-a]
