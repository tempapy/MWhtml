• bhālaṃ [bhā-lac]
      1 The forehead, brow; yaddhātrā nijabhālapaṭṭalikhitaṃ stokaṃ mahadvā dhanaṃ Bh. 2. 49; (smarasya) vapuḥ sadyo bhālānalabhasitajālāspadamabhūt Bv. 1. 84.
      2 Light.
      3 Darkness.
    Comp.
    • --aṃkaḥ {1} a man born with lucky lines on his forehead. {2} an epithet of Śiva. {3} a saw. {4} a tortoise.
    • --caṃdraḥ {1} an epithet of Śiva. {2} of Gaṇeśa. (--drā) N. of Durgā.
    • --darśanaḥ N. of Śiva.
    • --darśanaṃ red lead.
    • --darśin a. ‘looking at or watching the brow’, said of a servant who is attentive to his master's wishes.
    • --dṛś m., --locanaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
