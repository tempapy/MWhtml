• utsargaḥ 1 Laying or leaving side, abandoning, suspension; Ku. 7. 45. --2 Pouring out, dropping down, emission; toyotsargadrutataragatiḥ Me. 19, 37; so śukra°. --3 A gift, donation, giving away; Ms. 11. 194. --4 Spending; artha° Mu. 3. --5 Loosening, letting loose; as in vṛṣotsargaḥ. --6 An oblation, libation. --7 Excretion, voiding by stool &c. purīṣa°, malamūtra°. --8 Completion (as of study or a vow); cf. utsṛṣṭā vai vedāḥ (opp. upākṛtā vai vedāḥ). --9 A general rule or precept (opp. apavāda a particular rule or exception); apavādairivotsargāḥ kṛtavyāvṛttayaḥ paraiḥ Ku. 2. 27; apavāda ivotsargaṃ vyāvartayitumīśvaraḥ R. 15. 7 --10 Offering what is promised (to gods, Brāhmaṇas &c.) with due ceremonies. --11 The anus; mitramutsarge Ms. 12. 121.
