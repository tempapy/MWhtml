vyāhṛtiḥ f.
      1 Utterance, speech, words; na hīśvaravyāhṛtayaḥ kadācitpuṣṇaṃti loke viparītamarthaṃ Ku. 3. 63.
      2 Statement, expression; bhūtārthavyāhṛtiḥ sā hi na stutiḥ paramoṣṭhinaḥ R. 10. 33.
      3 A mystic word uttered by every Brāhmaṇa in performing his daily Saṃdhyā adoration; (these Vyāhṛtis are three bhūr, bhuvas and svas or svar usually repeated after om; cf. Ms. 2. 76; according to some they are seven in number). [1024-c]
