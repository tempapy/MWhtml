• apī [api-i] {c2c} P. (Mostly Vedic) 1 To go in or near, enter into or upon, approach. --2 To have a share in, partake of, suffer; jarāmṛtyuṃ punarevāpiyaṃti Muṇḍ. --3 To join, flow, flow into, dissolve, be resolved into; svamapīto bhavati tasmādenaṃ svapitītyācakṣate Ch. Up. --4 To die. --5 To pour out (as a river). [0118-a]
