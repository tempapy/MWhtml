śākta a. ktī f. [śaktirdevatā'sya aṇ.]
      1 Relating to power.
      2 Relating to Śakti or the female personification of divine energy --ktaḥ A worshipper of Śakti; (the Śāktas are generally worshippers of Durgā representing the female personification of divine energy, and the ritual enjoined to them is of two kinds, the pure or right-hand ritual dakṣiṇācāra, and impure or left-hand ritual vāmācāra q. q. v. v.). [1038-c]
