• vaidarbhaḥ [vidarbha-aṇ]
      1 A king of Vidarbha.
      2 A gum-boil.
    • --rbhaṃ Crafty speech.
    • --rbhīṃ
      1 N. of Dams yantī.
      2 Of Rukmiṇī.
      3 A particular style of composition; thus defined in S. D.: --mādhuryavyaṃjakairvarṇai racanā lalitātmikā . avṛttiralpavṛttirvā vaidarbhī rītiriṣyate .. 626. Daṇḍin very minutely distinguishes this style from the Gauḍīya; see Kāv. 1. 41-53.
      4 N. of the wife of Agastya. [1013-b]
