kedāraḥ
      1 A field under water; meadow.
      2 A basin for water round the root of a tree.
      3 A mountain.
      4 A particular mountain forming part of the Himālayas (modern Kedār).
      5 A form of Śiva.
    Comp.
      --khaḍaṃ a small dyke, earth raised to keep out water.
      --nāthaḥ a particular form of Śiva. [0423-a]
