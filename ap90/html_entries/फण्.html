phaṇ 1 P. (phaṇati, phaṇita)
      1 To move, move about; rurujurbhejire pheṇurbahudhā harirākṣasāḥ Bk. 14. 78.
      2 To produce easily or without exertion; (this sense according to some belongs to the Caus. of phaṇ). --Caus. (phāṇayati) To skim, take off (the surface of a fluid.). [0782-1]
