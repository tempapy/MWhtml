unnam 1 P.
      1 (a) To rise, appear; unnamyonnamya līyaṃte daridrāṇāṃ manorathāḥ Pt. 2. 91. (b) To hang over, arise, impend; unnamatyakāladurdinam Mk. 4, 5 an untimely storm impends; bahalonnamadaṃbudharāḥ Māl. 9. 18. (c) To rise, ascend, go up (fig. also); unnamati namati varṣati garjati meghaḥ Mk. 5; namratvenonnamaṃtaḥ Bh. 2. 69.
      2 To bend up, raise, elevate, erect; Ki. 16. 35. --Caus. (namayati)
