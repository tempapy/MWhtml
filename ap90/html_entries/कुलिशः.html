• kuliśaḥ, --śaṃ
      1 The thunderbolt of Indra: vṛtrasya haṃtuḥ kuliśaṃ kuṃṭhitāśrīva lakṣyate Ku. 2. 20; Pt. 1; avedanājñaṃ kuliśakṣatānāṃ Ku. 1. 20; R. 3. 68; 4. 88; Amaru. 96.
      2 Ved. An axe, a hatchet.
      3 The point or end of a thing; Me. 61.
