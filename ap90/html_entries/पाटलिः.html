• pāṭaliḥ f. The trumpet-flower. --Comp. --putraṃ N. of an ancient city, the capital of Magadha, situated near the confluence of the Śoṇa and the Ganges, and identified by some with the modern Pātnā. It is also known by the names of puṣpapura, kusumapura; see Mu. 2, 3, and 4. 16, and R. 6. 24 also. [0691-a]
