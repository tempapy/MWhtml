• rūpaka a. [rūp-ṇvul]
      1 Bodily, corporeal.
      2 Figurative (as words &c.).
    • --kaḥ A particular coin, a rupee.
    • --kaṃ
      1 Form, figure, shape (at the end of comp.).
      2 Any manifestation or representation.
      3 A sign, feature.
      4 A kind, species
      5 A drama, play, a dramatic composition; (one of the two main subdivisions of dramatic compositions; it is divided into ten classes; there are eighteen minor divisions of it called uparūpaka); dṛśyaṃ tatrābhineyaṃ tadrūpāropāttu rūpakaṃ S. D. 272-3.
      6 (In Rhet.) A figure of speech corresponding to the English metaphor, in which the upameya is represented as being identical with the upamāna; tadrūpakamabhedo ya upamānopameyayoḥ K. P. 10 (see ad loc. for details).
      7 A kind of weight (= three gunjās).
    Comp.
    • --tālaḥ a particular time in music.
    • --śabdaḥ a figurative or metaphorical expression. [0916-b]
