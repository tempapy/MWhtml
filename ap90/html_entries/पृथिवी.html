pṛthivī [cf. Uṇ. 1. 184]
      1 The earth; (sometimes written pṛthivi also)
      2 Ground, soil.
      3 The earth considered as one of the nine substances or five primary elements.
    Comp.
      --iṃdraḥ, --īśaḥ, --kṣit m., --pālaḥ, --pālakaḥ, --bhuj m., --bhujaḥ, --śukraḥ a king.
      --talaṃ the surface of the earth.
      --patiḥ {1} a king. {2} Yama, the god of death.
      --bhaṃḍalaḥ, --laṃ the circuit of the earth.
      --ruhaḥ a tree: pavamānaḥ pṛthivīruhāniva R. 8. 9. --lokaḥ terrestrial world, the earth. [0718-c]
