• mārīca a. (cī f.) Belonging to or composed by Marīci. --caḥ 1 N. of a demon, son of Sunda and Tāḍakā. He assumed the form of a golden deer, and thus enticed Rāma to a considerable distance from Sītā, so that Rāvaṇa found a good opportunity to carry her off. --2 A large or royal elephant. --3 A kind of plant. --4 N. of the sage Kaśyapa; Ś. 7. 9. --5 A sacrificing priest. --cī N. of the mother of Śākyamuni. --caṃ A collection of pepper shrubs.
