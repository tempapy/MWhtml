• akutaḥ adv. [na kutaḥ na. ta.] Not from any where (in comp. only). --Comp. --calaḥ N. of Śiva (not moveable from any cause). --bhaya a [nāsti kutopi bhayaṃ yasya] Secure, not threatened from any quarter, free from danger or fear, safe; mādṛśānāmapi °yaḥ saṃcāro jātaḥ U. 2; yāni trīṇyakutobhayāni ca padānyāsankharāyodhane v. l. for aparāṅmukhāṇi 5. 35. akutāścidbhaya also in the same sense.
