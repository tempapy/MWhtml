• upadravaḥ 1 An unhappy accident, misfortune, calamity. --2 Injury, trouble, harm; puṃsāmasamarthānāmupadravāyātmano bhavetkopaḥ Pt. 1. 324; nirupadravaṃ sthānaṃ Pt. 1. --3 Outrage, violence. --4 A national distress (whether caused by the king or famine, seasons &c.). --5 A national disturbance, rebellion. --6 A symptom, a supervenient disease (one brought on whilst a person is suffering from another).
