• āyu a. [i-uṇ Uṇ. 1. 2] Ved. Living, going, moveable. --yuḥ 1 A living being, man. --2 Mankind, human race. --3 Living beings taken collectively. --4 The first man. --5 Life, duration of life. --6 Wind. --7 A son, descendant, offspring. --8 The son of Purūravas and Urvaśī. --Comp. --ṣak a. attached to; joined with men.
