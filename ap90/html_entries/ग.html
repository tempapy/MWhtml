ga a. (Used only at the end of comp.) Who or what goes, going, moving, being, staying, remaining, having sexual intercourse with &c.
      --gaḥ
      1 A Gandharva.
      2 An epithet of Gaṇeśa.
      3 A long syllable (used as an abbreviation of guru), (in prosody).
      --gā, --gaṃ A song. [0446-a]
