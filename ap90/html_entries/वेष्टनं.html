• veṣṭanaṃ [veṣṭ-lyuṭ] 1 Encompassing, encircling, surrounding; aṃguliveṣṭanaṃ ‘a finger-ring.’ --2 Coiling round, twisting round; bhogiveṣṭanamārgeṣu caṃdanānāṃ samarpitaṃ R. 4. 48. --3 An envelope, a wrapper, cover, covering, case. --4 A turban, tiara; aspṛṣṭālakaveṣṭanau R. 1. 42; śirasā veṣṭana śobhinā 8. 12. --5 An enclosure, a fence; krīḍāśailaḥ kanakakadalīveṣṭanaprekṣaṇīyaḥ Me. 77. --6 A girdle, zone. --7 A bandage. --8 The outer ear. --9 Bdellium. --10 A particular attitude in dancing. --11 Grasping, seizing.
