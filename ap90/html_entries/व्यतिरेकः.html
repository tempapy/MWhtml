• vyatirekaḥ 1 Distinction, difference. --2 Separation from. --3 Exclusion, exception. --4 Excellence, surpassing, excelling. --5 Contrast, dissimilarity. --6 (In logic) Logical discontinuance (opp. anvaya q. v.); yatra sādhyābhāvastatra hetvabhāva iti vyatirekavyāptiḥ (e. g. yatra vahnirnāsti tatra dhūmo nāsti is an instance of vyatirekavyāpti). --7 (In Rhet.) A figure of speech which consists in representing the Upameya as superior to the Upamāna in some particular respects; upamānādyadanyasya vyatirekaḥ sa eva saḥ K. P. 10. (vyatirekeṇa means ‘except, without’; vyatireke ‘on the contrary supposition.’). --Comp. --vyāpti see vyatireka (6) above. [1017-b]
