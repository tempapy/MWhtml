• pakṣin a. (ṇī f.) [pakṣa astyarthe ini] 1 {1} Winged. --2 Furnished with wings. --3 Siding with, adhering to the party of. --m. 1 A bird. --2 An arrow. --3 An epithet of Śiva. --Comp. --iṃdraḥ, --pravaraḥ, -rāj m. --rājaḥ siṃhaḥ, -svāmin m. epithets of Garuḍa. --kīṭaḥ an insignificant bird. --patiḥ an epithet of Sampāti. --pānīyaśālikā a trough or reservoir for watering birds. --puṃgavaḥ an epithet of Jaṭāyu. --bālakaḥ, --śāvakaḥ a young bird. --śālā {1} a nest. {2} an aviary.
