samarth 10 U.
      1 To believe, consider, regard, think; samarthaye yatprathamaṃ priyāṃ prati V. 4. 39; mayā na sādhu samarthitaṃ V. 2; anupayuktamiva ātmānaṃ samarthaye Ś. 7, 3. 19.
      2 To corroborate, support, substantiate by proof; uktamevārthamudāharaṇena samarthayati.
      3 To deliberate, think or reflect about.
      4 To make ready, prepare.
      5 To maintain, assert, enforce.
      6 To imply, suggest.
      7 To approve. [1094-b]
