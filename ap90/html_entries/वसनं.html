vasanaṃ [vas-ādhāre lyuṭ]
      1 Dwelling, residing, staying.
      2 A house, residence.
      3 Dressing, clothing, covering.
      4 A garment, cloth, dress, clothes; vasane paridhūsare vasānā Ś. 7. 21; utsaṃge vā malinavasane saumya nikṣipya vīṇāṃ Me. 86, 41.
      5 An ornament worn (by women) round the loins, (probably for rasanā). [0953-a]
