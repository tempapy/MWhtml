idā ind. Now, at this (present) moment; oft. with ahan; idācidahnaḥ; idā hyaḥ only yesterday.
    Comp.
      --vatsaraḥ, so iduvatsaraḥ or idvatsaraḥ one of the five years in which gifts of clothes and food are said to be productive of great rewards.
      --vatsarīya or idvatsarīya a. belonging to such a year. [0276-b]
