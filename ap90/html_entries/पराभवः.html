• parābhavaḥ 1 (a) Defeat, discomfiture, overthrow; parābhavo'pyutsava eva mānināṃ Ki. 1. 41. (b) Mortification, humiliation; kuberasya manaḥ śalyaṃ śaṃsatīva parābhavaṃ Ku. 2. 22; tava padapallavavairiparābhavamidamanubhavatu suveśaṃ Gīt. 12. --2 Contempt, disregard, disrespect. --3 Destruction. --4 Disappearance, separation (sometimes written parābhāva).
