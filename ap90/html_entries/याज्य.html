• yājya a.
      1 To be sacrificed.
      2 Sacrificial.
      3 One for whom a sacrifice is performed.
      4 One who is allowed by Śāstras to sacrifice.
    • --jyaḥ
      1 A sacrificer, the institutor of a sacrifice.
      2 The performer of a sacrifice for another.
    • --jyaṃ The presents or fee received for officiating at a sacrifice. [0892-a]
