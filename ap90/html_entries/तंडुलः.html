• taṃḍulaḥ [taṃḍ-ulac] Grain after threshing, unhusking and winnowing; (especially rice); śasya, dhānya, taṃḍula and anna are thus distinguished from one another:--śasyaṃ kṣetragataṃ proktaṃ satuṣaṃ dhānyamucyate . nistuṣastaṃḍulaḥ proktaḥ svinnamannamudāhṛtaṃ ... --Comp. --aṃbu n. gruel. --utthaṃ-kaṃ rice-gruel. --oghaḥ {1} a prickly sort of bamboo. {2} a heap of grain.
