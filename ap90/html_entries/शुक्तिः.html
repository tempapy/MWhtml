śuktiḥ f. [śac-ktin]
      1 An oyster. shell, pearl-oyster; pātraviśeṣanyastaṃ guṇāṃtaraṃ vrajati śilpamādhātuḥ . jalamiva samudraśuktau muktāphalatāṃ payodasya M. 1. 6; Bh. 2. 67; R. 13. 17.
      2 A conchshell.
      3 A small shell, muscle.
      4 A portion of the skull.
      5 A curl of hair on a horse's breast (or neck); Śi. 5. 4; see Malli. thereon.
      6 A kind of perfume.
      7 A particular weight equal to two Karṣas.
      8 Hemorrhoids.
    Comp.
      --udbhavaṃ, --jaṃ a pearl.
      --puṭaṃ, --peśī a pearl-oyster shell.
      --vadhūḥ the pearl-oyster.
      --vījaṃ a pearl.
      --sparśaḥ dusky spots on a pearl. [1049-a]
