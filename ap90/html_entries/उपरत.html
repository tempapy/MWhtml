uparata p. p.
    • 1 Stopped, ceased; rajasyuparate Ms. 5. 66.
    • 2 Dead; adya daśamo māsastātasyoparatasya Mu. 4.
    • 3 Withdrawn or retired from; raṇāta, kalahāt &c.
    • 4 One who is disgusted with the world and has retired from it.
      Comp. a. having no foe.
      a. ceasing from works, not relying on worldly acts.
      a. one who has renounced all desire for worldly things.
      a. void of desire, indifferent to worldly attachments or possessions. [0328-b]
