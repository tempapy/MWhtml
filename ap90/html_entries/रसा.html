• rasā
      1 The lower or infernal regions, hell.
      2 The earth, ground, soil; Bv. 1. 59; smarasya yuddharaṃgatāṃ rasārasāra sārasā Nalod. 2. 10.
      3 The tongue.
      4 A vine or grapes.
      5 Ved. Moisture.
    Comp.
    • --okas m. an inhabitant of the lower world.
    • --khataḥ a cock.
    • --talaṃ {1} N. of one of the seven regions below the earth; see pātāla. {2} the lower world or hell in general; rājyaṃ yātu rasātalaṃ punaridaṃ na prāṇituṃ kāmaye Bv. 2. 63; or jātiryātu rasātalaṃ Bh. 2. 39. --pāyin m. a dog. [0906-c]
