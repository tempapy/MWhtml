• paraṃpara a. 1 One following the other. --2 Successive, repeated. --raḥ 1 A great-grandson. --2 A kind of deer. --rā 1 An uninterrupted series, regular series, succession; mahatīyaṃ khalvanarthaparaṃparā K. 103; karṇaparaṃparayā ‘from ear to ear, by hear-say’; paraṃparayā āgam ‘to be handed down in regular succession’. --2 A row, line, collection, assemblage (of regular things); toyāṃtarbhāskarālīva reje muniparaṃparā Ku. 6. 49; R. 6. 5, 35. 40; 12. 100. --3 Method, order, due arrangement. --4 Race, family, lineage. --5 Injury, hurting, killing. --raṃ ind. Successively, one after the other. [0668-a]
