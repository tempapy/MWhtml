• luṭ
      I. 1 A. (loṭate)
      1 To resist, repel, oppose.
      2 To shine.
      3 To suffer pain.
      II. 10 U. (loṭayati-te)
      1 To speak.
      2 To shine.
      III. {1} 4 P. (loṭati, luṭyati)
      1 To roll, wallow on the ground; cf. luṭh.
      2 To take away, rob, plunder (perhaps for luṃṭh or luṃṭ). [0930-c]
    • luṭ A technical term used by Pāṇini to denote the First or Periphrastic Future or its terminations. [0930-c]
