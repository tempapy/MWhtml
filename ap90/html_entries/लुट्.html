• luṭ {vI.v} {c1c} A. (loṭate) 1 To resist, repel, oppose. --2 To shine. --3 To suffer pain. {vII.v} {c10c} U. (loṭayati-te) 1 To speak. --2 To shine. {vIII.v} {1} {c4c} P. (loṭati, luṭyati) 1 To roll, wallow on the ground; cf. luṭh. --2 To take away, rob, plunder (perhaps for luṃṭh or luṃṭ). [0930-c]
  • luṭ A technical term used by Pāṇini to denote the First or Periphrastic Future or its terminations. [0930-c]
