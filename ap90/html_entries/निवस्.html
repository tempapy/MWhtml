• nivas
      I. 1 P.
      1 To live, dwell, stay; āho nivatsyati samaṃ hariṇāṃganābhiḥ Ś. 1. 26; nivasiṣyasi mayyeva Bg. 12. 8.
      2 To be, exist; Pt. 1. 31.
      3 To occupy, settle in, take possession of.
      4 To sojourn, pass the night.
      II. 2 A.
      1 To dress, wear or put on clothes.
      2 To change one's clothes. [0637-a]
