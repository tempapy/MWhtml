saikatika a. (kī f.)
      1 Belonging or relating to a sand-bank.
      2 Fluctuating, wavering, living in doubt and error (saṃdehjīvin).
      --kaḥ
      1 A religious mendicant.
      2 An ascetic.
      --kaṃ A thread worn round the wrist or neck to secure good fortune. [1138-b]
