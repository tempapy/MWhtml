jaṃtuḥ [jan-tun]
      1 A creature, a living being, man; Ś. 5. 2; Ms. 3. 77.
      2 The (individual) soul.
      3 An animal of the lowest organization.
      4 People, mankind.
    Comp.
      --kaṃvuḥ {1} a snail's shell. {2} a snail.
      --ghnaḥ {1} the citron. {2} a snail.
      --phalaḥ the Udumbara tree. [0506-a]
