śāriḥ
      1 A chessman, a piece at chess &c.
      2 A small round ball.
      3 A kind of die.
      --riḥ f.
      1 The bird called Sārikā.
      2 Fraud, trick.
      3 An elephant's housings.
    Comp.
      --paṭṭaḥ, --phalaṃ, --phalakaḥ --kaṃ a chequered cloth for playing at chess, draughts &c. [1041-a]
