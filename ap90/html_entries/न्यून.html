nyūna a.
      1 Lessened, diminished, shortened.
      2 Defective, inferior, deficient, wanting, destitute of; as in arthanyūna.
      3 Less (opp. adhika); Y. 2. 116.
      4 Defective (in some organ); pāda°.
      5 Low, wicked, vile, despicable.
      --naṃ ind. Less, in a less degree.
    Comp.
      --aṃga a. maimed, mutilated.
      --adhika a. more or less, unequal.
      --dhī a. deficient in intellect, ignorant, foolish. [0653-c]
