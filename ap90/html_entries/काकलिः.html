• kākaliḥ, --lī f. 1 A low and sweet tone; anubaddhamugdhakākalīsahitaṃ U. 3; Rs. 1. 8. --2 A musical instrument with a low tone used by thieves to ascertain whether a person is asleep or not; phaṇimukhakākalīsaṃdaṃśaka… prabhṛtyanekopakaraṇayuktaḥ Dk. 49. --3 Scissors. --4 The Guñjā plant. --Comp. --ravaḥ the (Indian) cuckoo.
