• citrā [citr-ac]
      1 N. of the fourteenth lunar mansion consisting of one star; himanirmuktayoryoge citrācaṃdramasoriva R. 1. 46.
      2 A kind of snake.
      3 Worldly illusion, unreality.
    Comp.
    • --aṭīraḥ {1} the moon. {2} the forehead spotted with the blood of a goat offered as a victim to a deity.
    • --īśaḥ the moon.
    • --maghā the dawn. [0494-b]
