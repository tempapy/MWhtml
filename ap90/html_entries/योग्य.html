• yogya a. [yogamarhati yat, yuj-ṇyat vā] 1 Fit, proper, suitable, appropriate, qualified; yogyoyaṃ dṛśyate naraḥ. --2 Fit or suitable for, qualified for, capable of, able to (with loc., dat. or even gen. or in comp.). --3 Useful, serviceable. --4 Fit for Yoga or abstract meditation. --5 (In Nyāya phil.) Amenable to the sense, capable of being directly cognized. --gyaḥ 1 A calculator of expedients. --2 The asterism Puṣya. --gyā 1 Exercise or practice in general.; aparaḥ praṇidhānayogyayā marutaḥ paṃcaśarīragocarān R. 8. 19; so mānayogyā Kāv. 2. 243; dhanuryogyā, astrayorayā &c. --2 Martial exercise, drill. --gyaṃ 1 A conveyance, carriage, vehicle. --2 Sandal-wood. --3 A cake. --4 Milk.
