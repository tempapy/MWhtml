• gāṃdharva a. (rvī) [gaṃdharvasyedam-aṇ] Relating to the Gandharvas. --rvaḥ 1 A singer, celestial chorister. --2 One of the eight forms of marriage; gāṃdharvaḥ samayānmithaḥ Y. 1. 61; (for explanation, see gaṃdharvavivāha). --3 A subordinate Veda treating of music attached to the Sāmaveda; see upaveda. --4 A horse. --rvaṃ The art of the Gandharvas; i. e. music, singing; kāpi velā cārudattasya gāṃdharvaṃ śrotuṃ gatasya Mk. 3. --rvī 1 Speech. --2 An epithet of Durgā. --Comp. --citta a. one whose mind is possessed by a Gandharva. --śālā a music saloon, concert-hall.
