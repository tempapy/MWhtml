• jagat a.(tī f.)
      1 Moving, moveable; sūrya ātmā jagatastasthuṣaśca Rv. 1. 115. 1; idaṃ viśvaṃ jagatsarvamajagaccāpi yadbhavet Mb. --m. Wind, air. --n. The world, the universe; jagataḥ pitarau vaṃde pārvatīparameśvarau R. 1. 1.
      2 ‘The world of the soul’, body; Māl. 5. 2. --tī (dual) Heaven and the lower world.
