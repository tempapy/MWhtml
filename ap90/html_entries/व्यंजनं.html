• vyaṃjanaṃ
      1 Making clear, indicating, manifesting.
      2 A mark, token, sign.
      3 A reminder; Māl. 9.
      4 Disguise, garb; nānāvyaṃjanāḥ praṇidhayaḥ Mu. 1; Śi. 2. 56; tapasvivyaṃjanopetāḥ &c.
      5 A consonant.
      6 A mark of the sex, i. e. the male or female organ.
      7 Insignla.
      8 A mark or sign of puberty.
      9 The beard.
      10 A limb, member.
      11 (a) A condiment, sauce, a seasoned article; N. 16. 104. (b) An article used in seasoning food, spices &c.
      12 The last of the three powers of a word by virtue of which it suggests or insinuates a sense; see aṃjana-nā (8) (written vyaṃjanā also in this sense).
      13 A day.
      14 A privy part.
    Comp.
    • --udaya a. followed by a consonant.
    • --saṃdhiḥ the junction or coalition of consonants. [1016-c]
