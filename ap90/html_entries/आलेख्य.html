ālekhya pot. p. To be written, painted &c.
      --khyaṃ
      1 A painting, picture; iti saṃraṃbhiṇo vāṇīrbalasyālekhyadevatāḥ Śi. 2. 67; R. 3. 15; V. 2. 10.
      2 A writing.
    Comp.
      --lekhā a painting.
      --śeṣa a. having nothing left but a painting, i. e. deceased, dead; ālekhyaśeṣasya pituḥ R. 14. 15. [0258-c]
