• upagrahaḥ 1 Confinement, seizure. --2 Defeat, frustration; Mu. 4. 2. --3 A prisoner. --4 Joining, addition. --5 (a) Favour, encouragement, assistance, conciliation; sopagrahaṃ K. 156. (b) Favourableness, kindness, complacence; sopagraha K. 264. --6 Use. --7 A kind of peace purchased by giving over every thing; cf H. 4. 121. --8 The voice or pada of a verb. --9 A pile or heap of kuśa grass. --10 The presiding spirit or cause which directs a planet's motion. --11 A minor planet (rāhu, ketu &c.), a secondary heavenly body, such as a comet, meteor.
