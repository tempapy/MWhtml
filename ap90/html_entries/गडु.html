• gaḍu a. [gaḍ-un] Crooked, hump backed.
    • --ḍuḥ
      1 A hump on the back.
      2 A javelin.
      3 A water-pot.
      4 An earth-worm.
      5 Any superfluous excrescence or addition, a useless object; see aṃtargaḍu.
      6 A goitre, excrescence on the neck.
      7 A hump-backed person. [0447-b]
