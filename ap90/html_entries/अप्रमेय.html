• aprameya a. 1 Immeasurable, unbounded, boundless; °mahimā Mv. 4. 30. --2 That which cannot be properly ascertained, understood &c. inscrutable, unfathomable (of person or thing); aciṃtyasyāprameyasya kāryatattvārthavitprabhuḥ Ms. 1. 3; 12. 94. --3 Not to be proved or demonstrated (as Brahma). --yaṃ Brahma. --Comp. --anubhāva a. of unlimited might. --ātman ‘of inscrutable spirit,’ epithet of Śiva.
