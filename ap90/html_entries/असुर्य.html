asurya a. [asurāya hitā gavā° yat]
      1 Incorporeal, spiritual, divine.
      2 Demoniacal, belonging to the Asuras or sprung from them (asurasya svaṃ P. IV. 4. 123).
      --ryaṃ
      1 The water of the clouds.
      2 Spirituality, divine nature.
      3 The collective body of spiritual beings. [0214-b]
