• jāgṛ {c2c} P. (jāgārti, jāgarita) 1 To be awake, be watchful or attentive (fig. also); so'pasarpairjajāgāra yathākālaṃ svapannapi R. 17. 51; gurau ṣāḍguṇyaciṃtāyāmārye cārye ca jāgrati Mu. 7. 13; to sit up during the night; yā niśā sarvabhūtānāṃ tasyāṃ jāgarti saṃyamī Bg. 2. 69. --2 To be roused from sleep, awake. --3 To foresee, be provident.
