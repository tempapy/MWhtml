kuṭ
      I. 6 P. (kuṭati, kuṭita)
      1 To be crooked or curved.
      2 To curve or bend.
      3 To act dishonestly, cheat, deceive.
      II. 4 P. (kuṭyati)
      1 To break to pieces, break asunder, divide, split; Pt. 2.
      2 To speak indistinctly.
      3 To be warm, burn. [0407-a]
