advaya a. [nāsti dvayaṃ yasya]
    • 1 Not two.
    • 2 Without a second, unique; sole; advayaṃ brahma Ved. Sūtra.
      [advayaṃ vijñānābhedaḥ padārthānāṃ astyasya vādakatvena astyarthe ac] N. of Buddha.
      [na. ta.] Non-duality, unity, identity; especially, the identity of Brahma and the universe, or of spirit and matter; the highest truth.
      Comp. = advaitānaṃdaḥ q. v. --vādin (= advaita°) one who propounds the identity of spirit and matter or of Brahma and the universe (sarvameva vastu citsvarūpaṃ nānyadato dvitīyamasti iti yaḥ pratipādayati). {2} Buddha. [0043-b]
