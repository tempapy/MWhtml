• āśāsya pot. p.
      1 To be obtained by a boon.
      2 To be blessed; Mv. 4. 13.
      3 To be wished for, desirable; anāśāsyajayo yayau R. 4. 44 (who had not to wish for victory, to whom victory came unsought).
    • --syaṃ
      1 A thing to be wished for, wish, desire; saṃpannāste sarvāśiṣaḥ Mu. 7; M. 5. 20.
      2 A blessing, benediction; āśāsyaciṃtāstimito babhūva Ku. 7. 87; āśāsyamanyat punaruktabhūtaṃ R. 5. 34. [0264-a]
