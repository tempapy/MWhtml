• pāripārśvakaḥ, pāripārśvikaḥ [paripārśvaṃ pārśvaṃ vyāpya vartate ṭhak]
      1 A servant or an attendant.
      2 An assistant of the manager of a play, one of the interlocutors in the prologue; praviśya pāripārśvakaḥ; tatkimiti pāripārśvika nāraṃbhayasi kuśīlavaiḥ saha saṃgītaṃ Ve. 1. [0697-a]
