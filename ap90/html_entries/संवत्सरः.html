• saṃvatsaraḥ [saṃvasaṃti ṛtavo'tra saṃvas-saran Tv.] 1 A year. --2 A year of Vikramāditya's era. --3 N. of Śiva. --4 The first year in the cycle of five years. --Comp. --karaḥ an epithet of Śiva. --bhrami a. revolving in a year, completing one revolution in a year (said of the sun). --rayaḥ a year's course. [1068-c]
