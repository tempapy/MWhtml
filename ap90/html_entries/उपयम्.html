• upayam 1 U.
      1 To marry, take a wife (A. in this sense); bhavān mithaḥsamayādimāmupāyaṃsta Ś. 5; ātmānurūpāṃ vidhinopayeme Ku. 1. 18; R. 14. 87; Śi. 15. 27; Ms. 3. 11; Bk. 4. 20, 28; 7. 101.
      2 (a) To seize, hold; upayaccha śūrpaṃ Av.; upāyaṃsta mahāstrāṇi Bk. 15. 21; śastrāṇyupāyaṃsata jitvarāṇi 1. 16. (b) To take, receive, accept; kopātkāścitpriyaiḥ prattamupāyaṃsata nāsavaṃ Bk. 8. 33.
      3 To show, indicate (sūc); mopayadhvaṃ bhayaṃ Bk. 7. 101.
      4 To lie under, support, prop up (Ved.).
      5 To go to (a woman); Ms. 11. 173.
      6 To curb, restrain. [0327-b]
