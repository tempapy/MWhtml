dhanvin a. (nī f.) [dhanvaṃ cāpo'styasya ini]
      1 Armed with a bow.
      2 Cunning, shrewd. --m.
      1 An archer; ke mama dhanvino'nye Ku. 3. 10; utkarṣaḥ sa ca dhanvināṃ yadiṣavaḥ sidhyaṃti lakṣye cale Ś. 2. 5.
      2 An epithet of Arjuna.
      3 Of Śiva.
      4 of Viṣṇu.
      5 The sign Sagittarius of the zodiac. [0591-c]
