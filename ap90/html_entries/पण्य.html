paṇya a. [paṇ-karmaṇi yat]
      1 Saleable, vendible.
      2 To be transacted.
      --ṇyaḥ
      1 A ware, an article, a commodity; pūrābabhāse vipaṇisthapaṇyā R. 16. 41; paṇyānāṃ gāṃdhikaṃ paṇyaṃ Pt. 1. 13; saubhāgyapaṇyākaraḥ Mk. 8. 38; Ms. 5. 129; M. 1. 17; Y. 2. 245.
      2 Trade, business.
      3 Price; mahatā puṇyapaṇyena krīteyaṃ kāyanaustvayā Śānti. 3. 1.
    Comp.
      --aṃganā, --yoṣit f., --vilāsinī, --strī f. a harlot, a courtezan; paṇyastrīṣu vivekakalpalatikāśastrīṣurajyeta kaḥ Bh. 1. 90; Me. 25. --ajiraṃ a market.
      --ājīvaḥ a trader. --ājīvakaṃ a market, fair.
      --patiḥ a great merchant.
      --phalatvaṃ prosperity or profit in trade.
      --bhūmiḥ f. a warehouse.
      --vīthikā, --vīthī, --śālā {1} a market. {2} a stall, shop. [0660-a]
