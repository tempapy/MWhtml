• bhairava a. (vī f.) [bhīroridaṃ aṇ] 1 Terrible, frightful, horrible, formidable; U. 5. 6. --2 Miserable. --3 Relating to Bhairava. --vaḥ 1 A form of Śiva (of which {8} kinds are enumerated). --2 The sentiment of terror (bhayānaka). --3 Fear, terror. --4 N. of a musical mode (rāga) calculated to excite emotions of fear or terror. --vī 1 A form of the goddess Durgā. --2 N. of a Rāgiṇī in the Hindu musical system. --3 A girl of 12 or a young girl representing the goddess Durgā at the Durga festival. --vaṃ Terror, horror. --Comp. --īśaḥ an epithet of Viṣṇu (or Śiva ?); so bhairavatarjakaḥ --yātanā a sort of purificatory torment inflicted by Bhairava of Benares on those who die there, to make their spirits fit for absorption into the Supreme Spirit.
