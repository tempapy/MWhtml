• cāraka a. [cārayati car-ṇic-ṇvul] Acting, doing, proceeding.
    • --kaḥ
      1 A spy.
      2 A herdsman.
      3 A leader, driver.
      4 An associate.
      5 A groom, cavalier.
      6 A prison; nigaḍitacaraṇā cārake niroddhavyā Dk. 32.
      7 A bond, fetter.
      8 Going, motion.
      9 A wandering Brāhmaṇical student. [0490-c]
