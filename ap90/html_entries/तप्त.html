• tapta p. p. [tap-kta] 1 Heated, burnt. --2 Red-hot, hot. --3 Melted, fused. --4 Distressed, pained, afflicted. --5 Practised (as penance). --Comp. --kāṃcanaṃ gold purified with fire. --kṛcchraṃ a kind of penance consisting in drinking hot water, milk and ghee for three days each, and inhaling hot air for three days; Ms. 11. 214; Y. 3. 318. --rūpaṃ, --rūpakaṃ purified silver.
