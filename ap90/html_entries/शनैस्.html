(śanaiḥ śanaiḥ slowly, by slow degrees). --Comp. --cara a. going or moving slowly; śanaiśvarābyāṃ pādābyāṃ reje grahamayīva sā Bh. 1. 17. (where it means ‘Saturn’ also). --raḥ the planet Saturn.
