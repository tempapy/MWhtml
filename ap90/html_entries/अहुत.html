• ahuta a.
      1 Not sacrificed or offered (as an oblation); Ms. 12. 68.
      2 One who has not yet received any oblation.
    • --taḥ Religious meditation, prayer, and the study of the Vedas (considered as one of the five great Yajñas and necessary duties); ahutaṃ ca hutaṃ caiva tathā prahutameva ca . brāhmaṃ hutaṃ prāśitaṃ ca paṃcayajñān pracakṣate .. Ms. 3. 73, 74.
    Comp.
    • --ada a. {1} not eating of a sacrifice. {2} not allowed to partake of a sacrifice. [0219-b]
