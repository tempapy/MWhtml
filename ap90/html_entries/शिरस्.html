• śiras n. [śṝ-asun nipātaḥ Uṇ. 4. 193] 1 The head; śirasā ślāghate pūrvaṃ (guṇaṃ) paraṃ (doṣaṃ) kaṃṭhe niyacchati Subhāṣ. --2 Skull. --3 A peak, summit, top (as of a mountain); himagaurairacalādhipaḥ śirobhiḥ Ki. 5. 17; Śi. 4. 54. --4 The top of a tree. --5 The head or top of anything; śirasi mapīpaṭalaṃ dadhāti dīpaḥ Bv. 1. 74. --6 Pinnacle, acme, highest point. --7 Front, forepart, van (as of an army); putrasya te raṇaśirasyayamagrayāyī Ś. 7. 26; U. 5. 3. --8 Chief, principal, head (usually at the end of comp.). --Comp. --asthi n.
