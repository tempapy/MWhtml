apās [apa-as] 4 P.
    • 1 (a) To throw or fling away, cast away or off, leave, keep or put aside, reject, discard (opinion also); sāraṃ tato grāhyamapāsya phalgu Pt. 1; kimityapāsyābharaṇāni yauvane dhṛtaṃ tvayā vārdhakaśobhi valkalaṃ Ku. 5. 44; nirastagāṃbhīryamapāstapuṣpakaṃ Śi. 1. 55; ityādīnāmapi kāvyalakṣaṇatvamapāstaṃ S. D. rejected, discarded. (b) To leave, abandon, desert, quit, retire or withdraw from; yadi samaramapāsya nāsti mṛtyorbhayaṃ Ve. 3. 4.
    • 2 To scare, disperse, drive away; apāsya cāsya yaṃtāraṃ Mb.
    • 3 To leave behind, leave in a deserted condition; to disregard, take no notice of, contemn. [0116-c]
