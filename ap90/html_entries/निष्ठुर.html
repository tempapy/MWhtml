• niṣṭhura a. [ni-sthā-urac ṣatvaṭutve] 1 Hard, rugged, coarse, rough. --2 Severe, sharp, smart (as a bow); Śi. 5. 49. --3 Cruel, harsh, hard-hearted (said of persons or things); vyavasāyaḥ pratipattiniṣṭhuraḥ R. 8. 65. 3. 62. --4 Motionless, stiff. --5 Contumelious. --raṃ A harsh speech, abusive language.
