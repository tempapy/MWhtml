• anvādeśaḥ Subsequent or repeated mention, referring to what has been previously mentioned; reemployment of the same word in a subsequent part of a sentence, or of the same thing to perform a subsequent operation; ādeśaḥ kathanaṃ anvādeśaḥ anukathanaṃ Kāśi. on P. II. 4. 32. (idaṃ and etad are said to assume the forms ene, enau, enān &c. in the sense of anvādeśa; kiṃcitkāryaṃ vidhātumupāttasya kāryāṃtaraṃ vidhātuṃ punarupādānaṃ anvādeśaḥ; e. g. anena vyākaraṇamadhītaṃ enaṃ chaṃdo'dhyāpaya; anayoḥ pavitraṃ kulaṃ enayoḥ prabhūtaṃ svaṃ Sk.).
