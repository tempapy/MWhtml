• maṃdiraṃ [maṃdyate'tra maṃd kirac] 1 A dwelling house, habitation, place, mansion; Ku. 7. 55; Bk. 8. 96; R. 12. 83. --2 An abode, a dwelling in general; as in kṣarībdhimaṃdiraḥ. --3 A town. --4 A camp. --5 A temple. --raḥ 1 The sea. --2 The hollow of the knee, ham. --Comp. --paśuḥ a cat. --maṇiḥ an epithet of Śiva. [0844-c]
