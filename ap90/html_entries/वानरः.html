• vānaraḥ [vānaṃ vanasaṃbaṃdhi phalādikaṃ rātigṛhṇati rā-ka; vā kikalpena naro vā] A monkey, an ape.
    • --rī A female monkey.
    Comp.
    • --akṣaḥ a wild goat.
    • --āghātaḥ the tree called Lodhra.
    • --iṃdraḥ N. of Sugrīva or of Hanumat.
    • --priyaḥ the tree called kṣīrin. [0959-b]
