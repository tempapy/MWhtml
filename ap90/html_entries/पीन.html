pīna a. [pyāy --kta saṃprasāraṇe dīrghaḥ]
      1 {1} Fat, fleshy, corpulent; U. 6. 13 ‘strong’.
      2 Plump, large, thick; as in pīnastanī.
      3 Full, round.
      4 Swollen, large, big.
      5 Brawny.
      6 Profuse, excessive.
    Comp.
      --ūdhas f. (pīnodhnī) a cow with full udders.
      --vakṣas a. full chested, having a full bosom. [0705-b]
