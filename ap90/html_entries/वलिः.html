• valiḥ --lī f. (Also written baliḥ-lī) 1 A fold or wrinkle (on the skin); valibhirmukhamākrāṃtam. --2 A fold of skin on the upper part of the belly (especially of females, regarded as a mark of beauty); madhyena sā vedivilagnamadhyā valitrayaṃ cāru babhāra bālā Ku. 1. 39. --3 The ridge of a thatched roof. --4 A line made on the body with fragrant unguents. --Comp. --bhṛt a. curled, having curls (as hair); kusumotkhacitān valībhṛtaścalayan bhṛṃgarucastavālakān R. 8. 53. --mukhaḥ, --vadanaḥ a monkey; Māl. 9. 31.
