• preṣya a. To be ordered, sent, despatched &c. --ṣyaḥ 1 A servant, menial, slave; Pt. 1. 424. --2 A messenger. --ṣyā A female servant, hand-maid. --ṣyaṃ 1 Sending on a mission. --2 Servitude. --Comp. --javaḥ servants taken collectively. --bhāvaḥ capacity of a servant, servitude, bondage; M. 5. 12. --vadhraḥ {1} the wife of a servant. {2} a female servant, hand-maid. --vargaḥ the body of servants, suite, train.
