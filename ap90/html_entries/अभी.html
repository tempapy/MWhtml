abhī [abhi-i] 2 P.
      1 To approach, come or draw near, go up to (with acc.) asmānattumito'bhyeti Bk. 7. 84.; Ki. 2. 54.
      2 To go along or after, follow, serve.
      3 (a) To go into, enter; Bk. 5. 67; Ms. 8. 75. (b) To go over to, reach, arrive at; Me. 34 v. l. (c) To fall to one's share, come to; Bk. 7. 99.
      4 To get, meet with, fall into, suffer, undergo (said of good or bad things); kāryaṃ saṃsiddhimabhyeti Pt. 1. [0142-b]
      abhī a. Without fear; R. 9. 63; 15. 8. [0142-b]
