• udāhṛ 1 P.
      1 To relate, narrate, declare, announce.
      2 To say, speak, utter; udājahāra drupadātmajā giraḥ Ki. 1. 27; Mk. 9. 4; Pt. 2.; cikitsakā doṣamudāharaṃti M. 2; Māl. 1; tvāṃ kāmino madanadūtimudāharaṃti V. 4. 11; mutter, repeat; dharmākṣarāṇi Mk. 8.
      3 To cite as an instance or illustration, illustrate; tvamudāthiyasva kathamanyathā janaiḥ Śi. 15. 29. [0304-a]
