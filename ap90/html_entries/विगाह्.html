• vigāh {c1c} A. 1 To plunge or dive into, bathe; (dīrghikāḥ) sa vyagāhata vigāḍhamanmathaḥ R. 19. 9. --2 To enter, penetrate into, pervade (fig. also); viṣamo'pi vigāhyate nayaḥ kṛtatīrthaḥ payasāmivāśayaḥ Ki. 2. 3; R. 13. 1, Mu. 1. 26. --3 To stir about, agitate; vigāhyamānāṃ sarayūṃ ca naubhiḥ R. 14. 30. --4 To follow, practise; tayā munīnāṃ caritaṃ vyagāhyata Ku. 5. 19. --5 To approach, set in (as season &c.).
