• dadhi n. [dadh-in]
      1 Coagulated milk, thick sour milk; kṣīraṃ dadhibhāvena pariṇamate S. B; dadhyodanaḥ &c.
      2 Turpentine.
      3 A garment.
    Comp.
    • --annaṃ, --odanaṃ boiled rice mixed with dadhi.
    • --uttaraṃ, --uttarakaṃ(gaṃ) the skim of curdled milk, whey.
    • --udaḥ, --udakaḥ the ocean of coagulated milk.
    • --kūrcikā mixture of boiled and coagulated milk.
    • --cāraḥ a churningstick.
    • --jaṃ fresh butter.
    • --phalaḥ the wood-apple (kapittha).
    • --maṃḍaḥ, --vāri n. whey.
    • --maṃthanaṃ churning coagulated milk.
    • --śoṇaḥ a monkey.
    • --saktu m. pl. barley-meal mixed with coagulated milk.
    • --sāraḥ, --snehaḥ fresh butter.
    • --svedaḥ buttermilk. [0556-a]
