• caṃpakaḥ [caṃp-ṇvul] 1 A tree bearing yellow, fragrant flowers. --2 A kind of perfume. --kaṃ 1 A flower of this tree; adyāpi tāṃ kanaṃkacapakadāmagaurīṃ Ch. P. 1. --2 The fruit of a variety of plantain. --Comp. --mālā {1} N. of a neck-ornament worn by women. {2} a garland of Campaka flowers. {3} a kind of metre (see App.). --raṃbhā a species of plantain.
