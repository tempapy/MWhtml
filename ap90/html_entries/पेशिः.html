peśiḥ --śī f.
      1 A piece of flesh.
      2 A ball or mass of flesh.
      3 An egg.
      4 A muscle; Y. 3. 100.
      5 The fœtus shortly after conception.
      6 A bud on the point of blowing.
      7 The thunderbolt of Indra (said to be m. also).
      8 A kind of musical instrument.
      9 The shell or rind (of fruits).
      10 A sheath, scabbard.
      11 A shoe.
    Comp.
      --kośaḥ --ṣaḥ a bird's egg. [0720-b]
