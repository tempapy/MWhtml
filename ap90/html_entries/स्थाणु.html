• sthāṇu a. [sthā-nu pṛṣo° ṇatvam] Firm, fixed, steady, stable, immoveable, motionless. --ṇuḥ 1 An epithet of Śiva; sa sthāṇuḥ sthirabhaktiyogasulabho niḥśreyasāyāstu vaḥ V. 1. 1. --2 A stake, post, pillar; kiṃ sthāṇurayamuta puruṣaḥ; Pt. 1. 49. --3 A peg, pin. --4 The gnomon of a dial. --5 A spear, dart. --6 A nest of white ants. --7 The drug or perfume called Jīvaka. --m. n. A branchless trunk or stem, any bare stalk or stem, pollard; --Comp. --chedaḥ one who cuts down the trunks of trees, one who clears away timber; sthāṇucchedasya kedāramāhuḥ śalyavato mṛgaṃ Ms. 9. 44. --bhramaḥ mistaking anything for a post.
