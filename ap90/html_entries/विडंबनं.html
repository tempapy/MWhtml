viḍaṃbanaṃ --nā
      1 Imitation.
      2 Disguise, imposture.
      3 Deception, fraud.
      4 Vexation, mortification.
      5 Paining, distressing.
      6 Disappointing.
      7 Ridiculousness, mockery, a matter for laughter; iyaṃ ca te'nyā purato viḍaṃbanā Ku. 5. 70; asati tvayi vāruṇīmadaḥ pramadānāmadhunā viḍaṃbanā 4. 12. [0972-c]
