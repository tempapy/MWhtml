• pācana a. (nī f.) [pācayati kartari lyuḥ pācyate'nena karaṇe lyuṭ vā]
      1 Cooking.
      2 Ripening.
      3 Digestive.
      4 Softening, relaxing.
      5 Suppurative.
    • --naḥ
      1 Fire.
      2 Sourness, acidity.
    • --naṃ
      1 The act of cooking.
      2 The act of ripening.
      3 A dissolvent, digestive medicine.
      4 Causing a wound to close.
      5 Penance, expiation (prāyaścitta).
      6 Infusion, decoction.
      7 Extracting extraneous substances from a wound.
      8 A cataplasm.
      9 A substance used in closing wounds or restraining bleeding. [0690-a]
