• avaśa a. [nāsti vaśaṃ āyattatvaṃ yasya] 1 Independent, free. --2 Not compliant or docile, disobedient, selfwilled; strī cāvaśā Pt. 1. 424; Ms. 5. 33. --3 Not subjected to or swayed; avaśo viṣayāṇāṃ K. 45; uncontrolled, unrestrained; °iṃdriyacittānāṃ H. 1. 18; 2. 182; Dk. 34; madhurairavaśāni laṃbhayan vaśaṃ Ki. 2. 55 wild. --4 Not master of oneself, subject to the senses; kamaparamavaśaṃ na viprakuryuḥ Ku. 6. 95. --5 Not having one's own will, dependent, helpless, powerless; sakalamavaśaṃ sīdati jagat H. 2. 77; kāryate hyavaśaḥ Bg. 3. 5; K. 174; Pt. 1. 335; U. 3; kathamavaśo hyayaśoviṣaṃ pibāmi Mk. 10. 13; vimuṃcaṃtyavaśā dehaṃ kālasya vaśamāgatāḥ Rām.; Mu. 1. 12. --6 Necessary, certain; kimasya bhavato yathā suhṛda eva nāśo'vaśaḥ Mu. 6. --Comp. --iṃdriyacitta a. whose mind and senses are not held in subjection.
