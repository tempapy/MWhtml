• vallabha a. [vall-abhac Uṇ. 3. 125]
      1 Beloved, desired, dear.
      2 Supreme.
    • --bhaḥ
      1 A lover, husband; (khedaḥ) tvayi vilasati tulyaṃ vallabhālokanena Māl. 3. 8; Śi. 11. 33.
      2 A favourite; Pt. 1. 53.
      3 A superintendent, an overseer.
      4 A chief herdsman.
      5 A good horse (one with auspicious marks).
    • --bhā A beloved female, mistress, wife; bahuvallabhā rājānaḥ śrūyaṃte Ś. 3; Mu. 3. 9.
    Comp.
    • --ācāryaḥ N. of the celebrated founder of a Vaiṣṇava sect.
    • --pālaḥ a groom. [0951-c]
