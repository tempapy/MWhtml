• saṃyugaḥ 1 Conjunction, union, mixture. --2 Fight, war, battle, contest; saṃyuge sāṃyugīnaṃ tamudyataṃ prasaheta kaḥ Ku. 2. 57; R. 9. 19. --Comp. --goṣpadaṃ ‘a contest in a cow's footstep’; i. e. an insignificant or trifling quarrel; cf. the English phrase ‘a storm in a tea-pot’. [1067-b]
