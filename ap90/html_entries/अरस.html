• arasa a.
      1 Sapless, not juicy, tasteless, insipid.
      2 Dull, flat.
      3 Weak, having no strength, inefficacious.
    • --saḥ No juice, absence of juice.
    Comp.
    • --āśaḥ {1} eating sapless food. {2} maceration of the body.
    • --āśin a. {1} eating sapless food. {2} macerating the body. [0160-c]
