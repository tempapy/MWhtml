• grāsaḥ [gras karmaṇi ghañ] 1 A mouthful, a quantity of anything equal to a mouthful; Ms. 3. 133; 6. 28; Y. 3. 55. --2 Food, nourishment. --3 The part of the sun or moon eclipsed. --4 The morsel bitten. --5 The act of swallowing. --6 Slurring, indistinct pronunciation; fault in the pronunciation of the gutturals. --7 (In geom.) A piece cut out by the intersection of two circles. --8 An eclipse. --Comp. --ācchādanaṃ food and clothing; i. e. bare subsistence. --śalyaṃ any extraneous substance lodged in the throat. [0471-c]
