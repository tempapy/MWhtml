• glai {c1c} P. (glāyati, glāna) 1 To feel aversion or dislike, be unwilling or disinclined to do anything (with inf.). --2 To be fatigued or wearied, feel tired or exhausted. --3 To despond, sink in spirit, be dejected; Bk. 19. 17, 6. 12. --4 To wane, fade, faint away. --Caus. (gla-glāpayati, but praglāpayati) 1 To cause to fade away, wither up; Ś. 3. 18; Ku. 3. 49. --2 To tire out, exhaust. --3 To injure, trouble, hurt. --4 To emaciate, waste; Ku. 5. 29; U. 3. 5. [0474-c]
