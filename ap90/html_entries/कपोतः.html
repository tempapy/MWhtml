• kapotaḥ [ko vāyuḥ pota iva yasya Tv.] 1 A dove, pigeon. --2 A bird in general. --3 A particular position of the hands. --4 The grey colour of a pigeon. --Comp. --aṃghriḥ f. a sort of perfume. --aṃjanaṃ antimony. --ariḥ a hawk, falcon. --ābha a. of the colour of a pigeon. (--bhaḥ) a pale or dirty white colour. --caraṇā a sort of perfume. --pālikā,--pālī f. an aviary, a pigeon-house, dove-cot. --rājaḥ the king of pigeons. --varṇī small cardamoms. --vāṇā a kind of perfume. --sāraṃ antimony. --hastaḥ a mode of folding the hands in supplication, fear &c. kapotahastakaṃ kṛtvā Ś. 6.
