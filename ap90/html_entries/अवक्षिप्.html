• avakṣip 6 U.
      1 To fling away, to throw or cast off; sūkṣmavastramavakṣipya munivastrāṇyavasta ha Rām.; to hurl.
      2 To cause to fly down or away.
      3 To reprimand, revile any one, censure, slander; madalekhāmavakṣipya K. 317; avākṣipadvāsudevaṃ Mb.
      4 To grant, yield. --Caus. To cause to fall down. [0177-a]
