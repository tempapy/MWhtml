• vinipātaḥ 1 Falling down, a fall. --2 A great fall, calamity, an evil, loss, ruin, destruction; vivekabhraṣṭānāṃ bhavati vinipātaḥ śatamukhaḥ Bh. 2. 10 (where it has sense 1 also); vidhiheturaheturāgasāṃ vinipātopi samaḥ samunnateḥ Ki. 2. 34. --3 Decay, death. --4 Hell, perdition; Ś. 5. --5 Occurrence, happening. --6 Pain, distress. --7 Disrespect. --Comp. --śaṃsin a. portentous, foreboding ruin.
