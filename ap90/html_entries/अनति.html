• anati ind. Not very much; compounds beginning with anati may be analysed by referring to ati; e. g. anatikramaḥ moderation; anatikramaṇīya not to be transgressed, inviolable; anatidṛśya opaque; anatidbhuta unsurpassed, real true, proper; anativyādhya invulnerable; anatyaṃtagati sense of diminutive words; anatyaya imperishable, undecaying &c. anatipraśna not to be asked to excess; anativilaṃbitā absence of delay; fluency as a speaker's qualification, one of the 35 Vāgguṇas, q. v.
