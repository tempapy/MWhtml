• uras a. Best, excellent. --n. (uraḥ) The breast, bosom; vyūḍhorasko vṛṣaskaṃdhaḥ R. 1. 13; Ku. 6. 51; urasi kṛ to clasp to the bosom. --Comp. --kaṭaḥ {1} the sacrificial thread hung round the neck and upon the breast. {2} an upper garment (for boys). --kṣataṃ injury to the chest. --gāmin a. going on the breast (as a reptile), creeping, crawling. --grahaḥ, --ghātaḥ a disease of the chest, pleurisy. --chadaḥ, --trāṇaṃ a cuirass, breast-plate; Śi. 15. 80. --jaḥ, --bhūḥ, --urasijaḥ, --urasiruhaḥ the female breast; rejāte ruciradṛśāmurojakuṃbhau Śi. 8. 53, 25, 59. --bhūṣaṇaṃ an ornament of the breast. --sūtrikā a necklace of pearls hanging over the breast. --sthalaṃ the breast, bosom.
