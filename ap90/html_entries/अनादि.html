anādi a. [ādiḥ kāraṇaṃ pūrvakālo vā nāsti yasya saḥ] Having no beginning, eternal, existing from eternity, epithet of parameśvara; jagadādiranādistvaṃ Ku. 2. 9; anādirādirgoviṃdaḥ sarvakāraṇakāraṇaṃ; also of hiraṇyagarbha.
      Comp., --aṃta a. without beginning and end; eternal. (--taḥ) N. of Śiva.
      a. having neither beginning nor end, eternal.
      a. having no beginning, middle or end; eternal. [0061-b]
