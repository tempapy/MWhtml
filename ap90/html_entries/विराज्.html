• virāj 1 U.
      1 To shine, glitter; Bv. 1. 88.
      2 To appear or look like; R. 2. 20.
      3 To be eminent or illustrious. --Caus. To brighten, illuminate, irradiate. [0988-b]
    • virāj m.
      1 Beauty, splendour.
      2 A man of the Kṣatriya or warrior tribe.
      3 The first progeny of Brahman; cf. Ms. 1. 32; tasmāt virāḍajāyata Rv. 10. 90. 5 (where virāj is represented as born from Puruṣa).
      4 The body.
      5 (In Vedānta phil.) N. of ‘intellect’ considered as ruling over the aggregate of bodies. --f. N. of a Vedic metre. [0988-c]
