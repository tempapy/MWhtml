• praśastiḥ f.
      1 Praise, eulogy, laudation.
      2 Description; U. 7.
      3 A panegyric or small poem written in praise of any one (e. g. a patron.).
      4 Excellence, eminence.
      5 Bonediction.
      6 Guidance, instruction, rule for guidance; as in lekhapraśastiḥ ‘a form of writing’. [0762-b]
