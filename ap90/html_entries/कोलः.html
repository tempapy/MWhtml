• kolaḥ [kul saṃstyāne ac]
      1 A hog, boar; Śi. 14. 43, 86.
      2 A raft, boat.
      3 The breast.
      4 The haunch, hip, lap.
      5 An embrace.
      6 The planet Saturn.
      7 An out-cast, one of a degraded tribe.
      8 A barbarian.
    • --laṃ
      1 The weight of one Tola.
      2 Black pepper.
      3 A kind of berry.
    Comp.
    • --aṃcaḥ N. of the country of the Kaliṅgas.
    • --pucchaḥ a heron. [0426-b]
