• kaiśika a. (kī f.) [keśa ṭhak]
      1 Hair-like, fine as hair.
    • --kaḥ The sentiment of love, lust.
    • --kaṃ A quantity of hair.
    • --kī One of the four varieties of dramatic style, more usually and correctly written kauśikī q. v.
      2 An epithet of Durgā. [0425-a]
