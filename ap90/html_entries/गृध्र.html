gṛdhra a. [gṛdh-kran] Greedy, covetous.
      --dhraḥ, --dhraṃ A vulture; mārjārasya hi doṣeṇa hato gṛdhro jaradgavaḥ H. 1. 56; R. 12. 50, 54. --dhrī The female vulture.
    Comp.
      --kūṭaḥ N. of a mountain near Rājagṛha.
      --patiḥ, --rājaḥ the lord of the vultures, an epithet of Jaṭāyu; asyaivāsīnmahati śikhare gṛdhrarājasya vāsaḥ U. 2. 25. --vāja, --vājita a. furnished with vulture feathers (as an arrow). [0465-a]
