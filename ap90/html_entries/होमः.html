• homaḥ [hu-man] 1 Offering oblations to gods by throwing ghee into the consecrated fire, (one of the five daily Yajñas, to be performed by a Brāhmaṇa, called devayajña q. v.). --2 A burnt offering. --3 A sacrifice. --Comp. --agniḥ the sacrificial fire. --kuṃḍaṃ a hole in the ground for receiving the consecrated fire. --turaṃgaḥ a sacrificial horse; R. 3. 38. --dhānaṃ a sacrificial chamber. --dhānyaṃ {1} sesamum. {2} barley. --dhūmaḥ the smoke of a burnt-offering or sacrificial fire. --bhasman n. the ashes of a burnt offering. --velā the time for offering oblations. --śālā a sacrificial hall or chamber. [1176-c]
