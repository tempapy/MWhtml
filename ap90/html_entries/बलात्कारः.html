• balātkāraḥ 1 Using violence, employing force. --2 Outrage, violence, force, oppression, exaction; R. 10. 47; balātkāreṇa nirvartya &c. --3 Injustice. --4 (In law) Detention of the person of a debter by the creditor and the employment of forcible means to recover the debt. [0790-b]
