• gādhiḥ, gādhin m. [gādha-in] N. of the father of Viśvāmitra (he is supposed to have been an incarnation of Indra and born as the son of king Kauśāmba).
    Comp.
    • --jaḥ, --naṃdanaḥ, --putraḥ an epithet of Viśvāmitra.
    • --nagaraṃ, --puraṃ an epithet of Kānyakubja, the modern Kanoja. [0457-a]
