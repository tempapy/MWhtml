• pravrajyā 1 Going abroad, migration. --2 Roaming, wandering about as a religious mendicant; Māl. 4. 6. --3 The order of a religious mendicant, a mendicant's life, the fourth (or bhikṣu) order in the religious life of a Brāhmaṇa; pravrajyāṃ kalpavṛkṣā ivāśritāḥ Ku. 6. 6 (where Malli. says pravrajyā means the vānaprastha or third order). --Comp. --avasitaḥ a religious mendicant who renounces his order.
