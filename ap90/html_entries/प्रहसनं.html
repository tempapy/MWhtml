• prahasanaṃ
      1 Loud or violent laughter, laughing, mirth.
      2 Ridicule, mockery, irony, joke; dhik prahasanaṃ U. 4.
      3 Satire, satirical writing.
      4 A farce, a kind of low comedy; S. D.thus defines it: --bhāṇavatsaṃdhisaṃdhyaṃgalāsyāṃgāṃkaurvinimartiṃ . bhavet prahasanaṃ vṛttaṃ niṃdyānāṃ kavikalpitaṃ .. 533 et seq.; e. g. kaṃdarpakeli. [0768-a]
