• carvaṇaṃ, --ṇā [carv bhāve lyuṭ]
      1 Chewing, eating.
      2 Sipping, tasting.
      3 Food which must be chewed, solid food.
      4 (Fig.) Tasting, relishing, enjoying; pramāṇaṃ carvaṇaivātra svābhinne viduṣāṃ mataṃ S. D. 57; (com. = carvaṇā āsvādanaṃ tacca svādaḥ kāvyārthasaṃbhedādātmānaṃdasamudbhava ityuktaprakāraṃ); so also; niṣpattyā carvaṇasyāsya niṣpattirupacārataḥ 58. [0488-a]
