• pragrah 9 U.
      1 To take, hold, seize, grasp.
      2 To receive, accept.
      3 To curb, restrain.
      4 To stretch forth, extend.
      5 To favour, behave friendly with.
      6 To keep separate (as the pragṛhya vowels).
      7 To offer.
      8 To unite with. --Caus. To receive, accept. [0728-a]
