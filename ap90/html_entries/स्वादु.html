• svādu a. [svad-uṇ] (du or dvī f.; compar. svādīyas, superl. svādiṣṭha) 1 Sweet, pleasant to the taste, sapid, savoury, dainty, tasteful; tṛṣā śuṣyatyāsye pibati salilaṃ svādu surabhi Bh. 3. 92, Me. 24. --2 Pleasing, agreeable, attractive, lovely, charming. --m. 1 Sweet flavour, sweetness of taste, relish. --2 Treacle, molasses. --n. Sweetness, relish, taste; kaviḥ kareti kāvyāni svādu jānāti paṃḍitaḥ Subhāṣ. --duḥ f. A grape. --Comp. --anna sweet or choice food, dainties, delicacies. --amlaḥ the pomegranate tree. --khaṃḍaḥ {1} a piece of any sweet substance. {2} molasses. --dhanvan m. N. of the god of love. --phalaṃ the jujube. (--lā) the jujube tree. --mūlaṃ a carrot. --rasā {1} the fruit of the hog-plum. {2} the Śatāvarī plant. {3} the root kākolī. {4} spirituous liquor. {5} a grape. --śuddhaṃ {1} rock-salt. {2} marine-salt.
