• vikramaḥ 1 A step, stride, pace; gateṣu līlāṃcitavikrameṣu Ku. 1. 34; Ś. 7. 6; cf. trivikrama. --2 Stepping over, walking. --3 Overcoming, overpowering. --4 Heroism, prowess, heroic valour; anutsekaḥ khalu vikramālaṃkāraḥ V. 1; R. 12. 87, 93. --5 N. of a celebrated king of Ujjayinī; see App. II. --6 N. of Viṣṇu. --7 Strength, power. --Comp. --arkaḥ, --ādityaḥ see vikrama. --karman n. a heroic deed, feat of valour.
