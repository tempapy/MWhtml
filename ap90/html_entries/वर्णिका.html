varṇikā [varṇā akṣarāṇi lekhyatvena saṃtyasyāḥ ṭhan]
      1 The mask or dress of an actor.
      2 A colour, paint.
      3 Ink.
      4 A pen, pencil.
      5 Chalk.
    Comp.
      --parigrahaḥ the assumption of a character or mask; tataḥ prakaraṇanāyakasya mālatīvallabhasya mādhavasya varṇikāparigrahaḥ kathaṃ Māl. 1. [0948-b]
