V. 3. 39.] 1 Below, down; patatyadho dhāma visāri sarvataḥ Śi. 1. 2; in the lower region, to the infernal regions or hell; vyasanyadho'dhovrajati svaryātyavyasanī mṛtaḥ Ms. 7. 53. (According to the context adhaḥ may have the sense of the nominative, °aṃkuśaḥ &c. ablative, adho vṛkṣāt patati; or locative, adho gṛhe śete). --2 Beneath, under, used like a preposition with gen.; tarūṇāṃ° Ś. 1. 14; rarely with abl. also; bāhitthaṃ tu tatopyadhaḥ Hemacandra; (when repeated) lower and lower, down and down; adhodhaḥ paśyataḥ kasya mahimā nopacīyate H. 2. 2; yātyadhodho vrajatyuccairnaraḥ svaireva karmabhiḥ H. 2. 48; adhodho gaṃgeyaṃ padamupagatā stokaṃ Bh. 2. 10; from under, just below (with acc.); navānadho'dho bṛhataḥ payodharān Śi. 1. 4.
