• mārjana a. (nī f.) [mṛj-lyu lyuṭ vā] Cleansing, purifying. --naṃ 1 Cleansing, cleaning, purifying. --2 Wiping or rubbing off. --3 Effacing, wiping away. --4 Cleansing the person by rubbing it with unguents. --5 Sprinkling the person with water by means of the hand, a blade of Kuśa grass &c. --naḥ The tree called Lodhra. --nā 1 Cleansing, purifying, cleaning. --2 The sound of a drum; māyūrīmadayati mārjanā manāṃsi M. 1. 21. --nī A broom, brush.
