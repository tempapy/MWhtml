amata a.
      1 Not felt, not perceptible by the mind, unknown.
      2 Disliked, not agreed to, see under am also.
    Comp.
      --parārthatā ‘an unaccepted second sense’, one of the faults of a word (śabdadoṣa); amataḥ prakṛtaviruddhaḥ parārtho yatra; e. g. in rāmamanmathaśareṇa tāḍitā &c. (R. 11. 20) the second sense suggestive of śṛṃgārarasa is opposed to the proper rasa of the passage which is either vīra or bībhatsa; K. P. 7. [0149-a]
