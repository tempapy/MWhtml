ākhyā 2 P.
      1 (a) To tell, say, inform, communicate, narrate (usually with dat. of person); te rāmāya vadhopāyamācakhyurvibudhadviṣaḥ R. 15. 5, 41, 71, 93; 12. 42, 91; Bg. 11. 31, 18. 63; Me. 100; Ms. 8. 224, 9. 73, Y. 1. 66, 2. 65; sometimes with gen. of person; ākhyāhi bhadre priyadarśanasya Pt. 4. 15; kenāhaṃ tavākhyātaḥ Mb. (b) To declare, announce, signify; R. 2. 11.
      2 To call, denominate, name; suvarṇabiṃdurityākhyāyate Māl. 9; R. 10. 21, Ms. 4. 6.
      3 To look at, count; to recite (Ved.). --Caus. (khyāpayati)
      1 To cause to tell or narrate.
      2 To declare. [0225-b]
