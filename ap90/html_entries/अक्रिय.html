akriya a. [nāsti kriyā yasya na. ba.]
      1 Inactive, dull, torpid.
      2 Without essential works.
      3 Abstaining from religious rites.
      4 Without action of any kind, epithet of God.
      5 Worthless, good for nothing.
      --yā [na. ta] Inactivity; neglect of duty; pradhānasyākriyā yatra sāṃgaṃ tatkriyate punaḥ . tadaṃgasyākriyāyāṃ tu nāvṛttirna ca tatkriyā .. [0005-b]
