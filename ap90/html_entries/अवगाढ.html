avagāḍha p. p.
      1 Plunged into, entered into, immersed; jalāvagāḍhasya vanadvipasya Mk. 2; amṛtathadamivāvagāḍhosmi Ś. 7; avagāḍhaḥ śokasāgaraṃ Rām.; samudramavagāḍhāni pattanāni Rām.
      2 Depressed, low, deep (lit. and fig.); abhyunnatā purastādavagāḍhā jaghanagauravātpaścāt Ś. 3. 8; manyurabhajadavagāḍhataraḥ Śi. 15. 2.
      3 That in which one bathes; avagāḍhā ca pītā ca (gaṃgā) punātyāsaptamaṃ kulaṃ Mb.
      4 Congealed, curdling (as blood). [0177-c]
