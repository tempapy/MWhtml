• avasanna p. p. 1 Sunk down (fig. also), enervated, sinking, drooping; atiprabalapipāsāvasannāni K. 36; Śi. 15. 85; madāvasannahasta Dk. 62 powerless. --2 Dispirited, sad, melancholy, unhappy; dadhikarṇo'vasanno brūte H. 2 lean, weakened. --3 Ended, terminated, gone off; avasannatāpamatamisramabhāt Śi. 9. 12 devoid of heat; avasannāyāṃ rātrau H. 1. --4 Unable to do one's duty. --5 Lost, deprived of; pitroḥ sakāśamavasannadṛśornināya R. 9. 77 who had lost their sight. --6 (In law) Non-suited, defeated.
