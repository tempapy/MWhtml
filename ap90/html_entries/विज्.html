• vij
      I. 3 U. (vevekti, vevikte, vikta)
      1 To separate, divide.
      2 To distinguish, discern, discriminate (usually with vi and allied to vic with vi q. v.)
      II. 6 A., 7 P. (vijate, vinākti, vigna)
      1 To shake, tremble.
      2 To be agitated, tremble with fear.
      3 To fear, be afraid; cakraṃda vignā kurarīva bhūyaḥ R. 14. 68.
      4 To be distressed or afflicted. --Caus. (vejayati-te) To terrify, frighten. [0971-a]
    • vij m. A bird; a die (?). [0971-a]
