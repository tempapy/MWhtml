• avabhās {c1c} A. 1 To shine; nātyarthamastrairavabhāsamānaḥ Ki. 3. 46; sa tejasā sūrya ivāvabhāsate Mb. --2 To shine forth, become manifest; āhosvinmukhamavabhāsate yuvatyāḥ Śi. 8. 29. --3 To appear, seem (apparently or falsely); etattrayaṃ taptāyaḥpiṃḍavadekatvenāvabhāsate Vedānta. --Caus. To irradiate, illuminate; adhikāvabhāsitadiśāṃ nikaraḥ Śi. 9. 37. [0184-c]
