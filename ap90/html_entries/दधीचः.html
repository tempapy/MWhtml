dadhīcaḥ, --ciḥ N. of a celebrated sage, who became ready to die, and offered his bones to the gods; with these bones the architect of the gods made a thunderbolt with which Indra defeated Vṛtra and other demons.
    Comp.
      --asthi n. {1} the thunderbolt of Indra. {2} a diamond. [0556-b]
