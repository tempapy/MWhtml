• pralap 1 P.
      1 To speak, talk; vaco vai dehīti (vaidehīti) pratipadamudaśru pralapitaṃ S. D. 6.
      2 To talk at ran dom or incoherently, prate, chatter talk wildly or nonsensically; pralapatyeṣa vaidheyaḥ Ś. 2.
      3 To lament, mourn, cry, bewail.
      4 To call, invoke. [0758-a]
