• auśīraṃ [uśīraṃ-aṇ]
      1 The handle of a fan or Chowri.
      2 A bed; auśīre kāmacāraḥ kṛtobhūt Dk. 72 at liberty to sleep or sit.
      3 A seat (chair, stool &c.).
      4 An unguent made of Uśīra.
      5 The root of the fragrant grass uśīra q. v.
      6 A fan. [0364-c]
