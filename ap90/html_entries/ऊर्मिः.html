ūrmiḥ m. f. [ṛ-mi arterucca Uṇ. 4. 44]
    • 1 A wave, billow; payo vetravatyāścalormi Me. 24; R. 5. 61, 12. 85.
    • 2 Current, flow.
    • 3 Light.
    • 4 Speed, velocity.
    • 5 A fold or plait in a garment.
    • 6 A row, line.
    • 7 A human infirmity (Wilson); śokamohau jarāmṛtyū kṣutpipāse ṣaḍūrmayaḥ.
    • 8 Distress, uneasiness, anxiety.
    • 9 The course of a horse.
    • 10 Missing, regretting.
    • 11 Association, number, quantity. Comp. --mālin a. wreathed or adorned with waves. (--m.) the ocean. [0346-b]
