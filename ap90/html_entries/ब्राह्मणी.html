• brāhmaṇī
      1 A woman of the Brāhmaṇa caste.
      2 The wife of a Brāhmaṇa.
      3 Intellect; (buddhi according to nīlakaṃṭha).
      4 A kind of lizard.
      5 A kind of wasp.
      6 A kind of grass.
    Comp.
