• mocana a. (nī f.) [muc-lyu lyuṭ vā] Releasing, freeing from.
    • --naṃ
      1 Releasing, liberating, setting free, emancipating.
      2 Unyoking.
      3 Discharging, emitting.
      4 Acquittance of a debt or obligation.
      5 Arrogance, pride.
      6 Deceit, fraud.
    Comp.
    • --paṭṭaṃkaḥ a filter. [0880-b]
