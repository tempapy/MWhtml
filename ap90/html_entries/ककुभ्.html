    • 2 Splendour, beauty.

    • 5 A peak, summit.
    • 6 A Rāgiṇī or personified mode of music.
    • 7 The personified quarter of the sky.
    • 8 Breath, animation.
    • 9 Unornamented hair; or hair hanging down as a tail. [0366-a]
