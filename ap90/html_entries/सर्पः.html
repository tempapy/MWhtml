• sarpaḥ
      1 [sṛp-ghañ] Serpentine or winding motion, gliding.
      2 Flowing, going.
      3 A snake, serpent.
      4 N. of a tree (nāgakeśara).
      5 The Āśleṣā constellation.
      6 N. of a tribe of Mlecchas or barbarians.
