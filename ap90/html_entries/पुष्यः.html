• puṣyaḥ
      1 The Kali age.
      2 The month called pauṣa.
      3 The eighth lunar mansion (consisting of three stars), written also tiṣya.
    • --ṣyaṃ Ved.
      1 The blossom.
      2 Foam, scum.
    • --ṣyā The asterism called puṣya.
    Comp.
    • --abhiṣekaḥ, --snānaṃ a ceremony of coronating a king &c. when the moon stands in the asterism Puṣya.
    • --yogaḥ the moon when in conjunction with Puṣya --rathaḥ = puṣparathaḥ q. v. [0714-b]
