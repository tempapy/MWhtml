• turīya a. 1 The fourth. --2 Consisting of four parts. --3 Mighty. --yaṃ 1 A quarter, a fourth part, fourth. --2 (In Vedānta phil.) The fourth state of the soul in which it becomes one with Brahman or the Supreme Spirit. --Comp. --varṇaḥ a man of the fourth caste, a Śūdra. [0542-a]
