• miṣṭa a. [miṣ-kta]
      1 Sweet.
      2 Dainty, savoury; kiṃ miṣṭamannaṃ kharasūkarāṇāṃ cf. ‘why cast pearls before swine.’
      3 Moistened, wetted.
    • --ṣṭaṃ
      1 A sweetmeat.
      2 A dainty or savoury dish.
    • --ṣṭaḥ Sweetness.
    Comp.
    • --annaṃ sweet or savoury food, dainty, sweets. [0866-c]
