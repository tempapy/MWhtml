• mūrta a. [murcch-kta]
      1 Fainted, insensible.
      2 Stupid, foolish.
      3 Embodied, incarnate; mūrto vighnastapasa iva no bhinna māraṃgayūthaḥ Ś. 1. 33; prasāda iva mūrtaste sparśaḥ snehārdraśatilaḥ U. 3. 14; R. 2. 69; 7. 70; Ku. 7. 42; Pt. 2. 99.
      4 Material, corporeal.
      5 Solid, hard.
      6 Real.
      7 Thickened, coagulated (Ved.). [0873-c]
