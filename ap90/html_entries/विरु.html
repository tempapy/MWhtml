• viru {c2c} P. 1 To cry, bewail, lament; nanu sahavarīṃ dūre matvā virauṣi samutsukaḥ V. 4. 20; Bk. 5. 54; Rs. 6. 27. --2 To make a sound, sound in general; na sa virauti na cāpi sa śobhate Pt. 1. 75; jīrṇatvāda gṛhasya virauti kapāṭaṃ Mk. 3; ete ta eva girayo viruvanmayūrāḥ U. 2. 23. --3 To cry out, shout, scream.
