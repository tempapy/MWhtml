maṃḍūkaḥ [maṃḍayati varṣāsamayaṃ, maṃḍ ūkaṇ Uṇ. 4. 42] A frog; nipānamiva maṃḍūkāḥ sodyogaṃ naramāyāṃti vivaśāḥ sarvasaṃpadaḥ Subhāṣ. --kaṃ A kind of coitus or mode of sexual enjoyment.
      --kī
      1 A female frog.
      2 A wanton or unchaste woman.
      3 N. of several plants.
    Comp.
      --anuvṛttiḥ, --plutiḥ f. ‘the leap of a frog’, skipping over or omitting at intervals (in grammar the word is used to denote the skipping of several Sūtras and supplying from a previous Sūtra); kriyāgrahaṇaṃ maṃḍūkaplutyānuvartate Sk. --kulaṃ a collection of frogs.
      --yogaḥ a kind of abstract meditation in which the person who meditates sits motion. less like a frog.
      --saras n. a pond full of frogs. [0834-b]
