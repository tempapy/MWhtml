• div {vI.v} {c4c} P. (devati, devayati-te) 1 To cause to lament, pain, vex, torment. --2 To ask, beg. {vIII.v} {c10c} A. (devayate) To suffer pain, lament, moan. --WITH pari to lament, moan, suffer pain; Bk. 4. 34.
