chātraḥ [chatraṃ gurorvaiguṇyāvaraṇaṃ śīlamasya Sk.; chatrā° ṇa] A pupil, disciple.
      --traṃ A kind of honey.
    Comp.
      --gaṃḍaḥ an indifferent poetical scholar, knowing only the beginnings of stanzas.
      --darśanaṃ fresh butter prepared from milk one day old.
      --vyaṃsakaḥ a roguish or dull-witted pupil. [0501-a]
