alābuḥ --būḥ f. [na-laṃbate; na-laṃb-uṇit nalopaśca vṛddhiḥ Tv.] The bottle-gourd.
      --bu (n.)
      1 A vessel made of gourd.
      2 A fruit of the gourd which is very light and floats in water; kiṃ hi nāmaitat aṃbuni majjaṃtyalābūni grāvāṇaḥ plavaṃta iti Mv. 1; Ms. 6. 54.
    Comp.
      --kaṭaṃ the dust or down (rajaḥ) of the bottle-gourd.
      --pātraṃ a jar made of the bottle-gourd. [0173-b]
