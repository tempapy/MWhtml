nāsā [nās-bhāve a]
      1 The nose; sphuradadharanāsāpuṭatayā U. 1. 29; Bg. 5. 27.
      2 The trunk of an elephant.
      3 The upper timber of a door.
      4 A sound.
    Comp.
      --agraṃ the tip of the nose; Māl. 1. 1. --chidraṃ, --raṃdhraṃ, --vivaraṃ a nostril.
      --dāru n. the upper timber of a door-frame.
      --nāhaḥ the thickening of the membrane of the nose.
      --parisrāvaḥ running at the nose. a running cold.
      --puṭaḥ, --puṭaṃ a nostril, °maryādā the septum of the nose. --vaṃśaḥ the bridge of the nose.
      --srāvaḥ a running cold. [0616-c]
