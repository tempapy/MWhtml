abhimata p. p.
      1 Desired, wished, liked, dear, beloved, favourite (person or thing); agreeable, desirable; nāsti jīvitādanyadabhimatataramiha jagati sarvajaṃtūnāṃ K. 35, 58; abhimataphalaśaṃsī cāru pusphora bāhuḥ Bk. 1. 27; yadyevamabhimataṃ Pt. 1 if you like to do so; tayorabhimataṃ vada Mv. 6. 21 choose which you will; U. 1. 44, Ś. 3. 4, Ku. 3. 23, Pt. 1. 70, Me. 49, Mu. 3. 4.
      2 Agreed or assented to, liked, approved, accepted, admitted; na kila bhavatāṃ sthānaṃ devyā gṛhe'bhimataṃ tataḥ U. 3. 32; prasiddhamāhātmyābhimatānāmapi kapilakaṇabhukprabhṛtīnāṃ Ś. B. honoured, respected.
      --taṃ Wish, desire.
      --taḥ A beloved person, lover; Śi. 7. 72, 8. 68, 10. 9. [0134-b]
