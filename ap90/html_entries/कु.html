ku ind. A prefix implying ‘badness’, ‘deterioration,’ ‘depreciation’, ‘sin,’ ‘reproach,’ ‘littleness,’ ‘want,’ ‘deficiency’ &c. Its various substitutes are kad(kadaśva), kava(kavoṣṇa), kā(koṣṇa), kiṃ (kiṃprabhuḥ); cf. Pt. 5. 17.
