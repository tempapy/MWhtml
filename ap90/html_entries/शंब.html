• śaṃba(va) a [śaṃb-ac] 1 Happy, fortunate. --2 Poor, unfortunate. --baḥ 1 The thunderbolt of Indra. --2 The iron head of a pestle. --3 An iron chain worn round the loins. --4 Ploughing ‘with the grain’ or in the regular direction. --5 The second ploughing of a field. (śaṃbākṛ means ‘to plough twice’). [1034-b]
