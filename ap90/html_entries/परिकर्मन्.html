• parikarman m. A servant. --n. 1 Painting or perfuming the body, personal decoration, dressing, toilet; kṛtācāraparikarmāṇaṃ Ś. 2. --2 Painting or dyeing the foot; Ku. 4. 19. --3 Preparation. --4 Worship, adoration. --5 (In Yoga phil.) Purifying, a means of purifying the mind; Śi. 4. 55; (see Malli. thereon). --6 An arithmetical operation (of which there are {8} divisions). [0671-a]
