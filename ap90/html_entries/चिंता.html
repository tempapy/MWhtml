• ciṃtā [ciṃt-bhāve a]
      1 Thinking, thought.
      2 Sad or sorrowful thought, care, anxiety; ciṃtājaḍaṃ darśanaṃ Ś. 4. 5; so vītaciṃtaḥ 12.
      3 Reflection, consideration.
      4 (In Rhet.) Anxiety, considered as one of the 33 subordinate feelings; dhyānaṃ ciṃtā hitānāpteḥ śūnyatāśvāsatāpakṛt S. D. 201.
    Comp.
    • --ākula a. full of care, disturbed in mind, anxious. --karman n. anxiety.
    • --para a. thoughtful, anxious.
    • --maṇiḥ {1} a fabulous gem supposed to yield to its possessor all desires, the philosopher's stone; kācamūlyena vikrīto haṃta ciṃtāmaṇirmayā Śānti. 1. 12; api ciṃtāmaṇiściṃtāpariśramamapekṣate Māl. 10. 22; tadekalubdhe hṛdi me'sti labdhuṃ citā na ciṃtāmaṇimapyanardhyaṃ N. 3. 81, 1. 145. {2} N. of Brahmā.
    • --veśman n. a council-hall. [0494-c]
