mlāna p. p. [mlai-kta tasya naḥ]
      1 Faded, withered.
      2 Wearied, weary, languid.
      3 Enfeebled, weak, feeble, faint.
      4 Sad, dejected, melancholy.
      5 Black.
      6 Foul, dirty.
      --naṃ Withering, fading.
    Comp.
      --aṃga a. weak-bodied. (--gī) a woman during her menses.
      --manas a. depressed in mind, dispirited, disheartened. [0882-c]
