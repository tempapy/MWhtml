• kaurava a. (vī f.) [kurorapatyaṃ, taddeśasya rājā teṣu bhavo vā, kuru-aṇ] 1 Relating to the Kurus; kṣetraṃ kṣatrapradhānapiśunaṃ kauravaṃ tadbhajethāḥ Me. 48. --vaḥ A descendant of Kuru; mathnāmi kauravaśataṃ samare na kopāt Ve. 1. 15; Chāṇ. 50. --2 A ruler of the Kurus. (so kauravaka, kauravāyaṇi and kauraveya). [0428-b]
