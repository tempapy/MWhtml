• nyagrodhaḥ
      1 The (Indian) figtree.
      2 A fathom (measured by the arms extended).
      3 The Śamī tree.
      4 An epithet of Viṣṇu.
    Comp.
    • --parimaṃḍalaḥ a man being a fathom in circumference; (thus described: --mahā dhanurdharāścaiva treta yāṃ cakravartinaḥ . sarvalakṣaṇasaṃpannā nyagrodhaparimaṃḍalāḥ ..).
    • --parimaṃḍalā an excellent woman; she is thus described: --stanau sukaṭhinau yasyā ni beṃ ca viśālatā . madhye kṣīṇā bhavedyā sā nyagrodhaparimaṃḍalā (Śabdak.); dūrvākāṃḍamiva śyāmā nyagrodhaparimaṃḍalā Bk. 5. 18. [0650-b]
