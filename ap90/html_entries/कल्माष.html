• kalmāṣa a. (ṣī f.) 1 Variegated, spotted. --2 Black and white. --ṣaḥ 1 The variegated colour. --2 A mixture of black and white. --3 A demon, goblin. --4 The black colour. --5 A form of Agni. --6 A kind of fragrant rice. --ṣī 1 N. of the river Yamunā --2 The spotted cow of Jamadagni. --ṣaṃ Stain. --Comp. --kaṃṭhaḥ an epithet of Śiva. --pādaḥ N. of a king (saudāsa).
