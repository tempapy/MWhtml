kalmāṣa a. (ṣī f.)

    • 1 The variegated colour.

    • 3 A demon, goblin.
    • 4 The black colour.
    • 5 A form of Agni.
    • 6 A kind of fragrant rice.
    • 1 N. of the river Yamunā
    • 2 The spotted cow of Jamadagni.

      N. of a king (saudāsa). [0388-c]
