• vai 1 P. (vāyati)
      1 To dry, be dried.
      2 To be languid or weary, be exhausted. [1012-b]
    • vai ind. A particle of affirmation or certainty (indeed, truly, forsooth), but it is generally used as an expletive; āpo vai narasūnavaḥ Ms. 1. 10; 2. 231, 9. 49, 11. 78. &c. It is also said to be a vocative particle, and sometimes shows entreaty or persuasion (anunaya). [1012-b]
