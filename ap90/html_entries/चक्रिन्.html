• cakrin a. [cakramastyasya ini] 1 Having a wheel, wheeled. --2 Bear ing a discus. --3 Driving in a carriage. --4 Circular, round. --5 Indicative (sūcaka). --m. 1 An epithet of Viṣṇu; Śi. 13. 22. --2 A potter. --3 An oilman. --4 An emperor, a universal monarch, absolute ruler. --5 The governor of a province. --6 An ass. --7 The ruddy goose. --8 An informer. --9 A snake. --10 A crow. --11 A kind of tumbler or juggler.
