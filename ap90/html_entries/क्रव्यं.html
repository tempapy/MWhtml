• kravyaṃ Raw flesh, carrion, sthapuṭagatamapi kravyamavyagramatti Māl. 5. 16. [cf. Gr. kreas; L. caro] --Comp. --ad, ad, --bhuj a. eating raw flesh; Ms. 5. 131. (--m.) {1} a carnivorous animal, such as a tiger &c. U. 1. 49. {2} a demon, goblin; R. 15. 16. --ghātanaḥ a deer (killed for its flesh.) --vāhanaḥ Ved. an epithet of Agni. [0430-c]
