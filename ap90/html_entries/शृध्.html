      I. 1 A. (but Paras. also in the Second Future, Aorist, and Conditional) (śardhate) To break wind downwards.
      II. 1 U. (śardhati-te)
      1 To moisten, wet.
      2 To cut off.
      III. 10 U. (śardhayati te)
      1 To strive.
      2 To take, grasp.
      3 To insult (as by breaking wind), mock, ridicule. [1054-a]
