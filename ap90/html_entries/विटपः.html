viṭapaḥ [viṭaṃ vistāraṃ vā pāti pibati pā° ka Tv.]
      1 A branch, bough (of a creeper or tree); komalaviṭapānukāriṇau bāhū Ś. 1. 21, 32; yadanena tarurta pātitaḥ kṣapitā tadviṭapāśritā latā R. 8. 47; Śi. 4. 48; Ku. 6. 41.
      2 A bush.
      3 A new shoot or sprout; Śi. 7. 53.
      4 A cluster, clump, thicket.
      5 Extension.
      6 The septum of the scrotum. [0972-b]
