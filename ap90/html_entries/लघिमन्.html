• laghiman m. [laghorbhāvaḥ imanic ḍicca] 1 Lightness, absence of weight. --2 Littleness, smallness, insignificance. --3 Lightness, levity, lowness or meanness of spirit; mānuṣatāsulabho laghimā praśnakarmaṇi māṃ niyojayati K. --4 Thoughtlessness, frivolity. --5 The supernatural power of assuming excessive lightness at will, one of the eight Siddhis q. v.
