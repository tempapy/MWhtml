• pragalbh {c1c} A. 1 To be bold or confident; yā kathaṃcana sakhīvacanena prāgabhipriyatamaṃ prajagalbhe Śi. 1. 18; na mauktikacchidrakarī śalākā pragalbhate karmaṇi ṭaṃkikāyāḥ Vikr. 1. 16 ‘cannot be bold (competent) enough to do the work of a hatchet’. --2 To be determined or resolute. --3 To be proud. --4 To be ready or able (with inf.).
