• bahala a. [baṃh --kalac nalopaśca]
      1 Very much, copious, abundant, plentiful, manifold, great, strong; U. 1. 38; 3. 23; Śi. 9. 8; Bv. 4. 27.
      2 Thick, dense.
      3 Shaggy (as a tail); bahalottuṃgalāṃgūla Māl. 3.
      4 Hard, firm, compact.
      5 Harsh (as a sound).
    • --laḥ A kind of sugar-cane.
    • --lā Large cardamoms.
    Comp.
    • --gaṃdhaḥ a kind of sandal.
    • --tvacaḥ the white flowering lodhra. [0791-b]
