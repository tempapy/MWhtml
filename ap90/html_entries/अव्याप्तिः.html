• avyāptiḥ f.
      1 Inadequate extent or pervasion of a proposition.
      2 Non-inclusion or exclusion of a part of the thing defined, one of the three faults of a definition; lakṣyaikadeśe lakṣaṇasyāvartanamavyāptiḥ; yathā śikhāsūtravān brāhmaṇaḥ ityasya saṃnyāsinyavyāptiḥ, tasya brāhmaṇatvepi śikhāsūtratvābhāvāt. [0199-b]
