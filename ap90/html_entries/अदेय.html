• adeya a. Not to be given; what cannot or ought not to be given away; adeyamāsīttrayameva bhūpateḥ R. 3. 16. --yaṃ That which it is not right or necessary to give. Wife, sons, deposits, and a few other things belong to this class: anvāhitaṃ yācitakamādhiḥ sādhāraṇaṃ ca yat . nikṣepaḥ putradārāśca sarvasvaṃ cānvaye sati .. āpatsvapi ca kaṣṭāsu vartamānena dehinā . adeyānyāhurācāryā yaccānyasmai pratiśrutam ..
