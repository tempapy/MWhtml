• kuṃjaḥ, --jaṃ
      1 A place overgrown with plants or creepers, a bower, an arbour; cala sakhi kuṃjaṃ satimirapuṃjaṃ śīlaya nīlanicolaṃ Gīt. 5; vaṃjulalatākuṃje 12; Me. 19; R. 9. 64.
      2 The lower jaw.
      3 A cave.
      4 A tooth.
      5 The tusk of an elephant.
    Comp.
    • --kuṭīraḥ a bower, a place overgrown with plants and creepers; guṃjatkuṃjakuṭīrakauśikaghaṭā U. 2. 29; Māl. 5. 19; kokilakūjitakuṃjakuṭīre Gīt. 1. [0406-c]
