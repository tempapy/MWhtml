    • ajakā, --ajikā(svārthe kan ṭāp)
      1 A young she-goat.
      2 [ajasya vikāraḥ avayavaḥ galastanaḥ purīṣaṃ vā] The fleshy protuberance on the neck, or its excrement.
      3 A disease of the pupil of the eye.
