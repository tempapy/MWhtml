• nivāta a. [nivṛttoniruddho vā vāto'smāt] 1 Sheltered from the wind, not windy, calm; R. 3. 17; 19. 42. --2 Unhurt, uninjured, unobstructed. --3 Safe, secure. --4 Well-armed, accoutred in strong mail. --taḥ 1 A refuge, dwelling, an asylum. --2 An impenetrable coat of mail. --taṃ 1 A place sheltered from the wind; nivātaniṣkaṃpamiva pradīpaṃ Ku. 3. 48; Ki. 14. 37; R. 13. 52, 3. 17; Bg. 6. 19. --2 Absence of wind, calm, stillness; R. 12. 36. --3 A secure spot. --4 A strong armour.
