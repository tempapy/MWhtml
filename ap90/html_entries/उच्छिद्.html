• ucchid 7 U.
      1 (a) To cut off, extirpate, eradicate, destroy; nocchiṃdyādātmano mūlaṃ pareṣāṃ cātitṛṣṇayā Mb.; kiṃ vā ripūṃstava guruḥ svayamucchinatti R. 5. 71, 2. 23; Pt. 1. 47, 365. (b) To dispel, chase away; ucchettuṃ prabhavati yanna saptasaptiḥ Ś. 6. 29. (c) To snap (thread); Māl. 9. 26.
      2 To interfere with, interrupt, stop; nyāyānucchidya Mb.; taistairbhāvairanucchinnāḥ S. D. --pass.
      1 To be cut or snapped.
      2 To be stopped or interrupted, cease; ucchidyaṃte kriyāḥ sarvāḥ grīṣme kusarito yathā Pt. 2.
      3 To waste away, fail, be wanting or deficient; etānyapi satāṃ gehe nocchidyaṃte kadācana Ms. 3. 101. [0287-b]
