• vatsala a. [vatsaṃ lāti lā-ka] 1 Child-loving, affectionate towards children or offspring; as vatsalā dhenuḥ mātā &c. --2 Affectionate towards, fondly loving, devoted to, fond of, kind or compassionate towards; tvadvatsalaḥ, kva sa tapasvijanasya haṃtā Māl. 8. 8; 6. 14; R. 2. 69, 8. 41; so śaraṇāgatavatsala; dīnavatsala &c. --laḥ 1 A fire fed with grass. --2 N. of Viṣṇu. --3 The sentiment of affection (vātsalyarasa). --lā A cow fond of her calf. --laṃ Affection, fondness; Pt. 2. 9.
