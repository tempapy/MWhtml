• vivarṇa a.
      1 Colourless, pale, wan, pallid; nareṃdramārgaṭṭa iva prapede vivarṇabhāvaṃ sa sa bhūmipālaḥ R. 6. 67.
      2 Discoloured, deprived of water (as a gem.); vivarṇamaṇīkṛtaṃ (kanakavalayaṃ) Ś. 3. 13.
      3 Low, vile.
      4 Ignorant, stupid, unlettered.
    • --rṇaḥ An outcast, a man belonging to low caste. [0992-b]
