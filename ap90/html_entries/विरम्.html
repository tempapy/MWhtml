• viram {c1c} P. 1 To end, terminate, come to an end; aviditagatayāmā rātrireva vyaraṃsīt U. 1. 27; 6. 33. --2 To cease, desist, stop, leave off (speaking &c.); etāvaduktvā virate mṛgeṃdre R. 2. 51; Śi. 2. 13; oft. with abl.; hā haṃta kimiti cittaṃ viramati nādyapi viṣayebhyaḥ Bv. 4. 25; na sthirakarmā virarāma karmaṇaḥ R. 8. 22; vatsaitasmādvirama viramātaḥ paraṃ na kṣamosmi U. 1. 33; Bh. 2. 80.
