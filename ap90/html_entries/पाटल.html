pāṭala a. [paṭ-ṇic kalac]
      1 Palered, of a pink or pale-red colour; agre strīnakhapāṭalaṃ kurabakaṃ V. 2. 7; R. 1. 83; 2. 29; 7. 27; pāṭalapāṇijāṃkitamuraḥ Gīt. 12. --laḥ The pale-red or pink colour; kapolapāṭalādeśi babhūva raghuceṣṭitaṃ R. 4. 68.
      2 The trumpetflower, Bignonia Suaveolens; pāṭalasaṃsargasuramivanavātāḥ Ś. 1. 3. --laṃ
      1 The flower of this tree; R. 16. 52; 19. 46.
      2 A kind of rice ripening in the rains.
      3 Saffron.
    Comp.
