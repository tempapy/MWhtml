• hiṃgu m., n. [himaṃ gacchati gam-ḍu ni0]
      1 The plant called Asa foetida.
      2 The substance prepared from this plant (asa fœtida) for household use, especially in seasoning articles of food.
    Comp.
    • --niryāsaḥ {1} the gummy exudation of the hingu tree. {2} the nimba tree.
    • --patraḥ the ingudi tree. [1171-b]
