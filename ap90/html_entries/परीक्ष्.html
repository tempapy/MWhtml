parīkṣ 1 A.
      1 To examine, look at or scrutinize carefully; ataḥ parīkṣya kartavyaṃ viśeṣātsaṃgataṃ rahaḥ Ś. 5. 24; saṃtaḥ parīkṣyānyataradbhajaṃte M. 1. 2; Ms. 9. 14.
      2 To test, try, put to the test; māyāṃ mayodbhāvya parīkṣito'si R. 2. 65; yatnātparīkṣitaḥ puṃstve Y. 1. 55 ‘carefully tested as to potency’.
      3 To observe, perceive. [0683-b]
