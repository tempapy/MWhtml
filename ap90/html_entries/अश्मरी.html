aśmarī [aśmānaṃ rāti rā-ka gaurā° ṅīṣ] (In medicine) A disease called stone (in the bladder), gravel, strangury.
    Comp.
      --ghnaḥ N. of the tree varuṇa (used as a lithontriptic). --bhedanaṃ a lithontriptic.
      --haraḥ N. of the tree Pentaptera Arjuna, used as a lithontriptic. [0202-b]
