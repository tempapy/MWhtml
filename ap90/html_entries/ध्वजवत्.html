dhvajavat a.
      1 Adorned with flags.
      2 Having a mark.
      3 Having the mark of a criminal, branded. --m.
      1 A standard-bearer.
      2 A vendor of spirituous liquors, distiller.
      3 A Brāhmaṇa who carries with him the skull of the man murdered by him to places of pilgrimage by way of penance. [0603-b]
