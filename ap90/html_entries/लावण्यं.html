lāvaṇyaṃ [lavaṇasya bhāvaḥ ṣyañ]
      1 Saltness.
      2 Beauty, loveliness, charm; tathāpi tasyā lāvaṇyaṃ rekhayā kiṃcidanvitaṃ Ś. 6. 13; 7. 18; (lāvaṇya is thus defined in Śabdak: --muktāphaleṣu chāyāyāstaralatvamivāṃtarā . pratibhāti yadaṃgeṣu tallāvaṇyamihocyate ..).
    Comp.
      --arjitaṃ the private property of a married woman given to her at her marriage by her father or mother-in-law. [0928-b]
