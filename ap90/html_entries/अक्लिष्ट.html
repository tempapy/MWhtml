akliṣṭa a. [na. ta.]
    • 1 Unwearied, untroubled, not annoyed, undisturbed, indefatigable.
    • 2 Not marred, unimpaired,; idamupanatamevaṃ rūpamakliṣṭakāṃti Ś. 5. 19 of unimpaired or unblemished beauty; anyathā kathamiyaṃ °tā lāvaṇyasya K. 12 unmarred state, perfection.
    • 3 Not laboured or elaborate.
      Comp. a. unwearied in actions.
      a. not confused, distinct; °rṇāṃ gamanābhyanujñāṃ K. 293 given in plain, distinct terms (of unfaded colour).
      a. not swerving from religious vows, unwearied in observing them. [0005-c]
