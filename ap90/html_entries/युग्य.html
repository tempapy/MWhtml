• yugya a. [yugāya hitaḥ yat] 1 Fit to be yoked. --2 Belonging to a yoke. --3 Yoked, harnessed. --4 Drawn by; as in aśvayugyo rathaḥ, --gyaḥ Any yoked or draught animal, especially a chariot-horse; hariyugyaṃ rathaṃ tasmai prajighāya puraṃdaraḥ R. 12. 84. --gyaṃ A vehicle, carriage. --Comp. --vāhaḥ a coachman.
