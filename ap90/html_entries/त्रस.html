• trasa a. [tras-ghañarye ka] Moveable, locomotive.
    • --saḥ The heart.
    • --saṃ
      1 A wood, forest.
      2 Animals.
      3 The aggregate of moving or living beings.
      4 Animals and men.
    Comp.
    • --reṇaḥ {1} an atom, the mote or atom of dust which is seen moving in a sunbeam; cf. jālāṃtaragate bhānau sūkṣmaṃ yaddṛśyate rajaḥ . prathamaṃ tatpramāṇānāṃ trasareṇuṃ pracakṣate .. Ms. 8. 132; also Y. 1. 361. {2} N. of one of the wives of the sun. [0548-b]
