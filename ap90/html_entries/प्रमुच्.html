• pramuc {c6c} P. 1 To set free, liberate, release. --2 To throw, cast, hurl. --3 To shed, emit, send forth. --4 To abandon, forsake, give up, renounce. --5 To loosen, untie, unbind. --6 To expel, drive away, banish. --7 To utter. --8 To put on, wear (as a garland &c.). --Pass. 1 To be loose or detached. --2 To leave off, cease. --3 To free oneself from. --Caus. 1 To loosen, unbind. --2 To liberate, release.
