• saṃj 1 P. (sajati, sakta; the s of the root being changed to ṣ after a preposition ending in i or u)
      1 To stick or adhere to, cling to; tulyagaṃdhiṣu mattebhakaṭeṣu phalareṇavaḥ (sasaṃjuḥ); R. 4. 47.
      2 To go, move.
      3 To fasten. --Pass. (sajyate) To be attached to, cling or adhere to. [1081-c]
