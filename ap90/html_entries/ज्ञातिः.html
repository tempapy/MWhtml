• jñātiḥ [jñā-ktic] 1 A paternal relation, a father, brother &c. agnate relatives collectively. --2 A kinsman or kindred in general. --3 A distant kinsman who is not entitled to the oblations offered to deceased ancestors. --4 A father. --Comp. --karman n., --kāryaṃ the duty of a kinsman. --bhāvaḥ kin, relationship. --bhedaḥ dissension among relatives. --vid a. one who has or makes near relatives.
