• śrauta a. (tī f.) [śrutau vihitaṃ aṇ] 1 Relating to the ear. --2 Relating to, founded on, or prescribed by, the Veda. --3 Sacrificial. --taṃ 1 Any observance prescribed by the Vedas. --2 Ritual enjoined by the Vedas. --3 Preservation or maintenance of the sacred fire. --4 The three sacred fires collectively; (i. e. gārhapatya, āhavanīya and dakṣiṇa). --Comp. --karman n. a Vedic rite. --sūtraṃ N. of a class of Sūtra works based on the Veda (ascribed to āśvalāyana, sāṃkhyāyana, kātyāyana &c.).
