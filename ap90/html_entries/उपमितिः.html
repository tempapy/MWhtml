• upamitiḥ f. 1 Resemblance, comparison, similarity; pallavopamitisāmyasapakṣaṃ S. D.; tadānanasyopamitau daridratā N. 1. 24. --2 (In Nyāya phil.) Analogy, inference, deduction, knowledge of things derived from analogy, a conclusion deduced by means of an upamāna; pratyakṣamapyanumitistathopamitiśabdaje Bhāṣā P. 52. --3 A figure of speech = upamā q. v. --4 A likeness, picture.
