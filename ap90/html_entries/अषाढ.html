aṣāḍha a.
    • 1 Ved. Not to be overcome, invincible; Rv. 2. 21. 2.
    • 2 Born under the constellation Aṣāḍha (P. IV. 3. 34).
    • 1 The month Aṣāḍha commencing with the sun's entrance into Gemini (usually written āṣāḍha).
    • 2 A staff made of the wood of Palāśa, carried by a religious student or ascetic.
    • 3 N. of the Malaya mountain.
      N. of a constellation, the twentieth (pūrvāṣāḍhā) and twenty-first (uttarāṣāḍhā) lunar mansions. [0205-c]
