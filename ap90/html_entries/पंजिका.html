• paṃjikā 1 A perpetual or running commentary which explains and analyses every word; ṭīkā niraṃtaravyākhyā paṃjikā padanaktikā. --2 A journal, a book in which accounts of receipts and expenditure are entered. --3 The record or register of human actions kept by Yama. (For other senses see paṃji above). --Comp. --kārakaḥ a scribe, a man of the Kāyastha tribe. [0658-b]
