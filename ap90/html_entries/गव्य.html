gavya a. [gave hitaṃ yat]
      1 Consisting of cattle or cows.
      2 Coming or got from a cow (as milk, curds &c.).
      3 Proper or fit for cattle.
      4 Sacred to the cow, worshipping the cow.
      --vyaṃ
      1 Cattle, a herd of cows.
      2 Pasture-land.
      3 The milk of a cow.
      4 A bow-string.
      5 Colouring substance, yellow pigment.
      --vyā
      1 A herd of cows.
      2 A measure of distance equal to two Krośas.
      3 A bow-string.
      4 A colouring substance, yellow pigment. [0456-a]
