• agrima a. [agre bhavaḥ; agra-ḍimac P. IV. 3. 23. Vārt.]
      1 First (in order, rank &c); foremost, best, excellent, preferable, superior; vīrāṇāmagrimo bhūtvā yuyudhe pārthivaiḥ saha Mb.
      2 Prior, preceding.
      3 Elder, eldest.
      4 Furthest, advanced, first ripe.
      5 Further.
    • --maḥ An elder brother.
    • --mā A kind of fruit, Annona Reticulata. [0015-c]
