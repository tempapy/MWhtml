jyotiṣmat a. [jyotirastyasya matup]
      1 Luminous, bright, shining, possessed of luminous bodies; nakṣatratārāgrahasaṃkulāpi jyotiṣmatī caṃdramasaiva rātriḥ R. 6. 22.
      2 Celestial. --m.
      1 The sun.
      2 N. of the third foot of Brahmā.
      3 N. of one of the seven suns appearing at the destruction of the world.
      --tī
      1 The night (as illuminated by the stars).
      2 (In phil.) A state of mind pervaded by sattvaguṇa i. e. a tranquil state of mind. [0520-a]
