anaḍuh m. [anaḥ śakaṭaṃ vahati] ( anaḍvān, °ḍvāhau, °ḍudbhyāṃ &c.)
      1 An ox, bull.
      2 The sign Taurus.
      --hī or anaḍvāhī A cow.
    Comp.
      --jihvā [anaḍuho jihveva patrāṇi yasyāḥ sā] a plant (gojihvā) ( anaṃtamūla.)
      --da a. [upa. sa.] a giver of oxen. [0055-c]
