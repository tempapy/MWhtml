nṛ [nī-ṛn ḍicca; cf. Uṇ. 2. 101] (Nom. sing. nā, gen. pl. nṛṇāṃ or nṝṇāṃ)
      1 A man, a person whether male or female; Ms. 3. 81; 4. 61, 7. 61, 10. 33.
      2 Mankind.
      3 A piece at chess.
      4 The pin of a sun-dial.
      5 A masculine word; saṃdhirnā vigraho yānaṃ Ak.
      6 A leader.
