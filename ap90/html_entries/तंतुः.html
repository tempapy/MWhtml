• taṃtuḥ [tan tun]
      1 A thread, cord, wire, string, line; ciṃtāsaṃtatitaṃtu Māl. 5. 10; Me. 70.
      2 A cob-web; R. 16. 20.
      3 A filament; bisataṃtuguṇasya kāritaṃ Ku. 4. 29.
      4 An offspring, issue, race.
      5 A shark.
      6 The Supreme Being.
    Comp.
