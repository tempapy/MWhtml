• puṣkala a. [puṣ-kalac kicca; puṣkasidhmā° lac vā Tv.] 1 Much, copious, abundant; bhakṣitenāpi bhavatā nāhāro mama puṣkalaḥ H. 1. 84; Ms. 3. 277; Pt. 1. 63. --2 Full, complete; Bg. 11. 21. --3 Rich, magnificent, splendid. --4 Excellent, best, eminent. --5 Near. --6 Loud, resonant, resounding. --laḥ 1 A kind of drum. --2 An epithet of Śiva. --3 Of mount Meru. --laṃ 1 A particular measure of capacity = 64 handfuls. --2 Alms to the extent of four morsels of food.
