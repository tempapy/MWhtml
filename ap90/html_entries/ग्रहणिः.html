• grahaṇiḥ, --ṇī f. 1 An imaginary organ supposed to lie between the stomach and the intestines. --2 The small intestines or that part of the alimentary canal where the bile assists digestion and from which vital warmth is diffused. --3 Diarrhœa, dysentery. --Comp. --haraṃ cloves. [0473-a]
