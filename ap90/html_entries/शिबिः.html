• śibiḥ(viḥ)
      1 A beast of prey.
      2 The birch tree.
      3 N. of a country (pl).
      4 N. of a king (who is said to have saved Agni in the form of a dove from Indra in the form of a hawk by offering an equal quantity of his own flesh weighed in a balance); Mu. 6. 17. [1045-a]
