snātakaḥ
      1 A Brāhmaṇa who has performed the ceremony of ablution which has to be performed on his finishing his first Āśrama (that of a Brahmacārin).
      2 A Brāhmaṇa just returned from the house of his preceptor and become an initiated householder (gṛhastha).
      3 A Brāhmaṇa who is a Bhikṣu (beggar of alms) for any religious object; Ms. 11. 2.
      4 Any man of the first three classes who is an initiated householder. [1151-b]
