  • kṣuṇṇa p. p. [kṣud-kta] 1 Pounded, crushed to pieces, bruised. --2 Powdered, pulverized, ground. --3 Beaten, trodden (as a path); R. 1. 17. --4 (Fig.) Practised, followed; kṣudrajanakṣuṇṇa eṣa mārgaḥ K. 146; a° unusual; Māl. 3. --5 Violated (a vow). --6 Exercised, practised, skilful. --7 One versed in the sacred science but unable to teach it. --8 Overcome, defeated. --Comp. --manas a. penitent, repentant.
