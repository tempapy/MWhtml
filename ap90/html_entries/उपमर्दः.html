• upamardaḥ 1 Friction, rubbing or pressing down, crushing under one's weight; anyāsu tāvadupamardasahāsu bhṛṃga lolaṃ vinodaya manaḥ sumanolatāsu S. D. (where u° also means rough handling or enjoyment). --2 Destruction, injury, killing. --3 Reproach, abuse, insult, harsh or insulting treatment; Mk. 1. --4 Unhusking. --5 Refutation of a charge. --6 Stirring, shaking.
