rucira a. [ruciṃ rāti dadāti rā-ka]
      1 Bright, shining, brilliant, radiant; hemarucirāṃbara Ch. P. 14; kanakaruciraṃ, ratnaruciraṃ &.
      2 Tasteful, palatable.
      3 Sweet, dainty.
      4 Stomachic, exciting appetite.
      5 Cordial, restorative.
      --rā
      1 A kind of yellow pigment.
      2 N. of a metre; see App. I.
      --raṃ
      1 Saffron.
      2 Cloves.
      3 A radish. [0914-b]
