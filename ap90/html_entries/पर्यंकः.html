paryaṃkaḥ
      1 A bed, couch, sofa.
      2 A palanquin.
      3 A cloth girt round the back, loins, and kness (by a person) when sitting on his hams; cf. avasakthikā.
      4 A particular kind of posture practised by ascetics in meditation, sitting on the hams; it is the same as vīrāsana which is thus defined by Vasiṣṭha: --ekaṃ pādamathaikasmin vinyasyorau tu saṃsthitaṃ . itarasmiṃsvathaivoruṃ vīrāsanamuda hṛtaṃ ..; paryaṃkagraṃthibaṃdha &c. Mk. 1. 1.
    Comp.
      --baṃdhaḥ sitting on the hams, the posture called paryaṃkaḥ paryaṃkabaṃdhasthirapūrvakāyaṃ Ku. 3. 45, 59. --bhogin m. a kind of serpent. [0684-c]
