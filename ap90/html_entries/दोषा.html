• doṣā ind. At night; doṣā'pi nūnamahimāṃśurasau kileti Śi. 4. 46; 62. --f.
      1 The arm.
      2 The darkness of night, night; gharmakāladivasa iva kṣapitadoṣaḥ K. 37 (where the word means ‘a fault or sin’ also).
    Comp.
    • --āsyaḥ, --tilakaḥ a lamp.
    • --karaḥ the moon. [0582-b]
