• ekādaśa a. (śī f.) 1 Eleventh. --2 Consisting of eleven. --3 Lasting for eleven months. --śī 1 The eleventh day of every fortnight of a lunar month, sacred to Viṣṇu; (when fasting is enjoined and is considered to be productive of great religious merit). --2 Presentations of offerings to deceased ancestors or Pitṛs on the eleventh day after decease. --śaṃ The number eleven. --Comp. --ahaḥ {1} a collection of 11 days. {2} a sacrifice lasting for eleven days. --uttamaḥ N. of Śiva (the chief of the 11 Rudras). --dvāraṃ the eleven holes of the body; see kha. --rudrāḥ (pl.) the eleven Rudras; see rudra.
