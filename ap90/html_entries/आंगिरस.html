• āṃgirasa a. (sī f.) Descended from or referring to Aṅgiras.
    • --saḥ
      1 N. of Bṛhaspati, son of Aṅgiras; Ms. 2. 151.
      2 Descendants of Angiras (pl.).
      3 A particular Sūkta or hymn in the Atharvaveda.
      4 The soul.
      5 A Kṣatriya by will of Brahmā and by profession. [0228-b]
