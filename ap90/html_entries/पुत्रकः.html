• putrakaḥ [putra anukaṃpāyāṃ saṃjñāyāṃ vā kan svārthe ka vā] 1 A little son or boy, boy, chap, lad (often used as a term of endearment). --2 A doll, puppet; Ku. 1. 29. --3 A rogue, cheat. --4 A locust, grass-hopper. --5 A fabulous animal with eight feet (śarabha). --6 Hair. --7 A pitiable person.
