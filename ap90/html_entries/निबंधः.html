      3 Composing, writing down.

      5 A compendium.
      6 Restraint, obstruction, confinement.
      7 Suppression of urine.
      8 A bond, fetter.
      9 A grant of property, an assignment of cattle, money &c. for support; bhūryā pitāmahopāttā nibaṃdho dravyameva vā Y. 2. 121.
      10 Fixed property.
      11 Foundation, origin.
      12 Cause, reason.
