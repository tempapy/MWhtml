• kṣviḍ 1 U. (kṣveḍati-te, kṣveṭṭa or kṣveḍita) To hum, roar, whistle, growl, murmur, sound indistinctly; Ms. 4. 64. [0440-c]
    • kṣviḍ 1 A., kṣvid 4 P.

      2 To emit sap, or discharge juice, ichor &c., exude. --WITH pra to murmur, hum; Bk. 7. 103. [0440-c]
