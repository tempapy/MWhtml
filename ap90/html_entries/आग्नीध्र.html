• āgnīdhra a. Belonging to the agnīdhra (the priest who kindles the fire). --dhraṃ [āgnimiṃdhe agnīt tasya śaraṇaṃ, raṇ bhatvānna jaś Tv.] 1 The place where the sacrificial or sacred fire is kindled. --2 The duty of the priest who kindles the sacred fire. --dhrā Care of the sacred fire. --dhraḥ The priest who kindles the sacred fire.
