• pramukha a. 1 Facing, turning the face towards. --2 Chief, principal, foremost, first. --3 Respectable, honourable,. --4 (At the end of comp.) (a) Headed by, having as chief or at the head; vāsukipramukhāḥ Ku. 2. 38. (b) Accompanied with; prītipramukhavacanaṃ svāgataṃ vyājahāra Me. 4. --khaḥ 1 A respectable man. --2 A heap, multitude. --3 The tree called Punnāga. --khaṃ 1 The mouth --2 The beginning of a chapter or section. --3 The time being, the present. (pramukhatas and pramukhe are used adverbially in the sense of ‘in front of’, ‘before’ ‘opposite to’; Bg. 1. 25; Ś. 7. 22). [0755-c]
