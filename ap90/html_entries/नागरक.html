• nāgaraka, nāgarika a. [nagare bhavaḥ vuñ] 1 Town-bred, town-born. --2 Polite, courteous, courtly; nāgarikavṛttyā saṃjñāpayaināṃ Ś. 5; sādhu ārya nāgarikosi V. 2. --3 Clever, shrewd, cunning (vidagdha). --kaḥ 1 A citizen. --2 A polite or courteous man, a gallant; one who shows exaggerated attention to his first mistress while he is courting some one else. --3 One who has contracted the vices of a town. --4 A thief. --5 An artist. --6 The chief of the police; V. 5; Ś. 6. --kaṃ Dry ginger. (--rikaṃ) The toll levied from a town. [0613-a]
