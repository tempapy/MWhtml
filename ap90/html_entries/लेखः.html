lekhaḥ [likh-bhāve ghañ]
      1 A writing, document, written document (of any kind), a letter; lekhoyaṃ na mameti nottaramidaṃ mudrā madīyā yataḥ Mu. 5. 18; nirdhārite'rthe lekhena khalūktvā khalu vācikaṃ Śi. 2. 70; anaṃgalekha Ku. 1. 7; manmathalekha Ś. 3. 26.
      2 A god, deity.
    Comp.
      --akṣaraṃ writing (opp. ālekhya).
      --ādhikārin m. one in charge of writing letters, the secretary (of a king &c.).
      --arhaḥ a kind of palm tree.
      --ṛṣabhaḥ N. of Indra.
      --patraṃ, --patrikā {1} an epistle, a letter, writing in general. {2} a deed, document (legal).
      --saṃdeśaḥ a written message.
      --hāraḥ, --hārin m. a letter-carrier. [0932-a]
