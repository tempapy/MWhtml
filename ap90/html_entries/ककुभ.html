kakubha a. Ved. Distinguished, superior.
    • 1 A crooked piece of wood at the end of the lute.
    • 2 The tree Arjuna; kakubhasurabhiḥ śailaḥ U. 1. 33.
    • 3 A kind of goblin or evil spirit.
    • 4 One of the Rāgas or personified musical modes.
    • 1 Space; quarter.
    • 2 One of the Rāgiṇīs.
      A flower of the Kuṭaja tree; Me. 22.
      Comp. ‘food of the sky,’ a kind of fragrance or perfume. [0366-a]
