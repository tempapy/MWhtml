• kakubha a. Ved. Distinguished, superior. --bhaḥ 1 A crooked piece of wood at the end of the lute. --2 The tree Arjuna; kakubhasurabhiḥ śailaḥ U. 1. 33. --3 A kind of goblin or evil spirit. --4 One of the Rāgas or personified musical modes. --bhā 1 Space; quarter. --2 One of the Rāgiṇīs. --bhaṃ A flower of the Kuṭaja tree; Me. 22. --Comp. --adanī ‘food of the sky,’ a kind of fragrance or perfume.
