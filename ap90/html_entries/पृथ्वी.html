• pṛthvī [pṛthu-ṅīṣ]
      1 The earth.
      2 The earth as one of the five elements.
      3 Large cardamoms.
      4 N. of a metre; (see App. I.)
    Comp.
    • --īśaḥ, --patiḥ, --pālaḥ, --bhuj m. a king, sovereign.
    • --khātaṃ a cavern.
    • --garbhaḥ an epithet of Gaṇeśa.
    • --gṛhaṃ a cave, grotto.
    • --jaḥ {1} a tree. {2} the planet Mars.
    • --dharaḥ a mountain. [0719-a]
