• aha ind. A particle implying(a) praise (pūjā); (b) separation; (c) resolution, ascertainment, certainty; and translated by ‘surely,’ ‘certainly,’ ‘yes,’ ‘well’; (d) rejecting; (e) sending; (f) deviation from custom, impropriety; tvamaha grāmaṃ gaccha, tvamaha rathetāraṇyaṃ gaccha Sk.; svayamaha rathena yāti 3 upādhyāyaṃ padātiṃ gamayati Sk. [0217-b]
