apnaḥ, apnas n. [āp --asun nuṭ thasvaśca, Uṇ. 4. 207]
      1 Possession, property.
      2 Work, sacrificial act or rite, any act.
      3 Offspring.
      4 Form, shape.
      5 To be obtained (used actively); [cf. L. ops.]
    Comp.
      --stha a. superintending work (karmaṇyadhikṛta); (or m.) proprietor of an estate.
      --rājaḥ lord of wealth or property, illustrious by means of works; directing works to be done (karmapreraka). [0120-c]
