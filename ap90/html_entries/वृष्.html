• vṛṣ {vI.v} {c1c} P. (varṣati, vṛṣṭa) 1 To rain (usually with words signifying ‘Indra’, ‘Parjanya’, ‘cloud’, &c. as the subject of the verb, or sometimes used impersonally); dvādaśa varṣaṇi na vavarṣa daśaśatākṣaḥ Dk.; kāle varṣaṃtu meghāḥ; garja vā varṣa vā śakra Mk. 5. 31; meghā varṣaṃtu garjaṃtu muṃcaṃtvaśanimeva vā 5. 16. --2 To rain or pour down, shower down; varṣatīvāṃjanaṃ nabhaḥ Mk. 1. 34; so śaravṛṣṭiṃ, kusumavṛṣṭiṃ, varṣati &c. --3 To pour forth, shed. --4 To grant, bestow. --5 To moisten. --6 To produce, engender. --7 To have supreme power. --8 To strike, hurt. --9 To drink. {vII.v} {c10c} A. (varṣayate) 1 To be powerful or eminent. --2 To have the power of production. [1008-a]
