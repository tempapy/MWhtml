• nakraḥ [na krāmatīti] 1 A crocodile, an alligator; nakraḥ svasthānamāsādya gajeṃdramapi karṣati Pt. 3. 46; R. 7. 30; 16. 55. --2 The sign Scorpio of the zodiac. --kraṃ 1 The upper timber of a door. --2 The nose. --krā 1 The nose. --2 A swarm of bees or wasps. --Comp. --rāj m., --rājaḥ, --hārakaḥ a shark or any other large sea-animal.
