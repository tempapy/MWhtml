mardana a. (nī f.) [mṛd-lyu lyuṭ vā]
      1 Crushing, grinding, destroying, tormenting, rubbing, &c.
      --naṃ Crushing, grinding.
      2 Rubbing, shampooing.
      3 Anointing (with unguents &c.).
      4 Pressing, kneading.
      5 Paining, tormenting, afflicting.
      6 Destroying.
      7 Devastating, laying waste.
      8 Opposition of planets.
      9 Breaking up (as of ice &c.). [0846-c]
