gehaṃ [go gaṇeśo gaṃdharvo vā īhaḥ īpsito yatra Tv.] A house, habitation; sā nārī vidhavā jātā gehe roditi tatpatiḥ Subhāṣ. N. B. The loc. of this word is used with several words to form aluk Tat. compounds; e. g. gehekṣveḍin a. ‘bellowing at home only’, i. e. a coward, poltroon. gehedāhin a. ‘sharp at home only’ i. e. a coward. gehenardin a. ‘shouting defiance at home only’; i. e. a coward, dunghill-cock. gehemehin a. ‘making water at home;’ i. e. indolent. gehevyāḍaḥ a braggadocio, braggart, boaster. geheśūraḥ ‘a househero’, a carpet-knight, boasting coward. [0466-c]
