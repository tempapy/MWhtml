kaṃṭakin a. (nī f.)
      1 Thorny, prickly; kaṃṭakino vanāṃtāḥ Vikr. 1. 116.
      2 Vexatious, troublesome. --m. A fish. --m. f. N. of several plants: --khadira, madana, gokṣura, vaṃśa, badara.
    Comp.
      --jaṃ a. {1} produced from a fish. {2} produced by the Mimosa tree.
      --phalaḥ the bread-fruit tree (panasa). (--lā) madhukharjurī plant. [0371-c]
