bahulībhū 1 P.
      1 To spread, increase, multiply; chidreṣvanarthā bahulībhavaṃti Pt. 2. 175.
      2 To get abroad, to become public or notorious, be generally known, become wide-spread; bahulībhūtametat kiṃ na kathyate Ś. 6; paureṣu sohaṃ bahu lībhavaṃtaṃ… soḍhuṃ na tatpūrvamavarṇamīśe R. 14. 38. [0793-b]
