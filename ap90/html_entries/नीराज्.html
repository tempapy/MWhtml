nīrāj(nis-rāj) Caus.
      1 To cause to shine, illuminate, make brilliant, adorn, irradiate; divyāstrasphuradugradīdhitiśikhānīrājitajyaṃ dhanuḥ U. 6. 18; nīrājayaṃti bhūpālāḥ pādapīṭhāṃtabhūtalaṃ Prab. 2; Śi. 17. 17.
      2 To perform the ceremony called nīrājana (q. v. below) over a person or thing (wave lights before one as a mark of respect or by way of worship); nānāyodhasamākīrṇo nīrājitahayadvipaḥ Kām. 4. 66. [0645-c]
