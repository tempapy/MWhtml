vārṣika a. (kī f.) [varṣe varṣātu vā bhavaḥ ṭhañ]
      1 Belonging to the rains or rainy season vārṣikaṃ saṃjahāreṃdro dhanurjaitraṃ raghurdadhau R. 4. 16.
      2 Annual, yearly.
      3 Lasting for one year; mānuṣāṇāṃ pramāṇaṃ syādbhuktirvai daśavārṣikī; so vārṣikamannaṃ Y. 1. 124. --kaṃ N. of a medicinal plant. [0962-a]
