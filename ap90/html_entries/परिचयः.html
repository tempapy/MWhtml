• paricayaḥ
      1 Heaping up, accumulation.
      2 Acquaintance, familiarity, intimacy; puruṣaparicayena Mk. 1. 56; atiparicayādavajñā ‘familiarity breeds contempt’; paricayaṃ calalakṣyanipātane R. 9. 49; sakalakalāparicayaḥ K. 76.
      3 Trial, study, practice, frequent repetition; hetuḥ paricayasthairye vakturguṇanikaiva sā Śi. 2. 75; 11. 5; varṇaparicayaṃ karoti Ś. 5.
      4 Recognition; Me. 9.
    Comp.
    • --karuṇā increasing love or tenderness; Māl. 6. 16. [0673-b]
