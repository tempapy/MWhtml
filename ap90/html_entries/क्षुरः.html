• kṣuraḥ [kṣur-ka]
      1 A razor; R. 7. 46; Ms. 9. 292.
      2 A razor-like barb attached to an arrow.
      3 The hoof of a cow or horse.
      4 An arrow.
      5 The foot of a bed-stead.
    Comp.
    • --karman n. --kriyā the act of shaving; Pt. 1. 386. --catuṣṭayaṃ the four things necessary for shaving.
    • --dhānaṃ, --bhāṃḍaṃ a razor-case.
    • --dhāra a. as sharp as a razor.
    • --praḥ {1} an arrow with a sharp horse-shoe-shaped head; taṃ kṣurapraśakalīkṛtaṃ kṛtī R. 11. 29; 9. 62. {2} a sort of hoe, a weeding-spade.
    • --mardin, muṃḍin m. a barber. [0439-a]
