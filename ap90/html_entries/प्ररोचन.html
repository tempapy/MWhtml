• prarocana a. Exciting or enticing. --naṃ 1 Exciting, or stimulating. --2 Illustration, explanation. --3 Seduction. --4 Exhibition (of a person) for being seen and liked (by the people); alokasāmānyaguṇastanūjaḥ prarocanārthaṃ prakaṭīkṛtaśca Māl. 1. 10 (where Jagaddhara interprets prarocanārthaṃ by pravṛttipāṭavārthaṃ ‘in order to be thoroughly acquainted with the world’). --5 Favourable description of that which is to follow in a play. --6 Representation of the end as all but accomplished; see S. D. 388; (prarocanā also in the last two senses). [0757-c]
