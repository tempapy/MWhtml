• paṭahaḥ
      1 A kettle-drum, a wardrum, drum, tabor; kurvan saṃdhyābalipaṭahatāṃ śūlinaḥ ślāghanīyāṃ Me. 34; paṭpaṭahadhvanibhirvinītanidraḥ R. 9. 71.
      2 Beginning, undertaking.
      3 Injuring, killing.
    Comp.
    • --ghoṣakaḥ a crier (who beats a drum and then makes the proclamation).
    • --bhramaṇaṃ going about with a drum to call people together. [0659-a]
