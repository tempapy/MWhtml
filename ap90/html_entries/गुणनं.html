• guṇanaṃ [guṇ-lyuṭ]
      1 Multiplication.
      2 Enumeration.
      3 Describing merits or qualities, pointing out or enumerating merits; iha rasabhaṇane kṛtahariguṇane madhuripupadasevake Gīt. 7. --nī Examining books, studying; collating and correcting copies to determine the value of variants. [0461-c]
