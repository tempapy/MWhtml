• darśaka a. (kā or rśikā f.) [dṛś-ṇic ṇvul]
      1 Seeing, observing, &c.
      2 Showing, pointing out; Ku. 6. 52.
      3 Examining, looking out for.
      4 Explaining, making clear, elucidating.
    • --kaḥ
      1 One who shows or exhibits.
      2 A door-keeper, warder.
      3 A skilful man, one proficient in any art or science. [0576-c]
