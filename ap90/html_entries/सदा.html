• sadā ind. Always, ever, perpetually, at all times. --Comp. --ānaṃda a. ever happy. (--daḥ) an epithet of Śiva. --gati {1} wind. {2} the universal spirit. {3} the sun. {4} everlasting happiness, final beatitude. --toyā, --nīrā {1} N. of the Karatoyā river. {2} a river always bearing water, a running stream. --dāna a. always making gifts or exuding rut; sadādānaḥ parikṣīṇaḥ śasta eva karīśvaraḥ Pt. 2. 70. (--naḥ) {1} a ruttish elephant. {2} a scent-elephant (= gaṃdhadviṣa q. v.). {3} N. of the elephant of Indra. {4} N. of Gaṇeśa. --nartaḥ a kind of bird, the wagtail --puṣpa a. everflowering. (--ṣpaḥ) the cocoa-nut tree. --prasūnaḥ N. of various plants:-- sohita, arka and kuṃda. --phala a. always bearing fruit. (--laḥ) {1} the Bilva tree. {2} the jack tree. {3} the glomerous fig-tree. {4} the cocoanut tree. --bhavya a. always present. --yogin m. an epithet of Kṛṣṇa. --śivaḥ N. of Śiva. [1085-b]
