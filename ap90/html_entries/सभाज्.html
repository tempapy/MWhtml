sabhāj 10 U. (sabhājayati-te)
      1 To salute, pay respects, greet, render homage to, congratulate; snehātsabhājayitumetya U. 1. 7; Śi. 13. 14; Ś. 5.
      2 To honour, worship, respect.
      3 To please, gratify.
      4 To beautify, adorn, grace; U. 4. 19.
      5 To show. [1092-c]
