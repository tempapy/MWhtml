tara A Taddhita affix of the comparative degree, added to adjectives, nouns, and also to verbs and indeclinables in which latter case it is changed to tarām. It is added, like tama, to pronouns in the sense of ‘one of two,’ e. g. katara, tatara, yatara &c. [0532-a]
      tara, taraṇa, taraṇi, taraṃḍa, tari-rī, tarīṣa &c. See under tṝ. [0532-a]
      tara a. [tṝ-bhave-ap]
      1 Crossing.
      2 Surpassing, excelling.
      3 Conquering, overpowering; cf. dustara.
      --raḥ
      1 Passing over, crossing, passage; Bk. 7. 55.
      2 Freight; dīrghādhvani yathādeśaṃ yathākālaṃ taro bhavet Ms. 8. 406.
      3 A road.
      4 A ferry-boat.
      5 Fire.
    Comp.
      --paṇyaṃ freight.
      --paṇyikaḥ one who receives the freight. --sthānaṃ a landing-place, wharf. [0545-b]
