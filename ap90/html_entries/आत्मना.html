• ātmanā ind. (instr. of ātman) Used reflexively; atha cāratamitā tvamātmanā R. 8. 51 thou thyself; ātmanā śukanāsamādāya K. 293. It is oft. compounded with ordinal numerals; e. g. °dvitīyaḥ second including himself, i. e. himself and one more; so °tṛtīyaḥ himself with two others &c. [0235-b]
