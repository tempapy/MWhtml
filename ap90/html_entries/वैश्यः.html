vaiśyaḥ A man of the third tribe, his business being trade and agriculture; viśatyāśu paśubhyaśca kṛṣyādāvaruciḥ śuciḥ . vedādhyayanasaṃpannaḥ sa vaiśya iti saṃjñitaḥ Padma Purāṇa. (He is supposed to have sprung from the thighs of Puruṣa; cf. ūrū tadasya yadvaiśyaḥ Rv. 10. 90).
    Comp.
      --karman n. --vṛttiḥ f. the business or occupation of a Vaiśya; trade, agriculture &c. [1015-c]
