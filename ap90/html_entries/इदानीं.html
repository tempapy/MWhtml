idānīṃ ind. [idaṃ-dānīṃ iś ca]
    • 1 Now, at this moment, in this case, just now, even now; vatse pratiṣṭhasvedānīṃ Ś. 4; āryaputra idānīmasi U. 3; idānīmahnaḥ now a days; idānīmeva just now; idānīmapi now also, in this case also; tata idānīṃ thereupon, then, from that time.
    • 2 As a measure of time, it is equal to onefifteenth part of an etarhi; cf. yāvaṃtyetarhīṇi tāvaṃti paṃcadaśakṛtva idānīni Śat. Br. [0276-b]
