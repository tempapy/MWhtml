chāga a. (gī f.) Relating to a goat or she-goat; Y. 1. 258. --gaḥ (gī f.)
      1 A goat; brāhmaṇaśchāgato yathā (vaṃcitaḥ) H. 4. 53; Ms. 3. 269.
      2 The sign Aries of the zodiac.
      --gaṃ
      1 The milk of a she-goat.
      2 An oblation.
    Comp.
      --bhojin m. a wolf.
      --mukhaḥ an epithet of Kārtikeya.
      --rathaḥ, --vāhanaḥ an epithet of Agni, the god of fire. [0501-a]
