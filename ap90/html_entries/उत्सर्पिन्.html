• utsarpin a. 1 Moving or gliding upwards, rising; payodharotsarpiṣu ( śīkareṣu) R. 16. 62. --2 Soaring, towering; utsarpiṇī khalu mahatāṃ prārthanā Ś. 7. --3 Transcendental. --ṇī f. A division of time (with the Jainas), a long period said to be = ten crores of crores of oceans of year; cf. avasarpiṇī. [0300-b]
