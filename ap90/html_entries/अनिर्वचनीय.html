anirvacanīya a.
    • 1 Unutterable, indescribable, undefinable, epithet of the Supreme Being.
    • 2 Improper to be mentioned.
      (In Vedānta)
    • 1 Māyā or illusion, ignorance.
    • 2 The world. Comp. --sarvasvaṃ N. of a work by Śrīharṣa, also called khaṃḍanakhaṃḍakhādya; tatra sarveṣāṃ padārthānāṃ idaṃtayā nirvaktumaśakyatā darśitā. [0065-a]
