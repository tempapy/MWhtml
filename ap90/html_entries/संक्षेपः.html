• saṃkṣepaḥ
      1 Throwing together.
      2 Compression, abridgment.
      3 Brevity, conciseness.
      4 An epitome, a brief exposition.
      5 Throwing, sending.
      6 Taking away.
      7 Assisting in another's duty. (saṃkṣepeṇa, saṃkṣepatas are used adverbially in the sense of ‘briefly, concisely, shortly’). [1078-b]
