      --laḥ
      1 The country of the Pañcālas.
      2 A prince of the Pañcālas --lāḥ (m. pl.)
      1 The people of the Pañcālas.
      2 An association of five guilds (i. e. of a car penter, weaver, barber, washerman, and shoe-maker). [0690-b]
