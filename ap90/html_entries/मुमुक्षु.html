• mumukṣu a.
      1 Desirous of releasing or liberating.
      2 Wishing to discharge.
      3 About to shoot (arrows &c.); R. 9. 58.
      4 Wishing to be free from worldly existence, striving after final emancipation.
    • --kṣuḥ A sage striving after final emancipation or beatitude; aṃtaryaśca mumukṣubhirniyamitaprāṇādibhirmṛgyate V. 1. 1; Ku. 2. 51; Bg. 4. 15. [0871-b]
