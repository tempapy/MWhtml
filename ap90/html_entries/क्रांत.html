krāṃta p. p. Gone, passed over, traversed &c. (p. p. of kram q. v.).
      --taḥ
      1 A horse.
      2 A foot, step.
      3 Declination.
      --taṃ
      1 Going, passing.
      2 A step.
      3 A certain aspect in astronomy (when the moon is in conjunction with a planet).
    Comp.
      --darśin a. omniscient. [0430-b]
