• hayaḥ [hay-hi-vā ac]
      1 A horse; Bg. 1. 14; Ms. 8. 296, R. 9. 10.
      2 A man of a particular class; see under aśva.
      3 The number ‘seven’.
      4 N. of Indra.
      5 (In prosody) A foot of four short syllables.
