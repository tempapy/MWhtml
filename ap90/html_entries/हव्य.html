havya a. [hu-karmaṇi yat] To be offered in oblations.
      --vyaṃ
      1 Clarified butter.
      2 An oblation or offering to the gods (opp. kavya q. v.).
      3 An oblation in general.
    Comp.
      --āśaḥ fire.
      --kavyaṃ oblations to the gods and to the Manes, or spirits of deceased ancestors; Ms. 1. 94, 3. 97, 128; et seq. --pākaḥ an oblation cooked with butter and milk, or the pot in which it is cooked.
      --vāh, --vāha, --vāhana m. ‘the bearer of oblations’, fire. [1168-a]
