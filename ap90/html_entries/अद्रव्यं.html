• adravyaṃ [na. ta.] A worthless thing, an object which is good for nothing; nādravye vihitā kācitkriyā phalavatī bhavet H. Pr. 43; hence, a worthless or bad pupil or recipient of instruction; vineturadravyaparigraha eva buddhilāghavaṃ prakāśayati M. 1; adravyametya bhuvi śuddhanayopi maṃtrī Mu. 7. 14. [0043-a]
