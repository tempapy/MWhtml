• vyaṃgya a.
      1 Indicated by implication, indicated by covert or indirect allusion.
      2 Suggested (as sense).
    • --gyaṃ Suggested sense, insinuation, the meaning hinted at (opp. vācya ‘the primary or expressed meaning’, and lakṣya ‘the secondary or indicated meaning’); idamuttamamatiśayini vyaṃgye vācyād dhvanirbudhaiḥ kathitaḥ K. P. 1.
    Comp.
    • --uktiḥ f. covert expression, insinuation, inuendo. [1016-b]
