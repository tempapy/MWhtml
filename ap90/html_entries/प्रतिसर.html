• pratisara a. Dependent, subject. --raḥ --raṃ 1 A cord or ribbon worn round the wrist or neck as an amulet. --2 An ornament. --3 A watch, guard. --raḥ 1 A servant, follower. --2 A bracelet, marriage-string; srastoragapratisareṇa kareṇa pāṇiḥ (agṛhyata) Ki. 5. 33 (= kautukasūtra); Māl. 5. 18. --3 A garland, wreath. --4 Day-break. --5 The rear of an army. --6 A form of incantation. --7 Healing or dressing a wound. --rā 1 A female servant. --2 A thread, fillet.
