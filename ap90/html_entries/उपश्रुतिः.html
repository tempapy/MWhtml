upaśrutiḥ f.
      1 Hearing, listening.
      2 Range of hearing.
      3 A supernatural voice heard at night, and personified as a nocturnal deity revealing the future; naktaṃ nirgatya yatkiṃcicchubhāśubhakaraṃ vacaḥ . śrūyate tadvidurdhīrā devapraśnamupaśrutiṃ .. Hārāvalī; parijano'pi cāsyāḥ satatamupaśrutyai nirjagāma K. 65.
      4 Aspect of the stars, fortune-telling.
      5 Promise, assent. [0331-c]
