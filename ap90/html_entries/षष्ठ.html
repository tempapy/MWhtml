ṣaṣṭha a. (ṣṭhī f.) Sixth, the sixth; ṣaṣṭhaṃ tu kṣetrajasyāśaṃ pradadyātpaitṛkāddhatāt Ms. 9. 164, 7. 130; ṣaṣṭhe bhāge V. 2. 1; R. 17. 78.
    Comp.
      --aṃśaḥ {1} a sixth part in general. {2} particularly, the sixth part of the produce of fields &c., which the king takes from his subjects as land-tax; ūdhasyamicchāmi tavopabhoktuṃ ṣaṣṭhāṃśamurvyā iva rakṣitāyāḥ R. 2. 66; (the different kinds of produce, to the sixth part of which a king is entitled, are specified in Ms. 7. 131-132). °vṛttiḥ a king (entitled to the sixth part of the produce); ṣaṣṭhāṃśavṛtterapi dharma eṣaḥ Ś. 5. 4. --annaṃ the sixth meal. °kālaḥ taking food once in three days, as an expiatory act.
