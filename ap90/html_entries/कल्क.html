• kalka a. [kal-ka Uṇ. 3. 40] Sinful, wicked. --lkaḥ, --lkaṃ 1 The viscous sediment deposited by oily substances when ground. --2 A kind of tenacious paste; Y. 1. 277. --3 (Hence) Dirt, filth (in general). --4 Ordure, fæces. --5 Meanness, deceit, hypocrisy; Śi. 19. 98. --6 Sin. --7 Levigated powder; tāṃ lodhrakalkena hṛtāṃgatailāṃ Ku. 7. 9. --8 Incense. --9 The wax of the ear. --Comp. --phalaḥ the pomegranate plant.
