uccayaḥ
    • 1 A collection, heap, multitude; rūpoccayena Ś. 2. 9; padoccayaḥ S. D. 2; cf. śiloccaya also.
    • 2 Gathering, collecting (flowers &c.); puṣpoccayaṃ nāṭayati Ś. 4; Ku. 3. 61.
    • 3 The knot of a woman's (wearing) garment (nīvibaṃdha); Ki. 8. 15, 51.
    • 4 Nīvāra rice (collected by winnowing).
    • 5 Prosperity, rise; uccayāpacayau H. 3. 133.
    • 6 The opposite side of a triangle. [0287-a]
