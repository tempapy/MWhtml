• bhujiṣya [bhuj-kiṣyan] Independent.
    • --ṣyaḥ
      1 A slave, servant.
      2 A companion.
      3 The string worn round the wrist.
      4 A disease (roga).
    • --ṣyā
      1 A hand maid, maid-servant, female slave; athāṃgadāśliṣṭabhujaṃ bhujiṣyā R. 6. 53; Mk. 4. 8; Y. 2. 290.
      2 A harlot, prostitute. [0819-c]
