• mahiṣaḥ [mah-ṭiṣac Uṇ. 1. 45]
      1 A buffalo; (considered as the vehicle of Yama); gāhaṃtāṃ mahiṣā nipānasalilaṃ śṛṃgairmuhustāḍitaṃ Ś. 2. 6.
      2 N. of a demon killed by Durgā.
    Comp.
    • --akṣaḥ, --akṣakaḥ a kind of bdellium.
    • --ardamaḥ an epithet of Kārtikeya.
    • --asuraḥ the demon Mahiṣa. °ghātinī, °mathanī, °ardanī, °sūdanī epithets of Durgā.
    • --dhnī an epithet of Durgā.
    • --dhvajaḥ an epithet of Yama.
    • --pālaḥ, --pālakaḥ a buffalo-keeper.
