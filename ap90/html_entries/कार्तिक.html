kārtika a. (kī f.) [kṛttikā-aṇ] Belonging to the month of Kārtika; R. 19. 39. --kaḥ
      1 N. of the month in which the full moon is near the kṛttikā or Pleiades (corresponding to October-November).
      2 An epithet of Skanda.
      --kī The full moon day in the month of Kārtika. [0397-c]
