• vibhaj {c1c} U. 1 To divide, distribute; vibhajya merurna yadarthisātkṛtaḥ N. 1. 16; patriṇāṃ vyabhajadāśramādbahiḥ R. 11. 29; 10. 54; vapurvibhaktāvayavaṃ pumāniti Śi. 1. 3; saṃdhyāmaṃgaladīpikā vibhajate śuddhāṃtavṛddho janaḥ V. 3. 2. ‘distributes or places’. --2 To divide (as property, patrimony &c.); vibhaktā bhrātaraḥ ‘divided brothers’. --3 To distinguish, discriminate. --4 To honour, worship.
