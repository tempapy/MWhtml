• dūṣaka a. (ṣikā f.) [duṣ-ṇic-ṇvul] 1 Corrupting, polluting, vitiating, defiling, spoiling. --2 Violating, dishonouring, seducing. --3 Offending, trespassing, guilty. --4 Disfiguring. --5 Sinful, wicked (as an action). --kaḥ 1 A seducer, a corrupter. --2 Any infamous or wicked person. [0572-c]
