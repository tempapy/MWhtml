• hastin a. (nī f.) [hastaḥ śuṃḍādaṃḍo'styasya ini]
      1 Having hands.
      2 Having a trunk. --m. An elephant; Ms. 7. 96, 12. 43; (elephants are said to be of four kinds; bhadra, maṃdra, mṛga, and miśra).
