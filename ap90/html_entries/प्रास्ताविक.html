• prāstāvika a. (kī f.) 1 Serving as an introduction, introductory, prefatory; as in prāstāvikavilāsa (the first or introductory part of Bhāminīvilāsa); prāstāvikaṃ vacanaṃ ‘prefatory remarks.’ --2 Seasonable, opportune, timely. --3 Pertinent, relevant (to the matter in hand); aprāstāvikī mahatyeṣā kathā Māl. 2.
