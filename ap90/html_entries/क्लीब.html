klība(va) a.
      1 Impotent, neuter, emasculated; Ms. 3. 150, 4. 205; Y. 1. 223.
      2 Unmanly, timid, weak, weak-minded; R. 8. 84; klībān pālayitā Mk. 9. 5.
      3 Cowardly.
      4 Mean, base.
      5 Idle.
      6 Of the neuter gender.
      --baḥ, --baṃ (--vaḥ, --vaṃ)
      1 An impotent man, a eunuch; na mūtraṃ phenilaṃ yasya viṣṭhā cāpsu nimajjati . meḍhaṃ conmādaśukrābhyāṃ hīnaṃ klībaḥ sa ucyate .. Kātyāyana quoted in Dāyabhāga.
      2 The neuter gender. [0433-b]
