nigadaḥ, nigādaḥ
      1 Recitation, audible recitation of prayers.
      2 A prayer repeated aloud.
      3 Speech, discourse.
      4 Learning anything without knowing the meaning; yadadhītamavijñātaṃ nigadenaiva śabdyate Nir.
      5 Mention, mentioning; iti nigadenaiva vyākhyātam. [0619-a]
