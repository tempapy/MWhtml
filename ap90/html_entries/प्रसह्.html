• prasah {c1c} A. 1 To bear, endure; na tejastejasvī prasṛtamapareṣāṃ prasahate U. 6. 14. --2 To withstand, resist, overpower; saṃyuge sāyugīnaṃ tamudyataṃ prasaheta kaḥ Ku. 2. 57. --3 To exert oneself, attempt. --4 To dare, venture, be able. --5 To have power or energy; see prasahya. [0764-c]
  • prasa(sā)h m. Ved. 1 Force, violence. --2 An epithet of Indra. [0764-c]
