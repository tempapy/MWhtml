• recaka a. (cikā f.) [recayati ricṇic ṇvul] 1 Emptying, purging. --2 Purgative, aperient. --3 Emptying the lungs, emitting the breath. --kaḥ 1 Emission of breath, breathing out, exhalation, especially through one of the nostrils (opp. pūraka which means ‘inhaling breath’, and kuṃbhaka ‘suspending breath’). --2 A syringe. --3 Nitre, salt-petre. --kaṃ A purgative, cathartic.
