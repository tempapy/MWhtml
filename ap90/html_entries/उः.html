• uḥ 1 N. of Śiva, the second of the three syllables in om; see a. --2 N. of Brahmā. --3 The orb of the moon. --ind. 1 As a particle used expletively; u umeśaḥ Sk. --2 An interjection of:-- (a) calling; u meti mātrā tapaso niṣiddhā paścādumākhyāṃ sumukhī jagāma Ku. 1. 26; (b) anger; (c) compassion; (d) command; (e) acceptance; (f) interrogation; or (g) used merely as an expletive. In the Veda used as an enclitic copula implying restriction and emphasis (and, but, on the other hand, now, I ask &c.); in classical literature used chiefly with atha (atho), na (no) and kim (kimu); see these words. u-u or uta on the one hand-on the other hand, partly-partly.
