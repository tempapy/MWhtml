anadyatana a. (nī f.) [na. ta.] Not pertaining to this or the current day; a term used by Pāṇini to denote the sense of the Imperfect or the Periphrastic future P. III. 2. 111, III. 3. 15; °bhūte laṅ-apacat; °ne bhaviṣyati luṭ-paktā; parokṣānadyatane liṭ papāca.
      Not the current day; atītāyāḥ rātreḥ paścārdhena āgāminyā rātreḥ pūrvārdhena sahito divaso'dyatanaḥ Sk., tadbhinnaḥ kālaḥ. [0055-c]
