pṛtanā
      1 An army (in general).
      2 A division of an army consisting of 243 elephants, as many chariots, 729 horse, and 1215 foot.
      3 Battle, fight, encounter.
      4 A hostile army.
      5 (pl.) Men, mankind (Ved.).
    Comp.
      --sāhaḥ an epithet of Indra. [0718-b]
