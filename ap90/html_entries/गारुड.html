• gāruḍa a. (ḍī f.) [garuḍasyedaṃ aṇ] 1 Shaped like Garuḍa. --2 Coming from or relating to Garuḍa. --ḍaḥ, --ḍaṃ 1 An emerald; R. 13. 53. --2 A charm against (snake) poison; saṃgṛhītagāruḍena K. 51 (where it has sense 1 also). --3 A missile presided over by Garuḍa. --4 A military array (vyūha) of the shape of Garuḍa. --5 Gold. [0457-c]
