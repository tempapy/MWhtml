ripuḥ [rap-un pṛṣo° Uṇ. 1. 26]
      1 An enemy, a foe, an opponent.
      2 A hostile planet.
      3 N. of the sixth astrological house.
      4 Ved. A cheat, rogue.
    Comp.
      --ghātin, ghna, --jaya, --nipātin, --sūdana a. killing or vanquishing foes.
      --bhavanaṃ, --sthānaṃ N. of the 6th astrological house. [0913-a]
