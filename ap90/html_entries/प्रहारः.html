• prahāraḥ 1 Striking, beating, hitting; Y. 3. 248. --2 Wounding, killing. --3 A stroke, blow, hit, knock, thump; R. 7. 44; muṣṭiprahāra, talaprahāra &c. --4 A cut or thrust, as in khaḍgaprahāra. --5 A kick; as in pādaprahāra; lattāprahāra. --6 Shooting. --Comp. --ārta a. wounded by a blow. (--rtaṃ) acute pain caused by a wound. [0768-c]
