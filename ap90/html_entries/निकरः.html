• nikaraḥ [nikṝ bhāvādau ap]
      1 A heap, pile.
      2 A flock, multitude, collection; papāta svedāṃbuprasara iva harṣāśrunikaraḥ Gīt. 11; Śi. 4. 58; Rs. 6. 18.
      3 A bundle.
      4 Sap, pith, essence.
      5 A suitable gift, honorarium.
      6 A treasure. [0617-c]
