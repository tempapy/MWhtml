• abhikram {c1c} U., {c4c} P. 1 To step or go near to, approach; tamabhikramya sarve'dya vayaṃ cārthāmahe vasu Mb. --2 To roam over, wander, pass or walk over. --3 To attack, assail, fall upon. --4 To undertake, begin; to set about, prepare; gamanāyābhicakrāma Rām. --Caus. To bring near. [0127-b]
