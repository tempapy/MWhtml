• piṃjara a. [piṃj-arac] Reddishyellow, tawny, gold-coloured; śikhā pradīpasya suvarṇapiṃjarā Mk. 3. 17; R. 18. 40. --raḥ 1 The reddish-yellow or tawny-brown colour. --2 The yellow colour. --raṃ 1 Gold. --2 Yellow orpiment. --3 A skeleton. --4 A cage (for paṃjara). --5 The ribs or the cavity formed by them, the thorax.
