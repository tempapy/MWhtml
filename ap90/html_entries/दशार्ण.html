• daśārṇa N. of a country, through which flows the Daśārṇā (Dasan). It was the eastern part of Mālava or Mālvā, its capital being Vidiśā--the modern Bhilsā--situated on the Vetravatī or Betva, cf. Me. 24, 25 and Kādambarī. Kālidāsa also makes Vidiśā a river which is probably the same as the Bees that joins the Betva. [1194-b]
