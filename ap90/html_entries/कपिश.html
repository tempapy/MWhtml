kapiśa a. [kapi-atvarthe śa]
    • 1 Brown, reddish-brown.
    • 2 Reddish; (chāyāḥ) saṃdhyāpayodakapiśāḥ piśitāśanānāṃ Ś. 3. 27; toye kāṃcanapadmareṇukapiśe 7. 12; V. 2. 7; Me. 21; R. 12. 28. --śaḥ
    • 1 The brown colour.
    • 2 A compound of red and black colour.
    • 3 Storax or coarse benzoin.
    • 1 The Mādhavī creeper.
    • 2 N. of a river.
      A spirit, a kind of rum.
      Comp. N. of Śiva.
      {1} spirit, a kind of rum. {2} a deity. [0377-c]
