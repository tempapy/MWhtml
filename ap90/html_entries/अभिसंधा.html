• abhisaṃdhā {c3c} U. 1 To hold together --2 To acknowledge, recognize; own. --3 To fit or fix a missile, arrow &c. to the bow. --4 To throw or shoot at, aim at; (śūlaṃ) cikṣepa paramakruddho lakṣmaṇāyābhisaṃhitaṃ Rām. --5 To aim at, have in view, think of, (with acc.); padāviddhaṃ yāṃtī skhalitamabhisaṃdhāya bahuśaḥ V. 4. 28 calling to mind, thinking of my fault; ṛṣyamūkamabhisaṃdhāya Mv. 5 towards, in the direction of; Mv. 6; Bg. 17. 12, 25; sometimes with dat.; abhisaṃdadhate ye ca viśvāsāyāsya mānavāḥ Mb. --6 To deceive, cheat; janaṃ vidvānekaḥ sakalamabhisaṃdhāya Māl. 1. 14. --7 To come to an understanding or agreement; phalaṃ tvanabhisaṃdhāya Ms. 9. 52 (evaṃ yatra niyamo na kṛtaḥ). --8 To win over, make friendship with, ally oneself with; tān sarvānabhisaṃdadhyātsāmādibhirupakramaiḥ Ms. 7. 159 (vaśīkuryāt). --9 To declare solemnly, agree, promise. --10 To calumniate, traduce. --11 To add. --12 To prefer.
