• anupūrva a. [anugataḥ pūrvaṃ paripāṭīṃ kramaṃ vā] 1 Regular, having a suitable measure, regularly shaped, symmetrical; vṛttānupūrve ca na cātidīrghe Ku. 1. 35; tatonupūrvāyatavṛttabāhuḥ Ki. 17. 50 (gopucchākāra Malli.); jaṃghe anupūrvavṛtte Dk. 131; °tāmrāṃgulī bāhulate ibid.; °keśa who has regular hair; °gātra having regularly shaped limbs; so °daṃṣṭra, °nābhi, pāṇi. --2 Orderly, successive, in due order or succession; coming in the order, following. --3 The lowest ( nikṛṣṭapramāṇa); anupūrvā itare Kāty. --Comp. --ja a. descended in a regular line. --vatsā a cow that calves regularly.
