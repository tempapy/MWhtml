• lekhya a. [likh ṇyat] To be drawn, written, painted, scratched &c.
    • --khyaṃ
      1 The art of writing.
      2 Writing, transcribing.
      3 A writing, a letter, document, manuscript.
      4 An inscription.
      5 Painting, drawing.
      6 A painted figure.
    Comp.
    • --ārūḍha, kṛta a. committed to writing, done in writing.
    • --gata a. painted, drawn in picture. --cūrṇikā a paint-brush, writing-pencil.
    • --patraṃ, --patrakaṃ {1} a writing, letter, document. {2} a palm-leaf.
    • --prasaṃgaḥ a document.
    • --sthānaṃ a writing place. [0932-b]
