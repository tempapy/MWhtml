    • āghātaḥ [ā-han-ghañ]
      1 Striking, striking against; abhyasyaṃti taṭāghātaṃ Ku. 2. 50; U. 5. 9.
      2 A blow, stroke; tīvrāghātapratihatataruskaṃdhalagnaikadaṃtaḥ Ś. 1. 33; kaṭhinakucataṭa° Amaru. 55; pavana°; pāda° &c.
      3 A wound.
      4 Killing: prāṇāghātānnivṛttiḥ Bh. 2. 26; Y. 3. 275.
      5 One who beats or strikes.
      6 A misfortune, distress.
      7 Retention of urine (mūtrāghāta).
      8 A slaughter-house; āghātaṃ nīyamānasya vadhyasyeva pade pade H. 4. 67. [0271-c]
