• nartakaḥ [nṛt-kartari ṣvun] 1 A dancer; sometimes a dancing preceptor. --2 An actor, mime, mummer. --3 A bard, herald. --4 An elephant. --5 A king. --6 A peacock. --7 An epithet of Śiva. --8 N. of a mixed tribe; ( veśyāyāṃ rajakājjāto nartako gāyako bhavet). --kī 1 A female dancer, a singing girl, an actress; raṃgasya darśayitvā nivartate nartakī yathā nṛtyāt Sān. K. 59; Ki. 10. 41; R. 19. 14, 19. --2 A female elephant. --3 A peahen. [0609-c]
