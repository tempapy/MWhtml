• samaṣṭiḥ f.
      1 Collective pervasion or aggregate, an aggregate which is considered as made up of parts each of which is consubstantially the same with the whole (opp. vyaṣṭi q. v.); samaṣṭirīśaḥ sarveṣāṃ svātmatādātmyavedanāt . tadabhāvāttadanye tu jñāyaṃte vyaṣṭisaṃjñayā .. Pañcadaśī.
      2 Totality. [1095-b]
