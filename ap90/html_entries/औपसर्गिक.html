aupasargika a. (kī f.) [ upasargaṭhañ]
    • 1 Able to cope with adversity.
    • 2 Portentous.
    • 3 Relating to change &c.
    • 4 Superinduced (as a disease).
    • 5 Connected with a preposition.
      Irregular action of the humours of the body, producing cold sweats &c. (vātādisannipāta). [0363-c]
