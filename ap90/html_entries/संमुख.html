saṃmukha a. (khā or khī f.), saṃmukhīna a.
      1 Facing, fronting, face to face, opposite, con fronting; kāmaṃ na tiṣṭhati madānanasaṃmukhī sā Ś. 1. 31; R. 15. 17; Śi. 10. 86.
      2 Encountering, meeting.
      3 Disposed to.
      4 Looking or directed towards.
      5 Propitious.
      --khaṃ ind. In front of, opposite to, before, in the presence of. [1106-c]
