• paripaṃthin a. Standing in the way, obstructing, opposing, hindering (said by Pāṇini to be admissible only in the Veda, but see the quotations given below); arthaparipaṃthī mahānarātiḥ Mu. 5; nābhaviṣyamahaṃ tatra yadi tatparipaṃthinī Māl. 9. 50; so Bv. 1. 62, Bg. 3. 34; Ms. 7. 107, 1. 10. --m.
      1 An enemy, antagonist, opponent, a foe.
      2 A robber, thief, highwayman. [0676-c]
