• ātyaṃtika a. (kī f.) [atyaṃta bhavārthe ṭhañ] 1 Continual, uninterrupted, endless, infinite, permanent, everlasting; sa ātyaṃtiko bhaviṣyati Mu. 4; viṣṇuguptahatakasyātyaṃtikaśreyase 2. 15; Ms. 2. 242; Bg. 6. 21. --2 Excessive, abundant, superlative. --3 Supreme, absolute; ātyaṃtikī svatvamivṛttiḥ Mit. --Comp. --duḥkhanivṛttiḥ f. absolution. --pralayaḥ the grand or universal destruction of the world.
