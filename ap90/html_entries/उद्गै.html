• udgai {c1c} P. 1 To sing in a loud tone, sing aloud; udgāsyatāmicchati kinnarāṇāṃ Ku. 1. 8; geyamudgātukāmā Me. 86; to sing (in general); udgīyamānaṃ vanadevatābhiḥ R. 2. 12; nibhṛtākṣaramujjage Śi. 6. 20. --2 To begin to sing. --3 To sing or chant (applied to the singing of Sāmaveda); sāma sāmavidasaṃgamujjagau Śi. 14. 21; cf. udgātṛ. --4 To announce, to celebrate in song. --5 To sing before one (with acc.). --6 To fill or make resonant with song.
