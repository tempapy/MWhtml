• tiraska(skā)riṇī
      1 A curtain, veil; tiraskariṇyo jaladā bhavaṃti Ku. 1. 14; M. 2. 1
      2 An outer tent, screen of cloth.
      3 A kind of magical veil (or spell) rendering the wearer invisible; Ś. 6. and V. 2, inter alia. It is properly the science or art possessed by celestial beings of rendering themselves invisible by repeating some Mantras. [0538-b]
