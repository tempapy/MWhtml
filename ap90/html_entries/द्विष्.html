• dviṣ 2 U. (dveṣṭi, dviṣṭe; dviṣṭa) To hate, dislike, be hostile towards; na dvekṣi yajjanamatastvamajātaśatruḥ Ve. 3. 13; Bg. 2. 57, 18. 10; Bk. 17. 61; 18. 9; ramyaṃ dveṣṭi Ś. 6. 4. (Prepositions like pra, vi and saṃ are prefixed to this root without any change of meaning.) [0589-a]
