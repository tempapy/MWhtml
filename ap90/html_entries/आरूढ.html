• ārūḍha p. p.
      1 Mounted, ascended; seated on; ārūḍho vṛkṣo bhavatā Sk.; oft. used actively; ārūḍhamadrīn R. 6. 77; Me. 8, 18; Ś. 4; so vṛkṣaṃ, nāvaṃ, hayaṃ, rathaṃ &c. cakra°, dolā°.
      2 Raised up, elevated on high.
      3 Arisen, produced.
    • --ḍhaṃ Ascending, mounting; see atyārūḍha. [0255-a]
