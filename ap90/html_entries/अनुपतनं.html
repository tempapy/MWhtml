• anupatanaṃ, pātaḥ
      1 Falling upon, alighting upon in succession.
      2 Following, going after, pursuit; upavanapavanānupātadakṣaiḥ Śi. 7. 27.
      3 Going or proceeding in order or as a consequence.
      4 [anurūpaḥ trairāśikena pātaḥ] Proportion.
      5 Rule of three.
      6 A degree of latitude, opposite to one given.
    • --taṃ ind. (regarded as a ṇamul from pat) Following in succession, going after; latānupātaṃ kusumānyagṛhṇāt Bk. 2. 11 (latāṃ latāmanupātya going to creeper after creeper, or after bending the creepers). [0073-a]
