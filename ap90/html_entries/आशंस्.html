• āśaṃs 1 A. (rarely P.)
      1 To hope for, expect, desire, wish or long for; svakāryasiddhiṃ punarāśaśaṃse Ku. 3. 57; Bk. 14. 70, 90; Ś. 2. 15; M. 1; manorathāya nāśaṃse Ś. 7. 13.
      2 To bless, wish well to; evaṃ te devā āśaṃsaṃtu Mk. 1; ityāśaśaṃse karaṇairabāhyaiḥ R. 14. 50.
      3 To speak, say.
      4 To tell; Ku. 3. 14.
      5 To ask for, beg.
      6 To praise.
      7 To repeat, recite.
      8 To fear, be afraid of. --Caus. To render famous or celebrated. [0263-a]
