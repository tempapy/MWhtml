• avimukta a. Unloosed, not quitted or let go. --ktaṃ 1 N. of a Tīrtha or sacred place near Benares, or Benares itself; na vimuktaṃ śivābhyāṃ yadavimuktaṃ tato viduḥ. --2 The space between the chin and the head. --Comp. --īśvaraḥ a celebrated Śiva-linga at Benares. [0195-c]
