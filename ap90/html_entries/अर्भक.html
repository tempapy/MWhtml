• arbhaka a. [According to Nir. avahṛtaṃ bhavati hrasvaṃ tasmādarbhakaḥ.] 1 Small, minute, short; namo mahadbhyo namoarbhakebhyaḥ Rv. 1. 27. 13. --2 Weak, emaciated, lean. --3 Foolish. --4 Young, childish. --5 Like, similar. --kaḥ 1 A boy, child; śrutasya yāyādayamaṃtamarbhakaḥ R. 3. 21, 25, 7. 67. --2 The young of an animal. --3 A fool, idiot.
