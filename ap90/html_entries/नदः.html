• nadaḥ [nadati śabdāyate-ac] 1 A river. great river (such as the Indus); Śi. 4. 66 (where Malli. remarks: --prāksretaso nadyaḥ pratyaksrotaso nadānarmadāṃ vinetyahuḥ). --2 A stream, flowing stream, rivulet; Ki. 5. 27. --3 The ocean. --4 Ved. A horse. --5 A cloud. --6 A praiser (ṛṣi). --Comp. --patiḥ, --rājaḥ the ocean. [0606-b]
