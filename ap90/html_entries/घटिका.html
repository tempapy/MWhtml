• ghaṭikā 1 A small water-jar, bucket, a small earthen vessel: nāryaḥ śpaśānaghaṭikā iva varjanīyāḥ Pt. 1. 192; eṣa krīḍati kūpayaṃtraghaṭikānyāyaprasakto vidhiḥ Mk. 10. 59. --2 A measure of time equal to 24 minutes. --3 A water-pot used in calculating the Ghaṭikās of the day. --4 The ankle.
