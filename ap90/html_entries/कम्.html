kam ind. Ved. A particle used as an expletive or enclitic. [0378-b]
      kam 1 10 A. (kāmayate, kāmita, cakabhe-kāmayāṃcakre, kāṃta)
      1 To love, be enamoured of, be in love with; kanye kāmayamānaṃ māṃ na tvaṃ kāmayase kathaṃ Kāv. 1. 63 (an instance of grāmyatā); kalahaṃsako maṃdārikāṃ kāmayate Māl. 1.
      2 To long for, wish, desire; na vīrasūśabdamakāmayetāṃ R. 14. 4; niṣkraṣṭumarthaṃ cakame kuberāt 5. 26; 4. 48; 10. 53; Bk. 14. 82.
      3 To have intercourse with.
      4 To value highly. [0378-b]
