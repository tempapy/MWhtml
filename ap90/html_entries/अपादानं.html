• apādānaṃ
      1 Taking away, removal; ablation; a thing from which another is removed.
      2 (In gram.) The sense of the ablative case; dhruvamapāye'pādānaṃ P. I. 4. 24; apādāne paṃcamī II. 3. 28; apāye yadudāsīnaṃ calaṃ vā yadi vā'calaṃ . dhruvameva tadāveśāttadapādānamucyate .. Hariv.; apādāna is of three kinds: -nirdiṣṭaviṣayaṃ kiṃcidupāttaviṣayaṃ tathā . apekṣitakriyaṃ ceti tridhāpādānamiṣyate .. e. g. vṛkṣāt patraṃ patati, meghādvidyotate vidyut, & kuto bhavān. [0116-a]
