• sakhi m. [saha samānaṃ khyāyate ni° Uṇ. 4. 136] (nom. sakhā, sakhāyau, sakhayaḥ; acc. sakhāyaṃ, sakhāyau; sakhyuḥ gen. sing.; loc. sing.) A friend, companion, an associate; tasmātsakhā tvama si yanmama tattavaiva U. 5. 10; sakhīniva prītiyujo'nujīvinaḥ Ki. 1. 10. (At the end of comp. sakhi is changed to sakha; vanitāsakhānāṃ Ku. 1. 10; sacivasakhaḥ R. 4. 87; 1. 48, 12. 9; Bk. 1. 1). [1076-a]
