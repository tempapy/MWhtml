• sakāma a.
      1 Full of love, impassioned, loving.
      2 Lustful, amorous.
      3 One who has got his desired object, satisfied, contented; kāma idānīṃ sakāmo bhavat Ś. 4; kimanyat sakāmā kapālakuṃḍalā Māl. 9. --maṃ ind.
      1 With pleasure.
      2 Contentedly.
      3 Assuredly, indeed. [1075-b]
