• prasūna p. p. Produced, born.
    • --naṃ.
      1 A flower; latāyāṃ pūrvalūtāyāṃ prasūnasyāgamaḥ kutaḥ U. 5. 20, R. 2. 10.
      2 A bud, blossom.
      3 A fruit.
    Comp.
    • --iṣuḥ, --bāṇaḥ, --vāṇaḥ epithets of the god of love.
    • --varṣaḥ a shower of flowers. [0765-c]
