• bālā
      1 A girl, a female child.
      2 A young woman under sixteenyears of age.
      3 A young woman (in general); jāne tapaso vīryaṃ sā bālā paravatīti me viditaṃ Ś. 3. 1; iyaṃ bālā māṃ pratyanavaratamiṃdīvaradalaprabhācoraṃ cakṣuḥ kṣipati Bh. 3. 67; Me. 83.
      4 A variety of jasmine.
      5 The cocoa-nut.
      6 The plant ghṛtakumārī.
      7 Small cardamoms.
      8 Turmeric.
    Comp.
    • --hatyā female infanticide. [0795-b]
