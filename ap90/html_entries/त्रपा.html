trapā [trap bhāve aṅ]
      1 Bashfulness modesty; maṃdatrapābhara Gīt. 12.
      2 Shame (in a good or bad sense).
      3 A libidinous or unchaste woman.
      4 Family, race.
      5 Fame, celebrity
    Comp.
      --nirasta, hīna a. shameless, impudent.
      --raṃḍā a harlot. [0548-a]
