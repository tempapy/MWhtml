avyākṛta a.
    • 1 Not developed, not manifest; taddhedaṃ tarhyavyākṛtamāsīt, idaṃ nāmarūpābhyāmavyākṛtaṃ. Ś. B.
    • 2 Not decomposed, elementary.
      (In Vedānta phil.)
    • 1 An elementary substance from which all things were created (considered identical with Brahma.)
    • 2 (In Sān. phil.) The prime germ of nature (pradhāna). [0199-b]
