kūrcaḥ, --rcaṃ
      1 A bunch of any thing, a bundle.
      2 A handful of Kuśa grass.
      3 A peacock's feather
      4 The beard; āgatamanadhyāyakāraṇaṃ siveśaṣabhūtamadya jīrṇakūrcānāṃ U. 4; or pūrayitavyamanena citraphalakaṃ laṃbakūrcānāṃ tāpasānāṃ kadaṃbaiḥ Ś. 6.
      5 The tip of the thumb and the middle finger brought in contact so as to pinch &c.
      6 The upper part of the rose, the part (or hair) between the eye-brows.
      7 A brush.
      8 Deceit, fraud.
      9 Boasting, bragging.
      10 Hypocrisy.
      --rcaḥ
      1 The head.
      2 A store-room.
    Comp.
      --śiras n. the upper part of the palm of the hand and foot.
      --śīrṣaḥ, --śekharaḥ the cocoa-nut tree. [0416-c]
