• lāṃchanaṃ [lāṃch-karmaṇi lyuṭ]
      1 A sign, mark, token, characteristic mark; navāṃbudānīkamuhūrtalāṃchane (dhanuṣi) R. 3. 53; U. 4. 20; Mv. 1. 18; oft. at the end of comp. in the sense of ‘marked with’, ‘characterized by’ &c. jāte'tha devasya tayā vivāhamahotsave sāhasalāṃchanasya Vikr. 10. 1; R. 6. 18, 16. 84; so śrīkaṃdapadalāṃchanaḥ Māl. 1 ‘bearing the characteristic epithet śrīkaṃṭha.’
      2 A name, an appellation.
      3 A stain, stigma, a mark of ignominy.
      4 The spot on the moon; Ku. 7. 35.
      5 A landmark. [0927-b]
