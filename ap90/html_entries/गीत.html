gīta p. p. [gai-kta]
      1 Sung, chanted (lit.); ārye sādhu gītaṃ Ś. 1; cāraṇadvaṃdvagītaḥ śabdaḥ Ś. 2. 14.
      2 Declared, told, said; gītaścāyamarthoṃ'girasā Māl. 2; (see under gai also).
      --taṃ Singing, a song; tavāsmi gītarāgeṇa hāriṇā prasabhaṃ hṛtaḥ Ś. 1. 5; gītamutsādakāri mṛgāṇāṃ K. 32.
    Comp.
      --ayanaṃ a means or instrument of singing, i. e. a lute, flute &c.
      --kramaḥ the arrangement of a song.
      --jña a. versed in the art of singing.
      --priya a. fond of song or music. (--yaḥ) an epithet of Śiva. (--yā) N. of one of the Mātṛs attending on Skanda.
      --modin m. a Kinnara.
      --śāstraṃ the science of music. [0459-b]
