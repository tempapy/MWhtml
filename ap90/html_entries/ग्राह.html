      2 A crocodile, shark; rāgagra havatī Bh. 3. 45.
      3 A prisoner.
      4 Accepting.
      5 Understanding, knowledge.
      6 Persistence, importunity.
      7 Determination, resolve; Bg. 17. 19.
      8 A disease.
      9 Any large fish or marine animal.
      10 Morbid affection, disease.
      11 Beginning, undertaking.
      --hī A female crocodile. [0473-a]
