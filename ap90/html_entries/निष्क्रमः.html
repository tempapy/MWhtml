• niṣkramaḥ
      1 Going out, coming forth.
      2 Departure from, exit.
      3 One of the Saṃskāras or religious rites; i. e. taking out a child for the first time into the open air (which is usually performed in the fourth month of its age); caturthe māsi niṣkramaḥ Y. 1. 12; cf. upaniṣkramaṇa also.
      4 Degradation, loss of caste, inferiority of tribe.
      5 Intellectual faculty. [0641-a]
