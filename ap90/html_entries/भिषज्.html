bhiṣaj m. [bibhetyasmāt rogaḥ, bhī-ṣuk hrasvaśca Uṇ. 1. 134]
      1 A physician, doctor bhiṣajāmasādhyaṃ R. 8. 93.
      2 N. of Viṣṇu.
      3 Medicine, a remedy. --m. dual. The two Aśvins (physicians of gods).
    Comp.
      --jitaṃ a drug or medicine.
      --pāśaḥ a quack doctor.
      --varaḥ an excellent physician. (--rau) the two Aśvins. [0817-c]
