• vidhṛ {c10c} U. 1 To seize, catch, catch or take hold of; aṃśukapallavena vidhṛtaḥ Amaru. 79, 85. --2 To put on, wear, use; R. 12. 40. --3 To maintain, bear, support, hold up; śirasā vidhṛtā nityaṃ Pt. 1. 82 (where sense 2 is also intended); Bh. 3. 23. --4 To fix upon, direct towards. --5 To separate, divide. --6 To arrange; manage. --7 To with hold, obstruct, restrain.
