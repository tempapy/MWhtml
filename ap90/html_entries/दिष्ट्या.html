• diṣṭyā ind. (Strictly the instr. sing. of diṣṭi) Fortunately, luckily, thank God, how glad I am, how fortunate, bravo; (an exclamation of joy or gratulation); diṣṭyā pratihataṃ durjātaṃ Māl. 4; diṣṭyā soyaṃ mahābāhuraṃjanānaṃdavardhanaḥ U. 1. 32; Ve. 2. 12. (diṣṭyā vṛdh means ‘to be congratulated upon’; as in diṣṭyā dharmapatnīsamāgamena putramukhadarśanena cāyuṣmānvardhate Ś. 7). [0567-a]
