piṃḍiḥ --ḍī f.
      1 A round mass, ball.
      2 The nave of a wheel.
      3 The calf of the leg.
      4 The Aśoka tree.
      5 The long gourd (alābu).
      6 A house.
      7 A species of palm.
      8 A stool or seat.
      9 A pedestal for the image of a deity.
    Comp.
      --puṣpaḥ the Aśoka tree.
      --lepaḥ a kind of unguent.
      --śūraḥ ‘brave in the house,’ or ‘a cake-hero’, a braggart, cowardly boaster, poltroon, cotquean; cf. gehenardin, gehesūra &c. [0701-c]
