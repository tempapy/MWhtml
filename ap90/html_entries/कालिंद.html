kāliṃda a. (dī f.) [kaliṃda-aṇ] Connected with or coming from the mountain Kalinda or the river Yamunā.
      --dī
      1 The river Yamunā; kāliṃdyāḥ pulineṣu kelikupitām Ve. 1. 2; R. 15. 28; Śānti. 4. 13.
      2 A sort of vessel.
      3 N. of a wife of Kṛṣṇa.
      --daṃ A water melon.
    Comp.
      --karṣaṇaḥ, --bhedanaḥ an epithet of Balarāma q. v. --sūḥ f. Saṃjñā (saṃjñā), a wife of the sun. (--m.) the sun.
      --sodaraḥ Yama, the god of death. [0400-c]
