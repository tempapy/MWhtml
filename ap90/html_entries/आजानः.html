ājānaḥ [ā-jan-ghañ]
      1 Birth, origin.
      2 Generating cause (said to be n. also in these two senses).
      --naṃ
      1 Birth-place.
      2 Birth, descent, species (Ved.). --ind. From the time of the creation.
    Comp.
      --ja a. born since the creation (as gods &c).
      --devaḥ a god from the very creation. [0230-b]
