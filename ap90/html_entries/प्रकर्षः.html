• prakarṣaḥ 1 Excellence, eminence, superiority; vapuḥprakarṣādajayadguruṃ raghuḥ R. 3. 34; varṇaprakarṣe sati Ku. 3. 28. --2 Intensity, high degree, excess; prakarṣagatena śokasaṃtānena U. 3. --3 Strength, power. --4 Absoluteness. --5 Length, protraction. --6 Speciality. --7 Universality. (prakarṣeṇa and prakarṣāt are used adverbially in the sense of ‘exceedingly’, ‘pre-eminently’, ‘in a high degree’). [0725-c]
