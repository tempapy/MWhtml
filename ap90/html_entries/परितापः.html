• paritāpaḥ
      1 Extreme or scorching heat; (pādapaḥ) śamayati paritāpaṃ chāyayā saṃśritānāṃ Ś. 5. 7; guruparitāpāni gātrāṇi 3. 18; Rs. 1. 22.
      2 Pain, agony, anguish, grief; prasakte nirvāṇe hṛdaya paritāpaṃ vahasi kiṃ M. 3. 1.
      3 Lamentation, wailing; viracitavividhavilāpaṃ sā paritāpaṃ cakāroccaiḥ Gīt. 7.
      4 Trembling, fear.
      5 Hell. [0675-b]
