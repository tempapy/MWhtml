vāsas [vas-āchādane asi ṇicca]
      1 A cloth, garment, clothes; vāsāṃsi jīrṇāniyathā vihāya navāni gṛhṇāti naro'parāṇi Bg. 2. 22; Ku. 7. 9; Me. 59.
      2 A pall.
      3 A curtain.
    Comp.
      --kuṭī (vāsaḥkuṭī) a tent.
      --khaṃḍaḥ ‘a piece of cloth’, a rag, tatter. [0963-c]
