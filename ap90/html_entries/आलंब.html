• ālaṃba a. Hanging down. --baḥ 1 Depending on or from. --2 That on which one rests or leans, prop, stay; iha hi patatāṃ nāstyālaṃbo na cāpi nivartanaṃ Śānti. 3. 2. --3 Support, protection; tavālaṃbādaṃva sphuradalaghugarveṇa sahasā Jag.; sītāṃ śūnye yathāsukhaṃ nirālaṃbāṃ hariṣyāmi Rām. --4 Receptacle. --5 A perpendicular. --bā N. of a plant with poisonous leaves.
