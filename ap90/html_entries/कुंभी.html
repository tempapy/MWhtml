• kuṃbhī
      1 A small water-jar.
      2 An earthen cooking vessel.
      3 A measure of grain.
      4 N. of several plants.
    Comp.
    • --dhānyaṃ grain stored in jars sufficient for six days. --dhānyakaḥ a house-holder who stores grain.
    • --nasaḥ a kind of venomous serpent; U. 2. 29. --pākaḥ {1} the contents of a cooking-vessel. {2} (sing. or pl.) a particular hell in which the wicked are baked like potter's vessels; Y. 3. 224; Ms. 12. 76. [0411-b]
