• saṃgaḥ [saṃj bhāve ghañ] 1 Coming together, joining. --2 Meeting, union, confluence (as of rivers). --3 Touch, contact. --4 Company, association, friendship, friendly intercourse; satāṃ sadbhiḥ saṃgaḥ kathamapi hi puṇyena bhavati U. 2. 1; saṃgamanuvraj ‘to keep company with, herd with’; mṛgāḥ mṛgaiḥ saṃgamanuvrajaṃti Subhāṣ. --5 Attachment, fondness, desire; dhyāyato viṣayānpuṃsaḥ saṃgasteṣūpajāyate Bg. 2. 62. --6 Attachment to worldly ties, association with men; daurmaṃtryānnṛpatirvinaśyati yatiḥ saṃgāt Bh. 2. 42; vimukta° Ku. 1. 53; Pt. 1. 169. --7 Encounter, fight. --8 Hindrance, obstruction; R. 2. 42; 3. 63.
