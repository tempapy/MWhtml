• abhinaṃda a. That which delights, encourages, praises &c. --daḥ 1 Rejoicing, delighting, joy, delight. --2 Praising, applauding, approving, greeting, congratulating. --3 Wish, desire. --4 Encouraging, inciting to action. --5 Very little happiness (sukhalava). --6 An epithet of paramātman the Supreme Being. --dā Delight; wish, desire.
