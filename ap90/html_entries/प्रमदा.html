• pramadā
      1 A young handsome woman; R. 9. 31; Ś. 5. 17.
      2 A wife or woman in general; Ku. 4. 12; R. 8. 72.
      3 The sign virgo of the zodiac.
    Comp.
    • --kānanaṃ, --vanaṃ a pleasure garden attached to the royal harem (for the use of the wives of a king).
    • --janaḥ {1} a young woman. {2} womankind. [0754-c]
