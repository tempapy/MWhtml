• bhojya pot. p.. [bhuj-ṇyat] 1 To be eaten. --2 To be enjoyed or possessed. --3 To be suffered or experienced. --4 To be enjoyed carnally. --jyaṃ 1 Food, meal; tvaṃ bhoktā ahaṃ ca bhojyabhūtaḥ Pt. 2; Ku. 2. 15; Ms. 3. 240. --2 A store of provisions, eatables. --3 A dainty. --4 Enjoyment. --5 Advantage, profit. --6 Food given to the Manes. --Comp. --kālaḥ mealtime. --saṃbhavaḥ chyme, the primary juice of the body.
