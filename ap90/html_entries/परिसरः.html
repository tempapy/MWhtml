• parisaraḥ
      1 Verge, border, proximity, vicinity, neighbourhood, environs (of a river, mountain, town &c.); godāvarīparisarasya girerataṭāni U. 3. 8; parisaraviṣayeṣu līḍhamuktāḥ Ki. 5. 38.
      2 Position, site.
      3 Width, breadth.
      4 Death.
      5 A rule, precept.
      6 A god. [0682-c]
