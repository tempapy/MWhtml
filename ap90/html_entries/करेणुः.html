kareṇuḥ [kṛ-eṇu Uṇ. 2. 1; ke mastake reṇurasya Tv.]
      1 An elephant in general; kareṇurārohayate niṣādinaṃ Śi. 12. 5, 5. 48.
      2 The Karṇikāra tree.
      --ṇuḥ --ṇūḥ f.
      1 A female elephant; dadau rasātpaṃkajareṇugaṃdhi gajāya gaṃḍūṣajalaṃ kareṇuḥ Ku. 3. 37; R. 16. 16.
      2 N. of the mother of Pālakāpya.
      3 N. of a medicinal plant.
    Comp.
      --bhūḥ, --sutaḥ N. of Pālakāpya the founder of the science of elephants. [0381-b]
