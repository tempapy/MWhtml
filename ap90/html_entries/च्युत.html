cyuta p. p. [cyu-kta cyut-ka vā]
      1 Fallen down, slipped, fallen.
      2 Removed, expelled.
      3 Strayed, erred, deviated from.
      4 Deprived.
      5 Broken, disordered.
      6 Dropped, oozed out.
      7 Lost, gone, perished; R. 3. 45.
      8 Moved, shaken.
    Comp.
      --adhikāra a. dismissed from office.
      --ātman a. of a depraved soul, evil-minded; Ku. 5. 81. [0499-b]
