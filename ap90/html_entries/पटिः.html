• paṭiḥ --ṭī f. 1 The curtain of a stage. --2 A cloth. --3 Coarse cloth, canvas. --4 A screen of cloth surrounding a tent --5 A coloured garment. --Comp. --kṣepaḥ tossing aside the curtain (of the stage); used as a stage-direction to denote the hurried entrance of a character on the stage; cf. apaṭīkṣepa.
