satata a. Constant, eternal, everlasting, perpetual.
      --taṃ ind. Constantly, continually, eternally, always; sulabhāḥ puruṣā rājan satataṃ priyavādinaḥ Rām.
    Comp.
      --gaḥ, --gatiḥ wind; salilatale satatagatīnaṃtaḥsaṃcāriṇaḥ saṃnigṛhya śayyā kāryā Dk.; satatagāstatagānagiro'libhiḥ Śi. 6. 50; netrā nītāḥ satatagatinā yadvimānāgrabhūmīḥ Me. 69. --yāyin a. {1} always moving. {2} constantly tending to decay. [1083-c]
