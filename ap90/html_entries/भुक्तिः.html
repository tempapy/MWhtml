• bhuktiḥ f. [bhuj-ktin]
      1 Eating, enjoyment.
      2 (In law) Possession, fruition; Pt. 3. 94; Y. 2. 22.
      3 Food.
      4 The daily motion of a planet.
      5 A limit.
    Comp.
    • --pradaḥ a kind of plant (mudga).
    • --varjita a. not allowed to be enjoyed. [0819-b]
