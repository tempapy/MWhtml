• harṣaṇa (ṇā or ṇī f.) [hṛṣ-ṇic lyu] Causing delight, gladdening, delightful, pleasant. --ṇaḥ 1 N. of one of the five arrows of Kāmadeva. --2 A morbid affection of the eyes. --3 A deity presiding over the funeral ceremonies. --ṇaṃ Joy, delight, happiness, gladdening, delighting; durhṛdāmapraharṣāya suhṛdāṃ harṣaṇāya ca Mb.
