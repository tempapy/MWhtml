• arāla a. [ṛ-vic araṃ ālāti, lāka] Spreading like the spokes of a wheel, curved, crooked; pādāvarālāṃgulī M. 2. 3. --laḥ
      1 A bent or crooked arm.
      2 The resin of the plant Shorea Robusta (sarjarasa).
      3 An elephant in rut.
    • --lā
      1 An unchaste woman, harlot, courtezan.
      2 A modest woman (adhṛṣṭā).
    Comp.
    • --keśī a woman with curled hair; bhittvā nirākrāmadarālakeśyāḥ R. 6. 81. --pakṣman a. having curved eyelashes; Ku. 5. 49. [0161-a]
