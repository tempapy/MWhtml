• mahiṣī 1 A she-buffalo, buffalocow; Ms. 9. 55; Y. 2. 159. --2 The principal queen, queen-consort; mahiṣīsakhaḥ R. 1. 48, 2. 25, 3. 9. --3 A queen in general. --4 The female of a bird. --5 A lady's maid, female servant (sairiṃdhrī). --6 An immoral woman. --7 Money acquired by the prostitution of one's wife; cf. māhiṣika. --Comp. --pālaḥ a keeper of she-buffaloes. --staṃbhaḥ a pillar adorned with a buffalo's head.
