unmath, unmaṃth 1 9 P.
      1 To shake up, disturb, excite, stir; pain.
      2 To strike, kill, destroy; mīmāṃsākṛtamunmamātha sahasā hastī muniṃ jaiminiṃ Pt. 2. 33; dhairyamunmathya Māl. 1. 18; vikalpanidrāmunmathya Prab.
      3 To tear, cut off, peel off; vanyadvipenonmathitā tvagasya R. 2. 37.
      4 To mix. [0313-c]
