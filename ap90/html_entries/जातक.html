jātaka a. [jāta-svārthe ka] Born, produced.
      --kaḥ
      1 A new-born infant.
      2 A mendicant.
      --kaṃ
      1 A ceremony performed after the birth of a child (jātakarman).
      2 Astrological calculation of a nativity.
      3 An aggregate of similar things.
    Comp.
      --dhvaniḥ a leech. [0511-a]
