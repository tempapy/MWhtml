samuccayaḥ
      1 Collection, assemblage, aggregation, mass, multitude.
      2 Conjunction of words or sentences; see ca.
      3 A figure of speech; K. P. 10. (Kārikās 115 and 116), (it consists in joining together two or more things, independent of each other, but connected in idea with reference to some common action). [1099-c]
