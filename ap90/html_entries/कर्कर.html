karkara a.
      1 Hard, solid.
      2 Firm.
      --raḥ
      1 A hammer.
      2 A mirror.
      3 A bone, broken piece (of skull); fragment; Māl. 5. 19.
      4 A strap or rope of leather.
    Comp.
      --akṣaḥ a wag-tail.
      --aṃgaḥ the Khañjana bird.
      --aṃdhukaḥ a blind well; cf. aṃdhakūpa. [0381-c]
