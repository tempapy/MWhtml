pāṭakaḥ [paṭ-ṇvul]
      1 A splitter, divider.
      2 Part of a village.
      3 The half of a village.
      4 A kind of musical instrument.
      5 A bank, shore.
      6 A flight of steps leading to the water.
      7 Loss of capital or stock.
      8 A long span.
      9 Throwing dice. [0690-c]
