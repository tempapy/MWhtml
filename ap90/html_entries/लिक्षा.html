• likṣā, likhyā
      1 A nit, the egg of a louse.
      2 A very minute measure of weight (said to be equal to 4 or {8} trasarenus); jālāṃtaragate bhānau yaccāṇu dṛśyate rajaḥ . taiścaturbhirbhavellikṣā; or trasareṇavoṣṭau vijñeyā likṣaikā parimāṇataḥ Ms. 8. 133; see Y. 1. 362 also. [0928-c]
