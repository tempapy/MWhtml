• arvan a. [ṛ-vanip] 1 Going, moving, running. --2 Mean, unworthy, censurable (garhya Uṇ. 4. 112; kutsita 5. 54). --m. (arvā, arvaṃtau, arvaṃtaḥ &c.) 1 A horse: ślathīkṛtapragrahamarvatāṃ vrajāḥ Śi. 12. 31. --2 An epithet of a horse or its driver. --3 One of the ten horses of the moon. --4 Indra. --5 A short span (gokarṇaparimāṇa). --tī 1 A mare. --2 A bawd, procuress. --3 A nymph. --Comp. --vasuḥ one of the principal seven rays of the sun.
