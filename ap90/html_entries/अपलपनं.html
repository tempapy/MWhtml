• apalapanaṃ, apalāpaḥ 1 Concealing, hiding; kṛtatigmadyutimaṃḍalāpalāpaiḥ Śi. 20. 28. --2 Concealment or denial of knowledge, evasion; na hi pratyakṣasiddhasyāpalāpaḥ kartuṃ śakyate Ś. B. --3 Detraction, concealment of truth, thoughts, feelings &c. unmukhadarśanāpalapanaiḥ piṃḍārthamāyasyataḥ Mu. 3. 14. --4 Affection, regard. --5 (In medicine) The part between the shoulder and the ribs. --Comp. --daṃḍaḥ (in law) a fine laid on one who denies the charge on which he is convicted.
