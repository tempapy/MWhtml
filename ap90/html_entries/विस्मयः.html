• vismayaḥ 1 Wonder, surprise, astonishment, amazement; puruṣaḥ prababhūvāgnervismayena sahartvijāṃ R. 10. 50. --2 Astonishment or wonder, being the feeling which produces the adbhuta sentiment; S. D. thus defines it: --vividheṣu padārtheṣu lokasīmātivatiṃṣu . visphāraścetaso yastu sa vismaya udāhṛtaḥ .. 207. --3 Pride, arrogance; tapaḥ kṣarati vismayāt Ms. 4. 237. --4 Uncertainty, doubt. --Comp. --ākula, --āviṣṭa a. astonished, struck with wonder.
