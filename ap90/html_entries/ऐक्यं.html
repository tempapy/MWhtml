aikyaṃ
      1 Oneness, unity, harmony; R. 10. 82; U. 6. 33.
      2 Unanimity.
      3 Identity, sameness.
      4 Especially, the identity of the human soul or of the universe with the Deity.
      5 An aggregate, whole.
      6 (In alg.) The product of the length and depth of the portions or little excavations differing in depth (Colebrooke). [0358-b]
