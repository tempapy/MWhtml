avajñā 9 P. To have a low opinion of, to despise, to treat with contempt, disregard; avajānāsi māṃ yasmāt R. 1. 77; avajānaṃti māṃ mūḍhā mānuṣīṃ tanumāśritaṃ Bg. 9. 11; Bk. 3. 8. [0179-b]
      avajñā [ava-jñā-aṅ] Disrespect, contempt; slighting, low opinion; nātmanyavajñā kartavyā H. 1; disregard (with the obj. in loc. or gen.); ātmanyavajñāṃ śithilīcakāra R. 2. 41; ye nāma kecidiha naḥ prathayaṃtyavajñāṃ Māl. 1. 6; Śānti. 3. 23; avijñāvajñeyaṃ paritapati noccairapi budhaṃ Udb.
