• śī {c2c} A. (śete, śayita; pass. śayyate; desid. śiśayiṣate) 1 To lie, lie down, recline, rest; itaśca śaraṇarthinaḥ śikhariṇāṃ gaṇāḥ śerate Bh. 2. 76. --2 To sleep (fig. also); kiṃ niḥśaṃke śeṣe śeṣevayasaḥ samāgato mṛtyuḥ . athavā sukhaṃ śayīthā nikaṭe jāgarti jāhavī jananī Bv. 4. 30; Bh. 3. 79; Ku. 5. 12. --3 To rest, repose. --Caus. (śāyayati-te) 1 To cause to sleep or lie down; pārśve śāyaya rāvaṇaṃ Bk. 8. 83. --2 To allow to rest or repose. [1047-b]
