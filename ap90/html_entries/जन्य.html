• janya a. [jan kartari yat] 1 To be born or produced. --2 Born, produced. --3 (At the end of comp.) Born from, occasioned by. --4 Belonging to a race or family. --5 Vulgar, common. --6 National. --7 Relating to, or fit for men. --nyaḥ 1 A father. --2 A friend, attendant or relative of a bridegroom; Māl. 6. 2. --3 A common man. --4 A report, rumour. --nyaṃ 1 Birth, production, creation. --2 That which is born or created, a created thing, an effect (opp. janaka); janyānāṃ janakaḥ kālaḥ Bhāṣā P. 45; janakasya svabhāvo hi janye tiṣṭhati niścitaṃ Śabdak. --3 The body. --4 A portent occurring at birth. --5 A market, a fair. --6 War, battle; tatra janyaṃ raghorghoraṃ parvatīyairgaṇairabhūt R. 4. 77. --7 Censure, abuse. --8 A community, nation. --9 People. --10 Report, rumour. --nyā 1 The friend of a mother. --2 The relation of a bride, a bride's maid; yāhīti janyāmavadatkumārī R. 6. 30. --3 Pleasure, happiness. --4 Affection.
