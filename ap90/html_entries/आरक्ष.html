• ārakṣa a. Preserved, defended, fit to be protected.
    • --kṣaḥ, --kṣā
      1 Protection, preservation, guard; ārakṣe madhyame sthitān Rām; Śānti. 3. 5; Ms. 3. 204.
      2 The junction of the frontal sinuses of an elephant (kaṃbhasaṃdhi).
      3 The part of the forehead below this junction.
      4 An army. [0253-a]
