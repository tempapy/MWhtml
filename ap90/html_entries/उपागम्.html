upāgam 1 P.
    • 1 To come, approach, go or draw near (a person or place).
    • 2 (a) To go to, go to the state of; paṃcatvamupāgataḥ or parāṃ tṛptimupāgataḥ; so krodhaṃ, mohaṃ, kṣayaṃ &c. (b) To undergo, suffer, endure.
    • 3 To obtain, get; dāyādupāgato dāsaḥ Mit.; Y. 2. 143.
    • 4 To occur, fall to the lot of. [0336-b]
