• śakunaḥ [śak-unan Uṇ. 3. 49] 1 A bird (in general); śakunocchiṣṭaṃ Y. 1. 168. --2 A kind of bird, a vulture or kite. --3 A kind of song (sung at festivals). --naṃ 1 An omen, a prognostic, any omen presaging good or evil; Śi. 9. 83. --2 An auspicious omen. --Comp. --jña a. knowing omens. (--jñā) a small house-lizard. --jñānaṃ knowledge of omens, augury. --śāstraṃ ‘the science of omens’, N. of a work. [1028-a]
