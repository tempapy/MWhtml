avaṭuḥ [ava-ṭīk mita° ḍu]
    • 1 A hole in the ground.
    • 2 A well.
    • 3 The back or nape of the neck.
    • 4 The depressed part of the body.
    • 5 A kind of tree.
      f. The raised portion of the neck.
      n. A hole, a rent.
      Comp. a hind curl, the hair on the back of the head; Śi. 10. 12. [0179-c]
