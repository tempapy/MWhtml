• avaṭuḥ [ava-ṭīk mita° ḍu]
      1 A hole in the ground.
      2 A well.
      3 The back or nape of the neck.
      4 The depressed part of the body.
      5 A kind of tree.
    • --ṭuḥ f. The raised portion of the neck.
    • --ṭu n. A hole, a rent.
    Comp.
    • --jaḥ a hind curl, the hair on the back of the head; Śi. 10. 12. [0179-c]
