• syaṃdana a. (nā or nī f.) [syaṃd-lyu lyuṭ vā] 1 Going quickly, fleet; flowing. --2 Quick, swift of foot, fleet; syaṃdanā no caturagāḥ Ki. 15. 16. --naḥ 1 A war-chariot, a car or chariot in general; dharmāraṇyaṃ praviśati gajaḥ syaṃdanālokabhītaḥ Ś. 1. 33. --2 Air, wind. --3 A kind of tree (tiniśa). --naṃ 1 Flowing, trickling, oozing. --2 Rushing, going or flowing quickly. --3 Water. --Comp. --ārohaḥ a warrior who fights while seated in a chariot.
