anuśuc 1 P. To bewail, mourn over, regret; kathaṃ tameva caurahatakamanuśocasi Mk. 3; naṣṭaṃ mṛtamatikrāṃtaṃ nānuśocaṃti paṃḍitāḥ Pt. 1. 333; śaiśavaṃ te smṛtvātmānamanuśocāmi K. 333; Ve. 5. 4 condole with, weep with. --Caus. To mourn over, deplore, grieve for, regret; tṛṇamiva vane śūnye tyaktā na vāpyanuśocitā U. 3. 32 nor was she (her loss) regretted. [0083-a]
