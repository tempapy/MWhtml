• viṣvac, viṣvaṃc a. (Nom. sing. m. viṣvaṅ; f. viṣūcī, n. viṣvak) 1 Going or being everywhere, all-pervading; viṣvaṅmohaḥ sthagayati kathaṃ maṃdabhāgyaḥ karomi U. 3. 38, Māl. 9. 20. --2 Separating into parts. --3 Different. --4 Alternately. (viṣvak is used adverbially in the sense of ‘everywhere, on all sides, all around’; Ki. 14. 59; Pt. 2. 2; Māl. 5. 4, 9. 25). --Comp. --senaḥ ( viṣvaksenaḥ or viṣvakṣeṇaḥ) an epithet of Viṣṇu; sāmyamāpa kamalāsakhaviṣvaksenasevitayugāṃtapayodheḥ Śi. 10. 55; viṣvaksenaḥ svatanumaviśatsarvalokapratiṣṭhāṃ R. 15. 103. °priyā N. of Lakṣmī.
