• saṃpanna p. p.
      1 Prosperous, thriving, rich.
      2 Fortunate, successful, happy.
      3 Effected, brought about, accomplished.
      4 Finished, completed.
      5 Perfect.
      6 Full-grown, mature.
      7 Procured, obtained.
      8 Right, correct.
      9 Endowed with, possessed of.
      10 Turned out, become; īdṛśaḥ saṃpannaḥ U. 3.
      11 Perfectly acquainted or conversant with.
    • --nnaḥ An epithet of Śiva.
    • --nnaṃ
      1 Riches, wealth; Pt. 4. 115.
      2 A dainty, delicacy. [1102-c]
