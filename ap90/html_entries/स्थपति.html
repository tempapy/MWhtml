sthapati a. [sthā-ka tasya patiḥ] Chief, principal.
      --tiḥ
      1 A king, sovereign.
      2 An architect.
      3 A wheel-wright, master-carpenter.
      4 A charioteer.
      5 One who offers a sacrifice to Bṛhaspati.
      6 An attendant on the women's apartments.
      7 N. of Kubera. [1147-a]
