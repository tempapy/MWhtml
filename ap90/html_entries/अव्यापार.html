• avyāpāra a. Having no work, unemployed.
    • --raḥ
      1 Cessation from work, state of being unemployed.
      2 A business not practised or understood.
      3 Not one's own business; avyāpāreṣu vyāpāraṃ kṛ to meddle with affairs not one's own (which do not concern one). [0199-c]
