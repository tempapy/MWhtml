dṛṣad f. [dṝ-adi ṣuk hrasvaśca; cf. Uṇ. 1. 128]
      1 A rock, large stone, or stone in general; Me. 55; R. 4. 74; Bh. 1. 38.
      2 A mill-stone, a flat stone for grinding condiments upon.
    Comp.
      --upalaḥ a grind-stone for grinding condiments upon. ( dṛṣadimāṣakaḥ a tax raised from mill-stones). [0577-c]
