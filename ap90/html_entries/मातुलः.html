• mātulaḥ [māturbhrātā mātṛ-ḍulac]
      1 A maternal uncle; Bg. 1. 26; Ms. 2. 130, 5. 81.
      2 The Dhattūra plant.
      3 An epithet of the solar year.
      4 A kind of rice.
      5 A kind of snake.
    Comp.
    • --putrakaḥ {1} the son of a maternal uncle. {2} the fruit of the Dhattūra plant. [0857-c]
