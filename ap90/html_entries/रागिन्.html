• rāgin a. [rāga-ini] 1 Coloured, dyed. --2 Colouring, painting. --3 Red. --4 Full of passion or feeling, impassioned. --5 Full of love, subject to love. --6 Passionately fond of, devotedly attached to, desirous of, yearning after (at the end of comp.). --7 Delighting, rejoicing. --m. 1 A painter. --2 A lover; eko rāgiṣu rājate priyatamādehārdhahārī haraḥ Bh. 3. 121. --3 A libertine, sensualist. --ṇī 1 A modification of a musical mode (rāga), of which 30 or 36 kinds are enumerated. --2 A wanton and intriguing woman, a lustful woman.
