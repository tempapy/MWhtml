haviṣyaṃ [haviṣe hitaṃ yat]
      1 Anything fit for an oblation; Ms. 3. 256, 11. 78, 107; Y. 1. 239.
      2 Clarified butter.
      3 Wild rice.
      4 Rice mixed with ghee.
    Comp.
      --annaṃ food fit to be eaten during certain holidays or days of fast.
      --āśin, --bhuj m. fire. [1168-a]
