vilakṣa a.
      1 Having no characteristic or distinguishing marks.
      2 Bewildered, embarrassed.
      3 Surprised, astonished.
      4 Ashamed, abashed, disconcerted; gotreṣu skhalitastadā bhavati ca vrīḍāvilakṣaściraṃ Ś. 6. 4.
      5 Forced, unnatural, embarrassed (as a smile); vilakṣasmitasphuritairdaśanāṃśubhiḥ K. 233; Ratn. 3. 14. [0990-a]
