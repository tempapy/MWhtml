• tyakta p. p. [tyaj karmaṇi-kta] 1 Abandoned, forsaken, left, quitted. --2 Resigned, surrendered. --3 Shunned, avoided; see tyaj. --Comp. --agniḥ a Brāhmaṇa who has given up household fire. --jīvita, --prāṇa a. ready to abandon life, willing to run any risk; madarthe tyaktajīvitāḥ Bg. 1. 9. --lajja a. shameless.
