• vyāghātaḥ 1 Striking against. --2 A blow, stroke. --3 An impediment, obstacle. --4 Contradiction. --5 Disobedience; prathamaṃ tāvanmamājñāvyāghātaḥ Mu. 3. --6 A figure of speech in which opposite effects are shown to be produced from the same cause or by the same agency; it is thus defined by Mammaṭa: --tadyathā sādhitaṃ kenāpyapareṇa tadanyathā . tathaiva yadvidhīyeta sa vyāghāta iti smṛtaḥ K. P. 10; e. g. see Vb. 1. 2, or the quotation under virūpākṣa. [1022-a]
