• abhilāṣaḥ (°saḥ sometimes) A desire, wish, longing for, craving after; affection, longing of a lover, love, (usually with loc. of the object of desire); ato'bhilāṣe prathamaṃ tathāvidhe mano babaṃdha R. 3. 4; bhava hṛdaya sābhilāṣaṃ Ś. 1. 28; Me. 110 sābhilāṣaṃ nirvarṇya Ś. 3 casting a coveting or wistful look; na khalu satyameva śakuṃtalāyāṃ ma mābhilāṣaḥ Ś. 2, Pt. 5. 67; sometimes with prati and acc., or in comp.
