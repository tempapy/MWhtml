• niṣkaḥ --kaṃ 1 A golden coin (of different values, but generally taken to be equal to one Karṣa or Suvarṇa of 16 Māṣas). --2 A weight of gold equal to 108 or 150 Suvarṇas q. v. --3 A golden ornament for the neck or the breast; Ku. 2. 49. --4 Gold in general. --5 A golden vessel. --ṣkaḥ A Cāṇḍāla.
