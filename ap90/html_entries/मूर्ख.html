mūrkha a. Stupid, dull-headed, foolish, silly.
      --rkhaḥ
      1 A fool, blockhead; na tu prātaniviṣṭamūrkhajanacittamārādhayat Bh. 2. 5, 8; mūrkha balādapagādhinaṃ māṃ pratipādāyaṣyasi V. 2.
      2 A kind of bean. Comp. --paṃḍitaḥ a learned fool; Pt. 5. 40. --bhūyaṃ folly, stupidity, ignorance.
      --bhrātṛka a. one who has a foolish brother.
      --maṃḍalaṃ an assembly of fools. [0873-b]
