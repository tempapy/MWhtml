usra a.
      1 Relating to, or seen in, the morning.
      2 Bright, shining.
      --sraḥ
      1 A ray (of light), beam sarvairusraiḥ samagraistvamiva nṛpaguṇairdīpyate saptasaptiḥ M. 2. 13; R. 4. 66; Ki. 5. 31, 34.
      2 A bull.
      3 A god.
      4 The sun.
      5 A day.
      6 The two Aśvini-kumāras.
      --strā
      1 Morning, dawn.
      2 Light; bright sky.
      3 A cow.
      4 The earth.
    Comp.
      --dhanvan a. having a bright bow. (--m.) N. of Indra.
      --yāman a. going out early in the morning (as the Aśvins). [0344-c]
