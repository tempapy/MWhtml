jānu n. [jan-ñuṇ] The knee; jānubhyāmavaniṃ gatvā kneeling (or falling on one's knees) on the ground.
    Comp.
      --daghna a. reaching to, as high as, the knees, knee-deep.
      --phalakaṃ, --maṃḍalaṃ the knee-pan.
      --vijānu n. a peculiar position in fighting (contracting and extending the knees).
      --saṃdhiḥ the knee-joint. [0512-a]
