• vedhas m. [vidhā asun guṇaḥ]
      1 A creator; Māl. 1. 21.
      2 N. of Brahman, the creator; taṃ vedhā vidadhe nūnaṃ mahābhūtasamādhinā R. 1. 29; Ku. 2. 16, 5. 41.
      3 A secondary creator (such as Dakṣa, sprung from Brahman); Ku. 2. 14.
      4 N. of Śiva.
      5 Of Viṣṇu.
      6 The sun.
      7 The Arka plant.
      8 A learned man.
      9 A priest.
      10 N. of Soma.
      11 A poet. [1011-b]
