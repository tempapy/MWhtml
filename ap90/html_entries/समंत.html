• samaṃta a. [samyak aṃtaḥ, sa yatra vā]
      1 Being on every side, universal.
      2 Complete, entire.
    • --taḥ Limit, boundary, term. (samaṃtaṃ, samaṃtataḥ, samaṃtāt are used adverbially in the sense of ‘from every side’, ‘all around’, ‘on all sides’, ‘wholly’, ‘completely’).
    Comp.
    • --dugdhā the plant called snuhī q. v. --paṃcakaṃ N. of the district called Kurukṣetra or of a place near it; Ve. 6. --bhadraḥ a Buddha or the Buddha.
    • --bhuj m. fire. [1094-a]
