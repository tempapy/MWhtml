• cūḍā
      1 The hair on the top of the head, a single lock on the crown of the head (left after the ceremony of tonsure); R. 18. 51; U. 5. 36; 5. 3; 4. 20.
      2 The ceremony of tonsure.
      3 The crest of a cock or peacock; Pt. 2. 73.
      4 Any crest, plume or diadem.
      5 The head.
      6 Top, summit.
      7 A room on the top of a house.
      8 A well.
      9 An ornament (like a bracelet worn on the wrist). Comp. --karaṇaṃ, --karman n. the ceremony of tonsure; Ms. 2. 35. --pāśaḥ a mass of hair; cūḍāpāśe navakurabakaṃ Me. 65. --maṇiḥ, --ratnaṃ {1} a jewel worn on the top of the head, a crest jewel (fig. also). {2} best, excellent (usually at the end of comp.).
    • --lakṣaṇaṃ tonsure. [0497-b]
