• pāṃḍavaḥ [pāṃḍorapatyaṃ pumān āreñ] ‘A son or descendant of Pāṇḍu’, N. of any one of the five sons of Pāṇḍu; i. e. yudhiṣṭhira, bhīma, arjuna, nakula and sahadeva; haṃsāḥ saṃprati pāṃḍavā iva vanādajñātacaryāṃgatāḥ Mk. 5. 6. --Comp. --ābhīlaḥ N. of Kṛṣṇa. --śreṣṭhaḥ N. of Yudhiṣṭhira. [0691-c]
