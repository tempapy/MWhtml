• saṃmīl 1 P.
      1 To shut the eyes.
      2 To close up, contract (as flowers &c.); saṃmīlaṃti na tāvadbaṃdhanakoṣāstayāvacitapuṣpāḥ Ś. 3. 6. --Caus.
      1 To shut, close; upāṃtasaṃmīlitalo cano nṛpaḥ R. 3. 26; 13. 10.
      2 To obscure, darken, make dim; vikāraścaitanyaṃ bhramayati ca saṃmīlayati ca U. 1. 36.
      3 To make insensible. [1106-c]
