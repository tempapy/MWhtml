• lālana a. (nī f.) Fondling, coaxing, caressing.
    • --naḥ A sort of poisonous mouse.
    • --naṃ [lal-lyuṭ]
      1 Caressing, fondling, coaxing; sutalālanaṃ &c.
      2 Indulging, over-indulgence, fondling too much; lālane bahavo doṣāstāḍane bahavo guṇāḥ; Pt. 1. 169; Bh. 2. 42. [0927-c]
