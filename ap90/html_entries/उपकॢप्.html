• upakḷp {c1c} A. 1 To be fit for; tatredamupakalpate Bhāg. --2 To be prepared or ready (at hand); āsaneṣūpakḷpteṣu Ms. 3. 208, 8. 333. --3 To result in, serve as, lead to (with dat.); vāryapi śraddhayā dattamakṣayāyopakalpate Ms. 3. 202. --4 To become, take a (particular) form or shape. --Caus. 1 To prepare, get ready, equip; rathaṃ Ve. 2. --2 To appoint, assign, allot; upakalpitaṃ paśuṃ Pt. 1; U. 2; Y. 1. 109. --3 To vow, promise; devatopakalpita Pt. 4. --4 To set up, exhibit. --5 To render (homage), communicate. --6 To bring near, fetch. --7 To assume, take.
