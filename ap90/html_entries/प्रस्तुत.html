prastuta p. p.
      1 Praised, eulogized.
      2 Begun, commenced.
      3 Accomplished, done, effected.
      4 Happened.
      5 Approached.
      6 Proposed, declared, under discussion, taken in hand.
      7 Expected, desired.
      8 Ready, prepared.
      9 Executed with effort or energy.
      10 Made or consisting of.
      --taṃ
      1 The matter in hand, the subject under discussion or consideration; adhunā prastutamanusriyatāṃ.
      2 (In Rhet.) Forming the subject of discussion, the upameya; see prakṛta; aprastutapraśaṃsā sā yā saiva prastutāśrayā K. P. 10.
    Comp.
      --aṃkuraḥ a figure of speech in which a reference is made to a passing circumstance to bring out something latent in the hearer's mind; see Chandr. 5. 64 and Kuval. under prastutāṃkura. [0767-a]
