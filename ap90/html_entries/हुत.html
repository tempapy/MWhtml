• huta p. p. [hu-kta]
      1 Offered as an oblation to fire, burnt as a sacrificial offering.
      2 One to whom an oblation is offered; Ś. 4; R. 2. 71. --taḥ N. of Śiva.
    • --taṃ An oblation, offering.
