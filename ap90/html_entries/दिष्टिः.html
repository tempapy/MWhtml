• diṣṭiḥ f. [diś bhāve ktin, saṃjñāyāṃ kartari ktic vā] 1 Assignment, allotment. --2 Direction, command, instruction, rule, precept. --3 Fate, fortune, destiny. --4 Good fortune, happiness, any auspicious event (such as the birth of a son); diṣṭivṛddhimiva śuśrāva K. 55; diṣṭivṛddhisaṃbhramo mahānabhūt K. 70. --5 A sort of measure of length. [0566-c]
