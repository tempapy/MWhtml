āstika a. (kī f.) [asti paralokaḥ iti matiryasya, ṭhak]
      1 One who believes in God and another world; yannāstyeva tadasti vastviti mṛṣā jalpadbhirevāstikaiḥ Prab. 2.
      2 A believer in sacred tradition.
      3 Pious, faithful, believing; āstikaḥ śraddadhānaśca Y. 1. 268. --kaḥ or āstīkaḥ N. of a Muni. [0269-c]
