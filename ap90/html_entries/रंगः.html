• raṃgaḥ [raṃj-bhāve ghañ]
      1 Colour, hue, dye, paint.
      2 A stage, theatre, play house, an arena, any place of public amusement, as in raṃgavighnopaśāṃtaye S. D. 281.
      3 A place of assembly.
      4 The members of an assembly, the audience; aho rāgabaddhacittavṛttirālikhita iva sarvato raṃgaḥ Ś. 1; raṃgasya darśayitvā nivartate nartakī yathā nṛtyāt . puruṣasya tathātmānaṃ prakāśya vinivartate prakṛtiḥ .. Sarva. S.
      5 A field of battle.
      6 Dancing, singing, acting.
      7 Mirth, diversion.
      8 The nasal modification of a vowel; saraṃgaṃ kaṃpayetkaṃpaṃ rathīveti nidarśanaṃ Śik. 30; see 26, 27, 28 also.
      9 An extract of Khadira.
