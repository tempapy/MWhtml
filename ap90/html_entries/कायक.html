kāyaka (--yikā f.), kāyika (kī f.) a. [kāya-ṭhak] Relating to the body, bodily, corporeal; kāyikatapaḥ Ms. 12. 8. --kā Interest (whatever is given for the use of money).
      Comp. f. {1} interest consisting in the use of any animal or capital stock pawned. {2} interest of which the payment does not affect the principal, or the use of the body of an animal pledged by the person to whom it is pledged. [0396-a]
