• vartana a. [vṛt-lyu lyuṭa vā] 1 Abiding, living, staying, being &c. --2 Stationary. --naḥ A dwarf. --nī 1 A road, way. --2 Living, life. --3 Pounding, grinding. --4 Sending off, despatching. --5 A spindle. --naṃ 1 Living, being. --2 Staying, abiding, residing. --3 Action, movement, mode or manner of living; smarasi ca tadupāṃteṣvāvayorvartanāni U. 1. 26; (the word may here mean ‘abode or residence’, also). --4 Living on, subsisting (at the end of comp.) --5 Livelihood, maintenance, subsistence. --6 Turning round, revolving. --7 Rolling on, moving about. --8 Appointing. --9 A means of subsistence, profession, occupation. --10 Conduct, behaviour, proceeding. --11 Wages, salary, hire. --12 Commerce, traffic. --13 A spindle. --14 A globe, ball.
