• māṣaḥ [maṣ saṃjñāyāṃ kartari ghañ] 1 A bean; (the sing. being used for the plant and the pl. for the fruit or seed); tilebhyaḥ prati yacchati māṣān Sk. mudgābhāve māṣādyāḥ pratinidhitvamarhaṃti J. N. V. --2 A particular weight of gold; māṣo viṃśatimo bhāgaḥ paṇasya parikīrtitaḥ or guṃjābhiraṣṭabhimārṣaḥ --3 A fool, blockhead. --4 A kind of pulse. --5 A cutaneous eruption resembling beans. --Comp. --adaḥ, --ādaḥ a tortoise. --ājyaṃ a dish of beans cooked with ghee. --āśaḥ a horse. --ūna a. less by a Māṣa. --vardhakaḥ a goldsmith.
