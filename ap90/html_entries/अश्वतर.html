aśvatara a. Swift, speedy.
      --raḥ [tanuḥ aśvaḥ, aśva tanutve ṣṭarac, P. V. 3. 91]
      1 A mule.
      2 One of the chiefs of the Nāgas or serpent-race inhabiting the lower regions.
      3 A male calf.
      4 A class of Gandharvas.
      --rī A mule; udarasya samutthāne svagarbho' śvatarīmiva Rām.; Pt. 2. 32; Chāṇ. 19. [cf. Pers. aster]. [0204-c]
