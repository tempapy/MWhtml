• goṣṭhiḥ --ṣṭhī f. 1 An assembly, meeting. --2 Society, association. --3 Conversation, chitchat, discourse; goṣṭhī satkavibhiḥ samaṃ Bh. 1. 28; Māl. 10. 25; tenaiva saha sarvadā goṣṭhīmanubhavati Pt. 2. --4 A multitude or collection. --5 Family connections, relatives, especially such as require to be maintained. --6 A kind of dramatic composition in one act. °patiḥ {1} the chief of an assembly, president. {2} the master of a family.
