• aye ind.
      1 As a vocative particle, or as a kind of gentle address ( = ayi); aye gaurīnātha tripurahara śaṃbho trinayana Bh. 3. 123.
      2 An interjection showing (a) ‘surprise’ or ‘wonder’ and translated by ‘oh’ ‘ah’, aye mātaliḥ Ś. 6; aye kumāralakṣmaṇaḥ prāptaḥ U. 1; aye mayyeva bhrukuṭīdharaḥ saṃvṛttaḥ U. 5; (b) ‘grief’, ‘dejection’; aye davepādapadmopajīvino'vastheyaṃ Mu. 2 (alas!); (c) ‘anger’; aye aśvamedha iti viśvavijayināṃ kṣatriyāṇāṃ mahānutkarṣanikaṣaḥ U. 4; (d) ‘fear,’ ‘flurry’, ‘agitation;’ (e) ‘recollection’; (f) ‘fear’; (g) ‘fatigue’.
