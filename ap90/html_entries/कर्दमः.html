kardamaḥ [Uṇ. 4. 85]
      1 Mud, slime, mire; pādau nūpuralagnakardamadharau prakṣālayaṃtī sthitā Mk. 5. 35; pathaścāśyānakardamān R. 4. 24.
      2 Dirt, filth.
      3 (Fig.) Sin.
      4 N. of a Prajāpati.
      --maṃ Flesh.
    Comp.
      --āṭakaḥ a receptacle for filth, sewer &c. [0383-b]
