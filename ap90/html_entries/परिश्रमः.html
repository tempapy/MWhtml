pariśramaḥ
      1 Fatigue, exhaustion, trouble, pain; ātmā pariśramasya padamupanītaḥ Ś. 1; R. 1. 58; 11. 12.
      2 Exertion, labour.
      3 (Hence) Close application to or study of, being constantly occupied with; ārye kṛtapariśramosmi catuḥṣaṣṭyaṃge jyotiḥśāstre Mu. 1. [0681-c]
