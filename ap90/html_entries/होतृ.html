• hotṛ a. (trī f.) [hu-tṛc] Sacrificing, offering oblations with fire; vahati vidhihutaṃ yā haviryā ca hotrī Ś. 1. 1. --m.
      1 A sacrificial priest, especially one who recites the prayers of the Ṛgveda at a sacrifice.
      2 A sacrificer; R. 1. 62. 82; Ms. 11. 36.
      3 An epithet of Agni. [1176-c]
