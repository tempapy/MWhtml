• daridrā {c2c} P. (daridrāti, daridrita; caus. daridrayati; desid. didaridrāsati, didaridriṣati) 1 To be poor or needy; adho'dhaḥ paśyataḥ kasya mahimā nopajāyate . uparyupari paśyaṃtaḥ sarva eva daridrati .. H. 2. 2; Bk. 18. 31. --2 To be in distress; yuktaṃ mamaiva kiṃ vaktuṃ daridrāti yathā hariḥ Bk. 5. 86. --3 To become thin or sparse; daridrati viyaddrume kusumakāṃtayastārakāḥ Vikr. 11. 74. [0558-b]
