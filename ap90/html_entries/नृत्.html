nṛt 4 P. (nṛtyati, praṇṛtyati, nṛtta)
      1 To dance, move about; nṛtyati yuvatijanena samaṃ sakhi Gīt. 1; lolormau payasi mahotpalaṃ nanarta Śi. 8. 23; Bk. 3. 43.
      2 To act on the stage.
      3 To gesticulate, play. --Caus. (nartayati-te)
      1 To cause to dance; tvamāśe moghāśe kimaparamato nartayasi māṃ Bh. 3. 6; tālaiḥ śiṃjāvalayasubhagairnartitaḥ kāṃtayā me Me. 79, U. 3. 19.
      2 To cause to move. --WITH ā (caus.) {1} to cause to dance. {2} to cause to dance or move quickly, shake; marudbhirānartitanaktamāle R. 5. 42; Amaru. 32; Rs. 3. 10. --pra to dance &c.
      --prati to ridicule by dancing in return. [0647-c]
