• nagaraṃ [nagā iva prāsādāḥ saṃtyatra bā° ra; cf. P. V. 2. 107 Vārt.] A town, city (opp. grāma); nagaragamanāya matiṃ na karoti Ś. 2.
    Comp.
    • --adhikṛtaḥ, --adhipaḥ, --adhyakṣaḥ {1} the chief magistrate of a town, head police-officer. {2} governor or superintendent of a town.
    • --abhyāśaḥ --saḥ the vicinity of a town.
    • --upāṃtaḥ a suburb, the skirt of a town.
    • --okas m. a townsman.
    • --kākaḥ ‘a town-crow’, an expression of contempt.
    • --kīrtanaṃ repeating the name of a god while wandering through a city.
    • --ghātaḥ an elephant.
    • --janaḥ {1} townsfolk. {2} a citizen.
    • --pradakṣiṇā carrying an idol round a city in procession.
    • --prāṃtaḥ a suburb.
    • --mardin m. an intoxicated elephant.
    • --mārgaḥ a principal road, high-way.
    • --rakṣā superintendence or government of a town.
    • --rakṣin m. {1} the superintendent of a town. {2} a town-watchman.
    • --sthaḥ a townsman, citizen. [0605-b]
