• śikharaḥ --raṃ [śikhā astyasya-arac ālopaḥ] 1 The top, summit, or peak of a mountain; jagāma gaurī śikharaṃ śikhaḍimat Ku. 5. 7, 4; Me. 18. --2 The top of a tree. --3 Crest, tuft. --4 The point or edge of a sword. --5 Top, peak, point in general. --6 The arm-pit. --7 Bristling of the hair. --8 The bud of the Arabian jasmine. --9 A kind of ruby-like gem. --rā N. of a plant (mūrvā). --Comp. --vāsinī an epithet of Durgā. [1043-c]
