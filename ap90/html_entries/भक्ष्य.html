bhakṣya a. [bhakṣ-karmaṇi ṇyat] Eatable, fit for food.
      --kṣyaṃ
      1 Anything eatable, an article of food, food (fig. also); bhakṣyabhakṣakayoḥ prītirvipattereva kāraṇaṃ H. 1. 55; Ms. 1. 113.
      2 Water.
    Comp.
      --kāraḥ (also bhakṣyaṃkāraḥ) a baker, cook.
      --vastu n. eatables, victuals. [0805-c]
