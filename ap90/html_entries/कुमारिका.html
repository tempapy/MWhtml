• kumārikā, kumārī 1 A young girl, one from 10 to 12 years old. --2 A maiden, virgin; trīṇi varṣāṇyudīkṣeta kumāryṛtumatī satī Ms. 9. 90; 11. 59; vyāvartatānyopagamātkumārī R. 6. 69. --3 A girl or daughter in general. --4 N. of Durgā. --5 N. of several plants. --6 N. of Sītā. --7 Large cardamoms. --8 The southern extremity of the Indian peninsula (cf. the modern name Cape Comorin). --Comp. --putraḥ {1} the son of an unmarried woman. {2} N. of Karṇa. --śvaśuraḥ the fatherin-law of a girl defiled before marriage.
