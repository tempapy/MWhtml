• tāṃḍavaḥ --vaṃ
      1 Dancing in general; madatāṃḍavotsavāṃte U. 3. 18; bhrū° dance or playful movement of the eyebrows; 3. 19.
      2 Particularly, the frantic or violent dance of Śiva; tryaṃbakānaṃdi vastāṃḍavaṃ devi bhūyādabhīṣṭyai ca hṛṣṭyai ca naḥ Māl. 5. 23, 1. 1.
      3 The art of dancing.
      4 A sort of grass.
      5 (In prosody) A foot of three short syllables.
    Comp.
    • --tālikaḥ an epithet of Nandin, the door-keeper of Śiva.
    • --priyaḥ N. of Śiva. [0534-b]
