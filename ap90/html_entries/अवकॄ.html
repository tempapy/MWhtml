• avakṝ {c6c} P. 1 To bestow, pour upon, to shower upon, cover with, fill; Śi. 11. 62; (taṃ) avākiran bālalatāḥ prasūnaiḥ R. 2. 10, 4. 27; lājāṃjalibhiravakīryamāṇaḥ K. 86; Ki. 6. 2; avakīryottarīyāṇi Mb. spreading on the ground; tīrthāni avakīrṇāni tapasvibhiḥ Mb. filled with; Y. 3. 281; Me. 54. --2 To pour out, throw out Ms. 6. 48. --3 To shake off, throw off, leave. --4 (Atm.) (Used reflexively) (a) To extend, spread itself; avakirate hastī svayameva Sk. (b) To fall asunder. (c) To pass away, fall off. (d) To become faithless or untrue. [0176-a]
