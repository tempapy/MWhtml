strī
      1 A woman.
      2 A female of any animal; gajastrī, hariṇastrī &c. Ś. 5. 22.
      3 A wife; strīṇāṃ bhartā dharmadārāśca puṃsāṃ Māl. 6. 18; Me. 28.
      4 The feminine gender, or a word used in that gender; āpaḥ strībhūmni Ak.
