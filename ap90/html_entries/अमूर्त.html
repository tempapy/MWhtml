amūrta a. Formless, shapeless, incorporeal, unembodied (opp. mūrta where Muktā. says mūrtatvaṃ = avacchinnaparimāṇavattvaṃ).
      N. of Śiva.
      Comp. (In Vaiś. phil.) a quality considered to be amūrta or incorporeal such as dharma, adharma &c. dharmādharmau bhāvanā ca śabdo buddhyādayopi ca . ete'mūrtaguṇāḥ sarve Bhāṣa [0152-a]
