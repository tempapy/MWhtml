• tuccha a.
      1 Empty, void, vain, light.
      2 Small, little, trifling.
      3 Abandoned, deserted.
      4 Low, mean, insignificant, contemptible, worthless.
      5 Poor, miserable, wretched.
    • --cchaṃ Chaff.
    Comp.
    • --druḥ the castor-oil tree.
    • --dhānyaḥ --dhānyakaḥ straw, chaff. [0540-c]
