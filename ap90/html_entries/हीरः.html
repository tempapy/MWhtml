• hīraḥ [hṛ-ka ni.]
      1 A snake.
      2 A necklace.
      3 A lion.
      4 N. of the father of Śrīharṣa, the author of the Naiṣadha-charita.
      5 N. of Śiva.
    • --raḥ, --raṃ
      1 The thunderbolt of Indra.
      2 A diamond; (occurring in the concluding stanza of each canto of naiṣadhacarita).
    Comp.
    • --aṃgaḥ the thunder-bolt of Indra. [1173-a]
