• plakṣaḥ [plakṣyate kīṭaiḥ plakṣ-karmaṇi ghañ]
      1 {1} The Indian fig-tree; plakṣapraroha iva saudhatalaṃ bibheda R. 8. 93; 13. 71.
      2 One of the seven Dvīpas or continents of the world.
      3 A side or back-door, a private entrance.
      4 The space at the side of a door.
    Comp.
    • --jātā, --samudravācakā an epithet of the river Sarasvatī.
    • --tīrtha, prasravaṇaṃ, -rāj m. the place where the Sarasvatī rises. [0781-b]
