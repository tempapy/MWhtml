• jñāpaka a. [jñā --ṇic --lyu] Making known, teaching, informing, indicating &c. --kaḥ 1 A teacher. --2 A commander, a master. --3 A master of requests, an officer of the court of an Indian prince; Pt. 3. --kaṃ (In phil.) A significant expression, a suggestive rule or precept, said of such rules as imply something more than what is actually expressed by the words of those rules themselves. [0519-a]
