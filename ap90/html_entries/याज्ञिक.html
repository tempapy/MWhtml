• yājñika a. (kī f.) [yajñāya hitaṃ, yajñaḥ prayojanamasya bā ṭhak] Belonging to a sacrifice.
    • --kaḥ
      1 A sacrificer or a sacrificing priest.
      2 A ritualist.
      3 The Kuśa grass.
      4 N. of several trees aśvattha, khadira, palāśa, &c.
    Comp.
    • --āśrayaḥ N. of Viṣṇu. [0891-c]
