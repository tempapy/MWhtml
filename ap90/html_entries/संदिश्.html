• saṃdiś {c6c} P. 1 To give, grant, assign, make over; Bk. 6. 141; Y. 2. 232. --2 To order, direct, instruct, advise, send as a message; kiṃ nu khalu duṣyaṃtasya yuktarūpamasmābhiḥ saṃdeṣṭavyaṃ Ś. 4; Śi. 9. 56, 61. --3 To send as a messenger, entrust with a message; atha viśvātmane gaurī saṃdideśa mithaḥ sakhīṃ Ku. 6. 1. --4 To appoint. --5 To commission or depute in general.
