• dhīratā 1 Fortitude, courage, strength of mind; vipattau ca mahāl̐loke dhīratāmanugacchati H. 3. 44; V. 2. --2 Suppression of jealousy &c. --3 Gravity, solemnity (as shown by silence &c.); pratyādeśānna khalu bhavato dhīratāṃ kalpayāmi Me. 114. --4 Steadiness, firmness. --5 Wisdom, cleverness. --6 Refusal. (For other meanings see dhairya). [0598-a]
