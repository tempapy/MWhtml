mākṣi(kṣī)ka a. (kī f.) Coming or derived from a bee.
      --kaṃ [bhakṣikābhiḥ saṃbhṛtya kṛtaṃ aṇ]
      1 Honey; Bv. 4. 43.
      2 A kind of honey-like mineral substance. Comp. --āśrayaṃ {1} bees'-wax. {2} honey-comb.
      --jaṃ bees'-wax.
      --phalaḥ a kind of cocoa-nut.
      --śarkarā candied sugar. [0856-c]
