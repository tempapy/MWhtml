• saṃkalita p. p.
      1 Heaped up, piled up, collected.
      2 Blended together, intermixed.
      3 Seized, laid hold of.
      4 Added.
      5 Resumed, regained; Māl. 3. --tā (In arith.) The first sum.
    • --taṃ Addition.
    Comp.
    • --aikyaṃ the sum of the terms of an arithmetical progression. [1076-c]
