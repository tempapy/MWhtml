• hṛṣṭa p. p. [hṛṣ-kta] Pleased, rejoiced (= hṛṣita).
    Comp.
    • --citta, --mānasa a. rejoiced in mind, glad at heart, happy.
    • --roman a. having the hair on the body bristling or thrilling (with joy).
    • --vadana a. having a cheerful countenance.
    • --saṃkalpa a. contented, pleased.
    • --hṛdaya a. joyoushearted, cheerful, merry. [1175-a]
