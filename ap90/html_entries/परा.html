parā ind. A prefix to verbs and nouns in the sense of ‘away, back, in an inverted order, aside, towards.’ According to G. M. the senses of parā are: 1. killing, injuring &c. (parāhata). 2. going (parāgata). 3. seeing, encountering (parādṛṣṭa). 4. prowess (parākrāṃta). 5. direction towards (parāvṛtta). 6. excess (parājita). 7. dependence (parādhīna). 8. liberation (parākṛta). 9. inverted order, backwards (parāṅmukha). 10 setting aside, disregarding.
