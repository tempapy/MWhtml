sthāpanaṃ [sthā-ṇic-lyuṭ]
      1 (a) The act of causing to stand, fixing, founding, establishing, instituting. (b) Regulating, directing.
      2 Fixing the thoughts, concentration of the mind, steady application, abstraction.
      3 A dwelling, habitation.
      4 A ceremony performed on a woman's perceiving the first signs of living conception; cf. puṃsavana. [1148-c]
