• paribhū {c1c} P. 1 To defeat, subdue, conquer, overcome; (hence) to surpass, excel; lagnadvi phaṃ paribhūya padmaṃ Ku. 7. 16; R. 10. 35. --2 To despise, slight, treat with contempt, disrespect, insult; mā māṃ mahātman paribhraḥ Bk. 1. 22; 4. 37. --3 To injure, destroy, ruin. --4 To afflict, grieve. --5 To humiliate, disgrace. --6 To disappear. --7 Ved. To surround, encircle. --8 To go or fly round. --9 To accompany. --10 To take care of. --11 To guide, govern. --Caus. 1 To think of, reflect on, meditate, contemplate; U. 7. 20. --2 To contain, include. --3 To make known.
