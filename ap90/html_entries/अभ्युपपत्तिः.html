abhyupapattiḥ f.
      1 Approaching to assist, taking pity or compassion on, favouring; a favour, kindness; abhyupapattyā asyā jīvitamavalaṃbasva Ś. 3; anayābhyupapattyā Ś. 4; mama° nimittaṃ Mk. 1.
      2 Consolation.
      3 Protection, defence; brāhmaṇābhyupapattau ca śapathe nāsti pātakaṃ Ms. 8. 112, 349; 10. 62; ārta° Dk. 39.
      4 An agreement, assent, promise.
      5 Impregnation of a woman (especially of a brother's widow as an act of duty). [0147-c]
