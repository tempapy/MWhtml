• smārta a. [smṛtau vihitaḥ, smṛtiṃ vettyadhīte vā aṇ] 1 Relating to memory. remembered, memorial. --2 Being within memory. --3 Based on or recorded in a Smṛti, prescribed in a code of laws; karma smārtavivāhāgnau kurvīta pratyahaṃ gṛhī Y. 1. 97; Ms. 1. 108. --4 Legal. --5 Following or professing the law-books. --6 Domestic (as fire). --rtaḥ 1 A Brāhmaṇa well-versed in traditional law. --2 One who follows the traditional law. --3 N. of a sect. --rtaṃ An act or rite enjoined by the Smṛti, a legal act.
