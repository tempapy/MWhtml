• kṛpaṇa a. [kṛp kyun na latvam] 1 Poor, pitiable, wretched, helpless; rājannapatyaṃ rāmaste pālyāśca kṛpaṇāḥ prajāḥ U. 4. 25. --2 Void of judgment, unable or unwilling to discriminate or to do a thing; kāmārtā hi prakṛtikṛpaṇāścetanācetaneṣu Me. 5; so jarājīṇaiśvaryagrasanagahanākṣepakṛpaṇaḥ Bh. 3. 17. --3 Low, mean, vile; Bg. 2. 49; Mu. 2. 18; Bh. 2. 49. --4 Miserly, stingy. --5 Avaricious. --ṇaṃ Wretchedness. --ṇaḥ 1 A worm. --2 A miser; kṛpaṇena samo dātā bhuvi ko'pi na vidyate . anaśnanneva vittāni yaḥ parebhyaḥ prayacchati Vyāsa. --Comp. --dhī, --buddhi a. little or low-minded. --vatsala a. kind to the poor.
