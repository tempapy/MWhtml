• anūkaḥ --kaṃ [anu-uc samavāye kanipātaḥ kutvaṃ; or fr. aṃc with anu] 1 The back bone, spine (vaṃkrayādhāraḥ āyataḥ pṛṣṭhāsthiviśeṣaḥ); sadaṃ cānūkaṃ ca gṛhapateḥ Ait. Br. (where Sāy. remarks anūkaṃ mūtravastiḥ syāt sāsnetyeke vadaṃti ca). --2 A kind of sacrificial vessel; according to some, the back part of the altar; ayugmāgaṇamadhyamānūke. --3 Former birth or state of existence. --kaṃ 1 Family, race. --2 Disposition, temperament; character, peculiarity of race; vyādharkṣanakulānūkaiḥ paittikā hi narāḥ smṛtāḥ Suśr.
