sīraḥ [si-rak pṛṣo° Uṇ. 2. 25]
      1 A plough; sadyaḥ sīrotkaṣaṇasurabhi kṣetramāruhya mālaṃ Me. 16.
      2 The sun.
      3 The Arka plant.
    Comp.
      --dhvajaḥ an epithet of Janaka.
      --pāṇiḥ, --bhṛt m. epithets of Balarāma.
      --yogaḥ the yoking of cattle to a plough, or a team so yoked. [1125-c]
