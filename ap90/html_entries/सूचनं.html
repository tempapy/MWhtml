• sūcanaṃ --nā [sūc-bhāve lyuṭ] 1 The act of piercing or perforating, boring, perforation. --2 Pointing out, indication, intimation. --3 Informing against, betraying, calumniating, traducing. --4 Gesticulation, indicating by proper sings or gestures. --5 Hinting, hint. --6 Information. --7 Teaching, showing, describing. --8 Spying out, spying, seeing, ascertaining. --9 Villainy, wickedness. --10 Hurting, killing.
