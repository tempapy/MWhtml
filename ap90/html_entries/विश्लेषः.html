• viśleṣaḥ 1 Disunion, disjunction. --2 Especially separation of lovers, or of husband and wife. --3 Separation (in general); tanayāviśleṣaduḥkhaiḥ Ś. 4. 5; caraṇāraviṃdaviśleṣa R. 13. 23. --4 Absence, loss, bereavement. --5 A chasm. --6 (In arith.) The converse of addition. --Comp. --jātiḥ f. the reduction of fractional difference. [0996-c]
