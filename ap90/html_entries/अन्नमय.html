annamaya a. (yī f.) Consisting or made of food, composed of or containing boiled rice; °kośaḥ-ṣaḥ the gross material body, the sthūlaśarīra, which is sustained by food and which is the fifth or last vesture or wrapper of the soul; see anna (2) above and also kośa; hence, also the material world, the coarsest or lowest form in which Brahma is considered as manifesting itself in the worldly existence.
      --yaṃ Plenty of food. [0098-a]
