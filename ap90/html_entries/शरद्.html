• śarad f. [śṝ-adi Uṇ. 1. 129] 1 The autumn, autumnal season (comprising the two months āśvina and kārtika); yātrāyai codayāmāsa taṃ śakteḥ prathamaṃ śarad R. 4. 24. --2 A year; tvaṃ jīva śaradaḥ śataṃ; R. 10. 1; U. 1. 15; dhāriṇībhūtadhāriṇyārbhava bhartā śaracchataṃ M. 1. 15. --Comp. --aṃtaḥ the end of autumn, winter. --aṃbudharaḥ an autumnal cloud. --udāśayaḥ an autumnal lake. --kāmin m. a dog. --kālaḥ the autumnal season. --ghanaḥ, --meghaḥ an autumnal cloud. --caṃdraḥ (śaraccaṃdraḥ) the autumnal moon --triyāmā an autumnal night --padmaḥ, --dmaṃ the white lotus. --parvan n. the festival called Kojāgara; q. v. --mukhaṃ the commencement of autumn.
