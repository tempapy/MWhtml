• pīyūṣaḥ --ṣaṃ [pīy --ūṣan, Uṇ. 4. 76]
      1 Nectar, ambrosia; manasi vacasi kāye puṇyapīyūṣapūrṇāḥ Bh. 2. 78; imāṃ pīyūṣalaharīṃ G. L. 53.
      2 Milk in general.
      3 The milk of a cow during the first seven days after calving.
      4 The first milk given by a cow after calving.
    Comp.
