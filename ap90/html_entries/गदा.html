gadā [gad-ac ṭāp] A mace, club; saṃcūrṇayāmi gadayā na suyodhanorū Ve. 1. 15.
    Comp.
      --agrapāṇi a. having a mace in the right hand.
      --dharaḥ an epithet of Viṣṇu.
      --bhṛt a. a clubbearer, one who fights with a mace. (--m.) an epithet of Viṣṇu.
      --yuddhaṃ a fight with clubs.
      --hasta a. armed with a club. [0449-b]
