• akara a. [na. ba.]
      1 Handless, maimed.
      2 Exempt from tax or duty.
      3 [na. ta.] Not doing or acting; not disposed to work, ceasing from work.
    • --rā N. of a plant āmalakī, Emblic Myrobalan, Phyllanthus Emblica (akaṃ duḥkhaṃ sevanāt lokānāṃ rāti gṛhṇāti nāśayatīti; rā-ka Tv.) [0003-a]
