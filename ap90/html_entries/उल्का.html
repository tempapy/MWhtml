ulkā (cf. Uṇ. 3. 42)
      1 A fiery phenomenon in the sky, a meteor; virarāja kācana samaṃ maholkayā Śi. 15. 92; Ms. 1. 38, 4. 103; Y. 1. 145.
      2 A fire-brand, torch; na hi tāpayituṃ śakyaṃ sāgarāṃbhastṛṇolkayā H. 1. 86.
      3 Fire, flame; Me. 53.
    Comp.
      --dhārin a. a torch-bearer.
      --pātaḥ the fall of a meteor.
      --mālin m. N. of one of Śiva's attendants.
      --mukhaḥ a demon or goblin (having a mouth of fire); Ms. 12. 71; Māl. 5. 13. [0342-a]
