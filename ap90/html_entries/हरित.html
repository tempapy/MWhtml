harita a. (tā or hariṇī f.) [hṛ-itac]
      1 Green, of a green colour, verdant; ramyāṃtaraḥ kamalinīharitaiḥ sarobhiḥ Ś. 4. 10; Ku. 4. 14; Me. 21; Ki. 5. 38.
      2 Tawny.
      3 Dark-blue.
      --taḥ
      1 The green colour.
      2 A lion.
      3 A kind of grass.
    Comp.
      --aśman m. {1} an emerald. {2} blue vitriol.
      --chada a. green-leaved. [1166-c]
