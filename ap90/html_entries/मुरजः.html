• murajaḥ [murāt veṣṭanāt jāyate jaṇ-ḍa Tv.] 1 A kind of drum or tabor; sānaṃdaṃ naṃdihastāhatamurajarava &c. Māl. 1. 1; saṃgītāya prahatamurajāḥ Me. 64, 56; M. 1. 22; Ku. 6. 40. --2 A stanza artificially arranged in the form of a drum; also called murajabaṃdha, see K. P. 9. ad loc. --Comp. --phalaḥ the jackfruit tree. [0871-c]
