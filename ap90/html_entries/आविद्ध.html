    • 2 Curved, crooked, uneven; V. 4. 28; harṣāviddhamabhyutthitaḥ Dk. 37.
    • 3 Cast with force; dūranikṣepa° Māl. 8 cast forth in taking long strides; Mv. 2; Ms. 9. 40; thrown, put in motion.
    • 4 Disappointed.
    • 5 Fallacious, false.
    • 6 Stupid, foolish.
