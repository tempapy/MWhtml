aṃtarayaḥ --rāyaḥ
      1 An impediment, obstacle, hindrance, what stands in the way; sa cet tvamaṃtarāyo bhavasi cyuto vidhiḥ R. 3. 45, 14. 65; bahvaṃtarāyayuktasya dharmasya tvaritā gatiḥ Pt. 3. 101; asya te bāṇapathavartinaḥ kṛṣṇasārasya aṃtarāyau tapasvinau saṃvṛttau Ś. 1 vl. standing in the way.
      2 (In Vedānta) Hindrance to the concentration of mind which is said to be of four kinds, laya, vikṣepa, kaṣāya and rasāsvāda.
      3 An intervention, a covering, screen; dāhapremṇā sarasabisinīpatramātrāṃtarāyaḥ Māl. 3. 12.
      4 (With the Jainas) Interference or obstruction offered to those who are engaged in seeking deliverance, and consequent prevention of their accomplishment of it; one of the 8 classes of karman. [0093-c]
