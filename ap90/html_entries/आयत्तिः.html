• āyattiḥ f. [ā-yat-ktin] 1 Dependence, subjection. --2 Affection. --3 Strength, power, might. --4 Boundary, limit. --5 An expedient, remedy. --6 Majesty, dignity. --7 A day. --8 Steadiness of conduct, continuance in the right path. --9 Length. --10 Future time (These two senses should perhaps be referred to āyati q. v.).
