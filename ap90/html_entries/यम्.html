yam 1 P. (yacchati, yayāma, ayaṃsīt, yaṃsyati, yaṃtuṃ, yata; desid. yiyaṃsati)
      1 To check, curb, restrain, control, subdue, stop, suppress; yacchedbāṅmanasī prajñaḥ Kaṭh; yatacittātman Bg. 4. 21; see yata.
      2 To offer, give, bestow.
      3 Ved. To support, sustain.
      4 To raise, lift up.
      5 To extend, stretch.
      6 To go.
      7 To exhibit, show. --Caus. (yamayati-te) To restrain, check &c. [0888-c]
