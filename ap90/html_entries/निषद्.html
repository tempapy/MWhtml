niṣad 1 P. (niṣīdati &c.)
      1 To sit down, lie, recline; uṣṇāluḥ śiśire niṣīdati tarormūlālavāle śikhī V. 2. 23.
      2 To sink down, fail, be disappointed.
      3 To dwell.
      4 To suffer pain, be afflicted. [0639-c]
      niṣad f. Consecration for a sacrifice (yajñadīkṣā). [0639-c]
