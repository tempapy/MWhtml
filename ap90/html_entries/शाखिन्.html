• śākhin a. [śākhā astyasya iti]
      1 Having branches (fig. also).
      2 Branching, ramifying.
      3 Belonging to any branch or school (as of the Veda). --m.
      1 A tree; kulyāṃbhobhiḥ pavanacapalaiḥ śākhino dhautūlāḥ Ś. 1. 15.
      2 A Veda.
      3 A follower of any Vedic school or recension. [1039-a]
