• śaṃkhinī 1 A woman of one of the four classes into which writers on erotic science divide women; the Ratimañjarī thus describes her: --dīrghātidīrghanayanā varasuṃdarī yā kāmopabhogarasikā guṇaśīlayuktā . rekhātrayeṇa ca vibhūṣitakaṃṭhadeśā sabhogakelira saṃkā kila śaṃkhinī sā .. cf. citriṇī, hastinī, and padminī also. --2 A female spirit, or a kind of fairy. --Comp. --phalaḥ the Śirīṣa tree. [1030-b]
