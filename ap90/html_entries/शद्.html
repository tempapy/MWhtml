• śad
      I. 1 P. (but A. in conjugational tenses) śīyate, śanna
      1 To fall, perish, decay, wither.
      2 To go. --Caus. śādayati-te
      1 To cause to go, impel.
      2 śātayati-te (a) To fell, throw down, cut down; kiṃ bhūbhṛtkaṭakasthitivyasaninā vyarthaṃ khurāḥ śātitāḥ Subhāṣ.; Śi. 14. 80, 15. 24, (b) To kill, destroy.
      II. 1 P. ( śadati) To go (usually with ā). [1032-a]
