• nikāyaḥ [ni-ci-ghañ kutvam] 1 A heap, an assemblage, a class, multitude, flock, group in general; Mv. 1. --2 A congregation, school, an association of persons who perform like duties. --3 A house, habitation, dwelling-place; kāśīnikāyaḥ &c. --4 The body. --5 Aim, butt, mark. --6 The Supreme Being. --7 Ved. Air, wind. [0617-c]
