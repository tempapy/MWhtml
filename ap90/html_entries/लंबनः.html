• laṃbanaḥ [laṃb-lyu lyuṭ vā]
      1 An epithet of Śiva.
      2 The phlegmatic humour.
    • --naṃ
      1 Hanging down, depending, descending &c.
      2 Fringe.
      3 The parallax in longitude (of the moon).
      4 A sort of long necklace.
      5 A mode of fighting. [0924-c]
