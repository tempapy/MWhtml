• madana a. (nī f.) [mādyati anena, mad-karaṇe lyuṭ]
      1 Intoxicating, maddening.
      2 Delighting, exhilarating.
    • --naḥ
      1 The god of love, Cupid; vyāpārarodhi madanasya niṣevitavyaṃ Ś. 1. 27; hatamapi nihaṃtyeva madanaḥ Bh. 3. 18.
      2 Love, passion, sexual love, lust; vinayavāritavṛttiratastayā na vivṛto madano na ca saṃvṛtaḥ Ś. 2. 11; sataṃtrigītaṃ madanasya dīpakaṃ Rs. 1. 3, R. 5. 63; so madanātura, madanapīḍita &c.
      3 The spring season.
      4 A bee.
      5 Bees’-wax.
      6 A kind of embrace.
      7 The Dhattūra plant.
      8 The Khadira tree.
      9 The Bakula tree.
    • --nā, --nī
      1 Spirituous liquor.
      2 Musk.
      3 The atimukta creeper. (nī only in these two senses).
    • --naṃ
      1 Intoxicating.
      2 Gladdening, delighting.
