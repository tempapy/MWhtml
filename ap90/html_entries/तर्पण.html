• tarpaṇa a. [tṛp-ṇic vā lyuṭ] Satisfying, pleasing, refreshing. --ṇaṃ 1 Pleasing, satisfying. --2 Satisfaction, pleasure. --3 Satiety, fulness. --4 One of the five daily Yajñas (performed by men), presenting libations of water to the Manes of the deceased ancestors (pitṛyajña). --5 Fuel for the sacred fire. --6 Food. --7 Filling the eyes with oil &c. --Comp. --icchuḥ an epithet of Bhīṣma. [0544-c]
