adhyagni ind. [agnau agnisamīpe vā] Over, by or near the nuptial fire. --n. (gni) One of the six kinds of strīdhana (woman's property) mentioned in Ms. 9. 194; a gift made to a woman at the time of marriage: vivāhakāle yatstrībhyo dīyate hyagnisannidhau . tadadhyagnikṛtaṃ sadbhiḥ strīdhanaṃ parikīrtitam .. So adhyagnikṛtaṃ-adhyagnyupāgataṃ; pitṛmātṛpatibhrātṛdattamadhyagnyupāgatam . ādhivedanikādyaṃ ca strīdhanaṃ parikīrtitam. [0052-b]
