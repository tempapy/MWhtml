• maṃcaḥ [maṃc-ghañ] 1 A couch, bedstead, sofa, bed. --2 A raised seat, dais, a platform resting on columns, a seat of honour or state, throne; sa tatra maṃceṣu manojñaveṣān R. 6. 1, 3. 10. --3 An elevated shed in a field (for a watchman). --4 A pulpit. --5 A stage, platform. --Comp. --maṃḍapaḥ {1} a temporary shed resting upon bamboo posts. {2} a platform erected on festive occasions (as marriages &c.).
