patita p. p.
      1 Fallen, descended, alighted.
      2 Dropped.
      3 Fallen (in a moral sense), abandoned, wicked.
      4 A prostate.
      5 Degraded, outcast.
      6 Fallen in battle, defeated or overthrown.
      7 Being in, fallen into; as in avaṃśapatita.
      8 Placed, kept; Pt. 1. 14. --taṃ Flying.
    Comp.
      --utpanna a. sprung from an outcast.
      --sāvitrīkaḥ a man of the first three classes whose thread-ceremony has been improperly performed, or not performed at all. [0661-a]
