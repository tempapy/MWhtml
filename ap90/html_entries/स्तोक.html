• stoka a. [stuc-ghañ]
      1 Little, small; stokenonnatimāyāti stokenāyātyadhāgetiṃ Pt. 1. 150; stokaṃ mahadvā dhanaṃ Bh. 2. 49.
      2 Short.
      3 Few.
      4 Low, abject.
    • --kaḥ
      1 A small quantity, drop.
      2 The Cātaka bird.
    • --kaṃ ind. A little, less; paśyodagraplutatvādviyati bahutaraṃ stokamurvyāṃ prayāti Ś. 1. 7.
    Comp.
    • --kāya a. little-bodied, small, dwarfish, diminutive.
    • --namra a. a little bent down, slightly stooping or depressed; śroṇībhārādalasagamanā stokanamrā stanābhyāṃ Me. 82. [1145-c]
