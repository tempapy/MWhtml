• upaviś {c6c} P. 1 To sit down, take a seat. --2 To sit near to, wait upon. --3 To pitch a camp, encamp. --4 To go down, set (as the sun). --5 To enter upon, practise; as prāyaṃ upaviśati; so anaśanopaviṣṭa. --6 To abstain from food, fast; sopyuktvaivamupāviśat Bk. 7. 75. --7 To seize, take possession of. --Caus. To cause to sit down, place or set down. [0330-c]
