• varaṇaṃ [vṛ-lyuṭ lyu vā] 1 Choosing, selecting. --2 Begging, soliciting, requesting. --3 Surrounding, encircling. --4 Covering, screening, protecting. --5 The choice of a bride. --6 Worshipping (of priests &c.). --7 Keeping off, prohibiting, warding. --ṇaḥ 1 A rampart, surrounding wall. --2 A bridge. --3 The tree called Varuṇa. --4 A tree in general; iha siṃdhavaśca varaṇāvaraṇāḥ kariṇāṃ mude sanaladānaladāḥ Ki. 5. 25. --5 A camel. --6 A kind of ornament on a bow --7 N. of Indra. --Comp. --mālā, --sraj see varasraj.
