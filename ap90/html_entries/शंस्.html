• śaṃs {c1c} P. (śaṃsati, śasta; pass. śasyate) 1 To praise, extol, approve of; sādhu sādhviti bhūtāni śaśaṃsurmārutātmajaṃ Rām.; Bg. 5. 1. --2 To tell, relate, express, declare, communicate, announce, report (with dat. or sometimes gen. of person or by itself); śaśaṃsa sītā paridevanāṃtamanuṣṭhitaṃ śāsanamagrajāya R. 14. 83; na me hriyā śaṃsati kiṃcidīpsitaṃ 3. 5, 2. 68, 4. 72, 9. 77, 11. 84; Ku. 3. 60, 5. 51. --3 To indicate, bespeak, show; yaḥ (aśokaḥ) sāvajño mādhavaśrīniyoge puṣpaiḥ śaṃsatyādaraṃ tvatprayatne M. 5. 8; Ki. 5. 23, Ku. 2. 22. --4 To repeat, recite. --5 To hurt injure. --6 To revile, traduce.
