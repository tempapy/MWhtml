śīrṇa [śṝ-kta] p. p.
      1 Withered, decayed, rotten.
      2 Dry, sere.
      3 Shattered, torn; shivered.
      4 Thin, emaciated; (see śṝ).
      5 Small, slender.
      --rṇaṃ A kind of perfume.
    Comp.
      --aṃghriḥ, --pādaḥ {1} epithets of Yama. {2} of the planet Saturn.
      --parṇaṃ a withered leaf; (so śīrṇapatraṃ). (--rṇaḥ) the Nimba tree.
      --vṛtaṃ a water-melon. [1048-b]
