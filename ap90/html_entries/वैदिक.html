• vaidika a. (kī f.) [vedaṃ-ve ttyadhīte vā ṭhañ vedeṣu vihitaḥ ṭhak vā] 1 Derived from or conformable to the Vedas, Vedic. --2 Sacred, scriptural, holy; Ku. 5. 73. --kaḥ A Brāhmaṇa wellversed in the Vedas. --Comp. --pāśaḥ a smatterer in Veda, one possessing an imperfect knowledge of the Vedas.
