māṃsaṃ [man-sa dīrghaśca Uṇ. 3. 64]
      1 Flesh, meat; samāṃso madhuparkaḥ U. 4. (The word is thus fancifully derived in Ms. 5. 55: --pāṃ sa bhakṣayitā'putra yasya māṃsamihādmyaham . etanmāṃsasya māṃsatvaṃ pravadaṃti manīṣiṇaḥ ..).
      2 The flesh of fish.
      3 The fleshy part of a fruit.
      --saḥ
      1 A worm.
      2 N. of a mixed tribe, selling meat.
      3 A worm.
      4 Time.
