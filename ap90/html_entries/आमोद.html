• āmoda a. Gladdening, cheering up, delighting. --daḥ 1 Joy, pleasure, delight; āmodaṃ paramaṃ jagmuḥ Rām. --2 Fragrance (diffusive), perfume; āmodamupajighraṃtau svaniḥśvāsānukāriṇaṃ R. 1. 43; āmodaṃ kusumabhavaṃ mṛdeva dhatte mṛdgaṃdhaṃ na hi kusumāni dhārayaṃti Subhāṣ.; Śi. 2. 20; Me. 31. --3 Strong smell.
