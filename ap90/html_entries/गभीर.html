• gabhīra a. [gacchati jalamatra, gam īran bhāṃtādeśaśca Uṇ. 4. 35] 1 Deep (in all senses); u ttālāsta ime gabhīrapayasaḥ puṇyāḥ saritsaṃgamāḥ U. 2. 30; Bv. 2. 105. --2 Deepsounding (as a drum). --3 Thick, dense, impervious (as a forest). --4 Profound, sagacious. --5 Grave, serious, solemn, earnest. --6 Secret, mysterious. --7 Inscrutable, difficult to be perceived or understood. --Comp. --ātman the Supreme Soul. --vedha a. very penetrating.
