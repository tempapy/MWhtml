abhūmiḥ f.
      1 Non-earth, any thing but earth.
      2 An unfit place or object, no proper object for, beyond the reach or scope of; abhūmiriyaṃ mālavikāyāḥ M. 3; abhūmiriyamavinayasya Ś. 7; sa khalu manorathānāmapyabhūmirvisarjanāvasarasatkāraḥ ibid. far exceeded or transcended my (highest) expectations; Śi. 1. 42; Śānti. 4. 22, K. 45, 196, 204.
    Comp.
      --ja {1} produced in a bad or improper place. {2} not produced in earth. [0143-b]
