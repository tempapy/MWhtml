mīlita p. p. [mīl-kta]
      1 Shut, closed.
      2 Twinkled.
      3 Half-opened, unblown.
      4 Vanished, disappeared.
      5 Assembled, gathered (for milita).
      --taṃ (In Rhet.) A figure of speech in which the difference or distinction between two objects is shown to be completely obscured on account of their similarity, whether natural or artificial, in some respects; it is thus defined by Mammaṭa: --samena lakṣmaṇā vastu vastunā yannigṛhyate . nijenāgaṃtunā vāpi tanmīlitamiti smṛtaṃ .. K. P. 10. [0867-b]
