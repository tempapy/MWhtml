caṃc 1 P. (cacati, caṃcita)
      1 To move, wave, shake; samaraśirasi caṃca paṃcacūḍaścamūnāṃ U. 5. 3; Māl. 5. 23; caṃcaccacū Nāg. 4; caṃcatparāga Gīt. 1.
      2 To dangle about; vilapati hasati viṣīdati roditi caṃcati muṃcati tāpaṃ Gīt. 4.
      3 To leap, jump.
      4 To go, move. [0481-c]
