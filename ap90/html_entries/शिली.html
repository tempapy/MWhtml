śilī
      1 The lower timber of a door.
      2 A kind of earthworm.
      3 The top of a pillar.
      4 A dart.
      5 An arrow.
      6 A female frog.
    Comp.
      --mukhaḥ {1} a bee; militaśilīmukhapāṭalipaṭalakṛtasmaratūṇavilāse Gīt. 1.; R. 4. 57. {2} an arrow; sā kusumaghaṭitaśilīmukhamatoharānmadatacāpādiva pramadavanāstrasyati K. 225; or yugapadvikāśamudayādgamite śaśinaḥ śilīmukhagaṇo' labhata Śi. 9. 41 (in both passages the word is used in senses {1} and {2}). {3} a fool. {4} war, fight.
