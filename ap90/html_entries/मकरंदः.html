makaraṃdaḥ [makaramapi dyati kāmajanaṃkatvāt do-avakhaṃḍane ka pṛṣo° mum Tv.]
      1 The honey of flowers, flower-juice; makaraṃdatuṃdilānāmaraviṃdānāmayaṃ mahāmānyaḥ Bv. 1. 6, 8.
      2 A kind of jasmine.
      3 The cuckoo.
      4 A bee.
      5 A kind of fragrant mango tree.
      --daṃ A filament. [0830-b]
