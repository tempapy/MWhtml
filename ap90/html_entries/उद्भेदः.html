udbhedaḥ, --danaṃ
    • 1 Breaking through or out, becoming visible, appearance, display, manifestation, growth, development; kisalayodbhedapratidvaṃdvibhiḥ Ś. 4. 4; umāstanodbhedamanu pravṛddhaḥ Ku. 7. 24; taṃ yauvanodbhedaviśeṣakāṃtaṃ R. 5. 38; Śi. 18. 36; Mu. 5. 3.
    • 2 Breaking, splitting; prastarodbhadayogyaḥ U. 3. 25.
    • 3 A spring, fountain.
    • 4 Horripilation; as in pulakodbheda, romodbheda.
    • 5 Treason, betrayal. [0309-c]
