kṣepaṇaṃ [kṣip-lyuṭ]
      1 Throwing, casting, sending, directing &c.
      2 Spending (as time).
      3 Omitting.
      4 Abusing.
      5 A sling.
      --ṇiḥ, --ṇī f.
      1 An oar.
      2 A net for fishing.
      3 A sling or any instrument with which missiles are thrown. [0437-c]
