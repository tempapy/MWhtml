• saṃnah 4 U.
      1 To tie, bind, fasten.
      2 To wear, put on, dress.
      3 To put on (as armour), arm oneself, be accoutred; samanātsīttato sainyaṃ Bk. 15. 111, 112; 14. 7; 16. 4.
      4 To make oneself ready, prepare oneself (for any action) (Atm. in this sense); yuddhāya saṃnahyate Mbh.; chettuṃ vajramaṇīñ śirīṣakusumaprāṃtena saṃnahyate Bk. 2. 6. [1090-a]
