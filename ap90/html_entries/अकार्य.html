akārya a. [na. ta.] Improper, not fit to be done.
      --ryaṃ An improper, unworthy or bad act, a criminal or sinful action; mā nāma vaiklavyādakāryaṃ kuryāt Mk. 3 (ātmaghātādirūpaṃ).
    Comp.
      --kārin a. {1} an evil-doer, one who commits a misdeed; mahāpātakinaścaiva śeṣāścākāryakāriṇaḥ Ms. 11. 240. {2} one that neglects one's duty; dānenākāryakāriṇaḥ (śudhyaṃti) 5. 107. [0004-a]
