• saṃhitā 1 Combination, union, conjunction. --2 A collection, compilation, compendium. --3 Any systematically arranged collection of texts or verses. --4 A compendium or compilation of laws, code, digest; manusaṃhitā. --5 The continuous hymnical text of the Veda as formed out of the Padas or individual words by proper phonetic changes according to different Śākhās or schools; padaprakṛtiḥ sahitā Nir. --6 (In gram) Compination or junction of letters according to the rules of Saṃdhi or euphony; paraḥ saṃnikarṣaḥ saṃhitā P. I. 4. 109; varṇānāmatiśayitaḥ saṃnidhiḥ saṃhitāsaṃjñaḥ syāt Sk.; or varṇānāme kaprāṇayogaḥ saṃhitā. --7 The Supreme Being who hold and supports the universe. --Comp. --pāṭhaḥ the continuous text of the Veda (opp. padapāṭha q. v.). [1074-c]
