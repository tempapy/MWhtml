D.thus defines it: --varṇaiḥ śeṣaiḥ (i. e. mādhuryavyaṃjakaujaḥprakāṃśakābhyāṃ bhinnaiḥ) punardvayoḥ samastapaṃcaṣapado baṃdhaḥ pāṃcāliko mataḥ .. 628. [0690-c]
