• pāṃcālī
      1 A woman or princess of the Pañcālas.
      2 N. of Draupadī, the wife of the Pāṇḍavas.
      3 A doll, puppet.
      4 (In Rhet.) One of the four styles of composition. The S. D.thus defines it: --varṇaiḥ śeṣaiḥ (i. e. mādhuryavyaṃjakaujaḥprakāṃśakābhyāṃ bhinnaiḥ) punardvayoḥ samastapaṃcaṣapado baṃdhaḥ pāṃcāliko mataḥ .. 628. [0690-c]
