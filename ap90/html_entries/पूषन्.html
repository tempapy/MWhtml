pūṣan m. (nom. pūṣā, --ṣaṇau, --ṣaṇaḥ) [pūṣ-kanin; Uṇ. 1. 156] The sun; sadāpāṃthaḥ pūṣā gaganaparimāṇaṃ kalayati Bh. 2. 114; iṃdhanaughadhagapyagnistviṣā nātyeti pūṣaṇaṃ Śi. 2. 43. Comp. --asuhṛd m. an epithet of Śiva. --ātmajaḥ {1} a cloud. {2} an epithet of Indra.
      --daṃtaharaḥ an epithet of Vīrabhadra; see adaṃta.
      --bhāsā the city of Indra. [0717-c]
