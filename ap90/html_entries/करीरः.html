karīraḥ [kṝ-īran Uṇ. 4. 30]
      1 The shoot of a bamboo.
      2 A shoot in general; āninyire vaṃśakarīranīlaiḥ Śi. 4. 14; N. 5. 14.
      3 A thorny plant growing in deserts and eaten by camels; patraṃ naiba yadā karīraviṭape doṣo vasaṃtasya kiṃ Bh. 2. 93; cf. also kiṃ puṣpaiḥ kiṃ phalaistasya karīrasya durātmanaḥ . yena vṛddhiṃ samāsādya na kṛtaḥ patrasaṃgrahaḥ Subhāṣ.
      4 A waterjar.
