vilaṃgh 10 U.
      1 To pass or spring over, traverse; niveśayamāsa vilaṃghitādhvā R. 5. 42; 16. 32; Śi. 12. 24, 8. 26.

      3 To violate the limits of propriety; R. 9. 74.
      4 To rise towards, ascend or go up to; Ki. 5. 1; N. 5. 2.
      5 To give up, abandon, leave aside; mano babaṃdhānyarasān vilaṃghya sā R. 3. 4.
      6 To surpass, excel; iti karṇotpalaṃ prāyastava dṛṣṭyā vilaṃghyate Kāv. 2. 224.
      7 To cause to fast.
      8 To overcome, surmount.
      9 To offend, insult. [0990-a]
