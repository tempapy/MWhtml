uccāraṇaṃ
      1 Pronunciation, utterance; vācaḥ Śik. 2; veda°.
      2 Declaration, announcement, enunciation.
    Comp.
      --artha a. {1} useful for pronunciation. {2} necessary for pronunciation, such as a redundant letter only used to facilitate pronunciation.
      --jñaḥ a linguist.
      --sthānaṃ the part of the throat from which certain sounds (such as nasals, gutturals &c.) issue. [0287-a]
