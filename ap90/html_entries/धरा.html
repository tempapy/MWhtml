dharā [dharati viśvaṃ dhṛ-ac]
      1 The earth; dharā dhārāpātairmaṇimayaśarairbhidyata iva Mk. 5. 22.
      2 A vein.
      3 Marrow.
      4 The womb or uterus.
      5 A mass of gold or other valuables given as a present to Brāhmaṇas. Comp. --adhipaḥ a king.
      --amaraḥ, --devaḥ, --suraḥ a Brāhmaṇa.
      --ātmajaḥ, --putraḥ, --sūnuḥ {1} epithets of the planet Mars. {2} epithets of the demon Naraka.
      --ātmajā an epithet of Sītā. --uddhāraḥ deliverance of the earth.
      --dharaḥ {1} a mountain. {2} an epithet of Viṣṇu or Kṛṣṇa. {3} of Śeṣa.
      --patiḥ {1} a king. {2} an epithet of Viṣṇu.
      --bhuj m. a king.
      --bhṛt m. a mountain. [0592-a]
