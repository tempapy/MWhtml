• kṣatiḥ f. [kṣaṇ-ktin]
      1 Injury, wound.
      2 Destruction, cutting, tearing down; visrabdhaṃ kriyatāṃ varāhatatibhirmustākṣatiḥ palvale Ś. 2. 6.
      3 (Fig.) Ruin, loss, disadvantage; sukhaṃ saṃjāyate tebhyaḥ sarvebhyopīti kā kṣatiḥ S. D. 37.
      4 Decline, decay, diminution; pratāpakṣatiśītalāḥ Ku. 2. 24; H. 1. 114. [0434-b]
