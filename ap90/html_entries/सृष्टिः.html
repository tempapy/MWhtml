• sṛṣṭiḥ f. [sṛj-ktin] 1 Creation, anything created; kiṃ mānasī sṛṣṭiḥ Ś. 4; yā sṛṣṭiḥ sraṣṭurādyā Ś. 1. 1; strīratnasṛṣṭiraparā pratibhāti sā me Ś. 2. 9; sṛṣṭirādyeva dhātuḥ Me. 82. --2 The creation of the world. --3 Nature, natural property. --4 Letting loose, emission. --5 Giving away, a gift. --6 The existence of properties or qualities. --7 The absence of properties. --Comp. --kartṛ m. the creator.
