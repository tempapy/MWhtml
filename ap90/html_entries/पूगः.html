pūgaḥ [pū-gan kicc; Uṇ. 1. 121]
      1 {1} A multitude, heap, collection, quantity; Śi. 9. 64.
      2 An association, corporation, union; Y. 2. 30; Ms. 3. 151.
      3 The areca or betelnut-tree (pūgī also); R. 4. 44; 6. 64; 13. 17.
      4 Nature, property, disposition.
      --gaṃ Areca-nut, betelnut.
    Comp.
      --kṛta a. heaped, collected.
      --pātraṃ {1} a spitting-pot, spittoon. {2} a betel-box.
      --pīṭhaḥ-ṭhaṃ a spitting-pot.
      --puṣpikā betel-nut and flowers given to guests at a marriage ceremony.
      --phalaṃ the areeanut.
      --vairaṃ enmity against many men. [0714-c]
