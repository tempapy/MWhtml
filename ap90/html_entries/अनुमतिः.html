anumatiḥ f.
      1 Permission, consent, approval, assent.
      2 The 15th day of the moon's age on which she rises one digit less than full, when the gods and manes receive oblations with favour; personified as a goddess or worshipped in the Rājasūya sacrifice (kalāhīnacaṃdravatī śuklacaturdaśīyutapūrṇimātithiḥ); anumatyai haviraṣṭākapālaṃ puroḍāśa nirvapati Śat. Br.; Ms. 3. 86-87.
    Comp.
      --patraṃ a deed expressing assent. [0077-a]
