• jālma a. (lmī f.) 1 Cruel, severe, harsh. --2 Rash, inconsiderate. --lmaḥ (lmī f.) 1 A rogue, rascal, villain, wretch, miscreant; api jñāyate katamena digbhāgena ga taḥ sa jālma iti V. 1. --2 A poor man, a low or degraded man. --3 One who reads or recites badly; cf. P. VI. 2. 158. [0513-a]
