• pramṛj 2 P.
      1 To wipe off or out, cleanse (fig. also); svabhāvaloletyayaśaḥ pramṛṣṭaṃ R. 6. 41.
      2 To wipe off, away or out, blow out, efface; Mu. 1. 20.
      3 To remove, rid oneself of.
      4 To atone for, make amends for, expiate; paṇipātalaṃghataṃ prasārṣṭukāmā V. 3; sarvathā pramārjitaṃ tvayā pratyādeśaduḥkhaṃ Ś. 6.
      5 To stroke or rub gently.
      6 To prepare.
      7 To mark useless, frustrate. [0756-b]
