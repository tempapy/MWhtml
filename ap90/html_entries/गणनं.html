gaṇanaṃ [gaṇ-bhāve lyuṭ]
      1 Counting, calculation.
      2 Adding, enumerating.
      3 Considering supposing, regarding,
      4 Believing, thinking.
      5 Account.
      --nā Calculation, consideration, regard, account; kā vāgaṇanā sacetaneṣu apagatacetanānyapi saṃghaṭṭayitumalaṃ (madanaḥ) K. 157 (what need we say of &c. cf. kathā); Me. 10, 87; R. 11. 66; Śi. 16. 59; Amaru. 64.
    Comp.
      --gatiḥ f. = gaṇagati q. v. --patiḥ {1} an arithmetician. {2} an epithet of Gaṇeśa.
      --mahāmātraḥ a minister of finance. [0448-b]
