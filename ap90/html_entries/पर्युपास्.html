• paryupās {c2c} A. 1 To attend upon, worship, attend respectfully; paryupāsyaṃta lakṣyā R. 10. 62; Ku. 2. 38; Ms. 7. 37. --2 To go to (for protection), resort to, seek shelter or patronage with; aśaktā eva sarvatra naredraṃ paryupāsate Pt. 1. 241. --3 To enclose, surround. --4 To share in partake of. --5 To sit on. --6 To live round.
