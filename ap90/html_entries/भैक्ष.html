• bhaikṣa a. (kṣī f.) [mikṣaiva tatsamūho vā aṇ] Living on alms.
    • --kṣaṃ
      1 Begging, mendicancy; Ms. 6. 55; Y. 3. 42.
      2 Anything got by begging, alms, charity; bhaikṣeṇa vartayennityaṃ Ms. 2. 188; 4. 5.
    Comp.
    • --annaṃ alms, food obtained by begging.
    • --āśin a. eating food obtained by begging. (--m.) a beggar, mendicant.
    • --āhāraḥ a beggar.
    • --kālaḥ the time for begging.
    • --caraṇaṃ, --caryaṃ, --caryā going about begging, begging, collecting alms.
    • --jīvikā, --vṛttiḥ f. mendicancy. --bhuj m. a beggar, mendicant. [0826-a]
