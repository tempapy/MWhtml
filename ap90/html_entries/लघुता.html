• laghutā, --tvaṃ 1 Lightness, levity. --2 Smallness, littleness. --3 (a) Insignificance, unimportance, contempt, absence of dignity; iṃdro'pi laghutāṃ yāti svayaṃ prakhyāpitairguṇaiḥ. (b) Obscurity of birth, humbleness of origin. --4 Dishonour, disrespect; Pt. 1. 140, 353. --5 Activity, quickness. --6 Shortness, brevity. --7 Ease, facility. --8 Thoughtlessness, frivolity. --9 Wantonness.
