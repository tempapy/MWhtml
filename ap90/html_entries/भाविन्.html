• bhāvin a. [bhū-bhaviṣyati ṇini] 1 Being, becoming; bhṛtyabhāvi R. 11. 49. --2 To be or to come to pass in future, what will take place; lokena bhāvī pitureva tulyaḥ R. 18. 38; Me. 41. --3 Future; samatītaṃ ca bhavacca bhāvi ca R. 8. 78; pratyakṣā iva yadbhāvāḥ kriyaṃte bhūtabhāvinaḥ K. P. 10; N. 3. 11. --4 Capable of taking place --5 What must take place or is destined to happen, predestined; yadabhāvi na tadbhāvi bhāvi cenna tadanyathā H. 1. --6 Noble, beautiful, illustrious. --7 Attached or devoted to. --8 Possessed of (at the end of comp.). --m. N. given to every vowel except a and ā. --nī 1 A handsome woman. --2 A noble or virtuous lady; Ku. 5. 38. --3 A wanton woman. [0815-b]
