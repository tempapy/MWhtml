• madraḥ [mad-rak Uṇ. 2. 13] 1 N. of a country. --2 A ruler of that country. --drāḥ (pl.) The inhabitants of Madra. --draṃ Joy, happiness. (madrākṛ = bhadrakṛ ‘to shave or shear’). --Comp. --kāra a. (also madraṃkāra) producing delight. --sutā N. of Mādrī, second wife of Pāṇḍu. [0837-b]
