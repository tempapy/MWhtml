• maya a. (yī f.) An affix used to indicate ‘made of,’ ‘consisting or composed of,’ ‘full of’: kanakamaya, kāṣṭhamaya, tejomaya, jalamaya &c.
    • --yaḥ
      1 N. of a demon, the architect of the demons. (He built the ‘three cities’ for the demons; cf. tripura. He is also said to have built a splendid hall for the Pāṇḍavas).
      2 A horse.
      3 A camel.
      4 A mule.
    • --yī A mare. [0845-b]
