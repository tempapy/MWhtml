utkaṃṭha a. [unnataḥ kaṃṭho yasya]
    • 1 Having the neck uplifted, ready, on the point of (doing anything), in comp.; ājñāvacanotkaṃṭhaḥ Ś. 2; rathasvanotkaṃṭhamṛge vālmīkīye tapovane R. 15. 11.
    • 2 (Hence) Anxious. eager.
      , --ṭhā A mode of sexual enjoyment. [0290-b]
