• viplu {c1c} A. 1 To float about, swing to and fro, fluctuate. --2 To drift (in the sea), be scattered; yadi na syānnarapatiḥ samyaṅ netā tataḥ prajā . akarṇadhārā jaladhau viplaveteha nairiva H. 3. 2, Pt. 3. 73. --3 To be confused (as mind). --4 To be ruined or destroyed. --5 To fail. --Caus. 1 To cause to float or swim. --2 To divulge, spread abroad. --3 To teach (to unworthy persons); Ms. 11. 199. --4 To cause to fail, spoil, mar; guṇānāmāyathātarthyādarthaṃ viplāvayaṃti ye Śi. 2. 56. --5 To confound, bewilder.
