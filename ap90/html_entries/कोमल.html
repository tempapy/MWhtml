komala a. [ku-kalac muṭ ca ni° guṇaḥ; of. Uṇ. 1. 106]
      1 Tender, soft, delicate (fig. also); baṃdhurakomalāṃguliṃ (karaṃ) Ś. 6. 12; komalaviṭapānukāriṇau bāhū 1. 21; saṃpatsu mahatāṃ cittaṃ bhavatyutpalakomalaṃ Bh. 2. 66.
      2 (a) Soft, low; komalaṃ gītaṃ. (b) Agreeable, pleasing, sweet; re re kokila komalaiḥ kalaravaiḥ kiṃ tvaṃ vṛthā jalpasi Bh. 3. 100.
      3 Handsome, beautiful.
      --laṃ
      1 Water.
      2 Clay, earth. [0426-a]
