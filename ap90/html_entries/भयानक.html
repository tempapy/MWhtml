• bhayānaka a. [bibhetyasmāt, --bhī-ānak; Uṇ. 3. 82] Fearful, horrible, terrible, frightful; kimataḥ paraṃ bhayānakaṃ syāt U. 2; Śi. 17. 20; Bg. 11. 27. --kaḥ 1 A tiger. --2 N. of Rāhu. --3 The sentiment of terror, one of the eight or nine sentiments in poetry; see under rasa. --kaṃ Terror, fear.
