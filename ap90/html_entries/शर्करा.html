• śarkarā [śṝ-karan kasya netvam Uṇ. 4. 3] 1 Candied sugar. --2 A pebble, gravel, small stone; pādukāṃtarapraviṣṭiva śarkarā Mk. 5. --3 Gravelly mould. --4 Soil abounding in stony fragments, sand. --5 A piece, fragment. --6 A potsherd. --7 Any hard particle, as in jalaśarkarā a nodule of water, i. e. hail. --8 The disease called gravel. --Comp. --udaka sugar-water, water sweetened with sugar. --saptamī N. of an observance on the 7th day in the bright half of VaiŚākha.
