• picchaṃ [picch-ac] 1 A feather of a tail (as of a peacock). --2 The tail of a peacock; Śi. 4. 50. --3 The feathers of an arrow. --4 A wing. --5 A crest. --cchaḥ A tail in general. --cchā 1 A sheath, covering, coat. --2 The scum of boiled rice. --3 A row, line. --4 A heap, multitude. --5 The gum or exudation of the silk-cotton tree. --6 A plantain. --7 An armour. --8 The calf of the leg. --9 The venomous saliva of a snake. --10 A betel-nut. --11 A diseased affection of a horse's feet. --Comp. --bāṇaḥ a hawk.
