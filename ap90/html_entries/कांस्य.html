kāṃsya a. [kaṃsāya pānapātrāya hitaṃ kaṃsīyaṃ tasya vikāraḥ yañ chalopaḥ cf. P. IV. 3. 168] Made of white copper or bell-metal; Ms. 4. 65. --syaṃ
      1 Bell-metal or white copper; Ms. 5. 114; Y. 1. 190.
      2 A gong of bell-metal.
      --syaḥ, --syaṃ A drinking vessel (of brass), a goblet; Śi. 15. 81.
    Comp.
      --kāraḥ (rī f.) a brazier, a worker in bellmetal.
      --tālaḥ a cymbal.
      --bhājanaṃ a brass-vessel.
      --malaṃ verdigris. [0390-c]
