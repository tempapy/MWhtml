jaṃgama a. [gam-yaṅ ac]
      1 Moving, living, moveable (opp. immoveable sthāvara); citāgniriva jaṃgamaḥ R. 15. 16; śokāgniriva jaṃgamaḥ Mv. 5. 20; Ms. 1. 41.
      2 Derived from living beings.
      --maṃ A moveable thing; R. 2. 44.
    Comp.
      --itara a. immoveable.
      --kuṭī an umbrella. [0503-c]
