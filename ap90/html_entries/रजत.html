• rajata a. [raṃj atac nalopaḥ Uṇ. 3. 111]
      1 Silvery, made of silver.
      2 Whitish.
    • --taṃ
      1 Silver; śuktau rajatamidamiti jñānaṃ bhramaḥ; Ki. 5. 41; N. 22. 52.
      2 Gold.
      3 A pearl-ornament or necklace.
      4 Blood.
      5 Ivory.
      6 An asterism, a constellation.
      7 A mountain.
