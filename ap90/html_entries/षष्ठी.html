• ṣaṣṭhī 1 The sixth day of a lunar fortnight --2 The sixth or genitive case (in gram.). --3 An epithet of Durgā in the form of Kātyāyanī, one of the 16 divine mothers. --Comp. --tatpuruṣaḥ the genitive Tatpurusha compound, one in which, when dissolved, the first member usually stands in the genitive case. --pūjanaṃ, --pūjā worship of the goddess ṣaṣṭhī performed on the sixth day after a woman's delivery. [1066-a]
