• udgrāhaḥ
      1 Lifting or taking up.
      2 (In the Prātiśākhyas) The rule of Saṃdhi which causes the change of the terminations aḥ, e and o to a before a following vowel.
      3 Replying in argument; rejoinder.
      4 An objection.
    Comp.
    • --padavṛttiḥ f. the Saṃdhi called Udgrāha; see above. [0306-b]
