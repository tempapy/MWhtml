• chadman n. [chādyate svarūpamanena; chad-manin Uṇ. 4. 144] 1 A deceptive dress, a disguise. --2 A plea, pretext, guise; brahmachadmā sāmarthyasāraḥ Mv. 2. 25; palitachadmanā jarā R. 12. 2; Śi. 2. 21; kuto'nyathā sravatyeṣa svedacchadmā mṛtadravaḥ Ratn. 2. 17. --3 Fraud, dishonesty, trick; chadmanā paridadāmi mṛtyave U. 1. 46; Ms. 4. 199; 9. 72. --4 The thatch or roof of a house. --Comp. --tāpasaḥ a religious hypocrite. --rūpeṇa ind. incognito, in disguise. --veśin m. a player, a cheat, one dressed in disguise.
