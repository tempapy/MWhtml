• cāṇūraḥ A celebrated wrestler in the service of Kaṃsa. When Kṛṣṇa was taken by Akrūra to Mathurā, Kaṃsa sent this redoubtable wrestler to fight with him; but in the duel which ensued, Kṛṣṇa whirled him round and round several times and smashed his head. --Comp. --mardanaḥ, --sūdanaḥ N. of Kṛṣṇa.
