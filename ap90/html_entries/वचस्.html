• vacas n.
      1 A speech, word, sentence; uvāca dhātryā prathamoditaṃ vacaḥ R. 3. 25, 47; ityavyabhicāri tadvacaḥ Ku. 5. 36; vacastatra prayoktavyaṃ yatrokta labhate phalaṃ Subhāṣ.
      2 A command, order, precept, injunction.
      3 Advice, counsel.
      4 A hymn.
      5 Singing, a song of birds.
      6 (In gram.) Number.
    Comp.
    • --kara a. {1} obedient, compliant. {2} executing the orders of another.
    • --kramaḥ discourse.
    • --grahaḥ the ear.
    • --pravṛttiḥ f. an attempt at speaking; avyaktavarṇaramaṇīyavacaḥ pravṛttīn Ś. 7. 17. [0938-c]
