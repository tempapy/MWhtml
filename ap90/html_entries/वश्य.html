• vaśya a. Pr. 18; oft. in comp.; (manaḥ) hṛdi vyavasthāpya samādhivaśyaṃ Ku. 3. 50. --śyaḥ A servant, dependant. --śyā An humble or obedient wife; yaṃ brāhmaṇamiyaṃ devī vāgvaśyevānuvartate U. 1. 2 (who has full command of language). --śyaṃ Cloves.
