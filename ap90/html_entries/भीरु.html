bhīru a. (ru or rū f.) [bhī-kru; cf. P. III. 2. 174]
      1 Timid, cowardly, fearful; kṣāṃtyā bhīruḥ H. 2. 26.
      2 Afraid of; (mostly in comp.); pāpa°, adharma°, pratijñābhaṃga° &c.
      --ruḥ
      1 A jackal.
      2 A tiger.
      3 A kind of sugarcane.
      --ru n. Silver. --f.
      1 A timid woman.
      2 A goat.
      3 A shadow.
      4 A centipede.
    Comp.
      --cetas m. a deer.
      --raṃdhraḥ an oven, a furnace. --sattva a. timid, fearful.
      --hṛdayaḥ a deer. [0818-b]
