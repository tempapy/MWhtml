prapātaḥ
      1 Going forth or away, departure.
      2 Falling down or into, a fall; manorathānāmataṭaprapātaḥ Ś. 6. 9; Ku. 6. 57.
      3 A sudden attack.
      4 A cascade, waterfall, the place over which water falls down; R. 2. 26.
      5 A bank, shore.
      6 A precipice, steep rock.
      7 Falling out or loss, as in keśaprapāta.
      8 Emission, discharge, efflux, as in vīryaprapāta.
      9 Throwing oneself down from a rock.
      10 A particular mode of flight.
    Comp.
      --aṃbu n. water falling from a rock. [0751-a]
