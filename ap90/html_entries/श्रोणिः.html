• śroṇiḥ --ṇī f. [śroṇ-in vā ṅīp]
      1 The hip or loins, the buttocks; śroṇībhārādalasagamanā Me. 82; śroṇībhārastyajati tanutāṃ K. P. 10.
      2 A road, way.
    Comp.
    • --taṭaḥ the slope of the hips.
    • --phalakaṃ {1} the broad hips. {2} the buttocks.
    • --biṃbaṃ {1} the round hips; V. 4. 18. {2} a waist-band.
    • --sūtraṃ {1} a string worn round the loins. {2} a sword-belt. [1061-c]
