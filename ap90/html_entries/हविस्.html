• havis n. [hūyate hu-karmaṇi asun] 1 An oblation or burnt offering in general; vahati vidhihutaṃ yā haviḥ Ś. 1. 1; Ms. 3. 87, 132; 5. 7, 6. 12. --2 Clarified butter. --3 Water. --4 N. of Śiva. --5 A sacrifice. --Comp. --aśanaṃ (haviraśanaṃ) devouring clarified butter or oblations. (--naḥ) fire. --gaṃdhā (haviṃrgadhā) the Śamī tree. --gehaṃ (havirgehaṃ) a house in which sacrificial oblations are offered. --bhuj m. (havirbhuj) fire; anvāsitamaruṃdhatyā svāhayeva havirbhujaṃ R. 1. 56, 10. 69, 13. 41; Ku. 5. 20, Śi. 1. 2; Kāv. 2. 168. --yajñaḥ ( haviryajñaḥ) a kind of sacrifice. --yājin (haviryājin) m. a priest.
