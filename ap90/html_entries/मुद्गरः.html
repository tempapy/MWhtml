• mudgaraḥ [mudaṃ girati gṝ-ac] 1 A hammer, mallet, as in mohamudgaraḥ (a small poem by Śaṅkarācārya); R. 12. 73. --2 A club, mace. --3 A staff for breaking clods of earth. --4 A kind of dumb-bell. --5 A bud. --6 A kind of jasmine (said to to be n. also in this sense). [0870-c]
