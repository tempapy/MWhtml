prati(tī)hāraḥ
      1 Striking back.
      2 A door, gate.
      3 A porter, doorkeeper.
      4 A juggler.
      5 Juggling, a juggling trick.
      6 (In gram.) The hard contact of the tongue with the edge of the teeth in pronouncing dental letters.
      --rī A female doorkeeper.
    Comp.
      --bhūmiḥ f. the threshold (of a house &c.); Ku. 3. 58. --rakṣī a female door-keeper; R. 6. 20. [0743-b]
