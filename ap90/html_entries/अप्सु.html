apsu a. [nāsti psu rūpaṃ yasya Nir.]
    • 1 Formless, shapeless.
    • 2 Not beautiful. Note --apsu forms the first member of several compounds.
      a god.
      a. aquatic; moving in waters.
      a. born in the waters or in the atmosphere. (--jāḥ) --yoniḥ {1} a horse. {2} a cane or reed.
      vanquishing the aerial Asuras.
      a. possessed of what is in water; not losing one's nature in water (as lightning); getting sufficient water. [0124-b]
