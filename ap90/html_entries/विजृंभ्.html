• vijṛṃbh {c1c} A. 1 To yawn, gape, open the mouth; vyajṛṃbhiṣata cāpare Bk. 15. 108; vijṛṃbhitamivāṃtarikṣeṇa Mk. 5. --2 To open, expand (as a flower) --3 To spread everywhere, pervade, fill; sukhaśravā maṃgalatūryanisvanāḥ… na kevalaṃ sadmani māgadhīpateḥ pathi vyajṛṃbhaṃta divaukasāmapi R. 3. 19; 12. 72; rajoṃdhakārasya vijṛṃbhitasya 7. 42. --4 To rise, appear, show, manifest oneself; tatkhalvidaṃ vijṛṃbhate Māl. 7. --5 To spring back (as a bow). --6 To display activity. --7 To become increased or developed.
