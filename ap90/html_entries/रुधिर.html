rudhira a. [rudh-kirac] Red, red coloured.
      --raṃ
      1 Blood.
      2 Saffron.
      --raḥ
      1 The red colour.
      2 The planet Mars.
    Comp.
      --aśanaḥ ‘a blood-eater’, a demon, an evil spirit.
      --ākhyaḥ a kind of precious stone.
      --ānanaṃ one of the five retrograding motions of Mars.
      --āmayaḥ hemorrhage, piles.
      --udgārin a. emitting blood.
      --pāyin m. a demon. [0915-b]
