• toraṇaḥ --ṇaṃ [tur-yuc ādhāre lyuṭ Tv.] 1 An arched doorway, a portal. --2 An outer door or gateway; gaṇo nṛpāṇāmatha toraṇād bahiḥ Śi. 12. 1; dūrāllakṣyaṃ surapatidhanuścāruṇā toraṇena Me. 75. --3 Any temporary and ornamental arch; Ku. 7. 3; R. 1. 41, 7. 4, 11. 5. --4 An elevated place near a bathing-place. --ṇaṃ The neck, throat. --ṇaḥ N. of Śiva.
