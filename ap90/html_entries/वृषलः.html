vṛṣalaḥ [vṛṣ-kalac]
      1 A Śūdra.
      2 A horse.
      3 Garlic.
      4 A sinner, wicked or irreligious man.
      5 An outcast.
      6 N. of Candragupta (particularly used by Cāṇakya, see inter alia Mu. acts 1, and 3).
      7 A man of one of the three highest classes who has lost his caste by the omission of prescribed duties. [1009-a]
