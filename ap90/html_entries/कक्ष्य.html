• kakṣya a. Ved. 1 Consisting of shrubs or dry grass. --2 Secret. --3 Filling the girth (Sāy.). --kṣyā 1 The girth of an elephant or horse. --2 A woman's girdle or zone; Ki. 7. 8; Śi. 10. 62. --3 Fingers. --4 The upper garment. --5 The border of a garment. --6 The inner apartment of a palace. --7 A wall, enclosure. --8 Similarity. --9 A shrub yielding the black and red berry that serves as a weight. --kṣyaṃ 1 The cup or receptacle of a balance. --2 A part of a carriage. --3 The hinder part. --Comp. --avekṣaka = kakṣāvekṣaka q. v.
