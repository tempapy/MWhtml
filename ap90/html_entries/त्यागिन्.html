• tyāgin a.
      1 Leaving, abandoning, giving up &c.
      2 Giving away, a donor.
      3 Heroic, brave.
      4 Liberal.
      5 Sacrificing.
      6 One who does not look to any reward or result from the performance of ceremonial rites; yastu karmaphalatyāgī sa tyāgītyabhidhīyate Bg. 18. 11. [0547-c]
