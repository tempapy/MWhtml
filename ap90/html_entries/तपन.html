• tapana a. (2) of Karṇa. (3) of Sugrīva. --ātmajā, --tanayā an epithet of the Yamunā and of the Godāvarī. --iṣṭaṃ copper. --upalaḥ, --maṇiḥ the sun-stone. --chadaḥ the sun-flower. [0530-b]
