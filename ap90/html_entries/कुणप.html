• kuṇapa a. (pī f.) [cf. Uṇ. 3. 143] Smelling like a dead body, stinking.
    • --paḥ, --paṃ A dead body, corpse; śāsanīyaḥ kuṇapabhojanaḥ V. 5. (a vulture); amedhyakuṇapāśī ca Ms. 12. 71; often used as a term of contempt with living beings.
    • --paḥ
      1 A spear.
      2 A foul smell, stench. [0408-b]
