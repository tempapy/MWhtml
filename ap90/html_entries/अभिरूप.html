abhirūpa a. [abhigato rūpaṃ]
    • 1 Corresponding with, conformable or suitable to, congruous; abhirūpamasyā vayaso valkalaṃ Ś. 1. v. l.
    • 2 Pleasing, delightful, handsome, charming, beautiful, well-formed; abhirūpeṇāpi svadārasaṃtuṣṭena K. 51; utkṛṣṭāyābhirūpāya varāya sadṛśāya ca (kanyāṃ dadyāt) Ms. 9. 88.
    • 3 Dear to, beloved or liked by, favourite; yo yaḥ pradeśaḥ sakhyā me'bhirūpaḥ Ś. 6.
    • 4 Learned, wise, enlightened; abhirūpabhūyiṣṭhā pariṣadiyaṃ Ś. 1; K. 78; Ms. 3. 144. --paḥ
    • 1 The moon.
    • 2 Śiva.
    • 3 Viṣṇu.
    • 4 Cupid,
      Comp. ‘having an agreeable husband,’ N. of a fast or rite performed to secure a good husband in the next world; Mk. 1. [0137-a]
