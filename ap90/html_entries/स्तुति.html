• stuti f. [stu-ktin] 1 Praise, eulogy, commendation, laudation, stutibhyo vyatiricyaṃte dūrāṇi caritāni te R. 10. 30. --2 A hymn of praise, panegyric; R. 4. 6. --3 Adulation; flattery, empty or false praise; bhūtārthavyāhṛtiḥ sā hi na stutiḥ paramoṣṭhinaḥ R. 10. 33. --4 N. of Durgā. --Comp. --gītaṃ a panegyric, hymn. --padaṃ an object of praise. --pāṭhakaḥ a panegyrist, an encomiast, a minstrel, bard, herald. --vādaḥ a laudatory speech, panegyric. --vrataḥ a bard.
