vyūḍha p. p.
      1 Expanded, developed, broad, wide; vyūḍhorasko vṛṣaskaṃdhaḥ R. 1. 13.
      2 Firm, compact.
      3 Placed in order, arranged, marshalled (as an army); Bg. 1. 3.
      4 Disarranged, placed out of order.
      5 Married.
      6 Large, great.
    Comp.
      --kaṃkaṭa a. mailed, clad in armour. [1025-b]
