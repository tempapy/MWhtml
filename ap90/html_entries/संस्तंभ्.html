• saṃstaṃbh {c5c} {c9c} P. (caus. also) 1 To stop, restrain, control; prayatnasaṃstaṃbhitavikriyāṇāṃ kathaṃcidīśā manasāṃ babhūvuḥ Ku. 3. 34. --2 To paralyze, benumb; Ku. 3. 73. --3 To take heart or courage, cheer up, compose, collect (oneself); devi saṃstaṃbhayātmānaṃ U. 4. --4 To make firm or immoveable; Bg. 3. 43. --5 To support, prop up. --6 To confirm, establish, corroborate.
