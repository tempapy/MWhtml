• lāṃgalaṃ [laṃg kalac pṛṣo° vṛddhiḥ]
      1 A plough.
      2 A plough-shaped beam or timber.
      3 The plam tree.
      4 The membrum virile.
      5 A kind of flower.
      6 A particular appearance of the moon.
      7 A kind of timber (used in building houses).
    Comp.
    • --grahaḥ a ploughman, peasant.
    • --daṃḍaḥ the pole of a plough.
    • --dhvajaḥ N. of Balarāma.
    • --paddhatiḥ f. a furrow.
    • --phālaḥ a ploughshare. [0927-a]
