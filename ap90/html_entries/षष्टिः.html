• ṣaṣṭiḥ f. Sixty: Ms. 3. 177; Y. 3. 84. °tama sixtieth.
    Comp.
    • --bhāgaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
    • --mattaḥ an elephant who has reached the age of sixty and is in rut at that time.
    • --yojanī f. a journey or extent of sixty Yojanas.
    • --saṃvatsaraḥ a period of sixty years.
    • --hāyanaḥ {1} an elephant (sixty years old). {2} a kind of rice. [1065-c]
