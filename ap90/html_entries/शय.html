• śaya a. (yā, --yī f.) [śī-ac] Lying down, sleeping (usually at the end of comp.); rātrijāgaraparo divāśayaḥ R. 19. 34; so uttānaśaya, pārśvaśaya, vṛkṣeśaya, bileśaya &c.
    • --yaḥ
      1 Sleep.
      2 A bed, couch.
      3 A hand.
      4 A snake, especially the boa.
      5 Abuse, imprecation, curse.
      6 A stake. [1034-c]
