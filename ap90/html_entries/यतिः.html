• yatiḥ f. [yam-ktin]
      1 Restraint, check, control.
      2 Stopping, ceasing, rest.
      3 Guidance.
      4 A pause in music.
      5 (In prosody) A cæsura; yatijihveṣṭaviśrāmasthānaṃ kavibhirucyate . sā vicchedavirāmādyaiḥ padairvācyā nijecchayā .. Cand. M. 1; mrabhnairyānāṃ trayeṇa trimuniyatiyutā sragdharā kīrtiteyam.
      6 A widow.
    • --tiḥ [yatate mokṣāya yat-in]
      1 An ascetic, one who has renounced the world and controlled his passions; yathā dānaṃ vigā hastī tathā jñānaṃ vinā yatiḥ Bv. 1. 119.
      2 N. of Viṣṇu. [0885-b]
