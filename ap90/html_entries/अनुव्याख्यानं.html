• anuvyākhyānaṃ [anurūpaṃ vyākhyānaṃ] That which comments on and explains Mantras, Sūtras &c. ( maṃtravivaraṇaṃ); especially, that portion of a Brāhmaṇa which explains difficult Sūtras, texts &c. occurring in another place ( maṃtrādīnāmanurūpārthaprakāśakaṃ vyākhyānaṃ.) [0082-a]
