• geya pot. p. [gai kartari ni° yat]
      1 A singer, one who sings; geyo māṇavakaḥ sāmnāṃ P. III. 4. 68 Sk.
      2 To be sung.
    • --yaṃ A song, singing, also the art of singing; geye kena vinītau vāṃ R. 15. 69; Me. 86; anaṃtā vāṅmayasyāho geyasyeva vicitratā Śi. 2. 72. [0467-a]
