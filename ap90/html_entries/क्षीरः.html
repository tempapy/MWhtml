kṣīraḥ, raṃ
      1 Milk; haṃso hi kṣīramādatte tanmiśrā varjayatyapaḥ Ś. 6. 27.
      2 The milky juice or sap of trees, exudation; resin; ye tatkṣīrasrutisurabhayo dakṣiṇena pravṛttāḥ Me. 107; Ku. 1. 9.
      3 Water.
    Comp.
