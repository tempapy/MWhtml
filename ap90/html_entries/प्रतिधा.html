pratidhā 3 U.
      1 To place or lay on.
      2 To return, restore.
      3 To fix (as an arrow).
      4 To offer, present.
      5 To use, employ.
      6 To begin, commence.
      7 To draw near, approach (as the night); (mostly Vedic in these senses). [0737-b]
