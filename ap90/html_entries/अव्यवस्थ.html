avyavastha a.
      1 Not fixed, moving, unstable; sthalāraviṃdaśriyamavyavasthāṃ Ku. 1. 33.
      2 Unlimited.
      3 Unsettled, indiscriminate; bhaṃgaṃ jayaṃ cāpaturavyavasthaṃ R. 7. 54; disorderly, irregular.
      --sthā
      1 Irregularity, deviation from established rule; kimavyavasthāṃ calito'pi keśavaḥ Śi. 12. 36.
      2 An incorrect opinion given on a point of religious or civil law. [0199-a]
