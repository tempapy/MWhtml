tāraka a. (rikā f.) [tṝ-ṇic ṇvula]
      1 Carrying over.
      2 Protecting, preserving, rescuing.
      3 Helping another through a difficulty.
      --kaḥ
      1 A pilot, helmsman.
      2 A deliverer, saviour.
      3 N. of Śiva.
      --kaḥ, --kaṃ A boat, raft.
      --kaṃ
      1 The pupil of the eye.
      2 The eye (also f.). [0545-c]
