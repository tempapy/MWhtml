• śramaṇa a. (ṇā --ṇī f.) [śram-yuc]
      1 Labouring, toiling.
      2 Low, base, vile.
    • --ṇaḥ
      1 An ascetic, a devotee, religious mendicant in general.
      2 A Buddhist ascetic.
      3 A beggar.
    • --ṇā-ṇī
      1 A female devotee or mendicant.
      2 A lovely woman.
      3 A woman of low caste.
      4 Bengal madder.
      5 The spikenard. [1058-c]
