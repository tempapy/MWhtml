• śamana a. nī f. [śam ṇic lyu lyuṭ vā] Quelling, allaying, subduing &c. --naṃ 1 Appeasing, allaying, soothing, conquering, alleviating, &c. --2 Calmness, tranquillity. --3 End, stop, cessation, destruction. --4 Hurting, injuring. --5 Killing animals for sacrifice, immolation. --6 Swallowing, chewing. --naḥ 1 A kind of deer, an antelope. --2 N of Yama, the god of death. --Comp. --svasṛ f. 'Yama's sister', epithet of the river Yamunā. [1034-a]
