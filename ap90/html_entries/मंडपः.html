• maṃḍapaḥ [maṃḍaṃ bhūṣāṃ pāti pā-ka, maṃḍ-kapan vā] 1 A temporary hall erected on ceremonial occasions, an open hall; vivāhamaṃḍapa. --2 A tent, pavilion; R. 5. 73. --3 An arbour, a bower, as in latāmaṃḍapa Me. 78. --4 A building consecrated to a deity. --Comp. --pratiṣṭhā the consecration of a temple.
