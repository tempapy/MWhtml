yāmya a. [yamo devatāsya tasyedaṃ vāṇya]
      1 Southern; dvāraṃ raraṃdhaturyāmyaṃ Bk. 14. 15.
      2 Belonging to or resembling Yama.
      --myaḥ
      1 A servant of Yama.
      2 N. of Agastya.
      3 Of Śiva.
      4 Of Viṣṇu.
      5 Sandal-wood.
      --myaṃ The Bharaṇī Nakṣatra.
    Comp.
      --ayanaṃ the winter solstice.
      --uttara a. going from south to north. [0893-a]
