ārta a. [ā-ṛ-kta]
    • 1 Afflicted with, struck by, suffering from, pained by; usually in comp., kāmārta, kṣudhārta tṛṣārta &c.
    • 2 Sick, diseased; ārtasya yathauṣadhaṃ R. 1. 28; Ms. 4. 236.
    • 3 Distressed, afflicted, struck by calamity, oppressed, unhappy; ārtatrāṇāya vaḥ śastraṃ na grahartumanāgasi Ś. 1. 11, R. 2. 28, 8. 31, 12. 10, 32.
    • 4 Perishable (vināśin).
      Comp. a cry of distress, --badhuḥ, --sādhuḥ a friend of the distressed. [0256-a]
