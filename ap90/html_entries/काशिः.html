• kāśiḥ, --śī f. N. of a celebrated city on the Ganges, the modern Benares and one of the seven sacred cities; see kāṃcī. --śiḥ 1 The clenched hand, fist. --2 A handful. --3 The sun. --4 Light, splendour. --Comp. --paḥ an epithet of Śiva. --rājaḥ N. of a king, father of aṃbā, aṃbikā and aṃbālikā, q. v.
