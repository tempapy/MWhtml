kāśiḥ, --śī f. N. of a celebrated city on the Ganges, the modern Benares and one of the seven sacred cities; see kāṃcī.
    • 1 The clenched hand, fist.
    • 2 A handful.
    • 3 The sun.
    • 4 Light, splendour.
      Comp. an epithet of Śiva.
      N. of a king, father of aṃbā, aṃbikā and aṃbālikā, q. v. [0401-b]
