vivikta p. p.
      1 Separated, detached, disjoined, abstracted.
      2 Lonely, solitary, retired, sequestered.
      3 Single, alone.
      4 Distinguished, discriminated.
      5 Judicious
      6 Pure, faultless; Ratn. 1. 21.
      7 Profound (as a judgment or thought).
      8 Intent on.
      9 Devoid of, free from; Ku. 1. 23. --ktaṃ
      1 A lonely or solitary place; viviktādṛte nānyadutsukasya śaraṇamasti V. 2, Ś. 5. 5, Śi. 8. 70.
      2 Loneliness, privacy, seclusion.
      --ktā An unlucky or ill-fated woman, one disliked by her husband ( durbhagā).
