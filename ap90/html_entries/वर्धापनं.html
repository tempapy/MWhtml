• vardhāpanaṃ [vardhaṃ chedaṃ karoti, vṛdh ṇic-āp ca tato bhāve lyuṭ] 1 Cutting, dividing. --2 Cutting the umbilical cord, or the ceremony connected with this act. --3 A festival on a birth-day. --4 Any festival in general when wishes for prosperity and other congratulatory expressions are offered; cf. pūrṇapātra.
