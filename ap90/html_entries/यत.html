yata p. p.
      1 [yam-kta] Restrained, curbed, controlled, subdued.
      2 Limited, moderate, see yam.
      --taṃ The spurring of an elephant by means of the rider's feet.
    Comp.
      --ātman a. governing oneself, self-restrained, curbing the senses; (tasmai) yatātmane rocayituṃ yatasva Ku. 3. 16, 1. 54. --āhāra a. moderate or temperate in eating, abstemious.
      --iṃdriya a. one who has restrained his senses or subdued his passions, pure, chaste.
      --citta, --manas, --mānasa a. subdued in mind.
      --vāc a. restraining one's speech, observing silence, reticent; see vāgyata.
      --vrata a. {1} observing vows. {2} keeping to one's engagements or promised observances. [0885-a]
