• tulasī [tulāṃ sādṛśyaṃ syati, so-ka gaurā° ṅīṣ śakaṃdhvā. Tv.] The holy basil held in veneration by the Hindus, especially by the worshippers of Viṣṇu. --Comp. --patraṃ (lit.) a Tulasī leaf; (fig.) a very small gift. --vivāhaḥ the marriage of an image of Bālakṛshṇa with the holy basil, performed on the 12th day of the bright half of Kārtika. --vṛṃdāvanaḥ --naṃ a square pedestal in which the sacred basil is planted. [0542-c]
