kaulika a. (kī f.) [kulādāgataḥ ṭhak]
      1 Belonging to a family.
      2 Customary in a family, ancestral.
      --kaḥ
      1 A weaver; kauliko viṣṇurūpeṇa rājakanyāṃ niṣevate Pt. 1. 202.
      2 An impostor, a heretic.
      3 A follower of the left hand Śākta ritual. [0428-b]
