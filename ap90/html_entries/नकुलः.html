• nakulaḥ 1 The mungoose, an ichneumon; yadayaṃ nakuladveṣī sakuladveṣī punaḥ piśunaḥ Vās. --2 N. of the fourth Pāṇḍava prince; ahaṃ tasya atiśayitadivyarūpiṇo nakulasya darśanenotsukā jātā Ve. 2 (where nakula has really sense 1, but is taken in sense 2 by Duryodhana). --3 A son. --4 An epithet of Śiva. --lī 1 A female mungoose. --2 Saffron.
