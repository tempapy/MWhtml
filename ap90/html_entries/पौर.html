• paura a. (rī f.) [pure vasati śaiṣike aṇ] 1 Relating to a city or town, produced in a town, civic. --2 Ved. Filling one's own belly. --raḥ 1 A townsman, citizen; (opp. jānapada); Ku. 6. 41; Me. 27; R. 2. 10, 74; 12. 3; 16. 9. --2 A term applied to a prince engaged in war under particular circumstances. --3 A planet in a state of opposition to other planets. --rī The language of the servants in a palace. --raṃ A sort of grass (rohiṣa). --Comp. --aṃganā, --yoṣit f., --strī a woman living in a town. --kāryaṃ public business; arthajātasya gaṇanābahulatayaikabheva paurakāryamavekṣitaṃ Ś. 6. --janaḥ, --lokaḥ {1} a citizen. {2} citizens, burghers. --jānapada a. belonging to town and country. (--dāḥ pl.) citizens and rustics, townsmen and country people; kathaṃ durjanāḥ paurajānapadāḥ U. 1. --vṛddhaḥ an eminent citizen, an elder man. --sakhyaṃ fellow-citizenship. [0722-a]
