• udgamanīya pot. p. To be gone up or ascended. --yaṃ A pair of bleached or washed clothes (tatsyādudgamanīyaṃ yaddhautayorvastrayoryugaṃ); dhautodgamanīyavāsinī Dk. 42; gṛhītapatyudgamanīyavastrā Ku. 7. 11 (where Malli. renders u° by dhautavastraṃ, and says yugagrahaṇaṃ tu prāyikābhiprāyaṃ &c. see ad loc.).
