anuttara a. [nāsti uttaro yasmāt]
    • 1 Principal, chief.
    • 2 Best, excellent; anuttarāṇi vilasitāni Dk. 162 unsurpassed.
    • 3 [nāsti uttaraṃ yasya] Without a reply, silent, unable to answer; bhavatyavajñā ca bhavatyanuttarāt Naiṣadha.
    • 4 Fixed, firm (na uttarati calati).
    • 5 Low, inferior, base, mean.
    • 6 Southern.
      [na. ta.] No reply, a reply which, being evasive, is considered to be no reply.
      (pl.) A class of gods among Jainas (°upapātika).
      The south. [0070-c]
