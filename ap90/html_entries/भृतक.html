• bhṛtaka a. [bhṛtaṃ bharaṇaṃ vetanamupajīvati kan] Hired, paid.
    • --kaḥ A hired servant.
    Comp.
    • --adhyāpakaḥ a hired teacher.
    • --adhyāpita a. taught by a paid teacher. (--taḥ) a student who pays his teacher for his labour (= ‘a paying student’ of the modern days); Ms. 3. 156. [0824-a]
