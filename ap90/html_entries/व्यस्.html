• vyas {c4c} P. 1 To toss about, scatter, cast or throw asunder; dispel, destroy; prāpto vyālatamān vyasyan bhujaṃgebhyo'pi rākṣasān Bk. 8. 116, 9. 31. --2 To divide into parts, separate, arrange; svayaṃ vedān vyasyan Pt. 4. 50; vivyāta vedān yasmātsa tasmād vyāsa iti smṛtaḥ Mb.; R. 10. 84. --3 To take separately or singly; see vyasta below. --4 To throw over, overturn, upset. --5 To expel, remove, drive away.
