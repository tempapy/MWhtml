vāsaka a. (kā or sikā f.) [ vāsvas-ṇic vā ṇvul]
      1 Scenting, perfuming, infusing, fumigating &c.
      2 Causing to dwell, populating.
      --kaṃ Clothes.
    Comp.
      --sajjā, --sajjikā a woman who dresses herself in all her ornaments and keeps herself (and her house) ready to receive her lover, especially when he has made an appointment with her; an expectant heroine, one of the several classes of a Nāyikā; S. D. thus defines her: --kuruta maṃḍanaṃ yāsyāḥ(yātu)sajjite vāsaveśmani . sā tu vāsakasajjā syādviditapriyasaṃgamā 120; bhavati vilaṃbini vigalitalajjā vilapati rediti vāsakasajjā Gīt. 6. [0963-a]
