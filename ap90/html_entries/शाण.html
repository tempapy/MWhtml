śāṇa a. (ṇī f.) [śaṇena nirvṛttaṃ aṇ] Hempen, flaxen.
      --ṇaḥ
      1 A touchstone; maṇiḥ śāṇo llīḍhaḥ Bh. 2. 44; Bv. 1. 73.
      2 A whetstone.
      3 A saw.
      4 A weight of four Māṣas.
      --ṇaṃ
      1 Sack-cloth, coarse cloth.
      2 A hempen garment; Ms. 2. 41, 10. 87.
    Comp.
      --ājīvaḥ an armourer. [1039-b]
