• utkala a. Excessive, piteous; K. 306. --laḥ 1 N. of a country, the modern Orissa, or the inhabitants of that country (pl.); jagannāthaprāṃtadeśa utkalaḥ parikīrtitaḥ; see āṃḍra; utkalādarśitapathaḥ R. 4. 38. --2 A fowler, bird-catcher. --3 A porter (carrying a load with him). --4 A sub-division of Brāhmaṇas. [0290-c]
  • utkala or oḍ N. of a country, the modern Orissa, which lay to the south of Tāmralipta, and extended to the river Kapiśā; cf. R. 4. 38. The chief to was of this province are Cuttak and Purī where the celebrated temple of Jagannātha is situated. [1193-a]
