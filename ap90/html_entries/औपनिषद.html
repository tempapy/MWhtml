aupaniṣada a. (dī f.) [upaniṣad-aṇ]
    • 1 Contained or taught in an Upaniṣad; scriptural, theological.
    • 2 Based or founded on, derived from, the Upaniṣads; aupaniṣadaṃ darśanaṃ (another name for Vedānta phil.) --daḥ
    • 1 The supreme soul, Brahman.
    • 2 A follower of the doctrines of the Upaniṣads. [0363-b]
