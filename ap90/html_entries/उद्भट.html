• udbhaṭa a. 1 Excellent, pre-eminent; pade pade saṃti bhaṭā raṇodbhaṭāḥ N. 1. 132. --2 Exalted, magnanimous. --ṭaḥ 1 A fan for winnowing corn. --2 A tortoise. [0309-b]
  • udbhaṭa One of the earliest writers on Alaṃkāra. He was the chief Paṇḍita or Sabhāpati of king Jayāpīda of Kāshmir (779-813 A. D.). [1191-a]
