• muṃjaḥ 1 A sort of rush or grass (of which the girdle of a Brāhmaṇa should be made); Ms. 2. 43. --2 The sacred cord or girdle itself. --3 N. of a king of Dhārā (said to be the uncle of the celebrated Bhoja). --Comp. --keśaḥ {1} an epithet of Śiva. {2} of Viṣṇu. --keśin m. an epithet of Viṣṇu. --baṃdhanaṃ investiture with the sacred thread (or girdle). --mekhalin m. {1} N. of Śiva. {2} of Viṣṇu. --vāsas m. an epithet of Śiva.
