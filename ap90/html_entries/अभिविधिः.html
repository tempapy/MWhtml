abhividhiḥ [abhito vidhirvyāptiḥ]
      1 Complete comprehension or inclusion; one of the senses of the particle ā; āṅ maryādābhividhyoḥ P. II. 1. 13, the limit inceptive as opposed to the limit conclusive and translated by ‘from,’ ‘commencing with’, ‘including’; as in ābālaṃ-ābālebhyaḥ haribhaktiḥ (maryādā and abhividhi are thus distinguished tena vinā maryādā, tatsahito'bhividhiḥ).
      2 Complete pervasion, filling up or occupying completely; abhividhau bhāva inuṇ P. III. 3. 44; also V. 4. 53 and Sk. thereon. [0138-a]
