• varṇ {c10c} U. (varṇayati-te, varṇita) 1 To colour, paint, dye; yathā hi bharatā varṇairvarṇayaṃtyātmanastanuṃ Subhāṣ. --2 To describe, relate, explain, write, depict delineate, illustrate; varṇitaṃ jayadevena haroridaṃ praṇatena Gīt. 3; Ki. 5. 18. --3 To praise; extol. --4 To spread, extend. --5 To illuminate. --6 To exert oneself. --7 To send, cast. --8 To pound, grind.
