dyo f. (Nom. sing. dyauḥ) Heaven, paradise, the sky; dyaurbhūmirāpo hṛdayaṃ yamaṃśca Pt. 1. 182; Ś. 2. 14. (In Dvandva compounds dyo is changed to dyāvā, e. g. dyāvāpṛthivyau, dyāvābhūmī, dyāva kṣame ‘heaven and earth’).
    Comp.
      --bhūmiḥ a bird.
      --sad m. (dyoṣad) a god. [0584-a]
