• kāpāla, kāpālika a. [kapāla-aṇ-ṭhak-vā] Relating to skulls. --laḥ, --likaḥ A follower of a certain Śaiva sect (the left-hand order) characterized by carrying skulls of men in the form of garlands and eating and drinking from them; Pt. 1. 212. --laṃ A kind of leprosy. --lī 1 A wreath of skulls. --2 A clever woman.
