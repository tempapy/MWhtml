kāpāla, kāpālika a. [kapāla-aṇ-ṭhak-vā] Relating to skulls.
      , --likaḥ A follower of a certain Śaiva sect (the left-hand order) characterized by carrying skulls of men in the form of garlands and eating and drinking from them; Pt. 1. 212. --laṃ A kind of leprosy.
    • 1 A wreath of skulls.
    • 2 A clever woman. [0393-c]
