• abhiman {c4c} A. (P. epic) 1 To wish or desire, long for, covet, like; na tvevaṃ jyāyasīṃ vṛttimabhimanyeta karhicit Ms. 10. 95; see abhimata below. --2 To assent to, approve of, allow, admit; give a grant (to any one, dat.). --3 To think, fancy, imagine, consider, believe, regard; tāmāryāmasāvanyathā'bhyamanyata Dk. 164; Bk. 5. 71, U. 5. --4 To injure, threaten (Ved.) --5 ({c8c} A.) To think of self. [0134-b]
