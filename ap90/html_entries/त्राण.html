      trāṇa p. p. [trai karmaṇi kta, bhāve lyuṭ vā] Protected, guarded, preserved, saved.
      --ṇaṃ
      1 Protection, defence, preservation; ārtatrāṇāya vaḥ śastraṃ na prahartumanāgasi Ś. 1. 11; R. 15. 3; Māl. 9. 26.
      2 Shelter, help, refuge.
      3 Protecting, preserving.
      4 An armour. [0551-c]
