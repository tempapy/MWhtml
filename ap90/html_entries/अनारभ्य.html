• anārabhya a. Unfit to be commenced or undertaken --ind. Without commencing; without reference to any particular thing; e. g. °vādaḥ detached remark (upon sacrifices &c); kiṃcitkarmārabhya udyate ucyate ityārabhyavādaḥ na ārabhyavādaḥ. --Comp. --adhīta a. [na ārabhya kiṃcidadhītaḥ] studied or taught or read without reference to any particular subject (not as part of a regular or authoritative work); learnt as a detached subject; yeṣāṃ maṃtrāṇāṃ karmaviśeṣe viniyogo noktaḥ teṣāṃ maṃtrāṇāṃ anārabhyādhītatvāt brahmayajñe eva viniyoga iti mīmāṃsā.
