• pradānaṃ
      1 Giving, granting, bestowing, offering; vara°, agni°, kāṣṭha° &c.
      2 Giving away in marriage; vaikhānasaṃ kimanayā vratamāpradānādvyāpārarodhi bhadanasya niṣovetavyaṃ Ś. 1. 27.
      3 Imparting, instructing; vidyā°.
      4 A gift, donation, present.
      5 A goad.
      6 An oblation.
    Comp.
    • --śūraḥ a very munificent man, donor. [0748-b]
