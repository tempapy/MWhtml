saṃnyāsaḥ
      1 Leaving, abandonment.
      2 Complete renunciation of the world and its possessions and attachments, abandonment of temporal concerns; kāmyānāṃ karmaṇāṃ tyāgaṃ saṃnyāsaṃ kavayo viduḥ; Bg. 6. 2, 18. 2; Ms. 1. 114; 5. 108.
      3 A deposit, trust.
      4 A stake or wager in a game.
      5 Giving up the body, death.
      6 Indian spikenard. [1091-b]
