• dāṃta p. p. [dam --kartari kta]
      1 Tamed, subdued, overpowered, curbed, restrained, bridled; see dam.
      2 Docile, tame, mild.
      3 Self-possessed, self-controlled; U. 5.
      4 Subdued, conquered, vanquished; U. 5. 33.
      5 Resigned.
      6 Liberal.
      7 Dental.
      8 Patient of bodily mortifications or austerities &c.
    • --taḥ
      1 A tamed ox.
      2 A donor.
      3 N. of a tree (damanaka). [0557-c]
    • dāṃta, dāṃtiḥ See under dam. [0563-a]
