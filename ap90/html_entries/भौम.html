• bhauma a. (mī f.) [bhūmerapatyaṃ tasyā idaṃ vā aṇ]
      1 Belonging to the earth.
      2 Being on the earth, earthly, terrestrial; bhaumo muneḥ sthānaparigrahoyaṃ R. 13. 36; 15. 59.
      3 Earthy, made of earth.
      4 Relating to Mars.
    • --maḥ
      1 The planet Mars.
      2 An epithet of the demon Naraka.
      3 Water.
      4 Light.
      5 Sky, atmosphere.
      6 N. of Atri.
    Comp.
    • --dinaṃ, --vāraḥ, --vāsaraḥ Tuesday; Śi. 15. 17. --ratnaṃ coral. [0827-c]
