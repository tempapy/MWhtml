ijya pot. p. (of yaj) To be worshipped.
      --jyaḥ
      1 A teacher.
      2 An epithet of bṛhaspati, the teacher of the gods.
      3 The Puṣya Nakshatra.
      4 The Supreme being.
      5 An epithet of Viṣṇu.
      --jyā
      1 A sacrifice; jagatprakāśaṃ tadaśeṣamijyayā R. 3. 48, 1. 68, 15. 2; Bg. 11. 53, 9. 25.
      2 A gift, donation.
      3 An image.
      4 Worship, reverence.
      5 Meeting, union.
      6 A bawd or procuress.
      7 A cow.
    Comp.
      --śīlaḥ a constant sacrificer. [0274-c]
