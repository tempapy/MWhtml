prakṛta p. p.
      1 Accomplished, completed.
      2 Commenced, begun.
      3 Appointed, charged.
      4 Genuine, real.
      5 Forming the subject of discussion, that which is under consideration, the subject in hand (often used in works on Alaṃkāra for upameya); saṃbhāvanamathotprekṣā prakṛtasya samena yat K. P. 10.
      6 Important, interesting.
      7 Wished, expected.
      8 Original.
      --taṃ The original subject, the matter or subject in hand; yātu kimanena prakṛtameva anusarāmaḥ ‘come to the point.’
    Comp.
      --artha a. {1} having the original sense. {2} true, real. (--rthaḥ) the original sense. [0725-a]
