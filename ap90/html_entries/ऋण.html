ṛṇa a. [ṛ-kta]
    • 1 Going.
    • 2 Guilty.
    • 1 Debt; (as to the three kinds of debt, see anṛṇa); ṛṇaṃ kṛ to incur debt; ṛṇaṃ dā to pay off or discharge debt; aṃtyaṃ ṛṇaṃ (pitṝṇaṃ) the last debt to be paid to the manes, i. e. creation of a son.
    • 2 An obligation in general.
    • 3 (In alg.) The negative sign or quantity, minus (opp. dhana).
    • 4 A fort, strong-hold.
    • 5 Water.
    • 6 Land.
      Comp. the planet Mars.
      , --muktiḥ, --mokṣaḥ, --śodhanaṃ paying off debt, discharge or liquidation of debt.
      ‘recovery of a debt,’ receipt of money lent &c. (one of the 18 titles or subjects of litigation).
      recovering a debt in any way from the creditor (by friendly or legal proceedings).
      (ṛṇārṇaṃ) debt for a debt, debt incurred to liquidate another debt.
      a. one who gets into debt.
      a. one to whom praise is due as a debt; or one who receives praise as a debt to be repaid in benefits (Sāy.).
      a. indebted, involved in debt.
      {1} borrowing (money). {2} a borrower.
      a. borrowing. (--m.) a debtor, borrower.
      a. acknowledging (praise) as a debt to be paid for.
      a. one who pays a debt.
      [ṛṇāt mocanena kṛto dāsaḥ] one who is bought as a slave by paying off his debts; ṛṇamocanena dāsyatvamabhyupagataḥ ṛṇadāsaḥ Mit. --matkuṇaḥ, mārgaṇaḥ security, bail.
      released from debt.
      &c. see ṛṇāpanayanaṃ.
      ‘debt-bond,’ a bond acknowledging a debt (in law); (Mar. karjarokhā). [0348-b]
