vastiḥ m., f.
      1 Residing, dwelling, staying.
      2 The abdomen, the lower belly.
      3 The pelvis.
      4 The bladder.
      5 A syringe, clyster.
    Comp.
      --malaṃ urine.
      --śiras n. {1} the pipe of a clyster. {2} the neck of the bladder.
      --śodhanaṃ a diuretic (which clears the bladder). [0953-c]
