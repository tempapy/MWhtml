• śūnā [śvi adhikaraṇe --kta saṃpra0 dīrghaḥ Tv.]
      1 The soft palate, uvula.
      2 A slaughter house in general.
      3 Anything (such as a piece of household furniture), whereby life is likely to be destroyed; (these are five; a fire place, a grind-stone, a broom, a mortar, and a water-pot; paṃca śūnā gṛhasthasya cullī peṣaṇyupaskaraḥ . kaṃḍanī codakuṃbhaśca vadhyate yāstu vāhayan Ms. 3. 68.) [1052-b]
