• rājanya a. [rājan-yat nalopaḥ] Royal, kingly.
    • --nyaḥ
      1 A man of the Kṣatriya caste, royal personage; rājanyān svapuranivṛttaye'numene R. 4. 87; saṃprati karaṇīyo rājanyepi praśrayaḥ U. 6; R. 3. 48; Me. 48.
      2 N. of Agni.
      3 A noble or distinguished personage.
    • --nyā A lady of royal rank. [0909-c]
