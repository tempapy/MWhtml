prabhṛtiḥ f.
      1 Beginning, commencement; generally used in this sense as the last member of Bah. compounds; iṃdraprabhṛtayo devāḥ &c.
      2 Ved. An oblation.
      3 Throwing or casting (Ved.). --ind. From, ever since, beginning with (with abl.); śaiśavātprabhṛti poṣitāṃ priyāṃ U. 1. 45; Ku. 3. 26 R. 2. 38; adya prabhṛti henceforward; tataḥ prabhṛti, ataḥ prabhṛti &c. [0754-a]
