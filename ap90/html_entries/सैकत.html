• saikata a. (tī f.) [sikatāḥ saṃtyatra aṇ]
      1 Consisting or made of sand, sandy, gravelly; toyasyevāpratihatarayaḥ saikataṃ setumoghaḥ U. 3. 36.
      2 Having sandy soil.
    • --taṃ
      1 A sand-bank; suragaja iva gāṃgaṃ saikataṃ supratīkaḥ R. 5. 75, 5. 8; 10. 62, 13. 17, 62; 14. 76; 16. 21; Ku. 1. 29; Ś. 6. 16.
      2 An island with sandy shores.
      3 A bank or shore (in general).
    Comp.
    • --iṣṭaṃ ginger. [1138-b]
