• urarī ind. A particle implying (1) assent, admission or acceptance. (In this sense it is usually used with the roots kṛ, bhū, or as, and it has the force of a gati or preposition; urarīkṛtya not urarīkṛtvā. Other forms of the word are urī, ururī, ūrī and ūrurī); (2) extension. [0340-b]
