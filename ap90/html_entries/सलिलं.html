• salilaṃ [salati gacchati nimnaṃ sal-ilac Uṇ. 1. 54]
      1 Water; subhagasalilāvagāhāḥ Ś. 1. 3.
      2 The constellation uttarāṣāḍhā.
    Comp.
    • --ārthin a. thirsty.
    • --āśayaḥ a tank, reservoir of water.
    • --iṃdhanaḥ the submarine fire.
    • --upaplavaḥ inundation, deluge, flood of water.
    • --kuṃtalaḥ moss. --kriyā the funeral rite of washing a corpse. {2} = udakakriyā q. v. --jaṃ a lotus.
    • --nidhiḥ, --rāśiḥ the ocean.
    • --rayaḥ a current, stream. [1110-a]
