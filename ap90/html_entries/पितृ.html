pitṛ m. [pāti rakṣati, pā-tṛc ni°]
      1 A father; tenāsa lokaḥ pitṛmān vinetrā R. 14. 23; 1. 24; 11. 67. --rau (dual) Parents, father and mother; jagataḥ pitarau vaṃde pārvatīparameśvarau R. 1. 1; Y. 2. 117. --raḥ (pl.) {1} Fore-fathers, ancestors, fathers; Ś. 6. 24.
