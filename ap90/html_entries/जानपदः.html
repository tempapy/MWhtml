jānapadaḥ [jānena utpattyā padyate pad ap; janapade bhavaḥ, aṇ vā]
      1 An inhabitant of the country, a rustic, boor, peasant (opp. paura).
      2 A country.
      3 A tax &c. from peasants.
      4 subject.
      --dā A popular expression.
      --dī Profession, business. [0512-a]
