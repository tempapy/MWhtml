saṃbaraḥ
      1 A dam, bridge.
      2 A kind of deer.
      3 N. of a demon slain by Pradyumna; see śaṃbara and pradyumna.
      4 N. of a mountain.
      --raṃ
      1 Restraint.
      2 Water.
      3 A kind of religious observance (with the Buddhists).
    Comp.
      --ariḥ, --ripuḥ Cupid. [1104-c]
