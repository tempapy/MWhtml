• grasta p. p. [gras karmaṇi kta] 1 Eaten, devoured. --2 Seized, stricken, affected, possessed; graha°, vipad°, jarā° U. 6. 39. --3 Slurred; °muktaṃ U. 5. 13; °āmiṣaṃ Pt. 1. 193. --4 Eclipsed. --5 Taken, seized. --staṃ A word or sentence half-uttered or slurred over. --Comp. --astaṃ the setting of the sun or moon while eclipsed. --udayaḥ rising of the sun or moon while eclipsed. [0471-c]
