• vigrah {c9c} P. 1 To hold or seize, catch hold of; vijagṛhe tisṛṇāṃ jayinaṃ purāṃ Ki. 18. 12. --2 To quarrel, fight, contend; vigṛhya cakre namucidviṣā balī ya itthamasvāsthyamahardivaṃ divaḥ Śi. 1. 51; Bk. 6. 86; 17. 23, Ki. 14. 24. --3 To divide into parts, separate (into constituent members). --4 To dissolve, analyse, resolve in general. --5 To receive, welcome (as a guest). --6 To perceive, observe. --7 To stretch or spread out.
