ataṭa a. [na. ba.] Having no shore or beach; precipitate, steep.
      --ṭaḥ
      1 A precipice, a steep crag.
      2 N. of a hill.
      3 The lower part of the earth.
    Comp.
      --prapātaḥ a steep precipice; a fall headlong from a precipice; a precipitate fall; manorathānāmataṭaprapātaḥ Ś. 6. 9 of my hopes there is a precipitate fall (some read the line as manorathā nāma taṭaprapātaḥ and take it to mean ‘verily our desires are like the crumblings of river banks).
