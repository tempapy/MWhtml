• mātaṃgaḥ [mataṃgasya munerayaṃ aṇ] 1 An elephant; mātaṃgāḥ kimu valgitaiḥ K. P. 7; Śi. 1. 64. --2 A man of the lowest caste, a Cāṇḍāla. --3 A Kirāta, a mountaineer or barbarian. --4 (At the end of comp.) Any thing the best of its kind; e. g. balāhakamātaṃgaḥ. --Comp. --divākaraḥ N. of a poet. --takraḥ a crocodile as large as an elephant; R. 13. 11. [0857-b]
