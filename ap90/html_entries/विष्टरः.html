• viṣṭaraḥ 1 A seat (a stool, chair &c.); R. 8. 18. --2 A layer, bed (as of Kuśa grass.). --3 A handful of Kuśa grass. --4 The seat of the presiding priest (or Brahman) at a sacrifice. --5 A tree. --Comp. --bhāj a. seated on or occupying a seat; Ku. 7. 72. --śravas m. an epithet of Viṣṇu or Kṛṣṇa; Śi. 14. 12.
