abda a. [apo dadāti, dā-ka; said in Uṇ. 4. 98 to be from ab; abatītyabdaḥ] Giving water.
    • 1 A cloud.
    • 2 A year (in this sense n. also).
    • 3 N. of a grass (mustā).
    • 4 N. of a mountain
      Comp. half a year.
      N. of Śiva.
      a century.
      a kind of camphor. [0125-b]
