• mānuṣa a. (ṣī f.) [manorayaṃ aṇ suk ca] 1 Human; mānuṣī tanuḥ, mānuṣī vāk &c. R. 1. 60, 16. 22; Bg. 4. 12; 9. 11; Ms. 4. 124. --2 Humane, kind. --ṣaḥ 1 A man, human being. --2 An epithet of the three signs of the zodiac, Gemini, Virgo, and Libra. --ṣī 1 A woman. --2 A branch of medicine, administering drugs and herbs. --ṣaṃ 1 Humanity. --2 Human effort or action. [0860-c]
