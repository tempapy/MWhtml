pheṇaḥ, --naḥ
      1 Foam, froth; gaurīvaktraṃbhrukuṭiracanāṃ yā vihasyeva phenaiḥ Me. 50; R. 13. 11; Ms. 2. 61.
      2 Foam of the mouth.
      3 Saliva.
      4 White cuttle-fish bone.
    Comp.
      --agraṃ a bubble.
      --aśaniḥ N. of Indra; cf. namuci.
      --āhāra a. living on foam.
      --giriḥ N. of a mountain near the mouth of the Indus.
      --piṃḍaḥ {1} a mere bubble. {2} an empty idea, non-entity.
      --vāhin m. a filtering cloth. [0785-c]
