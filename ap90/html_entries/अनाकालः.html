• anākālaḥ [na. ta. nipātaḥ]
      1 Inopportune time.
      2 [āsamyak annādisaṃpannaḥ kālaḥ ākālaḥ na. ta. Tv.] Famine (perhaps an irregular form for annākāla).
    Comp.
    • --bhṛtaḥ one who, to save himself from starvation in a famine, voluntarily becomes a slave of another. [0060-b]
