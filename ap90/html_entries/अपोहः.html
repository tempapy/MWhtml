• apohaḥ 1 Removing, driving away, healing &c. --2 Removal of doubt by the exercise of the reasoning faculty. --3 Reasoning, arguing; reasoning faculty. --4 Negative reasoning (opp. ūha) (aparatarkanirāsāya kṛto viparītastarkaḥ), one of the dhīgunas q. v.; svayamūhāpohāsamarthaḥ Mbh.; ūhāpohamimaṃ sarojanayanā yāvadvidhattetarāṃ Bv. 2. 74; hence ūhāpoha = complete discussion of a question. --5 Excluding all things not coming under the category in point; tadvānapoho vā śabdārthaḥ (where Maheśvara paraphrases apoha by atadvyāvṛtti i. e. tadbhinnatyāgaḥ) [0120-b]
