ave 2 P. [ava i]
      1 (a) To know, understand, learn, perceive, be aware of; avaimi te tasyāṃ sodaryasnehaṃ Ś. 4; avaimi caināmanagheti R. 14. 40; dviṣadabhiyoga ityavaiti Mu. 1. 7.; avaimi te sāraṃ Ku. 3. 13; R. 2. 35.       2 To go or move away.
      3 To go to, approach (with acc.).
      4 To rush upon, come down upon (Ved.) --Freq. or Pass. (aveyate) To beg pardon for, conciliate. [0197-b]
