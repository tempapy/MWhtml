prayāgaḥ
      1 A sacrifice.
      2 N. of Indra.
      3 A horse.
      4 N. of a celebrated place of pilgrimage at the confluence of the Gaṅgā and Yamunā near the modern Allahabad; Ms. 2. 21; (said to be n. also in this sense).
    Comp.
      --bhayaḥ an epithet of Indra. [0757-a]
