• āśu a. [aś-vyāptau uṇ] Fast, quick.
    • --śuḥ Ved.
      1 ‘The quick one’, a horse.
      2 Rice (ripening quickly in the rainy season).
    • --śu ind. Fast, quickly, immediately, directly; vartma bhānostyajāśu Me. 39, 22. [cf. L. acu; Gr. okus.].
    Comp.
    • --āpas a. obtaining quickly.
    • --kārin, --kṛt a. {1} doing anything quickly, smart, active. {2} operating quickly (as a medicine).
    • --kopin a. irascible, irritable.
    • --kriyā quick operation of a medicine.
    • --ga a. swift, quick. (--gaḥ) {1} the wind. {2} the sun. {3} an arrow; papāvanāsvāditapūrvamāśugaḥ R. 3. 54, 11. 83, 12. 91. --gāmin a. going quickly. (--m.). the sun.
    • --toṣa a. easily appeased or pleased. (--ṣaḥ) an epithet of Śiva.
    • --patrī a tree which yields frankincense (śallakīlatā).
    • --patvan a. flying quickly.
    • --bodha a. teaching quickly, N. of a grammar.
    • --yā a. going quickly. (--ind.) quickly.
    • --vrīhiḥ rice ripening in the rainy season.
    • --ṣeṇa a. Ved. having swift arrows.
    • --heman a. running on quickly, urging the horses.
    • --heṣas a. Ved. quickly neighing; having quick horses, quickly praised; (śīghraśabdāyamāna). [0264-b]
