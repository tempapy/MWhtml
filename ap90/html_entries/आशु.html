āśu a. [aś-vyāptau uṇ] Fast, quick.
      Ved.
    • 1 ‘The quick one’, a horse.
    • 2 Rice (ripening quickly in the rainy season).
      ind. Fast, quickly, immediately, directly; vartma bhānostyajāśu Me. 39, 22. [cf. L. acu; Gr. okus.].
      Comp. a. obtaining quickly.
      a. {1} doing anything quickly, smart, active. {2} operating quickly (as a medicine).
      a. irascible, irritable.
      quick operation of a medicine.
      a. swift, quick. (--gaḥ) {1} the wind. {2} the sun. {3} an arrow; papāvanāsvāditapūrvamāśugaḥ R. 3. 54, 11. 83, 12. 91. --gāmin a. going quickly. (--m.). the sun.
      a. easily appeased or pleased. (--ṣaḥ) an epithet of Śiva.
      a tree which yields frankincense (śallakīlatā).
      a. flying quickly.
      a. teaching quickly, N. of a grammar.
      a. going quickly. (--ind.) quickly.
      rice ripening in the rainy season.
      a. Ved. having swift arrows.
      a. running on quickly, urging the horses.
      a. Ved. quickly neighing; having quick horses, quickly praised; (śīghraśabdāyamāna). [0264-b]
