uparāgaḥ
    • 1 An eclipse of the sun or moon; uparāgāṃte śaśinaḥ samupagatā rohiṇī yogaṃ Ś. 7. 22; Śi. 20. 45.
    • 2 Hence, Rāhu or the ascending node.
    • 3 Redness, red colour, colour; kopoparāgajanitāṃ Ratn. 3. 14; Mk. 4; kalahakopa° Māl. 3.
    • 4 A calamity, affliction, injury; blighting influence; mṛṇālinī haimamivoparāgaṃ R. 16. 7.
    • 5 Misbehaviour, ill conduct.
    • 6 Reproach, blame, abuse. [0328-a]
