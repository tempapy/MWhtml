• saṃdehaḥ 1 Doubt, uncertainty, suspense; atra kaḥ saṃdehaḥ. --2 Risk, danger, peril; jīvitasaṃdehadolāmāropitaḥ K.; arthārjane pravṛttiḥ sasaṃdehā H. 1; Pt. 1. 176. --3 (In Rhet.) Doubt regarded as a figure of speech, in which the close resemblance between two objects leads to one of them being mistaken for the other (this figure is also called sasaṃdeha by Mammaṭa and others); sasaṃdehastu bhedoktau tadanuktau ca saṃśayaḥ K. P. 10; e. g. see Māl. 1. 2 (l. 3); V. 3. 2. --Comp. --dolā the swing of uncertainty, a state of suspense, dilemma, fix. [1088-a]
