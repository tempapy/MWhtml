• aṃte ind. (loc. of aṃta; oft. used adverbially) 1 In the end, at last, at length, lastly, finally. --2 Inside. --3 In the presence of, near, close by. --Comp. --vāsaḥ {1} a neighbour; companion; tava vā imeṃ'tevāsāstvamevaibhiḥ saṃpibasva Ait. Br. {2} a pupil; rutāni śṛṇvanvayasāṃ gaṇoṃ'tevāsitvamāpa sphuṭamaṃganānāṃ Śi. 3. 55; Ve. 3. 7. --vāsi ind. in a state of pupilage, (in statu pupilari). --vāsin = aṃtavāsin q. v. above. [0088-c]
