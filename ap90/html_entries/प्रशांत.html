• praśāṃta p. p. 1 Calmed, tranquillized, composed. --2 Calm, serene, quiet, sedate, still; aho praśāṃtaramaṇīyatodyānasya. --3 Tamed, subdued, quelled. --4 Ended, ceased, over; tatsarvamekapada eva mama praśāṃtaṃ Māl. 9. 36; praśāṃtamastraṃ U. 6 ‘ceased to work or withdrawn.’ --5 Dead, deceased; (see śam with pra). --Comp. --ātman a. composed in mind, peaceful, calm. --ūrja a. weakened, enervated, prostrated. --kāma a. content. --ceṣṭa a. resting, ceased to work. --bādha a. having all obstacles or calamities removed; Ki. 1. 18.
