atala a. Bottomless.
      [asya bhūkhaṃḍasya talaṃ pṛṣo° idamo'ttvam] N. of a pātāla or lower region, one of the 7 Pātālas, being the first among them.
      N. of Śiva.
      Comp., --sparśa a. [na tale spṛśyate karmaṇi kvip; na talasya sparśo yatra] bottomless, very deep, unfathomable. [0029-a]
