• sagotra a. Being of the same family or kin, related.
    • --traḥ
      1 A kinsman sprung from a common ancestor; tatramavato yuṣmatsagotrasya kaṇvasyāparāddhommi Ś. 7.
      2 A kinsman of the same family, one connected by funeral oblations of food and water.
      3 A distant kinsman.
    • --traṃ Family, race, lineage. [1076-b]
