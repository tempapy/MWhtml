• maurvī [mūrvālatā tattaṃtunā nirvṛttā aṇ]
      1 {1} A bow-string; maurvīkiṇāṃko bhujaḥ Ś. 1. 13; maurvī dhanuṣi cātatā R. 1. 19; 18. 48; Ku. 3. 55.
      2 A girdle made of Mūrvā grass (to be worn by a kshatriya); Ms. 2. 42.
      3 (In geom.) The chord of an arc; (also maurvikā). [0881-c]
