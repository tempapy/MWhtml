sākṣin a. (ṇī f.) [saha akṣi asya; sākṣād draṣṭā sākṣī vā P. V. 2. 91]
      1 Seeing, observing, witnessing.
      2 Attesting, testifying. --m.
      1 A witness, an observer, an eye-witness; phalaṃ tapaḥsākṣiṣu dṛṣṭameṣvapi Ku. 5. 60.
      2 The Supreme Being.
    Comp.
      --dvaidhaṃ divergent evidence, discrepancy between witnesses. --pratyayaḥ the evidence of witnesses.
      --bhāvita a. borne out or proved by evidence. [1113-b]
