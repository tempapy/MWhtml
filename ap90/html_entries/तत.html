      1 Extended, spread; Śi. 9. 23, 6. 50; Ki. 5. 11.

      4 Protracted, continued.

      6 Spreading wide &c. see tan.

      1 A father.
      2 Wind, air.
      3 Extent.
      4 Offspring, a child (n. also).
      5 A son.
