• avas n. [av-bhāve asic] Ved. 1 Protection, assistance. --2 Fame, celebrity. --3 Food, provision. --4 Wealth; riches. --5 Going. --6 Satisfaction, pleasure. --7 Wish, will, desire, aspiration. --ind. [avara-prathamāyāḥ paṃcamyā saptamyā vā'rthe asi, av ādeśaḥ P. V. 3. 39] 1 Below, downwards, hitherward. --2 (As a preposition with instr. or abl.) (a) Below, at the lowest place. (b) Without, on the outside. [0189-c]
