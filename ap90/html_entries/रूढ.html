• rūḍha p. p. [ruh-kta] 1 Grown, sprung up, shot forth, germinated. --2 Born, produced; viṣayavyāsagarūḍhātmanā Mu. 2. 5. --3 Grown up, increased, developed. --4 Risen, ascended. --5 Large, great, grown, strong. --6 Diffused, spread about. --7 Commonly known, become current or widely known; kṣatātkila trāyata ityudagraḥ kṣatrasya śabdo bhuvaneṣu rūḍhaḥ R. 2. 53; (here kṣatra has a sense which is yogarūḍha q. v.). --8 Popularly accepted, traditional, conventional, popular (as the meaning of a word, or the word itself; as opposed to yaugika or etymological sense); vyutpattirahitāḥ śabdā rūḍhā ākhaṃḍalādayaḥ; nāma rūḍhamapi ca vyudapādi Śi. 10. 23. --9 Certain, ascertained. --10 Obscure. --Comp. --graṃthi a. having formed a knot; U. 2. 26. --yauvana a. one who has attained to youth. --sauhṛda a. firm in friendship, of deep-rooted friendship; V. 1. 10.
