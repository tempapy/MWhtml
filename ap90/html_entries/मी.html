• mī {vI.v} {c9c} U. (mīnāti, mīnīte; seldom used in classical literature) 1 To kill, destroy, hurt, injure. --2 To lessen, diminish. --3 To change, alter. --4 To transgress, violate. --5 To disappear, be lost. --6 To stray, go astray. {vII.v} P., {c10c} U. (mayati, māyayati-te) 1 To go, move. --2 To know, understand (gatimatyayoḥ). {vIII.v} {c4c} A. (mīyate) To die, perish. [0867-a]
