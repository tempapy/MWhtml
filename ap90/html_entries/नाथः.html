nāthaḥ [nāth-ac]
      1 A lord, master, protector, leader; nāthe kutastvayyaśubhaṃ prajānāṃ R. 5. 13, 2. 73, 3. 45; triloka°, kailāsa° &c.
      2 A husband.
      3 A rope passed through the nose of a draft-ox.
      4 A possessor.
    Comp.
      --hariḥ a beast (paśu). [0613-c]
