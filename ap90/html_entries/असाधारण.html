• asādhāraṇa a.
      1 Not common, peculiar, special, specific.
      2 (In logic) Existing neither in sapakṣa or vipakṣa as a hetu; yastūbhayasmād vyāvṛttaḥ sa tvasādhāraṇo mataḥ.
      3 Not to be claimed by any one else, exclusively belonging to one (as wealth &c.); pitā putrāṃtareṣvapi sādhāraṇo mātā tvasādhāraṇī Mit. --ṇaḥ A fallacy or hetvābhāsa in Logic; one of the three kinds of anaikāṃtika q. v. --ṇaṃ Speciality, special property. [0212-b]
