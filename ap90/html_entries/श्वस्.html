• śvas {c2c} P. (śvasiti, śvasta or śvasita) 1 To breathe, respire, draw breath; sa karmakārabhastreva śvasannapi na jīvati H. 2. 11. kṣaṇamapyavatiṣṭhate śvasan yahi jaṃturnanu lābhavānasau R. 8. 87. --2 To sigh, pant, heave; śvasiti vihagavargaḥ Rs. 1. 13. --3 To hiss, snort. --Caus. (śvāsayati-te) To cause to breathe or live. [1063-b]
