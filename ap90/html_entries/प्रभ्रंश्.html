• prabhraṃś 1 A., 4 P.
      1 To fall or drop down, slip; prabhraśyamānābharaṇaprasūnā R. 14. 54.
      2 To lose, be deprived of; prabhraśyate tejasaḥ Mk. 1. 14.
      3 To escape or free oneself from. --Caus. To throw or bring down from, cause to fall down from; prabhraṃśayāṃ yo nahuṣaṃ cakāra R. 13. 36. [0754-a]
