• upapadaṃ
      1 A word prefixed or previously uttered; dhanurupapadaṃ vedaṃ Ki. 18. 44 (dhanurvedaṃ); tasyāḥ sa rājopapadaṃ niśāṃtaṃ R. 16. 40.
      2 A title, a degree; epithet of respect, such as ārya, śarman; kathaṃ nirupapadameva cāṇakyamiti na āryacāṇakyamiti Mu. 3.
      3 A secondary word of a sentence, a preposition, particle &c. prefixed to a verb or a noun derived from a verb which determines or qualifies the sense of the verb; upapadamatiṅ P. II. 2. 19; (see Sk. thereon). °tatpuruṣaḥ a kind of Tat. comp. in which the last member is some form of a verbal character; e. g. vedavid, grāmaṇī, ākarṇalaṃbin &c. [0325-b]
