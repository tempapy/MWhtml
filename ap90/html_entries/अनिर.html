anira a. [na īrayituṃ śakyate, īr-ka pṛ° thasvaḥ] That cannot be propelled or driven along; apa tyā asthuranirā Rv. 8. 48. 11 (prerayitumaśakya).
    • 1 Want of food; utter destitution (annarahitadāridryaṃ); yuyutamasmadanirāmamīvāṃ Rv. 7. 71. 2.
    • 2 [nāsti irā annaṃ yasyāḥ paṃ. ba.] A calamity such as ativṛṣṭi, anāvṛṣṭi (= īti). [0064-c]
