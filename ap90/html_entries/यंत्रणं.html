• yaṃtraṇaṃ --ṇā [yaṃtr-lyuṭ vā ṭāp] 1 Rest raining, curbing, stopping; karayaṃtraṇa daṃturāṃtare vyalikhaccaṃcupuṭena pakṣatī N. 2. 2 --2 A restraint, restriction; check; hrīyaṃtraṇāṃ tatkṣaṇamanvabhūvannanyonyalo lāni vilocanāni Ku. 7. 75; R. 7. 23; gurujanayaṃtraṇā K. 94. --3 Fastening, binding (baṃdha); nibiḍapīnakucadvaya yaṃtraṇā tamaparādhamadhāt pratibadhnatī N. 4. 10. --4 Force, compulsion, constraint, trouble, pain or anguish (arising from compulsion); alamalamupacārayaṃtraṇayā M. 4. --5 Guarding, protecting. --6 A bandage.
