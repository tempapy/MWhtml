• jan {c4c} A. (jāyate, jajñe, ajāni-ajaniṣṭa, janituṃ, jāta; pass. janyate or jāyate) 1 To be born or produced (with abl. of source of birth); ajani te vai putraḥ Ait. Br.; Ms. 1. 9; 3. 39, 41; prāṇādvāyurajāyata Rv. 10. 90. 12; Ms. 10. 8; 3. 76; 1. 75. --2 To rise, spring up, grow (as a plant &c.). --3 To be, become, happen, take place, occur; aniṣṭādiṣṭalābhe'pi na gatirjāyate śubhā H. 1. 6; raktanetro'jāni kṣaṇāt Bk. 6. 32; Y. 3. 226; Ms. 1. 99. --4 To be possible, applicable &c. --5 To be born or destined for anything. --Caus. (janayati) 1 To give birth, beget, cause, produce. --2 To cause, occasion. [0505-a]
