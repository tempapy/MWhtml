• apāc, apāṃc (m. ṅ, f. cī, n. k) [apāṃcati, aṃc kvip]
      1 Going or situated backwards, behind.
      2 Not open or clear (aprakāśa).
      3 Western.
      4 Southern (opp. udaṃc); probably for avāṃc.

      1 Behind, backwards.
