akaraṇaṃ [kṛ-bhāve lyuṭ na. ta.] Not doing, absence of action; akaraṇāt maṃdakaraṇaṃ śreyaḥ; cf. the English phrases “Something is better than nothing,” “Better late than never”.
      --ṇa a. [na. ba.]
      1 Not artificial, natural.
      2 Devoid of all organs, epithet of the Supreme Spirit. [0003-a]
