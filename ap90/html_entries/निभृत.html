• nibhṛta a. 1 Placed down, deposited, lowered. --2 Filled with, full of; ciṃtayā nibhṛtaḥ Bhāg. --3 Concealed, hidden, out of sight, unperceved, unobserved; nibhṛto bhūtvā Pt. 1; nabhasā nibhṛteṃdunā R. 8. 15 ‘with the moon become invisible, about to set or go down’; Ve. 6. 2, Śi. 6. 20. --4 Secret, covert; Śi. 13. 42. --5 (a) Still, silent; nibhṛtadvirephaṃ ( kānanaṃ) Ku. 3. 42, 6. 2.
