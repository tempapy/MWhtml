• anumānaṃ
      1 Inferring as the instrument of an anumiti, conclusion from given premises; an inference, conclusion; one of the four means of obtaining knowledge according to the Nyāya system; (anumitikaraṇamanumānaṃ tacca dhūmo vahnivyāpya iti vyāptijñānaṃ. It is of two kinds svārthānumānaṃ & parārthānumānaṃ); pratyakṣaṃ cānumānaṃ ca śāstraṃ ca vividhāgamaṃ . trayaṃ suviditaṃ kāryaṃ dharmaśuddhimabhīpsatā .. Ms. 12. 105.
      2 A guess, conjecture, sign to know; iṃgitairanumānaiśca mayā jñeyā bhaviṣyati Rām.
      3 Analogy, similarity; ātmano hṛdayānumānena prekṣase Ś. 5 you judge (of others) by the analogy of your own heart; svānumānātkādaṃbarīmutprekṣya K. 305.
      4 (In Rhet.) A figure which consists in a notion, expressed in a peculiarly striking manner, of a thing established by proof; S. D. 711; yatra patatyabalānāṃ dṛṣṭirniśitāḥ pataṃti tatra śarāḥ . taccāparopitaśaro dhāvatyāsāṃ puraḥ smaro manye .. anumānaṃ taduktaṃ yatsādhyasādhanayorvacaḥ K. P. 10.
    Comp.
