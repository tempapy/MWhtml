• nāliḥ --lī f. [nal-ṇic in vā ṅīp] 1 Any tubular vessel of the body. --2 A hollow stalk, especially that of the lotus. --3 A period of 24 minutes (ghaṭikā). --4 An instrument for boring an elephant's ear. --5 A canal, drain. --6 A lotusflower. --7 A piece of metal on which the hours are struck (ghaṭī).
