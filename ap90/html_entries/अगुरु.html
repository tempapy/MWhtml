aguru a. [na. ta.]
    • 1 Not heavy, light.
    • 2 (In prosody) Short.
    • 3 Having no teacher.
    • 4 One different from a teacher.
      n. (m. also) [na gururyasmāt]
    • 1 The fragrant aloe wood and tree; Aquiluria Agallocha.
    • 2 That which yields Bdellium, Amyris Agallocha.
    • 3 The Śiśu tree (śiṃśapā).
      Comp. [aguruḥ sāro yasyāstādṛśī śiṃśapā madhyamapadalo.] the Śiśu tree.
      a sort of perfume. [0010-c]
