anūpa a. [anu-ap ūdanordeśe P. VI. 3. 98, anugatāḥ āpaḥ yasmin] Situated near water, watery, rich or abounding in water, wet, marshy.
    • 1 A watery place or country; syaṃdanāśvaiḥ same yudhyedanūpe naudvipaistathā Ms. 7. 192; Y. 3. 42; nānādrumalatāvīrunnirjharaprāṃtaśītalaiḥ . vanairvyāptamanūpaṃ tatsasyairvrīhiyavādibhiḥ.
    • 2 N. of a particular country (--pāḥ pl.); R. 6. 37.
    • 3 A marsh, bog.
    • 4 A pond or tank of water.
    • 5 Bank or side (of a river, mountain); sāgarātparvatānūpāt Rām.; nadīṃ goyutānūpāṃ atarat ibid.
    • 6 A buffalo.
    • 7 A frog.
    • 8 A kind of partridge.
    • 9 An elephant.
      Comp. moist ginger.
      a. marshy, boggy. [0085-c]
