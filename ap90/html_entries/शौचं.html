• śaucaṃ [śucerbhāvaḥ aṇ] 1 Purity, clearness; kāke śaucaṃ dyūtakāre ca satyaṃ Pt. 1. 147. --2 Purification from personal defilement caused by voiding excrement, but particularly by the death of a relative. --3 Cleansing, purifying. --4 Voiding of excrement. --5 Uprightness, honesty. --Comp. --ācāraḥ, --karman n. --kalpaḥ a purificatory rite. --kūpaḥ a privy.
