• hastinī
      1 A female elephant.
      2 A kind of drug and perfume.
      3 A woman of a particular class, one of the four classes into which writers on erotical science divide women (described as having thick lips, thick hips, thick fingers, large breasts, dark complexion, and libidinous appetite); the Ratimañjarī thus describes her: --sthūlādharā sthūlanitaṃbabiṃbā sthūlāṃguliḥ sthūlakucā suśīlā . kāmotsukā gāḍharatipriyā ca nitāṃtabhoktrī (nitaṃbakharvā) khalu hastinī syāt (kariṇī matā sā) {8} [1169-a]
