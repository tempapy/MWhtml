• pīḍanaṃ [pīḍ bhāve lyuṭ]
      1 Paining, distressing, oppressing, inflicting pain; Ms. 9. 299; Pt. 1. 345.
      2 (a) Squeezing, pressing; dorvallibaṃdhanibiḍastanapīḍanāni Gīt. 10; daṃtauṣṭhapīḍananakhakṣataraktasiktāṃ Ch. P. 44. (b) Pressure; Māl. 9. 38.
      3 An instrument for pressing.
      4 Taking, holding, seizing; as in karapīḍana or pāṇipīḍana q. v.
      5 Laying waste, devastation.
      6 Threshing corn.
      7 An eclipse; as in grahapīḍana q. v.
      8 Suppressing sounds, a fault in the pronunciation of vowels. [0704-c]
