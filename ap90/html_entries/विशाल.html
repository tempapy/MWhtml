viśāla a.
      1 Large, great, extensive, spacious, broad, wide; gṛhairviśālairapi bhūriśālaiḥ Śi. 3. 50; rathacaraṇaviśālaśra ṇilolekṣaṇena 11. 23, 17. 47; R. 2. 21. 6. 32, Bg. 9. 21.
      2 Rich or abounding in: śrīviśālāṃ viśālāṃ Me. 30.
      3 Eminent, illustrious, great, noble, celebrated.
      --laḥ
      1 A kind of deer.
      2 A kind of bird.
      --lā
      1 N. of the town Ujjayinī; pūrvoddiṣṭāmanusara purīṃ śrīviśālāṃ viśālāṃ Me. 30.
      2 N. of a river.
    Comp.
      --akṣa a. large-eyed. (--kṣaḥ) {1} N. of Viṣṇu. {2} of Garuḍa. {3} an epithet of Śiva. (--kṣī) an epithet of Pārvatī. [0994-c]
