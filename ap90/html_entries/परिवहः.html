• parivahaḥ
      1 N. of one of the seven courses of wind; it is the sixth course, and bears along the Saptarshis and the celestial Ganges; saptarṣicakraṃ svargaṃgāṃ ṣaṣṭhaḥ parivahastathā; (for the other courses of wind see under vāyu; cf. the description of parivaha given by Kālidāsa: --trisrotasaṃ vahati yo gaganapratiṣṭhāṃ jyotīṃṣi vartayati ca pravibhaktaraśmiḥ . tasya dvitīyaharivikramanistamaskaṃ vāyorimaṃ parivahasya badaṃti mārgaṃ Ś. 7. 6.).
      2 One of the seven tongues of fire. [0680-a]
