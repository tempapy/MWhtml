• jina a. [ji-nak]
      1 Victorious, triumphant.
      2 Very old.
    • --naḥ
      1 A generic term applied to a chief Bauddha or Jaina saint.
      2 N. applied to the Arhats of the Jainas.
      3 A very old man.
      4 An epithet of Viṣṇu.
    Comp.
    • --iṃdraḥ, --īśvaraḥ {1} a chief Bauddha saint. {2} an Arhat of the Jainas.
    • --sadman n. a Jaina temple or monastery. [0514-b]
