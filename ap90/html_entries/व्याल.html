      2 Bad, villainous.
      3 Cruel, fierce, savage; Ki. 13. 4. --laḥ
      1 A vicious elephant; vyālaṃ bālamṛṇālataṃtubhirasau roddhuṃ samujjṛṃbhate Bh. 2. 6.
      2 A beast of prey; varnaṃ vyālaniṣevitaṃ Rām.
      3 A snake; H. 3. 29.
      4 A tiger; Māl. 3.
      5 A leopard.
      6 A king.
      7 A cheat, rogue.
      8 N. of Viṣṇu.
