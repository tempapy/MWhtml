kaluṣa a. [kal-uṣac Uṇ. 4. 75]
      1 Turbid, dirty, muddy, foul; gaṃgā rodhaḥ patanakaluṣā gṛhṇatīva prasādaṃ V. 1. 8; Ki. 8. 32; Ghaṭ. 13.
      2 Choked, hoarse, husky; kaṃṭhaḥ staṃbhitabāṣpavṛttikaluṣaḥ Ś. 4. 5.
      3 Bedimmed; full of; Ś. 6. 8.
      4 Angry, displeased, excited; U. 3. 13; bhāvāvabodhakaluṣā dayiteva rātrau R. 5. 64 (Malli. takes kaluṣa to mean ‘unable’, ‘incompetent’).
      5 Wicked, sinful, bad.
      6 Cruel, censurable; R. 14. 73.
      7 Dark, opaque.
      8 Idle, lazy.
      9 Perverted; °bhūtāyāṃ buddhau Pt. 3. 184; kāluṣyamupayāti buddhiḥ &c.
      --ṣaḥ A buffalo.
      --ṣaṃ
      1 Dirt, filth, mud; vigatakaluṣamaṃbhaḥ Rs. 3. 22.
      2 Sin.
      3 Wrath.
    Comp.
      --yonija a. illegitimate, of impure origin; Ms. 10. 57, 58. [0387-c]
