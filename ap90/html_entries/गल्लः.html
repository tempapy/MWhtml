• gallaḥ The cheek; especially, the part of the cheek near the corners of the mouth. (Rhetoricians consider this word to be grāmya or vulgar; cf. the instance given in K. P. 7: --tāṃbūlabhṛtagalloyaṃ bhallaṃ jalpati mānuṣaḥ; but cf. Bhavabhūti's use: --pātālapratimallagallavivaraprakṣiptasaptārṇavaṃ Māl. 5. 22).
    Comp.
    • --cāturī a small round pillow to put underneath the cheek. [0455-c]
