• śokaḥ [śuc-ghañ] Sorrow, grief, distress, affliction, lamentation, wailing, deep anguish; ślākatvamāpadyata yasya śokaḥ R. 14. 70. --Comp. --agniḥ, --analaḥ the fire of grief. --apanodaḥ removal of grief. --abhibhūta. --ākula, --āviṣṭa, --upahata, --vihvala a. afflicted or agonized by grief. --ariḥ the Kadamba tree. --karṣita a. afflicted or agonized with grief. --carvā indulgence in grief. --nāśaḥ the Aśoka tree. --parāyaṇa, --lāsaka a. engrossed in grief, wholly given up to grief. --vikala a. overwhelmed with grief. --sthānaṃ any cause of sorrow.
