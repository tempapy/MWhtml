āstaraṇa a. Spreading, covering.
    • 1 Spreading, strewing.
    • 2 A bed, layer; kusuma° a bed of flowers; Ku. 4. 35; sakusuma° strewn with flowers Ś. 3; tamālapatrāstaraṇāsu raṃtuṃ R. 6. 64.
    • 3 A cushion, quilt, bedclothes; gata° without the bedclothes.
    • 4 A rug, carpet.
    • 5 An elephant's housings, painted cloth (thrown on his back).
    • 6 A layer of Kuśa grass spread out at a sacrifice. [0269-c]
