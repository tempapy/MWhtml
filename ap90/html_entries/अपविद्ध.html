  • apaviddha p. p. 1 Cast or thrown off, thrown or cast away, abandoned, forsaken, dismissed, rejected, neglected, removed; oft. used in the sense of ‘freed from,’ ‘devoid of’; rakṣoviprakṛtāvāstāmapaviddhaśucāviva R. 10. 74. --2 Abject, mean. --ddhaḥ, °putraḥ A son that is abandoned by the father or mother or by both, and adopted by a stranger; one of the 12 kinds of sons among Hindus; Ms. 9. 171; Y. 2. 132. --Comp. --loka a. dead, departed from this world.
