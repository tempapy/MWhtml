• cūrṇiḥ --rṇī f.
      1 Pounding, powder.
      2 A sum of hundred cowries.
      3 N. of Patañjali's Mahābhāṣya on the Sūtras of Pāṇini.
      4 A selection of an unanswerable argument.
    Comp.
    • --kṛt m. {1} an epithet of Patañjali. {2} an annotator, commentator. [0497-c]
