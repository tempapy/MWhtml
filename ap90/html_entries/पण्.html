• paṇ {vI.v} {c1c} A. (paṇate, paṇita) 1 To deal in, barter, purchase, buy; N. 2. 91. --2 To bargain, transact business. --3 To bet or stake at play (usually with gen. of the thing staked, but sometimes with acc.); prāṇānāmapaṇiṣṭāsau Bk. 8. 121; paṇasva kṛṣṇāṃ pāṃcālīṃ Mb. --4 To risk or hazard (a battle). --5 To win anything at play. {vII.v} {c1c} A., {c10c} U. (paṇate, paṇāyati-te) 1 To praise. --2 To honour. --WITH vi to sell, barter; ābhīradeśe kila caṃdrakāṃtaṃ tribhirvarāṭairvipaṇaṃti gopāḥ Subhāṣ. [0659-c]
