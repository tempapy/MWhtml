• śiṣyaḥ [śas --kyap]
      1 A pupil, disciple, scholar; śiṣyaste'haṃ śādhi mā tvāṃ prapannaṃ Bg. 2. 7.
      2 Anger, passion.
      3 Violence, force.
    Comp.
    • --paraṃparā a succession of pupils.
    • --putraḥ a pupil regarded as a son.
    • --śiṣṭriḥ f. the correction of a pupil. [1047-b]
