• hārin a. (ṇī f.) [hāro astyasya ini, hṛ-ṇini vā]
      1 Taking, conveying, carrying.
      2 Robbing, taking away; vājiṃkujarāṇāṃ ca hāriṇaḥ Y. 2. 273, 3. 208.
      3 Seizing, disturbing; Ms. 12. 28.
      4 Obtaining, securing.
      5 Attracting, captivating, pleasing, delighting, ravishing; tavāsmi gītarāgeṇa hāriṇā prasabhaṃ hṛtaḥ Ś. 1. 5; Śi. 10. 13, 69; viṣṭapahāriṇi harau Bh. 2. 25.
      6 Surpassing, excelling.
      7 Having a necklace. [1170-a]
