maghaḥ
      1 N. of one of the Dvīpas or divisions of the universe.
      2 N. of a country.
      3 A kind of drug or medicine.
      4 Pleasure.
      5 N. of the tenth lunar mansion; see maghā.

      1 A kind of flower.
