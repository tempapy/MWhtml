• malimlucaḥ
      1 A robber, thief; na pareṣu mahaujasaśchalādapakurvaṃti malimlucā iva Śi. 16. 52.
      2 A demon.
      3 A gnat, mosquito.
      4 An intercalary month.
      5 Air, wind.
      6 Fire.
      7 A Brāhmaṇa who neglects the five daily Yajñas or sacrifices.
      8 The Citraka tree.
      9 Frost, snow. [0848-b]
