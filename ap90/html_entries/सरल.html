• sarala a. [sṛ-alac] 1 Straight, not crooked. --2 Honest, upright, sincere, candid. --3 Simple, artless, simple-minded; sarale sāhasarāgaṃ parihara Māl. 6. 10; ayi sarale kimatra mayā bhagavatyā śakyaṃ 2. --laḥ 1 A kind of pine tree; vighaṭṭitānāṃ saraladrumāṇāṃ Ku. 1. 9; Me. 53; R. 4. 75. --2 Fire. --Comp. --aṃgaḥ ‘the exudation of Sarala’, resin, turpentine. --dravaḥ fragrant resin. --yāyinī a plant with an erect stem.
