apaghanaḥ [apahanyate saṃhatya karma kṛtvā viyujyate, han-ap-ghanādeśaḥ P. III 3. 77]. A limb or member of the body, as a hand or foot (apaghanoṃ'gam III. 3. 81; aṃgaṃ śarīrāvayavaḥ sa ca na sarvaṃ kiṃ tu pāṇiḥ pādaścetyāhuḥ Sk. and Kāśikā.); but it is also used in the sense of ‘the body’; lauhodghanaghanaskaṃdhā lalitāpaghanāṃ striyaṃ Bk. 7. 62 (where the commentators take apaghana to mean the body itself). --a. Free from the obstruction of clouds, cloudless. [0105-a]
