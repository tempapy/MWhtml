• ajita a. 1 Invincible, unconquerable, irresistible; °taṃ puṇyaṃ… mahaḥ U. 5. 27. --2 Not conquered or won (as a country &c.); not restrained, curbed, controlled, °ātman; °iṃdriya of uncontrolled soul or passion. --taḥ 1 N. of Viṣṇu or Śiva or of Buddha. --2 N. of a powerful antidote, or a poisonous sort of rat. --(pl.) A class of deities in the first Manvantara. --Comp. --āpīḍaḥ having an invincible crown; N. of a King. --balā N. of a Jaina deity who acts under orders from the Arhat Ajita. --vikramaḥ ‘of unsubdued power’, N. of Candragupta II.
