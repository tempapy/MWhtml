• śṛṃgārin a.
      1 Amorous, impassioned, enamoured,
      2 Relating to love.
      3 Stained with red-lead. --m.
      1 An impassioned lover.
      2 A ruby.
      3 An elephant.
      4 Dress, decoration.
      5 The betel-nut tree.
      6 A preparation of betel-leaves and pieces of areca-nut, see tāṃbūla. [1054-a]
