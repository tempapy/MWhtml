• pārvaṇa a. (ṇī f.) [parvaṇi bhavaḥ aṇ] 1 Belonging or relating to a Parvan, falling on a Parva day, such as the full-moon, new-moon &c. R. 11. 82; Mu. 3. 10. --2 Waxing, increasing (as the moon). --ṇaṃ The ceremony of offering oblations to all the Manes at a Parvan. [0697-c]
