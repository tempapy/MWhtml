• āśās {c2c} A. 1 To bless, pronounce or give a blessing; ṛkchaṃdasā āśāste Ś. 4; kimanyadāśāsmahe kevalaṃ vīraprasavā bhūyāḥ U. 1. --2 To desire, wish, hope, expect; yasya bhavān maṃgalamāśāste Ve. 6; Ms. 3. 80; sarvamasminvayamāśāsmahe Ś. 7; śāṃtiṃ Bk. 17. 1. --3 To order, command, relate (P. also in this sense). --4 To praise.
