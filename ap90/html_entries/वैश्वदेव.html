vaiśvadeva a. (vī f.) Belonging to the Viśvedevas, q. v. --vaṃ [viśvebhyo devebhyo deyo baliḥ aṇ]
      1 An offering made to the Viśvedevas.
      2 An offering to all deities (made by presenting oblations to fire before meals).
      --vī The eighth day of the second half of Māgha. [1015-c]
