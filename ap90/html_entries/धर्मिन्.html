• dharmin a. [dharmo'styasya-ini] 1 Virtuous, just, pious. --2 Knowing one's duties. --3 Obeying the law. --4 Having the properties of, having the nature, peculiar properties, or characteristics of anything, (at the end of comp.); ṣaṭ sutā dvijadharmiṇaḥ Ms. 10. 41; kalpavṛkṣaphaladharmi kāṃkṣitaṃ R. 11. 50. --5 Following the habits of any person. --m. An epithet of Viṣṇu. [0594-a]
