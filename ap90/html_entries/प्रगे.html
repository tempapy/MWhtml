• prage ind. Early in the morning, at day-break; itthaṃ rathāśvebhāniṣādināṃ prage gaṇo nṛpāṇāmatha toraṇādbahiḥ Śi. 12. 1; sāyaṃ snāyātprage tathā Ms. 6. 6; 4. 62.
    Comp.
    • --tana a. to be performed in the morning.
    • --niśa-śaya a. who is asleep at day-break. [0728-a]
