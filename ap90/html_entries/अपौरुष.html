apauruṣa --pauruṣeya a.
    • 1 Unmanly, cowardly, timid.
    • 2 Superhuman, not of the authorship of man, of divine origin; aporuṣeyā vedāḥ; apauruṣeyapratiṣṭhaḥ suvarṇabiṃdurityākhyāyate Māl. 9. not set up by (the hand of) man.
    • 1 Cowardice, pussilanimity.
    • 2 Superhuman power. [0120-b]
