• vṛṣan m. [vṛṣ-kanin] 1 A bull. --2 The sign Taurus of the zodiac. --3 The chief of a class; prācetaso munivṛṣā prathamaḥ kavīnāṃ Mv. 1. 7. --4 A stallion, horse. --5 Pain, sorrow. --6 Insensibility to pain. --7 N. of Indra; vṛṣeva sītāṃ tadavagrahakṣatāṃ Ku. 5. 61, 80; R. 10. 52, 17. 77. --8 N. of Karṇa. --9 Of Agni. --10 Of Soma.
