yamaka a. [yama-svārthe ka]
      1 Twinborn, twin.
      2 Two-fold, double.
      --kaḥ
      1 A restraint, check.
      2 A twin; one of a pair, a fellow.
      3 A great moral or religious duty; see yama (4).
      --kaṃ
      1 A double bandage.
      2 (In Rhet.) Repetition in the same stanza (in any part of it) of words or syllables similar in sound, but different in meaning, a kind of rhyme, (of which various kinds are enumerated; see Kāv. 3. 2-52); āvṛttiṃ varṇasaṃghātagocarāṃ yamakaṃ viduḥ Kāv. 1. 61, 3. 1; S. D. 640. [0889-b]
