cetas n. [cit karaṇe asun]
      1 Consciousness, sense.
      2 Thinking soul, reasoning faculty.
      3 The mind, heart, soul; cetaḥ prasādayati Bh. 2. 23; gacchati puraḥ śarīraṃ dhāvati paścādasaṃstutaṃ cetaḥ Ś. 1. 34.
      4 Will.
    Comp.
      --janman, --bhavaḥ, --bhūḥ m. {1} love, passion. {2} the god of love.
      --vikāraḥ disturbance of the mind, emotion, agitation. [0493-b]
