• pravrajita p. p.

      2 Turned a recluse.
    • --taḥ
      1 A religious mendicant or ascetic in general.
      2 Especially, a Brāhmaṇa who has entered on the fourth (bhikṣu) order.
      3 The pupil of a Jaina or Buddhist mendicant.
    • --tā
      1 A female ascetic.
      2 A spikenard.
    • --taṃ Turning a recluse, the life of a religious mendicant. [0762-a]
