• bhṛśa a. (compar. bhraśīyas, superl. bhraśiṣṭha) 1 Strong, powerful, mighty, intense excessive, very much. --2 Frequent. --śaṃ ind. 1 Much, very much, exceedingly, intensely, violently, excessively in a high degree, greatly; tamavekṣya ruroda sā bhṛśaṃ Ku. 4. 26; raghurbhṛśaṃ vakṣasi sena tāḍitaḥ R. 3. 61 cukrepa tasmai sa bhṛśaṃ 3. 56; Ms. 7. 170; Rs. 1. 11. --2 Often, repeatedly. --3 In a better or superior manner. --Comp. --kopana a. highly choleric or irascible. --duḥkhita, pīḍita a. exceedingly afflicted. --saṃhṛṣṭa a. very much delighted.
