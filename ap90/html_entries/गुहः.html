• guhaḥ [guh-ka] 1 An epithet of Kārtikeya; guha ivāpratihataśaktiḥ K. 8; Ku. 5. 14. --2 A horse. --3 N. of a Cāṇḍāla or Niṣāda, king of Śṛṅgavera and a friend of Rāma. --4 An epithet of Viṣṇu; also of Śiva. --Comp. --rājaḥ the peculiar form of construction of a temple. --ṣaṣṭhī the sixth day in the first fortnight of mārgaśīrṣa.
