• sādhaka a. [sādh-ṇvul, sidh-ṇic ṇvul sādhādeśaḥ vā Tv.] (dhakā or dhikā f.) 1 Accomplishing, fulfilling, effecting, completing. --2 Efficient, effective; Ku. 3. 12. --3 Skilful, adept. --4 Effecting by magic, magical. --5 Assisting, helping. --kaḥ A magician, one possessed of supernatural powers, a yogin; Māl. 5. 1. --kā N. of Durgā. [1114-c]
