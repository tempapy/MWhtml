• viprakṛ 8 U.
      1 (a) To tease, trouble, harass, harm; kiṃ sattvāni viprakaroṣi Ś. 7; (b) To oppress; tasmin viprakṛtāḥ kāle tārakeṇa divaukasaḥ Ku. 2. 1.
      2 To wrong, ill-treat, offend; Ś. 4. 17.
      3 To affect, cause a change in; kamaparamavaśaṃ naviprakuryurvibhumapi taṃ yadamī spṛśaṃti bhāvāḥ Ku. 6. 95.
      4 To disfigure, deform; ābharaṇocitaṃ rūpamāśramasulabhaiḥ prasādhanairviprakāryate Ś. 4. [0982-b]
