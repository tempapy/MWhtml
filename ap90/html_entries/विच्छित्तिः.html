vicchittiḥ f.
      1 (a) Cutting off or asunder, tearing off; Bh. 3. 11. (b) Breaking off, fracture.
      2 Dividing, separating.
      3 Disappearance, absence, loss.
      4 Cessation.
      5 Colouring the body with paints and unguents, painting colours, rouge; vicchittiśeṣaiḥ surasuṃdarīṇāṃ Ś. 7. 5; Śi. 16. 84.
      6 Limit, boundary (of a house &c.).
      7 A pause in a verse, cæsura.
      8 A particular kind of amorous gesture, consisting in carelessness in dress and decoration (through pride of personal beauty); stokāpyākalparacanā vicchittiḥ kāṃtipoṣakṛt S. D. 138. [0970-c]
