• pratiśāsanaṃ
      1 Giving orders, sending on an errand, ordering.
      2 Ordering or despatching an inferior after calling him to attend.
      3 Counter-manding.
      4 A rival command or authority; apratiśāsanaṃ jagat R. 8. 27 ‘completely under the sway of one ruler’. [0741-c]
