sthapuṭa a. [tiṣṭhati sthā-ka, sthaṃ puṭaṃ yatra]
      1 Being in contracted or difficult circumstances.
      2 Unevenly raised, elevated and depressed.
      3 The soul.
    Comp.
      --gata a. being in contracted or uneven parts, being in difficult places; aṃkasthādasthisaṃstha sthapuṭagatamapi kravyamavyagramatti Māl. 5. 16. [1147-a]
