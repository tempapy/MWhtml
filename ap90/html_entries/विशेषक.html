viśeṣaka a. Distinguishing, distinctive.
      --kaḥ, --kaṃ
      1 A distinguishing feature or characteristic, an attribute.
      2 A discriminative or distinguishing quality.
      3 A mark on the forehead with sandal, saffron &c. M. 3. 5.
      4 Drawing lines of painting on the face and person with coloured unguents and cosmetics; svedodgamaḥ kiṃpuruṣāṃganānāṃ cakra padaṃ patraviśeṣakeṣu Ku. 3. 33, R. 9. 29, Śi. 3. 63, 10. 84. --kaṃ A group of three stanzas forming one grammatical sentence; dvābhyāṃ yugmamiti prāktaṃ tribhiḥ ślokairviśeṣakam . kalāpakaṃ caturbhiḥ syāttadūrdhvaṃ kulakaṃ smṛtam ...
