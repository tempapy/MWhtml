sānu m., n.
      1 A peak, summit, ridge; sānūni gaṃdhaḥ surabhīkaroti Ku. 1. 9; Me. 2; Ku. 1. 5; Ki. 5. 36.
      2 A level ground on the top of a mountain, table-land.
      3 A shoot, sprout.
      4 A forest, wood.
      5 A road.
      6 Any surface, point, end.
      7 A precipice.
      8 A gale of wind.
      9 A learned man.
      10 The sun. [1116-b]
