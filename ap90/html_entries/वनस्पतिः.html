• vanaspatiḥ [vanasya patiḥ ni° suṭ] 1 A large forest tree, especially one that bears fruit apparently without any blossoms. --2 A tree in general; tamāśu vighnaṃ tapasastapasvī vanaspatiṃ vajra ivāvabhajya Ku. 3. 74. --3 The Soma plant. --4 A stem, trunk. --5 A beam; pole, post. --6 A sacrificial post. --7 An offering to Vanaspati. --8 A wooden amulet. --9 A scaffold. --10 An ascetic. --Comp. --kāvaḥ the whole world of plants, vegetable kingdom.
