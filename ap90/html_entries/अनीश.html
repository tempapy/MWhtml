anīśa a.
      1 Having no lord or superior, paramount, supreme, without a controller, uncontrolled; sarvaprabhuranīśastvaṃ R. 10. 20.
      2 Not a master or lord, having no mastery or control over, not master of (with gen.); powerless; gātrāṇāmanīśo'smi saṃvṛttaḥ Ś. 2; anīśayā śarīrasya hṛdayaṃ svavaśaṃ mayi nyastaṃ V. 2. 19; Ms. 9. 104.
      3 Not one's own master, not independent (asvataṃtra); eko hyanīśaḥ sarvatra.
      --śaḥ N. of Viṣṇu (sarvaniyaṃtā ananyasvāmiko hi saḥ).
      --śā Helplessness ( dīnabhāva); samāne vṛkṣe puruṣo nimagno' nīśayā śocati muhyamānaḥ Muṇḍ. [0066-b]
