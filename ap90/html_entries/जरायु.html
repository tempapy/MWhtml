jarāyu n. [jarāmeti i-ñuṇ]
      1 The slough or cast-off skin of a serpent.
      2 The outer skin of the embryo.
      3 After-birth.
      4 Secundines.
      5 The uterus, womb.
    Comp.
      --ja a. born from the womb, viviparous; Ms. 1. 43 and Malli. on Ku. 3. 42. [0508-a]
