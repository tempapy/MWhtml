apogaṃḍaḥ [a-pogaṃḍaḥ, or apasi (vaidha) karmaṇi gaṃḍaḥ tyājyaḥ Tv.]
    • 1 Having a limb too many or too few (redundant or deficient).
    • 2 Not under 16 years of age; bāla āṣoḍaśādvarṣātpogaṃḍaścāpi saṃjñitaḥ . Nārada; Ms. 8. 148; pogaṃḍaḥ paṃcamādabdādarvāk ca daśamābdataḥ .
    • 3 A child or infant (kiśora, śiśu)
    • 4 Very timid;
    • 5 Wrinkled, flaccid.
    • 6 One past 16. [0120-a]
