• praphulla p. p. 1 Full-blown, blossoming, blooming; na hi praphullaṃ sahakārametya vṛkṣāṃtaraṃ kākṣati ṣaṭpadālī R. 6. 69; 2. 29; Ku. 3. 45; 7. 11. --2 Expanded or dilated like a full-blown flower (as eyes). --3 Smiling. --4 Shining. --5 Gay, cheerful, pleased. --Comp. --nayana, --netra, --locana a. with eyes expanded with joy. --vadana a. having a beaming or cheerful countenance, looking cheerful.
