prārthanaṃ --nā
      1 A request, entreaty, prayer, solicitation; ye vardhate dhanapatipuraḥprārthanāduḥkhabhājaḥ Bh. 3. 47.
      2 A wish, desire; labdhāvakāśā me prārthanā or na duravāpeyaṃ khalu prārthana Ś. 1; 2. 1; utsarpiṇī khalu mahatāṃ prārthanā Ś. 7; 7. {2}
      3 A suit, petition, supplication, a love-suit; kadācidasmatprārthanāmaṃtaḥpurebhyaḥ kathayet Ś {2} (the object is expressed by the loc.; as in śakuṃtalāyāṃ prārthanā).
    Comp.
      --bhaṃgaḥ refusal of a request.
      --siddhiḥ f. fulfilment of a desire; prārthanāsiddhiśaṃsinaḥ R. 1. 42. [0775-c]
