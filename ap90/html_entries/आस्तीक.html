• āstīka a. Relating to, or treating of, the sage āstīka. --kaḥ N. of an old saint, son of Jaratkāru; (at whose intercession King Janamejaya spared the Nāga Takṣaka from the destruction to which he had doomed the serpent race). Mb. gives the following etymology of the name; nāma cāsyābhavatkhyātaṃ lokeṣvāstīka ityuta . astītyuktvā gato yasmātpitā garbhasthameva tam ... --kaṃ A section (parva) of the first book of the Mahābhārata.
