tāpaḥ [tap ghañ]
      1 Heat, glow; arkamayūkhatāpaḥ Ś. 4. 10; M. 2. 13; Ms. 12. 76; Ku. 7. 84.
      2 Torment, pain, affliction, misery, agony; itaratāpaśatāni tavecchayā vitara tāni sahe caturānana Udb.; samastāpaḥ kāmaṃ manasijanidāghaprasarayoḥ Ś. 3. 9; Bh. 1. 16.
      3 Sorrow, distress.
    Comp.
      --trayaṃ the three kinds of miseries which human beings have to suffer in this world; i. e. ādhyātmika, ādhidaivika and ādhibhautika.
      --hara a. {1} removing heat, cooling. {2} consoling. [0531-a]
