chaviḥ f. [chyati asāraṃ chinatti tamo vā cho-vi kicc vā ṅīp; cf. Uṇ. 4. 56]

      2 Colour in general.
      3 Beauty, splendour, brilliance; chavikaraṃ mukhacūrṇamṛtuśriyaḥ R. 9. 45.
      4 Light, lustre.
      5 Skin, hide. [0500-c]
