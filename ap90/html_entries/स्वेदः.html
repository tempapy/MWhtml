• svedaḥ [svid-bhāve ghañ]
      1 Sweat, perspiration; aṃgulisvedena dūṣyerannakṣarāṇi V. 2.
      2 Heat, warmth.
      3 Vapour.
    Comp.
    • --udaṃ, --udakaṃ, --jalaṃ perspiration.
    • --cūṣakaḥ a cooling breeze (sucking up sweat).
    • --ja a. generated by warm vapour or sweat (said of insects). [1163-b]
