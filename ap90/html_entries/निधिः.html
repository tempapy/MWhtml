• nidhiḥ [ni-dhā-ādhāre ki] 1 Abode, receptacle, reservoir; jala°, toya°, taponidhi &c. --2 A store-house, treasury. --3 A treasure, store, hoard, (for the nine treasures of Kubera, see navanidhi). --4 The ocean. --5 An epithet of Viṣṇu. --6 A man endowed with many good qualities. --Comp. --īśaḥ, --nāthaḥ an epithet of Kubera.
