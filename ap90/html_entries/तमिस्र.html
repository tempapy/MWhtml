• tamisra a. Dark. --sraṃ 1 Darkness; etattamāladalanīlatamaṃ tamisraṃ Gīt. 11; karacaraṇorasi maṇigaṇabhūṣaṇakiraṇavibhinnatami sraṃ 2.; Ki. 5. 2. --2 Mental darkness, illusion. --3 Anger, wrath. --sraḥ The dark half of the month. --Comp. --pakṣaḥ the dark fortnight (of a lunar month); R. 6. 34.
