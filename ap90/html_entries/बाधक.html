• bādhaka a. (dhikā f.) [bādh ṇvul]
      1 Troubling, tormenting, oppressing.
      2 Vexing, annoying.
      3 Annulling.
      4 Suspending, contradicting, invalidating (as a rule &c.).
      5 Hindering.
    • --kaḥ A particular disease of women (ṛtukāle prajājananaśaktipratirodhakaḥ). [0794-a]
