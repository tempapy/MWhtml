• tānaḥ [tan-ghañ] 1 A thread, fibre. --2 (In music) A protracted tone, a key-note; yathā tānaṃ vinā rāgaḥ Bv. 1. 119; tānapradāyitvamivopagaṃtuṃ Ku. 1. 8. (the number of tānas is said to be 49). --3 A monotonous tone. --naṃ 1 Expanse, extension. --2 An object of sense. --Comp. --karman n. {1} tuning the voice as a preparatory step to singing. {2} running over the notes to catch the key. [0534-c]
