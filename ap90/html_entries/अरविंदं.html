araviṃdaṃ [arān cakrāṃgānīva patrāṇi viṃdate viṃd śa P. III. 1. 138 Vārt.]
    • 1 A lotus (it is one of the 5 arrows of Cupid; see under paṃcabāṇa); śakyamaraviṃdasurabhiḥ Ś. 3. 7. It is a sun lotus; cf. sūryāṃśubhirbhinnamivāraviṃdaṃ Ku. 1. 32; sthala°, caraṇa°, mukha° &c.
    • 2 Also a red or blue lotus.
    • 1 The (Indian) crane.
    • 2 Copper.
      Comp. a. lotus-eyed, an epithet of Viṣṇu.
      copper.
      , --bhaḥ N. of Viṣṇu, from whose navel sprang the lotus which supported Brahmā; hṛdaye madīye devaścakāstu bhagavānaraviṃdanābhaḥ Bv. 4. 8. --sad m. N. of Brahmā. [0160-b]
