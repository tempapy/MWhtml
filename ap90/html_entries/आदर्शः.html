• ādarśaḥ [ādṛśyate'tra, dṛś ādhāre ghañ] 1 A mirror, a looking-glass; ātmānamālokya ca śobhamānamādarśabiṃbe stimitāyatākṣī Ku. 7. 22. --2 The original manuscript from which a copy is taken; (fig.) a pattern, model, type; ādarśaḥ śikṣitānāṃ Mk. 1. 48; ādarśaḥ sarvaśāstrāṇāṃ K. 5; so guṇānāṃ &c. --3 A copy of a work. --4 A commentary, gloss. --5 A particular boundary of a country. --6 N. of a country. --Comp. --maṃḍalaḥ {1} a globular mirror. {2} the surface of a mirror (--laṃ). {3} a kind of snake (with globular spots).
