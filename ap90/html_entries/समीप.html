• samīpa a. [saṃgatā āpo yatra] Near, close by, adjacent, at hand.
    • --paṃ Proximity, vicinity (samīpaṃ, samīpatas and samīpe are used adverbially in the sense of ‘near, before, in the presence of’); ataḥ samīpe pariṇeturiṣyate Ś. 5. 17.
    Comp.
    • --vartin, stha a. adjacent, neighbouring. [1099-b]
