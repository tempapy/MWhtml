tāmra a. [tam-rak dīrghaḥ Uṇ. 2. 16]
      1 Made of copper.
      2 Of a coppery red colour, red; udeti savitā tāmrastāmra evāstameti ca.
      --mraḥ A kind of leprosy with red spots.
      --mraṃ
      1 Copper.
      2 A dark or coppery red.
      --mrī A copper pot having a small hole at the bottom used in measuring time by placing it in a watervessel.
