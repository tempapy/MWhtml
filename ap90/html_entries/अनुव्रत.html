anuvrata a. [anukūlaṃ vrataṃ karma yasya]
      1 Devoted or faithful to, attached to (with acc. or gen.); priyatamā kā anuvratā Bh. 2. 103; vaiśyāḥ kṣatramanuvratāḥ Rām.
      2 Duly performing the vows or duties prescribed (opp. apavrata).
      --taḥ A class of Jaina ascetics. [0082-b]
