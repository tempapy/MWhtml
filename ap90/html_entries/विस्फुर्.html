• visphur 6 P.
      1 To quiver, palpitate, tremble.
      2 To move about, struggle.
      3 To shine, gleam; kiṃ nāma visphurati śastrāṇi U. 4.
      4 To draw or twang (as a bow, used in caus. in the same sense); eko'pi visphuritamaṃḍalacāpacakraṃ kaḥ siṃdhurājamabhiṣeṇayituṃ samarthaḥ Ve. 2. 25. [1001-c]
