• kāliṃga a. (gī f.) [kaliṃga-aṇ] 1 Produced in, or belonging to, the Kaliṅga country. --gaḥ {1} A king of that country; pratijagrāha kāliṃgastamastrairgajasādhanaḥ R. 4. 40. --2 A snake of that country. --3 An elephant. --4 A species of cucumber. --5 A poisonous plant. --6 A sort of iron. --gāḥ (pl.) N. of a country; see kaliṃga. --gaṃ A water-melon. [0400-c]
