bhauta a. (tī f.) [bhūtāni prāṇino'dhikṛtya pravṛttaḥ, tāni devatā vā asya aṇ]
      1 Relating to living beings.
      2 Elemental, material.
      3 Demoniacal.
      4 Mad, crazy.
      --taḥ
      1 A worshipper of demons and spirits.
      2 An attendant upon an idol (devala).
      3 One of the five daily Yajñas to be performed by a householder (also called bhūtayajña, q. v.).
      --taṃ A collection of evil spirits.
      --tī Night. [0827-b]
