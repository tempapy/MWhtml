sādharmyaṃ
      1 Sameness or community of duty, office &c. paṃcamaṃ lokapālānāmūcuḥ sādharmyayogataḥ R. 17. 78.
      2 Sameness of nature, common character, likeness, community of properties; sādharmyamupamā bhede K. P. 10; Bg. 14. 2, Bhāṣā P. 12.
      3 Being of the same religion. [1115-c]
