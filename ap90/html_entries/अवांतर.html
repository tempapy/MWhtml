• avāṃtara a. [avagatamaṃtaraṃ madhyaṃ prā. sa.] 1 Situated or standing between; see compounds. --2 Included, involved. --3 Subordinate, secondary. --4 Not closely connected, extraneous, extra. --Comp. --diś, --diśā an intermediate quarter (such as the āgneyī, aiśānī, nairṛtī and vāyavī). --deśaḥ a place situated between (two others), an intermediate region.
