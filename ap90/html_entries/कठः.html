kaṭhaḥ
    • 1 N. of a sage, pupil of Vaiśampāyana, teacher of that branch of Yajurveda which is called after him.
    • 2 A Brāhmaṇa.
    • 3 A note or simple sound.
    • 4 A kind of ṛk.
      The followers of that sage.
    • 1 A female follower of Kaṭha.
    • 2 The wife of a Brāhmaṇa. Comp. --adhyāpakaḥ a teacher of the Kaṭha branch of the Yajurveda.
      a Brāhmaṇa well-versed in the kaṭha branch of the Yajurveda.
      a Brāhmaṇa who has mastered the kaṭha branch of the Yajurveda. [0370-a]
