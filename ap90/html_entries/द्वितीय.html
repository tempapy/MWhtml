• dvitīya a. Second; tvaṃ jīvitaṃ tvamasi me hṛdayaṃ dvitīyaṃ U. 3. 26; Me. 83; R. 3. 49. --yaḥ 1 The second in a family, a son --2 A companion, partner, friend; (usually at the end of comp.); prayataparigrahadvitīyaḥ R. 1. 95; Ku. 3. 35; so chāyā°, duḥkha° &c. --3 The second letter of a class. --4 The second person (in gram.). --yā 1 The second day of a lunar fortnight. --2 A wife, companion, partner. --3 (In gram.) The accusative case. --yaṃ The half. --yaṃ ind. A second time, again. --Comp. --āśramaḥ the second stage or period of the religious life of a Brāhmaṇa, i. e. gārhasthya. --vayas a. having arrived at the second stage of life. [0588-c]
