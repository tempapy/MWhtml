• jahat a. (tī f.) Leaving, abandoning.
    Comp.
    • --lakṣaṇā, --svārthā a kind of lakṣaṇā (also called lakṣaṇalakṣaṇā) in which a word loses its primary sense, but is used in one which is in some way connected with the primary sense; e. g. in the familiar instance gaṃgāyāṃ ghoṣaḥ ‘a hamlet on the Ganges,’ gaṃgā loses its primary sense and means gaṃgātaṭa; cf. ajahatsvārthā also. {2} irony.
