• viṣuvaṃ The first point of Aries or Libra into which the sun enters at the vernal or autumnal equinox, the equinoctial point.
    Comp.
    • --chāyā the shadow of the gnomon at noon.
    • --dinaṃ the day of the equinox.
    • --rekhā the equinoctial line.
    • --saṃkrāṃtiḥ f. the sun's equinoctial passage. [0999-b]
