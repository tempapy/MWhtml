prasabhaḥ Force, violence, impetuosity; prasabhoddhṛtāriḥ R. 2. 30. --bhaṃ ind.
      1 Violently, forcibly, perforce; iṃdriyāṇi pramāthīni haraṃti prasabhaṃ manaḥ Bg. 2. 60; Ms. 8. 332.
      2 Very much, exceedingly; tavāsmi gītarāgeṇa hāriṇā prasabhaṃ hṛtaḥ Ś. 1. 5; Rs. 6. 25.
      3 Importunately; Bg. 11. 41.
    Comp.
      --damanaṃ subduing by force; Ś. 7. 33. --haraṇaṃ forcible abduction. [0764-c]
