snih 4 P. (snihyati, snigdha)
      1 To feel or have affection for, love, be fond of (with loc. of the person or thing that is loved or liked); kiṃ nu khalu bāle'sminnaurasa iva putre snihyati me manaḥ Ś. 7; sa ca snihyatyāvayoḥ U. 6 (where āvayoḥ may be genitive also).
      2 To be easily attached.
      3 To be pleased with, be kind to.
      4 To be sticky, viscid, or adhesive.
      5 To be smooth or bland. --Caus. (snehayati-te)
      1 To make unctuous, anoint, besmear, lubricate.
      2 To cause to love.
      3 To dissolve, destroy, kill. [1151-c]
