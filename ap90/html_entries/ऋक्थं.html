• ṛkthaṃ [ṛc-thak]
      1 Wealth.
      2 Especially, property, possessions, effects (left at death); see riktha.
      3 Gold.
    Comp.
    • --ādānaḥ an inheritor, heir.
    • --grahaṇaṃ receiving or inheriting property.
    • --grāhaḥ an inheritor or receiver of property.
    • --bhāgaḥ {1} division of property, partition. {2} a share, inheritance.
    • --bhāgin, --hara, --hārin m. {1} an heir. {2} a co-heir. [0347-1]
