• bhāṇaḥ A species of dramatic composition; in it only one character is introduced on the stage which supplies the place of interlocutors by a copious use of ākāśabhāṣita q. v.; bhāṇaḥ syāddhūrtacarito nānāvasthāṃtarātmakaḥ . ekāṃka eka evātra nipuṇaḥ paṃḍito viṭaḥ .. S. D. 513; see the next stanzas also; e. g. vasaṃtatilaka, mukudānaṃda, līlāmadhukara &c. [0812-b]
