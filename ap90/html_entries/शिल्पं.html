śilpaṃ [śil-pak]
      1 An art, a fine or mechanical art; (64 such arts are enumerated).
      2 Skill (in any art); craft; pātraviśeṣanyastaṃ guṇāṃtaraṃ vrajati śilpamādhātuḥ M. 1. 6.
      3 Ingenuity, cleverness.
      4 Work, manual work or labour.
      5 A rite, ceremony.
      6 A kind of ladle or spoon used at sacrifices.
      7 Form, shape.
      8 Creation, procreation.
    Comp.
      --karman, n., --kriyā any manual labour, handicraft.
      --kāraḥ, --kārakaḥ, --kārin m. an artisan, a mechanic.
      --vidyā {1} mechanical science. {2} any manual skill, handicraft.
      --śālaṃ, --lā a workshop, manufactory (a technical school).
      --śāstraṃ {1} a book on any art, fine or mechanical. {2} mechanics. [1046-a]
