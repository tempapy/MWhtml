• upasaṃhṛ {c1c} P. 1 To draw or bring together, collect. --2 To sum or wind up, conclude; idānīmupasaṃharati; prakṛtamupasaṃharati Kull. on Ms. 1. 57. --3 To contract; as kūrmoṃ'gāni. --4 To withdraw, take away, withhold: chettuḥ pārśvagatāṃ chāyāṃ nopasaṃharate drumaḥ H. 1. 59. --5 To check, stop, curb. --6 To absorb, destroy, annihilate.
