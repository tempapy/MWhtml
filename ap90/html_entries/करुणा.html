karuṇā Compassion, pity, tenderness; prāyaḥ sarvo bhavati karuṇāvṛttirārdrāṃtarātmā Me. 93; so sakaruṇa kind; akaruṇa unkind.
      Comp. a. tender-hearted.
      a. tenderhearted, moved with pity, sensitive.
      store of mercy.
      a. very kind.
      a. merciless, cruel; karuṇāvimukhena mṛtyunā R. 8. 67. --svanaḥ --dhvaniḥ a cry of distress, a piteous tone, wail. [0381-a]
