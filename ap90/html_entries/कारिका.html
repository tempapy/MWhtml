• kārikā
      1 A female dancer.
      2 A business, or trade.
      3 A memorial verse, or a collection of such verses, on grammatical, philosophical, or scientific subjects; e. g. Bhartṛhari's Kārikās on grammar; sāṃkhyakārikā.
      4 Torment, torture.
      5 Interest. [0397-a]
