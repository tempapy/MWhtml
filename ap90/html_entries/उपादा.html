• upādā {c3c} A. (p. p. upātta) 1 To receive, accept, take. --2 To acquire, obtain; bhūryā pitāmahopāttā nibaṃdho dravyameva ca Y. 2. 121. --3 To give to, furnish with; Ku. 7. 41. --4 To take, appropriate to oneself, assume; Śi. 6. 23. --5 To take away or off, carry away; steal. --6 To seize, attack; Pt. 3. 154. --7 To take, lay hold of; R. 9. 54; to draw (water). --8 To assume a form --9 To feel, perceive, experience; upāttaharṣaiḥ puṃskokilaiḥ Rs. 6. 21. --10 To consider, regard. --11 To take in addition, include, comprise; atra tailaśabdastilabhavasneharūpamukhyārthamupādāya sārṣapādiranehaṃṣu vartate S. D. 2. --12 To employ, apply, use; yatparasya kutsārthamupādīyate Mbh. --13 To undertake, begin; as in upāttayajñaḥ. --14 To mention, enumerate; iti pūrvasūtropāttānāṃ Sk. --Caus. 1 To cause to use, apply or employ. --2 To make use of.
