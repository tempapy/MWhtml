• auduṃbara a. (rī f.) [udaṃbara-añ] Made of, or coming from, the Udumbara tree --raḥ 1 N. of a region abounding in Udumbara trees. --2 A form of Yama, the god of death. --rī A branch of the Udumbara tree. --raṃ 1 The wood of the Udumbara tree. --2 The Udumbara fruit. --3 A kind of leprosy. --4 Copper.
