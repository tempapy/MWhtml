vitṝ 1 P.
      1 To cross or pass over, go beyond; R. 6. 77.
      2 To give, grant, impart, confer or bestow on, vouchsafe, favour with; bhagavān māraciste darśanaṃ vitarati Ś. 7; vitarati guruḥ prājñe vidyāṃ yathaiva tathā jaḍe U. 2. 4; vitarati nṛpo nocitamahā Pt. 1. 11; U. 3. 48; nivāsahetoruṭajaṃ viteruḥ R. 14. 81; Māl. 1. 3; 6. 7; 9. 5, 44.
      3 To cause, produce; jyotsnāśaṃkāmiha vitarati haṃsaśreṇī Ki. 5. 31.
      4 To carry over, ferry over.
      5 To give (as medicine), apply a remedy.
      6 To do, perform.
      7 To forgive, pardon.
      8 To increase, enhance. --Caus. To carry through, accomplish (fig. also). [0973-c]
