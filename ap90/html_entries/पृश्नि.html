• pṛśni(ṣṇi) a. [spṛa-ni° kicca pṛṣo° salopaḥ; Uṇ. 4. 52]
      1 Short, small, dwarfish.
      2 Delicate, feeble.
      3 Diversified, spotted.
      4 Various, diverse (Ved.).
    • --śniḥ A dwarf.
    • --śni f.
      1 A ray of light.
      2 The earth.
      3 The starry sky.
      4 N. of Devakī, mother of Kṛṣṇa.
      5 The earth.
      6 A cloud.
      7 Milk.
    Comp.
    • --garbhaḥ, --dharaḥ, --bhadraḥ epithets of Kṛshṇa.
    • --śṛṃgaḥ {1} an epithet of Kṛ shṇa. {2} of Gaṇeśa. [0719-b]
