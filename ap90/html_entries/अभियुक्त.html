• abhiyukta p. p.
      1 (a) Engaged or occupied or absorbed in, applying oneself to, intent on; svasvakarmaṇyadhikataramabhiyuktaḥ parijanaḥ Mu. 1. (b) Diligent, persevering, resolute, zealous, intent, assiduous, zealously engaged, attentive, careful; idaṃ viśvaṃ pālyaṃ vidhivadabhiyuktena manasā U. 3. 30; Mu. 1. 13; Dk. 55; aśvāvekṣaṇe' nabhiyukte Mu. 3; bhavatu bhūṃyobhiyuktaḥ svaravyaktimupalapsye Mu. 1; Bg. 9. 22; Kām. 5. 77.
      2 Well-versed or proficient in; śāstrārtheṣvabhiyuktānāṃ puruṣāṇāṃ Kumārila.
      3 (Hence) Learned, of acknowledged position; a competent judge, an expert, connoisseur, a learned person (m. also in this sense); na hi śakyate daivamanyathā kartumabhiyuktenāpi K. 62; anye'bhiyuktā api naivedamanyathā manyaṃte Ve. 2; sūktamidamabhiyuktaiḥ prakṛtirdustyajeti ibid.
      4 Attacked, assailed; abhiyuktaṃ tvayainaṃ te gaṃtārastvāmataḥ pare Śi. 2. 101; Mu. 3. 25.
      5 Accused, charged, indicted; Mk. 9. 9; prosecuted; a defendant; abhiyukto' bhiyogasya yadi kuryādapahnavaṃ Nārada.
      6 Appointed.
      7 Said, spoken. [0136-a]
