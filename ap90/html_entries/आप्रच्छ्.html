āpracch 6 A.
      1 To bid adieu, bid farewell; āpṛcchasva priyasakhamamuṃ tuṃgamāliṃgya śailaṃ Me. 12; R. 8. 49, 12. 103; Māl. 7.; Bk. 14. 63.
      2 To salute on receiving, but particularly parting with a visitor (sādho yāmi iti vacanaṃ).
      3 To ask.
      4 To extol. [0246-a]
