• nirvā {c2c} P. 1 To blow. --2 To be cooled, be cool or assuaged (fig. also); vapurjalārdrāpavanairna nirvavau Śi. 1. 65; tvayi dṛṣṭa eva tasyā nirvāti mano manobhavajvalitaṃ Subhāṣ. --3 To blow out, be extinguished, be extinct; nirvāṇadīpe kimu tailadātaṃ Subhāṣ.; nirvāṇabhūyiṣṭhamathāsya vīryaṃ saṃdhukṣayaṃtīva vapurguṇena Ku. 3. 52; Śi. 14. 85; Mu. 3. 28. --Caus. (vāpayati) 1 To blow or put out, extinguish destroy, kill; R. 3. 58. --2 To cool, alleviate the heat of, act as a refrigerant; sakhi anyādṛśa eva tedya nirvāpayati śarīrasparśaḥ Māl. 6, Ratn. 3. 11; R. 19. 56. --3 To gratify, soothe, comfort; R. 12. 63.
