• ākruś {c1c} P. 1 To cry, cry out loudly; ayaṃ gaurīnātha tripurahara śaṃbho trinayana prasīdetyākrāśan Bh. 3. 123; call out loudly to; patimācukruśuḥ Rām. --2 To revile, abuse, scold, assail with angry words; parasya hetormāmākrośasi Dk. 58, 97; śataṃ brāhmaṇamākruśya kṣatriyo daṃḍamarhati Ms. 8. 267; 6. 48; Bk. 5. 39. --3 To curse. --4 To censure, express displeasure.
