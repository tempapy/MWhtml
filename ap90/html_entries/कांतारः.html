• kāṃtāraḥ, --raṃ
      1 A large or dreary forest; gṛhaṃ tu gṛhiṇīhīnaṃ kāṃtārādatiricyate Pt. 4. 81; Bh. 1. 86; Y. 2. 38.
      2 A bad road.
      3 A hole, cavity.
    • --raḥ
      1 A red variety of the sugarcane.
      2 Mountain ebony.
      3 A bamboo.
    • --rī A kind of sugarcane.
    • --raṃ
      1 A symptom.
      2 A lotus. [0393-b]
