• camaraḥ [cam-arac Uṇ. 3. 31] A kind of deer.
    • --raḥ, --raṃ A chowrie most usually made of the tail of Camara.
    • --rī
      1 A shoot, sprout (maṃjarī).
      2 The female Camara; yasyātharyuktaṃ girirājaśabdaṃ kurvaṃti bālavyajanaiścaptaryaḥ Ku. 1. 13, 48; Śi. 4. 60; Me. 53.
    Comp.
    • --pucchaṃ the tail of a Camara used as a fan. (--cchaḥ) a squirrel. [0485-c]
