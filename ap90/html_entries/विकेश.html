• vikeśa a. (śī f.) 1 Having loose hair. --2 Having no hair, bald (as head). --śī 1 A woman with loose hair. --2 A woman without hair. --3 A small tress of hair tied up separately and then collected into the larger braid or Veṇi. --4 N. of the wife of Śiva in the form of the Earth (one of the 8 forms of Śiva; cf. yāmāhuḥ sarvabhūtaprakṛtiḥ Ś. 1. 1.). [0966-c]
