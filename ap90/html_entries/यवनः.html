(--ṣṭā) {1} the wild datetree. (--ṣṭaṃ) {1} lead. {2} an onion or garlic. {3} pepper. --deśajaṃ benzoin. --dviṣṭaḥ bdellium. --priyaṃ pepper. [0890-a]
