• maṃḍ
      I. 1 P., 10 U. (maṃḍati, maṃḍayatite, maṃḍita)
      1 To adorn, decorate; prabhavati maṃḍayituṃ vadhūranaṃgaḥ Ki. 10. 59; Bk. 10. 23.
      2 To rejoice.
      II. 1 A. (maṃḍate)
      1 To clothe, dress.
      2 To surround, encompass.
      3 To distribute, divide. [0833-b]
