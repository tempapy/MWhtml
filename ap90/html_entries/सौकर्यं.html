• saukaryaṃ [sukarasya bhāvaḥ karma vā ṣyañ]
      1 Hoggishness.
      2 Ease, facility; saukaryaṃ ca kāryasyānāyāseḍha siddhyā sāṃgasiddhyā ca bodhyam.
      3 Practicability, feasibility.
      4 Adroitness, skill.
      5 An easy or extempore preparation of food or medicine. [1140-a]
