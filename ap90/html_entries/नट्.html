• naṭ
      I. 1 P. (naṭati, the na not changed to ṇa after pra in the sense of ‘hurting’)
      1 To dance; yadi manasā naṭanīyaṃ Gīt. 4.
      2 To act.
      3 To injure (by a deceptive trick). --Caus. (nāṭayati-te)
      1 To act, gesticulate, represent dramatically (in dramas); śarasaṃdhānaṃ nāṭayati Ś. 1. &c.
      2 To imitate, copy; sphaṭikakaṭakabhūmirnāṭayatyeṣa śailaḥ… adhigatadhavalimnaḥ śūlapāṇerabhikhyāṃ Śi. 4. 65. (N. B. naṭ forms naṭayati in the sense of ‘causing to dance’; Bh. 3. 126).
      II. 10 U. (nāṭayati-te)
      1 To drop or fall.
      2 To shine.
      3 To injure. [0605-c]
