• mūrcchita p. p. [mūrcchā jātā asya tāra° itac, murch kta vā]
      1 Fainted, swooning, insensible.
      2 Foolish, stupid, silly.
      3 Increased, augmented.
      4 Made violent, intensified.
      5 Perplexed, bewildered.
      6 Filled.
      7 Calcined.
      8 Rising upwards, lofty.
    • --taṃ A kind of song or air. [0873-c]
