• varāka a. (kī f.)
      1 Poor, pitiable, miserable, wretched, unhappy, unfortunate (often used to show pity); tanmayā na yuktaṃ kṛtaṃ yatsa varāko' pamānitaḥ Pt. 1; tatkimujjihānajīvitāṃ varākīṃ nānukaṃpase Māl. 10.
      2 Low, vile.
      3 Impure.
    • --kaḥ
      1 N. of Śiva.
