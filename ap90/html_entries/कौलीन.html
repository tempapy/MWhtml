• kaulīna a. [kau pṛthivyāṃ līnaḥ aluksa°; kulādāgataḥ khañ vā] Belonging to a noble family. --naḥ 1 The son of a female beggar. --2 A follower of the left hand Śākta ritual. --naṃ 1 An evil report, a scandal; mālavikāgataṃ kimapi kaulīnaṃ śrūyate M. 3; tadeva kaulīnamiva pratibhāti V. 2; Me. 112; kaulīnamātmāśrayamācacakṣe R. 14. 36, 84. --2 An improper act, bad or scandalous conduct; khyāte tasmin vitamasi kule janma kaulīnametat Ve. 2. 10. --3 A combat of animals. --4 Cock-fighting. --5 War, battle (in general). --6 High birth. --7 A privity, the pudenda. [0428-c]
