• sphāy 1 A. (sphāyate, sphīta)
      1 To grow large or fat, to become big or bulky.
      2 To swell, increase, expand; saṃdudhukṣe tayoḥ kopaḥ pasphāye śastralāghavaṃ Bk. 14. 109. --Caus. ( sphāvayati-te) To cause to grow large, augment, increase; tāvasphāvayatāṃ śaktīrbāṇāṃścākiratāṃ muhuḥ Bk. 17. 43, 4. 33, 12. 76, 15. 99. [1153-c]
