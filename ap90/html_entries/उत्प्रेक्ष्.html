• utprekṣ {c1c} A. [utpra-īkṣ] 1 To look up to; Pt. 1. to expect; K. 35, 229; to anticipate, see in prospect; utprekṣamāṇā jaghanābhighātaṃ Mu. 2. --2 To see, perceive, behold. --3 To guess, conjecture; bhagavati kimutprekṣase kutastyoyamiti U. 4, 6; K. 67, 121, 198-9. --4 To believe, fancy; K. 108; Ve. 2. 9; utprekṣāmo vayaṃ tāvanmatimaṃtaṃ bibhīṣaṇaṃ Rām. --5 To remember, think of; Amaru. 38; U. 6. 37. --6 To transfer (with loc.). --7 To illustrate by a supposed simile; see utpretkṣā below.
