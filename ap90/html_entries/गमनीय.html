gamanīya pot. p.
      1 Accessible, approachable; vikārasya gamanīyāsmi saṃvṛttā Ś. 1.
      2 Intelligible, easy to be comprehended.
      3 Fit to be practised or observed.
      4 Relating to sexual intercourse; gurustrī° Ms. 11. 103 (pāpaṃ); for other senses see gamya. [0452-b]
