tamālaḥ
      1 N. of a tree with a very dark bark; taruṇatamālanīlabahalonnamadaṃbudharāḥ Māl. 9. 18; R. 13. 15, 49; Gīt. 11.
      2 A sectarial mark of sandal upon the forehead.
      3 A sword, scimitar.
      4 The bark of the bamboo.
    Comp.
      --patraṃ {1} a sectarial mark upon the forehead. {2} a Tamāla leaf. [0531-c]
