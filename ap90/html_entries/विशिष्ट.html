viśiṣṭa p. p.
      1 Distinguished, distinct.
      2 Particular, special, peculiar, distinctive.
      3 Characterized by, endowed with, possessed of, having.
      4 Superior, best (of all), eminent, excellent, choice; viśiṣṭāyā viśeṣeṇa saṃgamo guṇavān bhavet Mb. --ṣṭaḥ N. of Viṣṇu. Comp. --advaitavādaḥ a doctrine of Rāmānuja which regards Brahman and Prakṛti as identical and real entities.
      --buddhiḥ f. a distinguishing knowledge, differentiation.
      --varṇa a. of an eminent or excellent colour. [0995-a]
