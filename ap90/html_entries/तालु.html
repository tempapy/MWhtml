• tālu n. [taraṃtyanena varṇāḥ tṝ-aṇ rasya laḥ; cf. Uṇ. 1. 5] The palate; tṛṣā mahatyā pariśuṣkatālavaḥ Rs. 1. 11.
    Comp.
    • --jihvaḥ {1} a crocodile. {2} the uvula.
    • --pākaḥ an abscess in the palate.
    • --puppuṭaḥ an indolent swelling of the palate.
    • --mūlaṃ the root or back-part of the palate.
    • --sthāna a. palatal. (--naṃ) the palate. [0537-a]
