unmeṣaḥ, --ṣaṇaṃ

    • 3 Light, flash, brilliancy; satāṃ prajñonmeṣaḥ Bh. 2. 114 light or flash; vidyudunmeṣadṛṣṭiṃ Me. 81.
    • 4 Awakening, rising, becoming visible, manifestation; jñāna° Śānti. 3. 13. [0314-c]
