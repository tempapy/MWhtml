• vāha a. [vah-ghañ] Bearing, carrying &c. (at the end of comp.); as in aṃbuvāha, toyavāha &c.
    • --haḥ
      1 Carrying, bearing.
      2 A porter.
      3 A draught-animal, a beast of burden.
      4 A horse; R. 4. 56, 5. 73, 14. 52.
      5 A bull; Ku. 7. 49.
      6 A buffalo.
      7 A carriage, conveyance in general.
      8 The arm.
      9 Air, wind.
      10 A measure equal to ten Kumbhas or four Bhāras; vāho bhāracatuṣṭayaṃ.
    Comp.
    • --dviṣat m. a buffalo.
    • --śreṣṭhaḥ a horse. [0964-b]
