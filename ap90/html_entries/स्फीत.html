sphīta p. p. [sphāy-kta sphībhāvaḥ]
      1 Swollen, increased; Ve. 5. 40.
      2 Fat, thick, big, large, bulky.
      3 Many, numerous, much, copious, plentiful, abundant.
      4 Delighted, joyful.
      5 Pure; Bv. 4. 13.
      6 Successful, prosperous, thriving.
      7 Affected by hereditary disease. ( sphītīkṛ means ‘to augment or enlarge’). [1154-a]
