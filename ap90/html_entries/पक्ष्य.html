• pakṣya a. [pakṣe bhavaḥ, yat]
      1 Produced or occurring in a fortnight.
      2 Siding with.
      3 Lateral.
      4 Changing every half month.
    • --kṣyaḥ A partisan, follower, friend, ally; nanu vajriṇa eva vīryametadvijayaṃte dviṣato yadasya pakṣyāḥ V. 1. 16. [0655-a]
