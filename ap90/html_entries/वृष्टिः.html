• vṛṣṭiḥ f. [vṛṣ-ktin] 1 Rain, a shower of rain; ādityājjāyate vṛṣṭirvṛṣṭerannaṃ tataḥ prajāḥ Ms. 3. 76. --2 A shower (of anything); astravṛṣṭi R. 3. 58; puṣpavṛṣṭi 2. 60; so śara°, dhana°, upala° &c. --Comp. --kālaḥ the rainy season. --jīvana a. nourished or watered by rain (as a country); cf. devamātṛka. (--naḥ) the Cātaka bird. --bhūḥ a frog. --saṃpātaḥ a shower of rain. [1008-c]
