• muh {c4c} P. (muhyati, mugdha or mūḍha) 1 To faint, swoon, lose consciousness, become senseless; ihāhaṃ draṣṭumāhnaṃ tāṃ smarannevaṃ mumoha saḥ Bk. 6. 21, 1. 20; 15. 16. --2 To be perplexed or bewilddered, to be disturbed in mind, be at a loss; āpatsvapi na muhyaṃti narāḥ paṃḍitabuddhayaḥ H. 1. 166, Ki. 18. 9. --3 To be foolish, stupid, or infatuated. --4 To fail. --5 To err, mistake. --Caus. (mohayati-te) 1 To stupefy, infatuate; mā mūmuhatkhalu bhavaṃtamananyajanmā Māl. 1. 32. --2 To confound, bewilder, perplex; Bg. 3. 2, 4. 16. --3 To throw into confusion. --4 To cause to err or mistake.
