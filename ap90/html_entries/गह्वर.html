gahvara a. (rā or rī f.). Deep, impervious.
      --raṃ
      1 An abyss, a depth.
      2 A thicket, forest.
      3 A cave, cavern; gaurīgurorgahvaramāviveśa R. 2. 26, 46; Rs. 1. 21.
      4 An inaccessible place.
      5 A hiding-place.
      6 A riddle.
      7 Hypocrisy.
      8 Weeping, crying.
      9 Water.
      10 A deep sigh.
      --raḥ An arbour, bower.
      --rī A cave, cavern, recess in a rock or mountain. [0456-b]
