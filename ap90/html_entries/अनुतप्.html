• anutap 1 P.
      1 To heat; vex, annoy (fig.).
      2 (4 A. or pass.) To repent, grieve, be sorry for, be stung with remorse; vividhairanutapyaṃte dayitānunayairmanasvinyaḥ V. 3. 5 (v. l. for nibhṛtairvyapatrapaṃte &c.); Ki. 17. 40. --Caus. To pain, afflict, distress virahaḥ kimivānutāpayedvada bāhyairviṣayairvipaścitaṃ R. 8. 89. [0070-b]
