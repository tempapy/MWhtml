mud

      1 To mix, blend.

      mud, mudā f. [mud-kvip vā ṭāp] Joy, delight, pleasure, gladness, satisfaction; piturmudaṃ tena tatāna so'rbhakaḥ R. 3. 25; aśnan puro haritako mudamādadhānaḥ Śi. 5. 58; 1. 23; viṣāde kartavye vidadhati jaḍāḥ pratyuta mudaṃ Bh. 3. 25; dviparaṇamudā Gīt. 11; Ki. 5. 25; R. 7. 30. [0870-c]
