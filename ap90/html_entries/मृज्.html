mṛj
      I. 1 P. (mārjati) To sound.
      II. 2 P., 10 U. (mārṣṭi, mārjayati te, mamārjaṃ mārjayāṃcakāra-cakre, amārjit-amārkṣīt, amamārjat-ta mārjiṣyati, mārkṣyati, mārjayiṣyatite, mārjituṃ-mārṣṭuṃ, mārjayituṃ, mṛṣṭa, marjita desid. mimṛkṣati or mimārjiṣati)
      1 To wipe or wash off, cleanse, clean, sweep clean (fig. also); svedalavānmamārja Śi. 3. 79, doṣapravādamamṛjat 5. 28.
      2 To rub, stroke.
      3 To make smooth, curry (as a horse).
      4 To dock, adorn.
      5 To purify, wash with water, sharpen; lalu khaḍgān mamārjuśca mamṛjuśca paraśvadhān Bk. 14. 92 (śuddhān cakruḥ or śodhitavaṃtaḥ). [0876-a]
