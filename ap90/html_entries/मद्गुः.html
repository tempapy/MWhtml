madguḥ [masj-u nyaṃkvā°; cf. Uṇ. 1. 7]
      1 A kind of aquatic bird, a cormorant or diver.
      2 A kind of snake.
      3 A kind of wild animal.
      4 A kind of galley or vessel of war; kopi madgurabhyadhāvat Dk.
      5 N. of a degraded mixed tribe, the offspring of a Brāhmaṇa by a woman of the bard class; see Ms. 10. 48.
      6 An outcast. [0837-b]
