maṃḍana a. [maṃḍayati maṃḍa-lyu lyuṭ vā]
      1 Adorning, decorating.
      2 Fond of ornaments.
      --naṃ
      1 The act of decorating or ornamenting, adorning; māma kṣamaṃ maṃḍanakālahāneḥ R. 13. 16; maṃḍanavidhiḥ Ś. 6. 5.
      2 An ornament, decoration, embellishment; sā maṃḍanānmaṃḍanamanvabhuṃkta Ku. 7. 5; Ki. 8. 40; R. 8. 71. --naḥ (or maṃḍananiśraḥ) N. of a philosopher who is said to have been defeated in controversy by Śaṅkarācārya. [0833-b]
