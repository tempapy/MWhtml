• ayuj a. Not being in couples, odd, uneven (opp. yuj even); ayuji nayugarephato yakāro yuji tu najau jaragāśca puṣpitāgrā V. Ratn. --Comp. --iṣuḥ, --bāṇaḥ, --śaraḥ N. of Cupid (having {5} arrows). --chadaḥ = saptavarṇa (Mar. sātavaṇa); vavurayukchadagucchasugaṃdhayaḥ Śi. 6. 50. --palāśaḥ = saptapalāśa. --pādayamakaṃ a kind of alliteration having the same syllables (in a different sense) in the first and third pādas. --netra, --locana, --akṣa, --śaktiḥ N. of Śiva.
