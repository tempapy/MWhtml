• pratibuddha p. p.
      1 Awakened, roused, wide awake; pratibuddhāpi kiṃ kareṣyāmi Ś. 4; Māl. 4, Ms. 1. 74.
      2 Restored to consciousness.
      3 Opened, blown; apratibuddho'pi cūtaprasavo' trabaṃdhanabhaṃgasurabhirbhavati Ś. 6.
      4 Illuminated, enlightened.
      5 Great, exalted.
      6 Recognized, observed.
      7 Celebrated, known. [0739-c]
