tij
      I. 1 A. (Strictly desid. of tij) (titikṣate, titikṣita)
      1 To endure, bear.
      2 To put up with, suffer patiently or with courage; titikṣamāṇasya pareṇa niṃdāṃ M. 1. 17; tāṃstitikṣasva bhārata Bg. 2. 14; Mv. 2. 12; Ki. 13. 68; Ms. 6. 47.
      II. 10 U. or Caus. (tejayati-te, tejita)
      1 To sharpen, whet; kusumacāpamatejayadaṃśubhiḥ R. 9. 39.
      2 To stir up, excite, instigate. [0537-c]
