• pāṣāṇaḥ [pināṣṭi piṣ saṃcūrṇane ānac pṛṣo° Tv.] A stone. --ṇī 1 A small stone used as a weight. --2 A spear. --Comp. --gardabhaḥ a hard swelling on the maxillary joint. --caturdaśī N. of a festival on the 14th day of the month of Mārgaśīrṣa, in honour of Gaurī. --dārakaḥ, --dāraṇaḥ a stone-cutter's chisel. --saṃdhiḥ a cave or chasm in a rock. --hṛdaya a. stone-hearted, cruel, relentless. [0699-c]
