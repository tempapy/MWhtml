• bhāmaḥ [bhām-ghañ]
      1 Brightness, lustre, splendour.
      2 The sun
      3 Passion, wrath, anger.
      4 A sister's husband.
    • --mā
      1 A passionate woman.
      2 N. of one of the wives of Kṛṣṇa, usually called satyabhāmā.
    Comp.
    • --nī m. the Supreme being. [0813-a]
