• kūrmaḥ [kau jale ūrmirvego'sya pṛṣo° Tv.] 1 A tortoise; gūhetkūrma ivāṃgāni rakṣedvivaramātmanaḥ Ms. 7. 105; Bg. 2. 58. --2 Viṣṇu in his second or Kūrma incarnation. --3 One of the outer winds of the body. --4 A particular gesticulation with the fingers. --rmī A female tortoise. --Comp. --avatāraḥ the Kūrma incarnation of Viṣṇu; cf. Gīt. 1: --kṣitirativipulatare tava tiṣṭhati pṛṣṭhe dharaṇidharaṇakiṇacakragariṣṭhe . keśava dhṛtakacchaparūpa jaya jagadīśa hare ... --pṛṣṭhaṃ --pṛṣṭhakaṃ {1} the back or shell of a tortoise. {2} a lid or cover of a dish. --rājaḥ Viṣṇu in the shape of a tortoise in his second incarnation.
