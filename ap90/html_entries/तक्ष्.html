• takṣ {c1c} {c5c} P. (takṣati, takṣṇoti, taṣṭa) 1 To chop, cut off, pare, chisel, slice, split; ātmānaṃ takṣati hyeṣa vanaṃ paraśunā yathā Mb.; nidhāya takṣyate yatra kāṣṭhe kāṣṭhaṃ sa udghanaḥ Ak. --2 To fashion, shape, form (out of wood &c.). --3 To make, create in general. --4 To wound, hurt. --5 To invent, form in the mind. --6 To make one's own, appropriate. --7 To cover. --8 To peel. --9 To make thin. --WITH nis 1 to slice out of. --2 to form, create. [0525-a]
  • takṣ a. (At the end of comp.) Paring, cutting &c. also takṣa. [0525-a]
