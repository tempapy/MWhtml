vaṃjula a. Crooked.
      --laḥ [vaṃc-ulac pṛṣo° casya jaḥ Tv.]
      1 The common cane or reed; āmaṃjuvaṃjulalatāni ca tānyamūni nīraṃdhranīlaniculāni sarittaṭāni U. 2. 23; or maṃjulavaṃjulakuṃjagataṃ vicakarṣa kareṇa dukūle Gīt. 1.
      2 A kind of flower.
      3 The Aśoka tree.
      4 A kind of bird.
      --lā A cow that yields much milk.
    Comp.
      --drumaḥ the Aśoka tree.
      --priyaḥ the ratan. [0940-a]
