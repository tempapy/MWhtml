• 1 udan 2 P. (ud-an) To breathe upwards, send out the breath in an upward direction (opp. apān). [0301-c]
    • 2 udan n. [uṃd-kanin] Water (usually occurring in compounds either at the beginning or at the end, and as an optional substitute for udaka after the acc. dual. It has no forms for the first five inflections. In comp. it drops its n); e. g. udadhi, acchoda, kṣīroda &c.
