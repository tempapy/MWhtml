oṣadhiḥ, --dhī f. [oṣaḥ pāko dhīyate atra, -dhā ki Tv.]
      1 A herb, plant (in general); oṣadhyaḥ phalapākāṃtāḥ Ms. 1. 46; cf. saṃjīvana°.
      2 A medicinal plant or drug.
      3 An annual plant or herb which dies after becoming ripe.
    Comp.
      --īśaḥ,--garbhaḥ, --nāthaḥ the moon (as presiding over and feeding plants); cf. puṣṇāmi cauṣadhīḥ savoḥ somo bhūtvā rasātmakaḥ Bg. 15. 13; R. 2. 73: Ku. 7. 1; Ś. 4. 2. --ja a. produced from plants. (--jaḥ) fire.
      --dharaḥ, --patiḥ {1} a dealer in medicinal drugs. {2} a physician. {3} the moon; Śi. 9. 36 (where it means ‘physician’ also).
      --prasthaḥ the capital of Himālaya; tatprayātauṣadhiprasthaṃ sthitaye himavatpuraṃ Ku. 6. 33, 36. [0361-b]
