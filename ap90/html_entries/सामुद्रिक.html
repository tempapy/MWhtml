sāmudrika a. (kī f.) [samudreṇa proktaṃ vettyadhīte vā ṭhañ]
      1 Sea-born, oceanic.
      2 Relating to marks on the body (which are supposed to indicate good or bad fortune).
      --kaḥ
      1 One who is acquainted with palmistry, who knows how to interpret the various marks on the body.
      2 A fortune-teller.
      --kaṃ The science of palmistry. [1118-a]
