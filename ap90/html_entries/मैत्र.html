• maitra a. (trī f.) [mitra-aṇ] 1 Belonging to a friend. --2 Given by a friend. --3 Friendly, well-disposed, amicable, kind; Ms. 2. 87; Bg. 12. 13. --4 Relating to the god Mitra (as a Muhūrta); Ku. 7. 6. --traḥ 1 A high or perfect Brāhmaṇa. --2 N. of a particular mixed tribe; Ms. 10. 23. --3 The anus. --4 A friend. --trī 1 Friendship, good will. --2 Intimate connection or association, union, contact; pratyūṣeṣu sphuṭitakatalāmodamaitrīkaṣāyaḥ Me. 31. --3 The lupar mansion called anurādhā. --traṃ 1 Friendship. --2 Voiding or evacuation of excrement; Ms. 4. 152. --3 A prayer addressed to Mitra. --4 The lunar mansion anurādhā (maitrabhaṃ) in the same sense.
