• dhāraṇa a. (ṇī f.) [dhṛ-ṇic-lyuṭ] Holding, bearing, carrying, preserving, sustaining, protecting, having, assuming, &c. --ṇau (du.) The two female breasts. --ṇaṃ 1 The act of holding, bearing, supporting, preserving or keeping back. --2 Possessing, possession. --3 Observing; holding fast. --4 Retaining in the memory; grahaṇadhāraṇapaṭurbālakaḥ. --5 Being indebted (to any one.) --6 Steady abstraction of the mind. --7 Keeping, maintaining. --8 Restraining. --9 (In gram.) Pronouncing imperfectly, --ṇī 1 A row or line. --2 A vein or tubular vessel. --3 Steadiness. [0596-a]
