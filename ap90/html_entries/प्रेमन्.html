preman m., n. [priyasya bhāvaḥ imanica prādeśaḥ ekāckatvāt na ṭilopaḥ Tv.]
      1 Love, affection; tatpremahemanikaṣopalatāṃ tanoti Gīt. 11; Me. 44.
      2 Favour, kindness, kind or tender regard.
      3 Sport, pastime.
      4 Joy, delight, gladness. --m.
      1 A jest, joke.
      2 Wind, air.
      3 An epithet of Indra.
    Comp.
      --aśru n. a tear of joy or affection.
      --ṛddhiḥ f. increase of affection, ardent love.
      --para a. affectionate, loving.
      --pātanaṃ {1} tears (of joy). {2} the eye (that sheds them).
      --pātraṃ ‘an object of love, any beloved person or thing.
      --baṃdhaḥ baṃ dhanaṃ a bond or tie of affection, --bhāvaḥ affection, love. [0779-b]
