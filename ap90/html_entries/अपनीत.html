• apanīta p. p.
      1 Taken away; removed, pulled off &c.
      2 Removed from, deviating from, opposed to, contradictory; tattvadharmāpanītasya dṛśyate karmaṇaḥ phalaṃ Rām.
      3 Badly done or executed, spoiled; apanītaṃ sunītena yo'rthaṃ pratyāninīṣate Mb.
      4 Paid off, discharged.
    • --taṃ Bad conduct. [0107-c]
