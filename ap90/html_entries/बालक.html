bālaka a. (likā f.) [bāla svārthe ka]
      1 Childlike, young, not yet fullgrown.
      2 Ignorant.
      --kaḥ
      1 A child, boy.
      2 A minor (in law).
      3 A finger-ring.
      4 A fool or blockhead.
      5 A bracelet.
      6 The tail of a horse or elephant.
      7 Hair.
      --kaṃ
      1 A finger-ring.
      2 A bracelet.
    Comp.
      --priya a. fond of children. (--yā) colocynth or plantain. --hatyā infanticide. [0795-a]
