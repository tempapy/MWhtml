• āho ind. An interjection expressing (a) Doubt or alternative (or), and usually standing as a correlative of kiṃ; kiṃ vaikhānasaṃ vrataṃ niṣevitavyaṃ… āho nivatsyati samaṃ hariṇāṃganābhiḥ Ś. 1. 27; dāratyāgī bhavāmyāho parastrīsparśapāṃsulaḥ Ś. 5. 29. (b) Interrogation.
