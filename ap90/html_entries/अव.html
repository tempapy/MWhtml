• ava ind. (the initial a is sometimes dropped, as in pūrvāparau toyanidhī vagāhya Ku. 1. 1) 1 (As a preposition) Away, off, away from, down. --2 (As a prefix to verbs) It expresses (a) determination; avadhṛ, avaso; (b) diffusion, pervasion; avakṝ, --kīrṇa; (c) disrespect; avajñā, avaman; (d) littleness; vrīhīnavahaṃti; (e) support, resting upon; avalaṃb; (f) purification, avadāta; (g) depreciation, discomfiture; avahaṃti śatrūn (parābhavati); (h) commanding; avakḷp; (i) depression, bending down; avatṝ, avagāh; (j) knowledge; avagam, avai. --3 As the first member of Tat. compounds it means avakruṣṭa; avakokilaḥ = avakruṣṭaḥ kokilayā Sk.
