• maṃtrin a. [maṃtrayate maṃtr ṇini]
      1 Wise, clever in counsel.
      2 Familiar with sacred texts or spells.
      3 Ved Eloquent. --m.
      1 A minister, coun- sellor, a king's minister; R. 8. 17. Ms. 8. 1.
      2 A conjurer, an enchanter.
    Comp.
    • --dhura a. able to bear the burden of a minister's office.
    • --patiḥ, --pradhānaḥ --pramukhaḥ, --mukhyaḥ, varaḥ, --śreṣṭhaḥ the prime minister, premier. --prakāṃḍaḥ an excellent or eminent minister.
    • --śrotrayaḥ a minister conversant with the Vedas. [0843-b]
