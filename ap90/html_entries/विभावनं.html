• vibhāvanaṃ --nā 1 Clear perception or ascertainment, discrimination, judgment. --2 Discussion, investigation, examination. --3 Conception, imagination. --nā (In Rhet.) A figure of speech in which effects are represented as taking place though their usual causes are absent; kriyāyāḥ pratiṣedhepi phalavyaktirvibhāvanā K. P. 10. [0985-a]
