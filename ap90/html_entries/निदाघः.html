• nidāghaḥ [ni-dah-ādhāre ghañ nyaṃkvādi kutvaṃ] 1 Heat, warmth. --2 The hot season, summer (the months of jyeṣṭha and āṣāḍha); nidāghamihirajvālāśataiḥ Bv. 1. 16; nidāghakālaḥ samupāgataḥ priye Rs. 1. 1; Pt. 1. 104; Ku. 7. 84. --3 Sweat, perspiration. --4 The water of perspiration. --Comp. --karaḥ the sun. --kālaḥ summer. --siṃdhuḥ a river in hot season, (nearly dry).
