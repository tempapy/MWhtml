• prakaṭa a
      1 Evident, plain, clear, apparent, manifest.
      2 Undisguised, public.
      3 Visible.
    • --ṭaṃ ind.
      1 Clearly, manifestly; evidently.
      2 Publicly, openly, undisguisedly ( prakaṭīkṛ to manifest, unfold, display; guhyaṃ ca gūhati guṇān prakaṭīkaroti Bh. 2 72; Pt. 1. 31; prakaṭībhū ‘to become manifest, appear’). Comp. --prītivardhanaḥ an epithet of Śiva. [0723-b]
