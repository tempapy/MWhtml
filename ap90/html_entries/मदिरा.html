• madirā
      1 Spirituous liquor; kāṃkṣatyanyo vadanamadirāṃ dohadacchadmanāsyāḥ Me. 78; Śi. 11. 49.
      2 A kind of wagtail.
      3 N. of Durgā. Comp. --utkaṭa, --unmatta a. intoxicated with spirituous liquor.
    • --gṛhaṃ, --śālā an alehouse, dram-house, a tavern.
    • --sakhaḥ the mango tree. [0837-a]
