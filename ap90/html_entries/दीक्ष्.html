• dīkṣ {c1c} A. (dīkṣate, dīkṣita) 1 To consecrate or prepare oneself for the performance of a sacred rite; see dīkṣita below. --2 To dedicate oneself to. --3 To initiate or introduce a pupil. --4 To invest with the sacred thread. --5 To sacrifice. --6 To practise self-restraint. --7 To shave one's head, to be shaved.
