• paitra a. (trī f.) [pituridaṃ aṇ] 1 Relating to a father or ancestors generally, paternal, ancestral. --2 Sacred to the Manes. --traṃ 1 The part of the hand between the forefinger and the thumb. (Also paitrpa in this sense). --2 A year, month, or day sacred to the Pitṛs. [0720-c]
