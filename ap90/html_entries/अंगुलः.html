• aṃgulaḥ [aṃg-ul] 1 A finger. --2 The thumb, aṃgau pāṇau līyate (n. also). --3 A finger's breadth (n. also), equal to 8 barley-corns, 12 Aṅgulas making a vitasti or span, and 24, a hasta or cubit; śaṃkurdaśāṃgulaḥ Ms. 8. 271. --4 (Astr.) A digit or 12th part. --5 N. of the sage Chāṇakya or Vātsyāyana. [0021-a]
