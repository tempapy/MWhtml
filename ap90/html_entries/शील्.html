• śīl
      I. 1 P. (śīlati)
      1 To meditate, contemplate.
      2 To serve, honour, worship.
      3 To do, practise.
      II. 10 U. (śīlayati-te)
      1 To honour, worship.
      2 To practise repeatedly, exercise, study, think of, ponder over; śrutiśatamapi bhūyaḥ śīlitaṃ bhārataṃ vā Bv. 2. 35; śīlayati munayaḥ suśīlatāṃ Ki. 13. 43.
      3 To put on, wear; cala sakhi kujaṃ satimirapuṃjaṃ śīlaya nīlanicīlaṃ Gīt. 5.
      4 To go to, visit, frequent; yadanugamanāya niśi gahanamapi śīlitaṃ Gīt. 7; smerānatā sapadi śīlaya saudhamauliṃ Bv. 2. 4. --WITH anu, --pari to practise repeatedly, cultivate, think of; śaśvacchruto'si manasā pariśīlito'pi Rāj. P. [1048-c]
