nihnavaḥ
      1 Denial, concealment of knowledge; kāryaḥ svamatinihnavaḥ Māl. 1. 12; Chandr. 5. 27.
      2 Secrecy, concealment in general; Y. 2. 11 267.
      3 A secret.
      4 Mistrust; doubt, suspicion.
      5 Wickedness.
      6 Atonement, expiation.
      7 Excuse, exculpation.
    Comp.
      --uttaraṃ an evasive reply.
      --vādin m. a defendant or witness who prevaricates or gives evasive replies. [0644-c]
