• vyāghraḥ [vyājighrati, vyāghrā-ka] 1 A tiger. --2 (At the end of comp.) Best, pre-eminent, chief; as in naravyāghra, puruṣavyāghra. --3 The red variety of the castor-oil plant. --ghrī A tigress; vyāghrīva tiṣṭhati jarā paritarjayaṃtī Bh. 3. 109. --Comp. --aṭaḥ a skylark. --āsyaḥ a cat. --dalaḥ, --pucchaḥ the castor-oil plant. --nakhaḥ -khaṃ {1} a tiger's claw. {2} a kind of perfume. {3} a scratch, the impression of a finger-nail. --nāyakaḥ a jackal. --pāda (d) a. tiger-footed. --śvan m. a tiger-like dog.
