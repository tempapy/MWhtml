• nepathyaṃ 1 Decoration, an ornament. --2 Dress, apparel, costume, attire; udāranepathyabhṛt R. 6. 6; rājeṃdranepathyavidhānaśobhā 14. 9; ujjvalanepathyaviracanā Māl. 1; Ku. 7. 7; V. 5. --3 Particularly, the costume of an actor; viralanepathyayoḥ pātrayoḥ praveśostu M. 1. --4 The tiring-room, the space where the actors attire themselves (which is always behind the curtain), the postscenium; nepathye ‘behind the scenes’. --Comp. --vidhānaṃ arrangements of the tiring-room; yadi nepathyavidhānamavasitaṃ Ś. 1.
