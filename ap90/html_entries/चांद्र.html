• cāṃdra a. (drī f.) [caṃdrasyedam aṇ] Relating to the moon, lunar; gurukāvyānugāṃ bibhraccāṃdrīmabhinabhaḥ śriyaṃ Śi. 2. 2. --draḥ
      1 A lunar month.
      2 The bright fortnight (śuklapakṣa).
      3 The moon-stone.
    • --draṃ
      1 The vow called cāṃdrāyaṇa q. v.
      2 Fresh ginger.
      3 The lunar mansion called mṛgaśīrṣa.
    • --drī Moonlight.
    Comp.
    • --ākhyaṃ fresh ginger.
    • --bhāgā the river Chandrabhāgā.
    • --māsaḥ a lunar month. --vratikaḥ one who observes the cāṃdrāyaṇa vow q. v. [0490-a]
