• pretya ind. Having departed (from this world), after death, in the next world; na ca tatpretya no iha Bg. 17. 28; Ms. 2. 9, 26.
    Comp.
    • --jātiḥ f. position in the world to come.
    • --bhāj a. enjoying the fruits of actions in the next world.
    • --bhāvaḥ the condition of soul after death. [0779-a]
