ālasya a. Idle, slothful, apathetic.
      --syaṃ [alasasya bhāvaḥ, ṣyañ] Idleness, sloth, want of energy; śaktasya cāpyanutsāhaḥ karmasvālasyamucyate Suśr.; ālasya ‘want of energy’ is regarded as one of the 33 subordinate feelings (vyabhicāribhāva); for example: --na tathā bhūṣayatyaṃgaṃ na tathā bhāṣate sakhīṃ . jṛṃbhate muhurāsīnā bālā garbhabharālasā S. D. 183. [0258-c]
