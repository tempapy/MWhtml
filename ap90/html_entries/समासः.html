• samāsaḥ
      1 Aggregation, union, composition.
      2 Composition of words, a compound; (the principal kinds of compounds are four:--dvaṃdva, tatpuruṣa, bahuvrīhi, and avyayībhāva q. q. v. v.).
      3 Reconciliation, composition of differences.
      4 A collection, an assemblage.
      5 Whole, totality.
      6 Contraction, conciseness, brevity.
      7 Euphonic combination (saṃdhi). (samāsena, samāsataḥ means ‘in short’, ‘briefly’, ‘succinctly’; eṣā dharmasya vo yoniḥ samāsena prakīrtitā Ms. 2. 25, 3. 20; Bg. 13. 18; samāsataḥ śrūyatāṃ V. 2). Comp. --adhyāhāraḥ supplying an ellipsis in a compound.
    • --arthā a part of a stanza proposed to be completed (= samasyā q. v.) --uktiḥ f. a figure of speech thus defined by Mammaṭa: --paroktirbhedakaiḥ śliṣṭaiḥ samāsoktiḥ K. P. 10. [1098-b]
