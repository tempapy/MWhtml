• krūra a. [kṛta-rak dhātoḥ krūḥ cf. Uṇ. 2. 21] 1 Cruel, wicked, hard-hearted, pitiless; tasyābhiṣekasaṃbhāraṃ kalpitaṃ krūraniścayā R. 12. 4; Me. 105; Ms. 10. 9. --2 Hard, rough. --3 Formidable, terrible, fierce, ferocious, savage. --4 Destructive, mischievous. --5 Wounded, hurt. --6 Bloody. --7 Raw. --8 Strong. --9 Inauspicious. --10 Hard, solid, hardened; Ś. 2. 4. --11 Hot; disagreeable, sharp; Ms. 2. 33. --raḥ, --raṃ Boiled rice. --raḥ 1 A hawk. --2 A heron. --3 An uneven sign of the zodiac. --4 N. of a planet (Rāhu or Saturn). --raṃ 1 A wound. --2 Slaughter, cruelty. --3 Any horrible deed. --4 Any frightful appearance. --Comp. --ākṛti a. terrible in form. (--tiḥ) epithet of Rāvaṇa. --ācāra a. following cruel or savage practices. --āśaya a. {1} containing fierce animals (as a river). {2} of a cruel disposition. --karman n. {1} a bloody act. {2} any hard labour. --kṛt a. fierce, cruel, unrelenting. --koṣṭha a. having costive bowels (unaffected by strong purgatives). --gaṃdhaḥ sulphur. --dṛś a. {1} evil-eyed, having a malignant look. {2} mischievous, villainous. (--m.) N. of Saturn; also of Mars. --rāvin m. a raven. --locanaḥ an epithet of the planet Saturn. [0432-b]
