• kalya a. [kalayati ceṣṭāmatra, kal-yak; kal karmaṇi yat, kalāsu sādhu kalā-yat vā Tv.] 1 Sound, free from sickness, healthy; sarvaḥ kalye vayasi yatate labdhumarthānkuṭuṃbī V. 3. 1; Y. 1. 28; yāvadeva bhavetkalyastāvacchreyaḥ samācaret Mb. --2 Ready, prepared; kathayasva kathāmetāṃ kalyāḥ smaḥ śravaṇe tava Mb. --3 Clever. --4 Agreeable, auspicious (as a discourse). --5 Deaf and dumb. --6 Instructive. --lyaṃ 1 Dawn, day-break. --2 To-morrow. --3 Sprituous liquor. --4 Congratulation, good wishes. --5 Good news. --Comp. --āśaḥ, --jagdhiḥ f. the morning meal, breakfast. --pālaḥ, --pālakaḥ a distiller. --vartaḥ morning meal, breakfast. (--rtaṃ) (hence) anything light, trivial, or unimportant; a trifle; nanu kalyavartametat Mk. 2 but a trifle; strīkalyavartasya kāraṇena 4; sa idānīmarthakalyavartasya kāraṇādidamakāryaṃ karoti 9.
