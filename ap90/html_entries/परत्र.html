• paratra ind.
      1 In another world, in a future birth; paratreha ca śarmaṇe R. 1. 69; Ku. 4. 37; Ms. 3. 275, 5. 166; 8. 127.
      2 In the sequel, further or later on.
      3 Hereafter, in future.
    • --traṃ Future world.
    Comp.
    • --bhīruḥ one who stands in awe of the future world, a pious or religious man. [0667-c]
