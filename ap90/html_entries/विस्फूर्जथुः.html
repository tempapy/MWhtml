• visphūrjathuḥ 1 Roaring, thundering, rumbling. --2 A clap or peal of thunder. --3 (Hence) A thunderlike manifestation or rise, any sudden appearance or stroke; mamaiva janmāṃtarapātakānāṃ vipākavisphūrjathuraprasahyaḥ R. 14. 62. --4 Rolling (as of waves); swell, surging appearance; mahormivisphūrjathunirviśeṣāḥ R. 13. 12.
