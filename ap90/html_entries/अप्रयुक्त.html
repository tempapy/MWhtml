• aprayukta a.
      1 Not used or employed, not applied.
      2 Wrongly used, as a word.
      3 (In Rhet.) Rare, unusual (as a word when used in a particular sense or gender though that sense or gender be sanctioned by lexicographers); aprayuktaṃ tathāmnātamapi kavibhirnādṛtaṃ; tathā manye daivatosya piśāco rākṣaso'thavā . where the mas. gender of daivata, though sanctioned (by Amara), is not used by poets and is, therefore, aprayukta.
