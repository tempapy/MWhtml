• capalā
      1 Lightning; kurabakakusumaṃ capalāmuṣamaṃ ratipatimṛgakānane Gīt. 7.
      2 An unchaste or disloyal wife.
      3 Spirituous liquor.
      4 Lakṣmī, the goddess of wealth.
      5 The tongue.
      6 Long pepper.
    Comp.
    • --janaḥ {1} a fickle or unsteady woman; Śi. 9. 16. {2} the goddess of wealth. [0485-b]
