• taḍ 10 U. (tāḍayati-te, tāḍita)
      1 To beat, strike (in general), dash against; gāhaṃtāṃ mahiṣā nipānasalilaṃ śṛṃgairmuhastāḍitaṃ Ś. 2. 5; (nauḥ) tāḍitā mārutairyathā Rām.; R. 3. 61; Ku. 5. 24; Bh. 1. 50.
      2 To beat. strike, punish by beating, hit; lālayetpaṃca varṣāṇi daśa varṣāṇi tāḍayet Chāṇ. 11, 12; na tāḍayettṛṇenāpi Ms. 4. 169; pādena yastāḍyate Amaru. 52.
      3 To strike, beat (as a drum); tāḍyamānāsubherīṣu Mb.; atāḍayan mṛdaṃgāṃśca Bk. 17. 7; Ve. 1. 22.
      4 To play on, strike the wires of (a musical instrument); śroturvitaṃtrīriva tāḍyamānā Ku. 1. 45.
      5 To shine.
      6 To speak.
      7 (In astr.) To strike against, touch, obscure or eclipse partially.
      8 (In Math.) To multiply. [0525-c]
