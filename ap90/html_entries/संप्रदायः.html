saṃpradāyaḥ
      1 Tradition, traditional doctrine or knowledge, traditional handing down of instruction; U. 2; 5. 15.
      2 A peculiar system of religious teaching, a religious doctrine inculcating the worship of one peculiar deity.
      3 An established custom, usage. [1103-b]
