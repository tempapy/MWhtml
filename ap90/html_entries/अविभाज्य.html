• avibhājya a. Indivisible.
    • --jyaṃ
      1 Indivisibility.
      2 Not being liable to be partitioned; (said of certain articles which are not to be divided at the time of partition); e. g. vastraṃ pātramalaṃkāraṃ kṛtānnamudakaṃ striyaḥ . yogakṣemaṃ pracāraṃ ca na vibhājyaṃ pracakṣate Ms. 9. 219. °tā indivisibility, unfitness for partition. [0195-c]
