• sphoṭana a. (nī f.) [sphuṭ --lyuṭ] Breaking asunder, manifesting, disclosing, making clear. --naḥ Separated utterance of a close combination of consonants. --naṃ 1 Rending, suddenly bursting, splitting, cracking. --2 Winnowing grain. --3 Cracking the finger-joints, snapping the fingers. --4 The separation of a double consonant.
