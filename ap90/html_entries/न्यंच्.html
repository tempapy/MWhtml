nyaṃc 1 P.
      1 To go down, bend down; Māl. 5. 22.
      2 To incline.
      3 To diminish, pass away; nyaṃcati vayasi prathame Bv. 2. 47. [0650-c]
      nyaṃc a. (nīcī f.)
      1 Going or turned downwards, turned or bent down.
      2 Lying on the face.
      3 Low, contemptible, base, mean, vile; Śi. 15. 21 (where it also means nimna or downward).
      4 Slow, lazy.
      5 Whole, entire. [0650-c]
