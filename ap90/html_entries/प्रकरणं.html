• prakaraṇaṃ 1 Treating, explaining, discussing. --2 (a) A subject, topic, department, a subject (of representation); katamatprakaraṇamāśritya Ś. 1. (b) A head or subject of treatment. (c) A province or department. --3 A section, chapter or any smaller division of a work. --4 An opportunity, occasion. --5 An affair, a matter. --6 An introduction, prologue. --7 Relation. --8 Doing much or well. --9 A species of drama with invented or fictitious plot; as the mṛcchakaṭika, mālatīmādhava, puṣpabhūṣita &c. The S. D. thus defines it: --bhavet prakaraṇe vṛttaṃ laukikaṃ kavikalpitaṃ . śṛṃgāroṃ'gī nāyakastu vipro'mātyo'thavā vaṇik . sāpāyadharmakāmārthaparo dhīrapraśāṃtakaḥ .. 511. [0724-c]
