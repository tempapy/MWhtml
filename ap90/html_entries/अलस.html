alasa a. [na lasati vyāpriyate, las-ac]
    • 1 Inactive, without energy, lazy, idle, indolent.
    • 2 Tired, fatigued, languid; mārgaśramādalasaśarīre dārike M. 5; Amaru. 4, 90; khedālaseva K. 143, 197, 211, 62, 98; Śi. 8. 7; V. 3. 2; Dk. 20, Śi. 13. 48; 9. 39; U. 1. 24; Ki. 10. 60, V. 5; gamanamalasaṃ Māl. 1. 17.
    • 3 Soft, gentle.
    • 4 Slow, dull (as in gait or motion); śroṇībhārādalasagamanā Me. 82; tasyāḥ parisphuritagarbhabharālasāyāḥ U. 3. 28. --saḥ
    • 1 A sore or ulcer between the toes.
    • 2 A kind of tree.
    • 3 N. of a sage.
    • 4 N. of a small poisonous animal.
      N. of a plant (haṃsapadī). Comp. --īkṣaṇā a woman with languishing looks. [0173-b]
