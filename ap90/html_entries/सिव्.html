siv 4 P. (sīvyati, syūta)
      1 To sew, darn, stitch together; manobhavaḥ sīvyati duryaśaḥpaṭau N. 1. 80; Māl. 5. 10.
      2 To unite, bring or join together; sa hi snehātmakastaṃturaṃtarmarmāṇi sīvyati U. 5. 17. --WITH anu to string together, connect very closely or uninterruptedly. [1124-c]
