• mīnaḥ [mī-nak] 1 A fish; suptamīna iva hradaḥ R. 1. 73; mīno nu haṃta katamāṃ gatimabhyupaitu Bv. 1. 17. --2 The twelfth sign of the zodiac (Pisces). --3 The first incarnation of Viṣṇu; see matsyāvatāra. --Comp. --aṃḍaṃ roe, fish-spawn (--ḍā) moist sugar. --ā ghātin, ghātin m. {1} a fisherman. {2} a crane. --ālayaḥ the sea. --ketanaḥ the god of love. --gaṃdhaḥ an epithet of Satyavatī. --gaṃdhikā a pond, pool of water. --raṃkaḥ, --raṃgaḥ a kingfisher.
