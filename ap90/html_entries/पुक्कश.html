• pukkaśa a. (śī f.), pukkasa a. (sī f.) Low, vile. --śaḥ, --saḥ N. of a degraded mixed caste, the offspring of a Niṣāda by a Śūdra woman; jāto niṣādācchūdrāyāṃ jātyā bhavati pukkasaḥ Ms. 10. 18. --śī, --sī 1 A bud. --2 The indigo plant. --3 A woman of the Pukkasa caste. [0706-b]
