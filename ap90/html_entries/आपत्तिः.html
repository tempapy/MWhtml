• āpattiḥ f. [ā-pad-ktin]
      1 Turning or changing into, entering into any state or condition.
      2 Obtaining, procuring, getting; sthānāpatterdravyeṣu dharmalābhaḥ Kāty.
      3 Misfortune, calamity, adversity; Y. 3. 42.
      4 A fault, transgression.
      5 Remonstrance, expostulation.
      6 (In phil.) An undesirable conclusion or occurrence (aniṣṭaprasaṃga). [0244-c]
