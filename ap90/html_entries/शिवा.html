      2 A jackal (in general); jahāsi nidrāmaśivaiḥ śivārutaiḥ Ki. 1. 38; hareradya dvāre śiva śiva śivānāṃ kalakalaḥ Bv. 1. 32; R. 7. 50, 11. 61, 12. 39.
      3 A fortunate woman.
      4 Final beatitude.
      5 The Śamī tree.
      6 The yellow myrobalan.
      7 Dūrvā grass.
      8 A kind of yellow pigment.
      9 Turmeric.
