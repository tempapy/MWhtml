vī
      I. 2 P. (veti, rarely used in classical literature)
      1 To go, move.
      2 To approach.
      3 To pervade.
      4 To bring, convey.
      5 To throw, cast.
      6 To eat, consume.
      7 To obtain.
      8 To conceive, bring forth.
      9 To be born or produced.
      10 To shine, be beautiful.
      11 To desire, wish.
      12 To shine.
      II. (vi + i) 2 P.
      1 To go away, depart; tasyāmahaṃ tvayi ca saprāṃti vītaciṃtaḥ Ś. 4. 12; so vītabhaya, vītakrodha.
      2 To undergo a change; sadṛśaṃ triṣu liṃgeṣu yanna vyoti tadavyayaṃ Sk.
      3 To spend.
      4 To be diffused, to spread.
      5 To vanish, disappear.
      6 To cross over, traverse. [1003-b]
