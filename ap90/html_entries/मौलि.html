• mauli a. [mūlasyādūrabhavaḥ iñ] Head, foremost, best; akhilaparimalānāṃ maulinā saurabheṇa Bv. 1. 121. --liḥ 1 The head, the crown of the head; maulau vā racayāṃjaliṃ Ve. 3. 40; R. 13. 59; Ku. 5. 79. --2 The head or top of anything, top-most point; U. 2. 30. --3 The Aśoka tree. --liḥ (m. f.) 1 A crown, diadem, tiara; Bv. 1. 73. --2 Hair on the crown of the head, tuft or lock of hair; jaṭāmauli Ku. 2. 26 (jaṭājūṭa Malli.). --3 Braided hair, hair braided and ornamented; Ve. 6. 34; --liḥ, --lī f. The earth. --Comp. --maṇiḥ, --ratnaṃ a crest-jewel, a jewel worn in the crown. --maṃḍanaṃ a head-ornament. --mukuṭaṃ a crown. tiara. [0882-a]
