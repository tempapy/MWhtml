anādara a. [na. ba.] Showing no respect, indifferent, calm, regardless; M. 3. 15. --raḥ [na. ta.]
      1 Disregard, disrespect, disdain, contempt; ṣaṣṭhī cānādare P. II. 3. 38, manyakarmaṇi anādare vibhāṣā' prāṇiṣu 17.
      2 Ease, facility (one of the senses of ādara being ‘effort or care’, see the word); °khaṃḍitaśaṃkaraśarāsanaḥ U. 1 (perhaps also ‘without any respect for the bow of the great god’); anādaropāttadhṛtaikasāyakaṃ Ki. 14. 36. [0061-b]
