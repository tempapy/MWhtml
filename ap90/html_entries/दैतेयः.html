• daiteyaḥ [diterapatyaṃ-ḍhak] ‘A son of Diti,’ a Rākṣasa, demon.
    Comp.
    • --ijyaḥ, --guruḥ, --purodhas m., --pūjyaḥ epithets of Śukra, the preceptor of the Asuras.
    • --niṣūdanaḥ an epithet of Viṣṇu.
    • --mātṛ f. Diti, mother of the demons.
    • --medajā the earth. [0581-b]
