vimānaḥ --naṃ
      1 Disrespect, dishonour.
      2 A measure.
      3 A balloon, a heavenly car (moving through the skies); padaṃ vimānena vigāhamānaḥ R. 13. 1, 7. 51; vimānīkṛtarājahaṃsamaṃḍalaḥ K.; R. 12. 104; Ku. 2. 45, 7. 40; V. 4. 43; Ki. 7. 11.
      4 A vehicle or conveyance in general; R. 16. 68.
      5 A palace (with seven stories); netrā nītāḥ satatagatinā yadvimānāgrabhūmīḥ Me. 69.
      6 A horse. Comp. --cārin, --yāna a. moving in a balloon.
      --rājaḥ {1} an excellent heavenly car; U. 3. {2} the driver of a heavenly car. [0986-a]
