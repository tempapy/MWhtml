• hanu --nū m. f. [han-un strītve vā uñ] The chin.
    • --nu f.
      1 That which injures life.
      2 A weapon.
      3 A disease, sickness.
      4 Death.
      5 A kind of drug.
      6 A wanton woman, prostitute.
    Comp.
    • --grahaḥ locked jaw.
    • --mūlaṃ the root of the jaw. [1165-b]
