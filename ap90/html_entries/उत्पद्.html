• utpad {c4c} A. 1 To be born or produced, arise, originate, spring up; utpatsyate'sti mama kopi samānadharmā Māl. 1. 6; vāyoḥ jyotirutpadyate Ms. 1. 77. --2 To occur, happen; udapādi cāsya manasi K. 132 it occurred to his mind; yuddhamutpatsyate mahat Mb.; utpannāvasaramarthitvaṃ M. 3 for which an occasion has presented itself; Ms. 5. 48. --Caus. 1 To produce, beget, cause, effect, bring about, prepare; vastrāṇi sadaivotpādayati Pt. 2; Ms. 1. 63, 2. 147, 9. 60; so duḥkhaṃ, doṣaṃ, bhayaṃ, śaṃkāṃ &c. --2 To bring forward.
