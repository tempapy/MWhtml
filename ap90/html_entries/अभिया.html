• abhiyā {c2c} P. 1 To go up to, approach, go or repair to; abhiyayau sa himācalamucchritaṃ Ki. 5. 1; R. 9. 27. --2 To come or draw near (without an object); come (as time). --3 To march against, attack, assail, encounter; kuberādabhiyāsyamānāt R. 5. 30; Dk. 30. --4 To give or resign oneself to, devote, attach or betake oneself to. --5 To partake or share in, get, obtain. --Caus. To send away. [0135-c]
  • abhiyā m. f. Going to, approaching, assailing. [0136-a]
