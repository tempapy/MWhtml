• anācāra a. Devoid of customary observances or duties, improper in behaviour, unprincipled, regardless of custom, law or propriety &c. also anācārin in this sense.
    • --raḥ, --anācaraṇaṃ Absence of due observances or customary duties, improper conduct, departure from established usage or principle; anācāra is of two kinds vihitasya ananuṣṭhānaṃ niṣiddhasya cānuṣṭhānam. [0061-a]
