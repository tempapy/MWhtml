• khaṭvā [khaṭ-kvun; cf. Uṇ. 1. 150] 1 A bed-stead, couch, cot. --2 A swing, hammock. --3 A kind of bandage. --Comp. --aṃgaḥ {1} a club or staff with a skull at the top considered as the weapon of Śiva and carried by ascetics and Yogins; Māl. 5. 4, 23. {2} N. of Dilīpa. °dhara, bhṛt an epithet of Śiva. --aṃgin m. an epithet of Śiva. --āpluta, --ārūḍha a. {1} lying on a bed. {2} low, vile {3} abandoned, wicked. {4} silly, stupid. {5} erring, going wrong or astray.
