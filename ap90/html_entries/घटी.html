ghaṭī
      1 A small jar.
      2 A measure of time equal to 24 minutes.
      3 A small water-pot used in calculating the Ghaṭikās or time of the day.
    Comp.
      --kāraḥ a potter.
      --graha, --grāha a. see ghaṭagraha.
      --yaṃtraṃ {1} a machine for raising water (largely used in India), the rope and bucket of a well; see araghaṭṭa. {2} a contrivance (like a clepsydra) to ascertain the ghaṭikās or time of the day. [0475-c]
