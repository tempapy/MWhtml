tithiḥ m. or f. [at-ithin pṛṣo° vā ṅīp cf. Uṇ. 4. 2]
      1 A lunar day; tithireva tāvanna śuddhyati Mu. 5; Ku. 6. 93, 7. 1.
      2 The number ‘15’.
    Comp.
      --īśaḥ the regent of a lunar day.
      --kṣayaḥ {1} the day of new moon. {2} the day on which a tithi begins and ends without one sunrise or between two sunrises.
      --patrī an almanac.
      --pālanaṃ observance of the rites prescribed for the several lunar days.
      --praṇīḥ the moon.
      --vṛddhiḥ f. the day in which a tithi is completed under two suns (one which comprises two sunrises). [0537-c]
