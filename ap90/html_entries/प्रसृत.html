prasṛta p. p.
      1 Gone forward.
      2 Stretched out, extended.
      3 Spread, diffused.
      4 Long, lengthened.
      5 Engaged in, attached to.
      6 Swift, or quick.
      7 Manifested, displayed; U. 6. 14.
      8 Modest, humble.
      --taḥ The palm of the hand stretched out and hollowed.
      --taḥ, --taṃ A measure equal to two palas.
      --tā The leg.
    Comp.
      --jaḥ a particular class of sons, an adulterine (kuṃḍagolakarūpa). [0766-b]
