paraspara a. Mutual; parasparāṃ vismayavaṃti lakṣmīmālokayāṃcakrurivādareṇa Bk. 2. 5. --pron. a. Each other, one another (used in the sing. only; often in comp.); parasparasyopari paryacīyata R. 3. 24; 7. 38; avijñātaparasparaiḥ apasarpaiḥ 17. 51; parasparākṣisādṛkṣyaṃ 1. 40, 3. 24. Note. The acc. and abl. singulars are often used adverbially in the sense of ‘mutually,’ ‘reciprocally’, ‘one another,’ ‘by, from, or to one another’, ‘against one another’ &c. see Bg. 3. 11, 10. 9; R. 4. 79; 6. 46; 7. 14, 53; 12. 94.
    Comp.
      --jñaḥ a friend. [0668-c]
