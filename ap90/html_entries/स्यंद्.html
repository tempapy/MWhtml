• syaṃd 1 A. (syaṃdate, sasyaṃde, asyadat-asyaṃtta-asyaṃdiṣṭa, syaṃtsyati-te, syaṃdiṣyate, syaṃdituṃ-syaṃttuṃ, syanna; desid. sisyaṃdiṣate, sisyaṃtsati-te; the s of syaṃd is changed to ṣ after a preposition ending in i or u)
      1 To ooze, trickle, drop, drip, distil, flow; ayi daladaraviṃda syaṃdamānaṃ maraṃdaṃ tava kimapi lihaṃto maṃju guṃjaṃtu bhṛṃgāḥ Bv. 1. 5.
      2 To shed, pour forth.
      3 To run, flee. [1156-c]
