udita p. p.
      1 Risen, ascended; uditabhūyiṣṭhaḥ Māl. 1 mostly risen; Bv. 2. 85; nityoditaḥ Bh. 3. 80 ever-existing.
      2 High, tall, lofty.
      3 Grown, augmented.
      4 Born, produced.
      5 Spoken, uttered (fr. vad).
      --taṃ
      1 A kind of perfume.
      2 A kind of accent.
    Comp.
      --udita a. well grounded in the Śāstras.
      --homin a. sacrificing after sunrise. [0304-c]
