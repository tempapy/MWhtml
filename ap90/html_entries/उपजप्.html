• upajap {c1c} P. 1 To whisper into the ears of (another), win over to one's party by secretly suggesting anything in the ear; aṃgāravarṣaṃ rājavadhāyopajapya Dk. 104; kṣattāraṃ kururājastu śanaiḥ karṇamupājapat Mb. --2 (Hence) to instigate to rebellion or treachery, conspire with; upajapyānupajapet Ms. 7. 197. --3 To commit mischief.
