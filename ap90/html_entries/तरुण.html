• taruṇa a. [tṝ-unan Uṇ. 3. 54]
      1 Young, youthful, juvenile, (as a man).
      2 (a) Young, newly-born or produced, tender, soft; Bh. 3. 49. (b) Newly risen, not high in the sky (as the sun) Ku. 3. 54.
      3 New, fresh; taruṇaṃ dadhi Chāṇ. 64; taruṇaṃ sarṣapaśākaṃ navaudanaṃ picchilāni ca dadhīni . alpavyayena suṃdari gra myajano miṣṭamaśnāti .. Chand. M. 1.
      4 Lively, vivid.
    • --ṇaḥ
      1 A young man, youth; Pt. 1. 11; Bv. 2. 62.
      2 The castor-oil plant.
      3 Large cumin-seed.
    • --ṇī A young or youthful woman; vṛddhasya taruṇī viṣaṃ Chāṇ. 78. --ṇaṃ
      1 Cartilage.
      2 A sprout.
    Comp.
    • --asthi n. cartilage.
    • --jvaraḥ fever lasting for a week.
    • --dadhi n. coagulated milk five days old.
    • --pītikā red arsenic. [0532-c]
