• uddhā I. {c3c} P. Ved. 1 To abandon or expose (an infant). --2 To set up, erect, build. II. {c3c} A. 1 To go or move upwards, rise (as the sun, dust &c.); yato rajaḥ pārthivamujjihīte R. 13. 64; Mu. 4. 21; Bk. 18. 27; N. 22. 45, 55. --2 To go away from, depart; ujjihānajīvitāṃ Māl. 10. --3 To raise; śirasā yūpamujjihīte Kāty. --4 To throw up, knit (as eyebrows); Bk. 3. 47.
