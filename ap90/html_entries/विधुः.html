vidhuḥ [vyadh-ku; Uṇ. 1. 23]
      1 The moon; savitā vidhavati vidhurapi savitarati dinaṃti yāminyaḥ K. P. 10.
      2 Camphor.
      3 A demon, fiend.
      4 An expiatory oblation.
      5 N. of Viṣṇu.
      6 N. of Brahman.
      7 N. of Śiva.
      8 Wind.
      9 War, battle.
    Comp.
      --kṣayaḥ waning of the moon, the period of the dark fortnight of a month.
      --paṃjaraḥ (also piṃjaraḥ) a scimitar, sabre.
      --priyā a Nakṣatra or lunar mansion. [0977-c]
