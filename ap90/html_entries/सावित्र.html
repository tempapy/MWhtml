• sāvitra a. (trī f.) [savitā davetā'sya aṇ]
      1 Belonging to the sun.
      2 Descended from the sun, belonging to the solar dynasty (of kings); yatsāvitrairdīpitaṃ bhūmipālaiḥ U. 1. 43.
      3 Accompanied by the Gāyatrī.
    • --traḥ
      1 The sun.
      2 An embryo or fœtus.
      3 A Brāhmaṇa.
      4 An epithet of Śiva
      5 Of Karṇa.
    • --traṃ The sacrificial thread (so called because the repetition of the Gāyatrī forms a principal part of the ceremony of putting on the sacred thread). [1121-a]
