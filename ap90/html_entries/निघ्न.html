• nighna a. 1 Dependent, subservient, obedient (as a servant); tathāpi nighnaṃ nṛpa tāvakīnaiḥ prahvīkṛtaṃ me hṛdayaṃ guṇaughaiḥ Ki. 3. 12; nighnasya me bhartṛnideśaraukṣyaṃ devi kṣamasveti babhūva namraḥ R. 14. 58. --2 Docile, tractable. --3 Dependent on (i. e. following the gender &c. of) a substantive; iti viśeṣyanighnavargaḥ. --4 (After a numeral) Multiplied by.
