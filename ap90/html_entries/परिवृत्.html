• parivṛt 1 A.
      1 To turn round, revolve; Ku. 1. 16.
      2 To roam about, move hither and thither.
      3 To change, barter, exchange.
      4 To turn back; R. 4. 72; V. 1. 17.
      5 To be, fall into; Māl. 9. 8.
      6 To decay, perish, disappear; Māl. 10. 6. [0680-b]
