phulla p. p. (of phal)
      1 Expanded, opened, blown; puṣpaṃ ca phullaṃ navamallikāyāḥ prayāti kāṃtiṃ pramadājatānāṃ Rs. 6. 6; phullāraviṃdavadanāṃ Ch. P. 1.
      2 Flowering, blossomed; R. 9. 63.
      3 Expanded, dilated, wide opened (as eyes).
      4 Smiling, gay.
      --lla A fullblown flower.
    Comp.
      --tuvarī alum.
      --nayana, --netra --locana a. having eyes dilated (with joy); Pt. 1. 136. (--naḥ) a kind of deer. (--naṃ) a large, full eye.
      --phālaḥ the wind raised in winnowing corn. [0785-b]
