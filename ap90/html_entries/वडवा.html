• vaḍavā 1 A mare. --2 The nymph Aśvinī who in the form of a mare bore to the sun two sons, the Aśvins; see saṃjñā. --3 A female slave. --4 A harlot, prostitute. --5 A woman of the Brāhmaṇa caste ( dvijayoṣit). --Comp. --agniḥ, --analaḥ the submarine fire. --mukhaḥ {1} the submarine fire. {2} N. of Śiva.
