• aratniḥ (m. or f.) [ṛ-katni ratniḥ sa nāsti yatra]
      1 The elbow; sometimes the fist itself.
      2 A cubit of the middle length, from the elbow to the tip of the little finger, an ell; aratnistu niṣkaniṣṭhena muṣṭinā Ak.; madhyāṃgulīkūrparayormadhye prāmāṇikaḥ karaḥ . baddhamuṣṭikaro ratniraratniḥ sakaniṣṭhikaḥ .. Halāy.; Ki. 18. 6.
      3 The arm. [0160-a]
