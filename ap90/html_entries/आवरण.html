āvaraṇa a. Covering, hiding, obscuring, obstructing; netrāvaraṇamaśru R. 14. 71. --ṇaṃ
      1 Covering, concealing, hiding, obscuring; sūrye tapatyāvaraṇāya dṛṣṭeḥ kalpeta lokasya kathaṃ tamisrā R. 5. 13, 10. 46, 19. 16.
      2 Shutting, enclosing, fencing.
      3 A covering, anything that covers or protects &c. hastau svau nayati stanāvaraṇatāṃ M. 4. 14; Ś. 3. 22; (fig.) protection, defence; śīlamāvaraṇaṃ striyāḥ Rām.; caritrāvaraṇāḥ striyaḥ Chāṇ. 76.
      4 Obstruction, interruption, restraint (of bashfulness &c.); kālenāvaraṇātyayāt U. 1. 39.
      5 An enclosure, fence, surrounding wall; labdhāṃtarā sāvaraṇepi gehe R. 16. 7; Ki. 5. 25.
      6 A bolt, latch.
      7 A shield.
    Comp.
      --śaktiḥ mental ignorance (which veils the real nature of things). [0261-c]
