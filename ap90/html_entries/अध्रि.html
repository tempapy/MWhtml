adhri a. [na-dhṛ-ki] Not restrained, irresistible.
    Comp.
      --gu-gū a. [adhri-gam-kū-ḍic ūṅādeśovā] of irresistible motion or course (adhṛtagamana), impetuous; yadadhrigāvo agnigū idā cidahno aśvinā Rv. 8. 22. 11 (--guḥ) N. of a heavenly killer of sacrificial victims, or the name of the formula itself ending with an invocation of Agni.
      --ja a. [adhriṃ janayati, jan-ḍa] making irresistible.
