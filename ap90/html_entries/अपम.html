apama a. [apakṛṣṭaṃ mīyate, mā bāhu° ka] Ved. The most distant or remote, the last, lowest in caste, most degraded (jātyātinikṛṣṭa).
      --maḥ (In astr.) Declination; the ecliptic; parisaratāṃ gaganasadāṃ calanaṃ kiṃcidbhavedapamaḥ Sid. Śir.
    Comp.
      --jyā the line of the ecliptic; see krāṃtijyā.
      --maṃḍalaṃ, --vṛttaṃ the ecliptic. [0108-a]
