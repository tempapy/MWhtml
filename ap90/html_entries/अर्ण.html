• arṇa a. [ṛ-na]
      1 Being in motion, agitated; restless.
      2 Foaming, effervescing.
    • --rṇaḥ
      1 A flood, stream; water (Ved.)
      2 The teak tree.
      3 A letter (of the alphabet); paṃcārṇo manurīritaḥ.
      4 N. of a metre having 10 feet and belonging to the class called Daṇḍaka.
    • --rṇā A river (Ved.).
    • --rṇaṃ Tumult or din of battle confused noise. [0165-b]
