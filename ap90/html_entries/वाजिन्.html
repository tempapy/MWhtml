• vājin a. [vāja-astyarthe ini] 1 Swift, quick. --2 Strong. --m. 1 A horse; na gardabhā vājidhuraṃ vahaṃti Mk. 4. 17; satyamatītya harito harīṃśca vartaṃte vājinaḥ Ś. 1; R. 3. 43, 4. 25, 67; Śi. 18. 31. --2 An arrow. --3 A follower of the Vājasaneyin branch of the Yajurveda. --4 N. of Indra, Bṛhaspati and other gods. --nī 1 A mare. --2 N. of Uṣas (dawn). --3 Food (Ved.). --Comp. --pṛṣṭhaḥ the globe-amaranth. --bhakṣaḥ a chickpea. --bhojanaḥ a kind of kidneybean. --medhaḥ a horse-sacrifice. --śālā a stable.
