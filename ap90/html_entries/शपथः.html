• śapathaḥ [śap-athan] 1 Cursing. --2 A curse, an imprecation, anathema. --3 An oath, swearing, taking or administering an oath, asseveration by oath or ordeal; āmodo na hi kastūryāḥ śapathenānubha vyate Bv. 1. 120; Ms. 8. 109. --4 Conjuration, binding by oaths; Māl. 3. 2. --Comp. --patraṃ an affidavit, a statement on oath.
