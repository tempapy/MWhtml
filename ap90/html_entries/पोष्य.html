poṣya a. [puṣ-karmaṇi ṇyat]
      1 To be fed, nourished or supported.
      2 Well-fed, thriving.
      3 Causing prosperity.
      4 Abundant, copious.
    Comp.
      --putraḥ, --sutaḥ an adopted son.
      --vargaḥ a class of relatives, who must be nourished and protected. [0721-c]
