tutthaḥ [tud-thak]
      1 Fire.
      2 A stone.
      --tthaṃ Sulphate of copper, usually applied to the eyes as a sort of collyrium or medical ointment.
      --tthā
      1 Small cardamoms.
      2 The indigo plant.
    Comp.
      --aṃjanaṃ blue vitriol applied to the eyes as a medical ointment. [0541-b]
