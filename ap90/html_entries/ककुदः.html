• kakudaḥ --daṃ 1 The peak or summit of a mountain. --2 A hump (on the shoulders of an Indian bull). --3 Chief, foremost, pre-eminent; kakudaṃ vedavidāṃ tapodhanaśca Mk. 1. 5; ikṣvākuvaṃśyaḥ kakudaṃ nṛpāṇāṃ R. 6. 71. --4 A sign or symbol of royalty; nṛpatikakudaṃ R. 3. 70, 17. 27. --5 A species of serpent.
