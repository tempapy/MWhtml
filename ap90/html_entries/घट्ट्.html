• ghaṭṭ {c1c} A. (ghaṭṭte), usually {c10c} U. (ghadṛyati-te, ghaṭṭita) 1 To shake, stir about; as in vāyughaṭṭitā latāḥ. --2 To touch, rub, rub the hands over; viṭajananakhaghaṭṭiteva vīṇā Mk. 1. 24; Bk. 14. 2. --3 To smooth, stroke. --4 To speak spitefully or malignantly. --5 To disturb. [0476-a]
