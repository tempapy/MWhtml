• graṃth {c1c} {c9c} P., {c10c} U., {c1c} A. (graṃthati, grathnāti, graṃthayati-te, also grathati, grathate) 1 To fasten, tie or string together; Bk. 7. 105; srajo grathayate &c. --2 To arrange, class together, connect in a regular series. --3 To wind round. --4 To write, compose; grathnāmi kāvyaśaśinaṃ vitatārtharaśiṃma K. P. 10. --5 To form, make, produce; grathnaṃti bāṣpabiṃdunikaraṃ pakṣmapaṃktayaḥ K. 60; Bk. 17. 69. --6 To set or strew with. [0471-a]
