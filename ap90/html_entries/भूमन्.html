• bhūman m. [bahobhaviḥ bahu imanic ilope bhvādeśaḥ Tv.]
      1 A great quantity, abundance, plenty, large number; bhūmtā rasānāṃ gahanāḥ prayogāḥ Māl. 1. 4; saṃbhūyeba sukhāni cetasi paraṃ bhūmānamātanvata 5. 9.
      2 Wealth. --n.
      1 The earth
      2 A territory, district, piece of ground.
      3 A being, creature.
      4 Plurality (of number); āpaḥ strībhūmni Ak.; cf. puṃbhūman. [0822-b]
