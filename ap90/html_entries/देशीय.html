• deśīya a. [deśe bhavaḥ --cha] 1 Belonging to a province, provincial. --2 Native, local. --3 Inhabiting any country (at the end of comp.); as in magadhadeśīya, taddeśīya, vaṃgadeśīya &c. --4 Not far or distant from, almost, bordering on (used as an affix at the end of words); aṣṭādaśavarṣadeśīyāṃ kanyāṃ da darśa K. 131 ‘a girl about 18 years old (whose age bordered on 18); R. 18. 39; so paṭudeśīya &c. [0580-c]
