ballavaḥ
      1 A cowherd; kuṃjeṣvākrāṃtavīrunnicayaparicayā ballavāḥ saṃcaraṃtu Ve. 6. 2; Śi. 11. 8.
      2 A cook.
      3 The name assumed by Bhīma when serving as a cook at the court of Virāṭa.
      --vī A cowherdess; Ki. 4. 17.
    Comp.
      --yuvatiḥ --tī f. a young cowherdess (gopī); harivirahākulaballavayuvatisakhīvacanaṃ paṭhanīyaṃ Gīt. 4. [0791-a]
