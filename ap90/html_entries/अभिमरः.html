• abhimaraḥ [mṛ-ac] 1 Killing, destruction, slaughter. --2 War, combat. --3 Treachery in one's own camp; danger from one's own men or party. --4 Binding, confinement; a tie or fetter. --5 One's own party or army. --6 One who desperately goes to fight with tigers, elephants &c. [0135-a]
