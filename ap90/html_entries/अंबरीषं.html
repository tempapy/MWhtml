• aṃbarīṣaṃ [In some senses aṃbarīṣaḥ also; °ṣaḥ only by Uṇ. 4. 29; klībeṃ' barīṣaṃ bhrāṣṭro nā Ak.] 1 A frying-pan. --2 Regret, remorse. --3 War, battle. --4 One of the hells. --5 A young animal, colt. --6 The sun. --7 The hog-plum plant (āmrātaka). --8 N. of Viṣṇu. --9 N. of Śiva. --10 N. of a king of the solar race who was celebrated as a worshipper of Viṣṇu. [0153-c]
