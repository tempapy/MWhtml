• advaita a. [na. ba.] 1 Not dual; of one or uniform nature, equable, unchanging; °taṃ sukhaduḥkhayoḥ U. 1. 39. --2 Matchless, peerless, sole, only, unique. --taṃ [na. ta.] 1 Non-duality, identity; especially that of Brahma with the universe or with the soul, or of soul and matter; See advaya also. --2 The supreme or highest truth or Brahma itself. --3 N. of an Upanishad; advaitena solely, without any duplicity. --Comp. --ānaṃdaḥ (advaya°) {1} the joy arising from a knowledge of the identity of the universe and the supreme spirit. {2} N. of an author who, according to Colebrooke, flourished at the close of the 15th century. --vādin = advayavādin q. v. above; a Vedāntin.
