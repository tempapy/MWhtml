aprakāśa a.
      1 Not shining or bright, dark, wanting in brightness (fig. also); prakāśaścāprakāśaśca lokālokaṃ ivācalaḥ R. 1. 68.
      2 Selfilluminated.
      3 Hidden, secret, concealed; Ms. 8. 251; 9. 256. --śaṃ, --śe ind. In secret, secretly; ahamaprakāśe tiṣṭhāmi M. 4; satyavatyā haste'prakāśaṃ nikṣiptaḥ V. 5 secretly.
      --śaḥ Indistinctness, secrecy; a secret. [0121-a]
