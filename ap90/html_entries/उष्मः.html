• uṣmaḥ, uṣmakaḥ [uṣ-mak]
      1 Heat.
      2 The hot season, summer.
      3 Spring.
      4 Anger, warmth of temper.
      5 Ardour, eagerness, zeal.
    Comp.
    • --anvita a. enraged. --āgamaḥ, --upagamaḥ the hot season.
    • --paḥ {1} the son of Bhṛgu. {2} the Manes; a deceased ancestor.
    • --bhās m. the sun.
    • --svedaḥ a vapour bath. [0343-c]
