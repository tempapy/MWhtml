uṣmaḥ, uṣmakaḥ [uṣ-mak]
    • 1 Heat.
    • 2 The hot season, summer.
    • 3 Spring.
    • 4 Anger, warmth of temper.
    • 5 Ardour, eagerness, zeal.
      Comp. a. enraged. --āgamaḥ, --upagamaḥ the hot season.
      {1} the son of Bhṛgu. {2} the Manes; a deceased ancestor.
      m. the sun.
      a vapour bath. [0343-c]
