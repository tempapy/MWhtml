bilvaḥ A species of tree, Aegle Marmelos or wood-apple.
      --lvaṃ
      1 The fruit of this tree.
      2 A particular weight (= one pala).
    Comp.
      --daṃḍaḥ an epithet of Śiva.
      --peśikā, peśī the shell of the Bilva fruit.
      --vanaṃ a thicket or wood of Bilva trees. [0797-b]
