• kaupīnaṃ [kūpa-khañ; see Sk. on P. V. 2. 20] 1 The pudenda. --2 A privity, privy part. --3 A small piece of cloth (usually a small strip) worn over the privities; kaupīnaṃ śatakhaṃḍajarjarataraṃ kaṃthā punastādṛśī Bh. 3. 101. --4 (Hence sometimes) A ragged or tattered garment. --5 Sin, an improper or wrong act; Pt. 3. 97.
