• saṃdeśaḥ
      1 Information, news, tidings.
      2 A message, an errand; saṃdeśaṃ me hara dhanapatikrodhaviśleṣitasya Me. 7, 13; R. 12. 63; Ku. 6. 2.
      3 Commission, command; anuṣṭhito guroḥ saṃdeśaḥ Ś. 5.
    Comp.
    • --arthaḥ the subject of a message; Me. 5. --vāc f. a message.
    • --haraḥ, --hārakaḥ {1} a news-bearer, messenger. {2} an envoy, ambassador. [1087-c]
