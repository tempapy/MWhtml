virūpa a. (pā or pī f.)
      1 Deformed, ugly, misshapen, disfigured; Pt. 1. 143.
      2 Unnatural, monstrous.
      3 Multiform, diverse.
      --paṃ
      1 Deformity, ugliness.
      2 Variety of form, nature, or character.
    Comp.
      --akṣa a. having deformed eyes: vapurvirūpākṣaṃ Ku. 5. 72. (--kṣaḥ) N. of Śiva (having an unusual number of eyes); dṛśā dagdhaṃ manasijaṃ jīvayaṃti dṛśaiva yāḥ . virūpākṣasya jayinīstāḥ stuve vāmalocanāḥ Vb. 1. 2; Ku. 6. 21. --karaṇaṃ {1} disfiguring. {2} injuring.
      --cakṣus m. an epithet of Śiva.
      --rūpa a. deformed. [0989-c]
