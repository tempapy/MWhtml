• apunar ind. Not again, once for all, for ever.
    Comp.
    • --anvaya a. not returning; dead.
    • --ādānaṃ not taking back or again.
    • --āvṛttiḥ f. ‘nonreturn’, exemption of the soul from further transmigration, final beatitude.
    • --prāpya a. irrecoverable.
    • --bhavaḥ {1} not being born again (of diseases also). {2} final beatitude. {3} knowledge of the Supreme Soul which tends to this step. {4} a person released from metempsychosis. [0118-b]
