• vaktṛ a. or m. [ucitaṃ bahu vakti, vactṛc] 1 Speaking, talking, a speaker. --2 Eloquent, an orator; apriyasya ca pathyasya vaktā śrotā ca durlabhaḥ Rām.; kiṃ kariṣvaṃti vaktāraḥ śrotā yatra na vidyate; dardurā yatra vaktārastatra maunaṃ hi śobhanaṃ Subhāṣ. --3 A teacher, an expounder. --4 A learned or wise man in general. --5 Honest, sincere.
