• adoṣa a. [na. ba.]
      1 Free from faults, demerits, vices, or defects &c. innocent; jāyāmadoṣāmuta saṃtyajāmi R. 14. 34.
      2 Free from the faults of composition, such as aślīlatā, grāmyatā &c., See doṣa; adoṣau śabdārthau K. P. 1; adoṣaṃ guṇavat kāvyaṃ Sar. K. 1. --ṣaḥ Not a fault; kimityadoṣeṇa tiraskṛtā vayaṃ Ki. 14. 11 though not at fault. [0042-b]
