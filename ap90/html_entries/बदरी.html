• badarī
      1 The jujube tree; see bādarāyaṇa.
      2 = badarikā (2) above.
      3 The cotton shrub.

    • --nārāyaṇaḥ N. of a place.
    • --patraṃ a kind of perfume (naravī).
    • --phalaṃ a fruit of the jujube tree.
    • --vanaṃ (ṇaṃ) a wood or thicket of jujube trees.
    • --vāsā an epithet of Durgā.
    • --śailaḥ a rocky eminence at Badarī. [0786-b]
