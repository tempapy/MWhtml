prāktana a. (nī f.)
      1 Former, previous, antecedent; prapedire prāktanajanmavidyāḥ Ku. 1. 30.
      2 Old, ancient, early.
      3 Relating to a former life or acts in a former life; saṃskārāḥ prāktanā iva R. 1. 20; Ku. 6. 10. --naṃ (or prāktanakarman) n. Fate, destiny. [0770-a]
