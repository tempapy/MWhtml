apacchāya a. [apagatā chāyā yasmāt]
    • 1 Shadowless.
    • 2 Having a bad or unlucky shadow.
    • 3 Devoid of brightness, dim.
      One that has no shadow, i. e. a god; cf. N. 14. 21; śriyaṃ bhajaṃtāṃ kiyadasya devāśchāyā nalasyāsti tathāpi naiṣām . itīrayaṃtīva tayā niraikṣi sā (chāyā) naiṣadhe na tridaśeṣu teṣu .. --yā An unlucky shadow, apparition, phantom. [0105-c]
