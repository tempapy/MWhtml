vaivasvataḥ [vivasvato'patyaṃ aṇ]
      1 N. of the seventh Manu who is supposed to preside over the present age; see under Manu; vaivasvato manurnāma mānanīyo manīṣiṇāṃ R. 1. 11; U. 6. 18.
      2 N. of Yama; R. 15. 45.
      3 N. of Agni.
      4 One of the eleven Rudras.
      5 The planet Saturn.
      --taṃ The present age or Manvantara, as presided over by Manu Vaivasvata or the seventh Manu. [1015-a]
