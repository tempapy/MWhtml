• saudha a. (dhī f.) [sudhayā nirmitaṃ raktaṃ vā aṇ] 1 Relating to, or having, nectar. --2 Having plaster, or plastered. --dhaṃ 1 A white-washed mansion, any stuccoed house. --2 Any great mansion or palace, large house; saudhavāsamuṭajena vismṛtaḥ saṃcikāya phalaniḥspṛhastapaḥ R. 19. 2, 7. 5, 13. 40. --3 Silver. --4 Opal. --Comp. --kāraḥ {1} a plasterer. {2} a builder of a house. --vāsaḥ a palatial building. [1140-c]
