• saviśeṣa a. 1 Possessing characteristic qualities. --2 Peculiar, extraordinary. --3 Special, particular; U. 4. --4 Pre-eminent, superior, excellent. --5 Discriminative. ( saviśeṣaṃ and saviśeṣatas are used adverbially in the sense of ‘especially’, particularly’, ‘exceedingly’; anena dharmaḥ saviśeṣamadya me trivargasāraḥ pratibhāti bhāmini Ku. 5. 38; oft. in comp.; Ku. 1. 27, R. 16. 53).
