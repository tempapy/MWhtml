• sakala a.
      1 Together with the parts
      2 All, whole, entire, complete.
      3 Having all the digits, full (as the moon); as in sakaleṃdumukhī.
      4 Having a soft or low sound.
    • --laṃ
      1 Everything.
      2 The whole.
    Comp.
    • --varṇa a. (i. e. pada or vākya) having the letters ka & la, i. e. quarrelling; Nalod. 2. 14. [1075-b]
