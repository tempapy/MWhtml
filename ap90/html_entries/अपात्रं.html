apātraṃ
    • 1 A worthless vessel or utensil.
    • 2 (fig.) An unworthy or undeserving person, unfit receptacle or recipient
    • 3 One unfit or disqualified to receive gifts; adeśakāle yaddānamapātrebhyaśca dīyate Bg. 17. 22; Ks. 24. 26.
      Comp. doing degrading or unworthy acts; disqualification, any action which makes a man unworthy; four disqualifications for a Brāhmaṇa are mentioned in Ms. 11. 70: -niṃditebhyo dhanādānaṃ vāṇijyaṃ śūdrasevanaṃ . apātrīkaraṇaṃ jñeyamasatyasya ca bhāṣaṇam ..; see also 11. 126. --dāyin a. giving to unworthy persons.
      a. supporting the unworthy or worthless; prāyeṇāpātrabhṛdbhavati rājā Pt. 1. [0115-c]
