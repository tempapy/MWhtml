• praśaṃsā
      1 Praise, eulogy, panegyric, applause; praśaṃsāvacanaṃ ‘a complimentary or laudatory remark.’
      2 Description, reference to; as in aprastutapraśaṃsā q. v.
      3 Glory, fame, reputation.
    Comp.
    • --upamā one of the several kinds of upamā mentioned by Daṇḍin; brahmaṇo'pyudbhavaḥ padmaścaṃdraḥ śaṃbhuśirodhṛtaḥ . tau tulyau tvanmukheneti sā praśaṃsopamocyate .. Kāv. 2. 31. --mukhara a. loudly praising. [0762-b]
