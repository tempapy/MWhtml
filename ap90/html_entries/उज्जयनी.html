• ujjaya(yi)nī N. of a city, the modern Oujein in Mālvā, the capital of Vikramāditya. It is one of the seven sacred cities of the Hindus (cf. avaṃti), and the first meridian of their geographers from which they calculate longitude; saudhotsaṃgapraṇayavimukho mā sma bhūrujjayinyāḥ Me. 27. [0288-c]
