anukram 1 U., 4 P.
      1 To go after, follow (fig. also); maharṣibhiranukrāṃtaṃ dharmapaṃthānamāsthitaḥ Rām.; to betake oneself to; tīrthayātrāmanukrāman Mb.; vyavasāyamanukrāṃtā kāṃte tvamatiśobhanaṃ Rām.
      2 To count up, enumerate, state or go through in order; yaccānukrāṃtaṃ yaccānukraṃsyate Mbh. on P. I. 1. 72; give a table of contents, used frequently in the Nirukta. [0068-b]
