• prakhyāta p. p.
      1 Famous, celebrated, renowned, noted.
      2 Forestalled, claimed by right of pre-emption.
      3 Happy, pleased.
      4 Recognised, acknowledged.
    Comp.
    • --bhāṃḍaṃ a commodity the pre-emption of which is claimed by a king.
    • --vaptṛka a. having a celebrated father. [0727-b]
