karṇāṭaḥ (pl.) N. of a country in the south of the Indian Peninsula; (kāvyaṃ) karṇāṭeṃdorjagati viduṣāṃ kaṃṭhabhūṣātvametu Vikr. 18. 102. --ṭī f.
    • 1 A woman of the above country; karṇāṭīcikurāṇāṃ tāṃḍavakaraḥ Vb. 1. 29.
    • 2 The haṃsapadī plant.
    • 3 One of the Rāgiṇīs or musical modes. [0383-a]
