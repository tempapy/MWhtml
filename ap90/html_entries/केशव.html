• keśava a. [keśāḥ praśastāḥ saṃtyasya; keśa-va P. V. 2. 109] 1 Having much, fine or luxuriant hair. --vaḥ An epithet of Viṣṇu; keśava jaya jagadīśa hare Gīt. 1; keśavaṃ patitaṃ dṛṣṭvā pāṃḍavā harṣanirbharāḥ Subhāṣ. --2 The Supreme Being. --Comp. --āyudhaḥ the mango tree. (--dhaṃ) a weapon of Viṣṇu. --ālayaḥ, --āvāsaḥ the Aśvattha tree. [0424-a]
