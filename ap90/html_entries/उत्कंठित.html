• utkaṃṭhita p. p.
      1 Anxious, uneasy.
      2 Regretting, grieving for, sorrowful.
      3 Longing for a beloved person or thing.
    • --tā A mistress longing for her absent lover or husband, one of the eight heroines; she is thus defined --āgaṃtuṃ kṛtacitto'pi daivānnāyāti yatpriyaḥ . taṃdanāgamaduḥkhārtā virahotkaṃṭhitā tu sā .. S. D. 121. [0290-b]
