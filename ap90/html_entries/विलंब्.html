• vilaṃb {c1c} A. 1 To hang down, hang from, be suspended from; R. 10. 62. --2 To set, decline (as the sun &c.). --3 To stay or lag behind, stay or remain, wait, stand still, tāṃ prāṅmukhīṃ tatra niveśya tanvīṃ kṣaṇaṃ vyalabaṃta puro niṣaṇṇāḥ Ku. 7. 13. --4 To delay, be retarded; vilāṃbitaphalaiḥ kālaṃ nināya sa manorathaiḥ R. 1. 33; kiṃ vilaṃvyate tvaritaṃ taṃ praveśaya U. 1. --Caus. 1 To delay, detain. --2 To put off, retard, procrastinate, postpone. --3 To waste, lose.
