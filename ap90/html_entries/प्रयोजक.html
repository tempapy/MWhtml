• prayojaka a. Occasioning, causing. effecting, leading to; inciting, stimulating, deputing, appointing &c.
    • --kaḥ
      1 An employer, one who uses or employs.
      2 An author.
      3 A founder, an institutor.
      4 A money-lender.
      5 A law-giver, legislator.
      6 An instigator. [0757-c]
