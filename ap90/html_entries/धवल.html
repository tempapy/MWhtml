dhavala a. [dhavaṃ kaṃpaṃ lāti lā-ka; Tv.]
      1 White; dhavalātapatra, dhavalagṛhaṃ, dhavalavastraṃ &c.
      2 Handsome.
      3 Clear, pure.
      --laḥ
      1 The white colour.
      2 An excellent bull.
      3 China camphor (cīnakarpūra).
      4 N. of a tree (dhava).
      --laṃ Whitepepper.
      --lā A woman with a white complexion.
      --lī A white cow; ( dhavalā also).
    Comp.
      --utpalaṃ the white water-lily (said to open at moonrise).
      --giriḥ N. of the highest peak of the Himālaya mountain.
      --gṛhaṃ a house whitened with chunam, a palace.
      --pakṣaḥ {1} a goose. {2} the bright half of a lunar month.
      --mṛttikā chalk. [0594-b]
