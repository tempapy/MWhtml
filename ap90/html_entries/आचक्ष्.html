• ācakṣ 2 A.
      1 To speak, announce, declare, teach, tell, relate, communicate, say, narrate (usually with dat. of person); gurave… prastutamācacakṣe R. 5. 19, 12. 55, 14. 36; Ms. 4. 59, 81; M. 5; ityākhyānavida ācakṣate Māl. 2; sometimes with acc.; tattvāṃ kimācakṣmahe Bv. 1. 63 say to or address.
      2 To name, call, say; śarva iti prācyā ācakṣate Śat. Br.
      3 To divine, bespeak; yathā me hṛdayamācaṣṭe M. 4.
      4 To make known, acquaint, introduce to.
      5 To look at, inspect (Ved.). [0228-c]
