jyotsnā [jyotirastyasyāṃ jyotiṣa upadhālopo naśca pratyayaḥ P. V. 2. 114 Sk.]
      1 Moonlight; sphuratsphārajyotsnādhavalitatale kvāpi puline Bh. 3. 42; jyotsnāvato nirviśati pradoṣān R. 6. 34.
      2 Light (in general).
      3 An epithet of Durgā.
      4 A moonlight-night.
    Comp.
      --īśaḥ the moon.
      --priyaḥ the Cakora bird.
      --vṛkṣaḥ a lamp-stand, a candle-stick. [0520-a]
