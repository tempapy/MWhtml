• araṇa a. (ṇī f.) Ved. 1 Departed, gone away; belonging to others, strange, unusual, foreign; distant, remote. (opp. sva, nitya or amā); (Sāy. grieved, sorry duḥkhita, aramamāṇa); inimical, hostile, (with whom one is not on speaking terms). --2 Not fighting. --ṇaṃ 1 Moving, going. --2 Entering into, being inserted. --3 A refuge.
