• ānī {c1c} P. 1 To bring, fetch; bhuvanaṃ matpārśvamānīyate Ś. 7. 8; teṣāmudakamānīya Ms. 3. 210. --2 To bring on, produce, cause; ānināya bhuvaḥ kaṃpaṃ R. 15. 24. --3 To lead towards or near, convey. --4 To mix in. --5 To reduce or lead to any condition; vaśaṃ ānī to reduce to subjection. --6 To lead off, divert. --Caus. To cause to bring (with instr. of person who brings); R. 12. 12, 15. 74. [0241-c]
