• vṛṃdāraka a. (kā or rikā f.) 1 Much, great, many. --2 Eminent, best, excellent. --3 Pleasing, attractive, handsome, lovely. --4 Respectable, venerable. --kaḥ 1 A god, deity; prito vṛṃdāraṇyaṃ natanikhilavṛṃdārakavṛtaḥ Bv. 4. 5. --2 The chief of anything (at the end of comp.); see (2) above. [1008-a]
