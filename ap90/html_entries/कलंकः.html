kalaṃkaḥ
    • 1 A spot, a mark, a dark spot (lit.); R. 13. 15.
    • 2 (Fig.) A stain, stigma, obloquy, disrepute; vyapanayatu kalaṃkaṃ svasvabhāvena saiva Mk. 10. 34; R. 14. 37; so kula°.
    • 3 A fault, defect; Bh. 3. 48.
    • 4 Rust of iron.
      Comp. m. an epithet of Śiva [0386-a]
