• mausala a. (lī f.) [musala-aṇ] 1 Formed like a club, club-shaped. --2 Fought with clubs (as a battle). --3 Relating to the battle with clubs (as a parvan; in this parvan is narrated the death of Kṛṣṇa and Balarāma, and the self-destruction of Kṛṣṇa's family through the curse of Brāhmaṇas). --laḥ A kind of madhuparka.
