• bāhya a. [bahirbhavaḥ ṣyañ ṭilopaḥ] 1 Outer, outward, external, exterior, being or situated without; virahaḥ kimivānutāpayedvada bāhyairviṣayairvipaścitaṃ R. 8. 89; bāhyodyāna Me. 7; Ku. 6. 46; bāhyanāman ‘the outer name’, i. e. the address or superscription written on the back of a letter; adattabāhyatāmānaṃ lekhaṃ lekhayitvā Mu. 1. --2 Foreign, strange; Pt. 1. --3 Excluded from, out of the pale of; jātāstadūrvorupamānabāhyāḥ Ku. 1. 36. --4 Expelled from society, outcast. --hyaḥ 1 A stranger, foreigner; Pt. 1. 259; 5. 26. --2 One who is excommunicated, an outcast. --hyaṃ, bāhyena, bāhye ind. Outside, on the outside, externally. [0796-b]
