• preta p. p. [pra-i-kta] Departed from this world, dead, deceased; svajanāśru kilātisaṃtataṃ dahati pretamiti pracakṣate R. 8. 86. --taḥ
      1 The departed spirit, the spirit before obsequial rites are performed.
      2 A ghost, evil spirit, Bg. 17. 4; Ms. 12. 71.
