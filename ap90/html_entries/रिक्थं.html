• rikthaṃ [ric-thak]
      1 Inheritance, bequest, property left at death; (in law) unobstructed property; vibhajeran sutāḥ pitrorūrdhvaṃ rikthamṛṇaṃ samaṃ Y. 1. 117; Ms. 9. 104; nanu garbhaḥ pitryaṃ rikthamarhati Ś. 6.
      2 Property in general, wealth, possessions; Ms. 8. 27.
      3 Gold.
    Comp.
    • --āda, --grāha, --bhāgin, --hara a. receiving an inheritance, inheriting property. (--m.) an heir.
    • --vibhāgaḥ partition of property.
    • --hārin m. {1} an heir. {2} a maternal uncle. {3} the seed of the fig-tree. [0912-c]
