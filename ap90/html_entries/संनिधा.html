• saṃnidhā 3 U.

      2 To place near; Ś. 3. 19.
      3 To fix upon, direct towards; (dṛṣṭiṃ) punaḥ sahasrārciṣi saṃnidhatte R. 13. 44.
      4 To draw near, approach.
      5 To collect, pile up.
      6 To observe, inspect. --Pass. To be near, be present. --Caus. To collect, bring together, assemble; U. 7. [1090-b]
