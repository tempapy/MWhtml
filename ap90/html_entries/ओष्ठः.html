oṣṭhaḥ [uṣyate uṣṇāhāreṇa, uṣ-karmaṇi than Uṇ. 2. 4] A lip (lower or upper); dvāvoṣṭhau chedayennṛpaḥ Ms. 8. 282; adhara°, biṃba°.
      A creeper bearing a red fruit to which the lip is commonly compared (biṃbaphala). In comp. the a or ā of words before oṣṭha may be optionally dropped, and the fem. may end in ā or ī; as biṃbo(bau) ṣṭhā-ṣṭhī. [cf. L. ostium].
      Comp. the upper and lower lip.
      the creeper Bryonia Grandis (whose fruit resembles a lip).
      a disease of the lips.
      a. labial (produced by the lips).
      the root of the lip.
      a sprout-like or tender lip.
      the cavity made by opening the lips.
      the tree vaṃdhuka.
      any disease of the lips. [0361-b]
