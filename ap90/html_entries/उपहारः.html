• upahāraḥ
      1 An oblation.
      2 A gift, present (in general); ratnapuṣpopahāreṇa chāyāmānarca pādayoḥ R. 4. 84; nṛtyopahāraḥ Me. 32; K. 17, 41, 130, 183.
      3 A victim, sacrifice, an offering to a deity; saparyāṃ sapaśūpahārāṃ R. 16. 39; Māl. 1; upahārīkṛtāsmi Māl. 2; Ve. 4. 7.
      4 A complimentary gift, present to a superior &c.
      5 (a) Offering of flowers &c., flowery gifts; collection of flowers; mlānapuṣpopahāraḥ R. 5. 74; Ku. 6. 42. (b) Presents (to gods) of flowers &c., materials of worship; V. 3; Śi. 11. 36.
      6 Honour.
      7 Indemnity, presents given as the price of peace; H. 4. 110.
      8 Food distributed to guests.
      9 Exultation, mirth (consisting of laughter, dance, singing &c.). [0335-c]
