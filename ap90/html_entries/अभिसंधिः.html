• abhisaṃdhiḥ 1 Speech; deliberate declaration, promise. --2 Intention, object, purpose, aim; tasyā abhisaṃdhinā vidheyīkṛtopi Māl. 1; Dk. 38; svarga° Ku. 6. 47. --3 Implied sense, the meaning intended, as in ayamabhisaṃdhiḥ (frequently occurring in explanatory glosses). --4 Opinion, belief. --5 Special agreement, terms of an agreement, condition, stipulation; athāvaśyameva mādhavasenaḥ pūjyena mocayitavyaḥ śrūyatāmabhisaṃdhiḥ M. 1. --6 Deception. --7 Making peace or alliance. --8 Junction, combination. --Comp. --kṛta a. done intentionally.
