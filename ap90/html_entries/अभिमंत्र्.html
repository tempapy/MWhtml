• abhimaṃtr {c10c} A. (P. also) 1 To consecrate or accompany with sacred hymns; paśurasau yo'bhimaṃtrya kratau hataḥ Ak.; vāmadevābhimaṃtrito'śvaḥ U. 2; Y. 3. 326; 2. 102; 3. 278. --2 To consecrate with magical formulas, charm, enchant, invoke or invite by means of charms; tadabhimaṃtritena caraṇena Dk. 138; pānīyairabhimaṃtritaiḥ Mb. --3 To speak to, address, invite.
