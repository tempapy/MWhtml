• vicar {c1c} P. 1 To wander about, roam over (oft. with acc.); iṣṭāndeśān vicara jalada Me. 115; vicacāra dāvaṃ R. 2. 8; Ku. 3. 31. --2 To do, perform, practise. --3 To act, deal, behave. --4 To deviate from the right path, go about wantonly; Ms. 9. 20. --5 To pass through, pervade. --6 To attack, march against. --7 To associate or have intercourse with. --8 To go badly, fail. --Caus. 1 To think, reflect or meditate upon. --2 To discuss, call in question, debate; ājñā gurūṇāṃ hyavicāraṇīyā R. 14. 46. --3 To calculate, estimate; take into account, consider; pareṣāmātmanaścaiva yo vicārya balābalaṃ Pt. 3; suvicārya yatkṛtaṃ H. 1. 22. --4 To hesitate, doubt. --5 To examine, investigate. --6 To seduce.
