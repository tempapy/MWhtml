• sautra a. (trī f.) [sūtra-aṇ] 1 Belonging to or having a thread or string. --2 Belonging to, mentioned, occurring or declared in, a Sūtra q. v. --traḥ 1 A Brāhmaṇa. --2 An artificial root occurring in grammatical Sūtras which can not be conjugated like a regular verb, but is used only to form derivative words. [1140-c]
