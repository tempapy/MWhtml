vyavaso 4 P.
      1 To strive, endeavour, try, seek, attempt, set about; dhruvaṃ sa nīlotpalapatradhārayā śamīlatāṃ chettumṛṣirvyavasyati Ś. 1. 18; V. 4.
      2 To think of, wish, desire; pātuṃ na prathamaṃ vyavasyati jalaṃ yuṣmāsvapīteṣu yā Ś. 4. 8.
      3 To exert strenuously, be industrious or diligent.
      4 To resolve, determine, settle, decide; Ś. 5. 19.
      5 To accept, undertake; kaccitsaumya vyavasitamidaṃ baṃdhukṛtyaṃ tvayā me Me. 114.
      6 To be convinced or persuaded.
      7 To reflect. [1019-c]
