• chinna p. p. (--stā, --kā) a headless form of Durgā. --mūla a. cut up by the roots; R. 7. 43. --śvāsaḥ a kind of asthma. --saṃśaya a. ‘one whose doubt is dispelled’, free from doubt, confirmed. [0502-a]
