• aṃganā [praśastaṃ aṃgaṃ yasyāḥ sā; aṃgāt kalyāṇe naḥ P. V. 2. 100.] 1 A woman or female in general; nṛpa°, gaja°, hariṇa° &c. --2 A woman with wellrounded limbs, a beautiful woman. --3 (Astr.) Virgo. --4 The female elephant of the north. --Comp. --janaḥ {1} the female sex, woman-kind. {2} women. --priya a. beloved of women. (--yaḥ) N. of a plant (aśoka) Jonesia Aśoka, for women are fond of decking their persons with Aśoka flowers. [0020-a]
