• viparyas 4 P.
      1 To overturn, reverse, invert.
      2 To change, alter.
      3 To take wrongly, misunderstand; pratīkāro vyādheḥ sukhamiti viparyasyati janaḥ Bh. 3. 92.
      4 To undergo change, be affected, (intrans.); daivenopahatasya buddhirathavā sarvā viparyasyati Mu. 6. 8; (cf. the Latin adage ‘quem deus vult perdere prius dementat’). [0981-c]
