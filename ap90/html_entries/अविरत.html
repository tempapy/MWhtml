avirata a.
    • 1 Not desisting or ceasing from (with abl.); uninterrupted, continual, unbroken, perpetual; aviratotkaṃṭhamutkaṃṭhitena Me. 102; Prov. maṃdopyaviratodyogaḥ sadaiva vijayī bhavet ‘slow and steady wins the race’.
    • 2 Abandoned.
      ind. Eternally, continually; avirataṃ parakāryakṛtāṃ satāṃ Bv. 1. 113. [0196-a]
