• varman n. [vṛ-manin] 1 An armour, a coat of mail; svahṛdayamarmāṇi varma karoti sajalanalinīdalajālaṃ Gīt. 4; R. 4. 56; Mu. 2. 8. --2 (Hence) Shelter, protection. --3 Bark, rind. --m. An affix added to the names of Kshatriyas; as caṃḍavarman, prahāravarman; cf. dāsa. --Comp. --hara a. {1} wearing armour {2} old enough to wear armour (i. e. to take part in battle); samyagvinītamatha varmaharaṃ kumāraṃ R. 8. 94.
