• samudra a. Sealed, bearing a seal, stamped; samudro lekhaḥ.
    • --draḥ
      1 The sea, ocean.
      2 An epithet of Śiva.
      3 The number ‘four’.
    • --drā
      1 The plant zedoary.
      2 The Śamī tree.
    Comp.

    • --aruḥ, --āruḥ {1} a crocodile. {2} a large fabulous fish. {3} Rāma's bridge; cf. rāmasetu.
    • --kaphaḥ, --phenaḥ the cuttle fish-bone.
    • --kāṃtā a river.
