• udac, udaṃc a. (m. udaṅ, n. udak, f. udīcī)
      1 Turned or going upwards.
      2 Upper, higher; °kūlaḥ, °tīraḥ &c.
      3 Northern, turned towards the north.
      4 Subsequent.
    • --ka ind.
      1 Above.
      2 Northward, to the north of (with abl.); Ms. 3. 217.
      3 Subsequently.
    Comp.
    • --adriḥ the northern mountain, Himālaya.
    • --ayanaṃ the sun's progress north of the equator (= uttarāyaṇaṃ q. v.).
    • --āvṛttiḥ f. return from the north; udagāvṛttipathena nāradaḥ R. 8. 33. --daśa a. having its skirts or border turned upwards or towards the north (as a garment).
    • --pathaḥ a northern country.
    • --pravaṇa a. {1} inclining or sloping towards the north. {2} proceeding well (as a sacrifice).
    • --bhūmaḥ, --bhūmiḥ good or fertile soil.
    • --mukha a. facing the north; utpatodaṅmukhaḥ khaṃ Me. 14. [0301-b]
