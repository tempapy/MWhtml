• jhaṃjhā
      1 The noise of the wind or of falling rain.
      2 Wind and rain, hurricane, gale.
      3 A clanking sound, jingling.
      4 Raining in large drops.
      5 Anything lost. Comp. --anilaḥ, --marut, --vātaḥ wind with rain, a storm, squall, stormy gale; jhaṃjhāvātaḥ savṛṣṭikaḥ Ak. himāṃbujhaṃjhānilavihvalasya (padmasya) Bv. 2. 169; Amaru. 48; Māl. 9. 17. [0520-1]
