• āsphoṭanaṃ
      1 Flapping, moving to and fro.
      2 Trembling, shaking.
      3 Blowing, expanding.
      4 Contracting, closing.
      5 Slapping or clapping the arms, or the sound produced by it.
      6 Disclosing, manifesting.
      7 Winnowing, thrashing.
    • --nī A gimlet. [0270-c]
