anumud 1 A. (P. in epic poetry)
      1 To rejoice with, join in rejoicing or sympathizing with; muditāmanumodate Bhāg.
      2 To express approval; tadeva rāmamātṛbhiranumoditaṃ U. 2; approve, second, applaud; niṣeddhumāsīdanumodituṃ vā R. 14. 43; to allow with pleasure, permit; anumoditamācāryaiḥ K. 77; ananumoditāmātrā 160. --Caus. To gladden, delight, favour, permit. [0077-c]
