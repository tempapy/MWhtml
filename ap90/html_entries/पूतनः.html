• pūtanaḥ A dead body in a cemetery. --nā 1 N. of a female demon who, while attempting to kill Kṛṣṇa, when but an infant, was herself crushed by him. --2 A demoness, or Rākṣasī in general; mā pūtanātvamupagāḥśivatātiredhi Māl. 9. 49. --Comp. --ariḥ, --sūdanaḥ, --han m. epithets of Kṛṣṇa. [0715-a]
