• śravaṇaḥ, --ṇaṃ [śṛṇotyanena śru-karaṇe lyuṭ] 1 The ear. dhvanati madhupasamūhe śravaṇamapidadhāti Gīt. 5. --2 The hypothenuse of a triangle. --ṇaḥ --ṇā N. of a lunar mansion containing three stars. --ṇaṃ 1 The act of hearing; śravaṇasubhagaṃ Me. 11. --2 Study. --3 Fame, glory. --4 That which is heard or revealed, the Veda; iti śrabaṇāt ‘because of such a Vedic text’. --5 Wealth. --6 Flowing, oozing. --Comp. --iṃdriyaṃ the sense of hearing, the ear. --utpalaṃ a lotus fastened in the ear. --udaraṃ the hollow of the outer ear. --gocara a. within the range of hearing. (--raḥ) ear-shot; as in śravaṇagocare tiṣṭha ‘be within ear-shot’. --pathaḥ, --viṣayaḥ the reach or range of the ear; vṛttāṃtena śravaṇaviṣayaprāpiṇā R. 14. 87. --pālaḥ-lī f. the tip of the ear. --pāśaḥ a beautiful ear. --sumaga a. pleasing to the ear.
