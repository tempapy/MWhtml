• āśvina a. (nī f.)
      1 Belonging or sacred to the Aśvins (aśvinau devate asya).
      2 Pervading.
    • --naḥ
      1 N. of a month (in which the moon is near ths constellation Aśvinī).
      2 A sacrifice or a weapon presided over by the Aśvins.
      3 (du.) The Aśvins.
    • --nī
      1 N. of certain bricks.
      2 A pile, stack (citibhedaḥ).
    • --naṃ A day's journey for a horse or rider (Ved.). [0266-c]
