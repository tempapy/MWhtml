• carcarikā, carcarī [carc-bā° aran gairā. ṅīṣ]
      1 A kind of song.
      2 Striking the hands to beat time (in music).
      3 The recitation of scholars.
      4 Festive sport, festive cries or merriment.
      5 A festival.
      6 Flattery.
      7 Curled hair.
      8 Triple symphony.
      9 Alternate recitation of a poem by two persons. [0487-b]
