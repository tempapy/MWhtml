arśas n. [ṛ-asun vyādhau śuṭ ca Uṇ. 4. 195] Piles.
      Comp. a. destroying piles. (--ghnaḥ) {1} N. of the plant śuraṇa, so called because it is said to cure piles. {2} one part of buttermilk with three parts of water. (--ghnī) {1} N. of the plant Curculigo Archioides Lin. {2} the marking nut plant (bhallātaka).
      a. afflicted with piles.
      a. curing piles. (--taḥ) the marking nut plant. [0170-c]
