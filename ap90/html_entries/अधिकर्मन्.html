• adhikarman n. [adhikaṃkarma]
      1 A higher or superior act.
      2 Superintendence. --m. One who is charged with superintendence.
    Comp.
    • --karaḥ, --kṛt a sort of servant, overseer of workmen.
    • --kṛtaḥ [adhikarma kṛtaṃ yena niṣṭhāṃtasya paranipātaḥ] one appointed to superintend an institution or establishment; sarveṣvadhikṛto yaḥ syāt kuṭuṃbasya tathopari . sodhikarmakṛto jñeyaḥ sa ca kauṭuṃbikaḥ smṛtaḥ .. In families he is the head or paterfamilias. [0047-a]
