viśīrṇa p. p.
      1 Shattered, broken to pieces.
      2 Decayed, withered.
      3 Dropped or fallen down; Ku. 5. 28.
      4 Shrunk, shrivelled.
      5 Impaired, wasted, spoiled.
    Comp.
      --parṇaḥ the Nimba tree.
      --mūrti a. having the body destroyed; Ku. 5. 54. (--rtiḥ) an epithet of the god of love. [0996-a]
