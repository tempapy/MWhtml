kathita p. p.
      1 Told, described, narrated.
      2 Expressed.
      --taḥ The supreme being.
      --taṃ A conversation, discourse; pūrvavṛttakathitaiḥ purāvidaḥ R. 11. 10.
    Comp.
      --padaṃ, --padatā tautology, repetition, considered as a fault of composition relating to a sentence, where a word is used without any specific purpose; see K. P. 7; and S. D. 575 ad loc. [0374-a]
