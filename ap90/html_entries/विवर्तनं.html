vivartanaṃ
      1 Revolving, revolution, whirling round.
      2 Rolling about, turning round; śayyāprāṃtavivartanairvigamayatyunnidra eva kṣapāḥ Ś. 6. 4; Ve. 2. 8, 5. 40: Mv. 7. 5.
      3 Rolling back, returning.
      4 Rolling down, descending.
      5 Existing, abiding.
      6 Reverential salutation.
      7 Passing through various states or existences.
      8 An altered condition; punarakāṃḍavivarnanadāruṇo praviśitaṣṭi vidhirtanaso rujaṃ U. 4. 15; Māl. 4. 7. [0993-c]
