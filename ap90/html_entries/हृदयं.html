• hṛdayaṃ 1 The heart, soul, mind; hṛdaye digdhaśarairivāhataḥ Ku. 4. 25; so ayohṛdayaḥ R. 9. 9; pāṣāṇahṛdaya &c. --2 The bosom, chest, breast; bāṇabhinnahṛdayā nipetuṣī R. 11. 19. --3 Love, affection. --4 The interior or essence of anything. --5 The secret science; aśva°, akṣa° &c. --6 True or divine knowledge. --7 The Veda. --Comp. --ātman m. a heron. --āvidh a. heart-rending, heart-piercing; Bk. 6. 73. --īśaḥ, īśvaraḥ a husband.
