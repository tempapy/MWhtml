• ullāsaḥ 1 Joy, delight; sollāsaṃ U. 6; sakautukollāsaṃ U. 2; ullāsaḥ phullapaṃkeruhapaṭalapatanmattapuṣpaṃdhayānāṃ S. D. --2 Light, splendour. --3 (In Rhet.) A figure of speech in which a reference is made to the merits or demerits of one thing by comparing or contrasting the merits or demerits of another; anyadīyaguṇadoṣaprayuktamanyasya guṇadoṣayorādhānamullāsaḥ R. G.; for examples, see R. G. ad loc.; cf. Chandr. 5. 131-133. --4 A division of a book, such as chapter, section &c. as the ten Ullāsas of the Kāvyaprakāśa. --5 Beginning, commencement. [0342-c]
