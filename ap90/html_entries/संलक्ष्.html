• saṃlakṣ 10 U.
      1 To observe, perceive, see, notice; āścaryadarśanaḥ saṃlakṣyate manuṣyalokaḥ Ś. 7; saṃlakṣyate na chiduropi hāraḥ R. 16. 62 ‘is not noticed or known’; 8. 42.
      2 To test, prove, determine; hemnaḥ saṃlakṣyate hyagnau viśuddhiḥ śyāmikāpi vā R. 1. 10.
      3 To hear, learn, understand.
      4 To characterize, distinguish. [1068-b]
