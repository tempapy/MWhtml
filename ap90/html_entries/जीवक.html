• jīvaka a. [jīv --ṇic ṇvul] 1 Living, making a livelihood by, generating &c. --2 One who lives a long time. --kaḥ 1 A living being. --2 A servant. --3 A Buddhist mendicant, any mendicant who lives by begging. --4 A usurer. --5 A snakecatcher. --6 A tree. --7 A medicinal plant of that name.
