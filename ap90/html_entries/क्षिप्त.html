• kṣipta p. p. [kṣip-kta]
      1 Thrown, scattered, hurled, cast.
      2 Abandoned.
      3 Disregarded, neglected, disrespected.
      4 Placed.
      5 Distracted, mad; (see kṣip).
    • --ptā Night.
    • --ptaṃ A wound caused by shooting.
    Comp.
    • --kukkuraḥ a mad dog.
    • --citta a. distracted in mind, absent-minded.
    • --deha a. prostrating the body, lying down. [0437-b]
