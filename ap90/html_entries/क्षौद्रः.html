• kṣaudraḥ

      2 N. of a mixed caste.
    • --draṃ
      1 Smallness.
      2 Meanness, lowness.
      3 Honey; sakṣaudrapaṭalairiva R. 4. 63.
      4 Water.
      5 A particle of dust.
    Comp.
    • --jaṃ wax.
    • --dhātuḥ a kind of mineral substance; (mākṣika).
    • --mehaḥ the disease diabetes mellitus. [0440-a]
