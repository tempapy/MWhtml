• camūḥ f. [cam-ū Uṇ. 1. 81] 1 An army (in general); paśyaitāṃ pāṃḍuputrāṇāmācārya mahatīṃ camūṃ Bg. 1. 3; vāsavīnāṃ camūnāṃ Me. 43; gajavatī javatīvrahayā camūḥ R. 9. 10. --2 A division of an army consisting of 729 elephants, as many cars, 2187 horse, and 3645 foot. --3 Ved. A dish or vessel. --4 A grave. --Comp. --caraḥ a soldier, warrior. --nāthaḥ, --paḥ, --patiḥ the leader of an army, a general, commander; R. 13. 74. --haraḥ an epithet of Śiva. [0485-c]
