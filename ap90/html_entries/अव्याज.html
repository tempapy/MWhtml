avyāja a. Free from guile or fraud.

    • 2 Simplicity, artlessness; oft. in comp. with suṃdara, manohara &c. in the sense of ‘artlessly’, ‘naturally’; idaṃ kilāvyājamanoharaṃ vapuḥ Ś. 1. 18; M. 2. 14; °ramaṇīyaṃ prema K. 175. [0199-b]
