• kad
      I. 4 A. (kadyate) To be confounded or confused, to suffer mentally.
      II. 1 A. (kadate) also 1 P.
      1 To cry, to weep or shed tears.
      2 To grieve.
      3 To call.
      4 To kill or hurt; see kaṃd. [0374-b]
    • kad ind. [kad-kvip] Ved. A particle of interrogation, ‘where’. This particle which is a substitute for the word ku is often used as the first member of a comp. and expresses the senses of badness, littleness, deterioration, uselessness, defectiveness &c. of anything.
