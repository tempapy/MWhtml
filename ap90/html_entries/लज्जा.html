lajjā [lajj bhāve a]
      1 Shame, kāmāturāṇāṃ na bhayaṃ na lajjā Subhāṣ.; vihāya lajjāṃ R. 2. 40; Ku. 1. 48.
      2 Bashfulness, modesty; śṛṃgāralajjāṃ nirūpayati Ś. 1; Ku. 3. 7; R. 7. 25; lajje tvaṃ majja siṃdhau Subhāṣ.
      3 N. of the sensitive plant.
    Comp.
      --anvita a. modest, bashful.
      --āvaha, --kara a. (rā or rī f.) causing shame, shameful, disgraceful, ignominious; Pt. 5. 10. --śīla a. bashful, modest.
      --rahita, --śūnya, --hīna, a. shameless, impudent, immodest. [0922-c]
