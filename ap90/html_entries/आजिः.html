• ājiḥ m. f. [ajaṃtyasyāṃ, aj-iṇ P. III. 3. 108 Vārt.] 1 A battle, fight, combat; te tu yāvanta evājau tāvāṃśca dadṛśe sa taiḥ R. 12. 45. --2 A fighting or running match; ājiṃ dhāv or i, -aj or sṛ to run with or against any one for prize. --3 Battle-field, place for running; race-course, level ground; śastrāṇyājau nayanasalilaṃ cāpi tulyaṃ mumoca Ve. 3. 9. --4 Limit, boundary. --5 A road, way (m.). --6 An instant (m.). --7 Abuse, invective. --Comp. --aṃtaḥ the goal in a race-course. --kṛt a. Ved. {1} running for a prize. {2} making a war. --kriyā fighting. --tur a. Ved. victorious in battles. [0230-c]
