vāhinī [vāho astyasyāḥ ini ṅīp]
      1 An army; āśiṣaṃ prayuyuje na vāhinīṃ R. 11. 6, 13. 66.
      2 A division of an army consisting of 81 elephants, as many chariots, 243 horse, and 405 foot.
      3 A river.
    Comp.
      --niveśaḥ the camp of an army. --patiḥ {1} a general, a commanding officer. {2} the ocean (lord of rivers). [0964-b]
