• kutra ind.
      1 Where, in which place; kutra me śiśuḥ Pt. 1; pravṛttiḥ kutra kartavyā H. 1.
      2 In which case; tejasā saha jātānāṃ vayaḥ kutropayujyate Pt. 1. 328. (kutra is sometimes used for the loc. sing. of kim). When connected with the particles cid, cana or api, kutra becomes indefinite in sense. kutrāpi, --kutracit somewhere, anywhere; na kutrāpi no where; kutracit-kutracit in one place-in another place, herehere; Ms. 9. 34.
