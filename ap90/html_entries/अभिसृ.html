abhisṛ 1 P.
    • 1 To go up to, go towards, approach; to go to some place or other, go or proceed; purobhisasre surasuṃdarījanaiḥ Ki. 8. 4.
    • 2 To attack, assail.
    • 3 To go or advance to meet (as at an appointed place); suṃdarīrabhisasāra K. 58; Dk. 51, 52, 91; Śi. 6. 26. --Caus. To visit, approach, go to meet; vallabhānabhisisārayiṣūṇāṃ 10. 20, 21; S. D. 115; Ki. 9. 38; Mk. 8. [0141-b]
