puṣṭa p. p. [puṣ-kta]
      1 Nourished, fed, reared, brought up.
      2 Thriving, growing, strong, fat.
      3 Tended, cared for.
      4 Rich, magnificently provided.
      5 Complete, perfect.
      6 Full-sounding, loud.
      7 Eminent.
      --ṣṭaḥ N. of Viṣṇu.
      --ṣṭaṃ
      1 Nourishment.
      2 Acquisition, gain (Ved.). [0712-c]
