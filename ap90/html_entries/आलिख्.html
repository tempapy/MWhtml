• ālikh 6 P.
      1 To write, delineate, draw lines; mano niṣṭhāśūnyaṃ bhramati ca kimapyālikhati ca Māl. 1. 31; Mk. 2.
      2 To paint, draw in a picture; ālikhita iva sarvato raṃgaḥ Ś. 1; V. 2; tvāmālikhya praṇayakupitāṃ Me. 105; R. 19. 19; M. 2. 2.
      3 To portray, write, sketch.
      4 To scratch, scrape, touch; as in viṃdhyamālikhaṃtamivāṃbaraṃ. [0258-c]
