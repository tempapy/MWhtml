kāṃtā
      1 A beloved or lovely woman.
      2 A mistress, wife in general; kāṃtāsakhasya śayanīyaśilātalaṃ te U. 3. 21; so Śi. 10. 73.
      3 The Priyangu creeper.
      4 Large cardamoms.
      5 A kind of perfume.
      6 The earth.
    Comp.
      --aṃghridohadaḥ the Aśoka tree; see aśoka. [0393-b]
