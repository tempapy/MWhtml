• kliś
      I. 4 A. (also P. according to some authorities) (kliśyate, kliṣṭa or kliśita)
      1 To be tormented, be afflicted, suffer; apyupadeśagrahaṇe nātikliśyate vaḥ śiṣyā M. 1; trayaḥ parārthe kliśyaṃti sākṣiṇaḥ pratibhūḥ kulaṃ Ms. 8. 169.
      2 To torment, molest.
      II. 9 P. (kliśnāti, kliṣṭa, kliśita)
      1 To torment, afflict, molest, distress; kliśnāti labdhaparipālanavṛttireva Ś. 5. 6; evamārādhyamānopi kliśnāti bhuvanatrayaṃ Ku. 2. 40; R. 11. 58.
      2 To suffer, feel pain. [0433-a]
