rūp 10 U. (rūpayati te, rūpita)
      1 To form, fashion.
      2 To represent on the stage, act, gesticulate; rathavegaṃ nirūpya Ś. 1.
      3 To mark, observe carefully, behold, look at.
      4 To find out, seek.
      5 To consider, ponder over.
      6 To settle, fix upon.
      7 To examine, investigate.
      8 To feign.
      9 To appoint. [0916-a]
