māhātmyaṃ [mahātmano bhāvaḥ ṣyañ]
      1 Magnanimity, noble-mindedness, greatness; U. 4. 5.
      2 Majesty, dignity, exalted position.
      3 The peculiar virtue of any divinity or sacred shrine; or a work giving an account of the merits of such divinities or shrines; as davomīhātmya, śanimāhātmya &c. [0864-c]
