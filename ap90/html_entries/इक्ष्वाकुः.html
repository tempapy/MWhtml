• ikṣvākuḥ 1 N. of the celebrated ancestor of the solar kings who ruled in Ayodhyā; he was the first of the Solar kings and was a son of Manu Vaivasvata; (cf. Bhāg. kṣuvatastu manorjajña ikṣvākurghrāṇataḥ sutaḥ); ikṣvākuvaṃśo' bhimataḥ prajānāṃ U. 1. 44. --2 A descendant of Ikṣvāku; galitavayasāmikṣvākūṇāmidaṃ hi kulavrataṃ R. 3. 70. --kuḥ f. A kind of bitter gourd. [0274-a]
