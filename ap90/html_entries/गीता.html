• gītā [gai karmaṇi kta] A name given to certain sacred writings in verse (often in the form of a dialogue) which are devoted to the exposition of particular religious and theosophical doctrines; e. g. śivagītā, rāmagītā, bhagavadgītā. But the name appears to be especially confined tothe last, the Bhagavadgītā; gītāsugītā kartavyā kimanyaiḥ śāstravistaraiḥ . yā svayaṃ padmanābhasya mukhapadmādviniḥsṛtā .. quoted by Śrīdharasvāmin. [0459-c]
