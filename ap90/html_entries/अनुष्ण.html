anuṣṇa a.
      1 Not hot, cold, chilly; anuṣṇairānaṃdāśrubiṃdubhiḥ R. 12. 62.
      2 Apathetic; lazy, sluggish (alasa).
      --ṣṇaḥ Cold touch or sensation.
      --ṣṇā N. of a stream.
      --ṣṇaṃ A waterlily, blue lotus (utpala).
    Comp.
      --guḥ (--go ray) having cold rays, the moon.
      --vallikā N. of a plant nīladūrvā. [0084-a]
