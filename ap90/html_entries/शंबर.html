• śaṃbara a. [śaṃb-arac] Best, excellent.
    • --raḥ
      1 N. of a demon slain by Pradyumna, q v.
      2 A mountain.
      3 A kind of deer.
      4 A Jina.
      5 N. of the trees citraka, loghra, and arjuna.
      6 A kind of fish.
      7 War.
    • --raṃ
      1 Water.
      2 A cloud.
      3 Wealth.
      4 A rite or religious observance
    Comp.
    • --ariḥ, sadanaḥ epithets of Pradyumna or the god of love. --asuraḥ the demon Sambara. [1034-b]
