• khuraḥ [khura-ka]
      1 A hoof; R. 1. 85, 2. 2; Ms. 4. 67.
      2 A kind of perfume.
      3 A razor.
      4 The foot of a bed-stead.
    Comp.
    • --āghātaḥ, --kṣepaḥ a kick.
    • --ṇas, --ṇasa a. flat nosed.
    • --padavī a horse's foot-marks.
    • --praḥ an arrow with a semi-circular head; see kṣurapra.
    • --nyāsaḥ prints of hoof; R. 2. 2. [0445-a]
