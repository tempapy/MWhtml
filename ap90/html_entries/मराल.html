marāla a. [mṛ-ālac]
      1 Soft, greasy, unctuous.
      2 Bland, tender.
      --laḥ (lī f.)
      1 A swan, flamingo, goose; marālakulanāyakaḥ kathaya ra kathaṃ vartatāṃ Bv. 1. 3; vidhehi marālavikāraṃ Gīt. 11; N. 6. 72.
      2 A kind of duck (kāraṃḍava).
      3 A horse.
      4 A cloud.
      5 Collyrium.
      6 A grove of pomegranate trees.
      7 A rogue, cheat. [0846-a]
