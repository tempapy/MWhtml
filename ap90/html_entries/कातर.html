• kātara a. [īṣattaratisvakāryāsīddhiṃ gacchati, tṝ-ac koḥ kādeśaḥ Tv.] 1 Cowardly, timid, discouraged; varjayaṃti ca kātarān Pt. 4. 42; Amaru. 7, 30, 75; R. 11. 78; Me. 77. --2 Distressed, grieved, afraid; kimevaṃ kātarāsi Ś. 4. --3 Agitated; perplexed, confused; Bh. 1. 60. --4 Tremulous through fear (as eyes); R. 2. 52; Amaru. 79. --raḥ 1 A large kind of fish. --2 A boat, raft.
