• śakta p. p. [śak-kta] 1 Able, capable, competent (with gen. or loc. or inf.); bahavo'sya karmaṇaḥ śaktāḥ Ve. 3; tasyopakāre śaktastvaṃ kiṃ jīvan kimutānyathā ibid. --2 Strong, mighty, powerful. --3 Rich, opulent; Ms. 11. 9. --4 Significant, expressive, conveying a meaning by denotation ( abhidhā or śakti) and not by indication (lakṣaṇā) (as a word). --5 Clever, intelligent. --6 Speaking kindly or agreeably.
