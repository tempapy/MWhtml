aśmaṃtakaḥ --kaṃ
      1 A fire-place.
      2 The shade for a lamp (dīpādhāra).
      3 A kind of grass.
      --kaḥ N. of several trees:--(a) amloṭaka; its leaf; Māl. 9. 7; (b) N. of a tree kovidāraka; (c) N. of a plant from the fibres of which a Brāhmaṇa's girdle may be made. [0202-c]
