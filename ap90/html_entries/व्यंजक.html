• vyaṃjaka a. (jikā f.)
      1 Making clear, indicating, showing, manifesting.
      2 Suggesting or insinuating a meaning (as a word, opp. vācaka and lākṣaṇika q. q. v. v.).
    • --kaḥ
      1 Dramatic action or gesture, external indication of an internal feeling by suitable gesticulation.
      2 A sign, symbol.
      3 Figurative expression or insinuation. [1016-c]
