• vibhram 1 4 P.
      1 To roam, wander about.
      2 To hover, whirl or wheel round.
      3 To scare away, disperse, scatter about.
      4 To be confused or disordered, be bewildered or perplexed; Bg. 16. 16. --Caus. To confuse, confound; prabhāmattaścaṃdro jagādidamaho vibhramayati K. P. 10. [0985-c]
