• nilayaḥ 1 A hiding place, the lair or den of animals, a nest (of birds); Śi. 9. 4. --2 An abode, residence, house, dwelling; oft. at the end of comp. in the sense of ‘living or residing in’. --3 Hiding oneself. --4 Total destruction. --5 Setting, disappearance; dināṃte nilayāya gaṃtuṃ R. 2. 15 (where the word is used in sense 1 also).
