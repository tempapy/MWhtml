• samunnatiḥ f. 1 Lifting up, raising. --2 Height, loftiness, elevation (mental also); manasaḥ śikharāṇāṃ ca saṭṭaśī te samunnatiḥ Ku. 6. 66; R. 3. 10. --3 Eminence, high position or dignity, exaltation; uttamaiḥ saha saṃgena ko na yāti samunnatiṃ; sa jāto yena jāteva yāti vaṃśaḥ samunnatiṃ Subhāṣ. --4 Rise, prosperity, increase, success; vinipātopi samaḥ samunnateḥ Ki. 2. 34, or prakṛtiḥ khalu sā mahīyasaḥ sahate nānyasamunnatiṃ yayā 2. 21. --5 Pride, arrogance.
