• patraṃ [pat-ṣṭran]
      1 A leaf (of a tree); dhatte bharaṃ kusumapatraphalāvalīnāṃ Bv. 1. 94.
      2 The leaf of a flower, lotus &c. nīlotpalapatradhārayā Ś. 1. 18.
      3 A leaf for writing upon, a paper, a leaf written upon; patramāropya dīyatāṃ Ś. 6 ‘commit to writing;’ V. 2. 14.
      4 A letter, document; Pt. 1. 403.
      5 Any thin leaf or plate of metal, a gold-leaf.
      6 The wing of a bird, a pinion, feather of an arrow; R. 2. 31; Ku. 3. 27.
      7 A vehicle in general (car, horse, camel &c.); diśaḥ papāta patreṇa veganiṣkaṃpaketunā R. 15. 84; N. 3. 16.
      8 Painting the person (particularly the face) with musk, sandal-juice or other fragrant substances; racaya kucayoḥ patraṃ citraṃ kuruṣva kapolayoḥ Gīt. 12; R. 13. 55.
      9 The blade of a sword, knife &c.
      10 A knife, dagger.
