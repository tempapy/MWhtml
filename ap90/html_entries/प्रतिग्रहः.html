• pratigrahaḥ
      1 Receiving, accepting.
      2 Receiving or accepting a donation.
      3 The right of receiving or accepting a donation.
      4 The right of receiving gifts (which is a peculiar prerogative of Brāhmaṇas); Ms. 1. 88; 4. 86; Y. 1. 118.
      5 A gift, present, donation; rājñaḥ pratigraho'yaṃ Ś. 1; Śi. 14. 35.
      6 A receiver (of a gift).
      7 Kind or friendly reception.
      8 Favour, grace.
      9 Marrying.
      10 Listening to.
      11 The rear of an army.
      12 A spitting-pot.
      13 The sun near the moon's node. [0736-b]
