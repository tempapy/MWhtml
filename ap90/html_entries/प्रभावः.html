• prabhāvaḥ
      1 Lustre, splendour, brilliance.
      2 Dignity, glory, majesty grandeur, majestic lustre; prabhāvavāniva lakṣyate Ś. 1.
      3 Strength, valour, power, efficacy; Pt. 1. 7.
      4 Regal power (one of the three Śaktis q. v.).
      5 A superhuman power or faculty, miraculous power; R. 2. 41, 62; 3. 40, V. 1, 2, 5.
      6 Magnanimity.
      7 Extension, circumference.
    Comp.
    • --ja a. proceeding from majesty or regal power. [0753-c]
