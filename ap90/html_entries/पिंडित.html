• piṃḍita a. [piṃḍ-kta]
      1 Pressed or rolled into a ball or lump.
      2 Thick. lumpish.
      3 Heaped together, collected: eṣa piṃḍitārthaḥ M. 1 ‘this is the meaning on the whole’.
      4 Mixed with.
      5 Added, multi plied.
      6 Counted, numbered.
    • --taḥ Incense. [0702-a]
