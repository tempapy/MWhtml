• amukta a.
      1 Not loosened, not let go.
      2 Not liberated from recurring birth and death, not having got final beatitude.
    • --ktaṃ A weapon (a knife, sword &c.) that is always grasped and not thrown.
    Comp.
    • --hasta a. one whose hand is not open or free (to give), sparing, stingy (in a bad sense); frugal, economical, prudent (in a good sense); sadā prahṛṣṭayā bhāvyaṃ vyaye cāmuktahastayā Ms. 5. 150. [0151-b]
