• dīna a. [dī-kta taspa na] 1 Poor, indigent. --2 Distressed, ruined, afflicted, miserable, wretched. --3 Sorry, dejected, melancholy, sad; sā virahe tava dīnā Gīt. 4. --4 Timid, frightened. --5 Mean, piteous; Bh. 2. 51. --naḥ A poor person, one in distress or misery; dīnānāṃ kalpavṛkṣaḥ Mk. 1. 48; dināni dīnoddharaṇocitasya R. 2. 25. --naṃ Distress, wretchedness. --nā The female of a mouse or shrew. --Comp. --dayālu, --vatsala a. kind to the poor. --baṃdhuḥ a friend of the poor. --locanaḥ a cat.
