• avagāh 1 A.
      1 (a) To bathe oneself in, plunge into, dive into; with acc. or loc.; tamopahaṃtrīṃ tamasāṃ vagāhya R. 14. 76; anyamavakāśamavagāhiṣye V. 4; svapne'vagāhatetyarthaṃ jalaṃ Y. 1. 272; Bk. 6. 29, 16. 38. (b) To go deep into, be absorbed into (fig.); amātyarākṣasenāpyanavagāhitayāryacāṇakyasya caritamavagāhitumicchasi Mu. 6.
      2 To enter, penetrate, fully pervade; vimānaśṛṃgāṇyavagāhamānaḥ (ghoṣaḥ) Ku. 7. 40; pūrvāparau toyanidhī vagāhya Ku. 1. 1; pūrvāparasamudrāvagāḍhaḥ Ś. 7; Mk. 2; see avagāḍha also. --Caus. To bathe, cause to bathe. [0177-b]
