• pāraśava a. (vī f.) 1 Made of iron. --2 Relating to or derived from an axe. --vaḥ 1 Iron. --2 The son of a Brāhmaṇa by a Śūdra woman; yaṃ brahmaṇastu śūdāyāṃ kāmādutpādayetsutaṃ . sa pārayannaṃva śavastasmātpāraśavaḥ smṛtaḥ Ms. 9. 178; or paraṃ śavāt brāhmaṇasyaiṣa putraḥ śūdra putraṃ pāraśavaṃ tamāhuḥ Mb. --3 An adulterine, a bastard.
