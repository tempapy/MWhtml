• pratibhā {c2c} P. 1 To shine, appear bright or luminous; pratibhāṃtyadya vanāni ketakīnāṃ Ghaṭ. 15. --2 To show oneself, become manifest. --3 To seem, appear; strīratnasṛṣṭiraparā pratibhāti sā me Ś. 2. 9; R. 2. 47; Ku. 5. 38; 6. 54. --4 To occur to, come into the mind of; as in nottaraṃ pratibhāti me. --5 To fall to the lot or share of. --6 To seem fit or proper; Pt. 3. [0739-c]
  • pratibhā 1 An appearance, a look. --2 Light, splendour. --3 Intellect, understanding; Ki. 16. 27; Vikr. 1. 18, 23. --4 Genius, bright conception, vivid imagination; (prajñā navanavonmeṣaśālinī pratibhā matā). --5 An image, reflection. --6 Audacity, impudence. --7 Suitableness, agreeableness. --Comp. --anvita a. {1} endowed with genius, intelligent. {2} audacious, bold. --mukha a. bold, confident. --hāniḥ f. {1} darkness. {2} absence of intellect or genius.
