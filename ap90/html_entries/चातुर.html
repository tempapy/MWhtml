cātura a. (rī f.) [catura eva svārthe aṇ]
      1 Relating to four.
      2 Clever, able, shrewd.
      3 Speaking well, flattering.
      4 Drawn by four (as a carriage).
      5 Governing, ruling.
      6 Visible, perceptible.
      --raḥ A small round pillow.
      --raṃ A four-wheeled carriage.
      --rī Skill, dexterity, ability; tadbhaṭacāturīturī N. 1. 12. [0489-b]
