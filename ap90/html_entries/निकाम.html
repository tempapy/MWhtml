• nikāma a. [ni kam ghañ] 1 Plentiful copious, abundant; nikājalāṃ strotovahāṃ Ś. 6. 16. --2 Desirous of. --maḥ, --maṃ Wish, desire. --maṃ ind. 1 According to one's wish or desire, agreeably to desire. --2 To one's satisfaction, to the heart's content; rātrau nikāmaṃ śayitavyamapi nāsti Ś. 2 ‘I cannot even sleep at ease or comfortably at night.’ --3 Very much, exceedingly nikāmaṃ kṣāmāṃgī Māl. 2. 3; often used as the first member of comp. when it loses its final m; nikāmaniraṃkuśaḥ Gīt. 7; Ku. 5. 23; Śi. 4. 54. [0617-c]
