vākyaṃ [vac-ṇyat casya kaḥ]
      1 Speech, words, a sentence, saying, what is spoken; śṛṇu me vākyaṃ ‘here my words’, ‘hear me’; vākye na saṃtiṣṭhate ‘does not obey’; Śi. 2. 24.
      2 A sentence, period (complete utterance of a thought); vākyaṃ syādyogyatākāṃkṣāsattiyuktaḥ padoccayaḥ S. D. 6; padasamūho vākyaṃ Tarka K.; śrautyārthī ca bhavedvākye samāse taddhite tathā K. P. 10.
      3 An argument or syllogism (in logic).
      4 A precept, rule, an aphorism.
      5 (In astr.) The solar process for all astronomical computations.
    Comp.
      --arthaḥ the meaning of a sentence. °upamā a variety of Upamā according to Daṇḍin; see Kāv. 2. 43. --ālāpaḥ conversation, discourse.
      --khaṃḍanaṃ refutation of an assertion or argument.
      --padaṃ a word in a sentence.
      --padīyaṃ N. of a work attributed to Bhartṛhari.
      --paddhatiḥ f. the manner of composing sentences, diction, style.
      --prabaṃdhaḥ {1} a treatise, connected composition. {2} the flow of sentences.
      --prayogaḥ employment of speech, use of language.
      --bhedaḥ a different assertion, a divergent statement; vākyabhedānbahūnagamat Mu. 2. --racanā, --vinyāsaḥ arrangement of words in a sentence, syntax.
      --viśārada a. eloquent, skilled in speech.
      --śeṣaḥ {1} the remainder of a speech, an unfinished or incomplete sentence; sadoṣāvakāśa iva te vākyaśeṣaḥ V. 3. {2} an elliptical sentence. [0955-b]
