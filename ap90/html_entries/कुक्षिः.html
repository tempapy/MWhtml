• kukṣiḥ 1 The belly (in general); jihmitādhmātakuṃkṣiḥ (bhujagapatiḥ) Mk. 9. 12. --2 The womb, the part of the belly containing the fœtus; kuṃbhīnasyāśca kukṣijaḥ R. 15. 15; Śi. 13. 40. --3 The interior of anything: R. 10. 65 (where the word is used in sense 2 also). --4 A cavity in general. --5 A cavern, cave; R. 2. 38, 67. --6 The sheath of a sword. --7 N. of Bali. --8 A bay, gulf. --Comp. --śūlaḥ belly-ache, colic.
