• abhidheya pot. p.
      1 To be named, mentioned, expressed &c. vāgeva me nābhidheyaviṣayamavatarati trapayā K. 151 words refuse, through shame, to express what I have to say.
      2 Nameable, as a category or predicament (in logic); abhidheyāḥ padārthāḥ, abhidheyatvaṃ padārthasāmānyalakṣaṇaṃ.
    • --yaṃ
      1 Signification, meaning, sense, import; P. I. 1. 34 Sk.; Ki. 14. 5.
      2 A substance.
      3 The subject-matter; ihābhidheyaṃ saprayojanaṃ K. P. 1; iti prayojanābhidheyasaṃbaṃdhāḥ Mugdha.
      4 The primary or literal sense of a word (= abhidhā); abhidheyāvinābhūtapratītirlakṣaṇocyate K. P. 2. [0130-c]
