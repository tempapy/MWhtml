• upagrahaṇaṃ
      1 Seizing (from below); taking hold of; as in pādopagrahaṇaṃ.
      2 Seizure, capture, taking one prisoner.
      3 Supporting, furthering, promoting.
      4 Holy study, study of the Vedas (after being initiated into them); vedopagrahaṇārthāya tāvagrāhayata prabhuḥ Rām. [0318-b]
