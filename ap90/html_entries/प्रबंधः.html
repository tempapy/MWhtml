• prabaṃdhaḥ 1 A bond, tie. --2 Uninterruptedness, continuance, continuity, uninterrupted series or succession; vicchedamāpa bhuvi yastu kathāprabaṃdhaḥ K. 239; kriyāprabaṃdhādayamadhvarāṇāṃ R. 6. 23; 3. 58; Māl. 6. 3. --3 A continued or connected narrative or discourse; anujjhitārthasaṃbaṃdhaḥ prabaṃdho durudāharaḥ Śi. 2. 73. --4 Any literary work or composition; prathitayaśasāṃ bhāsakavisaumilla kavimiśrādīnāṃ prabaṃdhānatikramya M. 1; pratyakṣaraśleṣamayaprabaṃdha &c. Vās. --5 Arrangement, plan, scheme; as in kapaṭaprabaṃdhaḥ --Comp. --arthaḥ the subject-matter of a composition or treatise. --kalpanā a feigned story, a work of imagination founded on a substratum of fact, prabaṃdhakalpanāṃ stokasatyāṃ prājñāḥ kathāṃ viduḥ. --varṣaḥ continuous or incessant rain.
