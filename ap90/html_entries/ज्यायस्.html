• jyāyas a. (sī f.; compar. of praśasya, vṛddha) 1 Elder, senior; prasavakrameṇa sa kila jyāyān U. 6. --2 Superior, more excellent or worthy; Ms. 4. 8, 3. 137; Bg. 3. 1, 8. --3 Larger, greater. --4 (In law) One not a minor, i. e. come of age and responsible for his own action. --5 Aged, old. --6 Decayed, worn out.
