• pauruṣeya a. (yī f.) [puruṣa-ṭhañ] 1 Derived from or belonging to man, human, incidental to man; made, established or propounded by man; as in apauruṣeyā vai vedāḥ. --2 Manly, virile. --3 Spiritual. --yaḥ 1 Manslaughter (puruṣavadha). --2 A crowd of men. --3 A day labourer, hireling. --4 Human action, man's work. --5 Law as affecting persons. --yaṃ Human work, action of man.
