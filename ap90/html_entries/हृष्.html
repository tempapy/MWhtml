• hṛṣ {c1c} {c4c} P. (harṣati, hṛṣyati, hṛṣṭa or hṛṣita) 1 To be delighted or rejoiced, be pleased or glad, to exult, rejoice; advitīyaṃ rucātmānaṃ matvā kiṃ caṃdra hṛṣyasi Bv. 2. 54. --2 To bristle or stand erect, stand on end (as the hair of the body); hṛṣitāstanūruhāḥ Dk.; hṛṣyaṃti romakūpāni Mb. --3 To become erect (said of other things, e. g. the penis). --4 To lie, tell a lie. --Caus. ( harṣayati-te) To please, delight, fill with pleasure. [1174-c]
