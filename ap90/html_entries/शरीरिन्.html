• śarīrin a. (ṇī f.) 1 Embodied, corporeal, incarnate; karuṇasya mūrtirathavā śarīriṇī birahavyathaiva vanameti jānakī U. 3. 4; bhāvāviva śarīriṇau M. 1. 10. --2 Living. --m. 1 Anything endowed with a body (whether animate or inanimate); śarīriṇāṃ sthāvarajaṃgamānāṃ sukhāya tajjanmadinaṃ babhūva Ku. 1. 23; maraṇaṃ prakṛtiḥ śarīriṇāṃ R. 8. 87, 43. --2 A sentient being. --3 A man. --4 The soul (clad with the body); R. 8. 89; Bg. 2. 18.
