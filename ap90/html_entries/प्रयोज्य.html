prayojya pot. p.
      1 To be used or employed.
      2 To be practised.
      3 To be produced or caused.
      4 To be appointed.
      5 To be thrown or discharged (as a missile).
      6 To be set to work.
      --jyaḥ A servant, an employè.
      --jyaṃ Capital, principal. [0757-c]
