• paryāp {c5c} P. (used generally in p. p.) 1 To be competent; paryāptaṃ tvidameteṣāṃ balaṃ bhīṣmābhirakṣitaṃ Bg. 1. 10; Ms. 11. 7. --2 To be able. --3 To be full: as in paryaptakalaḥ, paryāptadakṣiṇaḥ. --4 To save, defend, preserve; imāṃ parīpsurdurjāteḥ M. 5. 11. --5 To finish. [0685-c]
