    • 5 Possible; ubhayamapyanupapannaṃ V. 2; Ku. 3. 12.
    • 6 Full of, endowed with, possessed of, furnished with; upapanno guṇairiṣṭaiḥ Nala. 1. 1; Ms. 9. 141, 244; tallakṣaṇopapannaḥ Ś. 5.
    • 7 Demonstrated, proved.
    • 8 Offered, presented.
    • 9 Cured. [0325-a]
