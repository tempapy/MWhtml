• pātaṃjala a. (lī f.) Composed by Patañjali; pātaṃjale mahābhāṣye kṛtabhūripariśramaḥ Paribhāṣenduśekhara.
    • --laṃ The Yoga system of philosophy taught by Patañjali. (It is generally believed that Patañjali, the author of the Mahābhāṣya, is the same as the author of the Yoga system: but it is a dubious point). [0692-c]
