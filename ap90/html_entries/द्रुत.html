• druta p. p. [dru-kta]
      1 Quick, swift, speedy.
      2 Flown, run away, escaped.
      3 Melted, liquid, dissolved.
      4 Scattered, diffused.
      5 Indistinct.
      6 Moved, softened; Māl. 5. 28; see dru.
    • --taḥ
      1 A scorpion.
      2 A tree.
      3 A cat.
    • --taṃ ind. Quickly, swiftly, speedily, immediately.
    Comp.
    • --pada a. going quickly.
    • --vilaṃbitaṃ N. of a metre; see App. I. [0585-b]
    • druta, drutiḥ See under dru. [0585-b]
