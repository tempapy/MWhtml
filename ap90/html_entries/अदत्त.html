adatta a.
    • 1 Not given.
    • 2 Unjustly or improperly given.
    • 3 Not given in marriage.
    • 4 Not having given any thing.
      An unmarried girl.
      A gift which is null and void (having been given under particular circumstances which make it revocable).
      Comp. a. the receiver of such a gift; one who takes what has not been given away, such as a thief; adattādāyino hastāllipseta brāhmaṇo dhanaṃ . yājanādhyāpanenāpi yathā stenastathaiva saḥ .. --pūrvā Not affianced or betrothed before; adattapūrvetyāśaṃkyate Māl. 4. [0040-a]
