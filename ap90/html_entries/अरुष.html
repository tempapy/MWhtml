aruṣa a.
      1 Not angry.
      2 Shining, bright; reddish.
      3 Unhurt.
      4 Moving, going about (as a horse).
      --ṣaḥ
      1 The red horse of Agni; a flame.
      2 The sun; the day as presided over by the sun.
      3 The red storm-cloud.
      --ṣī
      1 The dawn.
      2 A flame.
      3 N. of the wife of Bhṛgu and mother of Aurva. [0162-c]
