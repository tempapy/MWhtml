• anuvraj 1 P.
      1 To follow, go after; tāṃ vrajaṃtīmanuvavrāja K. 132, 210; attend especially a departing guest (as far as the bank of water, lake, &c.) as a mark of respect; Y. 1. 113; taṃ mātaro devamanuvrajaṃtyaḥ Ku. 7. 38; yamicchetpunarāyāṃtaṃ nainaṃ dūramanuvrajet Rām.
      2 To visit in order, seek.
      3 To go to or near; betake oneself to; mṛgā mṛgaiḥ saṃgamanuvrajaṃti Pt. 1 deer herd or associate with deer.
      4 To obey, to do homage to. [0082-b]
