aṭṭa a. [aṭṭayati anādriyate anyat yatra; aṭṭ-ghañ]
    • 1 High, lofty; loud.
    • 2 Frequent, constant (in comp).
    • 3 Dried, dry.
      [ādhāre ghañ]
    • 1 An apartment on the roof or upper story, a garret.
    • 2 A turret, buttress, tower; gopura° Māl. 9. 1; nareṃdramārgāṭṭaïva R. 6. 67, 16. 11.
    • 3 A market-place, market (probably for haṭṭa).
    • 4 A fine linen cloth.
    • 5 A palace, palatial building.
    • 6 Killing, injuring.
    • 7 Excess, superiority.
      Food, boiled rice; aṭṭaśūlā janapadāḥ Mb. (aṭṭaṃ annaṃ śūlaṃ vikreyaṃ yeṣāṃ te Nīlakaṇṭha).
      Comp. very loud laughter.
      [aṭṭapradhānā sthalī śāka. ta.] a place or country full of palaces &c.
      , --hasitaṃ, --hāsyaṃ [karma°] a loud or boisterous laughter, a horse-laugh, cachinnation, usually of Śiva; tryaṃbakasya Me. 58; giriśa° Dk. 1. --hāsin [aṭṭaṃ hasati-has-ṇini] {1} N. of Śiva. {2} one who laughs very loudly.
      [aṭṭahāsena kāyate; kai-ka] N. of a plant (kuṃda) Jasminum Multiflorum or Hirsutum ( śubhrapuṣpatvācchubhrahāsatulyatā). [0027-c]
