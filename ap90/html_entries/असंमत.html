asaṃmata a.
      1 Disapproved, not allowed or permitted, not consented to; asaṃmataḥ kastava muktimārgaṃ Ku. 3. 5.
      2 Disliked, averse.
      3 Dissentient, differing from.
      --taḥ An enemy; dyatu doṣairasaṃmatān K. P. 7.
    Comp.
      --ādāyin a. taking without the consent of the possessor, such as a thief. [0211-c]
