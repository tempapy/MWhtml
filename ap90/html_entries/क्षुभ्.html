• kṣubh {c1c} A., {c4c} {c9c} P. (kṣobhate, kṣubhyati, kṣubhnāti, kṣubhita-kṣubdha) 1 To shake, tremble, to be agitated or disturbed; mahāhrada iva kṣubhyan Bk. 9. 118; R. 4. 21; Śi. 8. 24. --2 To be unsteady. --3 To stumble (fig. also). --Caus. (kṣobhayati) To agitate, stir up, excite, perturb. [0438-c]
  • kṣubh f. Ved. A blow, push. [0438-c]
