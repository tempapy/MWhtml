• vastraṃ [vas-ṣṭran] 1 A garment, cloth, clothes, raiment. --2 Dress, apparel. --Comp. --agāraḥ --raṃ, --gṛhaṃ a tent. --aṃcalaḥ, --aṃtaḥ the hem of a garment. --āgāraṃ a clothier's shop. --kuṭṭimaṃ {1} a tent. {2} an umbrella. --graṃthiḥ the knot of the lower garment (which fastens it near the navel); cf. nībi. --nirṇejakaḥ a washerman. --paridhānaṃ putting on garments, dressing. --putrikā a doll, puppet. --pūta a. filtered through a cloth; vastrapūtaṃ pibejjalaṃ Ms. 6. 46. --bhedakaḥ, --bhedin m. a tailor. --yoniḥ the material of cloth (as cotton). --raṃjanaṃ safflower. --vilāsaḥ foppery in dress.
