• vaiśvānara a. (rī f.) Relating or common to all mankind, fit for all men. --raḥ 1 An epithet of fire; tvattaḥ khāṃḍavaraṃgatāṃḍavanaṭo dūre'stu vaiśvānaraḥ Bv. 1. 57. --2 The fire of digestion (in the stomach); ahaṃ vaiśvānaro bhūtvā prāṇināṃ dehamāśritaḥ . prāṇāpānasamāyuktaḥ pacāmyannaṃ caturbidhaṃ Bg. 15. 14. --3 General consciousness (in Vedānta phil.). --4 The Supreme Being. --5 The Chitraka tree.
