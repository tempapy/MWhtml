    • tāraṇa a. [tārayatyanena tṝ-lyuṭ]

      2 Saving, delivering, liberating.
      3 Helping one through a difficulty &c.

      1 N. of Śiva; also of Viṣṇu.
      2 A boat, raft.
    • --ṇaṃ
      1 Crossing.
      2 Conquering.
      3 Carrying or conveying across.
      4 Rescuing, delivering, liberating. [0545-c]
