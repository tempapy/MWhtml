iha ind. [idaṃ-ha iśādeśaḥ P. V. 3. 11 Sk.]

    • 2 In this world (opp. paratra or amutra); oft. with jagati; K. 35.
    • 3 In this case; in this book or system.
    • 4 Now, at this time. [cf. Zend idha].
