utkalikā
      1 Anxiety in general, uneasiness; jātā notkalikā Amaru. 78; K. 138, 205, 210, 234; Dk. 17, 20.
      2 Longing for, regretting, missing anything or person.
      3 Wanton sport, dalliance (helā)
      4 A bud.
      5 A wave; vanāvalīrutkalikāsahasraṃ Śi. 3. 70; kṣubhitamutkalikātaralaṃ manaḥ ruffled by waves Māl. 3. 10 (where utkalikā also means anxiety); K. 161.
    Comp.
      --prāyaṃ a variety of prosecomposition abounding in compound words and hard letters; bhavedutkalikāprāyaṃ samāsāḍhyaṃ dṛḍhākṣaraṃ Cand. M. 6. [0290-c]
