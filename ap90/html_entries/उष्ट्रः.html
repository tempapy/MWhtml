• uṣṭraḥ [uṣ-ṭran-kicca Uṇ. 4. 161] 1 A camel; athoṣṭravāmīśatavāhitārthaṃ R. 5. 32; Ms. 3. 162, 4. 120, 11. 202. --2 A buffalo. --3 A bull with a hump. --4 A cart or carriage. --ṣṭrī 1 A she-camel. --2 An earthen vessel in the shape of a camel. [cf. Pers. ushtar; Zend ustra.] --Comp. --karṇikā (pl.) N. of a country or its inhabitants, in the south. --kāṃḍī a flower of a red colour. --krośin a. making a noise like a camel. --goyugaṃ a couple of camels --grīvaḥ, --śirodharaḥ piles. --yānaṃ camel-litter.
