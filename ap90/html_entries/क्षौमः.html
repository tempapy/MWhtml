kṣaumaḥ a. [kṣu-man svārthe aṇ] Linen.
      --maḥ, --maṃ
      1 Silken cloth, wove silk; kṣaumaṃ kenacidiṃdupāṃḍutaruṇā māṃgalyamāviṣkṛtaṃ Ś. 4. 4; kṣaumāṃtaritamekhale (aṃke) R. 10. 8.
      2 An airy room on the top of a house.
      3 The back of an edifice.
      4 A fortified place before a building.
      --maṃ
      1 Linen cloth.
      2 Linseed.
      --mī Flax. [0440-b]
