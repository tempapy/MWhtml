• piṃga a. [piṃj-varṇe aṃc nyaṃkva° kutvam] Reddish-brown, tawny, yellow-red. aṃtarniviṣṭāmalapiṃgatāraṃ (vilocanaṃ) Ku. 7. 33. --gaḥ 1 The tawny colour. --2 A buffalo. --3 A rat. --gā 1 Turmeric. --2 Saffron. --3 A kind of yellow pigment. --4 An epithet of Durgā. --5 A bow-string. --gaṃ A young animal. --Comp. --akṣa a. having reddish-brown eyes, redeyed. (--kṣaḥ) {1} an ape. {2} an epithet of Śiva. --īkṣaṇaḥ an epithet of Śiva. --īśaḥ an epithet of fire. --kapiśā a species of cock-roach. --cakṣus m. a crab. --jaṭaḥ an epithet of Śiva. --sāraḥ yellow orpiment. --sphaṭikaḥ ‘yellow crystal’, a kind of gem (gomeda).
