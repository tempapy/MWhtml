• trayī 1 The three Vedas taken collectively (ṛgyajuḥsāmāni); trayī mayāya triguṇātmane namaḥ K. 1; tau trayī varjamitarā vidyāḥ paripāṭhitau U. 2; Ms. 4. 125. --2 A triad, triplet; vyadyotiṣṭa sabhāvedyāmasau naraśikhitrayī Śi. 2. 3. --3 A matron or married woman whose husband and children are living. --4 Intellect, understanding. --Comp. --tanuḥ {1} an epithet of the sun; so trayīmayaḥ. {2} an epithet of Śiva. --dharmaḥ the duty enjoined by the three Vedas: Bg. 9. 21. --mukhaḥ a Brāhmaṇa.
