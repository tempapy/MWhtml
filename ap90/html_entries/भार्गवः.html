• bhārgavaḥ [bhṛgorapatyaṃ aṇ] 1 N. of Śukra, regent of the planet Venus and preceptor of the Asuras. --2 N. of Paraśurāma; see paraśurāma. --3 An epithet of Śiva. --4 An archer. --5 An elephant. --6 An epithet of Jamadagni. --7 Of Mārkaṇḍeya. --8 N. of an eastern country. --Comp. --priyaḥ a diamond.
