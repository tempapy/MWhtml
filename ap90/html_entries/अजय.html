ajaya a. [nāsti parairjayo yasya]
      1 Invincible, unsurpassed, unconquerable.
      --yaḥ [na. ta.] A defeat; lābhālābhau jayājayau Bg. 2. 38.
      2 N. of Agni (ajena chāgena yāti; yā-ka), or of Viṣṇu (according to some); of a lexicographer.
      --yā [nāsti jayo mādakatvena asyāṃ]
      1 Hemp or bhāṃga.
      2 N. of a friend of Durgā.
      3 Māyā or illusion. [0024-b]
