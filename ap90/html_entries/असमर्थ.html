• asamartha a. 1 Not able or competent. --2 Weak, feeble. --3 (In Rhet.) Powerless to convey the intended meaning, a defect of a word; yattadarthaṃ paṭhyate na ca tatrāsya śāktiḥ tadasamarthatvaṃ K. P. 7; e. g. in kuṃjaṃ haṃti kṛśodarī, han, though read in root-books in the sense of ‘going’, is here powerless to convey that meaning. --Comp. --samāsaḥ a compound in which the conjunction of words is not as it should be; e. g. in aśrāddhabhojin, asūryaṃpaśya, a goes not with śrāddha or sūrya but with bhojin or paśya.
