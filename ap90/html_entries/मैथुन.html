maithuna a. (nī f.) [mithupena nirvṛttaṃ aṇ]
      1 Paired, coupled.
      2 United by marriage.
      3 Relating to copula tion.
      --naṃ
      1 Copulation, sexual union; mṛtaṃ maithunamaprajaṃ Pt. 2. 94.
      2 Marriage.
      3 Union, connection.
      4 Consecrating the fire (agnyādhāna).
    Comp.
      --jvaraḥ the excitement of sexual passion.
      --dharmin a. copulating. --vairāgyaṃ abstinence from sexual intercourse. [0879-c]
