• bharaṇa a. (ṇī f.) [bhṛ-lyu-lyuṭ vā] Bearing, maintaining, supporting, nourishing.
    • --ṇaṃ
      1 The act of nourishing, maintaining or supporting; R. 1. 24; Ś. 7. 33.
      2 (a) The act of bearing or carrying. (b) Wearing, putting on.
      3 Bringing or procuring.
      4 Nutriment.
      5 Hire, wages.
    • --ṇaḥ The constellation Bharaṇī. [0809-a]
