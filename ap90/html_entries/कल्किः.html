• kalkiḥ The tenth and last incarnation of Viṣṇu in his capacity of the destroyer of the wicked and liberator of the world from its enemies; (Jayadeva, while referring to the several avatāras of Viṣṇu, thus refers to the last or Kalki avatāra: --mlecchanivahanidhane kalayasi karavālam dhūmaketumiva kimapi karālam . keśava dhṛtakalkiśarīraṃ jaya jagadīśa hare .. Gīt. 1. 10.) [0387-c]
