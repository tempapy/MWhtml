• anīśvara a. 1 Having no superior, uncontrolled. --2 Unable; śayitā savidhepyanīśvarā saphalīkartumaho manorathān Bv. 2. 182. --3 Not relating to God; dhyānenānīśvarān guṇān-dahet Ms. 6. 72. --4 Not acknowledging God, atheistical. --raṃ The godless one (with Sāṃkhyas), epithet of the world; jagadāhuranīśvaram. --Comp. --vādaḥ atheism, not acknowledging God as the Supreme Ruler. --vādin m. one who maintains the doctrine of no god or atheism, an atheist.
