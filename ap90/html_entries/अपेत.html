apeta p. p.
      1 Gone away, disappeared; apetayuddhabhiniveśasaumyaḥ Śi. 3. 1.
      2 Departing or deviating from, swerving from, contrary (with abl.); arthādanapetaṃ arthyaṃ P. IV. 4. 92 Sk. (dharmapathyarthanyāyādanapete); smṛtyapetādikāriṇaḥ Y. 2. 4.
      3 Free from, devoid of, deprived of (with abl. or in comp.); sukhādapetaḥ Sk.; udavahadanavadyāṃ tāmavadyādapetaḥ R. 7. 70 faultless; °bhīḥ Ms. 7. 197; bhartaryapetatamasi prabhutā tavaiva Ś. 7. 32 freed from (mental) darkness.
    Comp.
      --rākṣasī N. of a plant, Ocimum Sanctum (tulasī) (asyāḥ rākṣasatulyapāpādiharaṇāttannāmatvaṃ). [0119-b]
