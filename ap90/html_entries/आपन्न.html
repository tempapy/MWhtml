• āpanna p. p.
      1 Gained, obtained, acquired; jīvikāpannaḥ for āpannajīvikaḥ Sk.
      2 Gone or reduced to, fallen into; kaṣṭāṃ daśāmāpannopi Bh. 2. 29; so duḥkha°.
      3 Afflicted, distressed, being in difficulty; āpannābhayasatreṣu dīkṣitāḥ khalu pauravāḥ Ś. 2. 16; Me. 53; H. 4. 106, V. 2.
      4 Befallen; paricchedo hi pāṃḍityaṃ yadāpannā vipattayaḥ H. 1. 148.
    Comp.
    • --sattvā pregnant, quick or big with child; a pregnant woman; samamāpannasattvāstā rejurāpāṃḍuratviṣaḥ R. 10. 59. [0245-a]
