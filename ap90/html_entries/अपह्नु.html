apahnu 2 A.
    • 1 To conceal, hide, disguise; Ms. 8. 53; kāmadevavyapadeśena sakhīpurato'pahnutaḥ Ratn. 2 was passed off as Cupid upon her friend.

    • 3 To exculpate, excuse. [0115-a]
