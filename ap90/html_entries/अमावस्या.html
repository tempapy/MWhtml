• amāvasyā, --vāsyā, --vasī, --vāsī (also written amā masī-māsī) [ amāvas -ṇyat, amā saha vasataḥ caṃdrārkau asyāṃ sā P. III. 1. 122 Sk.] 1 The day of new moon, when the sun and moon dwell together or are in conjunction; the 15th day of the dark half of every lunar month; sūryācaṃdramasoḥ yaḥ paraḥ sannikarṣaḥ sā'māvāsyā Gobhila. --2 A sacrifice offered at that time. --3 The sacrificial oblation.
