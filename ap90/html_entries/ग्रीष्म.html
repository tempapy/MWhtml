grīṣma a. [grasate rasān; gras-manin Uṇ. 1. 147] Hot, warm.
      --ṣmaḥ
      1 The summer, the hot season, corresponding to the months of Jyeshṭha and Āṣāḍha; grīṣmasamayamadhikṛtya gīyatāṃ Ś. 1; R. 16. 54; Bv. 1. 35.
      2 Heat, warmth.
      --ṣmī The navamallikā plant.
    Comp.
      --kālīna a. pertaining to summer.
      --udbhavā, --jā, --bhavā the Navamallikā creeper, (double jasmine). [0474-b]
