dharṣaḥ [dhṛṣ-bhāve ghañ]
      1 Boldness, insolence, haughtiness, impudence.
      2 Pride, arrogance.
      3 Impatience.
      4 Restraint.
      5 Violation, seduction (of a woman).
      6 Injury, wrong, insult.
      7 A eunuch.
    Comp.
      --kāriṇī a violated woman. [0594-a]
