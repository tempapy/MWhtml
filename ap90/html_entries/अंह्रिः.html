aṃhriḥ [aṃh vaṃkryādi krin, aṃhate gacchatyanena]
      1 A foot.
      2 The root of a tree, cf. aṃghri.
      3 The number four.
    Comp.
      --paḥ ‘foot-drinker’, a tree; (mūlena pibati siktatoyaṃ).
      --skaṃdhaḥ [aṃhreḥ skaṃdha iva] the upper part of the sole of the foot. [0002-c]
