• ātmanīna a. [ātmane hitaḥ kha] 1 Belonging to oneself, one's own kasyaiṣa ātmanīnaḥ M. 4 on whose side is he? --2 Beneficial to oneself; ātmanīnamupatiṣṭhate Ki. 13. 69, 3. 16; Bh. 3. 77; good, fit, suitable, appropriate (as diet &c.). --3 Existent. sentient. --naḥ 1 A son. --2 A wife's brother. --3 A jester (in dramas).
