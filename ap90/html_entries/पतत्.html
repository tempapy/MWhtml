• patat (ntī f.) Flying, descending, alighting, coming down &c. --m. A bird; paramaḥ pumaniva patiṃ patatāṃ Ki. 6. 1; kvacitpathā saṃcarate surāṇāṃ kvaciddhanānāṃ patatāṃ kvacicca R. 13. 19; Śi. 9. 15. --Comp. --grahaḥ {1} the reserve of an army. {2} a spitting pot, spittoon; tamekamāṇikyamayaṃ mahonnataṃ patadgrahaṃ grāhitavānnalena saḥ N. 16. 27. --bhīruḥ a hawk, falcon.
