• stomaḥ [stu-man Uṇ. 1. 137] 1 Praise, eulogium, hymn. --2 A sacrifice, oblation; as in jyotiṣṭoma, agniṣṭoma. --3 A Soma libation. --4 A collection, multitude, number, group, assemblage; U. 1. 51. --5 A large quantity, mass; bhasmastomapavitralāṃchanamuro dhatte tvacaṃ rauravīṃ U. 4. 20, Mv. 1. 18. --maṃ 1 The head. --2 Riches, wealth. --3 Grain, corn. --4 An iron-pointed stick or shaft.
