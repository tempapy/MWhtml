• sirā 1 Any tubular vessel of the body (as a vein, artery, nerve &c.). --2 A bucket, baling vessel. --3 Ved. A stream. --Comp. --utpātaḥ {1} a disease of the veins &c. {2} redness and inflammation of the eyes. --jālaḥ enlargement of the vessels of the eye. --mokṣaḥ, --vyadhaḥ, --vyadhanaṃ venesection.
