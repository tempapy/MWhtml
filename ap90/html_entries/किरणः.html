kiraṇaḥ [kṝ-kyu Uṇ. 2. 81]
    • 1 A ray or beam of light, a ray (of the sun, moon or any shining substance); ravikiraṇasahipṇu Ś. 2. 4; eko hi doṣo guṇasaṃnipāte nimajjatīṃdoḥ kiraṇeṣvivāṃkaḥ Ku. 1. 3; Śānti. 4. 6; R. 5. 74; Śi. 4. 58; °maya radiant, brilliant.
    • 2 A small particle of dust.
    • 3 The sun.
      Comp. m. the sun. [0404-a]
