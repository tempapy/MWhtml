anujīv 1 P.
      1 To depend upon for subsistence, hang on, live by or upon (something); ye ca tvāmanujīvaṃti nāhaṃ teṣāṃ na te mama Rām.; sa tu tasyāḥ pāṇigrāhakamanujīviṣyati Dk. 122 hang or depend on, live (submissively) under, live as a subordinate to.
      2 To see without envy; yāṃ tāṃ śriyamasūyāmaḥ purā dṛṣṭvā yudhiṣṭhire adya tāmanujīvāmaḥ Mb.
      3 To live for any one.
      4 To follow or imitate in living; R. 19. 15 v. l.
      5 To survive. [0070-a]
