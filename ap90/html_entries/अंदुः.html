aṃduḥ --dūḥ f. [aṃdyate badhyate anena, aṃd-kū, kūpratyayāṃtaḥ nipātaḥ Uṇ. 1. 93] also aṃdukaḥ, aṃdūkaḥ
    • 1 A chain or fetter.
    • 2 A chain for the elephant's feet; gajamaṃdūriva niścalaṃ cakāra Śi. 20. 51; °ninādaḥ 11. 7.
    • 3 A sort of ornament worn round the ankles; cf. nūpura. [0096-b]
