• vārttika a. (kī f.) [vṛtti-ṭhak] 1 Relating to news. --2 Bringing news. --3 Explanatory, glossarial. --kaḥ 1 An emissary, a spy. --2 A husbandman (a man of the third tribe). --kaṃ [vṛttirūpeṇa kṛto graṃyaḥ] An explanatory or supplementary rule which explains the meaning of that which is said, of that which is left unsaid, and of that which is imperfectly said; or a rule which explains what is said or but imperfectly said and supplies omissions; uktānuktaduruktārthavyakti (or ciṃtā) kāritu vārttikam (the term is particularly applied to the explanatory rules of Kātyāyana on Pāṇini's Sūtras). --Comp. --karaḥ N. of Kātyāyana.
