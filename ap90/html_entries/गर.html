gara a. (rī f.) [gīryate gṝ-karmādau ac] Swallowing.
      --raḥ
      1 Any drink or fluid, beverage.
      2 Sickness, disease.
      3 Swallowing (garā also in this sense).
      4 A factitious poison.
      --raḥ, --raṃ
      1 Poison.
      2 An antidote.
      --raṃ
      1 Sprinkling, wetting.
      2 The fifth of the eleven Karaṇas.
    Comp.
      --adhikā {1} the insect called Lākṣā. {2} the red dye obtained from it.
      --ghna a. {1} destroying poison. {2} healthy.
      --śrīḥ a kind of fish.
      --da a. poisoning, giving poison. (--daṃ) poison.
      --vrataḥ a peacock. [0452-c]
