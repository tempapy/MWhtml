mastakaḥ --kaṃ [masmati parimātyanena mas-karaṇe ta svārthe ka Tv.]
      1 The head, skull; atilobhā (v. l. tṛṣṇā) bhibhūtasya cakraṃ bhramati mastake Pt. 5. 22.
      2 The head or top of anything, peak, summit; na ca pavartamastake Ms. 4. 47; vṛkṣa°, cullī° &c.
      3 The tuft of leaves growing at the top of palm trees.
    Comp.
      --ākhyaḥ the top of a tree.
      --jvaraḥ, --śūlaṃ an acute head-ache.
      --piṃḍakaḥ --kaṃ a round protuberance on the temples of an elephant in rut.
      --mūlakaṃ the neck.
      --luṃgaḥ the membrance surrounding the brain.
      --snehaḥ the brain. [0849-c]
