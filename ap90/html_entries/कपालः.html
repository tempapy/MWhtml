kapālaḥ, --laṃ [kaṃ śiro jalaṃ vā pālayati]
    • 1 The skull, skull-bone; cūḍāpīḍakapālasaṃkulagalanmaṃdākinīvārayaḥ Māl. 1. 2; rudro yena kapālapāṇipuṭake bhikṣāṭanaṃ kāritaḥ Bh. 2. 95.
    • 2 A piece of a broken jar, potsherd; kapālena bhikṣārthī Ms. 8. 93.
    • 3 A multitude, collection.
    • 4 A beggar's bowl; Ms. 6. 44.
    • 5 A cup, jar in general; paṃcakapāla.
    • 6 A cover or lid.
    • 7 A treaty of peace on equal terms.
    • 1 The shell of an egg.
    • 2 The cotyla of the leg of a man, any flat bone.
    • 3 A kind of leprosy.
      A beggar's bowl. [cf. L. caput; Gr. kephale].
      Comp. a sort of pin or spindle for winding cotton &c.
      --bhṛt, --mālin, --śiras m. epithets of Śiva.
