• uttṝ {c1c} P. 1 To pass out of (water), disembark, come out of; Śi. 8. 63, 64; to get or jump out of, rise from; 12. 31; palvalottīrṇa R. 2. 17; abhiṣekottīrṇāya, snānottīrṇaḥ Ś. 4. --2 To cross, pass or get over (a river &c.); udatāriṣuraṃbhodhiṃ Bk. 15. 33, 10; śokasāgaramuttīrya Ve. 3; tenottīrya pathā R. 12. 71, 16. 33; Me. 47. --3 To vanquish, overcome, get out of, escape from (a difficulty); vyasanamaha rṇavāduttīrṇaṃ Mk. 10. 49. --4 To descend, alight; tadgṛhe uttīrṇau Vet. --5 To give up, leave, quit. --6 To raise, strengthen, increase. --Caus. 1 To cause to come out, deliver, lift up, rescue; Śi. 11. 44; Dk. 30, 77. --2 To take down, to take off (as ornaments); let down, place down. --3 To cause to cross over, convey, transport across. --4 To land, disembark. --5 To vomit up, emit.
