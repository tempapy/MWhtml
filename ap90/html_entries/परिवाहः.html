pari(rī)vāhaḥ
      1 Overflowing (of a tank &c.), inundation, overflow, natural or artificial; prathamaṃ ( kautūhalaṃ) saparivāhamāsīt Ś. 2.
      2 A water-course, drain or channel to carry off excess of water; pūrotpīḍe taḍāgasya parīvāhaḥ pratikriyā U. 3. 29; Pt. 2. 150; Śi. 16. 51; R. 8. 74. [0680-a]
