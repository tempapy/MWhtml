• kaṃcukin a. Furnished with armour or mail. --m. 1 An attendant on the women's apartments, a chamberlain; (an important character in dramas); aṃtaḥpuracaro vṛddho vipro guṇagaṇānvitaḥ . sarvakāryārthakuśalaḥ kaṃcakītyabhidhīyate .. (he must be a Brāhmaṇa, very old, &c. cf. V. 3. 1 and Ś. 5. 3). --2 A libidinous man, debauchee. --3 A serpent. --4 A door keeper. --5 Barley. [0368-c]
