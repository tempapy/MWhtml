• turagaḥ [tureṇa gacchati gam-ḍa] 1 A horse; turagakhurahataratathā hi reṇuḥ Ś. 1. 31; R. 1. 42, 3. 51. --2 The mind, thought. --gī A mare. --Comp. --ārohaḥ a horseman. --upacārakaḥ a groom. --priyaḥ --yaṃ barley. --brahmacaryaṃ forced or compulsory celibacy leading a life of celibacy simply in consequence of the absence of female society. --medhaḥ a horse-sacrifice. --rakṣaḥ a groom, an equery.
