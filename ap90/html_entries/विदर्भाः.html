vidarbhāḥ (m. pl.) [vigatāḥ darbhāḥ kuśā yataḥ Tv.]
      1 N. of a district, the modern Berar; asti vidarbho nāma janapadaḥ Dk.; asti vidarbheṣu padmapuraṃ nāma nagaraṃ Māl. 1; R. 5. 40, 60; N. 1. 50.
      2 The natives of Vidarbha.
      --rbhaḥ
      1 A king of the Vidarbhas.
      2 Any dry or desert soil.
    Comp.
      --jā, --tanayā, --rājatanayā, --subhrūḥ epithets of Damayantī, daughter of the king of the Vidarbhas.
      --jā N. of the wife of Agastya. [0974-c]
