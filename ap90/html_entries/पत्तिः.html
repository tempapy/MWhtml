• pattiḥ [pad-tin] 1 A footman, a foot-soldier; R. 7. 37. --2 A pedestrian. --3 A hero. --f. 1 The smallest division of an army, consisting of one chariot, one elephant; three horsemen and five foot-soldiers. --2 Going, walking. --Comp. --kāyaḥ infantry. --gaṇakaḥ an officer whose business it is to muster the infantry --saṃhatiḥ f. a body of infantry, infantry.
