• saṃcārin a. (ṇī f.) 1 Moving, moveable; saṃcāriṇī nagaradevateva Māl. 1; Ku. 3. 54; saṃcāriṇī dīpāśikheva rātrau R. 6. 67. --2 Roaming, wandering. --3 Changing, unsteady, fickle. --4 Difficult to be passed, inaccessible, --5 Evanescent, as a Bhāva; see below. --6 Influencing. --7 Hereditary, successively communicated (as a disease). --8 Contagious. --9 Impelling. --m. 1 Wind, air. --2 Incense. --3 A transient or evanescent feeling which serves to strengthen the prevailing sentiment; see vyabhicārin.
