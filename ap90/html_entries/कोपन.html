• kopana a. [kup-tācchīlye yuc]
      1 Passionate, irascible, angry.
      2 Causing anger.
      3 Irritating, causing morbid disorder of the humours of the body.
    • --naṃ Becoming angry.
    • --nā A passionate or angry woman; kayāsi kāmin suratāparādhāt pādānataḥ kopanayā' vadhūtaḥ Ku. 3. 8; Amaru. 65. [0409-c]
