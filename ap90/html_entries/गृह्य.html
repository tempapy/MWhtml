• gṛhya a. [gṛha kyap]
      1 To be attracted or pleased, as in guṇagṛhya q. v.
      2 Domestic.
      3 Not master of oneself, dependent.
      4 Tame, domesticated
      5 Situated out-side of; grāmagṛhyā senā ‘an army out-side a village’.
    • --hyaḥ
      1 The inmate of a house.
      2 A tame animal or bird.
      3 The domestic fire.
    • --hyaṃ The anus.
