• ṛkṣaḥ [ṛṣ-sa kicca Uṇ. 3. 66]
      1 A bear; Ms. 12. 67.
      2 N. of a mountain.
      3 N. of a plant (bhallaka).
    • --kṣaḥ, --kṣaṃ
      1 A star, constellation, lunar mansion; Ms. 2. 101; 3. 9; 6. 10.
      2 A sign of the zodiac.
      3 A star under which a man happens to be born.
    • --kṣāḥ (m. pl.) The seven stars called Pleiades; afterwards the seven Ṛṣis; R. 12. 25. --kṣā The north.
    • --kṣī A female bear. [cf. Gr. arkos; L. ursus].
    Comp.
    • --gaṃdhā the plant Argyreia Argentea (jāṃgalī, mahāśvetā, kṣīravidārī).
    • --gaṃdhikā the plant Batatas Paniculata.
    • --cakraṃ the circle of stars.
    • --nāthaḥ, --īśaḥ ‘lord of stars,’ the moon.
    • --nemiḥ N. of Viṣṇu.
    • --rāj, --jaḥ {1} the moon. {2} Jāmbuvat, the king of bears.
    • --harīśvaraḥ the lord of bears and apes; R. 13. 72. [0347-1]
