ṛkṣaḥ [ṛṣ-sa kicca Uṇ. 3. 66]
    • 1 A bear; Ms. 12. 67.
    • 2 N. of a mountain.
    • 3 N. of a plant (bhallaka).
    • 1 A star, constellation, lunar mansion; Ms. 2. 101; 3. 9; 6. 10.
    • 2 A sign of the zodiac.
    • 3 A star under which a man happens to be born.
      (m. pl.) The seven stars called Pleiades; afterwards the seven Ṛṣis; R. 12. 25. --kṣā The north.
      A female bear. [cf. Gr. arkos; L. ursus].
      Comp. the plant Argyreia Argentea (jāṃgalī, mahāśvetā, kṣīravidārī).
      the plant Batatas Paniculata.
      the circle of stars.
      ‘lord of stars,’ the moon.
      N. of Viṣṇu.
      , --jaḥ {1} the moon. {2} Jāmbuvat, the king of bears.
      the lord of bears and apes; R. 13. 72. [0347-1]
