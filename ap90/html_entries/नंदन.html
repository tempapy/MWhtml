naṃdana a. [naṃd-lyu] Delighting, pleasing, gladdening; Ś. 7; Māl. 9. 21. --naḥ
      1 A son; Y. 1. 274; R. 3. 41.
      2 A frog.
      3 An epithet of Viṣṇu.
      4 N. of Śiva.
      5 N. of the twentysixth year (saṃvatsara).
      --nā A daughter.
      --naṃ
      1 N. of the garden of Indra, the Elysium; abhijñāśchedapātānāṃ kriyaṃte naṃdanadrumāḥ Ku. 2. 41; R. 8. 95.
      2 Rejoicing, being glad.
      3 Joy.
    Comp.
      --jaṃ yellow sandal-wood (haricaṃdana). [0607-a]
