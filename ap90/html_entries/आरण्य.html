āraṇya a. (ṇyā, --ṇyī f.) [araṇye bhavaḥ ṇ] Wild, forest-born, relating to a forest (opp. grāmya); °paśuḥ Ms. 10. 48; (āraṇyapaśu is of {7} kinds: --sarīsṛpo ruruścaiva mahiṣo vānarastathā . pṛṣatarkṣau mṛgaścaiva paśurvai saptadhā mataḥ .) --ṇyaḥ, --ṇyaṃ
    • 1 A forest.
    • 2 A kind of corn growing without sowing seed.
    • 3 N. of certain signs of the zodiac (see °rāśi below).
    • 4 Cow-dung (--ṇyaḥ only).
    • 5 N. of a Parvan in the Mahābhārata.
    • 6 N. of a Kāṇḍa in the Rāmāyaṇa.
      Comp. a wild cock.
      one of the four Gānas or psalmbooks of the Sāma-veda --parvan n. N. of the third book of the Mahābhārata, usually called Vana-Parvan.
      a wild beast.
      a kind of bean.
      (in the zodiac) {1} the signs Leo, Aries, and Taurus. {2} the former half of Capricorn. [0253-b]
