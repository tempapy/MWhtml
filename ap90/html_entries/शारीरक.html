• śārīraka a. (kī f.) 1 Relating to the body, corporeal. --2 Incorporate, embodied (as the soul). --kaṃ 1 The embodied spirit. --2 The inquiry into the nature of that spirit (a term applied to the Bhāṣya of Śankarāchārya on the Brahma-sūtras). --Comp. --sūtraṃ the aphorisms of the Vedānta philosophy.
