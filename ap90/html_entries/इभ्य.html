ibhya a. [ibhaṃ gajamarhati yat]
      1 Wealthy, opulent, rich; Dk. 41.
      2 Belonging to one's servants (Ved.).
      --bhyaḥ
      1 A king.
      2 An elephant-driver.
      3 An enemy or foe (Sāy.).
      --bhyā
      1 A female elephant.
      2 N. of the Olibanum tree, Boswellia Serrata.
    Comp.
      --tilvi (va) la a. wealthy, rich. [0279-c]
