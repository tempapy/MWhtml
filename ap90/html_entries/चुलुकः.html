• culukaḥ [cul-ukañ]
      1 Deep mud.
      2 A mouthful of water or the hand hollowed to hold water or anything; mamau sa bhadraṃ culuke samudraḥ N. 8. 45; jñātvā vidhātuśculukāt prasūtiṃ Vikr. 1. 37.
      3 A small vessel.
    • --ka Water in which pulse has been steeped. [0497-a]
