sasyaṃ [sas-yat]
      1 Corn, grain; (etāni) sasyaiḥ pūrṇe jaṭharapiṭhare prāṇināṃ saṃbhavaṃti Pt. 5. 97; see śasya also.
      2 Fruit or produce of any plant.
      3 A weapon.
      4 A good quality, merit.
    Comp.
      --ad, --bhakṣaka a. granivorous.
      --iṣṭiḥ f. a sacrifice made on the ripening of new grain.
      --prada a. fertile.
      --mārin a. destructive of grain. (--m.) a kind of rat or mouse.
      --saṃvaraḥ the Sāla tree. [1111-a]
