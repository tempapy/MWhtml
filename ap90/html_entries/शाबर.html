• śāba(va)ra a. (rī f.) [śaba(va) ra-aṇ] 1 Savage, barbarous. --2 Low, vile, base. --raḥ 1 An offence, a fault. --2 Sin, wickedness. --3 The tree called Lodhra. --rī A low form of the Prākṛta dialect (spoken by mountaineers &c.) --Comp. --bhedākhyaṃ (also bhedākṣaṃ) copper. [1040-b]
