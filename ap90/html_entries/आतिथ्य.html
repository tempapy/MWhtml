• ātithya a. [atitheridaṃ ṣyañ] Hospitable, proper for a guest &c. ( = ātitheya); havirātithyaṃ, ātithyā iṣṭiḥ &c. --thyaḥ A guest. --thyaṃ 1 Hospitable reception, hospitality; tamātithyakriyāśāṃtarathakṣobhapariśramaṃ R. 1. 58. --2 A particular rite, the reception of Soma when it is brought to the sacrificial place (also called ātithyeṣṭiḥ). --Comp. --rūpa a. being in the place of the ātithya sacrifice. --satkāraḥ, --satkriyā the rites of hospitality.
