• prakup {c4c} P. 1 To be angry, to be enraged or provoked at: nimittamuviśya hi yaḥ prakupyati dhruvaṃ sa tasyāpagame prasīdati Pt. 1. 283. --2 To be excited, gather strength, increase. --Caus. To provoke, irritate, exasperate; sādhoḥ prakīpitasyāpi mano nāyāti vikriyāma Subhāṣ. [0724-b]
