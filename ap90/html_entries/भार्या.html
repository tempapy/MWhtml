• bhāryā [bhartuṃ yogyā]
      1 A lawful wife; sā bhāryā yā gṛhe dakṣā sā bhāryā yā prajāvatī . sā bhāryā yā patiprāṇā sā bhāryā yā pativratā .. H. 1. 196.
      2 The female of an animal.
    Comp.
    • --āṭa a. living by the prostitution of his wife.
    • --ūḍha a. married (as a man); bhāryoḍhaṃ tamavajñāya Bk. 4. 15. --jitaḥ, --āṭikaḥ {1} a hen-pecked husband. {2} a kind of deer. [0813-c]
