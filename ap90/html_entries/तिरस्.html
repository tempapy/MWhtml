• tiras ind. [tṝ-asun svarādi]
      1 Crookedly, obliquely, awry; sa tiryaṅ yastiroṃ'cati Ak.
      2 Without; apart from.
      3 Secretly, covertly, invisibly.
      4 Across, beyond, over.
      5 Indirectly, badly. [In classical literature tiras is rarely used by itself, but chiefly occurs in composition with (a) kṛ, (b) dhā, and (c) bhū; see below.]
    Comp.
    • --kuḍya, --prākāra a. looking through a wall.
    • --gata a. vanished, disappeared.
    • --varṣa a. protected from rain. [0538-b]
