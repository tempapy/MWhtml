• vinud 6 P.
      1 To strike, pierce.
      2 To play on a musical instrument (vīṇāṃ, ātodyaṃ &c.).
      3 To remove, drive away, dispel. --Caus.
      1 To remove, drive away, dispel, cast off; tāpaṃ vinodaya dṛṣṭibhiḥ Gīt. 10; Śi. 4. 66; Ś. 3. 21; Māl. 9. 41.
      2 To pass, spend (as time).
      3 To divert, amuse, entertain; kva nu khalvātmānaṃ vinodayāmi Ś. 3; latāsu dṛṣṭiṃ vinodayāmi Ś. 6; R. 14. 77.
      4 To amuse oneself with; lakṣmīrvimodayāti yena digaṃtalaṃbī sopi tvadānanaruciṃ vijahāti caṃdraḥ R. 5. 67. [0980-a]
