• radaḥ [rad ac]
      1 Splitting, scratching.
      2 A tooth; tusk (of an elephant); yātāścenna parāṃcaṃti dviradānāṃ radā iva Bv. 1. 65.
      3 A symbolical expression for the number ‘thirty-two’.
    Comp.
