amedhya a.
      1 Not able or allowed to sacrifice.
      2 Unfit for a sacrifice; nāmedhyaṃ prakṣipedagnau Ms. 4. 53, 56; 5. 5, 132.
      3 Unholy, filthy, foul, dirty, impure; Bg. 17. 10; Bh. 3. 106. --dhyaṃ
      1 Excrement, ordure; samutsṛjedrājamārge yastvamedhyamanāpadi Ms. 9. 282; 5. 126, 128; 12. 71.
      2 An unlucky or inauspicious omen; amedhyaṃ dṛṣṭvā sūryamupatiṣṭheta Kāty.
    Comp.
      --kuṇapāśin a. feeding on carrion.
      --yukta, --lipta a. smeared with ordure, foul, defiled, dirty.
      --lepaḥ smearing with ordure. [0153-a]
