• ghṛṣ {c1c} P. (gharṣati, ghṛṣṭa) 1 To rub, strike against; adyāpi tatkanakakuṃḍalaghṛṣṭamārayaṃ Ch. P. 11; Pt. 1. 144. --2 To brush, furbish, polish. --3 To crush, grind, pound; draupadyā nanu matsyarājabhavane ghṛṣṭaṃ na kiṃ caṃdanaṃ Pt. 3. 175. --4 To compete, rival (as in saṃgharṣa q. v.).
