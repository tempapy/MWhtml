• nata p. p. [nam-kta]
      1 Bent, bowed, stooping, inclined.
      2 Sunk, depressed.
      3 Crooked, curved.
    • --taṃ
      1 The distance of any planet from the meridian.
      2 The zenith distance at a meridian transit.
    Comp.
    • --aṃśaḥ zenith distance.
    • --aṃga a. {1} bending the body. {2} stooping, bowed. (--gī) {1} a woman with stooping limbs. {2} a woman in general.
    • --unnata a. high and low; Ś. 4. 15. --nāḍī, --nāḍikā {1} the distance in time of any planet from the meridian. {2} any hour of birth after noon or before midnight.
    • --nāsika a. flat-nosed.
    • --parvan a. flatjointed.
    • --bhrūḥ a woman with curved eye-brows. [0606-a]
