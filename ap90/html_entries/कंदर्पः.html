• kaṃdarpaḥ
      1 N. of Cupid, the god of love; prajanaścāsmi kaṃdarpaḥ Bg. 10. 28; kaṃdarpa iva rūpeṇa Mb.
      2 Love. (kaṃdarpa is thus derived: --kaṃ darpayāmīti madājjātamātro jagāda ca . tena kaṃdarpanāmānaṃ taṃ cakāra caturmukhaḥ ..)
    Comp.
    • --kūpaḥ pudenda muliebre.
    • --jvaraḥ fever of love, passion, vehement desire. --dahanaḥ, --mathanaḥ N. of Śiva.
    • --muṣalaḥ, --musalaḥ the male organ of generation. --śṛṃkhalaḥ {1} membrum virile. {2} a particular mode of sexual enjoyment or coitus (ratibaṃdha). [0376-a]
