• hṛta p. p. [hṛ-kta]
      1 Taken or carried away.
      2 Seized.
      3 Captivated.
      4 Accepted.
      5 Divided; see hṛ.
    • --taṃ A portion, share.
    Comp.
    • --adhikāra a. {1} dismissed from authority, turned out. {2} deprived of one's due rights. --uttarīya a. having the upper garments stripped off.
    • --dravya, --dhana a. spoiled of wealth.
    • --sarvasva a. stripped of all one's property, utterly ruined. [1174-a]
