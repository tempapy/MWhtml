• adhīra a.
      1 Not bold, timid.
      2 Confused, lacking self-command, excited, excitable.
      3 Fitful, capricious.
      4 Unsteady, not fixed, tremulous, rolling; °viprekṣitamāyatākṣyā Ku. 1. 46; °locanaḥ Śi. 1. 53; 6. 25.
      5 Querulous, foolish, weak-minded.
    • --rā
      1 Lightning.
      2 A capricious or quarrelsome mistress; see under nāyikā. [0051-c]
