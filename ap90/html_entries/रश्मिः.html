• raśmiḥ [aś-mi dhāto ruṭ, raś-mivā; cf. Uṇ. 4. 46] 1 A string, cord, rope. --2 A bridle, rein; mukteṣu raśmiṣu nirāyatapūrvakāyāḥ Ś. 1. 8; raśmisaṃyamanāt Ś. 1. --3 A goad, whip. --4 A beam, ray of light; Ś. 7. 6; N. 22. 56; so himaraśmi &c. --5 An eye-lash. --6 A measuring cord. --7 A finger (Ved.). --Comp. --kalāpaḥ a pearl-necklace of 54 threads. --mucaḥ the sun. [0905-c]
