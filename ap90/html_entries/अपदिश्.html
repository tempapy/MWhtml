• apadiś 6 P.
      1 To point out, indicate, show; tadāgatitvenāhamapadeśyaḥ Dk. 60; assign, allot.
      2 To declare, put forward, adduce, communicate; announce, say, tell; inform against, accuse; apadiśyāpadeśyaṃ ca Ms. 8. 54.
      3 To feign, pretend, hold out or plead as a pretext or excuse; mitrakṛtyamapadiśya R. 19. 31, 32, 54; śiraḥśūlasparśanamapadiśan Dk. 56 pleading head-ache as an excuse.
      4 To refer to, have reference to; rahasi bhartrā madgotrāpadiṣṭā Dk. 102 referred to, called by the name of. [0107-a]
