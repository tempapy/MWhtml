• śilīṃdhraḥ [śilīṃ dharati dhṛ ka pṛṣo° mum]
      1 A kind of fish.
      2 A kind of tree.
    • --dhraṃ
      1 A mushroom, fungus, as in ucchilīṃdhra q. v.
      2 The flower of the plantain tree; adhipuraṃdhri śilīṃdhrasugaṃdhibhiḥ Śi. 6. 32, or alināramatālinī śilīṃdhra 72.
      3 Hail. [1046-a]
