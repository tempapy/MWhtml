• upasaṃkhyānaṃ 1 Addition. --2 Supplementary addition, further or additional enumeration (a term technically applied to the Vārtikas of Kātyāyana which are intended to supply omissions in Pāṇini's Sūtras and generally to supplement them); e. g. jugupsāvirāmapramādārthānāmupasaṃkhyānaṃ; cf. iṣṭi. --3 (In gram.) A substitute in form or sense. [0332-a]
