• pūraṇa a. (ṇī f.) [pūr-kartari lyu]
      1 Filling up, completing.
      2 Ordinal (as applied to numbers) (dvitīya, tṛtīya &c.); na pūraṇī taṃ samupaiti saṃkhyā Ki. 3. 51.
      3 Satisfying.
      4 Drawing (as a bow).
    • --ṇaḥ
      1 A bridge, dam, causeway.
      2 The ocean.
      3 The Śālmali tree.
      4 A kind of medicinal oil (viṣṇutailaṃ).
    • --ṇī
      1 An epithet of Durgā.
      2 The silk-cotton tree.
    • --ṇaṃ
      1 Filling.
      2 Filling up, completing; R. 9. 73.
      3 Puffing or swelling.
      4 Fulfilling, accomplishing.
      5 A sort of cake.
      6 A funeral cake.
      7 Rain, raining.
      8 Warp.
      9 Multiplication (in math.).
      10 Injection of fluids (in Medic.).
      11 Drawing, bending (as a bow).
      12 Decorating, adorning.
    Comp.
    • --pratyayaḥ an affix forming an ordinal number. [0715-b]
