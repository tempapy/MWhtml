alaghu (ghu-ghvī f.)
    • 1 Not light, heavy, big, weighty (as breasts, hips &c.); āyāsādalaghutarastanaiḥ Śi. 8. 1; 7. 5.
    • 2 Not short, long (in prosody).
    • 3 Serious, solemn.
    • 4 Intense, violent, very great.
      Comp. a rock.
      m. intense heat. --pratijña a. solemnly pledged or promised. [0172-a]
