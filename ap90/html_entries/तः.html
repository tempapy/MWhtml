taḥ
      1 A tail.
      2 The tail of a jackal.
      3 The breast.
      4 The womb.
      5 The hip or flank.
      6 A warrior.
      7 A thief.
      8 A wicked man.
      9 An outcast, a barbarian.
      10 A Bud dha.
      11 A jewel.
      12 Nectar.
      13 (In prosody) One of the eight syllabic feet.
      --tā, --taṃ
      1 Passing, crossing.
      2 Virtue, religious merit.
      --tā N. of Lakṣmī. [0525-a]
