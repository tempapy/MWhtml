āgrayaṇaḥ [agre ayanaṃ bhojanaṃ śasyāderyena karmaṇā pṛ° hrasvadīrghaḥ vyatyayaḥ]
    • 1 The first Soma libation at the Agnishṭoma sacrifice.
    • 2 A form of Agni.
    • 3 The time of the sacrifice.
      An oblation consisting of firstfruits; see āgrayaṇaṃ.
      An oblation consisting of first-fruits at the end of the rainy season (āśvinīpūrṇimā); āgrayaṇaṃ trividhaṃ śyāmāka°, vrīhi°, yava°, yathāpūrvaṃ varṣāsu śaradi vasaṃte ca tatkaraṇopadeśāt Ārya. S.; āgrayaṇeneṣṭvā navānnaṃ prāśnīyāt Ait. Br.; āgrayaṇaṃ vrīhiśyāmākayavānāṃ Āśval. [0227-c]
