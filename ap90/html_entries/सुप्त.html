• supta p. p. [svap-kartari kta]
      1 Slept, sleeping, asleep; na hi suptasya siṃhasya praviśaṃti mukhe mṛgāḥ H. Pr. 36.
      2 Paralyzed, benumbed, insensible; see svap.
    • --ptaṃ Sleep, sound sleep.
    Comp.
    • --janaḥ midnight.
    • --jñānaṃ a dream.
    • --tvac a. paralytic. [1131-c]
