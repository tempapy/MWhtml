• dākṣāyaṇī [dakṣasyāpatyaṃ strī iñi phakṅīp] 1 Any one of the 27 lunar mansions (they being mythologically regarded as so many daughters of Dakṣa). --2 N. of Diti, wife of Kaśyapa and mother of the demons. --3 N. of Pārvatī. --4 The lunar constellation called Revatī. --5 N. of Kadru or Vinatā. --6 N. of Aditi, mother of the gods. --7 The Dantī plant. --Comp. --patiḥ {1} an epithet of Śiva. {2} the moon. --putraḥ a god.
