• maṃgalya a. [maṃgalāya hitaṃ yat] 1 Auspicious, fortunate, happy, lucky, prosperous; Ms. 2. 31. --2 Pleasing, agreeable, beautiful. --3 Holy, pure, pious; trilokīmaṃ galyāṃ U. 4. 10. --lya 1 The sacred fig-tree. --2 The cocoanut tree. --3 A sort of pulse. --4 The Bilva tree. --lyā 1 A species of fragrant sandal. --2 N. of Durgā. --3 A kind of aloe-wood. --4 A particular perfume. --5 A particular yellow pigment. --lyaṃ 1 Auspicious water for the coronation of a king (brought from various holy places). --2 Gold. --3 Sandal-wood. --4 Red lead. --5 Sour curds.
