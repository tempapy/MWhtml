• maṃdāraḥ [maṃd-ārak]
      1 The coral tree, one of the five trees in Indra's paradise; hastaprāpyastabakanamito bālamaṃdāravṛkṣaḥ Me. 75, 67; V. 4. 35.
      2 The plant called Arka.
      3 The Dhattūra plant.
      4 Heaven.
      5 An elephant.
    • --raṃ A flower of the coral tree; Ku. 5. 80; R. 6. 23.
    Comp.
    • --mālā a garland of Mandāra flowers; maṃdāramālā hariṇā pinaddhā Ś. 7. 2. --ṣaṣṭhī the sixth day in the bright half of Māgha. [0844-c]
