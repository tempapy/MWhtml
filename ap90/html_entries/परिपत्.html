• paripat {c1c} P. 1 To fly round or about, wheel or whirl round, hover about; biṃdūtkṣepān pipāsuḥ paripatati śikhī bhrāṃtimadvāriyaṃtraṃ M. 2. 13; Amaru. 48. --2 To spring down upon, attack, fall upon (as in battle). --3 To run in all directions; (hayāḥ) paripeturdiśo daśa Mb. --4 To go to or fall into; Śi. 11. 41. --Caus. To shoot off or down. [0676-c]
