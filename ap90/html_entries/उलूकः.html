ulūkaḥ
      1 An owl; nolūkopyavalokate yadi divā sūryasya kiṃ dūṣaṇaṃ Bh. 2. 93 tyajati mudamulūkaḥ prītimāṃścakravākaḥ Śi. 11. 64.
      2 N. of Indra.
      3 (pl.) N. of a country and its king who was an ally of the Kurus.
      --kaṃ N. of the reed Saccharum Cylindricum; see ulapa. [0341-c]
