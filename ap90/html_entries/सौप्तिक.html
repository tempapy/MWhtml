• sauptika a. (kī f.) 1 Connected with or relating to sleep. --2 Somniferous. --kaṃ A night-attack, an attack on sleeping men. --Comp. --parvan n. N. of the tenth parvan or book of the Mahābhārata which relates how Aśvatthāman, Kṛtavarman and Kṛpa-the only surviving Kuru warriors-attacked by night the Pāṇḍava camp and slaughtered thousands of warriors while asleep. --vadhaḥ the great nocturnal slaughter of Pāṇḍava camp (above referred to); mārgo hyeṣa nareṃdrasauptikavadhe pūrvaṃ kṛto drauṇinā Mk. 3. 11.
