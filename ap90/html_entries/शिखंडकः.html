• śikhaṃḍakaḥ [śikhaṃḍa iva-kan] 1 A lock of hair left on the crown of the head at tonsure. --2 Locks or tufts of hair left on the sides of the head; (these are three or five in the case of the Kṣatriyas); kuvalayadalaśyāmāsnigdhaḥ śikhaṃḍakamaṃḍanaḥ U. 4. 19. --3 A crest, tuft. plume. --4 A peacock's tail. [1043-c]
