āmnāyaḥ [ā-mnā-ghañ]
      1 (a) Sacred tradition, sacred texts handed down by tradition or repetition. (b) Hence, the Veda, Vedas taken collectively (including Brāhmaṇas, Upaniṣads and Āraṇyakas also); adhītī caturṣvāmnāyeṣu Dk. 120; āmnāyavacanaṃ satyamityayaṃ lokasaṃgrahaḥ . āmnāyebhyaḥ punarvedāḥ prasṛtāḥ sarvatomukhāḥ .. Mb.
      2 A sacred text or precept in general; U. 4.
      3 Traditional usage, family or national customs.
      4 Received doctrine.
      5 Advice or instruction (in past and present usage).
      6 A Tantra.
      7 A series of families.
    Comp.
      --sārin a. {1} pious, observing the traditional usages. {2} containing the essence of the Veda. [0250-b]
