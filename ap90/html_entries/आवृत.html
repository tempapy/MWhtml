āvṛta p. p.
      1 Covered, screened, concealed.
      2 Invested, blocked.
      3 Enclosed, surrounded (by a ditch, wall &c.).
      4 Spread, overspread, overcast; āvṛte nabhastale H. 3.
      5 Filled or abounding with.
      --taḥ A man of mixed origin, the son of a Brāhmaṇa by a woman of the Ugra caste; Ms. 10. 15. [0262-a]
