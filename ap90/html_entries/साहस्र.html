• sāhasra a. (srī f.) [sahasra-aṇ] 1 Relating to a thousand. --2 Consisting of a thousand. --3 Bought with a thousand. --4 Paid per thousand (as interest &c.). --5 A thousand-fold. --sraḥ An army or detachment consisting of a thousand men. --sraṃ An aggregate of a thousand. [1122-a]
