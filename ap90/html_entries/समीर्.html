• samīr caus.
      1 To shake, move, agitate, stir up, set in motion.
      2 To excite, rouse.
      3 (a) To send forth, emit; (b) To speak, utter; Ku. 2. 16.
      4 To cast, throw, toss.
      5 To raise or lift up.
      6 To accomplish, bring about.
      7 To bestow on, endow with. [1099-b]
