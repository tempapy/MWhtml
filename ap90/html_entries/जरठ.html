• jaraṭha a. [jṝ bā °aṭha] 1 Hard, solid. --2 Old, aged; ayamatijaraṭhāḥ prakāmagurvīḥ pariṇatadikkarikāstaṭīrbibharti Śi. 4. 29 (where jaraṭha means ‘hard’ also). --3 Decayed, decrepit, infirm. --4 Bent, bowed down, drooping. --5 Pale, yellowish-white. --6 Full-grown, ripe, matured; jaraṭhakamala Śi. 11. 14. --7 Hard-hearted, cruel. --ṭhaḥ 1 N. of Pāṇḍu, father of the five Pāṇḍavas. --2 Old age.
