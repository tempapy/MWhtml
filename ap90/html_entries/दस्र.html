• dasra a. [dasyati pāṃsūn das-rak] Savage, fierce, destructive. --srau (m. du.) 1 The two Aśvins, the physicians of the gods. --2 The number ‘two’. --sraḥ 1 An ass. --2 A robber. --sraṃ 1 The cold season. --2 The lunar mansion Aśvinī. --Comp. --devatā the constellation Aśvinī. --sūḥ f. wife of the sun and mother of the Aśvins, = saṃjñā q. v.
