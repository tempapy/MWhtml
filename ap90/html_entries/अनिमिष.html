• animi(me)ṣa a. [na. ba.] 1 Not winking, steadfastly or intently fixed; °locanaṃ suciramālokya K. 102; °pakṣmaṇā 131; śataistamakṣṇāmanimeṣavṛttibhiḥ R. 3. 43; °darśanaramaṇīyaiḥ K. 50 fish and twinkleless glances. --2 Vigilant, watchful. --3 Open (as eyes, flowers). --ṣaḥ 1 A god (for the eyes of gods do not twinkle); Śi. 5. 57. --2 A fish. --3 Viṣṇu. --4 N. of Mahākāla. --Comp. --dṛṣṭi, --nayana, --locana a. looking steadfastly or with a fixed gaze, gazing intently.
