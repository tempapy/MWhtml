• māsika a. (kī f.) [ma se bhavaḥ ṭhañ]
      1 Relating to a month.
      2 Happening every month, monthly.
      3 Lasting for a month.
      4 Payable in a month.
      5 Engaged for a month.
    • --kaṃ A funeral rite or Śrāddha performed every newmoon (during the first year of a man's death); pitṝṇāṃ māsikaṃ śrāddhamanvāhāryaṃ vidurbudhāḥ. [0864-b]
