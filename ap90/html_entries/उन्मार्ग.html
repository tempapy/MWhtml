• unmārga a. [utkrāṃtaḥ mārgāt] Going to a wrong path. --rgaḥ 1 A wrong road, deviation from the right road (fig. also); nityamunmārgagāmināṃ Pt. 1. --2 An improper conduct, evil course, moral turpitude; unmārgaprasthitāni iṃdriyāṇi K. 155; °pravartakaḥ 103. --rgaṃ ind. Astray, by a wrong way; Pt. 1. 161. --Comp. --gata a. going wrong. --gamanaṃ following evil courses. --gāmin, --vartin a. going wrong, erring, taking a wrong road. [0314-b]
