ūrdhva a.
      1 Erect, upright, above; °keśa &c. rising or tending upwards.
      2 Raised, elevated, erected; hastaḥ, °pādaḥ &c.
      3 High, superior, upper.
      4 Not sitting (opp. āsīna).
      5 Torn (as hair).
      6 Thrown up.
