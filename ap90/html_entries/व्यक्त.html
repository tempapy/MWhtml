vyakta p. p.
      1 Manifested, displayed.
      2 Developed, created; Ku. 2. 11.
      3 Evident, manifest, clear, plain, distinct, clearly visible; Pt. 2. 92.
      4 Specified, known, distinguished.
      5 Individual.
      6 Wise, learned.
      7 Ved. Adorned, decorated.
      --ktaḥ N. of Viṣṇu.
      --ktaṃ That which is developed as the product of avyakta q. v. --ktaṃ ind. Clearly, evidently, certainly.
    Comp.
      --gaṇitaṃ arithmetic.
      --dṛṣṭārthaḥ an eyewitness, a witness in general.
      --rāśiḥ a known quantity.
      --rūpaḥ an epithet of Viṣṇu.
      --vikrama a. displaying valour. [1016-c]
