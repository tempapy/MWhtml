• anuraṃj {c4c} U. 1 To be red; bhṛśamanvarajyadatuṣārakaraḥ Śi. 9. 7 (became red or attached). --2 To be delighted, find pleasure; tava prakīrtyā jagatprahṛṣyatyanurajyate ca Bg. 11. 36. --3 To be attached or devoted to, be fond of, love, like (with acc. or loc.); samasthamanurajyaṃti viṣamasthaṃ tyajaṃti ca Rām.; aśuddhaprakṛtau rājñi janatā nānurajyate Pt. 1. 301; bhrāturmṛtasya bhāryāyāṃ yo'nurajyeta kāmataḥ Ms. 3. 173. --Caus. 1 To make red, redden, dye, colour; sarasakisalayānuraṃjitairvā Śi. 7. 64, 8. 17, 12. 68; Dk. 168; Ki. 12. 23, 4. 27. --2 To attach oneself to, please, gratify, conciliate, keep contented or satisfied; anuraktaḥ prajābhiśca prajāścāpyanvaraṃjayat Rām.; kaṃṣṭaṃ janaḥ kuladhanairanuraṃjanīyaḥ U. 1. 14; śiṣyaguṇānurāṃjitamanasaṃ Pt. 1 pleased, gratified.
