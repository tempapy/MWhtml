asaṃbaddha a.

      2 Nonsensical, absurd, unmeaning; °pralāpiṇī talking nonsense; asaṃbaddhaḥ khalvasi Mk. 9 absurd fellow; °manorathā Māl. 2; °ddhaṃ pralapituṃ pravṛttaḥ Ratn. 2.
      3 Improper, wrong; Ms. 12. 6. --ddhaṃ An absurd sentence, unmeaning or nonsensical speech; e. g. yāvajjīvamahaṃ maunī when uttered by some one; see abaddha also. [0211-c]
