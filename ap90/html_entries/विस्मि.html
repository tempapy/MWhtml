vismi 1 A.
      1 To wonder or be surprised at; ubhayorna tathā lokaḥ prāvīṇyena visiṣmiye R. 15. 68; udvīkṣya ko bhuvi na vismayate nageśaṃ Śi. 4. 19, Bk. 5. 51.
      2 To admire.
      3 To be proud or conceited; na vismayeta tapasā Ms. 4. 236. --Caus. To cause to smile, cause to be surprised, fill with wonder or astonishment; vismāpayan vismitamātmavṛttau R. 2. 33; Bk. 5. 58; 8. 42. [1002-a]
