• vrajaḥ [vraj-ghañarthe ka] 1 A multitude, collection, flock, group; netravrajāḥ paurajanasya tāsmin vihāya sarvānnṛpatīnnipetuḥ R. 6. 7; 7. 60; Śi. 6. 6, 14. 33. --2 A station of cowherds. --3 A cow-pen, cow-shed; Śi. 2. 64. --4 An abode, a resting-place. --5 A road. --6 A cloud. --7 N. of a district near Mathurā. --jaṃ Wandering, going. --Comp. --aṃganā, --yuvatiḥ f. a woman of Vraja, a cowherdess; Bv. 2. 165. --ajiraṃ a cow-pen. --kiśoraḥ, --nāthaḥ, --mohanaḥ, --varaḥ, --vallabhaḥ epithets of Kṛṣṇa.
