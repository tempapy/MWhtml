bhekaḥ [bhī kan kasya netvaṃ; Uṇ. 3. 43]
      1 A frog; paṃke nimagne kariṇi bheko bhavati mūrdhagaḥ.
      2 A timid man.
      3 A cloud.
      --kī
      1 A small frog.
      2 A female frog.
    Comp.
      --bhuj m. a serpent.
      --ravaḥ, --śabdaḥ the croaking of frogs. [0825-a]
