• pratiyogin a. 1 Opposing, counteracting, impeding. --2 Related or corresponding to, being or forming a counter-part of (anything); often used in works on Nyāya; as a ghaṭa is the pratiyogī of ghaṭābhāva; (yasyābhāvo bivakṣyate sa pratiyogī). --3 Co-operating with. --4 Equally matched. --m. 1 An adversary, opponent, enemy; dahatyaśeṣaṃ pratiyogigarvaṃ Vikr. 1. 117. --2 A counter-part, match. --3 A partner, an associate. --4 An object dependent upon another. --5 A counter-part, counter-entity; pratiyoginānādhīnajñānaviṣayatvamabhāvalakṣaṇam.
