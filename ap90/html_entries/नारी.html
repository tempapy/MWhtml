• nārī [nṝ-nara-vā jātau ṅīṣ ni°] A woman; arthataḥ puruṣo nārī yā nārī rsāthataḥ pumān Mk. 3. 27.
    Comp.
    • --taraṃgakaḥ {1} a paramour. {2} a libertine.
    • --dūṣaṇaṃ a woman's vice, (they are: --pānaṃ durjanasaṃsargaḥ patyā ca viraho'ṭanam . svapno'nyagṛhavāsaśca nārīṇāṃ dūṣaṇāni ṣaṭ Ms. 9. 13).
    • --prasaṃgaḥ lechery, libertinism.
    • --ratnaṃ a jewel of a woman, an excellent woman. [0616-a]
