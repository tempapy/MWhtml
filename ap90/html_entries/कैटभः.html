• kaiṭabhaḥ N. of a demon killed by Viṣṇu. [He was a very powerful demon. He and Madhu are said to have sprung from the ears of Viṣṇu while he was asleep; and when they were about to devour Brahman they were slain by Viṣṇu]. --bhā --bhī An epithet of Durgā. --Comp. --ariḥ, --jit m., --ripuḥ, --han epithets of Viṣṇu.
