aṃbālikā
      1 A mother; good woman (as a term of respect or endearment.)
      2 N. of a plant (Mar. aṃbāḍā).
      3 N. of the youngest daughter of Kāśīrāja, wife of Vichitra-Vīrya. She became the mother of Pāṇḍu by Vyāsa who was invoked by Satyavatī to beget a son to Vicitra-Vīrya who had died without issue. [0154-a]
