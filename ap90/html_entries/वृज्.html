• vṛj {vI.v} {c2c} A. (vṛkte) To avoid, shun, abandon. {vII.v} {c7c} P. (vṛṇakti) 1 To avoid, shun, give up, abandon. --2 To choose; āsāmekatamāṃ vṛṃgdhi savarṇāṃ svargabhūṣaṇāṃ Bhāg. --3 To atone for, efface, purify; tanme retaḥ pitā vṛṃktāmityasyaitannidarśanaṃ Ms. 9. 20. --4 To turn away, avert. --5 To remove, set aside. --6 To give, bestow. --7 To hurt, injure, kill. {vIII.v} {c1c} P., {c10c} U. (varjati, varjayati-te, varjita) 1 To shun, avoid. --2 To give up, abandon. --3 To exclude, set aside, leave out, except; Ś. 6. 27. --4 To abstain from. --5 To cut to pieces. --6 To take away, deprive (one) of. --7 To discharge, pour or give out, emit. (The following verse from K.R. illustrates the root in its different conjugations: --vṛṇakti vṛjinaiḥ saṃgaṃ vṛkte ca vṛṣalaiḥ saha . varjatyanārjavopetaiḥ sa varjayati durjanaiḥ ..). [1005-c]
