vṛj
      I. 2 A. (vṛkte) To avoid, shun, abandon.
      II. 7 P. (vṛṇakti)
      1 To avoid, shun, give up, abandon.
      2 To choose; āsāmekatamāṃ vṛṃgdhi savarṇāṃ svargabhūṣaṇāṃ Bhāg.
      3 To atone for, efface, purify; tanme retaḥ pitā vṛṃktāmityasyaitannidarśanaṃ Ms. 9. 20.
      4 To turn away, avert.
      5 To remove, set aside.
      6 To give, bestow.
      7 To hurt, injure, kill.
      III. 1 P., 10 U. (varjati, varjayati-te, varjita)
      1 To shun, avoid.
      2 To give up, abandon.
      3 To exclude, set aside, leave out, except; Ś. 6. 27.
      4 To abstain from.
      5 To cut to pieces.
      6 To take away, deprive (one) of.
      7 To discharge, pour or give out, emit. (The following verse from K.R. illustrates the root in its different conjugations: --vṛṇakti vṛjinaiḥ saṃgaṃ vṛkte ca vṛṣalaiḥ saha . varjatyanārjavopetaiḥ sa varjayati durjanaiḥ ..). [1005-c]
