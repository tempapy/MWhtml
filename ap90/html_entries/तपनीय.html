tapanīya a.
      1 To be heated.
      2 To be suffered or practised (as a penance).
      --yaṃ Gold; especially gold purified with fire; tapanīyāśokaḥ M. 3; tapanīyopānadyugalamāryaḥ prasādīkarotu Mv. 4; asaṃspṛśaṃtau tapanīyapīṭhaṃ R. 18. 41. (Also tapanīyakaṃ in this sense.) [0530-b]
