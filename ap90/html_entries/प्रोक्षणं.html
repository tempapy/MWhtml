• prokṣaṇaṃ 1 Sprinkling, sprinkling with water; Ms. 5. 118; Y. 1. 184. --2 Consecration by sprinkling. --3 Immolation (of animals) at a sacrifice. --4 A text to be repeated at an animal-sacrifice. --ṇī, --prokṣaṇiḥ f. Water used for sprinkling or consecrating, holy water (used in pl., and sometimes used to denote ‘the vessel containing holy water,’ in which sense the word generally used is prokṣaṇīpātra). [0780-b]
