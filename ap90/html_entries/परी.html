parī(pari-i) 2 P.
      1 To go round, circumambulate; (caraṇanyāsaṃ) bhaktinamraḥ parīyāḥ Me. 55; Ms. 2. 48.
      2 To surround, encompass; hutavahaparītaṃ gṛhamiva Ś. 5. 10; viṣavallībhiḥ parītābhirmahauṣadhiḥ R. 12. 61; so kopaparītamānasaṃ Ki. 2. 25.
      3 To go to, think of (objects &c.).
      4 To be changed or transformed.
      5 To reach to.
      6 To run against. [0683-b]
