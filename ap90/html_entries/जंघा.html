• jaṃghā [jaṃghanyate kuṭilaṃ gacchati han yaṅluki ac pṛṣo°; cf. Uṇ. 5. 31]
      1 Leg from the ankle to the knee, the shank.
      2 The upper part of the leg, the part about the loins.
      3 A part of a bed-stead.
    Comp.
    • --karaḥ, --karikaḥ, --kāraḥ, --kārikaḥ a runner, courier, an express.
    • --trāṇaṃ an armour for the legs. [0504-a]
