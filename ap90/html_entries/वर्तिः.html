• vartiḥ --rtī f. [vṛt-in vā ṅīp] 1 Anything wrapped round, a pad, roll. --2 An unguent, ointment, eye-salve, collyrium or any cosmetic (in the form of a ball or pill); sā punarmama prathamadarśanātprabhṛtyamṛtavartiriva cakṣuṣorānaṃdamutpādayaṃtī Māl. 1; iyamamṛtavartirnayanayoḥ U. 1. 38; karpūravartiriva locanatāpahaṃtrī Bv. 3. 16; Vb. 1. --3 The wick of a lamp; Māl. 10. 4. --4 The projecting threads or unwoven ends (of a cloth), the fringe. --5 A magical lamp. --6 The protuberance round a vessel. --7 A surgical instrument (such as a bougie). --8 A streak, line. --9 Swelling in the throat. --10 A swelling formed by internal rupture.
