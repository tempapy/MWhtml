• kāmuka a. (kā or kī f.) [kam-ukañ]
      1 Wishing, desirous.
      2 Lustful, libidinous.
    • --kaḥ
      1 A lover, a libidinous man; kāmukaiḥ kuṃbhīlakaiśca parihartavyā caṃdrikā M. 4; R. 19. 33; Rs. 6. 9.
      2 A sparrow.
      3 The Aśoka tree.
    • --kā A woman desirous of wealth.
    • --kī A libidinous or lustful woman. [0395-b]
