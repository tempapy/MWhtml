• vilāsaḥ
      1 Sport, play, pastime.
      2 Amorous pastime, diversion, pleasure; as in vilāsamekhalā R. 8. 64; so vilāsakānanaṃ, vilāsamaṃdiraṃ &c.
      3 Coquetry, dalliance, affectation, wantonness, graceful movement or play, any feminine gesture indicative of amorous sentiment; Ś. 2. 2; kavikulaguruḥ kālidāso vilāsaḥ P. R. 1. 22; Śi. 9. 26.
      4 Grace, beauty, elegance, charm; Māl. 2. 6.
      5 Flash, gleam.
    Comp.
    • --kānanaṃ a pleasuregrove.
    • --maṃdira a pleasure-house. --ceṣṭitaṃ amorous movement; Ku. 5. 13. [0991-a]
