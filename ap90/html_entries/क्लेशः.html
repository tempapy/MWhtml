• kleśaḥ [kliś-bhāve ghañ]
      1 Pain, anguish, suffering, distress, trouble; kimātmā kleśasya padamupanītaḥ Ś. 1; kleśaḥ phalena hi punarnavatāṃ vidhatte Ku. 5. 86; Bg. 12. 5.
      2 Wrath, anger.
      3 Care, trouble.
      4 Worldly occupation.
    Comp.
    • --apaha a. allaying pain, consoling, palliative. (--haḥ) a son.
    • --kara a. causing pain or trouble.
    • --kṣama a capable of enduring trouble. [0433-b]
