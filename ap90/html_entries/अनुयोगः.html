anuyogaḥ
    • 1 A question, inquiry, examination; aniyaṃtraṇānuyogastapasvijanaḥ Ś. 1; nigṛhyānuyoge P. VIII. 2. 94; vārtā°madhurākṣarayā ca vācā R. 13. 71.
    • 2 Censure, reproof.
    • 3 Solicitation.
    • 4 Exertion, effort; anuyogaṃ vinā tailaṃ tilānāṃ nopajāyate Pt. 2. 140.
    • 5 Religious meditation; spiritual union.
    • 6 Comment.
      Comp. {1} an interrogator. {2} a teacher, spiritual preceptor (anuyogaṃ kṛṃtati praśnaviṣayasaṃśayaṃ chinatti). [0078-a]
