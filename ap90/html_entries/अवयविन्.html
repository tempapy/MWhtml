avayavin a. [avayavaḥ kāraṇatvenāstyasya ini] Having limbs, having portions or subdivisions (as a whole); avayavinā saha pūrvādayaḥ samasyaṃte P. II. 2. 1. Sk. --m. (vī)
    • 1 A whole, any substance formed of several constituents; nanu avayavini kiṃ mānaṃ Sid. Mukt.
    • 2 A syllogism, or any logical argument. [0185-c]
