nivid 2 P. (generally in the caus.)
      1 To tell, communicate, inform (with dat.); upasthitāṃ homavelāṃ gurave nivedayāmi Ś. 4; kāśyapāya vanaspatisevāṃ nivedayāvaḥ ibid., R. 2. 68.
      2 To declare or announce oneself; kathamātmānaṃ nivedayāmi Ś. 1.
      3 To indicate, betray, show; śaṃkāpīragrahanivedayitā Mu. 1; digaṃbaratvena niveditaṃ vasu Ku. 5. 72; R. 17. 40.
      4 To offer, present, give, make an offer of; svarājyaṃ caṃdrāpīḍāya nyavedayat K. 367; rājyamasmai nyavedayat R. 15. 70, 11. 47; Ms. 2. 51; Y. 1. 27.
      5 To entrust to the care of, make or deliver over to. [0637-b]
