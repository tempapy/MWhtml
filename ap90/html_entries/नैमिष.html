• naimiṣa a. (ṣī f.) Lasting for a ‘nimisha’ or twinkling, momentary, transient. --ṣaṃ N. of a sacred forest celebrated as the residence of certain sages to whom Sauti related the Mahābhārata; R. 19. 1; (the name is thus derived: --yatastu nimiṣeṇedaṃ nihataṃ dānavaṃ balaṃ . araṇye'smiṃstatastena naimiṣāraṇyasaṃjñitaṃ ..).
