• bhagavat a. 1 Glorious, illustrious. --2 Revered, venerable, divine, holy. (an epithet applied to gods, demigods and other holy or respectable personages); atha bhagavān kuśalī kāśyapaḥ Ś. 5; bhagavanparavānayaṃ janaḥ R. 8. 81: so bhagavān vāsudevaḥ &c. --3 Fortunate (Ved.). --m. 1 god, A deity. --2 An epithet of Viṣṇu. --3 Of Śiva. --4 Of Jina. --5 Of Buddha. --Comp. --gītā N. of a celebrated sacred work (it is an episode of the great Bhārata and purports to be a dialogue between Kṛṣṇa and Arjuna).
