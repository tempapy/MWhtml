• aviveka a. Wanting in judgment or discrimination, thoughtless. --kaḥ 1 Want of discrimination or judgment, absence of prudence, imprudence; avivekaḥ paramāpadāṃ padaṃ Ki. 2. 30. --2 Hastiness, rashness, recklessness. --3 Ignorance ( anyonyatādātmyarūpamithyājñānaṃ). --Comp. --kṛta a. done rashly.
