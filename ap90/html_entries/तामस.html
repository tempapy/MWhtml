• tāmasa a. (sī f.) [tamo'styasya aṇ]
      1 Dark, consisting of darkness; Māl. 9. 52; U. 5. 12.
      2 Affected by or relating to tamas or the quality of darkness (the third of the three qualities of nature); Bg. 7. 12, 17. 2; M. 1. 1; Ms. 12. 33-34.
      3 Ignorant.
      4 Vicious.
    • --saḥ
      1 A malignant person, an incendiary, a villain.
      2 A snake.
      3 An owl.
      4 N. of a son of Rāhu.
    • --saṃ Darkness.
    • --sī
      1 Night, a dark night.
      2 Sleep.
      3 An epithet of Durgā. [0535-a]
