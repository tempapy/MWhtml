• āvartaka a. [āvarta eva svārthe kan] Revolving again and again. --kaḥ 1 N. of a form of cloud personified; jātaṃ vaṃśe bhuvanavidite puṣkarāvartakānāṃ Me. 6; Ku. 2. 50. --2 Depression above the eye-brows. --3 A whirlpool. --4 Revolution. --5 Revolution of the mind from the influence of the senses. --6 A curl of hair. --7 A sort of poisonous insect. --kī N. of a creeping plant.
