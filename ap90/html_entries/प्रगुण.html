• praguṇa a. 1 Straight, honest, upright (lit. and fig.); bahiḥ sarvākārapraguṇaramaṇīyaṃ vyavaharan Māl. 1. 14. --2 Being in the right state or condition, having excellent qualities; śramajayātpraguṇāṃ ca karotyasau tanumato'numataḥ sacibairyayau R. 9. 49. --3 (a) Worthy, suitable, meritorious; Māl. 1. 16. (b) Efficient; 9. 45. --4 Skilful, clever. (praguṇīkṛ means 1 To make straight, put in order, arrange. --2 To make smooth. --3 To nourish, bring up). [0727-c]
