• khaṃḍ {c10c} P. (khaṃḍayati, khaṃḍita) 1 To break, cut, tear, break to pieces, crush; Bk. 15. 54; sauhārdaṃ śakaṭena khaṃḍitaṃ Mu. 5. 18; Śi. 7. 31, 20. 24, 6. 16, 12. 3. --2 To defeat completely, destroy, dispel; rajanīcaranāthena khaṃḍite timire niśi H. 2. 111. --3 To disappoint; frustrate, cross in love; strībhiḥ kasya na khaṃḍitaṃ bhuvi manaḥ Pt. 1. 146. --4 To disturb. --5 To cheat.
