• bhujaṃgaḥ [bhujaḥ san gacchati gam-khac mum ḍicca] 1 A serpent, snake; bhujagaṃmapi kopinaṃ śirasi puṣpavaddhārayet Bh. 2. 4. --2 A paramour, gallant; abhūmireṣā bhujaṃgabhaṃgibhāṣitānāṃ K. 196. --3 A husband or lord in general. --4 A catamite. --5 The dissolute friend of a king. --6 The constellation āśleṣā. --7 The number ‘eight’. --Comp. --iṃdraḥ an epithet of Śeṣa, the lord of snakes. --īśaḥ an epithet of {1} Vāsuki. {2} of Śeṣa. {3} of Patanjali. {4} of the sage Piṅgala. --kanyā a young female snake. --bhaṃ the asterism āśleṣa. --bhuj m. {1} an epithet of {1} Garuḍa. {2} a peacock. --latā betel-pepper (tāṃbūlī). --han m. an epithet of Garuḍa; see bhujagāṃtaka &c.
