avakṛṣṭa p. p.
      1 Drawn away or down, pulled down.
      2 Removed.
      3 Expelled, turned out or away.
      4 Inferior, low, degraded, outcast (opp. utkṛṣṭa or prakṛṣṭa); °jātiḥ Ms. 8. 177; pratikartuṃ prakṛṣṭasya nāvakṛṣṭena yujyate Rām. --ṣṭaḥ A servant who performs the lowest menial duties (such as sweeping &c.) ( saṃmārjanaśodhanaviniyukta); paṇo deyo'vakṛṣṭasya ṣaḍutkṛṣṭasya vetanaṃ Ms. 7. 126. [0176-a]
