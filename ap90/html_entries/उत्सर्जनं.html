• utsarjanaṃ
      1 Leaving, abandoning, letting loose, quitting &c.
      2 A gift, donation.
      3 Suspension of a Vedic study.
      4 A ceremony connected with this suspension (to be performed half yearly); Ms. 4. 96; vedotsarjanākhyaṃ karma kariṣye Śrāvaṇī Mantra. [0300-b]
