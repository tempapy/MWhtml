      1 A slaughter-house, butcher's house; bhavānapi sūnāparicara iva gṛdhra āmiṣalolupo bhīrukaśca M. 2.
      2 The sale of meat.
      3 Hurting, killing, destroying.
      4 The soft palate, uvula.
      5 A girdle, zone.
      6 Inflammation of the glands of the neck called mumps.
      7 A ray of light.
      8 A river.
      9 A daughter.
      10 An elephant's trunk.
    • --nāḥ (f. pl.) The five things in a house by which animal life is likely to be destroyed; see under śūnā or paṃcaśūnā. [1135-b]
