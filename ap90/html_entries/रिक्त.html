rikta p. p. [ric-kta]
      1 Emptied, cleared, evacuated.
      2 Empty, void; devoid or deprived of, without.
      3 Hollowed (as hands).
      4 Indigent.
      5 Divided, separated.
      6 Worthless, useless.
      7 Unloaded; see ric.
      --ktaṃ
      1 An empty space, vacuum.
      2 A forest, desert, wilderness.
    Comp.
      --arkaḥ a Sunday falling on one of the riktā days.
      --pāṇi, --hasta a. empty-handed, bringing no present (of flowers &c.), ahamapi devīṃ prekṣitumariktapāṇirbhavāmi M. 4. [0912-c]
