nidrā 2 P. To fall asleep, sleep. [0621-c]
      nidrā
      1 Sleep, sleepiness; pracchāyasulabhanidrā divasāḥ Ś. 1. 3; nidrāmudrā kṣipan Māl. 2. 12.
      2 Sloth.
      3 Shutting, budding state.
    Comp.
      --alasa a dull or languid with drowsiness, fast asleep; nidrālasā bahiṃṇaḥ V. 3. 2. --bhaṃgaḥ awaking.
      --vṛkṣaḥ darkness.
      --saṃjananaṃ phlegm, phlegmatic humour. [0621-c]
