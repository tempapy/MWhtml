vipralabdha p. p.
      1 Deceived, cheated.
      2 Disappointed.
      3 Hurt, injured.
      --bdhā A woman disappointed by her lover's breaking his appointment; (one of the several classes of a Nāyikā in poetic composition); she is thus defined in S. D. : --priyaḥ kṛtvāpi saṃketaṃ yasyā nāyāti saṃnidhim . vipralabdheti sā jñeyā nitāṃtamavamānitā .. 118. [0983-a]
