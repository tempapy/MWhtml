• pradīpaḥ 1 A lamp, light (fig. also); atailapūrāḥ saratapradīpāḥ Ku. 1. 10; R. 2. 24; 16. 4; kulapradīpo nṛpatirdilīpaḥ R. 6. 74 ‘light or ornament of the family’; 7. 29. --2 That which enlightens or elucidates, elucidation; especially at the end of titles of works; as in mahābhāṣyapradīpaḥ, kāvyapradīpaḥ &c.
