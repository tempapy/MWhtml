• kauṭilyaṃ
      1 Crookedness(lit. and fig.).
      2 Wickedness
      3 Dishonesty, fraud; Pt. 2. 185. --lyaḥ ‘The crooked’, N. of Cāṇakya. a celebrated writer on civil polity, (the work being known as cāṇakyanīti), the friend and adviser of Candragupta and a very important character in the Mudrārākṣasa; kauṭilyaḥ kuṭilamatiḥ sa eṣa yena krodhāgnau prasabhamadāhi naṃdavaṃśaḥ Mu. 1. 7; spṛśati māṃ bhṛtyabhāvena kauṭilyaśiṣyaḥ Mu. 7.
