taras a. [tṝ-karaṇādau asun] Ved. Quick, energetic. --n.
      1 Speed, velocity.
      2 Vigour, strength, energy; kailāsanāthaṃ tarasā jigīṣuḥ R. 5. 28, 11. 77; Śi. 9. 72.
      3 A bank, a place of crossing.
      4 A float, raft.
      5 A monkey.
      6 A disease. [0532-b]
