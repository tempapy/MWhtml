apaṭī [alpaḥ paṭaḥ paṭī, na. ta.]
      1 A screen or wall of cloth, particularly the screen or kanāt surrounding a tent.
      2 A curtain.
    Comp.
      --kṣepaḥ (apaṭakṣepaḥ) tossing aside the curtain; °kṣepeṇa (= akasmāt) ‘with a (hurried) toss of the curtain’, frequently occurring as a stage-direction and denoting precipitate entrance on the stage which arises from fear, hurry, agitation &c., as when a character tossing up the curtain suddenly enters without the usual introduction tataḥ praviśati &c. [0105-c]
