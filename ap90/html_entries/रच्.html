• rac {c10c} U. (racayati-te, racita) 1 To arrange, prepare, make ready, contrive, plan; puṣpāṇāṃ prakaraḥ smitena racito no kuṃdajātyādibhiḥ Amaru. 40; racayati śayanaṃ sacakitanayanaṃ Gīt. 5. --2 To make, form, effect, create, produce; māyāvikalparacitaiḥ syaṃdanaiḥ R. 13. 75; mādhuryaṃ madhuviṃdunā racayituṃ kṣārāṃbudherīhate Bh. 2. 6; maulau vā racayāṃjaliṃ Ve. 3. 40. --3 To write, compose, put together (as a work); aśvadhāṭīṃ jagannātho viśvahṛdyāmarīracat Aśvad. 26; Ś. 3. 15. --4 To place in or upon, fix on; racayati cikure kurabakakusumaṃ Gīt. 7; Ku. 4. 18, 34; Ś. 6. 17. --5 To adorn, decorate; Me. 66. --6 To direct (the mind &c.) towards.
