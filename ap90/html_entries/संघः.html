• saṃghaḥ 1 A group, collection, multitude, flock; as maharṣisaṃgha, manuṣyasaṃgha &c. --2 A number of people living together. --3 Close contact or combination. --cārin m. a fish. --jīvin m. a hired labourer, coolie. --talaḥ the two hands with the open palms brought together. --vṛttiḥ f. close combination.
