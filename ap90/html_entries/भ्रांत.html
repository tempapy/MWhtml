• bhrāṃta p. p. [bhram-kta] 1 Wandered or roamed about. --2 Turned round, whirled, revolved. --3 Erred, mistaken, gone astray. --4 Perplexed, confused. --5 Moving about, moving to and fro, wheeling. --6 Whirling or turning round, roaming or wandering about. --taḥ 1 An elephant in rut. --2 A kind of thorn-apple. --taṃ 1 Roaming, moving about; varaṃ parvatadurgeṣu bhrāṃtaṃ vanacaraiḥ saha Bh. 2. 14. --2 A mistake, an error.
