hrasva a. [hras-van] (compar. hrasīyas, superl. hrasiṣṭha)
      1 Short, small, little.
      2 Dwarfish, low or short in stature.
      3 Short (opp. to dīrgha in prosody).
      --svaḥ
      1 A dwarf.
      2 A short vowel.
      --svaṃ Green or black sulphate of iron.
    Comp.
      --agniḥ the gigantic swallow = wort (Arka).
      --aṃga a. dwarfish, shortbodied. (--gaḥ) a dwarf.
      --garbhaḥ the Kuśa grass.
      --darbhaḥ --kuśaḥ the short or white Kuśa grass.
      --dā gum olibanum.
      --bāhuka a. short-armed.
      --mūrti a. short in stature, dwarfish, pigmy.
      --mūlaḥ the short red cane. [1177-b]
