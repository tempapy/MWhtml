• hrasva a. (--gaḥ) a dwarf. --garbhaḥ the Kuśa grass. --darbhaḥ --kuśaḥ the short or white Kuśa grass. --dā gum olibanum. --bāhuka a. short-armed. --mūrti a. short in stature, dwarfish, pigmy. --mūlaḥ the short red cane.
