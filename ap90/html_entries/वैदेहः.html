• vaidehaḥ [videha aṇ] 1 A king of Videha. --2 An inhabitant of Videha. --3 A trader by caste. --4 The son of a Vaiśya by a Brāhmaṇa woman; Ms. 10. 11. --5 An attendant on the women's apartments. --hāḥ (m. pl.) The people of Videha. --hī 1 N. of Sītā; vaidehibaṃrdhohṛdayaṃ vidadre R. 14. 13 (the final vowel in vaidehī being shortened.) --2 The wife of a merchant. --3 A sort of pigment (rocanā). --4 Turmeric. --5 A cow. --6 Long pepper. [1013-c]
