aṃj 7 P. (rarely A.) (anakti or aṃkte, ānaṃja, aṃjiṣyati-aṃkṣyati, aṃjitvā-aṃktvā-aktvā, aṃjituṃ, aṃktuṃ, akta)
      1 To anoint, smear with, bedaub.
      2 To make clear, show, represent, characterize; mā nāṃjī rākṣasīrmāyāḥ Bk. 9. 49
      3 To go.
      4 To shine, be beautiful.
      5 To honour, celebrate.
      6 To decorate. --Caus.
      1 To smear with, nāṃjayaṃtīṃ svake netre Ms. 4. 44.
      2 To speak or shine.
      3 To cause to go. [cf. Zend anj; L. unctum, ungo.] --With adhi to fit out, equip, furnish.
      --ā {1} to anoint, smear. {2} to smooth, polish, prepare. {3} to honour, respect.
      --ni {1} to smear. {2} to conceal or hide oneself.
      --prati {1} to smear. {2} to adorn, decorate.
      --saṃ {1} to smear. {2} to fit out, equip, harness. {3} to honour. {4} to join together, to consume, devour; to adorn, decorate, beautify (mostly Ved. in these senses). {5} to unite, to put together, compose. [0026-b]
