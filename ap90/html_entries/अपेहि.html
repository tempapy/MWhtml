• apehi (Imper. 2nd sing.). Used as the first member of some compounds (belonging to the class mayūravyaṃsakādi P. II. 1. 72); °karā, °dvitīyā, °prakasā, °vāṇijā, °svāgatā where it has the sense of ‘excluding,’ ‘expelling’, ‘refusing admission to’; e. g. °vāṇijā a ceremony where merchants are excluded; so °dvitīyā &c.
    Comp.
    • --vātā N. of a plant which expels wind. [0120-a]
