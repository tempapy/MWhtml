puraskṛ 8 U.
      1 To place before or in front, make one's leader, put at the head; hate jarati gāṃgeye puraskṛtya śikhaṃḍinaṃ Ve. 2. 4; U. 1. 3; Ku. 2. 52.
      2 To introduce, present; madvacanātsa rājā śakuṃtalāṃ puraskṛtya vaktavyaḥ Ś. 4, 7
      3 To honor, respect, esteem, hospitably receive or entertain; darśanenaiva bhavatīnāṃ puraskṛto'smi Ś. 1.
      4 To adopt, choose, follow; sa puraskṛtamadhyamakramaḥ R. 8. 9.
      5 To appoint.
      6 To show, indicate, evince,
      7 To lead.
      8 To use as a pretext. [0710-a]
