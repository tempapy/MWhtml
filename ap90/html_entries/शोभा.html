śobhā [śubh-a]
      1 Light, lustre, brilliance, radiance.
      2 (a) Splendour, beauty, elegance, grace, loveliness; vapurabhinavamasyāḥ puṣyati svāṃ na śobhāṃ Ś. 1. 19; Me. 52, 59. (b) Natural beauty, grandeur (as of a mountain); adriśobhā R. 2. 27.
      3 An ornament, graceful expression; śobhaiva maṃdarakṣu bdhakṣubhitāṃbhodhivarṇanā Śi. 2. 107.
      4 Turmeric.
      5 A kind of pigment (= gorocanā q. v.)
    Comp.
      --aṃjanaḥ N. of a very useful tree. [1056-b]
