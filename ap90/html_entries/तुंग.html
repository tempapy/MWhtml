• tuṃga a.
      1 High, elevated, tall, lofty, prominent; jalanidhimiva vidhumaṃḍaladarśanataralitatuṃgataraṃgaṃ Gīt. 11; tuṃgaṃ nagotsaṃgamivāruroha R. 6. 3, 4. 70; Śi. 2. 48; Me. 12, 64.
      2 Long.
      3 Vaulted.
      4 Chief, principal.
      5 Strong, passionate.
    • --gaḥ
      1 A height, elevation.
      2 A mountain.
      3 Top, summit.
      4 The planet Mercury.
      5 A rhinoceros.
      6 The cocoa-nut tree.
      7 The aphelion of a planet.
      8 (Fig.) A throne.
      9 A wise man.
      10 An epithet of Śiva.
    • --gaṃ The stamina of the lotusblossoms.
    Comp.
    • --bījaḥ quicksilver.
    • --bhaṃ the apsis of a planet.
    • --bhadraḥ a restive elephant, an elephant in rut.
    • --bhadrā N. of a river flowing into the Kṛṣṇā.
    • --mukhaḥ a rhinoceros.
    • --veṇā N. of a river.
    • --śekharaḥ a mountain. [0540-c]
