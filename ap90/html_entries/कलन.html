• kalana a. (at the end of comp.) Causing, effecting.
    • --naḥ A sort of cane.
    • --naṃ
      1 A spot, mark.
      2 A defect, an offence, fault.
      3 Taking, seizing, grasping; kalanātsarvabhūtānāṃ sa kālaḥ parikīrtitaḥ.
      4 Knowing, understanding, apprehension.
      5 Sounding.
      6 An embryo at the first stage after conception.
    • --nā
      1 Taking, seizing. grasping; kālakalanā Ā. L. 29.
      2 Doing, effecting.
      3 Subjection.
      4 Understanding, comprehension.
      5 Putting on, wearing. [0385-c]
