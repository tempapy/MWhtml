• moṭṭāyitaṃ Silent involuntary expression of affection towards an absent lover, as when a woman, her mind being taken up by her lover, scratches the ear &c. when he is remembered or talked of; it is thus defined by ujjvalamaṇiḥ - kāṃtasmaraṇavārtādau hṛdi tadbha vabhāvataḥ . prākaṭyamabhilāṣasya moṭṭāyitamudīryate; see S. D. 141 also; sadyo moṭṭāyitamadhurimollāsabhaṃgīvidhātā Ud. S. 35. [0880-c]
