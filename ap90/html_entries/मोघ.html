• mogha a. [mah-gha ac vā kutvaṃ]
      1 Vain, useless, fruitless, unprofitable, unsuccessful; yācñā moyā varamadhigu ṇe nādheme labdhakāmā Me. 6; moghavṛtti kalabhasya ceṣṭitaṃ R. 11. 39; 14. 65; Bg. 9. 12.
      2 A imless, purposeless, indefinite.
      3 Left, abandoned
      4 Idle.
    • --ghaḥ A fence, an enclosure, a hedge.
    • --ghā The trumpet flower --ghaṃ ind. In vain, to no purpose, uselessly.
    Comp.
    • --karman a. engaging in useless rites.
    • --puṣpā a barren woman. [0880-b]
