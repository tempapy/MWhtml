• nalinī 1 A lotus-plant; na parvatāgre nalinī prarohati Mk. 4. 17; nalinīdalagatajalamatitaralaṃ Moha M. 5; Ku. 4 6. --2 An assemblage of lotuses. --3 A pond or place abounding in lotuses. --4 A lotus or the stalk of it. --5 The celestial Ganges. --6 The intoxicating juice of the cocoa-nut. --Comp. --khaṃḍaṃ, --ṣaṃḍaṃ a group or assemblage of lotuses. --ruhaḥ an epithet of Brahmā. (--haṃ) a lotus-stalk, the fibres of a lotus. [0610-b]
