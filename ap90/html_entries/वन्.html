van
      I. 1 P. (vanati)
      1 To honour, worship.
      2 To aid.
      3 To sound.
      4 To be occupied or engaged.
      II. 8 U. (vanoti, vanute, usually vanute only)
      1 To beg, ask, request (said to govern two acc.); toyadāditaraṃ naiva cātako vanute jalam.
      2 To seek for, seek to obtain.
      3 To conquer, possess.
      4 Ved. To like, love.
      5 To wish, desire.
      6 To make ready, prepare for.
      7 To hurt, injure.
      III. 1 P., 10 U. (vanati, vānayati-te)
      1 To favour, aid.
      2 To hurt, injure.
      3 To sound.
      4 To confide in. [0942-b]
