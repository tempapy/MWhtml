haima a. (mī f.) [hima-heman-aṇ]
      1 Cold, wintry, frigid.
      2 Caused by frost; mṛṇālinī haimamivoparāgaṃ R. 16. 7.
      3 Golden, made of gold; pādena haimaṃ vililekha pīṭhaṃ R. 6. 15; Bk. 5. 89; Ku. 6. 6.
      4 Of a golden yellow colour.
      --mā --mī Yellow jasmine.
      --maṃ Hoar-frost, dew.
      --maḥ An epithet of Śiva.
    Comp.
      --mudrā, --mudrikā a golden coin. [1176-b]
