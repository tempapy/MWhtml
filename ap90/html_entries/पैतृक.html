• paitṛka a. (kī f.) [pitṛta āgataṃ pitṛridaṃ vā ṭhañ] 1 Relating to a father. --2 Coming or derived from a father, ancestral, paternal; padamṛddhamajaṃna paitṛkaṃ vinayenāsya navaṃ ca yauvanaṃ R. 8. 6; 18. 40; Ms. 9. 104; Y. 2. 47. --3 Sacred to the Manes. --kaṃ A Śrāddha performed in honour of the Manes or deceased ancestors.
