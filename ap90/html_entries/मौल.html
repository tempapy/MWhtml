• maula a. (lā, --lī f.) [mūlaṃ vetti mūlādāgato vā aṇ] 1 Radical, original. --2 Ancient, old, of long standing (as a custom). --3 Nobly born, of a good family. --4 Brought up in the service of a king for generations, holding office from ancient times, hereditary; Ms. 7. 54; R. 19 57. --laḥ An old or hereditary minister; (prakṛtayaḥ) maulairānāyayāmāsurbharataṃ staṃbhitāśrabhiḥ R. 12. 12, 14. 10; 18. 38.
