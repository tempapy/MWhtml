pākṣika a. (kī f.)
      1 Belonging to a fortnight, fortnightly.
      2 Belonging to a bird.
      3 Favouring a party of faction.
      4 Belonging to an argument.
      5 Optional, subject to an alternative, allowed but not specifically laid down; niyamaḥ pākṣeke sati.
      --kaḥ
      1 A fowler.
      2 An alternative. [0690-a]
