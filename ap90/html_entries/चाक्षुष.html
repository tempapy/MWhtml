cākṣuṣa a. (ṣī f.) [cakṣuṣā gṛhyate, cakṣus-aṇ]
      1 Depending on, or produced from, sight.
      2 Belonging to the eye, visual, optical. M. 1. 4.
      3 Visible, to be seen.
      --ṣaḥ N. of the sixth Manu.
      --ṣaṃ Knowledge dependent on vision.
    Comp.
      --jñānaṃ ocular evidence or proof. [0488-c]
