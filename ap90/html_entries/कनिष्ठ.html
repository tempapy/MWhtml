• kaniṣṭha a. (Snperl. of alpa or yuvan)
      1 The smallest, least.
      2 The youngest.
      3 Lower.
      4 Having the feet downwards.
    • --ṣṭhaḥ N. of Śiva.
    • --ṣṭhā
      1 The little finger.
      2 A kind of heroine.
      3 The wife of a younger brother.
    Comp.
    • --padaṃ --mūlaṃ the least or first root. [0375-b]
