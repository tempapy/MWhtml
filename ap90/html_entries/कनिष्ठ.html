kaniṣṭha a. (Snperl. of alpa or yuvan)
    • 1 The smallest, least.
    • 2 The youngest.
    • 3 Lower.
    • 4 Having the feet downwards.
      N. of Śiva.
    • 1 The little finger.
    • 2 A kind of heroine.
    • 3 The wife of a younger brother.
      Comp. the least or first root. [0375-b]
