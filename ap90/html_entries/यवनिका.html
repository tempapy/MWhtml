• yavanikā, yavanī 1 A Yavana female, a Greek or Mahomedan woman; yavanī navanītakomalāṃgī Jag.; yatanīmukhapadmānāṃ sehe madhumadaṃ na saḥ R. 4. 61; (from dramas it appears that Yavana girls were formerly employed as attendants on kings, particularly to be in charge of their bows and quivers; cf. eṣa bāṇāsanahastābhiryavanībhiḥ parivṛta ita evāgacchati priyavayasyaḥ Ś. 2; praviśya śārṅgahastā yavanī Ś. 6; praviśya cāpahastā yavanī V. 5. &c.). --2 A curtain.
