āhavanīya pot. p. To be offered as an oblation.
      A consecrated fire taken from the house-holder's perpetual fire, one of the three fires (i. e. the eastern) burning at a sacrifice; gārhapatyādāhavanīyaṃ jvalaṃtamuddharet . pitā vā eṣo'gnīnāṃ yaddakṣiṇaḥ putro gārhapatyaḥ pautra āhavanīyaḥ Āśval.; see also agnitretā under agni. [0271-c]
