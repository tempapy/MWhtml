• praṇīta p. p.
      1 Put forward, advanced, presented.
      2 Delivered, given, offered, presented.
      3 Brought into, reduced to.
      4 Executed, effected, performed.
      5 Taught, prescribed.
      6 Cast, sent, discharged.
      7 Brought to, set.
      8 Written, composed.
      9 Set aside, removed.
      10 Agreeable or pleasing.
      11 Inflicted (as punishment); see praṇī above.
    • --taḥ Fire consecrated by prayers.
    • --tā A sacrificed vessel.
    • --taṃ Anything cooked or dressed, such as a condiment. [0732-c]
