• yodhaḥ [yudh-ac]
      1 A warrior, soldier, combatant; sahāsmadīyairapi yodhamukhyaiḥ Mb.
      2 War, battle.
    Comp.
    • --agāraḥ, --raṃ a soldier's dwelling, a barrack.
    • --dharmaḥ the law of soldiers, a military law. --saṃrāvaḥ mutual defiance of combatants, a challenge. [0898-b]
