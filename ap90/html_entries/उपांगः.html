upāṃgaḥ A mark of sandal on the forehead.
      --gaṃ
      1 A subdivision, a subhead.
      2 Any minor limb or member (of anything); vargāḥ… sāṃgopāṃgairihoditāḥ Ak.
      3 A supplement of a supplement.
      4 A supplementary work (of inferior value).
      5 A secondary portion of science; a class of writings supplementary to the Vedāṅgas; (these are four: --purāṇanyāyamīmāṃsādharmaśāstrāṇi). [0336-c]
