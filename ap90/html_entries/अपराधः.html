aparādhaḥ An offence, a fault, guilt, crime, sin, mistake, error; (32 kinds are usually stated in Śāstras); kamaparādhalavaṃ mayi paśyasi V. 4. 29; yathāparādhadaṃḍānāṃ R. 1. 6; yatheṃdraśatruḥ svarato'parādhāt Śik. 52 mistake or error in accent; sometimes with gen. or loc. of person; adaḥ sureṃdrasya kṛtāparādhān daityān V. 1. 18; kṛtāparādhamiva tvayyātmānamavagacchati K. 203.
    Comp.
      --bhaṃjanaḥ the destroyer of sins, epithet of Śiva; °stotraṃ a short poem by Śankarāchārya in praise of Śiva, the fourth line of the verses being usually kṣaṃtavyo meparādhaḥ śiva śiva śivabhoḥ śrīmahāṃdeva śaṃbho. [0110-a]
