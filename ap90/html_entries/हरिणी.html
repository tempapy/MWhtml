hariṇī
      1 A female deer, doe; cakitahariṇīprekṣaṇā Me. 82, R. 9. 55, 14. 69.
      2 One of the four classes of women (also called citriṇī q. v.)
      3 Yellow jasmine.
      4 A good golden image.
      5 N. of a metre.
      6 The green colour.
      7 Turmeric.
      8 Madder.
    Comp.
      --dṛś a. deer-eyed. (--f.) a deer-eyed woman; kimabhavadvipine hariṇīdṛśaḥ U. 3. 27. [1166-c]
