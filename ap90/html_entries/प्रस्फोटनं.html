• prasphoṭanaṃ
      1 Expanding, blooming, opening.
      2 Making clear or manifest, disclosing, revealing.
      3 Splitting.
      4 Causing to bloom or blow.
      5 Threshing corn.
      6 A winnowing basket.
      7 Striking, beating.
      8 Wiping away, rubbing out. [0767-c]
