śas
      I. 1 P. (śasati) To cut up, kill, destroy.
      II. 2 P. (śasti) To sleep. Cf. śaṃs also. [1037-c]
      śas
      1 A technical name for the termination of the acc. plural.
      2 A Taddhita affix forming adverbs from nouns, especially from numerals; as dviśaḥ, śataśaḥ, bahuśaḥ &c. [1038-a]
