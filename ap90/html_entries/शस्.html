• śas {vI.v} {c1c} P. (śasati) To cut up, kill, destroy. {vII.v} {c2c} P. (śasti) To sleep. Cf. śaṃs also. [1037-c]
  • śas 1 A technical name for the termination of the acc. plural. --2 A Taddhita affix forming adverbs from nouns, especially from numerals; as dviśaḥ, śataśaḥ, bahuśaḥ &c. [1038-a]
