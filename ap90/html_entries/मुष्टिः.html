• muṣṭiḥ m. f. [muṣ-ktic] 1 The clenched hand, fist; karṇāṃtametya bimide nibiḍopi muṣṭiḥ R. 9. 58; 15. 21; Śi. 10. 59. --2 A handful, fistful; śyāmākamuṣṭiparivardhitakaḥ Ś. 4. 13; R. 19. 57; Ku. 7. 69; Me. 68. --3 A handle or hilt. --4 A particular measure (= pala). --5 A measure of capacity equal to one handful. --6 The penis. --7 Stealing (only f.). --8 A compendium, abridgment, --Comp. --karaṇaṃ clenching the fist. --deśaḥ the middle of a bow, that part of it which is grasped in the hand. --dyūtaṃ a kind of game. --pātaḥ boxing. --baṃdhaḥ {1} clenching the fist. {2} a handful. --meya a. to be measured with the fist, to be spanned with the fingers. --yuddhaṃ a pugilistic encounter, boxing.
