• saṃcaraḥ

      2 A way, path; yatrauṣadhipra kāśena naktaṃ darśitasaṃcarāḥ Ku. 6. 43; R. 16. 12
      3 A narrow road, defile, difficult passage.
      4 Entrance, gate.
      5 The body.
      6 Killing.
      7 Development. [1081-a]
