• śāpaḥ [śap-ghañ]
      1 A curse, an imprecation, anathema; śāpenāstaṃ gamitamahimā varṣabhogyeṇa bhartuḥ Me. 1, 92; R. 1. 78, 5. 56, 59; 11. 14.
      2 An oath, asseveration.
      3 Abuse, calumny.
      4 An interdiction, a ban.
    Comp.
    • --aṃtaḥ, --avasānaṃ, --nivṛttiḥ f. the end of a curse; Me. 110; R. 8. 82. --astraḥ ‘having a curse for a weapon', a sage, saint; R. 15. 3. --utsargaḥ the utterance of a curse.
    • --uddhāraḥ, --māktiḥ f., --mokṣaḥ release or deliverance from a curse.
    • --grasta a. labouring under a curse.
    • --mukta a. released from a curse.
    • --yaṃtrita a. restrained by a curse. [1040-b]
