• maraṇaṃ [mṛ-bhāve lyuṭ] 1 Dying, death; maraṇaṃ prakṛtiḥ śarīriṇāṃ R. 8. 87; or saṃbhāvitasya cākīrtirmaraṇādatiricyate Bg. 2. 34. --2 A kind of poison. --Comp. --aṃta, --aṃtaka a. ending in death. --abhimukha, --unmukha a. on the point of death, near death, moribund. --ātmaka a. causing death, fatal. --dharman a. mortal. --niścaya a. determined to die; Pt. 1. --śīla a. mortal.
