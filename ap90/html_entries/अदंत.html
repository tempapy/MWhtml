• adaṃta a. [na. ba.] 1 Toothless. --2 Not yet having the teeth formed or grown (said of young ones of men or animals before the teething time). --3 Ending in at or a. --taḥ 1 A leech. --2 N. of Pūṣan, one of the 12 Ādityas, he having lost his teeth at the destruction of Dakṣa's sacrifice by Vīrabhadra.
