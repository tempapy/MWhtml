cīnaḥ [ci-nak pṛṣo° dīrghaḥ]
      1 N. of a country, the modern China.
      2 A kind of deer.
      3 A sort of cloth.
      4 A thread.
      --nāḥ (m. pl.) The rulers or people of China.
      --naṃ
      1 A banner.
      2 A kind of bandage for the corners of the eyes.
      3 Lead.
