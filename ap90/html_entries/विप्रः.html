• vipraḥ [vap-ran pṛṣo °ata itvam; Uṇ. 2. 28] 1 A Brāhmaṇa; see the quotations under brāhmaṇa. --2 A sage, wise man. --3 The Aśvattha tree. --4 (In prosody) A foot of four short syllables. --5 A singer of hymns, praiser. --Comp. --ṛṣiḥ = brahmaṣiṃḥ q. v. --kāṣṭhaṃ the cotton-plant. --priyaḥ the Palāśa tree. --samāgamaḥ a concourse or synod of Brāhmaṇas. --svaṃ the property of a Brāhmaṇa.
