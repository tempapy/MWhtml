• viraṃj {c1c} {c4c} U. 1 To grow discoloured or soiled, be coarse or rough; keśā api virajyaṃte niḥsnehāḥ kiṃ na sevakāḥ Pt. 1. 82 (where it has sense 2 also). --2 To be discontented or disaffected, to dislike, hate; cirānurakto'pi virajyate janaḥ Mk. 1. 53; yāṃ ciṃtayāmi satataṃ mayi sā viraktā Bh. 2. 2; Bk. 18. 22. --3 To become disgusted with the world and hence to renounce all worldly attachments. --Caus. To colour, dye. [0987-c]
