vibhāvaḥ
      1 (In Rhet.) Any condition which produces or develops a particular state of body or mind; (one of the three main divisions of Bhāvas, the other two being anubhāva and byābhicāribhāva q. v.); ratyādyudvodhakā loke vibhāvāḥ kāvyanāṭyayoḥ S. D. 62; its chief subdivisions are ālaṃbana and uddīpaka; see ālaṃbana.
      2 A friend, an acquaintance.
      3 Any exciting circumtance, (as dress &c.). [0985-a]
