• āyaḥ [ā i-ac, ay-ghañ vā] 1 Arrival, approach. --2 Gaining or acquisition of money, acquiring (opp. vyaya); āye duḥkhaṃ vyaye duḥkhaṃ Pt. 1. 163. --3 Income, revenue, receipt; grāmeṣu svāmigrāhyo bhāga āyaḥ Sk.; Y. 1. 322, 326; Mk. 2. 6; āyadvārāṇi Dk. 162 sources of income; āyavyayau ca niyatāvākarān koṣameva ca Ms. 8. 419; āyādhikaṃ vyayaṃ karoti he lives beyond his means. --4 Gain, profit. --5 The eleventh house (in astrology). --6 The guard of the women's apartments. --Comp. --vyayau (dual) receipt and disbursement, income and expenditure. --sthānaṃ a place where revenues are collected.
