• bhavanaṃ [bhū-ādhāre lyuṭ]
      1 Being, existence.
      2 Production, birth.
      3 An abode, residence, dwelling, mansion; athavā bhavanapratyayāt praviṣṭosmi Mk. 3, Me. 32.
      4 A site, abode, receptacle; as in avinayabhavanaṃ Pt. 1. 191.
      5 A building.
      6 A field.
      7 Nature.
    Comp.
    • --udaraṃ the interior of a house.
    • --patiḥ, --svāmin m. the lord of the house, a pater familias. [0810-b]
