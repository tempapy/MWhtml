• parihā 3 P.
      1 To leave, abandon, quit
      2 To omit, neglect; yathoktānyapi karmāṇi parihāya Ms. 12. 92. --Pass.
      1 To be wanting or deficient in; āryasya suvihitaprayogatayā na kimapi parihāsyate Ś. 1.
      2 To be inferior to; ojasvitayāna parihīyate śacyāḥ V. 3; na praticchaṃdātparihīyate madhuratā M. 2.
      3 To wane, fail, waste away; anudivasaṃ parihīyase aṃgaiḥ Ś. 3; Pt. 1. 78.
      4 To pass away; parihīyate gamanavelā Ś. 4.
      5 To be deprived of or excluded from. [0683-a]
