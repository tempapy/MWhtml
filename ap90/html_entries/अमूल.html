• amūla, --laka a. 1 Rootless(lit.); paśavo'mūlā oṣadhayo mūlinyaḥ Śat. Br.; (fig.) without basis or support, baseless, groundless. --2 Without authority; not being in the original; nāmūlaṃ likhyate kiṃcit Malli. --3 Without material cause, as the Pradhāna of the Sāṃkhyas; mūlaṃ mūlābhāvādamūlaṃ. --4 Not fixed in the earth, moving. --lā N. of a plant (agniśikhā).
