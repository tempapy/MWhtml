• bhogavat a. 1 Giving pleasure or delight, delightful. --2 Happy, prosperous. --3 Having curves, ringed, coiled. --m. 1 A snake. --2 A mountain. --3 Dancing, acting, and singing together. --f. (tī) 1 An epithet of the Ganges of Pātāla or the lower world (pātālagaṃgā). --2 A female snake-demon. --3 N. of the city of the snake-demons in the lower world. --4 The night of the second day of a lunar month.
