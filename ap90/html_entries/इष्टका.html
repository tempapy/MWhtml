• iṣṭakā [iṣ-takan ṭāp Uṇ. 3. 148]
      1 A brick; Mk. 3.
      2 A brick used in preparing the sacrificial altar &c.
    Comp.
    • --gṛhaṃ a brick house.
    • --cayanaṃ collecting fire by means of a brick.
    • --cita a. made of bricks; Dk. 84; also iṣṭakacita.
    • --nyāsaḥ laying the foundation of a house.
    • --pathaḥ a road made of bricks.
    • --rāśiḥ a pile of bricks. [0282-b]
