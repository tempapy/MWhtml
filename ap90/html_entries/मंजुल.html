• maṃjula a. [maṃju-sidhmā° lac, maṃj-u lac vā] Lovely, beautiful, agreeable, charming, sweet, melodious (voice &c.); saṃprati maṃjulavaṃjulasīmani keliśayanamanuyātaṃ Gīt. 11; kūjitaṃ rājahaṃsānāṃ vardhate madamaṃjulaṃ Kāv. 2. 334. --laḥ A kind of gallinule.
    • --laṃ
      1 An arbour, a bower.
      2 A spring, well.
      3 The state of being variegated. [0832-b]
