• upahṛ 1 P.
      1 To fetch, bring near; bhaktyupahṛta Bg. 9. 26; phalamiśramarghamupahara Ś. 1; tamasyopahara Ś. 7; so baliṃ, annaṃ &c.
      2 To offer, present, give (with dat. or gen.); tathāpi rājaparigraho'sya prabhutvamupaharati M. 1 invests with importance; mātṛbhyo balimupahara Mk. 1; nīvārabhāgadheyamasmākamupaharaṃtu Ś. 2; darbhān ṛtvigbhya upaharāmi Ś. 3; Mv. 6. 22; K. 40, 65; R. 14. 19, 16. 86, 19. 12; upahara pādaṃ M. 3 give.
      3 To serve out or distribute (food).
      4 To offer as a victim, sacrifice; prāgupayācitaṃ strīratnamupahartavyaṃ Māl. 5; mālatīmupaharan 9.
      5 To put down, throw.
      6 To collect, gather together.
      7 To take away, destroy.
      8 To employ, apply, use. --Caus. To cause to offer or bring; māṃ priyāṇyupahāraya Bk. 8. 84. [0335-b]
