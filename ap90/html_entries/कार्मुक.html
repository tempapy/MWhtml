• kārmuka a. (kī f.) [karmaṇe prabhavati ukañ P. V. 1. 103.] Fit for or able to do a work, doing it well and completely. --kaṃ 1 A bow; tatkārmukaṃ karmasu yasya śaktiḥ Ki. 3. 48; tvayi cādhijyakārmuke Ś. 1. 6. --2 A bamboo. --3 The ninth sign of the zodiac. --4 A kind of machine or instrument shaped like a bow. --Comp. --bhṛt m. {1} the archer or the sign Sagittarius of the zodiac. {2} an archer in general.
