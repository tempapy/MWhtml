• ayājya a. 1 (A person) for whom one must not perform sacrifices, not competent to offer sacrifices (as a Śūdra &c.). --2 (Hence), Out-cast; degraded, not admissible to or incapable of religious ceremonies. --3 Not fit for sacrificial offerings. --Comp. --yājanaṃ, --saṃyājyaṃ sacrificing for a person for whom one must not perform sacrifices; Ms. 3. 65; 11. 60.
