aṃgin a. [aṃga-astyarthe ini]
      1 Corporeal, having limbs, embodied, incarnate; dharmārthakāmamokṣāṇāmavatāra ivāṃgavān R. 10. 84, 38; one who has a body; yenāṃgena vikṛtena aṃgino vikārastasmāt tṛtīyā Sk.; vyāyāme vṛddhiraṃgināṃ Śi. 2. 94.
      2 Having subordinate parts; chief, principal; ye rasasyāṃgino dharmāḥ, eka eva bhavedaṃgī śṛṃgāro vīra eva vā, aṃgī atra karuṇaḥ, rasasyāṃgitvamāptasya dharmāḥ śauryādayo yathā S. D. [0019-c]
