• māṃgalya a. [maṃgalāya hitaṃ ṣyañ] Auspicious, indicative of good fortune; Ś. 4. 4. --lyaṃ 1 Auspiciousness, prosperity, welfare, good fortune. --2 A blessing or benediction. --3 A festivity, festival, any auspicious rite. --4 An auspicious thing, amulet. --Comp. --mṛdaṃgaḥ a drum beaten on auspicious occasions; U. 6. 25.
