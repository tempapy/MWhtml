avamarṣaḥ

    • 2 One of the five principal parts or Saṃdhis of a play; yatra mukhyaphalopāya udbhinno garbhato'dhikaḥ . śāpādyaiḥ sāṃtarāyaśca so'vamarṣa iti smṛtaḥ S. D. 336; also written vimarṣa.
    • 3 Attacking; vīryāvadāneṣu kṛtāvamarṣaḥ Ki. 3. 43. [0185-b]
