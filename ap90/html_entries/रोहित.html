• rohita a. (rohitā or rohiṇī f.) Red, red-coloured.
    • --taḥ
      1 Red colour.
      2 A fox.
      3 A kind of deer.
      4 A red horse.
      5 N. of Hariśchandra's son.
      6 A kind of fish.
    • --taṃ
      1 Blood.
      2 Saffron.
      3 A straight rain-bow.
    Comp.
    • --aśvaḥ fire. [0919-a]
