• kṛttikā (pl.) [kṛt-tikan kicca; Uṇ. 3. 147]
      1 The third of the 27 lunar mansions or asterisms, (consisting of {6} stars) the Pleiades.
      2 The six stars represented as nymphs acting as nurses to Kārtikeya, the god of war.
    Comp.
    • --aṃjiḥ a kind of horse in an Aśvamedha sacrifice having a carriage as an emblem.
    • --tanayaḥ, --putraḥ, --sutaḥ epithets of Kārtikeya.
    • --bhavaḥ the moon. [0420-a]
