bārhaspatya a. [bṛhaspati-yak] Relating to Bṛhaspati.
      --tyaḥ
      1 A pupil of Bṛhaspati.
      2 A follower of Bṛhaspati who taught the rankest form of materialism, a materialist.
      3 An epithet of Agni.
      --tyaṃ
      1 The constellation Puṣya.
      2 Morality. [0794-b]
