• gāṃdhāraḥ
      1 The third of the seven primary notes of the Indian Gamut; (commonly denoted by ga in musical notation).
      2 Red lead.
      3 N. of a country between India and Persia, the modern Kandāhāra.
      4 A native or a ruler of that country.
    • --raṃ Gum myrrh. [0457-b]
