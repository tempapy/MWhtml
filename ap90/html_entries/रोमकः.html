• romakaḥ
      1 The city of Rome.
      2 A Roman, an inhabitant of Rome (usually in pl.).
    • --kaṃ
      1 A kind of salt (pāṃśulaṣaṇa).
      2 A kind of magnet.
    Comp.
    • --pattanaṃ the city of Rome.
    • --siddhāṃtaḥ one of the five chief Siddhāntas (so called because it was probably derived from the Romans). [0918-b]
