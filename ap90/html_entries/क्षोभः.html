• kṣobhaḥ [kṣubh-ghañ]
      1 Shaking, moving, tossing; Me. 28, 95; so kānanakṣobhaḥ &c.
      2 Jolting; R. 1. 58; V. 3. 11.
      3 (a) Agitation, disturbance, excitement, emotion; kṣoka° U. 3, 3. 29; svayaṃvarakṣobhakṛtāmabhāvaḥ R. 7. 3; atheṃdriyakṣobhamayugmanetraḥ punarvaśitvādbalavannigṛhya Ku. 3. 69. (b) Provocation, irritation; prāyaḥ svaṃ mahimānaṃ kṣobhātpratipadyate jaṃtuḥ Ś. 6. 30. [0439-a]
