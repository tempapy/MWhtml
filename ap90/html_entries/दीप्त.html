• dīpta p. p. [dīp kta]
      1 Lighted, inflamed, kindled.
      2 Glowing, hot, flashing, radiant.
      3 Illuminated.
      4 Excited, stimulated.
      5 Luminous, bright.
      6 Heated by the sun, exposed to sunshine.
      7 Inauspicious (in general).
    • --ptaḥ
      1 A lion.
      2 The citron tree.
      3 Inflammation of the nose.
    • --ptaṃ Gold.
