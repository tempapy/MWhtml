• avaśyaṃ ind. [ava-śyai-ḍamu Tv.] 1 Necessarily, inevitably; tvāmapyasraṃ navajalamayaṃ mocayiṣyaṃtyavaśyaṃ Me. 93. --2 Certainly, at all events, by all means, surely, of course; avaśyaṃ yāti tiryaktvaṃ jagdhvā caivāhutaṃ haviḥ Ms. 12. 68; avaśyaṃ yātāraścirataramuṣitvāpi viṣayāḥ Bh. 3. 16; tāṃ cāvaśyaṃ divasagaṇanātatparāmekapatnīṃ (drakṣyasi) Me. 10, 61. avaśyameva Most surely; if compounded with pot. pass. the final nasal is dropped; avaśyapācya to be necessarily cooked; avaśyakārya to be necessarily done.
