khyā 2 P. (m. also in non-conjugational tenses) (khyāti, khyāta) To tell, declare, communicate (with dat. of person). --Pass. (khyāyate)
      1 To be named or called; Bk. 6. 97.
      2 To be known or famous. --Caus. (khyāpayati-te)
      1 To make known, proclaim; Ms. 7. 201.
      2 To tell, declare, relate; Bh. 2. 69; Ms. 11. 99.
      3 To extol, make renowned, praise. [0445-c]
