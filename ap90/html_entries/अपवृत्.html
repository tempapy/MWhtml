apavṛt 1 A. To turn back, turn away, depart; tasmādapāvartata dūrakṛṣṭā R. 6. 58; tasmādapāvartata kuṃḍineśaḥ 7. 33. --Caus.
    • 1 To turn back or away, turn aside, bend; vyapoḍhapārśvairapavartitatrikāḥ Ki. 4. 15; āsaktatiryagapavartitadṛṣṭi vaktraṃ (vahaṃtī) Māl. 1. 40.
    • 2 (Math.) To divide (as by a common measure) without remainder; samena kenāpyapavartya hārabhājyau bhajedvā sati saṃbhave tu Līlā.
    • 3 To reduce to a common measure. [0112-b]
