cikkaṇa a. (ṇā or ṇī f.) [cikk-kvip cik, taṃ kaṇati, kaṇ--śabde ac Tv.]
      1 Smooth, glossy.
      2 Slippery.
      3 Bland.
      4 Unctuous, greasy; laghu paritrāyatāmenāṃ bhavān mā kasyāpi tapasvina iṃgudītailacikkaṇaśīrṣasya haste patiṣyati Ś. 2. --ṇaḥ The betel-nut tree.
      --ṇā An excellent cow.
      --ṇaṃ A betel-nut. [0492-b]
