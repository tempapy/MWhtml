• kḷpta p. p. [kḷp-kta]
      1 Arranged, prepared, done, got ready, equipped; kḷptavivāhaveṣā R. 6. 10 decked in her nuptial attire.
      2 Cut, pared; kḷptakeśanakhaśmaśru Ms. 4. 35.
      3 Caused, produced.
      4 Fixed, settled.
      5 Thought of, invented.
      6 Formed, framed.
      7 Ascertained, determined.
