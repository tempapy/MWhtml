tiṣya a. [tuṣyaṃtyasmin tuṣ-kyap ni°]
      1 Auspicious, fortunate.
      2 Born under the asterism puṣya.
      --ṣyaḥ
      1 The eighth of the 27 constellations, (also called puṣya).
      2 The lunar month Pauṣa.
      --ṣyaṃ The Kali Yuga.
    Comp.
      --ketuḥ an epithet of Śiva. [0539-b]
