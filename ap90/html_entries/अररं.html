araraṃ [ṛ-aran; araścit syāt Uṇ. 3. 132]
    • 1 The leaf or panel of a door (kapāṭaṃ), sarabhasamararāṇi drāgapāvṛtya Mv. 6. 27 (--raḥ, --rī also); caṃcūkoṭivipāṭitārarapuṭo yāsyāmyahaṃ paṃjarāt Bv. 1. 58.
    • 2 A door.
    • 3 The sheath of a bamboo shoot (karīrakoṣa).
    • 4 A covering or sheath in general.
    • 1 An awl.
    • 2 A part of a sacrifice.
    • 3 War, fighting. [0160-b]
