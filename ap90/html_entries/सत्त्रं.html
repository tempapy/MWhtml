sattraṃ [sad-ṣṭran] (usually written satraṃ)
      1 A sacrificial session, especially one lasting from 13 to 100 days.
      2 A sacrifice in general.
      3 An oblation, offering, or gift.
      4 Liberality, munificence.
      5 Virtue.
      6 A house, residence.
      7 Covering.
      8 Wealth.
      9 A wood, forest; Ki. 13. 9.
      10 A tank, pond.
      11 Fraud, cheating.
      12 A place of refuge, asylum, covert. Comp. --apaśrayaḥ a place of refuge, asylum. --ayanaṃ (ṇaṃ) a long sacrificial session.
      --pariveṣaṇaṃ distribution of food at a sacrifice.
      --śālā an alms-house. [1083-c]
