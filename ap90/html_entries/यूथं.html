• yūthaṃ [yu-thak pṛṣo° dīrghaḥ] A herd, flock, multitude, a large number or troop (as of beasts); strīratneṣu mamorvaśī priyatamā yūthe taveyaṃ vaśā V. 4. 25; Ś. 5. 5.
    Comp.
    • --nāthaḥ, --paḥ, --patiḥ {1} the leader of a troop or band. {2} the head of a flock or herd (usually of elephants), a lordly elephant; gajayūthapa yūthikāśavalakeśī V. 4. 24. --mukhyaḥ the chief of a troop or herd (as of elephants &c.). [0896-a]
