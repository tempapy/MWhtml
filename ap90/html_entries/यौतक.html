yautaka a. (kī f.) [yute vivāhakāle adhigataṃ vuṇ] Forming the rightful or exclusive property of any one, rightfully belonging to any one; vibhāgabhāvanā jñeyā gṛhakṣetraiśca yautakaiḥ Y. 2. 149. --kaṃ
      1 Private property in general.
      2 A woman's dowry, a woman's private property (given to her at marriage); mātustu yautakaṃ yatsyātkumārībhāga eva saḥ Ms. 9. 131; also yautuka in this sense. [0898-c]
