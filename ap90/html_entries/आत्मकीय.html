ātmakīya, ātmīya a. Belonging to oneself, one's own; sarvaḥ kāṃtamātmīyaṃ paśyati Ś. 2; svāninamātmīyaṃ kariṣyā ni H. 2 win over; ahamātmīyo na bhaviṣyāmi Mk. 8 I shall lose my life. ātmīyaṃ kurvaṃti subject K. 135, 167; ayanātmīyaḥ kriyatāṃ 12 let him be taken under your protection; prasādanātnīyanivātmadarśaḥ R. 7. 68; Ku. 2. 19; kindred, related, of kin, Bh. 2. 57.
