• phālgunaḥ 1 N. of a Hindu month (corresponding to February, March). --2 An epithet of Arjuna; Mb. thus explains the epithet: --uttarābhyāṃ phalgunībhyāṃ nakṣatrābhyāmahaṃ divā . jato himavataḥ pṛṣṭhe tena māṃ phālgunaṃ viduḥ .. --3 N. of a tree, also called arjuna. --Comp. --anujaḥ {1} the month Chaitra. {2} the vernal season (vasaṃtakāla). {3} an epithet of nakula and sahadeva.
