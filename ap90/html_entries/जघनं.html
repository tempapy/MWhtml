• jaghanaṃ [vakraṃ haṃti han yaṅ ac pṛṣo°; Uṇ. 5. 32] 1 The hip and the loins, the buttocks; ghaṭaya jaghane kāṃcīmaṃca srajā kabarībharaṃ Gīt. 12. --2 The pudenda. --3 Rear-guard, the reserve of an army. --Comp. --ardhaḥ {1} the hinder part. {2} rear-guard. --kūpakau (dual) the hollows of the loins of a handsome woman. --gauravaṃ weight of the hips; Ś. 3. 8. --capalā {1} an unchaste or libidinous woman; patyaurvideśayāte paramasukhaṃ jaghanacapalāyāḥ Pt. 1. 173. {2} a woman active in dancing.
