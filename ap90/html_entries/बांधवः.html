• bāṃdhavaḥ [baṃdhu svārthe idamarthe vā'ṇ] 1 A relation, kinsman (in general); yasyārthāstasya bāṃdhavāḥ H. 1; Ms. 5. 74, 101; 4. 179. --2 A maternal relation. --3 A friend; dhatebhyaḥ paro bāṃdhavo nāsti loke Subhāṣ. --4 A brother. --Comp. --janaḥ relatives, kinsmen (taken collectively); dāridryātpuruṣasya bāṃdhavajano vākye na saṃtiṣṭhate Mk. 1. 36; Pt. 4. 78.
