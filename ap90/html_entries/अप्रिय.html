apriya a.
    • 1 Disliked, disagreeable, unpleasant, offensive apriyasya ca pathyasya vaktā śrotā ca durlabhaḥ Rām.; Ms. 4. 138; Ś. 5. 17; hated, distasteful.
    • 2 Unkind, unfriendly.
      A foe, an enemy.
      A sort of fish (śṛṃgīmatsyaḥ).
      An unfriendly or offensive act; pāṇigrāhasya sādhvī strī nācaretkiṃcidapriyaṃ Ms. 5. 156.
      Comp. a. unfriendly, ill-disposed.
      a. ill-fated.
      a. speaking unkind or harsh words; vaṃdhyārthaghnyapriyaṃvadā Y. 1. 73; mātā yasya gṛhe nāsti bhāryā cāpriyavādinī Chāṇ. 44. [0123-c]
