karṇī
    • 1 An arrow of a particular shape (barbed arrow).
    • 2 N. of the mother of Mūladeva, the father of the science and art of thieving.
      Comp. a covered litter; a lady's vehicle, palanquin; karṇīrathasthāṃ raghuvīrapatnīṃ R. 14. 13. --sutaḥ Mūadeva, father of the science and art of thieving; karṇīsutakatheva saṃnihitavipulācalā K. 19; karṇīsutaprahite ca pathi matimakaravam Dk. [0383-a]
