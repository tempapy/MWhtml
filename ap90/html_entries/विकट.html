• vikaṭa a.
      1 Hideous, ugly.
      2 (a) Formidable, frightful, horrible, dreadful; pṛthulalāṭataṭavaṭitavikaṭabhrūkuṭinā Ve. 1; vidhumiva vikaṭavidhuṃtudadaṃtadalanagalitāmṛtadhāraṃ Gīt. 4. (b) Fierce, savage.
      3 Great, large, broad, spacious, wide; jṛṃbhāviḍaṃbi vikaṭodaramastu cāpaṃ U. 4. 30; āvariṣṭa vikaṭena vivoḍhurvakṣasaiva kucamaṃḍalamanyā Śi. 10. 42, 13. 10; Māl. 7.
      4 Proud, haughty; vikaṭaṃ parikrāmati U. 6; Mv. 6. 32.
      5 Beautiful; Mk. 2.
      6 Frowning.
      7 Obscure.
      8 Changed in appearance.
      9 Large-toothed.
    • --ṭaṃ A boil, tumour. [0965-a]
