apadānaṃ --dānakaṃ [apadāyati pariśudhyati yena karmaṇā, dai karaṇe lyuṭ]
    • 1 Pure conduct, approved course of life; (pariśuddhācaraṇaṃ).
    • 2 A great or noble work, excellent work (perhaps for avadānaṃ q. v.).
    • 3 A work well or completely done, an accomplished work. [0106-c]
