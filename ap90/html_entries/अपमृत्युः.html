• apamṛtyuḥ [apakṛṣṭo duṣṭahetujanyatvena mṛtyuḥ]
      1 Sudden or untimely death, accidental or unnatural death; bhartṛsakto'pamṛtyustasya saṃcarati Pt. 4.
      2 Any great danger, illness &c. from which a person, hopelessly given up for lost, recovers, quite contrary to expectation. [0108-b]
