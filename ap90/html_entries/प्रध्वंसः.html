• pradhvaṃsaḥ Utter destruction, annihilation.
    Comp.
    • --abhāvaḥ ‘nonexistence caused by destruction’, one of the four kinds of abhāva or nonexistence, in which the non-existence of a thing is caused by destruction, as of an effect subsequently to its production. [0750-b]
