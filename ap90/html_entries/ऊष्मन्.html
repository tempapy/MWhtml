• ūṣman m. 1 Heat, warmth. --2 The hot season, summer. --3 Steam, vapour, exhalation. --4 Ardour, passion, violence. --5 (In gram.) The sounds ś, ṣ, s and hṛ; applied also to ṣk, ṣp, aṃ and aḥ. --Comp. --aṃta ending in an Ūṣman. --aṃtasthāḥ (m. pl.) the sounds called Ūṣman and semivowels. --upagamaḥ approach of summer. --pa a. drinking the steam of hot food. (--paḥ) {1} fire. {2} a class of Manes (pl.). [0346-c]
