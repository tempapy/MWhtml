D. 232 or K. P. 4. --4 N. of Yama, --5 Winter. --draṃ 1 Wrath, rage. --2 Formidableness, fierceness, savageness. --3 Heat, warmth; solar heat. --Comp. --karman a. doing dreadful acts. (--n.) a terrible magic rite. --darśana a. frightful-looking, terrific. [0919-b]
