• ślokaḥ [ślok ac]
      1 Praising in verse, extolling.
      2 A hymn or verse of praise.
      3 Celebrity, fame, renown, name; as in puṇyaśloka q. v.
      4 An object of praise.
      5 A proverb or saying.
      6 A stanza or verse in general; ślokatvamāpadyata yasya śokaḥ R. 14. 70; pādaiścaturaḥ saṃyuktamidaṃ vākyaṃ samākṣaraiḥ . śocatoktaṃ mayā yasmāttasmācchloko bhavatviti Rām.
      7 A stanza or verse in the Anuṣṭubh metre. [1063-a]
