māsaḥ, --saṃ [mā eva aṇ]
      1 A month; (it may be cāṃdra, saura, sāvana, nākṣatra or bārhaspatya); na māse pratipattāse māṃ cenmartāsi maithili Bk. 8. 95.
      2 The moon (Ved.).
      3 The number ‘twelve’.
    Comp.
      --anumāsika a. monthly.
      --aṃtaḥ the day of new moon. --avadhika a. lasting for or occurring in a month.
      --āhāra a. eating only once a month.
      --upavāsinī {1} a woman who fasts for a whole month. {2} a procuress, a lascivious or lewd woman (ironically). --kālika a. monthly, lasting for a month.
      --jāta a. a month old, born a month ago.
      --jñaḥ a kind of gallinule.
      --deya a. to be paid in a month.
      --pramitaḥ the new-moon, --praveśaḥ the beginning of a month --mānaḥ a year. [0864-b]
