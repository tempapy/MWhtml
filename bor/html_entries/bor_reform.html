• REFORM [580] (v. t.):
    I Lit.: pratisṛjati (sṛjsṛj, c. 6.): v. To form, create, again.
    II To amend, correct: q. v.:
    (1) vi-śodhayati, saṃ-, pari-, (c. of śudhśudh);
    (2) durmatiṃ nāśayati (c. of naśnaś), chinatti (chidchid, c. 7.), etc. (of men: with gen.).
    III To improve, remove defects: perh.: pra-karṣati, ut-, (kṛṣkṛṣ, c. 1.).
  • REFORM [581] (v. i.):
    (1) ātmānaṃ viśodhayati;
    (2) pūtaṃ panthānaṃ bhajate (bhaja, c. l.), Vi. iii. and sim. comp.s.
  • REFORM [581] (subs.): perh. saṃśodhanam: v. Also reformation.
