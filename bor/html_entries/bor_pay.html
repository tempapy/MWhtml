• PAY [510] (v. t.):
    I To discharge a debt: q. v.: dadātidadāti or datte (dādāda, c. 3.), to p. interest: vṛddhiṃ dadāti, Kat.; to p. a debt: ṛṇaṃ dadāti, M.
    II. To give p.: vetanaṃ dadāti (dādāda, c. 3.: with dat.), Viv.
    III In gen.: bhūlyaṃ dadāti, if a master discharges a servant before time, he shall p. him fully: svāmī ced bhṛtakamapūrṇe kāle jahyāt tasya sakalameva mūlyaṃ dadyāt. Viv.; after being paid: mūlyamādāya, Mat.
    IV To revenge: śodhayati, pariśodhayati (c. of śudhśudh).
    V To perform: karoti.
  • PAY [510] (subs.):
    (1) vetanamvetana, after taking p.: gṛhīta- vetanaḥ, Vri.; the p. settled: kṛtavetanam, Viv.;
    (2) bhṛtiḥbhṛti (prop. maintenance), he is entitled to half the p.: bhṛterardhaṃ labheta saḥ, Vriddha Manu;
    (3) mūlyammūlya (prop. price), shall p. his whole p.: sarvameva mūlyaṃ dadyāt, Viṣṇu.
