• ASSERT: [043] To affirm positively:
    (1) prati-jānīte (jñājñājña, c. 9.), if he were to a. that the duties of castes and religious orders were as unsettled in former times as at present idānīmiva ca kālāntarepya- vyavasthitaprāyān varṇāśramadharmān pratijānīta, S. iii. 33.;
    (2) bravīti (brūbrū, c. 2.), vadati (vadvad, c. 1.), or vakti (vacvac, c. 2.) (=to say, but used in the sense of a. ing), S.;
    (3) pratipādayati (c. of padpad) (=to establish), which a. that its nature is not earthly and deny that its nature is earthly, asaṃsārisvarūpapratipādanāḥ saṃsārisvarūpapratiṣedhanāśca, S. iii. 43.
    II To maintain, claim, vindicate: q. v.
