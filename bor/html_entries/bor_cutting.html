    I Lit.:
    (1) chitvaraḥ (rī, raṃ);
    (2) vraścanaḥ (nā, naṃ).
    II Of wind or cold: perh. udvegakārin (f. ṇī).
    III Biting, severe: q. v.: marmacchid (mfn.).

    I The act:
    (1) chedaḥ, -naṃ (best equiv.);
    (2) kartanam or kṛntanam, ni-;
    (3) lavanam;
    (4) vraścanam;
    (5) chidā.
    II A part cut off: chedaḥ: v. Part.
    III A trench: q. v.: khātam.
