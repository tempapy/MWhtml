• CELEBRATE [109] (v. t.):
    I To extol:
    (1) kīrtayatikīrtayat, (kīrtkīrt, c. 10), to c. one's adventures caritāni kīrta- yatiyatiyatinyat, Ki.;
    (2) gāyatigāyat, upa-, (gaigai, c. 1) (in verse or song), may your fame be c. d by distinguished men ślokaḥ praśastacaritairupagīyatāṃ te, Vi. vii.;
    (3) prathayati: v. Known, to make.
    II To solemnize:
    (1) aminandayati (nandnand, c. l), c d my birth with a festival mahotsavena mama janmābhi- nanditavān, K.;
    (2) anutiṣṭhati (sthāsthāstha, c. 1) (=to perform: q. v.).
