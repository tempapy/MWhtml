• PROVOKE: [554]
    I To irritate, make angry:
    (1) pra-kopayati (c. of kupkup);
    (2) kopaṃ or krodhaṃ janayati (janjan, c. 10.) or uddīpayati (c. of dīpdīp).
    II To excite: q. v.:
    (1) uddīpayati;
    (2) uttejayati (c. of tijtij).
    III To challenge: āhvayate (hvehvehvā, c. 1.).
