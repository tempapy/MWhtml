• FORWARD [294] (adj.):
    I In place:
    (1) agrimaḥagrima (māmāma, maṃ);
    (2) agrasthaḥ (sthāsthāstha, sthaṃ).
    II Early:
    (1) śīghrapakva (f. kvākvakukū) (= precocious);
    (2) ākālikaḥākālika (kīki, kaṃ) (= untimely), a f. spring: ākālikī madhupravṛttiḥ, Ku. iii. 34.
    III Bold, confident: q.v.: pragalbhapragalbha (f. lbhā).
  • FORWARD [294] (v.):
    I To send on: q.v.: prerayati (īrīr, c. 10.).
    II To hasten: q.v.: tvarayati (c. of tvartvar).
    III To promote: vardhayati (c. of vṛdhvṛdh).
  • FORWARD, [294] FORWARDS [294] (adv.):
    (1) agrataḥagratas;
    (2) purataḥpuratas. To go f.: puraḥ sarati; to go backwards and f.: parikrāmati; from this day f.: adyārabhya, adyaprabhṛti; f.: puro bhaba, Ku.
