• PROVOKE: [554]
    I To irritate, make angry:
    (1) pra-kopayati (c. of kup);
    (2) kopaṃ or krodhaṃ janayati (jan, c. 10.) or uddīpayati (c. of dīp).
    II To excite: q. v.:
    (1) uddīpayati;
    (2) uttejayati (c. of tij).
    III To challenge: āhvayate (hve, c. 1.).
