    IV Of a book:
    (1) vacanam;
    (2) sthalam (= place). V. Event: ghaṭanā.
    VI Reception, currency: pracāraḥ. Ph.: birds of p,: *anavasthitasthitayaḥ pakṣiṇaḥ; p. at arms: *samardo bāhūbāhavi.
