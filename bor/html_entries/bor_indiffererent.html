• INDIFFERERENT: [375]
    I Apathetic:
    (1) udāsaḥudāsaudās (sāsāsa, saṃ);
    (2) udāsīnaḥudāsīna (nānāna, naṃ), even on an i. son or minister: sute'mātye'pyudāsīne, H. ii. 134.;
    (3) nirīhaḥnirīha (hāhāha, haṃ) (= without any effort). To bei.:
    (1) udāste (āsāsaāas, c. 2.), men who are i. to enemies: naro'rau ya udāsate, Si. ii. 42.;
    (2) upekṣate (īkṣīkṣ, c. 1.), who is i. to (your) loosely hanging matted hair: upekṣateyaḥ ślathalambinīrjaṭāḥ, Ku. v. 47.
    II Impartial, neutral: q.v.: niḥsaṅganiḥsaṅga (f. ṅgā).
    III Passable: nātyuttamaḥ (māmāma, maṃ); nāti- mandaḥmandamand (ndā, ndaṃ) (?).
    IV Having no differ- -ence: nirviśeṣaḥnirviśeṣa (ṣāṣa, ṣaṃ); better by subs: v. Difference.
