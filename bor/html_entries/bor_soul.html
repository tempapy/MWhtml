• SOUL: [662]
    I The spiritual part:
    (1) ātmanātman (m.), with (their) whole s.: sarvātmanaiva, N. xi. 2.; you are my s., heart, my life: tvamātmā me manaḥ prāṇāḥ, B. v.; individual s.: pratyagātmā; animat- ing s.: jīvātmā; rational s.: cidātmā; pure (universal) s.: paramātmā;
    (2) jīvaḥjīva (animating s.); s. the chief minister of life: jīvo mukhayaprāṇa- sacivaḥ, S.
    II Heart, mind: q. v.: hṛdayamhṛdaya.
    III A person: q. v.: not expr., not a single s. returned: naiko'pi nivavṛte; good s.s: bhadramukhāḥ; poor s.: mandamatiḥ; etc.
    IV Fig., leader, inspirer:
    (1) jīvanamjīvana (=life: q. v.);
    (2) balambala (= force: q. v.).
    V Essence, chief part: q. v.: sāraḥsārassāra.
    VI After death:
    (1) jīvaḥjīva;
    (2) pretaḥpreta (= dead, departed s.);
    (3) Sometimes not expr., “yāgādyanuṣṭhāyināmevottareṇa pathā gamanam”, S.
