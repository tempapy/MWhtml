• SO FAR: [657]
    I With as: yāvat (f. tīti), so far as lies in my power: yāvacchaktitaḥ, Ka.
    II Abs.: etāvatetāvatetāvad or iyat (f. tīti), so far and no further: etāvannādhikam.
    III With from expr. with āstām (=let, alone) or dūrataḥ, far from seeing: dūre taddarśanam, K. s. 25. 31.
