• FIRE [278] (subs.):
    I Lit.:
    (1) agniḥagni, who threw f. on our fort: kenāsmaddurge nikṣipto'gniḥ, H.; to throw oneself into f.: agniṃ viśati (viśviś, c. 6.),;
    (2) vahniḥvahni, the f. spread according to the turning of the wind: tatāna vahniḥ pavanānuvṛttyā, Ki. xvi. 52;
    (3) analaḥanala, burning f.: jvalitānalaḥ, Si.; forest f.: dāvānalaḥ, Ki.
    (4) jvalanaḥjvalana, the weapon of f.: jvalana- syāstram, Ki.;
    (5) pāvakaḥpāvaka, a good f. susandīptaḥ pāvakaḥ, P.;
    (6) dahanaḥdahana.
    II Fig.: tejastejastejatej (n.), that is ever-rising f. (of horses): tattejaḥsatatotthitam, M. n.
    III Of fire-arms: agniḥ: v. (I).
  • FIRE [279] (v.t.):
    I To set on f.: q.v.
    II To inflame, animate: q.v.: uttejayati (tijtij, c. 10.).
    III To f. up.: dīpayatidīpayat (c. of dīpdīp): v. Light (I).
    IV To discharge, as a gun: muñcati (mucmuc, c. 6.) (?).
  • FIRE [279] (v.i.):
    I To kindle: q.v.: dīpyate (dīpdīp, c. 4.).
    II Of f. arms: āgneyāstraṃ muñcati.
  • FIRE, [279] ON: [279]
    (1) agniparītaḥ (tātāta, taṃ), and sim. comp.s., the inner apartment of the princess as if on f.: rājakanyāntaḥpuramagniparītamiva, D. i.;
    (2) agninābhibhūtaḥ (tātāta, taṃ) and sim., comp.s., Ku. xvii. 40.
  • FIRE, [279] TO SET ON: [279]
    (1) dāhayati (c. of dahdah) (= cause to burn), set the house on f.: dāhitaṃ gṛham, Mu. ii.;
    (2) dīpayatidīpayat, ā-, (c. of dīpdīp) (= to light: q.v.) Ram.;
    (3) by circumlo, set the house of Rāvaṇa on f.: jvaladagni rāvaṇagṛhaṃ kṛtavān, B. x. 2.;
    (4) agniṃ muñcati (mucmuc, c. 6.), visṛjati (sṛjsṛj, c. 6.) (= to put f.), and setting the houses of Rākṣasas on f.: visṛjya rakṣobhavaneṣu cāgnim, Ram. v. 50. 21.
  • FIRE, [279] TO TAKE: [279]
    (1) agniṃ prāpnoti (āpāp, c. 5.), vahniṃ samāgacchati (gamgamga, c. 1.), etc., P. x. 11.;
    (2) jvalatijvalat (jvaljval, c. 1.) (= to burn: q.v.).
