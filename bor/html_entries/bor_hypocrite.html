• HYPOCRITE: [358]
    (1) dāmbhikaḥ (kī, kaṃ);
    (2) dharma- dhvajin (f. nī) (religious);
    (3) vaiḍālavratikaḥ (like cats) “yasya dharmadhvajo nityaṃ suradhvaja ivocchritaḥ pracchannāni ca pāpāni vaiḍālaṃ nāma tadvratam ..” Sridhara;
    (4) bakavṛttiḥ (like herons), “arvagdṛṣṭirnaikṛtikaḥ svārthasādhanatatparaḥ . śaṭho mithyāvinītaśca bakavṛttirudā- hṛtaḥ ..” (comp. M. iv. 195. 6.).
