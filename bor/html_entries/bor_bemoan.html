• BEMOAN [066] (v. t.):
    (1) vi- lapati (laplaplabh, c. l.), b. s. the loss of his son putraśokena vilapati; b. s. the death of his brother bhrātṛmaraṇāt vilapati or bhātṛ- maraṇaśokena vilapati;
    (2) anuśocati (śucśuc, c. l.) (rare) I b. my fate bhāgyamanuśocāmi;
    (3) paridevayati (devdev, c. 10.) (rare) v. To lament, bewail. N.B. Observe that only
    (2) takes a direct object.
