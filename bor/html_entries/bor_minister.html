• MINISTER [449] (subs.):
    I Helper, servant: q. v.: sacivaḥsaciva; mistress, minister, private friend: gṛhiṇī sacivaḥ sakhī mithaḥ, R.
    II. Of state:
    (1) sacivaḥsaciva, and then entrusting to m.s: sanniveśya saciveṣvataḥ param, R. xix. 4.; m.s should be of good descent, pure, valorous... kṛlīnāḥ śucayaḥ śūrāḥ sacivāḥ syurmahīpateḥ, Ka.;
    (2) mantrinmantrin (m (= counsellor), placing the burden of state on another m.: rājakāryadhṛrāmanyatra mantriṇi samāsajya, Mu. iv.; principal m.: mukhyamantrī, P.;
    (3) amātyaḥamātya, m.'s failing: amātyavyasanam, Mu.
    III A preacher, pastor; perh. ācāryaḥācāryaācārī.
  • MINISTER [449] (v. t.):

    III To conduce: q. v.: kalpatekalpatā, upa-, (kḷpkḷp, c. l.), Mah.
