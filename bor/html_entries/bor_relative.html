• RELATIVE: [584]
    I Kinsman:
    (1) sapiṇḍa (f. ṇḍā, agnates up to the seventh degree), near r. s inherit the property of a childless (man), in their absence distant r. s: aputradhanādhikāryāsanna- sapiṇḍastadabhāve vyavahitaḥ. Viv.;
    (2) samānodaka (f. kā: 8th to 14th.), Mit.;
    (3) sagotra (f. trā) or gotraja (f. jā = of the same gotra);
    (4) bandhuḥ or bāndhavaḥ (through females);
    (5) sanābhi (mfn.: by blood);
    (6) jñātiḥ (= 5);
    (7) sakulya (f. lyā = of the same class);
    (8) sometimes not expr., in the absence of any r.: sarvāmāve, Mit.
    II Not absolute:
    (1) sāpekṣa (f. kṣā);
    (2) by circumlo. Ph.: r. knowledge: sāpekṣabuddhiḥ, Par. 107.
    III In gram.: *sambandha- dyotaka (f. tikā) and sim. comp.s.
