• CHILD: [118]
    I A boy or girl: q. v.:
    (1) śiśuḥśiśu;
    (2) arbhakaḥarbhaka (rare);
    (3) pṛthukaḥpṛthuka (rare);
    (4) ḍimbhaḥḍimbhaḍimbh (rare).
    II With reference to parents, a son or daughter: q. v.:
    (1) apatyamapatya;
    (2) sūnuḥsūnu (mf.);
    (3) santānaḥ;
    (4) tokamtoka (rare). In addres- -sing children vatsavatsa (m.) and vatsāvatsāvatsa (f.) is generally used. To be with c.: putriṇī, Mah: v. Pregnant.
