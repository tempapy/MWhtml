• PURE: [558]
    I Unmixed, unalloyed: śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā), vi-, with hilts of p. ivory: śuddhadantatsaru (mfn.), Mah. ii. 51. 16.; p. gold: śuddhaṃ suvarṇam, Bha.
    II Clear, clean: q. v.: śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā), vi-, but on the p. surface of a mirror: śuddhe tu darpaṇatale, Sa. vii.
    III Spotless, chaste:
    (1) śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā), vi-, internally p.: antaḥśuddha (f. ddhā), Me.; p. fame: viśuddhayaśas, D.;
    (2) śuciśuciśucīśucinśuc (mfn.), p. knowledge: śuci śrutam, Ki. ii. 32.
    IV Of language: śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā), vi-, Ki.
    V Mere: q. v.: śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā), from a p. desire for children: śuddhasantānakāmaiḥ. R. xviii. 53. Ph.: to become p.: śudhyati (śudhśudh, c. 4.); or pūyate (pass. pūpūpu).
