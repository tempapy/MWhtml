• KEEN: [400]
    I Sharp: q.v.: tīkṣṇatīkṣṇa (f. kṣṇā), K.- pointed: tikṣṇāgra (f. grāgra), with a k. edge: tīkṣṇadhāraḥ (rārāra, raṃ).
    II Severe: q.v.: tījvra (f. vrāvrāvra), also, thundering saying with k. impetus: ahaha tīvrasaṃvego vāgvajraḥ, U. i.
    III Eager: q.v.
    IV In mind: sūkṣmabuddhi (mfn.): v. Also sharp.
