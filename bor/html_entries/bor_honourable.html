• HONOURABLE: [350]
    I Possessing a high mind: māninmānin (f. nīnīni).
    II Illustrious, respected:
    (1) mānyamānya (f. nyānyaninī) or mānanīyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ), sam-;
    (2) pūjyapūjya (f. jyājyājyajijī) or pūjanīyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ), I have been commanded by h. sir (you): pūjyenāhamādiṣṭaḥ, Mah.: v. Respectable.
    III Conferring honour: sammānakaraḥsammānakara (rīrīri, raṃ) and sim. comp.s.: v. Also famous.
    IV Good, noble: q.v.: śuddhaśuddha (f. ddhā).
