    I Conveyance: vāhanamvāhana.
    II A vehicle:
    (1) yānamyāna (most gen. term), drive. in a c. and four caturaśvavāhyena yānena calati;
    (2) rathaḥratha (spec. a war-chariot), a c. road rathyā
    (3) syandanaḥsyandana (=a chariot);
    (4) puṣparathaḥpuṣparatha (= a pleasure-car);
    (5) karṇīrathaḥkarṇīratha;
    (6) pravahaṇampravahaṇa (=a light covered car for ladies).
    III Gesture conduct, deportment: q. v. vyavahāraḥvyavahāra.
    IV Management: q. v.: nirvāhaḥnirvāha.
