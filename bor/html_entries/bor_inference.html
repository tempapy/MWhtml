• INFERENCE: [378]
    (1) anumānamanumāna, i. is of there kinds- antecedent (or a priori: where an effect is inferred from its cause), consequent (or a posteriori: where a cause is deduced from its effect), and analogous (where an object is guessed from common sign): trividhamanumānaṃ pūrvavat (yatra kāraṇena kāryamanumīyate), śeṣavatśeṣavat (yatra kāryeṇa kāraṇamanumīyate), sāmānyatodṛṣṭañca (yatra liṅgasya sāmānyāt....liṅgī gamyate), N. d.;
    (2) anumitiḥanumiti (rare). To draw an i.: v. To infer.
