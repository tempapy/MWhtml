• SPARE [664] (v.):
    I Not to throw away:
    (1) na kṣipati, vi-, vini-, pra- (kṣip, c. 1.);
    (2) saṃci- noti (ci, c. 5.): v. To save;
    (3) rakṣati, saṃ-, (rakṣ, c. 1. = to keep), shall s. money for calamities: āpadarthe dhanaṃ rakṣet, H.
    II To grant, give: q. v.: dadāti (dā, c. 3.). Ph.: s. him: mucyatām, Mr. x.: v. To release.
    III To give up: jahāti, vi-, (hā, c. 3.: v. To abandon).
    IV To omit, forbear: q. v.
  • SPARE [664] (adj.):
    I Superfluous:
    (1) udvṛtta (f. ttā); avyāpṛta (f. tā = unemployed).
    II Scanty, little: q. v.: alpa (f. lpā).
    III Lean, slender: q. v.: kṛśa (f. śā).
