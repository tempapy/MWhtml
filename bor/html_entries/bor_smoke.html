• SMOKE [654] (v. t.):
    I As tobacco:
    (1) pibati, (pāpāpa, c. 1.), s. ing a cigar: paripītadhūmavartiḥ, K.;
    (2) sevate, (sevsev, c. 1.: habitually), Bha.
    II To purify by s.: dhūme(pe)na śodhayati (c. of śudhśudh): v. To purify.
    III To dry by s.: dhūme(pe)na śoṣayati (c. of śuṣśuṣ): v. To dry.
  • SMOKE [654] (v. i.): dhūmā(pā)yate (nomi.), making the quarters s.: dhūmāyayan diśaḥ, Mah. iii. 38. 30.
  • SMOKE [654] (subs.):
    I Lit.: dhūmaḥ(paḥ), dark like s.: dhūmadhūmradhūmadhūmra (f. mrā), R. xv. 16.; forming clouds of s.: dhṛtadhūmamaṇḍalaḥ, Ki. xvi., 59.; where there is s., there is fire: yato dhūmastato vahniḥ.
    II Fig.: ‘ending in s.’: perh. śūnyagarbha (f. rbhā).
