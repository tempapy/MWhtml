  • MEET [442] (subs.): melā: v. Meeting.
  • MEET [442] (v. t.):
    I To come with: paśyatipaśyat (dṛśdṛś, c. l (= to see). To come or go out to m.:
    (1) pratyudgacchati (gamgamga, c. l.);
    (2) pratyudyāti (yāyāyaiī, c. 2.).
    II To encounter: q. v.: abhi- yātiyātiyāt, etc., to m. death: mṛtyumeti, Mah.
    III To m. together, assemble: expr. with: saṃgacchate (gamgamga, c. l.), sameti (iiīin, c. 2.), etc.: the lords of quarters have met: samāgatā lokapālāḥ, V. ; the ladies have met to remove the marriage- string: devyaḥ kaṅkaṇamokṣaṇāya militāḥ, Vi. ii. 50.
    IV In geo.: sampatati (patpatpathpad, c. l.).
