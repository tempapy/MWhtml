• COURTESY: [159]
    I Courteousness:
    (1) dākṣiṇyamdākṣiṇya, by her politeness, c., and affability: tasyā vinayena dākṣiṇyena madhurālāpitayā. K.;
    (2) vinayaḥvinaya;
    (3) praśrayaḥpraśrayapraśri, accepted with c.: sapraśrayaṃ pratijagrāha, K.;
    (4) saujanyamsaujanya. c., agreeable as free from arrogance: apagatābhimānamadhurā sujanatā. K.
    II An act of c.: upacāraḥupacāra, by c. s to the invited ladies: upacāreṇa nimantritastrīṣu, C.
    III A bow: praṇāmaḥpraṇāma, R. vi. 25.
