• BITTER [073] (adj.):
    I Of the taste: tiktaḥ (ktā, ktaṃ).
    II Harsh, stern:
    (1) kaṭu. (mfn.) even by the b. words of Śiśupāla kaṭunāpi caidyavacanena. Si. xv. 40.;
    (2) marmāvidh (mfn.) (Lit. piercing the vital parts);
    (3) niṣṭhuraḥ (rā, raṃ) (prop= cruel.), we are very b. ly reviled ati niṣṭhuramupā- labdhāḥ smaḥ, U. iv.
    III Painful, severe: q. v.: udvejanakaraḥ (rī, raṃ): Ph. b. cold atyantaśītam.
    IV Mournful, distressing, pitiable: q. v.
