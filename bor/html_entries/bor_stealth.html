    I Stealing:
    (1) steyamsteya or stainyam;
    (2) caurīcaurī (or rikā: rare);
    (3) moṣaḥmoṣa.

    (1) caurikācaurikā, marriage by s.: caurikā- vivāhaḥ, Ma. i.;
    (2) moṣaḥmoṣa, carrying off Kanaka- vatī by s.: kanakavatīṃ moṣeṇāpahṛtya, D. vi.
