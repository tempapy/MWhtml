    (2) prāṇāḥprāṇa (m. pl.), takes one's l.: prāṇān harati, A r.; my l. is moving (i. e. I am dying): pracalanti hi me prāṇāḥ, Vi.; I will give you my l.: prāṇān te dāsyāmi, A. r.;
    (3) asavaḥasu, (m. pl.), why my l. did not go out at the time: asavo me tatkṣaṇe kena hetunā nodgacchanti sma, K. II. Life- -time:
    (1) jīvanamjīvana or jīvitam, the l. of animals consists of four or five days: jīvitaṃ janmavatāṃ catuḥpañcānyahāni, D.; throughout one's l.: yāvajjīvam. H. s.;
    (2) āyusāyusāyu (n.), having long l.: dīrghāyus (mfn.), Sr.
    III Fig.:
    (1) jīvanamjīvana or jīvitam, you are (my) l.: tvaṃ jīvitam, U. iii. 26.;
    (2) prāṇāḥprāṇa, D.
    IV. Conduct: q. v.: vṛttamvṛtta or vṛttiḥ.
    V. Vivacity, energy: q. v.: perh. prāṇāḥprāṇa.
