• PLAIN [524] (subs.):
    (1) samabhūmiḥ;
    (2) samasthalam, -lī.
  • PLAIN [524] (adj.):
    I Smooth, level: q. v.: sama (f. mā).
    II Clear, evident: q. v.: spaṣṭa (f. ṣṭā), in p.: spaṣṭam.
    III Open, public: q. v.: prakāśa (f. śā).
    IV Frank, sincere: q. v.: sphuṭamāṣin (f. ṇī = p. spoken).
    V Homely, simple:
    (1) sahaja (f. jā = natural, as of p. tune);
    (2) sāmānya (f. nyā = common, as of p. dress);
    (3) asaṃskṛta (f. tā = unadorned, poor: q. v.).
