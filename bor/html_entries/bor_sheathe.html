• SHEATHE [635] (v.):
    I To put in a case: koṣekoṣakoṣā (śeśaśi) nidadhāti (dhādhādha, c. 3.), niveśayati (c. of viśviś), arpayati (c. of ṛṛ), etc., Mah. iv. 42. 13. Ph.: to s. the sword (fig.): khaṅgaṃ samāpayati, (after paraśuṃ samāpayati, Vi. iii.).
    II To be busy, thrust deeply: nidadhāti.
