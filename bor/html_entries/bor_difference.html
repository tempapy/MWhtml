    I Lit.:
    (1) bhedaḥbheda, pra-, vi-, and (yet) did not find any d. among the five: bhedaṃ viveda na ca pañcasu kañcideṣā, N. xiii. 41.; from the d. of the results and subjects of enquiry: phalajijñāsyabhedāt, S.;
    (2) viśeṣaḥviśeṣa (=distinction: q. v.);
    (3) tāratamyamtāratamya (=d. in quality), O, what a d. in men: aho tāratamyaṃ narāṇām, Mr. x.;
    (4) antaramantaramantara (=distance: to contrast two objects), the d. between you and me is like the d. between the ocean and the pond: atramavataḥ kila mama ca samudra- palvalayorivāntaram, Mal. Ph.: it makes no d.: atra nāsti viśeṣaḥ.
    II Discordance: q. v.: viprati- pattiḥpatti, S.
    III Dissension: vivādaḥvivāda.
    IV In arith.: antaramantaramantara, sum and d.: yogāntare, Vij.
