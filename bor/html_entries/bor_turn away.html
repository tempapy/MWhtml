• TURN AWAY: [724]
    I To remove: q. v.: apanayati (nī, c. 1.).
    II Drive out, dispel: q. v.: dūrīkaroti.
    III Intrans.: parāṃmukhībhavati, vimukhī- bhavati; etc., (although) she generally t. s away from me: kāmaṃ na tiṣṭhati madānanasammukhīyam. Sa. i. 32; when I look she t. s away: abhimukhe mayi saṃhṛtamīkṣitam, Sa. ii. 12.
