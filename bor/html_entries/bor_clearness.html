• CLEARNESS: [124]
    I Trasparency, brightness:
    (1) prasādaḥprasāda or prasannatā;
    (2) vaimalyamvaimalya;
    (3) vaiśadyamvaiśadya.
    II Plainness, perspicuity:
    (1) vaiśadyamvaiśadya, impressive on account of c.: vaiśadyahṛdyaḥ (dyādyadī, dyaṃ), N.;
    (2) vaimalyamvaimalya, perspicuity consists in c. of meaning: arthavaimalyātmā prasādaḥ, Kav.;
    (3) nirma- latvam, S. Ph.: with c. of voice: spaṣṭavarṇayā girā, K.
