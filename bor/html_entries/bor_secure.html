• SECURE [624] (adj.):
    I Careless: q. v.
    II Safe: q. v.:
    (1) vītabhaya (f. yā);
    (2) nirviśaṅka (f. ṅkā);
    (3) sthira (f. rā = firm), fancying very s.: susthiraṃmanyaḥ, Si. ii. 32.
    II Certain: q. v.: niścita (f. tā).
  • SECURE [624] (v.):
    I In gen.:
    (1) rakṣati (rakṣ, c. 1.): v. Protect;
    (2) sthirīkaroti (= to make firm or give).
    II To insure: dhruvam or niścitam āvahati (vah, c. 1.).
    III To make fast, seize: badhnāti (bandh, c. 9.).
