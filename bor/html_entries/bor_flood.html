• FLOOD [284] (subs.):
    I A flow, f. of water: jalaughaḥ, Ki. or jalasaṃghātaḥ, Ku.
    II Deluge:
    (1) āplāvaāplāva;
    (2) saṃplavaḥ, universal f.: ābhūtasaṃplavaḥ, Mat.;
    (3) vanyāvanyāvanyavanivanī or vānam (= high f.). Ph.: all was a f. of water: sarvamāmbhasamevāsīt, Mah. or etadekārṇavaṃ sarvam, Mat.
    III Of tide: v. Tide.
    IV Abun- -dance: q.v.: prācuryamprācurya. V. A stream: pravāhaḥpravāhapravāh, f. of tears: aśrupravāhaḥ.
