• WRITE: [769] likhati (likhlikh, c. 6.), to w. with one's own hand: svahastena likhati, Y. ii. 89.; I saw this couplet written on it: tasyāmimāmamilikhitāmāryā- mapaśyam, K.; he has written this back: idamanena pratilikhitam, Mal. i.; getting a letter written by Śakaṭadāsa in these words: ebhirakṣarairlekhaṃ śakaṭa- dāsena lekhayitvā, Mu. i.; what is not written on our minds: asmākaṃ cetasi kiṃ nāma na likhitamasti, Ana. vii.: v. Also to compose.
