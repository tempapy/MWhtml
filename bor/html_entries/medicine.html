• MEDICINE: [441]
    I A medical remedy:
    (1) auṣadham (lit. and fig.), m. is good for the sick: vyādhita- syauṣadhaṃ pathyam, H. ; in all distress there is no m. like wife: na ca bhāryāsamaṃ kiñcidauṣadhaṃ sarvakṛcchreṣu, Mah.;
    (2) bheṣajam or bhaiṣajyam, this is your m. (antidote): bheṣajamidaṃ te, P. iii. 10.; as in very strong m.s: ativīryavatīva bheṣaje, Ki. ii. 4.;
    (3) agadaḥ (rare), is not m. drunk: agadaḥ kiṃ na pīyate, H.
    II. The art or theory:
    (1) āyu- rvedaḥ (= Hindu m.), teach us m.: āyurvedamasmāna- dhyāpayatu, Bha.;
    (2) cikitsā, well up in m. in its eight branches: cikitsāyāmaṣṭāṅgāyāṃ viśāradāḥ, Mah.;
    (3) vaidyakam, A. p.
