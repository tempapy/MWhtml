• MIDDLE [446] (adj.):
    (1) madhyaḥmadhya (dhyādhyādhidhī, dhyaṃ), the m. men leave off, beset with obstacles: vighnanihatā viramanti madhyāḥ, Mu.;
    (2) madhyamaḥmadhyama (māmāma, maṃ), following a m. course: puraskṛtamadhyamakramaḥ, R. ; the m. world (i. e. the earth): madhyamalokaḥmadhyamaloka, Vi.;
    (3) madhyasthaḥmadhyastha (sthāsthāstha, sthaṃ) (= on the m.);
    (4) madhyamīyaḥmadhyamīya (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) (rare). The m. age: *madhya- yugam or madhyakālaḥ.
  • MIDDLE [446] (subs.):
    (1) madhyammadhyammadhya, the m. of the sky: vyomamadhyam, V. ; in the m. of the ocean: madhye- samudram, Si.; in the m. of the army: sainyasya madhye, K. ; making the m. (= waist) slender: madhyaṃ tanūkṛtya, N. vii. 81.;
    (2) madhyabhāgaḥmadhyabhāga, and there in the m.: tatra ca madhyamāge, K.
