• DISTRESS [212] (subs.):
    I Misery, affliction:
    (1) duḥkham, seeing no end of (my) d.: duḥkhasyāntamapaśyantī, Mat.;
    (2) kaṣṭam;

    IV Distraining: *sampīḍanagrahaṇam.
