• MORTAL [457] (subs.): martyaḥ: v. Man.
  • MORTAL [458] (adj.):
    I Subject to death:
    (1) mṛtyuvaśaḥ (śāśa, śaṃ) and sim. comps.;
    (2) by circumlo., every one is m., all are m.: sarveṇa khalu martavyam, sarvānāviśate mṛtyuḥ, Mah. s.
    II Causing death:
    (1) prāṇaharaḥprāṇahara (rīrīri, raṃ);
    (2) sāṅghātikaḥ (kīki, kaṃ), etc.
    III Human: q. v.: martya- in comp.
