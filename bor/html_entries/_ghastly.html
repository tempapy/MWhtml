• GHASTLY: [311]
    I Lit.: bhūtasarūpaḥ (pā, paṃ); śavasyeva (= as of a corpse), Mah.
    II Pale, dismal: vivarṇa (f. rṇā), these (ghosts) with long g. bodies: ete vivarṇadirghadehāḥ, Ma. v. 15.
    III Horrible, dreadful:
    (1) karālaḥ (lā, laṃ);
    (2) vikaṭaḥ (ṭā, ṭaṃ).
