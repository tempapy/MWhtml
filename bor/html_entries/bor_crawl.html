• CRAWL [162] (v.):
    I Of men:
    (1) riṅgati (riṅgriṅg, c. l.), they then used to c. about in the cowpen: riṅgitau tau tadā vraje, V. p.;
    (2) jānubhyāṃ raṃhati (raṃhraṃh, c. l.), S.;
    (3) jānubhyāṃ karābhyāṃ ca bhuvaṃ saṃghṛṣya sañcarati (carcar, c. l.), V. p. com.
    II Of animals: sarpatisarpat, pra-, (sṛpsṛpsṛbh, c. l.). C. up to: upasarpati.
  • CRAWL,-ING [162] (subs.):
    (1) riṅganam (of boys etc.);
    (2) sarpaṇamsarpaṇa (of reptiles).
