• SCALE [616] (subs.):
    I In pl.- a balance: q. v.: tulā: the fulcrum: dhaṭaḥ, Mit.
    II Its dish:
    (1) śikyamśikya (= loop), to put in the s.: śikyam āropayati, V. s.;
    (2) pārśvaḥpārśva (= side), Mit.
    III In astro.: tulātulātula,
    IV Of fishes:
    (1) śalkamśalka, -lam;
    (2) valkamvalkavalk, -lam.
    V Of serpents: maṇḍalammaṇḍala (= circular spot), Sr.
    VI Of metal etc: patram (= leaf); phalakamphalaka (= plate).
    VII A ladder: q. v.: sopānamsopāna.
    VIII In music: grāmaḥgrāma, Si. i. 10.
    IX A basis:
    (1) Often by mūlammūla (= root);
    (2) parimāṇamparimāṇa or pramāṇam (= mea- -sure). Ph.: on a small s.: alpaparimāṇam.
  • SCALE [616] (v.):
    I To climb. q. v.: ārohati (ruharuha, c. 1.).
    II To cross by s. ing: laṅghayati, ut-, vi-, abhi- (laṅghlaṅgh, c. 10.), D. iii.
    III To take off s.s: va(śa)lkayati (nomi.); viva(śa)lkī- karoti; etc.
