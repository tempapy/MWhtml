• DULL [224] (adj.):
    I Of intellect:
    (1) mandaḥmandamand (ndā, ndaṃ);
    (2) mūḍhamūḍha (ḍhāḍha, ḍhaṃ) (= stupid).
    II Of colours, etc.:
    (1) mandaḥmandamand (ndā, ndaṃ); when the d. -lighted (moon) was about to set: athāstamāseduṣi mandakāntau, B.;
    (2) malinaḥmalinamali (nānāna, naṃ) (= dark).
    III Blunt: q. v.: nistejasnistejas (mfn.).
    IV Sad, melancholy: q. v.: viṣaṇṇaviṣaṇṇa (f. ṇṇā).
    V Flat, insipid: q. v.: arasaḥarasa (sāsāsa, saṃ).
    VI Heavy, insensible: q. v.
    VII Cloudy, dark: q. v.
  • DULL [224] (v.):
    I To stupify: jaḍīkaroti.
