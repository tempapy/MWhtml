• PALE [500] (adj.):
    I Dusky white:
    (1) pāṇḍupāṇḍu (mfn.);
    (2) pāṇḍuraḥpāṇḍura (rārāra, raṃ);
    (3) hariṇaḥhariṇahariharin (ṇī, ṇaṃ), “vivarṇo hariṇaḥ kṛśaḥ”, Mah. 137.
    II Not ruddy, wan: vivarṇavivarṇa (f. rṇā), p. with lustre gone: vivarṇā vigataśrīkā, Mah. i. 8. 18.; those kings grew p.: prapede vivarṇabhāvaṃ sa sa bhūmipālaḥ, R. vi. 67.
    III Dim, not bright: mandaprabhaḥ (bhābhābha, bhaṃ) and sim. comp.s., then when the p. moon: nidhau kalānāmatha mandakāntau, B. xi. 1.
    IV Light (of colours): prob. pāṇḍura- in comp.: v. Also slight.
  • PALE [500] (subs.):
    I A stake: śalākāśalākāśalāka.
    II Limit, fence: q. v.: sībhā.
  • PALE [500] (v.):
    I To enclose: q. v.: parivṛṇoti (vṛvṛ, c. 5.).
    II To check, stop: q. v.: avarūṇaddhi (rūdh, c. 7.).
    III To turn p.: vivarṇībhavati.
