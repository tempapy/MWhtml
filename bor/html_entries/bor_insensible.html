• INSENSIBLE: [383]
    I Imperceptible: q.v.: dulakṣya (f. kṣyākṣikṣī).
    II Not conscious:
    (1) acetanaḥacetana (nānāna, naṃ), vicetanaḥvicetana (nānāna, naṃ), vi-gatacetanaḥ (nānāna, naṃ), etc.;
    (2) visaṃjñavisaṃjña (f. jñājñājña).
    III Benumbed: jaḍaḥjaḍa (ḍāḍāḍa, ḍaṃ).
    IV To emotion:
    (1) by upekṣate (īkṣīkṣ, c. 1.): v. Indifferent;
    (2) na cetayati, -te (citcitcid, c. 10.) (with acc.): v. Also hard cruel.
