• ATTENTION: [047]
    I The act or state of attending:
    (1) avadhānam, please lend me your a. for a moment māṃ muhūrtamavadhānadānenānugṛhvantu, Ma. ix.;
    (2) manoyogaḥ;
    (3) apramādaḥ or apramattatā (=carefulness);
    (4) abhiniveśaḥ (=application, study);
    (5) avekṣā (=looking after). Ph.: his whole a. being devoted to it ananyahṛdayatayā, K.; it engages my a. mano me badhnāti, (after R. xiii. 47.); to draw the a. of the king narapatiprabodhanārtham, K.
    II. Politeness, courtesy: q. v. ādaraḥ or samādaraḥ.
  • ATTENTION, PAY: [047]
    (1) avadhānaṃ dadāti (dā, c. 3.), paying a. to the buzzing of bees dattāvadhānaṃ madhulehi- gītau B. ii. 7.;
    (2) ādriyate (dṛ, c. 6.) (=to respect), the king without paying any a. (to what he said) rājā anādṛtya, Sa. vi.:
    (3) āsajyate (=to devote to: q. v.): v. Attend to.
