• TAME [700] (adj.):
    I Of animals:
    (1) gṛhya (f. hyā = domestic: q. v. );
    (2) grāmya (f. myā = village: q. v. );
    (3) vinīta (f. tā).
    II Spiritless: nistejas (mfn.), and sim. comp.s. : v. Also dull.
  • TAME [700] (v.):
    (1) damayati (c. of dam), t.ing all: sarvadamana (f. nī), Sa. vii.;
    (2) vinayati (nī, c. 1.), as if to t. the wild beasts: vanyān vineṣyanniva duṣṭasattvān, R. ii. 8.
