    I To attack: q. v.: abhiyunakti (yujyuj, c. 7.).
    II To accuse: q. v.: abhiyunakti.
    III To load: q. v. Ph.: to c. a gun *vandukaṃ pūrayati.
    IV To command or exhort: q. v.: śāstiśāsti (śāsśāsśaśas, c. 2.). V. To regard as a debt (?): yācate (yācyāc, c. l.) (=ask), to c. interest: vṛddhiṃ yācate; to c. for delivery: samarpaṇavyayaṃ yācate (?).

    I Attack: q. v.: abhiyogaḥabhiyoga
    II Accusation: q. v.: abhiyogaḥabhiyoga.
    III Burden, load: q. v.
    IV Injunction: q. v.
    V A trust: q. v.: nikṣepaḥnikṣepa.
    VI Care, management: q. v.: rakṣārakṣārakṣarakṣ or rakṣaṇaṃ, to give in c.: rakṣaṇārthaṃ dadāti.
