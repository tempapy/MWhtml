• LONE, LONELY: [422]
    I Solitary:
    (1) vivikta (f. ktā), l. place giving content to soul: citta- nirvṛtividhāyi viviktam, Ki. ix. 71.;
    (2) nirjanaḥ (nā, naṃ) (= without any man).
    II Alone:
    (1) expr. with ekākin (f. nī);
    (2) anāthaḥ (thā, thaṃ) (= helpless).
