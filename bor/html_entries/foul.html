• FOUL [295] (v.t.): kaluṣayati (nomi.): v. To befoul.
  • FOUL [295] (adj.):
    I Dirty: q.v.:
    (1) malapūrṇa (f. rṇā) (= full of dirt);
    (2) aśuci (mfn.), amedhya (f. dhyā), etc. (= impure), in a ditch full of f. (matter): aśucisaṃpūrṇe khātake, K.s. xiii. 148.
    II Obscene, abusive: q.v.: aślīlaḥ (lā, laṃ).
    III Abominable, detestable: q.v.: malinaḥ (nā, naṃ), K.
    IV Unfair, fraudulent: q.v.: kūṭa- in comp., f. play: kūṭavyavahāraḥ.
    V Unfavour- -able: q.v. Ph.: f. weather: durdinam; fall f. of: upari patati.
