• LONE, LONELY: [422]
    I Solitary:
    (1) viviktavivikta (f. ktā), l. place giving content to soul: citta- nirvṛtividhāyi viviktam, Ki. ix. 71.;
    (2) nirjanaḥnirjana (nānāna, naṃ) (= without any man).
    II Alone:
    (1) expr. with ekākinekākin (f. nīnīni);
    (2) anāthaḥanātha (thātha, thaṃ) (= helpless).
