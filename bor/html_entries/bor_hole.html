• HOLE: [348]
    I A pierce:
    (1) gartaḥgarta (spec. of h.s in earth: not fig.);
    (2) chidramchidra, making a h. on a side: pārśve chidraṃ vidhāya, Bha.;
    (3) rangharam, dexterous in picking h. s: ranghrānveṣaṇadakṣaḥ, R.;
    (4) vivaramvivara (rare), in other's h. s.: parasya vivare, Ki.
    II A cave, den: q.v.: vivaramvivara.
    III A hovel: perh. vivaramvivara.
