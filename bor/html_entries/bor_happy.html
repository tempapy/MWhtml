• HAPPY: [334]
    I Fortunate; q.v.: dhanyaḥdhanya (nyānyaninī, nyaṃ).
    II Good: q.v.:
    (1) praśastaḥpraśasta (stā, staṃ) (= commendable);
    (2) śreyam (f. sīsīsi) (rare), of this speech although h.: śreyaso'pyastha vacasaḥ, Ki. xi. 44.
    III Delighted: q.v.:
    (1) sukhinsukhin (f. nīnīni), be delightedly h.: modamānaḥ sukhī bhava, Mah.;
    (2) santuṣṭaḥ (ṣṭā, ṣṭaṃ) (= content).
    IV Able: q.v.: paṭupaṭu (f. also ṭvī).
