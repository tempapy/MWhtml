• CARELESS: [104]
    I Inattentive, neglectful: q. v.: pramattaḥpramatta (ttātta, ttaṃ). To be c. of: na cintayati (cintcint, c. 10) or vimāvayati (c. of bhūbhūbhu) (with acc.), is c. even of herself ātmānamapi na vibhāvayati.
    II Said or done without care: pramatta- in comp.
    III Free from care: niścintaḥniścinta (ntā, ntaṃ).
