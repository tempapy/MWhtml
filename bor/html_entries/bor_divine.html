• DIVINE [213] (adj.):
    (1) divyaḥdivyadivī (vyāvyavivī, vyaṃ) (best equiv.), d. hide: divyamajinam, S. p.; d. bed: śayanaṃ divyam, Mah.;
    (2) daivaḥdaiva (vīvīvi, vaṃ), d. language: daivī vāka, U.;
    (3) by comp, d. intellect: devadhīḥ, B.;
    (4) amānuṣaḥamānuṣa (ṣī, ṣaṃ) (= not human); apārthivaḥapārthiva (vīvīvi, vaṃ) (not earthly), R.
  • DIVINE [213] (subs.): vedavidvedavid (mfn.): v. Theologian.
  • DIVINE [213] (v.):
    I To foresee: q. v.: paśyatipaśyat (dṛśdṛś, c. 1.) (= to see) or jānāti (jñājñājña, c. 7.) (= to know) (the context determining the exact sense).
    II To guess: ūhate (uhūuhū, c. 1.).
    III To practise divination: anāgataṃ or bhavi- ṣyantaṃ vakti (vacvac, c. 1.) or vadati (vadvad, c. 1.): v. Also augury.
