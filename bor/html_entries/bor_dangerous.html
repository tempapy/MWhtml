• DANGEROUS: [173]
    I Beset with danger:
    (1) pratyavāyamūyiṣṭhaḥ (ṣṭhā, ṣṭhaṃ);
    (2) saṃkaṭaḥ (ṭā, ṭaṃ);
    (3) sambādhaḥ (dhā, dhaṃ), in a place not d. and pleasant: asambādhaśive deśe, Ki.
    II Causing danger: bhayāvahaḥ (hā, haṃ): v. Dreadful, danger. Ph.: d. illness: sāṃghātikarogaḥ.
