• DANGEROUS: [173]
    I Beset with danger:
    (1) pratyavāyamūyiṣṭhaḥ (ṣṭhāṣṭhā, ṣṭhaṃ);
    (2) saṃkaṭaḥsaṃkaṭa (ṭāṭāṭa, ṭaṃ);
    (3) sambādhaḥsambādhasambādh (dhādhādha, dhaṃ), in a place not d. and pleasant: asambādhaśive deśe, Ki.
    II Causing danger: bhayāvahaḥbhayāvaha (hāhāha, haṃ): v. Dreadful, danger. Ph.: d. illness: sāṃghātikarogaḥ.
