• OWN [498] (adj.):
    (1) svaḥsvaḥsvasūsvar (svāsvāsvasusū, svaṃ), by giving my o. life: svadehārpaṇena, R.; towards his o. daughter: svaduhitari, D.; your o. land: svabhūmiḥ, K.;
    (2) svakīyaḥsvakīya or svīyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) (= one's o.), if you want your o. life: yadi svakīyajīvanamicchasi, Vet.;
    (3) nijaḥnijanij (jājāja, jaṃ), to his o. preceptor: nijāya gurave, Ana.;
    (4) svakaḥsvaka (kākāka, kaṃ), went to their o. houses: svakaṃ svakaṃ niveśanaṃ pratyudgatāḥ, Kar.;
    (5) ātmīyaḥātmīya (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) (= one's o.), make it your o.: ayamātmīyaḥ kriyatām, K. N. B. Note that possessive pronouns are not expr. in Sanskrit.
