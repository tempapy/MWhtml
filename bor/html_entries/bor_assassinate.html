• ASSASSINATE: [043]
    (1) ghātayati (c. of hanhan), or mārayati (c. of mṛmṛ), or vadhaṃ or hatyāṃ karoti (with gen.) (=to kill, murder);
    (2) upāṃśuupāṃśu or nibhṛtaṃ or gopanena vadhaṃ karoti etc. (=to kill by secret assault);
    (3) avaskandya vadhaṃ karoti or ghātayati (=to kill by surprise).
