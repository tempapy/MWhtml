• GRACE [320] (subs.):
    I Favour: q.v.: prasādaḥprasāda, she obtaining the g. of the gods: prasādamāsādya suraiḥ kṛtaṃ sā, N. xiv. 8.
    II Beauty: q.v.: lāvaṇyamlāvaṇya.
    III Good quality: guṇaḥguṇa.
    IV Ornament: q.v.: bhūṣaṇambhūṣaṇa, these various (actions) become their g.s.: iti vividhamiyāya tāsu bhūṣām, Ki. x. 59.
    V As a title: devaḥdeva (for dukes), bhagavatbhagavatbhagavad (m.) (for archbishops).
    VI A goddess: apsarasapsaras (f.).
    VII A prayer before or after meal: to say g.: *īśvarasya prasādaṃ kīrtayati or prasādanām ādriyate (dṛdṛ, c. 4.), N. xiv. 1.
  • GRACE [320] (v.):
    I To adorn, decorate: q.v.: bhūṣayati, chaste knowledge graces flesh: śuci bhūṣayati śrutaṃ vapuḥ, Ki. ii. 32.
    II To honour: q.v.: sammānayati (c. of manmanmat).
