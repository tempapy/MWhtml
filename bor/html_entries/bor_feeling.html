• FEELING [272] (subs.):
    I The sense: sparśaḥsparśasparś.
    II Perception:
    (1) anubhavaḥanubhava, (rarely) anubhūtiḥanubhūti; upalabdhiḥ; (rarely),
    (2) upalammaḥ. (actual f.), from the f. of pleasure: sukhopalambhāt, R.;
    (3) saṃvedaḥsaṃveda, -nam (= knowledge), only for f. pain: duḥkhasaṃvedanāyaiva, U. i. 47.
    III Susceptibility to emotions: expr. by hṛdayamhṛdaya (= heart), a man of f.: sahṛdayaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ), destitute of f.: niṣkaruṇa (f. ṇāṇa): v. Hard.
    IV Any state of mind:
    (1) bhāvaḥbhāva;
    (2) vṛttiḥvṛtti. Ph.: f. of pride: abhimānitā.
