• NEARLY [468] (adj.):
    I Within a little:
    (1) prāyaḥprāyasprāya (= almost: q. v.), n. dead: mṛtaprāyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ);
    (2) kiñcidūnam (= little less), n. ten thousand winters: kiñcidūnaṃ śaradāmayutam, R. x. 1.
    II Closely: expr. with adj.: v. Near (VI)
    III Pressingly: bhṛśambhṛśambhṛśabhṛś.
