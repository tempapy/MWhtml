• DOMESTIC: [216]
    I Belonging to a house:
    (1) by comp., d. affluence: gṛhabhūtiḥ, Mu.;
    (2) gṛhyaḥgṛhya (hyāhihī, hyaṃ), d. fire: gṛhyo vahniḥ, Vi.;
    (3) gṛhajaḥgṛhaja (jājāja, jaṃ); gṛhotpannaḥ (nnā, nnaṃ) (born in the home), d. bullocks: gṛhotpannau vṛṣabhau, P.
    II Not wild:
    (1) grāmyagrāmya (f. myāmimī);
    (2) grāma- in comp., d. fowl: grāmakukkuṭaḥ, M.
    III Intestine: q. v.: antara- in comp.
    IV Fond of home: gṛhapriyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ).
