• ILL: [360]
    I Bad: q.v.: expr. by dur- or a- in comp., i. health: asusthatā.
    II In ill health:
    (1) asusthaśarīraḥ (rārāra, raṃ), Kādambari, who is very i.: balavadasusthaśarīrā kādabhbarī, K.;
    (2) asusthaasustha or asvastha (f. sthāsthāstha);
    (3) pīḍitaḥpīḍita, i. with fever: jvarapīḍitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ): v. Also sick.
  • ILL [360] (subs.):
    (1) aniṣṭamaniṣṭa;
    (2) aśubhabh, amaṅgalam, etc., crying i. of the boy: vatsasya maṅgalaviruddhaṃ raṭan, Vi. iii. 31.;
    (3) pāpampāpampāpa, to wish i.: pāpaṃ vāñchati, Vi. ii. 50.: v. Also calamity.
  • ILL [360] (adv.): expr. by adj. or subs: v. Also badly.
