• ARREST [038] (v. t.):
    I To obstruct, stop: q. v.: rūṇaddhi (rudhrudh, c. 7).
    II To apprehend legally:
    (1) āsedhati (sidha, c. 1);
    (2) dharati or dhārayati (dhṛdhṛ, c. 1 and 10):
    (3) gṛhvāti (grahgrah, c. 9).
    III To fix, engage: Ph. (it) a. s my eyes badhnāti me cakṣuḥ; R.; it a. s my attention māmananya- manaskaṃ karoti.
