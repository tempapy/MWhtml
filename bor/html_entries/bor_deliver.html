• DELIVER: [185]
    I To release, rescue: q. v.: mocayati (c. of mucmuc) or mokṣayati (mokṣmokṣ, c. 10.), to d. my friend: suhṛdaḥ parimokṣaṇāya, Mr. iv.
    II To make over: arpayati, sam-, (c. of ṛṛ), d. ed two letters: lekhadvitayamarpayāmbabhūva, K. ; d. ing Patralekhā to Meghanāda: patralekhāṃ meghanādāya samarpya, K. : v. To give. Ph.: d. (this) back to him: [idaṃ] tasmai pratiniryātaya, Mr. iv.: v. To give back.
    III To utter, pronounce: q. v.:
    (1) udīrayati (īrīr, c. 10.);
    (2) kathayati (kathkath, c. 10.) (to tell), d. Kādambari's message: kathaya kādambaryāḥ sandeśam, K.;
    (3) āvedayati, ni-, (c. of vidvid) (=to d. respect- -fully), d. ed the return message: pratisandeśa- māvedayat, D. i.
    IV To discharge (of missi- -les): q. v.: muñcati (mucmuc c. 6.).
    V In child- -birth: garbhaṃ mocayati, P. A woman who has recently d. ed: navaprasūtā: v. To bring forth.
