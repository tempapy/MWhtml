• FAITHFUL: [264]
    I Full of faith:
    (1) śraddhāluśraddhālu (mfn.);
    (2) śraddhāmayaḥśraddhāmaya (yī, yaṃ).
    II Loyal: bhaktaḥbhakta (ktā, ktaṃ). Ph.: a f. wife: pativratā nārī; a f. husband: patnīrataḥ pumān (?).
    III Not breaking promises: satyasandhaḥ (ndhā, ndhaṃ); satyapratijhaḥ (jhājhājha, jhaṃ), etc.
    IV Worthy of belief: viśvasanīyaḥviśvasanīya (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) or viśvāsya (f. syāsyasisī).
    V True: yathārthaḥyathārtha (rthā, rthaṃ).
