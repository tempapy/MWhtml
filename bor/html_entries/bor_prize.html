• PRIZE [545] (subs.):
    I Of honour:
    (1) pāritoṣikam (= reward);
    (2) better by circumlo., if a p. is offered and won: yadi bhavān bhadrakamupanyasyati tadā bhavate mayaitāvaddeyamiti paṇitaṃ tatropanyāsaṃ nistīrya labhate; so a p. won in a literary contest: tathā śāstravijñāna- vivāde nirjitya pallabdham, Da. 93.
    II Booty: expr. with: yuddhe prāpta (f. ptā), N. s. or -gṛhīta (f. tā).
  • PRIZE [545] (v.):
    I To estimate highly: bahu manyate (man, c. 4.).
    II To care: q. v.
