• DOUBLE [217] (adj.):
    I Two-fold:
    (1) dvi- in comp.;
    (2) dvikaḥdvika (kākāka, kaṃ).
    II As much again: dviguṇaḥdviguṇa (ṇāṇa, ṇaṃ), d. pay: dviguṇavetanam.
    III In pairs: dvandvībhūya.
    IV Deceitful, ambiguous; q. v.: perh. dvi- in comp., having a d. meaning: dvyarthaḥdvyartha (rthā, rthaṃ), Va.
  • DOUBLE [217] (subs.):
    I Twice as much: dviguṇamdviguṇa.
    II A trick: q. v.
    III Companion: q. v.
  • DOUBLE [217] (v.):
    I Lit.: dviguṇīkaroti.
    II To be d. of: dviguṇaḥdviguṇa (ṇāṇa, ṇaṃ).
    III To double up: dvibhaṅgiṃ karoti (?). Ph.: to d. a cape: *pralambaṃ laṅghayati.
  • DOUBLE [217] (v. i.):
    I Lit.: dviguṇībhavati.
    II To return: parāvartate (vṛtvṛtvṛdh, c. 1.).
    III To play false: chalanāṃ karoti.
