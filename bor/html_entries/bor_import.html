• IMPORT [365] (v.):
    I Of goods: videśāt or deśāntarāt ānayani (nī, c. 1.) or āharati (hṛ, c. 1.). I. ed from may be expr. also by: āgataḥ (tā, taṃ) (= come), i. from China: cīnāgataḥ (tā, taṃ).
    II To imply, mean: expr. by artha (= meaning) or amiprāyaḥ (= purport).
    III To concern:
    (1) avalambate (lamb,) c. 1.);
    (2) by gen.
  • IMPORT [365] (subs.):
    I In. pl. imported goods: videśīyapaṇyāni; videśāgatadravyāṇi. and sim. comp.s.
    II Purport, meaning: q.v.: bhāvaḥ.
    III Importance: q.v.
