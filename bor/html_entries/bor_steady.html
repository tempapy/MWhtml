• STEADY: [674]
    I In gen.:
    (1) sthirasthira (f. rārāra = cons- -tant, fixed), s. minded: sthiradhīḥ, R. viii. 22.; one whose actions are steady: sthirakarman (mfn.);
    (2) dṛḍhadṛḍha (f. ḍhāḍha = firm: q. v.).
    II Of men: dhīradhīra (f. rārāra). Ph.: to lead a s. life: na vyabhicarati, (carcar, c. 1.) or sanmārge tiṣṭhati (sthāsthāstha, c. 1.).
