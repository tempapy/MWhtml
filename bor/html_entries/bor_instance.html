• INSTANCE [384] (subs.):
    I Importance: gurutvamgurutva.
    II Solicitation: prarthanā.
    III Example: q.v.: udāharaṇamudāharaṇa, for i.: tathāhi; for i. Purūravas met destruction from a thirst after Brāhmin's money: tathāhi purūravā brāhmaṇadhanatṛṣṇayā vinanāśa, Va. As for i.: tadyathā; as for i., in the entirety of the family etc. one attributes to himself “I am entire”: tadyathā putramāryādiṣu sakaleṣvahameva sakala ityātmanyadhyasyati, S., Ph.: in the first i.: prathamataḥ; Visva Prakāśa in the three i. s.: triṣu viśvaprakāśaḥ, Va. com.
  • INSTANCE [385] (v.): udāgaraṇaṃ, nidarśanaṃ, or dṛṣṭāntaṃ dadāti (dādāda, c. 3.).
