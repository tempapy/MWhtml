• TELL [702] (v.t.):
    (1) kathayati (kathkath, c. 10.), learnt without being told: akathitaśikṣita (f. tātāta), K.; when I tell the history of (his) former life: janmāntaravṛttantaṃ mayi kathayati, K.; that (my) internal wish is told by the eyes: yaccakṣuṣaiva hṛdayābhilāṣa kathyate, K.; whom should I tell: kasya kathayāmi, K.;
    (2) āvedayati (c. of vidvid = inform, narrate), I will t. (you) everything from the beginning: āditaḥ prabhṛti sarvaṃ nivedayiṣyāmaḥ, K.: v. To say, relate.
  • TELL [703] (v. i.):
    I In good sense: expr. with guṇaḥguṇa (effect) or valaṃ (strength) dṛśyatedṛśyatā.
    II In bad sense with on: durbalīkaroti.
