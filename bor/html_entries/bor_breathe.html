• BREATHE [087] (v. i.):
    I To respire: śvasiti (śvasśvasśvaśū, c. l.). to b. the last prāṇān jahāti (hāhāha, c. 3.): v. To die.
    II To live: q. v.: śvasiti (I) who b. yaḥ śvasimi N. v. 109.
    III Pause, rest: q. v. to b. hard viniḥśvasiti N. xii. 2.; to b. freely ucchvasiti, Ki. ix. 21.
  • BREATHE [087] (v. t.):
    I Lit.: we b. the air *vāyuṃ gṛhṇīmaḥ (grahgrah, c. 9.): n. b. pibati (pāpāpa, c. l.) is used, but not in the exact sense.
    II To exhale: udgirati (gaga, c. 6.). b. ing the smell of parimalodgāribhiḥ, Me. i. 26.
    III To express, manifest: q. v.
    IV To whisper.
