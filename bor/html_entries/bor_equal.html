    I A like:
    (1) samaḥsamassama (māmāma, maṃ), I do not see any one greater than or e. to Rāma: paśyāmi rāmādadhikaṃ samaṃ vā nānyam, B.; e. share: samabhāgaḥsamabhāga, Da.;
    (2) samānaḥsamāna (nānāna, naṃ), e. in caste and number: samānā jātisaṃkhyayā, Da.; to (men) of e. valour, lineage, and manliness: samānavīryānvaya- pauruṣeṣu, Ki.;
    (3) tulyaḥtulya (lyālilī, lyaṃ) (= match- -ing, like), of e. strength: tulyaśkti (mfn.), M. n.;
    (4) sadṛśaḥsadṛśasadṛś (śīśīśi, śaṃ) (= tulyatulya), with those, who are not his e. s: anātmasadṛśeṣu, Mu.

    (1) samaḥsamassama (māmāma, maṃ);
    (2) paryāptaḥparyāpta (ptāptā, ptaṃ) (= enough: q. v.).
    III Equitable: samaḥsamassama (māmāma, maṃ).
    IV Indifferent: samaḥsamassama (māmāma, maṃ) or samānaḥ (nānāna, naṃ).
