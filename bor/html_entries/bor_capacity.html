• CAPACITY: [101]
    I Lit.:
    (1) dhāraṇaśaktiḥ;
    (2) grahaṇa- sāmarthyamsāmarthya;
    (3) better by circumlo.: v. Extent. Ph. a measure of c. pratīmānam, M.
    II. Mental c.: grahaṇasāmarthyam, c. for all knowledge aśeṣavidyā- grahaṇasāmarthyam, K.
    III. Ability: q. v.: śaktiḥśakti, to the extent of one's c. yathāśakti.
    IV Outward condition: rūpamrūparūp, in the c. of a cook sūdarūpeṇa or sūdo bhūtvā. V. Legal c.: kṣamatākṣamatā (?).
    VI In geo.: garbhaphalam, Li.
