• CERTAINLY: [111]
    I Assuredly:
    (1) avaśyamavaśyamavaśya (with- -out failure), what would c. take place avaśya- mbhāvin (f. nīnīni). H;
    (2) dhruvamdhruvamdhruva (=unalterably), would c. have victory dhruvaṃ jayāḥ (bhavantibhavantibhavantībhavat) (with gen.), Ka. xv. 12,
    (3) nūnamnūnam (=surely), c. he is not as his dress (i. e. as he looks) nūnaṃ tathā naiṣa yathāsya veṣaḥ, Ki.;
    (4) asaṃśayam: v. Undoub- -tedly;
    (5) niyatamniyatamniyataniyat (fixedly); would c. give permission niyatamanujñāsyati, D.;
    (6) niścitamniścitamniścita, N. com.
