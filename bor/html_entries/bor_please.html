• PLEASE [527] (v. t.):
    I To delight: q. v.: sukhayati, the same rays of the moon p. the body: pādāsta eva śaśinaḥ sukhayanti gātram, V. iii.
    II To satisfy: q. v.: prīṇāti or prīṇayati (prīprī, c. 9. and 10.) when Śiva will be p. d (with you): prīte pinākini, Ki. xi. 81. Ph.: to be p. d. to .: not expr., His Majesty Chandragupta is p. d to command: devaścandraguptaḥ samājñāpayati, Mu. iii.
  • PLEASE [527] (v. i.):
    (1) by passive imperative or occasionally by sim. imper, p. sit here: ita āsyatām, U. i.; p. take this seat: idamāsanam, upaviśatvāryaḥ, Mu. ii.;
    (2) by arhatiarhat (arhaarha, c. 1.), anugṛhṇāti (grahagraha, c. 9.), or prasīdati (sadsad, c. 1.), p. put on these ornaments: imānyābharaṇāni dhārayitumarhatyāryaḥ, Mu. ii.; p. promise: prasīda sandhehi, Ki. i. 42. Ph.: as you p.: yadabhirocate bhavate,
    V ii. (note also yadājñāpayati devaḥ, Mal. 1.).
