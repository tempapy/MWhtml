    I To press: aṅkayati (aṅk, c. 10.).
    II To implant: q.v.: nidadhāti (dhā, c. 3.).

    IV To take by force for public service:
    (1) dharati (dhṛ, c. 1.) (in Assamese);
    (2) balād gṛhṇāti (grah, c. 9.).
