• RUB [606] (v.):
    I In gen.: gharṣati, ut-, ni-, vini-, saṃ-, (ghṛṣ, c. 1.), r. bed diadems: cūḍāmaṇibhiru- dghṛṣṭa (f. ṣṭā), R. xvii. 28.; r. bing his ring against a bed of red arsenic: nighṛṣyāṅguliṃ manaḥśilā- girau, Ram. ii. 105. 17.
    II To besmear: q. v.: abhyanakti (añj, c. 7.).
    III To sham- -poo: q. v.: saṃvāhayati (c. of vah).
    IV To r. down, out, off, away, i. e. to cleanse, erase: q. v.: pra-mārṣṭi or mārjayati (mṛj, c. 2. and c. 10).
    V To r. off, i. e. wear away: q. v.: kṣīyate (pass. of kṣī).
  • RUB [606] (subs.):
    I The act;
    (1) gharṣaḥ, -ṇam, ni-, vini-, ut-, saṃ-;
    (2) saṃvāhanam;
    (3) abhyañjanam;
    (4) saṃmārjanam: for dif.: v. To rub.
    II Obs- -truction, difficulty: q. v.: antarāyaḥ.
