• FEMININE: [273]
    I As opposed to manly: straiṇastraiṇa (f. ṇī), hesitates from f. (consideration): straiṇena vicikitsati, Vi. i. 37.: v. Also effeminate.
    II In gram.: strīstrīstri, f. gender: strīliṅgam (the word); iṣudhiiṣudhi is also f.: iṣudhiḥ strī ca, S.k.; the five words ap etc. are f. and (used in the) plural: abādīnāṃ pañcānāṃ srītvaṃ bahutvaṃ ca, S.k.
