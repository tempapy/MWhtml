• HEIGHT [341] (subs.):
    I Lit.:
    (1) ucchāyaḥ (or rarely ucchritiḥucchriti), of great h.: mahocchrāyaḥ (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ); and whose h. is three cubits: trikarocchritiśca, Li.;
    (2) utsedhaḥutsedha, M. n.;
    (3) uccatāuccatā or rarely auccyam, Li.;
    (4) tuṅgatā.: v. Also loftiness, tallness.
    II Fig.: expr. by the above words: v. Lofti- -ness.
    III An elevated place:
    (1) uccasthalī;
    (2) unnatabhūmāgaḥ; etc.: v. Also summit.
    IV Utmost degree: expr. by adj.: in the h. of fever: prabale jvare; the h. of wisdom: paramaṃ jñānam: v. Extreme, acme.
