  • SETTLE [631] (v. t.):
    I To fix, establish: q. v.: sthāpayati, saṃ-, ava- (c. of sthāsthāstha).
    II To deter- -mine, decide: q. v.: vyavasthāpayati.
    III Of lands: vāsayati, adhi-, (c. of vasvasvaū).
    IV As an annuity: dadātidadāti (dādāda, c. 3. = to give).
    V To adjust, arrange: q. v.
    VI As disputes:
    (1) nivartayati (c. of vṛtvṛtvṛdh);
    (2) expr. with sandhiṃ karoti.
  • SETTLE [631] (v. i.):
    I To fall to the bottom: tale patati (patpatpathpad, c. 1.).
    II To become fixed:
    (1) sthirībhavati;
    (2) vyavatiṣṭhate (sthāsthāstha, c. 1.: rare).
    III To fix one's habitation:
    (1) nivāsaṃ sthirīkaroti;
    (2) saṃvasatisaṃvasati (vasvasvaū, c. 1.: rare).
    IV To sink, as a building: sīdati, ava-, vi-, (sadsad, c. 1.).
    V To come to an agree- -ment: sandhiṃ karoti, Mit.: to s. out of court: mithaḥ praśamaṃ yāti, Mit.
    VI To make a join- -ture: vṛttiṃ or saudāyikaṃ dadāti.
