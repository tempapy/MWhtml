• SINGING [646] (subs.):
    I Of men:
    (1) gītiḥgīti, -tam, adapted to s.: gītikṣama (f. māmāma), Sa. vii. 5.; time for s.: gītasamayaḥ, Mr. iii. 5.;
    (2) gānamgāna;
    (3) geyamgeya, who taught you s.: geye ko nu vinetā vām, R. xv. 69.
    II Of birds:
    (1) kūjitamkūjita or kūjanamkūjana;
    (2) rutamruta (= sound), Ki.
  • SINGING [646] (adj.):
    I Of men: gāyatgāyat (f. ntī), J.
    II Of birds: madhurasvaraḥmadhurasvara (rārāra, raṃ = sweet toned), and sim. comp. s.
