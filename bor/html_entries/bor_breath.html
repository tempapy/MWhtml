• BREATH: [087]
    I Lit.:
    (1) śvāsaḥśvāsa, to take b. śvasiti;
    (2) niḥśvāsaḥniḥśvāsa (=air exhaled), to hold b. niḥśvāsaṃ ruṇaddhi;
    (3) prāṇaḥprāṇa (=air inhaled), restraint of b. (a religious exercise) prāṇāyāmaḥprāṇāyāma; or prāṇa- saṃyamaḥ
    (4) śvasitamśvasita, out of b. muhurmuhuratyulvaṇaṃ niḥśvasiti, K.
    II. Life: prāṇāḥprāṇa (m. pl.).
    III Respite, pause: q. v. virāmaḥvirāma.
    IV An instant: nimeṣaḥnimeṣa, in a b. nimeṣeṇa. V. Breeze: q. v.
