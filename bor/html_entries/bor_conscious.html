• CONSCIOUS: [145]
    I Possessing c. ness: cetanaḥcetana or sacetanaḥsacetana (nānāna, naṃ), even by (those) who are not c.: vicetanairapi, Ku.
    II Aware, sensible: expr. by the verbs; jānāti (jñājñājña, c. 9.), vetti (vidvid, c. 2.), etc, (=to know: q. v.); though c. unable to control the organs: jānānāpyaprabhavantī karaṇānām, K.; which one can only be c. of: svasaṃvedyaḥ (dyādyadī, dyaṃ), K.
