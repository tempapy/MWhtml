• PLEASING [527] (adj.):
    I Gratifying:
    (1) by verb, it is not as p. that you are without your friend: na tathā nandayasi māṃ sakhyā virahitā tayā, V. ii. 13.;
    (2) sukhāvaha (f. hā);
    (3) sukhakara (f. rī); etc.
    II Charming, delightful: q. v.: ramya (f. myā), it is easy to be p. in this world: sulabhā ramyatā loke, Ki. xi. 11.; of p. deportment: ramyahāvāḥ, Ki. xi. 1. Ph.: p. to the ear: sukhaśrava (f. vā): śrutimadhura (f. rā) and sim. comp.s.
