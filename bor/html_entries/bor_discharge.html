• DISCHARGE [201] (v.):
    I Of burdens:
    (1) avatāra- yatiyatiyatinyat (c. of tṝtṝ);
    (2) avaropayati (c. of ruhruh).
    II Of rivers: to enter the sea:
    (1) viśati, pra-, (viśviś, c. 6.);
    (2) yātiyātiyāt (yāyāyaiī, c. 2.) (to go).
    III To utter, give vent to, emit: q. v.: udgirati (gṝgṝ, c. 6.), Ki.; to d. rage: krūdhaṃ visṛjati, Si.
    IV To let fly:
    (1) muñcati or mocayati, nir-, vi-, pra-, (mucmuc, c. 6. and 10.), d. ed a shower of arrows: śaravarṣaṃ mumoca, Ram.;
    (2) sṛjatisṛjati, vi-, (sṛjsṛj, c. 6.), to d. fire: agniṃ visṛjati, Sa.;
    (3) asyati (asasa, c. 4.).
    V To dismiss: q. v.: (karmaṇaḥ or adhikārāt) apakarṣati (kṛṣkṛṣ, c. 1.).
    VI To pay:
    (1) śodhayati, vi-, to d. a debt: ṛṇaṃ śodhayati, Vri.;
    (2) niryātayati (c. of yatyatyad), for d. ing a debt: ṛṇaniryātanāya, Viv.;
    (3) taratitarat, ā-, ut-, (tṝtṝ, c. 1.), N. s.
    VII To perform: q. v.: nirvāhayati (c. of vahvah). Ph.: you also d. your duty: tvamapi svaniyoga- maśūnyaṃ kuru, Sa. ii.
    VIII Of prisoners: muñcati.
    IX Of pus: pūyate (pūypūy, c. 1.).
