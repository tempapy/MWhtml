• LEARNING [412] (subs.):
    (1) vidyā, as a rule, men of equal l. cavil at each other's reputation: prāyaḥ samānavidyāḥ parasparayaśaḥpurobhāgāḥ, Mal i. 19.;
    (2) śikṣā, (rare), they may show their l.: te darśayeyuḥ svāṃ śikṣām, Mah. i. 132. 3.;
    (3) pāṇḍityam (= erudition);
    (4) vaiduṣyam (= 3.: rare);
    (5) abhijñatā (= knowingness): v. Proficient.
