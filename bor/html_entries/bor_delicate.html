• DELICATE: [184]

    II Pleasing to the senses: madhuraḥmadhura (rārāra, raṃ), d. meat: madhuraṃ māṃsam, Bha.
    III Fine, soft, feeble:
    (1) sukumāraḥsukumāra (rārāra, raṃ);
    (2) komalaḥkomala (lālāla, laṃ), with d. limbs: komalairaṅgaiḥ, S.
    IV Refined, polite: q. v.
    V Luxurious: q. v.
    VI Of subjects, difficult: viṣamaḥviṣama (māmāma, maṃ).
    VII Nice, critical: q. v.
