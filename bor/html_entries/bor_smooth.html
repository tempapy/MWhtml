• SMOOTH [654] (adj.):
    (1) sama (f. mā = equal, equable, level: q. v.), although the ground is s.: bhittau samāyāmapi, Sa. vi. 16.; naturally s., glossy, and black: nisargasamasnidhanīlaḥ, D. vi.;
    (2) ślakṣṇa (f. kṣṇā = glossy), quieting by s. words: śāntvayan ślakṣṇayā vācā, Mah. iii. 37. 56.: v. Mild, soft.
  • SMOOTH [654] (v.):
    (1) samīkaroti: v. To level; (S) ślakṣṇīkaroti (in other senses);
    (3) ślakṣṇayati (nomi.=2).
