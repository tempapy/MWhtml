• ALONE [023] (adj.)
    I Without anybody else: ekākinekākin (f. nīnīni), he could not remain a. even for a moment without him nimiṣamapi tena vinā sthātumekākī na śaśāka, K. v. Solitary.
    II One out of many: ekaḥeka (kākāka, kaṃ) (with evaeva or kevalam), let Bhāgu- rāyana a. accompany me bhāgurāyaṇa eka eva māmanugacchatu, Mu. iv.; let my intellect a. remain with me ekā kevalam.....buddhistu mā gānmama, Mu. i.
    III By itself: kevalaḥkevala (lālāla, laṃ), policy a. is cowardice kātaryaṃ kevalā nītiḥ R. xvii. 47. Ph. let imperiality be a. āstāñcakravartitā, D. viii.: v. To let alone.
