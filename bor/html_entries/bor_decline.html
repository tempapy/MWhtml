• DECLINE [179] (v. i.):
    I To bend over: lambate, ava-, (lamblamb, c. l.).
    II To decay, diminish: q. v.: hāsamupaiti (iiīin, c. 2.).
    III Of day, etc.:
    (1) pariṇamati (namnamna c. l.), by this time the day d. d.: anena samayena pariṇato divasaḥ, K. ;
    (2) galati (galgal, c. l.), the third watch d. ing: galati tṛtīye yāme, C. ;
    (3) kṣīyate, when the night d. d.: kṣīṇāyāṃ kṣapāyām, K.
    IV. To deviate: q. v.
    V. To refuse: q. v. pratyākhyāti (khyākhyā, c. 2).
  • DECLINE [179] (v. t.):
    I To refuse, reject: q. v.: pratyākhyāti.
    II To inflect: Ph.: d. patiḥ *pate rūpāṇyākhyāhi (khyākhyā, c. 2.).
    III To bend: q. v.
  • DECLINE [179] (subs.):
    (1) hrāsaḥhrāsa (=decrease), by the rise and d. of prices: arghasya vṛddhihrāsena, Vy.;
    (2) kṣayaḥkṣayaskṣayakṣi (=decay), d. of fame: yaśaḥkṣayaḥ, Ki.;
    (3) pariṇāmaḥpariṇāma, or pariṇatiḥ, (lit., bending: of life or time), d. of life: vayaḥpariṇāmaḥ, K. ; d. of night: rātreḥ pariṇatiḥ, Si.
