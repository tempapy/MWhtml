• HEALTHY: [339]
    I Of men:
    (1) susthaḥsustha (sthāsthāstha, sthaṃ);
    (2) arogaḥaroga (gāgāga, gaṃ), nirāmayaḥnirāmaya (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) and sim. comp.s. (= without disease);
    (3) kuśalinkuśalin (f. nīnīni) (= well: q.v.).
    II Of places:
    (1) kṣemyakṣemya (f. myāmimī), M. vii. 212.;
    (2) anāmayakṣamaḥ (māmāma, maṃ), K. b. and sim. comp.s.
    III Of food:
    (1) hitaḥhita (tātāta, taṃ) (= good: q.v.):
    (2) pathyapathya (f. thyā), Sr.: v. Wholesome.
