• SAVE [615] (v.):
    I In theological sense:
    (1) tārayati, nis, (c. of tṝtṝ), instantly s. ed Yayāti: yayātiṃ tārayāmāsurañjasā, Mah. v. 122. 16.;
    (2) uddharati or -dhārayati (hṛhṛ, c. 1. and its c., accord- -ing to its sense), one s. himself: uddharedātmanā- tmānam, V. c.;
    (3) trāti or trāyate, pari (trātrātra, c. 2. or traitrai, c. 1.), s. (me), Lord: trāhi trāhi jagannātha.

    (2) rakṣati, pari-, saṃ-, (rakṣrakṣ, c. 1.), s. him from Bhima: rakṣainaṃ bhīmāt, Ve. iii.: v. Also to protect, deliver.
    III To lay by:
    (1) saṃcinoti (cicicin, c. 5.);
    (2) rakṣati, saṃ-.
    IV To spare: q. v.
