    (3) śilā (not applied to precious s. s: but applied also to s. for grinding), sitting on another s.: dvitīyaśilā- talopaviṣṭaḥ, K.; like a (grinding) s. of gold: kanakaśilānibhaḥ, Ki. xvii. 63.;
    (4) pāṣāṇaḥ (=3.);
    (5) prastaraḥ (=3);
    (6) grāvan (m. = 3);
    (7) dṛṣad (f.: rare).

    V A weight: *saptaseram.
