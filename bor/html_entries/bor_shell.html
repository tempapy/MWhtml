• SHELL [636] (subs.):
    I In gen.:
    (1) koṣaḥ, long-s. ed (a mollusk) dīrghakoṣikā, A. ; egg-s.: aṇḍa- koṣaḥ;
    (2) kañcukaḥ (hard s., as of the cocoa- -nut);
    (3) tvac (f. = bark, as of the pome- -granate), N. xxii. 14. Ph.: conch-s: śaṅkhaḥ, s.-dealer: śaṅkhakāraḥ or śāṅkhikaḥ; bivalve-s.: kambu (mn.); oyster-s.: śuktiḥ; bazar-s. (used as a coin): kapardaḥ, -kam.
    II Bomb: q. v.: *āgneya- gulī.
    III Fig.: kañcukaḥ, clad in false s. of religion: kapaṭadharmakañcukaḥ, D. i.
  • SHELL [636] (v.): koṣam or kañcukam or tvacam apanayati (nī, c. 1.) or nirharati (hṛ, c. 1.), unmuñcati or mocayati (muc, c. 6. and 10.), etc.
