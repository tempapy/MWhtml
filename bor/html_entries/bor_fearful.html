• FEARFUL [271] (adj.):
    I Inspiring fear:
    (1) bhayaṅkaraḥ (rīrīri, raṃ);
    (2) bhayāvahaḥbhayāvaha (hāhāha, haṃ);
    (3) bhayadaḥbhayada (dādāda, daṃ);
    (4) bhayadāyinbhayadāyin or -vidhāyin (f. nīnīni);
    (5) bhayānakaḥbhayānaka (kākāka, kaṃ);
    (6) bhīṣaṇaḥbhīṣaṇa (ṇāṇa, ṇaṃ): v. Dreadful, terrible.
    II Full of fear:
    (1) bhītaḥbhīta (tātāta, taṃ) (with abl. of the source of fear): v. Afraid;
    (2) sabhayaḥsabhaya (yāyāyaiī, yaṃ) (= possess- -ed of fear);
    (3) bhayāturaḥbhayātura (rārāra, raṃ) (= habi- -tually);
    (4) trasnutrasnu (mfn.): v. Timid.
