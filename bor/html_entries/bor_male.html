• MALE [432] (adj.):
    I To indicate males:
    (1) expr. by the m. gender, a m. tiger: vyāghraḥ;
    (2) puṃ-, in comp. (rare), a m. cuckoo: puṃsko- kilaḥ, Ku.
    II Belonging to a m.:
    (1) puṃ-, female and. m. union: strīpuṃsaṃyogaḥ, N. s.;
    (2) the m. organ: puruṣāṅgam.
  • MALE [432] (subs.):
    (1) pumān, of the m. and the female: strīpuṃsayoḥ, D. ;
    (2) puruṣaḥpuruṣa. N.B. When standing for a m. animal, better expr. by the word itself, she said to the m.: sā ṭiṭṭi- bhamūce, P.
