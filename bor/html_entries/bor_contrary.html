• CONTRARY [150] (adj.):
    I In direction etc.:
    (1) pratipratipratī in comp., driven by c. wind: prativātapreritaḥ (tātāta, taṃ), D.;
    (2) pratīpaḥpratīpa, vi-, (pāpāpa, paṃ), c. wind: pratīpapavanaḥ, R. xi. 62.
    II Opposite:
    (1) viparītaḥviparīta (tātāta, taṃ), but it is c. with learned men: viparītantu vipaścitām, M. n.; as you are of a c. mind: yato viparītabuddhirasi P.;
    (2) viruddhaḥviruddha (ddhā, ddhaṃ) (=contradictory).
  • CONTRARY [151] (subs.):
    (1) viparītamviparīta, in saying that the weak are incurable we get its c.: kṛśā duścikitsyā ityukte viparītaṃ gṛhyate;
    (2) viparyayaḥviparyaya, on the c.: tadviparyayeṇa, S.;
    (3) prātilomyamprātilomya, Sr.
