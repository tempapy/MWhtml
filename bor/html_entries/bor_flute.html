• FLUTE [286] (subs.):
    I A pipe:

    (2) vaṃśaḥvaṃśa, -śī;
    (3) muralīmuralī, enchanted with the music of the f.: muralīnādamohitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ).
    II A channel, groove: rekhārekhārekha (?).
  • FLUTE [286] (v.):
    I Intrans: veṇuṃ pūrayati (pūrpūr, c. 10.) etc.
    II Trans: gāyatigāyat (gaigai, c. 1.): v. To sing.
