• WISE: [764]
    I Having wisdom:
    (1) prājña (f. jñā);
    (2) vijña (f. jñā);
    (3) prajñāvat (f. tī);
    (4) dhīmat (f. tī): v. Intelligent;
    (5) jñānin (f. nī): v. Learned;
    (6) vivekin (f. nī): v. Prudent.
    II Of acts etc.: w. saying: budhabhāṣitam; w. action: jñānamūlā kriyā; w. intellect: vivekinī matiḥ; w. deportment: vivekavyavahāraḥ; etc.
  • WISE [764] (subs.): v. Manner, way. Ph.: in no w.: na kathañcana; in any wise: kenāpi prakāreṇa.
