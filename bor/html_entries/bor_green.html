• GREEN [324] (adj.):
    I Of colour:
    (1) haritaḥharitaharit (tātāta, tāṃ) or harit (mfn.), about g. paddy: harite dhānye, M. viii. 330.;
    (2) palāśaḥpalāśa (śāśa, śaṃ) (rare), a wood g. like new leaves: navapalāśapalāśavanam, Si. vi. 2.
    II Fresh: q.v.: g. manhood: navavayas; g. crop: navaśsyam.
    III Raw, immature: q.v.: apariṇataḥ (tātāta, taṃ).
    IV Not dry, as of g. wood: ārdraārdra. (f. rdrā).
  • GREEN [324] (subs.):
    I The colour:
    (1) haritaḥharitaharit;
    (2) haridvarṇaḥharidvarṇa;
    (3) palāśaḥpalāśa (rare).
    II A grassy plain: śādvalasthalī: v. Also grassy.
    III In pl., vegetables: śākamśāka.
  • GREEN, TO BE [324] or BECOME: [324] haritāyate (nomi.) (rare).
