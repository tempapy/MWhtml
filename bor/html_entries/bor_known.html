• KNOWN [404] (adj.): i. e. celebrated, renowned: q.v.: prasiddhaprasiddha (f. ddhā). Well-k.: suprasiddha (f. ddhā). Ill-k.: aprasiddha (f. ddhā).
  • KNOWN, [404] BE: [404]
    (1) by jñātaḥjñāta (tātāta, taṃ), is it k. that: jñāto nu kim, Mr. ix. 9.;
    (2) by viditaḥvidita (tātāta, taṃ), be it k. to your excellent Majesty: viditamastu deva- pādānām, Sa. vi.;
    (3) by prasiddhaprasiddha (f. ddhā) (of what is well-k.), if God is well-k. from peo- ples' souls: yadi loke brahmātmatvena prasiddhamasti, S. i. 1.
  • KNOWN, [404] BECOME: [404] prathate (prathprath, c. 1.), afterwards k. among people by her name: prajāsu paścātprathitaṃ tadākhyayā, Ku. v. 7.
  • KNOWN, [404] MAKE: [404]
    I To disclose: q.v.: prakāśī- karoti.
    II To inform: q. v.: vijñāpayati (c. of jñājñājña).
