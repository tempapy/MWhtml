• QUENCDH: [565]
    I To put out, extinguish: q. v.: nirvāpayati (c. of vāvāvauū).
    II Of thirst:
    (1) śamayati, pra-, (c. of śamśamśaśaṃ = to allay), thirst is q. ed with water: pipāsutā śāntimupaiti vāriṇā, N.
    (2) nivartayati (c. of vṛtvṛtvṛdh = to destroy);
    (3) nirasyati (asasa, c. 4. = to drive away);
    (4) apanayati (nīnīni, c. 1.) = to remove).
    III To destroy, crush: q. v.: ut-chinatti (chidchid, c. 7.).
