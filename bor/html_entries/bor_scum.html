• SCUM [621] (subs.):
    I Lit.:
    (1) maṇḍamaṇḍa (mn.), poured off rice-s.: annamaṇḍamagālayat, D. vi.;
    (2) phenaḥphena (= froth).
    II Fig. malaḥmala, the Vāhikas are the s. of the world: malaṃ pṛthivyā vāhīkāḥ, Mah. viii. 45. 23.
  • SCUM [621] (v.): maṇḍaṃ gālayati (c. of galgal), srāvayati (c. of srusrusrū), etc.
