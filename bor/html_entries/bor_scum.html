• SCUM [621] (subs.):
    I Lit.:
    (1) maṇḍa (mn.), poured off rice-s.: annamaṇḍamagālayat, D. vi.;
    (2) phenaḥ (= froth).
    II Fig. malaḥ, the Vāhikas are the s. of the world: malaṃ pṛthivyā vāhīkāḥ, Mah. viii. 45. 23.
  • SCUM [621] (v.): maṇḍaṃ gālayati (c. of gal), srāvayati (c. of sru), etc.
