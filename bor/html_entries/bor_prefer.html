• PREFER: [539]
    I To offer, address: to p. a charge: abhiyogaṃ karoti or nivedayati (= to inform).
    II To advance, raise: q. v.: unnamayati (c. of namnamna), p. your friends: unnamaya svabandhuvargam, Ki.
    III To regard more: bahubahu (much) or adhikam (more) manyate (manmanmat, c. 4.): v. Also to excel.
    IV To like more: adhikaṃ rocate (rucruc, c. 1.: with dat. of the person): v. Also better.
