• HAUGHTY: [337]
    (1) uddhataḥ (tā, taṃ), struck with the h. words of the Kirāta: kirātasya vacobhiruddhataiḥ parāhataḥ, Ki. xiv. i.;
    (2) utsiktaḥ (ktā, ktaṃ), on the one hand, from the arrival of the h. mine of penances and powers: utsiktasya tapaḥparākramanidherabhyā- gamādekataḥ, Vi. ii. 22: v. Also arrogant, proud, insolent.
