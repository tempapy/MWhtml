• ROOT [604] (subs.):
    I Lit.: bhūlam, r. of a tree: tarumūlamtarumūla, Sa.; a hurricane does not pluck up grass by the r.: tṛṇāni nonmūlayati prabhañjanaḥ, H. ii. 81. Ph.: Fibrous r.: śiphā or jaṭā; bulbous r.: kandakanda (mn.).
    II Fig.: mūlammūla, the r. of faults: mūlaṃ doṣasya, Ki. xi. 20.; with pure progeny from the r.: āmūlaśuddhasantati, Sa. vi. 28.; destroying by the r.: samūlaghātamaghnantaḥ, Si. ii. 33.; firmly taken r.: sthirabaddhamūla (f. lālāla), B. xi. 5.: v. Also cause, source.
    III In arith.: mūlammūla, cube r.: ghanamūlam, Li.; to extract a r.: mūlī- karoti, A.c.
    IV Of words: prakṛtiḥprakṛti. Ph.: verbal r.: dhātuḥ; secondary r.: nāmadhātuḥ; names are derived from r. s: nāma ca dhātujam, Kāśikā.
  • ROOT [604] (v.): mūlaṃ badhnāti (bandhbandh, c. 9.). R. ed:
    (1) baddhamūlabaddhamūla (f. lālāla);
    (2) rūḍhamūlarūḍhamūla (f. lālāla), M.n. on Si. i. 38. R. up or out: unmūlayati.
