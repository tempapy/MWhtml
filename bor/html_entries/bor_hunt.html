• HUNT [356] (v. t.):
    I Lit.: mṛgayate (mṛgmṛg, c. 10.) or mṛgayāṃ karoti or carati (carcar, c. 1.), Ram.
    II Fig.: mṛgayate, a gem does not seek, but it is h. ed: na ratnamanviṣyati mṛgyate hi tat, Sa.: v. To search.
  • HUNT, HUNTING [356] (subs.):
    (1) mṛgayāmṛgayāmṛgayamṛgāmṛga, there is nothing so beneficial as h. yathā mṛgayā hyaupakārikī na tathānyat, D. viii.; to go out (for) h.: mṛgayāṃ yāti, Mah.; devoted to h.: mṛgayāśīlaḥ (lālāla, laṃ), Sa. ii.;
    (2) ākheṭaḥākheṭa, -kaḥ, deer. h.: hariṇākheṭkaḥ, K. s. ix. 74.;
    (3) mṛgavyammṛgavya (rare), h.-forest: mṛgavyasatram, Ki. xiii. 9.
