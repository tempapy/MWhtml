• STEADY: [674]
    I In gen.:
    (1) sthira (f. rā = cons- -tant, fixed), s. minded: sthiradhīḥ, R. viii. 22.; one whose actions are steady: sthirakarman (mfn.);
    (2) dṛḍha (f. ḍhā = firm: q. v.).
    II Of men: dhīra (f. rā). Ph.: to lead a s. life: na vyabhicarati, (car, c. 1.) or sanmārge tiṣṭhati (sthā, c. 1.).
