• REMEMBER: [586]
    I To recollect: smarati, saṃ-, anu-, (smṛsmṛ, c. l.: with acc. or gen.), I do not r. having accepted this lady: na khalu svīkaraṇamatrabhavatyāḥ smarāmi, Sa. v.; r. ing the powers of the son of Indra: anusmṛtākhaṇḍalasūnuvikramaḥ, Ki. i. 24.: v. Also to think of.
    II To keep in mind:
    (1) smarati, r. what you say: smarasi khalvetadātmano vacanam, V. i.;
    (2) hṛdaye nidadhāti (dhādhādha, c. 3.) and sim. expr. s, N. ii. 47.
    III To remind: q. v.
