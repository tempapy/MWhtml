• FLOWER [285] (subs.):
    I In gen. sense:
    (1) puṣpampuṣpa, the stars, the f.s of heaven: tārāḥ khapuṣpāṇi, N.;
    (2) kusumamkusuma, f. -bed: kusumāstaraṇam, Ku.;
    (3) prasūnaṃ or prasavaḥprasavaprasu (rare), coming out of f.s: prasūnasyāgamaḥ, U. In f.: kusumitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ) or puṣpitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ), Mah.
    II Best part: sāramāgaḥ. F. of life: prauḍhayauvanam (?).
    III Menstrual discharge: puṣpampuṣpa.
  • FLOWER [285] (v.): expr. by subs., ever f. ing: nitya- puṣpa (f. ṣpā), Me. v. Also to blossom.
