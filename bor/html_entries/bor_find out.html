• FIND OUT: [277]
    I Lit.: upalabhate: v. To find.
    II To discover: jānāti (jñā, c. 9. =to know: q.v.), f. out the cause of his anxiety: jānīhi tasyotkaṇṭhākāraṇam, V. ii.
    III To solve: expr. by vadati (vad, c. 1.), bravīti (brū, c. 2.), etc. (= to say), f. out. the amount of their money: tayorvada dhane mama kiṃpramāṇe, Vij.; f. out how many pearls this necklace is made of: kathaya katipayairmaukti- kaireṣa hāraḥ, Li.
