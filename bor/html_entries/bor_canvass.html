• CANVASS [101] (v.):
    I To solicit for votes: prārthayate (arthaartha, c. 10.) or yācate (yācyāc, c. l)
    II To sift, examine: q. v. parīkṣate (īkṣīkṣ c. l.)
    III To discuss: q. v.: vicārayati (c. of carcar).
  • CANVASS, CANVASSING [101] (subs.):
    I Inspection: q. v.: parīkṣāparīkṣāparīkṣ.
    II Discussion: q. v.: vicāraḥvicāra.
    III Solicitation for votes: *mataprārthanā or simply prārthanāprārthanāprārthana (when the context determines the meaning).
