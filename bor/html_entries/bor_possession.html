• POSSESSION: [533]
    I In law: bhogaḥbhoga, sambhogaḥ, paribhogaḥ, (rarely) bhuktiḥbhukti (= enjoyment), p. with title of long standing, without interruption, without dispute: sāgamo dīrghakālo nirantaro nirākrośo bhogaḥ, Mi. on Y. ii. 28.; p. is a proof of ownership: bhogasya svatve prāmāṇyam, Mi.; adhikāraḥadhikāra (?). To take p. of:
    (1) adhikaroti (?);
    (2) gṛhṇāti, pari- (grahgrah, c. 9.), another crocodile has taken p. of your house: tvadīyaṃ gṛhamapareṇa makareṇa gṛhītam, P. iv. 9.
    II That which is possessed:
    (1) bhuktiḥbhukti;
    (2) by circumlo.
    III By evil spirits: āveśaḥāveśa.
