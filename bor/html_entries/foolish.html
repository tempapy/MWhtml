• FOOLISH: [288]
    I Silly: mūḍhaḥ (ḍhā, ḍhaṃ), f. (men) are troubled with passion: mūḍho madanenāyāsyate, K.: v. Also unwise.
    II Of acts, etc.:
    (1) vāliśaḥ (śā, śaṃ) (=children), attachment to f. acts: vāliśacariteṣvāsaktiḥ, K.;
    (2) nirarthakaḥ (kā, kaṃ) (= useless: q.v.).
    III Ridiculous: q.v.
