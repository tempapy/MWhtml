• GHASTLY: [311]
    I Lit.: bhūtasarūpaḥ (pāpāpa, paṃ); śavasyeva (= as of a corpse), Mah.
    II Pale, dismal: vivarṇavivarṇa (f. rṇā), these (ghosts) with long g. bodies: ete vivarṇadirghadehāḥ, Ma. v. 15.
    III Horrible, dreadful:
    (1) karālaḥkarāla (lālāla, laṃ);
    (2) vikaṭaḥvikaṭa (ṭāṭāṭa, ṭaṃ).
