• AIRY [020] (adj.):
    I Consisting of air.
    (1) vāyavaḥ (vī, vaṃ);
    (2) vāyumayaḥ (yī, yaṃ).
    II Relating to air: Ph. took an a. flight viyadutpapāta, N. or vihāyasā jagāma; the a. regions vāyuvartman, pravahamārgaḥ.
    III Open to air: suvātaḥ (tā, taṃ).
    IV Vain, trifling:
    (1) śūnyaḥ (nyā, nyaṃ);
    (2) asāraḥ (rā, raṃ). V. Gay, lively: q. v.
