• HEARING [339] (subs.):
    I The act: śravaṇamśravaṇa, from first h.: prathamaśravāṇāt, M. n.: v. To hear.
    II The faculty;
    (1) śravaṇaśaktiḥ;
    (2) śrutiḥśruti.
    III An audience: q.v.: darśanamdarśana.
    IV Judicial: darśanamdarśana, of not h. or of h. improperly: adarśanānyathādarśanayoḥ, Mit.
    V Reach of the ear.: śravaṇaviṣayaḥśravaṇaviṣaya. Ph.: within h.: śravaṇagocaraḥ (rārāra, raṃ); out of h.: asaṃśravaḥ (vāvāvauū, vaṃ); going beyond the h. of a cow's lowing: gorutāntaramatikramya, D.v.
