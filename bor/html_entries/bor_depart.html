    (1) pratiṣṭhate, sam-, (sthāsthāstha, c. l.) (on a journey), d. ed for Hariprastha: hariprasthaṃ pratasthe, Si. iii. 1.;

To leave, one whose life is d. ed: vigatajīvitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ), K. ; to d. this life (=to die: q. v.): paralokaṃ gacchati.
    II Ph.: did not d. a line from the beaten track: kṣuṇṇādvartmano rekhāmātramapi navyatīyuḥ, R.; d. ed from virtue: dharmabhraṣṭaḥ (ṣṭā, ṣṭaṃ).
