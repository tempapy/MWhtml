• GOVERN: [320]
    I As a country:
    (1) śāstiśāsti, pra-, anu- (śāsśāsśaśas, c. 2.), g. the world: śādhi pṛthvīm, B. iii. 53.;
    (2) pālayati, pari. (pālpāl, c. 10.) (= to protect; not to regulate), let kings g. the earth always standing by virtue: rājānaḥ paripālayantu vasudhāṃ dharme sthitāḥ sarvadā, Ma. x.;
    (3) by subs. pramuḥ, īśvaraḥ, etc.: v. Lord, master.
    II To control: q.v.: niyacchati (yamyamya, c. 1.).
    III In gram.: expr. by saṃbadhyate (pass. of bandhbandh) or anvīyate (pass of iiīin) (= to agree: with ins.), Da.; or by karmankarman (n.) (= accusative).
