• MARK [435] (subs.):
    I A sign, character: cihnamcihna, m. of one condemned to death: vadhyacihnam, Na.
    II Characteristic:
    (1) cihnamcihna;
    (2) lakṣaṇamlakṣaṇa;
    (3) vyañjanamvyañjana.
    III Butt.: lakṣyam(kṣam), hitting (lit. piercing) the m.: lakṣyabhedaḥ.
    IV A coin: mudrāviśeṣaḥ. Ph.: (a man) of m.: labdhavarṇa (f. rṇā); to make a m.: *lakṣaṇaṃ sthāpayati (c. of sthāsthāstha).
  • MARK [436] (v.):
    I Lit.:
    (1) cihnayati (nomi.), pari-, -saṃ-, m. ed with one's own hand: svahastaparicihnitaḥ (tātāta, taṃ), Y. ii. 95.;
    (2) cihnaṃ karoti, Si.;
    (3) aṅkayati (with lines, etc.), m. ed by the ocean: sāgarāṅkitā (mahīmahīmahimahinmah), Mah.
    II To indicate: q. v.: sūcayati.
    III To attend to, regard: q. v.: lakṣayati, abhi-, upa-, saṃ-, (lakṣlakṣ, c. 10.).
