• SERVICE: [630]
    I Lit.: sevāsevāseva, seeking money by s.: sevayā dhanamicchadbhiḥ, H. ii. 20.; not acquainted with s. (how to serve): sevānabhijñaḥ, H; by s., by agriculture: nṛpasevayā kṛṣikarmaṇā, P.; I have done fruitless s.: sevā kṛtā niṣphalā, J. Ph.: I am quite at your s.: yathecchaṃ niyujyatāmayaṃ janaḥ.
    II Work: karmankarman (n.,) pay is the price for s.: vetanaṃ karmamūlyam, Mit.; military s.: sainikakarmanṃ (n.); to be in s.: karma karoti; out of s.: niṣkarman (mfn.).
    III Help:
    (1) upakāraḥupakāra (= benefit), to render s. to friends: suhṛdāmupakāra- kāraṇāt, H. ii. 35.;
    (2) sāhāyyamsāhāyya (= assistance).
    IV Use: q. v.: upayogaḥupayoga.
    V Religious: sevāsevāseva, divine s.: īśvarasevā (after harisaṃkīrtanamharisaṃkīrtana of the Vaiṣṇavas).
    VI Of vessels: silver s.: rajata- bhājanāni (n. pl.).
    VII In law: perh. nirvāhaḥnirvāha.
