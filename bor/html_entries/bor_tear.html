    (2) bāṣpaḥbāṣpa, -jalam, etc., the throat almost choked with t.s: bāṣpa- kuṇṭhakaṇṭhaḥ, D. v.; drew t. s in the shape of sap from boughs; akarot......srutaśākhārasabāṣpadūṣitān, R. viii. 70.;
    (3) asram(śram), blinded by t.s: asrāndhatayā, R. xiv. 2.;
    (4) nayanajalamnayanajala, and sim. comp.s.
