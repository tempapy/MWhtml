    I To hunt, pursue: q. v.: mṛgyati or mṛgayate (mṛgmṛg, c. 4 and 10).
    II To chase away or off: i. e. to drive away: q. v.: nirasyati (asasa, c. 4.).
    III To engrave: q. v.
  • CHASE [115] (subs.):
    I Hunting: q. v.: mṛgayāmṛgayāmṛgayamṛgāmṛga.
    II Pursuit: q. v.: vimārgaṇamvimārgaṇa.
    III An open hunting ground: anāvṛtā mṛgayābhūmiḥ.
    IV That which is chased: mṛgyaḥmṛgyamṛgī (gyāgī, gyaṃ).
