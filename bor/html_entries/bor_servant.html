    (1) bhṛtyaḥbhṛtya, engages a s. without fixing his wages: vetanaparicchedamakṛtvaiva bhṛtyaṃ kārayati, Mit.; you (are our) master, we (are) s. s: vayaṃ bhṛtyā bhavān svāmī, M.p.;
