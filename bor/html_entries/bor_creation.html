• CREATION: [162]
    I Lit.:
    (1) sṛṣṭiḥsṛṣṭi;
    (2) sargaḥsarga, the sole cause of the c. continuation and destruction of the world: viśvasya sargasthitipralayānāmekaṃ kāraṇam;
    (3) sṛjanamsṛjana.
    II Fig., as of a poet:
    (1) sṛṣṭiḥsṛṣṭi;
    (2) nirmāṇamnirmāṇa or nirmitiḥ.
    III A thing created: sṛṣṭiḥsṛṣṭi, as if the first c. of God as regards women: yuvativiṣaye sṛṣṭirādye va dhātuḥ, Me. ii. 21.
    IV Appointment: q. v.
