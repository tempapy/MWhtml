• JOIN [397] (v. t.:)
    I To unite: q.v.: yunakti or yoja- yati, saṃ-, ā-, (yujyuj, c. 7. and 10.), you have j. ed yourself in great penances: āyojijtastapasi mahatyātmā, K.
    II To come to go over: gacchatigacchat (gamgamga, c. 1.). yātiyātiyāt (yāyāyaiī, c. 2.), etieti (iiīin, c. 2.), etc., j. ed Yudhiṣṭhira with his forces: balenāgāduyudhiṣṭhiram. Dhṛṣṭaketu j. ed the sons of Pāṇḍu: dhṛṣṭaketyrupāga- cchat pāṇḍavān; with the hill-chiefs, j. ed the sons of Pāṇḍu: pārvatīyairmahīpālaiḥ sahitaḥ pāṇḍavāniyāt; j. ed Duryodhana: duryodhanamupayātau, Mah. v. 19. 1-17.; to j. battle: raṇaṃ gacchati, V. p.
