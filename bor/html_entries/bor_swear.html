• SWEAR [695] (v. t.):
    I To make: to s. an oath: śapathaṃ śapati or śapyati-te (śapśap, c. 1. and 4.), B. iii. 36.
    II To administer in oath to: śāpayati (c. of śapśap), shall s. Brahmins on truth: satyena śāpayedvipram, M.
  • SWEAR [695] (v. i.): śapati or śapyati, who have sworn together: saṃśaptakāḥ, Mah. vii.: v. Also to curse;
    (2) śapathaṃ karoti (=take an oath), swore before her friends: sakhīnāṃ purataḥ saśapatha- mabhihitavatī, K.
