• SCENT [617] (subs.):
    I The sense of smell: ghrāṇamghrāṇa : v. Smell.
    II Smell, odour:
    (1) gandhaḥgandhagandh, with the mere s.: gandhamātreṇa, Sa. iii.; sweet s. ed: sugandhin (mfn.), Bha.; therefore it is not consonant to implicate here even the s. of action: tasmāt kriyāyā gandhamātrasyāpyanupraveśa iha noppadyate, S.;
    (2) saurabhamsaurabha (= fragrance: q. v.), of uncommon s.: asādhāraṇasaurabhaḥ (bhābhābha, bhaṃ), K.
  • SCENT [617] (v.):
    I To smell: q. v.: ghrātighrāti, ā- (ghrāghrā, c. 2.).
    II To perfume: q. v.:
    (1) vāsayati (vāsvāsvavāvasuū, c. 10.);
    (2) surabhīkaroti, s. ed: surabhitaḥsurabhita, Me. i. 53.
