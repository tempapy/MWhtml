• DECLARE [179] (v. t.):
    I To clear: q. v.
    II To assert: q. v.: bravīti or brūte (brūbrū, c. 2.).
    III To make known, manifest: q. v.: bravīti or brūte, for good men d. their use by results: bruvate hi phalena sādhavo nijopayogitām, N.
    IV. To proclaim: q. v.: ghoṣayati (c. of ghaṣa).
  • DECLARE [179] (v. i.): bravīti v. To say. To d. for: avalambate. To d. against: pratipakṣam avalambate (lamblamb, c. l.).
