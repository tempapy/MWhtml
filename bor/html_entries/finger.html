• FINGER [278] (subs.): aṅguliḥ (lī), the fore f.: tarjanī; the middle f.: madhyamā; a ring for the middle f.: madhyamo- palaḥ; Ki. ix. 2.; the third or ring f.: anāmikā; the little f.: kaniṣṭhā. Measuring two f. s in breadth: dvyaṅgulaḥ (lā, laṃ). To snap or crack the f. s: aṅgulisphoṭanaṃ or -moṭanaṃ karoti, M. Five-f. ed wood: pañcāṅgulaṃ dāru, S. k.
  • FINGER [278] (v.t.):
    I Lit.: aṅgulyā spṛśati (spṛś, c. 1.).
    II To pilfer: perh. aṅguliṃ cālayati (c. of cal) (with loc.).
