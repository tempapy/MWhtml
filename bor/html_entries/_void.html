• VOID [750] (adj.):
    I Empty, vacant: q. v.: śūnya (f. nyā).
    II Devoid: q. v.: v. of anything: akiñcana (f. nā).
    III Null, invalid:
    (1) nirarthaka (f. kā);
    (2) akiñcitkara (f. rā), asiddha (f. ddhā). Ph.: such a document is v.: tadapramāṇaṃ likhitam, N. s.
  • VOID [750] (subs.):
    (1) śūnyam:
    (2) riktam;
    (3) śūnyaṃ sthānam; etc.
  • VOID [750] (v.):
    I To empty: q. v.: śūnyīkaroti.
    II To nullify: niṣphalīkaroti.
    III To emit, discharge: q. v.: utsṛjati (sṛj, c. 6.). Ph.: to v. excrements:
    (1) guvati (gu, c. 6.);
    (2) hadati (had, c. 1.).
