• CHILD: [118]
    I A boy or girl: q. v.:
    (1) śiśuḥ;
    (2) arbhakaḥ (rare);
    (3) pṛthukaḥ (rare);
    (4) ḍimbhaḥ (rare).
    II With reference to parents, a son or daughter: q. v.:
    (1) apatyam;
    (2) sūnuḥ (mf.);
    (3) santānaḥ;
    (4) tokam (rare). In addres- -sing children vatsa (m.) and vatsā (f.) is generally used. To be with c.: putriṇī, Mah: v. Pregnant.
