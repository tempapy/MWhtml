    (2) devī (in addressing), L. Kādambarī: devī kādambarī.

    (1) āryā;
    (2) gen. expr. by vanitā, etc., ladies go the way of their husbands: pramadāḥ pativartmagāḥ, Ku. iv. 33.: v. Woman.
